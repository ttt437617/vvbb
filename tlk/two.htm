Chapter 2: The Transcendental Matter and Supreme Yogī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Teachings of Lord Kapila, the Son of Devahūti

TLK 2: Śaunaka continued: There is no one who knows more than the Lord Himself. No one is more worshipable or more mature a yogī than Me. He is therefore the master of the Vedas, and to hear about Him always is the actual pleasure of the senses.
