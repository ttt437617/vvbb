Chapter 18: Devotional Service: The Final Perfection

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Teachings of Lord Kapila, the Son of Devahūti

TLK 43: The yogīs, equipped with transcendental knowledge and renunciation and engaged in devotional service for their eternal benefit, take shelter at My lotus feet, and since I am the Lord, they are thus eligible to enter into the kingdom of Godhead without fear.

TLK 44: Therefore persons whose minds are fixed on the Lord engage in the intensive practice of devotional service. That is the only means to attain the final perfection of life.
