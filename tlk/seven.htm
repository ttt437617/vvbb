Chapter 7: Lord Kapila Begins to Explain Self-realization

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Teachings of Lord Kapila, the Son of Devahūti

TLK 12: After hearing of His mother's uncontaminated desire for transcendental realization, the Lord thanked her within Himself for her questions, and thus, His face smiling, He explained the path of the transcendentalists, who are interested in self-realization.

TLK 13: The Personality of Godhead answered: That yoga system which relates to the Lord and the individual soul, which is meant for the ultimate benefit of the living entity, and which causes detachment from all happiness and distress in the material world, is the highest yoga system.
