Chapter 5: Lord Kapila Takes Charge of His Mother, Devahūti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Teachings of Lord Kapila, the Son of Devahūti

TLK 5: Maitreya said: When Kardama left for the forest, Lord Kapila stayed on the strand of the Bindu-sarovara to please His mother, Devahūti.
