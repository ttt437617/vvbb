Chapter 5: Hymn to the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Brahma-samhitā 5.25

tapas tvam tapa etena

tava siddhir bhavishyati

SYNONYMS

tapah — spiritual austerity; tvam — you; tapa — practice; etena — by this; tava — your; siddhih — fulfillment; bhavishyati — will be.

TRANSLATION

"O Brahmā, do thou practice spiritual association by means of this mantra; then all your desires will be fulfilled."

PURPORT

Its purport is clear.
