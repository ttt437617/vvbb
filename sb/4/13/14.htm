Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 13: Description of the Descendants of Dhruva Mahārāja

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.13.14

pradosho niśitho vyushta

iti doshā-sutās trayah

vyushtah sutam pushkarinyām

sarvatejasam ādadhe

SYNONYMS

pradoshah — Pradosha; niśithah — Niśitha; vyushtah — Vyushta; iti — thus; doshā — of Doshā; sutāh — sons; trayah — three; vyushtah — Vyushta; sutam — son; pushkarinyām — in Pushkarinī; sarva-tejasam — named Sarvatejā (all-powerful); ādadhe — begot.

TRANSLATION

Doshā had three sons — Pradosha, Niśitha and Vyushta. Vyushta's wife was named Pushkarinī, and she gave birth to a very powerful son named Sarvatejā.
