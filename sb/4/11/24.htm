Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 11: Svāyambhuva Manu Advises Dhruva Mahārāja to Stop Fighting

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.11.24

na caite putraka bhrātur

hantāro dhanadānugāh

visargādānayos tāta

pumso daivam hi kāranam

SYNONYMS

na — never; ca — also; ete — all these; putraka — my dear son; bhrātuh — of your brother; hantārah — killers; dhanada — of Kuvera; anugāh — followers; visarga — of birth; ādānayoh — of death; tāta — my dear son; pumsah — of a living entity; daivam — the Supreme; hi — certainly; kāranam — the cause.

TRANSLATION

My dear son, those Yakshas, who are descendants of Kuvera, are not actually the killers of your brother; the birth and death of every living entity are caused by the Supreme, who is certainly the cause of all causes.
