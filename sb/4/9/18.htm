Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 9: Dhruva Mahārāja Returns Home

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.9.18

maitreya uvāca

athābhishtuta evam vai

sat-sańkalpena dhīmatā

bhrityānurakto bhagavān

pratinandyedam abravīt

SYNONYMS

maitreyah uvāca — Maitreya said; atha — then; abhishtutah — being worshiped; evam — thus; vai — certainly; sat-sańkalpena — by Dhruva Mahārāja, who had only good desires in his heart; dhī-matā — because he was very intelligent; bhritya-anuraktah — very favorably disposed towards devotees; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; pratinandya — having congratulated him; idam — this; abravīt — said.

TRANSLATION

The great sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidura, when Dhruva Mahārāja, who had good intentions in his heart, finished his prayer, the Supreme Lord, the Personality of Godhead, who is very kind to His devotees and servants, congratulated him, speaking as follows.
