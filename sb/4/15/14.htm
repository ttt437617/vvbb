Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.14

tasmai jahāra dhanado

haimam vīra varāsanam

varunah salila-srāvam

ātapatram śaśi-prabham

SYNONYMS

tasmai — unto him; jahāra — presented; dhana-dah — the treasurer of the demigods (Kuvera); haimam — made of gold; vīra — O Vidura; vara-āsanam — royal throne; varunah — the demigod Varuna; salila-srāvam — dropping particles of water; ātapatram — umbrella; śaśi-prabham — as brilliant as the moon.

TRANSLATION

The great sage continued: My dear Vidura, Kuvera presented the great King Prithu with a golden throne. The demigod Varuna presented him with an umbrella that constantly sprayed fine particles of water and was as brilliant as the moon.
