Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.5

iyam ca sudatī devī

guna-bhūshana-bhūshanā

arcir nāma varārohā

prithum evāvarundhatī

SYNONYMS

iyam — this female child; ca — and; su-datī — who has very nice teeth; devī — the goddess of fortune; guna — by good qualities; bhūshana — ornaments; bhūshanā — who beautifies; arcih — Arci; nāma — by name; vara-ārohā — very beautiful; prithum — unto King Prithu; eva — certainly; avarundhatī — being very much attached.

TRANSLATION

The female has such beautiful teeth and beautiful qualities that she will actually beautify the ornaments she wears. Her name will be Arci. In the future she will accept King Prithu as her husband.
