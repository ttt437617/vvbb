Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.13

so 'bhishikto mahārājah

suvāsāh sādhv-alańkritah

patnyārcishālańkritayā

vireje 'gnir ivāparah

SYNONYMS

sah — the King; abhishiktah — being coronated; mahārājah — Mahārāja Prithu; su-vāsāh — exquisitely dressed; sādhu-alańkritah — highly decorated with ornaments; patnyā — along with his wife; arcishā — named Arci; alańkritayā — nicely ornamented; vireje — appeared; agnih — fire; iva — like; aparah — another.

TRANSLATION

Thus the great King Prithu, exquisitely dressed with garments and ornaments, was coronated and placed on the throne. The King and his wife, Arci, who was also exquisitely ornamented, appeared exactly like fire.
