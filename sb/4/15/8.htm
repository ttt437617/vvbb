Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.8

śańkha-tūrya-mridańgādyā

nedur dundubhayo divi

tatra sarva upājagmur

devarshi-pitrinām ganāh

SYNONYMS

śańkha — conches; tūrya — bugles; mridańga — drums; ādyāh — and so on; neduh — vibrated; dundubhayah — kettledrums; divi — in outer space; tatra — there; sarve — all; upājagmuh — came; deva-rishi — demigods and sages; pitrinām — of forefathers; ganāh — groups.

TRANSLATION

Conchshells, bugles, drums and kettledrums vibrated in outer space. Great sages, forefathers and personalities from the heavenly planets all came to earth from various planetary systems.
