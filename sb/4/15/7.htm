Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.7

maitreya uvāca

praśamsanti sma tam viprā

gandharva-pravarā jaguh

mumucuh sumano-dhārāh

siddhā nrityanti svah-striyah

SYNONYMS

maitreyah uvāca — the great saint Maitreya said; praśamsanti sma — praised, glorified; tam — him (Prithu); viprāh — all the brāhmanas; gandharva-pravarāh — the best of the Gandharvas; jaguh — chanted; mumucuh — released; sumanah-dhārāh — showers of flowers; siddhāh — the personalities from Siddhaloka; nrityanti — were dancing; svah — of the heavenly planets; striyah — women (the Apsarās).

TRANSLATION

The great sage Maitreya continued: My dear Vidurajī, at that time all the brāhmanas highly praised and glorified King Prithu, and the best singers of Gandharvaloka chanted his glories. The inhabitants of Siddhaloka showered flowers, and the beautiful women in the heavenly planets danced in ecstasy.
