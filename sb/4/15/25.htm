Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 15: King Prithu's Appearance and Coronation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.15.25

prabhavo hy ātmanah stotram

jugupsanty api viśrutāh

hrīmantah paramodārāh

paurusham vā vigarhitam

SYNONYMS

prabhavah — very powerful persons; hi — certainly; ātmanah — of themselves; stotram — praise; jugupsanti — do not like; api — although; viśrutāh — very famous; hrī-mantah — modest; parama-udārāh — very magnanimous persons; paurusham — powerful actions; vā — also; vigarhitam — abominable.

TRANSLATION

As a person with a sense of honor and magnanimity does not like to hear about his abominable actions, a person who is very famous and powerful does not like to hear himself praised.
