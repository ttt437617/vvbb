Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 8: Dhruva Mahārāja Leaves Home for the Forest

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.8.75

caturtham api vai māsam

dvādaśe dvādaśe 'hani

vāyu-bhaksho jita-śvāso

dhyāyan devam adhārayat

SYNONYMS

caturtham — fourth; api — also; vai — in that way; māsam — the month; dvādaśe dvādaśe — on the twelfth; ahani — day; vāyu — air; bhakshah — eating; jita-śvāsah — controlling the breathing process; dhyāyan — meditating; devam — the Supreme Lord; adhārayat — worshiped.

TRANSLATION

In the fourth month Dhruva Mahārāja became a complete master of the breathing exercise, and thus he inhaled air only every twelfth day. In this way he became completely fixed in his position and worshiped the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
