Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 8: Dhruva Mahārāja Leaves Home for the Forest

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.8.50

padbhyām nakha-mani-śrenyā

vilasadbhyām samarcatām

hrit-padma-karnikā-dhishnyam

ākramyātmany avasthitam

SYNONYMS

padbhyām — by His lotus feet; nakha-mani-śrenyā — by the light of the jewellike nails on the toes; vilasadbhyām — glittering lotus feet; samarcatām — persons who are engaged in worshiping them; hrit-padma-karnikā — the whorl of the lotus flower of the heart; dhishnyam — situated; ākramya — seizing; ātmani — in the heart; avasthitam — situated.

TRANSLATION

Real yogīs meditate upon the transcendental form of the Lord as He stands on the whorl of the lotus of their hearts, the jewellike nails of His lotus feet glittering.
