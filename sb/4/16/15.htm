Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 16: Praise of King Prithu by the Professional Reciters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.16.15

rañjayishyati yal lokam

ayam ātma-viceshtitaih

athāmum āhū rājānam

mano-rañjanakaih prajāh

SYNONYMS

rañjayishyati — will please; yat — because; lokam — the entire world; ayam — this King; ātma — personal; viceshtitaih — by activities; atha — therefore; amum — him; āhuh — they call; rājānam — the King; manah-rañjanakaih — very pleasing to the mind; prajāh — the citizens.

TRANSLATION

This King will please everyone by his practical activities, and all of his citizens will remain very satisfied. Because of this the citizens will take great satisfaction in accepting him as their ruling king.
