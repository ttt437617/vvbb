Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 5: Frustration of the Sacrifice of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.5.19

juhvatah sruva-hastasya

śmaśrūni bhagavān bhavah

bhrigor luluñce sadasi

yo 'hasac chmaśru darśayan

SYNONYMS

juhvatah — offering sacrificial oblations; sruva-hastasya — with the sacrificial ladle in his hand; śmaśrūni — the mustache; bhagavān — the possessor of all opulences; bhavah — Vīrabhadra; bhrigoh — of Bhrigu Muni; luluñce — tore out; sadasi — in the midst of the assembly; yah — who (Bhrigu Muni); ahasat — had smiled; śmaśru — his mustache; darśayan — showing.

TRANSLATION

Vīrabhadra tore off the mustache of Bhrigu, who was offering the sacrificial oblations with his hands in the fire.
