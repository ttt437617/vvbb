Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 5: Frustration of the Sacrifice of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.5.18

sarva evartvijo drishtvā

sadasyāh sa-divaukasah

tair ardyamānāh subhriśam

grāvabhir naikadhādravan

SYNONYMS

sarve — all; eva — certainly; ritvijah — the priests; drishtvā — after seeing; sadasyāh — all the members assembled in the sacrifice; sa-divaukasah — along with the demigods; taih — by those (stones); ardyamānāh — being disturbed; su-bhriśam — very greatly; grāvabhih — by stones; na ekadhā — in different directions; adravan — began to disperse.

TRANSLATION

There was a continuous shower of stones, and all the priests and other members assembled at the sacrifice were put into immense misery. For fear of their lives, they dispersed in different directions.
