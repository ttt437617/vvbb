Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 24: Chanting the Song Sung by Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.24.8

havirdhānād dhavirdhānī

vidurāsūta shat sutān

barhishadam gayam śuklam

krishnam satyam jitavratam

SYNONYMS

havirdhānāt — from Havirdhāna; havirdhānī — the name of the wife of Havirdhāna; vidura — O Vidura; asūta — gave birth; shat — six; sutān — sons; barhishadam — of the name Barhishat; gayam — of the name Gaya; śuklam — of the name Śukla; krishnam — of the name Krishna; satyam — of the name Satya; jitavratam — of the name Jitavrata.

TRANSLATION

Havirdhāna, the son of Mahārāja Antardhāna, had a wife named Havirdhānī, who gave birth to six sons, named Barhishat, Gaya, Śukla, Krishna, Satya and Jitavrata.
