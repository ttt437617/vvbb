Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 10: Dhruva Mahārāja's Fight With the Yakshas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.10.27

samudra ūrmibhir bhīmah

plāvayan sarvato bhuvam

āsasāda mahā-hrādah

kalpānta iva bhīshanah

SYNONYMS

samudrah — the sea; ūrmibhih — with waves; bhīmah — fierce; plāvayan — inundating; sarvatah — in all directions; bhuvam — the earth; āsasāda — came forward; mahā-hrādah — making great sounds; kalpa-ante — (the dissolution) at the end of a kalpa; iva — like; bhīshanah — fearful.

TRANSLATION

Then, as if it were the time of the dissolution of the whole world, the fierce sea with foaming waves and great roaring sounds came forward before him.
