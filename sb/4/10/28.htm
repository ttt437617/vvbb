Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 10: Dhruva Mahārāja's Fight With the Yakshas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.10.28

evam-vidhāny anekāni

trāsanāny amanasvinām

sasrijus tigma-gataya

āsuryā māyayāsurāh

SYNONYMS

evam-vidhāni — (phenomena) like this; anekāni — many varieties of; trāsanāni — fearful; amanasvinām — to the less intelligent men; sasrijuh — they created; tigma-gatayah — of heinous nature; āsuryā — demoniac; māyayā — by illusion; asurāh — the demons.

TRANSLATION

The demon Yakshas are by nature very heinous, and by their demoniac power of illusion they can create many strange phenomena to frighten one who is less intelligent.
