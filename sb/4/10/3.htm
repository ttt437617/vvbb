Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 10: Dhruva Mahārāja's Fight With the Yakshas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.10.3

uttamas tv akritodvāho

mrigayāyām balīyasā

hatah punya-janenādrau

tan-mātāsya gatim gatā

SYNONYMS

uttamah — Uttama; tu — but; akrita — without; udvāhah — marriage; mrigayāyām — on a hunting excursion; balīyasā — very powerful; hatah — was killed; punya-janena — by a Yaksha; adrau — on the Himalaya Mountains; tat — his; mātā — mother (Suruci); asya — of her son; gatim — way; gatā — followed.

TRANSLATION

Dhruva Mahārāja's younger brother Uttama, who was still unmarried, once went on a hunting excursion and was killed by a powerful Yaksha in the Himalaya Mountains. Along with him, his mother, Suruci, also followed the path of her son [she died].
