Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 10: Dhruva Mahārāja's Fight With the Yakshas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.10.7

tato nishkramya balina

upadeva-mahā-bhatāh

asahantas tan-ninādam

abhipetur udāyudhāh

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereafter; nishkramya — coming out; balinah — very powerful; upadeva — of Kuvera; mahā-bhatāh — great soldiers; asahantah — unable to tolerate; tat — of the conchshell; ninādam — sound; abhipetuh — attacked; udāyudhāh — equipped with various weapons.

TRANSLATION

O hero Vidura, the greatly powerful heroes of the Yakshas, unable to tolerate the resounding vibration of the conchshell of Dhruva Mahārāja, came forth from their city with weapons and attacked Dhruva.
