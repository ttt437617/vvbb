Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 2: Daksha Curses Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.2.17

maitreya uvāca

vinindyaivam sa giriśam

apratīpam avasthitam

daksho 'thāpa upaspriśya

kruddhah śaptum pracakrame

SYNONYMS

maitreyah uvāca — Maitreya said; vinindya — abusing; evam — thus; sah — he (Daksha); giriśam — Śiva; apratīpam — without any hostility; avasthitam — remaining; dakshah — Daksha; atha — now; apah — water; upaspriśya — washing hands and mouth; kruddhah — angry; śaptum — to curse; pracakrame — began to.

TRANSLATION

The sage Maitreya continued: Thus Daksha, seeing Lord Śiva sitting as if against him, washed his hands and mouth and cursed him in the following words.
