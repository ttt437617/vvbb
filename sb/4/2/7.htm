Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 2: Daksha Curses Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.2.7

sadasas-patibhir daksho

bhagavān sādhu sat-kritah

ajam loka-gurum natvā

nishasāda tad-ājñayā

SYNONYMS

sadasah — of the assembly; patibhih — by the leaders; dakshah — Daksha; bhagavān — the possessor of all opulences; sādhu — properly; sat-kritah — was welcomed; ajam — to the unborn (Brahmā); loka-gurum — to the teacher of the universe; natvā — making obeisances; nishasāda — sat down; tat-ājñayā — by his (Brahmā's) order.

TRANSLATION

Daksha was adequately welcomed by the president of the great assembly, Lord Brahmā. After offering Lord Brahmā respect, Daksha, by the order of Brahmā, properly took his seat.
