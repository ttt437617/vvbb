Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 2: Daksha Curses Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.2.5

tatra pravishtam rishayo

drishtvārkam iva rocishā

bhrājamānam vitimiram

kurvantam tan mahat sadah

SYNONYMS

tatra — there; pravishtam — having entered; rishayah — the sages; drishtvā — seeing; arkam — the sun; iva — just like; rocishā — with luster; bhrājamānam — shining; vitimiram — free from darkness; kurvantam — making; tat — that; mahat — great; sadah — assembly.

TRANSLATION

When Daksha, the leader of the Prajāpatis, entered that assembly, his personal bodily luster as bright as the effulgence of the sun, the entire assembly was illuminated, and all the assembled personalities became insignificant in his presence.
