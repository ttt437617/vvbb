Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.6

āśāsānā jīvitam adhvarasya

lokah sa-pālah kupite na yasmin

tam āśu devam priyayā vihīnam

kshamāpayadhvam hridi viddham duruktaih

SYNONYMS

āśāsānāh — wishing to ask; jīvitam — for the duration; adhvarasya — of the sacrifice; lokah — all the planets; sa-pālah — with their controllers; kupite — when angered; na — not; yasmin — whom; tam — that; āśu — at once; devam — Lord Śiva; priyayā — of his dear wife; vihīnam — having been deprived; kshamāpayadhvam — beg his pardon; hridi — in his heart; viddham — very much afflicted; duruktaih — by unkind words.

TRANSLATION

Lord Brahmā also advised them that Lord Śiva is so powerful that by his anger all the planets and their chief controllers can be destroyed immediately. Also, he said that Lord Śiva was especially sorry because he had recently lost his dear wife and was also very much afflicted by the unkind words of Daksha. Under the circumstances, Lord Brahmā suggested, it would behoove them to go at once and beg his pardon.
