Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.51

jīvatād yajamāno 'yam

prapadyetākshinī bhagah

bhrigoh śmaśrūni rohantu

pūshno dantāś ca pūrvavat

SYNONYMS

jīvatāt — let him be alive; yajamānah — the performer of the sacrifice (Daksha); ayam — this; prapadyeta — let him get back; akshinī — by the eyes; bhagah — Bhagadeva; bhrigoh — of the sage Bhrigu; śmaśrūni — mustache; rohantu — may grow again; pūshnah — of Pūshādeva; dantāh — the chain of teeth; ca — and; pūrva-vat — like before.

TRANSLATION

My dear lord, by your mercy the performer of the sacrifice (King Daksha) may get back his life, Bhaga may get back his eyes, Bhrigu his mustache, and Pūshā his teeth.
