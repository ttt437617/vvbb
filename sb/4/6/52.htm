Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.52

devānām bhagna-gātrānām

ritvijām cāyudhāśmabhih

bhavatānugrihītānām

āśu manyo 'stv anāturam

SYNONYMS

devānām — of the demigods; bhagna-gātrānām — whose limbs are badly broken; ritvijām — of the priests; ca — and; āyudha-aśmabhih — by weapons and by stones; bhavatā — by you; anugrihītānām — being favored; āśu — at once; manyo — O Lord Śiva (in an angry mood); astu — let there be; anāturam — recovery from injuries.

TRANSLATION

O Lord Śiva, may the demigods and the priests whose limbs have been broken by your soldiers recover from the injuries by your grace.
