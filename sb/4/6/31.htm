Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.31

vaidūrya-krita-sopānā

vāpya utpala-mālinīh

prāptam kimpurushair drishtvā

ta ārād dadriśur vatam

SYNONYMS

vaidūrya-krita — made of vaidūrya; sopānāh — staircases; vāpyah — lakes; utpala — of lotus flowers; mālinīh — containing rows; prāptam — inhabited; kimpurushaih — by the Kimpurushas; drishtvā — after seeing; te — those demigods; ārāt — not far away; dadriśuh — saw; vatam — a banyan tree.

TRANSLATION

They also saw that the bathing ghātas and their staircases were made of vaidūrya-mani. The water was full of lotus flowers. Passing by such lakes, the demigods reached a place where there was a great banyan tree.
