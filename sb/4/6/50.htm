Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.50

kurv adhvarasyoddharanam hatasya bhoh

tvayāsamāptasya mano prajāpateh

na yatra bhāgam tava bhāgino daduh

kuyājino yena makho ninīyate

SYNONYMS

kuru — just execute; adhvarasya — of the sacrifice; uddharanam — complete regularly; hatasya — killed; bhoh — O; tvayā — by you; asamāptasya — of the unfinished sacrifice; mano — O Lord Śiva; prajāpateh — of Mahārāja Daksha; na — not; yatra — where; bhāgam — share; tava — your; bhāginah — deserving to take the share; daduh — did not give; ku-yājinah — bad priests; yena — by the bestower; makhah — sacrifice; ninīyate — gets the result.

TRANSLATION

My dear Lord Śiva, you are a shareholder of a portion of the sacrifice, and you are the giver of the result. The bad priests did not deliver your share, and therefore you destroyed everything, and the sacrifice remains unfinished. Now you can do the needful and take your rightful share.
