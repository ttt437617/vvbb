Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.11

nānāmala-prasravanair

nānā-kandara-sānubhih

ramanam viharantīnām

ramanaih siddha-yoshitām

SYNONYMS

nānā — various; amala — transparent; prasravanaih — with waterfalls; nānā — various; kandara — caves; sānubhih — with summits; ramanam — giving pleasure; viharantīnām — sporting; ramanaih — with their lovers; siddha-yoshitām — of the damsels of the mystics.

TRANSLATION

There are many waterfalls, and in the mountains there are many beautiful caves in which the very beautiful wives of the mystics are found.
