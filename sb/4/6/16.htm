Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.16

svarnārna-śata-patraiś ca

vara-renuka-jātibhih

kubjakair mallikābhiś ca

mādhavībhiś ca manditam

SYNONYMS

svarnārna — golden colored; śata-patraih — with lotuses; ca — and; vara-renuka-jātibhih — with varas, renukas and mālatīs; kubjakaih — with kubjakas; mallikābhih — with mallikās; ca — and; mādhavībhih — with mādhavīs; ca — and; manditam — decorated.

TRANSLATION

There are other trees also which decorate the hill, such as the golden lotus flower, the cinnamon tree, mālatī, kubja, mallikā and mādhavī.
