Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 6: Brahmā Satisfies Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.6.17

panasodumbarāśvattha-

plaksha-nyagrodha-hińgubhih

bhūrjair oshadhibhih pūgai

rājapūgaiś ca jambubhih

SYNONYMS

panasa-udumbara-aśvattha-plaksha-nyagrodha-hińgubhih — with panasas (jackfruit trees), udumbaras, aśvatthas, plakshas, nyagrodhas and trees producing asafoetida; bhūrjaih — with bhūrjas; oshadhibhih — with betel nut trees; pūgaih — with pūgas; rājapūgaih — with rājapūgas; ca — and; jambubhih — with jambus.

TRANSLATION

Kailāsa Hill is also decorated with such trees as kata, jackfruit, julara, banyan trees, plakshas, nyagrodhas and trees producing asafoetida. Also there are trees of betel nuts and bhūrja-patra, as well as rājapūga, blackberries and similar other trees.
