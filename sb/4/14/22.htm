Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 14: The Story of King Vena

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.14.22

yajñena yushmad-vishaye dvijātibhir

vitāyamānena surāh kalā hareh

svishtāh sutushtāh pradiśanti vāñchitam

tad-dhelanam nārhasi vīra ceshtitum

SYNONYMS

yajñena — by sacrifice; yushmat — your; vishaye — in the kingdom; dvijātibhih — by the brāhmanas; vitāyamānena — being performed; surāh — all the demigods; kalāh — expansions; hareh — of the Personality of Godhead; su-ishtāh — being properly worshiped; su-tushtāh — very much satisfied; pradiśanti — will give; vāñchitam — desired result; tat-helanam — disrespect to them; na — not; arhasi — you ought; vīra — O hero; ceshtitum — to do.

TRANSLATION

When all the brāhmanas engage in performing sacrifices in your kingdom, all the demigods, who are plenary expansions of the Lord, will be very much satisfied by their activities and will give you your desired result. Therefore, O hero, do not stop the sacrificial performances. If you stop them, you will disrespect the demigods.
