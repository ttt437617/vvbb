Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 14: The Story of King Vena

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.14.25

ko yajña-purusho nāma

yatra vo bhaktir īdriśī

bhartri-sneha-vidūrānām

yathā jāre kuyoshitām

SYNONYMS

kah — who (is); yajña-purushah — the enjoyer of all sacrifices; nāma — by name; yatra — unto whom; vah — your; bhaktih — devotional service; īdriśī — so great; bhartri — for the husband; sneha — affection; vidūrānām — bereft of; yathā — like; jāre — unto the paramour; ku-yoshitām — of unchaste women.

TRANSLATION

You are so much devoted to the demigods, but who are they? Indeed, your affection for these demigods is exactly like the affection of an unchaste woman who neglects her married life and gives all attention to her paramour.
