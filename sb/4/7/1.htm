Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.1

maitreya uvāca

ity ajenānunītena

bhavena paritushyatā

abhyadhāyi mahā-bāho

prahasya śrūyatām iti

SYNONYMS

maitreyah — Maitreya; uvāca — said; iti — thus; ajena — by Lord Brahmā; anunītena — pacified; bhavena — by Lord Śiva; paritushyatā — fully satisfied; abhyadhāyi — said; mahā-bāho — O Vidura; prahasya — smiling; śrūyatām — listen; iti — thus.

TRANSLATION

The sage Maitreya said: O mighty-armed Vidura, Lord Śiva, being thus pacified by the words of Lord Brahmā, spoke as follows in answer to Lord Brahmā's request.
