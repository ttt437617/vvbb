Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.8

vidhāya kārtsnyena ca tad

yad āha bhagavān bhavah

sandadhuh kasya kāyena

savanīya-paśoh śirah

SYNONYMS

vidhāya — executing; kārtsnyena — all in all; ca — also; tat — that; yat — which; āha — was said; bhagavān — the Lord; bhavah — Śiva; sandadhuh — executed; kasya — of the living (Daksha); kāyena — with the body; savanīya — meant for sacrifice; paśoh — of the animal; śirah — head.

TRANSLATION

After everything was executed exactly as directed by Lord Śiva, Daksha's body was joined to the head of the animal meant to be killed in the sacrifice.

PURPORT

This time, all the demigods and great sages were very careful not to irritate Lord Śiva. Therefore whatever he asked was done. It is specifically said here that Daksha's body was joined to the head of an animal (a goat).
