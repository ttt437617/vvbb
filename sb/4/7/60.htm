Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.60

etad bhagavatah śambhoh

karma dakshādhvara-druhah

śrutam bhāgavatāc chishyād

uddhavān me brihaspateh

SYNONYMS

etat — this; bhagavatah — of the possessor of all opulences; śambhoh — of Śambhu (Lord Śiva); karma — story; daksha-adhvara-druhah — who devastated the sacrifice of Daksha; śrutam — was heard; bhāgavatāt — from a great devotee; śishyāt — from the disciple; uddhavāt — from Uddhava; me — by me; brihaspateh — of Brihaspati.

TRANSLATION

Maitreya said: My dear Vidura, I heard this story of the Daksha yajña, which was devastated by Lord Śiva, from Uddhava, a great devotee and a disciple of Brihaspati.
