Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.16

maitreya uvāca

kshamāpyaivam sa mīdhvāmsam

brahmanā cānumantritah

karma santānayām āsa

sopādhyāyartvig-ādibhih

SYNONYMS

maitreyah — the sage Maitreya; uvāca — said; kshamā — forgiveness; āpya — receiving; evam — thus; sah — King Daksha; mīdhvāmsam — unto Lord Śiva; brahmanā — along with Lord Brahmā; ca — also; anumantritah — being permitted; karma — the sacrifice; santānayām āsa — began again; sa — along with; upādhyāya — learned sages; ritvik — the priests; ādibhih — and others.

TRANSLATION

The great sage Maitreya said: Thus being pardoned by Lord Śiva, King Daksha, with the permission of Lord Brahmā, again began the performance of the yajña, along with the great learned sages, the priests and others.
