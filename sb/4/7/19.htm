Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.19

tadā sva-prabhayā teshām

dyotayantyā diśo daśa

mushnams teja upānītas

tārkshyena stotra-vājinā

SYNONYMS

tadā — at that time; sva-prabhayā — by His own effulgence; teshām — all of them; dyotayantyā — by brightness; diśah — directions; daśa — ten; mushnan — diminishing; tejah — effulgence; upānītah — brought; tārkshyena — by Garuda; stotra-vājinā — whose wings are called Brihat and Rathantara.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nārāyana was seated on the shoulder of Stotra, or Garuda, who had big wings. As soon as the Lord appeared, all directions were illuminated, diminishing the luster of Brahmā and the others present.
