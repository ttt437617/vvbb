Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.48

maitreya uvāca

iti dakshah kavir yajñam

bhadra rudrābhimarśitam

kīrtyamāne hrishīkeśe

sanninye yajña-bhāvane

SYNONYMS

maitreyah — Maitreya; uvāca — said; iti — thus; dakshah — Daksha; kavih — being purified in consciousness; yajñam — the sacrifice; bhadra — O Vidura; rudra-abhimarśitam — devastated by Vīrabhadra; kīrtya-māne — being glorified; hrishīkeśe — Hrishīkeśa (Lord Vishnu); sanninye — arranged for restarting; yajña-bhāvane — the protector of sacrifice.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Maitreya said: After Lord Vishnu was glorified by all present, Daksha, his consciousness purified, arranged to begin again the yajña which had been devastated by the followers of Lord Śiva.
