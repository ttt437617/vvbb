Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 7: The Sacrifice Performed by Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.7.3

prajāpater dagdha-śīrshno

bhavatv aja-mukham śirah

mitrasya cakshusheksheta

bhāgam svam barhisho bhagah

SYNONYMS

prajāpateh — of Prajāpati Daksha; dagdha-śīrshnah — whose head has been burned to ashes; bhavatu — let there be; aja-mukham — with the face of a goat; śirah — a head; mitrasya — of Mitra; cakshushā — through the eyes; īksheta — may see; bhāgam — share; svam — his own; barhishah — of the sacrifice; bhagah — Bhaga.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śiva continued: Since the head of Daksha has already been burned to ashes, he will have the head of a goat. The demigod known as Bhaga will be able to see his share of sacrifice through the eyes of Mitra.
