Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 21: Instructions by Mahārāja Prithu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.21.19

śiśira-snigdha-tārākshah

samaikshata samantatah

ūcivān idam urvīśah

sadah samharshayann iva

SYNONYMS

śiśira — dew; snigdha — wet; tārā — stars; akshah — eyes; samaikshata — glanced over; samantatah — all around; ūcivān — began to speak; idam — this; urvīśah — highly elevated; sadah — amongst the members of the assembly; samharshayan — enhancing their pleasure; iva — like.

TRANSLATION

Just to encourage the members of the assembly and to enhance their pleasure, King Prithu glanced over them with eyes that seemed like stars in a sky wet with dew. He then spoke to them in a great voice.
