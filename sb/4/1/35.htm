Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 1: Genealogical Table of the Daughters of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.1.35

tat-putrāv aparāv āstām

khyātau svārocishe 'ntare

utathyo bhagavān sākshād

brahmishthaś ca brihaspatih

SYNONYMS

tat — his; putrau — sons; aparau — others; āstām — were born; khyātau — very famous; svārocishe — in the Svārocisha millennium; antare — of the Manu; utathyah — Utathya; bhagavān — very mighty; sākshāt — directly; brahmishthah ca — fully spiritually advanced; brihaspatih — Brihaspati.

TRANSLATION

Besides these four daughters, she also had another two sons. One of them was known as Utathya, and the other was the learned scholar Brihaspati.
