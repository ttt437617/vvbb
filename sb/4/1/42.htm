Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 1: Genealogical Table of the Daughters of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.1.42

cittis tv atharvanah patnī

lebhe putram dhrita-vratam

dadhyañcam aśvaśirasam

bhrigor vamśam nibodha me

SYNONYMS

cittih — Citti; tu — also; atharvanah — of Atharvā; patnī — wife; lebhe — got; putram — son; dhrita-vratam — completely dedicated to a vow; dadhyañcam — Dadhyañca; aśvaśirasam — Aśvaśirā; bhrigoh vamśam — generations of Bhrigu; nibodha — try to understand; me — from me.

TRANSLATION

Citti, wife of the sage Atharvā, gave birth to a son named Aśvaśirā by accepting a great vow called Dadhyañca. Now you may hear from me about the descendants of the sage Bhrigu.

PURPORT

The wife of Atharvā known as Citti is also known as Sānti. She was the eighth daughter of Kardama Muni.
