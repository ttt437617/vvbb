Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 1: Genealogical Table of the Daughters of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.1.58

evam sura-ganais tāta

bhagavantāv abhishtutau

labdhāvalokair yayatur

arcitau gandhamādanam

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; sura-ganaih — by the demigods; tāta — O Vidura; bhagavantau — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; abhishtutau — having been praised; labdha — having obtained; avalokaih — the glance (of mercy); yayatuh — departed; arcitau — having been worshiped; gandha-mādanam — to the Gandhamādana Hill.

TRANSLATION

[Maitreya said:] O Vidura, thus the demigods worshiped with prayers the Supreme Personality of Godhead appearing as the sage Nara-Nārāyana. The Lord glanced upon them with mercy and then departed for Gandhamādana Hill.
