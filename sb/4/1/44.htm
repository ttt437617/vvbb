Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 1: Genealogical Table of the Daughters of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.1.44

āyatim niyatim caiva

sute merus tayor adāt

tābhyām tayor abhavatām

mrikandah prāna eva ca

SYNONYMS

āyatim — Āyati; niyatim — Niyati; ca eva — also; sute — daughters; meruh — the sage Meru; tayoh — unto those two; adāt — gave in marriage; tābhyām — out of them; tayoh — both of them; abhavatām — appeared; mrikandah — Mrikanda; prānah — Prāna; eva — certainly; ca — and.

TRANSLATION

The sage Meru had two daughters, named Āyati and Niyati, whom he gave in charity to Dhātā and Vidhātā. Āyati and Niyati gave birth to two sons, Mrikanda and Prāna.
