Canto 4: Creation of the Fourth OrderChapter 1: Genealogical Table of the Daughters of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 4.1.63

agnishvāttā barhishadah

saumyāh pitara ājyapāh

sāgnayo 'nagnayas teshām

patnī dākshāyanī svadhā

SYNONYMS

agnishvāttāh — the Agnishvāttas; barhishadah — the Barhishadas; saumyāh — the Saumyas; pitarah — the forefathers; ājyapāh — the Ājyapas; sa-agnayah — those whose means is by fire; anagnayah — those whose means is without fire; teshām — of them; patnī — the wife; dākshāyanī — the daughter of Daksha; svadhā — Svadhā.

TRANSLATION

The Agnishvāttas, the Barhishadas, the Saumyas and the Ājyapas are the Pitās. They are either sāgnika or niragnika. The wife of all these Pitās is Svadhā, who is the daughter of King Daksha.
