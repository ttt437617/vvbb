Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.33

vrithā manorathas tasya

dūrah svargah pataty adhah

pratiśrutasyādānena

yo 'rthinam vipralambhate

SYNONYMS

vrithā — without any good result; manorathah — mental concoction; tasya — of him; dūrah — far away; svargah — elevation to the higher planetary system; patati — falls down; adhah — to a hellish condition of life; pratiśrutasya — things promised; adānena — being unable to give; yah — anyone who; arthinam — a beggar; vipralambhate — cheats.

TRANSLATION

Far from being elevated to the heavenly planets or fulfilling one's desire, one who does not properly give a beggar what he has promised falls down to a hellish condition of life.
