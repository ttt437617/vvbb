Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.11

anena yācamānena

śatrunā vatu-rūpinā

sarvasvam no hritam bhartur

nyasta-dandasya barhishi

SYNONYMS

anena — by Him; yācamānena — who is in the position of a beggar; śatrunā — by the enemy; vatu-rūpinā — in the form of a brahmacārī; sarvasvam — everything; nah — our; hritam — has been taken away; bhartuh — of our master; nyasta — had been given up; dandasya — of whom the power of giving punishment; barhishi — because of taking the vow of a ritualistic ceremony.

TRANSLATION

"Our lord, Bali Mahārāja, because of his position in performing the yajña, has given up the power to punish. Taking advantage of this, our eternal enemy, Vishnu, dressed in the form of a brahmacārī beggar, has taken away all his possessions.
