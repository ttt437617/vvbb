Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.13

tasmād asya vadho dharmo

bhartuh śuśrūshanam ca nah

ity āyudhāni jagrihur

baler anucarāsurāh

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — therefore; asya — of this brahmacārī Vāmana; vadhah — the killing; dharmah — is our duty; bhartuh — of our master; śuśrūshanam ca — and it is the way of serving; nah — our; iti — thus; āyudhāni — all kinds of weapons; jagrihuh — they took up; baleh — of Bali Mahārāja; anucara — followers; asurāh — all the demons.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore it is our duty to kill this Vāmanadeva, Lord Vishnu. It is our religious principle and the way to serve our master." After making this decision, the demoniac followers of Mahārāja Bali took up their various weapons with a view to killing Vāmanadeva.
