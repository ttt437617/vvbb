Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.10

na vāyam brahma-bandhur

vishnur māyāvinām varah

dvija-rūpa-praticchanno

deva-kāryam cikīrshati

SYNONYMS

na — not; vā — either; ayam — this; brahma-bandhuh — Vāmanadeva, in the form of a brāhmana; vishnuh — He is Lord Vishnu Himself; māyāvinām — of all cheaters; varah — the greatest; dvija-rūpa — by assuming the form of a brāhmana; praticchannah — is disguised for the purpose of cheating; deva-kāryam — the interest of the demigods; cikīrshati — He is trying for.

TRANSLATION

"This Vāmana is certainly not a brāhmana but the best of cheaters, Lord Vishnu. Assuming the form of a brāhmana, He has covered His own form, and thus He is working for the interests of the demigods.
