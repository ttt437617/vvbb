Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.14

te sarve vāmanam hantum

śūla-pattiśa-pānayah

anicchanto bale rājan

prādravañ jāta-manyavah

SYNONYMS

te — the demons; sarve — all of them; vāmanam — Lord Vāmanadeva; hantum — to kill; śūla — tridents; pattiśa — lances; pānayah — all taking in hand; anicchantah — against the will; baleh — of Bali Mahārāja; rājan — O King; prādravan — they pushed forward; jāta-manyavah — aggravated by usual anger.

TRANSLATION

O King, the demons, aggravated by their usual anger, took their lances and tridents in hand, and against the will of Bali Mahārāja they pushed forward to kill Lord Vāmanadeva.
