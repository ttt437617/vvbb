Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.15

tān abhidravato drishtvā

ditijānīkapān nripa

prahasyānucarā vishnoh

pratyashedhann udāyudhāh

SYNONYMS

tān — them; abhidravatah — thus going forward; drishtvā — seeing; ditija-anīka-pān — the soldiers of the demons; nripa — O King; prahasya — smiling; anucarāh — the associates; vishnoh — of Lord Vishnu; pratyashedhan — forbade; udāyudhāh — taking up their weapons.

TRANSLATION

O King, when the associates of Lord Vishnu saw the soldiers of the demons coming forward in violence, they smiled. Taking up their weapons, they forbade the demons to continue their attempt.
