Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.9

mahīm sarvām hritām drishtvā

tripada-vyāja-yācñayā

ūcuh sva-bhartur asurā

dīkshitasyātyamarshitāh

SYNONYMS

mahīm — land; sarvām — all; hritām — lost; drishtvā — after seeing; tri-pada-vyāja-yācñayā — by simply asking three steps of land; ūcuh — said; sva-bhartuh — of their master; asurāh — the demons; dīkshitasya — of Bali Mahārāja, who was so determined in the sacrifice; ati — very much; amarshitāh — for whom the function was unbearable.

TRANSLATION

When the demoniac followers of Mahārāja Bali saw that their master, who had been determined in performing sacrifice, had lost all his possessions to Vāmanadeva, who had taken them away on the plea of begging three paces of land, they were very angry and spoke as follows.
