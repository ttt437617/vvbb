Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.8

jāmbavān riksha-rājas tu

bherī-śabdair mano-javah

vijayam dikshu sarvāsu

mahotsavam aghoshayat

SYNONYMS

jāmbavān — who was named Jāmbavān; riksha-rājah tu — the king in the form of a bear also; bherī-śabdaih — by sounding the bugle; manah-javah — in mental ecstasy; vijayam — victory; dikshu — in all directions; sarvāsu — everywhere; mahā-utsavam — festival; aghoshayat — declared.

TRANSLATION

Jāmbavān, king of the bears, also joined in the ceremony. Sounding his bugle in all directions, he declared a great festival for Lord Vāmanadeva's victory.
