Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.27

hāhākāro mahān āsīd

rodasyoh sarvato diśam

nigrihyamāne 'sura-patau

vishnunā prabhavishnunā

SYNONYMS

hāhā-kārah — a tumultuous roar of lamentation; mahān — great; āsīt — there was; rodasyoh — in both the lower and upper planetary systems; sarvatah — everywhere; diśam — all directions; nigrihyamāne — because of being suppressed; asura-patau — when Bali Mahārāja, the King of the demons; vishnunā — by Lord Vishnu; prabhavishnunā — who is the most powerful everywhere.

TRANSLATION

When Bali Mahārāja was thus arrested by Lord Vishnu, who is the most powerful, there was a great roar of lamentation in all directions throughout the upper and lower planetary systems of the universe.
