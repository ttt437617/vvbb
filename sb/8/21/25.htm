Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 21: Bali Mahārāja Arrested by the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.21.25

śrī-śuka uvāca

patyur nigaditam śrutvā

daitya-dānava-yūthapāh

rasām nirviviśū rājan

vishnu-pārshada tāditāh

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; patyuh — of their master (Bali Mahārāja); nigaditam — what had been thus described; śrutvā — after hearing; daitya-dānava-yūtha-pāh — the leaders of the Daityas and demons; rasām — the lower regions of the universe; nirviviśūh — entered; rājan — O King; vishnu-pārshada — by the associates of Lord Vishnu; tāditāh — driven.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: O King, in accordance with the order of their master, Bali Mahārāja, all the chiefs of the demons and the Daityas entered the lower regions of the universe, to which they were driven by the soldiers of Vishnu.
