Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.35

namo marakata-śyāma-

vapushe 'dhigata-śriye

keśavāya namas tubhyam

namas te pīta-vāsase

SYNONYMS

namah — I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; marakata-śyāma-vapushe — whose bodily hue is blackish like the marakata gem; adhigata-śriye — under whose control is mother Lakshmī, the goddess of fortune; keśavāya — Lord Keśava, who killed the Keśī demon; namah tubhyam — I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; namah te — again I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; pīta-vāsase — whose garment is yellow.

TRANSLATION

My Lord, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, who are dressed in yellow garments, whose bodily hue resembles the marakata gem, and who have full control over the goddess of fortune. O my Lord Keśava, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.
