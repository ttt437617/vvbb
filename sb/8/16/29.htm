Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.29

namas tubhyam bhagavate

purushāya mahīyase

sarva-bhūta-nivāsāya

vāsudevāya sākshine

SYNONYMS

namah tubhyam — I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; bhagavate — unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; purushāya — the Supreme Person; mahīyase — the best of all personalities; sarva-bhūta-nivāsāya — the person who lives in everyone's heart; vāsudevāya — the Lord who lives everywhere; sākshine — the witness of everything.

TRANSLATION

O Supreme Personality of Godhead, greatest of all, who lives in everyone's heart and in whom everyone lives, O witness of everything, O Vāsudeva, supreme and all-pervading person, I offer my respectful obeisances unto You.
