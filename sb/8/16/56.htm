Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.56

bhuktavatsu ca sarveshu

dīnāndha-kripanādishu

vishnos tat prīnanam vidvān

bhuñjīta saha bandhubhih

SYNONYMS

bhuktavatsu — after feeding; ca — also; sarveshu — everyone present there; dīna — very poor; andha — blind; kripana — those who are not brāhmanas; ādishu — and so on; vishnoh — of Lord Vishnu, who is situated in everyone's heart; tat — that (prasāda); prīnanam — pleasing; vidvān — one who understands this philosophy; bhuñjīta — should take prasāda himself; saha — with; bandhubhih — friends and relatives.

TRANSLATION

One should distribute vishnu-prasāda to everyone, including the poor man, the blind man, the nondevotee and the non-brāhmana. Knowing that Lord Vishnu is very pleased when everyone is sumptuously fed with vishnu-prasāda, the performer of yajña should then take prasāda with his friends and relatives.
