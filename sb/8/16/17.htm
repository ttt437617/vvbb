Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.17

yathā tāni punah sādho

prapadyeran mamātmajāh

tathā vidhehi kalyānam

dhiyā kalyāna-krittama

SYNONYMS

yathā — as; tāni — all of our lost things; punah — again; sādho — O great saintly person; prapadyeran — can regain; mama — my; ātmajāh — offspring (sons); tathā — so; vidhehi — kindly do; kalyānam — auspiciousness; dhiyā — by consideration; kalyāna-krit-tama — O you who are the best person to act for our welfare.

TRANSLATION

O best of sages, best of all those who grant auspicious benedictions, please consider our situation and bestow upon my sons the benedictions by which they can regain what they have lost.
