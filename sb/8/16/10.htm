Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.10

api sarve kuśalinas

tava putrā manasvini

lakshaye 'svastham ātmānam

bhavatyā lakshanair aham

SYNONYMS

api — whether; sarve — all; kuśalinah — in full auspiciousness; tava — your; putrāh — sons; manasvini — O great-minded lady; lakshaye — I see; asvastham — not in tranquillity; ātmānam — the mind; bhavatyāh — of you; lakshanaih — by symptoms; aham — I.

TRANSLATION

O great-minded lady, are all your sons faring well? Seeing your withered face, I can perceive that your mind is not tranquil. How is this so?
