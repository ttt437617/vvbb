Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.59

tvam cānena mahā-bhāge

samyak cīrnena keśavam

ātmanā śuddha-bhāvena

niyatātmā bhajāvyayam

SYNONYMS

tvam ca — you also; anena — by this process; mahā-bhāge — O greatly fortunate one; samyak cīrnena — executed properly; keśavam — unto Lord Keśava; ātmanā — by oneself; śuddha-bhāvena — in a pure state of mind; niyata-ātmā — controlling oneself; bhaja — go on worshiping; avyayam — the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is inexhaustible.

TRANSLATION

O most fortunate lady, establishing your mind in a good spirit, execute this process of payo-vrata and thus worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Keśava, who is inexhaustible.
