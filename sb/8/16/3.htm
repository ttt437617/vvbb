Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.3

sa patnīm dīna-vadanām

kritāsana-parigrahah

sabhājito yathā-nyāyam

idam āha kurūdvaha

SYNONYMS

sah — Kaśyapa Muni; patnīm — unto his wife; dīna-vadanām — having a dry face; krita-āsana-parigrahah — after accepting a sitting place; sabhājitah — being honored by Aditi; yathā-nyāyam — according to time and place; idam āha — spoke as follows; kuru-udvaha — O Mahārāja Parīkshit, the best of the Kurus.

TRANSLATION

O best of the Kurus, when Kaśyapa Muni had been properly received and welcomed, he took his seat and then spoke as follows to his wife, Aditi, who was very morose.
