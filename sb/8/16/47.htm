Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.47

evam tv ahar ahah kuryād

dvādaśāham payo-vratam

harer ārādhanam homam

arhanam dvija-tarpanam

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; tu — indeed; ahah ahah — day after day; kuryāt — should execute; dvādaśa-aham — until twelve days; payah-vratam — the observance of the vrata known as payo-vrata; hareh ārādhanam — worshiping the Supreme Personality of Godhead; homam — by executing a fire sacrifice; arhanam — worshiping the Deity; dvija-tarpanam — and satisfying the brāhmanas by feeding them.

TRANSLATION

In this way, until twelve days have passed, one should observe this payo-vrata, worshiping the Lord every day, executing the routine duties, performing sacrifices and feeding the brāhmanas.
