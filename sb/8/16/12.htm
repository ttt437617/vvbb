Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.12

agnayo 'tithayo bhrityā

bhikshavo ye ca lipsavah

sarvam bhagavato brahmann

anudhyānān na rishyati

SYNONYMS

agnayah — worshiping the fires; atithayah — receiving the guests; bhrityāh — satisfying the servants; bhikshavah — pleasing the beggars; ye — all of them who; ca — and; lipsavah — as they desire (are taken care of); sarvam — all of them; bhagavatah — of you, my lord; brahman — O brāhmana; anudhyānāt — from always thinking; na rishyati — nothing is missed (everything is properly done).

TRANSLATION

O beloved husband, the fires, guests, servants and beggars are all being properly cared for by me. Because I always think of you, there is no possibility that any of the religious principles will be neglected.
