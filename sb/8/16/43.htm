Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.43

kritvā śirasi tac-cheshām

devam udvāsayet tatah

dvy-avarān bhojayed viprān

pāyasena yathocitam

SYNONYMS

kritvā — taking; śirasi — on the head; tat-śeshām — all the remnants (the water and flowers offered to the Deity); devam — unto the Deity; udvāsayet — should be thrown into a sacred place; tatah — thereafter; dvi-avarān — a minimum of two; bhojayet — should feed; viprān — brāhmanas; pāyasena — with sweet rice; yathā-ucitam — as each deserves.

TRANSLATION

After touching to one's head all the flowers and water offered to the Deity, one should throw them into a sacred place. Then one should feed at least two brāhmanas with sweet rice.
