Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.8

apy agnayas tu velāyām

na hutā havishā sati

tvayodvigna-dhiyā bhadre

proshite mayi karhicit

SYNONYMS

api — whether; agnayah — fires; tu — indeed; velāyām — in the fire sacrifice; na — not; hutāh — offered; havishā — by ghee; sati — O chaste woman; tvayā — by you; udvigna-dhiyā — because of some anxiety; bhadre — O auspicious woman; proshite — was away from home; mayi — when I; karhicit — sometimes.

TRANSLATION

O chaste and auspicious woman, when I left home for other places, were you in so much anxiety that you did not offer oblations of ghee into the fire?
