Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 16: Executing the Payo-vrata Process of Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.16.46

payo-bhaksho vratam idam

cared vishnv-arcanādritah

pūrvavaj juhuyād agnim

brāhmanāmś cāpi bhojayet

SYNONYMS

payah-bhakshah — one who drinks milk only; vratam idam — this process of worshiping with a vow; caret — one should execute; vishnu-arcana-ādritah — worshiping Lord Vishnu with great faith and devotion; pūrva-vat — as prescribed previously; juhuyāt — one should offer oblations; agnim — into the fire; brāhmanān — unto the brāhmanas; ca api — as well as; bhojayet — should feed.

TRANSLATION

Worshiping Lord Vishnu with great faith and devotion and living only by drinking milk, one should follow this vow. One should also offer oblations to the fire and feed the brāhmanas as mentioned before.
