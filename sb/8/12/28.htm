Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 12: The Mohinī-mūrti Incarnation Bewilders Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.12.28

so 'nuvrajyātivegena

grihītvānicchatīm striyam

keśa-bandha upānīya

bāhubhyām parishasvaje

SYNONYMS

sah — Lord Śiva; anuvrajya — following Her; ati-vegena — with great speed; grihītvā — catching; anicchatīm — although She was not willing to be caught; striyam — the woman; keśa-bandhe — on the cluster of hair; upānīya — dragging Her near; bāhubhyām — with his arms; parishasvaje — embraced Her.

TRANSLATION

After following Her with great speed, Lord Śiva caught Her by the braid of Her hair and dragged Her near him. Although She was unwilling, he embraced Her with his arms.
