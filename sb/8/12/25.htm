Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 12: The Mohinī-mūrti Incarnation Bewilders Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.12.25

tayāpahrita-vijñānas

tat-krita-smara-vihvalah

bhavānyā api paśyantyā

gata-hrīs tat-padam yayau

SYNONYMS

tayā — by Her; apahrita — taken away; vijñānah — good sense; tat-krita — done by Her; smara — by the smiling; vihvalah — having become mad for Her; bhavānyāh — while Bhavānī, the wife of Lord Śiva; api — although; paśyantyāh — was seeing all these incidents; gata-hrīh — bereft of all shame; tat-padam — to the place where She was situated; yayau — went.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śiva, his good sense taken away by the woman because of lusty desires to enjoy with Her, became so mad for Her that even in the presence of Bhavānī he did not hesitate to approach Her.
