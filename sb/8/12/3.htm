Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 12: The Mohinī-mūrti Incarnation Bewilders Lord Śiva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.12.3

sabhājito bhagavatā

sādaram somayā bhavah

sūpavishta uvācedam

pratipūjya smayan harim

SYNONYMS

sabhājitah — well received; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Vishnu; sa-ādaram — with great respect (as befitting Lord Śiva); sa-umayā — with Umā; bhavah — Lord Śambhu (Lord Śiva); su-upavishtah — being comfortably situated; uvāca — said; idam — this; pratipūjya — offering respect; smayan — smiling; harim — unto the Lord.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead welcomed Lord Śiva and Umā with great respect, and after being seated comfortably, Lord Śiva duly worshiped the Lord and smilingly spoke as follows.
