Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 1: The Manus, Administrators of the Universe

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.1.23

tritīya uttamo nāma

priyavrata-suto manuh

pavanah sriñjayo yajña-

hotrādyās tat-sutā nripa

SYNONYMS

tritīyah — the third; uttamah — Uttama; nāma — named; priyavrata — of King Priyavrata; sutah — the son; manuh — he became the Manu; pavanah — Pavana; sriñjayah — Sriñjaya; yajñahotra-ādyāh — Yajñahotra and others; tat-sutāh — the sons of Uttama; nripa — O King.

TRANSLATION

O King, the third Manu, Uttama, was the son of King Priyavrata. Among the sons of this Manu were Pavana, Sriñjaya and Yajñahotra.
