Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 6: The Demigods and Demons Declare a Truce

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.6.2

tenaiva sahasā sarve

devāh pratihatekshanāh

nāpaśyan kham diśah kshaunīm

ātmānam ca kuto vibhum

SYNONYMS

tena eva — because of this; sahasā — all of a sudden; sarve — all; devāh — the demigods; pratihata-īkshanāh — their vision being blocked; na — not; apaśyan — could see; kham — the sky; diśah — the directions; kshaunīm — land; ātmānam ca — also themselves; kutah — and where is the question of seeing; vibhum — the Supreme Lord.

TRANSLATION

The vision of all the demigods was blocked by the Lord's effulgence. Thus they could see neither the sky, the directions, the land, nor even themselves, what to speak of seeing the Lord, who was present before them.
