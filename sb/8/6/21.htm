Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 6: The Demigods and Demons Declare a Truce

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.6.21

amritotpādane yatnah

kriyatām avilambitam

yasya pītasya vai jantur

mrityu-grasto 'maro bhavet

SYNONYMS

amrita-utpādane — in generating nectar; yatnah — endeavor; kriyatām — do; avilambitam — without delay; yasya — of which nectar; pītasya — anyone who drinks; vai — indeed; jantuh — living entity; mrityu-grastah — although in imminent danger of death; amarah — immortal; bhavet — can become.

TRANSLATION

Immediately endeavor to produce nectar, which a person who is about to die may drink to become immortal.
