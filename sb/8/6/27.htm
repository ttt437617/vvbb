Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 6: The Demigods and Demons Declare a Truce

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.6.27

atha tasmai bhagavate

namaskritya pitāmahah

bhavaś ca jagmatuh svam svam

dhāmopeyur balim surāh

SYNONYMS

atha — after this; tasmai — unto Him; bhagavate — unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; namaskritya — offering obeisances; pitā-mahah — Lord Brahmā; bhavah ca — as well as Lord Śiva; jagmatuh — returned; svam svam — to their own; dhāma — abodes; upeyuh — approached; balim — King Bali; surāh — all the other demigods.

TRANSLATION

Then Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva, after offering their respectful obeisances to the Lord, returned to their abodes. All the demigods then approached Mahārāja Bali.
