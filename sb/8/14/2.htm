Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 14: The System of Universal Management

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.14.2

śrī-rishir uvāca

manavo manu-putrāś ca

munayaś ca mahī-pate

indrāh sura-ganāś caiva

sarve purusha-śāsanāh

SYNONYMS

śrī-rishih uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; manavah — all the Manus; manu-putrāh — all the sons of Manu; ca — and; munayah — all the great sages; ca — and; mahī-pate — O King; indrāh — all the Indras; sura-ganāh — the demigods; ca — and; eva — certainly; sarve — all of them; purusha-śāsanāh — under the rule of the Supreme person.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The Manus, the sons of Manu, the great sages, the Indras and all the demigods, O King, are appointed by the Supreme Personality of Godhead in His various incarnations such as Yajña.
