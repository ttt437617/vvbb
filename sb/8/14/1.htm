Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 14: The System of Universal Management

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.14.1

śrī-rājovāca

manvantareshu bhagavan

yathā manv-ādayas tv ime

yasmin karmani ye yena

niyuktās tad vadasva me

SYNONYMS

śrī-rājā uvāca — King Parīkshit said; manvantareshu — in the reign of each Manu; bhagavan — O great sage; yathā — as; manu-ādayah — the Manus and others; tu — but; ime — these; yasmin — in which; karmani — activities; ye — which persons; yena — by whom; niyuktāh — appointed; tat — that; vadasva — kindly describe; me — to me.

TRANSLATION

Mahārāja Parīkshit inquired: O most opulent Śukadeva Gosvāmī, please explain to me how Manu and the others in each manvantara are engaged in their respective duties, and by whose order they are so engaged.
