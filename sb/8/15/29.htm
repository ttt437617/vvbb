Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 15: Bali Mahārāja Conquers the Heavenly Planets

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.15.29

ojasvinam balim jetum

na samartho 'sti kaścana

bhavad-vidho bhavān vāpi

varjayitveśvaram harim

vijeshyati na ko 'py enam

brahma-tejah-samedhitam

nāsya śaktah purah sthātum

kritāntasya yathā janāh

SYNONYMS

ojasvinam — so powerful; balim — Bali Mahārāja; jetum — to conquer; na — not; samarthah — able; asti — is; kaścana — anyone; bhavat-vidhah — like you; bhavān — you yourself; vā api — either; varjayitvā — excepting; īśvaram — the supreme controller; harim — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; vijeshyati — will conquer; na — not; kah api — anyone; enam — him (Bali Mahārāja); brahma-tejah-samedhitam — now empowered with brahma-tejas, extraordinary spiritual power; na — not; asya — of him; śaktah — is able; purah — in front; sthātum — to stay; krita-antasya — of Yamarāja; yathā — as; janāh — people.

TRANSLATION

Neither you nor your men can conquer the most powerful Bali. Indeed, no one but the Supreme Personality of Godhead can conquer him, for he is now equipped with the supreme spiritual power [brahma-tejas]. As no one can stand before Yamarāja, no one can now stand before Bali Mahārāja.
