Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 15: Bali Mahārāja Conquers the Heavenly Planets

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.15.27

brūhi kāranam etasya

durdharshatvasya mad-ripoh

ojah saho balam tejo

yata etat samudyamah

SYNONYMS

brūhi — kindly inform us; kāranam — the cause; etasya — of all this; durdharshatvasya — of the formidableness; mat-ripoh — of my enemy; ojah — prowess; sahah — energy; balam — strength; tejah — influence; yatah — wherefrom; etat — all this; samudyamah — endeavor.

TRANSLATION

Kindly inform me. What is the cause for Bali Mahārāja's strength, endeavor, influence and victory? How has he become so enthusiastic?
