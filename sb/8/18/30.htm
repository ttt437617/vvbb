Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 18: Lord Vāmanadeva, the Dwarf Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.18.30

adya nah pitaras triptā

adya nah pāvitam kulam

adya svishtah kratur ayam

yad bhavān āgato grihān

SYNONYMS

adya — today; nah — our; pitarah — forefathers; triptāh — satisfied; adya — today; nah — our; pāvitam — purified; kulam — the whole family; adya — today; su-ishtah — properly executed; kratuh — the sacrifice; ayam — this; yat — because; bhavān — Your Lordship; āgatah — arrived; grihān — at our residence.

TRANSLATION

O my Lord, because You have kindly arrived at our home, all my forefathers are satisfied, our family and entire dynasty have been sanctified, and the sacrifice we are performing is now complete because of Your presence.
