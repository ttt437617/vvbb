Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 18: Lord Vāmanadeva, the Dwarf Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.18.14

tasyopanīyamānasya

sāvitrīm savitābravīt

brihaspatir brahma-sūtram

mekhalām kaśyapo 'dadāt

SYNONYMS

tasya — of Lord Vāmanadeva; upanīyamānasya — at the time of His being offered the sacred thread ceremony; sāvitrīm — the Gāyatrī mantra; savitā — the sun-god; abravīt — chanted; brihaspatih — Brihaspati, the guru of the demigods; brahma-sūtram — the sacred thread; mekhalām — the belt of straw; kaśyapah — Kaśyapa Muni; adadāt — offered.

TRANSLATION

At the sacred thread ceremony of Vāmanadeva, the sun-god personally uttered the Gāyatrī mantra, Brihaspati offered the sacred thread, and Kaśyapa Muni offered a straw belt.
