Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.24

manur vai dharma-sāvarnir

ekādaśama ātmavān

anāgatās tat-sutāś ca

satyadharmādayo daśa

SYNONYMS

manuh — the Manu; vai — indeed; dharma-sāvarnih — Dharmasāvarni; ekādaśamah — eleventh; ātmavān — the controller of the senses; anāgatāh — will come in the future; tat — his; sutāh — sons; ca — and; satyadharma-ādayah — Satyadharma and others; daśa — ten.

TRANSLATION

In the eleventh manvantara, the Manu will be Dharma-sāvarni, who will be extremely learned in spiritual knowledge. From him there will come ten sons, headed by Satyadharma.
