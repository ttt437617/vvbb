Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.25

vihańgamāh kāmagamā

nirvānarucayah surāh

indraś ca vaidhritas teshām

rishayaś cārunādayah

SYNONYMS

vihańgamāh — the Vihańgamas; kāmagamāh — the Kāmagamas; nirvānarucayah — the Nirvānarucis; surāh — the demigods; indrah — the king of heaven, Indra; ca — also; vaidhritah — Vaidhrita; teshām — of them; rishayah — the seven sages; ca — also; aruna-ādayah — headed by Aruna.

TRANSLATION

The Vihańgamas, Kāmagamas, Nirvānarucis and others will be the demigods. The king of the demigods, Indra, will be Vaidhrita, and the seven sages will be headed by Aruna.
