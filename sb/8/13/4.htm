Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.4

ādityā vasavo rudrā

viśvedevā marud-ganāh

aśvināv ribhavo rājann

indras teshām purandarah

SYNONYMS

ādityāh — the Ādityas; vasavah — the Vasus; rudrāh — the Rudras; viśvedevāh — the Viśvedevas; marut-ganāh — and the Maruts; aśvinau — the two Aśvinī brothers; ribhavah — the Ribhus; rājan — O King; indrah — the king of heaven; teshām — of them; purandarah — Purandara.

TRANSLATION

In this manvantara, O King, the Ādityas, the Vasus, the Rudras, the Viśvedevas, the Maruts, the two Aśvinī-kumāra brothers and the Ribhus are the demigods. Their head king [Indra] is Purandara.
