Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.32

devahotrasya tanaya

upahartā divaspateh

yogeśvaro harer amśo

brihatyām sambhavishyati

SYNONYMS

devahotrasya — of Devahotra; tanayah — the son; upahartā — the benefactor; divaspateh — of Divaspati, the Indra at that time; yoga-īśvarah — Yogeśvara, the master of mystic powers; hareh amśah — a partial representation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; brihatyām — in the womb of his mother, Brihatī; sambhavishyati — will appear.

TRANSLATION

The son of Devahotra known as Yogeśvara will appear as a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. His mother's name will be Brihatī. He will perform activities for the welfare of Divaspati.
