Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.15-16

gālavo dīptimān rāmo

drona-putrah kripas tathā

rishyaśrińgah pitāsmākam

bhagavān bādarāyanah

ime saptarshayas tatra

bhavishyanti sva-yogatah

idānīm āsate rājan

sve sva āśrama-mandale

SYNONYMS

gālavah — Gālava; dīptimān — Dīptimān; rāmah — Paraśurāma; drona-putrah — the son of Dronācārya, namely Aśvatthāmā; kripah — Kripācārya; tathā — as well; rishyaśrińgah — Rishyaśrińga; pitā asmākam — our father; bhagavān — the incarnation of Godhead; bādarāyanah — Vyāsadeva; ime — all of them; sapta-rishayah — the seven sages; tatra — in the eighth manvantara; bhavishyanti — will become; sva-yogatah — as a result of their service to the Lord; idānīm — at the present moment; āsate — they are all existing; rājan — O King; sve sve — in their own; āśrama-mandale — different hermitages.

TRANSLATION

O King, during the eighth manvantara, the great personalities Gālava, Dīptimān, Paraśurāma, Aśvatthāmā, Kripācārya, Rishyaśrińga and our father, Vyāsadeva, the incarnation of Nārāyana, will be the seven sages. For the present, they are all residing in their respective āśramas.
