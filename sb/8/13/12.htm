Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.12

tatra devāh sutapaso

virajā amritaprabhāh

teshām virocana-suto

balir indro bhavishyati

SYNONYMS

tatra — in that period of Manu; devāh — the demigods; sutapasah — the Sutapās; virajāh — the Virajas; amritaprabhāh — the Amritaprabhas; teshām — of them; virocana-sutah — the son of Virocana; balih — Mahārāja Bali; indrah — the king of heaven; bhavishyati — will become.

TRANSLATION

In the period of the eighth Manu, among the demigods will be the Sutapās, the Virajas and the Amritaprabhas. The king of the demigods, Indra, will be Bali Mahārāja, the son of Virocana.
