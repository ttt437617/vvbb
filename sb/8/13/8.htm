Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.8

vivasvataś ca dve jāye

viśvakarma-sute ubhe

samjñā chāyā ca rājendra

ye prāg abhihite tava

SYNONYMS

vivasvatah — of Vivasvān; ca — also; dve — two; jāye — wives; viśvakarma-sute — the two daughters of Viśvakarmā; ubhe — both of them; samjñā — Samjñā; chāyā — Chāyā; ca — and; rāja-indra — O King; ye — both of whom; prāk — before; abhihite — described; tava — unto you.

TRANSLATION

O King, I have previously described [in the Sixth Canto] the two daughters of Viśvakarmā, named Samjñā and Chāyā, who were the first two wives of Vivasvān.
