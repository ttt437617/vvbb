Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.33

manur vā indra-sāvarniś

caturdaśama eshyati

uru-gambhīra-budhādyā

indra-sāvarni-vīryajāh

SYNONYMS

manuh — the Manu; vā — either; indra-sāvarnih — Indra-sāvarni; caturdaśamah — fourteenth; eshyati — will become; uru — Uru; gambhīra — Gambhīra; budha-ādyāh — and others, such as Budha; indra-sāvarni — of Indra-sāvarni; vīrya-jāh — born of the semen.

TRANSLATION

The name of the fourteenth Manu will be Indra-sāvarni. He will have sons like Uru, Gambhīra and Budha.
