Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

manur vivasvatah putrah

śrāddhadeva iti śrutah

saptamo vartamāno yas

tad-apatyāni me śrinu

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; manuh — Manu; vivasvatah — of the sun-god; putrah — son; śrāddhadevah — as Śrāddhadeva; iti — thus; śrutah — known, celebrated; saptamah — seventh; vartamānah — at the present moment; yah — he who; tat — his; apatyāni — children; me — from me; śrinu — just hear.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: The present Manu, who is named Śrāddhadeva, is the son of Vivasvān, the predominating deity on the sun planet. Śrāddhadeva is the seventh Manu. Now please hear from me as I describe his sons.
