Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.18

navamo daksha-sāvarnir

manur varuna-sambhavah

bhūtaketur dīptaketur

ity ādyās tat-sutā nripa

SYNONYMS

navamah — ninth; daksha-sāvarnih — Daksha-sāvarni; manuh — the Manu; varuna-sambhavah — born as the son of Varuna; bhūtaketuh — Bhūtaketu; dīptaketuh — Dīptaketu; iti — thus; ādyāh — and so on; tat — his; sutāh — sons; nripa — O King.

TRANSLATION

O King, the ninth Manu will be Daksha-sāvarni, who is born of Varuna. Among his sons will be Bhūtaketu, and Dīptaketu.
