Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.30

manus trayodaśo bhāvyo

deva-sāvarnir ātmavān

citrasena-vicitrādyā

deva-sāvarni-dehajāh

SYNONYMS

manuh — the Manu; trayodaśah — thirteenth; bhāvyah — will become; deva-sāvarnih — Deva-sāvarni; ātmavān — well advanced in spiritual knowledge; citrasena — Citrasena; vicitra-ādyāh — and others, like Vicitra; deva-sāvarni — of Deva-sāvarni; deha-jāh — sons.

TRANSLATION

The thirteenth Manu will be named Deva-sāvarni, and he will be very advanced in spiritual knowledge. Among his sons will be Citrasena and Vicitra.
