Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.7

sańkshepato mayoktāni

sapta-manvantarāni te

bhavishyāny atha vakshyāmi

vishnoh śaktyānvitāni ca

SYNONYMS

sańkshepatah — in brief; mayā — by me; uktāni — explained; sapta — seven; manu-antarāni — changes of Manu; te — unto you; bhavishyāni — the future Manus; atha — also; vakshyāmi — I shall speak; vishnoh — of Lord Vishnu; śaktyā anvitāni — empowered by the energy; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly explained to you the position of the seven Manus. Now I shall describe the future Manus, along with the incarnations of Lord Vishnu.
