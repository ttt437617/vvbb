Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.19

pārā-marīcigarbhādyā

devā indro 'dbhutah smritah

dyutimat-pramukhās tatra

bhavishyanty rishayas tatah

SYNONYMS

pārā — the Pāras; marīcigarbha — the Marīcigarbhas; ādyāh — like that; devāh — the demigods; indrah — the king of heaven; adbhutah — Adbhuta; smritah — known; dyutimat — Dyutimān; pramukhāh — headed by; tatra — in that ninth period of Manu; bhavishyanti — will become; rishayah — the seven rishis; tatah — then.

TRANSLATION

In this ninth manvantara, the Pāras and Marīcigarbhas will be among the demigods. The king of heaven, Indra, will be named Adbhuta, and Dyutimān will be among the seven sages.
