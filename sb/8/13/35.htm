Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.35

satrāyanasya tanayo

brihadbhānus tadā harih

vitānāyām mahārāja

kriyā-tantūn vitāyitā

SYNONYMS

satrāyanasya — of Satrāyana; tanayah — the son; brihadbhānuh — Brihadbhānu; tadā — at that time; harih — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; vitānāyām — in the womb of Vitānā; mahā-rāja — O King; kriyā-tantūn — all spiritual activities; vitāyitā — will perform.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, in the fourteenth manvantara the Supreme Personality of Godhead will appear from the womb of Vitānā, and His father's name will be Satrāyana. This incarnation will be celebrated as Brihadbhānu, and He will administer spiritual activities.
