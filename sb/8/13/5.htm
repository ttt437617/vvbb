Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.5

kaśyapo 'trir vasishthaś ca

viśvāmitro 'tha gautamah

jamadagnir bharadvāja

iti saptarshayah smritāh

SYNONYMS

kaśyapah — Kaśyapa; atrih — Atri; vasishthah — Vasishtha; ca — and; viśvāmitrah — Viśvāmitra; atha — as well as; gautamah — Gautama; jamadagnih — Jamadagni; bharadvājah — Bharadvāja; iti — thus; sapta-rishayah — the seven sages; smritāh — celebrated.

TRANSLATION

Kaśyapa, Atri, Vasishtha, Viśvāmitra, Gautama, Jamadagni and Bharadvāja are known as the seven sages.
