Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.26

āryakasya sutas tatra

dharmasetur iti smritah

vaidhritāyām harer amśas

tri-lokīm dhārayishyati

SYNONYMS

āryakasya — of Āryaka; sutah — the son; tatra — in that period (the eleventh manvantara); dharmasetuh — Dharmasetu; iti — thus; smritah — celebrated; vaidhritāyām — from the mother, Vaidhritā; hareh — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; amśah — a partial incarnation; tri-lokīm — the three worlds; dhārayishyati — will rule.

TRANSLATION

The son of Āryaka known as Dharmasetu, a partial incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, will appear from the womb of Vaidhritā, the wife of Āryaka, and will rule the three worlds.
