Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.23

vishvakseno vishūcyām tu

śambhoh sakhyam karishyati

jātah svāmśena bhagavān

grihe viśvasrijo vibhuh

SYNONYMS

vishvaksenah — Vishvaksena; vishūcyām — in the womb of Vishūcī; tu — then; śambhoh — of Śambhu; sakhyam — friendship; karishyati — will create; jātah — being born; sva-amśena — by a plenary portion; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; grihe — in the home; viśvasrijah — of Viśvasrashtā; vibhuh — the supremely powerful Lord.

TRANSLATION

In the home of Viśvasrashtā, a plenary portion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead will appear from the womb of Vishūcī as the incarnation known as Vishvaksena. He will make friends with Śambhu.
