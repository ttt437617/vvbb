Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.31

devāh sukarma-sutrāma-

samjñā indro divaspatih

nirmoka-tattvadarśādyā

bhavishyanty rishayas tadā

SYNONYMS

devāh — the demigods; sukarma — the Sukarmās; sutrāma-samjñāh — and the Sutrāmas; indrah — the king of heaven; divaspatih — Divaspati; nirmoka — Nirmoka; tattvadarśa-ādyāh — and others, like Tattvadarśa; bhavishyanti — will become; rishayah — the seven sages; tadā — at that time.

TRANSLATION

In the thirteenth manvantara, the Sukarmās and Sutrāmas will be among the demigods, Divaspati will be the king of heaven, and Nirmoka and Tattvadarśa will be among the seven sages.
