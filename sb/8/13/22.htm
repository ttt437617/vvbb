Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 13: Description of Future Manus

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.13.22

havishmān sukritah satyo

jayo mūrtis tadā dvijāh

suvāsana-viruddhādyā

devāh śambhuh sureśvarah

SYNONYMS

havishmān — Havishmān; sukritah — Sukrita; satyah — Satya; jayah — Jaya; mūrtih — Mūrti; tadā — at that time; dvijāh — the seven sages; suvāsana — the Suvāsanas; viruddha — the Viruddhas; ādyāh — and so on; devāh — the demigods; śambhuh — Śambhu; sura-īśvarah — Indra, king of the demigods.

TRANSLATION

Havishmān, Sukrita, Satya, Jaya, Mūrti and others will be the seven sages, the Suvāsanas and Viruddhas will be among the demigods, and Śambhu will be their king, Indra.
