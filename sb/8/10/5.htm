Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.10.5

tatra daivāsuro nāma

ranah parama-dārunah

rodhasy udanvato rājams

tumulo roma-harshanah

SYNONYMS

tatra — there (at the beach of the ocean of milk); daiva — the demigods; asurah — the demons; nāma — as they are celebrated; ranah — fighting; parama — very much; dārunah — fierce; rodhasi — on the beach of the sea; udanvatah — of the ocean of milk; rājan — O King; tumulah — tumultuous; roma-harshanah — hair standing on the body.

TRANSLATION

O King, a fierce battle on the beach of the ocean of milk ensued between the demigods and the demons. The fighting was so terrible that simply hearing about it would make the hair on one's body stand on end.
