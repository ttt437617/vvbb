Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.10.52

evam daityair mahā-māyair

alakshya-gatibhī rane

srijyamānāsu māyāsu

visheduh sura-sainikāh

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; daityaih — by the demons; mahā-māyaih — who were expert in creating illusions; alakshya-gatibhih — but invisible; rane — in the fight; srijyamānāsu māyāsu — because of the creation of such an illusory atmosphere; visheduh — became morose; sura-sainikāh — the soldiers of the demigods.

TRANSLATION

While this magical atmosphere in the fight was being created by the invisible demons, who were expert in such illusions, the soldiers of the demigods became morose.
