Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.10.51

tatah samudra udvelah

sarvatah pratyadriśyata

pracanda-vātair uddhūta-

tarańgāvarta-bhīshanah

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereafter; samudrah — the sea; udvelah — being agitated; sarvatah — everywhere; pratyadriśyata — appeared before everyone's vision; pracanda — fierce; vātaih — by the winds; uddhūta — agitated; tarańga — of the waves; āvarta — whirling water; bhīshanah — ferocious.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, whirlpools and sea waves, agitated by fierce blasts of wind, appeared everywhere, before everyone's vision, in a furious flood.
