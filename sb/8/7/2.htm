Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 7: Lord Śiva Saves the Universe by Drinking Poison

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.7.2

harih purastāj jagrihe

pūrvam devās tato 'bhavan

SYNONYMS

harih — the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Ajita; purastāt — from the front; jagrihe — took; pūrvam — at first; devāh — the demigods; tatah — thereafter; abhavan — took the front portion of Vāsuki.

TRANSLATION

The Personality of Godhead, Ajita, grasped the front portion of the snake, and then the demigods followed.
