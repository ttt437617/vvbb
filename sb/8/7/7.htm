Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 7: Lord Śiva Saves the Universe by Drinking Poison

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.7.7

te sunirvinna-manasah

parimlāna-mukha-śriyah

āsan sva-paurushe nashte

daivenātibalīyasā

SYNONYMS

te — all of them (the demigods and demons); sunirvinna-manasah — their minds being very disappointed; parimlāna — dried up; mukha-śriyah — the beauty of their faces; āsan — became; sva-paurushe — with their own prowess; nashte — being lost; daivena — by a providential arrangement; ati-balīyasā — which is always stronger than anything else.

TRANSLATION

Because the mountain had been sunk by the strength of providence, the demigods and demons were disappointed, and their faces seemed to shrivel.
