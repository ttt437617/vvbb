Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.13

tān vinirjitya samare

durmadān asurarshabhān

pratilabdha-jaya-śrībhih

putrair icchasy upāsitum

SYNONYMS

tān — them; vinirjitya — defeating; samare — in the fight; durmadān — puffed up by strength; asura-rishabhān — the leaders of the demons; pratilabdha — getting back; jaya — victory; śrībhih — as well as the opulence; putraih — with your sons; icchasi — you are desiring; upāsitum — to come together and worship Me.

TRANSLATION

O Devī, O goddess, I can understand that you want to regain your sons and be together with them to worship Me, after defeating the enemies in battle and retrieving your abode and opulences.
