Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.11

śrī-śuka uvāca

adityaivam stuto rājan

bhagavān pushkarekshanah

kshetra-jñah sarva-bhūtānām

iti hovāca bhārata

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; adityā — by Aditi; evam — thus; stutah — being worshiped; rājan — O King (Mahārāja Parīkshit); bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; pushkara-īkshanah — whose eyes are like lotus flowers; kshetra-jñah — the Supersoul; sarva-bhūtānām — of all living entities; iti — thus; ha — indeed; uvāca — replied; bhārata — O best of the Bharata dynasty.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King Parīkshit, best of the Bharata dynasty, when the lotus-eyed Lord, the Supersoul of all living entities, was thus worshiped by Aditi, He replied as follows.
