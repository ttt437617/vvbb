Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.14

indra-jyeshthaih sva-tanayair

hatānām yudhi vidvishām

striyo rudantīr āsādya

drashtum icchasi duhkhitāh

SYNONYMS

indra-jyeshthaih — persons of whom King Indra is the eldest; sva-tanayaih — by your own sons; hatānām — who are killed; yudhi — in the fight; vidvishām — of the enemies; striyah — the wives; rudantīh — lamenting; āsādya — coming near the dead bodies of their husbands; drashtum icchasi — you want to see; duhkhitāh — very much aggrieved.

TRANSLATION

You want to see the wives of the demons lamenting for the death of their husbands when those demons, the enemies of your sons, are killed in battle by the demigods, of whom Indra is the chief.
