Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.17

athāpy upāyo mama devi cintyah

santoshitasya vrata-caryayā te

mamārcanam nārhati gantum anyathā

śraddhānurūpam phala-hetukatvāt

SYNONYMS

atha — therefore; api — in spite of this situation; upāyah — some means; mama — by Me; devi — O goddess; cintyah — must be considered; santoshitasya — very pleased; vrata-caryayā — observing the vow; te — by you; mama arcanam — worshiping Me; na — never; arhati — deserves; gantum anyathā — to become otherwise; śraddhā-anurūpam — according to one's faith and devotion; phala — of the result; hetukatvāt — from being the cause.

TRANSLATION

Yet because I have been satisfied by the activities of your vow, O goddess Aditi, I must find some means to favor you, for worship of Me never goes in vain but certainly gives the desired result according to what one deserves.
