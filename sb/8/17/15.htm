Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.15

ātmajān susamriddhāms tvam

pratyāhrita-yaśah-śriyah

nāka-prishtham adhishthāya

krīdato drashtum icchasi

SYNONYMS

ātma-jān — your own sons; su-samriddhān — completely opulent; tvam — you; pratyāhrita — having received back; yaśah — reputation; śriyah — opulence; nāka-prishtham — in the heavenly kingdom; adhishthāya — situated; krīdatah — enjoying their life; drashtum — to see; icchasi — you are desiring.

TRANSLATION

You want your sons to regain their lost reputation and opulence and live again on their heavenly planet as usual.
