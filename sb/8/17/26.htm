Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 17: The Supreme Lord Agrees to Become Aditi's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.17.26

namas te priśni-garbhāya

veda-garbhāya vedhase

tri-nābhāya tri-prishthāya

śipi-vishtāya vishnave

SYNONYMS

namah te — I offer my respectful obeisances unto You; priśni-garbhāya — who formerly lived within the womb of Priśni (Aditi in her previous birth); veda-garbhāya — who always remain within Vedic knowledge; vedhase — who are full of knowledge; tri-nābhāya — within the stem growing from whose navel live all the three worlds; tri-prishthāya — who are transcendental to the three worlds; śipi-vishtāya — who are within the cores of the hearts of all living entities; vishnave — unto the all-pervading Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, the all-pervading Lord Vishnu, who have entered the cores of the hearts of all living entities. All the three worlds reside within Your navel, yet You are above the three worlds. Formerly You appeared as the son of Priśni. To You, the supreme creator, who are understood only through Vedic knowledge, I offer my respectful obeisances.
