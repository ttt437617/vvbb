Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 24: Matsya, the Lord's Fish Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.24.27

nūnam tvam bhagavān sākshād

dharir nārāyano 'vyayah

anugrahāya bhūtānām

dhatse rūpam jalaukasām

SYNONYMS

nūnam — certainly; tvam — You (are); bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sākshāt — directly; harih — the Lord; nārāyanah — the Personality of Godhead; avyayah — inexhaustible; anugrahāya — to show mercy; bhūtānām — to all living entities; dhatse — You have assumed; rūpam — a form; jala-okasām — like an aquatic.

TRANSLATION

My Lord, You are certainly the inexhaustible Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana, Śrī Hari. It is to show Your mercy to the living entities that You have now assumed the form of an aquatic.
