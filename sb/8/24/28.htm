Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 24: Matsya, the Lord's Fish Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.24.28

namas te purusha-śreshtha

sthity-utpatty-apyayeśvara

bhaktānām nah prapannānām

mukhyo hy ātma-gatir vibho

SYNONYMS

namah — I offer my respectful obeisances; te — unto You; purusha-śreshtha — the best of all living entities, the best of all enjoyers; sthiti — of maintenance; utpatti — creation; apyaya — and destruction; īśvara — the Supreme Lord; bhaktānām — of Your devotees; nah — like us; prapannānām — those who are surrendered; mukhyah — the supreme; hi — indeed; ātma-gatih — the supreme destination; vibho — Lord Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

O my Lord, master of creation, maintenance and annihilation, O best of enjoyers, Lord Vishnu, You are the leader and destination of surrendered devotees like us. Therefore let me offer my respectful obeisances unto You.
