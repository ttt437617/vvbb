Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 9: The Lord Incarnates as Mohinī-Mūrti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.9.8

ity upāmantrito daityair

māyā-yoshid-vapur harih

prahasya rucirāpāńgair

nirīkshann idam abravīt

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; upāmantritah — being fervently requested; daityaih — by the demons; māyā-yoshit — the illusory woman; vapuh harih — the incarnation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; prahasya — smiling; rucira — beautiful; apāńgaih — by exhibiting attractive feminine features; nirīkshan — looking at them; idam — these words; abravīt — said.

TRANSLATION

Having thus been requested by the demons, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who had assumed the form of a beautiful woman, began to smile. Looking at them with attractive feminine gestures, She spoke as follows.
