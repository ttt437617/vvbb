Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 9: The Lord Incarnates as Mohinī-Mūrti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.9.27

pīta-prāye 'mrite devair

bhagavān loka-bhāvanah

paśyatām asurendrānām

svam rūpam jagrihe harih

SYNONYMS

pīta-prāye — when almost finished being drunk; amrite — the nectar; devaih — by the demigods; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead as Mohinī-mūrti; loka-bhāvanah — the maintainer and well-wisher of the three worlds; paśyatām — in the presence of; asura-indrānām — all the demons, with their chiefs; svam — own; rūpam — form; jagrihe — manifested; harih — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead is the best friend and well-wisher of the three worlds. Thus when the demigods had almost finished drinking the nectar, the Lord, in the presence of all the demons, disclosed His original form.
