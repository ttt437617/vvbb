Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 9: The Lord Incarnates as Mohinī-Mūrti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.9.13

ity abhivyāhritam tasyā

ākarnyāsura-puńgavāh

apramāna-vidas tasyās

tat tathety anvamamsata

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; abhivyāhritam — the words that were spoken; tasyāh — Her; ākarnya — after hearing; asura-puńgavāh — the chiefs of the demons; apramāna-vidah — because they were all foolish; tasyāh — of Her; tat — those words; tathā — let it be so; iti — thus; anvamamsata — agreed to accept.

TRANSLATION

The chiefs of the demons were not very expert in deciding things. Upon hearing the sweet words of Mohinī-mūrti, they immediately assented. "Yes," they answered. "What You have said is all right." Thus the demons agreed to accept Her decision.
