Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 22: Bali Mahārāja Surrenders His Life

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.22.12

śrī-śuka uvāca

tasyettham bhāshamānasya

prahrādo bhagavat-priyah

ājagāma kuru-śreshtha

rākā-patir ivotthitah

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; tasya — Bali Mahārāja; ittham — in this way; bhāshamānasya — while describing his fortunate position; prahrādah — Mahārāja Prahlāda, his grandfather; bhagavat-priyah — the most favored devotee of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ājagāma — appeared there; kuru-śreshtha — O best of the Kurus, Mahārāja Parīkshit; rākā-patih — the moon; iva — like; utthitah — having risen.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O best of the Kurus, while Bali Mahārāja was describing his fortunate position in this way, the most dear devotee of the Lord, Prahlāda Mahārāja, appeared there, like the moon rising in the nighttime.
