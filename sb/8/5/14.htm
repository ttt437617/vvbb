Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 5: The Demigods Appeal to the Lord for Protection

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.5.14

śrī-sūta uvāca

samprishto bhagavān evam

dvaipāyana-suto dvijāh

abhinandya harer vīryam

abhyācashtum pracakrame

SYNONYMS

śrī-sūtah uvāca — Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; samprishtah — being questioned; bhagavān — Śukadeva Gosvāmī; evam — thus; dvaipāyana-sutah — the son of Vyāsadeva; dvi-jāh — O brāhmanas assembled here; abhinandya — congratulating Mahārāja Parīkshit; hareh vīryam — the glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; abhyācashtum — to describe; pracakrame — endeavored.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: O learned brāhmanas assembled here at Naimishāranya, when Śukadeva Gosvāmī, the son of Dvaipāyana, was thus questioned by the King, he congratulated the King and then endeavored to describe further the glories of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
