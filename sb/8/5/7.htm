Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 5: The Demigods Appeal to the Lord for Protection

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.5.7

shashthaś ca cakshushah putraś

cākshusho nāma vai manuh

pūru-pūrusha-sudyumna-

pramukhāś cākshushātmajāh

SYNONYMS

shashthah — the sixth; ca — and; cakshushah — of Cakshu; putrah — the son; cākshushah — Cākshusha; nāma — named; vai — indeed; manuh — Manu; pūru — Pūru; pūrusha — Pūrusha; sudyumna — Sudyumna; pramukhāh — headed by; cākshusha-ātma-jāh — the sons of Cākshusha.

TRANSLATION

The son of Cakshu known as Cākshusha was the sixth Manu. He had many sons, headed by Pūru, Pūrusha and Sudyumna.
