Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 5: The Demigods Appeal to the Lord for Protection

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.5.2

pañcamo raivato nāma

manus tāmasa-sodarah

bali-vindhyādayas tasya

sutā hārjuna-pūrvakāh

SYNONYMS

pañcamah — the fifth; raivatah — Raivata; nāma — by the name; manuh — Manu; tāmasa-sodarah — the brother of Tāmasa Manu; bali — Bali; vindhya — Vindhya; ādayah — and so on; tasya — his; sutāh — sons; ha — certainly; arjuna — Arjuna; pūrvakāh — heading all the sons.

TRANSLATION

The brother of Tāmasa Manu was the fifth Manu, named Raivata. His sons were headed by Arjuna, Bali and Vindhya.
