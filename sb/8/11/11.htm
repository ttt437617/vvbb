Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 11: King Indra Annihilates the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.11.11

evam nirākrito devo

vairinā tathya-vādinā

nāmrishyat tad-adhikshepam

totrāhata iva dvipah

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; nirākritah — being defeated; devah — King Indra; vairinā — by his enemy; tathya-vādinā — who was competent to speak the truth; na — not; amrishyat — lamented; tat — of him (Bali); adhikshepam — the chastisement; totra — by the scepter or rod; āhatah — being beaten; iva — just like; dvipah — an elephant.

TRANSLATION

Since Mahārāja Bali's rebukes were truthful, King Indra did not at all become sorry, just as an elephant beaten by its driver's rod does not become agitated.
