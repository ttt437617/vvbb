Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 11: King Indra Annihilates the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.11.43

brahmanā preshito devān

devarshir nārado nripa

vārayām āsa vibudhān

drishtvā dānava-sańkshayam

SYNONYMS

brahmanā — by Lord Brahmā; preshitah — sent; devān — unto the demigods; deva-rishih — the great sage of the heavenly planets; nāradah — Nārada Muni; nripa — O King; vārayām āsa — forbade; vibudhān — all the demigods; drishtvā — after seeing; dānava-sańkshayam — the total annihilation of the demons.

TRANSLATION

O King, when Lord Brahmā saw the imminent total annihilation of the demons, he sent a message with Nārada, who went before the demigods to make them stop fighting.
