Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 19: Lord Vāmanadeva Begs Charity from Bali Mahārāja

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.19.15

bhavān ācaritān dharmān

āsthito grihamedhibhih

brāhmanaih pūrvajaih śūrair

anyaiś coddāma-kīrtibhih

SYNONYMS

bhavān — your good self; ācaritān — executed; dharmān — religious principles; āsthitah — being situated; grihamedhibhih — by persons in household life; brāhmanaih — by the brāhmanas; pūrva-jaih — by your forefathers; śūraih — by great heroes; anyaih ca — and others also; uddāma-kīrtibhih — very highly elevated and famous.

TRANSLATION

You also have observed the principles followed by great personalities who are householder brāhmanas, by your forefathers and by great heroes who are extremely famous for their exalted activities.
