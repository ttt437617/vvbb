Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 23: The Demigods Regain the Heavenly Planets

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.23.4

evam indrāya bhagavān

pratyānīya trivishtapam

pūrayitvāditeh kāmam

aśāsat sakalam jagat

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; indrāya — unto King Indra; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; pratyānīya — giving back; tri-vishtapam — his supremacy in the heavenly planets; pūrayitvā — fulfilling; aditeh — of Aditi; kāmam — the desire; aśāsat — ruled; sakalam — complete; jagat — universe.

TRANSLATION

Thus having delivered the proprietorship of the heavenly planets to Indra and having fulfilled the desire of Aditi, mother of the demigods, the Supreme Personality of Godhead ruled the affairs of the universe.
