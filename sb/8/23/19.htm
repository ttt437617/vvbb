Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 23: The Demigods Regain the Heavenly Planets

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.23.19

evam baler mahīm rājan

bhikshitvā vāmano harih

dadau bhrātre mahendrāya

tridivam yat parair hritam

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; baleh — from Bali Mahārāja; mahīm — the land; rājan — O King Parīkshit; bhikshitvā — after begging; vāmanah — His Lordship Vāmana; harih — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; dadau — delivered; bhrātre — unto His brother; mahā-indrāya — Indra, the King of heaven; tridivam — the planetary system of the demigods; yat — which; paraih — by others; hritam — was taken.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, thus having taken all the land of Bali Mahārāja by begging, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Vāmanadeva, delivered to His brother Indra all the land taken away by Indra's enemy.
