Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 8: The Churning of the Milk Ocean

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.8.31

athodadher mathyamānāt

kāśyapair amritārthibhih

udatishthan mahārāja

purushah paramādbhutah

SYNONYMS

atha — thereafter; udadheh — from the ocean of milk; mathyamānāt — while being churned; kāśyapaih — by the sons of Kaśyapa, namely the demigods and the demons; amrita-arthibhih — anxious to get nectar from the churning; udatishthat — there appeared; mahārāja — O King; purushah — a male person; parama — highly; adbhutah — wonderful.

TRANSLATION

O King, thereafter, while the sons of Kaśyapa, both demons and demigods, were engaged in churning the ocean of milk, a very wonderful male person appeared.
