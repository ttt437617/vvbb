Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 8: The Churning of the Milk Ocean

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.8.37

iti tad-dainyam ālokya

bhagavān bhritya-kāma-krit

mā khidyata mitho 'rtham vah

sādhayishye sva-māyayā

SYNONYMS

iti — in this way; tat — of the demigods; dainyam — moroseness; ālokya — seeing; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; bhritya-kāma-krit — who is always ready to fulfill the desires of His servants; mā khidyata — do not be aggrieved; mithah — by a quarrel; artham — to get nectar; vah — for all of you; sādhayishye — I shall execute; sva-māyayā — by My own energy.

TRANSLATION

When the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who always desires to fulfill the ambitions of His devotees, saw that the demigods were morose, He said to them, "Do not be aggrieved. By My own energy I shall bewilder the demons by creating a quarrel among them. In this way I shall fulfill your desire to have the nectar."
