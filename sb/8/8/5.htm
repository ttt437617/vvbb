Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 8: The Churning of the Milk Ocean

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.8.5

airāvanādayas tv ashtau

dig-gajā abhavams tatah

abhramu-prabhritayo 'shtau ca

karinyas tv abhavan nripa

SYNONYMS

airāvana-ādayah — headed by Airāvana; tu — but; ashtau — eight; dik-gajāh — elephants that could go in any direction; abhavan — were generated; tatah — thereafter; abhramu-prabhritayah — headed by the she-elephant named Abhramu; ashtau — eight; ca — also; karinyah — female elephants; tu — indeed; abhavan — also generated; nripa — O King.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, O King, eight great elephants, which could go in any direction, were generated. They were headed by Airāvana. Eight she-elephants, headed by Abhramu, were also generated.

PURPORT

The names of the eight elephants were Airāvana, Pundarīka, Vāmana, Kumuda, Añjana, Pushpadanta, Sārvabhauma and Supratīka.
