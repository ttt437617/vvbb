Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 8: The Churning of the Milk Ocean

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.8.35

tam ālokyāsurāh sarve

kalasam cāmritābhritam

lipsantah sarva-vastūni

kalasam tarasāharan

SYNONYMS

tam — him; ālokya — seeing; asurāh — the demons; sarve — all of them; kalasam — the container of nectar; ca — also; amrita-ābhritam — filled with nectar; lipsantah — desiring strongly; sarva-vastūni — all objects; kalasam — the jug; tarasā — immediately; aharan — snatched away.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing Dhanvantari carrying the jug of nectar, the demons, desiring the jug and its contents, immediately snatched it away by force.
