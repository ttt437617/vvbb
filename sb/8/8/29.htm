Canto 8: Withdrawal of the Cosmic CreationsChapter 8: The Churning of the Milk Ocean

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 8.8.29

nihsattvā lolupā rājan

nirudyogā gata-trapāh

yadā copekshitā lakshmyā

babhūvur daitya-dānavāh

SYNONYMS

nihsattvāh — without strength; lolupāh — very greedy; rājan — O King; nirudyogāh — frustrated; gata-trapāh — shameless; yadā — when; ca — also; upekshitāh — neglected; lakshmyā — by the goddess of fortune; babhūvuh — they became; daitya-dānavāh — the demons and Rākshasas.

TRANSLATION

O King, because of being neglected by the goddess of fortune, the demons and Rākshasas were depressed, bewildered and frustrated, and thus they became shameless.
