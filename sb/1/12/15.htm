Canto 1: CreationChapter 12: Birth of Emperor Parīkshit

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.12.15

tam ūcur brāhmanās tushtā

rājānam praśrayānvitam

esha hy asmin prajā-tantau

purūnām pauravarshabha

SYNONYMS

tam — unto him; ūcuh — addressed; brāhmanāh — the learned brāhmanas; tushtāh — very much satisfied; rājānam — unto the King; praśraya-anvitam — very much obliging; eshah — this; hi — certainly; asmin — in the chain of; prajā-tantau — descending line; purūnām — of the Pūrus; paurava-rishabha — the chief among the Pūrus.

TRANSLATION

The learned brāhmanas, who were very satisfied with the charities of the King, addressed him as the chief amongst the Pūrus and informed him that his son was certainly in the line of descent from the Pūrus.
