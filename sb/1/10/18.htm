Canto 1: CreationChapter 10: Departure of Lord Krishna for Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.10.18

uddhavah sātyakiś caiva

vyajane paramādbhute

vikīryamānah kusumai

reje madhu-patih pathi

SYNONYMS

uddhavah — a cousin-brother of Krishna's; sātyakih — His driver; ca — and; eva — certainly; vyajane — engaged in fanning; parama-adbhute — decorative; vikīryamānah — seated on scattered; kusumaih — flowers all around; reje — commanded; madhu-patih — the master of Madhu (Krishna); pathi — on the road.

TRANSLATION

Uddhava and Sātyaki began to fan the Lord with decorated fans, and the Lord, as the master of Madhu, seated on scattered flowers, commanded them along the road.
