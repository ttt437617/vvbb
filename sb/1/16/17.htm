Canto 1: CreationChapter 16: How Parīkshit Received the Age of Kali

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.16.17

tasyaivam vartamānasya

pūrveshām vrittim anvaham

nātidūre kilāścaryam

yad āsīt tan nibodha me

SYNONYMS

tasya — of Mahārāja Parīkshit; evam — thus; vartamānasya — remaining absorbed in such thought; pūrveshām — of his forefathers; vrittim — good engagement; anvaham — day after day; na — not; ati-dūre — far off; kila — verily; āścaryam — astonishing; yat — that; āsīt — was; tat — which; nibodha — know it; me — from me.

TRANSLATION

Now you may hear from me of what happened while Mahārāja Parīkshit was passing his days hearing of the good occupations of his forefathers and being absorbed in thought of them.
