Canto 1: CreationChapter 6: Conversation Between Nārada and Vyāsadeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.6.7

sāsvatantrā na kalpāsīd

yoga-kshemam mamecchatī

īśasya hi vaśe loko

yoshā dārumayī yathā

SYNONYMS

sā — she; asvatantrā — was dependent; na — not; kalpā — able; āsīt — was; yoga-kshemam — maintenance; mama — my; icchatī — although desirous; īśasya — of providence; hi — for; vaśe — under the control of; lokah — everyone; yoshā — doll; dāru-mayī — made of wood; yathā — as much as.

TRANSLATION

She wanted to look after my maintenance properly, but because she was not independent, she was not able to do anything for me. The world is under the full control of the Supreme Lord; therefore everyone is like a wooden doll in the hands of a puppet master.
