Canto 1: CreationChapter 7: The Son of Drona Punished

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.7.21

tatah prādushkritam tejah

pracandam sarvato diśam

prānāpadam abhiprekshya

vishnum jishnur uvāca ha

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereafter; prādushkritam — disseminated; tejah — glare; pracandam — fierce; sarvatah — all around; diśam — directions; prāna-āpadam — affecting life; abhiprekshya — having observed it; vishnum — unto the Lord; jishnuh — Arjuna; uvāca — said; ha — in the past.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon a glaring light spread in all directions. It was so fierce that Arjuna thought his own life in danger, and so he began to address Lord Śrī Krishna.
