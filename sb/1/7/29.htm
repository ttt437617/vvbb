Canto 1: CreationChapter 7: The Son of Drona Punished

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.7.29

sūta uvāca

śrutvā bhagavatā proktam

phālgunah para-vīra-hā

sprishtvāpas tam parikramya

brāhmam brāhmāstram sandadhe

SYNONYMS

sūtah — Sūta Gosvāmī; uvāca — said; śrutvā — after hearing; bhagavatā — by the Personality of Godhead; proktam — what was said; phālgunah — another name of Śrī Arjuna; para-vīra-hā — the killer of the opposing warrior; sprishtvā — after touching; āpah — water; tam — Him; parikramya — circumambulating; brāhmam — the Supreme Lord; brāhma-astram — the supreme weapon; sandadhe — acted on.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said: Hearing this from the Personality of Godhead, Arjuna touched water for purification, and after circumambulating Lord Śrī Krishna, he cast his brahmāstra weapon to counteract the other one.
