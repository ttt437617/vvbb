Canto 1: CreationChapter 7: The Son of Drona Punished

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.7.38

pratiśrutam ca bhavatā

pāñcālyai śrinvato mama

āharishye śiras tasya

yas te mānini putra-hā

SYNONYMS

pratiśrutam — it is promised; ca — and; bhavatā — by you; pāñcālyai — unto the daughter of the King of Pāñcāla (Draupadī); śrinvatah — which was heard; mama — by Me personally; āharishye — must I bring; śirah — the head; tasya — of him; yah — whom; te — your; mānini — consider; putra-hā — the killer of your sons.

TRANSLATION

Furthermore, I have personally heard you promise Draupadī that you would bring forth the head of the killer of her sons.
