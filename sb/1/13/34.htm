Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.13.34

pitary uparate pāndau

sarvān nah suhridah śiśūn

arakshatām vyasanatah

pitrivyau kva gatāv itah

SYNONYMS

pitari — upon my father; uparate — falling down; pāndau — Mahārāja Pāndu; sarvān — all; nah — of us; suhridah — well-wishers; śiśūn — small children; arakshatām — protected; vyasanatah — from all kinds of dangers; pitrivyau — uncles; kva — where; gatau — have departed; itah — from this place.

TRANSLATION

When my father, Pāndu, fell down and we were all small children, these two uncles gave us protection from all kinds of calamities. They were always our good well-wishers. Alas, where have they gone from here?
