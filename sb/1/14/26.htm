Canto 1: CreationChapter 14: The Disappearance of Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.14.26

śūro mātāmahah kaccit

svasty āste vātha mārishah

mātulah sānujah kaccit

kuśaly ānakadundubhih

SYNONYMS

śūrah — Śūrasena; mātāmahah — maternal grandfather; kaccit — whether; svasti — all good; āste — passing his days; vā — or; atha — therefore; mārishah — respectful; mātulah — maternal uncle; sa-anujah — with his younger brothers; kaccit — whether; kuśalī — all well; ānaka-dundubhih — Vasudeva.

TRANSLATION

Is my respectable grandfather Śūrasena in a happy mood? And are my maternal uncle Vasudeva and his younger brothers all doing well?
