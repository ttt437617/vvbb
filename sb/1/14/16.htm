Canto 1: CreationChapter 14: The Disappearance of Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.14.16

vāyur vāti khara-sparśo

rajasā visrijams tamah

asrig varshanti jaladā

bībhatsam iva sarvatah

SYNONYMS

vāyuh — wind; vāti — blowing; khara-sparśah — sharply; rajasā — by the dust; visrijan — creating; tamah — darkness; asrik — blood; varshanti — are raining; jaladāh — the clouds; bībhatsam — disastrous; iva — like; sarvatah — everywhere.

TRANSLATION

The wind blows violently, blasting dust everywhere and creating darkness. Clouds are raining everywhere with bloody disasters.
