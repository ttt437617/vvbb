Canto 1: CreationChapter 14: The Disappearance of Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.14.24

vilokyodvigna-hridayo

vicchāyam anujam nripah

pricchati sma suhrin madhye

samsmaran nāraderitam

SYNONYMS

vilokya — by seeing; udvigna — anxious; hridayah — heart; vicchāyam — pale appearance; anujam — Arjuna; nripah — the King; pricchati sma — asked; suhrit — friends; madhye — amongst; samsmaran — remembering; nārada — Sage Nārada; īritam — indicated by.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Arjuna pale due to heartfelt anxieties, the King, remembering the indications of the sage Nārada, questioned him in the midst of friends.
