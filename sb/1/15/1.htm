Canto 1: CreationChapter 15: The Pāndavas Retire Timely

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.15.1

sūta uvāca

evam krishna-sakhah krishno

bhrātrā rājñā vikalpitah

nānā-śańkāspadam rūpam

krishna-viślesha-karśitah

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; krishna-sakhah — the celebrated friend of Krishna; krishnah — Arjuna; bhrātrā — by his elder brother; rājñā — King Yudhishthira; vikalpitah — speculated; nānā — various; śańka-āspadam — based on many doubts; rūpam — forms; krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; viślesha — feelings of separation; karśitah — became greatly bereaved.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: Arjuna, the celebrated friend of Lord Krishna, was grief-stricken because of his strong feeling of separation from Krishna, over and above all Mahārāja Yudhishthira's speculative inquiries.

PURPORT

Being too much aggrieved, Arjuna practically became choked up, and therefore it was not possible for him to reply properly to the various speculative inquiries of Mahārāja Yudhishthira.
