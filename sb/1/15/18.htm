Canto 1: CreationChapter 15: The Pāndavas Retire Timely

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 1.15.18

narmāny udāra-rucira-smita-śobhitāni

he pārtha he 'rjuna sakhe kuru-nandaneti

sañjalpitāni nara-deva hridi-spriśāni

smartur luthanti hridayam mama mādhavasya

SYNONYMS

narmāni — conversation in jokes; udāra — talked very frankly; rucira — pleasing; smita-śobhitāni — decorated with a smiling face; he — note of address; pārtha — O son of Prithā; he — note of address; arjuna — Arjuna; sakhe — friend; kuru-nandana — son of the Kuru dynasty; iti — and so on; sañjalpitāni — such conversation; nara-deva — O King; hridi — heart; spriśāni — touching; smartuh — by remembering them; luthanti — overwhelms; hridayam — heart and soul; mama — my; mādhavasya — of Mādhava (Krishna).

TRANSLATION

O King! His jokings and frank talks were pleasing and beautifully decorated with smiles. His addresses unto me as "O son of Prithā, O friend, O son of the Kuru dynasty," and all such heartiness are now remembered by me, and thus I am overwhelmed.
