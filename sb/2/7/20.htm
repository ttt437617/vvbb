Canto 2: The Cosmic ManifestationChapter 7: Scheduled Incarnations with Specific Functions

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 2.7.20

cakram ca dikshv avihatam daśasu sva-tejo

manvantareshu manu-vamśa-dharo bibharti

dushteshu rājasu damam vyadadhāt sva-kīrtim

satye tri-prishtha uśatīm prathayamś caritraih

SYNONYMS

cakram — the Sudarśana wheel of the Lord; ca — as well as; dikshu — in all directions; avihatam — without being deterred; daśasu — ten sides; sva-tejah — personal strength; manvantareshu — in different incarnations of Manu; manu-vamśa-dharah — as the descendant of the Manu dynasty; bibharti — rules over; dushteshu — unto the miscreants; rājasu — upon the kings of that type; damam — subjection; vyadadhāt — performed; sva-kīrtim — personal glories; satye — in the Satyaloka planet; tri-prishthe — the three planetary systems; uśatīm — glorious; prathayan — established; caritraih — characteristics.

TRANSLATION

As the incarnation of Manu, the Lord became the descendant of the Manu dynasty and ruled over the miscreant kingly order, subduing them by His powerful wheel weapon. Undeterred in all circumstances, His rule was characterized by His glorious fame, which spread over the three lokas, and above them to the planetary system of Satyaloka, the topmost in the universe.

PURPORT

We have already discussed the incarnations of Manu in the First Canto. In one day of Brahmā there are fourteen Manus, changing one after another. In that way there are 420 Manus in a month of Brahmā and 5,040 Manus in one year of Brahmā. Brahmā lives for one hundred years according to his calculation, and as such there are 504,000 Manus in the jurisdiction of one Brahmā. There are innumerable Brahmās, and all of them live only during one breathing period of Mahā-Vishnu. So we can just imagine how the incarnations of the Supreme Lord work all over the material worlds, which comprehend only one-fourth of the total energy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

The manvantara incarnation chastises all the miscreant rulers of different planets with as much power as that of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who punishes the miscreants with His wheel weapon. The manvantara incarnations disseminate the transcendental glories of the Lord.
