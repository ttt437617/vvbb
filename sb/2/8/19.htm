Canto 2: The Cosmic ManifestationChapter 8: Questions by King Parīkshit

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 2.8.19

tattvānām parisańkhyānam

lakshanam hetu-lakshanam

purushārādhana-vidhir

yogasyādhyātmikasya ca

SYNONYMS

tattvānām — of the elements that constitute the creation; parisańkhyānam — of the number of such elements; lakshanam — symptoms; hetu-lakshanam — the symptoms of the causes; purusha — of the Lord; ārādhana — of devotional service; vidhih — rules and regulations; yogasya — of cultivation of the yoga system; adhyātmikasya — spiritual methods leading to devotional service; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

Kindly explain all about the elementary principles of creation, the number of such elementary principles, their causes, and their development, and also the process of devotional service and the method of mystic powers.
