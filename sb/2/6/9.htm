Canto 2: The Cosmic ManifestationChapter 6: Purusha-sūkta Confirmed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 2.6.9

pāyur yamasya mitrasya

parimokshasya nārada

himsāyā nirriter mrityor

nirayasya gudam smritah

SYNONYMS

pāyuh — the evacuating outlet; yamasya — the controlling deity of death; mitrasya — of Mitra; parimokshasya — of the evacuating hole; nārada — O Nārada; himsāyāh — of envy; nirriteh — of misfortune; mrityoh — of death; nirayasya — of hell; gudam — the rectum; smritah — is understood.

TRANSLATION

O Nārada, the evacuating outlet of the universal form of the Lord is the abode of the controlling deity of death, Mitra, and the evacuating hole and the rectum of the Lord is the place of envy, misfortune, death, hell, etc.
