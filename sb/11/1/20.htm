Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 1: The Curse Upon the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.1.20

śrutvāmogham vipra-śāpam

drishtvā ca mushalam nripa

vismitā bhaya-santrastā

babhūvur dvārakaukasah

SYNONYMS

śrutvā — hearing about; amogham — infallible; vipra-śāpam — the curse of the brāhmanas; drishtvā — seeing; ca — and; mushalam — the club; nripa — O King; vismitāh — amazed; bhaya — with fear; santrastāh — distraught; babhūvuh — they became; dvārakā-okasah — the inhabitants of Dvārakā.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, when the inhabitants of Dvārakā heard of the infallible curse of the brāhmanas and saw the club, they were astonished and distraught with fear.
