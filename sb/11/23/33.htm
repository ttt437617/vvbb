Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 23: The Song of the Avantī Brāhmana.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.23.33

tam vai pravayasam bhikshum

avadhūtam asaj-janāh

drishtvā paryabhavan bhadra

bahvībhih paribhūtibhih

SYNONYMS

tam — him; vai — indeed; pravayasam — old; bhikshum — the beggar; avadhūtam — unclean; asat — low-class; janāh — persons; drishtvā — seeing; paryabhavan — dishonored; bhadra — O kind Uddhava; bahvībhih — with many; paribhūtibhih — insults

TRANSLATION

O kind Uddhava, seeing him as an old, dirty beggar, rowdy persons would dishonor him with many insults.
