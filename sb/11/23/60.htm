Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 23: The Song of the Avantī Brāhmana.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.23.60

tasmāt sarvātmanā tāta

nigrihāna mano dhiyā

mayy āveśitayā yukta

etāvān yoga-sańgrahah

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — therefore; sarva-ātmanā — in all respects; tāta — My dear Uddhava; nigrihāna — bring under control; manah — the mind; dhiyā — with intelligence; mayi — in Me; āveśitayā — which is absorbed; yuktah — linked up; etāvān — thus; yoga-sańgrahah — the essence of spiritual practice.

TRANSLATION

My dear Uddhava, fixing your intelligence on Me, you should thus completely control the mind. This is the essence of the science of yoga.
