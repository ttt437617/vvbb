Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 6: The Yadu Dynasty Retires to Prabhāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.6.5

tasyām vibhrājamānāyām

samriddhāyām maharddhibhih

vyacakshatāvitriptākshāh

krishnam adbhuta-darśanam

SYNONYMS

tasyām — in that (Dvārakā); vibhrājamānāyām — resplendent; samriddhāyām — very rich; mahā-riddhibhih — with great opulences; vyacakshata — they saw; avitripta — unsatisfied; akshāh — whose eyes; krishnam — Lord Krishna; adbhuta-darśanam — wonderful to behold.

TRANSLATION

In that resplendent city of Dvārakā, rich with all superior opulences, the demigods beheld with unsatiated eyes the wonderful form of Śrī Krishna.
