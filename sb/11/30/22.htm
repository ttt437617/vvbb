Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.22

pratyanīkam manyamānā

balabhadram ca mohitāh

hantum krita-dhiyo rājann

āpannā ātatāyinah

SYNONYMS

pratyanīkam — an enemy; manyamānāh — thinking; balabhadram — Lord Balarāma; ca — also; mohitāh — confused; hantum — to kill; krita-dhiyah — having made up their minds; rājan — O King Parīkshit; āpannāh — they set upon Him; ātatāyinah — wielding weapons.

TRANSLATION

In their confused state, O King, they also mistook Lord Balarāma for an enemy. Weapons in hand, they ran toward Him with the intention of killing Him.
