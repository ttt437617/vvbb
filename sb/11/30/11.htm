Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.11

tasmin bhagavatādishtam

yadu-devena yādavāh

cakruh paramayā bhaktyā

sarva-śreyopabrimhitam

SYNONYMS

tasmin — there; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ādishtam — what was instructed; yadu-devena — by the Lord of the Yadus; yādavāh — the Yadus; cakruh — performed; paramayā — with transcendental; bhaktyā — devotion; sarva — all; śreyah — with auspicious rituals; upabrimhitam — enriched.

TRANSLATION

There, with great devotion, the Yādavas performed the religious ceremonies according to the instructions of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, their personal Lord. They also performed various other auspicious rituals.
