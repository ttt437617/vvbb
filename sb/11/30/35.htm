Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.35

ajānatā kritam idam

pāpena madhusūdana

kshantum arhasi pāpasya

uttamahśloka me 'nagha

SYNONYMS

ajānatā — who was acting without knowledge; kritam — has been done; idam — this; pāpena — by a sinful person; madhusūdana — O Madhusūdana; kshantum arhasi — please forgive; pāpasya — of the sinful person; uttamah-śloka — O glorious Lord; me — my; anagha — O sinless one.

TRANSLATION

Jarā said: O Lord Madhusūdana, I am a most sinful person. I have committed this act out of ignorance. O purest Lord, O Uttamahśloka, please forgive this sinner.
