Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.20

śareshu hīyamāeshu

bhajyamānesu dhanvasu

śastreshu kshīyamāneshu

mushtibhir jahrur erakāh

SYNONYMS

śareshu — the arrows; hīyamāneshu — as they became depleted; bhajyamāneshu — as they became broken; dhanvasu — the bows; śastreshu — the missile weapons; kshīyamāneshu — as they were used up; mushtibhih — with their fists; jahruh — they seized; erakāh — cane stalks.

TRANSLATION

When all their bows had been broken and their arrows and other missiles spent, they seized the tall stalks of cane with their bare hands.
