Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.48

svam svam parigraham sarve

ādāya pitarau ca nah

arjunenāvitāh sarva

indraprastham gamishyatha

SYNONYMS

svam svam — each his own; parigraham — family; sarve — all of them; ādāya — taking; pitarau — parents; ca — and; nah — Our; arjunena — by Arjuna; avitāh — protected; sarve — all; indraprastham — to Indraprastha; gamishyatha — you should go.

TRANSLATION

You should all take your own families, together with My parents, and under Arjuna's protection go to Indraprastha.
