Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.25

evam nashteshu sarveshu

kuleshu sveshu keśavah

avatārito bhuvo bhāra

iti mene 'vaśeshitah

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; nashteshu — when they had been destroyed; sarveshu — all; kuleshu — the clans of the dynasty; sveshu — His own; keśavah — Lord Krishna; avatāritah — diminished; bhuvah — of the earth; bhārah — the burden; iti — thus; mene — He thought; avaśeshitah — remaining.

TRANSLATION

When all the members of His own dynasty were thus destroyed, Lord Krishna thought to Himself that at last the burden of the earth had been removed.
