Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.23

atha tāv api sańkruddhāv

udyamya kuru-nandana

erakā-mushti-parighau

carantau jaghnatur yudhi

SYNONYMS

atha — then; tau — those two (Krishna and Balarāma); api — also; sańkruddhau — greatly angered; udyamya — joining the fight; kuru-nandana — O favorite son of the Kurus; erakā-mushti — the canes in their fists; parighau — using as clubs; carantau — moving about; jaghnatuh — they began to kill; yudhi — in the battle.

TRANSLATION

O son of the Kurus, Krishna and Balarāma then became very angry. Picking up cane stalks, They moved about within the battle and began to kill with these clubs.
