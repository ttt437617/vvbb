Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.9

vidhir esha hy arishta-ghno

mańgalāyanam uttamam

deva-dvija-gavām pūjā

bhūteshu paramo bhavah

SYNONYMS

vidhih — the prescribed process; eshah — this; hi — indeed; arishta — inauspicious obstacles; ghnah — which destroys; mańgala-ayanam — which brings good fortune; uttamam — the best; deva — of the demigods; dvija — brāhmanas; gavām — and cows; pūjā — worship; bhūteshu — among living beings; paramah — excellent; bhavah — rebirth.

TRANSLATION

This is indeed the appropriate process for counteracting our imminent adversity, and it is sure to bring about the highest good fortune. Such worship of the demigods, brāhmanas and cows can earn the highest birth for all living entities.
