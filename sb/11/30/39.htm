Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 30: The Disappearance of the Yadu Dynasty

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.30.39

śrī-bhagavān uvāca

mā bhair jare tvam uttishtha

kāma esha krito hi me

yāhi tvam mad-anujñātah

svargam su-kritinām padam

SYNONYMS

śrī-bhagavān uvāca — the Supreme Personality of Godhead said; mā bhaih — do not fear; jare — O Jarā; tvam — you; uttishtha — please get up; kāmah — the desire; eshah — this; kritah — done; hi — indeed; me — My; yāhi — go; tvam — you; mat-anujñātah — given permission by Me; svargam — to the spiritual world; su-kritinām — of the pious; padam — the abode.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead said: My dear Jarā, do not fear. Please get up. What has been done is actually My own desire. With My permission, go now to the abode of the pious, the spiritual world.
