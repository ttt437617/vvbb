Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 27: Lord Krishna's Instructions on the Process of Deity Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.27.10

pūrvam snānam prakurvīta

dhauta-danto 'ńga-śuddhaye

ubhayair api ca snānam

mantrair mrid-grahanādinā

SYNONYMS

pūrvam — first; snānam — bath; prakurvīta — one should perform; dhauta — having cleaned; dantah — his teeth; ańga — of the body; śuddhaye — for purification; ubhayaih — with both kinds; api ca — also; snānam — bathing; mantraih — with mantras; mrit-grahana-ādinā — by smearing with earth and so on.

TRANSLATION

One should first purify his body by cleansing his teeth and bathing. Then one should perform a second cleansing by smearing the body with earth and chanting both Vedic and tantric mantras.
