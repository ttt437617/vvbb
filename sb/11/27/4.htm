Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 27: Lord Krishna's Instructions on the Process of Deity Worship

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.27.3-4

nihsritam te mukhāmbhojād

yad āha bhagavān ajah

putrebhyo bhrigu-mukhyebhyo

devyai ca bhagavān bhavah

etad vai sarva-varnānām

āśramānām ca sammatam

śreyasām uttamam manye

strī-śūdrānām ca māna-da

SYNONYMS

nihsritam — emanated; te — Your; mukha-ambhojāt — from the lotus mouth; yat — which; āha — spoke; bhagavān — the great lord; ajah — self-born Brahmā; putrebhyah — to his sons; bhrigu-mukhyebhyah — headed by Bhrigu; devyai — to the goddess Pārvatī; ca — and; bhagavān bhavah — Lord Śiva; etat — this (process of Deity worship); vai — indeed; sarva-varnānām — by all the occupational classes of society; āśramānām — and spiritual orders; ca — also; sammatam — approved; śreyasām — of different kinds of benefit in life; uttamam — the topmost; manye — I think; strī — for women; śūdrānām — and low-class workers; ca — also; māna-da — O magnanimous Lord.

TRANSLATION

O most magnanimous Lord, the instructions on this process of Deity worship first emanated from Your lotus mouth. Then they were spoken by the great Lord Brahmā to his sons, headed by Bhrigu, and by Lord Śiva to his wife, Pārvatī. This process is accepted by and appropriate for all the occupational and spiritual orders of society. Therefore I consider worship of You in Your Deity form to be the most beneficial of all spiritual practices, even for women and śūdras.
