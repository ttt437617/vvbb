Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 14: Lord Krishna Explains the Yoga System to Śrī Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.14.35

evam pranava-samyuktam

prānam eva samabhyaset

daśa-kritvas tri-shavanam

māsād arvāg jitānilah

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; pranava — with the syllable om; samyuktam — joined; prānam — the prānāyāma system of controlling the bodily airs; eva — indeed; samabhyaset — one should carefully practice; daśa-kritvah — ten times; tri-savanam — at sunrise, noon and sunset; māsāt — one month; arvāk — after; jita — one will conquer; anilah — the life air.

TRANSLATION

Being fixed in the omkāra, one should carefully practice the prānāyāma system ten times at each sunrise, noon and sunset. Thus, after one month one will have conquered the life air.
