Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 29: Bhakti-yoga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.29.11

prithak satrena vā mahyam

parva-yātrā-mahotsavān

kārayed gīta-nrityādyair

mahārāja-vibhūtibhih

SYNONYMS

prithak — alone; satrena — in assembly; vā — or; mahyam — for Me; parva — monthly observances, such as Ekādaśi; yātrā — special gatherings; mahā-utsavān — and festivals; kārayet — one should arrange to perform; gīta — with singing; nritya-ādyaih — dancing and so on; mahā-rāja — royal; vibhūtibhih — with signs of opulence.

TRANSLATION

Either alone or in public gatherings, with singing, dancing and other exhibitions of royal opulence, one should arrange to celebrate those holy days, ceremonies and festivals set aside specially for My worship.
