Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 22: Enumeration of the Elements of Material Creation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.22.60

śrī-uddhava uvāca

yathaivam anubudhyeyam

vada no vadatām vara

SYNONYMS

śrī-uddhavah uvāca — Śrī Uddhava said; yathā — how; evam — thus; anubudhyeyam — I may properly understand; vada — please speak; nah — to us; vadatām — of all speakers; vara — O You who are the best.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Uddhava said: O best of all speakers, please explain to me how I may properly understand this.
