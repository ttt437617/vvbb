Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.13

indro 'ham sarva-devānām

vasūnām asmi havya-vāt

ādityānām aham vishnū

rudrānām nīla-lohitah

SYNONYMS

indrah — Lord Indra; aham — I am; sarva-devānām — among the demigods; vasūnām — among the Vasus; asmi — I am; havya-vāt — the carrier of oblations, the fire-god Agni; ādityānām — among the sons of Aditi; aham — I am; vishnuh — Vishnu; rudrānām — among the Rudras; nīla-lohitah — Lord Śiva.

TRANSLATION

Among the demigods I am Indra, and among the Vasus I am Agni, the god of fire. I am Vishnu among the sons of Aditi, and among the Rudras I am Lord Śiva.

PURPORT

Lord Vishnu appeared among the sons of Aditi as Vāmanadeva.
