Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.20

tīrthānām srotasām gańgā

samudrah sarasām aham

āyudhānām dhanur aham

tripura-ghno dhanushmatām

SYNONYMS

tirthānām — among holy places; srotasām — among flowing things; gańgā — the sacred Ganges; samudrah — the ocean; sarasām — among steady bodies of water; aham — I am; āyudhānām — among weapons; dhanuh — the bow; aham — I am; tri-pura-ghnah — Lord Śiva; dhanuh-matām — among those who wield the bow.

TRANSLATION

Among sacred and flowing things I am the holy Ganges, and among steady bodies of water I am the ocean. Among weapons I am the bow, and of the wielders of weapons I am Lord Śiva.

PURPORT

Lord Śiva used his bow to completely cover with arrows the three demoniac cities built by Maya Dānava.
