Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.27

samvatsaro 'smy animishām

ritūnām madhu-mādhavau

māsānām mārgaśīrsho 'ham

nakshatrānām tathābhijit

SYNONYMS

samvatsarah — the year; asmi — I am; animishām — among the vigilant cycles of time; ritūnām — among seasons; madhu-mādhavau — spring; māsānām — among months; mārgaśīrshah — Mārgaśīrsha (November-December); aham — I am; nakshatrānām — among asterisms; tathā — similarly; abhijit — Abhijit.

TRANSLATION

Among the vigilant cycles of time I am the year, and among seasons I am spring. Among months I am Mārgaśīrsha, and among lunar houses I am the auspicious Abhijit.
