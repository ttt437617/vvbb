Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.15

siddheśvarānām kapilah

suparno 'ham patatrinām

prajāpatīnām daksho 'ham

pitrinām aham aryamā

SYNONYMS

siddha-īśvarānām — among perfected beings; kapilah — I am Lord Kapila; suparnah — Garuda; aham — I am; patatrinām — among birds; prajāpatīnām — among the progenitors of mankind; dakshah — Daksha; aham — I am; pitrinām — among the forefathers; aham — I am; aryamā — Aryamā.

TRANSLATION

I am Lord Kapila among perfected beings and Garuda among birds. I am Daksha among the progenitors of mankind, and I am Aryamā among the forefathers.
