Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.12

hiranyagarbho vedānām

mantrānām pranavas tri-vrit

aksharānām a-kāro 'smi

padāni cchandusām aham

SYNONYMS

hiranya-garbhah — Lord Brahmā; vedānām — of the Vedas; mantrānām — of mantras; pranavah — the omkāra; tri-vrit — consisting of three letters; aksharānām — of letters; a-kārah — the first letter, a; asmi — I am; padāni — the three-line Gāyatrī mantra; chandasām — among sacred meters; aham — I am.

TRANSLATION

Among the Vedas I am their original teacher, Lord Brahmā, and of all mantras I am the three-lettered omkāra. Among letters I am the first letter, "a," and among sacred meters I am the Gāyatrī mantra.
