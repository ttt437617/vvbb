Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.14

brahmarshīnām bhrigur aham

rājarshīnām aham manuh

devarshīnām nārado 'ham

havirdhāny asmi dhenushu

SYNONYMS

brahma-rishīnām — among the saintly brāhmanas; bhriguh — Bhrigu Muni; aham — I am; rāja-rishīnām — among the saintly kings; aham — I am; manuh — Manu; deva-rishīnām — among the saintly demigods; nāradah — Nārada Muni; aham — I am; havirdhānī — Kāmadhenu; asmi — I am; dhenushu — among cows.

TRANSLATION

Among saintly brāhmanas I am Bhrigu Muni, and I am Manu among saintly kings. I am Nārada Muni among saintly demigods, and I am Kāmadhenu among cows.
