Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 16: The Lord's Opulence

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.16.22

purodhasām vasishtho 'ham

brahmishthānām brihaspatih

skando 'ham sarva-senānyām

agranyām bhagavān ajah

SYNONYMS

purodhasām — among priests; vasishthah — Vasishtha Muni; aham — I am; brahmishthānām — among those fixed in the Vedic conclusion and purpose; brihaspatih — Brihaspati, the spiritual master of the demigods; skandah — Kārtikeya; aham — I am; sarva-senānyām — among all military leaders; agranyām — among those advancing in pious life; bhagavān — the great personality; ajah — Lord Brahmā.

TRANSLATION

Among priests I am Vasishtha Muni, and among those highly situated in Vedic culture I am Brihaspati. I am Kārtikeya among great military leaders, and among those advancing in superior ways of life I am the great personality Lord Brahmā.
