Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 31: The Disappearance of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.31.26

śrutvā suhrid-vadham rājann

arjunāt te pitāmahāh

tvām tu vamśa-dharam kritvā

jagmuh sarve mahā-patham

SYNONYMS

śrutvā — hearing; suhrit — of their friend; vadham — the death; rājan — O King; arjunāt — from Arjuna; te — your; pitāmahāh — grandfathers (Yudhishthira and his brothers); tvām — you; tu — and; vamśa-dharam — the maintainer of the dynasty; kritvā — making; jagmuh — they left; sarve — all of them; mahā-patham — for the great journey.

TRANSLATION

Hearing from Arjuna of the death of their friend, my dear King, your grandfathers established you as the maintainer of the dynasty and left to prepare for their departure from this world.
