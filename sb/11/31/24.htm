Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 31: The Disappearance of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.31.24

nityam sannihitas tatra

bhagavān madhusūdanah

smrityāśeshāśubha-haram

sarva-mańgala-mańgalam

SYNONYMS

nityam — eternally; sannihitah — present; tatra — there; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; madhusūdanah — Madhusūdana; smrityā — by remembrance; aśesha-aśubha — of everything inauspicious; haram — which takes away; sarva-mańgala — of all auspicious things; mańgalam — the most auspicious.

TRANSLATION

Lord Madhusūdana, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is eternally present in Dvārakā. It is the most auspicious of all auspicious places, and merely remembering it destroys all contamination.
