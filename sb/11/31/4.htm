Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 31: The Disappearance of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.31.4

vavrishuh pushpa-varshāni

vimānāvalibhir nabhah

kurvantah sańkulam rājan

bhaktyā paramayā yutāh

SYNONYMS

vavrishuh — they showered; pushpa-varshāni — showers of flowers; vimāna — of airplanes; āvalibhih — by great numbers; nabhah — the sky; kurvantah — making; sańkulam — filled up; rājan — O King Parīkshit; bhaktyā — with devotion; paramayā — transcendental; yutāh — endowed.

TRANSLATION

O King, crowding the sky with their many airplanes, they showered down flowers with great devotion.
