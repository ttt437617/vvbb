Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 9: Detachment from All that Is Material

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.9.24

evam gurubhya etebhya

eshā me śikshitā matih

svātmopaśikshitām buddhim

śrinu me vadatah prabho

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; gurubhyah — from the spiritual masters; etebhyah — from these; eshā — this; me — by me; śikshitā — learned; matih — knowledge; sva-ātma — from one's own body; upaśikshitām — learned; buddhim — knowledge; śrinu — please hear; me — from me; vadatah — as I am speaking; prabho — O King.

TRANSLATION

O King, from all these spiritual masters I have acquired great wisdom. Now please listen as I explain what I learned from my own body.
