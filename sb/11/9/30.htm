Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 9: Detachment from All that Is Material

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.9.30

evam sañjāta-vairāgyo

vijñānāloka ātmani

vicarāmi mahīm etām

mukta-sańgo 'nahańkritah

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; sañjāta — completely developed; vairāgyah — detachment; vijñāna — realized knowledge; ālokah — having vision; ātmani — in the Supreme Personality of Godhead; vicarāmi — I wander; mahīm — the earth; etām — this; mukta — freed; sańgah — from attachment; anahańkritah — without false ego.

TRANSLATION

Having learned from my spiritual masters, I remain situated in realization of the Supreme Personality of Godhead and, fully renounced and enlightened by realized spiritual knowledge, wander the earth without attachment or false ego.
