Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 7: Lord Krishna Instructs Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.7.36

yato yad anuśikshāmi

yathā vā nāhushātmaja

tat tathā purusha-vyāghra

nibodha kathayāmi te

SYNONYMS

yatah — from whom; yat — what; anuśikshāmi — I have learned; yathā — how; vā — and; nāhusha-ātma-ja — O son of King Nāhusha (Yayāti); tat — that; tathā — thus; purusha-vyāghra — O tiger among men; nibodha — listen; kathayāmi — I will recount; te — to you.

TRANSLATION

Please listen, O son of Mahārāja Yayāti, O tiger among men, as I explain to you what I have learned from each of these gurus.
