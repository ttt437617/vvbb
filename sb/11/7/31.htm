Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 7: Lord Krishna Instructs Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.7.31

śrī-bhagavān uvāca

yadunaivam mahā-bhāgo

brahmanyena su-medhasā

prishtah sabhājitah prāha

praśrayāvanatam dvijah

SYNONYMS

śrī-bhagavān uvāca — the Supreme Personality of Godhead said; yadunā — by King Yadu; evam — in this way; mahā-bhāgah — greatly fortunate; brahmanyena — who was very respectful to brāhmanas; su-medhasā — and intelligent; prishtah — asked; sabhājitah — honored; prāha — he spoke; praśraya — out of humility; avanatam — bowing his head; dvijah — the brāhmana.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna continued: The intelligent King Yadu, always respectful to the brāhmanas, waited with bowed head as the brāhmana, pleased with the King's attitude, began to reply.
