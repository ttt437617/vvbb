Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 7: Lord Krishna Instructs Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.7.12

sarva-bhūta-suhric chānto

jñāna-vijñāna-niścayah

paśyan mad-ātmakam viśvam

na vipadyeta vai punah

SYNONYMS

sarva-bhūta — to all creatures; su-hrit — a well-wisher; śāntah — peaceful; jñāna-vijñāna — in knowledge and transcendental realization; niścayah — firmly fixed; paśyan — seeing; mat-ātmakam — pervaded by Me; viśvam — the universe; na vipadyeta — will never fall into the cycle of repeated birth and death; vai — indeed; punah — again.

TRANSLATION

One who is the kind well-wisher of all living beings, who is peaceful and firmly fixed in knowledge and realization, sees Me within all things. Such a person never again falls down into the cycle of birth and death.
