Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 7: Lord Krishna Instructs Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.7.2

mayā nishpāditam hy atra

deva-kāryam aśeshatah

yad-artham avatīrno 'ham

amśena brahmanārthitah

SYNONYMS

mayā — by Me; nishpāditam — accomplished; hi — certainly; atra — within this world; deva-kāryam — work for the benefit of the demigods; aśeshatah — completely, with nothing remaining; yat — for whose; artham — sake; avatīrnah — incarnated; aham — I; amśena — with My plenary portion, Baladeva; brahmanā — by Lord Brahmā; arthitah — being prayed for.

TRANSLATION

Answering the prayer of Lord Brahmā, I descended within this world along with My plenary portion, Lord Baladeva, and performed various activities on behalf of the demigods. I have now completed My mission here.
