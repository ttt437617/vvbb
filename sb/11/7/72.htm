Canto 11: General HistoryChapter 7: Lord Krishna Instructs Uddhava

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 11.7.72

tam labdhvā lubdhakah krūrah

kapotam griha-medhinam

kapotakān kapotīm ca

siddhārthah prayayau griham

SYNONYMS

tam — him; labdhvā — taking; lubdhakah — the hunter; krūrah — cruel; kapotam — the pigeon; griha-medhinam — the materialistic householder; kapotakān — the pigeon children; kapotīm — the pigeon wife; ca — also; siddha-arthah — having achieved his purposes; prayayau — he set off; griham — for his home.

TRANSLATION

The cruel hunter, having fulfilled his desire by capturing the head pigeon, his wife and all of their children, set off for his own home.
