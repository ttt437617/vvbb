Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 7: The Purānic Literatures

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.7.17

naimittikah prākritiko

nitya ātyantiko layah

samstheti kavibhih proktaś

caturdhāsya svabhāvatah

SYNONYMS

naimittikah — occasional; prākritikah — elemental; nityah — continuous; ātyantikah — ultimate; layah — annihilation; samsthā — the dissolution; iti — thus; kavibhih — by learned scholars; proktah — described; caturdhā — in four aspects; asya — of this universe; svabhāvatah — by the inherent energy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

There are four types of cosmic annihilation — occasional, elemental, continuous and ultimate — all of which are effected by the inherent potency of the Supreme Lord. Learned scholars have designated this topic dissolution.
