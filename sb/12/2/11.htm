Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 2: The Symptoms of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.2.11

trimśad vimśati varshāni

paramāyuh kalau nrinām

SYNONYMS

trimśat — thirty; vimśati — plus twenty; varshāni — years; parama-āyuh — the maximum duration of life; kalau — in Kali-yuga; nrinām — of men.

TRANSLATION

The maximum duration of life for human beings in Kali-yuga will become fifty years.
