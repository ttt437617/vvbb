Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 2: The Symptoms of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.2.22

teshām prajā-visargaś ca

sthavishthah sambhavishyati

vāsudeve bhagavati

sattva-mūrtau hridi sthite

SYNONYMS

teshām — of them; prajā — of progeny; visargah — the creation; ca — and; sthavishthah — abundant; sambhavishyati — will be; vāsudeve — Lord Vāsudeva; bhagavati — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sattva-mūrtau — in His transcendental form of pure goodness; hridi — in their hearts; sthite — when He is situated.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Vāsudeva, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, appears in their hearts in His transcendental form of goodness, the remaining citizens will abundantly repopulate the earth.
