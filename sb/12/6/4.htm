Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.4

purāna-samhitām etām

aśraushma bhavato vayam

yasyām khalūttamah-śloko

bhagavān anavarnyate

SYNONYMS

purāna-samhitām — essential summary of all the Purānas; etām — this; aśraushma — have heard; bhavatah — from you; vayam — we; yasyām — in which; khalu — indeed; uttamah-ślokah — who is always described in choice poetry; bhagavān — the Personality of Godhead; anuvarnyate — is fittingly described.

TRANSLATION

I have heard from you this Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, which is the perfect summary of all the Purānas and which perfectly describes the Supreme Lord, Uttamahśloka.
