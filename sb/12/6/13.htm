Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.13

brahma-bhūtasya rājarsher

deho 'hi-garalāgninā

babhūva bhasmasāt sadyah

paśyatām sarva-dehinām

SYNONYMS

brahma-bhūtasya — of the fully self-realized; rāja-risheh — the saint among kings; dehah — the body; ahi — of the snake; garala — from the poison; agninā — by the fire; babhūva — turned; bhasma-sāt — to ashes; sadyah — immediately; paśyatām — while they were watching; sarva-dehinām — all embodied living beings.

TRANSLATION

While living beings all over the universe looked on, the body of the great self-realized saint among kings was immediately burned to ashes by the fire of the snake's poison.
