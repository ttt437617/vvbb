Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.61

vaiśampāyana-śishyā vai

carakādhvaryavo 'bhavan

yac cerur brahma-hatyāmhah

kshapanam sva-guror vratam

SYNONYMS

vaiśampāyana-śishyāh — the disciples of Vaiśampāyana; vai — indeed; caraka — named the Carakas; adhvaryavah — authorities of the Atharva Veda; abhavan — became; yat — because; ceruh — they executed; brahma-hatyā — due to the killing of a brāhmana; amhah — of the sin; kshapanam — the expiration; sva-guroh — for their own guru; vratam — the vow.

TRANSLATION

The disciples of Vaiśampāyana became authorities in the Atharva Veda. They were known as the Carakas because they executed strict vows to free their guru from his sin of killing a brāhmana.
