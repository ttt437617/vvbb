Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.60

bahvricāh samhitā hy etā

ebhir brahmarshibhir dhritāh

śrutvaitac-chandasām vyāsam

sarva-pāpaih pramucyate

SYNONYMS

bahu-ricāh — of the Rig Veda; samhitāh — the collections; hi — indeed; etāh — these; ebhih — by these; brahma-rishibhih — saintly brāhmanas; dhritāh — maintained through disciplic succession; śrutvā — hearing; etat — their; chandasām — of the sacred verses; vyāsam — the process of division; sarva-pāpaih — from all sins; pramucyate — one becomes delivered.

TRANSLATION

Thus these various samhitās of the Rig Veda were maintained through disciplic succession by these saintly brāhmanas. Simply by hearing of this distribution of the Vedic hymns, one will be freed from all sins.
