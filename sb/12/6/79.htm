Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.79

laugākshir māńgalih kulyah

kuśīdah kukshir eva ca

paushyañji-sishyā jagrihuh

samhitās te śatam śatam

SYNONYMS

laugākshih māńgalih kulyah — Laugākshi, Māńgali and Kulya; kuśīdah kukshih — Kuśīda and Kukshi; eva — indeed; ca — also; paushyañji-śishyāh — disciples of Paushyañji; jagrihuh — they took; samhitāh — collections; te — they; śatam śatam — each one hundred.

TRANSLATION

Five other disciples of Paushyañji, namely Laugākshi, Māńgali, Kulya, Kuśīda and Kukshi, each received one hundred samhitās.
