Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.35

namo bhagavate tasmai

krishnāyākuntha-medhase

yat-pādāmburuha-dhyānāt

samhitām adhyagām imām

SYNONYMS

namah — obeisances; bhagavate — to the Supreme Personality of Godhead; tasmai — to Him; krishnāya — Lord Śrī Krishna; akuntha-medhase — whose power is never impeded; yat — whose; pāda-ambu-ruha — upon the lotus feet; dhyānāt — by meditation; samhitām — the scripture; adhyagām — I have assimilated; imām — this.

TRANSLATION

I offer my obeisances to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the invincible Lord Śrī Krishna. Simply by meditating upon His lotus feet I have been able to study and appreciate this great literature.
