Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 6: Mahārāja Parīkshit Passes Away

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.6.8

sūta uvāca

ity uktas tam anujñāpya

bhagavān bādarāyanih

jagāma bhikshubhih sākam

nara-devena pūjitah

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; iti — thus; uktah — spoken to; tam — him; anujñāpya — giving permission; bhagavān — the powerful saint; bādarāyanih — Śukadeva, the son of Bādarāyana Vedavyāsa; jagāma — went away; bhikshubhih — the renounced sages; sākam — along with; nara-devena — by the King; pūjitah — worshiped.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: Thus requested, the saintly son of Śrīla Vyāsadeva gave his permission to King Parīkshit. Then, after being worshiped by the King and all the sages present, Śukadeva departed from that place.
