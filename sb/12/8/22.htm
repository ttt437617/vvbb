Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 8: Mārkandeya's Prayers to Nara-Nārāyana Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.8.22

anvīyamāno gandharvair

gīta-vāditra-yūthakaih

adriśyatātta-cāpeshuh

svah-strī-yūtha-patih smarah

SYNONYMS

anvīyamānah — being followed; gandharvaih — by Gandharvas; gīta — of singers; vāditra — and players of musical instruments; yūthakaih — by companies; adriśyata — was seen; ātta — holding up; cāpa-ishuh — his bow and arrows; svah-strī-yūtha — of hoardes of heavenly women; patih — the master; smarah — Cupid.

TRANSLATION

Cupid, the master of many heavenly women, then came there holding his bow and arrows. He was followed by groups of Gandharvas playing musical instruments and singing.
