Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 8: Mārkandeya's Prayers to Nara-Nārāyana Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.8.38

tayor āsanam ādāya

pādayor avanijya ca

arhanenānulepena

dhūpa-mālyair apūjayat

SYNONYMS

tayoh — to Them; āsanam — sitting places; ādāya — offering; pādayoh — Their feet; avanijya — hathing; ca — and; arhanena — with suitable respectful offerings; anulepena — by anointing Them with sandalwood pulp and other fragrant substances; dhūpa — with incense; mālyaih — and flower garlands; apūjayat — he worshiped.

TRANSLATION

He gave Them sitting places and washed Their feet, and then he worshiped Them with presentations of arghya, sandalwood pulp, fragrant oils, incense and flower garlands.
