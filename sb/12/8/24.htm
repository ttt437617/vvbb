Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 8: Mārkandeya's Prayers to Nara-Nārāyana Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.8.24

nanritus tasya puratah

striyo 'tho gāyakā jaguh

mridańga-vīnā-panavair

vādyam cakrur mano-ramam

SYNONYMS

nanrituh — danced; tasya — of him; puratah — in front; striyah — women; atha u — and furthermore; gāyakāh — singers; jaguh — sang; mridańga — with drums; vīnā — stringed instruments; panavaih — and cymbals; vādyam — instrumental music; cakruh — they made; manah-ramam — charming.

TRANSLATION

The women danced before the sage, and the celestial singers sang to the charming accompaniment of drums, cymbals and vīnās.
