Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 8: Mārkandeya's Prayers to Nara-Nārāyana Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.8.12

brahmā bhrigur bhavo daksho

brahma-putrāś ca ye 'pare

nri-deva-pitri-bhūtāni

tenāsann ati-vismitāh

SYNONYMS

brahmā — Lord Brahmā; bhriguh — Bhrigu Muni; bhavah — Lord Śiva; dakshah — Prajāpati Daksha; brahma-putrāh — the great sons of Brahmā; ca — and; ye — who; apare — others; nri — human beings; deva — demigods; pitri — forefathers; bhūtāni — and ghostly spirits; tena — with that (conquest of death); āsan — they all became; ati-vismitāh — extremely amazed.

TRANSLATION

Lord Brahmā, Bhrigu Muni, Lord Śiva, Prajāpati Daksha, the great sons of Brahmā, and many others among the human beings, demigods, forefathers and ghostly spirits — all were astonished by the achievement of Mārkandeya Rishi.
