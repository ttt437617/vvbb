Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 8: Mārkandeya's Prayers to Nara-Nārāyana Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.8.14

tasyaivam yuñjataś cittam

mahā-yogena yoginah

vyatīyāya mahān kālo

manvantara-shad-ātmakah

SYNONYMS

tasya — he; evam — thus; yuñjatah — while fixing; cittam — his mind; mahā-yogena — by powerful practice of yoga; yoginah — the mystic sage; vyatīyāya — passed by; mahān — a great; kālah — period of time; manu-antara — lifetimes of Manu; shat — six; ātmakah — consisting of.

TRANSLATION

While the mystic sage thus concentrated his mind by powerful yoga practice, the tremendous period of six lifetimes of Manu passed by.
