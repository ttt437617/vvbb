Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.28

tato 'shtau yavanā bhāvyāś

caturdaśa turushkakāh

bhūyo daśa gurundāś ca

maulā ekādaśaiva tu

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; ashtau — eight; yavanāh — Yavanas; bhāvyāh — will be; catuh-daśa — fourteen; turushkakāh — Turushkas; bhūyah — furthermore; daśa — ten; gurundāh — Gurundas; ca — and; maulāh — Maulas; ekādaśa — eleven; eva — indeed; tu — and.

TRANSLATION

Eight Yavanas will then take power, followed by fourteen Turushkas, ten Gurundas and eleven kings of the Maula dynasty.
