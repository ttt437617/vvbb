Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.12

sa eva candraguptam vai

dvijo rājye 'bhishekshyati

tat-suto vārisāras tu

tataś cāśokavardhanah

SYNONYMS

sah — he (Cānakya); eva — indeed; candraguptam — Prince Candragupta; vai — indeed; dvijah — the brāhmana; rājye — in the role of king; abhishekshyati — will install; tat — of Candragupta; sutah — the son; vārisārah — Vārisāra; tu — and; tatah — following Vārisāra; ca — and; aśokavardhanah — Aśokavardhana.

TRANSLATION

This brāhmana will enthrone Candragupta, whose son will be named Vārisāra. The son of Vārisāra will be Aśokavardhana.
