Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.37

sindhos tatam candrabhāgām

kauntīm kāśmīra-mandalam

bhokshyanti śūdrā vrātyādyā

mlecchāś cābrahma-varcasah

SYNONYMS

sindhoh — of the River Sindhu; tatam — the land on the shore; candrabhāgām — Candrabhāgā; kauntīm — Kauntī; kāśmīra-mandalam — the region of Kāśmīra; bhokshyanti — will rule; śūdrāh — śūdras; vrātya-ādyāh — brāhmanas who have fallen from the brahminical standard, and other disqualified men; mlecchāh — meat-eaters; ca — and; abrahma-varcasah — lacking spiritual potency.

TRANSLATION

The land along the Sindhu River, as well as the districts of Candrabhāgā, Kauntī and Kāśmīra, will be ruled by śūdras, fallen brāhmanas and meat-eaters. Having given up the path of Vedic civilization, they will have lost all spiritual strength.
