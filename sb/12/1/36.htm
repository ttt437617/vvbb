Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.36

saurāshtrāvanty-ābhīrāś ca

śūrā arbuda-mālavāh

vrātyā dvijā bhavishyanti

śūdra-prāyā janādhipāh

SYNONYMS

śaurāshtra — residing in Śaurāshtra; avantī — in Avantī; ābhīrāh — and in Ābhīra; ca — and; śūrāh — residing in the Śūra province; arbuda-mālavāh — residing in Arbuda and Mālava; vrātyāh — deviated from all purificatory rituals; dvijāh — the brāhmanas; bhavishyanti — will become; śūdra-prāyāh — no better than śūdras; jana-adhipāh — the kings.

TRANSLATION

At that time the brāhmanas of such provinces as Śaurāshtra, Avantī, Ābhīra, Śūra, Arbuda and Mālava will forget all their regulative principles, and the members of the royal order in these places will become no better than śūdras.
