Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 1: The Degraded Dynasties of Kali-yuga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.1.5

vidhisārah sutas tasyā-

jātaśatrur bhavishyati

darbhakas tat-suto bhāvī

darbhakasyājayah smritah

SYNONYMS

vidhisārah — Vidhisāra; sutah — the son; tasya — of Kshetrajña; ajātaśatruh — Ajātaśatru; bhavishyati — will be; darbhakah — Darbhaka; tat-sutah — the son of Ajātaśatru; bhāvī — will take birth; darbhakasya — of Darbhaka; ajayah — Ajaya; smritah — is remembered.

TRANSLATION

The son of Kshetrajña will be Vidhisāra, and his son will be Ajātaśatru. Ajātaśatru will have a son named Darbhaka, and his son will be Ajaya.
