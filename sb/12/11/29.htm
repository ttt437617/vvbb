Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.29

sūta uvāca

anādy-avidyayā vishnor

ātmanah sarva-dehinām

nirmito loka-tantro 'yam

lokeshu parivartate

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; anādi — beginningless; avidyayā — by the illusory energy; vishnoh — of Lord Vishnu; ātmanah — who is the Supreme Soul; sarva-dehinām — of all embodied living beings; nirmitah — produced; loka-tantrah — the regulator of the planets; ayam — this; lokeshu — among the planets; parivartate — travels.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: The sun travels among all the planets and thus regulates their movements. It has been created by Lord Vishnu, the Supreme Soul of all embodied beings, through His beginningless material energy.
