Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.43

tvashtā ricīka-tanayah

kambalaś ca tilottamā

brahmāpeto 'tha satajid

dhritarāshtra isham-bharāh

SYNONYMS

tvashtā — Tvashtā; ricīka-tanayah — the son of Ricīka (Jamadagni); kambalah — Kambala; ca — and; tilottamā — Tilottamā; brahmāpetah — Brahmāpeta; atha — and; śatajit — Śatajit; dhritarāshtrah — Dhritarāshtra; isham-bharāh — the maintainers of the month Isha (Āśvina).

TRANSLATION

Tvashtā as the sun-god; Jamadagni, the son of Ricīka, as the sage; Kambalāśva as the Nāga; Tilottamā as the Apsarā; Brahmāpeta as the Rākshasa; Śatajit as the Yaksha; and Dhritarāshtra as the Gandharva maintain the month of Isha.
