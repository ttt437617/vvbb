Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.21

vāsudevah sańkarshanah

pradyumnah purushah svayam

aniruddha iti brahman

mūrti-vyūho 'bhidhīyate

SYNONYMS

vāsudevah sańkarshanah pradyumnah — Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana and Pradyumna; purushah — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; svayam — Himself; aniruddhah — Aniruddha; iti — thus; brahman — O brāhmana, Śaunaka; mūrti-vyūhah — the expansion of personal forms; abhidhīyate — is designated.

TRANSLATION

Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana, Pradyumna and Aniruddha are the names of the direct personal expansions of the Supreme Godhead, O brāhmana Śaunaka.
