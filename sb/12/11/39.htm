Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.39

pūshā dhanañjayo vātah

sushenah surucis tathā

ghritācī gautamaś ceti

tapo-māsam nayanty amī

SYNONYMS

pūshā dhanañjayah vātah — Pūshā, Dhanañjaya and Vāta; sushenah surucih — Sushena and Suruci; tathā — also; ghritācī gautamah — Ghritācī and Gautama; ca — as well; iti — thus; tapah-māsam — the month of Tapas (Māgha); nayanti — rule; amī — these.

TRANSLATION

Pūshā as the sun-god, Dhanañjaya as the Nāga, Vāta as the Rākshasa, Sushena as the Gandharva, Suruci as the Yaksha, Ghritācī as the Apsarā and Gautama as the sage rule the month of Tapas.
