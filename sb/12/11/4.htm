Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.4

sūta uvāca

namaskritya gurūn vakshye

vibhūtīr vaishnavīr api

yāh proktā veda-tantrābhyām

ācāryaih padmajādibhih

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; namaskritya — offering obeisances; gurūn — to the spiritual masters; vakshye — I shall speak; vibhūtīh — the opulences; vaishnavīh — belonging to Lord Vishnu; api — indeed; yāh — which; proktāh — are described; veda-tantrābhyām — by the Vedas and the tantras; ācāryaih — by standard authorities; padmaja-ādibhih — beginning with Lord Brahmā.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: Offering obeisances to my spiritual masters, I shall repeat to you the description of the opulences of Lord Vishnu given in the Vedas and tantras by great authorities, beginning from lotus-born Brahmā.
