Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.32

madhv-ādishu dvādaśasu

bhagavān kāla-rūpa-dhrik

loka-tantrāya carati

prithag dvādaśabhir ganaih

SYNONYMS

madhu-ādishu — beginning with Madhu; dvādaśasu — in the twelve (months); bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; kāla-rūpa — the form of time; dhrik — assuming; loka-tantrāya — to regulate planetary motion; carati — travels; prithak — separately; dvādaśabhih — with twelve; ganaih — sets of associates.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead, manifesting His potency of time as the sun-god, travels about in each of the twelve months, beginning with Madhu, to regulate planetary motion within the universe. Traveling with the sun-god in each of the twelve months is a different set of six associates.
