Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.42

bhagah sphūrjo 'rishtanemir

ūrna āyuś ca pañcamah

karkotakah pūrvacittih

pushya-māsam nayanty amī

SYNONYMS

bhagah sphūrjah arishtanemih — Bhaga, Sphūrja and Arishtanemi; ūrnah — Ūrna; āyuh — Āyur; ca — and; pañcamah — the fifth associate; karkotakah pūrvacittih — Karkotaka and Pūrvacitti; pushya-māasam — the month of Pushya; nayanti — rule; amī — these.

TRANSLATION

Bhaga as the sun-god, Sphūrja as the Rākshasa, Arishtanemi as the Gandharva, Ūrna as the Yaksha, Āyur as the sage, Karkotaka as the Nāga and Pūrvacitti as the Apsarā rule the month of Pushya.
