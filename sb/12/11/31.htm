Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 11: Summary Description of the Mahāpurusha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.11.31

kālo deśah kriyā kartā

karanam kāryam āgamah

dravyam phalam iti brahman

navadhokto 'jayā harih

SYNONYMS

kālah — time; deśah — place; kriyā — endeavor; kartā — performer; karanam — instrument; kāryam — specific ritual; āgamah — scripture; dravyam — paraphernalia; phalam — result; iti — thus; brahman — O brāhmana, Śaunaka; navadhā — in nine phases; uktah — described; ajayā — in terms of the material energy; harih — Lord Hari.

TRANSLATION

Being the source of the material energy, the Personality of Godhead Lord Hari in His expansion as the sun-god is described in nine aspects, O Śaunaka: the time, the place, the endeavor, the performer, the instrument, the specific ritual, the scripture, the paraphernalia of worship and the result to be achieved.
