Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 13: The Glories of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.13.16

nimna-gānām yathā gańgā

devānām acyuto yathā

vaishnavānām yathā śambhuh

purānānām idam tathā

SYNONYMS

nimna-gānām — of rivers flowing down to the sea; yathā — as; gańgā — the Ganges; devānām — of all deities; acyutah — the infallible Supreme Personality of Godhead; yathā — as; vaishnavānām — of devotees of Lord Vishnu; yathā — as; śambhuh — Śiva; purānānām — of Purānas; idam — this; tathā — similarly.

TRANSLATION

Just as the Gańgā is the greatest of all rivers, Lord Acyuta the supreme among deities and Lord Śambhu [Śiva] the greatest of Vaishnavas, so Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is the greatest of all Purānas.
