Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 5: Śukadeva Gosvāmī's Final Instructions to Mahārāja Parīkshit

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.5.6

manah srijati vai dehān

gunān karmāni cātmanah

tan manah srijate māyā

tato jīvasya samsritih

SYNONYMS

manah — the mind; srijati — produces; vai — indeed; dehān — the material bodies; gunān — the qualities; karmāni — the activities; ca — and; ātmanah — of the soul; tat — that; manah — mind; srijate — produces; māyā — the illusory potency of the Supreme Lord; tatah — thus; jīvasya — of the individual living being; samsritih — the material existence.

TRANSLATION

The material bodies, qualities and activities of the spirit soul are created by the material mind. That mind is itself created by the illusory potency of the Supreme Lord, and thus the soul assumes material existence.
