Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.5

bhakti-yogah samākhyāto

vairāgyam ca tad-āśrayam

pārīkshitam upākhyānam

nāradākhyānam eva ca

SYNONYMS

bhakti-yogah — the process of devotional service; samākhyātah — is thoroughly enunciated; vairāgyam — renunciation; ca — and; tat-āśrayam — which is subsidiary to it; pārīkshitam — of Mahārāja Parīkshit; upākhyānam — the history; nārada — of Nārada; ākhyānam — the history; eva — indeed; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

The following topics are also narrated: the process of devotional service together with its subsidiary feature of renunciation, and the histories of Mahārāja Parīkshit and the sage Nārada.
