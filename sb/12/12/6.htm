Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.6

prāyopaveśo rājarsher

vipra-śāpāt parīkshitah

śukasya brahmarshabhasya

samvādaś ca parīkshitah

SYNONYMS

prāya-upaveśah — the fast until death; rāja-risheh — of the sage among kings; vipra-śāpāt — because of the curse of the brāhmana's son; parīkshitah — of King Parīkshit; śukasya — of Śukadeva; brahma-rishabhasya — the best of brāhmanas; samvādah — the conversation; ca — and; parīkshitah — with Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

Also described are saintly King Parīkshit's sitting down to fast until death in response to the curse of a brāhmana's son, and the conversations between Parīkshit and Śukadeva Gosvāmī, who is the best of all brāhmanas.
