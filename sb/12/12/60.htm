Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.60

dvādaśyām ekādaśyām vā

śrinvann āyushyavān bhavet

pathaty anaśnan prayatah

pūto bhavati pātakāt

SYNONYMS

dvādaśyām — on the twelfth day of either fortnight of the month; ekādaśyām — on the auspicious eleventh day; vā — or; śrinvan — hearing; āyushya-vān — possessed of long life; bhavet — one becomes; pathati — if one recites; anaśnan — while refraining from eating; prayatah — with careful attention; pūtah — purified; bhavati — one becomes; pātakāt — from sinful reactions.

TRANSLATION

One who hears this Bhāgavatam on the Ekādaśī or Dvādaśī day is assured of long life, and one who recites it with careful attention while fasting is purified of all sinful reactions.
