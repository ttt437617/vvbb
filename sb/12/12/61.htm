Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.61

pushkare mathurayām ca

dvāravatyām yatātmavān

uposhya samhitām etām

pathitvā mucyate bhayāt

SYNONYMS

pushkare — at the holy place Pushkara; mathurāyām — at Mathurā; ca — and; dvāravatyām — at Dvārakā; yata-ātma-vān — self-controlled; uposhya — fasting; samhitām — literature; etām — this; pathitvā — reciting; mucyate — one becomes freed; bhayāt — from fear.

TRANSLATION

One who controls his mind, fasts at the holy places Pushkara, Mathurā or Dvārakā, and studies this scripture will be freed from all fear.
