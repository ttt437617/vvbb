Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.63

rico yajūmshi sāmāni

dvijo 'dhītyānuvindate

madhu-kulyā ghrita-kulyāh

payah-kulyāś ca tat phalam

SYNONYMS

ricah — the mantras of the Rig Veda; yajūmshi — those of the Yajur Veda; sāmāni — and those of the Sāma Veda; dvijah — a brāhmana; adhītya — studying; anuvindate — obtains; madhu-kulyāh — rivers of honey; ghrita-kulyāh — rivers of ghee; payah-kulyāh — rivers of milk; ca — and; tat — that; phalam — fruit.

TRANSLATION

By studying this Bhāgavatam, a brāhmana can enjoy the same rivers of honey, ghee and milk he enjoys by studying the hymns of the Rig, Yajur and Sāma Vedas.
