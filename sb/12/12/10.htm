Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.10

kālasya sthūla-sūkshmasya

gatih padma-samudbhavah

bhuva uddharane 'mbhodher

hiranyāksha-vadho yathā

SYNONYMS

kālasya — of time; sthūla-sūkshmasya — gross and subtle; gatih — the movement; padma — of the lotus; samudbhavah — the generation; bhuvah — of the earth; uddharane — in connection with the deliverance; ambhodheh — from the ocean; hiranyāksha-vadhah — the killing of the demon Hiranyāksha; yathā — as it occurred.

TRANSLATION

Other topics include the subtle and gross movements of time, the generation of the lotus from the navel of Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu, and the killing of the demon Hiranyāksha when the earth was delivered from the Garbhodaka Ocean.
