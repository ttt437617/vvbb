Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.27

yatrāvatīrino bhagavān

krishnākhyo jagad-īśvarah

vasudeva-grihe janma

tato vriddhiś ca gokule

SYNONYMS

yatra — in which dynasty; avatīrnah — descended; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna-ākhyah — known as Krishna; jagat-īśvarah — the Lord of the universe; vasudeva-grihe — in the home of Vasudeva; janma — His birth; tatah — subsequently; vriddhih — His growing up; ca — and; gokule — in Gokula.

TRANSLATION

How Śrī Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead and Lord of the universe, descended into this Yadu dynasty, how He took birth in the home of Vasudeva, and how He then grew up in Gokula — all this is described in detail.
