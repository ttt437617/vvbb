Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 12: The Topics of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam Summarized

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.12.21

devāsura-mahā-yuddham

rāja-vamśānukīrtanam

ikshvāku-janma tad-vamśah

sudyumnasya mahātmanah

SYNONYMS

deva-asura — of the demigods and demons; mahā-yuddham — the great war; rāja-vamśa — of the dynasties of kings; anukīrtanam — the reciting in sequence; ikshvāku-janma — the birth of Ikshvāku; tat-vamśah — his dynasty; sudyamnasya — (and the dynasty) of Sudyumna; mahā-ātmanah — the great soul.

TRANSLATION

An account of the great battle fought between the demigods and the demons, a systematic description of the dynasties of various kings, and narrations concerning Ikshvāku's birth, his dynasty and the dynasty of the pious Sudyumna — all are presented within this literature.
