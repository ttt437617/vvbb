Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 9: Mārkandeya Rishi Sees the Illusory Potency of the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.9.1

sūta uvāca

samstuto bhagavān ittham

mārkandeyena dhīmatā

nārāyano nara-sakhah

prīta āha bhrigūdvaham

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; samstutah — properly glorified; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; ittham — in this way; mārkandeyena — by Mārkandeya; dhī-matā — the intelligent sage; nārāyanah — Lord Nārāyana; nara-sakhah — the friend of Nara; prītah — satisfied; āha — spoke; bhrigu-udvaham — to the most eminent descendant of Bhrigu.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: The Supreme Lord Nārāyana, the friend of Nara, was satisfied by the proper glorification offered by the intelligent sage Mārkandeya. Thus the Lord addressed that excellent descendant of Bhrigu.
