Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 10: Lord Śiva and Umā Glorify Mārkandeya Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.10.8

sūta uvāca

ity uktvā tam upeyāya

bhagavān sa satām gatih

īśānah sarva-vidyānām

īśvarah sarva-dehinām

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; iti — thus; uktvā — having said; tam — to the sage; upeyāya — going; bhagavān — the exalted demigod; sah — he; satām — of the pure souls; gatih — the shelter; īśānah — the master; sarva-vidyānām — of all branches of knowledge; īśvarah — the controller; sarva-dehinām — of all embodied living beings.

TRANSLATION

Sūta Gosvāmī said: Having spoken thus, Lord Śańkara — the shelter of pure souls, master of all spiritual sciences and controller of all embodied living beings — approached the sage.
