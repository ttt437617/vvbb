Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 10: Lord Śiva and Umā Glorify Mārkandeya Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.10.16

āha tv ātmānubhāvena

pūrna-kāmasya te vibho

karavāma kim īśāna

yenedam nirvritam jagat

SYNONYMS

āha — Mārkandeya said; tu — indeed; ātma-anubhāvena — by your own experience of ecstasy; pūrna-kāmasya — who is satisfied in all respects; te — for you; vibho — O mighty one; karavāma — I can do; kim — what; īśāna — O lord; yena — by whom; idam — this; nirvritam — is made peaceful; jagat — the entire world.

TRANSLATION

Mārkandeya said: O mighty lord, what can I possibly do for you, who are fully satisfied by your own ecstasy? Indeed, by your mercy you satisfy this entire world.
