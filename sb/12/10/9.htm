Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 10: Lord Śiva and Umā Glorify Mārkandeya Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.10.9

tayor āgamanam sākshād

īśayor jagad-ātmanoh

na veda ruddha-dhī-vrittir

ātmānam viśvam eva ca

SYNONYMS

tayoh — of the two of them; āgamanam — the arrival; sākshāt — in person; īśayoh — of the powerful personalities; jagat-ātmanoh — the controllers of the universe; na veda — he did not notice; ruddha — checked; dhī-vrittih — the functioning of his mind; ātmānam — himself; viśvam — the external universe; eva — indeed; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

Because Mārkandeya's material mind had stopped functioning, the sage failed to notice that Lord Śiva and his wife, the controllers of the universe, had personally come to see him. Mārkandeya was so absorbed in meditation that he was unaware of either himself or the external world.
