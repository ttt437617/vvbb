Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 10: Lord Śiva and Umā Glorify Mārkandeya Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.10.36

kāmo maharshe sarvo 'yam

bhaktimāms tvam adhokshaje

ā-kalpāntād yaśah punyam

ajarāmaratā tathā

SYNONYMS

kāmah — desire; mahā-rishe — O great sage; sarvah — all; ayam — this; bhakti-mān — full of devotion; tvam — you; adhokshaje — for the transcendental Personality of Godhead; ā-kalpa-antāt — up until the end of the day of Brahmā; yaśah — fame; punyam — pious; ajara-amaratā — freedom from old age and death; tathā — also.

TRANSLATION

O great sage, because you are devoted to Lord Adhokshaja, all your desires will be fulfilled. Until the very end of this creation cycle, you will enjoy pious fame and freedom from old age and death.
