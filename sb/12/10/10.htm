Canto 12: The Age of DeteriorationChapter 10: Lord Śiva and Umā Glorify Mārkandeya Rishi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 12.10.10

bhagavāms tad abhijñāya

giriśo yoga-māyayā

āviśat tad-guhākāśam

vāyuś chidram iveśvarah

SYNONYMS

bhagavān — the great personality; tat — that; abhijñāya — understanding; giriśah — Lord Giriśa; yoga-māyayā — by his mystic power; āviśat — entered; tat — of Mārkandeya; guhā-ākāśam — the hidden sky of the heart; vāyuh — the air; chidram — a hole; iva — as if; īśvarah — the lord.

TRANSLATION

Understanding the situation very well, the powerful Lord Śiva employed his mystic power to enter within the sky of Mārkandeya's heart, just as the wind passes through an opening.
