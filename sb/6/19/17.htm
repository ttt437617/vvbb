Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 19: Performing the Pumsavana Ritualistic Ceremony

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.19.17

patim ca parayā bhaktyā

mahāpurusha-cetasā

priyais tais tair upanamet

prema-śīlah svayam patih

bibhriyāt sarva-karmāni

patnyā uccāvacāni ca

SYNONYMS

patim — the husband; ca — and; parayā — supreme; bhaktyā — with devotion; mahā-purusha-cetasā — accepting as the Supreme Person; priyaih — dear; taih taih — by those (offerings); upanamet — should worship; prema-śīlah — being affectionate; svayam — himself; patih — the husband; bibhriyāt — should execute; sarva-karmāni — all activities; patnyāh — of the wife; ucca-avacāni — high and low; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

Accepting her husband as the representative of the Supreme Person, a wife should worship him with unalloyed devotion by offering him prasāda. The husband, being very pleased with his wife, should engage himself in the affairs of his family.

PURPORT

The family relationship of husband and wife should be established spiritually according to the process mentioned above.
