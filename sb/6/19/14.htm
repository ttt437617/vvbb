Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 19: Performing the Pumsavana Ritualistic Ceremony

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.19.14

yathā yuvām tri-lokasya

varadau parameshthinau

tathā ma uttamaśloka

santu satyā mahāśishah

SYNONYMS

yathā — since; yuvām — both of you; tri-lokasya — of the three worlds; vara-dau — givers of benedictions; parame-shthinau — the supreme rulers; tathā — therefore; me — my; uttama-śloka — O Lord, who are praised with excellent verses; santu — may become; satyāh — fulfilled; mahā-āśishah — great ambitions.

TRANSLATION

You are both the supreme rulers and benedictors of the three worlds. Therefore, my Lord, Uttamaśloka, may my ambitions be fulfilled by Your grace.
