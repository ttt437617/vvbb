Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 17: Mother Pārvatī Curses Citraketu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.17.26

tatas tu bhagavān rudro

rudrānīm idam abravīt

devarshi-daitya-siddhānām

pārshadānām ca śrinvatām

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereafter; tu — then; bhagavān — the most powerful; rudrah — Lord Śiva; rudrānīm — unto his wife, Pārvatī; idam — this; abravīt — said; devarshi — while the great sage Nārada; daitya — the demons; siddhānām — and the inhabitants of Siddhaloka, who are expert in yogic power; pārshadānām — his personal associates; ca — also; śrinvatām — were listening.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, in the presence of the great sage Nārada, the demons, the inhabitants of Siddhaloka, and his personal associates, Lord Śiva, who is most powerful, spoke to his wife, Pārvatī, while they all listened.
