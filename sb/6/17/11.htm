Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 17: Mother Pārvatī Curses Citraketu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.17.11

śrī-pārvaty uvāca

ayam kim adhunā loke

śāstā danda-dharah prabhuh

asmad-vidhānām dushtānām

nirlajjānām ca viprakrit

SYNONYMS

śrī-pārvatī uvāca — the goddess Pārvatī said; ayam — this; kim — whether; adhunā — now; loke — in the world; śāstā — the supreme controller; danda-dharah — the carrier of the rod of punishment; prabhuh — the master; asmat-vidhānām — of persons like us; dushtānām — criminals; nirlajjānām — who have no shame; ca — and; viprakrit — the restrainer.

TRANSLATION

The goddess Pārvatī said: Alas, has this upstart now received a post from which to punish shameless persons like us? Has he been appointed ruler, carrier of the rod of punishment? Is he now the only master of everything?
