Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 7: Indra Offends His Spiritual Master, Brihaspati.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.7.28

putrānām hi paro dharmah

pitri-śuśrūshanam satām

api putravatām brahman

kim uta brahmacārinām

SYNONYMS

putrānām — of sons; hi — indeed; parah — superior; dharmah — religious principle; pitri-śuśrūshanam — the service of the parents; satām — good; api — even; putra-vatām — of those who have sons; brahman — O dear brāhmana; kim uta — what to speak; brahmacārinām — of brahmacārīs.

TRANSLATION

O brāhmana, the highest duty of a son, even though he has sons of his own, is to serve his parents, and what to speak of a son who is a brahmacārī?
