Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 7: Indra Offends His Spiritual Master, Brihaspati.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.7.34

śrī-rishir uvāca

abhyarthitah sura-ganaih

paurahitye mahā-tapāh

sa viśvarūpas tān āha

prasannah ślakshnayā girā

SYNONYMS

śrī-rishih uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued to speak; abhyarthitah — being requested; sura-ganaih — by the demigods; paurahitye — in accepting the priesthood; mahā-tapāh — highly advanced in austerity and penances; sah — he; viśvarūpah — Viśvarūpa; tān — to the demigods; āha — spoke; prasannah — being satisfied; ślakshnayā — sweet; girā — with words.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: When all the demigods requested the great Viśvarūpa to be their priest, Viśvarūpa, who was advanced in austerities, was very pleased. He replied to them as follows.
