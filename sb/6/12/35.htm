Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 12: Vritrāsura's Glorious Death

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.12.35

vritrasya dehān nishkrāntam

ātma-jyotir arindama

paśyatām sarva-devānām

alokam samapadyata

SYNONYMS

vritrasya — of Vritrāsura; dehāt — from the body; nishkrāntam — coming out; ātma-jyotih — the spirit soul, which was as brilliant as the effulgence of Brahman; arim-dama — O King Parīkshit, subduer of enemies; paśyatām — were watching; sarva-devānām — while all the demigods; alokam — the supreme abode, filled with the Brahman effulgence; samapadyata — achieved.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, subduer of enemies, the living spark then came forth from Vritrāsura's body and returned home, back to Godhead. While all the demigods looked on, he entered the transcendental world to become an associate of Lord Sańkarshana.

PURPORT

Śrīla Viśvanātha Cakravartī Thākura explains that Indra, not Vritrāsura, was actually killed. He says that when Vritrāsura swallowed King Indra and his carrier, the elephant, he thought, "Now I have killed Indra, and therefore there is no more need of fighting. Now let me return home, back to Godhead." Thus he stopped all his bodily activities and became situated in trance. Taking advantage of the silence of Vritrāsura's body, Indra pierced the demon's abdomen, and because of Vritrāsura's trance, Indra was able to come out. Now, Vritrāsura was in yoga-samadhi, and therefore although King Indra wanted to cut his throat, the demon's neck was so stiff that Indra's thunderbolt took 360 days to cut it to pieces. Actually it was the body left by Vritrāsura that was cut to pieces by Indra; Vritrāsura himself was not killed. In his original consciousness, Vritrāsura returned home, back to Godhead, to become an associate of Lord Sańkarshana. Here the word alokam means the transcendental world, Vaikunthaloka, where Sańkarshana eternally resides.

Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports of the Sixth Canto, Twelfth Chapter, of the Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, entitled "Vritrāsura's Glorious Death."
