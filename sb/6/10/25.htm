Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.25

na te śastrāstra-varshaughā

hy āseduh sura-sainikān

chinnāh siddha-pathe devair

laghu-hastaih sahasradhā

SYNONYMS

na — not; te — those; śastra-astra-varsha-oghāh — showers of arrows and other weapons; hi — indeed; āseduh — reached; sura-sainikān — the armies of the demigods; chinnāh — cut; siddha-pathe — in the sky; devaih — by the demigods; laghu-hastaih — quick-handed; sahasradhā — into thousands of pieces.

TRANSLATION

The showers of various weapons and arrows released to kill the soldiers of the demigods did not reach them because the demigods, acting quickly, cut the weapons into thousands of pieces in the sky.
