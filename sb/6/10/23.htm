Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.23

śūlaih paraśvadhaih khadgaih

śataghnībhir bhuśundibhih

sarvato 'vākiran śastrair

astraiś ca vibudharshabhān

SYNONYMS

śūlaih — by spears; paraśvadhaih — by axes; khadgaih — by swords; śataghnībhih — by śataghnīs; bhuśundibhih — by bhuśundis; sarvatah — all around; avākiran — scattered; śastraih — with weapons; astraih — with arrows; ca — and; vibudha-rishabhān — the chiefs of the demigods.

TRANSLATION

Armed with lances, tridents, axes, swords and other weapons like śataghnīs and bhuśundis, the demons attacked from different directions and scattered all the chiefs of the demigod armies.
