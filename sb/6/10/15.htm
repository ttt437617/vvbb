Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.15

vritram abhyadravac chatrum

asurānīka-yūthapaih

paryastam ojasā rājan

kruddho rudra ivāntakam

SYNONYMS

vritram — Vritrāsura; abhyadravat — attacked; śatrum — the enemy; asura-anīka-yūthapaih — by the commanders or captains of the soldiers of the asuras; paryastam — surrounded; ojasā — with great force; rājan — O King; kruddhah — being angry; rudrah — an incarnation of Lord Śiva; iva — like; antakam — Antaka, or Yamarāja.

TRANSLATION

My dear King Parīkshit, as Rudra, being very angry at Antaka [Yamarāja] had formerly run toward Antaka to kill him, Indra angrily and with great force attacked Vritrāsura, who was surrounded by the leaders of the demoniac armies.
