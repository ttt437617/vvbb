Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.24

na te 'driśyanta sañchannāh

śara-jālaih samantatah

puńkhānupuńkha-patitair

jyotīmshīva nabho-ghanaih

SYNONYMS

na — not; te — they (the demigods); adriśyanta — were seen; sañchannāh — being completely covered; śara-jālaih — by networks of arrows; samantatah — all around; puńkha-anupuńkha — one arrow after another; patitaih — falling; jyotīmshi iva — like the stars in the sky; nabhah-ghanaih — by the dense clouds.

TRANSLATION

As the stars in the sky cannot be seen when covered by dense clouds, the demigods, being completely covered by networks of arrows falling upon them one after another, could not be seen.
