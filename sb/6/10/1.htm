Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.1

śrī-bādarāyanir uvāca

indram evam samādiśya

bhagavān viśva-bhāvanah

paśyatām animeshānām

tatraivāntardadhe harih

SYNONYMS

śrī-bādarāyanih uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; indram — Indra, the heavenly King; evam — thus; samādiśya — after instructing; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; viśva-bhāvanah — the original cause of all cosmic manifestations; paśyatām animeshānām — while the demigods were looking on; tatra — then and there; eva — indeed; antardadhe — disappeared; harih — the Lord.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After instructing Indra in this way, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Hari, the cause of the cosmic manifestation, then and there disappeared from the presence of the onlooking demigods.
