Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 10: The Battle Between the Demigods and Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.10.3

api vrindārakā yūyam

na jānītha śarīrinām

samsthāyām yas tv abhidroho

duhsahaś cetanāpahah

SYNONYMS

api — although; vrindārakāh — O demigods; yūyam — all of you; na jānītha — do not know; śarīrinām — of those who have material bodies; samsthāyām — at the time of death, or while quitting this body; yah — which; tu — then; abhidrohah — severe pain; duhsahah — unbearable; cetana — the consciousness; apahah — which takes away.

TRANSLATION

O elevated demigods, at the time of death, severe, unbearable pain takes away the consciousness of all living entities who have accepted material bodies. Don't you know about this pain?
