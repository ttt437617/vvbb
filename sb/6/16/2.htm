Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 16: King Citraketu Meets the Supreme Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.16.2

śrī-nārada uvāca

jīvātman paśya bhadram te

mātaram pitaram ca te

suhrido bāndhavās taptāh

śucā tvat-kritayā bhriśam

SYNONYMS

śrī-nāradah uvāca — Śrī Nārada Muni said; jīva-ātman — O living entity; paśya — just see; bhadram — good fortune; te — unto you; mātaram — the mother; pitaram — the father; ca — and; te — of you; suhridah — friends; bāndhavāh — relatives; taptāh — aggrieved; śucā — by lamentation; tvat-kritayā — because of you; bhriśam — very greatly.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nārada Muni said: O living entity, all good fortune unto you. Just see your father and mother. All your friends and relatives are overwhelmed with grief because of your passing away.
