Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 16: King Citraketu Meets the Supreme Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.16.3

kalevaram svam āviśya

śesham āyuh suhrid-vritah

bhuńkshva bhogān pitri-prattān

adhitishtha nripāsanam

SYNONYMS

kalevaram — body; svam — your own; āviśya — entering; śesham — the balance; āyuh — duration of life; suhrit-vritah — surrounded by your friends and relatives; bhuńkshva — just enjoy; bhogān — all enjoyable opulences; pitri — by your father; prattān — awarded; adhitishtha — accept; nripa-āsanam — the throne of the king.

TRANSLATION

Because you died untimely, the balance of your lifetime still remains. Therefore you may reenter your body and enjoy the remainder of your life, surrounded by your friends and relatives. Accept the royal throne and all the opulences given by your father.
