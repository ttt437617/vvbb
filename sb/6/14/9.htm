Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 14: King Citraketu's Lamentation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.14.9

śrī-śuka uvāca

śrinushvāvahito rājann

itihāsam imam yathā

śrutam dvaipāyana-mukhān

nāradād devalād api

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; śrinushva — please hear; avahitah — with great attention; rājan — O King; itihāsam — history; imam — this; yathā — just as; śrutam — heard; dvaipāyana — of Vyāsadeva; mukhāt — from the mouth; nāradāt — from Nārada; devalāt — from Devala Rishi; api — also.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King, I shall speak to you the same history I have heard from the mouths of Vyāsadeva, Nārada and Devala. Please listen with attention.
