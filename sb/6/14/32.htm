Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 14: King Citraketu's Lamentation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.14.32

atha kāla upāvritte

kumārah samajāyata

janayan śūrasenānām

śrinvatām paramām mudam

SYNONYMS

atha — thereafter; kāle upāvritte — in due course of time; kumārah — the son; samajāyata — took birth; janayan — creating; śūrasenānām — of the inhabitants of Śūrasena; śrinvatām — hearing; paramām — the highest; mudam — delight.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, in due course of time, a son was born to the King. Hearing news of this, all the inhabitants of the state of Śūrasena were extremely pleased.
