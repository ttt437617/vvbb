Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 14: King Citraketu's Lamentation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.14.48

papāta bhūmau parivriddhayā śucā

mumoha vibhrashta-śiroruhāmbarā

SYNONYMS

papāta — fell down; bhūmau — on the ground; parivriddhayā — highly increased; śucā — out of lamentation; mumoha — she became unconscious; vibhrashta — scattered; śiroruha — hair; ambarā — and dress.

TRANSLATION

In great lamentation, her hair and dress in disarray, the Queen fell to the ground unconscious.
