Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 9: Appearance of the Demon Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.9.54

yushmabhyam yācito 'śvibhyām

dharma-jño 'ńgāni dāsyati

tatas tair āyudha-śreshtho

viśvakarma-vinirmitah

yena vritra-śiro hartā

mat-teja-upabrimhitah

SYNONYMS

yushmabhyam — for all of you; yācitah — being asked; aśvibhyām — by the Aśvinī-kumāras; dharma-jñah — Dadhīci, who knows the principles of religion; ańgāni — his limbs; dāsyati — will give; tatah — after that; taih — by those bones; āyudha — of weapons; śreshthah — the most powerful (the thunderbolt); viśvakarma-vinirmitah — manufactured by Viśvakarmā; yena — by which; vritra-śirah — the head of Vritrāsura; hartā — will be taken away; mat-tejah — by My strength; upabrimhitah — increased.

TRANSLATION

When the Aśvinī-kumāras beg for Dadhyañca's body on your behalf, he will surely give it because of affection. Do not doubt this, for Dadhyañca is very experienced in religious understanding. When Dadhyañca awards you his body, Viśvakarmā will prepare a thunderbolt from his bones. This thunderbolt will certainly kill Vritrāsura because it will be invested with My power.
