Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 9: Appearance of the Demon Vritrāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.9.20

tatas te vismitāh sarve

vishannā grasta-tejasah

pratyañcam ādi-purusham

upatasthuh samāhitāh

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereafter; te — they (the demigods); vismitāh — being struck with wonder; sarve — all; vishannāh — being very morose; grasta-tejasah — having lost all their personal strength; pratyañcam — to the Supersoul; ādi-purusham — the original person; upatasthuh — prayed; samāhitāh — all gathered together.

TRANSLATION

Struck with wonder and disappointment upon seeing the strength of the demon, the demigods lost their own strength. Therefore they all met together to try to please the Supersoul, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana, by worshiping Him.
