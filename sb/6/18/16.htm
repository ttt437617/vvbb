Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.16

anuhrādasya sūryāyām

bāshkalo mahishas tathā

virocanas tu prāhrādir

devyām tasyābhavad balih

SYNONYMS

anuhrādasya — of Anuhlāda; sūryāyām — through Sūryā; bāshkalah — Bāshkala; mahishah — Mahisha; tathā — also; virocanah — Virocana; tu — indeed; prāhrādih — the son of Prahlāda; devyām — through his wife; tasya — of him; abhavat — was; balih — Bali.

TRANSLATION

The wife of Anuhlāda was named Sūryā. She gave birth to two sons, named Bāshkala and Mahisha. Prahlāda had one son, Virocana, whose wife gave birth to Bali Mahārāja.
