Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.23

śrī-śuka uvāca

hata-putrā ditih śakra-

pārshni-grāhena vishnunā

manyunā śoka-dīptena

jvalantī paryacintayat

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; hata-putrā — whose sons were killed; ditih — Diti; śakra-pārshni-grāhena — who was helping Lord Indra; vishnunā — by Lord Vishnu; manyunā — with anger; śoka-dīptena — kindled by lamentation; jvalantī — burning; paryacintayat — thought.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Just to help Indra, Lord Vishnu killed the two brothers Hiranyāksha and Hiranyakaśipu. Because of their being killed, their mother, Diti, overwhelmed with lamentation and anger, contemplated as follows.
