Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.31

evam śuśrūshitas tāta

bhagavān kaśyapah striyā

prahasya parama-prīto

ditim āhābhinandya ca

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; śuśrūshitah — being served; tāta — O dear one; bhagavān — the powerful; kaśyapah — Kaśyapa; striyā — by the woman; prahasya — smiling; parama-prītah — being very pleased; ditim — to Diti; āha — said; abhinandya — approving; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

O my dear one, the most powerful sage Kaśyapa, being extremely pleased by the mild behavior of his wife Diti, smiled and spoke to her as follows.
