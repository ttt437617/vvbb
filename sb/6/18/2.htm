Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.2

siddhir bhagasya bhāryāńga

mahimānam vibhum prabhum

āśisham ca varārohām

kanyām prāsūta suvratām

SYNONYMS

siddhih — Siddhi; bhagasya — of Bhaga; bhāryā — the wife; ańga — my dear King; mahimānam — Mahimā; vibhum — Vibhu; prabhum — Prabhu; āśisham — Āśī; ca — and; varārohām — very beautiful; kanyām — daughter; prāsūta — bore; su-vratām — virtuous.

TRANSLATION

O King, Siddhi, who was the wife of Bhaga, the sixth son of Aditi, bore three sons, named Mahimā, Vibhu and Prabhu, and one extremely beautiful daughter, whose name was Āśī.
