Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 18: Diti Vows to Kill King Indra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.18.21

ime śraddadhate brahmann

rishayo hi mayā saha

parijñānāya bhagavams

tan no vyākhyātum arhasi

SYNONYMS

ime — these; śraddadhate — are eager; brahman — O brāhmana; rishayah — sages; hi — indeed; mayā saha — with me; parijñānāya — to know; bhagavan — O great soul; tat — therefore; nah — to us; vyākhyātum arhasi — please explain.

TRANSLATION

My dear brāhmana, I and all the sages present with me are eager to know about this. Therefore, O great soul, kindly explain to us the reason.
