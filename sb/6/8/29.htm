Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 8: The Nārāyana-kavaca Shield

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.8.29

garudo bhagavān stotra-

stobhaś chandomayah prabhuh

rakshatv aśesha-kricchrebhyo

vishvaksenah sva-nāmabhih

SYNONYMS

garudah — His Holiness Garuda, the carrier of Lord Vishnu; bhagavān — as powerful as the Supreme Personality of Godhead; stotra-stobhah — who is glorified by selected verses and songs; chandah-mayah — the personified Vedas; prabhuh — the lord; rakshatu — may He protect; aśesha-kricchrebhyah — from unlimited miseries; vishvaksenah — Lord Vishvaksena; sva-nāmabhih — by His holy names.

TRANSLATION

Lord Garuda, the carrier of Lord Vishnu, is the most worshipable lord, for he is as powerful as the Supreme Lord Himself. He is the personified Vedas and is worshiped by selected verses. May he protect us from all dangerous conditions, and may Lord Vishvaksena, the Personality of Godhead, also protect us from all dangers by His holy names.
