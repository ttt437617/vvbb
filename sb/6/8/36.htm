Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 8: The Nārāyana-kavaca Shield

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.8.36

etad dhārayamānas tu

yam yam paśyati cakshushā

padā vā samspriśet sadyah

sādhvasāt sa vimucyate

SYNONYMS

etat — this; dhārayamānah — a person employing; tu — but; yam yam — whomever; paśyati — he sees; cakshushā — by his eyes; padā — by his feet; vā — or; samspriśet — may touch; sadyah — immediately; sādhvasāt — from all fear; sah — he; vimucyate — is freed.

TRANSLATION

If one employs this armor, whomever he sees with his eyes or touches with his feet is immediately freed from all the above-mentioned dangers.
