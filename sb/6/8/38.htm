Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 8: The Nārāyana-kavaca Shield

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.8.38

imām vidyām purā kaścit

kauśiko dhārayan dvijah

yoga-dhāranayā svāńgam

jahau sa maru-dhanvani

SYNONYMS

imām — this; vidyām — prayer; purā — formerly; kaścit — someone; kauśikah — Kauśika; dhārayan — using; dvijah — a brāhmana; yoga-dhāranayā — by mystic power; sva-ańgam — his own body; jahau — gave up; sah — he; maru-dhanvani — in the desert.

TRANSLATION

O King of heaven, a brāhmana named Kauśika formerly used this armor when he purposely gave up his body in the desert by mystic power.
