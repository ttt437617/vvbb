Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.16

vibhāvasor asūtoshā

vyushtam rocisham ātapam

pañcayāmo 'tha bhūtāni

yena jāgrati karmasu

SYNONYMS

vibhāvasoh — of Vibhāvasu; asūta — gave birth to; ūshā — named Ūshā; vyushtam — Vyushta; rocisham — Rocisha; ātapam — Ātapa; pañcayāmah — Pañcayāma; atha — thereafter; bhūtāni — the living entities; yena — by whom; jāgrati — are awakened; karmasu — in material activities.

TRANSLATION

Ūshā, the wife of Vibhāvasu, gave birth to three sons — Vyushta, Rocisha and Ātapa. From Ātapa came Pañcayāma, the span of day, who awakens all living entities to material activities.
