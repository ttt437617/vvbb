Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.14

skandaś ca krittikā-putro

ye viśākhādayas tatah

doshasya śarvarī-putrah

śiśumāro hareh kalā

SYNONYMS

skandah — Skanda; ca — also; krittikā-putrah — the son of Krittikā; ye — all of whom; viśākha-ādayah — headed by Viśākha; tatah — from him (Skanda); doshasya — of Dosha; śarvarī-putrah — the son of his wife Śarvarī; śiśumārah — Śiśumāra; hareh kalā — an expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

From Krittikā, another wife of Agni, came the son named Skanda, Kārttikeya, whose sons were headed by Viśākha. From the womb of Śarvarī, the wife of the Vasu named Dosha, came the son named Śiśumāra, who was an expansion of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
