Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.9

mauhūrtikā deva-ganā

muhūrtāyāś ca jajñire

ye vai phalam prayacchanti

bhūtānām sva-sva-kālajam

SYNONYMS

mauhūrtikāh — Mauhūrtikas; deva-ganāh — the demigods; muhūrtāyāh — from the womb of Muhūrtā; ca — and; jajñire — took birth; ye — all of whom; vai — indeed; phalam — result; prayacchanti — deliver; bhūtānām — of the living entities; sva-sva — their own; kāla-jam — born of time.

TRANSLATION

The demigods named the Mauhūrtikas took birth from the womb of Muhūrtā. These demigods deliver the results of actions to the living entities of their respective times.
