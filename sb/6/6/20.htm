Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.20

kriśāśvo 'rcishi bhāryāyām

dhūmaketum ajījanat

dhishanāyām vedaśiro

devalam vayunam manum

SYNONYMS

kriśāśvah — Kriśāśva; arcishi — Arcis; bhāryāyām — in his wife; dhūmaketum — to Dhūmaketu; ajījanat — gave birth; dhishanāyām — in the wife known as Dhishanā; vedaśirah — Vedaśirā; devalam — Devala; vayunam — Vayuna; manum — Manu.

TRANSLATION

Kriśāśva had two wives, named Arcis and Dhishanā. In the wife named Arcis he begot Dhūmaketu and in Dhishanā he begot four sons, named Vedaśirā, Devala, Vayuna and Manu.
