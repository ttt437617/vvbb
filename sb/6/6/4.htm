Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.4

bhānur lambā kakud yāmir

viśvā sādhyā marutvatī

vasur muhūrtā sańkalpā

dharma-patnyah sutāñ śrinu

SYNONYMS

bhānuh — Bhānu; lambā — Lambā; kakut — Kakud; yāmih — Yāmi; viśvā — Viśvā; sādhyā — Sādhyā; marutvatī — Marutvatī; vasuh — Vasu; muhūrtā — Muhūrtā; sańkalpā — Sańkalpā; dharma-patnyah — the wives of Yamarāja; sutān — their sons; śrinu — now hear of.

TRANSLATION

The ten daughters given to Yamarāja were named Bhānu, Lambā, Kakud, Yāmi, Viśvā, Sādhyā, Marutvatī, Vasu, Muhūrtā and Sańkalpā. Now hear the names of their sons.
