Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 6: The Progeny of the Daughters of Daksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.6.13

arkasya vāsanā bhāryā

putrās tarshādayah smritāh

agner bhāryā vasor dhārā

putrā dravinakādayah

SYNONYMS

arkasya — of Arka; vāsanā — Vāsanā; bhāryā — the wife; putrāh — the sons; tarsha-ādayah — named Tarsha and so on; smritāh — celebrated; agneh — of Agni; bhāryā — wife; vasoh — the Vasu; dhārā — Dhārā; putrāh — the sons; dravinaka-ādayah — known as Dravinaka and so on.

TRANSLATION

From the womb of Vāsanā, the wife of Arka, came many sons, headed by Tarsha. Dhārā, the wife of the Vasu named Agni, gave birth to many sons, headed by Dravinaka.
