Canto 6: Prescribed Duties for MankindChapter 13: King Indra Afflicted by Sinful Reaction

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 6.13.18

tam ca brahmarshayo 'bhyetya

hayamedhena bhārata

yathāvad dīkshayām cakruh

purushārādhanena ha

SYNONYMS

tam — him (Lord Indra); ca — and; brahma-rishayah — the great saints and brāhmanas; abhyetya — approaching; hayamedhena — with an aśvamedha sacrifice; bhārata — O King Parīkshit; yathāvat — according to the rules and regulations; dīkshayām cakruh — initiated; purusha-ārādhanena — which consists of worship of the Supreme Person, Hari; ha — indeed.

TRANSLATION

O King, when Lord Indra reached the heavenly planets, the saintly brāhmanas approached him and properly initiated him into a horse sacrifice [aśvamedha-yajña] meant to please the Supreme Lord.
