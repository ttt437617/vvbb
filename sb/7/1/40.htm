Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 1: The Supreme Lord Is Equal to Everyone

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.1.40

jajñāte tau diteh putrau

daitya-dānava-vanditau

hiranyakaśipur jyeshtho

hiranyāksho 'nujas tatah

SYNONYMS

jajñāte — were born; tau — the two; diteh — of Diti; putrau — the sons; daitya-dānava — by all the demons; vanditau — being worshiped; hiranyakaśipuh — Hiranyakaśipu; jyeshthah — the elder; hiranyākshah — Hiranyāksha; anujah — the younger; tatah — thereafter.

TRANSLATION

These two associates of the Lord — Jaya and Vijaya — later descended to the material world, taking birth as the two sons of Diti, Hiranyakaśipu being the elder and Hiranyāksha the younger. They were very much respected by the Daityas and Dānavas [demoniac species].
