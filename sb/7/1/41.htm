Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 1: The Supreme Lord Is Equal to Everyone

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.1.41

hato hiranyakaśipur

harinā simha-rūpinā

hiranyāksho dharoddhāre

bibhratā śaukaram vapuh

SYNONYMS

hatah — killed; hiranyakaśipuh — Hiranyakaśipu; harinā — by Hari, Vishnu; simha-rūpinā — in the form of a lion (Lord Narasimha); hiranyākshah — Hiranyāksha; dharā-uddhāre — to lift the earth; bibhratā — assuming; śaukaram — the boarlike; vapuh — form.

TRANSLATION

Appearing as Nrisimhadeva, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Hari, killed Hiranyakaśipu. When the Lord delivered the planet earth, which had fallen in the Garbhodaka Ocean, Hiranyāksha tried to hinder Him, and then the Lord, as Varāha, killed Hiranyāksha.
