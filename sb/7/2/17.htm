Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 2: Hiranyakaśipu, King of the Demons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.2.17

hiranyakaśipur bhrātuh

samparetasya duhkhitah

kritvā katodakādīni

bhrātri-putrān asāntvayat

SYNONYMS

hiranyakaśipuh — Hiranyakaśipu; bhrātuh — of the brother; samparetasya — deceased; duhkhitah — being very much distressed; kritvā — performing; katodaka-ādīni — ceremonies observed after a death; bhrātri-putrān — the sons of his brother; asāntvayat — pacified.

TRANSLATION

After performing the ritualistic observances for the death of his brother, Hiranyakaśipu, being extremely unhappy, tried to pacify his nephews.
