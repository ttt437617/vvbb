Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 10: Prahlāda, the Best Among Exalted Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.10.52

rājovāca

kasmin karmani devasya

mayo 'hañ jagad-īśituh

yathā copacitā kīrtih

krishnenānena kathyatām

SYNONYMS

rājā uvāca — King Yudhishthira inquired; kasmin — for what reason; karmani — by which activities; devasya — of Lord Mahādeva (Śiva); mayah — the great demon Maya Dānava; ahan — vanquished; jagat-īśituh — of Lord Śiva, who controls the power of the material energy and is the husband of Durgādevī; yathā — just as; ca — and; upacitā — again expanded; kīrtih — reputation; krishnena — by Lord Krishna; anena — this; kathyatām — please describe.

TRANSLATION

Mahārāja Yudhishthira said: For what reason did the demon Maya Dānava vanquish Lord Śiva's reputation? How did Lord Krishna save Lord Śiva and expand his reputation again? Kindly describe these incidents.
