Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 5: Prahlāda Mahārāja, the Saintly Son of Hiranyakaśipu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.5.36

vishnor vā sādhv asau kim nu

karishyaty asamañjasah

sauhridam dustyajam pitror

ahād yah pañca-hāyanah

SYNONYMS

vishnoh — unto Vishnu; vā — either; sādhu — good; asau — this; kim — whether; nu — indeed; karishyati — will do; asamañjasah — not trustworthy; sauhridam — affectionate relationship; dustyajam — difficult to relinquish; pitroh — of his father and mother; ahāt — gave up; yah — he who; pañca-hāyanah — only five years old.

TRANSLATION

Although Prahlāda is only five years old, even at this young age he has given up his affectionate relationship with his father and mother. Therefore, he is certainly untrustworthy. Indeed, it is not at all believable that he will behave well toward Vishnu.
