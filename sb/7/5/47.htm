Canto 7: The Science of GodChapter 5: Prahlāda Mahārāja, the Saintly Son of Hiranyakaśipu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 7.5.47

aprameyānubhāvo 'yam

akutaścid-bhayo 'marah

nūnam etad-virodhena

mrityur me bhavitā na vā

SYNONYMS

aprameya — unlimited; anubhāvah — glory; ayam — this; akutaścit-bhayah — having no fear from any quarter; amarah — immortal; nūnam — definitely; etat-virodhena — because of going against him; mrityuh — death; me — my; bhavitā — may be; na — not; vā — or.

TRANSLATION

I can see that this boy's strength is unlimited, for he has not feared any of my punishments. He appears immortal. Therefore, because of my enmity toward him, I shall die. Or maybe this will not take place.
