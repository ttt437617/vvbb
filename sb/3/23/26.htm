Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 23: Devahūti's Lamentation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.23.26

sāntah sarasi veśma-sthāh

śatāni daśa kanyakāh

sarvāh kiśora-vayaso

dadarśotpala-gandhayah

SYNONYMS

sā — she; antah — inside; sarasi — in the lake; veśma-sthāh — situated in a house; śatāni daśa — ten hundred; kanyakāh — girls; sarvāh — all; kiśora-vayasah — in the prime of youth; dadarśa — she saw; utpala — like lotuses; gandhayah — fragrant.

TRANSLATION

In a house inside the lake she saw one thousand girls, all in the prime of youth and fragrant like lotuses.
