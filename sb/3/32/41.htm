Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 32: Entanglement in Fruitive Activities

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.32.41

śraddadhānāya bhaktāya

vinītāyānasūyave

bhūteshu krita-maitrāya

śuśrūshābhiratāya ca

SYNONYMS

śraddadhānāya — faithful; bhaktāya — to the devotee; vinītāya — respectful; anasūyave — nonenvious; bhūteshu — to all living entities; krita-maitrāya — friendly; śuśrūshā — faithful service; abhiratāya — eager to render; ca — and.

TRANSLATION

Instruction should be given to the faithful devotee who is respectful to the spiritual master, nonenvious, friendly to all kinds of living entities and eager to render service with faith and sincerity.
