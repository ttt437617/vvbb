Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 18: The Battle Between Lord Boar and the Demon Hiranyāksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.18.16

punar gadām svām ādāya

bhrāmayantam abhīkshnaśah

abhyadhāvad dharih kruddhah

samrambhād dashta-dacchadam

SYNONYMS

punah — again; gadām — mace; svām — his; ādāya — having taken; bhrāmayantam — brandishing; abhīkshnaśah — repeatedly; abhyadhāvat — rushed to meet; harih — the Personality of Godhead; kruddhah — angry; samrambhāt — in rage; dashta — bitten; dacchadam — his lip.

TRANSLATION

The Personality of Godhead now exhibited His anger and rushed to meet the demon, who bit his lip in rage, took up his mace again and began to repeatedly brandish it about.
