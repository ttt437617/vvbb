Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 30: Description by Lord Kapila of Adverse Fruitive Activities

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.30.27

krintanam cāvayavaśo

gajādibhyo bhidāpanam

pātanam giri-śrińgebhyo

rodhanam cāmbu-gartayoh

SYNONYMS

krintanam — cutting off; ca — and; avayavaśah — limb by limb; gaja-ādibhyah — by elephants and so on; bhidāpanam — tearing; pātanam — hurling down; giri — of hills; śrińgebhyah — from the tops; rodhanam — enclosing; ca — and; ambu-gartayoh — in water or in a cave.

TRANSLATION

Next his limbs are lopped off and torn asunder by elephants. He is hurled down from hilltops, and he is also held captive either in water or in a cave.
