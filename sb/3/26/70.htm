Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 26: Fundamental Principles of Material Nature

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.26.70

cittena hridayam caityah

kshetra-jñah prāviśad yadā

virāt tadaiva purushah

salilād udatishthata

SYNONYMS

cittena — along with reason, consciousness; hridayam — the heart; caityah — the deity presiding over consciousness; kshetra-jñah — the knower of the field; prāviśat — entered; yadā — when; virāt — the virāt-purusha; tadā — then; eva — just; purushah — the Cosmic Being; salilāt — from the water; udatishthata — arose.

TRANSLATION

However, when the inner controller, the deity presiding over consciousness, entered the heart with reason, at that very moment the Cosmic Being arose from the causal waters.
