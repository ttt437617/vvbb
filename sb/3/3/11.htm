Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 3: The Lord's Pastimes Out of Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.3.11

śambaram dvividam bānam

muram balvalam eva ca

anyāmś ca dantavakrādīn

avadhīt kāmś ca ghātayat

SYNONYMS

śambaram — Śambara; dvividam — Dvivida; bānam — Bāna; muram — Mura; balvalam — Balvala; eva ca — as also; anyān — others; ca — also; dantavakra-ādīn — like Dantavakra and others; avadhīt — killed; kān ca — and many others; ghātayat — caused to be killed.

TRANSLATION

Of kings like Śambara, Dvivida, Bāna, Mura, Balvala and many other demons, such as Dantavakra, some He killed Himself, and some He caused to be killed by others [Śrī Baladeva, etc.].
