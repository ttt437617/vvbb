Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 12: Creation of the Kumāras and Others

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.12.40

shodaśy-ukthau pūrva-vaktrāt

purīshy-agnishtutāv atha

āptoryāmātirātrau ca

vājapeyam sagosavam

SYNONYMS

shodaśī-ukthau — types of sacrifice; pūrva-vaktrāt — from the eastern mouth; purīshi-agnishtutau — types of sacrifice; atha — then; āptoryāma-atirātrau — types of sacrifice; ca — and; vājapeyam — type of sacrifice; sa-gosavam — type of sacrifice.

TRANSLATION

All the different varieties of fire sacrifices [shodaśī, uktha, purīshi, agnishtoma, āptoryāma, atirātra, vājapeya and gosava] became manifested from the eastern mouth of Brahmā.
