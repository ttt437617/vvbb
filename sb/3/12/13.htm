Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 12: Creation of the Kumāras and Others

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.12.13

dhīr dhriti-rasalomā ca

niyut sarpir ilāmbikā

irāvatī svadhā dīkshā

rudrānyo rudra te striyah

SYNONYMS

dhīh, dhriti, rasalā, umā, niyut, sarpih, ilā, ambikā, irāvatī, svadhā, dīkshā rudrānyah — the eleven Rudrānīs; rudra — O Rudra; te — unto you; striyah — wives.

TRANSLATION

O Rudra, you also have eleven wives, called the Rudrānīs, and they are as follows: Dhī, Dhriti, Rasalā, Umā, Niyut, Sarpi, Ilā, Ambikā, Irāvatī, Svadhā and Dīkshā.
