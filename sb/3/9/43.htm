Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 9: Brahmā's Prayers for Creative Energy

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.9.43

sarva-veda-mayenedam

ātmanātmātma-yoninā

prajāh srija yathā-pūrvam

yāś ca mayy anuśerate

SYNONYMS

sarva — all; veda-mayena — under complete Vedic wisdom; idam — this; ātmanā — by the body; ātmā — you; ātma-yoninā — directly born of the Lord; prajāh — living entities; srija — generate; yathā-pūrvam — as it was hereinbefore; yāh — which; ca — also; mayi — in Me; anuśerate — lie.

TRANSLATION

By following My instructions you can now generate the living entities as before, by dint of your complete Vedic wisdom and the body you have directly received from Me, the supreme cause of everything.
