Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 19: The Killing of the Demon Hiranyāksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.19.8

karāla-damshtraś cakshurbhyām

sañcakshāno dahann iva

abhiplutya sva-gadayā

hato 'sīty āhanad dharim

SYNONYMS

karāla — fearful; damshtrah — having tusks; cakshurbhyām — with both eyes; sañcakshānah — staring; dahan — burning; iva — as if; abhiplutya — attacking; sva-gadayā — with his own club; hatah — slain; asi — You are; iti — thus; āhanat — struck; harim — at Hari.

TRANSLATION

The demon, who had fearful tusks, stared at the Personality of Godhead as though to burn Him. Springing into the air, he aimed his mace at the Lord, exclaiming at the same time, "You are slain!"
