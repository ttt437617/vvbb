Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 19: The Killing of the Demon Hiranyāksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.19.20

girayah pratyadriśyanta

nānāyudha-muco 'nagha

dig-vāsaso yātudhānyah

śūlinyo mukta-mūrdhajāh

SYNONYMS

girayah — mountains; pratyadriśyanta — appeared; nānā — various; āyudha — weapons; mucah — discharging; anagha — O sinless Vidura; dik-vāsasah — naked; yātudhānyah — demonesses; śūlinyah — armed with tridents; mukta — hanging loose; mūrdhajāh — hair.

TRANSLATION

O sinless Vidura, mountains discharged weapons of various kinds, and naked demonesses armed with tridents appeared with their hair hanging loose.
