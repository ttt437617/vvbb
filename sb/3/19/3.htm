Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 19: The Killing of the Demon Hiranyāksha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.19.3

sā hatā tena gadayā

vihatā bhagavat-karāt

vighūrnitāpatad reje

tad adbhutam ivābhavat

SYNONYMS

sā — that mace; hatā — struck; tena — by Hiranyāksha; gadayā — with his mace; vihatā — slipped; bhagavat — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; karāt — from the hand; vighūrnitā — whirling; apatat — fell down; reje — was shining; tat — that; adbhutam — miraculous; iva — indeed; abhavat — was.

TRANSLATION

Struck by the demon's mace, however, the Lord's mace slipped from His hand and looked splendid as it fell down whirling. This was miraculous, for the mace was blazing wonderfully.
