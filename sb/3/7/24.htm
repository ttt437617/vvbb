Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 7: Further Inquires by Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.7.24

yatra putraiś ca pautraiś ca

naptribhih saha gotrajaih

prajā vicitrākritaya

āsan yābhir idam tatam

SYNONYMS

yatra — wherein; putraih — along with sons; ca — and; pautraih — along with grandsons; ca — also; naptribhih — with grandsons from daughters; saha — along with; gotra-jaih — of the same family; prajāh — generations; vicitra — of different kinds; ākritayah — so done; āsan — exist; yābhih — by whom; idam — all these planets; tatam — spread.

TRANSLATION

O my lord, I think that the process manifest in the forms of sons, grandsons and family members has spread all over the universe in different varieties and species.
