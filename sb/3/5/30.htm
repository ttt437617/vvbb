Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 5: Vidura's Talks with Maitreya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.5.30

aham-tattvād vikurvānān

mano vaikārikād abhūt

vaikārikāś ca ye devā

arthābhivyañjanam yatah

SYNONYMS

aham-tattvāt — from the principle of false ego; vikurvānāt — by transformation; manah — the mind; vaikārikāt — by interaction with the mode of goodness; abhūt — generated; vaikārikāh — by interaction with goodness; ca — also; ye — all these; devāh — demigods; artha — the phenomenon; abhivyañjanam — physical knowledge; yatah — the source.

TRANSLATION

The false ego is transformed into mind by interaction with the mode of goodness. All the demigods who control the phenomenal world are also products of the same principle, namely the interaction of false ego and the mode of goodness.

PURPORT

False ego interacting with the different modes of material nature is the source of all materials in the phenomenal world.
