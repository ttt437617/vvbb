Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 5: Vidura's Talks with Maitreya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.5.34

anilo 'pi vikurvāno

nabhasoru-balānvitah

sasarja rūpa-tanmātram

jyotir lokasya locanam

SYNONYMS

anilah — air; api — also; vikurvānah — being transformed; nabhasā — sky; uru-bala-anvitah — extremely powerful; sasarja — created; rūpa — form; tat-mātram — sense perception; jyotih — electricity; lokasya — of the world; locanam — light to see.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter the extremely powerful air, interacting with the sky, generated the form of sense perception, and the perception of form transformed into electricity, the light to see the world.
