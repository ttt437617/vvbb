Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 20: Conversation Between Maitreya and Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.20.14

tāni caikaikaśah srashtum

asamarthāni bhautikam

samhatya daiva-yogena

haimam andam avāsrijan

SYNONYMS

tāni — those elements; ca — and; eka-ekaśah — separately; srashtum — to produce; asamarthāni — unable; bhautikam — the material universe; samhatya — having combined; daiva-yogena — with the energy of the Supreme Lord; haimam — shining like gold; andam — globe; avāsrijan — produced.

TRANSLATION

Separately unable to produce the material universe, they combined with the help of the energy of the Supreme Lord and were able to produce a shining egg.
