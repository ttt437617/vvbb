Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 20: Conversation Between Maitreya and Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.20.15

so 'śayishtābdhi-salile

āndakośo nirātmakah

sāgram vai varsha-sāhasram

anvavātsīt tam īśvarah

SYNONYMS

sah — it; aśayishta — lay; abdhi-salile — on the waters of the Causal Ocean; ānda-kośah — egg; nirātmakah — in an unconscious state; sāgram — a little more than; vai — in fact; varsha-sāhasram — a thousand years; anvavātsīt — became situated; tam — in the egg; īśvarah — the Lord.

TRANSLATION

For over one thousand years the shiny egg lay on the waters of the Causal Ocean in the lifeless state. Then the Lord entered it as Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu.

PURPORT

From this verse it appears that all the universes are floating in the Causal Ocean.
