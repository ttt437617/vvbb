Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 20: Conversation Between Maitreya and Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.20.39

visasarja tanum tām vai

jyotsnām kāntimatīm priyām

ta eva cādaduh prītyā

viśvāvasu-purogamāh

SYNONYMS

visasarja — gave up; tanum — form; tām — that; vai — in fact; jyotsnām — moonlight; kānti-matīm — shining; priyām — beloved; te — the Gandharvas; eva — certainly; ca — and; ādaduh — took possession; prītyā — gladly; viśvāvasu-purah-gamāh — headed by Viśvāvasu.

TRANSLATION

After that, Brahmā gave up that shining and beloved form of moonlight. Viśvāvasu and other Gandharvas gladly took possession of it.
