Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 20: Conversation Between Maitreya and Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.20.11

sa-dvitīyāh kim asrijan

svatantrā uta karmasu

āho svit samhatāh sarva

idam sma samakalpayan

SYNONYMS

sa-dvitīyāh — with their wives; kim — whether; asrijan — created; sva-tantrāh — remaining independent; uta — or; karmasu — in their actions; āho svit — or else; samhatāh — jointly; sarve — all the Prajāpatis; idam — this; sma samakalpayan — produced.

TRANSLATION

Did they evolve the creation in conjunction with their respective wives, did they remain independent in their action, or did they all jointly produce it?
