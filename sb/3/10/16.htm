Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 10: Divisions of the Creation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.10.16

bhūta-sargas tritīyas tu

tan-mātro dravya-śaktimān

caturtha aindriyah sargo

yas tu jñāna-kriyātmakah

SYNONYMS

bhūta-sargah — creation of matter; tritīyah — is the third; tu — but; tat-mātrah — sense perception; dravya — of the elements; śaktimān — generator; caturthah — the fourth; aindriyah — in the matter of the senses; sargah — creation; yah — that which; tu — but; jñāna — knowledge-acquiring; kriyā — working; ātmakah — basically.

TRANSLATION

The sense perceptions are created in the third creation, and from these the elements are generated. The fourth creation is the creation of knowledge and of working capacity.
