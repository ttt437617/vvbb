Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 33: Activities of Kapila

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.33.23

dhyāyatī bhagavad-rūpam

yad āha dhyāna-gocaram

sutah prasanna-vadanam

samasta-vyasta-cintayā

SYNONYMS

dhyāyatī — meditating; bhagavat-rūpam — upon the form of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; yat — which; āha — He instructed; dhyāna-gocaram — the object of meditation; sutah — her son; prasanna-vadanam — with a smiling face; samasta — on the whole; vyasta — on the parts; cintayā — with her mind.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, having heard with great eagerness and in all detail from her son, Kapiladeva, the eternally smiling Personality of Godhead, Devahūti began to meditate constantly upon the Vishnu form of the Supreme Lord.
