Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 33: Activities of Kapila

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.33.9

maitreya uvāca

īdito bhagavān evam

kapilākhyah parah pumān

vācāviklavayety āha

mātaram mātri-vatsalah

SYNONYMS

maitreyah uvāca — Maitreya said; īditah — praised; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; evam — thus; kapila-ākhyah — named Kapila; parah — supreme; pumān — person; vācā — with words; aviklavayā — grave; iti — thus; āha — replied; mātaram — to His mother; mātri-vatsalah — very affectionate to His mother.

TRANSLATION

Thus the Supreme Personality of Godhead Kapila, satisfied by the words of His mother, towards whom He was very affectionate, replied with gravity.

PURPORT

Since the Lord is all-perfect, His exhibition of affection for His mother was also complete. After hearing the words of His mother, He most respectfully, with due gravity and good manners, replied.
