Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 33: Activities of Kapila

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.33.36

etan nigaditam tāta

yat prishto 'ham tavānagha

kapilasya ca samvādo

devahūtyāś ca pāvanah

SYNONYMS

etat — this; nigaditam — spoken; tāta — O dear Vidura; yat — which; prishtah — was asked; aham — I; tava — by you; anagha — O sinless Vidura; kapilasya — of Kapila; ca — and; samvādah — conversation; devahūtyāh — of Devahūti; ca — and; pāvanah — pure.

TRANSLATION

My dear son, since you have inquired from me, I have answered. O sinless one, the descriptions of Kapiladeva and His mother and their activities are the purest of all pure discourses.
