Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 33: Activities of Kapila

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.33.31

tad vīrāsīt punyatamam

kshetram trailokya-viśrutam

nāmnā siddha-padam yatra

sā samsiddhim upeyushī

SYNONYMS

tat — that; vīra — O brave Vidura; āsīt — was; punya-tamam — most sacred; kshetram — place; trai-lokya — in the three worlds; viśrutam — known; nāmnā — by the name; siddha-padam — Siddhapada; yatra — where; sā — she (Devahūti); samsiddhim — perfection; upeyushī — achieved.

TRANSLATION

The palace where Devahūti achieved her perfection, my dear Vidura, is understood to be a most sacred spot. It is known all over the three worlds as Siddhapada.
