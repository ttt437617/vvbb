Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 8: Manifestation of Brahmā from Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.8.14

sa padma-kośah sahasodatishthat

kālena karma-pratibodhanena

sva-rocishā tat salilam viśālam

vidyotayann arka ivātma-yonih

SYNONYMS

sah — that; padma-kośah — bud of a lotus flower; sahasā — suddenly; udatishthat — appeared; kālena — by time; karma — fruitive activities; pratibodhanena — awakening; sva-rocishā — by its own effulgence; tat — that; salilam — water of devastation; viśālam — vast; vidyotayan — illuminating; arkah — the sun; iva — like; ātma-yonih — generating from the Personality of Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

Piercing through, this sum total form of the fruitive activity of the living entities took the shape of the bud of a lotus flower generated from the Personality of Vishnu, and by His supreme will it illuminated everything, like the sun, and dried up the vast waters of devastation.
