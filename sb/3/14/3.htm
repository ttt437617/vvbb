Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 14: Pregnancy of Diti in the Evening

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.14.3

tasya coddharatah kshaunīm

sva-damshtrāgrena līlayā

daitya-rājasya ca brahman

kasmād dhetor abhūn mridhah

SYNONYMS

tasya — His; ca — also; uddharatah — while lifting; kshaunīm — the earth planet; sva-damshtra-agrena — by the edge of His tusks; līlayā — in His pastimes; daitya-rājasya — of the king of demons; ca — and; brahman — O brāhmana; kasmāt — from what; hetoh — reason; abhūt — there was; mridhah — fight.

TRANSLATION

What was the reason, O brāhmana, for the fight between the demon king and Lord Boar while the Lord was lifting the earth as His pastime?
