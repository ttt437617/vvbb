Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 13: The Appearance of Lord Varāha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.13.21

kim etat sūkara-vyājam

sattvam divyam avasthitam

aho batāścaryam idam

nāsāyā me vinihsritam

SYNONYMS

kim — what; etat — this; sūkara — boar; vyājam — pretension; sattvam — entity; divyam — extraordinary; avasthitam — situated; aho bata — oh, it is; āścaryam — very wonderful; idam — this; nāsāyāh — from the nose; me — my; vinihsritam — came out.

TRANSLATION

Is this some extraordinary entity come in the pretense of a boar? It is very wonderful that He has come from my nose.
