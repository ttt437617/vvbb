Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 17: Victory of Hiranyāksha Over All the Directions of the Universe

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.17.6

uddhasat-tadid-ambhoda-

ghatayā nashta-bhāgane

vyomni pravishta-tamasā

na sma vyādriśyate padam

SYNONYMS

uddhasat — laughing loudly; tadit — lightning; ambhoda — of clouds; ghatayā — by masses; nashta — lost; bhā-gane — the luminaries; vyomni — in the sky; pravishta — enveloped; tamasā — by darkness; na — not; sma vyādriśyate — could be seen; padam — any place.

TRANSLATION

The luminaries in the heavens were screened by masses of clouds, in which lightning sometimes flashed as though laughing. Darkness reigned everywhere, and nothing could be seen.
