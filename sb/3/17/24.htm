Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 17: Victory of Hiranyāksha Over All the Directions of the Universe

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.17.24

tato nivrittah krīdishyan

gambhīram bhīma-nisvanam

vijagāhe mahā-sattvo

vārdhim matta iva dvipah

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; nivrittah — returned; krīdishyan — for the sake of sport; gambhīram — deep; bhīma-nisvanam — making a terrible sound; vijagāhe — dived; mahā-sattvah — the mighty being; vārdhim — in the ocean; mattah — in wrath; iva — like; dvipah — an elephant.

TRANSLATION

After returning from the heavenly kingdom, the mighty demon, who was like an elephant in wrath, for the sake of sport dived into the deep ocean, which was roaring terribly.
