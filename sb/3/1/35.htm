Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 1: Questions by Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.1.35

apisvid anye ca nijātma-daivam

ananya-vrittyā samanuvratā ye

hridīka-satyātmaja-cārudeshna-

gadādayah svasti caranti saumya

SYNONYMS

api — as also; svit — whether; anye — others; ca — and; nija-ātma — of one's own self; daivam — Śrī Krishna; ananya — absolutely; vrittyā — faith; samanuvratāh — followers; ye — all those who; hridīka — Hridīka; satya-ātmaja — the son of Satyabhāmā; cārudeshna — Cārudeshna; gada — Gada; ādayah — and others; svasti — all well; caranti — pass time; saumya — O sober one.

TRANSLATION

O sober one, others, such as Hridīka, Cārudeshna, Gada and the son of Satyabhāmā, who accept Lord Śrī Krishna as the soul of the self and thus follow His path without deviation — are they well?
