Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 1: Questions by Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.1.5

sūta uvāca

sa evam rishi-varyo 'yam

prishto rājñā parīkshitā

praty āha tam subahu-vit

prītātmā śrūyatām iti

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Śrī Sūta Gosvāmī said; sah — he; evam — thus; rishi-varyah — the great rishi; ayam — Śukadeva Gosvāmī; prishtah — being questioned; rājñā — by the King; parīkshitā — Mahārāja Parīkshit; prati — to; āha — replied; tam — unto the King; su-bahu-vit — highly experienced; prīta-ātmā — fully satisfied; śrūyatām — please hear me; iti — thus.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Suta Gosvāmī said: The great sage Śukadeva Gosvāmī was highly experienced and was pleased with the King. Thus being questioned by the King, he said to him, "Please hear the topics attentively."
