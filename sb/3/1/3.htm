Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 1: Questions by Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.1.3

rājovāca

kutra kshattur bhagavatā

maitreyenāsa sańgamah

kadā vā saha-samvāda

etad varnaya nah prabho

SYNONYMS

rājā uvāca — the King said; kutra — wherein; kshattuh — with Vidura; bhagavatā — and with His Grace; maitreyena — with Maitreya; āsa — there was; sańgamah — meeting; kadā — when; vā — also; saha — with; samvādah — discussion; etat — this; varnaya — describe; nah — unto me; prabho — O my lord.

TRANSLATION

The King asked Śukadeva Gosvāmī: Where and when did the meeting and discussion take place between Saint Vidura and His Grace Maitreya Muni? Kindly oblige, my lord, and describe this to us.

PURPORT

Exactly as Śaunaka Rishi inquired of Sūta Gosvāmī and Sūta Gosvāmī replied, so Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī replied to King Parīkshit's inquiries. The King was very anxious to understand the meaningful discussion that took place between the two great souls.
