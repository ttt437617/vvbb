Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 1: Questions by Vidura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.1.29

kaccit sukham sātvata-vrishni-bhoja-

dāśārhakānām adhipah sa āste

yam abhyashiñcac chata-patra-netro

nripāsanāśām parihritya dūrāt

SYNONYMS

kaccit — whether; sukham — is all well; sātvata — the Sātvata race; vrishni — the Vrishni dynasty; bhoja — the Bhoja dynasty; dāśārhakānām — the Dāśārha race; adhipah — King Ugrasena; sah — he; āste — does exist; yam — whom; abhyashiñcat — installed; śata-patra-netrah — Lord Śrī Krishna; nripa-āsana-āśām — hope of the royal throne; parihritya — giving up; dūrāt — at a distant place.

TRANSLATION

O my friend, [tell me] whether Ugrasena, the King of the Sātvatas, Vrishnis, Bhojas and Dāśārhas, is now doing well. He went far away from his kingdom, leaving aside all hopes of his royal throne, but Lord Krishna again installed him.
