Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 24: The Renunciation of Kardama Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.24.25

tatas ta rishayah kshattah

krita-dārā nimantrya tam

prātishthan nandim āpannāh

svam svam āśrama-mandalam

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; te — they; rishayah — the sages; kshattah — O Vidura; krita-dārāh — thus married; nimantrya — taking leave of; tam — Kardama; prātishthan — they departed; nandim — joy; āpannāh — obtained; svam svam — each to his own; āśrama-mandalam — hermitage.

TRANSLATION

Thus married, the sages took leave of Kardama and departed full of joy, each for his own hermitage, O Vidura.
