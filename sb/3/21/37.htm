Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 21: Conversation Between Manu and Kardama

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.21.37

tasmin sudhanvann ahani

bhagavān yat samādiśat

upāyād āśrama-padam

muneh śānta-vratasya tat

SYNONYMS

tasmin — on that; su-dhanvan — O great bowman Vidura; ahani — on the day; bhagavān — the Lord; yat — which; samādiśat — foretold; upāyāt — he reached; āśrama-padam — the holy hermitage; muneh — of the sage; śānta — completed; vratasya — whose vows of austerity; tat — that.

TRANSLATION

O Vidura, they reached the hermitage of the sage, who had just completed his vows of austerity on the very day foretold by the Lord.
