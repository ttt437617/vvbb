Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 21: Conversation Between Manu and Kardama

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.21.10

kirītinam kundalinam

śańkha-cakra-gadā-dharam

śvetotpala-krīdanakam

manah-sparśa-smitekshanam

SYNONYMS

kirītinam — adorned with a crown; kundalinam — wearing earrings; śańkha — conch; cakra — disc; gadā — mace; dharam — holding; śveta — white; utpala — lily; krīdanakam — plaything; manah — heart; sparśa — touching; smita — smiling; īkshanam — and glancing.

TRANSLATION

Adorned with a crown and earrings, He held His characteristic conch, disc and mace in three of His hands and a white lily in the fourth. He glanced about in a happy, smiling mood whose sight captivates the hearts of all devotees.
