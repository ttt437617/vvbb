Canto 3: The Status QuoChapter 21: Conversation Between Manu and Kardama

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 3.21.26

sa ceha vipra rājarshir

mahishyā śatarūpayā

āyāsyati didrikshus tvām

paraśvo dharma-kovidah

SYNONYMS

sah — Svāyambhuva Manu; ca — and; iha — here; vipra — O holy brāhmana; rāja-rishih — the saintly king; mahishyā — along with his queen; śatarūpayā — called Śatarūpā; āyāsyati — will come; didrikshuh — desiring to see; tvām — you; paraśvah — the day after tomorrow; dharma — in religious activities; kovidah — expert.

TRANSLATION

The day after tomorrow, O brāhmana, that celebrated emperor, who is expert in religious activities, will come here with his queen, Śatarūpā, wishing to see you.
