Canto 9: LiberationChapter 5: Durvāsā Muni's Life Spared

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.5.21

karmāvadātam etat te

gāyanti svah-striyo muhuh

kīrtim parama-punyām ca

kīrtayishyati bhūr iyam

SYNONYMS

karma — activity; avadātam — without any tinge; etat — all this; te — your; gāyanti — will sing; svah-striyah — women from the heavenly planets; muhuh — always; kīrtim — glories; parama-punyām — highly glorified and pious; ca — also; kīrtayishyati — will continuously chant; bhūh — the whole world; iyam — this.

TRANSLATION

All the blessed women in the heavenly planets will continuously chant about your spotless character at every moment, and the people of this world will also chant your glories continuously.
