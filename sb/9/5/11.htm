Canto 9: LiberationChapter 5: Durvāsā Muni's Life Spared

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.5.11

yadi no bhagavān prīta

ekah sarva-gunāśrayah

sarva-bhūtātma-bhāvena

dvijo bhavatu vijvarah

SYNONYMS

yadi — if; nah — unto us; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; prītah — is satisfied; ekah — without any duplicate; sarva-guna-āśrayah — the reservoir of all transcendental qualities; sarva-bhūta-ātma-bhāvena — by a merciful attitude toward all living entities; dvijah — this brāhmana; bhavatu — may become; vijvarah — freed from all burning.

TRANSLATION

If the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who is one without a second, who is the reservoir of all transcendental qualities, and who is the life and soul of all living entities, is pleased with us, we wish that this brāhmana, Durvāsā Muni, be freed from the pain of being burned.
