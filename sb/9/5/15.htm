Canto 9: LiberationChapter 5: Durvāsā Muni's Life Spared

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.5.15

dushkarah ko nu sādhūnām

dustyajo vā mahātmanām

yaih sańgrihīto bhagavān

sātvatām rishabho harih

SYNONYMS

dushkarah — difficult to do; kah — what; nu — indeed; sādhūnām — of the devotees; dustyajah — impossible to give up; vā — either; mahā-ātmanām — of the great persons; yaih — by which persons; sańgrihītah — achieved (by devotional service); bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sātvatām — of the pure devotees; rishabhah — the leader; harih — the Lord.

TRANSLATION

For those who have achieved the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the master of the pure devotees, what is impossible to do, and what is impossible to give up?
