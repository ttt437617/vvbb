Canto 9: LiberationChapter 5: Durvāsā Muni's Life Spared

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.5.3

ambarīsha uvāca

tvam agnir bhagavān sūryas

tvam somo jyotishām patih

tvam āpas tvam kshitir vyoma

vāyur mātrendriyāni ca

SYNONYMS

ambarīshah — Mahārāja Ambarīsha; uvāca — said; tvam — you (are); agnih — the fire; bhagavān — the most powerful; sūryah — the sun; tvam — you (are); somah — the moon; jyotishām — of all the luminaries; patih — the master; tvam — you (are); āpah — water; tvam — you (are); kshitih — earth; vyoma — sky; vāyuh — the air; mātra — the objects of the senses; indriyāni — and the senses; ca — also.

TRANSLATION

Mahārāja Ambarīsha said: O Sudarśana cakra, you are fire, you are the most powerful sun, and you are the moon, the master of all luminaries. You are water, earth and sky, you are the air, you are the five sense objects [sound, touch, form, taste and smell], and you are the senses also.
