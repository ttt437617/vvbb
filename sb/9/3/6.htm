Canto 9: LiberationChapter 3: The Marriage of Sukanyā and Cyavana Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.3.6

apy abhadram na yushmābhir

bhārgavasya viceshtitam

vyaktam kenāpi nas tasya

kritam āśrama-dūshanam

SYNONYMS

api — alas; abhadram — something mischievous; nah — among us; yushmābhih — by ourselves; bhārgavasya — of Cyavana Muni; viceshtitam — has been attempted; vyaktam — now it is clear; kena api — by someone; nah — among ourselves; tasya — of him (Cyavana Muni); kritam — has been done; āśrama-dūshanam — pollution of the āśrama.

TRANSLATION

How strange it is that one of us has attempted to do something wrong to Cyavana Muni, the son of Bhrigu. It certainly appears that someone among us has polluted this āśrama.
