Canto 9: LiberationChapter 3: The Marriage of Sukanyā and Cyavana Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.3.5

śakrin-mūtra-nirodho 'bhūt

sainikānām ca tat-kshanāt

rājarshis tam upālakshya

purushān vismito 'bravīt

SYNONYMS

śakrit — of stool; mūtra — and of urine; nirodhah — stoppage; abhūt — so became; sainikānām — of all the soldiers; ca — and; tat-kshanāt — immediately; rājarshih — the King; tam upālakshya — seeing the incident; purushān — to his men; vismitah — being surprised; abravīt — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon, all the soldiers of Śaryāti were immediately obstructed from passing urine and stool. Upon perceiving this, Śaryāti spoke to his associates in surprise.
