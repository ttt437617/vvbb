Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.36

janamejayas tvām viditvā

takshakān nidhanam gatam

sarpān vai sarpa-yāgāgnau

sa hoshyati rushānvitah

SYNONYMS

janamejayah — the eldest son; tvām — about you; viditvā — knowing; takshakāt — by the Takshaka serpent; nidhanam — death; gatam — undergone; sarpān — the snakes; vai — indeed; sarpa-yāga-agnau — in the fire of the sacrifice for killing all the snakes; sah — he (Janamejaya); hoshyati — will offer as a sacrifice; rushā-anvitah — because of being very angry.

TRANSLATION

Because of your death by the Takshaka snake, your son Janamejaya will be very angry and will perform a sacrifice to kill all the snakes in the world.
