Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.46-48

bhavitā sahadevasya

mārjārir yac chrutaśravāh

tato yutāyus tasyāpi

niramitro 'tha tat-sutah

sunakshatrah sunakshatrād

brihatseno 'tha karmajit

tatah sutañjayād viprah

śucis tasya bhavishyati

kshemo 'tha suvratas tasmād

dharmasūtrah samas tatah

dyumatseno 'tha sumatih

subalo janitā tatah

SYNONYMS

bhavitā — will take birth; sahadevasya — the son of Sahadeva; mārjārih — Mārjāri; yat — his son; śrutaśravāh — Śrutaśravā; tatah — from him; yutāyuh — Yutāyu; tasya — his son; api — also; niramitrah — Niramitra; atha — thereafter; tat-sutah — his son; sunakshatrah — Sunakshatra; sunakshatrāt — from Sunakshatra; brihatsenah — Brihatsena; atha — from him; karmajit — Karmajit; tatah — from him; sutañjayāt — from Sutañjaya; viprah — Vipra; śucih — a son named Śuci; tasya — from him; bhavishyati — will take birth; kshemah — a son named Kshema; atha — thereafter; suvratah — a son named Suvrata; tasmāt — from him; dharmasūtrah — Dharmasūtra; samah — Sama; tatah — from him; dyumatsenah — Dyumatsena; atha — thereafter; sumatih — Sumati; subalah — Subala; janitā — will take birth; tatah — thereafter.

TRANSLATION

Sahadeva, the son of Jarāsandha, will have a son named Mārjāri. From Mārjāri will come Śrutaśravā; from Śrutaśravā, Yutāyu; and from Yutāyu, Niramitra. The son of Niramitra will be Sunakshatra, from Sunakshatra will come Brihatsena, and from Brihatsena, Karmajit. The son of Karmajit will be Sutañjaya, the son of Sutañjaya will be Vipra, and his son will be Śuci. The son of Śuci will be Kshema, the son of Kshema will be Suvrata, and the son of Suvrata will be Dharmasūtra. From Dharmasūtra will come Sama; from Sama, Dyumatsena; from Dyumatsena, Sumati; and from Sumati, Subala.
