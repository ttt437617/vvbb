Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.7

brihadrathāt kuśāgro 'bhūd

rishabhas tasya tat-sutah

jajñe satyahito 'patyam

pushpavāms tat-suto jahuh

SYNONYMS

brihadrathāt — from Brihadratha; kuśāgrah — Kuśāgra; abhūt — a son was born; rishabhah — Rishabha; tasya — of him (Kuśāgra); tat-sutah — his (Rishabha's) son; jajñe — was born; satyahitah — Satyahita; apatyam — offspring; pushpavān — Pushpavān; tat-sutah — his (Pushpavān's) son; jahuh — Jahu.

TRANSLATION

From Brihadratha, Kuśāgra was born; from Kuśāgra, Rishabha; and from Rishabha, Satyahita. The son of Satyahita was Pushpavān, and the son of Pushpavān was Jahu.
