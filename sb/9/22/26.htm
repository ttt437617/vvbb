Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.26

gāndhāryām dhritarāshtrasya

jajñe putra-śatam nripa

tatra duryodhano jyeshtho

duhśalā cāpi kanyakā

SYNONYMS

gāndhāryām — in the womb of Gāndhārī; dhritarāshtrasya — of Dhritarāshtra; jajñe — were born; putra-śatam — one hundred sons; nripa — O King Parīkshit; tatra — among the sons; duryodhanah — the son named Duryodhana; jyeshthah — the eldest; duhśalā — Duhśalā; ca api — also; kanyakā — one daughter.

TRANSLATION

Dhritarāshtra's wife, Gāndhārī, gave birth to one hundred sons and one daughter, O King. The oldest of the sons was Duryodhana, and the daughter's name was Duhśalā.
