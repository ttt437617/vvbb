Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.29

yudhishthirāt prativindhyah

śrutaseno vrikodarāt

arjunāc chrutakīrtis tu

śatānīkas tu nākulih

SYNONYMS

yudhishthirāt — from Mahārāja Yudhishthira; prativindhyah — a son named Prativindhya; śrutasenah — Śrutasena; vrikodarāt — begotten by Bhīma; arjunāt — from Arjuna; śrutakīrtih — a son named Śrutakīrti; tu — indeed; śatānīkah — a son named Śatānīka; tu — indeed; nākulih — of Nakula.

TRANSLATION

From Yudhishthira came a son named Prativindhya, from Bhīma a son named Śrutasena, from Arjuna a son named Śrutakīrti, and from Nakula a son named Śatānīka.
