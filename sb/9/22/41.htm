Canto 9: LiberationChapter 22: The Descendants of Ajamīdha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.22.41

tasmāc ca vrishtimāms tasya

susheno 'tha mahīpatih

sunīthas tasya bhavitā

nricakshur yat sukhīnalah

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from him (Śuciratha); ca — also; vrishtimān — the son known as Vrishtimān; tasya — his (son); sushenah — Sushena; atha — thereafter; mahī-patih — the emperor of the whole world; sunīthah — Sunītha; tasya — his; bhavitā — will be; nricakshuh — his son, Nricakshu; yat — from him; sukhīnalah — Sukhīnala.

TRANSLATION

From Śuciratha will come the son named Vrishtimān, and his son, Sushena, will be the emperor of the entire world. The son of Sushena will be Sunītha, his son will be Nricakshu, and from Nricakshu will come a son named Sukhīnala.
