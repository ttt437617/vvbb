Canto 9: LiberationChapter 19: King Yayāti Achieves Liberation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.19.18

pūrnam varsha-sahasram me

vishayān sevato 'sakrit

tathāpi cānusavanam

trishnā teshūpajāyate

SYNONYMS

pūrnam — completely; varsha-sahasram — one thousand years; me — my; vishayān — sense gratification; sevatah — enjoying; asakrit — without cessation, continuously; tathā api — still; ca — indeed; anusavanam — more and more; trishnā — lusty desires; teshu — in sense gratification; upajāyate — are increased.

TRANSLATION

I have spent a full one thousand years enjoying sense gratification, yet my desire to enjoy such pleasure increases daily.

PURPORT

Mahārāja Yayāti is explaining, in terms of his actual experience, how strong are sexual desires, even in old age.
