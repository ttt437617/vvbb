Canto 9: LiberationChapter 19: King Yayāti Achieves Liberation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.19.7

tam eva preshthatamayā

ramamānam ajānyayā

vilokya kūpa-samvignā

nāmrishyad basta-karma tat

SYNONYMS

tam — the he-goat; eva — indeed; preshthatamayā — beloved; ramamānam — engaged in sexual activities; ajā — the she-goat; anyayā — with another she-goat; vilokya — by seeing; kūpa-samvignā — the she-goat who had fallen into the well; na — not; amrishyat — tolerated; basta-karma — the business of the goat; tat — that (sex is accepted here as the business of the goat).

TRANSLATION

When the she-goat who had fallen into the well saw her beloved goat engaged in sexual affairs with another she-goat, she could not tolerate the goat's activities.
