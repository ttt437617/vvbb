Canto 9: LiberationChapter 20: The Dynasty of Pūru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.3

tasya sudyur abhūt putras

tasmād bahugavas tatah

samyātis tasyāhamyātī

raudrāśvas tat-sutah smritah

SYNONYMS

tasya — of him (Cārupada); sudyuh — by the name Sudyu; abhūt — appeared; putrah — a son; tasmāt — from him (Sudyu); bahugavah — a son named Bahugava; tatah — from him; samyātih — a son named Samyāti; tasya — and from him; ahamyātih — a son named Ahamyāti; raudrāśvah — Raudrāśva; tat-sutah — his son; smritah — well known.

TRANSLATION

The son of Cārupada was Sudyu, and the son of Sudyu was Bahugava. Bahugava's son was Samyāti. From Samyāti came a son named Ahamyāti, from whom Raudrāśva was born.
