Canto 9: LiberationChapter 20: The Dynasty of Pūru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.7

tasya medhātithis tasmāt

praskannādyā dvijātayah

putro 'bhūt sumate rebhir

dushmantas tat-suto matah

SYNONYMS

tasya — of him (Kanva); medhātithih — a son named Medhātithi; tasmāt — from him (Medhātithi); praskanna-ādyāh — sons headed by Praskanna; dvijātayah — all brāhmanas; putrah — a son; abhūt — there was; sumateh — from Sumati; rebhih — Rebhi; dushmantah — Mahārāja Dushmanta; tat-sutah — the son of Rebhi; matah — is well-known.

TRANSLATION

The son of Kanva was Medhātithi, whose sons, all brāhmanas, were headed by Praskanna. The son of Rantināva named Sumati had a son named Rebhi. Mahārāja Dushmanta is well known as the son of Rebhi.
