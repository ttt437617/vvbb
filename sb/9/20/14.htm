Canto 9: LiberationChapter 20: The Dynasty of Pūru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.14

āsyatām hy aravindāksha

grihyatām arhanam ca nah

bhujyatām santi nīvārā

ushyatām yadi rocate

SYNONYMS

āsyatām — please come sit here; hi — indeed; aravinda-aksha — O great hero with eyes like the petals of a lotus; grihyatām — please accept; arhanam — humble reception; ca — and; nah — our; bhujyatām — please eat; santi — what there is in stock; nīvārāh — nīvārā rice; ushyatām — stay here; yadi — if; rocate — you so desire.

TRANSLATION

O King with eyes like the petals of a lotus, kindly come sit down and accept whatever reception we can offer. We have a supply of nīvārā rice that you may kindly take. And if you so desire, stay here without hesitation.
