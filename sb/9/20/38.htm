Canto 9: LiberationChapter 20: The Dynasty of Pūru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.38

mūdhe bhara dvājam imam

bhara dvājam brihaspate

yātau yad uktvā pitarau

bharadvājas tatas tv ayam

SYNONYMS

mūdhe — O foolish woman; bhara — just maintain; dvājam — although born by an illicit connection between two; imam — this child; bhara — maintain; dvājam — although born by an illicit connection between two; brihaspate — O Brihaspati; yātau — left; yat — because; uktvā — having said; pitarau — both the father and mother; bharadvājah — by the name Bharadvāja; tatah — thereafter; tu — indeed; ayam — this child.

TRANSLATION

Brihaspati said to Mamatā, "You foolish woman, although this child was born from the wife of one man through the semen discharged by another, you should maintain him." Upon hearing this, Mamatā replied, "O Brihaspati, you maintain him!" After speaking in this way, Brihaspati and Mamatā both left. Thus the child was known as Bharadvāja.
