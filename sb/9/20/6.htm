Canto 9: LiberationChapter 20: The Dynasty of Pūru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.20.6

riteyo rantināvo 'bhūt

trayas tasyātmajā nripa

sumatir dhruvo 'pratirathah

kanvo 'pratirathātmajah

SYNONYMS

riteyoh — from the son named Riteyu; rantināvah — the son named Rantināva; abhūt — appeared; trayah — three; tasya — his (Rantināva's); ātmajāh — sons; nripa — O King; sumatih — Sumati; dhruvah — Dhruva; apratirathah — Apratiratha; kanvah — Kanva; apratiratha-ātmajah — the son of Apratiratha.

TRANSLATION

Riteyu had a son named Rantināva, who had three sons, named Sumati, Dhruva and Apratiratha. Apratiratha had only one son, whose name was Kanva.
