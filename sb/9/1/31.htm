Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.31

rishayo 'pi tayor vīkshya

prasańgam ramamānayoh

nivrittāh prayayus tasmān

nara-nārāyanāśramam

SYNONYMS

rishayah — all the great saintly persons; api — also; tayoh — of both of them; vīkshya — seeing; prasańgam — engagement in sexual matters; ramamānayoh — who were enjoying in that way; nivrittāh — desisted from going further; prayayuh — immediately departed; tasmāt — from that place; nara-nārāyana-āśramam — to the āśrama of Nara-Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Lord Śiva and Pārvatī engaged in sexual affairs, all the great saintly persons immediately desisted from going further and departed for the āśrama of Nara-Nārāyana.
