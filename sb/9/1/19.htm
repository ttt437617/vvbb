Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.19

niśamya tad vacas tasya

bhagavān prapitāmahah

hotur vyatikramam jñātvā

babhāshe ravi-nandanam

SYNONYMS

niśamya — after hearing; tat vacah — those words; tasya — of him (Manu); bhagavān — the most powerful; prapitāmahah — the great-grandfather Vasishtha; hotuh vyatikramam — discrepancy on the part of the hotā priest; jñātvā — understanding; babhāshe — spoke; ravi-nandanam — unto Vaivasvata Manu, son of the sun-god.

TRANSLATION

The most powerful great-grandfather Vasishtha, after hearing these words of Manu, understood the discrepancy on the part of the priest. Thus he spoke as follows to the son of the sun-god.
