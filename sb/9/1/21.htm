Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.21

evam vyavasito rājan

bhagavān sa mahā-yaśāh

astaushīd ādi-purusham

ilāyāh pumstva-kāmyayā

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; vyavasitah — deciding; rājan — O King Parīkshit; bhagavān — the most powerful; sah — Vasishtha; mahā-yaśāh — very famous; astaushīt — offered prayers; ādi-purusham — unto the Supreme Person, Lord Vishnu; ilāyāh — of Ilā; pumstva-kāmyayā — for the transformation into a male.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King Parīkshit, after the most famous and powerful Vasishtha made this decision, he offered prayers to the Supreme Person, Vishnu, to transform Ilā into a male.
