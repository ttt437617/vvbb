Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.9

tasya nābheh samabhavat

padma-kosho hiranmayah

tasmiñ jajñe mahārāja

svayambhūś catur-ānanah

SYNONYMS

tasya — of Him (the Supreme Personality of Godhead); nābheh — from the navel; samabhavat — generated; padma-koshah — a lotus; hiranmayah — known as Hiranmaya, or golden; tasmin — on that golden lotus; jajñe — appeared; mahārāja — O King; svayambhūh — one who is self-manifested, who takes birth without a mother; catuh-ānanah — with four heads.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, from the navel of the Supreme Personality of Godhead was generated a golden lotus, on which the four-faced Lord Brahmā took his birth.
