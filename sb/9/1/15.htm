Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.15

preshito 'dhvaryunā hotā

vyacarat tat samāhitah

grihīte havishi vācā

vashat-kāram grinan dvijah

SYNONYMS

preshitah — being told to execute the sacrifice; adhvaryunā — by the ritvik priest; hotā — the priest in charge of offering oblations; vyacarat — executed; tat — that (sacrifice); samāhitah — with great attention; grihīte havishi — upon taking the clarified butter for the first oblation; vācā — by chanting the mantra; vashat-kāram — the mantra beginning with the word vashat; grinan — reciting; dvijah — the brāhmana.

TRANSLATION

Told by the chief priest "Now offer oblations," the person in charge of oblations took clarified butter to offer. He then remembered the request of Manu's wife and performed the sacrifice while chanting the word "vashat."
