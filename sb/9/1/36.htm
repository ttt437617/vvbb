Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.36

evam strītvam anuprāptah

sudyumno mānavo nripah

sasmāra sa kulācāryam

vasishtham iti śuśruma

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; strītvam — femininity; anuprāptah — having achieved in that way; sudyumnah — the male named Sudyumna; mānavah — the son of Manu; nripah — the king; sasmāra — remembered; sah — he; kula-ācāryam — the familial spiritual master; vasishtham — the most powerful Vasishtha; iti śuśruma — I have heard it (from reliable sources).

TRANSLATION

I heard from reliable sources that King Sudyumna, the son of Manu, having thus achieved femininity, remembered his familial spiritual master, Vasishtha.
