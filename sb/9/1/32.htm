Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.32

tad idam bhagavān āha

priyāyāh priya-kāmyayā

sthānam yah praviśed etat

sa vai yoshid bhaved iti

SYNONYMS

tat — because; idam — this; bhagavān — Lord Śiva; āha — said; priyāyāh — of his dear wife; priya-kāmyayā — for the pleasure; sthānam — place; yah — anyone who; praviśet — will enter; etat — here; sah — that person; vai — indeed; yoshit — female; bhavet — shall become; iti — thus.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon, just to please his wife, Lord Śiva said, "Any male entering this place shall immediately become a female!"
