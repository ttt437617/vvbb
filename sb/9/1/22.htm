Canto 9: LiberationChapter 1: King Sudyumna Becomes a Woman

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.1.22

tasmai kāma-varam tushto

bhagavān harir īśvarah

dadāv ilābhavat tena

sudyumnah purusharshabhah

SYNONYMS

tasmai — unto him (Vasishtha); kāma-varam — the desired benediction; tushtah — being pleased; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality; harih īśvarah — the supreme controller, the Lord; dadau — gave; ilā — the girl, Ilā; abhavat — became; tena — because of this benediction; sudyumnah — by the name Sudyumna; purusha-rishabhah — a nice male.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead, the supreme controller, being pleased with Vasishtha, gave him the benediction he desired. Thus Ilā was transformed into a very fine male named Sudyumna.
