Canto 9: LiberationChapter 16: Lord Paraśurāma Destroys the World's Ruling Class

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.16.15

te duhkha-roshāmarshārti-

śoka-vega-vimohitāh

hā tāta sādho dharmishtha

tyaktvāsmān svar-gato bhavān

SYNONYMS

te — all the sons of Jamadagni; duhkha — of grief; rosha — anger; amarsha — indignation; ārti — affliction; śoka — and lamentation; vega — with the force; vimohitāh — bewildered; hā tāta — O father; sādho — the great saint; dharmishtha — the most religious person; tyaktvā — leaving; asmān — us; svah-gatah — have gone to the heavenly planets; bhavān — you.

TRANSLATION

Virtually bewildered by grief, anger, indignation, affliction and lamentation, the sons of Jamadagni cried, "O father, most religious, saintly person, you have left us and gone to the heavenly planets !"
