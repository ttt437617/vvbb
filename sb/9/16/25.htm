Canto 9: LiberationChapter 16: Lord Paraśurāma Destroys the World's Ruling Class

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.16.25

jāmadagnyo 'pi bhagavān

rāmah kamala-locanah

āgāminy antare rājan

vartayishyati vai brihat

SYNONYMS

jāmadagnyah — the son of Jamadagni; api — also; bhagavān — the Personality of Godhead; rāmah — Lord Paraśurāma; kamala-locanah — whose eyes are like lotus petals; āgāmini — coming; antare — in the manvantara, the time of one Manu; rājan — O King Parīkshit; vartayishyati — will propound; vai — indeed; brihat — Vedic knowledge.

TRANSLATION

My dear King Parīkshit, in the next manvantara the lotus-eyed Personality of Godhead Lord Paraśurāma, the son of Jamadagni, will be a great propounder of Vedic knowledge. In other words, he will be one of the seven sages.
