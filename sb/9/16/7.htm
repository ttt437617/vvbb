Canto 9: LiberationChapter 16: Lord Paraśurāma Destroys the World's Ruling Class

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.16.7

varena cchandayām āsa

prītah satyavatī-sutah

vavre hatānām rāmo 'pi

jīvitam cāsmritim vadhe

SYNONYMS

varena cchandayām āsa — asked to take a benediction as he liked; prītah — being very pleased (with him); satyavatī-sutah — Jamadagni, the son of Satyavatī; vavre — said; hatānām — of my dead mother and brothers; rāmah — Paraśurāma; api — also; jīvitam — let them be alive; ca — also; asmritim — no remembrance; vadhe — of their having been killed by me.

TRANSLATION

Jamadagni, the son of Satyavatī, was very much pleased with Paraśurāma and asked him to take any benediction he liked. Lord Paraśurāma replied, "Let my mother and brothers live again and not remember having been killed by me. This is the benediction I ask."
