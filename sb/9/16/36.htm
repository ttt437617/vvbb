Canto 9: LiberationChapter 16: Lord Paraśurāma Destroys the World's Ruling Class

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.16.36

esha vah kuśikā vīro

devarātas tam anvita

anye cāshtaka-hārīta-

jaya-kratumad-ādayah

SYNONYMS

eshah — this (Śunahśepha); vah — like you; kuśikāh — O Kuśikas; vīrah — my son; devarātah — he is known as Devarāta; tam — him; anvita — just obey; anye — others; ca — also; ashtaka — Ashtaka; hārīta — Hārīta; jaya — Jaya; kratumat — Kratumān; ādayah — and others.

TRANSLATION

Viśvāmitra said, "O Kuśikas [descendants of Kauśika], this Devarāta is my son and is one of you. Please obey his orders." O King Parīkshit, Viśvāmitra had many other sons, such as Ashtaka, Hārīta, Jaya and Kratumān.
