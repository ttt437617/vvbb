Canto 9: LiberationChapter 17: The Dynasties of the Sons of Purūravā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.17.6

sa eva śatrujid vatsa

ritadhvaja itīritah

tathā kuvalayāśveti

prokto 'larkādayas tatah

SYNONYMS

sah — that Dyumān; eva — indeed; śatrujit — Śatrujit; vatsah — Vatsa; ritadhvajah — Ritadhvaja; iti — like that; īritah — known; tathā — as well as; kuvalayāśva — Kuvalayāśva; iti — thus; proktah — well known; alarka-ādayah — Alarka and other sons; tatah — from him.

TRANSLATION

Dyumān was also known as Śatrujit, Vatsa, Ritadhvaja and Kuvalayāśva. From him were born Alarka and other sons.
