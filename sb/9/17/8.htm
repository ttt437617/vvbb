Canto 9: LiberationChapter 17: The Dynasties of the Sons of Purūravā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.17.8

alarkāt santatis tasmāt

sunītho 'tha niketanah

dharmaketuh sutas tasmāt

satyaketur ajāyata

SYNONYMS

alarkāt — from Alarka; santatih — a son known as Santati; tasmāt — from him; sunīthah — Sunītha; atha — from him; niketanah — a son named Niketana; dharmaketuh — Dharmaketu; sutah — a son; tasmāt — and from Dharmaketu; satyaketuh — Satyaketu; ajāyata — was born.

TRANSLATION

From Alarka came a son named Santati, and his son was Sunītha. The son of Sunītha was Niketana, the son of Niketana was Dharmaketu, and the son of Dharmaketu was Satyaketu.
