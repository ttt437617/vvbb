Canto 9: LiberationChapter 17: The Dynasties of the Sons of Purūravā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.17.16

kuśāt pratih kshātravriddhāt

sañjayas tat-suto jayah

tatah kritah kritasyāpi

jajñe haryabalo nripah

SYNONYMS

kuśāt — from Kuśa; pratih — a son named Prati; kshātravriddhāt — the grandson of Kshatravriddha; sañjayah — a son named Sañjaya; tat-sutah — his son; jayah — Jaya; tatah — from him; kritah — Krita; kritasya — from Krita; api — as well as; jajñe — was born; haryabalah — Haryabala; nripah — the king.

TRANSLATION

From Kuśa, the grandson of Kshatravriddha, was born a son named Prati. The son of Prati was Sañjaya, and the son of Sañjaya was Jaya. From Jaya, Krita was born, and from Krita, King Haryabala.
