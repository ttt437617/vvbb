Canto 9: LiberationChapter 17: The Dynasties of the Sons of Purūravā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.17.5

tat-putrah ketumān asya

jajñe bhīmarathas tatah

divodāso dyumāms tasmāt

pratardana iti smritah

SYNONYMS

tat-putrah — his son (the son of Dhanvantari); ketumān — Ketumān; asya — his; jajñe — took birth; bhīmarathah — a son named Bhīmaratha; tatah — from him; divodāsah — a son named Divodāsa; dyumān — Dyumān; tasmāt — from him; pratardanah — Pratardana; iti — thus; smritah — known.

TRANSLATION

The son of Dhanvantari was Ketumān, and his son was Bhīmaratha. The son of Bhīmaratha was Divodāsa, and the son of Divodāsa was Dyumān, also known as Pratardana.
