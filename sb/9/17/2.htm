Canto 9: LiberationChapter 17: The Dynasties of the Sons of Purūravā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.17.1-3

śrī-bādarāyanir uvāca

yah purūravasah putra

āyus tasyābhavan sutāh

nahushah kshatravriddhaś ca

rajī rābhaś ca vīryavān

anenā iti rājendra

śrinu kshatravridho 'nvayam

kshatravriddha-sutasyāsan

suhotrasyātmajās trayah

kāśyah kuśo gritsamada

iti gritsamadād abhūt

śunakah śaunako yasya

bahvrica-pravaro munih

SYNONYMS

śrī-bādarāyanih uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; yah — one who; purūravasah — of Purūravā; putrah — son; āyuh — his name was Āyu; tasya — of him; abhavan — there were; sutāh — sons; nahushah — Nahusha; kshatravriddhah ca — and Kshatravriddha; rajī — Rajī; rābhah — Rābha; ca — also; vīryavān — very powerful; anenāh — Anenā; iti — thus; rāja-indra — O Mahārāja Parīkshit; śrinu — just hear from me; kshatravridhah — of Kshatravriddha; anvayam — the dynasty; kshatravriddha — of Kshatravriddha; sutasya — of the son; āsan — there were; suhotrasya — of Suhotra; ātmajāh — sons; trayah — three; kāśyah — Kāśya; kuśah — Kuśa; gritsamadah — Gritsamada; iti — thus; gritsamadāt — from Gritsamada; abhūt — there was; śunakah — Śunaka; śaunakah — Śaunaka; yasya — of whom (Śunaka); bahu-rica-pravarah — the best of those conversant with the Rig Veda; munih — a great saintly person.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: From Purūravā came a son named Āyu, whose very powerful sons were Nahusha, Kshatravriddha, Rajī, Rābha and Anenā. O Mahārāja Parīkshit, now hear about the dynasty of Kshatravriddha. Kshatravriddha's son was Suhotra, who had three sons, named Kāśya, Kuśa and Gritsamada. From Gritsamada came Śunaka, and from him came Śaunaka, the great saint, the best of those conversant with the Rig Veda.
