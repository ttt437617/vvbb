Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.30

tat-sutah kevalas tasmād

dhundhumān vegavāms tatah

budhas tasyābhavad yasya

trinabindur mahīpatih

SYNONYMS

tat-sutah — the son of him (Nara); kevalah — was named Kevala; tasmāt — from him (Kevala); dhundhumān — a son was born named Dhundhumān; vegavān — named Vegavān; tatah — from him (Dhundhumān); budhah — named Budha; tasya — of him (Vegavān); abhavat — there was; yasya — of whom (Budha); trinabinduh — a son named Trinabindu; mahīpatih — the king.

TRANSLATION

The son of Nara was Kevala, and his son was Dhundhumān, whose son was Vegavān. Vegavān's son was Budha, and Budha's son was Trinabindu, who became the king of this earth.
