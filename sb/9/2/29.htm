Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.29

maruttasya damah putras

tasyāsīd rājyavardhanah

sudhritis tat-suto jajñe

saudhriteyo narah sutah

SYNONYMS

maruttasya — of Marutta; damah — (was named) Dama; putrah — the son; tasya — of him (Dama); āsīt — there was; rājya-vardhanah — named Rājyavardhana, or one who can expand the kingdom; sudhritih — was named Sudhriti; tat-sutah — the son of him (Rājyavardhana); jajñe — was born; saudhriteyah — from Sudhriti; narah — named Nara; sutah — the son.

TRANSLATION

Marutta's son was Dama, Dama's son was Rājyavardhana, Rājyavardhana's son was Sudhriti, and his son was Nara.
