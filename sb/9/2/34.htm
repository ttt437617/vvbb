Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.34

hemacandrah sutas tasya

dhūmrākshas tasya cātmajah

tat-putrāt samyamād āsīt

kriśāśvah saha-devajah

SYNONYMS

hemacandrah — was named Hemacandra; sutah — the son; tasya — of him (Viśāla); dhūmrākshah — was named Dhūmrāksha; tasya — of him (Hemacandra); ca — also; ātmajah — the son; tat-putrāt — from the son of him (Dhūmrāksha); samyamāt — from he who was named Samyama; āsīt — there was; kriśāśvah — Kriśāśva; saha — along with; devajah — Devaja.

TRANSLATION

The son of Viśāla was known as Hemacandra, his son was Dhūmrāksha, and his son was Samyama, whose sons were Devaja and Kriśāśva.
