Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.20

vītihotras tv indrasenāt

tasya satyaśravā abhūt

uruśravāh sutas tasya

devadattas tato 'bhavat

SYNONYMS

vītihotrah — Vītihotra; tu — but; indrasenāt — from Indrasena; tasya — of Vītihotra; satyaśravāh — known by the name Satyaśravā; abhūt — there was; uruśravāh — Uruśravā; sutah — was the son; tasya — of him (Satyaśravā); devadattah — Devadatta; tatah — from Uruśravā; abhavat — there was.

TRANSLATION

From Indrasena came Vītihotra, from Vītihotra came Satyaśravā, from Satyaśravā came the son named Uruśravā, and from Uruśravā came Devadatta.
