Canto 9: LiberationChapter 2: The Dynasties of the Sons of Manu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.2.25

vivimśateh suto rambhah

khanīnetro 'sya dhārmikah

karandhamo mahārāja

tasyāsīd ātmajo nripa

SYNONYMS

vivimśateh — from Vivimśati; sutah — the son; rambhah — named Rambha; khanīnetrah — named Khanīnetra; asya — of Rambha; dhārmikah — very religious; karandhamah — named Karandhama; mahārāja — O King; tasya — of him (Khanīnetra); āsīt — was; ātmajah — the son; nripa — O King.

TRANSLATION

The son of Vivimśati was Rambha, whose son was the great and religious King Khanīnetra. O King, the son of Khanīnetra was King Karandhama.
