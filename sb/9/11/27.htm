Canto 9: LiberationChapter 11: Lord Rāmacandra Rules the World

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.11.27

prāsāda-gopura-sabhā-

caitya-deva-grihādishu

vinyasta-hema-kalaśaih

patākābhiś ca manditām

SYNONYMS

prāsāda — in palaces; gopura — palace gates; sabhā — assembly houses; caitya — raised platforms; deva-griha — temples wherein deities are worshiped; ādishu — and so on; vinyasta — placed; hema-kalaśaih — with golden waterpots; patākābhih — by flags; ca — also; manditām — bedecked.

TRANSLATION

The palaces, the palace gates, the assembly houses, the platforms for meeting places, the temples and all such places were decorated with golden waterpots and bedecked with various types of flags.
