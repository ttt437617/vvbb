Canto 9: LiberationChapter 11: Lord Rāmacandra Rules the World

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.11.35

tasmin sa bhagavān rāmah

snigdhayā priyayeshtayā

reme svārāma-dhīrānām

rishabhah sītayā kila

SYNONYMS

tasmin — in that celestial palace; sah — He; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; rāmah — Lord Rāmacandra; snigdhayā — always pleased by her behavior; priyayā ishtayā — with His dearmost wife; reme — enjoyed; sva-ārāma — personal pleasure; dhīrānām — of the greatest learned persons; rishabhah — the chief; sītayā — with mother Sītā; kila — indeed.

TRANSLATION

Lord Rāmacandra, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, chief of the best learned scholars, resided in that palace with His pleasure potency, mother Sītā, and enjoyed complete peace.
