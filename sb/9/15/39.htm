Canto 9: LiberationChapter 15: Paraśurāma, the Lord's Warrior Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.15.39

vayam hi brāhmanās tāta

kshamayārhanatām gatāh

yayā loka-gurur devah

pārameshthyam agāt padam

SYNONYMS

vayam — we; hi — indeed; brāhmanāh — are qualified brāhmanas; tāta — O my dear son; kshamayā — with the quality of forgiveness; arhanatām — the position of being worshiped; gatāh — we have achieved; yayā — by this qualification; loka-guruh — the spiritual master of this universe; devah — Lord Brahmā; pārameshthyam — the supreme person within this universe; agāt — achieved; padam — the position.

TRANSLATION

My dear son, we are all brāhmanas and have become worshipable for the people in general because of our quality of forgiveness. It is because of this quality that Lord Brahmā, the supreme spiritual master of this universe, has achieved his post.
