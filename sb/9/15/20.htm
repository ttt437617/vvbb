Canto 9: LiberationChapter 15: Paraśurāma, the Lord's Warrior Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.15.20

strī-ratnair āvritah krīdan

revāmbhasi madotkatah

vaijayantīm srajam bibhrad

rurodha saritam bhujaih

SYNONYMS

strī-ratnaih — by beautiful women; āvritah — surrounded; krīdan — enjoying; revā-ambhasi — in the water of the River Revā, or Narmadā; mada-utkatah — too puffed up because of opulence; vaijayantīm srajam — the garland of victory; bibhrat — being decorated with; rurodha — stopped the flow; saritam — of the river; bhujaih — with his arms.

TRANSLATION

Once while enjoying in the water of the River Narmadā, the puffed-up Kārtavīryārjuna, surrounded by beautiful women and garlanded with a garland of victory, stopped the flow of the water with his arms.
