Canto 9: LiberationChapter 15: Paraśurāma, the Lord's Warrior Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.15.38

rāma rāma mahābāho

bhavān pāpam akārashīt

avadhīn naradevam yat

sarva-devamayam vrithā

SYNONYMS

rāma rāma — my dear son Paraśurāma; mahābāho — O great hero; bhavān — you; pāpam — sinful activities; akārashīt — have executed; avadhīt — have killed; naradevam — the king; yat — who is; sarva-deva-mayam — the embodiment of all the demigods; vrithā — unnecessarily.

TRANSLATION

O great hero, my dear son Paraśurāma, you have unnecessarily killed the king, who is supposed to be the embodiment of all the demigods. Thus you have committed a sin.
