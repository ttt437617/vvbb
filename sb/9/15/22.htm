Canto 9: LiberationChapter 15: Paraśurāma, the Lord's Warrior Incarnation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.15.22

grihīto līlayā strīnām

samaksham krita-kilbishah

māhishmatyām sanniruddho

mukto yena kapir yathā

SYNONYMS

grihītah — was arrested by force; līlayā — very easily; strīnām — of the women; samaksham — in the presence; krita-kilbishah — thus becoming an offender; māhishmatyām — in the city known as Māhishmatī; sanniruddhah — was arrested; muktah — released; yena — by whom (Kārtavīryārjuna); kapih yathā — exactly as done to a monkey.

TRANSLATION

When Rāvana attempted to insult Kārtavīryārjuna in the presence of the women and thus offended him, Kārtavīryārjuna easily arrested Rāvana and put him in custody in the city of Māhishmatī, just as one captures a monkey, and then released him neglectfully.
