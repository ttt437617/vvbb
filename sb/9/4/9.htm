Canto 9: LiberationChapter 4: Ambarīsha Mahārāja Offended by Durvāsā Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.4.9

nābhāgas tam pranamyāha

taveśa kila vāstukam

ity āha me pitā brahmañ

chirasā tvām prasādaye

SYNONYMS

nābhāgah — Nābhāga; tam — unto him (Lord Śiva); pranamya — offering obeisances; āha — said; tava — yours; īśa — O lord; kila — certainly; vāstukam — everything in the arena of sacrifice; iti — thus; āha — said; me — my; pitā — father; brahman — O brāhmana; śirasā — bowing my head; tvām — unto you; prasādaye — I am begging your mercy.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon, after offering obeisances to Lord Śiva, Nābhāga said: O worshipable lord, everything in this arena of sacrifice is yours. This is the assertion of my father. Now, with great respect, I bow my head before you, begging your mercy.
