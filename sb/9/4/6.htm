Canto 9: LiberationChapter 4: Ambarīsha Mahārāja Offended by Durvāsā Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.4.6

tam kaścit svīkarishyantam

purushah krishna-darśanah

uvācottarato 'bhyetya

mamedam vāstukam vasu

SYNONYMS

tam — unto Nābhāga; kaścit — someone; svīkarishyantam — while accepting the riches given by the great sages; purushah — a person; krishna-darśanah — black-looking; uvāca — said; uttaratah — from the north; abhyetya — coming; mama — my; idam — these; vāstukam — remnants of the sacrifice; vasu — all the riches.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, while Nābhāga was accepting the riches, a black-looking person from the north came to him and said, "All the wealth from this sacrificial arena belongs to me."
