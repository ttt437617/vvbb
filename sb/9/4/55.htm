Canto 9: LiberationChapter 4: Ambarīsha Mahārāja Offended by Durvāsā Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.4.55

pratyākhyāto viriñcena

vishnu-cakropatāpitah

durvāsāh śaranam yātah

śarvam kailāsa-vāsinam

SYNONYMS

pratyākhyātah — being refused; viriñcena — by Lord Brahmā; vishnu-cakra-upatāpitah — being scorched by the blazing fire of Lord Vishnu's disc; durvāsāh — the great mystic named Durvāsā; śaranam — f or shelter; yātah — went; śarvam — unto Lord Śiva; kailāsa-vāsinam — the resident of the place known as Kailāsa.

TRANSLATION

When Durvāsā, who was greatly afflicted by the blazing fire of the Sudarśana cakra, was thus refused by Lord Brahmā, he tried to take shelter of Lord Śiva, who always resides on his planet, known as Kailāsa.
