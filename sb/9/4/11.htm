Canto 9: LiberationChapter 4: Ambarīsha Mahārāja Offended by Durvāsā Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.4.11

grihāna dravinam dattam

mat-satra-pariśeshitam

ity uktvāntarhito rudro

bhagavān dharma-vatsalah

SYNONYMS

grihāna — please take now; dravinam — all the wealth; dattam — is given (to you by me); mat-satra-pariśeshitam — the remnants of the sacrifice executed on my behalf; iti uktvā — after speaking like this; antarhitah — disappeared; rudrah — Lord Śiva; bhagavān — the most powerful demigod; dharma-vatsalah — adherent to the principles of religion.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śiva said, "Now you may take all the wealth remaining from the sacrifice, for I give it to you." After saying this, Lord Śiva, who is most adherent to the religious principles, disappeared from that place.
