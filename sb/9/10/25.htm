Canto 9: LiberationChapter 10: The Pastimes of the Supreme Lord, Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.10.25

svān svān bandhūn parishvajya

lakshmaneshubhir arditān

ruruduh susvaram dīnā

ghnantya ātmānam ātmanā

SYNONYMS

svān svān — their own respective husbands; bandhūn — friends; parishvajya — embracing; lakshmana-ishubhih — by the arrows of Lakshmana; arditān — who were killed; ruruduh — all the wives cried piteously; su-svaram — it was very sweet to hear; dīnāh — very poor; ghnantyah — striking; ātmānam — their breasts; ātmanā — by themselves.

TRANSLATION

Striking their breasts in affliction because their husbands had been killed by the arrows of Lakshmana, the women embraced their respective husbands and cried piteously in voices appealing to everyone.
