Canto 9: LiberationChapter 10: The Pastimes of the Supreme Lord, Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.10.49

evam krita-śirah-snānah

suvāsāh sragvy-alańkritah

svalańkritaih suvāsobhir

bhrātribhir bhāryayā babhau

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; krita-śirah-snānah — having completely bathed, washing the head; su-vāsāh — being nicely dressed; sragvi-alańkritah — being decorated with a garland; su-alańkritaih — decorated nicely; su-vāsobhih — dressed nicely; bhrātribhih — with His brothers; bhāryayā — and with His wife, Sītā; babhau — the Lord became very brilliant.

TRANSLATION

Lord Rāmacandra, fully bathed and His head clean-shaven, dressed Himself very nicely and was decorated with a garland and ornaments. Thus He shone brightly, surrounded by His brothers and wife, who were similarly dressed and ornamented.
