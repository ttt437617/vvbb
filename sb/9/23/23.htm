Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.23

durmado bhadrasenasya

dhanakah kritavīryasūh

kritāgnih kritavarmā ca

kritaujā dhanakātmajāh

SYNONYMS

durmadah — Durmada; bhadrasenasya — of Bhadrasena; dhanakah — Dhanaka; kritavīrya-sūh — giving birth to Kritavīrya; kritāgnih — by the name Kritāgni; kritavarmā — Kritavarmā; ca — also; kritaujāh — Kritaujā; dhanaka-ātmajāh — sons of Dhanaka.

TRANSLATION

The sons of Bhadrasena were known as Durmada and Dhanaka. Dhanaka was the father of Kritavīrya and also of Kritāgni, Kritavarmā and Kritaujā.
