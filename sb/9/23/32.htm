Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.32

tasya patnī-sahasrānām

daśānām sumahā-yaśāh

daśa-laksha-sahasrāni

putrānām tāsv ajījanat

SYNONYMS

tasya — of Śaśabindu; patnī — wives; sahasrānām — of thousands; daśānām — ten; su-mahā-yaśāh — greatly famous; daśa — ten; laksha — lakhs (one lakh equals one hundred thousand); sahasrāni — thousands; putrānām — of sons; tāsu — in them; ajījanat — he begot.

TRANSLATION

The famous Śaśabindu had ten thousand wives, and by each he begot a lakh of sons. Therefore the number of his sons was ten thousand lakhs.
