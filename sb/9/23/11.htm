Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.11

brihadratho brihatkarmā

brihadbhānuś ca tat-sutāh

ādyād brihanmanās tasmāj

jayadratha udāhritah

SYNONYMS

brihadrathah — Brihadratha; brihatkarmā — Brihatkarmā; brihadbhānuh — Brihadbhānu; ca — also; tat-sutāh — the sons of Prithulāksha; ādyāt — from the eldest (Brihadratha); brihanmanāh — Brihanmanā was born; tasmāt — from him (Brihanmanā); jayadrathah — a son named Jayadratha; udāhritah — celebrated as his son.

TRANSLATION

The sons of Prithulāksha were Brihadratha, Brihatkarmā and Brihadbhānu. From the eldest, Brihadratha, came a son named Brihanmanā, and from Brihanmanā came a son named Jayadratha.
