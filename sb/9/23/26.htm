Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.26

pañcāśīti sahasrāni

hy avyāhata-balah samāh

anashta-vitta-smarano

bubhuje 'kshayya-shad-vasu

SYNONYMS

pañcāśīti — eighty-five; sahasrāni — thousands; hi — indeed; avyāhata — inexhaustible; balah — the strength of whom; samāh — years; anashta — without deterioration; vitta — material opulences; smaranah — and memory; bubhuje — enjoyed; akshayya — without deterioration; shat-vasu — six kinds of enjoyable material opulence.

TRANSLATION

For eighty-five thousand years, Kārtavīryārjuna continuously enjoyed material opulences with full bodily strength and unimpaired memory. In other words, he enjoyed inexhaustible material opulences with his six senses.
