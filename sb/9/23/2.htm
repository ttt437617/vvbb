Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.2

janamejayas tasya putro

mahāśālo mahāmanāh

uśīnaras titikshuś ca

mahāmanasa ātmajau

SYNONYMS

janamejayah — Janamejaya; tasya — of him (Janamejaya); putrah — a son; mahāśālah — Mahāśāla; mahāmanāh — (from Mahāśāla) a son named Mahāmanā; uśīnarah — Uśīnara; titikshuh — Titikshu; ca — and; mahāmanasah — from Mahāmanā; ātmajau — two sons.

TRANSLATION

From Sriñjaya came a son named Janamejaya. From Janamejaya came Mahāśāla; from Mahāśāla, Mahāmanā; and from Mahāmanā two sons, named Uśīnara and Titikshu.
