Canto 9: LiberationChapter 23: The Dynasties of the Sons of Yayāti

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.23.37

aham bandhyāsapatnī ca

snushā me yujyate katham

janayishyasi yam rājñi

tasyeyam upayujyate

SYNONYMS

aham — I am; bandhyā — sterile; asa-patnī — I have no co-wife; ca — also; snushā — daughter-in-law; me — my; yujyate — could be; katham — how; janayishyasi — you will give birth to; yam — which son; rājñi — O my dear Queen; tasya — for him; iyam — this girl; upayujyate — will be very suitable.

TRANSLATION

Śaibyā said, "I am sterile and have no co-wife. How can this girl be my daughter-in-law? Please tell me." Jyāmagha replied, "My dear Queen, I shall see that you indeed have a son and that this girl will be your daughter-in-law."
