Canto 9: LiberationChapter 21: The Dynasty of Bharata;

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.21.27

yavīnaro dvimīdhasya

kritimāms tat-sutah smritah

nāmnā satyadhritis tasya

dridhanemih supārśvakrit

SYNONYMS

yavīnarah — Yavīnara; dvimīdhasya — the son of Dvimīdha; kritimān — Kritimān; tat-sutah — the son of Yavīnara; smritah — is well known; nāmnā — by name; satyadhritih — Satyadhriti; tasya — of him (Satyadhriti); dridhanemih — Dridhanemi; supārśva-krit — the father of Supārśva.

TRANSLATION

The son of Dvimīdha was Yavīnara, whose son was Kritimān. The son of Kritimān was well known as Satyadhriti. From Satyadhriti came a son named Dridhanemi, who became the father of Supārśva.
