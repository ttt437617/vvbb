Canto 9: LiberationChapter 21: The Dynasty of Bharata;

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.21.30

tato bahuratho nāma

purumīdho 'prajo 'bhavat

nalinyām ajamīdhasya

nīlah śāntis tu tat-sutah

SYNONYMS

tatah — from him (Ripuñjaya); bahurathah — Bahuratha; nāma — named; purumīdhah — Purumīdha, the younger brother of Dvimīdha; aprajah — sonless; abhavat — became; nalinyām — through Nalinī; ajamīdhasya — of Ajamīdha; nīlah — Nīla; śāntih — Śānti; tu — then; tat-sutah — the son of Nīla.

TRANSLATION

From Ripuñjaya came a son named Bahuratha. Purumīdha was sonless. Ajamīdha had a son named Nīla by his wife known as Nalinī, and the son of Nīla was Śānti.
