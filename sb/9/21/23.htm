Canto 9: LiberationChapter 21: The Dynasty of Bharata;

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.21.23

tat-suto viśadas tasya

syenajit samajāyata

rucirāśvo dridhahanuh

kāśyo vatsaś ca tat-sutāh

SYNONYMS

tat-sutah — the son of Jayadratha; viśadah — Viśada; tasya — the son of Viśada; syenajit — Syenajit; samajāyata — was born; rucirāśvah — Rucirāśva; dridhahanuh — Dridhahanu; kāśyah — Kāśya; vatsah — Vatsa; ca — also; tat-sutāh — sons of Syenajit.

TRANSLATION

The son of Jayadratha was Viśada, and his son was Syenajit. The sons of Syenajit were Rucirāśva, Dridhahanu, Kāśya and Vatsa.
