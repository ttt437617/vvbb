Canto 9: LiberationChapter 6: The Downfall of Saubhari Muni

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.6.20

purañjayasya putro 'bhūd

anenās tat-sutah prithuh

viśvagandhis tataś candro

yuvanāśvas tu tat-sutah

SYNONYMS

purañjayasya — of Purañjaya; putrah — son; abhūt — was born; anenāh — by the name Anenā; tat-sutah — his son; prithuh — of the name Prithu; viśvagandhih — of the name Viśvagandhi; tatah — his son; candrah — by the name Candra; yuvanāśvah — of the name Yuvanāśva; tu — indeed; tat-sutah — his son.

TRANSLATION

The son of Purañjaya was known as Anenā, Anenā's son was Prithu, and Prithu's son was Viśvagandhi. Viśvagandhi's son was Candra, and Candra's son was Yuvanāśva.
