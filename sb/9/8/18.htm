Canto 9: LiberationChapter 8: The Sons of Sagara Meet Lord Kapiladeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.8.18

ayodhyā-vāsinah sarve

bālakān punar āgatān

drishtvā visismire rājan

rājā cāpy anvatapyata

SYNONYMS

ayodhyā-vāsinah — the inhabitants of Ayodhyā; sarve — all of them; bālakān — their sons; punah — again; āgatān — having come back to life; drishtvā — after seeing this; visismire — became astounded; rājan — O King Parīkshit; rājā — King Sagara; ca — also; api — indeed; anvatapyata — very much lamented (the absence of his son).

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, when all the inhabitants of Ayodhyā saw that their boys had come back to life, they were astounded, and King Sagara greatly lamented the absence of his son.
