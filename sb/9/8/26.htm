Canto 9: LiberationChapter 8: The Sons of Sagara Meet Lord Kapiladeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.8.26

adya nah sarva-bhūtātman

kāma-karmendriyāśayah

moha-pāśo dridhaś chinno

bhagavams tava darśanāt

SYNONYMS

adya — today; nah — our; sarva-bhūta-ātman — O You, who are the Supersoul; kāma-karma-indriya-āśayah — being under the influence of lusty desires and fruitive activities; moha-pāśah — this hard knot of illusion; dridhah — very strong; chinnah — broken; bhagavan — O my Lord; tava darśanāt — simply by seeing You.

TRANSLATION

O Supersoul of all living entities, O Personality of Godhead, simply by seeing You I have now been freed from all lusty desires, which are the root cause of insurmountable illusion and bondage in the material world.
