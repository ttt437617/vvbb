Canto 9: LiberationChapter 8: The Sons of Sagara Meet Lord Kapiladeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.8.14

yo 'samañjasa ity uktah

sa keśinyā nripātmajah

tasya putro 'mśumān nāma

pitāmaha-hite ratah

SYNONYMS

yah — one of the sons of Sagara Mahārāja; asamañjasah — whose name was Asamañjasa; iti — as such; uktah — known; sah — he; keśinyāh — in the womb of Keśinī, the other queen of Sagara Mahārāja; nripa-ātmajah — the son of the King; tasya — of him (Asamañjasa); putrah — the son; amśumān nāma — was known as Amśumān; pitāmaha-hite — in doing good for his grandfather, Sagara Mahārāja; ratah — always engaged.

TRANSLATION

Among the sons of Sagara Mahārāja was one named Asamañjasa, who was born from the King's second wife, Keśinī. The son of Asamañjasa was known as Amśumān, and he was always engaged in working for the good of Sagara Mahārāja, his grandfather.
