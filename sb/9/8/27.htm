Canto 9: LiberationChapter 8: The Sons of Sagara Meet Lord Kapiladeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.8.27

śrī-śuka uvāca

ittham gītānubhāvas tam

bhagavān kapilo munih

amśumantam uvācedam

anugrāhya dhiyā nripa

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; ittham — in this way; gīta-anubhāvah — whose glories are described; tam — unto Him; bhagavān — the Personality of Godhead; kapilah — named Kapila Muni; munih — the great sage; amśumantam — unto Amśumān; uvāca — said; idam — this; anugrāhya — being very merciful; dhiyā — with the path of knowledge; nripa — O King Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, when Amśumān had glorified the Lord in this way, the great sage Kapila, the powerful incarnation of Vishnu, being very merciful to him, explained to him the path of knowledge.
