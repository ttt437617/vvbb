Canto 9: LiberationChapter 8: The Sons of Sagara Meet Lord Kapiladeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.8.25

tvan-māyā-racite loke

vastu-buddhyā grihādishu

bhramanti kāma-lobhershyā-

moha-vibhrānta-cetasah

SYNONYMS

tvat-māyā — through Your material energy; racite — which is manufactured; loke — in this world; vastu-buddhyā — accepting as factual; griha-ādishu — in hearth and home, etc.; bhramanti — wander; kāma — by lusty desires; lobha — by greed; īrshyā — by envy; moha — and by illusion; vibhrānta — is bewildered; cetasah — the cores of whose hearts.

TRANSLATION

O my Lord, those whose hearts are bewildered by the influence of lust, greed, envy and illusion are interested only in false hearth and home in this world created by Your māyā. Attached to home, wife and children, they wander in this material world perpetually.
