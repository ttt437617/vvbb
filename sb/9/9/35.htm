Canto 9: LiberationChapter 9: The Dynasty of Amśumān

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.9.35

yasmān me bhakshitah pāpa

kāmārtāyāh patis tvayā

tavāpi mrityur ādhānād

akrita-prajña darśitah

SYNONYMS

yasmāt — because; me — my; bhakshitah — was eaten up; pāpa — O sinful one; kāma-ārtāyāh — of a woman very much bereaved because of sexual desire; patih — husband; tvayā — by you; tava — your; api — also; mrityuh — death; ādhānāt — when you try to discharge semen in your wife; akrita-prajña — O foolish rascal; darśitah — this curse is placed upon you.

TRANSLATION

O foolish, sinful person, because you have eaten my husband when I was sexually inclined and desiring to have the seed of a child, I shall also see you die when you attempt to discharge semen in your wife. In other words, whenever you attempt to sexually unite with your wife, you shall die.
