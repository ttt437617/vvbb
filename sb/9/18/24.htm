Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.24

gate rājani sā dhīre

tatra sma rudatī pituh

nyavedayat tatah sarvam

uktam śarmishthayā kritam

SYNONYMS

gate rājani — after the departure of the King; sā — she (Devayānī); dhīre — learned; tatra sma — returning to her home; rudatī — crying; pituh — before her father; nyavedayat — submitted; tatah — thereafter; sarvam — all; uktam — mentioned; śarmishthayā — by Śarmishthā; kritam — done.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, when the learned King returned to his palace, Devayānī returned home crying and told her father, Śukrācārya, about all that had happened because of Śarmishthā. She told how she had been thrown into the well but was saved by the King.
