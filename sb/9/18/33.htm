Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.33

yadum ca turvasum caiva

devayānī vyajāyata

druhyum cānum ca pūrum ca

śarmishthā vārshaparvanī

SYNONYMS

yadum — Yadu; ca — and; turvasum — Turvasu; ca eva — as well as; devayānī — the daughter of Śukrācārya; vyajāyata — gave birth to; druhyum — Druhyu; ca — and; anum — Anu; ca — also; pūrum — Pūru; ca — also; śarmishthā — Śarmishthā; vārshaparvanī — the daughter of Vrishaparvā.

TRANSLATION

Devayānī gave birth to Yadu and Turvasu, and Śarmishthā gave birth to Druhyu, Anu and Pūru.
