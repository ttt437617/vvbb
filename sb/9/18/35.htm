Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.35

priyām anugatah kāmī

vacobhir upamantrayan

na prasādayitum śeke

pāda-samvāhanādibhih

SYNONYMS

priyām — his beloved wife; anugatah — following; kāmī — very, very lusty; vacobhih — by great words; upamantrayan — appeasing; na — not; prasādayitum — to appease; śeke — was able; pāda-samvāhana-ādibhih — even by massaging her feet.

TRANSLATION

King Yayāti, who was very lusty, followed his wife, caught her and tried to appease her by speaking pleasing words and massaging her feet, but he could not satisfy her by any means.
