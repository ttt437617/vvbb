Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.11

aho nirīkshyatām asyā

dāsyāh karma hy asāmpratam

asmad-dhāryam dhritavatī

śunīva havir adhvare

SYNONYMS

aho — alas; nirīkshyatām — just see; asyāh — of her (Śarmishthā); dāsyāh — just like our servant; karma — activities; hi — indeed; asāmpratam — without any etiquette; asmat-dhāryam — the garment meant for me; dhritavatī — she has put on; śunī iva — like a dog; havih — clarified butter; adhvare — meant for offering in the sacrifice.

TRANSLATION

Oh, just see the activities of this servant-maid Śarmishthā! Disregarding all etiquette, she has put on my dress, just like a dog snatching clarified butter meant for use in a sacrifice.
