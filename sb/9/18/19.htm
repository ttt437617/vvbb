Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.19

dattvā svam uttaram vāsas

tasyai rājā vivāsase

grihītvā pāninā pānim

ujjahāra dayā-parah

SYNONYMS

dattvā — giving; svam — his own; uttaram — upper; vāsah — cloth; tasyai — unto her (Devayānī); rājā — the King; vivāsase — because she was naked; grihītvā — catching; pāninā — with his hand; pānim — her hand; ujjahāra — delivered; dayā-parah — being very kind.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Devayānī naked in the well, King Yayāti immediately gave her his upper cloth. Being very kind to her, he caught her hand with his own and lifted her out.
