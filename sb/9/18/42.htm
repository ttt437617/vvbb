Canto 9: LiberationChapter 18: King Yayāti Regains His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.18.42

apricchat tanayam pūrum

vayasonam gunādhikam

na tvam agrajavad vatsa

mām pratyākhyātum arhasi

SYNONYMS

apricchat — requested; tanayam — the son; pūrum — Pūru; vayasā — by age; ūnam — although younger; guna-adhikam — better than the others by quality; na — not; tvam — you; agraja-vat — like your older brothers; vatsa — my dear son; mām — me; pratyākhyātum — to refuse; arhasi — ought.

TRANSLATION

King Yayāti then requested Pūru, who was younger than these three brothers but more qualified, "My dear son, do not be disobedient like your elder brothers, for that is not your duty."
