Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.23

tasmai tushto dadāv indrah

śātakaumbhamayam ratham

śunahśephasya māhātmyam

uparishtāt pracakshyate

SYNONYMS

tasmai — unto him, King Hariścandra; tushtah — being very pleased; dadau — delivered; indrah — the King of heaven; śātakaumbha-mayam — made of gold; ratham — a chariot; śunahśephasya — about Śunahśepha; māhātmyam — glories; uparishtāt — in the course of describing the sons of Viśvāmitra; pracakshyate — will be narrated.

TRANSLATION

King Indra, being very pleased with Hariścandra, offered him a gift of a golden chariot. Śunahśepha's glories will be presented along with the description of the son of Viśvāmitra.
