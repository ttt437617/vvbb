Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.9

yadi vīro mahārāja

tenaiva tvām yaje iti

tatheti varunenāsya

putro jātas tu rohitah

SYNONYMS

yadi — if; vīrah — there is a son; mahārāja — O Mahārāja Parīkshit; tena eva — even by that son; tvām — unto you; yaje — I shall offer sacrifice; iti — thus; tathā — as you desire; iti — thus accepted; varunena — by Varuna; asya — of Mahārāja Hariścandra; putrah — a son; jātah — was born; tu — indeed; rohitah — by the name Rohita.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, Hariścandra begged Varuna, "My lord, if a son is born to me, with that son I shall perform a sacrifice for your satisfaction." When Hariścandra said this, Varuna replied, "Let it be so." Because of Varuna's benediction, Hariścandra begot a son named Rohita.
