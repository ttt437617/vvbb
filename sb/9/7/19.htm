Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.19

evam dvitīye tritīye

caturthe pañcame tathā

abhyetyābhyetya sthaviro

vipro bhūtvāha vritra-hā

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; dvitīye — on the second year; tritīye — on the third year; caturthe — on the fourth year; pañcame — on the fifth year; tathā — as well as; abhyetya — coming before him; abhyetya — again coming before him; sthavirah — a very old man; viprah — a brāhmana; bhūtvā — becoming so; āha — said; vritra-hā — Indra.

TRANSLATION

In this way, at the end of the second, third, fourth and fifth years, when Rohita wanted to return to his capital, the King of heaven, Indra, approached him as an old brāhmana and forbade him to return, repeating the same words as in the previous year.
