Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.4

trasaddasyuh paurukutso

yo 'naranyasya deha-krit

haryaśvas tat-sutas tasmāt

prāruno 'tha tribandhanah

SYNONYMS

trasaddasyuh — by the name Trasaddasyu; paurukutsah — the son of Purukutsa; yah — who; anaranyasya — of Anaranya; deha-krit — the father; haryaśvah — by the name Haryaśva; tat-sutah — the son of Anaranya; tasmāt — from him (Haryaśva); prārunah — by the name Prāruna; atha — then, from Prāruna; tribandhanah — his son, Tribandhana.

TRANSLATION

The son of Purukutsa was Trasaddasyu, who was the father of Anaranya. Anaranya's son was Haryaśva, the father of Prāruna. Prāruna was the father of Tribandhana.
