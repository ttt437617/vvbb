Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.16

rohitas tad abhijñāya

pituh karma cikīrshitam

prāna-prepsur dhanush-pānir

aranyam pratyapadyata

SYNONYMS

rohitah — the son of Hariścandra; tat — this fact; abhijñāya — having thoroughly understood; pituh — of his father; karma — action; cikīrshitam — which he was practically doing; prāna-prepsuh — wishing to save his life; dhanuh-pānih — taking his bow and arrows; aranyam — to the forest; pratyapadyata — left.

TRANSLATION

Rohita could understand that his father intended to offer him as the animal for sacrifice. Therefore, just to save himself from death, he equipped himself with bow and arrows and went to the forest.
