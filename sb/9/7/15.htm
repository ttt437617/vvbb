Canto 9: LiberationChapter 7: The Descendants of King Māndhātā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.7.15

iti putrānurāgena

sneha-yantrita-cetasā

kālam vañcayatā tam tam

ukto devas tam aikshata

SYNONYMS

iti — in this way; putra-anurāgena — because of affection for the son; sneha-yantrita-cetasā — his mind being controlled by such affection; kālam — time; vañcayatā — cheating; tam — unto him; tam — that; uktah — said; devah — the demigod Varuna; tam — unto him, Hariścandra; aikshata — waited for the fulfillment of his promise.

TRANSLATION

Hariścandra was certainly very much attached to his son. Because of this affection, he asked the demigod Varuna to wait. Thus Varuna waited and waited for the time to come.
