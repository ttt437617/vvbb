Canto 9: LiberationChapter 12: The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.12.8

tatah prasenajit tasmāt

takshako bhavitā punah

tato brihadbalo yas tu

pitrā te samare hatah

SYNONYMS

tatah — from Viśvabāhu; prasenajit — a son named Prasenajit was born; tasmāt — from him; takshakah — Takshaka; bhavitā — would take birth; punah — again; tatah — from him; brihadbalah — a son named Brihadbala; yah — he who; tu — but; pitrā — by father; te — your; samare — in the fight; hatah — killed.

TRANSLATION

From Viśvabāhu came a son named Prasenajit, from Prasenajit came Takshaka, and from Takshaka came Brihadbala, who was killed in a fight by your father.
