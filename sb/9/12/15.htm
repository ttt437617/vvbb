Canto 9: LiberationChapter 12: The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.12.15

ranako bhavitā tasmāt

surathas tanayas tatah

sumitro nāma nishthānta

ete bārhadbalānvayāh

SYNONYMS

ranakah — Ranaka; bhavitā — will take birth; tasmāt — from Kshudraka; surathah — Suratha; tanayah — the son; tatah — thereafter; sumitrah — Sumitra, the son of Suratha; nāma — by the name; nishtha-antah — the end of the dynasty; ete — all the above-mentioned kings; bārhadbala-anvayāh — in the dynasty of King Brihadbala.

TRANSLATION

From Kshudraka will come Ranaka, from Ranaka will come Suratha, and from Suratha will come Sumitra, ending the dynasty. This is a description of the dynasty of Brihadbala.
