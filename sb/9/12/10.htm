Canto 9: LiberationChapter 12: The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.12.10

ūrukriyah sutas tasya

vatsavriddho bhavishyati

prativyomas tato bhānur

divāko vāhinī-patih

SYNONYMS

ūrukriyah — Ūrukriya; sutah — son; tasya — of Ūrukriya; vatsavriddhah — Vatsavriddha; bhavishyati — will take birth; prativyomah — Prativyoma; tatah — from Vatsavriddha; bhānuh — (from Prativyoma) a son named Bhānu; divākah — from Bhānu a son named Divāka; vāhinī-patih — a great commander of soldiers.

TRANSLATION

The son of Brihadrana will be Ūrukriya, who will have a son named Vatsavriddha. Vatsavriddha will have a son named Prativyoma, and Prativyoma will have a son named Bhānu, from whom Divāka, a great commander of soldiers, will take birth.
