Canto 9: LiberationChapter 12: The Dynasty of Kuśa, the Son of Lord Rāmacandra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.12.9

ete hīkshvāku-bhūpālā

atītāh śrinv anāgatān

brihadbalasya bhavitā

putro nāmnā brihadranah

SYNONYMS

ete — all of them; hi — indeed; ikshvāku-bhūpālāh — kings in the dynasty of Ikshvāku; atītāh — all of them are dead and gone; śrinu — just hear; anāgatān — those who will come in the future; brihadbalasya — of Brihadbala; bhavitā — there will be; putrah — a son; nāmnā — by the name; brihadranah — Brihadrana.

TRANSLATION

All these kings in the dynasty of Ikshvāku have passed away. Now please listen as I describe the kings who will be born in the future. From Brihadbala will come Brihadrana.
