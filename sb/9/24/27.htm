Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.27

devamīdhah śatadhanuh

kritavarmeti tat-sutāh

devamīdhasya śūrasya

mārishā nāma patny abhūt

SYNONYMS

devamīdhah — Devamīdha; śatadhanuh — Śatadhanu; kritavarmā — Kritavarmā; iti — thus; tat-sutāh — the sons of him (Hridika); devamīdhasya — of Devamīdha; śūrasya — of Śūra; mārishā — Mārishā; nāma — named; patnī — wife; abhūt — there was.

TRANSLATION

The three sons of Hridika were Devamīdha, Śatadhanu and Kritavarmā. The son of Devamīdha was Śūra, whose wife was named Mārishā.
