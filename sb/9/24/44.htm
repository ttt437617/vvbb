Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.44

sumitrārjunapālādīn

samīkāt tu sudāmanī

ānakah karnikāyām vai

ritadhāmā-jayāv api

SYNONYMS

sumitra — Sumitra; arjunapāla — Arjunapāla; ādīn — headed by; samīkāt — from King Śamīka; tu — indeed; sudāmanī — in the womb of Sudāmanī, his wife; ānakah — King Ānaka; karnikāyām — in the womb of his wife Karnikā; vai — indeed; ritadhāmā — Ritadhāmā; jayau — and Jaya; api — indeed.

TRANSLATION

From Śamīka, by the womb of his wife, Sudāmanī, came Sumitra, Arjunapāla and other sons. King Ānaka, by his wife, Karnikā, begot two sons, namely Ritadhāmā and Jaya.
