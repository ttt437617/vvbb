Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.50

viprishtho dhritadevāyām

eka ānakadundubheh

śāntidevātmajā rājan

praśama-prasitādayah

SYNONYMS

viprishthah — Viprishtha; dhritadevāyām — in the womb of the wife named Dhritadevā; ekah — one son; ānakadundubheh — of Ānakadundubhi, Vasudeva; śāntidevā-ātmajāh — the sons of another wife, named Śāntidevā; rājan — O Mahārāja Parīkshit; praśama-prasita-ādayah — Praśama, Prasita and other sons.

TRANSLATION

From the womb of Dhritadevā, one of the wives of Ānakadundubhi [Vasudeva], came a son named Viprishtha. The sons of Śāntidevā, another wife of Vasudeva, were Praśama, Prasita and others.
