Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.42

sriñjayo rāshtrapālyām ca

vrisha-durmarshanādikān

harikeśa-hiranyākshau

śūrabhūmyām ca śyāmakah

SYNONYMS

sriñjayah — Sriñjaya; rāshtrapālyām — through his wife, Rāshtrapālikā; ca — and; vrisha-durmarshana-ādikān — begot sons headed by Vrisha and Durmarshana; harikeśa — Harikeśa; hiranyākshau — and Hiranyāksha; śūrabhūmyām — in the womb of Śūrabhūmi; ca — and; śyāmakah — King Śyāmaka.

TRANSLATION

King Sriñjaya, by his wife, Rāshtrapālikā, begot sons headed by Vrisha and Durmarshana. King Śyāmaka, by his wife, Śūrabhūmi, begot two sons, named Harikeśa and Hiranyāksha.
