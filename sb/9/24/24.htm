Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.24

kamsah sunāmā nyagrodhah

kańkah śańkuh suhūs tathā

rāshtrapālo 'tha dhrishtiś ca

tushtimān augrasenayah

SYNONYMS

kamsah — Kamsa; sunāmā — Sunāmā; nyagrodhah — Nyagrodha; kańkah — Kańka; śańkuh — Śańku; suhūh — Suhū; tathā — as well as; rāshtrapālah — Rāshtrapāla; atha — thereafter; dhrishtih — Dhrishti; ca — also; tushtimān — Tushtimān; augrasenayah — the sons of Ugrasena.

TRANSLATION

Kamsa, Sunāmā, Nyagrodha, Kańka, Śańku, Suhū, Rāshtrapāla, Dhrishti and Tushtimān were the sons of Ugrasena.
