Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.19

kukuro bhajamānaś ca

śucih kambalabarhishah

kukurasya suto vahnir

vilomā tanayas tatah

SYNONYMS

kukurah — Kukura; bhajamānah — Bhajamāna; ca — also; śucih — Śuci; kambalabarhishah — Kambalabarhisha; kukurasya — of Kukura; sutah — a son; vahnih — Vahni; vilomā — Vilomā; tanayah — son; tatah — from him (Vahni).

TRANSLATION

Kukura, Bhajamāna, Śuci and Kambalabarhisha were the four sons of Andhaka. The son of Kukura was Vahni, and his son was Vilomā.
