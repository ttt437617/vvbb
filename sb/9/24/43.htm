Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.43

miśrakeśyām apsarasi

vrikādīn vatsakas tathā

taksha-pushkara-śālādīn

durvākshyām vrika ādadhe

SYNONYMS

miśrakeśyām — in the womb of Miśrakeśī; apsarasi — who belonged to the Apsarā group; vrika-ādīn — Vrika and other sons; vatsakah — Vatsaka; tathā — as well; taksha-pushkara-śāla-ādīn — sons headed by Taksha, Pushkara and Śāla; durvākshyām — in the womb of his wife, Durvākshī; vrikah — Vrika; ādadhe — begot.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, King Vatsaka, by the womb of his wife, Miśrakeśī, who was an Apsarā, begot sons headed by Vrika. Vrika, by his wife, Durvākshī, begot Taksha, Pushkara, Śāla and so on.
