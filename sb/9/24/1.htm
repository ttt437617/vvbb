Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

tasyām vidarbho 'janayat

putrau nāmnā kuśa-krathau

tritīyam romapādam ca

vidarbha-kula-nandanam

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; tasyām — in that girl; vidarbhah — the son born of Śaibyā named Vidarbha; ajanayat — gave birth; putrau — to two sons; nāmnā — by the name; kuśa-krathau — Kuśa and Kratha; tritīyam — and a third son; romapādam ca — Romapāda also; vidarbha-kula-nandanam — the favorite in the dynasty of Vidarbha.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: By the womb of the girl brought by his father, Vidarbha begot three sons, named Kuśa, Kratha and Romapāda. Romapāda was the favorite in the dynasty of Vidarbha.
