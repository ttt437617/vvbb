Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.25

kamsā kamsavatī kańkā

śūrabhū rāshtrapālikā

ugrasena-duhitaro

vasudevānuja-striyah

SYNONYMS

kamsā — Kamsā; kamsavatī — Kamsavatī; kańkā — Kańkā; śūrabhū — Śūrabhū; rāshtrapālikā — Rāshtrapālikā; ugrasena-duhitarah — the daughters of Ugrasena; vasudeva-anuja — of the younger brothers of Vasudeva; striyah — the wives.

TRANSLATION

Kamsā, Kamsavatī, Kańkā, Śūrabhū and Rāshtrapālikā were the daughters of Ugrasena. They became the wives of Vasudeva's younger brothers.
