Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.60

karmāny aparimeyāni

manasāpi sureśvaraih

saha-sańkarshanaś cakre

bhagavān madhusūdanah

SYNONYMS

karmāni — activities; aparimeyāni — immeasurable, unlimited; manasā api — even by such plans perceived within the mind; sura-īśvaraih — by the controllers of the universe like Brahmā and Śiva; saha-sańkarshanah — along with Sańkarshana (Baladeva); cakre — performed; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; madhu-sūdanah — the killer of the Madhu demon.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna, with the cooperation of Sańkarshana, Balarāma, performed activities beyond the mental comprehension of even such personalities as Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva. [For instance, Krishna arranged the Battle of Kurukshetra to kill many demons for the relief of the entire world.]
