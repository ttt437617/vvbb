Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.45

pauravī rohinī bhadrā

madirā rocanā ilā

devakī-pramukhāś cāsan

patnya ānakadundubheh

SYNONYMS

pauravī — Pauravī; rohinī — Rohinī; bhadrā — Bhadrā; madirā — Madirā; rocanā — Rocanā; ilā — Ilā; devakī — Devakī; pramukhāh — headed by; ca — and; āsan — existed; patnyah — wives; ānakadundubheh — of Vasudeva, who was known as Ānakadundubhi.

TRANSLATION

Devakī, Pauravī, Rohinī, Bhadrā, Madirā, Rocanā, Ilā and others were all wives of Ānakadundubhi [Vasudeva]. Among them all, Devakī was the chief.
