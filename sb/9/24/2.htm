Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.2

romapāda-suto babhrur

babhroh kritir ajāyata

uśikas tat-sutas tasmāc

cediś caidyādayo nripāh

SYNONYMS

romapāda-sutah — the son of Romapāda; babhruh — Babhru; babhroh — from Babhru; kritih — Kriti; ajāyata — was born; uśikah — Uśika; tat-sutah — the son of Kriti; tasmāt — from him (Uśika); cedih — Cedi; caidya — Caidya (Damaghosha); ādayah — and others; nripāh — kings.

TRANSLATION

The son of Romapāda was Babhru, from whom there came a son named Kriti. The son of Kriti was Uśika, and the son of Uśika was Cedi. From Cedi was born the king known as Caidya and others.
