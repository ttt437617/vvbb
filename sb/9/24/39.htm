Canto 9: LiberationChapter 24: Krishna the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.24.39

rājādhidevyām āvantyau

jayaseno 'janishta ha

damaghoshaś cedi-rājah

śrutaśravasam agrahīt

SYNONYMS

rājādhidevyām — through Rājādhidevī, another sister of Kuntī's; āvantyau — the sons (named Vinda and Anuvinda); jayasenah — King Jayasena; ajanishta — gave birth to; ha — in the past; damaghoshah — Damaghosha; cedi-rājah — the king of the state of Cedi; śrutaśravasam — Śrutaśravā, another sister; agrahīt — married.

TRANSLATION

Through the womb of Rājādhidevī, another sister of Kuntī's, Jayasena begot two sons, named Vinda and Anuvinda. Similarly, the king of the Cedi state married Śrutaśravā. This king's name was Damaghosha.
