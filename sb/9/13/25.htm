Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.25

vasvananto 'tha tat-putro

yuyudho yat subhāshanah

śrutas tato jayas tasmād

vijayo 'smād ritah sutah

SYNONYMS

vasvanantah — Vasvananta; atha — thereafter (the son of Upagupta); tat-putrah — his son; yuyudhah — by the name Yuyudha; yat — from Yuyudha; subhāshanah — a son named Subhāshana; śrutah tatah — and the son of Subhāshana was Śruta; jayah tasmāt — the son of Śruta was Jaya; vijayah — a son named Vijaya; asmāt — from Jaya; ritah — Rita; sutah — a son.

TRANSLATION

The son of Upagupta was Vasvananta, the son of Vasvananta was Yuyudha, the son of Yuyudha was Subhāshana, and the son of Subhāshana was Śruta. The son of Śruta was Jaya, from whom there came Vijaya. The son of Vijaya was Rita.
