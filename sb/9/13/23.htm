Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.23

arishtanemis tasyāpi

śrutāyus tat supārśvakah

tataś citraratho yasya

kshemādhir mithilādhipah

SYNONYMS

arishtanemih — Arishtanemi; tasya api — of Purujit also; śrutāyuh — a son named Śrutāyu; tat — and from him; supārśvakah — Supārśvaka; tatah — from Supārśvaka; citrarathah — Citraratha; yasya — of whom (Citraratha); kshemādhih — Kshemādhi; mithilā-adhipah — became the king of Mithilā.

TRANSLATION

The son of Purujit was Arishtanemi, and his son was Śrutāyu. Śrutāyu begot a son named Supārśvaka, and Supārśvaka begot Citraratha. The son of Citraratha was Kshemādhi, who became the king of Mithilā.
