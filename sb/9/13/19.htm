Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.19

kuśadhvajas tasya putras

tato dharmadhvajo nripah

dharmadhvajasya dvau putrau

kritadhvaja-mitadhvajau

SYNONYMS

kuśadhvajah — Kuśadhvaja; tasya — of Śīradhvaja; putrah — son; tatah — from him; dharmadhvajah — Dharmadhvaja; nripah — the king; dharmadhvajasya — from this Dharmadhvaja; dvau — two; putrau — sons; kritadhvaja-mitadhvajau — Kritadhvaja and Mitadhvaja.

TRANSLATION

The son of Śīradhvaja was Kuśadhvaja, and the son of Kuśadhvaja was King Dharmadhvaja, who had two sons, namely Kritadhvaja and Mitadhvaja.
