Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.26

śunakas tat-suto jajñe

vītahavyo dhritis tatah

bahulāśvo dhrites tasya

kritir asya mahāvaśī

SYNONYMS

śunakah — Śunaka; tat-sutah — the son of Rita; jajñe — was born; vītahavyah — Vītahavya; dhritih — Dhriti; tatah — the son of Vītahavya; bahulāśvah — Bahulāśva; dhriteh — from Dhriti; tasya — his son; kritih — Kriti; asya — of Kriti; mahāvaśī — there was a son named Mahāvaśī.

TRANSLATION

The son of Rita was Śunaka, the son of Śunaka was Vītahavya, the son of Vītahavya was Dhriti, and the son of Dhriti was Bahulāśva. The son of Bahulāśva was Kriti, and his son was Mahāvaśī.
