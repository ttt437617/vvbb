Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.15

tasmād brihadrathas tasya

mahāvīryah sudhrit-pitā

sudhriter dhrishtaketur vai

haryaśvo 'tha marus tatah

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from Devarāta; brihadrathah — a son named Brihadratha; tasya — of him (Brihadratha); mahāvīryah — a son named Mahāvīrya; sudhrit-pitā — he became the father of King Sudhriti; sudhriteh — from Sudhriti; dhrishtaketuh — a son named Dhrishtaketu; vai — indeed; haryaśvah — his son was Haryaśva; atha — thereafter; maruh — Maru; tatah — thereafter.

TRANSLATION

From Devarāta came a son named Brihadratha and from Brihadratha a son named Mahāvīrya, who became the father of Sudhriti. The son of Sudhriti was known as Dhrishtaketu, and from Dhrishtaketu came Haryaśva. From Haryaśva came a son named Maru.
