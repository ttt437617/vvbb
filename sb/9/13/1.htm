Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

nimir ikshvāku-tanayo

vasishtham avritartvijam

ārabhya satram so 'py āha

śakrena prāg vrito 'smi bhoh

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; nimih — King Nimi; ikshvāku-tanayah — the son of Mahārāja Ikshvāku; vasishtham — the great sage Vasishtha; avrita — appointed; ritvijam — the chief priest of the sacrifice; ārabhya — beginning; satram — the sacrifice; sah — he, Vasishtha; api — also; āha — said; śakrena — by Lord Indra; prāk — before; vritah asmi — I was appointed; bhoh — O Mahārāja Nimi.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After beginning sacrifices, Mahārāja Nimi, the son of Ikshvāku, requested the great sage Vasishtha to take the post of chief priest. At that time, Vasishtha replied, "My dear Mahārāja Nimi, I have already accepted the same post in a sacrifice begun by Lord Indra.
