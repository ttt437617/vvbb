Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.14

tasmād udāvasus tasya

putro 'bhūn nandivardhanah

tatah suketus tasyāpi

devarāto mahīpate

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from Mithila; udāvasuh — a son named Udāvasu; tasya — of him (Udāvasu); putrah — son; abhūt — was born; nandivardhanah — Nandivardhana; tatah — from him (Nandivardhana); suketuh — a son named Suketu; tasya — of him (Suketu); api — also; devarātah — a son named Devarāta; mahīpate — O King Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, from Mithila came a son named Udāvasu; from Udāvasu, Nandivardhana; from Nandivardhana, Suketu; and from Suketu, Devarāta.
