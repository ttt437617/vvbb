Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.16

maroh pratīpakas tasmāj

jātah kritaratho yatah

devamīdhas tasya putro

viśruto 'tha mahādhritih

SYNONYMS

maroh — of Maru; pratīpakah — a son named Pratīpaka; tasmāt — from Pratīpaka; jātah — was born; kritarathah — a son named Kritaratha; yatah — and from Kritaratha; devamīdhah — Devamīdha; tasya — of Devamīdha; putrah — a son; viśrutah — Viśruta; atha — from him; mahādhritih — a son named Mahādhriti.

TRANSLATION

The son of Maru was Pratīpaka, and the son of Pratīpaka was Kritaratha. From Kritaratha came Devamīdha; from Devamīdha, Viśruta; and from Viśruta, Mahādhriti.
