Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.2

tam nirvartyāgamishyāmi

tāvan mām pratipālaya

tūshnīm āsīd griha-patih

so 'pīndrasyākaron makham

SYNONYMS

tam — that sacrifice; nirvartya — after finishing; āgamishyāmi — I shall come back; tāvat — until that time; mām — me (Vasishtha); pratipālaya — wait for; tūshnīm — silent; āsīt — remained; griha-patih — Mahārāja Nimi; sah — he, Vasishtha; api — also; indrasya — of Lord Indra; akarot — executed; makham — the sacrifice.

TRANSLATION

"I shall return here after finishing the yajña for Indra. Kindly wait for me until then." Mahārāja Nimi remained silent, and Vasishtha began to perform the sacrifice for Lord Indra.
