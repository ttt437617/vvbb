Canto 9: LiberationChapter 13: The Dynasty of Mahārāja Nimi

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 9.13.17

kritirātas tatas tasmān

mahāromā ca tat-sutah

svarnaromā sutas tasya

hrasvaromā vyajāyata

SYNONYMS

kritirātah — Kritirāta; tatah — from Mahādhriti; tasmāt — from Kritirāta; mahāromā — a son named Mahāromā; ca — also; tat-sutah — his son; svarnaromā — Svarnaromā; sutah tasya — his son; hrasvaromā — Hrasvaromā; vyajāyata — were all born.

TRANSLATION

From Mahādhriti was born a son named Kritirāta, from Kritirāta was born Mahāromā, from Mahāromā came a son named Svarnaromā, and from Svarnaromā came Hrasvaromā.
