Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 23: The Brāhmanas' Wives Blessed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.23.18

śrutvācyutam upāyātam

nityam tad-darśanotsukāh

tat-kathākshipta-manaso

babhūvur jāta-sambhramāh

SYNONYMS

śrutvā — hearing; acyutam — Lord Krishna; upāyātam — come nearby; nityam — constantly; tat-darśana — for the sight of Him; utsukāh — eager; tat-kathā — by descriptions of Him; ākshpita — enchanted; manasah — their minds; babhūvuh — they became; jāta-sambhramāh — excited.

TRANSLATION

The wives of the brāhmanas were always eager to see Krishna, for their minds had been enchanted by descriptions of Him. Thus as soon as they heard that He had come, they became very excited.
