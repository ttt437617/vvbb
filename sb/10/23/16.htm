Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 23: The Brāhmanas' Wives Blessed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.23.16

namo vo vipra-patnībhyo

nibodhata vacāmsi nah

ito 'vidūre caratā

krishneneheshitā vayam

SYNONYMS

namah — obeisances; vah — unto you; vipra-patnībhyah — the wives of the brāhmanas; nibodhata — please hear; vacāmsi — words; nah — our; itah — from here; avidūre — not distant; caratā — who is going; krishnena — by Lord Krishna; iha — here; ishitāh — sent; vayam — we.

TRANSLATION

[The cowherd boys said:] Obeisances unto you, O wives of the learned brāhmanas. Kindly hear our words. We have been sent here by Lord Krishna, who is passing by not far from here.
