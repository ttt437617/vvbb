Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 23: The Brāhmanas' Wives Blessed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.23.3

prayāta deva-yajanam

brāhmanā brahma-vādinah

satram āńgirasam nāma

hy āsate svarga-kāmyayā

SYNONYMS

prayāta — please go; deva-yajanam — to the sacrificial arena; brāhmanāh — brāhmanas; brahma-vādinah — followers of the Vedic injunctions; satram — a sacrifice; āńgirasam nāma — known as Āńgirasa; hi — indeed; āsate — they are now performing; svarga-kāmyayā — with the motive of promotion to heaven.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:] Please go to the sacrificial arena where a group of brāhmanas, learned in the Vedic injunctions, are now performing the Āńgirasa sacrifice to gain promotion to heaven.
