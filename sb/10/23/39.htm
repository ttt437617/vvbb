Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 23: The Brāhmanas' Wives Blessed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.23.39

drishtvā strīnām bhagavati

krishne bhaktim alaukikīm

ātmānam ca tayā hīnam

anutaptā vyagarhayan

SYNONYMS

drishtvā — observing; strīnām — of their wives; bhagavati — for the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishne — Śrī Krishna; bhaktim — the pure devotion; alaukikīm — transcendental to this world; ātmānam — themselves; ca — and; tayā — of that; hīnam — devoid; anutaptāh — lamenting; vyagarhayan — they condemned.

TRANSLATION

Taking note of their wives' pure, transcendental devotion for Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and seeing their own lack of devotion, the brāhmanas felt most sorrowful and began to condemn themselves.
