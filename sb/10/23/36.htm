Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 23: The Brāhmanas' Wives Blessed

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.23.36

bhagavān api govindas

tenaivānnena gopakān

catur-vidhenāśayitvā

svayam ca bubhuje prabhuh

SYNONYMS

bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; api — moreover; govindah — Lord Govinda; tena — with that; eva — very same; annena — food; gopakān — the cowherd boys; catuh-vidhena — of four varieties; aśayitvā — feeding; svayam — Himself; ca — and; bubhuje — partook; prabhuh — the Almighty.

TRANSLATION

Govinda, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, fed the cowherd boys with that food of four varieties. Then the all-powerful Lord Himself partook of the preparations.
