Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 88: Lord Śiva Saved from Vrikāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.88.6

nivritteshv aśva-medheshu

rājā yushmat-pitāmahah

śrinvan bhagavato dharmān

apricchad idam acyutam

SYNONYMS

nivritteshu — when they were completed; aśva-medheshu — his performances of the horse sacrifice; rājā — the King (Yudhishthira); yushmat — your (Parīkshit's); pitāmahāh — grandfather; śrinvan — while hearing; bhagavatah — from the Supreme Lord (Krishna); dharmān — religious principles; apricchat — he asked; idam — this; acyutam — of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Your grandfather, King Yudhishthira, after completing his Aśvamedha sacrifices, asked Lord Acyuta this very same question while hearing the Lord's explanation of religious principles.
