Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 88: Lord Śiva Saved from Vrikāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.88.30

yadi nah śravanāyālam

yushmad-vyavasitam vibho

bhanyatām prāyaśah pumbhir

dhritaih svārthān samīhate

SYNONYMS

yadi — if; nah — Our; śravanāya — for the hearing; alam — suitable; yushmat — your; vyavasitam — intention; vibho — O powerful one; bhanyatām — please tell; prāyaśah — usually; pumbhih — with persons; dhritaih — taken help of; sva — one's own; arthān — purposes; samīhate — one accomplishes.

TRANSLATION

O mighty one, please tell Us what you intend to do, if We are qualified to hear it. Usually one accomplishes his purposes by taking help from others.

PURPORT

Even an envious demon will not refuse the help of a brāhmana's potency to gain his ends.
