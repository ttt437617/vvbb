Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 88: Lord Śiva Saved from Vrikāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.88.7

sa āha bhagavāms tasmai

prītah śuśrūshave prabhuh

nrinām nihśreyasārthāya

yo 'vatīrno yadoh kule

SYNONYMS

sah — He; āha — said; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; tasmai — to him; prītah — pleased; śuśrūshave — who was eager to hear; prabhuh — his master; nrinām — of all men; nihśreyasa — of ultimate benefit; arthāya — for the sake; yah — who; avatīrnah — descended; yadoh — of King Yadu; kule — in the family.

TRANSLATION

This question pleased Śrī Krishna, the King's Lord and master, who had descended into the family of Yadu for the purpose of bestowing the highest good on all men. The Lord replied as follows as the King eagerly listened.
