Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 88: Lord Śiva Saved from Vrikāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.88.9

sa yadā vitathodyogo

nirvinnah syād dhanehayā

mat-paraih krita-maitrasya

karishye mad-anugraham

SYNONYMS

sah — he; yadā — when; vitatha — useless; udyogah — his attempt; nirvinnah — frustrated; syāt — becomes; dhana — for money; īhayā — with his endeavor; mat — to Me; paraih — with those who are devoted; krita — for him who has made; maitrasya — friendship; karishye — I will show; mat — My; anugraham — mercy.

TRANSLATION

When he becomes frustrated in his attempts to make money and instead befriends My devotees, I bestow My special mercy upon him.
