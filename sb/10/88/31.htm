Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 88: Lord Śiva Saved from Vrikāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.88.31

śrī-śuka uvāca

evam bhagavatā prishto

vacasāmrita-varshinā

gata-klamo 'bravīt tasmai

yathā-pūrvam anushthitam

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Lord; prishtah — questioned; vacasā — with words; amrita — nectar; varshinā — which rained; gata — gone; klamah — his fatigue; abravīt — he said; tasmai — to Him; yathā — as; pūrvam — before; anushthitam — executed.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thus questioned by the Personality of Godhead in language that poured down upon him like sweet nectar, Vrika felt relieved of his fatigue. He described to the Lord everything he had done.
