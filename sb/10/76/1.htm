Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

athānyad api krishnasya

śrinu karmādbhutam nripa

krīdā-nara-śarīrasya

yathā saubha-patir hatah

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; atha — now; anyat — another; api — yet; krishnasya — of Lord Krishna; śrinu — please hear; karma — deed; adbhutam — wonderful; nripa — O King; krīdā — for playing; nara — humanlike; śarīrasya — whose body; yathā — how; saubha-patih — the lord of Saubha (Śālva); hatah — killed.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Now please hear, O King, another wondrous deed performed by Lord Krishna, who appeared in His humanlike body to enjoy transcendental pastimes. Hear how He killed the master of Saubha.
