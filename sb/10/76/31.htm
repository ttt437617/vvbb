Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.31

vyaktam me kathayishyanti

hasantyo bhrātri-jāmayah

klaibyam katham katham vīra

tavānyaih kathyatām mridhe

SYNONYMS

vyaktam — certainly; me — My; kathayishyanti — will speak of; hasantyah — laughing; bhrātri-jāmayah — My brothers' wives; klaibyam — unmanliness; katham — how; katham — how; vīra — O hero; tava — Your; anyaih — by Your enemies; kathyatām — tell us; mridhe — in battle.

TRANSLATION

"Certainly My sisters-in-law will laugh at Me and say, 'O hero, tell us how in the world Your enemies turned You into such a coward in battle.' "
