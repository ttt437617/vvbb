Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.3

śālvah pratijñām akaroc

chrinvatām sarva-bhūbhujām

ayādavām kshmām karishye

paurusham mama paśyata

SYNONYMS

śālvah — Śālva; pratijñām — a promise; akarot — made; śrinvatām — as they listened; sarva — all; bhū-bhujām — the kings; ayādavām — devoid of Yādavas; kshmām — the earth; karishye — I will make; paurusham — prowess; mama — my; paśyata — just see.

TRANSLATION

Śālva swore in the presence of all the kings: "I will rid the earth of Yādavas. Just see my prowess!"
