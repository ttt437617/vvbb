Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.16

tatah pravavrite yuddham

śālvānām yadubhih saha

yathāsurānām vibudhais

tumulam loma-harshanam

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; pravavrite — began; yuddham — a battle; śālvānām — of the followers of Śālva; yadubhih saha — with the Yadus; yathā — just like; asurānām — of the demons; vibudhaih — with the demigods; tumulam — tumultuous; loma-harshanam — making bodily hair stand on end.

TRANSLATION

A tumultuous, hair-raising battle then commenced between Śālva's forces and the Yadus. It equaled the great battles between the demons and demigods.
