Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.8

sa labdhvā kāma-gam yānam

tamo-dhāma durāsadam

yayas dvāravatīm śālvo

vairam vrishni-kritam smaran

SYNONYMS

sah — he; labdhvā — obtaining; kāma-gam — moving at his will; yānam — the vehicle; tamah — of darkness; dhāma — abode; durāsadam — unapproachable; yayau — went; dvāravatīm — to Dvārakā; śālvah — Śālva; vairam — the enmity; vrishni-kritam — shown by the Vrishnis; smaran — remembering.

TRANSLATION

This unassailable vehicle was filled with darkness and could go anywhere. Upon obtaining it, Śālva went to Dvārakā, remembering the Vrishnis' enmity toward him.
