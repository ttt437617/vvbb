Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.33

etad viditvā tu bhavān

mayāpovāhito ranāt

upasrishtah pareneti

mūrcchito gadayā hatah

SYNONYMS

etat — this; viditvā — knowing; tu — indeed; bhavān — You; mayā — by me; apovāhitah — removed; ranāt — from the battlefield; upasrishtah — hurt; parena — by the enemy; iti — thus thinking; mūrcchitah — unconscious; gadayā — by his club; hatah — struck.

TRANSLATION

With this rule in mind, I removed You from the battlefield, since You had been struck unconscious by Your enemy's club and I thought You were seriously injured.

Thus end the purports of the humble servants of His Divine Grace A. C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupāda to the Tenth Canto, Seventy-sixth Chapter, of the Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, entitled "The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis."
