Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 76: The Battle Between Śālva and the Vrishnis

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.76.6

devāsura-manushyānām

gandharvoraga-rakshasām

abhedyam kāma-gam vavre

sa yānam vrishni-bhīshanam

SYNONYMS

deva — by demigods; asura — demons; manushyānām — and humans; gandharva — by Gandharvas; uraga — celestial serpents; rakshasām — and Rākshasa spirits; abhedyam — indestructible; kāma — at will; gam — traveling; vavre — chose; sah — he; yānam — a vehicle; vrishni — for the Vrishnis; bhīshanam — terrifying.

TRANSLATION

Śālva chose a vehicle that could be destroyed by neither demigods, demons, humans, Gandharvas, Uragas nor Rākshasas, that could travel anywhere he wished to go, and that would terrify the Vrishnis.
