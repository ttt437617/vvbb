Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 77: Lord Krishna Slays the Demon Śālva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.77.16

hāhā-kāro mahān āsīd

bhūtānām tatra paśyatām

ninadya saubha-rād uccair

idam āha janārdanam

SYNONYMS

hāhā-kārah — a cry of dismay; mahān — great; āsīt — arose; bhūtānām — among the living beings; tatra — there; paśyatām — who were witnessing; ninadya — roaring; saubha-rāt — the lord of Saubha; uccaih — loudly; idam — this; āha — said; janārdanam — to Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Those who witnessed this all cried out in dismay. Then the master of Saubha roared loudly and addressed Lord Janārdana.
