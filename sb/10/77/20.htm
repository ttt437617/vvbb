Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 77: Lord Krishna Slays the Demon Śālva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.77.20

ity uktvā bhagavāñ chālvam

gadayā bhīma-vegayā

tatāda jatrau samrabdhah

sa cakampe vamann asrik

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; uktvā — speaking; bhagavān — the Lord; śālvam — Śālva; gadayā — with His club; bhīma — fearsome; vegayā — whose force and speed; tatāda — struck; jatrau — on the collarbone; samrabdhah — infuriated; sah — he; cakampe — trembled; vaman — vomiting; asrik — blood.

TRANSLATION

Having said this, the furious Lord swung His club with frightening power and speed and hit Śālva on the collarbone, making him tremble and vomit blood.
