Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 57: Satrājit Murdered, the Jewel Returned

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.57.14

pratyākhyātah sa cākrūram

pārshni-grāham ayācata

so 'py āha ko virudhyeta

vidvān īśvarayor balam

SYNONYMS

pratyākhyātah — refused; sah — he, Śatadhanvā; ca — and; akrūram — Akrūra; pārshni-grāham — for help; ayācata — begged; sah — he, Akrūra; api — also; āha — said; kah — who; virudhyeta — can stand against; vidvān — knowing; īśvarayoh — of the two Personalities of Godhead; balam — the strength.

TRANSLATION

His appeal refused, Śatadhanvā went to Akrūra and begged him for protection. But Akrūra similarly told him, "Who would oppose the two Personalities of Godhead if he knew Their strength?
