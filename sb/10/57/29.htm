Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 57: Satrājit Murdered, the Jewel Returned

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.57.29

akrūrah kritavarmā ca

śrutvā śatadhanor vadham

vyūshatur bhaya-vitrastau

dvārakāyāh prayojakau

SYNONYMS

akrūrah kritavarmā ca — Akrūra and Kritavarmā; śrutvā — hearing about; śatadhanoh — of Śatadhanvā; vadham — the killing; vyūshatuh — they went into exile; bhaya-vitrastau — seized with overwhelming fear; dvārakāyāh — from Dvārakā; prayojakau — the engagers.

TRANSLATION

When Akrūra and Kritavarmā, who had originally incited Śatadhanvā to commit his crime, heard that he had been killed, they fled Dvārakā in terror and took up residence elsewhere.
