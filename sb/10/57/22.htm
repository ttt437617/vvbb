Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 57: Satrājit Murdered, the Jewel Returned

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.57.22

alabdha-manir āgatya

krishna āhāgrajāntikam

vrithā hatah śatadhanur

manis tatra na vidyate

SYNONYMS

alabdha — not finding; manih — the gem; āgatya — approaching; krishnah — Lord Krishna; āha — said; agra-ja — of His elder brother; antikam — to the proximity; vrithā — uselessly; hatah — killed; śatadhanuh — Śatadhanvā; manih — the jewel; tatra — with him; na vidyate — is not present.

TRANSLATION

Not finding the jewel, Lord Krishna went to His elder brother and said, "We have killed Śatadhanvā uselessly. The jewel isn't here."
