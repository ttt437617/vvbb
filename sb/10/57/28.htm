Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 57: Satrājit Murdered, the Jewel Returned

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.57.28

tatah sa kārayām āsa

kriyā bandhor hatasya vai

sākam suhridbhir bhagavān

yā yāh syuh sāmparāyikīh

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; sah — He, Lord Krishna; kārayām āsa — had done; kriyā — the ritual duties; bandhoh — for His relative (Satrājit); hatasya — killed; vai — indeed; sākam — together with; suhridbhih — well-wishers; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; yāh yāh — all which; syuh — there are; sāmparāyikīh — for the time of one's departure from this world.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna then had the various funeral rites performed for His deceased relative, Satrājit. The Lord attended the funeral along with well-wishers of the family.
