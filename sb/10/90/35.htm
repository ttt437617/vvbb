Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.35

eteshām api rājendra

tanu-jānām madhu-dvishah

pradyumna āsīt prathamah

pitri-vad rukminī-sutah

SYNONYMS

eteshām — of these; api — and; rāja-indra — O most eminent of kings; tanu-jānām — sons; madhu-dvishah — of Krishna, enemy of the demon Madhu; pradyumnah — Pradyumna; āsīt — was; prathamah — first; pitrivat — just like His father; rukminī-sutah — son of Rukminī.

TRANSLATION

O best of kings, of these sons begotten by Lord Krishna, the enemy of Madhu, the most prominent was Rukminī's son Pradyumna. He was just like His father.
