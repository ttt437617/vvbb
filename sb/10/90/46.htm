Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.46

śayyāsanātanālāpa-

krīdā-snānādi-karmasu

na viduh santam ātmānam

vrishnayah krishna-cetasah

SYNONYMS

śayyā — of sleeping; āsana — sitting; atana — walking; ālāpa — conversing; krīda — playing; snāna — bathing; ādi — and so on; karmasu — in the activities; na viduh — they were not aware of; santam — present; ātmānam — their own selves; vrishnayah — the Vrishnis; krishna — (absorbed) in Krishna; cetasah — whose minds.

TRANSLATION

The Vrishnis were so absorbed in Krishna consciousness that they forgot their own bodies while sleeping, sitting, walking, conversing, playing, bathing and so on.
