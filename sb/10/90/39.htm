Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.39

na hy etasmin kule jātā

adhanā abahu-prajāh

alpāyusho 'lpa-vīryāś ca

abrahmanyāś ca jajñire

SYNONYMS

na — not; hi — indeed; etasmin — in this; kule — family; jātāh — appearing; adhanah — poor; a-bahu — not having many; prajāh — children; alpa-āyushah — short-lived; alpa — small; vīryāh — whose prowess; ca — and; abrahmanyāh — not devoted to the brahminical class; ca — and; jajñire — were born.

TRANSLATION

No one born in this family was poor in wealth or progeny, short-lived, weak or neglectful of brahminical culture.
