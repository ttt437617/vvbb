Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.38

pratibāhur abhūt tasmāt

subāhus tasya cātmajah

subāhoh śāntaseno 'bhūc

chatasenas tu tat-sutah

SYNONYMS

prati-bāhuh — Pratibāhu; abhūt — came; tasmāt — from him (Vajra); subāhuh — Subāhu; tasya — his; ca — and; ātma-jah — son; su-bāhoh — from Subāhu; śānta-senah — Śāntasena; abhūt — came; śata-senah — Śatasena; tu — and; tat — his (Śāntasena's); sutah — son.

TRANSLATION

From Vajra came Pratibāhu, whose son was Subāhu. Subāhu's son was Śāntasena, from whom Śatasena was born.
