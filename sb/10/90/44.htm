Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.44

tan-nigrahāya harinā

proktā devā yadoh kule

avatīrnāh kula-śatam

teshām ekādhikam nripa

SYNONYMS

tat — of them; nigrahāya — for the subduing; harinā — by Lord Krishna; proktāh — told; devāh — the demigods; yadoh — of Yadu; kule — in the family; avatīrnāh — descended; kula — of clans; śatam — one hundred; teshām — their; eka-adhikam — plus one; nripa — O King (Parīkshit).

TRANSLATION

To subdue these demons, Lord Hari told the demigods to descend into the dynasty of Yadu. They comprised 101 clans, O King.
