Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.45

teshām pramānam bhagavān

prabhutvenābhavad dharih

ye cānuvartinas tasya

vavridhuh sarva-yādavāh

SYNONYMS

teshām — for them; pramānam — authority; bhagavān — Lord Krishna; prabhutvena — on account of His being the Supreme Personality of Godhead; abhavat — was; harih — Lord Hari; ye — they who; ca — and; anuvartinah — personal associates; tasya — His; vavridhuh — prospered; sarva — all; yādavāh — the Yādavas.

TRANSLATION

Because Lord Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Yādavas accepted Him as their ultimate authority. And among them, all those who were His intimate associates especially flourished.
