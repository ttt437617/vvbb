Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.43

devāsurāhava-hatā

daiteyā ye su-dārunāh

te cotpannā manushyeshu

prajā driptā babādhire

SYNONYMS

deva-asura — among the demigods and demons; āhava — in wars; hatāh — killed; daiteyāh — demons; ye — who; su — very; dārunāh — ferocious; te — they; ca — and; utpannāh — arose; manushyeshu — among human beings; prajāh — the populace; driptāh — arrogant; babādhire — they troubled.

TRANSLATION

The savage descendants of Diti who had been killed in past ages in battles between the demigods and demons took birth among human beings and arrogantly harassed the general populace.
