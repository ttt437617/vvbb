Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.26

śruta-mātro 'pi yah strīnām

prasahyākarshate manah

uru-gāyoru-gīto vā

paśyantīnām ca kim punah

SYNONYMS

śruta — heard about; mātrah — merely; api — even; yah — who (Lord Krishna); strīnām — of women; prasahya — by force; ākarshate — attracts; manah — the minds; uru — numerous; gāya — by songs; uru — in numerous ways; gītah — sung about; vā — on the other hand; paśyantīnām — of those women who see him; ca — and; kim — what; punah — more.

TRANSLATION

The Lord, whom countless songs glorify in countless ways, forcibly attracts the minds of all women who simply hear about Him. What to speak, then, of those women who see Him directly?
