Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.31

ekaikasyām daśa daśa

krishno 'jījanad ātmajān

yāvatya ātmano bhāryā

amogha-gatir īśvarah

SYNONYMS

eka-ekasyām — in each one of them; daśa daśa — ten each; krishnah — Krishna; ajījanat — begot; ātma-jān — sons; yāvatyah — as many as; ātmanah — His; bhāryāh — wives; amogha — never frustrated; gatih — whose effort; īśvarah — the Supreme Lord.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Lord Krishna, whose endeavor never fails, begot ten sons in each of His many wives.

PURPORT

The total number of Lord Krishna's sons was thus 161,080, and He also had a daughter by each wife.
