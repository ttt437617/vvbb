Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 90: Summary of Lord Krishna's Glories

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.90.32

teshām uddāma-vīryānām

ashtā-daśa mahā-rathāh

āsann udāra-yaśasas

teshām nāmāni me śrinu

SYNONYMS

teshām — of these (sons); uddāma — unlimited; vīryānām — whose prowess; ashtā-daśa — eighteen; mahā-rathāh — mahā-rathas, the highest class of chariot warriors; āsan — were; udāra — widespread; yaśasah — whose fame; teshām — their; nāmāni — names; me — from me; śrinu — hear.

TRANSLATION

Among these sons, all possessing unlimited valor, eighteen were mahā-rathas of great renown. Now hear their names from me.
