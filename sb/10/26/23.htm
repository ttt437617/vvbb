Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 26: Wonderful Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.26.23

ity addhā mām samādiśya

garge ca sva-griham gate

manye nārāyanasyāmśam

krishnam aklishta-kārinam

SYNONYMS

iti — thus speaking; addhā — directly; mām — me; samādiśya — advising; garge — Gargācārya; ca — and; sva-griham — to his home; gate — going; manye — I consider; nārāyanasya — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana; amśam — an empowered expansion; krishnam — Krishna; aklishta-kārinam — who keeps us free from misery.

TRANSLATION

[Nanda Mahārāja continued:] After Garga Rishi spoke these words to me and returned home, I began to consider that Krishna, who keeps us free from trouble, is actually an expansion of Lord Nārāyana.
