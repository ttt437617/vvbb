Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 26: Wonderful Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.26.9

vatseshu vatsa-rūpena

praviśantam jighāmsayā

hatvā nyapātayat tena

kapitthāni ca līlayā

SYNONYMS

vatseshu — among the calves; vatsa-rūpena — appearing as if another calf; praviśantam — who had entered; jighāmsayā — wanting to kill; hatvā — killing him; nyapātayat — He made to fall; tena — by him; kapitthāni — the kapittha fruits; ca — and; līlayā — as a sport.

TRANSLATION

Desiring to kill Krishna, the demon Vatsa disguised himself as a calf and entered among Krishna's calves. But Krishna killed the demon and, using his body, enjoyed the sport of knocking kapittha fruits down from the trees.
