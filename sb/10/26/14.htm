Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 26: Wonderful Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.26.14

kva sapta-hāyano bālah

kva mahādri-vidhāranam

tato no jāyate śańkā

vraja-nātha tavātmaje

SYNONYMS

kva — where, in comparison; sapta-hāyanah — seven years old; bālah — this boy; kva — where; mahā-adri — of the great mountain; vidhāranam — the lifting; tatah — thus; nah — for us; jāyate — arises; śańkā — doubt; vraja-nātha — O master of Vraja; tava — your; ātmaje — concerning the son.

TRANSLATION

On the one hand this boy is only seven years old, and on the other we see that He has lifted the great hill Govardhana. Therefore, O King of Vraja, a doubt about your son arises within us.
