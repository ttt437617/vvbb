Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 26: Wonderful Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.26.3

yah sapta-hāyano bālah

karenaikena līlayā

katham bibhrad giri-varam

pushkaram gaja-rād iva

SYNONYMS

yah — who; sapta-hāyanah — seven years of age; bālah — a boy; karena — with a hand; ekena — one; līlayā — playfully; katham — how; bibhrat — He held up; giri-varam — the best of mountains, Govardhana; pushkaram — a lotus flower; gaja-rāt — a mighty elephant; iva — as.

TRANSLATION

How could this seven-year-old boy playfully hold up the great hill Govardhana with one hand, just as a mighty elephant holds up a lotus flower?
