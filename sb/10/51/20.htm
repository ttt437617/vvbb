Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.20

varam vrinīshva bhadram te

rite kaivalyam adya nah

eka eveśvaras tasya

bhagavān vishnur avyayah

SYNONYMS

varam — a benediction; vrinīshva — choose; bhadram — all good; te — unto you; rite — except; kaivalyam — liberation; adya — today; nah — from us; ekah — one; eva — only; īśvarah — capable; tasya — of that; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; vishnuh — Śrī Vishnu; avyayah — the inexhaustible.

TRANSLATION

"All good fortune to you! Now please choose a benediction from us — anything but liberation, since only the infallible Supreme Lord, Vishnu, can bestow that."
