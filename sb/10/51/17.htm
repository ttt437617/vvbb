Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.17

nara-lokam parityajya

rājyam nihata-kantakam

asmān pālayato vīra

kāmās te sarva ujjhitāh

SYNONYMS

nara-lokam — in the world of men; parityajya — abandoning; rājyam — a kingdom; nihata — removed; kantakam — whose thorns; asmān — us; pālayatah — who was protecting; vīra — O hero; kāmah — desires; te — your; sarve — all; ujjhitāh — thrown away.

TRANSLATION

"Abandoning an unopposed kingdom in the world of men, O valiant one, you neglected all your personal desires while engaged in protecting us.
