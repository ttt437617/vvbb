Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.37

kvacid rajāmsi vimame

pārthivāny uru-janmabhih

guna-karmābhidhānāni

na me janmāni karhicit

SYNONYMS

kvacit — at some time; rajāmsi — the particles of dust; vimame — one might count; pārthivāni — on the earth; uru-janmabhih — in many lifetimes; guna — qualities; karma — activities; abhidhānāni — and names; na — not; me — My; janmāni — births; karhicit — ever.

TRANSLATION

After many lifetimes someone might count the dust particles on the earth, but no one can ever finish counting My qualities, activities, names and births.
