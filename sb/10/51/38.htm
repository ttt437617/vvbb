Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.38

kāla-trayopapannāni

janma-karmāni me nripa

anukramanto naivāntam

gacchanti paramarshayah

SYNONYMS

kāla — of time; traya — in three phases (past, present and future); upapannāni — occurring; janma — births; karmāni — and activities; me — My; nripa — O King (Mucukunda); anukramantah — enumerating; na — not; eva — at all; antam — the end; gacchanti — reach; parama — the greatest; rishayah — sages.

TRANSLATION

O King, the greatest sages enumerate My births and activities, which take place throughout the three phases of time, but never do they reach the end of them.
