Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.33

so 'pi bhasmī-krito nūnam

ātmīyenaiva pāpmanā

anantaram bhavān śrīmān

lakshito 'mitra-śāsanah

SYNONYMS

sah api — that very person; bhasmī-kritah — turned to ashes; nūnam — indeed; ātmīyena — by his own; eva — only; pāpmanā — sinful karma; anantaram — immediately following; bhavān — Your good self; śrīmān — glorious; lakshitah — observed; amitra — of enemies; śāsanah — the chastiser.

TRANSLATION

The man who woke me was burned to ashes by the reaction of his sins. Just then I saw You, possessing a glorious appearance and the power to chastise Your enemies.

PURPORT

Kālayavana had declared himself the enemy of Śrī Krishna and the Yadu dynasty. Through Mucukunda, Śrī Krishna destroyed the opposition of that foolish barbarian.
