Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.16

labdhvā guham te svah-pālam

mucukundam athābruvan

rājan viramatām kricchrād

bhavān nah paripālanāt

SYNONYMS

labdhvā — after obtaining; guham — Kārttikeya; te — they; svah — of heaven; pālam — as the protector; mucukundam — to Mucukunda; atha — then; abruvan — said; rājan — O King; viramatām — please desist; kricchrāt — troublesome; bhavān — your good self; nah — our; paripālanāt — from the guarding.

TRANSLATION

When the demigods obtained Kārttikeya as their general, they told Mucukunda, "O King, you may now give up your troublesome duty of guarding us.
