Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 51: The Deliverance of Mucukunda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.51.34

tejasā te 'vishahyena

bhūri drashtum na śaknumah

hataujasā mahā-bhāga

mānanīyo 'si dehinām

SYNONYMS

tejasā — because of the effulgence; te — Your; avishahyena — unbearable; bhūri — much; drashtum — to see; na śaknumah — we are not able; hata — diminished; ojasā — with our faculties; mahā-bhaga — O most opulent one; mānanīyah — to be honored; asi — You are; dehinam — by embodied beings.

TRANSLATION

Your unbearably brilliant effulgence overwhelms our strength, and thus we cannot fix our gaze upon You. O exalted one, You are to be honored by all embodied beings.
