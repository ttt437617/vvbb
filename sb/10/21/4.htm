Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 21: The Gopīs Glorify the Song of Krishna's Flute

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.21.4

tad varnayitum ārabdhāh

smarantyah krishna-ceshtitam

nāśakan smara-vegena

vikshipta-manaso nripa

SYNONYMS

tat — that; varnayitum — to describe; ārabdhāh — beginning; smarantyah — remembering; krishna-ceshtitam — the activities of Krishna; na aśakan — they were incapable; smara-vegena — by the force of Cupid; vikshipta — agitated; manasah — whose minds; nripa — O King Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

The cowherd girls began to speak about Krishna, but when they remembered His activities, O King, the power of Cupid disturbed their minds, and thus they could not speak.
