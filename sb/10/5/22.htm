Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 5: The Meeting of Nanda Mahārāja and Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.5.22

pūjitah sukham āsīnah

prishtvānāmayam ādritah

prasakta-dhīh svātmajayor

idam āha viśāmpate

SYNONYMS

pūjitah — Vasudeva having been so dearly welcomed; sukham āsīnah — having been given a place to sit comfortably; prishtvā — asking; anāmayam — all-auspicious inquiries; ādritah — being honored and respectfully received; prasakta-dhīh — because of his being very much attached; sva-ātmajayoh — to his own two sons, Krishna and Balarāma; idam — the following; āha — inquired; viśām-pate — O Mahārāja Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

O Mahārāja Parīkshit, having thus been received and welcomed by Nanda Mahārāja with honor, Vasudeva sat down very peacefully and inquired about his own two sons because of intense love for them.
