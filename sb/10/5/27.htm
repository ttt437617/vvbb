Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 5: The Meeting of Nanda Mahārāja and Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.5.27

bhrātar mama sutah kaccin

mātrā saha bhavad-vraje

tātam bhavantam manvāno

bhavadbhyām upalālitah

SYNONYMS

bhrātah — my dear brother; mama — my; sutah — son (Baladeva, born of Rohinī); kaccit — whether; mātrā saha — with His mother, Rohinī; bhavat-vraje — in your house; tātam — as father; bhavantam — unto you; manvānah — thinking; bhavadbhyām — by you and your wife, Yaśodā; upalālitah — properly being raised.

TRANSLATION

My son Baladeva, being raised by you and your wife, Yaśodādevī, considers you His father and mother. Is he living very peacefully in your home with His real mother, Rohinī?
