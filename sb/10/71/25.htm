Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.25

drishtvā viklinna-hridayah

krishnam snehena pāndavah

cirād drishtam priyatamam

sasvaje 'tha punah punah

SYNONYMS

drishtvā — seeing; viklinna — melted; hridayah — his heart; krishnam — Lord Krishna; snehena — with affection; pāndavah — the son of Pāndu; cirāt — after a long time; drishtam — seen; priya-tamam — his dearmost friend; sasvaje — he embraced Him; atha — thereupon; punah punah — again and again.

TRANSLATION

The heart of King Yudhishthira melted with affection when he saw his dearmost friend, Lord Krishna, after such a long separation, and he embraced the Lord again and again.
