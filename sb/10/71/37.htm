Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.37

antah-pura-janaih prītyā

mukundah phulla-locanaih

sa-sambhramair abhyupetah

prāviśad rāja-mandiram

SYNONYMS

antah-pura — of the imperial precinct; janaih — by the people; prītyā — lovingly; mukundah — Lord Krishna; phulla — blossoming; locanaih — whose eyes; sa-sambhramaih — in a flurry; abhyupetah — approached with greetings; prāviśat — He entered; rāja — royal; mandiram — the palace.

TRANSLATION

With wide-open eyes, the members of the royal household came forward in a flurry to lovingly greet Lord Mukunda, and thus the Lord entered the royal palace.
