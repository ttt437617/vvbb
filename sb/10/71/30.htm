Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.30

evam suhridbhih paryastah

punya-śloka-śikhāmanih

samstūyamāno bhagavān

viveśālańkritam puram

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; su-hridbhih — by His well-wishing relatives; paryastah — surrounded; punya-śloka — of persons of pious renown; śikhā-manih — the crest jewel; samstūyamānah — being glorified; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; viveśa — entered; alańkritam — decorated; puram — the city.

TRANSLATION

Thus surrounded by His well-wishing relatives and praised on all sides, Lord Krishna, the crest jewel of the justly renowned, entered the decorated city.

PURPORT

Śrīla Prabhupāda writes, "While Lord Krishna was entering the city, all the people were talking among themselves about the glories of the Lord, praising His transcendental name, quality, form, etc."
