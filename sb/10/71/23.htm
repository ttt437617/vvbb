Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.23

tam upāgatam ākarnya

prīto durdarśanam nrinām

ajāta-śatrur niragāt

sopadhyāyah suhrid-vritah

SYNONYMS

tam — Him; upāgatam — arrived; ākarnya — hearing; prītah — pleased; durdarśanam — rarely seen; nrinām — by humans; ajāta-śatruh — King Yudhishthira, whose enemy was never born; niragāt — came out; sa — with; upadhyāyah — his priests; suhrit — by relatives; vritah — surrounded.

TRANSLATION

King Yudhishthira was delighted to hear that the Lord, whom human beings rarely see, had now arrived. Accompanied by his priests and dear associates, the King came out to meet Lord Krishna.
