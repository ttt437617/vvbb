Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.22

tato drishadvatīm tīrtvā

mukundo 'tha sarasvatīm

pañcālān atha matsyāmś ca

śakra-prastham athāgamat

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; drishadvatīm — the river Drishadvatī; tīrtvā — crossing; mukundah — Lord Krishna; atha — then; sarasvatīm — the river Sarasvatī; pañcālān — the Pañcāla province; atha — then; matsyān — the Matsya province; ca — also; śakra-prastham — to Indraprastha; atha — and; āgamat — He came.

TRANSLATION

After crossing the rivers Drishadvatī and Sarasvatī, He passed through Pañcāla and Matsya and finally came to Indraprastha.
