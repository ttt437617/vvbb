Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 71: The Lord Travels to Indraprastha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.71.4

asmākam ca mahān artho

hy etenaiva bhavishyati

yaśaś ca tava govinda

rājño baddhān vimuñcatah

SYNONYMS

asmākam — for us; ca — and; mahān — great; arthah — a gain; hi — indeed; etena — by this; eva — even; bhavishyati — there will be; yaśah — glory; ca — and; tava — for You; govinda — O Govinda; rājñah — the kings; baddhān — imprisoned; vimuñcatah — who will release.

TRANSLATION

By this decision there will be great gain for us, and You will save the kings. Thus, Govinda, You will be glorified.
