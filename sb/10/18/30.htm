Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 18: Lord Balarāma Slays the Demon Pralamba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.18.30

drishtvā pralambam nihatam

balena bala-śālinā

gopāh su-vismitā āsan

sādhu sādhv iti vādinah

SYNONYMS

drishtvā — seeing; pralambam — Pralambāsura; nihatam — killed; balena — by Lord Balarāma; bala-śālinā — who is by nature very powerful; gopāh — the cowherd boys; su-vismitāh — most astonished; āsan — became; sādhu sādhu — "very wonderful, very wonderful"; iti — these words; vādinah — speaking.

TRANSLATION

The cowherd boys were most astonished to see how the powerful Balarāma had killed the demon Pralamba, and they exclaimed, "Excellent! Excellent!"
