Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 18: Lord Balarāma Slays the Demon Pralamba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.18.31

āśisho 'bhigrinantas tam

praśaśamsus tad-arhanam

pretyāgatam ivālińgya

prema-vihvala-cetasah

SYNONYMS

āśishah — benedictions; abhigrinantah — offering profusely; tam — to Him; praśaśamsuh — they praised; tat-arhanam — Him who was worthy of such; pretya — having died; āgatam — come back; iva — as if; ālingya — embracing; prema — out of love; vihvala — overwhelmed; cetasah — their minds.

TRANSLATION

They offered Balarāma profuse benedictions and then glorified Him, who deserves all glorification. Their minds overwhelmed with ecstatic love, they embraced Him as if He had come back from the dead.
