Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 18: Lord Balarāma Slays the Demon Pralamba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.18.23

rāma-sańghattino yarhi

śrīdāma-vrishabhādayah

krīdāyām jayinas tāms tān

ūhuh krishnādayo nripa

SYNONYMS

rāma-sańghattinah — the members of Lord Balarāma's party; yarhi — when; śrīdāma-vrishabha-ādayah — Śrīdāmā, Vrishabha and others (such as Subala); krīdāyām — in the games; jayinah — victorious; tān tān — each of them; ūhuh — carried; krishna-ādayah — Krishna and the members of His party; nripa — O King.

TRANSLATION

My dear King Parīkshit, when Śrīdāmā, Vrishabha and the other members of Lord Balarāma's party were victorious in these games, Krishna and His followers had to carry them.
