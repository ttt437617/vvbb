Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.50

śrī-śuka uvāca

evam bhagavatā tanvī

rāmena pratibodhitā

vaimanasyam parityajya

mano buddhyā samādadhe

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Lord; tanvī — slender-waisted Rukminī; rāmena — by Balarāma; pratibodhitā — enlightened; vaimanasyam — her depression; parityajya — giving up; manah — her mind; buddhyā — by intelligence; samādadhe — composed.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thus enlightened by Lord Balarāma, slender Rukminī forgot her depression and steadied her mind by spiritual intelligence.
