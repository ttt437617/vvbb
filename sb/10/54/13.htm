Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.13

śaureh sapta-daśāham vai

samyugāni parājitah

trayo-vimśatibhih sainyair

jigye ekam aham param

SYNONYMS

śaureh — with Krishna; sapta-daśa — seventeen; aham — I; vai — indeed; samyugāni — battles; parājitah — lost; trayah-vimśatibhih — twenty-three; sainyaih — with armies; jigye — won; ekam — one; aham — I; param — only.

TRANSLATION

In battle with Krishna I and my twenty-three armies lost seventeen times; only once did I defeat Him.

PURPORT

Jarāsandha offers his own life as an example of the inevitable happiness and distress of this material world.
