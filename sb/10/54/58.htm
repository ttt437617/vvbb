Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.58

kuru-sriñjaya-kaikeya-

vidarbha-yadu-kuntayah

mitho mumudire tasmin

sambhramāt paridhāvatām

SYNONYMS

kuru-sriñjaya-kaikeya-vidarbha-yadu-kuntayah — of the members of the Kuru, Sriñjaya, Kaikeya, Vidarbha, Yadu and Kunti clans; mithah — with one another; mumudire — they took pleasure; tasmin — in that (festivity); sambhramāt — out of excitement; paridhāvatām — among those who were running about.

TRANSLATION

Those who belonged to the royal families of the Kuru, Sriñjaya, Kaikeya, Vidarbha, Yadu and Kunti clans joyfully met one another in the midst of the crowds of people excitedly running here and there.
