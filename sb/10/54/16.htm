Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.16

ripavo jigyur adhunā

kāla ātmānusārini

tadā vayam vijeshyāmo

yadā kālah pradakshinah

SYNONYMS

ripavah — our enemies; jigyuh — have conquered; adhunā — now; kāle — the time; ātma — them; anusārīni — favoring; tadā — then; vayam — we; vijeshyāmah — shall conquer; yadā — when; kālah — time; pradakshinah — turned toward us.

TRANSLATION

Now our enemies have conquered because time favors them, but in the future, when time is auspicious for us, we shall conquer.
