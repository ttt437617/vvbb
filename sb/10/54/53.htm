Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.53

bhagavān bhīshmaka-sutām

evam nirjitya bhūmi-pān

puram ānīya vidhi-vad

upayeme kurūdvaha

SYNONYMS

bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; bhīshmaka-sutām — the daughter of Bhīshmaka; evam — thus; nirjitya — defeating; bhūmi-pān — the kings; puram — to His capital; ānīya — bringing; vidhi-vat — in accordance with the injunctions of the Vedas; upayeme — married; kuru-udvaha — O protector of the Kurus.

TRANSLATION

Thus defeating all the opposing kings, the Supreme Personality of Godhead brought the daughter of Bhīshmaka to His capital and married her according to the Vedic injunctions, O protector of the Kurus.
