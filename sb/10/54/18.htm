Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.18

rukmī tu rākshasodvāham

krishna-dvid asahan svasuh

prishthato 'nvagamat krishnam

akshauhinyā vrito balī

SYNONYMS

rukmī — Rukmī; tu — however; rākshasa — in the style of demons; udvāham — the marriage; krishna-dvit — the hater of Krishna; asahan — unable to bear; svasuh — of his sister; prishthatah — from behind; anvagamat — he followed; krishnam — Lord Krishna; akshauhinyā — by an entire akshauhinī division; vritah — surrounded; balī — powerful.

TRANSLATION

Powerful Rukmī, however, was especially envious of Krishna. He could not bear the fact that Krishna had carried off his sister to marry her in the Rākshasa style. Thus he pursued the Lord with an entire military division.
