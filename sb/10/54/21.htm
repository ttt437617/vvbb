Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.21

ity uktvā ratham āruhya

sārathim prāha satvarah

codayāśvān yatah krishnah

tasya me samyugam bhavet

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; uktvā — speaking; ratham — on his chariot; āruhya — climbing; sārathim — to his driver; prāha — said; satvarah — quickly; codaya — drive; aśvān — the horses; yatah — to where; krishnah — Krishna; tasya — His; me — with me; samyugam — fight; bhavet — must be.

TRANSLATION

Having said this, he had mounted his chariot and told his charioteer, "Drive the horses quickly to where Krishna is. He and I must fight.
