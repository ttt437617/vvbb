Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.54

tadā mahotsavo nrinām

yadu-puryām grihe grihe

abhūd ananya-bhāvānām

krishne yadu-patau nripa

SYNONYMS

tadā — then; mahā-utsavah — great rejoicing; nrinām — by the people; yadu-puryām — in the capital of the Yadus, Dvārakā; grihe grihe — in each and every home; abhūt — arose; ananya-bhāvānām — who had exclusive love; krishne — for Krishna; yadu-patau — the chief of the Yadus; nripa — O King (Parīkshit).

TRANSLATION

At that time, O King, there was great rejoicing in all the homes of Yadupurī, whose citizens loved only Krishna, chief of the Yadus.
