Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.30

tato rathād avaplutya

khadga-pānir jighāmsayā

krishnam abhyadravat kruddhah

patańga iva pāvakam

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; rathāt — from his chariot; avaplutya — leaping down; khadga — a sword; panih — in his hand; jighāmsayā — with the desire to kill; krishnam — Lord Krishna; abhyadravat — he ran toward; kruddhah — furious; patańgah — a bird; iva — as; pāvakam — the wind.

TRANSLATION

Then Rukmī leaped down from his chariot and, sword in hand, rushed furiously toward Krishna to kill Him, like a bird flying into the wind.
