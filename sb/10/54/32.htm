Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.32

drishtvā bhrātri-vadhodyogam

rukminī bhaya-vihvalā

patitvā pādayor bhartur

uvāca karunam satī

SYNONYMS

drishtvā — seeing; bhrātri — her brother; vadha — to kill; udyogam — the attempt; rukminī — Śrīmatī Rukminī; bhaya — by fear; vihvalā — agitated; patitvā — falling; pādayoh — at the feet; bhartuh — of her husband; uvāca — spoke; karunam — pathetically; satī — saintly.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Lord Krishna ready to kill her brother, saintly Rukminī was filled with alarm. She fell at her husband's feet and piteously spoke as follows.
