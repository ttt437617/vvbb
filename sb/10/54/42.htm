Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.42

taveyam vishamā buddhih

sarva-bhūteshu durhridām

yan manyase sadābhadram

suhridām bhadram ajña-vat

SYNONYMS

tava — your; iyam — this; vishamā — biased; buddhih — attitude; sarva-bhūteshu — toward all living beings; durhridām — of those who have ill intentions; yat — that; manyase — you wish; sadā — always; abhadram — evil; suhridām — to your well-wishers; bhadram — good; ajña-vat — like an ignorant person.

TRANSLATION

[To Rukminī Balarāma said:] Your attitude is unfair, for like an ignorant person you wish good to those who are inimical to all living beings and who have done evil to your true well-wishers.
