Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 54: The Marriage of Krishna and Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.54.38

maivāsmān sādhvy asūyethā

bhrātur vairūpya-cintayā

sukha-duhkha-do na cānyo 'sti

yatah sva-krita-bhuk pumān

SYNONYMS

mā — please do not; eva — indeed; asmān — toward Us; sādhvi — O saintly lady; asūyethāh — feel inimical; bhrātuh — of your brother; vairūpya — over the disfigurement; cintayā — out of concern; sukha — of happiness; duhkha — and unhappiness; dah — bestower; na — not; ca — and; anyah — anyone else; asti — there is; yatah — since; sva — of his own; krita — action; bhuk — the sufferer of the reaction; pumān — a man.

TRANSLATION

Saintly lady, please do not be displeased with Us out of anxiety for your brother's disfigurement. No one but oneself is responsible for one's joy and grief, for a man experiences the result of his own deeds.
