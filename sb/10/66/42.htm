Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 66: Paundraka, the False Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.66.42

dagdhvā vārānasīm sarvām

vishnoś cakram sudarśanam

bhūyah pārśvam upātishthat

krishnasyāklishta-karmanah

SYNONYMS

dagdhvā — having burned; vārānasīm — Vārānasī; sarvām — all; vishnoh — of Lord Vishnu; cakram — the disc; sudarśanam — Sudarśana; bhūyah — once again; pārśvam — the side; upātishthat — went to; krishnasya — of Krishna; aklishta — without trouble or fatigue; karmanah — whose actions.

TRANSLATION

After burning down the entire city of Vārānasī, Lord Vishnu's Sudarśana cakra returned to the side of Śrī Krishna, whose actions are effortless.
