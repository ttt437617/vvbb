Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 66: Paundraka, the False Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.66.35

tam ābhicāra-dahanam

āyāntam dvārakaukasah

vilokya tatrasuh sarve

vana-dāhe mrigā yathā

SYNONYMS

tam — him; ābhicāra — created by the abhicāra ritual; dahanam — the fire; āyāntam — approaching; dvārakā-okasah — the residents of Dvārakā; vilokya — seeing; tatrasuh — became frightened; sarve — all; vana-dāhe — when there is a forest fire; mrigāh — animals; yathā — as.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the approach of the fiery demon created by the abhicāra ritual, the residents of Dvārakā were all struck with fear, like animals terrified by a forest fire.
