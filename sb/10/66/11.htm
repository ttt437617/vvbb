Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 66: Paundraka, the False Vasudeva

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.66.11

paundrako 'pi tad-udyogam

upalabhya mahā-rathah

akshauhinībhyām samyukto

niścakrāma purād drutam

SYNONYMS

paundrakah — Paundraka; api — and; tat — His; udyogam — preparations; upalabhya — noticing; mahā-rathah — the mighty warrior; akshauhinībhyām — by two full military divisions; samyuktah — joined; niścakrāma — went out; purāt — from the city; drutam — quickly.

TRANSLATION

Upon observing Lord Krishna's preparations for battle, the mighty warrior Paundraka quickly went out of the city with two full military divisions.
