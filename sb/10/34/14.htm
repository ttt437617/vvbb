Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.14

śāpo me 'nugrahāyaiva

kritas taih karunātmabhih

yad aham loka-gurunā

padā sprishto hatāśubhah

SYNONYMS

śāpah — the curse; me — my; anugrahāya — for the benediction; eva — certainly; kritah — created; taih — by them; karuna-ātmabhih — who are merciful by nature; yat — since; aham — I; loka — of all the worlds; gurunā — by the spiritual master; padā — with His foot; sprishtah — touched; hata — destroyed; aśubhah — all inauspiciousness.

TRANSLATION

It was actually for my benefit that those merciful sages cursed me, since now I have been touched by the foot of the supreme spiritual master of all the worlds and have thus been relieved of all inauspiciousness.
