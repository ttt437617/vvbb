Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.19

niśāmya krishnasya tad ātma-vaibhavam

vrajaukaso vismita-cetasas tatah

samāpya tasmin niyamam punar vrajam

nripāyayus tat kathayanta ādritāh

SYNONYMS

niśāmya — seeing; krishnasya — of Lord Krishna; tat — that; ātma — personal; vaibhavam — opulent display of power; vraja-okasah — the inhabitants of Vraja; vismita — amazed; cetasah — in their minds; tatah — then; samāpya — finishing; tasmin — at that place; niyamam — their vow; punah — again; vrajam — to the cowherd village; nripa — O King; āyayuh — they returned; tat — that display; kathayantah — describing; ādritāh — with reverence.

TRANSLATION

The inhabitants of Vraja were astonished to see the mighty power of Śrī Krishna. Dear King, they then completed their worship of Lord Śiva and returned to Vraja, along the way respectfully describing Krishna's powerful acts.
