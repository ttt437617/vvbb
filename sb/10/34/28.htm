Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.28

mā bhaishtety abhayārāvau

śāla-hastau tarasvinau

āsedatus tam tarasā

tvaritam guhyakādhamam

SYNONYMS

mā bhaishta — do not fear; iti — thus calling; abhaya — giving fearlessness; ārāvau — whose words; śāla — stones; hastau — in Their hands; tarasvinau — moving quickly; āsedatuh — They approached; tam — that demon; tarasā — with haste; tvaritam — who was moving swiftly; guhyaka — of Yakshas; adhamam — the worst.

TRANSLATION

The Lords called out in reply, "Do not fear! "Then They picked up logs of the śala tree and quickly pursued that lowest of Guhyakas, who swiftly ran away.
