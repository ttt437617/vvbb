Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.18

ity anujñāpya dāśārham

parikramyābhivandya ca

sudarśano divam yātah

kricchrān nandaś ca mocitah

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; anujñāpya — taking permission; dāśārham — from Lord Krishna; parikramya — circumambulating; abhivandya — offering obeisances; ca — and; sudarśanah — Sudarśana; divam — to heaven; yātah — went; kricchrāt — from his difficulty; nandah — Nanda Mahārāja; ca — also; mocitah — was delivered.

TRANSLATION

Thus receiving the permission of Lord Krishna, the demigod Sudarśana circumambulated Him, bowed down to offer Him homage and then returned to his heavenly planet. Nanda Mahārāja was thus delivered from peril.
