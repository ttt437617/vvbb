Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.11

ko bhavān parayā lakshmyā

rocate 'dbhuta-darśanah

katham jugupsitām etām

gatim vā prāpito 'vaśah

SYNONYMS

kah — who; bhavān — your good self; parayā — with great; lakshmyā — beauty; rocate — shine; adbhuta — wonderful; darśanah — to see; katham — why; yugupsitām — terrible; etām — this; gatim — destination; vā — and; prāpitah — made to assume; avaśah — beyond your control.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:] My dear sir, you appear so wonderful, glowing with such great beauty. Who are you? And who forced you to assume this terrible body of a snake?
