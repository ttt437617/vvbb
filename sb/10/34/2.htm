Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 34: Nanda Mahārāja Saved and Śańkhacūda Slain

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.34.2

tatra snātvā sarasvatyām

devam paśu-patim vibhum

ānarcur arhanair bhaktyā

devīm ca nripate 'mbikām

SYNONYMS

tatra — there; snātvā — bathing; sarasvatyām — in the river Sarasvatī; devam — the demigod; paśu-patim — Lord Śiva; vibhum — the powerful; ānarcuh — they worshiped; arhanaih — with paraphernalia; bhaktyā — devotedly; devīm — the goddess; ca — and; nri-pate — O King; ambikām — Ambikā.

TRANSLATION

O King, after arriving there, they bathed in the Sarasvatī and then devotedly worshiped with various paraphernalia the powerful Lord Paśupati and his consort, goddess Ambikā.
