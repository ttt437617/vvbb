Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 84: The Sages' Teachings at Kurukshetra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.84.34

athocur munayo rājann

ābhāshyānalsadundabhim

sarveshām śrinvatām rājñām

tathaivācyuta-rāmayoh

SYNONYMS

atha — then; ūcuh — said; munayah — the sages; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); ābhāshya — speaking; ānaka-dundubhim — to Vasudeva; sarveshām — all; śrinvatām — as they listened; rājñām — the kings; tathā eva — also; acyuta-rāmayoh — Krishna and Balarāma.

TRANSLATION

[Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued:l The sages then spoke again, O King, addressing Vasudeva while all the kings, along with Lord Acyuta and Lord Rāma, listened.
