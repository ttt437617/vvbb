Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 84: The Sages' Teachings at Kurukshetra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.84.70

bandhushu pratiyāteshu

vrishnayah krishna-devatāh

vīkshya prāvrisham āsannād

yayur dvāravatīm punah

SYNONYMS

bandhushu — their relatives; pratiyāteshu — having departed; vrishnayah — the Vrishnis; krishna-devatāh — whose worshipable Deity was Krishna; vīkshya — seeing; prāvrisham — the rainy season; āsannāt — imminent; yayuh — went; dvāravatīm — to Dvārakā; punah — again.

TRANSLATION

Their relatives having thus departed, and seeing that the rainy season was approaching, the Vrishnis, whose only Lord was Krishna, went back to Dvārakā.
