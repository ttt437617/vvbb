Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 49: Akrūra's Mission in Hastināpura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.49.9

bhrātreyo bhagavān krishnah

śaranyo bhakta-vatsalah

paitri-shvasreyān smarati

rāmaś cāmburuhekshanah

SYNONYMS

bhrātreyah — brother's son; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; krishnah — Krishna; śaranyah — the giver of shelter; bhakta — to His devotees; vatsalah — compassionate; paitri-svasreyān — the sons of His father's sister; smarati — remembers; rāmah — Lord Balarāma; ca — and; amburuha — like lotus petals; īkshanah — whose eyes.

TRANSLATION

Does my nephew Krishna, the Supreme Personality and the compassionate shelter of His devotees, still remember His aunt's sons? And does lotus-eyed Rāma remember them also?
