Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 49: Akrūra's Mission in Hastināpura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.49.12

nānyat tava padāmbhojāt

paśyāmi śaranam nrinām

bibhyatām mrityu-samsārād

īsvarasyāpavargikāt

SYNONYMS

na — no; anyat — other; tava — Your; pada-ambhojāt — than the lotus feet; paśyāmi — I see; śaranam — shelter; nrinām — for men; bibhyatām — fearful; mrityu — of death; samsārāt — and rebirth; īśvarasya — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; āpavargikāt — which give liberation.

TRANSLATION

For persons fearful of death and rebirth, I see no shelter other than Your liberating lotus feet, for You are the Supreme Lord.
