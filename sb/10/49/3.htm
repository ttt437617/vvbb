Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 49: Akrūra's Mission in Hastināpura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.49.3

yathāvad upasańgamya

bandhubhir gāndinī-sutah

samprishtas taih suhrid-vārtām

svayam cāpricchad avyayam

SYNONYMS

yathā-vat — appropriately; upasańgamya — meeting; bandhubhih — with his relatives and friends; gāndinī-sutah — Akrūra, son of Gāndinī; samprishtah — inquired from; taih — by them; suhrit — of their dear ones; vārtām — for news; svayam — himself; ca — in addition; apricchat — asked; avyayam — about their well-being.

TRANSLATION

After Akrūra, the son of Gāndinī, had appropriately greeted all his relatives and friends, they asked him for news of their family members, and he in turn asked about their welfare.
