Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 40: The Prayers of Akrūra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.40.26

yathābudho jalam hitvā

praticchannam tad-udbhavaih

abhyeti mriga-trishnām vai

tadvat tvāham parāń-mukhah

SYNONYMS

yathā — as; abudhah — someone who is unintelligent; jalam — water; hitvā — overlooking; praticchannam — covered; tat-udbhavaih — by the plants growing in it; abhyeti — approaches; mriga-trishnām — a mirage; vai — indeed; tadvat — in that same way; tvā — You; aham — I; parāk-mukhah — turned away.

TRANSLATION

Just as a fool overlooks a body of water covered by the vegetation growing in it and chases a mirage, so I have turned away from You.
