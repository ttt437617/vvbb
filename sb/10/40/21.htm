Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 40: The Prayers of Akrūra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.40.21

namas te vāsudevāya

namah sańkarshanāya ca

pradyumnāyaniruddhāya

sātvatām pataye namah

SYNONYMS

namah — obeisances; te — unto You; vāsudevāya — Lord Śrī Vasudeva; namah — obeisances; sańkarshanāya — to Lord Sańkarshana; ca — and; pradyumnāya — to Lord Pradyumna; aniruddhāya — and to Lord Aniruddha; sātvatām — of the Yādavas; pataye — to the chief; namah — obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Obeisances to You, Lord of the Sātvatas, and to Your forms of Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana, Pradyumna and Aniruddha.
