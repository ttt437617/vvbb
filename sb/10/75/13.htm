Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 75: Duryodhana Humiliated

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.75.13

sadasyartvig-dvija-śreshthā

brahma-ghoshena bhūyasā

devarshi-pitri-gandharvās

tushtuvuh pushpa-varshinah

SYNONYMS

sadasya — the officiating witnesses; ritvik — the priests; dvija — and brāhmanas; śreshthāh — most excellent; brahma — of the Vedas; ghoshena — with sounding; bhūyasā — abundant; deva — the demigods; rishi — divine sages; pitri — forefathers; gandharvāh — and singers of heaven; tushtuvuh — recited praises; pushpa — flowers; varshinah — raining down.

TRANSLATION

The assembly officials, the priests and other excellent brāhmanas resoundingly vibrated Vedic mantras, while the demigods, divine sages, Pitās and Gandharvas sang praises and rained down flowers.
