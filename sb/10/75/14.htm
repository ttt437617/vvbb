Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 75: Duryodhana Humiliated

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.75.14

sv-alankritā narā nāryo

gandha-srag-bhūshanāmbaraih

vilimpantyo 'bhisiñcantyo

vijahrur vividhai rasaih

SYNONYMS

su-alańkritāh — well decorated; narāh — men; nāryah — and women; gandha — with sandalwood paste; srak — flower garlands; bhūshana — jewelry; ambaraih — and clothing; vilimpantyah — smearing; abhishiñcantyah — and sprinkling; vijahruh — they played; vividhaih — various; rasaih — with liquids.

TRANSLATION

Men and women, all adorned with sandalwood paste, flower garlands, jewelry and fine clothing, sported by smearing and sprinkling one another with various liquids.
