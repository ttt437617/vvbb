Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 75: Duryodhana Humiliated

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.75.10

nārtakyo nanritur hrishtā

gāyakā yūthaśo jaguh

vīnā-venu-talonnādas

teshām sa divam aspriśat

SYNONYMS

nārtakyah — female dancers; nanrituh — danced; hrishtāh — joyful; gāyakāh — singers; yūthaśah — in groups; jaguh — sang; vīnā — of vīnās; venu — flutes; tala — and hand cymbals; unnādah — the loud sound; teshām — their; sah — it; divam — the heavens; aspriśat — touched.

TRANSLATION

Female dancers danced with great joy, and choruses sang, while the loud vibrations of vīnās, flutes and hand cymbals reached all the way to the heavenly regions.
