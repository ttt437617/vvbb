Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 78: The Killing of Dantavakra, Vidūratha and Romaharshana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.78.34

dīrgham āyur bataitasya

sattvam indriyam eva ca

āśāsitam yat tad brūte

sādhaye yoga-māyayā

SYNONYMS

dīrgham — long; āyuh — life span; bata — oh; etasya — for him; sattvam — strength; indriyam — sensory power; eva ca — also; āśāsitam — promised; yat — which; tat — that; brūte — please say: sādhaye — I shall make happen; yoga-māyayā — by My mystic power.

TRANSLATION

O sages, just say the word, and by My mystic power I shall restore everything you promised him — long life, strength and sensory power.
