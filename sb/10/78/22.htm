Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 78: The Killing of Dantavakra, Vidūratha and Romaharshana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.78.22

so 'rcitah sa-parīvārah

kritāsana-parigrahah

romaharshanam āsīnam

maharsheh śishyam aikshata

SYNONYMS

sah — He; arcitah — worshiped; sa — together with; parīvārah — His entourage; krita — having done; āsana — of a seat; parigrahah — acceptance; romaharshanam — Romaharshana Sūta; āsīnam — seated; mahā-risheh — of the greatest of sages, Vyāsadeva; śishyam — the disciple; aikshata — saw.

TRANSLATION

After being thus worshiped along with His entourage, the Lord accepted a seat of honor. Then He noticed that Romaharshana, Vyāsadeva's disciple, had remained seated.
