Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 78: The Killing of Dantavakra, Vidūratha and Romaharshana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.78.39

tam pāpam jahi dāśārha

tan nah śuśrūshanam param

pūya-śonita-vin-mūtra-

surā-māmsābhivarshinam

SYNONYMS

tam — that; pāpam — sinful person; jahi — please kill; dāśārha — O descendant of Daśārha; tat — that; nah — to us; śuśrūshanam — service; param — best; pūya — pus; śonita — blood; vit — feces; mūtra — urine; surā — wine; māmsa — and meat; abhivarshinam — who pours down.

TRANSLATION

O descendant of Daśārha, please kill that sinful demon, who pours down pus, blood, feces, urine, wine and meat upon us. This is the best service You can do for us.
