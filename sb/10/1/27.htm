Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 1: The Advent of Lord Krishna: Introduction

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.1.27

śūraseno yadupatir

mathurām āvasan purīm

māthurāñ chūrasenāmś ca

vishayān bubhuje purā

SYNONYMS

śūrasenah — King Śūrasena; yadu-patih — the chief of the Yadu dynasty; mathurām — at the place known as Mathurā; āvasan — went to live; purīm — in that city; māthurān — at the place known as the Māthura district; śūrasenān ca — and the place known as Śūrasena; vishayān — such kingdoms; bubhuje — enjoyed; purā — formerly.

TRANSLATION

Formerly, Śūrasena, the chief of the Yadu dynasty, had gone to live in the city of Mathurā. There he enjoyed the places known as Māthura and Śūrasena.
