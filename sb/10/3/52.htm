Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 3: The Birth of Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.3.52

devakyāh śayane nyasya

vasudevo 'tha dārikām

pratimucya pador loham

āste pūrvavad āvritah

SYNONYMS

devakyāh — of Devakī; śayane — on the bed; nyasya — placing; vasudevah — Vasudeva; atha — thus; dārikām — the female child; pratimucya — binding himself again; padoh loham — iron shackles on the two legs; āste — remained; pūrva-vat — like before; āvritah — bound.

TRANSLATION

Vasudeva placed the female child on the bed of Devakī, bound his legs with the iron shackles, and thus remained there as before.
