Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 3: The Birth of Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.3.42

tayor vām punar evāham

adityām āsa kaśyapāt

upendra iti vikhyāto

vāmanatvāc ca vāmanah

SYNONYMS

tayoh — of you two, husband and wife; vām — in both of you; punah eva — even again; aham — I Myself; adityām — in the womb of Aditi; āsa — appeared; kaśyapāt — by the semen of Kaśyapa Muni; upendrah — by the name Upendra; iti — thus; vikhyātah — celebrated; vāmanatvāt ca — and because of being a dwarf; vāmanah — I was known as Vāmana.

TRANSLATION

In the next millennium, I again appeared from the two of you, who appeared as My mother, Aditi, and My father, Kaśyapa. I was known as Upendra, and because of being a dwarf, I was also known as Vāmana.
