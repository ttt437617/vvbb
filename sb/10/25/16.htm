Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.16

tatra pratividhim samyag

ātma-yogena sādhaye

lokeśa-māninām maudhyād

dhanishye śrī-madam tamah

SYNONYMS

tatra — in that regard; prati-vidhim — counteracting measures; samyak — properly; ātma-yogena — by My mystic power; sādhaye — I shall arrange; loka-īśa — lords of the world; māninām — of those who falsely consider themselves; maudhyāt — out of foolishness; hanishye — I shall defeat; śrī-madam — their pride in opulence; tamah — the ignorance.

TRANSLATION

By My mystic power I will completely counteract this disturbance caused by Indra. Demigods like Indra are proud of their opulence, and out of foolishness they falsely consider themselves the Lord of the universe. I will now destroy such ignorance.
