Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.25

kham vyabhram uditādityam

vāta-varsham ca dārunam

niśamyoparatam gopān

govardhana-dharo 'bravīt

SYNONYMS

kham — the sky; vi-abhram — empty of clouds; udita — arisen; ādityam — with the sun; vāta-varsham — the wind and rain; ca — and; dārunam — fierce; niśamya — seeing; uparatam — ceased; gopān — to the cowherds; govardhana-dharah — the lifter of Govardhana Hill; abravīt — spoke.

TRANSLATION

Seeing that the fierce wind and rain had now ceased, the sky had become clear of rainclouds, and the sun had risen, Lord Krishna, the lifter of Govardhana Hill, spoke to the cowherd community as follows.
