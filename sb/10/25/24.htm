Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.24

krishna-yogānubhāvam tam

niśamyendro 'ti-vismitah

nistambho bhrashta-sańkalpah

svān meghān sannyavārayat

SYNONYMS

krishna — of Lord Krishna; yoga — of the mystic power; anubhāvam — the influence; tam — that; niśamya — seeing; indrah — Lord Indra; ati-vismitah — most amazed; nistambhah — whose false pride was brought down; bhrashta — ruined; sańkalpah — whose determination; svān — his own; meghān — clouds; sannyavārayat — stopped.

TRANSLATION

When Indra observed this exhibition of Lord Krishna's mystic power, he became most astonished. Pulled down from his platform of false pride, and his intentions thwarted, he ordered his clouds to desist.
