Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.15

apartv aty-ulbanam varsham

ati-vātam śilā-mayam

sva-yāge vihate 'smābhir

indro nāśāya varshati

SYNONYMS

apa-ritu — out of season; ati-ulbanam — unusually fierce; varsham — rain; ati-vātam — accompanied by great wind; śilā-mayam — full of hailstones; sva-yage — his sacrifice; vihate — having been stopped; asmābhih — by Ourselves; indrah — King Indra; nāśāya — for destruction; varshati — is raining.

TRANSLATION

[Śrī Krishna said to Himself:] Because We have stopped his sacrifice, Indra has caused this unusually fierce, unseasonable rain, together with terrible winds and hail.
