Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.12

śirah sutāmś ca kāyena

pracchādyāsāra-pīditāh

vepamānā bhagavatah

pāda-mūlam upāyayuh

SYNONYMS

śirah — their heads; sutān — their children; ca — and; kāyena — by their bodies; pracchādya — covering; āsāra-pīditāh — distressed by the rainfall; vepamānāh — trembling; bhagavatah — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; pāda-mūlam — the base of the lotus feet; upāyayuh — they approached .

TRANSLATION

Trembling from the distress brought about by the severe rainfall, and trying to cover their heads and calves with their own bodies, the cows approached the lotus feet of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
