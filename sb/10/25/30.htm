Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 25: Lord Krishna Lifts Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.25.30

yaśodā rohinī nando

rāmaś ca balinām varah

krishnam ālińgya yuyujur

āśishah sneha-kātarāh

SYNONYMS

yaśodā — mother Yaśodā; rohinī — Rohinī; nandah — Nanda Mahārāja; rāmah — Balarāma; ca — also; balinām — of the strong; varah — the greatest; krishnam — Krishna; ālińgya — embracing; yuyujuh — they all offered; āśishah — benedictions; sneha — by their affection for Him; kātarāh — beside themselves.

TRANSLATION

Mother Yaśodā, mother Rohinī, Nanda Mahārāja and Balarāma, the greatest of the strong, all embraced Krishna. Overwhelmed with affection, they offered Him their blessings.
