Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.37

prajā bhoja-pater asya

vayam cāpi vane-carāh

karavāma priyam nityam

tan nah param anugrahah

SYNONYMS

prajāh — subjects; bhoja-pateh — of the King of the Bhojas; asya — of him; vayam — We; ca — also; api — even though; vane-carāh — wandering in the forest; karavāma — We must execute; priyam — his pleasure; nityam — always; tat — that; nah — for Us; param — the greatest; anugrahah — benefit.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:] Although forest-dwellers, We are also subjects of the Bhoja king. We must gratify his desires, for such behavior will confer upon Us the greatest benefit.
