Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.8

pucche pragrihyāti-balam

dhanushah pañca-vimśatim

vicakarsha yathā nāgam

suparna iva līlayā

SYNONYMS

pucche — by his tail; pragrihya — grabbing him; ati-balam — the extremely powerful (elephant); dhanushah — bow-lengths; pañca-vimśatim — twenty-five; vicakarsha — He dragged; yathā — as; nāgam — a snake; suparnah — Garuda; iva — as; līlayā — playfully.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna then grabbed the powerful Kuvalayāpīda by the tail and playfully dragged him twenty-five bow-lengths as easily as Garuda might drag a snake.
