Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.2

rańga-dvāram samāsādya

tasmin nāgam avasthitam

apaśyat kuvalayāpīdam

krishno 'mbashtha-pracoditam

SYNONYMS

rańga — of the arena; dvāram — the gate; samāsādya — reaching; tasmin — in that place; nāgam — an elephant; avasthitam — standing; apaśyat — He saw; kuvalayāpīdam — named Kuvalayāpīda; krishnah — Lord Krishna; ambashtha — by his keeper; pracoditam — urged on.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Krishna reached the entrance to the arena, He saw the elephant Kuvalayāpīda blocking His way at the urging of his keeper.

PURPORT

The elephant-keeper revealed his malicious intent by blocking Lord Krishna's entrance into the arena.
