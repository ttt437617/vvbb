Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.4

ambashthāmbashtha mārgam nau

dehy apakrama mā ciram

no cet sa-kuñjaram tvādya

nayāmi yama-sādanam

SYNONYMS

ambashtha ambashtha — O elephant-keeper, elephant-keeper; mārgam — way; nau — to Us; dehi — give; apakrama — move aside; ma ciram — without delay; na u cet — if not; sa-kuñjaram — together with your elephant; tva — you; adya — today; nayāmi — I will send; yama — of the lord of death; sādanam — to the abode.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:l O driver, driver, move aside at once and let Us pass! If you don't, this very day I will send both you and your elephant to the abode of Yamarāja!
