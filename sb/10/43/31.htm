Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.31

janeshv evam bruvāneshu

tūryeshu ninadatsu ca

krishna-rāmau samābhāshya

cānūro vākyam abravīt

SYNONYMS

janeshu — as the people; evam — thus; bruvāneshu — were speaking; tūryeshu — as the musical instruments; ninadatsu — were resounding; ca — and; krishna-rāmau — Krishna and Balarāma; samābhāshya — addressing; cānūrah — the demonic wrestler Cānūra; vākyam — words; abravīt — said.

TRANSLATION

While the people talked in this way and the musical instruments resounded, the wrestler Cānūra addressed Krishna and Balarāma with the following words.

PURPORT

Cānūra could not tolerate that the audience was praising Krishna so highly. Therefore he had to say something to the two brothers.
