Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.38

bālā vayam tulya-balaih

krīdishyāmo yathocitam

bhaven niyuddham mādharmah

spriśen malla-sabhā-sadah

SYNONYMS

bālāh — young boys; vayam — We; tulya — equal; balaih — with those whose strength; krīdishyāmah — We will play; yathā ucitam — in a fitting manner; bhavet — should occur; niyuddham — the wrestling match; mā — not; adharmah — irreligion; spriśet — should touch; malla-sabhā — of the assembly in the wrestling arena; sadah — the members.

TRANSLATION

We are just young boys and should play with those of equal strength. The wrestling match must go on properly so that irreligion does not taint the respectable members of the audience.
