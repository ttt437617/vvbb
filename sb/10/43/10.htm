Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.10

tato 'bhimakham abhyetya

pānināhatya vāranam

prādravan pātayām āsa

spriśyamānah pade pade

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; abhimukham — face to face; abhyetya — coming; pāninā — with His hand; āhatya — slapping; vāranam — the elephant; prādravan — running away; pātayām āsa — He made him fall; spriśyamānah — being touched; pade pade — with each step.

TRANSLATION

Krishna then came face to face with the elephant and slapped him and ran away. Kuvalayāpīda pursued the Lord, managing to touch Him again and again with each step, but Krishna outmaneuvered the elephant and made him trip and fall.
