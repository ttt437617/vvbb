Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.24

esha vai kila devakyām

jāto nītaś ca gokulam

kālam etam vasan gūdho

vavridhe nanda-veśmani

SYNONYMS

eshah — this (Krishna); vai — certainly; kila — indeed; devakyām — from the womb of Devaki; jātah — born; nītah — brought; ca — and; gokulam — to Gokula; kālam — time; etam — this much; vasan — living; gūdhah — hidden; vavridhe — He grew up; nanda-veśmani — in the house of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

This one [Krishna] took birth from mother Devakī and was brought to Gokula, where He has remained concealed all this time, growing up in the house of King Nanda.
