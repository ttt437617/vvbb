Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.28

gopyo 'sya nitya-mudita-

hasita-prekshanam mukham

paśyantyo vividhāms tāpāms

taranti smāśramam mudā

SYNONYMS

gopyah — the young gopīs; asya — His; nitya — always; mudita — cheerful; hasita — smiling; prekshanam — whose glance; mukham — the face; paśyantyah — seeing; vividhān — of various kinds; tāpān — distress; taranti sma — transcended; aśramam — free from fatigue; mudā — happily.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs overcame all kinds of distress and experienced great happiness by seeing His face, which is always cheerful with smiling glances and ever free of fatigue.
