Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.12

sva-vikrame pratihate

kuñjarendro 'ty-amarshitah

codyamāno mahāmātraih

krishnam abhyadravad rushā

SYNONYMS

sva — his; vikrame — prowess; pratihate — being thwarted; kuñjara-indrah — the lord of elephants; ati — extreme; amarshitah — with frustrated anger; codyamānah — urged on; mahāmātraih — by the elephant-keepers; krishnam — at Krishna; abhyadravat — he charged; rushā — furiously.

TRANSLATION

His prowess foiled, the lordly elephant Kuvalayāpīda went into a frenzied rage out of frustration. But the elephant-keepers goaded him on, and he furiously charged Krishna once again.
