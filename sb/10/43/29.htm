Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.29

vadanty anena vamśo 'yam

yadoh su-bahu-viśrutah

śriyam yaśo mahatvam ca

lapsyate parirakshitah

SYNONYMS

vadanti — they say; anena — by Him; vamśah — the dynasty; ayam — this; yadoh — descending from King Yadu; su-bahu — very much; viśrutah — famous; śriyam — riches; yaśah — glory; mahatvam — power; ca — and; lapsyate — it will achieve; parirakshitah — protected on all sides.

TRANSLATION

It is said that under His full protection the Yadu dynasty will become extremely famous and attain wealth, glory and power.
