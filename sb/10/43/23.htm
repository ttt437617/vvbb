Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 43: Krishna Kills the Elephant Kuvalayāpīda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.43.23

etau bhagavatah sākshād

dharer nārāyanasya hi

avatīrnāv ihāmśena

vasudevasya veśmani

SYNONYMS

etau — these two; bhagavatah — of the Supreme Lord; sākshāt — directly; hareh — of Lord Hari; nārāyanasya — Nārāyana; hi — certainly; avatīrnau — have descended; iha — to this world; amśena — as expansions; vasudevasya — of Vasudeva; veśmani — in the home.

TRANSLATION

[The people said:] These two boys are certainly expansions of the Supreme Lord Nārāyana who have descended to this world in the home of Vasudeva.
