Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.49

catvāro 'sya bhujāh śishtā

bhavishyaty ajarāmarah

pārshada-mukhyo bhavato

na kutaścid-bhayo 'surah

SYNONYMS

catvārah — four; asya — his; bhujāh — arms; śishtāh — remaining; bhavishyati — will be; ajara — unaging; amarah — and immortal; pārshada — an associate; mukhyah — principal; bhavatah — of yourself; na kutaścit-bhayah — having no fear on any account; asurah — the demon.

TRANSLATION

This demon, who still has four arms, will be immune to old age and death, and he will serve as one of your principal attendants. Thus he will have nothing to fear on any account.
