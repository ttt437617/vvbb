Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.17

viśīryamānam sva-balam

drishtvā bāno 'ty-amarshitah

krishnam abhyadravat sańkhye

rathī hitvaiva sātyakim

SYNONYMS

viśīryamānam — being torn apart; sva — his; balam — military force; drishtvā — seeing; bānah — Bānāsura; ati — extremely; amarshitah — infuriated; krishnam — Lord Krishna; abhyadravat — he attacked; sańkhye — on the battlefield; rathī — riding on his chariot; hitvā — leaving aside; eva — indeed; sātyakim — Sātyaki.

TRANSLATION

Bānāsura was furious to see his entire military force being torn apart. Leaving his fight with Sātyaki, he charged across the battlefield on his chariot and attacked Lord Krishna.
