Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.20

tan-mātā kotarā nāma

nagnā makta-śiroruhā

puro 'vatasthe krishnasya

putra-prāna-rirakshayā

SYNONYMS

tat — his (Bānāsura's); mātā — mother; kotarā nāma — named Kotarā; nagnā — naked; mukta — loosened; śirah-ruhā — her hair; purah — in front; avatasthe — stood; krishnasya — of Krishna; putra — her son's; prāna — life; rirakshayā — hoping to save.

TRANSLATION

Just then Bānāsura's mother, Kotarā, desiring to save her son's life, appeared before Lord Krishna naked and with her hair undone.
