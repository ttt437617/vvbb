Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.14

mohayitvā tu giriśam

jrimbhanāstrena jrimbhitam

bānasya pritanām śaurir

jaghānāsi-gadeshubhih

SYNONYMS

mohayitvā — bewildering; tu — then; giriśam — Lord Śiva; jrimbhana-astrena — with a yawning weapon; jrimbhitam — made to yawn; bānasya — of Bāna; pritanām — the army; śaurih — Lord Krishna; jaghāna — struck; asi — with His sword; gadā — club; ishubhih — and arrows.

TRANSLATION

After bewildering Lord Śiva by making him yawn with a yawning weapon, Lord Krishna proceeded to strike down Bānāsura's army with His sword, club and arrows.
