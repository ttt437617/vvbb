Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.51

akshauhinyā parivritam

su-vāsah-samalańkritam

sa-patnīkam puras-kritya

yayau rudrānumoditah

SYNONYMS

akshauhinyā — by a full military division; parivritam — surrounded; su — fine; vāsah — whose clothing; samalańkritam — and adorned with ornaments; sa-patnīkam — Aniruddha with His wife; purah-kritya — putting in front; yayau — He (Lord Krishna) went; rudra — by Lord Śiva; anumoditah — given leave.

TRANSLATION

At the front of the party Lord Krishna then placed Aniruddha and His bride, both beautifully adorned with fine clothes and ornaments, and surrounded them with a full military division. Thus Lord Krishna took His leave of Lord Śiva and departed.
