Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.16

kumbhānda-kūpakarnaś ca

petatur mushalārditau

dudruvus tad-anīkani

hata-nāthāni sarvatah

SYNONYMS

kumbhānda-kūpakarnah ca — Kumbhānda and Kūpakarna; petatuh — fell; mushala — by the club (of Lord Balarāma); arditau — distressed; dudruvuh — fled; tat — their; anīkāni — armies; hata — killed; nāthāni — whose leaders; sarvatah — in all directions.

TRANSLATION

Kumbhānda and Kūpakarna, tormented by Lord Balarāma's club, fell down dead. When the soldiers of these two demons saw that their leaders had been killed, they scattered in all directions.
