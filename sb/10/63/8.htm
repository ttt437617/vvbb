Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.8

kumbhānda-kūpakarnābhyām

balena saha samyugah

sāmbasya bāna-putrena

bānena saha sātyakeh

SYNONYMS

kumbhānda-kūpakarnābhyām — by Kumbhānda and Kūpakarna; balena saha — with Lord Balarāma; samyugah — a fight; sāmbasya — of Sāmba; bāna-putrena — with the son of Bāna; bānena saha — with Bāna; sātyakeh — of Sātyaki.

TRANSLATION

Lord Balarāma fought with Kumbhānda and Kūpakarna, Sāmba with Bāna's son, and Sātyaki with Bāna.
