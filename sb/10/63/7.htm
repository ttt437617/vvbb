Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.7

āsīt su-tumulam yuddham

adbhutam roma-harshanam

krishna-śańkarayo rājan

pradyumna-guhayor api

SYNONYMS

āsīt — there occurred; su-tumulam — very tumultuous; yuddham — a fight; adbhutam — astonishing; roma-harshanam — causing bodily hair to stand on end; krishna-śańkarayoh — between Lord Krishna and Lord Śiva; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); pradyumna-guhayoh — between Pradyumna and Kārtikeya; api — also.

TRANSLATION

A most astonishing, tumultuous and hair-raising battle then commenced, with Lord Krishna matched against Lord Śańkara, and Pradyumna against Kārtikeya.
