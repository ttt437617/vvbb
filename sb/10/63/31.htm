Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.31

tato bāhu-sahasrena

nānāyudha-dharo 'surah

mumoca parama-kruddho

bānāmś cakrāyudhe nripa

SYNONYMS

tatah — thereupon; bāhu — with his arms; sahasrena — one thousand; nānā — numerous; āyudha — weapons; dharah — carrying; asurah — the demon; mumoca — released; parama — supremely; kruddhah — angry; bānān — arrows; cakra-āyudhe — at Him whose weapon is the disc; nripa — O King (Parīkshit).

TRANSLATION

Carrying numerous weapons in his thousand hands, O King, the terribly infuriated demon shot many arrows at Lord Krishna, the carrier of the disc weapon.
