Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.1

śriī-śuka uvāca

apaśyatām cāniruddham

tad-bandhūnām ca bhārata

catvāro vārshikā māsā

vyatīyur anuśocatām

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; apaśyatām — who did not see; ca — and; aniruddham — Aniruddha; tat — His; bandhūnām — for the relatives; ca — and; bhārata — O descendant of Bharata (Parīkshit Mahārāja); catvārah — four; vārshikah — of the rainy season; māsāh — the months; vyatīyuh — passed; anuśocatām — who were lamenting.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O descendant of Bharata, the relatives of Aniruddha, not seeing Him return, continued to lament as the four rainy months passed.
