Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.47

avadhyo 'yam mamāpy esha

vairocani-suto 'surah

prahrādāya varo datto

na vadhyo me tavānvayah

SYNONYMS

avadhyah — not to be killed; ayam — he; mama — by Me; api — indeed; eshah — this; vairocani-sutah — son of Vairocani (Bali); asurah — demon; prahrādāya — to Prahlāda; varah — the benediction; dattah — given; na vadhyah — not to be killed; me — by Me; tava — your; anvayah — descendants.

TRANSLATION

I will not kill this demonic son of Vairocani, for I gave Prahlāda Mahārāja the benediction that I would not kill any of his descendants.
