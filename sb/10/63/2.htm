Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.2

nāradāt tad upākarnya

vārtām baddhasya karma ca

prayayuh śonita-puram

vrishnayah krishna-daivatāh

SYNONYMS

nāradāt — from Nārada; tat — that; upākarnya — hearing; vārtām — news; baddhasya — about Him who was captured; karma — actions; ca — and; prayayuh — they went; śonita-puram — to Śonitapura; vrishnayah — the Vrishnis; krishna — Lord Krishna; daivatāh — having as their worshipable Deity.

TRANSLATION

After hearing from Nārada the news of Aniruddha's deeds and His capture, the Vrishnis, who worshiped Lord Krishna as their personal Deity, went to Śonitapura.
