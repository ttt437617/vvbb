Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.32

tasyāsyato 'strāny asakric

cakrena kshura-neminā

ciccheda bhagavān bāhūn

śākhā iva vanaspateh

SYNONYMS

tasya — of him; asyatah — who was throwing; astrāni — weapons; asakrit — repeatedly; cakrena — with His disc; kshura — razor-sharp; neminā — whose circumference; ciccheda — cut off; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; bāhūn — the arms; śākhāh — branches; iva — as if; vanaspateh — of a tree.

TRANSLATION

As Bāna continued hurling weapons at Him, the Supreme Lord began using His razor-sharp cakra to cut off Bānāsura's arms as if they were tree branches.
