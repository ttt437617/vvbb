Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 63: Lord Krishna Fights with Bānāsura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.63.48

darpopaśamanāyāsya

pravriknā bāhavo mayā

sūditam ca balam bhūri

yac ca bhārāyitam bhuvah

SYNONYMS

darpa — the false pride; upaśamanāya — for subduing; asya — his; pravriknāh — severed; bāhavah — arms; mayā — by Me; sūditam — slain; ca — and; balam — the military force; bhūri — huge; yat — which; ca — and; bhārāyitam — having become a burden; bhuvah — for the earth.

TRANSLATION

It was to subdue Bānāsura's false pride that I severed his arms. And I slew his mighty army because it had become a burden upon the earth.
