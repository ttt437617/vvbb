Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.16

he rāmāgaccha tātāśu

sānujah kula-nandana

prātar eva kritāhāras

tad bhavān bhoktum arhati

SYNONYMS

he rāma — my dear son Balarāma; āgaccha — please come here; tāta — my dear darling; āśu — immediately; sa-anujah — with Your younger brother; kula-nandana — the great hope of our family; prātah eva — certainly in the morning; krita-āhārah — have taken Your breakfast; tat — therefore; bhavān — You; bhoktum — to eat something more; arhati — deserve.

TRANSLATION

My dear Baladeva, best of our family, please come immediately with Your younger brother, Krishna. You both ate in the morning, and now You ought to eat something more.
