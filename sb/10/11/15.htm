Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.15

krishna krishnāravindāksha

tāta ehi stanam piba

alam vihāraih kshut-kshāntah

krīdā-śrānto 'si putraka

SYNONYMS

krishna krishna aravinda-aksha — O Krishna, my son, lotus-eyed Krishna; tāta — O darling; ehi — come here; stanam — the milk of my breast; piba — drink; alam vihāraih — after this there is no necessity of playing; kshut-kshāntah — tired because of hunger; krīdā-śrāntah — fatigued from playing; asi — You must be; putraka — O my son.

TRANSLATION

Mother Yaśodā said: My dear son Krishna, lotus-eyed Krishna, come here and drink the milk of my breast. My dear darling, You must be very tired because of hunger and the fatigue of playing so long. There is no need to play any more.
