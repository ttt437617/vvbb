Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.26

yan na mriyeta drumayor

antaram prāpya bālakah

asāv anyatamo vāpi

tad apy acyuta-rakshanam

SYNONYMS

yat — then again; na mriyeta — did not die; drumayoh antaram — between the two trees; prāpya — although He was between; bālakah asau — that child, Krishna; anyatamah — another child; vā api — or; tat api acyuta-rakshanam — in that case also, He was saved by the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Even the other day, neither Krishna nor any of His playmates died from the falling of the two trees, although the children were near the trees or even between them. This also is to be considered the mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
