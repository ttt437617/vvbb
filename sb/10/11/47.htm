Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.47

te tatra dadriśur bālā

mahā-sattvam avasthitam

tatrasur vajra-nirbhinnam

gireh śrińgam iva cyutam

SYNONYMS

te — they; tatra — there; dadriśuh — observed; bālāh — all the boys; mahā-sattvam — a gigantic body; avasthitam — situated; tatrasuh — became afraid; vajra-nirbhinnam — broken by a thunderbolt; gireh śrińgam — the peak of a mountain; iva — like; cyutam — fallen there.

TRANSLATION

Right by the reservoir, the boys saw a gigantic body resembling a mountain peak broken and struck down by a thunderbolt. They were afraid even to see such a huge living being.
