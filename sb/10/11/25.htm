Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.25

cakra-vātena nīto 'yam

daityena vipadam viyat

śilāyām patitas tatra

paritrātah sureśvaraih

SYNONYMS

cakra-vātena — by the demon in the shape of a whirlwind (Trināvarta); nītah ayam — Krishna was taken away; daityena — by the demon; vipadam — dangerous; viyat — to the sky; śilāyām — on a slab of stone; patitah — fallen; tatra — there; paritrātah — was saved; sura-īśvaraih — by the mercy of Lord Vishnu or His associates.

TRANSLATION

Then again, the demon Trināvarta, in the form of a whirlwind, took the child away into the dangerous sky to kill Him, but the demon fell down onto a slab of stone. In that case also, by the mercy of Lord Vishnu or His associates, the child was saved.
