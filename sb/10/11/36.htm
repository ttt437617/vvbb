Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 11: The Childhood Pastimes of Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.11.36

vrindāvanam govardhanam

yamunā-pulināni ca

vīkshyāsīd uttamā prītī

rāma-mādhavayor nripa

SYNONYMS

vrindāvanam — the place known as Vrindāvana; govardhanam — along with Govardhana Hill; yamunā-pulināni ca — and the banks of the River Yamunā; vīkshya — seeing this situation; āsīt — remained or was enjoyed; uttamā prītī — first-class pleasure; rāma-mādhavayoh — of Krishna and Balarāma; nripa — O King Parīkshit.

TRANSLATION

O King Parīkshit, when Rāma and Krishna saw Vrindāvana, Govardhana and the banks of the River Yamunā, They both enjoyed great pleasure.
