Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 53: Krishna Kidnaps Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.53.22

bhīshma-kanyā varārohā

kāńkshanty āgamanam hareh

pratyāpattim apaśyantī

dvijasyācintayat tadā

SYNONYMS

bhīshma-kanyā — the daughter of Bhīshmaka; vara-ārohā — having lovely hips; kāńkshantī — waiting for; āgamanam — the arrival; hareh — of Krishna; pratyāpattim — the return; apaśyantī — not seeing; dvijasya — of the brāhmana; acintayat — thought; tadā — then.

TRANSLATION

The lovely daughter of Bhīshmaka anxiously awaited the arrival of Krishna, but when she did not see the brāhmana return she thought as follows.
