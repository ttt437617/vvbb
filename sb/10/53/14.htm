Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 53: Krishna Kidnaps Rukminī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.53.14

evam cedi-patī rājā

damaghoshah sutāya vai

kārayām āsa mantra-jñaih

sarvam abhyudayocitam

SYNONYMS

evam — in the same way; cedi-patih — the lord of Cedi; rājā damaghoshah — King Damaghosha; sutāya — for his son (Śiśupāla); vai — indeed; kārayām āsa — had done; mantra-jñaih — by expert knowers of mantras; sarvam — everything; abhyudaya — to his prosperity; ucitam — conducive.

TRANSLATION

Rājā Damaghosha, lord of Cedi, had also engaged brāhmanas expert in chanting mantras to perform all rituals necessary to assure his son's prosperity.
