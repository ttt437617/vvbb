Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 20: The Rainy Season and Autumn in Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.20 Summary

To enhance the description of Lord Krishna's pastimes, Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī describes in this chapter the beauty of Vrindāvana during autumn and the rainy season. In the course of his presentation he gives various charming instructions in metaphorical terms.
