Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 20: The Rainy Season and Autumn in Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.20.2

gopa-vriddhāś ca gopyaś ca

tad upākarnya vismitāh

menire deva-pravarau

krishna-rāmau vrajam gatau

SYNONYMS

gopa-vriddhāh — the elder cowherd men; ca — and; gopyah — the cowherd ladies; ca — also; tat — that; upākarnya — hearing; vismitāh — surprised; menire — they considered; deva-pravarau — two eminent demigods; krishna-rāmau — the brothers Krishna and Balarāma; vrajam — to Vrindāvana; gatau — come.

TRANSLATION

The elder cowherd men and ladies were amazed to hear this account, and they concluded that Krishna and Balarāma must be exalted demigods who had appeared in Vrindāvana.
