Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.32

sa tam grihītvā prapador

bhrāmayitvaika-pāninā

cikshepa trina-rājāgre

bhrāmana-tyakta-jīvitam

SYNONYMS

sah — He; tam — him; grihītvā — seizing; prapadoh — by the hooves; bhrāmayitvā — whirling around; eka-pāninā — with a single hand; cikshepa — He threw; trina-rāja-agre — into the top of a palm tree; bhrāmana — by the whirling; tyakta — giving up; jīvitam — his life.

TRANSLATION

Lord Balarāma seized Dhenuka by his hooves, whirled him about with one hand and threw him into the top of a palm tree. The violent wheeling motion killed the demon.
