Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.46

janany-upahritam prāśya

svādy annam upalālitau

samviśya vara-śayyāyām

sukham sushupatur vraje

SYNONYMS

jananī — by Their mothers; upahritam — offered; prāśya — eating fully; svādu — delicious; annam — food; upalālitau — being pampered; samviśya — entering; vara — excellent; śayyāyām — upon bedding; sukham — happily; sushupatuh — the two of Them slept; vraje — in Vraja.

TRANSLATION

After dining sumptuously on the delicious food given Them by Their mothers and being pampered in various ways, the two brothers lay down upon Their excellent beds and happily went to sleep in the village of Vraja.
