Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.37

tāms tān āpatatah krishno

rāmaś ca nripa līlayā

grihīta-paścāc-caranān

prāhinot trina-rājasu

SYNONYMS

tān tān — all of them, one by one; āpatatah — attacking; krishnah — Lord Krishna; rāmah — Lord Balarāma; ca — and; nripa — O King; līlayā — easily; grihīta — seizing; paścāt-caranān — their hind legs; prāhinot — threw; trina-rājasu — into the palm trees.

TRANSLATION

O King, as the demons attacked, Krishna and Balarāma easily seized them one after another by their hind legs and threw them all into the tops of the palm trees.
