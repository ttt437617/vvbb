Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.51

te sampratīta-smritayah

samutthāya jalāntikāt

āsan su-vismitāh sarve

vīkshamānāh parasparam

SYNONYMS

te — they; sampratīta — regaining perfectly; smritayah — their memory; samutthāya — rising up; jala-antikāt — from out of the water; āsan — they became; su-vismitāh — very surprised; sarve — all; vīkshamānāh — looking; parasparam — at one another.

TRANSLATION

Regaining their full consciousness, the cows and boys stood up out of the water and began to look at one another in great astonishment.
