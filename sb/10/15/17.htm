Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.17

pāda-samvāhanam cakruh

kecit tasya mahātmanah

apare hata-pāpmāno

vyajanaih samavījayan

SYNONYMS

pāda-samvāhanam — the massaging of the feet; cakruh — did; kecit — some of them; tasya — of Him; mahā-ātmanah — great souls; apare — others; hata-pāpmānah — who were free from all sins; vyajanaih — with fans; samavījayan — perfectly fanned Him.

TRANSLATION

Some of the cowherd boys, who were all great souls, would then massage His lotus feet, and others, qualified by being free of all sin, would expertly fan the Supreme Lord.

PURPORT

The word samavījayan indicates that the cowherd boys fanned the Lord very carefully and expertly, creating gentle and cooling breezes.
