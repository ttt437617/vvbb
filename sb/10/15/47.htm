Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 15: The Killing of Dhenuka, the Ass Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.15.47

evam sa bhagavān krishno

vrindāvana-carah kvacit

yayau rāmam rite rājan

kālindīm sakhibhir vritah

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; sah — He; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishnah — Krishna; vrindāvana-carah — wandering, and acting, in Vrindāvana; kvacit — once; yayau — went; rāmam rite — without Lord Balarāma; rājan — O King Parīkshit; kālindīm — to the river Yamunā; sakhibhih — by His friends; vritah — surrounded.

TRANSLATION

O King, the Supreme Lord Krishna thus wandered about the Vrindāvana area, performing His pastimes. Once, surrounded by His boyfriends, He went without Balarāma to the Yamunā River.
