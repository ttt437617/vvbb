Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 4: The Atrocities of King Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.4.32

kim udyamaih karishyanti

devāh samara-bhīravah

nityam udvigna-manaso

jyā-ghoshair dhanushas tava

SYNONYMS

kim — what; udyamaih — by their endeavors; karishyanti — will do; devāh — all the demigods; samara-bhīravah — who are afraid of fighting; nityam — always; udvigna-manasah — with agitated minds; jyā-ghoshaih — by the sound of the string; dhanushah — of the bow; tava — your.

TRANSLATION

The demigods always fear the sound of your bowstring. They are constantly in anxiety, afraid of fighting. Therefore, what can they do by their endeavors to harm you?
