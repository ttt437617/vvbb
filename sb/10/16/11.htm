Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 16: Krishna Chastises the Serpent Kāliya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.16.11

gāvo vrishā vatsataryah

krandamānāh su-duhkhitāh

krishne nyastekshanā bhītā

rudantya iva tasthire

SYNONYMS

gāvah — the cows; vrishāh — the bulls; vatsataryah — the female calves; krandamānāh — crying loudly; su-duhkhitāh — very much distressed; krishne — upon Lord Krishna; nyasta — fixed; īkshanāh — their sight; bhītāh — fearful; rudantyah — crying; iva — as if; tasthire — they stood still.

TRANSLATION

The cows, bulls and female calves, in great distress, called out piteously to Krishna. Fixing their eyes on Him, they stood still in fear, as if ready to cry but too shocked to shed tears.
