Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 16: Krishna Chastises the Serpent Kāliya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.16.59

bhavān hi kāranam tatra

sarva-jño jagad-īśvarah

anugraham nigraham vā

manyase tad vidhehi nah

SYNONYMS

bhavān — Your good self; hi — certainly; kāranam — the cause; tatra — in that matter (the removal of illusion); sarva-jñah — the knower of everything; jagat-īśvarah — the supreme controller of the universe; anugraham — favor; nigraham — punishment; vā — or; manyase — (whatever) You consider; tat — that; vidhehi — arrange; nah — for us.

TRANSLATION

O Lord, since You are the omniscient Lord of the universe, You are the actual cause of freedom from illusion. Please arrange for us whatever You consider proper, whether it be mercy or punishment.
