Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 16: Krishna Chastises the Serpent Kāliya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.16.53

vidhehi te kińkarīnām

anushtheyam tavājñayā

yac-chraddhayānutishthan vai

mucyate sarvato bhayāt

SYNONYMS

vidhehi — please order; te — Your; kińkarīnām — by the maidservants; anushtheyam — what should be done; tava — Your; ājñayā — by the command; yat — which; śraddhayā — with faith; anutishthan — executing; vai — certainly; mucyate — one will become freed; sarvatah — from all; bhayāt — fear.

TRANSLATION

Now please tell us, Your maidservants, what we should do. Certainly anyone who faithfully executes Your order is automatically freed from all fear.

PURPORT

The surrender of Kāliya's wives was now complete, and Lord Krishna immediately gave them His mercy, as described in the following verses.
