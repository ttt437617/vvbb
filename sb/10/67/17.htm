Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 67: Lord Balarāma Slays Dvivida Gorilla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.67.17

dvivido 'pi mahā-vīryah

śālam udyamya pāninā

abhyetya tarasā tena

balam mūrdhany atādayat

SYNONYMS

dvividah — Dvivida; api — also; mahā — great; vīryah — whose potency; śālam — a śāla tree; udyamya — lifting up; pāninā — with his hand; abhyetya — approaching; tarasā — swiftly; tena — with it; balam — Lord Balarāma; mūrdhani — on the head; atādayat — he struck.

TRANSLATION

Mighty Dvivida also came forward to do battle. Uprooting a śāla tree with one hand, he rushed toward Balarāma and struck Him on the head with the tree trunk.
