Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 59: The Killing of the Demon Naraka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.59.28

ajāya janayitre 'sya

brahmane 'nanta-śaktaye

parāvarātman bhūtātman

paramātman namo 'stu te

SYNONYMS

ajāya — to the unborn; janayitre — the progenitor; asya — of this (universe); brahmane — the Absolute; ananta — unlimited; śaktaye — whose energies; para — of the superior; avara — and the inferior; ātman — O Soul; bhūta — of the material creation; ātman — O Soul; parama-ātman — O Supreme Soul, who are all-pervading; namah — obeisances; astu — may there be; te — unto You.

TRANSLATION

Obeisances unto You of unlimited energies, the unborn progenitor of this universe, the Absolute. O Soul of the high and the low, O Soul of the created elements, O all-pervading Supreme Soul, obeisances unto You.
