Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 59: The Killing of the Demon Naraka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.59.25

bhūmir uvāca

namas te deva-deveśa

śańkha-cakra-gadā-dhara

bhaktecchopātta-rūpāya

paramātman namo 'stu te

SYNONYMS

bhūmih uvāca — the earth-goddess said; namah — obeisances; te — unto You; deva-deva — of the lords of the demigods; īśa — O Lord; śańkha — of the conchshell; cakra — disc; gadā — and club; dhara — O holder; bhakta — of Your devotees; icchā — by the desire; upātta — who have assumed; rūpāya — Your forms; parama-ātman — O Supreme Soul; namah — obeisances; astu — let there be; te — unto You.

TRANSLATION

Goddess Bhūmi said: Obeisances unto You, O Lord of the chief demigods, O holder of the conchshell, disc and club. O Supreme Soul within the heart, You assume Your various forms to fulfill Your devotees' desires. Obeisances unto You.
