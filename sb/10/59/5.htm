Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 59: The Killing of the Demon Naraka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.59.5

śańkha-nādena yantrāni

hridayāni manasvinām

prākāram gadayā gurvyā

nirbibheda gadādharah

SYNONYMS

śańkha — of His conchshell; nādena — with the resounding; yantrāni — the mystic talismans; hridayāni — the hearts; manasvinām — of the brave warriors; prākāram — the ramparts; gadayā — with His club; gurvyā — heavy; nirbibheda — He broke; gadādharah — Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

With the sound of His conchshell Lord Gadādhara then shattered the magic seals of the fortress, along with the hearts of its brave defenders, and with His heavy club He demolished the surrounding earthen ramparts.
