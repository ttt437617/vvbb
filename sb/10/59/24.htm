Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 59: The Killing of the Demon Naraka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.59.24

astaushīd atha viśveśam

devī deva-varārcitam

prāñjalih pranatā rājan

bhakti-pravanayā dhiyā

SYNONYMS

astaushīt — praised; atha — then; viśva — of the universe; īśam — the Lord; devī — the goddess; deva — of demigods; vara — by the best; arcitam — who is worshiped; prāñjalih — folding her palms; pranatā — bowed down; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); bhakti — of devotion; pravanayā — full; dhiyā — with a mentality.

TRANSLATION

O King, after bowing down to Him and then standing with joined palms, the goddess, her mind filled with devotion, began to praise the Lord of the universe, whom the best of demigods worship.
