Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 70: Lord Krishna's Daily Activities

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.70.42

tasmin deva kratu-vare

bhavantam vai surādayah

didrikshavah sameshyanti

rājānaś ca yaśasvinah

SYNONYMS

tasmin — in that; deva — O Lord; kratu — of sacrifices; vare — best; bhavantam — You; vai — indeed; sura — demigods; ādayah — and other exalted personalities; didrikshavah — eager to see; sameshyanti — will all come; rājānah — kings; ca — also; yaśasvinah — glorious.

TRANSLATION

O Lord, exalted demigods and glorious kings, eager to see You, will all come to that best of sacrifices.

PURPORT

The ācāryas explain that Nārada here means to say that since all the great personalities will come especially to see Lord Krishna, He should also come to that sacrifice.
