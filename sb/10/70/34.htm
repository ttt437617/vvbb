Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 70: Lord Krishna's Daily Activities

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.70.34

sabhājayitvā vidhi-vat

kritāsana-parigraham

babhāshe sunritair vākyaih

śraddhayā tarpayan munim

SYNONYMS

sabhājayitvā — worshiping; vidhi-vat — according to scriptural injunctions; krita — to him (Nārada) who had done; āsana — of a seat; parigraham — acceptance; babhāshe — He (Lord Krishna) spoke; su-nritaih — truthful and pleasing; vākyaih — with words; śraddhayā — with reverence; tarpayan — gratifying; munim — the sage.

TRANSLATION

After Nārada had accepted the seat offered to him, Lord Krishna honored the sage according to scriptural injunctions and, gratifying him with His reverence, spoke the following truthful and pleasing words.
