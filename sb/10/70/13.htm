Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 70: Lord Krishna's Daily Activities

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.70.13

samvibhajyāgrato viprān

srak-tāmbūlānulepanaih

suhridah prakritīr dārān

upāyuńkta tatah svayam

SYNONYMS

samvibhajya — distributing; agratah — first; viprān — to the brāhmanas; srak — garlands; tāmbūla — betel nut; anulepanaih — and sandalwood paste; suhridah — to His friends; prakritīh — to His ministers; dārān — to His wives; upāyuńkta — He partook; tatah — then; svayam — Himself.

TRANSLATION

After first distributing flower garlands, pān and sandalwood paste to the brāhmanas, He would give these gifts to His friends, ministers and wives, and finally He would partake of them Himself.
