Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 27: Lord Indra and Mother Surabhi Offer Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.27.13

tvayeśānugrihīto 'smi

dhvasta-stambho vrithodyamah

īśvaram gurum ātmānam

tvām aham śaranam gatah

SYNONYMS

tvayā — by You; īśa — O Lord; anugrihītah — shown mercy; asmi — I am; dhvasta — shattered; stambhah — my false pride; vrithā — fruitless; udyamah — my attempt; īśvaram — the Supreme Lord; gurum — the spiritual master; ātmānam — the true Self; tvām — to You; aham — I; śaranam — for shelter; gatah — have come.

TRANSLATION

O Lord, You have shown mercy to me by shattering my false pride and defeating my attempt [to punish Vrindāvana]. To You, the Supreme Lord, spiritual master and Supreme Soul, I have now come for shelter.
