Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 27: Lord Indra and Mother Surabhi Offer Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.27.12

mayedam bhagavan goshtha-

nāśāyāsāra-vāyubhih

ceshtitam vihate yajñe

māninā tīvra-manyunā

SYNONYMS

mayā — by me; idam — this; bhagavan — O Lord; goshtha — of Your cowherd community; nāśāya — for the destruction; āsāra — by hard rain; vāyubhih — and wind; ceshtitam — enacted; vihate — when it was disrupted; yajñe — my sacrifice; māninā — (by me) who was falsely proud; tīvra — fierce; manyunā — whose anger.

TRANSLATION

My dear Lord, when my sacrifice was disrupted I became fiercely angry because of false pride. Thus I tried to destroy Your cowherd community with severe rain and wind.
