Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 52: Rukminī's Message to Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.52.27

dvārakām sa samabhyetya

pratīhāraih praveśitah

apaśyad ādyam purusham

āsīnam kāñcanāsane

SYNONYMS

dvārakām — at Dvārakā; sah — he (the brāhmana); samabhyetya — arriving; pratīhāraih — by the gatekeepers; praveśitah — brought inside; apaśyat — he saw; ādyam — the original; purusham — Supreme Person; āsīnam — seated; kāñcana — golden; āsane — on a throne.

TRANSLATION

Upon reaching Dvārakā, the brāhmana was brought inside by the gatekeepers and saw the primeval Personality of Godhead sitting on a golden throne.
