Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 52: Rukminī's Message to Lord Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.52.22

rukmy agrajo rukmaratho

rukmabāhur anantarah

rukmakeśo rukmamālī

rukminy eshā svasā satī

SYNONYMS

rukmī — Rukmī; agra-jah — the first-born; rukma-rathah rukmabāhuh — Rukmaratha and Rukmabāhu; anantarah — following him; rukma-keśah rukma-mālī — Rukmakeśa and Rukmamālī; rukminī — Rukminī; eshā — she; svasā — sister; satī — of saintly character.

TRANSLATION

Rukmī was the first-born son, followed by Rukmaratha, Rukmabāhu, Rukmakeśa and Rukmamālī. Their sister was the exalted Rukminī.
