Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

evam carcita-sańkalpo

bhagavān madhusūdanah

āsasādātha canūram

mushttikam rohinī-sutah

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; carcita — fixing; sańkalpah — His determination; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; madhusūdanah — Krishna; āsasāda — confronted; atha — then; cānūram — Cānūra; mushtikam — Mushtika; rohinī-sutah — the son of Rohinī, Lord Balarāma.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Thus addressed, Lord Krishna made up His mind to accept the challenge. He paired off with Cānūra, and Lord Balarāma with Mushtika.
