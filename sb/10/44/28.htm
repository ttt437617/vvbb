Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.28

cānūre mushtike kūte

śale tośalake hate

śeshāh pradudruvur mallāh

sarve prāna-parīpsavah

SYNONYMS

cānūre mushtike kūte — Cānūra, Mushtika and Kūta; śale tośalake — Śala and Tośala; hate — being killed; śeshāh — those remaining; pradudruvuh — ran away; mallāh — wrestlers; sarve — all; prāna — their lives; parīpsavah — hoping to save.

TRANSLATION

Cānūra, Mushtika, Kūta, Śala and Tośala having been killed, the remaining wrestlers all fled for their lives.
