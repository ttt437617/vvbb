Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.48

sarveshām iha bhūtānām

esha hi prabhavāpyayah

goptā ca tad-avadhyāyī

na kvacit sukham edhate

SYNONYMS

sarveshām — of all; iha — in this world; bhūtānām — living beings; eshah — this (Śrī Krishna); hi — certainly; prabhava — the origin; apyayah — and disappearance; goptā — the maintainer; ca — and; tat — of Him; avadhyāyī — one who is neglectful; na kvacit — never; sukham — happily; edhate — prospers.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna causes the appearance and disappearance of all beings in this world, and He is their maintainer as well. One who disrespects Him can never prosper happily.
