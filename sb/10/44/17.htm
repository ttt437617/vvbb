Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.17

evam prabhāshamānāsu

strīshu yogeśvaro harih

śatrum hantum manaś cakre

bhagavān bharatarshabha

SYNONYMS

evam — in this manner; prabhāshamānāsu — while they were speaking; strīshu — the women; yoga-īśvarah — the master of all mystic power; harih — Lord Krishna; śatrum — His enemy; hantum — to kill; manah cakre — made up His mind; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; bharata-rishabha — O hero of the Bhāratas.

TRANSLATION

[Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued:] As the women spoke thus, O hero of the Bhāratas, Lord Krishna, the master of all mystic power, made up His mind to kill His opponent.
