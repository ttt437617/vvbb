Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.27

tarhy eva hi śalah krishna-

prapadāhata-śīrshakah

dvidhā vidīrnas tośalaka

ubhāv api nipetatuh

SYNONYMS

tarhi eva — and then; hi — indeed; śalah — the wrestler Śala; krishna — of Lord Krishna; prapada — by the toes; āhata — struck; śīrshakah — his head; dvidhā — in two; vidīrnah — torn; tośalaka — Tośala; ubhau api — both of them; nipetatuh — fell down.

TRANSLATION

Then Krishna struck the wrestler Śala in the head with His toes and tore him in half. The Lord dealt with Tośala in the same way, and both wrestlers fell down dead.
