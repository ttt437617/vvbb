Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.49

śrī-śuka uvāca

rāja-yoshita āśvāsya

bhagavān loka-bhāvanah

yām āhur laukikīm samsthām

hatānām samakārayat

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; rāja — of the King (and his brothers); yoshitah — the wives; āśvāsya — consoling; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; loka — of all the worlds; bhāvanah — the sustainer; yām — which; āhuh — they (Vedic authorities) enjoin; laukikīm samsthām — funeral rites; hatānām — for the deceased; samakārayat — -He arranged to be performed.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: After consoling the royal ladies, Lord Krishna, sustainer of all the worlds, arranged for the prescribed funeral rites to be performed.
