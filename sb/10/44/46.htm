Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 44: The Killing of Kamsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.44.46

tvayā virahitā patyā

purīyam purusharshabha

na śobhate vayam iva

nivrittotsava-mańgalā

SYNONYMS

tvayā — of you; virahitā — bereft; patyā — the master; purī — the city; iyam — this; purusha — of men; rishabha — O most heroic one; na śobhate — does not appear beautiful; vayam — us; iva — just like; nivritta — ceased; utsava — festivity; mańgalā — and auspiciousness.

TRANSLATION

O great hero among men, bereft of you, its master, this city has lost its beauty, just as we have, and all festivity and good fortune within it have come to an end.
