Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 42: The Breaking of the Sacrificial Bow

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.42.34

teshu paurā jānapadā

brahma-kshatra-purogamāh

yathopajosham viviśū

rājānaś ca kritāsanāh

SYNONYMS

teshu — upon these (platforms); paurāh — the city-dwellers; jānapadāh — and the people of the suburbs; brahma — by the brāhmanas; kshatra — and the kshatriyas; purah-gamāh — headed; yathā-upajosham — as suited their comfort; viviśuh — came and sat; rājānah — the kings; ca — also; krita — given; asanāh — special seats.

TRANSLATION

The city-dwellers and residents of the outlying districts, led by brāhmanas and kshatriyas, came and sat down comfortably in the galleries. The royal guests received special seats.
