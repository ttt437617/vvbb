Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 42: The Breaking of the Sacrificial Bow

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.42.37

cānūro mushtikah kūtah

śalas tośala eva ca

ta āsedur upasthānam

valgu-vādya-praharshitāh

SYNONYMS

cānūrah mushtikah kūtah — the wrestlers Cānūra, Mushtika and Kūta; śalah tośalah — Śala and Tośala; eva ca — also; te — they; āseduh — sat down; upasthānam — on the mat of the wrestling ring; valgu — pleasing; vādya — by the music; praharshitāh — enthused.

TRANSLATION

Enthused by the pleasing music, Canura, Mushtika, Kūta, Śala and Tośala sat down on the wrestling mat.
