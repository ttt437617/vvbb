Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 42: The Breaking of the Sacrificial Bow

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.42.19

tad-rakshinah sānucaram

kupitā ātatāyinah

grihītu-kāmā āvavrur

grihyatām vadhyatām iti

SYNONYMS

tat — its; rakshinah — guards; sa — along with; anucaram — His companions; kupitāh — angered; ātatāyinah — holding weapons; grihītu — to catch; kāmāh — wanting; āvavruh — surrounded; grihyatām — seize Him; vadhyatām — kill Him; iti — thus saying.

TRANSLATION

The enraged guards then took up their weapons and, wanting to seize Krishna and His companions, surrounded them and shouted, "Grab Him! Kill Him!"
