Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 42: The Breaking of the Sacrificial Bow

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.42.22

tayos tad adbhutam vīryam

niśāmya pura-vāsinah

tejah prāgalbhyam rūpam ca

menire vibudhottamau

SYNONYMS

tayoh — of Them; tat — that; adbhutam — amazing; vīryam — heroic deed; niśāmya — seeing; pura-vāsinah — the residents of the city; tejah — Their strength; prāgalbhyam — boldness; rūpam — beauty; ca — and; menire — they considered; vibudha — of demigods; uttamau — two of the best.

TRANSLATION

Having witnessed the amazing deed Krishna and Balarāma had performed, and seeing Their strength, boldness and beauty, the people of the city thought They must be two prominent demigods.
