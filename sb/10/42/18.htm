Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 42: The Breaking of the Sacrificial Bow

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.42.18

dhanusho bhajyamānasya

śabdah kham rodasī diśah

pūrayām āsa yam śrutvā

kamsas trāsam upāgamat

SYNONYMS

dhanushah — of the bow; bhajyamānasya — which was breaking; śabdah — the sound; kham — the earth; rodasī — the sky; diśah — and all the directions; pūrayām āsa — filled; yam — which; śrutvā — hearing; kamsah — King Kamsa; trāsam — fear; upāgamat — experienced.

TRANSLATION

The sound of the bow's breaking filled the earth and sky in all directions. Upon hearing it, Kamsa was struck with terror.
