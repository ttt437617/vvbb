Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 45: Krishna Rescues His Teacher's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.45.39

tam āha bhagavān āśu

guru-putrah pradīyatām

yo 'sāv iha tvayā grasto

bālako mahatorminā

SYNONYMS

tam — to him; āha — said; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; āśu — quickly; guru — of My spiritual master; putrah — the son; pradīyatām — must be presented; yah — who; asau — he; iha — in this place; tvayā — by you; grastah — seized; bālakah — a young boy; mahatā — mighty; ūrminā — by your wave.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Lord Krishna addressed the lord of the ocean: Let the son of My guru be presented at once — the one you seized here with your mighty waves.
