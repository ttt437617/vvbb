Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 45: Krishna Rescues His Teacher's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.45.21

pitar yuvābhyām snigdhābhyām

poshitau lālitau bhriśam

pitror abhyadhikā prītir

ātmajeshv ātmano 'pi hi

SYNONYMS

pitah — O Father; yuvābhyām — by you two; snigdhābhyām — affectionate; poshitau — maintained; lālitau — coddled; bhriśam — thoroughly; pitroh — for parents; abhyadhikā — greater; prītih — love; ātmajeshu — for their children; ātmanah — than for themselves; api — even; hi — indeed.

TRANSLATION

[Krishna and Balarāma said:] O Father, you and mother Yaśodā have affectionately maintained Us and cared for Us so much! Indeed, parents love their children more than their own lives.
