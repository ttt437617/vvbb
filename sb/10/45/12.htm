Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 45: Krishna Rescues His Teacher's Son

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.45.12

evam āśvāsya pitarau

bhagavān devakī-sutah

mātāmaham tūgrasenam

yadūnām akaron nripam

SYNONYMS

evam — in this way; āśvāsya — assuring; pitarau — His parents; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; devakī-sutah — the son of Devakī; mātāmaham — His maternal grandfather; tu — and; ugrasenam — Ugrasena; yadūnām — of the Yadus; akarot — He made; nripam — King.

TRANSLATION

Thus having comforted His mother and father, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, appearing as the son of Devakī, installed His maternal grandfather, Ugrasena, as King of the Yadus.
