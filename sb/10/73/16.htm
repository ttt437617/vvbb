Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Krishna Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.16

krishnāya vāsudevāya

haraye paramātmane

pranata-kleśa-nāśāya

govindāya namo namah

SYNONYMS

krishnāya — to Krishna; vāsudevāya — the son of Vasudeva; haraye — the Supreme Lord, Hari; parama-ātmane — the Supersoul; pranata — of those who have surrendered; kleśa — of the distress; nāśāya — to the destroyer; govindāya — to Govinda; namah namah — repeated obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Again and again we offer our obeisances unto Lord Krishna, Hari, the son of Vasudeva. That Supreme Soul, Govinda, vanquishes the suffering of all who surrender to Him.
