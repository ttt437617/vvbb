Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Krishna Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.29

ta evam mocitāh kricchrāt

krishnena su-mahātmanā

yayus tam eva dhyāyantah

kritāni ca jagat-pateh

SYNONYMS

te — they; evam — thus; mocitāh — freed; kricchrāt — from difficulty; krishnena — by Krishna; su-mahā-ātmanā — the greatest of personalities; yayuh — they went; tam — on Him; eva — alone; dhyāyantah — meditating; kritāni — the deeds; ca — and; jagat-pateh — of the Lord of the universe.

TRANSLATION

Thus liberated from all difficulty by Krishna, the greatest of personalities, the kings departed, and as they went they thought only of Him, the Lord of the universe, and of His wonderful deeds.
