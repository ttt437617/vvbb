Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Krishna Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.34

abhivandyātha rājānam

bhīmārjuna-janārdanāh

sarvam āśrāvayām cakrur

ātmanā yad anushthitam

SYNONYMS

abhivandya — offering their respects; atha — then; rājānam — the King; bhīma-arjuna-janārdanāh — Bhīma, Arjuna and Krishna; sarvam — everything; āśrāvayām cakruh — they told; ātmanā — by themselves; yat — what; anushthitam — executed.

TRANSLATION

Bhīma, Arjuna and Janārdana offered their respects to the King and informed him fully about what they had done.
