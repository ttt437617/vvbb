Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Krishna Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.7

krishna-sandarśanāhlāda

dhvasta-samrodhana-klamāh

praśaśamsur hrishīkeśam

gīrbhih prāñjalayo nripāh

SYNONYMS

krishna-sandarśana — of seeing Lord Krishna; āhlāda — by the ecstasy; dhvasta — eradicated; samrodhana — of imprisonment; klamāh — whose weariness; praśaśamsuh — they praised; hrishīkā-īśam — the supreme master of the senses; gīrbhih — with their words; prāñjalayah — with joined palms; nripāh — the kings.

TRANSLATION

The ecstasy of beholding Lord Krishna having dispelled the weariness of their imprisonment, the kings stood with joined palms and offered words of praise to that supreme master of the senses.
