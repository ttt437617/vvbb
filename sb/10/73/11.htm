Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 73: Lord Krishna Blesses the Liberated Kings

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.73.11

mriga-trishnām yathā bālā

manyanta udakāśayam

evam vaikārikīm māyām

ayuktā vastu cakshate

SYNONYMS

mriga-trishnām — a mirage; yathā — as; bālāh — men of childish intelligence; manyante — consider; udaka — of water; āśayam — a reservoir; evam — in the same way; vaikārikīm — subject to transformations; māyām — the material illusion; ayuktāh — those who lack discrimination; vastu — substance; cakshate — see as.

TRANSLATION

Just as men of childish intelligence consider a mirage in the desert to be a pond of water, so those who are irrational look upon the illusory transformations of Māyā as substantial.
