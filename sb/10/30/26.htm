Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.26

tais taih padais tat-padavīm

anvicchantyo 'grato'balāh

vadhvāh padaih su-priktāni

vilokyārtāh samabruvan

SYNONYMS

taih taih — by those various; padaih — footprints; tat — His; padavīm — path; anvicchantyah — tracing out; agratah — forward; abalāh — the girls; vadhvāh — of His special consort; padaih — with the footprints; supriktāni — thoroughly intermingled; vilokya — noticing; ārtāh — distressed; samabruvan — they spoke.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs began following Krishna's path, as shown by His many footprints, but when they saw that these prints were thoroughly intermixed with those of His dearmost consort, they became perturbed and spoke as follows.
