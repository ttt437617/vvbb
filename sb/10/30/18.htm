Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.18

āhūya dūra-gā yadvat

krishnas tam anuvartatīm

venum kvanantīm krīdantīm

anyāh śamsanti sādhv iti

SYNONYMS

āhūya — calling; dūra — who were far away; gāh — the cows; yadvat — just as; krishnah — Krishna; tam — him; anuvartatīm — one gopī who was imitating; venum — the flute; kvanantīm — vibrating; krīdantīm — playing games; anyāh — the other gopīs; śamsanti — praised; sādhu iti — "excellent!"

TRANSLATION

When one gopī perfectly imitated how Krishna would call the cows who had wandered far away, how He would play His flute and how He would engage in various sports, the others congratulated her with exclamations of "Well done! Well done!"
