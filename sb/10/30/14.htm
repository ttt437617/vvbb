Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.14

ity unmatta-vaco gopyah

krishnānveshana-kātarāh

līlā bhagavatas tās tā

hy anucakrus tad-ātmikāh

SYNONYMS

iti — thus; unmatta — maddened; vacah — speaking words; gopyah — the gopīs; krishna-anveshana — by searching for Krishna; kātarāh — distraught; līlāh — the transcendental pastimes; bhagavatah — of Him, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; tāh tāh — each of them; hi — indeed; anucakruh — they acted out; tat-ātmikāh — becoming absorbed in thought of Him.

TRANSLATION

Having spoken these words, the gopīs, distraught from searching for Krishna, began to act out His various pastimes, fully absorbed in thoughts of Him.
