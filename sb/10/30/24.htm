Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.24

evam krishnam pricchamānā

vrndāvana-latās tarūn

vyacakshata vanoddeśe

padāni paramātmanah

SYNONYMS

evam — in this manner; krishnam — about Krishna; pricchamānāh — inquiring; vrindāvana — of the Vrindāvana forest; latāh — from the creepers; tarūn — and the trees; vyacakshata — they saw; vana — of the forest; uddeśe — in one spot; padāni — the footprints; parama-ātmanah — of the Supersoul .

TRANSLATION

While the gopīs were thus imitating Krishna's pastimes and asking Vrindāvana's creepers and trees where Krishna, the Supreme Soul, might be, they happened to see His footprints in a corner of the forest.
