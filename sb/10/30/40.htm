Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 30: The Gopīs Search for Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.30.40

śrī-śuka uvāca

anvicchantyo bhagavato

mārgam gopyo 'vidūritah

dadriśuh priya-viśleshān

mohitām duhkhitām sakhīm

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; anvicchantyah — searching out; bhagavatah — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; mārgam — the path; gopyah — the gopīs; avidūritah — not far away; dadriśuh — saw; priya — from Her beloved; viśleshāt — because of the separation; mohitām — bewildered; duhkhitām — unhappy; sakhīm — their friend.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: While continuing to search out Krishna's path, the gopīs discovered their unhappy friend close by. She was bewildered by separation from Her lover.
