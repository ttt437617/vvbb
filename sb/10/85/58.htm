Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 85: Lord Krishna Instructs Vasudeva and Retrieves Devakī's Sons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.85.58

evam-vidhāny adbhutāni

krishnasya paramātmanah

vīryāny ananta-vīryasya

santy anantāni bhārata

SYNONYMS

evam-vidhāni — like this; adbhutāni — amazing; krishnasya — of Krishna; parama-ātmanah — the Supreme Soul; vīryāni — feats; ananta — unlimited; vīryasya — whose valor; santi — there are; anantāni — unlimited; bhārata — O descendant of Bharata.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Krishna, the Supreme Soul, the Lord of unlimited valor, performed countless pastimes just as amazing as this one, O descendant of Bharata.
