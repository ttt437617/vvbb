Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 85: Lord Krishna Instructs Vasudeva and Retrieves Devakī's Sons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.85.26

śrī-śuka uvāca

evam bhagavatā rājan

vasudeva udāhritah

śrutvā vinashta-nānā-dhīs

tūshnīm prīta-manā abhūt

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Lord; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); vasudevah — Vasudeva; udāhritah — spoken to; śrutvā — hearing; vinashta — destroyed; nānā — dualistic; dhīh — his mentality; tūshnīm — silent; prīta — satisfied; manāh — in his heart; abhūt — he was.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O King, hearing these instructions spoken to him by the Supreme Lord, Vasudeva became freed from all ideas of duality. Satisfied at heart, he remained silent.
