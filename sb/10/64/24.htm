Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 64: The Deliverance of King Nriga

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.64.24

pūrvam devāśubham bhuñja

iti prāha pateti sah

tāvad adrāksham ātmānam

krikalāsam patan prabho

SYNONYMS

pūrvam — first; deva — O lord; aśubham — the sinful reactions; bhuñje — I will experience; iti — thus saying; prāha — said; pata — fall; iti — thus; sah — he; tāvat — just then; adrāksham — I saw; ātmānam — myself; krikalāsam — a lizard; patan — falling; prabho — O master.

TRANSLATION

I replied, "First, my lord, let me suffer my sinful reactions," and Yamarāja said, "Then fall!" "At once I fell, and while falling I saw myself becoming a lizard, O master.
