Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 39: Akrūra's vision

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.39.34

gopyaś ca dayitam krishnam

anuvrajyānurañjitāh

pratyādeśam bhagavatah

kāńkshantyaś cāvatasthire

SYNONYMS

gopyah — the gopīs; ca — and; dayitam — their beloved; krishnam — Krishna; anuvrajya — following; anurañjitāh — pleased; pratyādeśam — some instruction in reply; bhagavatah — from the Lord; kāńkshantyah — hoping for; ca — and; avatasthire — they stood.

TRANSLATION

[With His glances] Lord Krishna somewhat pacified the gopīs, and they also followed behind for some time. Then, hoping He would give them some instruction, they stood still.
