Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 39: Akrūra's vision

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.39.37

tā nirāśā nivavritur

govinda-vinivartane

viśokā ahanī ninyur

gāyantyah priya-ceshtitam

SYNONYMS

tāh — they; nirāśāh — without hope; nivavrituh — turned back; govinda-vinivartane — of Govinda's returning; viśokāh — extremely sorrowful; ahanī — the days and nights; ninyuh — they spent; gāyantyah — chanting; priya — of their beloved; ceshtitam — about the activities.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs then turned back, without hope that Govinda would ever return to them. Full of sorrow, they began to spend their days and nights chanting about the pastimes of their beloved.
