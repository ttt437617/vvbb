Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 62: The Meeting of Ūshā and Aniruddha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.62.14

drishtah kaścin narah svapne

śyāmah kamala-locanah

pīta-vāsā brihad-bāhur

yoshitām hridayam-gamah

SYNONYMS

drishtah — seen; kaścit — a certain; narah — man; svapne — in my dream; śyāmah — dark blue; kamala — lotuslike; locanah — whose eyes; pīta — yellow; vāsāh — whose clothing; brihat — mighty; bāhuh — whose arms; yoshitām — of women; hridayam — the hearts; gamah — touching.

TRANSLATION

[Ūshā said:] In my dream I saw a certain man who had a darkblue complexion, lotus eyes, yellow garments and mighty arms. He was the kind who touches women's hearts.
