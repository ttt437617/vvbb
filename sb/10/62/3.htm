Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 62: The Meeting of Ūshā and Aniruddha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.62.3

bhagavān sarva-bhūteśah

śaranyo bhakta-vatsalah

varena chandayām āsa

sa tam vavre purādhipam

SYNONYMS

bhagavān — the lord; sarva — of all; bhūta — created beings; īśah — the master; śaranyah — the giver of shelter; bhakta — to his devotees; vatsalah — compassionate; varena — with a choice of benedictions; chandayām āsa — gratified him; sah — he, Bāna; tam — him, Lord Śiva; vavre — chose; pura — of his city; adhipam — as the guardian.

TRANSLATION

The lord and master of all created beings, the compassionate refuge of his devotees, gladdened Bānāsura by offering him the benediction of his choice. Bāna chose to have him, Lord Śiva, as the guardian of his city.
