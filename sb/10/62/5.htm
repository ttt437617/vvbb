Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 62: The Meeting of Ūshā and Aniruddha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.62.5

namasye tvām mahā-deva

lokānām gurum īśvaram

pumsām apūrna-kāmānām

kāma-pūrāmarāńghripam

SYNONYMS

namasye — I bow down; tvām — to you; mahā-deva — O greatest of gods; lokānām — of the worlds; gurum — to the spiritual master; īśvaram — to the controller; pumsām — for men; apūrna — unfulfilled; kāmānām — whose desires; kāma-pūra — fulfilling desires; amara-ańghripam — (like) a tree of heaven.

TRANSLATION

[Bānāsura said:] O Lord Mahādeva, I bow down to you, the spiritual master and controller of the worlds. You are like the heavenly tree that fulfills the desires of those whose desires are unfulfilled.
