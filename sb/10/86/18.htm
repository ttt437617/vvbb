Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Krishna Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.18

nārado vāmadevo 'trih

krishno rāmo 'sito 'runih

aham brihaspatih kanvo

maitreyaś cyavanādayah

SYNONYMS

nāradah vāmadevah atrih — the sages Nārada, Vāmadeva and Atri; krishnah — Krishna-dvaipāyana Vyāsa; rāmah — Lord Paraśurāma; asitah arunih — Asita and Aruni; aham — I (Śukadeva); brihaspatih kanvah — Brihaspati and Kanva; maitreyah — Maitreya; cyavana — Cyavana; ādayah — and others.

TRANSLATION

Among these sages were Nārada, Vāmadeva, Atri, Krishna-dvaipāyana Vyāsa, Paraśurāma, Asita, Aruni, myself, Brihaspati, Kanva, Maitreya and Cyavana.
