Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Krishna Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.4

tatra vai vārshitān māsān

avātsīt svārtha-sādhakah

pauraih sabhājito 'bhīkshnam

rāmenājānatā ca sah

SYNONYMS

tatra — there; vai — indeed; vārshikān — of the rainy season; māsān — for the months; avātsīt — he resided; sva — his own; artha — purpose; sādhakah — trying to achieve; pauraih — by the people of the city; sabhājitah — honored; abhīkshnam — constantly; rāmena — by Lord Balarāma; ajānatā — who was unaware; ca — and; sah — he.

TRANSLATION

He stayed there during the monsoon months to fulfill his purpose. Lord Balarāma and the other residents of the city, not recognizing him, offered him all honor and hospitality.
