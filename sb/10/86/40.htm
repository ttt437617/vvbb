Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Krishna Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.40

tad-ambhasā mahā-bhāga

ātmānam sa-grihānvayam

snāpayām cakra uddharsho

labdha-sarva-manorathah

SYNONYMS

tat — with that; ambhasā — water; mahā-bhāgah — very pious; ātmānam — himself; sa — along with; griha — his house; anvayam — and his family; snāpayām cakre — he bathed; uddharshah — overjoyed; labdha — having attained; sarva — all; manah-rathah — desires.

TRANSLATION

With the wash water, the virtuous Śrutadeva copiously sprinkled himself, his house and his family. Overjoyed, he felt that all his desires had now been fulfilled.
