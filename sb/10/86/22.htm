Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 86: Arjuna Kidnaps Subhadrā, and Krishna Blesses His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.86.22

te 'cyutam prāptam ākarnya

paurā jānapadā nripa

abhīyur muditās tasmai

grihītārhana-pānayah

SYNONYMS

te — they; acyutam — Lord Krishna; prāptam — arrived; ākarnya — hearing; paurāh — the people of the city; jānapadāh — and of the villages; nripa — O King; abhīyuh — came forward; muditāh — joyful; tasmai — to Him; grihīta — holding; arhana — offerings to present to Him; pānayah — in their hands.

TRANSLATION

Hearing that Lord Acyuta had arrived, O King, the residents of the cities and villages of Videha joyfully came forth to receive Him with offerings in their hands.
