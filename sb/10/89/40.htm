Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 89: Krishna and Arjuna Retrieve a Brāhmana's Sons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.89.40

na pradyumno nāniruddho

na rāmo na ca keśavah

yasya śekuh paritrātum

ko 'nyas tad-aviteśvarah

SYNONYMS

na — not; pradyumnah — Pradyumna; na — not; aniruddhah — Aniruddha; na — not; rāmah — Balarāma; na — not; ca — also; keśavah — Krishna; yasya — whose (infants); śekuh — were able; paritrātum — to save; kah — who; anyah — else; tat — in this situation; avitā — as a protector; īśvarah — capable.

TRANSLATION

"When neither Pradyumna, Aniruddha, Rāma nor Keśava can save a person, who else can possibly protect him?
