Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 89: Krishna and Arjuna Retrieve a Brāhmana's Sons

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.89.32

śrī-arjuna uvāca

nāham sańkarshano brahman

na krishnah kārshnir eva ca

aham vā arjuno nāma

gāndīvam yasya vai dhanuh

SYNONYMS

śrī-arjunah uvāca — Śrī Arjuna said; na — not; aham — I; sańkarshanah — Lord Balarāma; brahman — O brāhmana; na — not; krishnah — Lord Krishna; kārshnih — a descendant of Lord Krishna; eva ca — even; aham — I; vai — indeed; arjunah nāma — the one known as Arjuna; gāndīvam — Gāndīva; yasya — whose; vai — indeed; dhanuh — bow.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Arjuna said: I am neither Lord Sańkarshana, O brāhmana, nor Lord Krishna, nor even Krishna's son. Rather, I am Arjuna, wielder of the Gāndīva bow.
