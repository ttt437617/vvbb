Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 81: The Lord Blesses Sudāmā Brāhmana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.81.8

ittham vicintya vasanāc

cīra-baddhān dvi-janmanah

svayam jahāra kim idam

iti prithuka-tandulān

SYNONYMS

ittham — in this manner; vicintya — thinking; vasanāt — from the garment; cīra — in a strip of cloth; baddhān — tied up; dvi-janmanah — of the twice-born brāhmana; svayam — Himself; jahāra — He took hold of; kim — what; idam — this; iti — so saying; prithuka-tandulān — the grains of flat rice.

TRANSLATION

Thinking like this, the Lord snatched from the brāhmana's garment the grains of flat rice tied up in an old piece of cloth and exclaimed, "What is this?
