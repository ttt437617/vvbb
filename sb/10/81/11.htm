Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 81: The Lord Blesses Sudāmā Brāhmana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.81.11

etāvatālam viśvātman

sarva-sampat-samriddhaye

asmin loke 'tha vāmushmin

pumsas tvat-tosha-kāranam

SYNONYMS

etāvatā — this much; alam — enough; viśva — of the universe; ātman — O Soul; sarva — of all; sampat — opulent assets; samriddhaye — for the prospering; asmin — in this; loke — world; atha vā — or else; amushmin — in the next; pumsah — for a person; tvat — Your; tosha — satisfaction; kāranam — having as its cause.

TRANSLATION

[Queen Rukminī said:] This is more than enough, O Soul of the universe, to secure him an abundance of all kinds of wealth in this world and the next. After all, one's prosperity depends simply on Your satisfaction.
