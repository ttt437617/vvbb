Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 81: The Lord Blesses Sudāmā Brāhmana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.81.17

nivāsitah priyā-jushte

paryańke bhrātaro yathā

mahishyā vījitah śrānto

bāla-vyajana-hastayā

SYNONYMS

nivāsitah — seated; priyā — by His beloved; jushte — used; paryańke — on the bed; bhrātarah — brothers; yathā — just as; mahishyā — by His queen; vījitah — fanned; śrāntah — tired; bāla — of (yak-tail) hair; vyajana — a fan; hastayā — in whose hand.

TRANSLATION

He treated me just like one of His brothers, making me sit on the bed of His beloved consort. And because I was fatigued, His queen personally fanned me with a yak-tail cāmara.
