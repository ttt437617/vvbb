Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 81: The Lord Blesses Sudāmā Brāhmana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.81.14

sa cālabdhvā dhanam krishnān

na tu yācitavān svayam

sva-grihān vrīdito 'gacchan

mahad-darśana-nirvritah

SYNONYMS

sah — he; ca — and; alabdhvā — not having obtained; dhanam — wealth; krishnāt — from Lord Krishna; na — not; tu — however; yācitavān — did beg; svayam — on his own initiative; sva — to his; grihān — home; vrīditah — embarrassed; agacchat — he went; mahat — of the Supreme Lord; darśana — by the audience; nirvritah — made joyful.

TRANSLATION

Although he had apparently received no wealth from Lord Krishna, Sudāmā was too shy to beg for it on his own. He simply returned home, feeling perfectly satisfied to have had the Supreme Lord's audience.
