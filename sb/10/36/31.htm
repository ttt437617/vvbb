Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.31

nisrishtah kila me mrityur

devair vaikuntha-samśrayaih

tāv ānaya samam gopair

nandādyaih sābhyupāyanaih

SYNONYMS

nisrishtah — sent; kila — indeed; me — my; mrityuh — death; devaih — by the demigods; vaikuntha — of Lord Vishnu; samśrayaih — who take shelter; tau — the two of Them; ānaya — bring; samam — together with; gopaih — the cowherd men; nanda-ādyaih — headed by Nanda; sa — with; abhyupāyanaih — gifts.

TRANSLATION

The demigods, who are under the protection of Vishnu, have sent these two boys as my death. Bring Them here, and also have Nanda and the other cowherd men come with gifts of tribute.
