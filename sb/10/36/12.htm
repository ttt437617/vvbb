Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.12

so 'paviddho bhagavatā

punar utthāya satvaram

āpatat svinna-sarvāńgo

nihśvasan krodha-mūrcchitah

SYNONYMS

sah — he; apaviddhah — thrown back; bhagavatā — by the Lord; punah — again; utthāya — rising; satvaram — quickly; āpatat — attacked; svinna — sweating; sarva — all; ańgah — his limbs; nihśvasan — breathing hard; krodha — by anger; mūrcchitah — stupefied.

TRANSLATION

Thus repulsed by the Supreme Lord, the bull demon got up and, breathing hard and sweating all over his body, again charged Him in a mindless rage.
