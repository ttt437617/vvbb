Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.34

ugrasenam ca pitaram

sthaviram rājya-kāmukam

tad-bhrātaram devakam ca

ye cānye vidvisho mama

SYNONYMS

ugrasenam — King Ugrasena; ca — and; pitaram — my father; sthaviram — old; rājya — for the kingdom; kāmukam — greedy; tat-bhrātaram — his brother; devakam — Devaka; ca — also; ye — who; ca — and; anye — others; vidvishah — enemies; mama — my.

TRANSLATION

I will also kill my old father, Ugrasena, who is greedy for my kingdom, and I will kill his brother Devaka and all my other enemies as well.
