Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.37

etaj jñātvānaya kshipram

rāma-krishnāv ihārbhakau

dhanur-makha-nirīkshārtham

drashtum yadu-pura-śriyam

SYNONYMS

etat — this; jñātvā — knowing; ānaya — bring; kshipram — quickly; rāma-krishnau — Rāma and Krishna; iha — here; arbhakau — the young boys; dhanuh-makha — the bow sacrifice; nirīkshā-artham — in order to witness; drashtum — to see; yadu-pura — of the capital city of the Yadu dynasty; śriyam — the opulence.

TRANSLATION

Now that you understand my intentions, please go at once and bring Krishna and Balarāma to watch the bow sacrifice and see the opulence of the Yadus' capital.
