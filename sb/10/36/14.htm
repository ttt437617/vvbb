Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.14

asrig vaman mūtra-śakrit samutsrijan

kshipamś ca pādān anavasthitekshanah

jagāma kricchram nirriter atha kshayam

pushpaih kiranto harim īdire surāh

SYNONYMS

asrik — blood; vaman — vomiting; mūtra — urine; śakrit — and feces; samutsrijan — profusely excreting; kshipan — throwing about; ca — and; pādān — his legs; anavasthita — unsteady; īkshanah — his eyes; jagāma — he went; kricchram — with pain; nirriteh — of Death; atha — then; kshayam — to the abode; pushpaih — flowers; kirantah — scattering; harim — upon Lord Krishna; īdire — worshiped; surah — the demigods.

TRANSLATION

Vomiting blood and profusely excreting stool and urine, kicking his legs and rolling his eyes about, Arishtāsura thus went painfully to the abode of Death. The demigods honored Lord Krishna by scattering flowers upon Him.
