Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.36

jarāsandho mama gurur

dvivido dayitah sakhā

śambaro narako bāno

mayy eva krita-sauhridāh

tair aham sura-pakshīyān

hatvā bhokshye mahīm nripān

SYNONYMS

jarāsandhah — Jarāsandha; mama — my; guruh — elder (father-in-law); dvividah — Dvivida; dayitah — my dear; sakhā — friend; śambarah — Śambara; narakah — Naraka; bānah — Bāna; mayi — for me; eva — indeed; krita-sauhridāh — who have strong friendship; taih — with them; aham — 1; sura — of the demigods; pakshīyān — those who are allies; hatvā — killing; bhokshye — will enjoy; mahīm — the earth; nripān — the kings.

TRANSLATION

My elder relative Jarāsandha and my dear friend Dvivida are solid well-wishers of mine, as are Śambara, Naraka and Bāna. I will use them all to kill off those kings who are allied with the demigods, and then I will rule the earth.
