Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.9

so 'py evam kopito 'rishtah

khurenāvanim ullikhan

udyat-puccha-bhraman-meghah

kruddhah krishnam upādravat

SYNONYMS

sah — he; api — indeed; evam — in this way; kopitah — angered; arishtah — Arishta; khurena — with his hoof; avanim — the earth; ullikhan — scratching; udyat — raised; puccha — within his tail; bhraman — wandering; meghah — clouds; kruddhah — furious; krishnam — toward Lord Krishna; upādravat — he charged.

TRANSLATION

Thus provoked, Arishta pawed the ground with one of his hooves and then, with the clouds hovering around his upraised tail, furiously charged Krishna.
