Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.21

tato mushtika-cānūra

śala-tośalakādikān

amātyān hastipāmś caiva

samāhūyāha bhoja-rāt

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; mushtika-cānūra-śala-tośalaka-ādikān — Mushtika, Cānūra, Śala, Tośala and others; amātyān — his ministers; hasti-pān — his elephant-keepers; ca eva — also; samāhūya — calling together; āha — spoke; bhoja-rāt — the King of the Bhojas.

TRANSLATION

The King of the Bhojas next called for his ministers, headed by Mushtika, Cānūra, Śala and Tośala, and also for his elephant-keepers. The King addressed them as follows.
