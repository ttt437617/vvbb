Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 36: The Slaying of Arista, the Bull Demon

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.36.35

tataś caishā mahī mitra

bhavitrī nashta-kantakā

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; ca — and; eshā — this; mahī — earth; mitra — O friend; bhavitrī — will be; nashta — destroyed; kantakā — her thorns.

TRANSLATION

Then, my friend, this earth will be free of thorns.
