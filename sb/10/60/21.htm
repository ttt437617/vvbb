Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 60: Lord Krishna Teases Queen Rukminī.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.60.21

śrī-śuka uvāca

etāvad uktvā bhagavān

ātmānam vallabhām iva

manyamānām aviśleshāt

tad-darpa-ghna upāramat

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; etāvat — this much; uktvā — saying; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; ātmānam — herself; vallabhām — His beloved; iva — as; manyamānām — thinking; aviśleshāt — because of (His) never being separated (from her); tat — that; darpa — of the pride; ghnah — the destroyer; upāramat — desisted.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Rukminī had thought herself especially beloved by the Lord because He never left her company. By saying these things to her He vanquished her pride, and then He stopped speaking.
