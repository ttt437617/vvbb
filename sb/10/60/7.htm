Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 60: Lord Krishna Teases Queen Rukminī.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.60.7

vāla-vyajanam ādāya

ratna-dandam sakhī-karāt

tena vījayatī devī

upāsām cakra īśvaram

SYNONYMS

vāla — of (yak's) hair; vyajanam — a fan; ādāya — taking; ratna — jeweled; dandam — the handle of which; sakhī — of her maidservant; karāt — from the hand; tena — with it; vījayatī — fanning; devī — the goddess; upāsām cakre — she worshiped; īśvaram — her master.

TRANSLATION

From her maidservant's hand Goddess Rukminī took a yak-hair fan with a jeweled handle, and then she began to worship her master by fanning Him.
