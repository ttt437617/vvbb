Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 60: Lord Krishna Teases Queen Rukminī.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.60.18

caidya-śālva-jarāsandha

dantavakrādayo nripāh

mama dvishanti vāmoru

rukmī cāpi tavāgrajah

SYNONYMS

caidya-śālva-jarāsandha-dantavakra-ādayah — Caidya (Śiśupāla), Śālva, Jarāsandha, Dantavakra and others; nripāh — kings; mama — Me; dvishanti — hate; vāma-ūru — O beautiful-thighed one; rukmī — Rukmī; ca api — as well; tava — your; agra-jah — older brother.

TRANSLATION

Kings like Śiśupāla, Śālva, Jarāsandha and Dantavakra all hate Me, O beautiful-thighed one, and so does your elder brother Rukmī.
