Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 60: Lord Krishna Teases Queen Rukminī.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.60.19

teshām vīrya-madāndhānām

driptānām smaya-nuttaye

ānitāsi mayā bhadre

tejopaharatāsatām

SYNONYMS

teshām — of them; vīrya — with their power; mada — by the intoxication; andhānām — blinded; driptānām — proud; smaya — the arrogance; nuttaye — to dispel; ānitā asi — you were taken in marriage; mayā — by Me; bhadre — good woman; tejah — the strength; upaharatā — removing; asatām — of the wicked.

TRANSLATION

It was to dispel the arrogance of these kings that I carried you away, My good woman, for they were blinded by the intoxication of power. My purpose was to curb the strength of the wicked.
