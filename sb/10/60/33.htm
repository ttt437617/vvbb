Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 60: Lord Krishna Teases Queen Rukminī.

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.60.33

babhāsha rishabham pumsām

vīkshantī bhagavan-mukham

sa-vrīda-hāsa-rucira-

snigdhāpāńgena bhārata

SYNONYMS

babhāsha — she spoke; rishabham — to the most eminent; pumsām — of males; vīkshantī — looking upon; bhagavat — of the Supreme Lord; mukham — the face; sa-vrīda — shy; hāsa — with a smile; rucira — charming; snigdha — affectionate; apāńgena — and with glances; bhārata — O descendant of Bharata.

TRANSLATION

Smiling bashfully as she cast charming, affectionate glances upon the face of the Lord, the best of males, Rukminī spoke the following, O descendant of Bharata.
