Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 17: The History of Kāliya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.17.23

krishna krishna mahā-bhaga

he rāmāmita-vikrama

esha ghoratamo vahnis

tāvakān grasate hi nah

SYNONYMS

krishna — O Krishna; krishna — O Krishna; mahā-bhāga — O Lord of all opulence; he rāma — O Lord Balarāma, source of all happiness; amita-vikrama — You whose power is unlimited; eshah — this; ghora-tamah — most terrible; vahnih — fire; tāvakān — who are Yours; grasate — is devouring; hi — indeed; nah — us.

TRANSLATION

[Vrindāvana's residents said:] Krishna, Krishna, O Lord of all opulence! O Rāma, possessor of unlimited power! This most terrible fire is about to devour us, Your devotees!
