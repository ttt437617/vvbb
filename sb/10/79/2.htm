Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 79: Lord Balarāma Goes on Pilgrimage

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.79.2

tato 'medhya-mayam varsham

balvalena vinirmitam

abhavad yajña-śālāyām

so 'nvadriśyata śūla-dhrik

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; amedhya — abominable things; mayam — full of; varsham — a rain; balvalena — by Balvala; vinirmitam — produced; abhavat — occurred; yajña — of the sacrifice; śālāyām — upon the arena; sah — he, Balvala; anvadriśyata — appeared after this; śūla — a trident; dhrik — carrying.

TRANSLATION

Next, onto the sacrificial arena came a downpour of abominable things sent by Balvala, after which the demon himself appeared, trident in hand.
