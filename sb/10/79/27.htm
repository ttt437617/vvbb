Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 79: Lord Balarāma Goes on Pilgrimage

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.79.27

tasmād ekatarasyeha

yuvayoh sama-vīryayoh

na lakshyate jayo 'nyo vā

viramatv aphalo ranah

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — therefore; ekatarasya — of either of the two; iha — here; yuvayoh — of you; sama — equal; vīryayoh — whose prowess; na lakshyate — cannot be seen; jayah — victory; anyah — the opposite (defeat); vā — or; viramatu — it should stop; aphalah — fruitless; ranah — the battle.

TRANSLATION

Since you are so evenly matched in fighting prowess, I do not see how either of you can win or lose this duel. Therefore please stop this useless battle.
