Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 79: Lord Balarāma Goes on Pilgrimage

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.79.25

gadā-pānī ubhau drishtvā

samrabdhau vijayaishinau

mandalāni vicitrāni

carantāv idam abravīt

SYNONYMS

gadā — with clubs; pānī — in their hands; ubhau — both of them, Duryodhana and Bhīma; drishtvā — seeing; samrabdhau — furious; vijaya — victory; eshinau — striving for; mandalāni — circles; vicitrāni — artistic; carantau — moving in; idam — this; abravīt — He said.

TRANSLATION

Lord Balarāma found Duryodhana and Bhīma with clubs in their hands, each furiously striving for victory over the other as they circled about skillfully. The Lord addressed them as follows.
