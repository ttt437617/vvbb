Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 79: Lord Balarāma Goes on Pilgrimage

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.79.31

tebhyo viśuddham vijñānam

bhagavān vyatarad vibhuh

yenaivātmany ado viśvam

ātmānam viśva-gam viduh

SYNONYMS

tebhyah — upon them; viśuddham — perfectly pure; vijñānam — divine knowledge; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; vyatarat — bestowed; vibhuh — the Almighty; yena — by which; eva — indeed; ātmani — within Himself, the Supreme Lord; adah — this; viśvam — universe; ātmānam — Himself; viśva-gam — pervading the universe; viduh — they could perceive.

TRANSLATION

The all-powerful Lord Balarāma bestowed upon the sages pure spiritual knowledge, by which they could see the whole universe within Him and also see Him pervading everything.
