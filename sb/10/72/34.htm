Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.34

tatah samekhale vīrau

samyuktāv itaretaram

jaghnatur vajra-kalpābhyām

gadābhyām rana-durmadau

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; samekhale — on the level fighting grounds; vīrau — the two heroes; samyuktau — engaged; itara-itaram — each other; jaghnatuh — struck; vajra-kalpābhyām — like lightning bolts; gadābhyām — with their clubs; rana — by the fight; durmadau — driven to a mad fury.

TRANSLATION

The two heroes thus began battling each other on the level fighting grounds outside the city. Maddened with the fury of combat, they struck each other with their lightning-bolt-like clubs.
