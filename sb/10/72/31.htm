Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.31

na tvayā bhīrunā yotsye

yudhi viklava-tejasā

mathurām sva-purīm tyaktvā

samudram śaranam gatah

SYNONYMS

na — not; tvayā — with You; bhīrunā — cowardly; yotsye — I will fight; yudhi — in battle; viklava — impaired; tejasā — whose strength; mathurām — Mathurā; sva — Your own; purīm — city; tyaktvā — leaving; samudram — to the ocean; śaranam — for shelter; gatah — gone.

TRANSLATION

"But I will not fight with You, Krishna, for You are a coward. Your strength abandoned You in the midst of battle, and You fled Your own capital of Mathurā to take shelter in the sea.
