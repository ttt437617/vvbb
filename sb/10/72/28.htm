Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.28

śrī-bhagavān uvāca

yuddham no dehi rājendra

dvandvaśo yadi manyase

yuddhārthino vayam prāptā

rājanyā nānya-kāńkshinah

SYNONYMS

śrī-bhagavān uvāca — the Supreme Lord (Krishna) said; yuddham — battle; nah — to us; dehi — please give; rāja-indra — O exalted King; dvandvaśah — as a one-on-one duel; yadi — if; manyase — you think it proper; yuddha — for a fight; arthinah — desirous; vayam — we; prāptāh — have come here; rājanyāh — members of the royal order; na — not; anya — anything else; kāńkshinah — wanting.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Lord said: O exalted King, give us battle in the form of a duel, if you think it fitting. We are princes and have come to beg a fight. We have no other request to make of you.
