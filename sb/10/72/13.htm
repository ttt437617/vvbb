Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.13

sahadevam dakshinasyām

ādiśat saha sriñjayaih

diśi pratīcyām nakulam

udīcyām savyasācinam

prācyām vrikodaram matsyaih

kekayaih saha madrakaih

SYNONYMS

sahadevam — Sahadeva; dakshinasyām — to the south; ādiśat — he ordered; saha — with; sriñjayaih — the warriors of the Sriñjaya clan; diśi — to the direction; pratīcyām — western; nakulam — Nakula; udīcyām — to the north; savyasācinam — Arjuna; prācyām — to the east; vrikodaram — Bhīma; matsyaih — the Matsyas; kekayaih — the Kekayas; saha — together with; madrakaih — and the Madrakas.

TRANSLATION

He sent Sahadeva to the south with the Sriñjayas, Nakula to the west with the Matsyas, Arjuna to the north with the Kekayas, and Bhīma to the east with the Madrakas.
