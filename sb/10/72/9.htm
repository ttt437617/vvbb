Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.9

vijitya nripatīn sarvān

kritvā ca jagatīm vaśe

sambhritya sarva-sambhārān

āharasva mahā-kratum

SYNONYMS

vijitya — conquering; nri-patīn — the kings; sarvān — all; kritvā — making; ca — and; jagatīm — the earth; vaśe — under your control; sambhritya — collecting; sarva — all; sambhārān — the paraphernalia; āharasva — execute; mahā — great; kratum — the sacrifice.

TRANSLATION

First conquer all kings, bring the earth under your control and collect all the required paraphernalia; then execute this great sacrifice.
