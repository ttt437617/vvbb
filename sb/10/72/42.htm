Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.42

tad vijñāya mahā-sattvo

bhīmah praharatām varah

grihītvā pādayoh śatrum

pātayām āsa bhū-tale

SYNONYMS

tat — that; vijñāya — understanding; mahā — great; sattvah — whose strength; bhīmah — Bhīma; praharatām — of fighters; varah — the best; grihītvā — seizing; pādayoh — by the feet; śatrum — his enemy; pātayām asa — he made him fall; bhū-tale — to the ground.

TRANSLATION

Understanding this sign, mighty Bhīma, the best of fighters, seized his opponent by the feet and threw him to the ground.
