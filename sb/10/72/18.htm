Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 72: The Slaying of the Demon Jarāsandha

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.72.18

rājan viddhy atithīn prāptān

arthino dūram āgatān

tan nah prayaccha bhadram te

yad vayam kāmayāmahe

SYNONYMS

rājan — O King; viddhi — please know; atithīn — guests; prāptān — arrived; arthinah — desirous of gain; dūram — from far away; āgatān — come; tat — that; nah — to us; prayaccha — please grant; bhadram — all good, te — unto you; yat — whatever; vayam — we; kāmayāmahe — are desiring.

TRANSLATION

[Krishna, Arjuna and Bhīma said:] O King, know us to be needy guests who have come to you from afar. We wish all good unto you. Please grant us whatever we desire.
