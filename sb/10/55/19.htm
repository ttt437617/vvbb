Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.19

gadām āvidhya tarasā

pradyumnāya mahātmane

prakshipya vyanadan nādam

vajra-nishpesha-nishthuram

SYNONYMS

gadām — his club; āvidhya — whirling; tarasā — swiftly; pradyumnāya — at Pradyumna; mahā-ātmane — the wise; prakshipya — threw; vyanadan nādam — creating a resonance; vajra — of lightning; nishpesha — the striking; nishthuram — as sharp.

TRANSLATION

Śambara whirled his club swiftly about and then hurled it at the wise Pradyumna, producing a sound as sharp as a thunder crack.
