Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.23

tato gauhyaka-gāndharva-

paiśācoraga-rākshasīh

prāyuńkta śataśo daityah

kārshnir vyadhamayat sa tāh

SYNONYMS

tatah — then; gauhyaka-gāndharva-paiśāca-uraga-rākshasīh — (weapons) of the Guhyakas, Gandharvas, witches, celestial serpents and Rākshasas (man-eaters); prāyuńkta — he used; śataśah — hundreds; daityah — the demon; kārshnih — Lord Pradyumna; vyadhamayat — struck down; sah — He; tāh — these.

TRANSLATION

The demon then unleashed hundreds of mystic weapons belonging to the Guhyakas, Gandharvas, Piśācas, Uragas and Rākshasas, but Lord Kārshni, Pradyumna, struck them all down.
