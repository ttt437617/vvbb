Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.11

tām aha bhagavān kārshnir

mātas te matir anyathā

mātri-bhāvam atikramya

vartase kāminī yathā

SYNONYMS

tām — to her; āha — said; bhagavān — the Lord; kārshnih — Pradyumna; mātah — O mother; te — your; matih — attitude; anyathā — otherwise; mātri-bhāvam — the mood or affection of a mother; atikramya — overstepping; vartase — you are acting; kāminī — a girlfriend; yathā — like.

TRANSLATION

Lord Pradyumna told her, "O mother, your attitude has changed. You are overstepping the proper feelings of a mother and behaving like a lover."
