Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.12

ratir uvāca

bhavān nārāyana-sutah

śambarena hrito grihāt

aham te 'dhikritā patnī

ratih kāmo bhavān prabho

SYNONYMS

ratih uvāca — Rati said; bhavān — You; nārāyana-suta h — the son of Lord Nārāyana; śambarena — by Śambara; hritah — stolen; grihāt — from Your home; aham — I; te — Your; adhikritā — legitimate; patnī — wife; ratih — Rati; kāmah — Cupid; bhavān — You; prabho — O master.

TRANSLATION

Rati said: You are the son of Lord Nārāyana and were kidnapped from Your parents' home by Śambara. I, Rati, am Your legitimate wife, O master, because You are Cupid.
