Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.34

sa eva vā bhaven nūnam

yo me garbhe dhrito 'rbhakah

amushmin prītir adhikā

vāmah sphurati me bhujah

SYNONYMS

sah — He; eva — indeed; vā — or else; bhavet — must be; nūnam — for certain; yah — who; me — my; garbhe — in the womb; dhritah — was carried; arbhakah — child; amushmin — for Him; prītih — affection; adhikā — great; vāmah — left; sphurati — trembles; me — my; bhujah — arm.

TRANSLATION

Yes, He must be the same child I bore in my womb, since I feel great affection for Him and my left arm is quivering.
