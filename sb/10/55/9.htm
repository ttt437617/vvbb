Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.9

nāti-dīrghena kālena

sa kārshni rūdha-yauvanah

janayām āsa nārīnām

vīkshantīnām ca vibhramam

SYNONYMS

na — not; ati-dīrghena — very long; kālena — after a time; sah — He; kārshnih — the son of Krishna; rūdha — attaining; yauvanah — full youth; janayām āsa — generated; nārīnām — for the women; vīkshantīnām — who looked upon Him; ca — and; vibhramam — enchantment.

TRANSLATION

After a short time, this son of Krishna — Pradyumna — attained His full youth. He enchanted all women who gazed upon Him.
