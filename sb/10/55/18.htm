Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.18

so 'dhikshipto durvācobhih

padāhata ivoragah

niścakrāma gadā-pānir

amarshāt tāmra-locanah

SYNONYMS

sah — he, Śambara; adhikshiptah — insulted; durvācobhih — by harsh words; padā — by a foot; āhatah — struck; iva — like; uragah — a snake; niścakrāma — came out; gadā — a club; pānih — in his hand; amarshāt — out of intolerant anger; tāmra — copper-red; locanah — whose eyes.

TRANSLATION

Offended by these harsh words, Śambara became as agitated as a kicked snake. He came out, club in hand, his eyes red with rage.
