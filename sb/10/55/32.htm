Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.32

mama cāpy ātmajo nashto

nīto yah sūtikā-grihāt

etat-tulya-vayo-rūpo

yadi jīvati kutracit

SYNONYMS

mama — my; ca — and; api — also; ātmajah — son; nashtah — lost; nītah — taken; yah — who; sūtikā-grihāt — from the maternity room; etat — to Him; tulya — equal; vayah — in age; rūpah — and appearance; yadi — if; jīvati — He is living; kutracit — somewhere.

TRANSLATION

If my lost son, who was kidnapped from the maternity room, were still alive somewhere, He would be of the same age and appearance as this young man.
