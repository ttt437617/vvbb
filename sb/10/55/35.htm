Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.35

evam mīmāmsamanāyām

vaidarbhyām devakī-sutah

devaky-ānakadundubhyām

uttamah-śloka āgamat

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; mīmāmsamānāyām — as she was conjecturing; vaidarbhyām — Queen Rukminī; devakī-sutah — the son of Devakī; devakī-ānakadundubhyām — together with Devakī and Vasudeva; uttamah-ślokah — Lord Krishna; āgamat — came there.

TRANSLATION

As Queen Rukminī conjectured in this way, Lord Krishna, the son of Devakī, arrived on the scene with Vasudeva and Devakī.
