Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.17

sa ca śambaram abhyetya

samyugāya samāhvayat

avishahyais tam ākshepaih

kshipan sañjanayan kalim

SYNONYMS

sah — He; ca — and; śambaram — Śambara; abhyetya — approaching; samyugāya — to battle; samāhvayat — called him; avishahyaih — intolerable; tam — him; ākshepaih — with insults; kshipan — reviling; sañjanayan — inciting; kalim — a fight.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna approached Śambara and called him to battle, hurling intolerable insults at him to foment a conflict.
