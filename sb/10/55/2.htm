Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 55: The History of Pradyumna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.55.2

sa eva jāto vaidarbhyām

krishna-vīrya-samudbhavah

pradyumna iti vikhyātah

sarvato 'navamah pituh

SYNONYMS

sah — He; eva — indeed; jātah — taking birth; vaidarbhyām — in the daughter of the king of Vidarbha; krishna-vīrya — from the seed of Lord Krishna; samudbhavah — generated; pradyumnah — Pradyumna; iti — thus; vikhyātah — known; sarvatah — in all aspects; anavamah — not inferior; pituh — to His father.

TRANSLATION

He took birth in the womb of Vaidarbhī from the seed of Lord Krishna and received the name Pradyumna. In no respect was He inferior to His father.
