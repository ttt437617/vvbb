Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 82: Krishna and Balarāma Meet the Inhabitants of Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.82.22

śrī-śuka uvāca

vasudevograsenādyair

yadubhis te 'rcitā nripāh

āsann acyuta-sandarśa-

paramānanda-nirvritāh

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śrī Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; vasudeva-ugrasena-ādyaih — headed by Vasudeva and Ugrasena; yadubhih — by the Yādavas; te — they; arcitāh — honored; nripāh — the kings; āsan — became; acyuta — of Lord Krishna; sandarśa — by the seeing; parama — supreme; ānanda — in ecstasy; nirvritāh — pacified.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Vasudeva, Ugrasena and the other Yadus honored the various kings, who became supremely blissful and content upon seeing Lord Acyuta.
