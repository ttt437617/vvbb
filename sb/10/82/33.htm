Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 82: Krishna and Balarāma Meet the Inhabitants of Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.82.33

vasudevah parishvajya

samprītah prema-vihvalah

smaran kamsa-kritān kleśān

putra-nyāsam ca gokule

SYNONYMS

vasudevah — Vasudeva; parishvajya — embracing (Nanda Mahārāja); samprītah — overjoyed; prema — due to love; vihvalah — beside himself; smaran — remembering; kamsa-kritān — created by Kamsa; kleśān — the troubles; putra — of his sons; nyāsam — the leaving; ca — and; gokule — in Gokula.

TRANSLATION

Vasudeva embraced Nanda Mahārāja with great joy. Beside himself with ecstatic love, Vasudeva remembered the troubles Kamsa had caused him, forcing him to leave his sons in Gokula for Their safety.
