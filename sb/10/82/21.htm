Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 82: Krishna and Balarāma Meet the Inhabitants of Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.82.21

kamsa-pratāpitāh sarve

vayam yātā diśam diśam

etarhy eva punah sthānam

daivenāsāditāh svasah

SYNONYMS

kamsa — by Kamsa; pratāpitāh — severely troubled; sarve — all; vayam — we; yātāh — went away; diśam diśam — in various directions; etarhi eva — just now; punah — again; sthānam — to our proper places; daivena — by Providence; āsāditāh — brought; svasah — O sister.

TRANSLATION

Harassed by Kamsa, we all fled in various directions, but by the grace of Providence we have now finally been able to return to our homes, my dear sister.
