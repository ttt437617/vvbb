Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.13

vīraś candro 'śvasenaś ca

citragur vegavān vrishah

āmah śańkur vasuh śrīmān

kuntir nāgnajiteh sutāh

SYNONYMS

vīrah candrah aśvasenah ca — Vīra, Candra and Aśvasena; citraguh vegavān vrishah — Citragu, Vegavān and Vrisha; āmah śańkuh vasuh — Āma, Śańku and Vasu; śrī-mān — opulent; kuntih — Kuntī; nāgnajiteh — of Nagnajitī; sutāh — the sons.

TRANSLATION

The sons of Nāgnajitī were Vīra, Candra, Aśvasena, Citragu, Vegavān, Vrisha, Āma, Śańku, Vasu and the opulent Kunti.
