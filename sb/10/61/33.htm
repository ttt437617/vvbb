Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.33

tadābravīn nabho-vānī

balenaiva jito glahah

dharmato vacanenaiva

rukmī vadati vai mrishā

SYNONYMS

tadā — then; abravīt — spoke; nabhah — in the sky; vānī — a voice; balena — by Lord Balarāma; eva — indeed; jitah — won; glahah — the wager; dharmatah — fairly; vacanena — with words; eva — certainly; rukmī — Rukmī; vadati — speaks; vai — indeed; mrishā — duplicitous.

TRANSLATION

Just then a voice from the sky declared, "Balarāma has fairly won this wager. Rukmī is surely lying."
