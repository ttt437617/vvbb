Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.19

eteshām putra-pautrāś ca

babhūvuh kotiśo nripa

mātarah krishna-jātīnām

sahasrāni ca shodaśa

SYNONYMS

eteshām — of these; putra — sons; pautrāh — and grandsons; ca — and; babhūvuh — were born; kotiśah — by the tens of millions; nripa — O King; mātarah — the mothers; krishna-jātīnām — of the descendants of Lord Krishna; sahasrāni — thousands; ca — and; shodaśa — sixteen.

TRANSLATION

My dear King, the sons and grandsons of Lord Krishna's children numbered in the tens of millions. Sixteen thousand mothers gave rise to this dynasty.
