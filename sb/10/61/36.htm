Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.36

rukminaivam adhikshipto

rājabhiś copahāsitah

kruddhah parigham udyamya

jaghne tam nrimna-samsadi

SYNONYMS

rukminā — by Rukmī; evam — in this manner; adhikshiptah — insulted; rājabhih — by the kings; ca — and; upahāsitah — laughed at; kruddhah — angered; parigham — His club; udyamya — raising; jaghne — He struck dead; tam — him; nrimna-samsadi — in the auspicious assembly.

TRANSLATION

Thus insulted by Rukmī and ridiculed by the kings, Lord Balarāma was provoked to anger. In the midst of the auspicious wedding assembly, He raised His club and struck Rukmī dead.
