Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.38

anye nirbhinna-bāhūru-

śiraso rudhirokshitāh

rājāno dudravar bhītā

balena pańghārditāh

SYNONYMS

anye — others; nirbhinna — broken; bāhu — their arms; ūru — thighs; śirasah — and heads; rudhira — with blood; ukshitāh — drenched; rājānah — kings; dudruvuh — fled; bhītāh — frightened; balena — by Lord Balarāma; parigha — with His club; arditāh — tormented.

TRANSLATION

Tormented by Lord Balarāma's club, the other kings fled in fear, their arms, thighs and heads broken and their bodies drenched in blood.
