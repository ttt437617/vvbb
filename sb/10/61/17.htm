Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.17

sańgrāmajid brihatsenah

śūrah praharano 'rijit

jayah subhadro bhadrāyā

vāma āyuś ca satyakah

SYNONYMS

sańgrāmajit brihatsenah — Sańgrāmajit and Brihatsena; śūrah praharanah arijit — Śūra, Praharana and Arijit; jayah subhadrah — Jaya and Subhadra; bhadrāyāh — of Bhadrā (Śaibyā); vāmah āyuś ca satyakah — Vāma, Āyur and Satyaka.

TRANSLATION

Sańgrāmajit, Brihatsena, Śūra, Praharana, Arijit, Jaya and Subhadra were the sons of Bhadrā, together with Vāma, Āyur and Satyaka.
