Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.14

śrutah kavir vrisho vīrah

subāhur bhadra ekalah

śāntir darśah pūrnamāsah

kālindyāh somako 'varah

SYNONYMS

śrutah kavih vrishah vīrah — Śruta, Kavi, Vrisha and Vīra; subāhuh — Subāhu; bhadrah — Bhadra; ekalah — one of them; śāntih darśah pūrnamāsah — Śānti, Darśa and Pūrnamāsa; kālindyāh — of Kālindī; somakah — Somaka; avarah — the youngest.

TRANSLATION

Śruta, Kavi, Vrisha, Vīra, Subāhu, Bhadra, Śānti, Darśa and Pūrnamāsa were sons of Kālindī. Her youngest son was Somaka.
