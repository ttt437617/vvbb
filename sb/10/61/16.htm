Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 61: Lord Balarāma Slays Rukmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.61.16

vriko harsho 'nilo gridhro

vardhanonnāda eva ca

mahāmsah pāvano vahnir

mitravindātmajāh kshudhih

SYNONYMS

vrikah harshah anilah gridhrah — Vrika, Harsha, Anila and Gridhra; vardhana-unnādah — Vardhana and Unnāda; eva ca — also; mahāmsah pāvanah vahnih — Mahāmsa, Pāvana and Vahni; mitravindā — of Mitravindā; ātmajāh — sons; kshudhih — Kshudhi.

TRANSLATION

Mitravindā's sons were Vrika, Harsha, Anila, Gridhra, Vardhana, Unnāda, Mahāmsa, Pāvana, Vahni and Kshudhi.
