Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 74: The Deliverance of Śiśupāla at the Rājasūya Sacrifice

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.74.1

śrī-śuka uvāca

evam yudhishthiro rājā

jarāsandha-vadham vibhoh

krishnasya cānubhāvam tam

śrutvā prītas tam abravīt

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; evam — thus; yudhishthirah — Yudhishthira; rājā — the King; jarāsandha-vadham — the killing of Jarāsandha; vibhoh — of the almighty; krishnasya — Lord Krishna; ca — and; anubhāvam — the (display of) power; tam — that; śrutvā — hearing of; prītah — pleased; tam — Him; abravīt — he addressed.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: Having thus heard of the killing of Jarāsandha, and also of almighty Krishna's wonderful power, King Yudhishthira addressed the Lord as follows with great pleasure.
