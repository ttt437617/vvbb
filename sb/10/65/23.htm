Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 65: Lord Balarāma Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.65.23

upagīyamāna-carito

vanitābhir halāyudha

vaneshu vyacarat kshīvo

mada-vihvala-locanah

SYNONYMS

upagīyamāna — being sung; caritah — His pastimes; vanitābhih — with the women; halāyudhah — Lord Balarāma; vaneshu — among the forests; vyacarat — wandered; kshīvah — inebriated; mada — by the intoxication; vihvala — overcome; locanah — His eyes.

TRANSLATION

As His deeds were sung, Lord Halāyudha wandered as if inebriated among the various forests with His girlfriends. His eyes rolled from the effects of the liquor.
