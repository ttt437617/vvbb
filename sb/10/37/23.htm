Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 37: The Killing of the Demons Keśi and Vyoma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.37.23

tvām īśvaram svāśrayam ātma-māyayā

vinirmitāśesha-viśesha-kalpanam

krīdārtham adyātta-manushya-vigraham

nato 'smi dhuryam yadu-vrishni-sātvatām

SYNONYMS

tvām — to You; īśvaram — the supreme controller; sva-āśrayam — self-contained; ātma — Your own; māyayā — by the creative potency; vinirmita — constructed; aśesha — unlimited; viśesha — particular; kalpanam — arrangements; krīda — of playing; artham — for the sake; adya — now; ātta — taken on; manushya — among humans; vigraham — battle; natah — bowed down; asmi — I am; dhuryam — to the greatest; yadu-vrishni-sātvatām — of the Yadu, Vrishni and Sātvata dynasties.

TRANSLATION

I bow down to You, the supreme controller, who are dependent only on Yourself. By Your potency You have constructed the unlimited particular arrangements of this universe. Now you have appeared as the greatest hero among the Yadus, Vrishnis and Sātvatas and have chosen to participate in human warfare.
