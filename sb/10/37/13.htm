Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 37: The Killing of the Demons Keśi and Vyoma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.37.13

sa tvam bhūdhara-bhūtānām

daitya-pramatha-rakshasām

avatīrno vināśāya

sādhunām rakshanāya ca

SYNONYMS

sah — He; tvam — Yourself; bhū-dhara — as kings; bhūtānām — who are appearing; daitya-pramatha-rakshasām — of various kinds of demons; avatīrnah — You have descended; vināśāya — for the destruction; sādhūnām — of saintly persons; rakshanāya — for the protection; ca — and.

TRANSLATION

You, that very same creator, have now descended on the earth to annihilate the Daitya, Pramatha and Rākshasa demons who are posing as kings, and also to protect the godly.
