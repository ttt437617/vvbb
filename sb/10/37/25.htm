Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 37: The Killing of the Demons Keśi and Vyoma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.37.25

bhagavān api govindo

hatvā keśinam āhave

paśūn apālayat pālaih

prītair vraja-sukhāvahah

SYNONYMS

bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; api — and; govindah — Govinda; hatvā — having killed; keśinam — the demon Keśī; āhave — in battle; paśūn — the animals; apālayat — He tended; pālaih — together with the cowherd boys; prītaih — who were pleased; vraja — to the inhabitants of Vrindāvana; sukha — happiness; āvahah — bringing.

TRANSLATION

After killing the demon Keśī in battle, the Supreme Personality of Godhead continued to tend the cows and other animals in the company of His joyful cowherd boyfriends. Thus He brought happiness to all the residents of Vrindāvana.
