Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 37: The Killing of the Demons Keśi and Vyoma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.37.21

atha te kāla-rūpasya

kshapayishnor amushya vai

akshauhinīnām nidhanam

drakshyāmy arjuna-sāratheh

SYNONYMS

atha — then; te — by You; kāla-rūpasya — who are assuming the form of time; kshapayishnoh — who is intending to effect the destruction; amushya — of (the burden of) this world; vai — indeed; akshauhinīnām — of entire armies; nidhanam — the destruction; drakshyāmi — I will see; arjuna sāratheh — by the chariot driver of Arjuna.

TRANSLATION

Subsequently I will see You appear as time personified, serving as Arjuna's chariot driver and destroying entire armies of soldiers to rid the earth of her burden.
