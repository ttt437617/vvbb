Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.10

bhavān praviśatām agre

saha-yānah purīm griham

vayam tv ihāvamucyātha

tato drakshyāmahe purīm

SYNONYMS

bhavān — you; praviśatām — should enter; agre — ahead; saha — together with; yānah — the vehicle; purīm — the city; griham — and your home; vayam — we; tu — on the other hand; iha — here; avamucya — getting down; atha — then; tatah — afterwards; drakshyāmahe — will see; purīm — the city.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:] Take the chariot and enter the city ahead of us. Then go home. After resting here a while, we will go to see the city.
