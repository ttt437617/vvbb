Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.34

sa yācito bhagavatā

paripūrnena sarvatah

sākshepam rushitah prāha

bhrityo rājñah su-durmadah

SYNONYMS

sah — he; yācitah — requested; bhagavatā — by the Supreme Lord; paripūrnena — who is absolutely complete; sarvatah — in all respects; sa-ākshepam — insultingly; rushitah — angered; prāha — he spoke; bhrityah — the servant; rājñah — of the King; su — very much; durmadah — falsely proud.

TRANSLATION

Thus requested by the Supreme Lord, who is perfectly complete in all respects, that arrogant servant of the King became angry and replied insultingly.
