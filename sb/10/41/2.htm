Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.2

so 'pi cāntarhitam vīkshya

jalād unmajya satvarah

kritvā cāvaśyakam sarvam

vismito ratham āgamat

SYNONYMS

sah — he, Akrūra; api — indeed; ca — and; antarhitam — disappeared; vīkshya — seeing; jalāt — from the water; unmajya — emerging; satvarah — quickly; kritvā — performing; ca — and; āvaśyakam — his prescribed duties; sarvam — all; vismitah — surprised; ratham — to the chariot; āgamat — went.

TRANSLATION

When Akrūra saw the vision disappear, he came out of the water and quickly finished his various ritual duties. He then returned to the chariot, astonished.
