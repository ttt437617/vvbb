Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.45

prāha nah sārthakam janma

pāvitam ca kulam prabho

pitri-devarshayo mahyam

tushtā hy āgamanena vām

SYNONYMS

prāha — he said; nah — our; sa-arthakam — worthwhile; janma — the birth; pāvitam — purified; ca — and; kulam — the family; prabho — O Lord; pitri — my forefathers; deva — the demigods; rishayah — and the great sages; mahyam — with me; tushtāh — are satisfied; hi — indeed; āgamanena — by the arrival; vām — of You two.

TRANSLATION

[Sudāmā said:] O Lord, my birth is now sanctified and my family free of contamination. Now that You both have come here, my forefathers, the demigods and the great sages are certainly all satisfied with me.
