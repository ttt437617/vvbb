Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.37

evam vikatthamānasya

kupito devakī-sutah

rajakasya karāgrena

śirah kāyād apātayat

SYNONYMS

evam — thus; vikatthamānasya — who was brazenly speaking; kupitah — angered; devakī-sutah — Krishna, the son of Devakī; rajakasya — of the washerman; kara — of one hand; agrena — with the front; śirah — the head; kāyāt — from his body; apātayat — made fall.

TRANSLATION

As the washerman thus spoke brazenly, the son of Devakī became angry, and then merely with His fingertips He separated the man's head from his body.
