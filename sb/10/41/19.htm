Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.19

athāparāhne bhagavān

krishnah sańkarshanānvitah

mathurām prāviśad gopair

didrikshuh parivāritah

SYNONYMS

atha — then; apara-ahne — in the afternoon; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; krishnah — Krishna; sańkarshana-anvitah — together with Lord Balarāma; mathurām — Mathurā; prāviśat — entered; gopaih — by the cowherd boys; didrikshuh — wanting to see; parivāritah — joined.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna desired to see Mathurā, so toward evening He took Lord Balarāma and the cowherd boys with Him and entered the city.
