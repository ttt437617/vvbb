Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.33

dehy āvayoh samucitāny

ańga vāsāmsi cārhatoh

bhavishyati param śreyo

dātus te nātra samśayah

SYNONYMS

dehi — please give; āvayoh — to Us two; samucitāni — suitable; ańga — My dear; vāsāmsi — clothes; ca — and; arhatoh — to the two who are deserving; bhavishyati — there will be; param — supreme; śreyah — benefit; dātuh — for the giver; te — you; na — there is not; atra — in this matter; samśayah — doubt.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna said:] Please give suitable garments to the two of Us, who certainly deserve them. If you grant this charity, you will undoubtedly receive the greatest benefit.
