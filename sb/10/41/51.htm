Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.51

so 'pi vavre 'calām bhaktim

tasminn evākhilātmani

tad-bhakteshu ca sauhārdam

bhūteshu ca dayām parām

SYNONYMS

sah — he; api — and; vavre — chose; acalām — unshakable; bhaktim — devotion; tasmin — to Him; eva — alone; akhila — of everything; ātmani — the Supreme Soul; tat — toward His; bhakteshu — devotees; ca — and; sauhārdam — friendship; bhūteshu — toward living beings in general; ca — and; dayām — mercy; parām — transcendental.

TRANSLATION

Sudāmā chose unshakable devotion for Krishna, the Supreme Soul of all existence; friendship with His devotees; and transcendental compassion for all living beings.
