Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.47

na hi vām vishamā drishtih

suhridor jagad-ātmanoh

samayoh sarva-bhūteshu

bhajantam bhajator api

SYNONYMS

na — there is not; hi — indeed; vām — on Your part; vishamā — biased; drishtih — vision; suhridoh — who are well-wishing friends; jagat — of the universe; ātmanoh — the Soul; samayoh — equal; sarva — to all; bhūteshu — living beings; bhajantam — those who worship You; bhajatoh — reciprocating with; api — even.

TRANSLATION

Because You are the well-wishing friends and Supreme Soul of the whole universe, You regard all with unbiased vision. Therefore, although You reciprocate Your devotees' loving worship, You always remain equally disposed toward all living beings.
