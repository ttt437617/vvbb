Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 41: Krishna and Balarāma Enter Mathurā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.41.3

tam apricchad dhrishīkeśah

kim te drishtam ivādbhutam

bhūmau viyati toye vā

tathā tvām lakshayāmahe

SYNONYMS

tam — of him; apricchat — asked; hrishīkśah — Lord Krishna; kim — whether; te — by you; drishtam — seen; iva — indeed; adbhutam — something exceptional; bhūmau — on the earth; viyati — in the sky; toye — in the water; va — or; tathā — so; tvām — you; lakshayāmahe — We surmise.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna asked Akrūra: Have you seen something wonderful on the earth, in the sky or in the water? From your appearance, We think you have.
