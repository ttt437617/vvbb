Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 7: The Killing of the Demon Trināvarta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.7.34

ekadārbhakam ādāya

svāńkam āropya bhāminī

prasnutam pāyayām āsa

stanam sneha-pariplutā

SYNONYMS

ekadā — once upon a time; arbhakam — the child; ādāya — taking; sva-ańkam — on her own lap; āropya — and placing Him; bhāminī — mother Yaśodā; prasnutam — breast milk oozing out; pāyayām āsa — fed the child; stanam — her breast; sneha-pariplutā — with great affection and love.

TRANSLATION

One day mother Yaśodā, having taken Krishna up and placed Him on her lap, was feeding Him milk from her breast with maternal affection. The milk was flowing from her breast, and the child was drinking it.
