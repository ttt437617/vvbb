Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 7: The Killing of the Demon Trināvarta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.7.28

gala-grahana-niśceshto

daityo nirgata-locanah

avyakta-rāvo nyapatat

saha-bālo vyasur vraje

SYNONYMS

gala-grahana-niśceshtah — because of Krishna's grasping the neck of the demon Trināvarta, the demon choked and could not do anything; daityah — the demon; nirgata-locanah — his eyes popped out because of pressure; avyakta-rāvah — because of choking, he could not even make a sound; nyapatat — fell down; saha-bālah — with the child; vyasuh vraje — lifeless on the ground of Vraja.

TRANSLATION

With Krishna grasping him by the throat, Trināvarta choked, unable to make even a sound or even to move his hands and legs. His eyes popping out, the demon lost his life and fell, along with the little boy, down to the ground of Vraja.
