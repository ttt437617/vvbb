Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 7: The Killing of the Demon Trināvarta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.7.22

muhūrtam abhavad goshtham

rajasā tamasāvritam

sutam yaśodā nāpaśyat

tasmin nyastavatī yatah

SYNONYMS

muhūrtam — for a moment; abhavat — there was; goshtham — throughout the whole pasturing ground; rajasā — by big particles of dust; tamasā āvritam — covered with darkness; sutam — her son; yaśodā — mother Yaśodā; na apaśyat — could not find; tasmin — in that very spot; nyastavatī — she had placed Him; yatah — where.

TRANSLATION

For a moment, the whole pasturing ground was overcast with dense darkness from the dust storm, and mother Yaśodā was unable to find her son where she had placed Him.
