Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 7: The Killing of the Demon Trināvarta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.7.23

nāpaśyat kaścanātmānam

param cāpi vimohitah

trināvarta-nisrishtābhih

śarkarābhir upadrutah

SYNONYMS

na — not; apaśyat — saw; kaścana — anyone; ātmānam — himself; param ca api — or another; vimohitah — being illusioned; trināvarta-nisrishtābhih — thrown by Trināvartāsura; śarkarābhih — by the sands; upadrutah — and thus being disturbed.

TRANSLATION

Because of the bits of sand thrown about by Trināvarta, people could not see themselves or anyone else, and thus they were illusioned and disturbed.
