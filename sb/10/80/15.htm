Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.15

sa tān ādāya viprāgryah

prayayau dvārakām kila

krishna-sandarśanam mahyam

katham syād iti cintayan

SYNONYMS

sah — he; tān — them; ādāya — taking; vipra-agryah — the best of brāhmanas; prayayau — went; dvārakām — to Dvārakā; kila — indeed; krishna-sandarśanam — the audience of Lord Krishna; mahyam — for me; katham — how; syāt — will it happen; iti — thus; cintayan — thinking.

TRANSLATION

Taking the flat rice, the saintly brāhmana set off for Dvārakā, all the while wondering "How will I be able to have Krishna's audience?"

PURPORT

Among other things, Sudāmā assumed that the gatekeepers would stop him.
