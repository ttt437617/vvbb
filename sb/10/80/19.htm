Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.19

sakhyuh priyasya viprarsher

ańga-sańgāti-nirvritah

prīto vyamuñcad ab-bindūn

netrābhyām pushkarekshanah

SYNONYMS

sakhyuh — of His friend; priyasya — dear; vipra-risheh — the sagacious brāhmana; ańga — of the body; sańga — by the contact; ati — extremely; nirvritah — ecstatic; prītah — affectionate; vyamuñcat — He released; ap — of water; bindūn — drops; netrābhyām — from His eyes; pushkara-īkshanah — the lotus-eyed Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

The lotus-eyed Supreme Lord felt intense ecstasy upon touching the body of His dear friend, the wise brāhmana, and thus He shed tears of love.
