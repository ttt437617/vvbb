Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.27

kathayām cakratur gāthāh

pūrvā guru-kule satoh

ātmanor lalitā rājan

karau grihya parasparam

SYNONYMS

kathayām cakratuh — they discussed; gāthāh — topics; pūrvāh — of the past; guru-kule — in the school of their spiritual master; satoh — who used to reside; ātmanoh — of themselves; lalitāh — charming; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); karau — hands; grihya — taking hold of; parasparam — each other's.

TRANSLATION

[Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued:] Taking each other's hands, O King, Krishna and Sudāmā talked pleasantly about how they once lived together in the school of their guru.
