Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.1

śrī-rājovāca

bhagavan yāni cānyāni

mukundasya mahātmanah

vīryāny ananta-vīryasya

śrotum icchāmi he prabho

SYNONYMS

śrī-rājā uvāca — the King (Parīkshit) said; bhagavan — my lord (Śukadeva Gosvāmī); yāni — which; ca — and; anyāni — others; mukundasya — of Lord Krishna; mahā-ātmanah — the Supreme Soul; vīryāni — valorous deeds; ananta — unlimited; vīryasya — whose valor; śrotum — to hear; icchāmi — I wish; he prabho — O master.

TRANSLATION

King Parīkshit said: My lord, O master, I wish to hear about other valorous deeds performed by the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Mukunda, whose valor is unlimited.
