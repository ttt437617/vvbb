Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.5

sūta uvāca

vishnu-rātena samprishto

bhagavān bādarāyanih

vāsudeve bhagavati

nimagna-hridayo 'bravīt

SYNONYMS

sūtah uvāca — Sūta Gosvāmī said; vishnu-rātena — by Vishnurāta (Mahārāja Parīkshit); samprishtah — well questioned; bhagavān — the powerful sage; bādarāyanih — Śukadeva; vāsudeve — in Lord Vāsudeva; bhagavati — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; nimagna — fully absorbed; hridayah — his heart; abravīt — he spoke.

TRANSLATION

Suta Gosvāmī said: Thus questioned by King Vishnurāta, the powerful sage Bādarāyani replied, his heart fully absorbed in meditation on the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Vāsudeva.
