Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.18

tam vilokyācyuto dūrāt

priyā-paryańkam āsthitah

sahasotthāya cābhyetya

dorbhyām paryagrahīn mudā

SYNONYMS

tam — him; vilokya — seeing; acyutah — Lord Krishna; dūrāt — at a distance; priyā — of His beloved consort; paryańkam — on the bed; āsthitah — seated; sahasā — immediately; utthāya — rising; ca — and; abhyetya — coming forward; dorbhyām — in His arms; paryagrahīt — embraced; mudā — with pleasure.

TRANSLATION

At that time Lord Acyuta was seated on His consort's bed. Spotting the brāhmana at some distance, the Lord immediately stood up, went forward to meet him and with great pleasure embraced him.
