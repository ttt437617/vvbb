Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 80: The Brāhmana Sudāmā Visits Lord Krishna in Dvārakā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.80.24

antah-pura-jano drishtvā

krishnenāmala-kīrtinā

vismito 'bhūd ati-prītyā

avadhūtam sabhājitam

SYNONYMS

antah-pura — of the royal palace; janah — the people; drishtvā — seeing; krishnena — by Lord Krishna; amala — spotless; kīrtinā — whose fame; vismitah — amazed; abhūt — they became; ati — intense; prītyā — with loving affection; avadhūtam — the unkempt brāhmana; sabhājitam — honored.

TRANSLATION

The people in the royal palace were astonished to see Krishna, the Lord of spotless glory, so lovingly honor this shabbily dressed brāhmana.
