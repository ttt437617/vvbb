Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.9

tadaiva kuśalam no 'bhūt

sa-nāthās te kritā vayam

jñatīn nah smaratā krishna

bhrātā me preshitas tvayā

SYNONYMS

tadā — at that time; eva — only; kuśalam — well-being; nah — our; abhūt — arose; sa — with; nāthāh — a protector; te — by You; kritāh — made; vayam — we; jñātīn — Your relatives; nah — us; smaratā — who remembered; krishna — O Krishna; bhrātā — brother (Akrūra); me — my; preshitah — sent; tvayā — by You.

TRANSLATION

[Queen Kuntī said:l My dear Krishna, our welfare was assured only when You remembered us, Your relatives, and gave us Your protection by sending my brother to visit us.
