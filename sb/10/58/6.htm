Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.6

tathaiva sātyakih pārthaih

pūjitaś cābhivanditah

nishasādāsane 'nye ca

pūjitāh paryupāsata

SYNONYMS

tathā eva — similarly; sātyakih — Sātyaki; pārthaih — by the sons of Prithā; pūjitah — worshiped; ca — and; abhivanditah — welcomed; nishasāda — sat down; āsane — on a seat; anye — the others; ca — also; pūjitāh — worshiped; paryupāsata — sat around.

TRANSLATION

Sātyaki also accepted a seat of honor after receiving worship and welcome from the Pāndavas, and the Lord's other companions, being duly honored, sat down in various places.
