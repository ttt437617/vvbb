Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.28

sa tena samanujñātah

suhridbhiś cānumoditah

āyayau dvārakām bhūyah

sātyaki-pramakhair vritah

SYNONYMS

sah — He, Lord Krishna; tena — by him, Arjuna; samanujñātah — given leave; su-hridbhih — by His well-wishers; ca — and; anumoditah — allowed; āyayau — He went; dvārakām — to Dvārakā; bhūyah — again; sātyaki-pramukhaih — by those headed by Sātyaki; vritah — accompanied.

TRANSLATION

Then Lord Krishna, given leave by Arjuna and other well-wishing relatives and friends, returned to Dvārakā with Sātyaki and the rest of His entourage.
