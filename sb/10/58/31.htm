Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.31

rājādhidevyās tanayām

mitravindām pitri-shvasuh

prasahya hritavān krishno

rājan rājñām prapaśyatām

SYNONYMS

rājādhidevyāh — of Queen Rājādhidevī; tanayām — the daughter; mitravindām — Mitravindā; pitri — of His father; svasuh — of the sister; prasahya — forcibly; hritavān — took away; krishnah — Lord Krishna; rājan — O King (Parīkshit); rājñām — the kings; prapaśyatām — as they watched.

TRANSLATION

My dear King, Lord Krishna forcibly took away Princess Mitravindā, the daughter of His aunt Rājādhidevī, before the eyes of the rival kings.
