Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.57

sutām ca madrādhipater

lakshmanām lakshanair yatām

svayam-vare jahāraikah

sa suparnah sudhām iva

SYNONYMS

sutām — the daughter; ca — and; madra-adhipateh — of the ruler of Madra; lakshmanām — Lakshmanā; lakshanaih — with all good qualities; yutām — endowed; svayam-vare — during her ceremony to choose a husband; jahāra — took away; ekah — alone; sah — He, Lord Krishna; suparnah — Garuda; sudhām — nectar; iva — as.

TRANSLATION

Then the Lord married Lakshmanā, the daughter of the King of Madra. Krishna appeared alone at her svayamvara ceremony and took her away, just as Garuda once stole the demigods' nectar.
