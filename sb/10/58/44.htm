Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.44

yad ime nigrihītāh syus

tvayaiva yadu-nandana

varo bhavān abhimato

duhitur me śriyah-pate

SYNONYMS

yat — if; ime — they; nigrihītāh — subdued; syuh — become; tvayā — by You; eva — indeed; yadu-nandana — O descendant of Yadu; varah — groom; bhavān — You; abhimatah — approved; duhituh — for the daughter; me — my; śriyah — of the goddess of fortune; pate — O husband.

TRANSLATION

If You can subdue them, O descendant of Yadu, You will certainly be the appropriate bridegroom for my daughter, O Lord of Śrī.
