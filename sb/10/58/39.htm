Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 58: Krishna Marries Five Princesses

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.58.39

śrī-śuka uvāca

tam āha bhagavān hrishtah

kritāsana-parigrahah

megha-gambhīrayā vācā

sa-smitam kuru-nandana

SYNONYMS

śrī-śukah uvāca — Śukadeva Gosvāmī said; tam — to him; āha — said; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; hrishtah — pleased; krita — having done; āsana — of a seat; parigrahah — the acceptance; megha — like a cloud; gambhīrayā — deep; vācā — in a voice; sa — with; smitam — a smile; kuru — of the Kurus; nandana — O beloved descendant.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī said: O beloved descendant of Kuru, the Supreme Lord was pleased, and after accepting a comfortable seat He smiled and addressed the King in a voice as deep as the rumbling of a cloud.
