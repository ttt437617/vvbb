Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 24: Worshiping Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.24.7

tatra tāvat kriyā-yogo

bhavatām kim vicāritah

atha vā laukikas tan me

pricchatah sādhu bhanyatām

SYNONYMS

tatra tāvat — that being the case; kriyā-yogah — this fruitive endeavor; bhavatām — of yours; kim — whether; vicāritah — learned from the scriptures; atha vā — or else; laukikah — of ordinary custom; tat — that; me — to Me; pricchatah — who am inquiring; sādhu — clearly; bhanyatām — it should be explained.

TRANSLATION

Such being the case, this ritualistic endeavor of yours should be clearly explained to Me. Is it a ceremony based on scriptural injunction, or simply a custom of ordinary society?
