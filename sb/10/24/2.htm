Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 24: Worshiping Govardhana Hill

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.24.2

tad-abhijño 'pi bhagavān

sarvātmā sarva-darśanah

praśrayāvanato 'pricchad

vriddhān nanda-purogamān

SYNONYMS

tat-abhijñah — being in full knowledge about it; api — although; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; sarva-ātmā — the Supersoul within everyone's heart; sarva-darśanah — the omniscient Personality of Godhead; praśraya-avanatah — bowing down humbly; apricchat — He inquired; vriddhān — from the elders; nanda-purah-gamān — headed by Mahārāja Nanda.

TRANSLATION

Being the omniscient Supersoul, the Supreme Lord Krishna already understood the situation, yet He still humbly inquired from the elders, headed by His father, Nanda Mahārāja.

PURPORT

Lord Krishna was eager to enact His pastime of lifting Govardhana Hill and defeating the false pride of Indra, and thus He cleverly inquired from His father about the imminent sacrifice.
