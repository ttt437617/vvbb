Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 48: Krishna Pleases His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.48.12

akrūra-bhavanam krishnah

saha-rāmoddhavah prabhuh

kiñcic cikīrshayan prāgād

akrūra-prīya-kāmyayā

SYNONYMS

akrūra-bhavanam — the home of Akrūra; krishnah — Krishna; saha — with; rāma-uddhavah — Lord Balarāma and Uddhava; prabhuh — the Supreme Lord; kiñcit — something; cikīrshayan — wanting to have done; prāgāt — went; akrūra — of Akrūra; priya — the satisfaction; kāmyayā — desiring.

TRANSLATION

Then Lord Krishna, wanting to have some things done, went to Akrūra's house with Balarāma and Uddhava. The Lord also desired to please Akrūra.

PURPORT

The previous incident of Lord Krishna's visit to Trivakrā's house, and now His visit to Akrūra's, gives a fascinating glimpse into the daily activities of Śrī Krishna in Mathurā City.
