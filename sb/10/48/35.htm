Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 48: Krishna Pleases His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.48.35

gaccha jānīhi tad-vrittam

adhunā sādhv asādhu vā

vijñāya tad vidhāsyāmo

yathā śam suhridām bhavet

SYNONYMS

gaccha — go; jānīhi — learn; tat — his (Dhritarāshtra's); vrittam — activity; adhunā — at present; sādhu — good; asādhu — evil; vā — or; vijñāya — knowing; tat — that; vidhāsyāmah — We will arrange; yathā — so that; śam — the benefit; suhridām — of Our dear ones; bhavet — will be.

TRANSLATION

Go and see whether Dhritarāshtra is acting properly or not. When We find out, We will make the necessary arrangements to help Our dear friends.
