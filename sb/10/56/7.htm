Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 56: The Syamantaka Jewel

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.56.7

esha āyāti savitā

tvām didrikshur jagat-pate

mushnan gabhasti-cakrena

nrinām cakshūmshi tigma-guh

SYNONYMS

eshah — this; āyāti — comes; savitā — the sun-god; tvām — You; didrikshuh — wanting to see; jagat-pate — O Lord of the universe; mushnan — stealing; gabhasti — of his rays; cakrena — with the circle; nrinām — of men; cakshūmshi — the eyes; tigma — intense; guh — whose radiation.

TRANSLATION

Lord Savitā has come to see You, O Lord of the universe. He is blinding everyone's eyes with his intensely effulgent rays.
