Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 56: The Syamantaka Jewel

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.56.27

tvam hi viśva-srijām srashtā

srishtānām api yac ca sat

kālah kalayatām īśah

para ātmā tathātmanām

SYNONYMS

tvam — You; hi — indeed; viśva — of the universe; srijām — of the creators; srashtā — the creator; srishtānām — of created entities; api — also; yat — which; ca — and; sat — underlying substance; kālah — the subduer; kalayatām — of subduers; īśah — the Supreme Lord; parah ātmā — the Supreme Soul; tathā — also; ātmanām — of all souls.

TRANSLATION

You are the ultimate creator of all creators of the universe, and of everything created You are the underlying substance. You are the subduer of all subduers, the Supreme Lord and Supreme Soul of all souls.

PURPORT

As Lord Kapila states in the Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (3.25.42): mrityuś carati mad-bhayāt. "Death himself moves about out of fear of Me."
