Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 56: The Syamantaka Jewel

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.56.35

satrājitam śapantas te

duhkhitā dvārakaukasah

upatasthuś candrabhāgām

durgām krishnopalabdhaye

SYNONYMS

satrājitam — Satrājit; śapantah — cursing; te — they; duhkhitāh — sorrowful; dvārakā-okasah — the residents of Dvārakā; upatasthuh — worshiped; candrabhāgām — Candrabhāgā; durgām — Durgā; krishna-upalabdhaye — in order to obtain Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Cursing Satrājit, the sorrowful residents of Dvārakā approached the Durgā deity named Candrabhāgā and prayed to her for Krishna's return.
