Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 56: The Syamantaka Jewel

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.56.19

riksha-rāja-bilam bhīmam

andhena tamasāvritam

eko viveśa bhagavān

avasthāpya bahih prajāh

SYNONYMS

riksha-rāja — of the king of the bears; bilam — the cave; bhīmam — terrifying; andhena tamasā — by blinding darkness; āvritam — covered; ekah — alone; viveśa — entered; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; avasthāpya — stationing; bahih — outside; prajāh — the citizens.

TRANSLATION

The Lord stationed His subjects outside the terrifying, pitch-dark cave of the king of the bears, and then He entered alone.
