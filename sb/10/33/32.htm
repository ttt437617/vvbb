Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 33: The Rāsa Dance

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.33.32

kuśalācaritenaishām

iha svārtho na vidyate

viparyayena vānartho

nirahańkārinām prabho

SYNONYMS

kuśala — pious; ācaritena — by activity; eshām — for them; iha — in this world; sva-arthah — selfish benefit; na vidyate — does not accrue; viparyayena — by the opposite; vā — or; anarthah — undesirable reactions; nirahańkārinām — who are free from false ego; prabho — my dear sir.

TRANSLATION

My dear Prabhu, when these great persons who are free from false ego act piously in this world, they have no selfish motives to fulfill, and even when they act in apparent contradiction to the laws of piety, they are not subject to sinful reactions.
