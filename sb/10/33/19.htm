Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 33: The Rāsa Dance

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.33.19

kritvā tāvantam ātmānam

yāvatīr gopa-yoshitah

reme sa bhagavāms tābhir

ātmārāmo 'pi līlayā

SYNONYMS

kritvā — making; tāvantam — expanded that many times; ātmānam — Himself; yāvatīh — as many as; gopa-yoshitah — cowherd women; reme — enjoyed; sah — He; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; tābhih — with them; ātma-ārāmah — self-satisfied; api — although; līlayā — as a pastime.

TRANSLATION

Expanding Himself us many times as there were cowherd women to associate with, the Supreme Lord, though self-satisfied, playfully enjoyed their company.

PURPORT

As Śrīla Viśvanātha Cakravartī points out, it has already been explained that Lord Krishna is eternally free from all material desire, perfect on the platform of spiritual self-satisfaction.
