Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 28: Krishna Rescues Nanda Mahārāja from the Abode of Varuna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.28.8

mamāpy anugraham krishna

kartum arhasy aśesha-drik

govinda nīyatām esha

pitā te pitri-vatsala

SYNONYMS

mama — to me; api — even; anugraham — mercy; krishna — O Lord Krishna; kartum arhasi — please do; aśesha — of everything; drik — O You who see; govinda — O Govinda; nīyatām — may he be taken; eshah — this; pitā — father; te — Your; pitri-vatsala — O You who are most affectionate to Your parents.

TRANSLATION

O Krishna, O seer of everything, please give Your mercy even to me. O Govinda, You are most affectionate to Your father. Please take him home.
