Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 28: Krishna Rescues Nanda Mahārāja from the Abode of Varuna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.28.12

iti svānām sa bhagavān

vijñāyākhila-drik svayam

sańkalpa-siddhaye teshām

kripayaitad acintayat

SYNONYMS

iti — such; svānām — of His personal devotees; sah — He; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; vijñāya — understanding; akhila-drik — the seer of everything; svayam — Himself; sańkalpa — of the imagined desire; siddhaye — for the realization; teshām — their; kripayā — compassionately; etat — this (as follows in the next verse); acintayat — thought.

TRANSLATION

Because He sees everything, Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, automatically understood what the cowherd men were conjecturing. Wanting to show His compassion to them by fulfilling their desires, the Lord thought as follows.
