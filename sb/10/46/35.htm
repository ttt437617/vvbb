Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.35

hatvā kamsam rańga-madhye

pratīpam sarva-sātvatām

yad āha vah samāgatya

krishnah satyam karoti tat

SYNONYMS

hatvā — having killed; kamsam — Kamsa; rańga — the arena; madhye — within; pratīpam — the enemy; sarva-sātvatām — of all the Yadus; yat — what; āha — He spoke; vah — to you; samāgatya — by coming back; krishnah — Krishna; satyam — true; karoti — will make; tat — that.

TRANSLATION

Having killed Kamsa, the enemy of all the Yadus, in the wrestling arena, Krishna will now surely fulfill His promise to you by coming back.
