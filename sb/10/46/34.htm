Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.34

āgamishyaty adīrghena

kālena vrajam acyutah

priyam vidhāsyate pitror

bhagavān sātvatām patih

SYNONYMS

āgamishyati — He will return; adīrghena — not long; kālena — in time; vrajam — to Vraja; acyutah — Krishna, the infallible one; priyam — satisfaction; vidhāsyate — He will give; pitroh — to His parents; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; sātvatām — of the devotees; patih — master and protector.

TRANSLATION

Infallible Krishna, the Lord of the devotees, will soon return to Vraja to satisfy His parents.

PURPORT

Here Uddhava begins to deliver Lord Krishna's message.
