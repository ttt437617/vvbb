Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.21

smaratām krishna-vīryāni

līlāpāńga-nirīkshitam

hasitam bhāshitam cāńga

sarvā nah śithilāh kriyāh

SYNONYMS

smaratām — who are remembering; krishna-vīryāni — the valorous deeds of Krishna; līlā — playful; apāńga — with sidelong glances; nirīkshitam — His looking; hasitam — smiling; bhāshitam — speaking; ca — and; ańga — my dear (Uddhava); sarvāh — all; nah — for us; śithilāh — slackened; kriyāh — material activities.

TRANSLATION

As we remember the wonderful deeds Krishna performed, His playful sidelong glances, His smiles and His words, O Uddhava, we forget all our material engagements.
