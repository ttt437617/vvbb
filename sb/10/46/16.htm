Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.16

kaccid ańga mahā-bhāga

sakhā nah śūra-nandanah

āste kuśaly apatyādyair

yukto muktah suhrid-vratah

SYNONYMS

kaccit — whether; ańga — my dear; mahā-bhaga — O most fortunate one; sakhā — the friend; nah — our; śūra-nandanah — the son of King Śūra (Vasudeva); āste — lives; kuśalī — well; apatya-ādyaih — with his children and so on; yuktah — joined; muktah — freed; suhrit — to his friends; vratah — who is devoted.

TRANSLATION

[Nanda Mahārāja said:] My dear most fortunate one, does the son of Śūra fare well, now that he is free and has rejoined his children and other relatives?
