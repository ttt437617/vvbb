Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.47

bhagavaty udite sūrye

nanda-dvāri vrajaukasah

drishtvā ratham śātakaumbham

kasyāyam iti cābruvan

SYNONYMS

bhagavati — the lord; udite — when he rose; sūrye — the sun; nanda-dvāri — in the doorway of Nanda Mahārāja's home; vraja-okasah — the residents of Vraja; drishtvā — seeing; ratham — the chariot; śātakaumbham — made of gold; kasya — whose; ayam — this; iti — thus; ca — and; abruvan — they spoke.

TRANSLATION

When the godly sun had risen, the people of Vraja noticed the golden chariot in front of Nanda Mahārāja's doorway. "Who does this belong to?" they asked.
