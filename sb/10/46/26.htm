Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.26

pralambo dhenuko 'rishtas

trināvarto bakādayah

daityāh surāsura-jito

hatā yeneha līlayā

SYNONYMS

pralambah dhenukah arishtah — Pralamba, Dhenuka and Arishta; trināvartah — Trināvarta; baka-ādayah — Baka and others; daityāh — demons; sura-asura — both the demigods and the demons; jitah — who conquered; hatāh — killed; yena — by whom; iha — here (in Vrindāvana); līlayā — easily.

TRANSLATION

Here in Vrindāvana, Krishna and Balarāma easily destroyed demons like Pralamba, Dhenuka, Arista, Trināvarta and Baka, who had themselves defeated both demigods and other demons.
