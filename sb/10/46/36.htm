Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 46: Uddhava Visits Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.46.36

mā khidyatam mahā-bhāgau

drakshyathah krishnam antike

antar hridi sa bhūtānām

āste jyotir ivaidhasi

SYNONYMS

mā khidyatam — please do not lament; mahā-bhāgau — O most fortunate ones; drakshyathah — you will see; krishnam — Krishna; antike — in the near future; antah — within; hridi — the hearts; sah — He; bhūtānām — of all living beings; āste — is present; jyotih — fire; iva — just as; edhasi — within firewood.

TRANSLATION

O most fortunate ones, do not lament. You will see Krishna again very soon. He is present in the hearts of all living beings, just as fire lies dormant in wood.

PURPORT

Uddhava understood that Nanda and Yaśodā were very impatient to see Krishna, and thus he reassured them that Śrī Krishna would come soon.
