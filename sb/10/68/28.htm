Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 68: The Marriage of Sāmba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.68.28

katham indro 'pi kurubhir

bhīshma-dronārjunādibhih

adattam avarundhīta

simha-grastam ivoranah

SYNONYMS

katham — how; indrah — Lord Indra; api — even; kurubhih — by the Kurus; bhīshma-drona-arjuna-ādibhih — Bhīshma, Drona, Arjuna and others; adattam — not given; avarundhīta — would usurp; simha — by a lion; grastam — that which has been seized; iva — as; uranah — a sheep.

TRANSLATION

How would even Indra dare usurp anything that Bhīshma, Drona, Arjuna or the other Kurus have not given him? It would be like a lamb claiming the lion's kill.
