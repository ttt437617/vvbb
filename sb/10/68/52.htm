Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 68: The Marriage of Sāmba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.68.52

pratigrihya tu tat sarvam

bhagavān sātvatarshabhah

sa-sutah sa-snushah prāyāt

suhridbhir abhinanditah

SYNONYMS

pratigrihya — accepting; tu — and; tat — that; sarvam — all; bhagavān — the Supreme Lord; sātvata — of the Yādavas; rishabhah — the chief; sa — with; sutah — His son; sa — and with; snushah — His daughter-in-law; prāyāt — He departed; su-hridbhih — by His well-wishers (the Kurus); abhinanditah — bid farewell.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Lord, chief of the Yādavas, accepted all these gifts and then departed with His son and daughter-in-law as His well-wishers bid Him farewell.
