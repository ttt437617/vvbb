Canto 10: The Summum BonumChapter 68: The Marriage of Sāmba

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 10.68.38

bhuñjate kurubhir dattam

bhū-khandam vrishnayah kila

upānahah kila vayam

svayam tu kuravah śirah

SYNONYMS

bhuñjate — they enjoy; kurubhih — by the Kurus; dattam — granted; bhū — of land; khandam — a limited parcel; vrishnayah — the Vrishnis; kila — indeed; upānahah — shoes; kila — indeed; vayam — we; svayam — themselves; tu — however; kuravah — the Kurus; śirah — the head.

TRANSLATION

"We Vrishnis enjoy only whatever small parcel of land the Kurus allow us? And we are indeed shoes, whereas the Kurus are the head?
