Canto 5: The Creative ImpetusChapter 15: The Glories of the Descendants of King Priyavrata

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 5.15.2

tasmād vriddhasenāyām devatājin-nāma putro 'bhavat

SYNONYMS

tasmāt — from Sumati; vriddha-senāyām — in the womb of his wife, named Vriddhasenā; devatājit-nāma — named Devatājit; putrah — a son; abhavat — was born.

TRANSLATION

From Sumati, a son named Devatājit was born by the womb of his wife named Vriddhasenā.
