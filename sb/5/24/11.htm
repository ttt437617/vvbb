Canto 5: The Creative ImpetusChapter 24: The Subterranean Heavenly Planets

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 5.24.11

yatra ha vāva na bhayam aho-rātrādibhih kāla-vibhāgair upalakshyate

SYNONYMS

yatra — where; ha vāva — certainly; na — not; bhayam — fearfulness; ahah-rātra-ādibhih — because of days and nights; kāla-vibhāgaih — the divisions of time; upalakshyate — is experienced.

TRANSLATION

Since there is no sunshine in those subterranean planets, time is not divided into days and nights, and consequently fear produced by time does not exist.
