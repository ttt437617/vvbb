Canto 5: The Creative ImpetusChapter 9: The Supreme Character of Jada Bharata

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 5.9.19

evam eva khalu mahad-abhicārāti-kramah kārtsnyenātmane phalati

SYNONYMS

evam eva — in this way; khalu — indeed; mahat — to great personalities; abhicāra — in the form of envy; ati-kramah — the limit of offense; kārtsnyena — always; ātmane — unto oneself; phalati — gives the result.

TRANSLATION

When an envious person commits an offense before a great personality, he is always punished in the way mentioned above.
