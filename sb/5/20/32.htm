Canto 5: The Creative ImpetusChapter 20: Studying the Structure of the Universe

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 5.20.32

tad-varsha-purushā bhagavantam brahma-rūpinam sakarmakena karmanārādhayantīdam codāharanti

SYNONYMS

tat-varsha-purushāh — the inhabitants of that island; bhagavantam — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; brahma-rūpinam — exhibited as Lord Brahmā being seated on the lotus; sa-karmakena — for fulfillment of material desires; karmanā — by performing ritualistic activities according to the Vedas; ārādhayanti — worship; idam — this; ca — and; udāharanti — they chant.

TRANSLATION

For the fulfillment of material desires, the inhabitants of this tract of land worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead as represented by Lord Brahmā. They offer prayers to the Lord as follows.
