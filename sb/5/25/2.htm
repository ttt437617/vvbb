Canto 5: The Creative ImpetusChapter 25: The Glories of Lord Ananta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrīmad Bhāgavatam 5.25.2

yasyedam kshiti-mandalam bhagavato 'nanta-mūrteh sahasra-śirasa ekasminn eva śīrshani dhriyamānam siddhārtha iva lakshyate

SYNONYMS

yasya — of whom; idam — this; kshiti-mandalam — universe; bhagavatah — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ananta-mūrteh — in the form of Anantadeva; sahasra-śirasah — who has thousands of hoods; ekasmin — on one; eva — only; śīrshani — hood; dhriyamānam — is being sustained; siddhārthah iva — and like a white mustard seed; lakshyate — is seen.

TRANSLATION

Śukadeva Gosvāmī continued: This great universe, situated on one of Lord Anantadeva's thousands of hoods, appears just like a white mustard seed. It is infinitesimal compared to the hood of Lord Ananta.
