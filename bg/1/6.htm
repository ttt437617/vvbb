Chapter 1: Observing the Armies on the Battlefield of Kurukshetra

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Bhagavad-gītā As It Is 1.6

yudhāmanyuś ca vikrānta

uttamaujāś ca vīryavān

saubhadro draupadeyāś ca

sarva eva mahā-rathāh

SYNONYMS

yudhāmanyuh — Yudhāmanyu; ca — and; vikrāntah — mighty; uttamaujāh — Uttamaujā; ca — and; vīrya-vān — very powerful; saubhadrah — the son of Subhadrā; draupadeyāh — the sons of Draupadī; ca — and; sarve — all; eva — certainly; mahā-rathāh — great chariot fighters.

TRANSLATION

There are the mighty Yudhāmanyu, the very powerful Uttamaujā, the son of Subhadrā and the sons of Draupadī. All these warriors are great chariot fighters.
