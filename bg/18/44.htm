Chapter 18: Conclusion — The Perfection of Renunciation

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Bhagavad-gītā As It Is 18.44

krishi-go-rakshya-vānijyam

vaiśya-karma svabhāva-jam

paricaryātmakam karma

śūdrasyāpi svabhāva-jam

SYNONYMS

krishi — plowing; go — of cows; rakshya — protection; vānijyam — trade; vaiśya — of a vaiśya; karma — duty; svabhāva-jam — born of his own nature; paricaryā — service; ātmakam — consisting of; karma — duty; śūdrasya — of the śūdra; api — also; svabhāva-jam — born of his own nature.

TRANSLATION

Farming, cow protection and business are the natural work for the vaiśyas, and for the śūdras there is labor and service to others.
