Chapter 8: Attaining the Supreme

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Bhagavad-gītā As It Is 8.18

avyaktād vyaktayah sarvāh

prabhavanty ahar-āgame

rātry-āgame pralīyante

tatraivāvyakta-samjñake

SYNONYMS

avyaktāt — from the unmanifest; vyaktayah — living entities; sarvāh — all; prabhavanti — become manifest; ahah-āgame — at the beginning of the day; rātri-āgame — at the fall of night; pralīyante — are annihilated; tatra — into that; eva — certainly; avyakta — the unmanifest; samjñake — which is called.

TRANSLATION

At the beginning of Brahmā's day, all living entities become manifest from the unmanifest state, and thereafter, when the night falls, they are merged into the unmanifest again.
