Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.124

pūrve yaiche kurukshetre saba gopī-gana

krishnera darśana pāñā ānandita mana

SYNONYMS

pūrve yaiche — as previously; kuru-kshetre — in the holy place known as Kurukshetra; saba gopī-gana — all the gopīs of Vrindāvana; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; darśana — interview; pāñā — getting; ānandita mana — very pleased within the mind.

TRANSLATION

Formerly, all the gopīs of Vrindāvana were very pleased when they met with Krishna in the holy place Kurukshetra.
