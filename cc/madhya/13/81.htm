Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.81

eta padi' punarapi karila pranāma

yoda-hāte bhakta-gana vande bhagavān

SYNONYMS

eta padi' — reciting these; punarapi — again; karila — the Lord offered; pranāma — obeisances; yoda-hāte — with folded hands; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; vande — offer prayer; bhagavān — unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Having recited all these verses from scripture, the Lord again offered His obeisances, and all the devotees, with folded hands, also offered prayers to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
