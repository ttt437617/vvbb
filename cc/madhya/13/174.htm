Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.174

dekhite ākarshaye sabāra citta-mana

premāmrita-vrishtye prabhu siñce sabāra mana

SYNONYMS

dekhite — by seeing; ākarshaye — attracts; sabāra — of everyone; citta-mana — mind and consciousness; prema-amrita-vrishtye — by the pouring of the nectar of transcendental love for God; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; siñce — sprinkled; sabāra — everyone's; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

The sight of all these symptoms attracted everyone's mind and consciousness. Indeed, the Lord sprinkled everyone's mind with the nectar of transcendental love of Godhead.
