Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.176

prabhura nritya prema dekhi' haya camatkāra

krishna-prema uchalila hridaye sabāra

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya — dancing; prema — love; dekhi' — seeing; haya — become; camatkāra — astonished; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; uchalila — infatuated; hridaye — in the hearts; sabāra — of everyone.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing the dancing and ecstatic love of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone became astonished. In their hearts they became infatuated with love of Krishna.
