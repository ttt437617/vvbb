Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.204

yata bhakta kīrtanīyā āsiyā ārāme

prati-vriksha-tale sabe karena viśrāme

SYNONYMS

yata bhakta — all the devotees; kīrtanīyā — who were performing sańkīrtana; āsiyā — coming; ārāme — in the resting place; prati-vriksha-tale — under each and every tree; sabe — all of them; karena — take; viśrāme — rest.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees who had been performing sańkīrtana came there and took rest under each and every tree.
