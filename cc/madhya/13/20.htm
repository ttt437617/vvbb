Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.20

śata śata su-cāmara-darpane ujjvala

upare patākā śobhe cāńdoyā nirmala

SYNONYMS

śata śata — hundreds upon hundreds; su-cāmara — beautiful white whisks; darpane — with mirrors; ujjvala — very bright; upare — on the top; patākā — flag; śobhe — looks beautiful; cāńdoyā — canopy; nirmala — thoroughly cleansed.

TRANSLATION

The decorations included bright mirrors and hundreds and hundreds of cāmaras [white whisks made of yak tails]. On top of the car were a neat and clean canopy and a very beautiful flag.
