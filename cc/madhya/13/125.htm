Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.125

jagannātha dekhi' prabhura se bhāva uthila

sei bhāvāvishta hañā dhuyā gāoyāila

SYNONYMS

jagannātha dekhi' — by seeing Lord Jagannātha; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; se bhāva — that ecstasy; uthila — awakened; sei — that; bhāva-āvishta — absorbed in that ecstasy; hañā — becoming; dhuyā — refrain; gāoyāila — caused to sing.

TRANSLATION

Similarly, after seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu awoke with the ecstasy of the gopīs. Being absorbed in this ecstasy, He asked Svarūpa Dāmodara to sing the refrain.
