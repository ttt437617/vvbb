Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.55

kīrtana dekhiyā jagannātha harashita

sańkīrtana dekhe ratha kariyā sthagita

SYNONYMS

kīrtana dekhiyā — by seeing the performance of sańkīrtana; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; harashita — very pleased; sańkīrtana — performance of sańkīrtana; dekhe — sees; ratha — the car; kariyā sthagita — stopping.

TRANSLATION

Lord Jagannātha was very much pleased by the sańkīrtana, and He brought His car to a standstill just to see the performance.
