Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.85

āchāda khāñā pade bhūme gadi' yāya

suvarna-parvata yaiche bhūmete lotāya

SYNONYMS

āchāda khāñā — crashing; pade — falls; bhūme — on the ground; gadi' — rolling; yāya — goes; suvarna-parvata — a golden mountain; yaiche — as if; bhūmete — on the ground; lotāya — rolls.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down with a crash while dancing, He would roll on the ground. At such times it appeared that a golden mountain was rolling on the ground.
