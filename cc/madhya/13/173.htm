Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.173

prabhura śarīra yena śuddha-hemācala

bhāva-pushpa-druma tāhe pushpita sakala

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śarīra — body; yena — as if; śuddha — transcendental; hemācala — Himalayan mountain; bhāva — emotional; pushpa-druma — flower trees; tāhe — in that situation; pushpita — blooming with flowers; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's body appeared like a transcendental Himalayan mountain bearing ecstatic emotional flower trees, all of them blooming.
