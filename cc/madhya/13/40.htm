Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.40

vāsudeva, gopīnātha, murāri yāhāń gāya

mukunda — pradhāna kaila āra sampradāya

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha; murāri — Murāri; yāhāń — where; gāya — sing; mukunda — Mukunda; pradhāna — chief; kaila — formed; āra — another; sampradāya — group.

TRANSLATION

Another group was formed consisting of Vāsudeva, Gopīnātha and Murāri. All these were responsive singers, and Mukunda was the chief singer.
