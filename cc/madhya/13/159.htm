Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.159

eta tāńre kahi krishna, vraje yāite satrishna,

eka śloka padi' śunāila

sei śloka śuni' rādhā, khāndila sakala bādhā,

krishna-prāptye pratīti ha-ila

SYNONYMS

eta — so much; tāńre — unto Rādhārānī; kahi — speaking; krishna — Lord Krishna; vraje — in Vrindāvana; yāite — to go; sa-trishna — very anxious; eka śloka — one verse; padi' — reciting; śunāila — making Her hear; sei śloka — that verse; śuni' — hearing; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; khāndila — disappeared; sakala — all kinds of; bādhā — hindrances; krishna-prāptye — in achieving Krishna; pratīti ha-ila — there was assurance.

TRANSLATION

While speaking to Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, Krishna became very anxious to return to Vrindāvana. He made Her listen to a verse which banished all Her difficulties and which assured Her that She would again attain Krishna.
