Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.70

āge śuna jagannāthera gundicā-gamana

tāra āge prabhu yaiche karilā nartana

SYNONYMS

āge — ahead; śuna — hear; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; gundicā-gamana — going to the Gundicā temple; tāra āge — before that; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yaiche — as; karilā — did; nartana — dancing.

TRANSLATION

Now please hear about Lord Jagannātha's going to the Gundicā temple while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced before the Ratha car.
