Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.71

ei-mata kīrtana prabhu karila kata-kshana

āpana-udyoge nācāila bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; kīrtana — chanting; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karila — performed; kata-kshana — for some time; āpana — personal; udyoge — by endeavor; nācāila — caused to dance; bhakta-gana — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

The Lord performed kīrtana for some time and, through His own endeavor, inspired all the devotees to dance.
