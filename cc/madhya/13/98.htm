Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.98

prabhura nritya dekhi' loke haila camatkāra

anya āchuk, jagannāthera ānanda apāra

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya — dancing; dekhi' — seeing; loke — everyone; haila — became; camatkāra — astonished; anya āchuk — let alone others; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; ānanda apāra — there was extreme happiness.

TRANSLATION

Everyone was astonished by the dancing of Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and even Lord Jagannātha became extremely happy to see Him.
