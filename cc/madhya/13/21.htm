Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.21

ghāghara, kińkinī bāje, ghantāra kvanita

nānā citra-patta-vastre ratha vibhūshita

SYNONYMS

ghāghara — gongs; kińkinī — ankle bells; bāje — were sounding; ghantāra — of bells; kvanita — tinkling sound; nānā — various; citra — pictures; patta-vastre — with silken cloth; ratha — the car; vibhūshita — decorated.

TRANSLATION

The car was also decorated with silken cloth and various pictures. Many brass bells, gongs and ankle bells rang.
