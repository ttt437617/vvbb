Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.94

nrityāveśe śrīnivāsa kichui nā jāne

bāra bāra thele, teńho krodha haila mane

SYNONYMS

nritya-āveśe — fully absorbed in seeing the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrīnivāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; kichui — anything; nā — does not; jāne — know; bāra bāra — again and again; thele — when he pushes; teńho — Śrīvāsa; krodha — angry; haila — became; mane — in the mind.

TRANSLATION

Absorbed in watching Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dance, Śrīvāsa Thākura could not understand why he was being touched and pushed. After he was pushed again and again, he became angry.
