Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.112

tāndava-nritya chādi' svarūpere ājñā dila

hridaya jāniyā svarūpa gāite lāgila

SYNONYMS

tāndava-nritya chādi' — giving up such devastating dancing; svarūpere — unto Svarūpa Dāmodara; ājñā dila — gave an order; hridaya — mind; jāniyā — knowing; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; gāite lāgila — began to sing.

TRANSLATION

After abandoning the dancing, the Lord ordered Svarūpa Dāmodara to sing. Understanding His mind, Svarūpa Dāmodara began to sing as follows.
