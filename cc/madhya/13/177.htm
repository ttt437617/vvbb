Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.177

preme nāce, gāya, loka, kare kolāhala

prabhura nritya dekhi' sabe ānande vihvala

SYNONYMS

preme — in ecstatic love; nāce — dance; gāya — chant; loka — people in general; kare — make; kolāhala — a great noise; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya — dancing; dekhi' — seeing; sabe — everyone; ānande — in transcendental bliss; vihvala — overwhelmed.

TRANSLATION

Everyone danced and chanted in ecstatic love, and a great noise resounded. Everyone was overwhelmed with transcendental bliss just to see the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
