Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.171

ānandonmāde uthāya bhāvera tarańga

nānā-bhāva-sainye upajila yuddha-rańga

SYNONYMS

ānanda-unmāde — the madness of transcendental bliss; uthāya — causes to arise; bhāvera — of emotions; tarańga — waves; nānā — various; bhāva — emotions; sainye — among soldiers; upajila — there appeared; yuddha-rańga — fighting.

TRANSLATION

The madness of transcendental bliss created waves of various emotions. The emotions appeared like opposing soldiers staging a fight.
