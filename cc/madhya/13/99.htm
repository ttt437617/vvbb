Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.99

ratha sthira kaila, āge nā kare gamana

animisha-netre kare nritya daraśana

SYNONYMS

ratha — the car; sthira kaila — stopped; āge — forward; nā — not; kare — does; gamana — moving; animisha — unblinking; netre — with eyes; kare — does; nritya — of the dancing; daraśana — seeing.

TRANSLATION

The car came to a complete standstill and remained immobile while Lord Jagannātha, with unblinking eyes, watched the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
