Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.129

ihāń rāja-veśa, sańge saba kshatriya-gana

tāhāń gopa-veśa, sańge muralī-vādana

SYNONYMS

ihāń — here, at Kurukshetra; rāja-veśa — dressed like a royal prince; sańge — with You; saba — all; kshatriya-gana — great warriors; tāhāń — there, in Vrindāvana; gopa-veśa — dressed like a cowherd boy; sańge — with You; muralī-vādana — the blowing of Your transcendental flute.

TRANSLATION

"Here at Kurukshetra You are dressed like a royal prince, accompanied by great warriors, but in Vrindāvana You appeared just like an ordinary cowherd boy, accompanied only by Your beautiful flute.
