Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.90

bāhire pratāparudra lañā pātra-gana

mandala hañā kare loka nivārana

SYNONYMS

bāhire — outside; pratāparudra — King Pratāparudra; lañā — taking; pātra-gana — his own associates; mandala — circle; hañā — becoming; kare — does; loka — of the crowd; nivārana — checking.

TRANSLATION

Mahārāja Pratāparudra and his personal assistants formed a third circle around the two inner circles just to check the crowds from coming too near.
