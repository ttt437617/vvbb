Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.127

tathāpi āmāra mana hare vrindāvana

vrindāvane udaya karāo āpana-carana

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — yet; āmāra — My; mana — mind; hare — attracts; vrindāvana — Śrī Vrindāvana; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; udaya karāo — please cause to reappear; āpana-carana — the brilliance of Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"Although We are both the same, My mind is still attracted to Vrindāvana-dhāma. I wish that You will please again appear with Your lotus feet in Vrindāvana.
