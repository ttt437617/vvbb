Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.92

hena-kāle śrīnivāsa premāvishta-mana

rājāra āge rahi' dekhe prabhura nartana

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; śrīnivāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; prema-āvishta-mana — with a greatly ecstatic mind; rājāra āge — in front of the King; rahi' — keeping himself; dekhe — sees; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nartana — the dancing.

TRANSLATION

While the King beheld the dancing, Śrīvāsa Thākura, standing in front of him, became ecstatic as he saw the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
