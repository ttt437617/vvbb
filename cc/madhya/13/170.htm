Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.170

prabhura hridaye ānanda-sindhu uthalila

unmāda, jhañjhā-vāta tat-kshane uthila

SYNONYMS

prabhura hridaye — in the heart of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda-sindhu — the ocean of transcendental bliss; uthalila — arose; unmāda — madness; jhañjhā-vāta — hurricane; tat-kshane — immediately; uthila — intensified.

TRANSLATION

An ocean of transcendental bliss expanded in the heart of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and symptoms of madness immediately intensified like a hurricane.
