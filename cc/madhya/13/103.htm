Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.103

eka eka dantera kampa dekhite lāge bhaya

loke jāne, danta saba khasiyā padaya

SYNONYMS

eka eka — one after another; dantera — of teeth; kampa — movement; dekhite — to see; lāge — there is; bhaya — fear; loke jāne — the people understood; danta — the teeth; saba — all; khasiyā — being loosened; padaya — fall down.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, the people became afraid just to see His teeth chatter, and they even thought that His teeth would fall out.
