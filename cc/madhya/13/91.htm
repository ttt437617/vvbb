Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.91

haricandanera skandhe hasta ālambiyā

prabhura nritya dekhe rājā āvishta hañā

SYNONYMS

haricandanera — of Haricandana; skandhe — on the shoulder; hasta — hand; ālambiyā — putting; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya dekhe — sees the dancing; rājā — Mahārāja Pratāparudra; āvishta hañā — in great ecstasy.

TRANSLATION

With his hands on the shoulders of Haricandana, King Pratāparudra could see Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu dancing, and the King felt great ecstasy.
