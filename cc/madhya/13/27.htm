Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.27

'gauda' saba ratha tāne kariyā ānanda

kshane śīghra cale ratha, kshane cale manda

SYNONYMS

gauda — the pullers of the car; saba — all; ratha — the car; tāne — pull; kariyā — feeling; ānanda — happiness; kshane — sometimes; śīghra cale — goes very fast; ratha — the car; kshane — sometimes; cale — goes; manda — very slow.

TRANSLATION

The pullers of the car were known as gaudas, and they pulled with great pleasure. However, the car sometimes went very fast and sometimes very slow.
