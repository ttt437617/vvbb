Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.100

subhadrā-balarāmera hridaye ullāsa

nritya dekhi' dui janāra śrī-mukhete hāsa

SYNONYMS

subhadrā — of the goddess Subhadrā; balarāmera — and of Balarāma; hridaye — in the hearts; ullāsa — ecstasy; nritya — dancing; dekhi' — seeing; dui janāra — of the two persons; śrī-mukhete — in the beautiful mouths; hāsa — smiling.

TRANSLATION

The goddess of fortune, Subhadrā, and Lord Balarāma both felt great happiness and ecstasy within their hearts. Indeed, they were seen smiling at the dancing.
