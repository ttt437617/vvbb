Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.5

pāndu-vijaya dekhibāre karila gamana

jagannātha yātrā kaila chādi' simhāsana

SYNONYMS

pāndu-vijaya — the ceremony named Pāndu-vijaya; dekhibāre — for seeing; karila — did; gamana — go; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; yātrā — departure; kaila — did; chādi' — leaving; simhāsana — the throne.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His personal associates then went to see the ceremony of Pāndu-vijaya. During this ceremony, Lord Jagannātha leaves His throne and gets up onto the car.
