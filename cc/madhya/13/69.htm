Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.69

ei-mata haila krishnera rathe ārohana

tāra āge prabhu nācāila bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; haila — there was; krishnera — of Lord Śrī Krishna; rathe — on the car; ārohana — getting up; tāra āge — before it; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nācāila — caused to dance; bhakta-gana — all devotees.

TRANSLATION

Thus Lord Jagannātha mounted His car, and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inspired all His devotees to dance in front of it.
