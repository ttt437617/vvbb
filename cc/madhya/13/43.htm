Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.43

mādhava, vāsudeva-ghosha, — dui sahodara

nritya karena tāhāń pandita-vakreśvara

SYNONYMS

mādhava — Mādhava; vāsudeva-ghosha — Vāsudeva Ghosha; dui sahodara — two brothers; nritya karena — dances; tāhāń — there; pandita-vakreśvara — Vakreśvara Pandita.

TRANSLATION

Two brothers named Mādhava Ghosha and Vāsudeva Ghosha also joined this group as responsive singers. Vakreśvara Pandita was the dancer.
