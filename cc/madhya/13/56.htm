Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.56

pratāparudrera haila parama vismaya

dekhite vivaśa rājā haila premamaya

SYNONYMS

pratāparudrera — of King Pratāparudra; haila — there was; parama — very much; vismaya — astonishment; dekhite — to see; vivaśa — inactive; rājā — the King; haila — became; prema-maya — in ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

King Pratāparudra was also astonished to see the sańkīrtana. He became inactive and was converted to ecstatic love of Krishna.
