Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.122

ei śloka mahāprabhu pade bāra bāra

svarūpa vinā artha keha nā jāne ihāra

SYNONYMS

ei śloka — this verse; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pade — recites; bāra bāra — again and again; svarūpa vinā — except for Svarūpa Dāmodara; artha — meaning; keha — anyone; nā jāne — does not know; ihāra — of this.

TRANSLATION

This verse was recited by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again and again. But for Svarūpa Dāmodara, no one could understand its meaning.
