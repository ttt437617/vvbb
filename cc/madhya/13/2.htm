Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.2

jaya jaya śrī-krishna-caitanya nityānanda

jayādvaita-candra jaya gaura-bhakta-vrinda

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; śrī-krishna-caitanya — to Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nityānanda — to Nityānanda Prabhu; jaya — all glories; advaita-candra — to Advaita Ācārya; jaya — all glories; gaura-bhakta-vrinda — to the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Śrī Krishna Caitanya and Prabhu Nityānanda! All glories to Advaitacandra! And all glories to the devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu!
