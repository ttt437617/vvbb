Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.162

nritya-kāle sei bhāve āvishta hañā

śloka padi' nāce jagannātha-mukha cāñā

SYNONYMS

nritya-kāle — while dancing; sei bhāve — in such ecstasy; āvishta — absorbed; hañā — becoming; śloka padi' — reciting these verses; nāce — dances; jagannātha-mukha — the face of Jagannātha; cāñā — looking upon.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced completely absorbed in ecstatic emotion. While looking at the face of Lord Jagannātha, He danced and recited these verses.
