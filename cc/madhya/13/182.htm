Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.182

rājā dekhi' mahāprabhu karena dhikkāra

chi, chi, vishayīra sparśa ha-ila āmāra

SYNONYMS

rājā dekhi' — by seeing the King; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena — does; dhik-kāra — condemnation; chi chi — how pitiable it is; vishayīra — of a person interested in mundane affairs; sparśa ha-ila — there has been touching; āmāra — of Me.

TRANSLATION

After seeing the King, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu condemned Himself, saying, "Oh, how pitiful it is that I have touched a person who is interested in mundane affairs!"
