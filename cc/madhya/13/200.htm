Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.200

āge pāche, dui pārśve pushpodyāna-vane

yei yāhā pāya, lāgāya, — nāhika niyame

SYNONYMS

āge pāche — in front or at the end; dui pārśve — on two sides; pushpa-udyāna-vane — in the flower gardens; yei — one who; yāhā pāya — gets the opportunity; lāgāya — offers; nāhika niyame — there are no hard and fast rules.

TRANSLATION

The devotees offered their food everywhere — in front of the car and behind it, on the two sides and within the flower garden. Wherever possible, they made their offering to the Lord, for there were no hard and fast rules.
