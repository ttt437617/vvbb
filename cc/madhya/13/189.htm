Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.189

tabe mahāprabhu ratha pradakshina kariyā

ratha-pāche yāi' thele rathe māthā diyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ratha — the car; pradakshina — circumambulation; kariyā — doing; ratha-pāche — to the rear of the car; yāi' — going; thele — pushes; rathe — on the car; māthā diyā — by placing the head.

TRANSLATION

After circumambulating Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went behind the car and began pushing it with His head.
