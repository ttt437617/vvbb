Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.34

tabe mahāprabhu mane vicāra kariyā

cāri sampradāya dila gāyana bāńtiyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — after this; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mane — in the mind; vicāra kariyā — considering; cāri sampradāya — four parties; dila — gave; gāyana bāńtiyā — dividing the singers.

TRANSLATION

When the four parties were formed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, after some consideration, divided the chanters.
