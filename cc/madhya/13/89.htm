Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.89

kāśīśvara govindādi yata bhakta-gana

hātāhāti kari' haila dvitīya āvarana

SYNONYMS

kāśīśvara — Kāśīśvara; govinda-ādi — headed by Govinda; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees; hātāhāti — linked hand to hand; kari' — doing; haila — became; dvitīya — a second; āvarana — covering circle.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees headed by Kāśīśvara and Govinda linked hands and formed a second circle around the Lord.
