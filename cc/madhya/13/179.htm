Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.179

kabhu sukhe nritya-rańga dekhe ratha rākhi'

se kautuka ye dekhila, sei tāra sākshī

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; sukhe — in great happiness; nritya-rańga — amusement in dancing; dekhe — sees; ratha — the car; rākhi' — stopping; se kautuka — that amusement; ye — anyone who; dekhila — saw; sei — he; tāra — of that; sākshī — witness.

TRANSLATION

Lord Jagannātha and Lord Balarāma sometimes stopped the car and happily observed Lord Caitanya's dancing. Anyone who was able to see Them stop and watch the dancing bore witness to Their pastimes.
