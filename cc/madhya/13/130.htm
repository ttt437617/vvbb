Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.130

vraje tomāra sańge yei sukha-āsvādana

sei sukha-samudrera ihāń nāhi eka kana

SYNONYMS

vraje — at Vrindāvana; tomāra — of You; sańge — in the company; yei — what; sukha-āsvādana — taste of transcendental bliss; sei — that; sukha-samudrera — of the ocean of transcendental bliss; ihāń — here, at Kurukshetra; nāhi — there is not; eka — one; kana — drop.

TRANSLATION

"Here there is not even a drop of the ocean of transcendental happiness that I enjoyed with You in Vrindāvana.
