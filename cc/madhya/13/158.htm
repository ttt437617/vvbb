Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.158

tomāra ye prema-guna, kare āmā ākarshana,

ānibe āmā dina daśa biśe

punah āsi' vrindāvane, vraja-vadhū tomā-sane,

vilasiba rajanī-divase

SYNONYMS

tomāra — Your; ye — whatever; prema-guna — qualities in ecstatic love; kare — do; āmā — Me; ākarshana — attracting; ānibe — will bring; āmā — Me; dina daśa biśe — within ten or twenty days; punah — again; āsi' — coming; vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; vraja-vadhū — all the damsels of Vrindāvana; tomā-sane — with You; vilasiba — I shall enjoy; rajanī-divase — both day and night.

TRANSLATION

"Your loving qualities always attract Me to Vrindāvana. Indeed, they will bring Me back within ten or twenty days, and when I return I shall enjoy both day and night with You and all the damsels of Vrajabhūmi."
