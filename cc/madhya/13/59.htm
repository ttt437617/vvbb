Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.59

yāre tāńra kripā, sei jānibāre pāre

kripā vinā brahmādika jānibāre nāre

SYNONYMS

yāre — upon whom; tāńra — His; kripā — mercy; sei — that person; jānibāre — to know; pāre — is able; kripā — mercy; vinā — without; brahma-ādika — the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā; jānibāre — to know; nāre — are not able.

TRANSLATION

Only a person who has received the mercy of the Lord can understand. Without the Lord's mercy, even the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā, cannot understand.
