Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.123

ei ślokārtha pūrve kariyāchi vyākhyāna

ślokera bhāvārtha kari sańkshepe ākhyāna

SYNONYMS

ei śloka-artha — the meaning of this verse; pūrve — previously; kariyāchi — I have done; vyākhyāna — explanation; ślokera — of the same verse; bhāva-artha — purport; kari — I do; sańkshepe — in brief; ākhyāna — description.

TRANSLATION

I have already explained this verse. Now I shall simply describe it in brief.

PURPORT

In this connection, see Madhya-līlā, Chapter One, verses 53, 77-80 and 82-84.
