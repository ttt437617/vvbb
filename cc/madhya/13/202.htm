Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.202

premāveśe mahāprabhu upavana pāñā

pushpodyāne griha-pindāya rahilā padiyā

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; upavana pāñā — having come to a nice nearby garden; pushpa-udyāne — in that flower garden; griha-pindāya — on a raised platform; rahilā — remained; padiyā — falling flat.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered the garden and, immersed in great ecstatic emotion, fell flat on a raised platform there.
