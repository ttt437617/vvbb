Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.9

kataka dayitā kare skandha ālambana

kataka dayitā dhare śrī-padma-carana

SYNONYMS

kataka dayitā — some of the dayitās; kare — do; skandha — of the shoulders; ālambana — capturing; kataka — some; dayitā — servants called dayitās; dhare — catch; śrī-padma-carana — the lotus feet of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

While carrying the Deity of Lord Jagannātha, some of the dayitās took hold of the shoulders of the Lord, and some caught His lotus feet.
