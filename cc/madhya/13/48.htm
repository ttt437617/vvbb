Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.48

sāta sampradāye bāje caudda mādala

yāra dhvani śuni' vaishnava haila pāgala

SYNONYMS

sāta sampradāye — in seven groups; bāje — were beating; caudda — fourteen; mādala — drums; yāra — of which; dhvani — the sound; śuni' — hearing; vaishnava — all the devotees; haila — became; pāgala — mad.

TRANSLATION

There were altogether seven parties of sańkīrtana, and in each party two men were beating drums. Thus fourteen drums were being played at once. The sound was tumultuous, and all the devotees became mad.
