Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.192

tāhāń nritya kari' jagannātha āge āilā

jagannātha dekhi' nritya karite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

tāhāń — there; nritya kari' — after performing the dance; jagannātha — of Lord Jagannātha; āge — in front; āilā — appeared; jagannātha dekhi' — seeing Lord Jagannātha; nritya — dancing; karite — to perform; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

After finishing the dance before Lord Baladeva and Subhadrā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came before Lord Jagannātha's car. Upon seeing Lord Jagannātha, He began to dance again.
