Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.26

rathe cadi' jagannātha karilā gamana

dui-pārśve dekhi' cale ānandita-mana

SYNONYMS

rathe cadi' — riding on the car; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; karilā gamana — was passing; dui-pārśve — on both sides; dekhi' — seeing; cale — goes; ānandita — full of pleasure; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

As Lord Jagannātha rode in His car and saw the beauty on both sides, His mind was filled with pleasure.
