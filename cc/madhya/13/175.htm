Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.175

jagannātha-sevaka yata rāja-pātra-gana

yātrika loka, nīlācala-vāsī yata jana

SYNONYMS

jagannātha-sevaka — the servants of Lord Jagannātha; yata — all; rāja-pātra-gana — and the government officers; yātrika — pilgrim visitors; loka — people in general; nīlācala-vāsī — the residents of Jagannātha Purī; yata jana — as many people as there were.

TRANSLATION

He sprinkled the minds of the servants of Lord Jagannātha, the government officers, the pilgrim visitors, the general populace and all the residents of Jagannātha Purī.
