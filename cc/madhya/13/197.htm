Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.197

jagannāthera chota-bada yata bhakta-gana

nija nija uttama-bhoga kare samarpana

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; chota — neophyte; bada — advanced; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees; nija nija — personally cooked; uttama-bhoga — first-class food; kare — do; samarpana — offering.

TRANSLATION

All kinds of devotees of Lord Jagannātha — from neophytes to the most advanced — offered their best cooked food to the Lord.
