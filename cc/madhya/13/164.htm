Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.164

svarūpera indriye prabhura nijendriya-gana

āvishta hañā kare gāna-āsvādana

SYNONYMS

svarūpera — of Svarūpa Dāmodara; indriye — in the senses; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija-indriya-gana — own senses; āvishta hañā — being fully absorbed; kare — does; gāna — the singing; āsvādana — tasting.

TRANSLATION

The senses of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were identical with the senses of Svarūpa. Therefore Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to become fully absorbed in tasting the singing of Svarūpa Dāmodara.
