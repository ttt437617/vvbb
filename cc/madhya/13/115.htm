Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.115

dhīre dhīre jagannātha karena gamana

āge nritya kari' calena śacīra nandana

SYNONYMS

dhīre dhīre — slowly, slowly; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; karena — does; gamana — movement; āge — in front; nritya — dancing; kari' — performing; calena — goes forward; śacīra nandana — the son of mother Śacī.

TRANSLATION

The car of Lord Jagannātha began to move slowly while the son of mother Śacī went ahead and danced in front.
