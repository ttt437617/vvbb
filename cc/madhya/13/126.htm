Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.126

avaśeshe rādhā krishne kare nivedana

sei tumi, sei āmi, sei nava sańgama

SYNONYMS

avaśeshe — at last; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; kare — does; nivedana — submission; sei tumi — You are the same Krishna; sei āmi — I am the same Rādhārānī; sei nava sańgama — We are meeting in the same new spirit as in the beginning.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke thus to Lord Jagannātha: "You are the same Krishna, and I am the same Rādhārānī. We are meeting again in the same way that We met in the beginning of Our lives.
