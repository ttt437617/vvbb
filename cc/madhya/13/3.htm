Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.3

jaya śrotā-gana, śuna, kari' eka mana

ratha-yātrāya nritya prabhura parama mohana

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; śrotā-gana — to the listeners; śuna — please hear; kari' — keeping yourself; eka mana — in one attention; ratha-yātrāya — in the car festival; nritya — dancing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; parama — extremely; mohana — enchanting.

TRANSLATION

All glories to the listeners of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita! Please hear the description of the dancing of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu at the Ratha-yātrā festival. His dancing is very enchanting. Please hear of it with great attention.
