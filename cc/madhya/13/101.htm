Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.101

uddanda nritye prabhura adbhuta vikāra

ashta sāttvika bhāva udaya haya sama-kāla

SYNONYMS

uddanda — jumping; nritye — by dancing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; adbhuta — wonderful; vikāra — transformations; ashta sāttvika — eight transcendental kinds; bhāva — ecstasy; udaya haya — awaken; sama-kāla — simultaneously.

TRANSLATION

When Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and jumped high, eight wonderful transformations indicative of divine ecstasy were seen in His body. All these symptoms were visible simultaneously.
