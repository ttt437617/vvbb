Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.63

ei-mata līlā prabhu kaila kata-kshana

āpane gāyena, nācā'na nija-bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; līlā — pastimes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — performed; kata-kshana — for some time; āpane gāyena — personally sings; nācā'na — made to dance; nija-bhakta-gana — His own personal devotees.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His pastimes for some time in this way. He personally sang and induced His personal associates to dance.
