Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.52

āra eka śakti prabhu karila prakāśa

eka-kāle sāta thāñi karila vilāsa

SYNONYMS

āra — another; eka — one; śakti — mystic power; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karila — made; prakāśa — manifest; eka-kāle — simultaneously; sāta thāñi — in seven places; karila — performed; vilāsa — pastimes.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu then exhibited another mystic power by performing pastimes simultaneously in all seven groups.
