Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.108

kabhu bhūme pade, kabhu śvāsa haya hīna

yāhā dekhi' bhakta-ganera prāna haya kshīna

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; bhūme — on the ground; pade — falls down; kabhu — sometimes; śvāsa — breathing; haya — becomes; hīna — nil; yāhā dekhi' — seeing which; bhakta-ganera — of the devotees; prāna — life; haya — becomes; kshīna — feeble.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord fell to the ground, sometimes His breathing almost stopped. When the devotees saw this, their lives also became very feeble.
