Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.66

pūrve yaiche rāsādi līlā kaila vrindāvane

alaukika līlā gaura kaila kshane kshane

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; yaiche — as; rāsa-ādi līlā — the rāsa-līlā and other pastimes; kaila — performed; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; alaukika — uncommon; līlā — pastimes; gaura — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — performed; kshane kshane — moment after moment.

TRANSLATION

Just as Lord Śrī Krishna formerly performed the rāsa-līlā dance and other pastimes at Vrindāvana, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed uncommon pastimes moment after moment.
