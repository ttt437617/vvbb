Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.96

kruddha hañā tāńre kichu cāhe balibāre

āpani pratāparudra nivārila tāre

SYNONYMS

kruddha hañā — becoming angry; tāńre — unto Śrīvāsa Thākura; kichu — something; cāhe — wants; balibāre — to speak; āpani — personally; pratāparudra — King Pratāparudra; nivārila — stopped; tāre — unto him.

TRANSLATION

As the angered Haricandana was about to speak to Śrīvāsa Thākura, Pratāparudra Mahārāja personally stopped him.
