Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.25

sūkshma śveta-bālu pathe pulinera sama

dui dike totā, saba — yena vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

sūkshma — fine; śveta-bālu — white sand; pathe — on the path; pulinera sama — just like the bank of the Yamunā; dui dike — on two sides; totā — gardens; saba — all; yena — like; vrindāvana — the holy place Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

The fine, white sand spread all over the path resembled the bank of the Yamunā, and the small gardens on both sides looked just like those in Vrindāvana.
