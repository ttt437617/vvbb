Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.87

prabhu-pāche bule ācārya kariyā huńkāra

'hari-bola' 'hari-bola' bale bāra bāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu-pāche — behind the Lord; bule — was walking; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; kariyā — making; huńkāra — a loud vibration; hari-bola hari-bola — chant the holy name of Hari; bale — says; bāra bāra — again and again.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya would walk behind the Lord and loudly chant "Haribol! Haribol!" again and again.
