Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.75

ei daśa jana prabhura sańge gāya, dhāya

āra saba sampradāya cāri dike gāya

SYNONYMS

ei daśa jana — these ten persons; prabhura — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sańge — with; gāya — chant; dhāya — run; āra — others; saba — all; sampradāya — groups of men; cāri dike — all around; gāya — chant.

TRANSLATION

These devotees [Svarūpa Dāmodara and the devotees in his charge] sang along with the Lord, and they also ran beside Him. All the other groups of men also sang.
