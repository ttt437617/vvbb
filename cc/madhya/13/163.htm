Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.163

svarūpa-gosāñira bhāgya nā yāya varnana

prabhute āvishta yāńra kāya, vākya, mana

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñira — of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; bhāgya — the fortune; nā — not; yāya varnana — can be described; prabhute — in the service of the Lord; āvishta — fully absorbed; yāńra — of whom; kāya — body; vākya — words; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

No one can describe the good fortune of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, for he is always absorbed in the service of the Lord with his body, mind and words.
