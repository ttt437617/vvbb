Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.106

deha-kānti gaura-varna dekhiye aruna

kabhu kānti dekhi yena mallikā-pushpa-sama

SYNONYMS

deha-kānti — of the luster of the body; gaura-varna — white complexion; dekhiye — everyone saw; aruna — pink; kabhu — sometimes; kānti — the luster; dekhi — seeing; yena — as if; mallikā-pushpa-sama — resembling the mallikā flower.

TRANSLATION

Everyone saw the complexion of His body change from white to pink, so that His luster resembled that of the mallikā flower.
