Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.203

nritya-pariśrame prabhura dehe ghana gharma

sugandhi śītala-vāyu karena sevana

SYNONYMS

nritya-pariśrame — by fatigue due to dancing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dehe — on the body; ghana gharma — much perspiration; sugandhi — fragrant; śītala-vāyu — cool breeze; karena sevana — enjoyed very much.

TRANSLATION

The Lord was very much fatigued from the hard labor of dancing, and there was perspiration all over His body. He therefore enjoyed the fragrant, cool breeze of the garden.
