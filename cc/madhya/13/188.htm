Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.188

avasara jāni' āmi kariba nivedana

sei-kāle yāi' kariha prabhura milana

SYNONYMS

avasara jāni' — understanding an opportune moment; āmi — I; kariba — shall do; nivedana — submission; sei-kāle — at that time; yāi' — coming; kariha — you do; prabhura milana — meeting with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya continued, "I shall submit your petition when there is an opportune moment. It will then be easy for you to come and meet the Lord."
