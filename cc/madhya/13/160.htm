Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.160

mayi bhaktir hi bhūtānām

amritatvāya kalpate

dishtyā yad āsīn mat-sneho

bhavatīnām mad-āpanah

SYNONYMS

mayi — unto Me; bhaktih — the nine kinds of devotional service, such as śravana, kīrtana and smarana; hi — certainly; bhūtānām — of all living entities; amritatvāya — for becoming eternal associates of the Lord; kalpate — is quite befitting; dishtyā — by good fortune; yat — whatever; āsīt — there was; mat-snehah — love and affection for Me; bhavatīnām — of all you gopīs; mat-āpanah — the cause for getting Me back.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna said: "Devotional service unto Me is the only way to attain Me. My dear gopīs, whatever love and affection you have attained for Me by good fortune is the only reason for My returning to you."

PURPORT

This is a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (10.82.44).
