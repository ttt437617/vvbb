Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.207

rathārūdhasyārād adhipadavi nīlācala-pater

adabhra-premormi-sphurita-natanollāsa-vivaśah

sa-harsham gāyadbhih parivrita-tanur vaishnava-janaih

sa caitanyah kim me punar api driśor yāsyati padam

SYNONYMS

ratha-ārūdhasya — of the Supreme Lord, who was placed aboard the car; ārāt — in front; adhipadavi — on the main road; nīlācala-pateh — of Lord Jagannātha, the Lord of Nīlācala; adabhra — great; prema-ūrmi — by waves of love of Godhead; sphurita — which was manifested; natana-ullāsa-vivaśah — being overwhelmed by the transcendental bliss of dancing; sa-harsham — with great pleasure; gāyadbhih — who were singing; parivrita — surrounded; tanuh — body; vaishnava-janaih — by the devotees; sah caitanyah — that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kim — whether; me — my; punah api — again; driśoh — of vision; yāsyati — will enter; padam — the path.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced down the main road in great ecstasy before Lord Jagannātha, the master of Nīlācala, who was sitting on His car. Overwhelmed by the transcendental bliss of dancing and surrounded by Vaishnavas who sang the holy names, He manifested waves of ecstatic love of Godhead. When will Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu again be visible to my vision?"
