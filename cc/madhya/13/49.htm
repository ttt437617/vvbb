Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.49

vaishnavera megha-ghatāya ha-ila bādala

kīrtanānande saba varshe netra-jala

SYNONYMS

vaishnavera — of the devotees; megha-ghatāya — by the assembly of clouds; ha-ila — there was; bādala — rainfall; kīrtana-ānande — in the blissful situation of chanting; saba — all of them; varshe — rain; netra-jala — tears from the eyes.

TRANSLATION

All the Vaishnavas came together like an assembly of clouds. As the devotees chanted the holy names in great ecstasy, tears fell from their eyes like rain.
