Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.151

tomā-sabāra prema-rase, āmāke karila vaśe,

āmi tomāra adhīna kevala

tomā-sabā chādāñā, āmā dūra-deśe lañā,

rākhiyāche durdaiva prabala

SYNONYMS

tomā-sabāra — of all of you; prema-rase — by the ecstasy and mellows of loving affairs; āmāke — Me; karila — you have made; vaśe — subservient; āmi — I; tomāra — of you; adhīna — subservient; kevala — only; tomā-sabā — from all of you; chādāñā — separating; āmā — Me; dūra-deśe — to distant countries; lañā — taking; rākhiyāche — has kept; durdaiva — misfortune; prabala — very powerful.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, I am always subservient to the loving affairs of all of you. I am under your control only. My separation from you and residence in distant places have occurred due to My strong misfortune.
