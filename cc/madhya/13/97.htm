Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.97

bhāgyavān tumi — ińhāra hasta-sparśa pāilā

āmāra bhāgye nāhi, tumi kritārtha hailā

SYNONYMS

bhāgyavān tumi — you are very fortunate; ińhāra — of Śrīvāsa Thākura; hasta — of the hand; sparśa — touch; pāilā — have received; āmāra bhāgye — in my fortune; nāhi — there is no such thing; tumi — you; krita-artha hailā — have become graced.

TRANSLATION

King Pratāparudra said, "You are very fortunate, for you have been graced by the touch of Śrīvāsa Thākura. I am not so fortunate. You should feel obliged to him."
