Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.77

namo brahmanya-devāya

go-brāhmana-hitāya ca

jagad-dhitāya krishnāya

govindāya namo namah

SYNONYMS

namah — all obeisances; brahmanya-devāya — to the Lord worshipable by persons in brahminical culture; go-brāhmana — for cows and brāhmanas; hitāya — beneficial; ca — also; jagat-hitāya — to one who always is benefiting the whole world; krishnāya — unto Krishna; govindāya — unto Govinda; namah namah — repeated obeisances.

TRANSLATION

"'Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Lord Krishna, who is the worshipable Deity for all brahminical men, who is the well-wisher of cows and brāhmanas, and who is always benefiting the whole world. I offer my repeated obeisances to the Personality of Godhead, known as Krishna and Govinda.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Vishnu Purāna (1.19.65).
