Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.44

kulīna-grāmera eka kīrtanīyā-samāja

tāhāń nritya karena rāmānanda, satyarāja

SYNONYMS

kulīna-grāmera — of the village known as Kulīna-grāma; eka — one; kīrtanīyā-samāja — sańkīrtana party; tāhāń — there; nritya karena — dances; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda; satyarāja — Satyarāja Khān.

TRANSLATION

There was a sańkīrtana party from the village known as Kulīna-grāma, and Rāmānanda and Satyarāja were appointed the dancers in this group.
