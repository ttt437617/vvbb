Madhya-līlāChapter 13: The Ecstatic Dancing of the Lord at Ratha-yātrā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 13.195

āge nritya kare gaura lañā bhakta-gana

ratha rākhi' jagannātha karena daraśana

SYNONYMS

āge — in front; nritya kare — dances; gaura — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — accompanied by; bhakta-gana — the devotees; ratha rākhi' — after stopping the car; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; karena daraśana — sees.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees were dancing in front of the car, and Lord Jagannātha, having stopped the car, watched the dancing.
