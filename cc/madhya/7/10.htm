Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.10

ebe sabā-sthāne muñi māgoń eka dāne

sabe meli' ājñā deha, yāiba dakshine

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; sabā-sthāne — from all of you; muñi — I; māgoń — beg; eka dāne — one gift; sabe meli' — all combining together; ājñā deha — give permission; yāiba — I shall go; dakshine — to South India.

TRANSLATION

"I now beg all of you for one bit of charity. Please give Me permission to leave for a tour of South India.
