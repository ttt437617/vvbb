Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.102

grāmāntara haite dekhite āila yata jana

tāńra darśana-kripāya haya tāńra sama

SYNONYMS

grāma-antara haite — from different villages; dekhite — to see; āila — came; yata jana — all the persons; tāńra — his; darśana-kripāya — by the mercy of seeing him; haya — become; tāńra sama — similar Vaishnavas.

TRANSLATION

People from different villages who came to see such an empowered individual would become like him simply by seeing him and receiving the mercy of his glance.
