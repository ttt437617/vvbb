Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.107

prabhura kripāya haya mahābhāgavata

sei saba ācārya hañā tārila jagat

SYNONYMS

prabhura kripāya — by the mercy of the Lord; haya — become; mahā-bhāgavata — first-class devotees; sei saba — all such persons; ācārya — teachers; hañā — becoming; tārila — liberated; jagat — the whole world.

TRANSLATION

By the mercy of the Supreme Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone became a first-class devotee. Later they became teachers or spiritual masters and liberated the entire world.
