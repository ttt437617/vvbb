Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.41

tabe tāńra vākya prabhu kari' ańgīkāre

tāhā-sabā lañā gelā sārvabhauma-ghare

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; tāńra — of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; vākya — the words; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kari' — doing; ańgīkāre — acceptance; tāhā-sabā — all of them; lañā — taking; gelā — went; sārvabhauma-ghare — to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.

TRANSLATION

Accepting the request of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, Lord Caitanya took all His devotees and went to the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
