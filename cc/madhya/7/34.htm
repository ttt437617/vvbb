Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.34

tabe nityānanda kahe, — ye ājñā tomāra

duhkha sukha ye ha-uk kartavya āmāra

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; kahe — says; ye ājñā — whatever order; tomāra — Your; duhkha sukha — distress or happiness; ye — whatever; ha-uk — let there be; kartavya — the duty; āmāra — My.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon Lord Nityānanda said, "Whatever You order is My duty, regardless of whether it results in happiness or unhappiness.
