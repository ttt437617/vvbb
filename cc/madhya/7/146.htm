Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.146

kintu āchilāń bhāla adhama hañā

ebe ahańkāra mora janmibe āsiyā

SYNONYMS

kintu — but; āchilāń — I was; bhāla — all right; adhama — the lowest of mankind; hañā — being; ebe — now; ahańkāra — pride; mora — my; janmibe — will appear; āsiyā — coming.

TRANSLATION

Being meek and humble, the brāhmana Vāsudeva worried that he would become proud after being cured by the grace of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
