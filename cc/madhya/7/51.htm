Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.51

bhattācārya āgraha kari' karena nimantrana

grihe pāka kari' prabhuke karā'na bhojana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; āgraha — eagerness; kari' — showing; karena — did; nimantrana — invitation; grihe — at home; pāka — cooking; kari' — doing; prabhuke — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karā'na — made; bhojana — eating.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya eagerly invited Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home and fed Him very nicely.
