Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.112

prathamei kahila prabhura ye-rūpe gamana

ei-mata jāniha yāvat dakshina-bhramana

SYNONYMS

prathamei — at the beginning; kahila — I have explained; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ye-rūpe — as; gamana — the touring; ei-mata — in this way; jāniha — you should know; yāvat — as long as; dakshina-bhramana — touring in South India.

TRANSLATION

Whatever I have stated about the beginning of the Lord's tour should also be understood to hold for as long as the Lord toured South India.
