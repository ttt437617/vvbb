Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.85

madhyāhna kariyā āilā devatā-mandire

nija-gana praveśi' kapāta dila bahir-dvāre

SYNONYMS

madhyāhna kariyā — performing bathing, etc.; āilā — came back; devatā-mandire — to the temple of the Lord; nija-gana praveśi' — allowing His own men; kapāta dila — shut; bahir-dvāre — the outside door.

TRANSLATION

After finishing Their baths, They returned at noon to the temple. Admitting His own men, Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu closed the outside door.
