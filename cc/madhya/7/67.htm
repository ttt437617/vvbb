Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.67

tomāra prasāde ebe jāninu tāńra tattva

sambhāshile jānibe tāńra yemana mahattva

SYNONYMS

tomāra prasāde — by Your mercy; ebe — now; jāninu — I have understood; tāńra — of him (Rāmānanda Rāya); tattva — the truth; sambhāshile — in talking together; jānibe — You will know; tāńra — his; yemana — such; mahattva — greatness.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya said, "By Your mercy I can now understand the truth about Rāmānanda Rāya. In talking with him, You also will acknowledge his greatness."
