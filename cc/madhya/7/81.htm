Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.81

keha nāce, keha gāya, 'śrī-krishna' 'gopāla'

premete bhāsila loka, — strī-vriddha-ābāla

SYNONYMS

keha nāce — someone dances; keha gāya — someone chants; śrī-krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna's name; gopāla — Gopāla's name; premete — in love of Godhead; bhāsila — floated; loka — all the people; strī — women; vriddha — old men; ā-bāla — from the children.

TRANSLATION

Everyone — including children, old men and women — began to dance and to chant the holy names of Śrī Krishna and Gopāla. In this way they all floated in the ocean of love of Godhead.
