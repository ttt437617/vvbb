Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.104

sei yāi' āra grāme kare upadeśa

ei-mata 'vaishnava' haila saba dakshina-deśa

SYNONYMS

sei — that man; yāi' — going; āra — different; grāme — to the villages; kare — gives; upadeśa — instruction; ei-mata — in this way; vaishnava — devotees; haila — became; saba — all; dakshina-deśa — the people of South India.

TRANSLATION

In this way, as empowered men went from one village to another, all the people of South India became devotees.
