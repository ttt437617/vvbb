Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.117

krishna-nāma loka-mukhe śuni' avirāma

sei loka 'vaishnava' kaila anya saba grāma

SYNONYMS

krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; loka-mukhe — from the mouth of those people; śuni' — hearing; avirāma — always; sei loka — those persons; vaishnava — devotees; kaila — made; anya — other; saba — all; grāma — villages.

TRANSLATION

Always hearing them chant the holy names of Lord Krishna, the residents of all the other villages also became Vaishnavas.
