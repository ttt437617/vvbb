Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.135

prabhura anuvraji' kūrma bahu dūra āilā

prabhu tāńre yatna kari' ghare pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; anuvraji' — following behind; kūrma — the brāhmana named Kūrma; bahu — much; dūra — distance; āilā — came; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; yatna kari' — taking much care; ghare — to his home; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left, the brāhmana Kūrma followed Him a great distance, but eventually Lord Caitanya took care to send him back home.
