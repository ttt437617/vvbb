Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.61

tabe sārvabhauma kahe prabhura carane

avaśya pālibe, prabhu, mora nivedane

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kahe — said; prabhura carane — unto the lotus feet of the Lord; avaśya — certainly; pālibe — You will keep; prabhu — my Lord; mora — my; nivedane — request.

TRANSLATION

While Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was departing, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya submitted the following at His lotus feet: "My Lord, I have one final request that I hope You will kindly fulfill.
