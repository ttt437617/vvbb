Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.86

tabe gopīnātha dui-prabhure bhikshā karāila

prabhura śesha prasādānna sabe bāńti' khāila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha Ācārya; dui-prabhure — unto the two Lords Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu; bhikshā karāila — gave prasādam to eat; prabhura — of the Lord; śesha — the remnants; prasāda-anna — food; sabe — all of them; bāńti' — sharing; khāila — ate.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya then brought prasādam for the two Lords to eat, and after They had eaten, the remnants of the food were distributed to all the devotees.
