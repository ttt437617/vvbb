Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.9

tumi-saba bandhu mora bandhu-kritya kaile

ihāń āni' more jagannātha dekhāile

SYNONYMS

tumi-saba — all of you; bandhu — friends; mora — My; bandhu-kritya — duties of a friend; kaile — you have executed; ihāń — here; āni' — bringing; more — to Me; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; dekhāile — you have shown.

TRANSLATION

"You are all My friends, and you have properly executed the duties of friends by bringing Me here to Jagannātha Purī and giving Me the chance to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple.
