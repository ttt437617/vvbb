Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.53

āge ta' kahiba tāhā kariyā vistāra

ebe kahi prabhura dakshina-yātrā-samācāra

SYNONYMS

āge — later; ta' — indeed; kahiba — I shall speak; tāhā — all those incidents; kariyā — doing; vistāra — elaboration; ebe — now; kahi — let me describe; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dakshina — in South India; yātrā — of the touring; samācāra — the narration.

TRANSLATION

Later I shall tell about this in elaborate detail, but at present I wish to describe Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's South Indian tour.
