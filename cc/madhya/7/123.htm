Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.123

aneka-prakāra snehe bhikshā karāila

gosāñira śeshānna sa-vamśe khāila

SYNONYMS

aneka-prakāra — various kinds; snehe — in affection; bhikshā — food; karāila — made Him eat; gosāñira — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha-anna — remnants of food; sa-vamśe — with all the members of the family; khāila — ate.

TRANSLATION

With great affection and respect, that Kūrma brāhmana made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eat all kinds of food. After that, the remnants were shared by all the members of the family.
