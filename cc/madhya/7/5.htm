Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.5

phālgunera śeshe dola-yātrā se dekhila

premāveśe tāńhā bahu nritya-gīta kaila

SYNONYMS

phālgunera — of the month of Phālguna; śeshe — at the end; dola-yātrā — the Dola-yātrā festival; se — that; dekhila — saw; prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love of Godhead; tāńhā — there; bahu — much; nritya-gīta — chanting and dancing; kaila — performed.

TRANSLATION

At the end of the month of Phālguna, He witnessed the Dola-yātrā ceremony, and in His usual ecstatic love of God, He chanted and danced in various ways on the occasion.
