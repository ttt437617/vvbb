Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.94

bhakta-gana upavāsī tāhāńi rahilā

āra dine duhkhī hañā nīlācale āilā

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — the devotees; upavāsī — fasting; tāhāńi — there; rahilā — remained; āra dine — on the next day; duhkhī — unhappy; hañā — becoming; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āilā — returned.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees remained there and fasted, and the next day they all unhappily returned to Jagannātha Purī.
