Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.122

ghare āni' prabhura kaila pāda prakshālana

sei jala vamśa-sahita karila bhakshana

SYNONYMS

ghare āni' — after bringing Him home; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — did; pāda prakshālana — washing of the lotus feet; sei jala — that water; vamśa-sahita — with all the family members; karila bhakshana — drank.

TRANSLATION

This brāhmana brought Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home, washed His lotus feet and, with his family members, drank that water.
