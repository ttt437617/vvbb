Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.141

prabhu-sparśe duhkha-sańge kushtha dūre gela

ānanda sahite ańga sundara ha-ila

SYNONYMS

prabhu-sparśe — by the touch of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; duhkha-sańge — along with his unhappiness; kushtha — the infection of leprosy; dūre — to a distant place; gela — went; ānanda sahite — with great pleasure; ańga — whole body; sundara — beautiful; ha-ila — became.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu touched him, both the leprosy and his distress went to a distant place. Indeed, Vāsudeva's body became very beautiful, to his great happiness.
