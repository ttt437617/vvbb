Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.154

ithe aparādha mora nā la-io, bhakta-gana

tomā-sabāra carana — mora ekānta śarana

SYNONYMS

ithe — in this; aparādha — offenses; mora — my; nā la-io — do not take; bhakta-gana — O devotees; tomā — of your; sabāra — of all; carana — the lotus feet; mora — my; ekānta — only; śarana — shelter.

TRANSLATION

O devotees, please do not consider my offenses in this regard. Your lotus feet are my only shelter.
