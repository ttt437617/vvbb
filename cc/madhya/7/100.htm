Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.100

sei-jana nija-grāme kariyā gamana

'krishna' bali' hāse, kānde, nāce anukshana

SYNONYMS

sei-jana — that person; nija-grāme — to his own village; kariyā gamana — returning there; krishna bali' — saying the holy name of Lord Krishna; hāse — laughs; kānde — cries; nāce — dances; anukshana — always.

TRANSLATION

Each of these empowered persons would return to his own village, always chanting the holy name of Krishna and sometimes laughing, crying and dancing.
