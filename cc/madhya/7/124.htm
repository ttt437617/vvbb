Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.124

'yei pāda-padma tomāra brahmā dhyāna kare

sei pāda-padma sākshāt āila mora ghare

SYNONYMS

yei — those; pāda-padma — lotus feet; tomāra — Your; brahmā — Lord Brahmā; dhyāna kare — meditates on; sei pāda-padma — those lotus feet; sākshāt — directly; āila — have come; mora — my; ghare — to the home.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana then began to pray, "O my Lord, Your lotus feet are meditated upon by Lord Brahmā, and these very lotus feet have come into my home.
