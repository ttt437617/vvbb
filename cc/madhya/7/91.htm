Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.91

prātah-kāle snāna kari' karilā gamana

bhakta-gane vidāya dilā kari' ālińgana

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāle — in the morning; snāna — bath; kari' — after taking; karilā — started; gamana — tour; bhakta-gane — to all the devotees; vidāya — farewell; dilā — gave; kari' — doing; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

The next morning, after taking His bath, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu started on His South Indian tour. He bade farewell to the devotees by embracing them.
