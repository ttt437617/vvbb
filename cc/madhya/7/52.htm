Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.52

tāńhāra brāhmanī, tāńra nāma — 'shāthīra mātā'

rāndhi' bhikshā dena teńho, āścarya tāńra kathā

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra brāhmanī — his wife; tāńra nāma — her name; shāthīra mātā — the mother of Shāthī; rāndhi' — cooking; bhikshā dena — offers food; teńho — she; āścarya — wonderful; tāńra — her; kathā — narration.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya's wife, whose name was Shāthīmātā (the mother of Shāthī), did the cooking. The narrations of these pastimes are very wonderful.
