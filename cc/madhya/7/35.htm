Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.35

kintu eka nivedana karoń āra bāra

vicāra kariyā tāhā kara ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

kintu — but; eka — one; nivedana — petition; karoń — I do; āra bāra — again; vicāra — consideration; kariyā — giving; tāhā — that; kara — do; ańgīkāra — acceptance.

TRANSLATION

"Yet I still submit one petition to You. Please consider it, and if You think it proper, please accept it.
