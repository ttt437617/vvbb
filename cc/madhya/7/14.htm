Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.14

śuniyā sabāra mane haila mahā-duhkha

nihśabda ha-ilā, sabāra śukāila mukha

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing this; sabāra — of all the devotees; mane — in the minds; haila — there was; mahā-duhkha — great unhappiness; nihśabda — silent; ha-ilā — became; sabāra — of everyone; śukāila — dried up; mukha — the faces.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this message from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees became very unhappy and remained silent with sullen faces.
