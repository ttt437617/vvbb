Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.149

eteka kahiyā prabhu kaila antardhāne

dui vipra galāgali kānde prabhura gune

SYNONYMS

eteka — so much; kahiyā — speaking; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — made; antardhāne — disappearance; dui vipra — the two brāhmanas, Kūrma and Vāsudeva; galāgali — embracing one another; kānde — cry; prabhura gune — due to the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

After instructing the brāhmana Vāsudeva in that way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu disappeared from that place. Then the two brāhmanas, Kūrma and Vāsudeva, embraced each other and began to cry, remembering the transcendental qualities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
