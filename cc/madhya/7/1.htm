Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.1

dhanyam tam naumi caitanyam

vāsudevam dayārdra-dhī

nashta-kushtham rūpa-pushtam

bhakti-tushtam cakāra yah

SYNONYMS

dhanyam — auspicious; tam — unto Him; naumi — I offer obeisances; caitanyam — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vāsudevam — unto the brāhmana Vāsudeva; dayā-ārdra-dhī — being compassionate; nashta-kushtham — cured the leprosy; rūpa-pushtam — beautiful; bhakti-tushtam — satisfied in devotional service; cakāra — made; yah — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, being very compassionate toward a brāhmana named Vāsudeva, cured him of leprosy. He transformed him into a beautiful man satisfied with devotional service. I offer my respectful obeisances unto the glorious Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
