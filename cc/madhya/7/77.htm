Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.77

premāveśe nritya-gīta kaila kata-kshana

dekhite āilā tāhāń vaise yata jana

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in the great ecstasy of love of Godhead; nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; kaila — performed; kata-kshana — for some time; dekhite — to see; āilā — came; tāhāń — there; vaise — who live; yata jana — all the men.

TRANSLATION

In great ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and chanted for some time. Indeed, all the neighbors came to see Him.
