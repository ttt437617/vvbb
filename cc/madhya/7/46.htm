Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.46

śuni' sārvabhauma hailā atyanta kātara

carane dhariyā kahe vishāda-uttara

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing this; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; hailā — became; atyanta — greatly; kātara — agitated; carane — the lotus feet; dhariyā — taking; kahe — says; vishāda — of lamentation; uttara — a reply.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya became very much agitated. Catching hold of the lotus feet of Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he gave this sorrowful reply.
