Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.32

gune doshodgāra-cchale sabā nishedhiyā

ekākī bhramibena tīrtha vairāgya kariyā

SYNONYMS

gune — in the good qualities; dosha-udgāra-chale — on the plea of attributing faults; sabā — all of them; nishedhiyā — forbidding; ekākī — alone; bhramibena — will tour; tīrtha — the sacred places of pilgrimage; vairāgya — regulative principles of the renounced order of life; kariyā — observing.

TRANSLATION

Therefore, to prevent them from accompanying Him and becoming unhappy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu declared their good qualities to be faults.

PURPORT

The Lord wanted to tour all the places of pilgrimage alone and strictly observe the duties of the renounced order.
