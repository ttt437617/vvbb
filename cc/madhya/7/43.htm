Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.43

nānā krishna-vārtā kahi' kahila tāńhāre

'tomāra thāñi āilāń ājñā māgibāre

SYNONYMS

nānā — various; krishna-vārtā — topics on Lord Krishna; kahi' — discussing; kahila — He informed; tāńhāre — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; tomāra thāñi — to your place; āilāń — I have come; ājñā — order; māgibāre — to beg.

TRANSLATION

After they had discussed various topics about Lord Krishna, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, "I have come to your place just to receive your order.
