Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.150

'vāsudevoddhāra' ei kahila ākhyāna

'vāsudevāmrita-prada' haila prabhura nāma

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva-uddhāra — giving liberation to Vāsudeva; ei — this; kahila — is described; ākhyāna — narration; vāsudeva-amrita-prada — the giver of nectar to Vāsudeva; haila — became; prabhura nāma — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's holy name.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reclaimed the leper Vāsudeva and so received the name Vāsudevāmrita-prada.

PURPORT

The name Vāsudevāmrita-prada is mentioned in the verses composed by Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
