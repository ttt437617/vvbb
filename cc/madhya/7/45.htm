Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.45

ājñā deha, avaśya āmi dakshine caliba

tomāra ājñāte sukhe leuti' āsiba'

SYNONYMS

ājñā deha — please give permission; avaśya — certainly; āmi — I; dakshine — in South India; caliba — shall go; tomāra — your; ājñāte — by the order; sukhe — in happiness; leuti' — returning; āsiba — I shall come.

TRANSLATION

"Please permit Me to go, for I must tour South India. With your permission, I shall soon return very happily."
