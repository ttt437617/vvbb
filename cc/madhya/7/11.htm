Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.11

viśvarūpa-uddeśe avaśya āmi yāba

ekākī yāiba, kāho sańge nā la-iba

SYNONYMS

viśvarūpa-uddeśe — to find Viśvarūpa; avaśya — certainly; āmi — I; yāba — shall go; ekākī — alone; yāiba — I shall go; kāho — someone; sańge — in association; nā — not; la-iba — I shall take.

TRANSLATION

"I shall go to search out Viśvarūpa. Please forgive Me, but I want to go alone; I do not wish to take anyone with Me.
