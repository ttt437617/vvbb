Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.134

ei-mata sei rātri tāhāńi rahilā

prātah-kāle prabhu snāna kariyā calilā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; sei rātri — that night; tāhāńi — there; rahilā — stayed; prātah-kāle — in the morning; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; snāna — bath; kariyā — taking; calilā — again started.

TRANSLATION

Thus Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would remain at night in one place, and the next morning, after bathing, He would start again.
