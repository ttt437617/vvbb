Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.136

'vāsudeva'-nāma eka dvija mahāśaya

sarvāńge galita kushtha, tāte kīdā-maya

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva-nāma — of the name Vāsudeva; eka dvija — one brāhmana; mahāśaya — a great person; sarva-ańge — all over his body; galita — acute; kushtha — leprosy; tāte — in that; kīdā-maya — full of living worms.

TRANSLATION

There was also a brāhmana named Vāsudeva, who was a great person but was suffering from leprosy. Indeed, his body was filled with living worms.
