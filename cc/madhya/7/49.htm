Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.49

svatantra-īśvara tumi karibe gamana

dina katho raha, dekhi tomāra carana'

SYNONYMS

svatantra-īśvara — the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead; tumi — You; karibe — will make; gamana — departure; dina — days; katho — some; raha — please stay; dekhi — I may see; tomāra carana — Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You are the independent Supreme Personality of Godhead. Certainly You will depart. I know that. Still, I ask You to stay here a few days more so that I can see Your lotus feet."
