Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.105

ei-mata pathe yāite śata śata jana

'vaishnava' karena tāńre kari' ālińgana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; pathe — on the way; yāite — while passing; śata śata — hundreds and hundreds; jana — persons; vaishnava — devotees; karena — makes; tāńre — Him; kari' — doing; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

Thus many hundreds of people became Vaishnavas when they passed the Lord on the way and were embraced by Him.
