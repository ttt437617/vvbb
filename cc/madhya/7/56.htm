Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.56

darśana kari' thākura-pāśa ājñā māgilā

pūjārī prabhure mālā-prasāda āni' dilā

SYNONYMS

darśana kari' — visiting the Lord; thākura-pāśa — from the Lord; ājñā māgilā — begged permission; pūjārī — the priest; prabhure — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mālā — garland; prasāda — remnants of food; āni' — bringing; dilā — delivered.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also begged His permission. The priest then immediately delivered prasādam and a garland to Lord Caitanya.
