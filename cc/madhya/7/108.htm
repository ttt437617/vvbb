Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.108

ei-mata kailā yāvat gelā setubandhe

sarva-deśa 'vaishnava' haila prabhura sambandhe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; kailā — performed; yāvat — until; gelā — went; setubandhe — to the southernmost part of India; sarva-deśa — all the countries; vaishnava — devotees; haila — became; prabhura — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sambandhe — in connection with.

TRANSLATION

In this way the Lord went to the extreme southern part of India, and He converted all the provinces to Vaishnavism.
