Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.58

bhattācārya-sańge āra yata nija-gana

jagannātha pradakshina kari' karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya-sańge — with Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; āra — and; yata — all; nija-gana — personal devotees; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; pradakshina — circumambulation; kari' — finishing; karilā — made; gamana — departure.

TRANSLATION

Accompanied by His personal associates and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated the altar of Jagannātha. The Lord then departed on His South Indian tour.
