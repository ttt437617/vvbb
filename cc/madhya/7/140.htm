Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.140

aneka prakāra vilāpa karite lāgilā

sei-kshane āsi' prabhu tāńre ālińgilā

SYNONYMS

aneka prakāra — various kinds; vilāpa — lamentation; karite — to do; lāgilā — began; sei-kshane — immediately; āsi' — coming back; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; ālińgilā — embraced.

TRANSLATION

When Vāsudeva, the leper brāhmana, was lamenting due to not being able to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord immediately returned to that spot and embraced him.
