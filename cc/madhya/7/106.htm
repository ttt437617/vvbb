Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.106

yei grāme rahi' bhikshā karena yāńra ghare

sei grāmera yata loka āise dekhibāre

SYNONYMS

yei grāme — in whatever village; rahi' — staying; bhikshā — alms; karena — accepts; yāńra — whose; ghare — at home; sei — that; grāmera — of the village; yata loka — all the persons; āise — come; dekhibāre — to see.

TRANSLATION

In whatever village Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed to accept alms, many people came to see Him.
