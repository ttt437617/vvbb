Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.147

prabhu kahe, — "kabhu tomāra nā habe abhimāna

nirantara kaha tumi 'krishna' 'krishna' nāma

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; kabhu — at any time; tomāra — your; nā — not; habe — there will be; abhimāna — pride; nirantara — incessantly; kaha — chant; tumi — you; krishna krishna nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

To protect the brāhmana, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advised him to chant the Hare Krishna mantra incessantly. By doing so, he would never become unnecessarily proud.
