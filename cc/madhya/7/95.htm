Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.95

matta-simha-prāya prabhu karilā gamana

premāveśe yāya kari' nāma-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

matta-simha — a mad lion; prāya — almost like; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karilā — did; gamana — touring; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; yāya — goes; kari' — performing; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting Krishna's name.

TRANSLATION

Almost like a mad lion, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went on His tour filled with ecstatic love and performing sańkīrtana, chanting Krishna's names as follows.
