Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.93

vicchede vyākula prabhu calilā duhkhī hañā

pāche krishnadāsa yāya jala-pātra lañā

SYNONYMS

vicchede — in separation; vyākula — perturbed; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calilā — went on; duhkhī — unhappy; hañā — becoming; pāche — just behind; krishnadāsa — His servant Krishnadāsa; yāya — went; jala-pātra — the waterpot; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

In separation, the Lord became very much perturbed and walked on unhappily. His servant, Krishnadāsa, who was carrying His waterpot, followed behind.
