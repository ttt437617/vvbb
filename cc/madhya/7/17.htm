Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.17

dakshinera tīrtha-patha āmi saba jāni

āmi sańge yāi, prabhu, ājñā deha tumi"

SYNONYMS

dakshinera — of South India; tīrtha-patha — the ways to different places of pilgrimage; āmi — I; saba — all; jāni — know; āmi — I; sańge — with You; yāi — go; prabhu — O My Lord; ājñā — order; deha — give; tumi — You.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, I know all the paths to the different places of pilgrimage in South India. Just order Me, and I shall go with You."
