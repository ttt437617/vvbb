Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.75

bhakta-gana śīghra āsi' laila prabhura sātha

vastra-prasāda lañā tabe āilā gopīnātha

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — devotees; śīghra — very swiftly; āsi' — coming; laila — took; prabhura — of the Lord; sātha — the company; vastra — the garments; prasāda — and Lord Jagannātha's prasādam; lañā — with; tabe — thereafter; āilā — came; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha Ācārya.

TRANSLATION

Immediately all the devotees came and partook of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's company. Afterwards, Gopīnātha Ācārya came with the garments and prasādam.
