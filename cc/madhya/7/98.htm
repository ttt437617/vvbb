Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.98

sei loka prema-matta hañā bale 'hari' 'krishna'

prabhura pāche sańge yāya darśana-satrishna

SYNONYMS

sei loka — that person; prema-matta — maddened in love of Godhead; hañā — becoming; bale — says; hari krishna — the holy name of Lord Hari and Lord Krishna; prabhura pāche — behind the Lord; sańge — with Him; yāya — goes; darśana-satrishna — being very eager to see Him.

TRANSLATION

Whoever heard Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu chant "Hari! Hari!" also chanted the holy name of Lord Hari and Krishna. In this way they all followed the Lord, very eager to see Him.
