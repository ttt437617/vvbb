Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.19

sannyāsa kariyā āmi calilāń vrindāvana

tumi āmā lañā āile advaita-bhavana

SYNONYMS

sannyāsa kariyā — after accepting the renounced order; āmi — I; calilāń — went; vrindāvana — toward Vrindāvana; tumi — You; āmā — Me; lañā — taking; āile — went; advaita-bhavana — to the house of Advaita Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

"After accepting the sannyāsa order, I decided to go to Vrindāvana, but You took Me instead to the house of Advaita Prabhu.
