Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.119

kata-kshane prabhu yadi bāhya prakāśilā

kūrmera sevaka bahu sammāna karilā

SYNONYMS

kata-kshane — after some time; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yadi — when; bāhya — external consciousness; prakāśilā — manifested; kūrmera — of the Lord Kūrma Deity; sevaka — a servant; bahu — much; sammāna — respect; karilā — showed.

TRANSLATION

After some time, when Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu manifested His external consciousness, a priest of the Lord Kūrma Deity gave Him various offerings.
