Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.3

ei-mate sārvabhaumera nistāra karila

dakshina-gamane prabhura icchā upajila

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; sārvabhaumera — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; nistāra — the liberation; karila — was executed; dakshina-gamane — in going to South India; prabhura — of the Lord; icchā — a desire; upajila — arose.

TRANSLATION

After delivering Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, the Lord desired to go to South India to preach.
