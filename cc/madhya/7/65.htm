Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.65

pānditya āra bhakti-rasa, — duńhera teńho sīmā

sambhāshile jānibe tumi tāńhāra mahimā

SYNONYMS

pānditya — learning; āra — and; bhakti-rasa — the mellows of devotional service; duńhera — of these two; teńho — he; sīmā — the limit; sambhāshile — when You talk with him; jānibe — will know; tumi — You; tāńhāra — his; mahimā — glories.

TRANSLATION

"He is a most learned scholar as well as an expert in devotional mellows. Actually he is most exalted, and if You talk with him, You will see how glorious he is.
