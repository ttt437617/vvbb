Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.148

krishna upadeśi' kara jīvera nistāra

acirāte krishna tomā karibena ańgīkāra"

SYNONYMS

krishna upadeśi' — instructing about Krishna; kara — just do; jīvera — of all living entities; nistāra — the liberation; acirāte — very soon; krishna — Lord Krishna; tomā — you; karibena — will make; ańgīkāra — acceptance.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also advised Vāsudeva to preach about Krishna and thus liberate living entities. As a result, Krishna would very soon accept him as His devotee.

PURPORT

Although Vāsudeva Vipra was a leper and had suffered greatly, still, after Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu cured him He instructed him to preach Krishna consciousness. Indeed, the only return the Lord wanted was that Vāsudeva preach the instructions of Krishna and liberate all human beings. That is the process of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. Each and every member of this Society was rescued from a very abominable condition, but now they are engaged in preaching the cult of Krishna consciousness. They are not only cured of the disease called materialism but are also living a very happy life. Everyone accepts them as great devotees of Krishna, and their qualities are manifest in their very faces. If one wants to be recognized as a devotee by Krishna, he should take to preaching work, following the advice of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Then one will undoubtedly attain the lotus feet of Śrī Krishna Caitanya, Lord Krishna Himself, without delay.
