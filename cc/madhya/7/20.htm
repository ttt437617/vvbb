Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.20

nīlācala āsite pathe bhāńgilā mora danda

tomā-sabāra gādha-snehe āmāra kārya-bhańga

SYNONYMS

nīlācala — to Jagannātha Purī; āsite — going there; pathe — on the road; bhāńgilā — You broke; mora — My; danda — sannyāsa staff; tomā-sabāra — of all of you; gādha-snehe — on account of the deep affection; āmāra — My; kārya-bhańga — disturbance of activities.

TRANSLATION

"While on the way to Jagannātha Purī, You broke My sannyāsa staff. I know that all of you have great affection for Me, but such things disturb My activities.
