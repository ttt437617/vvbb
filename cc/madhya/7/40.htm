Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.40

jala-pātra-vastra vahi' tomā-sańge yābe

ye tomāra icchā, kara, kichu nā balibe

SYNONYMS

jala-pātra — waterpot; vastra — and garments; vahi' — carrying; tomā-sańge — with You; yābe — will go; ye — whatever; tomāra icchā — Your desire; kara — You do; kichu nā balibe — he will not say anything.

TRANSLATION

"He will carry Your waterpot and garments. You may do whatever You like; he will not say a word."
