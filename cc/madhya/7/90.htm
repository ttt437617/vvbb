Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.90

ei-rūpe sei thāñi bhakta-gana-sańge

sei rātri gońāilā krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

ei-rūpe — in this way; sei thāñi — in that place; bhakta-gana-sańge — with the devotees; sei rātri — that night; gońāilā — passed; krishna-kathā-rańge — in great pleasure discussing Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then passed the night there and discussed the pastimes of Lord Krishna with His devotees with great pleasure.
