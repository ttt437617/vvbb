Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.44

sannyāsa kari' viśvarūpa giyāche dakshine

avaśya kariba āmi tāńra anveshane

SYNONYMS

sannyāsa kari' — after accepting the sannyāsa order; viśvarūpa — Viśvarūpa (the elder brother of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu); giyāche — has gone; dakshine — to South India; avaśya — certainly; kariba — shall do; āmi — I; tāńra — of Him; anveshane — searching for.

TRANSLATION

"My elder brother, Viśvarūpa, has taken sannyāsa and gone to South India. Now I must go search for Him.
