Madhya-līlāChapter 7: The Lord Begins His Tour of South India

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 7.142

prabhura kripā dekhi' tāńra vismaya haila mana

śloka padi' pāye dhari, karaye stavana

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā — the mercy; dekhi' — seeing; tāńra — of the brāhmana Vāsudeva; vismaya haila mana — there was astonishment in his mind; śloka padi' — reciting a verse; pāye dhari — touching His lotus feet; karaye stavana — offers prayers.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana Vāsudeva was astonished to behold the wonderful mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and he began to recite a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, touching the Lord's lotus feet.
