Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.159

'vāmā' eka gopī-gana, 'dakshinā' eka gana

nānā-bhāve karāya krishne rasa āsvādana

SYNONYMS

vāmā — left wing; eka — one; gopī-gana — party of gopīs; dakshinā — right wing; eka — another; gana — party of gopīs; nānā-bhāve — in varieties of ecstatic love; karāya — cause to do; krishne — unto Krishna; rasa āsvādana — tasting of transcendental mellows.

TRANSLATION

"The gopīs can be divided into a left wing and a right wing. Both wings induce Krishna to taste transcendental mellows by various manifestations of ecstatic love.
