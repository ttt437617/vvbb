Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.240

jagannāthera prasāda āila bahu upahāra

lakshmīra prasāda āila vividha prakāra

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera prasāda — the prasādam offered to Jagannātha; āila — arrived; bahu — many; upahāra — offerings; lakshmīra prasāda — food offered to Lakshmīdevī; āila — arrived; vividha prakāra — all varieties.

TRANSLATION

Then there arrived in large quantities a variety of food that had been offered to Śrī Jagannātha and a variety that had been offered to the goddess of fortune.
