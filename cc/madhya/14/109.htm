Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.109

thākurera bhāndāre āra āmāra bhāndāre

citra-vastra-kińkinī, āra chatra-cāmare

SYNONYMS

thākurera — of the Deity; bhāndāre — in the storehouse; āra — and; āmāra — my; bhāndāre — in the storehouse; citra-vastra — printed cloth; kińkinī — small bells; āra — and; chatra — umbrellas; cāmare — yak-tail whisks.

TRANSLATION

"Take as many printed cloths, small bells, umbrellas and cāmaras as there are in my storehouse and in the Deity's storehouse.
