Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.98

prati-vriksha-tale prabhu karena nartana

vāsudeva-datta mātra karena gāyana

SYNONYMS

prati-vriksha-tale — underneath each tree; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena nartana — dances; vāsudeva-datta — Vāsudeva Datta; mātra — only; karena — performs; gāyana — chanting.

TRANSLATION

As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced beneath each and every tree, Vāsudeva Datta sang alone.
