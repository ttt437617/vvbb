Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.123

vrindāvana-līlāya krishnera sahāya gopī-gana

gopī-gana vinā krishnera harite nāre mana

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-līlāya — in the pastimes of Vrindāvana; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sahāya — assistants; gopī-gana — all the gopīs; gopī-gana vinā — except for the gopīs; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; harite — to attract; nāre — no one is able; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

"In the pastimes of Vrindāvana, the only assistants are the gopīs. But for the gopīs, no one can attract the mind of Krishna."
