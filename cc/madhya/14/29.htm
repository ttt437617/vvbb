Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.29

amrita-mandā, saravatī, āra kumdā-kurī

sarāmrita, sarabhājā, āra sarapurī

SYNONYMS

amrita-mandā — a variety of papaya; saravatī — a kind of orange; āra — and; kumdā-kurī — crushed squash; sarāmrita — cream; sara-bhājā — fried cream; āra — and; sara-purī — a kind of purī made with cream.

TRANSLATION

There were also papayas and saravatī, a type of orange, and also crushed squash. There were also regular cream, fried cream and a type of purī made with cream.
