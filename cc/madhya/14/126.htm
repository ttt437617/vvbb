Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.126

ataeva krishnera prākatye nāhi kichu dosha

tabe kene lakshmīdevī kare eta rosha?

SYNONYMS

ataeva — since; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; prākatye — by such a manifestation; nāhi — there is not; kichu — any; dosha — fault; tabe — therefore; kene — why; lakshmī-devī — the goddess of fortune; kare — does; eta — so much; rosha — anger.

TRANSLATION

"Since there is no fault at all in Krishna's pastimes, why does the goddess of fortune become angry?"
