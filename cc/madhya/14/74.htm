Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.74

rādhā-sańge krishna-līlā — ei haila jñāne

ei rase magna prabhu ha-ilā āpane

SYNONYMS

rādhā-sańge — with Rādhārānī; krishna-līlā — pastimes of Lord Krishna; ei haila jñāne — this was His consciousness; ei rase magna — merged in this mellow; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ilā āpane — remained personally.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was always thinking of the pastimes of Rādhā and Krishna, and He remained personally merged in this consciousness.
