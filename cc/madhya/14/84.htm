Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.84

pandita, gambhīra, duńhe — prāmānika jana

bāla-cāñcalya kare, karāha varjana

SYNONYMS

pandita — learned scholars; gambhīra — very grave; duńhe — both of them; prāmānika jana — authoritative persons; bāla-cāñcalya kare — sport like children; karāha varjana — ask them to stop.

TRANSLATION

"Tell the Bhattācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya to stop their childish play because they are both learned scholars and very grave and great personalities."
