Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.89

āpane tāńhāra upara karila śayana

'śesha-śāyī-līlā' prabhu kaila prakatana

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; tāńhāra upara — upon Advaita Ācārya; karila śayana — lay down; śesha-śāyī-līlā — the pastimes of Śeshaśāyī Vishnu; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila prakatana — demonstrated.

TRANSLATION

Lying down on Advaita Prabhu, who was floating on the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu demonstrated the pastime of Śeshaśāyī Vishnu.
