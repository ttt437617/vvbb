Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.10

"tava kathāmritam" śloka rājā ye padila

uthi' premāveśe prabhu ālińgana kaila

SYNONYMS

tava kathāmritam — beginning with the words tava kathāmritam; śloka — the verse; rājā — the King; ye padila — as he recited; uthi' — getting up; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ālińgana kaila — embraced.

TRANSLATION

As soon as the King recited the verse beginning with the words "tava kathāmritam," the Lord arose in ecstatic love and embraced him.
