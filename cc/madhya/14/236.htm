Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.236

nityānanda dekhiyā prabhura bhāvāveśa

nikate nā āise, rahe kichu dūra-deśa

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; dekhiyā — seeing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhāva-āveśa — the ecstatic love; nikate — nearby; nā āise — does not come; rahe — keeps; kichu — a little; dūra-deśa — far away.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the ecstatic love of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu did not approach but remained a little distance away.
