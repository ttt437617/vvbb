Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.59

'jaya gauracandra', 'jaya śrī-krishna-caitanya'

ei-mata kolāhala loke dhanya dhanya

SYNONYMS

jaya gauracandra — all glories to Gaurahari; jaya śrī-krishna-caitanya — all glories to Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei-mata — in this way; kolāhala — a tumultuous sound; loke — people in general; dhanya dhanya — began to chant, "Wonderful, wonderful!"

TRANSLATION

The crowd made a tumultuous vibration, chanting "Jaya Gauracandra! Jaya Śrī Krishna Caitanya!" Then the people began to chant, "Wonderful! Wonderful!"
