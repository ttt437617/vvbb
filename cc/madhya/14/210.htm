Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.210

lakshmīra carane āni' karāya pranati

dhana-danda laya, āra karāya minati

SYNONYMS

lakshmīra carane — at the lotus feet of the goddess of fortune; āni' — bringing; karāya pranati — made to bow down; dhana-danda laya — take a fine; āra — also; karāya — make them perform; minati — submission.

TRANSLATION

"When all the maidservants brought Lord Jagannātha's servants before the lotus feet of the goddess of fortune, the Lord's servants were fined and forced to submit.
