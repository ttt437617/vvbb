Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.35

prasāde pūrita ha-ila ardha upavana

dekhiyā santosha haila mahāprabhura mana

SYNONYMS

prasāde — with all the prasādam; pūrita ha-ila — became filled; ardha upavana — half of the garden; dekhiyā — seeing; santosha — satisfaction; haila — there was; mahāprabhura mana — in the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw half the garden filled with a variety of prasādam, He was very satisfied.
