Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.103

jala-krīdā kari' punah āilā udyāne

bhojana-līlā kailā prabhu lañā bhakta-gane

SYNONYMS

jala-krīdā — sporting in the water; kari' — performing; punah — again; āilā — came; udyāne — in the garden; bhojana-līlā — pastimes of accepting prasādam; kailā — performed; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā bhakta-gane — with all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

After sporting in the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to the garden and accepted prasādam with the devotees.
