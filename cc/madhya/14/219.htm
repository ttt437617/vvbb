Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.219

vrindāvane sāhajika ye sampat-sindhu

dvārakā-vaikuntha-sampat — tāra eka bindu

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; sāhajika — natural; ye — whatever; sampat-sindhu — ocean of opulence; dvārakā — of Dvārakā; vaikuntha-sampat — all the opulence of the spiritual world; tāra — of that; eka bindu — one drop.

TRANSLATION

"The natural opulence of Vrindāvana is just like an ocean. The opulence of Dvārakā and Vaikuntha is not even to be compared to a drop.
