Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.197

stanādharādi-grahane

hrit-prītāv api sambhramāt

bahih krodho vyathita-vat

proktam kuttamitam budhaih

SYNONYMS

stana — breasts; adhara — lips; ādi — and so on; grahane — when capturing; hrit-prītau — satisfaction of the heart; api — even though; sambhramāt — because of respectfulness; bahih — externally; krodhah — anger; vyathita — aggrieved; vat — as if; proktam — called; kuttamitam — the technical term kuttamita; budhaih — by learned scholars.

TRANSLATION

"'When the border of Her sari and the cloth veiling Her face are caught, She externally appears offended and angry, but within Her heart She is very happy. Learned scholars call this attitude kuttamita.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Ujjvala-nīlamani (Anubhāva-prakarana 44).
