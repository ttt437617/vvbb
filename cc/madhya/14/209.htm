Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.209

eta bali' mahā-lakshmīra saba dāsī-gane

kati-vastre bāndhi' āne prabhura nija-gane

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; mahā-lakshmīra — of the goddess of fortune; saba — all; dāsī-gane — maidservants; kati-vastre — by their waist cloths; bāndhi' — binding; āne — bring; prabhura — of Jagannātha; nija-gane — personal servants.

TRANSLATION

"In this way all the maidservants of the goddess of fortune arrested the servants of Jagannātha, bound them around the waist and brought them before the goddess of fortune.
