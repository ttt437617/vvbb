Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.58

nimeshe ta' gela ratha gundicāra dvāra

caitanya-pratāpa dekhi' loke camatkāra

SYNONYMS

nimeshe — in a moment; ta' — indeed; gela — arrived; ratha — the car; gundicāra dvāra — at the door of the Gundicā temple; caitanya-pratāpa — the strength of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhi' — seeing; loke — all the people; camatkāra — astonished.

TRANSLATION

In a moment the car reached the door of the Gundicā temple. Upon seeing the uncommon strength of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the people were struck with wonder.
