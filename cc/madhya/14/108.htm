Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.108

mahotsava kara taiche viśesha sambhāra

dekhi' mahāprabhura yaiche haya camatkāra

SYNONYMS

mahotsava — the festival; kara — perform; taiche — in such a way; viśesha sambhāra — with great gorgeousness; dekhi' — after seeing; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yaiche — so that; haya — there is; camatkāra — astonishment.

TRANSLATION

King Pratāparudra said, "Hold this festival in such a gorgeous way that upon seeing it Caitanya Mahāprabhu will be completely pleased and astonished.
