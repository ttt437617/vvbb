Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.205

vrindāvana dekhibāre gelā jagannātha

śuni' lakshmī-devīra mane haila āsoyātha

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana — Vrindāvana-dhāma; dekhibāre — to see; gelā — went; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; śuni' — hearing; lakshmī-devīra — of the goddess of fortune; mane — in the mind; haila — there was; āsoyātha — envy.

TRANSLATION

"When Jagannātha decided to see Vrindāvana, He went there, and upon hearing this, the goddess of fortune experienced restlessness and jealousy.
