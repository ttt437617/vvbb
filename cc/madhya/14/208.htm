Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.208

ei karma kare kāhāń vidagdha-śiromani?

lakshmīra agrete nija prabhure deha' āni'"

SYNONYMS

ei — this; karma — work; kare — does; kāhāń — where; vidagdha-śiromani — the chief of all experts; lakshmīra — of the goddess of fortune; agrete — in front; nija — your own; prabhure — master; deha' — present; āni' — bringing.

TRANSLATION

"'Your master is so expert at everything, but why does He do such things? Now bring your master before the goddess of fortune.'
