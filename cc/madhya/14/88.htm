Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.88

hāsi' mahāprabhu tabe advaite ānila

jalera upare tāńre śesha-śayyā kaila

SYNONYMS

hāsi' — smiling; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tabe — at that time; advaite ānila — called for Advaita Ācārya; jalera upare — on the surface of the water; tāńre — Him; śesha-śayyā — the Śesha Nāga bed; kaila — made.

TRANSLATION

After Gopīnātha Ācārya finished talking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and, calling for Advaita Ācārya, made Him act like the Śesha Nāga bed.
