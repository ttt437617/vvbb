Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.203

śrīvāsa hāsiyā kahe, — śuna, dāmodara

āmāra lakshmīra dekha sampatti vistara

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; hāsiyā — smiling; kahe — says; śuna — please hear; dāmodara — O Dāmodara Gosvāmī; āmāra lakshmīra — of my goddess of fortune; dekha — just see; sampatti vistara — the great opulence.

TRANSLATION

At this time, Śrīvāsa Thākura smiled and told Svarūpa Dāmodara, "My dear sir, please hear! Just see how opulent my goddess of fortune is!
