Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.44

prabhura ājñāya govinda dīna-hīna jane

duhkhī kāńgāla āni' karāya bhojane

SYNONYMS

prabhura ājñāya — on the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; govinda — His personal servant; dīna-hīna jane — unto all poor men; duhkhī — unhappy; kāńgāla — beggars; āni' — inviting; karāya bhojane — fed sumptuously.

TRANSLATION

Following the orders of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda, His personal servant, called for all the poor beggars, who were unhappy due to their poverty, and fed them sumptuously.
