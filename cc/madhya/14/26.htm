Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.26

chānā, pānā, paida, āmra, nārikela, kāńthāla

nānā-vidha kadalaka, āra bīja-tāla

SYNONYMS

chānā — curd; pānā — fruit juice; paida — coconut; āmra — mango; nārikela — dried coconut; kāńthāla — jackfruit; nānā-vidha — various kinds of; kadalaka — bananas; āra — and; bīja-tāla — palm-fruit seeds.

TRANSLATION

There were curd, fruit juice, coconut, mango, dried coconut, jackfruit, various kinds of bananas and palm-fruit seeds.

PURPORT

This is the first list of prasādam offered to Lord Jagannātha.
