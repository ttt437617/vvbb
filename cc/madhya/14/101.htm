Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.101

prabhu-sańge svarūpādi kīrtanīyā gāya

dik-vidik nāhi jñāna premera vanyāya

SYNONYMS

prabhu-sańge — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svarūpa-ādi — headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; kīrtanīyā — chanters; gāya — sing; dik-vidik — of time and circumstances; nāhi — not; jñāna — knowledge; premera — of ecstatic love; vanyāya — by the inundation.

TRANSLATION

Then devotees like Svarūpa Dāmodara and other kīrtana performers began to sing along with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Being inundated with ecstatic love, they lost all consideration of time and circumstance.
