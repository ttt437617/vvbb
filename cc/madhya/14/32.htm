Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.32

nārańga-cholańga-āmra-vrikshera ākāra

phula-phala-patra-yukta khandera vikāra

SYNONYMS

nārańga-cholańga-āmra-vrikshera ākāra — sweetmeats in the shape of varieties of orange, lemon and mango trees; phula-phala-patra-yukta — dressed with fruits, flowers and leaves; khandera vikāra — made from sugar candy.

TRANSLATION

There were sugar-candy sweetmeats formed into the shape of orange, lemon and mango trees and arranged with fruits, flowers and leaves.
