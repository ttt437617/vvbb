Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.85

gopīnātha kahe, — tomāra kripā-mahāsindhu

uchalita kare yabe tāra eka bindu

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha kahe — Gopīnātha Ācārya replied; tomāra kripā — of Your mercy; mahā-sindhu — the great ocean; uchalita kare — rises; yabe — when; tāra — of that; eka bindu — a drop.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya replied, "I believe that one drop of the ocean of Your great mercy has swelled up upon them.
