Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.136

dāmodara kahe, — aiche mānera prakāra

trijagate kāhāń nāhi dekhi śuni āra

SYNONYMS

dāmodara kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; aiche — such; mānera — of egoistic pride; prakāra — kind; tri-jagate — within the three worlds; kāhāń — anywhere; nāhi — not; dekhi — I see; śuni — I hear; āra — other.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara said, "There is no egoistic pride like this within the three worlds. At least I have never seen it or heard of it.
