Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.207

"tomāra thākura, dekha eta sampatti chādi'

patra-phala-phula-lobhe gelā pushpa-bādī

SYNONYMS

tomāra thākura — your Lord; dekha — just see; eta sampatti chādi' — giving so much opulence; patra-phala-phula — leaves, fruits and flowers; lobhe — for the sake of; gelā — went; pushpa-bādī — to the flower garden of Gundicā.

TRANSLATION

"Then the maidservants of the goddess of fortune said to the servants of Lord Jagannātha, 'Why did your Lord Jagannātha abandon the great opulence of the goddess of fortune and, for the sake of a few leaves, fruits and flowers, go see the flower garden of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī?
