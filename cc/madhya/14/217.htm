Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.217

ińho dāmodara-svarūpa — śuddha-vrajavāsī

aiśvarya nā jāne ińho śuddha-preme bhāsi'

SYNONYMS

ińho — here; dāmodara-svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; śuddha-vraja-vāsī — a pure inhabitant of Vrindāvana; aiśvarya nā jāne — he does not know opulence; ińho — he; śuddha-preme — in pure devotional service; bhāsi' — floating.

TRANSLATION

"Svarūpa Dāmodara is a pure devotee of Vrindāvana. He does not even know what opulence is, for he is simply absorbed in pure devotional service."
