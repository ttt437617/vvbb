Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.76

āpane sakala bhakte siñce jala diyā

saba bhakta-gana siñce caudike bediyā

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; sakala bhakte — all the devotees; siñce — sprinkles; jala diyā — with water; saba bhakta-gana — all the devotees; siñce — sprinkle; cau-dike bediyā — surrounding the Lord on all sides.

TRANSLATION

The Lord personally splashed all the devotees with water, and the devotees, surrounding Him on all sides, also splashed the Lord.
