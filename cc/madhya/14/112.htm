Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.112

seita' kariha, — prabhu lañā bhakta-gana

svacchande āsiyā yaiche karena daraśana

SYNONYMS

seita' kariha — do that; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā bhakta-gana — taking with Him all the devotees; svacchande — freely; āsiyā — coming; yaiche — as; karena daraśana — pays a visit to the temple.

TRANSLATION

"Arrange the festival in such a way that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu may freely go with His devotees to visit the Deity without difficulty."
