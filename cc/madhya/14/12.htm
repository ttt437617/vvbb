Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.12

eta bali' sei śloka pade bāra bāra

dui-janāra ańge kampa, netre jala-dhāra

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; sei śloka — that verse; pade — recites; bāra bāra — again and again; dui-janāra — of both of them (Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and King Pratāparudra); ańge — in the bodies; kampa — trembling; netre — in the eyes; jala-dhāra — flow of water.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to recite the same verse again and again. Both the King and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were trembling, and tears were flowing from their eyes.
