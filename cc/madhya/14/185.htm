Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.185

rādhā vasi' āche, kibā vrindāvane yāya

tāhāń yadi ācambite krishna-daraśana pāya

SYNONYMS

rādhā vasi' āche — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is sitting; kibā — or; vrindāvane yāya — is going to Vrindāvana; tāhāń — there; yadi — if; ācambite — all of a sudden; krishna-daraśana pāya — gets the opportunity to see Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Sometimes when Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is sitting or when She is going to Vrindāvana, She sees Krishna.
