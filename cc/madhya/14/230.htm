Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.230

rādhāra śuddha-rasa prabhu āveśe śunila

sei rasāveśe prabhu nritya ārambhila

SYNONYMS

rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; śuddha-rasa — pure transcendental mellows; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āveśe śunila — heard with great ecstasy; sei — that; rasa-āveśe — in absorption in ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya ārambhila — began dancing.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these discussions about the pure transcendental mellow of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. Absorbed in transcendental ecstasy, the Lord began to dance.
