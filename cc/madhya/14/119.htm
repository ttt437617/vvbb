Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.119

vrindāvana-sama ei upavana-gana

tāhā dekhibāre utkanthita haya mana

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-sama — exactly resembling Vrindāvana; ei — all these; upavana-gana — neighboring gardens; tāhā — those gardens; dekhibāre — for seeing; utkanthita — very eager; haya mana — His mind becomes.

TRANSLATION

Pointing out the neighboring gardens, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "All these gardens exactly resemble Vrindāvana; therefore Lord Jagannātha is very eager to see them again.
