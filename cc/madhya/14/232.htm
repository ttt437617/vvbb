Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.232

vraja-rasa-gīta śuni' prema uthalila

purushottama-grāma prabhu preme bhāsāila

SYNONYMS

vraja-rasa-gīta — songs about the mellows of Vrindāvana-dhāma; śuni' — hearing; prema — transcendental bliss; uthalila — awakened; purushottama-grāma — the place known as Purushottama, Jagannātha Purī; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; preme — with ecstatic love; bhāsāila — inundated.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love was awakened by hearing the songs of Vrindāvana. In this way He inundated Purushottama, Jagannātha Purī, with love of Godhead.
