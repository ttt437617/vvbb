Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.245

pūrvavat kaila prabhu lañā bhakta-gana

parama ānande karena nartana-kīrtana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as previously; kaila — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; parama ānande — in great pleasure; karena — performs; nartana-kīrtana — chanting and dancing.

TRANSLATION

As previously, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees chanted and danced with great pleasure.
