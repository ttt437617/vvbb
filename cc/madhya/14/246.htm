Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.246

jagannāthera punah pāndu-vijaya ha-ila

eka guti patta-dorī tāńhā tuti' gela

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; punah — again; pāndu-vijaya — the function of carrying the Lord; ha-ila — there was; eka guti — one bunch; patta-dorī — ropes of silk; tāńhā — there; tuti' gela — broke.

TRANSLATION

During the Pāndu-vijaya, Lord Jagannātha was carried, and while He was being carried, a bunch of silken ropes broke.
