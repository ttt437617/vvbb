Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.238

bhańgi kari' svarūpa sabāra śrama jānāila

bhakta-ganera śrama dekhi' prabhura bāhya haila

SYNONYMS

bhańgi kari' — showing an indication; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; sabāra — of everyone; śrama — the fatigue; jānāila — made known; bhakta-ganera — of the devotees; śrama — the fatigue; dekhi' — seeing; prabhura — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhya haila — become externally conscious.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara then informed the Lord that all the devotees were fatigued. Seeing this situation, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to His external senses.
