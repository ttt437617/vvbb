Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.170

kila-kiñcitādi-bhāvera śuna vivarana

ye bhāva-bhūshāya rādhā hare krishna-mana

SYNONYMS

kila-kiñcita-ādi — beginning with the ecstasy named kila-kiñcita; bhāvera — of ecstasies; śuna — hear; vivarana — the description; ye bhāva-bhūshāya — with these ecstatic ornaments; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; hare — enchants; krishna-mana — the mind of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Now hear a description of different ecstasies, beginning with kila-kiñcita. With these ecstatic ornaments, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī enchants the mind of Krishna.
