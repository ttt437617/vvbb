Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.51

matta-hasti-gana tāne yāra yata bala

eka pada nā cale ratha, ha-ila acala

SYNONYMS

matta-hasti-gana — the strong elephants; tāne — started pulling; yāra yata bala — with whatever strength they had; eka pada — a single step; nā cale — does not move; ratha — the car; ha-ila — was; acala — still.

TRANSLATION

The strong elephants pulled with all their strength, but still the car remained at a standstill, not budging an inch.
