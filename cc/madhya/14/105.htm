Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.105

'jagannātha-vallabha' nāma bada pushpārāma

nava dina karena prabhu tathāi viśrāma

SYNONYMS

jagannātha-vallabha — Jagannātha-vallabha; nāma — named; bada — very big; pushpa-ārāma — garden; nava dina — nine days; karena — does; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tathāi — there; viśrāma — resting.

TRANSLATION

The garden of His pastimes was very large and was named Jagannātha-vallabha. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His rest there for nine days.
