Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.83

mahāprabhu tāń dońhāra cāñcalya dekhiyā

gopīnāthācārye kichu kahena hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāń dońhāra — of these two persons; cāñcalya — restlessness; dekhiyā — seeing; gopīnātha-ācārye — unto Gopīnātha Ācārya; kichu — something; kahena — says; hāsiyā — smiling.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the exuberance of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya, He smiled and spoke to Gopīnātha Ācārya.
