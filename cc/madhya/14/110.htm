Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.110

dhvajāvrinda-patākā-ghantāya karaha mandana

nānā-vādya-nritya-dolāya karaha sājana

SYNONYMS

dhvajā-vrinda — all kinds of flags; patākā — big flags; ghantāya — with ringing bells; karaha — make; mandana — decoration; nānā-vādya — all kinds of musical parties; nritya — dancing; dolāya — on the carrier; karaha sājana — decorate attractively.

TRANSLATION

"Collect all kinds of small and large flags and ringing bells. Then decorate the carrier and have various musical and dancing parties accompany it. In this way decorate the carrier attractively.
