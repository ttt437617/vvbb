Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.27

nārańga, cholańga, tābā, kamalā, bīja-pūra

bādāma, chohārā, drākshā, pinda-kharjura

SYNONYMS

nārańga — oranges; cholańga — grapefruits; tābā — another type of orange; kamalā — tangerines; bīja-pūra — another type of tangerine; bādāma — almonds; chohārā — dried fruit; drākshā — raisins; pinda-kharjura — dates.

TRANSLATION

There were also oranges, grapefruit, tangerines, almonds, dried fruit, raisins and dates.
