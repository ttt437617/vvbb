Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.221

cintāmani-maya bhūmi ratnera bhavana

cintāmani-gana dāsī-carana-bhūshana

SYNONYMS

cintāmani-maya — made of transcendental touchstone; bhūmi — the ground; ratnera — of gems; bhavana — the original source; cintāmani-gana — such touchstones; dāsī-carana-bhūshana — foot decorations of the maidservants of Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"Vrindāvana-dhāma is made of transcendental touchstone. Its entire surface is the source of all valuable jewels, and the cintāmani stone is used to decorate the lotus feet of the maidservants of Vrindāvana.
