Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.7

āńkhi mudi' prabhu preme bhūmite śayāna

nripati naipunye kare pāda-samvāhana

SYNONYMS

āńkhi mudi' — with closed eyes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; preme — in ecstatic love; bhūmite — on the ground; śayāna — lying down; nripati — the King; naipunye — very expertly; kare — performs; pāda-samvāhana — massaging the legs.

TRANSLATION

As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lay on the raised platform with His eyes closed in ecstatic love and emotion, the King very expertly began to massage His legs.
