Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.92

purī, bhāratī ādi yata mukhya bhakta-gana

ācāryera nimantrane karilā bhojana

SYNONYMS

purī — Paramānanda Purī; bhāratī — Brahmānanda Bhāratī; ādi — beginning with; yata — all; mukhya — chief; bhakta-gana — devotees; ācāryera — of Advaita Ācārya; nimantrane — by the invitation; karilā bhojana — accepted their lunch.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī and all the other chief devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took lunch at the invitation of Advaita Ācārya.
