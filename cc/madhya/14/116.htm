Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.116

rasa-viśesha prabhura śunite mana haila

īshat hāsiyā prabhu svarūpe puchila

SYNONYMS

rasa-viśesha — a particular mellow; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śunite — to hear; mana haila — there was a desire; īshat hāsiyā — smiling mildly; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svarūpe puchila — inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara.

TRANSLATION

After taking His seat, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wanted to hear about a particular mellow of devotional service; therefore, mildly smiling, He began to question Svarūpa Dāmodara.
