Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.239

saba bhakta lañā prabhu gelā pushpodyāne

viśrāma kariyā kailā mādhyāhnika snāne

SYNONYMS

saba bhakta lañā — with all the devotees; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gelā — went; pushpa-udyāne — in the flower garden; viśrāma kariyā — resting; kailā — performed; mādhyāhnika snāne — bath in the afternoon.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then entered the flower garden with all His devotees. After resting there for some time, He took His afternoon bath.
