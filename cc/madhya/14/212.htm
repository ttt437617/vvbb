Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.212

saba bhritya-gana kahe, — yoda kari' hāta

'kāli āni diba tomāra āge jagannātha'

SYNONYMS

saba bhritya-gana kahe — all the servants said; yoda kari' hāta — folding the hands; kāli — tomorrow; āni — bringing; diba — we shall give; tomāra — of you; āge — in front; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

"Finally all of Lord Jagannātha's servants submitted to the goddess of fortune with folded hands, assuring her that they would bring Lord Jagannātha before her the very next day.
