Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.199

vyathā pāñā' kare yena śushka rodana

īshat hāsiyā krishne karena bhartsana

SYNONYMS

vyathā pāñā' — being offended; kare — does; yena — as if; śushka — dry; rodana — crying; īshat — mildly; hāsiyā — smiling; krishne — unto Krishna; karena — does; bhartsana — admonition.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīmatī Rādhārānī externally displays a kind of dry crying, as if She is offended. Then She mildly smiles and admonishes Lord Krishna.
