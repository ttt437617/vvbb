Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.31

padmacini, candrakānti, khājā, khandasāra

viyari, kadmā, tilākhājāra prakāra

SYNONYMS

padma-cini — sugar obtained from lotus flowers; candra-kānti — a kind of bread made from urad dhal; khājā — a crispy sweetmeat; khanda-sāra — sugar candy; viyari — a sweetmeat made from fried rice; kadmā — a sweetmeat made from sesame seeds; tilākhājāra — cookies made from sesame seeds; prakāra — all varieties.

TRANSLATION

There were lotus-flower sugar, a kind of bread made from urad dhal, crispy sweetmeats, sugar candy, fried-rice sweets, sesame-seed sweets and cookies made from sesame seeds.
