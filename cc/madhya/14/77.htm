Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.77

kabhu eka mandala, kabhu aneka mandala

jala-mandūka-vādye sabe bājāya karatāla

SYNONYMS

kabhu eka mandala — sometimes one circle; kabhu — sometimes; aneka mandala — various circles; jala-mandūka-vādye — like the croaking sound of frogs in the water; sabe — all of them; bājāya — play; karatāla — cymbals.

TRANSLATION

While in the water they sometimes formed one circle and sometimes many circles, and while in the water they used to play cymbals and imitate the croaking of frogs.
