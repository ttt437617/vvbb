Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.152

keha 'prakharā', keha 'mridu', keha haya 'samā'

sva-svabhāve krishnera bādāya prema-sīmā

SYNONYMS

keha — some; prakharā — very talkative; keha — some; mridu — very mild; keha haya — some of them are; samā — equipoised; sva-svabhāve — by their own characteristics; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; bādāya — increases; prema-sīmā — limit of loving ecstasy.

TRANSLATION

"Some of these heroines are very talkative, some are mild, and some are equipoised. Each heroine, according to her own character, increases Śrī Krishna's loving ecstasy.
