Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.42

tabe mahāprabhu vaise nija-gana lañā

bhojana karāila sabāke ākantha pūriyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaise — sits; nija-gana lañā — with His personal associates; bhojana karāila — fed; sabāke — all of them; ākantha pūriyā — filling to the neck.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with His personal associates and had every one of them fed very sumptuously until they were filled to the necks.
