Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.14

'bhūridā' 'bhūridā' bali' kare ālińgana

ińho nāhi jāne, — ihoń haya kon jana

SYNONYMS

bhūri-dā — the most munificent; bhūri-dā — the most munificent; bali' — crying; kare — does; ālińgana — embracing; ińho — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāhi jāne — does not know; ihoń — Pratāparudra Mahārāja; haya — is; kon jana — who.

TRANSLATION

After reciting this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately embraced the King and cried, "You are the most munificent! You are the most munificent!" At this point Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not know who the King was.
