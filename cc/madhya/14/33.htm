Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.33

dadhi, dugdha, nanī, takra, rasālā, śikharinī

sa-lavana mudgāńkura, ādā khāni khāni

SYNONYMS

dadhi — yogurt; dugdha — milk; nanī — butter; takra — buttermilk; rasālā — fruit juice; śikharinī — a preparation made of fried yogurt and sugar candy; sa-lavana — salty; mudga-ańkura — mung-dhal sprouts; ādā — ginger; khāni khāni — cut into pieces.

TRANSLATION

There were yogurt, milk, butter, buttermilk, fruit juice, a preparation made of fried yogurt and sugar candy, and salty mung-dhal sprouts with shredded ginger.
