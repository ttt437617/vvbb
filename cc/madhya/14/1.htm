Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.1

gaurah paśyann ātma-vrindaih

śrī-lakshmī-vijayotsavam

śrutvā gopī-rasollāsam

hrishtah premnā nanarta sah

SYNONYMS

gaurah — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; paśyan — by seeing; ātma-vrindaih — with His personal associates; śrī-lakshmī — of the goddess of fortune; vijaya-utsavam — the grand festival; śrutvā — by hearing; gopī — of the gopīs; rasa-ullāsam — the superexcellence of the mellows; hrishtah — being very pleased; premnā — in great ecstatic love; nanarta — danced; sah — He, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Accompanied by His personal devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the festival known as Lakshmī-vijayotsava. There He discussed the superexcellent love of the gopīs. Just by hearing about them, He became very pleased and danced in great ecstatic love for the Lord.
