Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.220

parama purushottama svayam bhagavān

krishna yāhāń dhanī tāhāń vrindāvana-dhāma

SYNONYMS

parama purusha-uttama — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; svayam bhagavān — personally the Lord; krishna — Lord Krishna; yāhāń — where; dhanī — actually opulent; tāhāń — there; vrindāvana-dhāma — Vrindāvana-dhāma.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, full of all opulences, and His complete opulences are exhibited only in Vrindāvana-dhāma.
