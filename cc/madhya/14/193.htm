Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.193

lalita-bhūshita rādhā dekhe yadi krishna

duńhe duńhā milibāre hayena satrishna

SYNONYMS

lalita-bhūshita — decorated with lalita-alańkāra; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; dekhe — sees; yadi — if; krishna — Lord Krishna; duńhe — both of Them; duńhā — the two of Them; milibāre — to meet; hayena — become; sa-trishna — very eager.

TRANSLATION

"When Lord Śrī Krishna happens to see Śrīmatī Rādhārānī decorated with these lalita ornaments, They both eagerly want to meet each other.
