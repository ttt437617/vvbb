Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.55

āpane rathera pāche thele māthā diyā

had had kari, ratha calila dhāiyā

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; rathera pāche — at the back of the car; thele — pushes; māthā diyā — with His head; had had kari — making a rattling sound; ratha — the car; calila — began to move; dhāiyā — running.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then went to the back of the car and began to push with His head. It was then that the car began to move and roll along, making a rattling sound.
