Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.81

śrīvāsa-sahita jala khele gadādhara

rāghava-pandita sane khele vakreśvara

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa-sahita — with Śrīvāsa Thākura; jala khele — performs this water sport; gadādhara — Gadādhara Pandita; rāghava-pandita sane — with Rāghava Pandita; khele — sports; vakreśvara — Vakreśvara Pandita.

TRANSLATION

Another duel took place between Śrīvāsa Thākura and Gadādhara Pandita, and yet another between Rāghava Pandita and Vakreśvara Pandita. Thus they all engaged in throwing water.
