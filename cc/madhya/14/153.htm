Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.153

prākharya, mārdava, sāmya svabhāva nirdosha

sei sei svabhāve krishne karāya santosha

SYNONYMS

prākharya — talkativeness; mārdava — mildness; sāmya — being equipoised; svabhāva — nature; nirdosha — faultless; sei sei svabhāve — in those transcendental qualities; krishne — Lord Krishna; karāya — they make; santosha — happy.

TRANSLATION

"Although some of the gopīs are talkative, some mild and some equipoised, all of them are transcendental and faultless. They please Krishna by their unique characteristics."
