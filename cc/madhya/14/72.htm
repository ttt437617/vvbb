Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.72

kabhu vakreśvare, kabhu āra bhakta-gane

trisandhyā kīrtana kare gundicā-prāńgane

SYNONYMS

kabhu vakreśvare — sometimes Vakreśvara Pandita; kabhu — sometimes; āra bhakta-gane — other devotees; tri-sandhyā — three times (morning, evening and noon); kīrtana kare — performs kīrtana; gundicā-prāńgane — in the yard of the Gundicā temple.

TRANSLATION

Sometimes Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged Vakreśvara and other devotees in chanting and dancing. Three times daily — morning, noon and evening — He would perform sańkīrtana in the yard of the Gundicā temple.
