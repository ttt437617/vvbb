Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.48

tānite nā pāre gauda, ratha chādi' dila

pātra-mitra lañā rājā vyagra hañā āila

SYNONYMS

tānite nā pāre — they could not pull; gauda — the gaudas; ratha chādi' dila — gave up the attempt; pātra-mitra — all the officers and friends; lañā — taking with him; rājā — the King; vyagra — in great anxiety; hañā — being; āila — arrived.

TRANSLATION

When the gaudas saw that they could not budge the car, they abandoned the attempt. Then the King arrived in great anxiety, and he was accompanied by his officers and friends.
