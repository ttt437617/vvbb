Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.251

ei patta-dorīte haya 'śesha'-adhishthāna

daśa-mūrti hañā yeńho seve bhagavān

SYNONYMS

ei patta-dorīte — in this rope; haya — there is; śesha-adhishthāna — the abode of Śesha Nāga; daśa-mūrti hañā — expanding into ten forms; yeńho — who; seve — worships; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then informed Rāmānanda Vasu and Satyarāja Khān that this rope was the abode of Lord Śesha, who expands Himself into ten forms and serves the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

PURPORT

For a description of Śesha Nāga, refer to Ādi-līlā 5.123-124.
