Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.50

vyagra hañā āne rājā matta-hātī-gana

ratha cālāite rathe karila yojana

SYNONYMS

vyagra hañā — with anxiety; āne — brings; rājā — the King; matta-hātī-gana — very strong elephants; ratha cālāite — to make the car move; rathe — to the car; karila yojana — harnessed.

TRANSLATION

Becoming even more anxious to move the car, the King had very strong elephants brought forth and harnessed to it.
