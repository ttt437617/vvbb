Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.69

eka dina nimantrana kare dui-tine mili'

ei-mata mahāprabhura nimantrana-keli

SYNONYMS

eka dina — one day; nimantrana — invitation; kare — make; dui-tine — two or three persons; mili' — combining; ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nimantrana — invitation; keli — pastimes.

TRANSLATION

Since they could not get one day each, two or three devotees combined to extend an invitation. These are the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's acceptance of invitations.
