Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.211

rathera upare kare dandera tādana

cora-prāya kare jagannāthera sevaka-gana

SYNONYMS

rathera upare — on the car; kare — do; dandera tādana — chastisement by sticks; cora-prāya — almost like thieves; kare — they treated; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; sevaka-gana — the personal servants.

TRANSLATION

"All the maidservants began to beat the Ratha car with sticks, and they treated the servants of Lord Jagannātha almost like thieves.
