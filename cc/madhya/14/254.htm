Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.254

tabe jagannātha yāi' vasilā simhāsane

mahāprabhu ghare āilā lañā bhakta-gane

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; yāi' — going; vasilā — sat; simhāsane — on His throne; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ghare — to His residence; āilā — went back; lañā — taking; bhakta-gane — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

Thus Lord Jagannātha returned to His temple and sat on His throne while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence with His devotees.
