Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.47

ihāń jagannāthera ratha-calana-samaya

gauda saba ratha tāne, āge nāhi yāya

SYNONYMS

ihāń — outside the garden; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; ratha-calana-samaya — at the time of drawing the car; gauda — the workers named gaudas who draw the car; saba — all; ratha tāne — pull the car; āge — forward; nāhi yāya — it does not go.

TRANSLATION

Outside the garden, when it was time to pull Jagannātha's car, all the workers called gaudas tried to pull it, but it would not move forward.
