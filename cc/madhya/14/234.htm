Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.234

cāri sampradāya gāna kari' bahu śrānta haila

mahāprabhura premāveśa dviguna bādila

SYNONYMS

cāri sampradāya — four groups of sańkīrtana parties; gāna kari' — after singing; bahu — much; śrānta haila — were fatigued; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-āveśa — the ecstatic love; dvi-guna — twofold; bādila — increased.

TRANSLATION

After much singing, all four sańkīrtana parties grew fatigued, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love increased twofold.
