Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.97

vriksha-vallī praphullita prabhura daraśane

bhrińga-pika gāya, vahe śītala pavane

SYNONYMS

vriksha-vallī — trees and creepers; praphullita — joyful; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; daraśane — by the sight; bhrińga — bumblebees; pika — birds; gāya — chant; vahe — were blowing; śītala — cool; pavane — breezes.

TRANSLATION

There were multifarious trees and creepers in the garden, and they were all jubilant to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Indeed, the birds were chirping, the bees were buzzing, and a cool breeze was blowing.
