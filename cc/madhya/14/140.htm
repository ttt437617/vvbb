Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.140

prabhu kahe, — kaha vrajera mānera prakāra

svarūpa kahe, — gopī-māna-nadī śata-dhāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; kaha — please tell; vrajera — of Vrindāvana; mānera — of the egoistic pride; prakāra — the varieties; svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; gopī-māna — the pride of the gopīs; nadī — like a river; śata-dhāra — with hundreds of branches.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please tell me of the varieties of egoistic pride manifested in Vrindāvana."

Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "The pride of the gopīs is like a river flowing with hundreds of tributaries.
