Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.177

nānā-svādu ashta-bhāva ekatra milana

yāhāra āsvāde tripta haya krishna-mana

SYNONYMS

nānā — various; svādu — palatable; ashta-bhāva — eight kinds of ecstatic symptoms; ekatra — at one place; milana — meeting; yāhāra — of which; āsvāde — by the tasting; tripta — satisfied; haya — is; krishna-mana — the mind of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"There are eight symptoms of ecstatic love on the platform of transcendental jubilation, and when they are combined and tasted by Krishna, the Lord's mind is completely satisfied.
