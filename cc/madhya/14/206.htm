Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.206

eta sampatti chādi' kene gelā vrindāvana

tāńre hāsya karite lakshmī karilā sājana

SYNONYMS

eta sampatti — so much opulence; chādi' — giving up; kene — why; gelā — He went; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; tāńre hāsya karite — to make Him a laughingstock; lakshmī — the goddess of fortune; karilā — made; sājana — so much decoration.

TRANSLATION

"She wondered, 'Why did Lord Jagannātha give up so much opulence and go to Vrindāvana?' To make Him a laughingstock, the goddess of fortune made arrangements for much decoration.
