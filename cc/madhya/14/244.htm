Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.244

āra dine jagannāthera bhitara-vijaya

rathe cadi' jagannātha cale nijālaya

SYNONYMS

āra dine — on the next day; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; bhitara-vijaya — coming out from inside the temple; rathe cadi' — riding on the car; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; cale — returns; nija-ālaya — to His own home.

TRANSLATION

The next day Lord Jagannātha came out from the temple and, riding on the car, returned to His own abode.
