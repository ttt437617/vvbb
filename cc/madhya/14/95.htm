Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.95

āra dina āsi' kaila īśvara daraśana

prāńgane nritya-gīta kaila kata-kshana

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; āsi' — coming; kaila — performed; īśvara daraśana — seeing the Lord; prāńgane — in the yard; nritya-gīta — chanting and dancing; kaila — performed; kata-kshana — for some time.

TRANSLATION

The next day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also went to the temple of Gundicā and saw the Lord. He then chanted and danced in the yard for some time.
