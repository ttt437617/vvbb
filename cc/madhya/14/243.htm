Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.243

udyāne āsiyā kaila vana-bhojana

ei-mata krīdā kaila prabhu ashta-dina

SYNONYMS

udyāne — to the garden; āsiyā — coming; kaila — performed; vana-bhojana — picnic in the forest; ei-mata — in this way; krīdā — pastimes; kaila — performed; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ashta-dina — constantly for eight days.

TRANSLATION

Then, entering the flower garden, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His meal. In this way He continuously performed all kinds of pastimes for eight days.
