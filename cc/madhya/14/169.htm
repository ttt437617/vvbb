Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.169

eta bhāva-bhūshāya bhūshita śrī-rādhāra ańga

dekhite uthale krishna-sukhābdhi-tarańga

SYNONYMS

eta — so many; bhāva-bhūshāya — with the ornaments of ecstasy; bhūshita — decorated; śrī-rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; ańga — the body; dekhite — to see; uthale — awakens; krishna-sukha-abdhi — of the ocean of Krishna's happiness; tarańga — waves.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrīmatī Rādhārānī's body manifests the ornaments of many ecstatic symptoms, the ocean of Krishna's happiness immediately displays transcendental waves.
