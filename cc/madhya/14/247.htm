Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.247

pāndu-vijayera tuli phāti-phuti yāya

jagannāthera bhare tulā udiyā palāya

SYNONYMS

pāndu-vijayera — of the ceremony of Pāndu-vijaya; tuli — batches of cotton; phāti-phuti yāya — become broken; jagannāthera bhare — by the weight of Lord Jagannātha; tulā — the cotton; udiyā palāya — floats into the air.

TRANSLATION

When the Jagannātha Deity is carried, at intervals He is placed on cotton pads. When the ropes broke, the cotton pads also broke due to the weight of Lord Jagannātha, and the cotton floated into the air.
