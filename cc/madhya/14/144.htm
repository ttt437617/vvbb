Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.144

'dhīrā' kānte dūre dekhi' kare pratyutthāna

nikate āsile, kare āsana pradāna

SYNONYMS

dhīrā — the sober; kānte — the hero; dūre — from a distance; dekhi' — after seeing; kare pratyutthāna — stands up; nikate āsile — when the hero comes nearby; kare — does; āsana — of a seat; pradāna — offering.

TRANSLATION

"When a sober heroine sees her hero approaching from a distance, she immediately stands up to receive him. When he comes near, she immediately offers him a place to sit.
