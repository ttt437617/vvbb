Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.190

krishna-āge rādhā yadi rahe dāndāñā

tina-ańga-bhańge rahe bhrū nācāñā

SYNONYMS

krishna-āge — in front of Krishna; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; yadi — if; rahe — remains; dāndāñā — standing; tina-ańga-bhańge — with three bends in the body; rahe — remains; bhrū — eyebrows; nācāñā — dancing.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrīmatī Rādhārānī stands before Krishna, She stands bent in three places — Her neck, waist and legs — and Her eyebrows dance.
