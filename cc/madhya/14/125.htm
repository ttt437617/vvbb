Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.125

gopī-sańge yata līlā haya upavane

nigūdha krishnera bhāva keha nāhi jāne

SYNONYMS

gopī-sańge — with the gopīs; yata līlā — all pastimes; haya upavane — that are in those gardens; nigūdha — very confidential; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; bhāva — ecstasies; keha — anyone; nāhi — does not; jāne — know.

TRANSLATION

"All the pastimes with the gopīs that take place in those gardens are very confidential ecstasies of Lord Krishna. No one knows them.
