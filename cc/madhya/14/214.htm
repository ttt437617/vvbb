Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.214

dugdha āuti' dadhi mathe tomāra gopī-gane

āmāra thākurānī vaise ratna-simhāsane

SYNONYMS

dugdha āuti' — boiling milk; dadhi — into yogurt; mathe — churn; tomāra — your; gopī-gane — gopīs; āmāra — my; thākurānī — mistress; vaise — sits down; ratna-simhāsane — on a throne of gems.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura continued to address Svarūpa Dāmodara: "Your gopīs are engaged in boiling milk and churning it into yogurt, but my mistress, the goddess of fortune, sits on a throne made of jewels and gems."
