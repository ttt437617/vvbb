Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.216

prabhu kahe, — śrīvāsa, tomāte nārada-svabhāva

aiśvarya-bhāve tomāte, īśvara-prabhāva

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; śrīvāsa — My dear Śrīvāsa; tomāte — in you; nārada-svabhāva — the nature of Nārada; aiśvarya-bhāve — the mood of full opulence; tomāte — in you; īśvara-prabhāva — the power of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told Śrīvāsa Thākura, "My dear Śrīvāsa, your nature is exactly like that of Nārada Muni. The Supreme Personality of Godhead's opulence is having a direct influence upon you.
