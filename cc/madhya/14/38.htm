Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.38

kīrtanīyāra pariśrama jāni' gaurarāya

tāń-sabāre khāoyāite prabhura mana dhāya

SYNONYMS

kīrtanīyāra — of all the singers; pariśrama — labor; jāni' — knowing; gaurarāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāń-sabāre — all of them; khāoyāite — to fill; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mana dhāya — the mind was very eager.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu understood the labor of all the kīrtana chanters; therefore He was very eager to feed them sumptuously.
