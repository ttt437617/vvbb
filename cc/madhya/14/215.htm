Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.215

nārada-prakriti śrīvāsa kare parihāsa

śuni' hāse mahāprabhura yata nija-dāsa

SYNONYMS

nārada-prakriti — with the nature of Nārada Muni; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; kare — does; parihāsa — joking; śuni' — hearing; hāse — smile; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yata — all; nija-dāsa — personal servants.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura, who was enjoying the mood of Nārada Muni, thus made jokes. Hearing him, all the personal servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to smile.
