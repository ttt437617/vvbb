Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.162

vāmya-svabhāve māna uthe nirantara

tāra madhye uthe krishnera ānanda-sāgara

SYNONYMS

vāmya-svabhāve — because of left-wing character; māna — womanly anger; uthe — awakens; nirantara — always; tāra madhye — in that dealing; uthe — is awakening; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; ānanda-sāgara — an ocean of transcendental bliss.

TRANSLATION

"Because She is a left-wing gopī, Her womanly anger is always awakening, but Krishna derives transcendental bliss from Her activities.
