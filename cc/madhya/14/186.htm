Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.186

dekhite nānā-bhāva haya vilakshana

se vailakshanyera nāma 'vilāsa'-bhūshana

SYNONYMS

dekhite — while seeing; nānā-bhāva — of various ecstasies; haya — there are; vilakshana — different symptoms; se — those; vailakshanyera — of different symptoms; nāma — the name; vilāsa — vilāsa; bhūshana — ornaments.

TRANSLATION

"The symptoms of various ecstasies that become manifest at that time are called vilāsa.

PURPORT

This is described in the following verse, taken from the Ujjvala-nīlamani (Anubhāva-prakarana 39).
