Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.201

ei-mata āra saba bhāva-vibhūshana

yāhāte bhūshita rādhā hare krishna mana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; āra — also; saba — all; bhāva-vibhūshana — ecstatic ornaments; yāhāte — by which; bhūshita — being decorated; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; hare — attracts; krishna mana — the mind of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is ornamented and decorated with various ecstatic symptoms, which attract the mind of Śrī Krishna.
