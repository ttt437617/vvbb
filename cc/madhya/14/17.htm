Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.17

prabhu bale, — ke tumi, karilā mora hita?

ācambite āsi' piyāo krishna-līlāmrita?

SYNONYMS

prabhu bale — the Lord said; ke tumi — who are you; karilā — you have done; mora — My; hita — welfare; ācambite — all of a sudden; āsi' — coming; piyāo — you make Me drink; krishna-līlā-amrita — the nectar of the pastimes of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Finally Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Who are you? You have done so much for Me. All of a sudden you have come here and made Me drink the nectar of the pastimes of Lord Krishna."
