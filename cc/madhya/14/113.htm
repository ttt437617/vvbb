Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.113

prātah-kāle mahāprabhu nija-gana lañā

jagannātha darśana kaila sundarācale yāñā

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāle — in the morning; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija-gana lañā — taking His associates; jagannātha darśana — visiting Lord Jagannātha; kaila — performed; sundarācale — to the Gundicā temple; yāñā — going.

TRANSLATION

In the morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His personal associates with Him to see Lord Jagannātha at Sundarācala.

PURPORT

Sundarācala is the Gundicā temple. The temple of Jagannātha at Jagannātha Purī is called Nīlācala, and the temple at Gundicā is called Sundarācala.
