Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.146

sarala vyavahāra, kare mānera poshana

kimvā solluntha-vākye kare priya-nirasana

SYNONYMS

sarala vyavahāra — plain dealings; kare — does; mānera — of jealous anger; poshana — maintenance; kimvā — or; solluntha — smiling a little; vākye — by words; kare — does; priya — of the lover; nirasana — rejection.

TRANSLATION

"The sober heroine is very simple in her behavior. She keeps her jealous anger within her heart, but with mild words and smiles she rejects the advances of her lover.
