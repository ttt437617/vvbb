Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.73

vrindāvane āilā krishna — ei prabhura jñāna

krishnera viraha-sphūrti haila avasāna

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; āilā krishna — Krishna arrived; ei prabhura jñāna — this was the consciousness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishnera — from Lord Krishna; viraha-sphūrti — feelings of separation; haila avasāna — ended.

TRANSLATION

At this time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt that Lord Krishna had returned to Vrindāvana. Thinking this, His feelings of separation from Krishna subsided.
