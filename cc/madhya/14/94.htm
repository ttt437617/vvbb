Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.94

aparāhne āsi' kaila darśana, nartana

niśāte udyāne āsi' karilā śayana

SYNONYMS

aparāhne — in the afternoon; āsi' — coming; kaila — performed; darśana nartana — visiting the Lord and dancing; niśāte — at night; udyāne — in the garden; āsi' — coming; karilā śayana — took rest.

TRANSLATION

In the afternoon, Śri Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the Gundicā temple to visit the Lord and dance. At night He went to the garden to take rest.
