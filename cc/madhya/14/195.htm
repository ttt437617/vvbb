Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.195

lobhe āsi' krishna kare kañcukākarshana

antare ullāsa, rādhā kare nivārana

SYNONYMS

lobhe — in greed; āsi' — coming; krishna — Lord Krishna; kare — does; kañcuka-ākarshana — snatching the border of Her sari; antare — within; ullāsa — very pleased; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; kare — does; nivārana — stopping.

TRANSLATION

"When Krishna comes forward and greedily snatches at the border of Her sari, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is actually very pleased within, but still She tries to stop Him.
