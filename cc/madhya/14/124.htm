Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.124

prabhu kahe, — yātrā-chale krishnera gamana

subhadrā āra baladeva, sańge dui jana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; yātrā-chale — on the plea of the car festival; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; gamana — departure; subhadrā — His sister; āra — and; baladeva — His brother; sańge — with Him; dui jana — two persons.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "Using the car festival as an excuse, Krishna goes there with Subhadrā and Baladeva.
