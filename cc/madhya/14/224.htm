Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.224

sahaja lokera kathā — yāhāń divya-gīta

sahaja gamana kare, — yaiche nritya-pratīta

SYNONYMS

sahaja lokera kathā — the talks of all the plain people; yāhāń — where; divya-gīta — transcendental music; sahaja gamana — natural walking; kare — they do; yaiche — like; nritya-pratīta — appearing like dancing.

TRANSLATION

"In Vrindāvana, the natural speech of the people sounds like music, and their natural motion resembles a dance.
