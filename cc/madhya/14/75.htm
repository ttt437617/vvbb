Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.75

nānodyāne bhakta-sańge vrindāvana-līlā

'indradyumna'-sarovare kare jala-khelā

SYNONYMS

nānā-udyāne — in various gardens; bhakta-sańge — with the devotees; vrindāvana-līlā — pastimes of Vrindāvana; indradyumna — Indradyumna; sarovare — in the lake; kare jala-khelā — performed sports in the water.

TRANSLATION

There were many gardens near the Gundicā temple, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees used to perform the pastimes of Vrindāvana in each of them. In the lake named Indradyumna, He sported in the water.
