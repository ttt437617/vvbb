Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.9

śunite śunite prabhura santosha apāra

'bala, bala' bali' prabhu bale bāra bāra

SYNONYMS

śunite śunite — by hearing; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; santosha apāra — great satisfaction; bala bala — go on reciting; bali' — saying; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bale — says; bāra bāra — again and again.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these verses, He was pleased beyond limits, and He said again and again, "Go on reciting, go on reciting."
