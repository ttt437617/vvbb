Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.11

tumi more dile bahu amūlya ratana

mora kichu dite nāhi, diluń ālińgana

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; more — unto Me; dile — delivered; bahu — various; amūlya — incalculable; ratana — gems; mora — of Me; kichu — anything; dite — to give; nāhi — there is not; diluń — I give; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing the verse recited by the King, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You have given Me invaluable gems, but I have nothing to give you in return. Therefore I am simply embracing you."
