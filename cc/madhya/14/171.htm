Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.171

rādhā dekhi' krishna yadi chuńite kare mana

dāna-ghāti-pathe yabe varjena gamana

SYNONYMS

rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; dekhi' — after seeing; krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — if; chuńite — to touch; kare mana — desires; dāna-ghāti-pathe — on the way leading toward the spot from which one crosses the river to the other side; yabe — when; varjena — prohibits; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrī Krishna sees Śrīmatī Rādhārānī and wants to touch Her body, He prohibits Her from going to the spot where one can cross the river Yamunā.
