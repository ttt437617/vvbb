Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.233

lakshmī-devī yathā-kāle gelā nija-ghara

prabhu nritya kare, haila tritīya prahara

SYNONYMS

lakshmī-devī — the goddess of fortune; yathā-kāle — in due course of time; gelā — returned; nija-ghara — to her apartment; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nritya kare — dances; haila — there arrived; tritīya prahara — the third period of the day, the afternoon.

TRANSLATION

Finally the goddess of fortune returned to her apartment. In due course of time, as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was dancing, afternoon arrived.
