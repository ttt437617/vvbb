Madhya-līlāChapter 14: Performance of the Vrindāvana Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 14.39

pāńti pāńti kari' bhakta-gane vasāilā

pariveśana karibāre āpane lāgilā

SYNONYMS

pāńti pāńti kari' — in different lines; bhakta-gane — all the devotees; vasāilā — made seated; pariveśana — distribution; karibāre — to do; āpane — personally; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees sat down in lines, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally began to distribute the prasādam.
