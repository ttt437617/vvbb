Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.260

tabe sanātana-gosāñira punar-āgamana

jyaishtha-māse prabhu tāńre kaila parīkshana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sanātana-gosāñira — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; punah-āgamana — again coming; jyaishtha-māse — in the month of Jyaishtha (May-June); prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — him; kaila — did; parīkshana — examination.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter Sanātana Gosvāmī met the Lord again, and the Lord tested him in scorching heat during the month of Jyaishtha.
