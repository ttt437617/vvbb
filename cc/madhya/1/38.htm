Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.38

rasāmrita-sindhu, āra vidagdha-mādhava

ujjvala-nīlamani, āra lalita-mādhava

SYNONYMS

rasāmrita-sindhu — the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu; āra — and; vidagdha-mādhava — the Vidagdha-mādhava; ujjvala-nīlamani — the Ujjvala-nīlamani; āra — and; lalita-mādhava — the Lalita-mādhava.

TRANSLATION

The books compiled by Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī include the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu, Vidagdha-mādhava, Ujjvala-nīlamani and Lalita-mādhava.
