Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.36

ei saba grantha kaila gosāñi sanātana

rūpa-gosāñi kaila yata, ke karu ganana

SYNONYMS

ei saba — all these; grantha — scriptures; kaila — compiled; gosāñi sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; kaila — did; yata — all; ke — who; karu ganana — can count.

TRANSLATION

We have already given the names of four books compiled by Sanātana Gosvāmī. Similarly, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī has also compiled many books, which no one can even count.
