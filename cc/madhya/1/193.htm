Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.193

brāhmana-jāti tārā, navadvīpe ghara

nīca-sevā nāhi kare, nahe nīcera kūrpara

SYNONYMS

brāhmana-jāti — born in a brāhmana family; tārā — they; navadvīpe — the holy place of Navadvīpa-dhāma; ghara — their house; nīca-sevā — service to degraded persons; nāhi — not; kare — do; nahe — not; nīcera — of low persons; kūrpara — an instrument.

TRANSLATION

"The brothers Jagāi and Mādhāi belonged to the brāhmana caste, and their residence was in the holy place of Navadvīpa. They never served low-class persons, nor were they instruments to abominable activities.
