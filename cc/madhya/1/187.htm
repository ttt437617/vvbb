Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.187

uthi' dui bhāi tabe dante trina dhari'

dainya kari' stuti kare karayoda kari

SYNONYMS

uthi' — standing up; dui — two; bhāi — brothers; tabe — then; dante — in the teeth; trina — straw; dhari' — holding; dainya kari' — in all humbleness; stuti kare — offer prayers; kara-yoda — folded hands; kari' — making.

TRANSLATION

The two brothers got up, and again taking straw between their teeth, they humbly offered their prayers with folded hands.
