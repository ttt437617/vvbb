Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.273

jaya jaya mahāprabhu — vrajendra-kumāra

jagat tārite prabhu, tomāra avatāra

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya mahāprabhu — all glories to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vrajendra-kumāra — originally Lord Krishna, the son of Mahārāja Nanda; jagat — the whole world; tārite — to deliver; prabhu — the Lord; tomāra — Your; avatāra — incarnation.

TRANSLATION

All the people began to call very loudly, "All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is the son of Mahārāja Nanda! Now You have appeared in order to deliver the whole world!
