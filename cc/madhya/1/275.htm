Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.275

śuniyā lokera dainya dravilā hridaya

bāhire āsi' daraśana dilā dayā-maya

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; lokera — of the people; dainya — humility; dravilā — became softened; hridaya — the heart; bāhire — outside; āsi' — coming; daraśana — audience; dilā — gave; dayā-maya — the merciful.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord heard the humble petition made by the people, His heart softened. Being very merciful, He immediately came out and gave audience to all of them.
