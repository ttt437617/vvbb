Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.173

yavane tomāra thāñi karaye lāgāni

tāńra himsāya lābha nāhi, haya āra hāni

SYNONYMS

yavane — your Muslim servant; tomāra — your; thāñi — place; karaye — does; lāgāni — instigation; tāńra — of Him; himsāya — to become jealous; lābha nāhi — there is no profit; haya — there is; āra — rather; hāni — loss.

TRANSLATION

Keśava Chatrī said, "Out of jealousy your Muslim servant plots against Him. I think that you should not be very interested in Him, for there is no profit in it. Rather, there is simply loss."
