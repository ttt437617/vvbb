Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.269

eka-dina śrīvāsādi yata bhakta-gana

mahāprabhura guna gāñā karena kīrtana

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; śrīvāsa-ādi — Śrīvāsa Thākura and others; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees; mahāprabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; guna — qualities; gāñā — describing; karena — perform; kīrtana — chanting.

TRANSLATION

One day all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura, were chanting the transcendental qualities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
