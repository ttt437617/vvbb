Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.172

bhikhārī sannyāsī kare tīrtha paryatana

tāńre dekhibāre āise dui cāri jana

SYNONYMS

bhikhāri — beggar; sannyāsī — mendicant; kare — does; tīrtha — of holy places; paryatana — touring; tāńre — Him; dekhibāre — to see; āise — come; dui cāri jana — only a few people.

TRANSLATION

Keśava Chatrī informed the Muslim King that Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a mendicant touring different places of pilgrimage and that, as such, only a few people came to see Him.
