Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.217

dońhā ālińgiyā prabhu balila bhakta-gane

sabe kripā kari' uddhāraha dui jane

SYNONYMS

dońhā — both of them; ālińgiyā — embracing; prabhu — the Lord; balila — said; bhakta-gane — unto the devotees; sabe — all of you; kripā — mercy; kari' — showing; uddhāraha — deliver; dui — the two; jane — persons.

TRANSLATION

After this, the Lord embraced both of them and requested all of the devotees present to be merciful upon them and deliver them.
