Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.223

tathāpi yavana jāti, nā kari pratīti

tīrtha-yātrāya eta sańghatta bhāla nahe rīti

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; yavana jāti — by caste a Muslim; nā — does not; kari — do; pratīti — confidence; tīrtha-yātrāya — in going for a pilgrimage; eta — so; sańghatta — crowd; bhāla — good; nahe — not; rīti — etiquette.

TRANSLATION

"Although the King is respectful toward You, he still belongs to the yavana class and should not be believed. We think that there is no need for such a great crowd to accompany You on Your pilgrimage to Vrindāvana.
