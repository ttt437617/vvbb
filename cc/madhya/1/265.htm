Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.265

gopīnātha pattanāyaka — rāmānanda-bhrātā

rājā māritechila, prabhu haila trātā

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha pattanāyaka — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; rāmānanda-bhrātā — the brother of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; rājā — the King; māritechila — condemned to death; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haila — became; trātā — the deliverer.

TRANSLATION

After this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu saved Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka, the younger brother of Rāmānanda Rāya, from being condemned to death by the King.
