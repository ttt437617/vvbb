Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.240

līlā-sthala dekhi' preme ha-ilā asthira

balabhadra kaila tāńre mathurāra bāhira

SYNONYMS

līlā-sthala — all the holy places of Lord Krishna's pastimes; dekhi' — visiting; preme — in great ecstasy; ha-ilā — became; asthira — agitated; balabhadra — Balabhadra; kaila — assisted; tāńre — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mathurāra — of the town of Mathurā; bāhira — outside.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited all twelve places of Śrī Krishna's pastimes, He became very much agitated because of ecstasy. Balabhadra Bhattācārya somehow or other got Him out of Mathurā.
