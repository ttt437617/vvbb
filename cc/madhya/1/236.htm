Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.236

balabhadra bhattācārya, āra pandita dāmodara

dui-jana-sańge prabhu āilā nīlācala

SYNONYMS

balabhadra bhattācārya — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; āra — and; pandita dāmodara — Dāmodara Pandita; dui-jana — two persons; sańge — with; prabhu — the Lord; āilā — went back; nīlācala — to Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

The two persons who accompanied Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Jagannātha Purī [Nīlācala] were Balabhadra Bhattācārya and Dāmodara Pandita.
