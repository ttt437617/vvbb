Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.102

tabe ta' karilā prabhu dakshina gamana

kūrma-kshetre kaila vāsudeva vimocana

SYNONYMS

tabe ta' — thereafter; karilā — did; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dakshina — to southern India; gamana — traveling; kūrma-kshetre — at the pilgrimage site known as Kūrma-kshetra; kaila — did; vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; vimocana — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

After bestowing mercy upon Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, the Lord started for southern India. When He came to Kūrma-kshetra, He delivered a person named Vāsudeva.
