Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.40

dāna-keli-kaumudī, āra bahu stavāvalī

ashtādaśa līlā-cchanda, āra padyāvalī

govinda-virudāvalī, tāhāra lakshana

mathurā-māhātmya, āra nātaka-varnana

SYNONYMS

dāna-keli-kaumudī — the Dāna-keli-kaumudī; āra — and; bahu stavāvalī — many prayers; ashtādaśa — eighteen; līlā-cchanda — chronological pastimes; āra — and; padyāvalī — the Padyāvalī; govinda-virudāvalī — the Govinda-virudāvalī; tāhāra lakshana — the symptoms of the book; mathurā-māhātmya — the glories of Mathurā; āra nātaka-varnana — and descriptions of drama (Nātaka-candrikā).

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī also compiled the Dāna-keli-kaumudī, Stavāvalī, Līlā-cchanda, Padyāvalī, Govinda-virudāvalī, Mathurā-māhātmya and Nātaka-varnana.
