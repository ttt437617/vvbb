Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.241

gańgā-tīra-pathe lañā prayāge āilā

śrī-rūpa āsi' prabhuke tathāi mililā

SYNONYMS

gańgā-tīra-pathe — the path on the bank of the Ganges; lañā — taking; prayāge — in Allahabad; āilā — arrived; śrī-rūpa — Śrī Rūpa; āsi' — coming there; prabhuke — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tathāi — there; mililā — met.

TRANSLATION

After leaving Mathurā, the Lord began to walk along the path on the bank of the Ganges, and finally He reached the holy place named Prayāga [Allahabad]. It was there that Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī came and met the Lord.
