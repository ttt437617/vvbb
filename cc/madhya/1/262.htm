Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.262

nityānanda-sańge yukti kariyā nibhrite

tāńre pāthāilā gaude prema pracārite

SYNONYMS

nityānanda-sańge — with Nityānanda Prabhu; yukti — discussion; kariyā — making; nibhrite — in privacy; tāńre — Him; pāthāilā — sent; gaude — to Bengal; prema — love of Godhead; pracārite — to preach.

TRANSLATION

After sending Sanātana Gosvāmī back to Vrindāvana, the Lord privately consulted with Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu. He then sent Him to Bengal to preach love of Godhead.
