Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.111

cāturmāsya-ante punah dakshina gamana

paramānanda-purī saha tāhāñi milana

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsya-ante — at the end of Cāturmāsya; punah — again; dakshina gamana — traveling in South India; paramānanda-purī — Paramānanda Purī; saha — with; tāhāñi — there; milana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

After the end of Cāturmāsya, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued traveling throughout South India. At that time He met Paramānanda Purī.
