Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.80

sei bhāva, sei krishna, sei vrindāvana

yabe pāi, tabe haya vāñchita pūrana

SYNONYMS

sei bhāva — that circumstance; sei krishna — that Krishna; sei vrindāvana — that Vrindāvana; yabe pāi — if I get; tabe — then; haya — is; vāñchita — desired object; pūrana — fulfilled.

TRANSLATION

Thus meeting with Krishna and thinking of the Vrindāvana atmosphere, Rādhārānī longed for Krishna to take Her to Vrindāvana again to fulfill Her desire in that calm atmosphere.
