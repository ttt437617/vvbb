Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.110

cāturmāsya tāńhā prabhu śrī-vaishnavera sane

gońāila nritya-gīta-krishna-sańkīrtane

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsya — observance of the four months of the rainy season; tāńhā — there; prabhu — the Lord; śrī-vaishnavera sane — with the Śrī Vaishnavas; gońāila — passed; nritya — dancing; gīta — singing; krishna-sańkīrtane — in chanting the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed the Cāturmāsya months with the Śrī Vaishnavas, singing, chanting the holy name and dancing.
