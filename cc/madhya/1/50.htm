Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.50

vimśati vatsara aiche kailā gatāgati

anyonye duńhāra duńhā vinā nāhi sthiti

SYNONYMS

vimśati — twenty; vatsara — years; aiche — thus; kailā — did; gata-āgati — going and coming; anyonye — mutually; duńhāra — of Lord Caitanya and the devotees; duńhā — the two; vinā — without; nāhi — there is not; sthiti — peace.

TRANSLATION

For twenty consecutive years this meeting took place, and the situation became so intense that the Lord and the devotees could not be happy without meeting one another.
