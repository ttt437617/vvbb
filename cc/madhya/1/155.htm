Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.155

vrindāvana yābena prabhu śuni' nrisimhānanda

patha sājāila mane pāiyā ānanda

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; yābena — will go; prabhu — the Lord; śuni' — hearing; nrisimhānanda — Nrisimhānanda; patha — the way; sājāila — decorated; mane — within the mind; pāiyā — getting; ānanda — pleasure.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī heard that Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go to Vrindāvana, he became very pleased and mentally began decorating the way there.
