Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.202

more dayā kari' kara sva-dayā saphala

akhila brahmānda dekhuka tomāra dayā-bala

SYNONYMS

more — to us; dayā — mercy; kari' — showing; kara — make; sva-dayā — Your own mercy; sa-phala — successful; akhila — throughout; brahmānda — the universe; dekhuka — let it be seen; tomāra — Your; dayā-bala — power of mercy.

TRANSLATION

"We are the most fallen; therefore by showing us Your mercy, Your mercy is most successful. Let the power of Your mercy be exhibited throughout the entire universe!
