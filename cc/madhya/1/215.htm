Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.215

janme janme tumi dui — kińkara āmāra

acirāte krishna tomāya karibe uddhāra

SYNONYMS

janme janme — birth after birth; tumi — you; dui — two; kińkara — servants; āmāra — My; acirāte — very soon; krishna — Lord Krishna; tomāya — of both of you; karibe — will do; uddhāra — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"Birth after birth you have been My eternal servants. I am sure that Krishna will deliver you very soon."
