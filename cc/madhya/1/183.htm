Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.183

ardha-rātre dui bhāi āilā prabhu-sthāne

prathame mililā nityānanda-haridāsa sane

SYNONYMS

ardha-rātre — in the dead of night; dui bhāi — the two brothers; āilā — came; prabhu-sthāne — to the place of Lord Caitanya; prathame — first; mililā — met; nityānanda-haridāsa — Lord Nityānanda and Haridāsa Thākura; sane — with.

TRANSLATION

Thus in the dead of night the two brothers, Dabira Khāsa and Sākara Mallika, went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu incognito. First they met Nityānanda Prabhu and Haridāsa Thākura.
