Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.65

ei tina madhye yabe thāke yei jana

tāńre āsi' āpane mile, — prabhura niyama

SYNONYMS

ei tina madhye — of these three; yabe — when; thāke — remains; yei jana — that person who; tāńre — to him; āsi' — coming; āpane mile — personally meets; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; niyama — regular practice.

TRANSLATION

If one of these three was not present, He would meet the others. That was His regular practice.
