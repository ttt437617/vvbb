Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.146

krishna-janma-yātrāte prabhu gopa-veśa hailā

dadhi-bhāra vahi' tabe laguda phirāilā

SYNONYMS

krishna-janma-yātrāte — on the birthday ceremony of Lord Krishna; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gopa-veśa — dressed like a cowherd boy; hailā — was; dadhi-bhāra — a balance for pots of yogurt; vahi' — carrying; tabe — at that time; laguda — a rod; phirāilā — wheeled about.

TRANSLATION

On Janmāshtamī, Lord Krishna's birthday, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dressed Himself as a cowherd boy. At that time He carried a balance with pots of yogurt and wheeled a rod about.
