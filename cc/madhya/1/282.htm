Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.282

eta bali' loke kari' śubha-drishti dāna

abhyantare gelā, lokera pūrna haila kāma

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — thus saying; loke — unto the people; kari' — doing; śubha-drishti — auspicious glance; dāna — charity; abhyantare — within the room; gelā — went; lokera — of all the people; pūrna — fulfilled; haila — was; kāma — the desire.

TRANSLATION

Thus speaking, the Lord entered His room after glancing auspiciously upon the people out of charity. In this way the desires of the people were completely fulfilled.
