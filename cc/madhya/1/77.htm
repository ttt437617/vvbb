Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.77

ei ślokera sańkshepārtha śuna, bhakta-gana

jagannātha dekhi' yaiche prabhura bhāvana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; ślokera — of the verse; sańkshepa-artha — a brief explanation; śuna — hear; bhakta-gana — O devotees; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; dekhi' — after seeing; yaiche — just as; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya; bhāvana — the thinking.

TRANSLATION

Now, O devotees, please hear a brief explanation of this verse. Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu was thinking in this way after having seen the Jagannātha Deity.
