Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.249

vrindāvana haite yadi nīlācale āilā

āthāra varsha tāhāń vāsa, kāhāń nāhi gelā

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana haite — from Vrindāvana; yadi — though; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āilā — came back; āthāra — eighteen; varsha — years; tāhāń — at Jagannātha Purī; vāsa — residence; kāhāń — anywhere; nāhi — not; gelā — went.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord returned to Jagannātha Purī from Vrindāvana, He remained there and did not go anywhere else for eighteen years.
