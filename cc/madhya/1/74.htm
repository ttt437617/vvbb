Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.74

yogya pātra haya gūdha-rasa-vivecane

tumio kahio tāre gūdha-rasākhyāne

SYNONYMS

yogya — suitable; pātra — recipient; haya — is; gūdha — confidential; rasa — the mellows; vivecane — in analyzing; tumio — you also; kahio — instruct; tāre — him; gūdha — confidential; rasa — the mellows; ākhyāne — in describing.

TRANSLATION

"I accept Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī as quite fit to understand the confidential mellows of devotional service, and I recommend that you explain devotional service to him further."
