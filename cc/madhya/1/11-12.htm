Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.11-12

tāra madhye yei bhāga dāsa-vrindāvana

'caitanya-mańgale' vistāri' karilā varnana

sei bhāgera ihāń sūtra-mātra likhiba

tāhāń ye viśesha kichu, ihāń vistāriba

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — amongst them; yei — which; bhāga — portion; dāsa-vrindāvana — Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; caitanya-mańgale — in his book Caitanya-mańgala; vistāri' — elaborating; karilā varnana — has described; sei bhāgera — of that portion; ihāń — here in this book; sūtra-mātra — the synopsis only; likhiba — I shall write; tāhāń — there; ye — whatever; viśesha — special details; kichu — something; ihāń vistāriba — I shall describe elaborately.

TRANSLATION

I shall describe only in synopsis that portion which Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has described very elaborately in his book Caitanya-mańgala. Whatever incidents are outstanding, however, I shall later elaborate.
