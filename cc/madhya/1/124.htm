Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.124

nityānanda-sārvabhauma āgraha kariñā

nīlācale āilā mahāprabhuke la-iñā

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; āgraha kariñā — showing great eagerness; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āilā — returned; mahāprabhuke — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; la-iñā — taking.

TRANSLATION

When the devotees from Bengal arrived at Jagannātha Purī, both Nityānanda Prabhu and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya greatly endeavored to take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu back to Jagannātha Purī.
