Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.185

dui guccha trina duńhe daśane dhariñā

gale vastra bāndhi' pade dandavat hañā

SYNONYMS

dui — two; guccha — bunches; trina — of straw; duńhe — both of them; daśane — in the teeth; dhariñā — catching; gale — on the neck; vastra — cloth; bāndhi' — binding; pade — fall; dandavat — like rods; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

In great humility, both brothers took bunches of straw between their teeth and, each binding a cloth around his neck, fell down like rods before the Lord.
