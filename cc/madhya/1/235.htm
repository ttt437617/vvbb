Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.235

janā dui sańge āmi yāba nīlācale

āmāre milibā āsi' ratha-yātrā-kāle

SYNONYMS

janā — persons; dui — two; sańge — with; āmi — I; yāba — shall go; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āmāre — Me; milibā — will meet; āsi' — coming there; ratha-yātrā-kāle — during the time of the car festival.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, although requesting all the devotees to go back, allowed two people to follow Him. He requested all the devotees to come to Jagannātha Purī and meet Him during the car festival.
