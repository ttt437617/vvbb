Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.142

prabhure mililā sarva vaishnava āsiyā

jala-krīdā kaila prabhu sabāre la-iyā

SYNONYMS

prabhure — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mililā — met; sarva — all; vaishnava — devotees; āsiyā — arriving at Jagannātha Purī; jala-krīdā — sporting in the water; kaila — performed; prabhu — the Lord; sabāre — all the devotees; la-iyā — taking.

TRANSLATION

After arriving at Jagannātha Purī, all the Vaishnavas met with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Later, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sported in the water, taking all the devotees with Him.
