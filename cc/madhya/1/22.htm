Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.22

ashtādaśa-varsha kevala nīlācale sthiti

āpani ācari' jīve śikhāilā bhakti

SYNONYMS

ashtādaśa-varsha — for eighteen years; kevala — only; nīlācale — in Jagannātha Purī; sthiti — staying; āpani — personally; ācari' — behaving; jīve — unto the living entities; śikhāilā — instructed; bhakti — devotional service.

TRANSLATION

For eighteen continuous years, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at Jagannātha Purī and, through His personal behavior, instructed all living entities in the mode of devotional service.
