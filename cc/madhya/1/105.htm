Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.105

trimalla-tripadī-sthāna kaila daraśana

sarvatra karila krishna-nāma pracārana

SYNONYMS

trimalla — a place named Trimalla, or Tirumala; tripadī — and Tripadī, or Tirupati; sthāna — the places; kaila — did; daraśana — visit; sarvatra — everywhere: karila — did; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; pracārana — preaching.

TRANSLATION

He visited the places known as Tirumala and Tirupati, where He extensively preached the chanting of the Lord's holy name.

PURPORT

This holy place is situated in the district of Tanjore (Chittoor), South India. The temple of Tirupati is situated in the valley of Vyeńkatācala and contains a Deity of Lord Rāmacandra. On top of Vyeńkatācala is the famous temple of Bālājī.
