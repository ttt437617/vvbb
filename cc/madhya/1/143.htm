Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.143

sabā lañā kaila gundicā-griha-sammārjana

ratha-yātrā-daraśane prabhura nartana

SYNONYMS

sabā lañā — taking all of them; kaila — performed; gundicā-griha-sammārjana — washing of the Gundicā temple; ratha-yātrā — the car festival; daraśane — in seeing; prabhura — of the Lord; nartana — dancing.

TRANSLATION

First the Lord washed the temple of Gundicā very thoroughly. Then everyone saw the Ratha-yātrā festival and the Lord's dancing before the car.
