Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.212

gauda-nikata āsite nāhi mora prayojana

tomā-duńhā dekhite mora ihāń āgamana

SYNONYMS

gauda-nikata — to Bengal; āsite — to come; nāhi — there was none; mora — My; prayojana — necessity; tomā — you; duńhā — two; dekhite — to see; mora — My; ihāń — here; āgamana — coming.

TRANSLATION

"I really had no business in coming to Bengal, but I have come just to see you two brothers.
