Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.5

śrīmān rāsa-rasārambhī

vamśī-vata-tata-sthitah

karshan venu-svanair gopīr

gopīnāthah śriye 'stu nah

SYNONYMS

śrīmān — the most beautiful form; rāsa — of the rāsa dance; rasa-ārambhī — the initiator of the mellow; vamśī-vata — the celebrated place named Vamśīvata; tata — on the bank of Yamunā; sthitah — being situated; karshan — attracting; venu-svanaih — by the sounds of the flute; gopīh — all the gopīs; gopī-nāthah — the master of all the gopīs; śriye — the opulence of love and affection; astu — let there be; nah — upon us.

TRANSLATION

May Gopīnāthajī, who attracts all the gopīs with the song of His flute and who has begun the most melodious rāsa dace on the bank of the Yamunā in Vamśīvata, be merciful upon us.
