Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.103

jiyada-nrisimhe kaila nrisimha-stavana

pathe-pathe grāme-grāme nāma-pravartana

SYNONYMS

jiyada-nrisimhe — the place of pilgrimage known as Jiyada-nrisimha; kaila — did; nrisimha — to Nrisimha; stavana — praying; pathe-pathe — on the way; grāme-grāme — every village; nāma-pravartana — introduction of the holy name of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

After visiting Kūrma-kshetra, the Lord visited the South Indian temple of Jiyada-nrisimha and offered His prayers to Lord Nrisimhadeva. On His way, He introduced the chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra in every village.
