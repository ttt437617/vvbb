Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.78

śrī-rādhikā kurukshetre krishnera daraśana

yadyapi pāyena, tabu bhāvena aichana

SYNONYMS

śrī-rādhikā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; kuru-kshetre — on the field of Kurukshetra; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; daraśana — meeting; yadyapi — although; pāyena — She gets; tabu — still; bhāvena — thinks; aichana — in this way.

TRANSLATION

The subject of His thoughts was Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, who met Krishna on the field of Kurukshetra. Although She met Krishna there, She was nonetheless thinking of Him in the following way.
