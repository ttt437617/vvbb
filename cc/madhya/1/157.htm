Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.157

pathe dui dike pushpa-bakulera śrenī

madhye madhye dui-pāśe divya pushkarinī

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the road; dui dike — on both sides; pushpa-bakulera — of bakula flower trees; śrenī — rows; madhye madhye — in the middle; dui-pāśe — on both sides; divya — transcendental; pushkarinī — lakes.

TRANSLATION

He mentally decorated both sides of the road with bakula flower trees, and at intervals on both sides he placed lakes of a transcendental nature.
