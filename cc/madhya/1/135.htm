Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.135

pratāparudrere kripā kaila sei sthāne

gaudīyā-bhakte ājñā dila vidāyera dine

SYNONYMS

pratāparudrere — unto King Pratāparudra; kripā — mercy; kaila — did; sei sthāne — in that garden; gaudīyā-bhakte — to all the devotees of Bengal; ājñā — the order; dila — gave; vidāyera — of departure; dine — on the day.

TRANSLATION

In that garden, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed His mercy upon King Pratāparudra. Afterwards, when the Bengali devotees were about to return home, the Lord gave separate orders to almost every one of them.
