Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.156

kuliyā nagara haite patha ratne bāndhāila

nivrinta pushpa-śayyā upare pātila

SYNONYMS

kuliyā nagara — the city of Kuliyā; haite — from; patha — way; ratne — with jewels; bāndhāila — constructed; nivrinta — stemless; pushpa-śayyā — flower bed; upare — on top; pātila — laid down.

TRANSLATION

First Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī contemplated a broad road starting from the city of Kuliyā. He bedecked the road with jewels, upon which he then laid a bed of stemless flowers.
