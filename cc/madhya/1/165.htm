Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.165

yāhāń yāhāń prabhura carana padaye calite

se mrittikā laya loka, garta haya pathe

SYNONYMS

yāhāń yāhāń — wherever; prabhura — of the Lord; carana — lotus feet; padaye — touch; calite — while walking; se — that; mrittikā — dirt; laya — take; loka — the people; garta — a hole; haya — there becomes; pathe — on the road.

TRANSLATION

Wherever the Lord touched the ground with His lotus feet, people immediately came and gathered the dirt. Indeed, they gathered so much that many holes were created in the road.
