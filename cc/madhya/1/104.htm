Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.104

godāvarī-tīra-vane vrindāvana-bhrama

rāmānanda rāya saha tāhāñi milana

SYNONYMS

godāvarī-tīra — on the bank of the river Godāvarī; vane — in the forest; vrindāvana-bhrama — mistook as Vrindāvana; rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; saha — with; tāhāñi — there; milana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

Once the Lord mistook the forest on the bank of the river Godāvarī to be Vrindāvana. In that place He happened to meet Rāmānanda Rāya.
