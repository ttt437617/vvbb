Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.62

śloka rākhi' gelā samudra-snāna karite

hena-kāle āilā prabhu tāńhāre milite

SYNONYMS

śloka rākhi' — keeping the verse in that way; gelā — went; samudra-snāna — a bath in the sea; karite — to take; hena-kāle — in the meantime; āilā — came; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńhāre — him; milite — to meet.

TRANSLATION

After composing this verse and putting it on the roof of his house, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī went to bathe in the sea. In the meantime, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to his hut to meet him.
