Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.179

tomāra citte caitanyere kaiche haya jñāna

tomāra citte yei laya, sei ta' pramāna

SYNONYMS

tomāra citte — in your mind; caitanyere — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaiche — how; haya — there is; jñāna — knowledge; tomāra — your; citte — mind; yei — whatever; laya — takes; sei ta' pramāna — that is evidence.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī informed the King about his mind as a way of knowing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He assured the King that whatever occurred in his mind could be considered evidence.
