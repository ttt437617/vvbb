Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.233

śacī-devī āni' tāńre kaila namaskāra

sāta dina tāńra thāñi bhikshā-vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

śacī-devī — mother Śacīdevī; āni' — calling her; tāńre — unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — did; namaskāra — obeisances; sāta dina — seven days; tāńra thāñi — from Śacīdevī; bhikshā-vyavahāra — accepting meals.

TRANSLATION

Taking this opportunity, Śrī Advaita Ācārya Prabhu sent for mother Śacīdevī, and she remained at His house for seven days to prepare the meals for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
