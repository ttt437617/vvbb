Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.13

caitanya-līlāra vyāsa — dāsa vrindāvana

tāńra ājñāya karoń tāńra ucchishta carvana

SYNONYMS

caitanya-līlāra vyāsa — the Vyāsadeva, or compiler of the pastimes, of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dāsa vrindāvana — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; tāńra — of him; ājñāya — upon the order; karoń — I do; tāńra — his; ucchishta — of the remnants of food; carvana — chewing.

TRANSLATION

Actually the authorized compiler of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa, the incarnation of Vyāsadeva. Only upon his orders am I trying to chew the remnants of food that he has left.
