Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.226

eta bali' carana vandi' gelā dui-jana

prabhura sei grāma haite calite haila mana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; carana vandi' — offering prayers to the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; gelā — went back; dui-jana — the two brothers; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sei — that; grāma — village; haite — from; calite — to go; haila — there was; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

Having spoken thus, the two brothers offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord and returned to their homes. Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu then desired to leave that village.
