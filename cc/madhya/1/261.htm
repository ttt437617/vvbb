Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.261

tushta hañā prabhu tāńre pāthāilā vrindāvana

advaitera haste prabhura adbhuta bhojana

SYNONYMS

tushta hañā — being very pleased; prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — him; pāthāilā — sent back; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; advaitera — of Advaita Ācārya; haste — in the hands; prabhura — of the Lord; adbhuta — wonderful; bhojana — feasting.

TRANSLATION

Being pleased, the Lord sent Sanātana Gosvāmī back to Vrindāvana. After that, He was fed wonderfully by the hands of Śrī Advaita Ācārya.
