Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.180

rājā kahe, śuna, mora mane yei laya

sākshāt īśvara ihań nāhika samśaya

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King replied; śuna — hear; mora — my; mane — mind; yei — what; laya — takes; sākshāt — personally; īśvara — the Supreme Personality; ihań — He; nāhika — there is not; samśaya — doubt.

TRANSLATION

The King replied, "I consider Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to be the Supreme Personality of Godhead. There is no doubt about it."
