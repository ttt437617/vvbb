Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.225

yadyapi vastutah prabhura kichu nāhi bhaya

tathāpi laukika-līlā, loka-ceshtā-maya

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; vastutah — in fact; prabhura — of the Lord; kichu — any; nāhi — there is not; bhaya — fear; tathāpi — still; laukika-līlā — general pastimes; loka-ceshtā-maya — consisting of popular behavior.

TRANSLATION

Although Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was Śrī Krishna Himself, the Supreme Lord, and was therefore not at all fearful, He still acted like a human being to teach neophytes how to act.
