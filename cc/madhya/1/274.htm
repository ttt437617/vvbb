Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.274

bahu-dūra haite āinu hañā bada ārta

daraśana diyā prabhu karaha kritārtha

SYNONYMS

bahu-dūra — a long distance; haite — from; āinu — we have come; hañā — becoming; bada — very much; ārta — aggrieved; daraśana — audience; diyā — giving; prabhu — O Lord; karaha — kindly show; krita-artha — favor.

TRANSLATION

"O Lord, we are very unhappy. We have come a long distance to see You. Please be merciful and show us Your favor."
