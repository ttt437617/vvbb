Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.121

punarapi nīlācale gamana karila

bhakta-gane meliyā snāna-yātrā dekhila

SYNONYMS

punarapi — again; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; gamana — going back; karila — did; bhakta-gane — all the devotees; meliyā — meeting; snāna-yātrā — the bathing ceremony of Lord Jagannātha; dekhila — saw.

TRANSLATION

After collecting these books, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī. At that time, the bathing ceremony of Jagannātha was taking place, and He saw it.
