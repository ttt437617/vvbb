Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.15

cabbiśa vatsara prabhura grihe avasthāna

tāhāń ye karilā līlā — 'ādi-līlā' nāma

SYNONYMS

cabbiśa vatsara — for twenty-four years; prabhura — of the Lord; grihe — at home; avasthāna — residing; tāhāń — there; ye — whatever; karilā — He performed; līlā — pastimes; ādi-līlā nāma — are called ādi-līlā.

TRANSLATION

For twenty-four years, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at home, and whatever pastimes He performed during that time are called the ādi-līlā.
