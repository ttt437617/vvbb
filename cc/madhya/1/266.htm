Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.266

rāmacandra-purī-bhaye bhikshā ghātāilā

vaishnavera duhkha dekhi' ardheka rākhilā

SYNONYMS

rāmacandra-purī-bhaye — due to fear of Rāmacandra Purī; bhikshā — the proportion of eating; ghātāilā — decreased; vaishnavera — of all the Vaishnavas; duhkha — unhappiness; dekhi' — understanding; ardheka — half of the portion; rākhilā — kept.

TRANSLATION

Rāmacandra Purī criticized Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's eating; therefore the Lord reduced His eating to a minimum. However, when all the Vaishnavas became very sorry, the Lord increased His portion to half as much as usual.
