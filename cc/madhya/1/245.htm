Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.245

mathurā pāthāilā tāńre diyā bhakti-bala

sannyāsīre kripā kari' gelā nīlācala

SYNONYMS

mathurā — to Mathurā; pāthāilā — sent; tāńre — him; diyā — giving; bhakti-bala — the strength of devotion; sannyāsīre — unto the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs; kripā — mercy; kari' — giving; gelā — went back; nīlācala — to Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

After fully instructing Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent him to Mathurā with empowered devotional service. In Benares He also bestowed His mercy upon the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs. He then returned to Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī].
