Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.49

prabhu-ājñāya bhakta-gana pratyabda āsiyā

gundicā dekhiyā yā'na prabhure miliyā

SYNONYMS

prabhu-ājñāya — upon the order of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; pratyabda — every year; āsiyā — coming there; gundicā — the festival of Gundicā-yātrā; dekhiyā — seeing; yā'na — return; prabhure — the Lord; miliyā — meeting.

TRANSLATION

Following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees used to visit Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu every year. They would see the Gundicā festival at Jagannātha Purī and then return home after four months.
