Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.221

sabā-pāśa ājñā māgi' calana-samaya

prabhu-pade kahe kichu kariyā vinaya

SYNONYMS

sabā — all of them; pāśa — from; ājñā — order; māgi' — taking; calana-samaya — at the time of departure; prabhu-pade — at the lotus feet of the Lord; kahe — say; kichu — something; kariyā — doing; vinaya — submission.

TRANSLATION

After begging the permission of all the Vaishnavas present, the two brothers, at the time of their departure, humbly submitted something at the lotus feet of the Lord.
