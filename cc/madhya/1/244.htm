Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.244

kāśīte prabhuke āsi' mililā sanātana

dui māsa rahi' tāńre karāilā śikshana

SYNONYMS

kāśīte — at Vārānasī; prabhuke — the Lord; āsi' — arriving; mililā — met; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; dui — two; māsa — months; rahi' — remaining; tāńre — unto him; karāilā — did; śikshana — instruction.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at Vārānasī, Sanātana Gosvāmī met Him there. The Lord remained there for two months and instructed Sanātana Gosvāmī perfectly.
