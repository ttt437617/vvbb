Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.242

dandavat kari' rūpa bhūmite padilā

parama ānande prabhu ālińgana dilā

SYNONYMS

dandavat kari' — offering obeisances; rūpa — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; bhūmite — on the ground; padilā — fell; parama — great; ānande — in delight; prabhu — the Lord; ālińgana — embracing; dilā — gave.

TRANSLATION

At Prayāga, Rūpa Gosvāmī fell down on the ground to offer obeisances to the Lord, and the Lord embraced him with great delight.
