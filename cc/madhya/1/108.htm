Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.108

trimalla bhattera ghare kaila prabhu vāsa

tāhāñi rahilā prabhu varshā cāri māsa

SYNONYMS

trimalla bhattera — of Trimalla Bhatta; ghare — at the house; kaila — did; prabhu — the Lord; vāsa — residence; tāhāñi — there; rahilā — lived; prabhu — the Lord; varshā — the rainy season; cāri — four; māsa — months.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lived at the house of Trimalla Bhatta for the four months of the rainy season.
