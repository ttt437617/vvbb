Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.210

tomāra hridaya āmi jāni patrī-dvāre

tomā śikhāite śloka pāthāila tomāre

SYNONYMS

tomāra — your; hridaya — hearts; āmi — I; jāni — understand; patrī-dvāre — by those letters; tomā — you; śikhāite — to instruct; śloka — a verse; pāthāila — I sent; tomāre — to you.

TRANSLATION

"By your letters, I could understand your heart. Therefore, in order to teach you, I sent you one verse, which reads as follows.
