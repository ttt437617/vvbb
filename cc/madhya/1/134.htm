Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.134

sabā-sańge ratha-yātrā kaila daraśana

ratha-agre nritya kari' udyāne gamana

SYNONYMS

sabā-sańge — with all of them; ratha-yātrā — the car festival; kaila — did; daraśana — seeing; ratha-agre — in front of the car; nritya — dancing; kari' — doing; udyāne — in the garden; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

After this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the devotees saw the Ratha-yātrā, the car festival ceremony. Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself danced in front of the car, and after dancing He entered a garden.
