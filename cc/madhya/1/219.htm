Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.219

nityānanda, haridāsa, śrīvāsa, gadādhara

mukunda, jagadānanda, murāri, vakreśvara

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; gadādhara — Gadādhara Pandita; mukunda — Mukunda; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; murāri — Murāri; vakreśvara — Vakreśvara.

TRANSLATION

All the Vaishnava associates of the Lord were present, including Nityānanda Prabhu, Haridāsa Thākura, Śrīvāsa Thākura, Gadādhara Pandita, Mukunda, Jagadānanda, Murāri and Vakreśvara.
