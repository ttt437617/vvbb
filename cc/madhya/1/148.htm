Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.148

vrindāvana yāite kaila gaudere gamana

pratāparudra kaila pathe vividha sevana

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana yāite — to go to Vrindāvana; kaila — did; gaudere — to Bengal; gamana — going; pratāparudra — King Pratāparudra; kaila — performed; pathe — on the road; vividha — various; sevana — services.

TRANSLATION

To visit Vrindāvana, the Lord went to Gauda-deśa [Bengal]. On the way, King Pratāparudra performed a variety of services to please the Lord.
