Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.145

gundicāte nritya-ante kaila jala-keli

herā-pañcamīte dekhila lakshmī-devīra kelī

SYNONYMS

gundicāte — in the neighborhood of the Gundicā temple; nritya-ante — after dancing; kaila — performed; jala-keli — sporting in the water; herā-pañcamīte — on the day of Herā-pañcamī; dekhila — saw; lakshmī-devīra — of the goddess of fortune; kelī — activities.

TRANSLATION

After dancing in the Gundicā temple, the Lord sported in the water with His devotees, and on Herā-pañcamī day they all saw the activities of the goddess of fortune, Lakshmīdevī.
