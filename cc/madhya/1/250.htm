Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.250

prativarsha āisena tāhāń gaudera bhakta-gana

cāri māsa rahe prabhura sańge sammilana

SYNONYMS

prativarsha — each year; āisena — visit; tāhāń — there; gaudera — of Bengal; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; cāri — four; māsa — months; rahe — remain; prabhura — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sańge — with; sammilana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

During those eighteen years, all the devotees of Bengal used to visit Him at Jagannātha Purī every year. They would remain there for four continuous months and enjoy the company of the Lord.
