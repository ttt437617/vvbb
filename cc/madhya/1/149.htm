Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.149

purī-gosāñi-sańge vastra-pradāna-prasańga

rāmānanda rāya āilā bhadraka paryanta

SYNONYMS

purī-gosāñi-sańge — with Purī Gosvāmī; vastra-pradāna-prasańga — incidents of exchanging cloth; rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; āilā — came; bhadraka — a place of the name Bhadraka; paryanta — as far as.

TRANSLATION

On the way to Vrindāvana via Bengal, there was an incident wherein some cloth was exchanged with Purī Gosāñi. Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya accompanied the Lord as far as the city of Bhadraka.
