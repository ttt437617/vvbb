Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.264

pradyumna miśrere prabhu rāmānanda-sthāne

krishna-kathā śunāila kahi' tāńra gune

SYNONYMS

pradyumna miśrere — Pradyumna Miśra; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rāmānanda-sthāne — at the place of Rāmānanda Rāya; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Śrī Krishna; śunāila — caused to hear; kahi' — explaining; tāńra — of Rāmānanda Rāya; gune — the transcendental qualities.

TRANSLATION

After explaining the transcendental qualities of Rāmānanda Rāya, the Lord sent Pradyumna Miśra to his residence, and Pradyumna Miśra learned krishna-kathā from him.
