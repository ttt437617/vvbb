Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.169

vinā dāne eta loka yāńra pāche haya

sei ta' gosāñā, ihā jāniha niścaya

SYNONYMS

vinā — without; dāne — charity; eta — so many; loka — persons; yāńra — whom; pāche — after; haya — become; sei ta' — He certainly; gosāñā — a prophet; ihā — this; jāniha — know; niścaya — surely.

TRANSLATION

"Such a person, who is followed by so many people without giving them charity, must be a prophet. I can surely understand this fact."
