Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.115

ananta, purushottama, śrī-janārdana

padmanābha, vāsudeva kaila daraśana

SYNONYMS

ananta — Anantadeva; purushottama — Purushottama; śrī-janārdana — Śrī Janārdana; padma-nābha — Padmanābha; vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; kaila — did; daraśana — visit.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then visited the Vishnu temples of Anantadeva, Purushottama, Śrī Janārdana, Padmanābha and Vāsudeva.

PURPORT

A temple of Ananta Padmanābha Vishnu is situated in the Trivandrum district (Thiruvananthapuram). This temple is very famous in those quarters. Another Vishnu temple, named Śrī Janārdana, is situated about twenty-six miles north of the Trivandrum district, near the railway station called Varkālā.
