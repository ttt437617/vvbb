Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.231

eta cinti prātah-kāle gańgā-snāna kari'

'nīlācale yāba' bali' calilā gaurahari

SYNONYMS

eta cinti — thus thinking; prātah-kāle — in the morning; gańgā-snāna — bathing in the Ganges; kari' — performing; nīlācale yāba — I shall go to Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī); bali' — saying; calilā — started; gaurahari — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Thinking like this, the Lord took His morning bath in the Ganges and started for Nīlācala, saying "I shall go there."
