Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.243

śrī-rūpe śikshā karāi' pāthāilā vrindāvana

āpane karilā vārānasī āgamana

SYNONYMS

śrī-rūpe śikshā karāi' — teaching Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; pāthāilā — sent; vrindāvana — toward Vrindāvana; āpane — Himself; karilā — did; vārānasī — to Benares; āgamana — coming.

TRANSLATION

After instructing Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī at Prayāga, at the Daśāśvamedha-ghāta, Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered him to go to Vrindāvana. The Lord then returned to Vārānasī.
