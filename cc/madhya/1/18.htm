Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.18

śesha-līlāra 'madhya' 'antya', — dui nāma haya

līlā-bhede vaishnava saba nāma-bheda kaya

SYNONYMS

śesha-līlāra — of the śesha-līlā, or pastimes at the end; madhya — the middle; antya — the final; dui — two; nāma — names; haya — are; līlā-bhede — by the difference of pastimes; vaishnava — the devotees of the Supreme Lord; saba — all; nāma-bheda — different names; kaya — say.

TRANSLATION

The final pastimes of the Lord, occurring in His last twenty-four years, are called madhya [middle] and antya [final]. All the devotees of the Lord refer to His pastimes according to these divisions.
