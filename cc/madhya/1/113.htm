Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.113

śrī-rańga-purī saha tāhāñi milana

rāma-dāsa viprera kaila duhkha-vimocana

SYNONYMS

śrī-rańga-purī — Śrī Rańga Purī; saha — with; tāhāñi — there; milana — meeting; rāma-dāsa — of the name Rāmadāsa; viprera — of the brāhmana; kaila — did; duhkha-vimocana — deliverance from all sufferings.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then met Śrī Rańga Purī and mitigated all the sufferings of a brāhmana named Rāmadāsa.
