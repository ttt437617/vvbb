Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.279

ke śikhāla ei loke, kahe kon bāta

ihā-sabāra mukha dhāka diyā nija hāta

SYNONYMS

ke — who; śikhāla — taught; ei — these; loke — people; kahe — they say; kon — what; bāta — topics; ihā — of them; sabāra — of all; mukha — the mouths; dhāka — just cover; diyā — with; nija — Your own; hāta — hand.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura continued, "Who has taught these people? What are they saying? Now You can cover their mouths with Your own hand.
