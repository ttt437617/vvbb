Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.27

caitanya-gosāñi yāńre bale 'bada bhāi'

teńho kahe, mora prabhu — caitanya-gosāñi

SYNONYMS

caitanya-gosāñi — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yāńre — unto whom; bale — says; bada bhāi — elder brother; teńho — He; kahe — says; mora prabhu — My Lord; caitanya-gosāñi — the supreme master, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to address Nityānanda Prabhu as His elder brother, whereas Nityānanda Prabhu addressed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as His Lord.
