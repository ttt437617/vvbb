Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.45

ei mata nānā grantha kariyā prakāśa

goshthī sahite kailā vrindāvane vāsa

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; nānā — various; grantha — books; kariyā — making; prakāśa — publication; goshthī — family members; sahite — with; kailā — did; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; vāsa — residence.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, Sanātana Gosvāmī and their nephew Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī, as well as practically all of their family members, lived in Vrindāvana and published important books on devotional service.
