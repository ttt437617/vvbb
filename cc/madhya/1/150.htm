Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.150

āsi' vidyā-vācaspatira grihete rahilā

prabhure dekhite loka-sańghatta ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

āsi' — coming to Bengal; vidyā-vācaspatira — of Vidyā-vācaspati; grihete — at the home; rahilā — remained; prabhure — unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhite — to see; loka-sańghatta — crowds of men; ha-ilā — there were.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reached Vidyānagara, Bengal, on the way to Vrindāvana, He stopped at the house of Vidyā-vācaspati, who was the brother of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya. When Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly arrived at his house, great crowds of people gathered.
