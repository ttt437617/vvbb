Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.73

prabhu kahe, — tāre āmi santushta hañā

ālińgana kailu sarva-śakti sañcāriyā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; tāre — him; āmi — I; santushta hañā — being very much satisfied; ālińgana kailu — embraced; sarva-śakti — all potencies; sañcāriyā — bestowing.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "I was so pleased with Rūpa Gosvāmī that I embraced him and bestowed upon him all necessary potencies for preaching the bhakti cult.
