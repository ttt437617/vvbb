Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.141

pathe sārvabhauma saha sabāra milana

sārvabhauma bhattācāryera kāśīte gamana

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the way; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; saha — with; sabāra — of everyone; milana — meeting; sārvabhauma bhattācāryera — of the devotee named Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kāśīte — to Vārānasī; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

Everyone met Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya on his way to Vārānasī.
