Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.246

chaya vatsara aiche prabhu karilā vilāsa

kabhu iti-uti, kabhu kshetra-vāsa

SYNONYMS

chaya vatsara — six years; aiche — in that way; prabhu — the Lord; karilā — did; vilāsa — pastimes; kabhu — sometimes; iti-uti — here and there; kabhu — sometimes; kshetra-vāsa — residing at Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

The Lord traveled all over India for six years. He was sometimes here and sometimes there, performing His transcendental pastimes, and sometimes He remained at Jagannātha Purī.
