Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.237

dina kata tāhāń rahi' calilā vrindāvana

lukāñā calilā rātre, nā jāne kona jana

SYNONYMS

dina kata — a few days; tāhāń — at Jagannātha Purī; rahi' — remaining; calilā — started; vrindāvana — for Vrindāvana; lukāñā — keeping secret; calilā — started; rātre — at night; nā jāne — did not know; kona — some; jana — person.

TRANSLATION

After remaining at Jagannātha Purī for a few days, the Lord secretly started for Vrindāvana at night. He did this without anyone's knowledge.
