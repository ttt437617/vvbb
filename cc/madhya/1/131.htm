Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.131

gauda ha-ite sarva vaishnavera āgamana

kulīna-grāma-vāsi-sańge prathama milana

SYNONYMS

gauda ha-ite — from Bengal; sarva — all; vaishnavera — of the Vaishnavas; āgamana — appearance; kulīna-grāma-vāsi — the residents of Kulīna-grāma; sańge — with them; prathama — first; milana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees from Bengal gradually began arriving at Jagannātha Purī. At this time, the residents of Kulīna-grāma also came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for the first time.
