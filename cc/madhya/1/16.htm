Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.16

cabbiśa vatsara śeshe yei māgha-māsa

tāra śukla-pakshe prabhu karilā sannyāsa

SYNONYMS

cabbiśa vatsara — of those twenty-four years; śeshe — at the end; yei — which; māgha-māsa — the month of Māgha (January-February); tāra — of that month; śukla-pakshe — during the fortnight of the waxing moon; prabhu — the Lord; karilā — accepted; sannyāsa — the renounced order of life.

TRANSLATION

At the end of His twenty-fourth year, in the month of Māgha, during the fortnight of the waxing moon, the Lord accepted the renounced order of life, sannyāsa.
