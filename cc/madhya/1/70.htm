Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.70

eta bali' tānre bahu prasāda kariyā

svarūpa-gosāñire śloka dekhāila lañā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; tāńre — unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; bahu — much; prasāda — mercy; kariyā — showing; svarūpa-gosāñire — unto Svarūpa Gosvāmī; śloka — the verse; dekhāila — showed; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed various benedictions upon Rūpa Gosvāmī, and taking the verse, He later showed it to Svarūpa Gosvāmī.
