Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.251

nirantara nritya-gīta kīrtana-vilāsa

ācandāle prema-bhakti karilā prakāśa

SYNONYMS

nirantara — without stopping; nritya-gīta — chanting and dancing; kīrtana — of sańkīrtana; vilāsa — enjoyment; ā-candāle — to everyone, even to the lowest person; prema-bhakti — love of Godhead; karilā — did; prakāśa — manifestation.

TRANSLATION

At Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed chanting and dancing unceasingly. Thus He enjoyed the pastime of sańkīrtana. He manifested His causeless mercy, pure love of God, to everyone, including the lowest man.
