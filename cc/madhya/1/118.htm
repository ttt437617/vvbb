Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.118

śuniyā prabhura ānandita haila mana

rāma-dāsa viprera kathā ha-ila smarana

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing this; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita — very happy; haila — became; mana — the mind; rāma-dāsa — of the name Rāmadāsa; viprera — with the brāhmana; kathā — of the conversation; ha-ila — was; smarana — remembrance.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very glad to read about the false Sītā, and He remembered His meeting with Rāmadāsa Vipra, who was very sorry that mother Sītā had been kidnapped by Rāvana.
