Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.19

tāra madhye chaya vatsara — gamanāgamana

nīlācala-gauda-setubandha-vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — within that period; chaya vatsara — for six years; gamana-āgamana — going and coming; nīlācala — from Jagannātha Purī; gauda — to Bengal; setubandha — and from Cape Comorin; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana-dhāma.

TRANSLATION

For six years of the last twenty-four, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu traveled all over India, from Jagannātha Purī to Bengal and from Cape Comorin to Vrindāvana.
