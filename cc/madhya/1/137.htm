Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.137

sārvabhauma-ghare prabhura bhikshā-paripātī

shāthīra mātā kahe, yāte rāndī ha-uk shāthī

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma-ghare — at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; prabhura — of the Lord; bhikshā — eating; paripātī — sumptuously; shāthīra mātā — the mother of Shāthī, who was the daughter of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kahe — says; yāte — by which; rāndī — widow; hauk — let her become; shāthī — Shāthī, the daughter.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was invited to dine at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya. While He was eating sumptuously, the son-in-law of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya [the husband of his daughter Shāthī] criticized Him. Because of this, Shāthī's mother cursed him by praying that Shāthī would become a widow. In other words, she cursed her son-in-law to die.
