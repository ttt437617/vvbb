Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.57

ei bhāve nritya-madhye pade eka śloka

sei ślokera artha keha nāhi bujhe loka

SYNONYMS

ei bhāve — in this ecstasy; nritya-madhye — during the dancing; pade — recites; eka — one; śloka — verse; sei ślokera — of that verse; artha — the meaning; keha — anyone; nāhi — not; bujhe — understands; loka — person.

TRANSLATION

In that ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited a verse when dancing in front of Lord Jagannātha. Almost no one could understand the meaning of that verse.
