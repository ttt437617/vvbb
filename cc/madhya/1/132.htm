Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.132

narahari dāsa ādi yata khanda-vāsī

śivānanda-sena-sańge mililā sabe āsi'

SYNONYMS

narahari dāsa — Narahari dāsa; ādi — heading the list; yata — all; khanda-vāsī — devotees of the place known as Khanda; śivānanda-sena — Śivānanda Sena; sańge — with; mililā — He met; sabe — all; āsi' — coming there.

TRANSLATION

Eventually Narahari dāsa and other inhabitants of Khanda, along with Śivānanda Sena, all arrived, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met them.
