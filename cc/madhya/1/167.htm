Madhya-līlāChapter 1: The Later Pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 1.167

tāhāń nritya kare prabhu preme acetana

koti koti loka āise dekhite carana

SYNONYMS

tāhāń — there; nritya — dancing; kare — performed; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; preme — in love of Godhead; acetana — unconscious; koti koti — innumerable; loka — people; āise — came; dekhite — to see; carana — His lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

While performing sańkīrtana in Rāmakeli-grāma, the Lord danced and sometimes lost consciousness due to love of God. While at Rāmakeli-grāma, an unlimited number of people came to see His lotus feet.
