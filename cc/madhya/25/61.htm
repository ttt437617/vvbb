Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.61

pathe sei vipra saba vrittānta kahila

śuni' mahāprabhu sukhe īshat hāsila

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the way; sei vipra — that brāhmana; saba vrittānta kahila — explained the whole incident; śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sukhe — in happiness; īshat — mildly; hāsila — smiled.

TRANSLATION

While the Lord was on His way, the Maharashtriyan brāhmana informed Him about the incident that took place in the camp of Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī. Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled happily.
