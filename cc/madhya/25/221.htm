Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.221

dina daśa rahi' rūpa gaude yātrā kaila

sanātana-rūpera ei caritra kahila

SYNONYMS

dina daśa — about ten days; rahi' — remaining; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; gaude yātrā kaila — went back to Bengal; sanātana-rūpera — of Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī and Rūpa Gosvāmī; ei — thus; caritra — character; kahila — I have described.

TRANSLATION

After staying in Vārānasī for about ten days, Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Bengal. In this way I have described the activities of Rūpa and Sanātana.
