Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.246

caturthe — mādhava purīra caritra-āsvādana

gopāla sthāpana, kshīra-curira varnana

SYNONYMS

caturthe — in the Fourth Chapter; mādhava purīra — of Mādhavendra Purī; caritra-āsvādana — relishing the characteristics; gopāla sthāpana — the installation of Gopāla; kshīra-curira varnana — a description of Gopīnātha's stealing condensed milk at Remunā.

TRANSLATION

In the Fourth Chapter I have described Mādhavendra Purī's installation of the Gopāla Deity as well as Gopīnātha's stealing a pot of condensed milk at Remunā.
