Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.4

'paramānanda kīrtanīyā' — śekharera sańgī

prabhure kīrtana śunāya, ati bada rańgī

SYNONYMS

paramānanda kīrtanīyā — Paramānanda Kīrtanīyā; śekharera sańgī — a friend of Candraśekhara's; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kīrtana śunāya — sings and chants; ati bada rańgī — very humorous.

TRANSLATION

For as long as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was in Vārānasī, Paramānanda Kīrtanīyā, who was a friend of Candraśekhara's, chanted the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra and other songs to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in a very humorous way.
