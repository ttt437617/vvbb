Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.44

vyāsa-sūtrera artha ācārya kariyāche ācchādana

ei haya satya śrī-krishna-caitanya-vacana

SYNONYMS

vyāsa-sūtrera — of the aphorisms of the Vedānta known as Vyāsa-sūtra; artha — the meanings; ācārya — Śańkarācārya; kariyāche ācchādana — has purposely covered; ei — this; haya — is; satya — the truth; śrī-krishna-caitanya-vacana — the words and explanation given by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

"The conclusion is that the import of the Vedānta-sūtra is covered by the imaginary explanation of Śańkarācārya. Whatever Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu has said is perfectly true.
