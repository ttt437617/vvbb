Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.255

shodaśe — vrindāvana-yātrā gauda-deśa-pathe

punah nīlācale āilā, nātaśālā haite

SYNONYMS

shodaśe — in the Sixteenth Chapter; vrindāvana-yātrā — departure for visiting Vrindāvana; gauda-deśa-pathe — on the way through the province of Bengal; punah — again; nīlācale āilā — came back to Jagannātha Purī; nātaśālā haite — from Kānāi Nātaśālā.

TRANSLATION

In the Sixteenth Chapter I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Vrindāvana and journeyed through Bengal. He later returned to Jagannātha Purī from Kānāi Nātaśālā.
