Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.200

āra krishna-nāma laite krishna-sthāne sthiti

mahā-pātakera haya ei prāyaścitti

SYNONYMS

āra — further; krishna-nāma — the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; laite — continuously chanting; krishna-sthāne sthiti — being situated in company with Lord Krishna; mahā-pātakera — of all kinds of sinful activity; haya — is; ei — this; prāyaścitti — atonement.

TRANSLATION

"When you are situated at the lotus feet of Krishna, no sinful reaction can touch you. This is the best solution to all sinful activity."
