Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.107

yaiche āmāra 'svarūpa', yaiche āmāra 'sthiti'

yaiche āmāra guna, karma, shad-aiśvarya-śakti

SYNONYMS

yaiche — as far as; āmāra — My; svarūpa — original form; yaiche — as far as; āmāra — My; sthiti — situation; yaiche — as far as; āmāra — My; guna — attributes; karma — activities; shat-aiśvarya-śakti — six kinds of opulence.

TRANSLATION

"'I shall explain to you My actual form and situation, My attributes, activities and six opulences.'
