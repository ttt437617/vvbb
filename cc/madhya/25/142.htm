Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.142

ataeva bhāgavata — sūtrera 'artha'-rūpa

nija-krita sūtrera nija-'bhāshya'-svarūpa

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; bhāgavata — Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; sūtrera — of the Brahma-sūtra; artha — of the meaning; rūpa — the form; nija-krita — made by himself; sūtrera — of the Vedānta-sūtra; nija-bhāshya — of his own commentary; svarūpa — the original form.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam gives the actual meaning of the Vedānta-sūtra. The author of the Vedānta-sūtra is Vyāsadeva, and he himself has explained those aphorisms in the form of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
