Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.182

sanātane kahilā, — tumi yāha' vrindāvana

tomāra dui bhāi tathā kariyāche gamana

SYNONYMS

sanātane kahilā — he advised Sanātana Gosvāmī; tumi — you; yāha' vrindāvana — go to Vrindāvana; tomāra — your; dui bhāi — two brothers; tathā — there; kariyāche gamana — have already gone.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advised Sanātana Gosvāmī to proceed toward Vrindāvana, and He informed him that his two brothers had already gone there.
