Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.111

srishti kari' tāra madhye āmi praveśiye

prapañca ye dekha saba, seha āmi ha-iye

SYNONYMS

srishti kari' — after creating; tāra madhye — within the creation; āmi praveśiye — I enter as Lord Vishnu; prapañca — the cosmic manifestation; ye — whatever; dekha — you see; saba — all; seha — that; āmi ha-iye — I am.

TRANSLATION

"'After creating the cosmic manifestation, I entered into it. Whatever you see in the cosmic manifestation is but an expansion of My energy.
