Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.59

e saba vrittānta śuni' mahārāshtrīya brāhmana

prabhure kahite sukhe karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

e saba vrittānta — all these descriptions; śuni' — hearing; mahārāshtrīya brāhmana — the Maharashtriyan brāhmana; prabhure — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahite — to inform; sukhe — very happily; karilā gamana — went.

TRANSLATION

After hearing all these statements, the Maharashtriyan brāhmana very jubilantly went to inform Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
