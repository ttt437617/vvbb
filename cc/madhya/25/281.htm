Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.281

śrī-rūpa-sanātana- raghunātha-jīva-carana,

śire dhari, — yāra karoń āśa

krishna-līlāmritānvita, caitanya-caritāmrita,

kahe kichu dīna krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

śrī-rūpa-sanātana — of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and of Sanātana Gosvāmī; raghunātha-jīva — of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī, Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī and Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī; carana — the lotus feet; śire dhari — taking on my head; yāra — of which; karoń āśa — I always desire; krishna-līlā-amrita-anvita — mixed with the nectar of krishna-līlā; caitanya-caritāmrita — the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — is trying to speak; kichu — something; dīna — most humble; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa Kavirāja Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Taking the feet of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī, Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī and Jīva Gosvāmī on my head, I always desire their mercy. Thus I, Krishnadāsa, humbly try to describe the nectar of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, which are mixed with the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
