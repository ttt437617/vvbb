Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.29

harer nāma-ślokera yei karilā vyākhyāna

sei satya sukhadārtha parama pramāna

SYNONYMS

harer nāma-ślokera — of the verse beginning harer nāma harer nāma; yei — whatever; karilā — made; vyākhyāna — the explanation; sei — that; satya — true; sukha-da-artha — a meaning that is pleasing to accept; parama pramāna — the supreme evidence.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's explanation of the verse beginning 'harer nāma harer nāma [Adi 17.21]' is not only pleasing to the ear but is strong, factual evidence.
