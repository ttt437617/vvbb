Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.21

upadeśa lañā kare krishna-sańkīrtana

sarva-loka hāse, gāya, karaye nartana

SYNONYMS

upadeśa lañā — getting instructions from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — join; krishna-sańkīrtana — the sańkīrtana movement; sarva-loka hāse — everyone began to laugh in pleasure; gāya — chant; karaye nartana — and dance.

TRANSLATION

As soon as people received instructions from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they began to chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra. Thus everyone laughed, chanted and danced with the Lord.
