Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.94

pranavera yei artha, gāyatrīte sei haya

sei artha catuh-ślokīte vivariyā kaya

SYNONYMS

pranavera — of the sound vibration, omkāra; yei — whatever; artha — meaning; gāyatrīte — in the Gāyatrī mantra; sei — that; haya — there is; sei artha — that same meaning; catuh-ślokīte — in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam summarized in four ślokas; vivariyā — describing elaborately; kaya — has said.

TRANSLATION

"The meaning of the sound vibration omkāra is present in the Gāyatrī mantra. The same is elaborately explained in the four ślokas of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam known as the catuh-ślokī.
