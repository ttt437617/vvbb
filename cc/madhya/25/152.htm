Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.152

vayam tu na vitripyāma

uttamahśloka-vikrame

yac chrinvatām rasa-jñānām

svādu svādu pade pade

SYNONYMS

vayam tu — we of course; na — never; vitripyāmah — are satisfied; uttamah-śloka-vikrame — in the activities and pastimes of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; yat — which; śrinvatām — of those hearing; rasa-jñānām — who know the taste of mellows; svādu svādu — more palatable; pade pade — in every step.

TRANSLATION

"'We never tire of hearing the transcendental pastimes of the Personality of Godhead, who is glorified by hymns and prayers. Those who enjoy association with Him relish hearing His pastimes at every moment.'"

PURPORT

This is a quotation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (1.1.19).
