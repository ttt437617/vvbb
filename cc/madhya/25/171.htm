Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.171

sabe kahe, — loka tārite tomāra avatāra

'pūrva' 'dakshina' 'paścima' karilā nistāra

SYNONYMS

sabe kahe — everyone says; loka tārite — to deliver the fallen souls; tomāra avatāra — Your incarnation; pūrva — east; dakshina — south; paścima — west; karilā nistāra — You have delivered.

TRANSLATION

All the Lord's devotees then said, "You have incarnated to deliver fallen souls. You have delivered them in the east and in the south, and now you are delivering them in the west.
