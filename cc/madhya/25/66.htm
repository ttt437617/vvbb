Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.66

nikate hari-dhvani śuni' parakāśānanda

dekhite kautuke āilā lañā śishya-vrinda

SYNONYMS

nikate — nearby; hari-dhvani śuni' — after hearing the chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; parakāśānanda — Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; dekhite — to see; kautuke — in great eagerness; āilā — came; lañā — taking; śishya-vrinda — all the disciples.

TRANSLATION

When Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī, who was staying nearby, heard this tumultuous chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra, he and his disciples immediately came to see the Lord.
