Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.122

sarva-deśa-kāla-daśāya janera kartavya

guru-pāśe sei bhakti prashtavya, śrotavya

SYNONYMS

sarva — all; deśa — countries; kāla — times; daśāya — and in circumstances; janera — of every man; kartavya — the duty; guru-pāśe — in the care of a spiritual master; sei — that; bhakti — devotional service; prashtavya — to be inquired; śrotavya — and to be heard.

TRANSLATION

"'It is therefore the duty of every man — in every country, in every circumstance and at all times — to approach a bona fide spiritual master, question him about devotional service and listen to him explain the process.
