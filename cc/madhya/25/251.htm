Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.251

ekādaśe — śrī-mandire 'bedā-sańkīrtana'

dvādaśe — gundicā-mandira-mārjana-kshālana

SYNONYMS

ekādaśe — in the Eleventh Chapter; śrī-mandire — in the Jagannātha temple; bedā-sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra all around; dvādaśe — in the Twelfth Chapter; gundicā-mandira — of the temple known as Gundicā; mārjana-kshālana — cleansing and washing.

TRANSLATION

In the Eleventh Chapter I have described the great chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra that surrounded the Lord. In the Twelfth Chapter I have given a narration of the cleansing and washing of the Gundicā temple.
