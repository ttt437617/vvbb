Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.139

ebe śuna, prema, yei — mūla 'prayojana'

pulakāśru-nritya-gīta — yāhāra lakshana

SYNONYMS

ebe śuna — now hear; prema — love of Godhead; yei — which; mūla prayojana — the chief objective; pulaka-aśru-nritya-gīta — trembling of the body, tears in the eyes, dancing and chanting; yāhāra lakshana — the symptoms of which.

TRANSLATION

"Now hear from Me what actual love of Godhead is. It is the prime object of life and is symptomized by bodily trembling, tears in the eyes, chanting and dancing.
