Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.6

sannyāsīre kripā pūrve likhiyāchoń vistāriyā

uddeśe kahiye ihāń sańkshepa kariyā

SYNONYMS

sannyāsīre kripā — mercy upon the sannyāsīs; pūrve — before this; likhiyāchoń — I have described; vistāriyā — elaborately; uddeśe — in reference to that; kahiye — let me speak; ihāń — here; sańkshepa kariyā — in brief.

TRANSLATION

In the Seventh Chapter of the Ādi-līlā I have already elaborately described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's deliverance of the sannyāsīs at Vārānasī, but I shall briefly repeat it in this chapter.
