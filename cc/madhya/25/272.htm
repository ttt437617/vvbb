Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.272

bhakta-gana, śuna mora dainya-vacana

tomā-sabāra pada-dhūli, ańge vibhūshana kari',

kichu muñi karoń nivedana

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — O devotees; śuna — please hear; mora — my; dainya-vacana — humble submission; tomā-sabāra — of all of you; pada-dhūli — the dust of the feet; ańge — on my body; vibhūshana kari' — taking as ornaments; kichu — something; muñi — I; karoń nivedana — wish to submit.

TRANSLATION

With all humility, I submit myself to the lotus feet of all of you devotees, taking the dust from your feet as my bodily ornaments. Now, my dear devotees, please hear one thing more from me.
