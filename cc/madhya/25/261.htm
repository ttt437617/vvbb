Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.261

pañcavimśe — kāśī-vāsīre vaishnava-karana

kāśī haite punah nīlācale āgamana

SYNONYMS

pañca-vimśe — in the Twenty-fifth Chapter; kāśī-vāsīre — the residents of Vārānasī (Kāśī); vaishnava-karana — making Vaishnavas; kāśī haite — from Kāśī; punah — again; nīlācale āgamana — coming back to Jagannātha Purī (Nīlācala).

TRANSLATION

In the Twenty-fifth Chapter there is a description of how the residents of Vārānasī were converted to Vaishnavism and how the Lord returned to Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī] from Vārānasī.
