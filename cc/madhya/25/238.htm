Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.238

ei ta' kahiluń, — prabhu dekhi' vrindāvana

punah karilena yaiche nīlādri gamana

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have described; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhi' vrindāvana — after visiting Vrindāvana; punah — again; karilena — did; yaiche — as; nīlādri gamana — coming back to Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī from Vrindāvana.
