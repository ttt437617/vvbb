Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.69

harsha, dainya, cāpalyādi 'sañcārī' vikāra

dekhi' kāśī-vāsī lokera haila camatkāra

SYNONYMS

harsha — jubilation; dainya — humility; cāpalya-ādi — talking in ecstasy and so on; sañcārī vikāra — the transient transformations; dekhi' — seeing; kāśī-vāsī — the inhabitants of Benares; lokera — of all the people; haila camatkāra — there was astonishment.

TRANSLATION

All the people were astonished to see the Lord's jubilation and humility and to hear Him talk in ecstasy. Indeed, all the residents of Benares [Kāśī] saw the bodily transformations and were astonished.
