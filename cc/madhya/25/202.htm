Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.202

kataka divasa rāya naimishāranye rahilā

prabhu vrindāvana haite prayāga yāilā

SYNONYMS

kataka divasa — a few days; rāya — Subuddhi Rāya; naimishāranye rahilā — stayed at Naimishāranya; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vrindāvana haite — from Vrindāvana; prayāga — to Allahabad; yāilā — went.

TRANSLATION

Subuddhi Rāya stayed for some time at Naimishāranya. During that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Prayāga after visiting Vrindāvana.
