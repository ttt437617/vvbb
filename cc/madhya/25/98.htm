Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.98

cāri-veda-upanishade yata kichu haya

tāra artha lañā vyāsa karilā sañcaya

SYNONYMS

cāri-veda — the four divisions of the Vedas (Sāma, Yajur, Rig and Atharva); upanishade — and in the 108 Upanishads; yata — whatever; kichu haya — is there; tāra artha — the meanings of those Vedic literatures; lañā — taking together; vyāsa — Vyāsadeva; karilā sañcaya — collected.

TRANSLATION

"Vyāsadeva collected whatever Vedic conclusions were in the four Vedas and 108 Upanishads and placed them in the aphorisms of the Vedānta-sūtra.
