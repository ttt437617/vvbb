Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.173

vārānasī-grāme yadi kolāhala haila

śuni' grāmī deśī loka āsite lāgila

SYNONYMS

vārānasī-grāme — in the city of Vārānasī; yadi — when; kolāhala haila — there was broadcasting of this news; śuni' — hearing; grāmī — from the villages; deśī — from the towns; loka āsite lāgila — people began to pour in.

TRANSLATION

After the news of these events was broadcast, everyone from the surrounding neighborhoods began to pour in to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
