Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.7

yāhāń tāhāń prabhura nindā kare sannyāsīra gana

śuni' duhkhe mahārāshtrīya vipra karaye cintana

SYNONYMS

yāhāń tāhāń — anywhere and everywhere; prabhura nindā — criticism of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — do; sannyāsīra gana — the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs; śuni' — hearing; duhkhe — in great unhappiness; mahārāshtrīya vipra — the brāhmana of Maharashtra province; karaye cintana — was contemplating.

TRANSLATION

When the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs were criticizing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu anywhere and everywhere in Vārānasī, the Maharashtriyan brāhmana, hearing this blasphemy, began to think about this unhappily.
