Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.145

sarva-vedetihāsānām

sāram sāram samuddhritam

SYNONYMS

sarva-veda — of all Vedic literature; itihāsānām — of historical literature; sāram sāram — the essence of the essence; samuddhritam — is collected (in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam).

TRANSLATION

"'The essence of all Vedic literature and all histories has been collected in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.'

PURPORT

Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam was collected by the incarnation of God, Vyāsadeva, and it was later taught to his son, Śukadeva Gosvāmī. This is a quotation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (1.3.41).
