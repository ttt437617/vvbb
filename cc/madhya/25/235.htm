Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.235

sabā sańge lañā prabhu miśra-vāsā āilā

sārvabhauma, pandita-gosāñi nimantrana kailā

SYNONYMS

sabā sańge lañā — taking all of them; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; miśra-vāsā āilā — came to Kāśī Miśra's house; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; pandita-gosāñi — Gadādhara Pandita; nimantrana kailā — invited the Lord to take prasādam.

TRANSLATION

The Lord and all His devotees then went to the residence of Kāśī Miśra. Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and Pandita Gosāñi also invited the Lord to dine at their homes.
