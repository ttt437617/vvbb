Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.25

upanishadera karena mukhyārtha vyākhyāna

śuniyā pandita-lokera judāya mana-kāna

SYNONYMS

upanishadera — of the Vedic version known as the Upanishads; karena — He does; mukhya-artha — the original meaning; vyākhyāna — explanation; śuniyā — hearing; pandita-lokera — of the learned scholars; judāya — satisfies; mana-kāna — the minds and ears.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu explains the direct meaning of the Upanishads. When all learned scholars hear this, their minds and ears are satisfied.
