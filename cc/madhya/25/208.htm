Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.208

māsa-mātra rūpa-gosāñi rahilā vrindāvane

śīghra cali' āilā sanātanānusandhāne

SYNONYMS

māsa-mātra — only one month; rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosāñi; rahilā — remained; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; śīghra — very soon; cali' āilā — returned; sanātana-anusandhāne — to search for Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Rūpa Gosvāmī remained in Mathurā and Vrindāvana for one month in the association of Subuddhi Rāya. After that, he left Vrindāvana to search for his elder brother, Sanātana Gosvāmī.
