Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.282

śrīman-madana-gopāla-

govinda-deva-tushtaye

caitanyārpitam astv etac

caitanya-caritāmritam

SYNONYMS

śrīman-madana-gopāla — of the Deity named Śrīman Madana-gopāla; govinda-deva — of the Deity named Govindadeva; tushtaye — for the satisfaction; caitanya-arpitam — offered unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; astu — let it be; etat — this; caitanya-caritāmritam — the book known as Caitanya-caritāmrita.

TRANSLATION

For the satisfaction of Śrī Madana-gopāla and Govindadeva, we pray that this book, Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita, may be offered to Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
