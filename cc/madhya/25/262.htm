Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.262

pañcavimśati paricchede ei kailuń anuvāda

yāhāra śravane haya granthārtha-āsvāda

SYNONYMS

pañca-vimśati paricchede — in the Twenty-fifth Chapter; ei — this; kailuń anuvāda — I have made repetition; yāhāra śravane — hearing which; haya — there is; grantha-artha-āsvāda — understanding of the whole contents of the book.

TRANSLATION

I have thus summarized these pastimes in the Twenty-fifth Chapter. Hearing this summary, one can understand the whole purport of this scripture.
