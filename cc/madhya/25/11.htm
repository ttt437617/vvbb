Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.11

eta cinti' nimantrila sannyāsīra gane

tabe sei vipra āila mahāprabhura sthāne

SYNONYMS

eta cinti' — thinking this; nimantrila — he invited; sannyāsīra gane — all the sannyāsīs; tabe — then; sei vipra — that brāhmana; āila — approached; mahāprabhura sthāne — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Thinking like this, the Maharashtriyan brāhmana extended an invitation to all the sannyāsīs of Vārānasī. After doing this, he finally approached Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to extend Him an invitation.
