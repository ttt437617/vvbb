Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.24

śrī-krishna-caitanya haya 'sākshāt nārāyana'

'vyāsa-sūtrera' artha karena ati-manorama

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haya — is; sākshāt nārāyana — directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana; vyāsa-sūtrera — the aphorisms of Vyāsadeva (Vedānta-sūtra); artha karena — He explains; ati-manorama — very nicely.

TRANSLATION

He said, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana Himself. When He explains the Vedānta-sūtra, He does so very nicely.
