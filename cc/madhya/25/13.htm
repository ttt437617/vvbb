Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.13

bhakta-duhkha dekhi' prabhu manete cintila

sannyāsīra mana phirāite mana ha-ila

SYNONYMS

bhakta-duhkha dekhi' — seeing the unhappiness of the devotees; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; manete cintila — considered within His mind; sannyāsīra mana — the minds of the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs; phirāite — to turn; mana ha-ila — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu decided.

TRANSLATION

They submitted their request, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, seeing His devotees' unhappiness, decided to turn the minds of the Māyāvādī sannyāsīs.
