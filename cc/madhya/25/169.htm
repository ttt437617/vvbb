Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.169

kāśīte grāhaka nāhi, vastu nā vikāya

punarapi deśe vahi' laoyā nāhi yāya

SYNONYMS

kāśīte — in Kāśī (Benares); grāhaka nāhi — there was no customer; vastu nā vikāya — it was not selling; punarapi — again; deśe — to My own country; vahi' — carrying (it); laoyā — to take; nāhi yāya — was not possible.

TRANSLATION

"Although I came to Vārānasī to sell My goods, there were no customers, and it appeared necessary for Me to carry them back to My own country.
