Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.55

parama kārana īśvara keha nāhi māne

sva-sva-mata sthāpe para-matera khandane

SYNONYMS

parama kārana — the supreme cause, the cause of all causes; īśvara — the Supreme Lord; keha nāhi māne — none of the above-mentioned philosophers accept; sva-sva-mata — their own personal opinions; sthāpe — they establish; para-matera khandane — busy in refuting the opinions of others.

TRANSLATION

"Of the philosophers mentioned, none really cares for the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the cause of all causes. They are always busy refuting the philosophical theories of others and establishing their own.
