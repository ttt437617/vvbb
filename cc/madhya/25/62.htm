Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.62

mādhava-saundarya dekhi' āvishta ha-ilā

ańganete āsi' preme nācite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

mādhava-saundarya dekhi' — after seeing the beauty of Lord Bindu Mādhava; āvishta ha-ilā — became ecstatic in love; ańganete āsi' — coming to the courtyard; preme — in great love; nācite lāgilā — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

Upon reaching the temple of Bindu Mādhava, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, seeing the beauty of Lord Bindu Mādhava, became overwhelmed in ecstatic love. He then began to dance in the courtyard of the temple.
