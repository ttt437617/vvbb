Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.86

ebe tomāra pādābje upajibe bhakti

tathi lāgi' kari tomāra carane pranati"

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; tomāra — Your; pāda-abje — to the lotus feet; upajibe — will grow; bhakti — devotional service; tathi lāgi' — for that reason; kari — I do; tomāra carane pranati — humble obeisances at Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"Henceforward I shall certainly develop devotional service unto Your lotus feet. For this reason I have come to You and have fallen down at Your lotus feet."
