Madhya-līlāChapter 25: How All the Residents of Vārānasī Became Vaishnavas

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 25.201

pāñā ājñā rāya vrindāvanere calilā

prayāga, ayodhyā diyā naimishāranye āilā

SYNONYMS

pāñā ājñā — getting this order; rāya — Subuddhi Rāya; vrindāvanere calilā — went toward Vrindāvana; prayāga — Allahabad; ayodhyā — Ayodhyā (the kingdom of Lord Rāmacandra); diyā — through; naimishāranye āilā — came to Naimishāranya (a place near Lucknow).

TRANSLATION

Thus receiving the order from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to go to Vrindāvana, Subuddhi Rāya left Vārānasī and went through Prayāga, Ayodhyā and Naimishāranya toward Vrindāvana.
