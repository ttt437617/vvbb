Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.87

gandha-pushpa-dhūpa-dīpe mahā-pūjā kaila

bhattācārye mānya kari' pāka karāila

SYNONYMS

gandha — scents; pushpa — flowers; dhūpa — incense; dīpe — by lamps; mahā-pūjā kaila — he worshiped the Lord with great pomp; bhattācārye — to Balabhadra Bhattācārya; mānya kari' — offering respect; pāka karāila — engaging in cooking.

TRANSLATION

Vallabhācārya worshiped the Lord with great pomp, offering scents, incense, flowers and lamps, and with great respect he induced Balabhadra Bhattācārya [the Lord's cook] to cook.
