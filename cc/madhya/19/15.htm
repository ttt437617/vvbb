Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.15

asvāsthyera chadma kari' rahe nija-ghare

rāja-kārya chādilā, nā yāya rāja-dvāre

SYNONYMS

asvāsthyera — of not being well; chadma — pretext; kari' — making; rahe — remains; nija-ghare — at home; rāja-kārya — government service; chādilā — relinquished; nā yāya — did not go; rāja-dvāre — to the court of the Nawab.

TRANSLATION

On the pretext of bad health, Sanātana Gosvāmī remained home. Thus he gave up government service and did not go to the royal court.
