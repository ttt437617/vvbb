Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.80

āste-vyaste sabe dhari' prabhure uthāila

naukāra upare prabhu nācite lāgila

SYNONYMS

āste-vyaste — with great haste; sabe — all of them; dhari' — catching; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uthāila — raised; naukāra — of the boat; upare — on top; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nācite lāgila — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

They all hastily grabbed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and pulled Him out of the water. Once on the boat's platform, the Lord began to dance.
