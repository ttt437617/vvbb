Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.81

mahāprabhura bhare naukā kare talamala

dubite lāgila naukā, jhalake bhare jala

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhare — because of the weight; naukā — the boat; kare — does; talamala — tilting; dubite — to sink; lāgila — began; naukā — the boat; jhalake — in gushes; bhare — fills; jala — water.

TRANSLATION

Due to the Lord's heavy weight, the boat began to tilt. It began filling up with water and was on the verge of sinking.
