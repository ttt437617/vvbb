Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.237

eta bali' prabhu tāńre kailā ālińgana

vārānasī calibāre prabhura haila mana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; kailā — did; ālińgana — embracing; vārānasī — toward Benares; calibāre — to go; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haila — was; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī. The Lord then decided to go to the city of Benares.
