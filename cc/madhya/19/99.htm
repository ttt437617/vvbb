Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.99

prabhu kahena, — kaha, teńho pade krishna-līlā

premāveśe prabhura deha-mana āyuyāilā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; kaha — please go on speaking; teńho — he; pade — recites; krishna-līlā — the pastimes of Lord Krishna; prema-āveśe — in great ecstasy of love; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; deha-mana — body and mind; āyuyāilā — became slackened.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested Raghupati Upādhyāya to continue speaking about the pastimes of Śrī Krishna. Thus the Lord was absorbed in ecstatic love, and His mind and body slackened.

PURPORT

Our minds and bodies are always engaged in material activities. When they are activated on the spiritual platform, they slacken on the material platform.
