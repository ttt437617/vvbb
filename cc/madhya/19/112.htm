Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.112

yāńra icchā, prayāge yāñā karibe nimantrana'

eta bali' prabhu lañā karila gamana

SYNONYMS

yāńra — of whom; icchā — there is a desire; prayāge yāñā — going to Prayāga; karibe — may do; nimantrana — invitations; eta bali' — saying this; prabhu lañā — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karila gamana — he departed for Prayāga.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta said, "If anyone likes, he can go to Prayāga and extend invitations to the Lord." In this way he took the Lord with him and departed for Prayāga.
