Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.201

krishna yadi rukminīre kailā parihāsa

'krishna chādibena' — jāni' rukminīra haila trāsa

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — although; rukminīre — unto Rukminī, the first queen; kailā — did; parihāsa — joking; krishna — Lord Krishna; chādibena — will give me up; jāni' — thinking; rukminīra — of Rukminī; haila — there was; trāsa — shock.

TRANSLATION

"Although Krishna was joking with Queen Rukminī, she was thinking that He was going to give up her company, and she was therefore shocked.
