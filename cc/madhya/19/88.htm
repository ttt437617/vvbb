Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.88

bhikshā karāila prabhure sasneha yatane

rūpa-gosāñi dui-bhāiye karāila bhojane

SYNONYMS

bhikshā karāila — made to take His lunch; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sasneha — with affection; yatane — with great care; rūpa-gosāñi — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; dui-bhāiye — the two brothers; karāila bhojane — made eat.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was offered lunch with great care and affection. The brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha were also offered food.
