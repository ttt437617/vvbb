Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.90

mukha-vāsa diyā prabhure karāila śayana

āpane bhatta karena prabhura pāda-samvāhana

SYNONYMS

mukha-vāsa — spices; diyā — offering; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karāila — made to do; śayana — resting; āpane — personally; bhatta — Śrīla Vallabha Bhatta; karena — does; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāda-samvāhana — massaging the leg.

TRANSLATION

The Lord was then given spices to purify His mouth. Afterwards He was made to rest, and Vallabha Bhattācārya personally massaged His legs.
