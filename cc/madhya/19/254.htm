Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.254

mahāprabhu āilā śuni' śishta śishta jana

brāhmana, kshatriya āsi' karena daraśana

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu āilā — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has arrived; śuni' — hearing; śishta śishta jana — all respectable persons; brāhmana — belonging to the brāhmana community; kshatriya — belonging to the kshatriya community; āsi' — coming; karena daraśana — see.

TRANSLATION

Hearing that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come, all the respectable members of the brāhmana and kshatriya communities came to see Him.
