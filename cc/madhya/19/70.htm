Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.70

duńhāra mukhe nirantara krishna-nāma śuni'

bhatta kahe, prabhura kichu ińgita-bhańgī jāni'

SYNONYMS

duńhāra mukhe — in the mouths of both Rūpa Gosvāmī and his brother Vallabha; nirantara — continuously; krishna-nāma śuni' — hearing the chanting of the holy name of Krishna; bhatta kahe — Vallabha Bhattācārya said; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kichu — some; ińgita — indications; bhańgī — hints; jāni' — understanding.

TRANSLATION

Hearing the holy name constantly vibrated by the two brothers, Vallabha Bhattācārya could understand the hints of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
