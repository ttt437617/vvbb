Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.79

huńkāra kari' yamunāra jale dilā jhāńpa

prabhu dekhi' sabāra mane haila bhaya-kāńpa

SYNONYMS

huńkāra kari' — making a loud sound; yamunāra jale — in the water of the river Yamunā; dilā — gave; jhāńpa — a plunge; prabhu dekhi' — seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāra — of everyone; mane — in the mind; haila — there was; bhaya-kāńpa — fear and trembling.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, as soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the river Yamunā, He immediately made a great sound and jumped into the water. Everyone was filled with fear and trembling to see this.
