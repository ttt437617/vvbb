Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.38

prabhu caliyāchena bindu-mādhava-daraśane

laksha laksha loka āise prabhura milane

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; caliyāchena — was going; bindu-mādhava-daraśane — to see Lord Bindu Mādhava; laksha laksha loka — many hundreds of thousands of people; āise — came; prabhura — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; milane — for meeting.

TRANSLATION

At Prayāga, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see the temple of Bindu Mādhava, and many hundreds of thousands of people followed Him just to meet Him.
