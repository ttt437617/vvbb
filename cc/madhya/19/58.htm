Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.58

madhyāhna karite vipra prabhure kahilā

rūpa-gosāñi se-divasa tathāñi rahilā

SYNONYMS

madhyāhna karite — to accept lunch; vipra — the brāhmana of Deccan; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahilā — requested; rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; se-divasa — that day; tathāñi — there; rahilā — remained.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana then requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to accept His lunch. Rūpa Gosvāmī also remained there that day.
