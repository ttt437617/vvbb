Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.49

krishnera karunā kichu nā yāya varnane

vishaya-kūpa haite kādila tomā dui-jane

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; karunā — the mercy; kichu — any; nā — not; yāya — is possible; varnane — to describe; vishaya-kūpa haite — from the well of material enjoyment; kādila — delivered; tomā — you; dui-jane — both.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "It is not possible to describe Krishna's mercy, for He has delivered you both from the well of material enjoyment.
