Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.249

bhikshā karāñā miśra kahe prabhu-pāya dhari'

eka bhikshā māgi, more deha' kripā kari'

SYNONYMS

bhikshā karāñā — after offering the lunch; miśra — Tapana Miśra; kahe — said; prabhu — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāya — the lotus feet; dhari' — touching; eka bhikshā — one favor; māgi — I beg; more — unto Me; deha' — kindly deliver; kripā kari' — by Your causeless mercy.

TRANSLATION

After offering lunch to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Tapana Miśra begged a favor from the Lord and requested Him to award him mercy.
