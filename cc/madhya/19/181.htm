Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.181

sāttvika-vyabhicāri-bhāvera milane

krishna-bhakti-rasa haya amrita āsvādane

SYNONYMS

sāttvika-vyabhicāri-bhāvera — of sāttvika and vyabhicārī with sthāyibhāva; milane — by mixing; krishna-bhakti-rasa — the transcendental mellows of devotional service to the Lord; haya — become; amrita — nectarean; āsvādane — in tasting.

TRANSLATION

"When the higher standard of ecstatic love is mixed with the symptoms of sāttvika and vyabhicārī, the devotee relishes the transcendental bliss of loving Krishna in a variety of nectarean tastes.
