Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.48

śrī-rūpe dekhiyā prabhura prasanna haila mana

'utha, utha, rūpa, āisa', balilā vacana

SYNONYMS

śrī-rūpe dekhiyā — seeing Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prasanna — very pleased; haila — was; mana — mind; utha — please stand up; utha — please stand up; rūpa — My dear Rūpa; āisa — come; balilā — He said; vacana — the words.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased to see Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, and He told him, "Stand up! Stand up! My dear Rūpa, come here."
