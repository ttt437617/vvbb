Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.255

śrī-rūpa-upare prabhura yata kripā haila

atyanta vistāra-kathā sańkshepe kahila

SYNONYMS

śrī-rūpa-upare — upon Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yata — as much; kripā — mercy; haila — there was; atyanta — very much; vistāra-kathā — elaborate topics; sańkshepe — in brief; kahila — I have described.

TRANSLATION

Much mercy was thus bestowed upon Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, and I have briefly described all those topics.
