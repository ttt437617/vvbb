Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.105

prabhu kahe, — bhāla tattva śikhāilā more

eta bali' śloka pade gadgada-svare

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — said; bhāla — good; tattva — conclusions; śikhāilā more — you have taught Me; eta bali' — saying this; śloka pade — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited the full verse; gadgada-svare — in a faltering voice.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "You have certainly given first-class conclusions." After saying this, He began to recite the full verse in a faltering voice.
