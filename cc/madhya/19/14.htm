Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.14

kona mate rājā yadi more kruddha haya

tabe avyāhati haya, kariluń niścaya

SYNONYMS

kona mate — somehow or other; rājā — the Nawab; yadi — if; more — upon me; kruddha haya — becomes angry; tabe — then; avyāhati — escape; haya — there is; kariluń niścaya — I have decided.

TRANSLATION

"If the Nawab somehow or other becomes angry with me, I shall be greatly relieved. That is my conclusion."
