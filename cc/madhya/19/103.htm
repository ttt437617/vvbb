Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.103

bālya, pauganda, kaiśore, śreshtha māna' kāya?

'vayah kaiśorakam dhyeyam' — kahe upādhyāya

SYNONYMS

bālya — childhood; pauganda — the boyhood age before youth; kaiśore — the beginning of youth; śreshtha — best; māna' — you think; kāya — which; vayah — the age; kaiśorakam — kaiśora or fresh youth; dhyeyam — most worshipable; kahe — said; upādhyāya — Raghupati Upādhyāya.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked, "Of the three ages of Krishna known as childhood, boyhood and fresh youth, which do you consider best?"

Raghupati Upādhyāya replied, "Fresh youth is the best age."
