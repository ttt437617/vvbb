Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.253

mahārāshtrīya vipra āsi' tāńhāre mililā

prabhu tāńre sneha kari' kripā prakāśilā

SYNONYMS

mahārāshtrīya vipra — the Maharashtrian brāhmana; āsi' — coming; tāńhāre — him; mililā — met; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — to him; sneha kari' — showing His affection; kripā prakāśilā — distributed His mercy.

TRANSLATION

The Maharashtrian brāhmana came, and the Lord met him. Out of affection, the Lord bestowed His mercy upon him.
