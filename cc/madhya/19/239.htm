Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.239

'ājñā haya, āsi muñi śrī-carana-sańge

sahite nā pāri muñi viraha-tarańge'

SYNONYMS

ājñā haya — if there is permission; āsi — may come; muñi — I; śrī-carana-sańge — with Your Lordship; sahite — to tolerate; nā pāri — not able; muñi — I; viraha-tarańge — the waves of separation.

TRANSLATION

"If You give me permission, I shall go with Your Lordship. It is not possible for me to tolerate the waves of separation."
