Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.64

antare gara-gara prema, nahe samvarana

dekhi' camatkāra haila vallabha-bhattera mana

SYNONYMS

antare — inside; gara-gara — raged; prema — ecstatic love; nahe — there was not; samvarana — checking; dekhi' — detecting; camatkāra — astonishment; haila — there was; vallabha-bhattera mana — in the mind of Vallabha Bhatta.

TRANSLATION

Although the Lord restrained Himself externally, ecstatic love raged within. There was no checking that. Vallabha Bhatta was astonished to detect this.
