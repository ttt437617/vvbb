Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.8

danda-bandha lāgi' cauthi sañcaya karilā

bhāla-bhāla vipra-sthāne sthāpya rākhilā

SYNONYMS

danda-bandha lāgi' — in case of legal implications; cauthi — one-fourth; sañcaya karilā — he collected; bhāla-bhāla — very respectable; vipra-sthāne — in the custody of a brāhmana; sthāpya rākhilā — kept deposited.

TRANSLATION

He kept one-fourth of his wealth with a respectable brāhmana. He kept this for his personal safety because he was expecting some legal complications.
