Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.193

gokule 'kevalā' rati — aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna

purī-dvaye, vaikunthādye — aiśvarya-pravīna

SYNONYMS

gokule — in Gokula Vrindāvana; kevalā rati — flawless attachment; aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna — without reverential considerations; purī-dvaye — in two purīs, namely Mathurā-purī and Dvārakā-purī; vaikuntha-ādye — in the Vaikuntha planets; aiśvarya-pravīna — prominence of awe and reverence.

TRANSLATION

"Pure attachment without reverence is found in Gokula Vrindāvana. Attachment in which awe and reverence are prominent is found in the two cities Mathurā and Dvārakā and in Vaikuntha.
