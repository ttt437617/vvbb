Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.128

'vipra-grihe' sthūla-bhikshā, kāhāń mādhu-karī

śushka rutī-cānā civāya bhoga parihari'

SYNONYMS

vipra-grihe — in the house of a brāhmana; sthūla-bhikshā — full meals; kāhāń — sometimes; mādhu-karī — begging little by little, like honeybees; śushka — dry; rutī — bread; cānā — chickpeas; civāya — chew; bhoga parihari' — giving up all kinds of material enjoyment.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīla Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmī beg a little food from the houses of brāhmanas. Giving up all kinds of material enjoyment, they take only some dry bread and fried chickpeas.
