Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.44

dākshinātya-vipra-sane āche paricaya

sei vipra nimantriyā nila nijālaya

SYNONYMS

dākshinātya — Deccan; vipra-sane — with a brāhmana; āche — there was; paricaya — acquaintance; sei — that; vipra — brāhmana; nimantriyā — inviting; nila — brought; nija-ālaya — to his own place.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had made an acquaintance with a brāhmana from Deccan [in South India], and that brāhmana invited Him for meals and took Him to his place.
