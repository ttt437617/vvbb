Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.36

anupama mallika, tāńra nāma — 'śrī-vallabha'

rūpa-gosāñira chota-bhāi — parama-vaishnava

SYNONYMS

anupama mallika — Anupama Mallika; tāńra nāma — his name; śrī-vallabha — Śrī Vallabha; rūpa-gosāñira — of Rūpa Gosvāmī; chota-bhāi — younger brother; parama-vaishnava — great devotee.

TRANSLATION

Rūpa Gosvāmī's younger brother was a great devotee whose actual name was Śrī Vallabha, but he was given the name Anupama Mallika.
