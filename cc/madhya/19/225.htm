Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.225

'mamatā' adhika, krishne ātma-sama jñāna

ataeva sakhya-rasera vaśa bhagavān

SYNONYMS

mamatā — intimacy; adhika — increase; krishne — with Krishna; ātma-sama jñāna — the notion of equality; ataeva — therefore; sakhya-rasera — by the mellow of fraternity; vaśa — controlled; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"On the platform of sakhya-rasa, the Supreme Personality of Godhead Krishna is controlled by the devotees who are intimate with Him and think themselves equal to Him.
