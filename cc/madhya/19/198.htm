Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.198

krishnera viśva-rūpa dekhi' arjunera haila bhaya

sakhya-bhāve dhārshtya kshamāpaya kariyā vinaya

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; viśva-rūpa — the universal form; dekhi' — seeing; arjunera — of Arjuna; haila bhaya — there was fear; sakhya-bhāve — as a friend; dhārshtya — impudence; kshamāpaya — begs pardon for; kariyā — showing; vinaya — submission.

TRANSLATION

"When Krishna manifested His universal form, Arjuna became reverent and fearful, and he begged forgiveness for his past impudence toward Krishna as a friend.
