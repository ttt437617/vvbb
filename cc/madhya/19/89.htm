Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.89

bhattācārya śrī-rūpe deoyāila 'avaśesha'

tabe sei prasāda krishnadāsa pāila śesha

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Vallabha Bhattācārya; śrī-rūpe — to Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; deoyāila — offered; avaśesha — the remnants; tabe — thereafter; sei — those; prasāda — remnants of food; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa; pāila — got; śesha — the balance.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhattācārya first offered the remnants of the Lord's food to Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, and then to Krishnadāsa.
