Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.229

se amritānande bhakta saha dubena āpane

'krishna — bhakta-vaśa' guna kahe aiśvarya-jñāni-gane

SYNONYMS

se — that Lord Krishna; amrita-ānande — in spiritual happiness; bhakta — the devotee; saha — with; dubena — plunges; āpane — Himself; krishna — Krishna; bhakta-vaśa — of being subjugated by the devotee; guna — the quality; kahe — say; aiśvarya-jñāni-gane — learned scholars knowing the opulence of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"The exchange of spiritual happiness between Krishna and His devotee in which Krishna is controlled by His devotee is compared to an ocean of nectar into which the devotee and Krishna plunge. This is the verdict of learned scholars who appreciate Krishna's opulence.
