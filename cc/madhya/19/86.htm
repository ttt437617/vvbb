Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.86

savamśe sei jala mastake dharila

nūtana kaupīna-bahirvāsa parāila

SYNONYMS

sa-vamśe — with all the family members; sei — that; jala — water; mastake — on the head; dharila — sprinkled; nūtana — fresh; kaupīna — underwear; bahirvāsa — external covering; parāila — put on.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhattācārya and his whole family then sprinkled that water over their heads. They then offered the Lord new underwear and outer garments.
