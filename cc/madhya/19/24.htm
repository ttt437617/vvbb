Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.24

tabe kruddha hañā rājā kahe āra-bāra

tomāra 'bada bhāi' kare dasyu-vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; kruddha hañā — becoming angry; rājā kahe — the Nawab said; āra-bāra — again; tomāra bada bhāi — your elder brother; kare — does; dasyu-vyavahāra — the activity of a plunderer.

TRANSLATION

Becoming angry with Sanātana Gosvāmī, the Nawab said, "Your elder brother is acting just like a plunderer.
