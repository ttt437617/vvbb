Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.118

śivānanda-senera putra 'kavi-karnapūra'

'rūpera milana' sva-granthe likhiyāchena pracura

SYNONYMS

śivānanda-senera — of Śivānanda Sena; putra — the son; kavi-karnapūra — Kavi-karnapūra; rūpera milana — of the meeting with Rūpa Gosvāmī; sva-granthe — in his own book; likhiyāchena pracura — has written profusely.

TRANSLATION

In his book Caitanya-candrodaya, Kavi-karnapūra, the son of Śivānanda Sena, has elaborately described the meeting between Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
