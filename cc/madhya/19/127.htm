Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.127

"aniketa duńhe, vane yata vriksha-gana

eka eka vrikshera tale eka eka rātri śayana

SYNONYMS

aniketa — without a residence; duńhe — both of them; vane — in the forest; yata vriksha-gana — as many trees as there are; eka eka vrikshera — of one tree after another; tale — at the base; eka eka rātri — one night after another; śayana — lying down to sleep.

TRANSLATION

"The brothers actually have no fixed residence. They reside beneath trees — one night under one tree and the next night under another.
