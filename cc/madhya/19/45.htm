Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.45

vipra-grihe āsi' prabhu nibhrite vasilā

śrī-rūpa-vallabha duńhe āsiyā mililā

SYNONYMS

vipra-grihe — to the house of that brāhmana; āsi' — coming; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nibhrite — in a solitary place; vasilā — sat down; śrī-rūpa-vallabha — the two brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha; duńhe — both of them; āsiyā — coming; mililā — met Him.

TRANSLATION

While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was sitting in a solitary place in the home of that Deccan brāhmana, Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha [Anupama Mallika] came to meet Him.
