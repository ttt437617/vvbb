Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.221

śāntera guna dāsye āche, adhika — 'sevana'

ataeva dāsya-rasera ei 'dui' guna

SYNONYMS

śāntera — of the platform of śānta-rasa; guna — the qualities; dāsye — on the platform of servitude; āche — are; adhika — additional; sevana — serving; ataeva — therefore; dāsya-rasera — of the platform of dāsya-rasa; ei dui guna — these two qualities (namely śānta and dāsya).

TRANSLATION

"The qualities of śānta-rasa are also present in dāsya-rasa, but service is added. Thus the dāsya-rasa platform contains the qualities of both śānta-rasa and dāsya-rasa.
