Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.51

ei śloka padi' duńhāre kailā ālińgana

kripāte duńhāra māthāya dharilā carana

SYNONYMS

ei śloka — this verse; padi' — reciting; duńhāre — the two brothers; kailā ālińgana — embraced; kripāte — out of causeless mercy; duńhāra — of the two of them; māthāya — on the heads; dharilā — placed; carana — His feet.

TRANSLATION

After reciting this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced the two brothers, and out of His causeless mercy He placed His feet on their heads.
