Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.192

punah krishna-rati haya duita prakāra

aiśvarya-jñāna-miśrā, kevalā-bheda āra

SYNONYMS

punah — again; krishna-rati — attachment for Krishna; haya — becomes; duita — twofold; prakāra — varieties; aiśvarya-jñāna-miśrā — knowledge of Krishna mixed with a reverential attitude; kevalā — pure attachment; bheda — division; āra — other.

TRANSLATION

"Attachment for Krishna is divided into two categories. One is attachment with awe and reverence, and the other is pure attachment without reverence.
