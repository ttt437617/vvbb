Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.243

dākshinātya-vipra tāńre ghare lañā gelā

tabe dui bhāi vrindāvanere calilā

SYNONYMS

dākshinātya-vipra — the brāhmana from Deccan; tāńre — him (Rūpa Gosvāmī); ghare lañā — taking to his home; gelā — went; tabe — thereafter; dui bhāi — the two brothers; vrindāvanere — toward Vrindāvana; calilā — departed.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana from Deccan took Rūpa Gosvāmī to his home, and thereafter the two brothers departed for Vrindāvana.
