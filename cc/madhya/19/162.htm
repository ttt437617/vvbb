Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.162

'prema-phala' pāki' pade, mālī āsvādaya

latā avalambi' mālī 'kalpa-vriksha' pāya

SYNONYMS

prema-phala — the fruit of love of God; pāki' — becoming mature; pade — falls down; mālī — the gardener; āsvādaya — tastes; latā avalambi' — taking advantage of the growing bhakti-latā; mālī — the gardener; kalpa-vriksha pāya — reaches the desire tree in Goloka Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"When the fruit of devotional service becomes ripe and falls down, the gardener tastes the fruit and thus takes advantage of the creeper and reaches the desire tree of the lotus feet of Krishna in Goloka Vrindāvana.
