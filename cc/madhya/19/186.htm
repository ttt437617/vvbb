Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.186

hāsyo 'dbhutas tathā vīrah

karuno raudra ity api

bhayānakah sa-bībhatsa

iti gaunaś ca saptadhā

SYNONYMS

hāsyah — laughter; adbhutah — wonder; tathā — then; vīrah — chivalry; karunah — compassion; raudrah — anger; iti — thus; api — also; bhayānakah — fear; sah — along with; bībhatsah — disaster; iti — thus; gaunah — indirect; ca — also; saptadhā — seven kinds.

TRANSLATION

"'Besides the five direct mellows, there are seven indirect mellows, known as laughter, wonder, chivalry, compassion, anger, disaster and fear.'

PURPORT

This verse is found in the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (2.5.116).
