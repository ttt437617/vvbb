Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.245

rātre teńho svapna dekhe, — prabhu āilā ghare

prātah-kāle āsi' rahe grāmera bāhire

SYNONYMS

rātre — at night; teńho — he (Candraśekhara); svapna — a dream; dekhe — saw; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — has come; ghare — to his home; prātah-kāle — in the morning; āsi' — coming; rahe — he remained; grāmera bāhire — outside the city.

TRANSLATION

In a dream Candraśekhara had seen that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come to his home; therefore in the morning Candraśekhara went outside the city to receive the Lord.
