Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.250

yāvat tomāra haya kāśī-pure sthiti

mora ghara vinā bhikshā nā karibā kati

SYNONYMS

yāvat — as long as; tomāra — Your; haya — there is; kāśī-pure — at Vārānasī; sthiti — stay; mora ghara — my place; vinā — except; bhikshā — lunch; nā karibā — kindly do not take; kati — anywhere.

TRANSLATION

Tapana Miśra said, "As long as Your Lordship stays in Vārānasī, please do not accept an invitation from anyone but me."
