Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.1

vrindāvanīyām rasa-keli-vārtām

kālena luptām nija-śaktim utkah

sañcārya rūpe vyatanot punah sa

prabhur vidhau prāg iva loka-srishtim

SYNONYMS

vrindāvanīyām — related to Vrindāvana; rasa-keli-vārtām — talks about the pastimes of Śrī Krishna; kālena — with the course of time; luptām — lost; nija-śaktim — His personal potency; utkah — being eager; sañcārya — infusing; rūpe — to Rūpa Gosvāmī; vyatanot — manifested; punah — again; sah — He; prabhuh — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vidhau — unto Lord Brahmā; prāk iva — as formerly; loka-srishtim — the creation of this cosmic manifestation.

TRANSLATION

Before the creation of this cosmic manifestation, the Lord enlightened the heart of Lord Brahmā with the details of the creation and manifested the Vedic knowledge. In exactly the same way, the Lord, being anxious to revive the Vrindāvana pastimes of Lord Krishna, impregnated the heart of Rūpa Gosvāmī with spiritual potency. By this potency, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī could revive the activities of Krishna in Vrindāvana, activities almost lost to memory. In this way, He spread Krishna consciousness throughout the world.
