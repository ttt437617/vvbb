Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.248

nija ghare lañā prabhure bhikshā karāila

bhattācārye candraśekhara nimantrana kaila

SYNONYMS

nija ghare — to his own place; lañā — taking; prabhure — to the Lord; bhikshā karāila — offered lunch; bhattācārye — unto Balabhadra Bhattācārya; candraśekhara — Candraśekhara; nimantrana — invitation; kaila — made.

TRANSLATION

Tapana Miśra took Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his house and gave Him lunch. Candraśekhara invited Balabhadra Bhattācārya to take lunch at his home.
