Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.182

yaiche dadhi, sitā, ghrita, marīca, karpūra

milane, 'rasālā' haya amrita madhura

SYNONYMS

yaiche — just as; dadhi — yogurt; sitā — sugar candy; ghrita — clarified butter; marīca — black pepper; karpūra — camphor; milane — in mixing together; rasālā — very tasteful; haya — becomes; amrita — nectarean; madhura — and sweet.

TRANSLATION

"These tastes are like a combination of yogurt, sugar candy, ghee [clarified butter], black pepper and camphor and are as palatable as sweet nectar.
