Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.40

gańgā-yamunā prayāga nārila dubāite

prabhu dubāila krishna-premera vanyāte

SYNONYMS

gańgā-yamunā — the river Ganges and river Yamunā; prayāga — Prayāga; nārila — were not able; dubāite — to flood; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dubāila — flooded; krishna-premera — of ecstatic love of Krishna; vanyāte — in an inundation.

TRANSLATION

Prayāga is located at the confluence of two rivers — the Ganges and the Yamunā. Although these rivers were not able to flood Prayāga with water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inundated the whole area with waves of ecstatic love for Krishna.
