Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.191

madhura-rase bhakta-mukhya — vraje gopī-gana

mahishī-gana, lakshmī-gana, asańkhya ganana

SYNONYMS

madhura-rase — in the mellow of conjugal love; bhakta-mukhya — the chief devotees; vraje — in Vrindāvana; gopī-gana — the gopīs; mahishī-gana — the queens in Dvārakā; lakshmī-gana — the goddesses of fortune in Vaikuntha; asańkhya ganana — of innumerable reckoning.

TRANSLATION

"The chief devotees in conjugal love are the gopīs in Vrindāvana, the queens in Dvārakā and the goddesses of fortune in Vaikuntha. These devotees are innumerable.
