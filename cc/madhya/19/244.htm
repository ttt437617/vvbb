Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.244

mahāprabhu cali' cali' āilā vārānasī

candraśekhara mililā grāmera bāhire āsi'

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; cali' cali' — walking and walking; āilā — arrived; vārānasī — at Vārānasī; candraśekhara — Candraśekhara; mililā — He met; grāmera — of the village; bāhire — outside; āsi' — coming.

TRANSLATION

After walking and walking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally arrived at Vārānasī, where He met Candraśekhara, who was coming out of the city.
