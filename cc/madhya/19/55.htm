Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.55

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre nikate vasāilā

'sanātanera vārtā kaha' — tāńhāre puchilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — them; nikate — near Him; vasāilā — sat down; sanātanera vārtā — news of Sanātana; kaha — please tell; tāńhāre — them; puchilā — questioned.

TRANSLATION

After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat them down by His side and asked them, "What news do you have of Sanātana?"
