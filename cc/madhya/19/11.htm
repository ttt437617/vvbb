Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.11

rūpa-gosāñi nīlācale pāthāila dui-jana

prabhu yabe vrindāvana karena gamana

SYNONYMS

rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; pāthāila — sent; dui-jana — two persons; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yabe — when; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; karena — makes; gamana — departure.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī sent two people to Jagannātha Purī to find out when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would depart for Vrindāvana.
