Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.100

prema dekhi' upādhyāyera haila camatkāra

'manushya nahe, ińho — krishna' — karila nirdhāra

SYNONYMS

prema dekhi' — seeing His ecstatic love; upādhyāyera — of Raghupati Upādhyāya; haila — there was; camatkāra — wonder; manushya nahe — not a human being; ińho — He; krishna — Lord Krishna Himself; karila nirdhāra — made assessment.

TRANSLATION

When Raghupati Upādhyāya saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic symptoms, he decided that the Lord was not a human being but Krishna Himself.
