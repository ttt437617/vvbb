Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.30

tabe tāńre bāndhi' rākhi' karilā gamana

ethā nīlācala haite prabhu calilā vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; tāńre — him; bāndhi' — arresting; rākhi' — keeping; karilā gamana — he went away; ethā — at this time; nīlācala haite — from Jagannātha Purī; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calilā vrindāvana — departed for Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

The Nawab again arrested Sanātana Gosvāmī and kept him in prison. At this time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Vrindāvana from Jagannātha Purī.
