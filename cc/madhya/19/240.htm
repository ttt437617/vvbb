Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.240

prabhu kahe, — tomāra kartavya, āmāra vacana

nikate āsiyācha tumi, yāha vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; tomāra kartavya — your duty; āmāra vacana — My order; nikate āsiyācha — have come near; tumi — you; yāha — go; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Your duty is to carry out My order. You have come near Vrindāvana. Now you should go there.
