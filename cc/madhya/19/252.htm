Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.252

eta jāni' tāńra bhikshā kailā ańgīkāra

vāsā-nishthā kailā candraśekharera ghara

SYNONYMS

eta jāni' — on this understanding; tāńra — His; bhikshā — lunch; kailā ańgīkāra — He accepted; vāsā-nishthā — residence; kailā — made; candraśekharera ghara — the house of Candraśekhara.

TRANSLATION

With this understanding, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu agreed to accept lunch at the place of Tapana Miśra. The Lord made His residence at the home of Candraśekhara.
