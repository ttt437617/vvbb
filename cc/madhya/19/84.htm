Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.84

bhaye bhatta sańge rahe, madhyāhna karāñā

nija-grihe ānilā prabhure sańgete lañā

SYNONYMS

bhaye — with fear; bhatta — Vallabha Bhattācārya; sańge — in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's association; rahe — remains; madhyāhna karāñā — after arranging for His bath; nija-grihe — to his own home; ānilā — brought; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sańgete — in the company; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

Fearing for the Lord's welfare, Vallabha Bhattācārya stayed in His association. After arranging for His bath, the Bhattācārya took the Lord to his own house.
