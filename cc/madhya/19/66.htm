Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.66

dui-bhāi dūra haite bhūmite padiyā

bhatte dandavat kailā ati dīna hañā

SYNONYMS

dui-bhāi — the two brothers; dūra haite — from a distance; bhūmite — on the ground; padiyā — falling flat; bhatte — to Vallabha Bhatta; dandavat kailā — offered obeisances; ati dīna hañā — being very humble.

TRANSLATION

From a distance, the brothers Rūpa Gosvāmī and Śrī Vallabha fell on the ground and offered obeisances to Vallabha Bhatta with great humility.
