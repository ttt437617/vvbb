Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.26

sanātana kahe, — tumi svatantra gaudeśvara

ye yei dosha kare, deha' tāra phala

SYNONYMS

sanātana kahe — Sanātana Gosvāmī said; tumi — you; svatantra — independent; gauda-īśvara — the ruler of Bengal; ye yei — whatever; dosha — faults; kare — one commits; deha' — you award; tāra phala — the results of that.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "You are the supreme ruler of Bengal and are completely independent. Whenever someone commits a fault, you punish him accordingly."
