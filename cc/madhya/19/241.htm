Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.241

vrindāvana haite tumi gauda-deśa diyā

āmāre milibā nīlācalete āsiyā

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana haite — from Vrindāvana; tumi — you; gauda-deśa diyā — by way of Bengal; āmāre — Me; milibā — will meet; nīlācalete — at Jagannātha Purī; āsiyā — coming.

TRANSLATION

"Later, you can go from Vrindāvana to Jagannātha Purī through Bengal [Gauda-deśa]. There you will meet Me again."
