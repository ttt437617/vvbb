Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.94

śuni' ānandita haila upādhyāyera mana

prabhu tāńre kahila, — 'kaha krishnera varnana'

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; ānandita — very pleased; haila — became; upādhyāyera mana — the mind of Upādhyāya; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — to him; kahila — spoke; kaha krishnera varnana — just try to describe Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Raghupati Upādhyāya was very pleased to hear the Lord's blessings. The Lord then asked him to describe Krishna.
