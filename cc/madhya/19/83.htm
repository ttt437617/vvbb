Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.83

deśa-pātra dekhi' mahāprabhu dhairya ha-ila

ādāilera ghāte naukā āsi' uttarila

SYNONYMS

deśa-pātra dekhi' — seeing the circumstances; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhairya ha-ila — became calm; ādāilera ghāte — at the shore of the village Ādāila; naukā — the boat; āsi' — coming; uttarila — landed.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the circumstances, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu finally became calm so that the boat was able to reach the shore of Ādāila and land there.
