Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.124

keha yadi deśe yāya dekhi' vrindāvana

tāńre praśna karena prabhura pārishada-gana

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; yadi — if; deśe — to his country; yāya — goes; dekhi' — after seeing; vrindāvana — Vrindāvana; tāńre — unto that person; praśna karena — put questions; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pārishada-gana — personal associates.

TRANSLATION

If someone returned to his country after seeing Vrindāvana, the associates of the Lord would ask him questions.
