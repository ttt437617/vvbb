Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.113

gańgā-pathe mahāprabhure naukāte vasāñā

prayāge āilā bhatta gosāñire lañā

SYNONYMS

gańgā-pathe — on the Ganges; mahāprabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; naukāte vasāñā — making to sit down on the boat; prayāge āilā — went to Prayāga; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; gosāñire lañā — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhattācārya avoided the river Yamunā. Putting the Lord on a boat in the river Ganges, he went with Him to Prayāga.
