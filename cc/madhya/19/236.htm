Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.236

bhāvite bhāvite krishna sphuraye antare

krishna-kripāya ajña pāya rasa-sindhu-pāre

SYNONYMS

bhāvite bhāvite — in this way when one is strictly in thought; krishna — Lord Krishna; sphuraye antare — manifests within; krishna-kripāya — by the mercy of Krishna; ajña — one not expert in knowledge; pāya — reaches; rasa-sindhu-pāre — the far shore of the ocean of transcendental mellows.

TRANSLATION

"When one thinks of Krishna constantly, love for Him manifests within the heart. Even though one may be ignorant, one can reach the far shore of the ocean of transcendental love by Lord Krishna's mercy."
