Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.62

teńho dandavat kaila, prabhu kailā ālińgana

dui jane krishna-kathā haila kata-kshana

SYNONYMS

teńho — he; dandavat — obeisances; kaila — made; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā — did; ālińgana — embracing; dui jane — between the two of them; krishna-kathā — topics about Lord Krishna; haila — there were; kata-kshana — for some time.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhattācārya offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu his obeisances, and the Lord embraced him. After that, they discussed topics about Krishna for some time.
