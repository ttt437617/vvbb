Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.57

prabhu kahe, — sanātanera hañāche mocana

acirāt āmā-saha ha-ibe milana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; sanātanera — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; hañāche — there has been; mocana — release; acirāt — very soon; āmā-saha — with Me; ha-ibe milana — there will be meeting.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately replied, "Sanātana has already been released from his confinement, and he will very soon meet with Me."
