Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.39

keha kānde, keha hāse, keha nāce, gāya

'krishna' 'krishna' bali' keha gadāgadi yāya

SYNONYMS

keha kānde — some cried; keha hāse — some laughed; keha nāce — some danced; gāya — chanted; krishna krishna bali' — saying "Krishna, Krishna"; keha — some; gadāgadi yāya — rolled on the ground.

TRANSLATION

Some of the people following the Lord were crying. Some were laughing, some dancing and some chanting. Indeed, some of them were rolling on the ground, exclaiming "Krishna! Krishna!"
