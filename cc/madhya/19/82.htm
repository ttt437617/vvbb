Madhya-līlāChapter 19: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 19.82

yadyapi bhattera āge prabhura dhairya haila mana

durvāra udbhata prema nahe samvarana

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; bhattera — of Vallabhācārya; āge — in front; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhairya — patient; haila — was; mana — the mind; durvāra — difficult to stop; udbhata — wonderful; prema — ecstatic love; nahe — there is not; samvarana — checking.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tried to restrain Himself as far as possible before Vallabhācārya, but although He tried to keep calm, His ecstatic love could not be checked.
