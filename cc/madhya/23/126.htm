Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.126

prabhura upadeśāmrita śune yei jana

acirāt milaye tāńre krishna-prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; upadeśa-amrita — the nectar of the instructions; śune — hears; yei jana — anyone who; acirāt — without delay; milaye — meets; tāńre — him; krishna-prema-dhana — the treasure of love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Whoever hears these instructions given to Sanātana Gosvāmī by the Lord comes very soon to realize love of God, Krishna.
