Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.28

'krishna kripā karibena' — dridha kari' jāne

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kripā karibena — will show His mercy; dridha kari' — making firm; jāne — he believes.

TRANSLATION

"A fully surrendered devotee always hopes that Lord Krishna will be kind to him. This hope is very firm in him.
