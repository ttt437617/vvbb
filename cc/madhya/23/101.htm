Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.101

sańkshepe kahiluń ei 'prayojana'-vivarana

pañcama-purushārtha — ei 'krishna-prema'-dhana

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe kahiluń — briefly I have spoken; ei — this; prayojana-vivarana — description of the ultimate achievement; pañcama-purusha-artha — the fifth and ultimate goal of life; ei — this; krishna-prema-dhana — the treasure of love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"This brief description is an elaboration of the ultimate goal of life. Indeed, this is the fifth and ultimate goal, which is beyond the platform of liberation. It is called krishna-prema-dhana, the treasure of love for Krishna.
