Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.22

krishna-sambandha vinā kāla vyartha nāhi yāya

SYNONYMS

krishna-sambandha vinā — without a connection with Krishna; kāla — time; vyartha — useless; nāhi yāya — does not become.

TRANSLATION

"Not a moment should be lost. Every moment should be utilized for Krishna or connected with Him.

PURPORT

Mahārāja Parīkshit's expression of anxiety is explained in this verse. He says, "Let whatever is destined to happen take place. It doesn't matter. Just let me see that not a moment of my time is wasted without a relationship with Krishna." One has to tolerate all obstacles on the path of Krishna consciousness, and one has to see that not a moment of his life is wasted outside of Krishna's service.
