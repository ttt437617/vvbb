Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.102

pūrve prayāge āmi rasera vicāre

tomāra bhāi rūpe kailuń śakti-sañcāre

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; prayāge — in Prayāga; āmi — I; rasera vicāre — in consideration of different mellows; tomāra bhāi — your brother; rūpe — unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; kailuń — I have done; śakti-sañcāre — endowment of all power.

TRANSLATION

"Previously I empowered your brother Rūpa Gosvāmī to understand these mellows. I did this while instructing him at the Daśāśvamedha-ghāta in Prayāga.
