Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.94

ei rasa anubhave yaiche bhakta-gana

yaiche rasa haya, śuna tāhāra lakshana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; rasa — mellow; anubhave — realize; yaiche — how; bhakta-gana — the devotees; yaiche — how; rasa — the mellow; haya — appears; śuna — hear; tāhāra — of them; lakshana — the symptoms.

TRANSLATION

"Now hear how the mellows appear and how they are realized by the devotees on different transcendental platforms.
