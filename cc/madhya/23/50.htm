Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.50

dvividha 'vibhāva', — ālambana, uddīpana

vamśī-svarādi — 'uddīpana', krishnādi — 'ālambana'

SYNONYMS

dvi-vidha — two kinds; vibhāva — particular ecstasy; ālambana — the support; uddīpana — awakening; vamśī-svara-ādi — such as the vibration of the flute; uddīpana — exciting; krishna-ādi — Krishna and others; ālambana — the support.

TRANSLATION

"There are two kinds of particular ecstasies [vibhāva]. One is called the support, and the other is called the awakening. The vibration of Krishna's flute is an example of the awakening, and Lord Krishna Himself is an example of the support.
