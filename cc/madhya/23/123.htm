Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.123

'muñi ye śikhāluń tore sphuruka sakala'

ei tomāra vara haite habe mora bala"

SYNONYMS

muñi — I; ye — whatever; śikhāluń — have instructed; tore — unto you; sphuruka sakala — let it be manifested; ei — this; tomāra vara — Your benediction; haite — from; habe — there will be; mora bala — my strength.

TRANSLATION

"Now, will You please tell me, 'Let whatever I have instructed all be fully manifested unto you.' By blessing me in this way, You will give me strength to describe all this."
