Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.121

tumi ye kahilā, ei siddhāntāmrita-sindhu

mora mana chuńite nāre ihāra eka-bindu

SYNONYMS

tumi ye — You; kahilā — have spoken; ei — this; siddhānta-amrita-sindhu — the ocean of the ambrosia of conclusive truth; mora mana — my mind; chuńite — to touch; nāre — is not able; ihāra — of it; eka-bindu — even a drop.

TRANSLATION

"The conclusions that You have told me are the ocean of the ambrosia of truth. My mind is unable to approach even a drop of that ocean.
