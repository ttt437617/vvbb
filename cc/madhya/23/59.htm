Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.59

'mādane' — cumbanādi haya ananta vibheda

'udghūrnā', 'citra-jalpa' — 'mohane' dui bheda

SYNONYMS

mādane — in the stage of mādana; cumbana-ādi — kissing and similar activities; haya — are; ananta vibheda — unlimited divisions; udghūrnā — unsteadiness; citra-jalpa — various mad talks; mohane — the stage of mohana; dui bheda — two divisions.

TRANSLATION

"On the mādana platform there are kissing and many other symptoms, which are unlimited. In the mohana stage, there are two divisions — udghūrnā [unsteadiness] and citra-jalpa [varieties of mad emotional talks].

PURPORT

For further information, see Madhya-līlā 1.87.
