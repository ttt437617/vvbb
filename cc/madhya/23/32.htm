Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.32

nāma-gāne sadā ruci, laya krishna-nāma

SYNONYMS

nāma-gāne — in chanting the holy names; sadā — constantly; ruci — taste, relish; laya — takes; krishna-nāma — the Hare Krishna mantra.

TRANSLATION

"Due to having great relish for the holy name, one is inclined to chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra constantly.
