Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.11

anartha-nivritti haile bhaktye 'nishthā' haya

nishthā haite śravanādye 'ruci' upajaya

anartha-nivritti — disappearance of all unwanted contamination; haile — when there is; bhaktye — in devotional service; nishthā — firm faith; haya — there is; nishthā haite — from such firm faith; śravana-ādye — in hearing, chanting and so on; ruci — taste; upajaya — awakens

TRANSLATION

"When one is freed from all unwanted contamination, he advances with firm faith. When firm faith in devotional service awakens, a taste for hearing and chanting also awakens.
