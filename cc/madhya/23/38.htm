Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.38

krishne 'ratira' cihna ei kailuń vivarana

'krishna-premera' cihna ebe śuna sanātana

SYNONYMS

krishne — for Krishna; ratira — of attraction; cihna — the symptoms; ei — all these; kailuń vivarana — I have described; krishna-premera — of love for Lord Krishna; cihna — the symptoms; ebe — now; śuna sanātana — please hear, Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

"These are the symptoms of a person who has developed attraction [bhāva] for Krishna. Now let Me describe the symptoms of a person who is actually elevated to love of Krishna. O Sanātana, please hear this from Me.
