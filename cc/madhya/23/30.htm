Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.30

samutkanthā haya sadā lālasā-pradhāna

SYNONYMS

samutkanthā — eagerness; haya — is; sadā — always; lālasā — ardent desire; pradhāna — chiefly characterized by.

TRANSLATION

"This eagerness is chiefly characterized by an ardent desire to associate with the Lord.
