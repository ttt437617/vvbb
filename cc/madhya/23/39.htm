Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.39

yāńra citte krishna-premā karaye udaya

tāńra vākya, kriyā, mudrā vijñeha nā bujhaya

yāńra citte — in whose heart; krishna-premā — love of Krishna; karaye udaya — awakens; tāńra — his; vākya — words; kriyā — activities; mudrā — symptoms; vijñeha — even a learned scholar; nā bujhaya — does not understand

TRANSLATION

"Even the most learned man cannot understand the words, activities and symptoms of a person situated in love of Godhead.
