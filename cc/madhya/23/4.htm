Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.4

krishne rati gādha haile 'prema'-abhidhāna

krishna-bhakti-rasera ei 'sthāyi-bhāva'-nāma

SYNONYMS

krishne — unto Lord Krishna; rati — affection; gādha — deep; haile — when it becomes; prema-abhidhāna — called love of God; krishna-bhakti-rasera — of the mellows of devotional service to Krishna; ei — this; sthāyi-bhāva-nāma — called sthāyi-bhāva.

TRANSLATION

"When affection for Krishna becomes deeper, one attains love of Godhead in devotional service. Such a position is called sthāyi-bhāva, permanent enjoyment of the mellows of devotional service to Krishna.
