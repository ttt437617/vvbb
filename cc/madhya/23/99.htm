Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.99

ei rasa-āsvāda nāhi abhaktera gane

krishna-bhakta-gana kare rasa āsvādane

SYNONYMS

ei — this; rasa-āsvāda — tasting of transcendental mellows; nāhi — not; abhaktera gane — among nondevotees; krishna-bhakta-gane — the pure devotees of Lord Krishna; kare — do; rasa — these transcendental mellows; āsvādane — tasting.

TRANSLATION

"The exchange between Krishna and different devotees situated in different transcendental mellows is not to be experienced by nondevotees. Advanced devotees can understand and appreciate the different varieties of devotional service reciprocated with the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
