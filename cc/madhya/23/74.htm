Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.74

dakshino vinayī hrīmān

śaranāgata-pālakah

sukhī bhakta-suhrit prema-

vaśyah sarva-śubhań-karah

SYNONYMS

dakshinah — simple and liberal; vinayī — humble; hrī-mān — bashful when glorified; śarana-āgata-pālakah — protector of the surrendered souls; sukhī — always happy; bhakta-suhrit — well-wisher of the devotees; prema-vaśyah — submissive to love; sarva-śubham-karah — all-auspicious.

TRANSLATION

"'Krishna is very simple and liberal, He is humble and bashful, and He is the protector of the surrendered souls. He is very happy, and He is always the well-wisher of His devotees. He is all-auspicious, and He is submissive to love.
