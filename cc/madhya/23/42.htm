Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.42

premā krame bādi' haya — sneha, māna, pranaya

rāga, anurāga, bhāva, mahābhāva haya

SYNONYMS

premā — love of God; krame — gradually; bādi' — increasing; haya — is; sneha — affection; māna — indignation due to affection; pranaya — love; rāga — attachment; anurāga — subattachment; bhāva — ecstasy; mahā-bhāva — exalted ecstasy; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

"Love of Godhead increases and is manifested as affection, counterlove, love, attachment, subattachment, ecstasy and sublime ecstasy.
