Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.71

vividhādbhuta-bhāshā-vit

satya-vākyah priyam-vadah

vāvadūkah su-pāndityo

buddhimān pratibhānvitah

SYNONYMS

vividha — various; adbhuta — wonderful; bhāshā-vit — knower of languages; satya-vākyah — whose words are truthful; priyam-vadah — who speaks very pleasingly; vāvadūkah — expert in speaking; su-pāndityah — very learned; buddhi-mān — very wise; pratibhā-anvitah — genius.

TRANSLATION

"'Krishna is the linguist of all wonderful languages. He is a truthful and very pleasing speaker. He is expert in speaking, and He is a very wise, learned scholar and a genius.
