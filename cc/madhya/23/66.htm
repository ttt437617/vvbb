Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.66

vrajendra-nandana krishna — nāyaka-śiromani

nāyikāra śiromani — rādhā-thākurānī

SYNONYMS

vrajendra-nandana krishna — Lord Krishna, the son of Mahārāja Nanda; nāyaka-śiromani — best of all heroes; nāyikāra śiromani — the best of all heroines; rādhā-thākurānī — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, who appeared as the son of Nanda Mahārāja, is the supreme hero in all dealings. Similarly, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is the topmost heroine in all dealings.
