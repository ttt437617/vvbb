Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.36

krishna-līlā-sthāne kare sarvadā vasati

SYNONYMS

krishna-līlā-sthāne — in the place where Krishna has His pastimes; kare — makes; sarvadā — always; vasati — abode.

TRANSLATION

"A devotee absorbed in ecstatic emotion for Krishna always resides in a place where Krishna's pastimes were performed.
