Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.20

ei nava prīty-ańkura yāńra citte haya

prākrita-kshobhe tāńra kshobha nāhi haya

SYNONYMS

ei — this; nava — nine; prīti-ańkura — fructification of the seed of love; yāńra — of whom; citte — in the mind; haya — there is; prākrita — material; kshobhe — in agitation; tāńra — his; kshobha — agitation; nāhi haya — there is not.

TRANSLATION

"If love for Krishna in a seedling state has fructified in one's heart, one is not agitated by material things.
