Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.122

pańgu nācāite yadi haya tomāra mana

vara deha' mora māthe dhariyā carana

SYNONYMS

pańgu — lame man; nācāite — to make dance; yadi — if; haya — it is; tomāra mana — Your mind; vara — a benediction; deha' — kindly give; mora māthe — on my head; dhariyā — holding; carana — Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"If You want to make a lame man like me dance, kindly bestow Your transcendental blessings by placing Your lotus feet on my head.
