Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.113

ye tu dharmāmritam idam

yathoktam paryupāsate

śraddadhānā mat-paramā

bhaktās te 'tīva me priyāh

SYNONYMS

ye — the devotees who; tu — but; dharma-amritam — eternal religious principle of Krishna consciousness; idam — this; yathā-uktam — as mentioned above; paryupāsate — worship; śraddadhānāh — having faith and devotion; mat-paramāh — accepting Me as the Supreme or the ultimate goal of life; bhaktāh — such devotees; te — they; atīva — very; me — to Me; priyāh — dear.

TRANSLATION

"'Devotees who follow these imperishable religious principles of Krishna consciousness with great faith and devotion, fully accepting Me as the supreme goal, are very, very dear to Me.'
