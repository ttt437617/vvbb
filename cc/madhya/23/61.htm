Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.61

udghūrnā, vivaśa-ceshtā — divyonmāda-nāma

virahe krishna-sphūrti, āpanāke 'krishna'-jñāna

SYNONYMS

udghūrnā — unsteadiness; vivaśa-ceshtā — boastful activities; divya-unmāda-nāma — named transcendental madness; virahe — in separation; krishna-sphūrti — manifestation of Krishna; āpanāke — oneself; krishna-jñāna — thinking as Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Udghūrnā [unsteadiness] and vivaśa-ceshtā [boastful activities] are aspects of transcendental madness. In separation from Krishna, one experiences the manifestation of Krishna, and one thinks oneself to be Krishna.
