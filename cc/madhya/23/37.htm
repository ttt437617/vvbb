Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.37

kadāham yamunā-tīre

nāmāni tava kīrtayan

udbāshpah pundarīkāksha

racayishyāmi tāndavam

SYNONYMS

kadā — when; aham — I; yamunā-tīre — on the bank of the Yamunā; nāmāni — holy names; tava — Your; kīrtayan — chanting; udbāshpah — full of tears; pundarīka-aksha — O lotus-eyed one; racayishyāmi — I shall create; tāndavam — dancing like a madman.

TRANSLATION

"'O Lord Pundarīkāksha, while chanting Your holy name with tears in my eyes, when shall I dance in ecstasy on the bank of the Yamunā?'

PURPORT

This verse is found in the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (1.2.156).
