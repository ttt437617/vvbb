Madhya-līlāChapter 23: Life's Ultimate Goal — Love of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 23.12

ruci haite bhaktye haya 'āsakti' pracura

āsakti haite citte janme krishne prīty-ańkura

SYNONYMS

ruci haite — from such a taste; bhaktye — in devotional service; haya — there is; āsakti — attachment; pracura — deep; āsakti haite — from attachment; citte — within the heart; janme — appears; krishne — for Krishna; prīti-ańkura — the seed of affection.

TRANSLATION

"After taste is awakened, a deep attachment arises, and from that attachment the seed of love for Krishna grows in the heart.
