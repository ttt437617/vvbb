Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.5

rātrye uthi' vana-pathe palāñā yāba

ekākī yāiba, kāhoń sańge nā la-iba

SYNONYMS

rātrye uthi' — rising at night; vana-pathe — on the road through the forest; palāñā yāba — I shall go away secretly; ekākī yāiba — I shall go alone; kāhoń — anyone; sańge — with Me; nā la-iba — I shall not take.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I shall leave early in the morning and go incognito, taking the road through the forest. I shall go alone — I shall not take anyone with Me.
