Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.125

"tāra āge yabe āmi tomāra nāma la-ila

seha tomāra nāma jāne, — āpane kahila

SYNONYMS

tāra āge — before him; yabe — when; āmi — I; tomāra — Your; nāma — name; la-ila — uttered; seha — he; tomāra — Your; nāma — name; jāne — knows; āpane kahila — he said himself.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana said, "As soon as I uttered Your name before him, he immediately confirmed the fact that he knew Your name.
