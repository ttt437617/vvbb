Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.108

ājānu-lambita bhuja, kamala-nayana

yata kichu īśvarera sarva sal-lakshana

SYNONYMS

ājānu-lambita — reaching down to the knees; bhuja — arms; kamala-nayana — eyes like the petals of a lotus flower; yata — as many as; kichu — any; īśvarera — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sarva — all; sat-lakshana — transcendental symptoms.

TRANSLATION

"He has arms that extend to His knees, and His eyes are like the petals of a lotus. In His person are all the transcendental symptoms of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
