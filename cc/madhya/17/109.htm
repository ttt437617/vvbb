Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.109

tāhā dekhi' jñāna haya — 'ei nārāyana

yei tāńre dekhe, kare krishna-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

tāhā dekhi' — seeing that; jñāna haya — one comes to the conclusion; ei nārāyana — He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana Himself; yei — anyone who; tāńre — Him; dekhe — sees; kare — performs; krishna-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy name of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"When one sees all these features, one takes Him to be Nārāyana Himself. Whoever sees Him immediately begins to chant the holy name of Krishna.
