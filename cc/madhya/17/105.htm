Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.105

eka vipra dekhi' āilā prabhura vyavahāra

prakāśānanda-āge kahe caritra tāńhāra

SYNONYMS

eka vipra — a brāhmana; dekhi' — seeing; āilā — came; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vyavahāra — activities; prakāśānanda-āge — before the Māyāvādī sannyāsī Prakāśānanda; kahe — says; caritra tāńhāra — His characteristics.

TRANSLATION

A brāhmana who saw the wonderful behavior of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came to Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī and described the Lord's characteristics.
