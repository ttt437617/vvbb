Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.78

muñi chāra, more tumi sańge lañā āilā

kripā kari' mora hāte 'prabhu' bhikshā kailā

SYNONYMS

muñi — I; chāra — most fallen; more — me; tumi — You; sańge — with; lañā — taking; āilā — have come; kripā kari' — showing great mercy; mora hāte — from my hand; prabhu — my Lord; bhikshā kailā — You accepted food.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, I am most fallen, yet You have brought me with You. Showing great mercy, You have accepted food prepared by me.
