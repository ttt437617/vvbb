Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.225

prabhura premāveśa dekhi' brāhmana — vismita

prabhura rakshā lāgi' vipra ha-ilā cintita

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-āveśa — ecstatic love; dekhi' — seeing; brāhmana — the brāhmana; vismita — astonished; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rakshā lāgi' — for the protection; vipra — the brāhmana; ha-ilā — became; cintita — very anxious.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana was astounded to see the symptoms of ecstatic love exhibited by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He then became anxious to give the Lord protection.
