Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.176

bhikshā lāgi' bhattācārye karāilā randhana

tabe mahāprabhu hāsi' balilā vacana

SYNONYMS

bhikshā lāgi' — for lunch; bhattācārye — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; karāilā randhana — made to cook; tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hāsi' — smiling; balilā vacana — said these words.

TRANSLATION

He asked Balabhadra Bhattācārya to cook Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lunch. At that time the Lord, smiling, spoke as follows.
