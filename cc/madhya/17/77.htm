Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.77

teńho kahena, — "tumi 'krishna', tumi 'dayāmaya'

adhama jīva muñi, more ha-ilā sadaya

SYNONYMS

teńho kahena — the Bhattācārya said; tumi krishna — You are Krishna Himself; tumi — You; dayā-maya — merciful; adhama — the lowest of the low; jīva — living entity; muñi — I; more — unto me; ha-ilā — You have been; sa-daya — favorable.

TRANSLATION

Balabhadra Bhattācārya replied, "My dear Lord, You are Krishna Himself, and therefore You are merciful. I am a fallen living entity, but You have bestowed a great favor upon me.
