Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.204

prati vriksha-latā prabhu karena ālińgana

pushpādi dhyāne karena krishne samarpana

SYNONYMS

prati — each and every; vriksha-latā — tree and creeper; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena ālińgana — embraced; pushpa-ādi — all the flowers and fruits; dhyāne — in meditation; karena — do; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; samarpana — offering.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to embrace each and every tree and creeper, and they began to offer their fruits and flowers as if in meditation.
