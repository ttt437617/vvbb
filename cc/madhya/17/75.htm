Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.75

kripāra samudra, dīna-hīne dayāmaya

krishna-kripā vinā kona 'sukha' nāhi haya"

SYNONYMS

kripāra samudra — ocean of mercy; dīna-hīne — unto the poor and fallen; dayā-maya — very merciful; krishna-kripā — the mercy of Krishna; vinā — without; kona — any; sukha — happiness; nāhi haya — there is not.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is an ocean of mercy. He is especially merciful to the poor and fallen. Without His mercy, there is no possibility of happiness."
