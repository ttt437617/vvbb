Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.232

tabu likhibāre nāre tāra eka kana

uddeśa karite kari dig-daraśana

SYNONYMS

tabu — yet; likhibāre — to write; nāre — is not able; tāra — of that; eka — one; kana — fragment; uddeśa — indication; karite — to make; kari — I perform; dik-daraśana — pointing out the direction.

TRANSLATION

Since Lord Ananta Himself cannot describe even a fragment of these pastimes, I am simply pointing out the direction.
