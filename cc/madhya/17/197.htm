Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.197

kashte-srishtye dhenu saba rākhila goyāla

prabhu-kantha-dhvani śuni' āise mrigī-pāla

SYNONYMS

kashte-srishtye — with great difficulty; dhenu — the cows; saba — all; rākhila — kept back; goyāla — the cowherd men; prabhu-kantha-dhvani — the musical voice of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śuni' — hearing; āise — came; mrigī-pāla — flocks of deer.

TRANSLATION

It was only with great difficulty that the cowherd men were able to keep the cows back. Then when the Lord chanted, all the deer heard His sweet voice and approached Him.
