Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.6

keha yadi sańga la-ite pāche uthi' dhāya

sabāre rākhibā, yena keha nāhi yāya

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; yadi — if; sańga la-ite — to take company; pāche — behind; uthi' — getting up; dhāya — runs; sabāre — everyone; rākhibā — please stop; yena — so that; keha — anyone; nāhi yāya — does not go.

TRANSLATION

"If someone wants to follow Me, please stop him. I don't want anyone to go with Me.
