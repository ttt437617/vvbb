Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.122

eta śuni' sei vipra mahā-duhkha pāilā

'krishna' 'krishna' kahi' tathā haite uthi' gelā

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; sei vipra — that brāhmana; mahā-duhkha pāilā — became very much aggrieved; krishna krishna kahi' — uttering the holy name of Lord Krishna; tathā haite — from there; uthi' gelā — got up and went away.

TRANSLATION

When the brāhmana heard Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī speak like this about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he became very much grief-stricken. Chanting the holy name of Krishna, he immediately left.
