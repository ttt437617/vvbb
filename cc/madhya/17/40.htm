Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.40

'krishna krishna kaha' kari' prabhu yabe balila

'krishna' kahi' vyāghra-mriga nācite lāgila

SYNONYMS

krishna krishna kaha — chant "Krishna, Krishna"; kari' — in this way; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yabe — when; balila — uttered; krishna kahi' — chanting the holy name of Krishna; vyāghra-mriga — the tigers and deer; nācite lāgila — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said "Chant 'Krishna! Krishna!'" the tigers and deer began to chant "Krishna!" and dance.
