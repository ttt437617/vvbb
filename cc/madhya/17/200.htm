Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.200

prabhu dekhi' vrindāvanera vriksha-latā-gane

ańkura pulaka, madhu-aśru varishane

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhi' — seeing; vrindāvanera — of Vrindāvana; vriksha-latā-gane — the trees and creepers; ańkura — twigs; pulaka — jubilant; madhu-aśru — tears in the form of honey; varishane — pour.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the trees and creepers of Vrindāvana became jubilant. Their twigs stood up, and they began to shed tears of ecstasy in the form of honey.
