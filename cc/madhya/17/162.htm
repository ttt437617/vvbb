Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.162

yāńhāra darśane loke preme matta hañā

hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya, krishna-nāma lañā

SYNONYMS

yāńhāra darśane — by seeing whom; loke — people; preme — in love; matta hañā — becoming mad; hāse — laugh; kānde — cry; nāce — dance; gāya — chant; krishna-nāma lañā — taking the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

The people said, "Simply by seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, everyone is maddened with love of Krishna. Indeed, everyone is laughing, crying, dancing, chanting and taking the holy name of Krishna.
