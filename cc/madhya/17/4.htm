Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.4

"mora sahāya kara yadi, tumi-dui jana

tabe āmi yāñā dekhi śrī-vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

mora — of Me; sahāya — help; kara — you do; yadi — if; tumi — you; dui jana — two persons; tabe — then; āmi — I; yāñā — going; dekhi — shall see; śrī-vrindāvana — Śrī Vrindāvana-dhāma.

TRANSLATION

The Lord requested Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī to help Him go to Vrindāvana.
