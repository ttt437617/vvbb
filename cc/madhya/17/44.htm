Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.44

mayūrādi pakshi-gana prabhure dekhiyā

sańge cale, 'krishna' bali' nāce matta hañā

SYNONYMS

mayūra-ādi — beginning with peacocks; pakshi-gana — different types of birds; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhiyā — seeing; sańge cale — go with Him; krishna bali' — chanting the holy name of Krishna; nāce — dance; matta hañā — becoming mad.

TRANSLATION

Various birds, including the peacock, saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and began to follow Him, chanting and dancing. They were all maddened by the holy name of Krishna.
