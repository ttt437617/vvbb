Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.100

ei-mata mahāprabhu dui bhrityera vaśe

icchā nāhi, tabu tathā rahilā dina-daśe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dui — two; bhrityera — by servants; vaśe — being obliged; icchā nāhi — there was no such desire; tabu — still; tathā — there; rahilā — remained; dina-daśe — for ten days.

TRANSLATION

Even though He had not made such a plan, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained for ten days at Vārānasī, being obligated by the requests of His two servants.
