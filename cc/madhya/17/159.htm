Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.159

duńhe preme nritya kari' kare kolākuli

hari krishna kaha duńhe bale bāhu tuli'

SYNONYMS

duńhe — both of them; preme — in ecstatic love; nritya kari' — dancing; kare — do; kolākuli — embracing; hari — the holy name of Hari; krishna — the holy name of Krishna; kaha — go on chanting; duńhe — both of them; bale — speak; bāhu tuli' — raising the arms.

TRANSLATION

The two of them danced in ecstatic love and embraced each other. Raising their arms, they said, "Chant the holy names of Hari and Krishna!"
