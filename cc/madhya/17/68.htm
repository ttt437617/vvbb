Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.68

śuna, bhattācārya, — "āmi gelāńa bahu-deśa

vana-pathe duhkhera kāhāń nāhi pāi leśa

SYNONYMS

śuna — please hear; bhattācārya — My dear Bhattācārya; āmi — I; gelāńa — traveled; bahu-deśa — many countries; vana-pathe — through the forest path; duhkhera — of unhappiness; kāhāń — anywhere; nāhi pāi — I do not get; leśa — even a trace.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Bhattācārya, I have traveled very far through the forest, and I have not even slightly received any trouble.
