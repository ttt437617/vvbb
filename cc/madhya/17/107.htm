Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.107

sakala dekhiye tāńte adbhuta-kathana

prakānda-śarīra, śuddha-kāñcana-varana

SYNONYMS

sakala dekhiye — I see everything; tāńte — in Him; adbhuta-kathana — wonderful description; prakānda-śarīra — very large body; śuddha — pure; kāñcana — gold; varana — complexion.

TRANSLATION

"Everything is wonderful about that sannyāsī. He has a very well built and luxuriant body, and His complexion is like purified gold.
