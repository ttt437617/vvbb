Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.101

mahārāshtrīya vipra āise prabhu dekhibāre

prabhura rūpa-prema dekhi' haya camatkāre

SYNONYMS

mahārāshtrīya — belonging to the Maharashtra state; vipra — one brāhmana; āise — comes; prabhu dekhibāre — to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rūpa-prema — beauty and ecstatic love; dekhi' — seeing; haya camatkāre — becomes astonished.

TRANSLATION

At Vārānasī there was a Maharashtriyan brāhmana who used to come daily to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. This brāhmana was simply astonished to see the Lord's personal beauty and ecstatic love for Krishna.
