Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.84

'pūrve śuniyāchi prabhu karyāchena sannyāsa'

niścaya kariyā haila hridaye ullāsa

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; śuniyāchi — I have heard; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karyāchena sannyāsa — has accepted the renounced order of life; niścaya kariyā — ascertaining that; haila — there was; hridaye — within the heart; ullāsa — great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

Tapana Miśra then began to think, "I have heard that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has accepted the renounced order." Thinking this, Tapana Miśra became very jubilant within his heart.
