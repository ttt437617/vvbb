Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.165

'ārya, sarala, tumi — vriddha brāhmana

kāhāń haite pāile tumi ei prema-dhana?'

SYNONYMS

ārya — advanced in devotional service; sarala — simple; tumi — you; vriddha brāhmana — elderly brāhmana; kāhāń haite — from where; pāile tumi — did you obtain; ei — this; prema-dhana — transcendental opulence of ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You are an elderly brāhmana, you are sincere, and you are advanced in spiritual life. Wherefrom have you gotten this transcendental opulence of ecstatic love for Krishna?"
