Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.169

śuni' prabhu kaila tāńra carana vandana

bhaya pāñā prabhu-pāya padilā brāhmana

SYNONYMS

śuni' — after hearing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — did; tāńra — of him; carana vandana — worshiping the feet; bhaya pāñā — being afraid; prabhu-pāya — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; padilā — fell down; brāhmana — the brāhmana.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard about Mādhavendra Purī's relationship with the brāhmana, He immediately offered obeisances at his feet. Becoming fearful, the brāhmana also immediately fell at the Lord's feet.
