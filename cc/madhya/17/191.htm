Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.191

svayambhu, viśrāma, dīrgha-vishnu, bhūteśvara

mahāvidyā, gokarnādi dekhilā vistara

SYNONYMS

svayambhu — Svayambhu; viśrāma — Viśrāma; dīrgha-vishnu — Dīrgha Vishnu; bhūteśvara — Bhūteśvara; mahāvidyā — Mahāvidyā; gokarna — Gokarna; ādi — and so on; dekhilā — saw; vistara — many.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited all the holy places on the banks of the Yamunā, including Svayambhu, Viśrāma-ghāta, Dīrgha Vishnu, Bhūteśvara, Mahāvidyā and Gokarna.
