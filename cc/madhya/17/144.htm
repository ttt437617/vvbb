Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.144

bhāvakāli vecite āmi āilāńa kāśīpure

grāhaka nāhi, nā vikāya, lañā yāba ghare

SYNONYMS

bhāvakāli — devotional sentiments; vecite — to sell; āmi — I; āilāńa — came; kāśīpure — to the city of Kāśī; grāhaka nāhi — there is no customer; nā vikāya — do not sell; lañā yāba ghare — then I must take my commodity back home.

TRANSLATION

"I have come here to sell My emotional ecstatic sentiments in this city of Kāśī, but I cannot find any customers. If they are not sold, I must take them back home.
