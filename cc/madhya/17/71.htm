Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.71

bhakta-gana-sańge avaśya kariba milana

bhakta-gane sańge lañā yāba 'vrindāvana'

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana-sańge — with all My devotees; avaśya — certainly; kariba — shall do; milana — meeting; bhakta-gane — all the devotees; sańge — along with Me; lañā — taking; yāba — I shall go; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana-dhāma.

TRANSLATION

"I thought that once again I would see and meet all the devotees and take them with Me to Vrindāvana.
