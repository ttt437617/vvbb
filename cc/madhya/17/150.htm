Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.150

yamunā dekhiyā preme pade jhāńpa diyā

āste-vyaste bhattācārya uthāya dhariyā

SYNONYMS

yamunā — the river Yamunā; dekhiyā — seeing; preme — in ecstatic love; pade — falls down; jhāńpa diyā — jumping; āste-vyaste — in great haste; bhattācārya — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; uthāya — raises; dhariyā — catching.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the river Yamunā, He threw Himself into it. Balabhadra Bhattācārya hastily caught the Lord and very carefully raised Him up again.
