Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.34

pathe yāite kare prabhu ucca sańkīrtana

madhura kantha-dhvani śuni' āise mrigī-gana

SYNONYMS

pathe yāite — while passing on the path; kare — does; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ucca — loud; sańkīrtana — chanting of Hare Krishna; madhura — sweet; kantha-dhvani — the voice from His throat; śuni' — hearing; āise — came; mrigī-gana — she-deer.

TRANSLATION

Sometimes Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted very loudly while passing through the jungle. Hearing His sweet voice, all the does came near Him.
