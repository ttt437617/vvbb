Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.113

jagat-mańgala tāńra 'krishna-caitanya'-nāma

nāma, rūpa, guna tāńra, saba — anupama

SYNONYMS

jagat-mańgala — all-auspicious to the whole world; tāńra — His; krishna-caitanya — Śrī Krishna Caitanya; nāma — name; nāma — name; rūpa — form; guna — the quality; tāńra — His; saba — all; anupama — unparalleled.

TRANSLATION

"His name, Krishna Caitanya, is all-auspicious for the world. Everything about Him — His name, form and qualities — is unparalleled.
