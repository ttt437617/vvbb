Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.180

tathāpi purī dekhi' tāńra 'vaishnava'-ācāra

'śishya' kari' tāńra bhikshā kaila ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; dekhi' — after seeing; tāńra — of the brāhmana; vaishnava-ācāra — behavior like a Vaishnava; śishya kari' — accepting him as his disciple; tāńra bhikshā — food offered by him; kaila ańgīkāra — accepted.

TRANSLATION

Although the brāhmana belonged to the Sanodiyā community, Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī had seen that he behaved like a Vaishnava and had therefore accepted him as his disciple. The food he had cooked had also been accepted by Mādhavendra Purī.
