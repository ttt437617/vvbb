Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.17

ińhāra sańge āche vipra eka 'bhritya'

ińho pathe karibena sevā-bhikshā-kritya

SYNONYMS

ińhāra sańge — with him; āche — is; vipra — brāhmana; eka — one; bhritya — servant; ińho — this man; pathe — on the way; karibena — will do; sevā — service; bhikshā-kritya — and arrangements for cooking.

TRANSLATION

"In addition, You may take another brāhmana who will act as a servant en route and make arrangements for Your food.
