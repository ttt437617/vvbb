Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.87

ghare lañā āilā prabhuke ānandita hañā

sevā kari' nritya kare vastra udāñā

SYNONYMS

ghare lañā — taking to his home; āilā — came; prabhuke — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita hañā — in great happiness; sevā kari' — rendering service; nritya kare — began to dance; vastra udāñā — waving his cloth.

TRANSLATION

With great pleasure Tapana Miśra brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home and rendered service unto Him. Indeed, he began to dance, waving his cloth.
