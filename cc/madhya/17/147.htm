Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.147

sei tina sańge cale, prabhu nishedhila

dūra haite tina-jane ghare pāthāila

SYNONYMS

sei tina — those three; sańge — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; cale — go; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nishedhila — forbade; dūra haite — from a distance; tina-jane — the three persons; ghare — home; pāthāila — sent back.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu started for Mathurā, all three devotees started to go with Him. But the Lord forbade them to accompany Him, and from a distance He asked them to return home.
