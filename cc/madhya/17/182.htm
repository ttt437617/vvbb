Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.182

tomāre 'bhikshā' diba — bada bhāgya se āmāra

tumi — īśvara, nāhi tomāra vidhi-vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

tomāre — unto You; bhikshā diba — I shall offer food; bada bhāgya — great fortune; se — that; āmāra — my; tumi — You; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; nāhi — there is not; tomāra — of You; vidhi-vyavahāra — regulative behavior.

TRANSLATION

"It is a great fortune for me to offer You food. You are the Supreme Lord, and being in the transcendental position, You are not restricted in any way.
