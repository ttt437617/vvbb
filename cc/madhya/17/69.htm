Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.69

krishna — kripālu, āmāya bahuta kripā kailā

vana-pathe āni' āmāya bada sukha dilā

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kripālu — very kind; āmāya — upon Me; bahuta — greatly; kripā — mercy; kailā — showed; vana-pathe — on the path in the forest; āni' — bringing; āmāya — unto Me; bada — very much; sukha — happiness; dilā — gave.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is very merciful, especially to Me. He has shown His mercy by bringing Me on this path through the forest. Thus He has given Me great pleasure.
