Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.114

dekhile se jāni tāńra 'īśvarera rīti'

alaukika kathā śuni' ke kare pratīti?"

SYNONYMS

dekhile — simply by seeing; se — Him; jāni — I understand; tāńra — His; īśvarera rīti — characteristics of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; alaukika — uncommon; kathā — story; śuni' — hearing; ke — who; kare pratīti — will believe.

TRANSLATION

"Simply by seeing Him, one understands that He possesses all the characteristics of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Such characteristics are certainly uncommon. Who will believe it?"
