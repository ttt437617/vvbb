Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.115

śuniyā prakāśānanda bahuta hāsilā

vipre upahāsa kari' kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; prakāśānanda — Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī; bahuta hāsilā — laughed very much; vipre — at the brāhmana; upahāsa kari' — jokingly laughing; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Prakāśānanda Sarasvatī laughed very much to hear this description. Joking and laughing at the brāhmana, he began to speak as follows.
