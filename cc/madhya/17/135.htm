Madhya-līlāChapter 17: The Lord Travels to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 17.135

krishna-nāma, krishna-guna, krishna-līlā-vrinda

krishnera svarūpa-sama — saba cid-ānanda

SYNONYMS

krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; krishna-guna — the transcendental qualities of Krishna; krishna-līlā-vrinda — the transcendental pastimes of Lord Krishna; krishnera svarūpa — Krishna's personality; sama — equal; saba — all; cit-ānanda — spiritual and full of bliss.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna's holy name, transcendental qualities and transcendental pastimes are all equal to Lord Krishna Himself. They are all spiritual and full of bliss.
