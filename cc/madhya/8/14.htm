Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.14

hena-kāle dolāya cadi' rāmānanda rāya

snāna karibāre āilā, bājanā bājāya

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; dolāya cadi' — riding on a palanquin; rāmānanda rāya — Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; snāna — bath; karibāre — to take; āilā — came there; bājanā bājāya — accompanied by a musical band.

TRANSLATION

At that time, accompanied by the sounds of music, Rāmānanda Rāya came there on a palanquin to take his bath.
