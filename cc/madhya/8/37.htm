Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.37

tomāra kripāya tomāya karāya nindya-karma

sākshāt īśvara tumi, ke jāne tomāra marma

SYNONYMS

tomāra kripāya — Your mercy; tomāya — unto You; karāya — induces; nindya-karma — forbidden actions; sākshāt īśvara — directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead; tumi — You; ke jāne — who can know; tomāra — Your; marma — purpose.

TRANSLATION

"You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself; therefore no one can understand Your purpose. By Your mercy You are touching me, although this is not sanctioned by the Vedas.

PURPORT

A sannyāsī is strictly forbidden to see the vishayīs, the materialistic people. But Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, out of His boundless and causeless mercy, could show favor to anyone, regardless of birth and position.
