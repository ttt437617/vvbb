Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.103

rādhā lāgi' gopīre yadi sākshāt kare tyāga

tabe jāni, — rādhāya krishnera gādha-anurāga

SYNONYMS

rādhā lāgi' — for the sake of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; gopīre — the gopīs; yadi — if; sākshāt — directly; kare — does; tyāga — rejection; tabe — then; jāni — we can understand; rādhāya — in Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; gādha — intense; anurāga — affection.

TRANSLATION

"If Lord Krishna rejected the company of the other gopīs for Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, we can understand that Lord Śrī Krishna has intense affection for Her."
