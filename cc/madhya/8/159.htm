Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.159

hlādinīra sāra amśa, tāra 'prema' nāma

ānanda-cinmaya-rasa premera ākhyāna

SYNONYMS

hlādinīra — of this pleasure potency; sāra — the essential; amśa — part; tāra — its; prema — love of God; nāma — name; ānanda — full of pleasure; cit-maya-rasa — the platform of spiritual mellows; premera — of love of Godhead; ākhyāna — the explanation.

TRANSLATION

"The most essential part of this pleasure potency is love of Godhead [prema]. Consequently, the explanation of love of Godhead is also a transcendental mellow full of pleasure.
