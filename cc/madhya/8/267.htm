Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.267

eka samśaya mora āchaye hridaye

kripā kari' kaha more tāhāra niścaye

SYNONYMS

eka samśaya — one doubt; mora — my; āchaye — there is; hridaye — in the heart; kripā kari' — being merciful; kaha — please say; more — unto me; tāhāra — of that; niścaye — the ascertainment.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya then said that he had but one doubt within his heart, and he petitioned the Lord, "Please be merciful upon me and just remove my doubt."
