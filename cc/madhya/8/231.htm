Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.231

tāhāte drishtānta — lakshmī karila bhajana

tathāpi nā pāila vraje vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

tāhāte — in that; drishtānta — the evidence; lakshmī — the goddess of fortune; karila — did; bhajana — worship; tathāpi — still; nā — not; pāila — got; vraje — in Vrindāvana; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Mahārāja Nanda, Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"The unspoken example in this connection is the goddess of fortune, who worshiped Lord Krishna in order to attain His pastimes in Vrindāvana. But due to her opulent life-style, she could not attain the service of Krishna in Vrindāvana.
