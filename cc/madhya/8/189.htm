Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.189

rātri-dina kuñje krīdā kare rādhā-sańge

kaiśora vayasa saphala kaila krīdā-rańge

SYNONYMS

rātri-dina — day and night; kuñje — in the gardens or bushes of Vrindāvana; krīdā — pastimes; kare — performs; rādhā-sańge — with Rādhārānī; kaiśora — the pre-youthful; vayasa — age; sa-phala — fruitful; kaila — made; krīdā-rańge — taking pleasure in different pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Day and night Lord Śrī Krishna enjoys the company of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī in the bushes of Vrindāvana. Thus His pre-youthful age is fulfilled through His affairs with Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
