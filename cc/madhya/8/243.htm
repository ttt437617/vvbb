Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.243

anyonye mili' duńhe nibhrite vasiyā

praśnottara-goshthī kahe ānandita hañā

SYNONYMS

anyonye — each other; mili' — meeting; duńhe — both of them; nibhrite — in a secluded place; vasiyā — sitting; praśna-uttara — of questions and answers; goshthī — a discussion; kahe — spoke; ānandita — jubilant; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

Thus they met time and time again, sitting in a secluded place and jubilantly discussing devotional service by the question-and-answer process.
