Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.286

mora tattva-līlā-rasa tomāra gocare

ataeva ei-rūpa dekhāiluń tomāre

SYNONYMS

mora — My; tattva-līlā — truth and pastimes; rasa — and mellows; tomāra — of you; gocare — within the knowledge; ataeva — therefore; ei-rūpa — this form; dekhāiluń — I have shown; tomāre — unto you.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu confirmed, "All the truths about My pastimes and mellows are within your knowledge. Therefore I have shown this form to you.
