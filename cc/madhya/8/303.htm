Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.303

sańkshepe kahiluń rāmānandera milana

vistāri' varnite nāre sahasra-vadana

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; kahiluń — I have described; rāmānandera milana — meeting with Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; vistāri' — expanding; varnite — to describe; nāre — not able; sahasra-vadana — Lord Śesha Nāga, who has thousands of hoods.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly described the meeting between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya. No one can actually describe this meeting exhaustively. It is even impossible for Lord Śesha Nāga, who has thousands of hoods.
