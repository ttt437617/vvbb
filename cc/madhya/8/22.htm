Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.22

tabe tāre kaila prabhu dridha ālińgana

premāveśe prabhu-bhritya dońhe acetana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; tāre — him; kaila — did; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dridha — firm; ālińgana — embracing; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; prabhu-bhritya — the servant and the master; dońhe — both; acetana — unconscious.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya very firmly. Indeed, both the master and the servant almost lost consciousness due to ecstatic love.
