Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.114

tāńhā vinu rāsa-līlā nāhi bhāya citte

mandalī chādiyā gelā rādhā anveshite

SYNONYMS

tāńhā vinu — without Her; rāsa-līlā — the rāsa dance; nāhi — not; bhāya — illuminates; citte — within the heart; mandalī chādiyā — leaving the circle of the rāsa dance; gelā — went; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; anveshite — to search for.

TRANSLATION

"The rāsa dance does not shine in the heart of Krishna without Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. Therefore, He also gave up the circle of the rāsa dance and went out to search for Her.
