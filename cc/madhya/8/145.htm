Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.145

lakshmī-kāntādi avatārera hare mana

lakshmī-ādi nārī-ganera kare ākarshana

SYNONYMS

lakshmī-kānta-ādi — of the goddess of fortune's husband (Nārāyana); avatārera — of the incarnation; hare — He enchants; mana — the mind; lakshmī — the goddess of fortune; ādi — headed by; nārī-ganera — of all women; kare — does; ākarshana — attraction.

TRANSLATION

"He also attracts Nārāyana, who is the incarnation of Sańkarshana and the husband of the goddess of fortune. He attracts not only Nārāyana but also all women, headed by the goddess of fortune, the consort of Nārāyana.
