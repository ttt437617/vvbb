Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.20

uthi' prabhu kahe, — ūtha, kaha 'krishna' 'krishna'

tāre ālińgite prabhura hridaya satrishna

SYNONYMS

uthi' — rising; prabhu — the Lord; kahe — said; utha — get up; kaha — chant; krishna krishna — the holy name of Lord Krishna; tāre — him; ālińgite — to embrace; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hridaya — the heart; sa-trishna — very eager.

TRANSLATION

The Lord stood up and asked Rāmānanda Rāya to arise and chant the holy name of Krishna. Indeed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very eager to embrace him.
