Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.134

parama īśvara krishna — svayam bhagavān

sarva-avatārī, sarva-kārana-pradhāna

SYNONYMS

parama — supreme; īśvara — controller; krishna — Lord Krishna; svayam — personally; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sarva-avatārī — the source of all incarnations; sarva-kārana-pradhāna — the supreme cause of all causes.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya then began to speak on krishna-tattva. "Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead," he said. "He is personally the original Godhead, the source of all incarnations and the cause of all causes.
