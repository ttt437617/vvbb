Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.141

nānā-bhaktera rasāmrita nānā-vidha haya

sei saba rasāmritera 'vishaya' 'āśraya'

SYNONYMS

nānā-bhaktera — of various types of devotees; rasa-amrita — the nectar of devotion or transcendental mellows; nānā-vidha — different varieties; haya — there are; sei saba — all these; rasa-amritera — of the nectar of devotion; vishaya — subject; āśraya — object.

TRANSLATION

"Each and every devotee has a certain type of transcendental mellow in relation to Krishna. But in all transcendental relationships the devotee is the worshiper [āśraya] and Krishna is the object of worship [vishaya].
