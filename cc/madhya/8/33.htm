Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.33

tāńra kripāya pāinu tomāra daraśana

āji saphala haila mora manushya-janama

SYNONYMS

tāńra kripāya — by his mercy; pāinu — I have gotten; tomāra — Your; daraśana — interview; āji — today; sa-phala — successful; haila — has become; mora — my; manushya-janama — birth as a human being.

TRANSLATION

"By his mercy I have received Your interview here. Consequently I consider that today I have become a successful human being.
