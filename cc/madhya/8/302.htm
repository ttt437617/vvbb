Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.302

rāmānanda hailā prabhura virahe vihvala

prabhura dhyāne rahe vishaya chādiyā sakala

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda — Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; hailā — became; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; virahe — in separation; vihvala — overwhelmed; prabhura dhyāne — in meditation on Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahe — remains; vishaya — worldly business; chādiyā — giving up; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

When Rāmānanda Rāya began to feel separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was overwhelmed. Meditating on the Lord, he gave up all his material business.
