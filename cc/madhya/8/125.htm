Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.125

sārvabhauma-sańge mora mana nirmala ha-ila

'krishna-bhakti-tattva kaha,' tāńhāre puchila

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma-sańge — in the company of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; mora — My; mana — mind; nirmala — clarified; ha-ila — became; krishna-bhakti-tattva — the truths of transcendental loving service to Krishna; kaha — please explain; tāńhāre — to him; puchila — I inquired.

TRANSLATION

"Due to the association of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, My mind became enlightened. Therefore I asked him about the truths of transcendental loving service to Krishna.
