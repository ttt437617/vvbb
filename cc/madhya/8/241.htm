Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.241

nīlācale tumi-āmi thākiba eka-sańge

sukhe gońāiba kāla krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

nīlācale — in Jagannātha Purī; tumi — you; āmi — I; thākiba — shall stay; eka-sańge — together; sukhe — in happiness; gońāiba — will pass; kāla — time; krishna-kathā-rańge — in the joy of talking about Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"You and I shall remain together at Jagannātha Purī. We shall pass our time together in joy, talking about Krishna and His pastimes."
