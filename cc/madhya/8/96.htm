Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.96

prabhu kahe, — ei 'sādhyāvadhi' suniścaya

kripā kari' kaha, yadi āge kichu haya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; ei — this; sādhya-avadhi — the highest limit of perfection; su-niścaya — certainly; kripā kari' — being merciful to Me; kaha — please speak; yadi — if; āge — further; kichu haya — there is something.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "This is certainly the limit of perfection, but please be merciful to Me and speak more if there is more."
