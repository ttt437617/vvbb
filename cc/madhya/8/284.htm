Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.284

prabhu tāńre hasta sparśi' karāilā cetana

sannyāsīra vesha dekhi' vismita haila mana

SYNONYMS

prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — unto Rāmānanda Rāya; hasta — the hand; sparśi' — touching; karāilā — made; cetana — conscious; sannyāsīra — of the sannyāsī; vesha — the dress; dekhi' — seeing; vismita — struck with wonder; haila — became; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

When Rāmānanda Rāya fell to the ground unconscious, Caitanya Mahāprabhu touched his hand, and he immediately regained consciousness. But when he saw Lord Caitanya in the dress of a sannyāsī, he was struck with wonder.
