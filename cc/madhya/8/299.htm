Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.299

eta bali' rāmānande kari' ālińgana

tāńre ghare pāthāiyā karila śayana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; rāmānande — to Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; kari' — doing; ālińgana — embracing; tāńre — him; ghare — to his home; pāthāiyā — sending; karila — did; śayana — lying down.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya, and after sending him back to his home, the Lord took rest.
