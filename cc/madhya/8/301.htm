Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.301

'vidyāpūre' nānā-mata loka vaise yata

prabhu-darśane 'vaishnava' haila chādi' nija-mata

SYNONYMS

vidyāpūre — in the town of Vidyānagara; nānā-mata — various opinions; loka — people; vaise — reside; yata — all; prabhu-darśane — in seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaishnava — devotees of Lord Vishnu; haila — became; chādi' — giving up; nija-mata — own opinions.

TRANSLATION

All the residents of Vidyānagara were of different faiths, but after seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they abandoned their own faiths and became Vaishnavas.
