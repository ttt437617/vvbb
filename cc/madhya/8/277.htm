Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.277

rādhā-krishne tomāra mahā-prema haya

yāhāń tāhāń rādhā-krishna tomāre sphuraya

SYNONYMS

rādhā-krishne — unto Rādhā and Krishna; tomāra — your; mahā-prema — great love; haya — there is; yāhāń tāhāń — anywhere and everywhere; rādhā-krishna — Lord Krishna and Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; tomāre — unto you; sphuraya — appear.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "My dear Rāya, you are an advanced devotee and are always filled with ecstatic love for Rādhā and Krishna. Therefore whatever you see — anywhere and everywhere — simply awakens your Krishna consciousness."
