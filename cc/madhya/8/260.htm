Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.260

ei-mata dui jana krishna-kathā-rase

nritya-gīta-rodane haila rātri-śeshe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; dui jana — both of them (Lord Caitanya and Rāmānanda Rāya); krishna-kathā-rase — in the mellows of discussing topics about Krishna; nritya-gīta — in dancing and chanting; rodane — in crying; haila — there was; rātri-śeshe — the end of the night.

TRANSLATION

In this way Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya passed the whole night relishing the mellows of krishna-kathā, topics about Krishna. While they were chanting, dancing and crying, the night ended.
