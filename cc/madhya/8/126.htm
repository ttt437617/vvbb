Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.126

teńho kahe — āmi nāhi jāni krishna-kathā

sabe rāmānanda jāne, teńho nāhi ethā

SYNONYMS

teńho kahe — he replied; āmi — I; nāhi — not; jāni — know; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; sabe — all; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; jāne — knows; teńho — he; nāhi — not; ethā — here.

TRANSLATION

"Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya told me, 'I do not actually know about the topics of Lord Krishna. They are all known only to Rāmānanda Rāya, but he is not present here.'"
