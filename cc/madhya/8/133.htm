Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.133

mora jihvā — vīnā-yantra, tumi — vīnā-dhārī

tomāra mane yei uthe, tāhāi uccāri

SYNONYMS

mora jihvā — my tongue; vīnā-yantra — a stringed instrument; tumi — You; vīnā-dhārī — the player of the stringed instrument; tomāra mane — in Your mind; yei uthe — whatever arises; tāhāi — that; uccāri — I vibrate.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, my tongue is just like a stringed instrument, and You are its player. Therefore I simply vibrate whatever arises in Your mind."
