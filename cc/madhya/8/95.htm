Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.95

tatrātiśuśubhe tābhir

bhagavān devakī-sutah

madhye manīnām haimānām

mahā-marakato yathā

SYNONYMS

tatra — there; ati-śuśubhe — was very beautiful; tābhih — by them; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; devakī-sutah — son of Devakī; madhye — in the midst; manīnām — of valuable jewels; haimānām — lined with gold; mahā-marakatah — the jewel of the name marakata; yathā — as.

TRANSLATION

"'Although the son of Devakī, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is the reservoir of all kinds of beauty, when He is among the gopīs He nonetheless becomes more beautiful, for He resembles a marakata jewel surrounded by gold and other jewels.'"
