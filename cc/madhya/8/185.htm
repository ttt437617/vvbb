Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.185

yāńra sadguna-ganane krishna nā pāya pāra

tāńra guna ganibe kemane jīva chāra

SYNONYMS

yāńra — whose; sat-guna — good qualities; ganane — in counting; krishna — Lord Krishna; nā — not; pāya — obtains; pāra — the limit; tāńra — Her; guna — qualities; ganibe — can count; kemane — how; jīva — a living entity; chāra — most insignificant.

TRANSLATION

"Even Lord Krishna Himself cannot reach the limit of the transcendental qualities of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. How, then, can an insignificant living entity count them?"
