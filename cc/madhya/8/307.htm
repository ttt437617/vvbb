Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.307

'rasa-tattva-jñāna' haya ihāra śravane

'prema-bhakti' haya rādhā-krishnera carane

SYNONYMS

rasa-tattva-jñāna — transcendental knowledge of the humors of conjugal love between Rādhā and Krishna; haya — is; ihāra — of this; śravane — by hearing; prema-bhakti — pure love of Godhead; haya — becomes possible; rādhā-krishnera carane — at the lotus feet of Rādhā and Krishna.

TRANSLATION

By hearing the talks between Rāmānanda Rāya and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, one becomes enlightened with the transcendental knowledge of the mellows of Rādhā and Krishna's pastimes. Thus one can develop unalloyed love for the lotus feet of Rādhā and Krishna.
