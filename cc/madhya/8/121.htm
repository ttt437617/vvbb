Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.121

rāya kahe, — ihā āmi kichui nā jāni

tumi yei kahāo, sei kahi āmi vānī

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya said; ihā — this; āmi — I; kichui — something; nā — not; jāni — know; tumi — You; yei — whatever; kahāo — make me say; sei — those; kahi — speak; āmi — I; vānī — words.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "I do not know anything about this. I simply vibrate the sound You make me speak.
