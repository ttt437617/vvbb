Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.115

itas-tatah bhrami' kāhāń rādhā nā pāñā

vishāda karena kāma-bāne khinna hañā

SYNONYMS

itah-tatah — here and there; bhrami' — wandering; kāhāń — anywhere; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; nā — not; pāñā — finding; vishāda — lamentation; karena — does; kāma-bāne — by the arrow of Cupid; khinna — hurt; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

"When Krishna went out to search for Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, He wandered here and there. Not finding Her, He became afflicted by the arrow of Cupid and began to lament.
