Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.311

rāmānanda rāye mora kotī namaskāra

yāńra mukhe kaila prabhu rasera vistāra

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda rāye — unto Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; mora — my; kotī — ten million; namaskāra — obeisances; yāńra mukhe — in whose mouth; kaila — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rasera vistāra — the expansion of transcendental mellows.

TRANSLATION

I offer ten million obeisances unto the lotus feet of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya because from his mouth much spiritual information has been expanded by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
