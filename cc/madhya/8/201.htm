Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.201

rādhā-krishnera līlā ei ati gūdhatara

dāsya-vātsalyādi-bhāve nā haya gocara

SYNONYMS

rādhā-krishnera līlā — the pastimes of Rādhā and Krishna; ei — this is; ati — very much; gūdhatara — more confidential; dāsya — of servitude; vātsalya-ādi — and of parental love, etc.; bhāve — in the moods; nā haya — is not; gocara — appreciated.

TRANSLATION

"The pastimes of Rādhā and Krishna are very confidential. They cannot be understood through the mellows of servitude, fraternity or parental affection.
