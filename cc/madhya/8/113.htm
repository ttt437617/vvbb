Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.113

samyak-sāra vāsanā krishnera rāsa-līlā

rāsa-līlā-vāsanāte rādhikā śrińkhalā

SYNONYMS

samyak-sāra — the complete and essential; vāsanā — desire; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; rāsa-līlā — the dancing in the rāsa-līlā; rāsa-līlā-vāsanāte — in the desire to dance the rāsa dance; rādhikā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; śrińkhalā — the medium of bondage.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna's desire in the rāsa-līlā circle is perfectly complete, but Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is the binding link in that desire.
