Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.217

nijendriya-sukha-hetu kāmera tātparya

krishna-sukha-tātparya gopī-bhāva-varya

SYNONYMS

nija-indriya — of one's own senses; sukha — of the happiness; hetu — for the reason; kāmera — of lusty desire; tātparya — intention; krishna — of Krishna; sukha — the happiness; tātparya — intention; gopī-bhāva-varya — the foremost mood of the gopīs.

TRANSLATION

"Lusty desires are experienced when one is concerned with his own personal sense gratification. The mood of the gopīs is not like that. Their only desire is to satisfy the senses of Krishna.
