Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.228

ataeva gopī-bhāva kari ańgīkāra

rātri-dina cinte rādhā-krishnera vihāra

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; gopī-bhāva — the loving mood of the gopīs; kari — making; ańgīkāra — acceptance; rātri-dina — day and night; cinte — one thinks; rādhā-krishnera — of Rādhā and Krishna; vihāra — the pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore one should accept the mood of the gopīs in their service. In such a transcendental mood, one should always think of the pastimes of Śrī Rādhā and Krishna.
