Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.150

ei ta' sańkshepe kahila krishnera svarūpa

ebe sańkshepe kahi śuna rādhā-tattva-rūpa

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; sańkshepe — in brief; kahila — I have said; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa — the original form; ebe — now; sańkshepe — in summary; kahi — I shall speak; śuna — please hear; rādhā — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; tattva-rūpa — the actual position.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya then said, "I have thus briefly explained the original form of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Now let me describe the position of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
