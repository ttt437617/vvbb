Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.179

krishna-nāma-guna-yaśa — avatamsa kāne

krishna-nāma-guna-yaśa-pravāha-vacane

SYNONYMS

krishna — of Lord Krishna; nāma — the holy name; guna — the qualities; yaśa — the fame; avatamsa — ornaments; kāne — on the ear; krishna — of Lord Krishna; nāma — of the holy name; guna — of the qualities; yaśa — of the fame; pravāha — waves; vacane — in Her talking.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīmatī Rādhārānī's earrings are the name, fame and qualities of Lord Krishna. The glories of Lord Krishna's name, fame and qualities are always inundating Her speech.
