Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.151

krishnera ananta-śakti, tāte tina — pradhāna

'cic-chakti', 'māyā-śakti', 'jīva-śakti'-nāma

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; ananta-śakti — unlimited potencies; tāte — in that; tina — three; pradhāna — chief; cit-śakti — spiritual potency; māyā-śakti — material potency; jīva-śakti — marginal potency, or living entities; nāma — named.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna has unlimited potencies, which can be divided into three main parts. These are the spiritual potency, the material potency and the marginal potency, which is known as the living entities.
