Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.272

prabhu kahe, — krishne tomāra gādha-prema haya

premāra svabhāva ei jāniha niścaya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; krishne — unto Krishna; tomāra — your; gādha-prema — deep love; haya — there is; premāra — of such transcendental love; svabhāva — the nature; ei — this; jāniha — please know; niścaya — certainly.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You have a deep love for Krishna, and one who has such deep ecstatic love for the Lord naturally sees things in such a way. Please take this from Me to be certain.
