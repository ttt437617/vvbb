Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.10

pūrvavat 'vaishnava' kari' sarva loka-gane

godāvarī-tīre prabhu āilā kata-dine

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as previously; vaishnava — devotees; kari' — making; sarva — all; loka-gane — the people; godāvarī-tīre — on the bank of the river Godāvarī; prabhu — the Lord; āilā — arrived; kata-dine — after some days.

TRANSLATION

As previously, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu converted to Vaishnavism many people He met on the road. After some days, the Lord reached the banks of the river Godāvarī.
