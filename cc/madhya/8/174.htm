Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.174

'sūddīpta-sāttvika' bhāva, harshādi 'sañcārī'

ei saba bhāva-bhūshana saba-ańge bhari'

SYNONYMS

su-uddīpta-sāttvika bhāva — blazing ecstasies of goodness; harsha-ādi — like jubilation; sañcārī — the continuously existing ecstasies; ei saba — all these; bhāva — ecstasies; bhūshana — ornaments; saba — all; ańge — body; bhari' — filling.

TRANSLATION

"The ornaments decorating Her body are the blazing ecstasies of goodness and the constantly existing ecstasies, headed by jubilation. All these ecstasies are the ornaments all over Her body.
