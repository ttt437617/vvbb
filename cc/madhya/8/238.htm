Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.238

prabhu kahe, — āilāńa śuni' tomāra guna

krishna-kathā śuni, śuddha karāite mana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; āilāńa — I have come; śuni' — hearing; tomāra — your; guna — qualities; krishna-kathā — these topics about Krishna; śuni — I hear; śuddha karāite — just to make pure; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

The Lord replied, "Having heard about your good qualities, I have come here. I have come to hear about Krishna from you and thus purify My mind.
