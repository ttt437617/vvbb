Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.148

āpana-mādhurye hare āpanāra mana

āpanā āpani cāhe karite ālińgana

SYNONYMS

āpana — own; mādhurye — by sweetness; hare — steals; āpanāra — His own; mana — mind; āpanā — Himself; āpani — He; cāhe — wants; karite — to do; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna's sweetness is so attractive that it steals away His own mind. Thus even He wants to embrace Himself.
