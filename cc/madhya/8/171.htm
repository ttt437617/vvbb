Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.171

krishnera ujjvala-rasa — mrigamada-bhara

sei mrigamade vicitrita kalevara

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; ujjvala-rasa — the conjugal mellow; mriga-mada — of musk; bhara — an abundance; sei — that; mriga-made — made by the aroma of the musk; vicitrita — decorated; kalevara — Her whole body.

TRANSLATION

"Conjugal love for Krishna is an abundance of musk, and with that musk Her whole body is decorated.
