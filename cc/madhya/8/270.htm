Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.270

tāhāte prakata dekhoń sa-vamśī vadana

nānā bhāve cañcala tāhe kamala-nayana

SYNONYMS

tāhāte — in that; prakata — manifested; dekhoń — I see; sa-vamśī — with the flute; vadana — the face; nānā bhāve — in various modes; cañcala — restless; tāhe — in that; kamala-nayana — the lotus eyes.

TRANSLATION

"I see that You are holding a flute to Your mouth, and Your lotus eyes are moving very restlessly due to various ecstasies.
