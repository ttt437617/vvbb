Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.40

mahad-vicalanam nrinām

grihinām dīna-cetasām

nihśreyasāya bhagavan

nānyathā kalpate kvacit

SYNONYMS

mahat-vicalanam — the wandering of saintly persons; nrinām — of human beings; grihinām — who are householders; dīna-cetasām — low-minded; nihśreyasāya — for the ultimate benefit; bhagavan — O my Lord; na anyathā — not any other purpose; kalpate — one imagines; kvacit — at any time.

TRANSLATION

"'My dear Lord, sometimes great saintly persons go to the homes of householders, although these householders are generally low-minded. When a saintly person visits their homes, one can understand that it is for no other purpose than to benefit the householders.'

PURPORT

This verse is from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (10.8.4).
