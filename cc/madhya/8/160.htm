Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.160

premera parama-sāra 'mahābhāva' jāni

sei mahābhāva-rūpā rādhā-thākurānī

SYNONYMS

premera — of love of Godhead; parama-sāra — the essential part; mahā-bhāva — the transcendental ecstasy of the name mahābhāva; jāni — we know; sei — that; mahā-bhāva-rūpā — the personification of the mahābhāva transcendental ecstasy; rādhā-thākurānī — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.

TRANSLATION

"The essential part of love of Godhead is called mahābhāva, transcendental ecstasy, and that ecstasy is represented by Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
