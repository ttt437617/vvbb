Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.296

āra dina rāya-pāśe vidāya māgilā

vidāyera kāle tāńre ei ājñā dilā

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; rāya-pāśe — before Rāmānanda Rāya; vidāya māgilā — begged farewell; vidāyera kāle — at the time of departure; tāńre — unto him; ei — this; ājñā — order; dilā — gave.

TRANSLATION

The next day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu begged Rāmānanda Rāya to give Him permission to leave, and at the time of farewell the Lord gave him the following orders.
