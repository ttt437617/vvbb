Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.19

dekhiyā tāńhāra mane haila camatkāra

āsiyā karila dandavat namaskāra

SYNONYMS

dekhiyā — seeing; tāńhāra — his; mane — in the mind; haila — there was; camatkāra — wonder; āsiyā — coming there; karila — did; danda-vat — like a rod; namaskāra — obeisances.

TRANSLATION

When Rāmānanda Rāya saw the wonderful sannyāsī, he was struck with wonder. He went to Him and immediately offered his respectful obeisances, falling down flat like a rod.
