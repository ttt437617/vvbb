Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.176

saubhāgya-tilaka cāru-lalāte ujjvala

prema-vaicittya — ratna, hridaya — tarala

SYNONYMS

saubhāgya-tilaka — the tilaka of good fortune; cāru — beautiful; lalāte — on the forehead; ujjvala — brilliant; prema — of love of Godhead; vaicittya — diversity; ratna — the jewel; hridaya — the heart; tarala — the locket.

TRANSLATION

"The tilaka of good fortune is on Her beautiful broad forehead. Her various loving affairs are a gem, and Her heart is the locket.
