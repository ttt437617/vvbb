Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.35

kāhāń tumi — sākshāt īśvara nārāyana

kāhāń muñi — rāja-sevī vishayī śūdrādhama

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — whereas; tumi — You; sākshāt — directly; īśvara nārāyana — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; kāhāń — whereas; muñi — I; rāja-sevī — government servant; vishayī — materialist; śūdra-adhama — worse than a śūdra, or fourth-class man.

TRANSLATION

"You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana Himself, and I am only a government servant interested in materialistic activities. Indeed, I am the lowest among men of the fourth caste.
