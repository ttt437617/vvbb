Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.158

sukha-rūpa krishna kare sukha āsvādana

bhakta-gane sukha dite 'hlādinī' — kārana

SYNONYMS

sukha-rūpa — embodiment of pleasure; krishna — Lord Krishna; kare — does; sukha — happiness; āsvādana — tasting; bhakta-gane — unto the devotees; sukha — happiness; dite — to give; hlādinī — the pleasure potency; kārana — the cause.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna tastes all kinds of transcendental happiness, although He Himself is happiness personified. The pleasure relished by His pure devotees is also manifested by His pleasure potency.
