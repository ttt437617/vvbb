Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.123

hridaye prerana kara, jihvāya kahāo vānī

ki kahiye bhāla-manda, kichui nā jāni

SYNONYMS

hridaye — within the heart; prerana — direction; kara — You give; jihvāya — on the tongue; kahāo — You make me speak; vānī — words; ki — what; kahiye — I am speaking; bhāla-manda — good or bad; kichui — something; nā — not; jāni — I know.

TRANSLATION

"You inspire me within the heart and make me speak with the tongue. I do not know whether I am speaking well or badly."
