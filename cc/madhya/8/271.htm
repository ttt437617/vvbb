Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.271

ei-mata tomā dekhi' haya camatkāra

akapate kaha, prabhu, kārana ihāra

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; tomā — You; dekhi' — seeing; haya — there is; camatkāra — wonder; akapate — without duplicity; kaha — please tell; prabhu — my Lord; kārana — the cause; ihāra — of this.

TRANSLATION

"I actually see You in this way, and this is very wonderful. My Lord, please tell me without duplicity what is causing this."
