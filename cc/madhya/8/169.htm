Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.169

krishna-anurāga dvitīya aruna-vasana

pranaya-māna-kañculikāya vaksha ācchādana

SYNONYMS

krishna-anurāga — attraction for Krishna; dvitīya — second; aruna-vasana — pinkish garment; pranaya — of love; māna — and anger; kañculikāya — by a short blouse; vaksha — breasts; ācchādana — covering.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīmatī Rādhārānī's affection for Krishna is Her upper garment, which is pinkish in color. She then covers Her breasts with another garment, composed of affection and anger toward Krishna.
