Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.11

godāvarī dekhi' ha-ila 'yamunā'-smarana

tīre vana dekhi' smriti haila vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

godāvarī — the river Godāvarī; dekhi' — seeing; ha-ila — there was; yamunā smarana — remembrance of the river Yamunā; tīre — on the banks; vana — the forests; dekhi' — seeing; smriti — remembrance; haila — there was; vrindāvana — Śrī Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

When He saw the river Godāvarī, the Lord remembered the river Yamunā, and when He saw the forest on the banks of the river, He remembered Śrī Vrindāvana-dhāma.
