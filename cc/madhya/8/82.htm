Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.82

krishna-prāptira upāya bahu-vidha haya

krishna-prāpti-tāratamya bahuta āchaya

SYNONYMS

krishna-prāptira — of achieving the lotus feet of Krishna; upāya — means; bahu-vidha — various; haya — there are; krishna-prāpti — of achieving the favor of Lord Krishna; tāratamya — comparisons; bahuta — various; āchaya — there are.

TRANSLATION

"There are various means and processes by which one may attain the favor of Lord Krishna. All those transcendental processes will be studied from the viewpoint of comparative importance.
