Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.220

sei gopī-bhāvāmrite yāńra lobha haya

veda-dharma-loka tyaji' se krishne bhajaya

SYNONYMS

sei — that; gopī — of the gopīs; bhāva-amrite — in the nectar of the ecstasy; yāńra — whose; lobha — attachment; haya — is; veda-dharma — religious principles of the Vedas; loka — popular opinion; tyaji' — giving up; se — he; krishne — unto Krishna; bhajaya — renders loving service.

TRANSLATION

"One who is attracted by that ecstatic love of the gopīs does not care about popular opinion or the regulative principles of Vedic life. Rather, he completely surrenders unto Krishna and renders service unto Him.
