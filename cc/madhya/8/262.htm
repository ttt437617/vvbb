Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.262

ishta-goshthī krishna-kathā kahi' kata-kshana

prabhu-pada dhari' rāya kare nivedana

SYNONYMS

ishta-goshthī — spiritual discussion; krishna-kathā — topics of Krishna; kahi' — talking; kata-kshana — for some time; prabhu-pada — the lotus feet of the Lord; dhari' — catching; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kare — makes; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

That evening, after discussing the topics of Krishna for some time, Rāmānanda Rāya caught hold of the lotus feet of the Lord and spoke as follows.
