Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.29

sustha hañā duńhe sei sthānete vasilā

tabe hāsi' mahāprabhu kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

su-stha hañā — becoming steady; duńhe — both of them; sei — that; sthānete — in the place; vasilā — sat down; tabe — then; hāsi' — smiling; mahāprabhu — Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahite — to speak; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

When they regained their sanity, they both sat down, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and began to speak as follows.
