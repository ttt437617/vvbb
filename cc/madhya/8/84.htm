Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.84

yathottaram asau svāda-

viśeshollāsa-mayy api

ratir vāsanayā svādvī

bhāsate kāpi kasyacit

SYNONYMS

yathā uttaram — one after another; asau — that; svāda-viśesha — of particular tastes; ullāsa — pleasing; mayī — empowered with; api — although; ratih — love; vāsanayā — by desire; svādvī — sweet; bhāsate — appears; kā api — someone; kasyacit — one of them.

TRANSLATION

"'Increasing love is experienced in various tastes, one above another. But that love which has the highest taste in the gradual succession of desires manifests itself in the form of conjugal love.'

PURPORT

This verse is from Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (2.5.38), and it also appears in Ādi-līlā, Chapter Four, verse 45.
