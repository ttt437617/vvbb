Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.162

premera 'svarūpa-deha' — prema-vibhāvita

krishnera preyasī-śreshthā jagate vidita

SYNONYMS

premera — love of Godhead; svarūpa-deha — actual body; prema — by love of Godhead; vibhāvita — influence; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; preyasī — of the dear friends; śreshthā — topmost; jagate — throughout the whole world; vidita — known.

TRANSLATION

"The body of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is a veritable transformation of love of Godhead; She is the dearmost friend of Krishna, and this is known throughout the world.
