Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.120

kripā kari' ei tattva kaha ta' āmāre

tomā-vinā keha ihā nirūpite nāre

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — showing your mercy; ei tattva — all these truths; kaha — explain; ta' — certainly; āmāre — unto Me; tomā-vinā — except for you; keha — someone; ihā — this; nirūpite — to ascertain; nāre — not able.

TRANSLATION

"Kindly explain all these truths to Me. But for yourself, no one can ascertain them."
