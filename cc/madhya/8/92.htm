Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.92

ei 'preme'ra anurūpa nā pāre bhajite

ataeva 'rinī' haya — kahe bhāgavate

SYNONYMS

ei — this; premera — of love of God; anurūpa — exactly to the proportion; nā — not; pāre — is able; bhajite — to reciprocate; ataeva — therefore; rinī — debtor; haya — becomes; kahe — is stated; bhāgavate — in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.

TRANSLATION

"In Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam [10.32.22] it is said that Lord Krishna cannot proportionately reciprocate devotional service in the mādhurya-rasa; therefore He always remains a debtor to such devotees.
