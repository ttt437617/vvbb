Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.32

rāya kahe, — sārvabhauma kare bhritya-jñāna

paroksheha mora hite haya sāvadhāna

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya replied; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kare — does; bhritya-jñāna — thinks of me as his servant; paroksheha — in my absence; mora — of me; hite — for the benefit; haya — is; sāvadhāna — always careful.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya replied, "Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya thinks of me as his servant. Even in my absence he is very careful to do me good.
