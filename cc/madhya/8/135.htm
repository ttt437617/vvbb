Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.135

ananta vaikuntha, āra ananta avatāra

ananta brahmānda ihāń, — sabāra ādhāra

SYNONYMS

ananta vaikuntha — innumerable Vaikuntha planets; āra — and; ananta avatāra — innumerable incarnations; ananta brahmānda — innumerable universes; ihāń — in this material world; sabāra — of all of them; ādhāra — the resting place.

TRANSLATION

"There are innumerable Vaikuntha planets, as well as innumerable incarnations. In the material world also there are innumerable universes, and Krishna is the supreme resting place for all of them.
