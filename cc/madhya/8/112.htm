Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.112

krodha kari' rāsa chādi' gelā māna kari'

tāńre nā dekhiyā vyākula haila śrī-hari

SYNONYMS

krodha kari' — becoming angry; rāsa chādi' — leaving the rāsa dance; gelā — went; māna kari' — being resentful; tāńre — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; nā dekhiyā — not seeing; vyākula — very anxious; haila — became; śrī-hari — Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"When Rādhārānī left the rāsa dance out of anger and resentment, Lord Śrī Krishna became very anxious because He could not see Her.
