Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.157

krishnake āhlāde, tā'te nāma — 'hlādinī'

sei śakti-dvāre sukha āsvāde āpani

SYNONYMS

krishnake — unto Krishna; āhlāde — gives pleasure; tā'te — therefore; nāma — the name; hlādinī — pleasure-giving potency; sei śakti — that potency; dvāre — by means of; sukha — happiness; āsvāde — tastes; āpani — Lord Krishna personally.

TRANSLATION

"The potency called hlādinī gives Krishna transcendental pleasure. Through this pleasure potency, Krishna personally tastes all spiritual pleasure.
