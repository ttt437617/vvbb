Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.13

ghāta chādi' kata-dūre jala-sannidhāne

vasi' prabhu kare krishna-nāma-sańkīrtane

SYNONYMS

ghāta chādi' — leaving the bathing place; kata-dūre — a short distance away; jala-sannidhāne — near the water; vasi' — sitting; prabhu — the Lord; kare — does; krishna-nāma-sańkīrtane — chanting of the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

After bathing in the river, the Lord walked a little distance from the bathing place and engaged in chanting the holy name of Krishna.
