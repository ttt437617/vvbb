Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.153

vishnu-śaktih parā proktā

kshetrajñākhyā tathā parā

avidyā-karma-samjñānyā

tritīyā śaktir ishyate

SYNONYMS

vishnu-śaktih — the potency of Lord Vishnu; parā — spiritual; proktā — it is said; kshetrajña-ākhyā — the potency known as kshetra-jña; tathā — as well as; parā — spiritual; avidyā — ignorance; karma — fruitive activities; samjñā — known as; anyā — other; tritīyā — third; śaktih — potency; ishyate — known thus.

TRANSLATION

"'The original potency of Lord Vishnu is superior, or spiritual, and the living entity actually belongs to that superior energy. But there is another energy, called the material energy, and this third energy is full of ignorance.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Vishnu Purāna (6.7.61).
