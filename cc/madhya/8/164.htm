Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.164

sei mahābhāva haya 'cintāmani-sāra'

krishna-vāñchā pūrna kare ei kārya tāńra

SYNONYMS

sei — that; mahā-bhāva — supreme ecstasy; haya — is; cintāmani-sāra — the essence of spiritual life; krishna-vāñchā — all the desires of Lord Krishna; pūrna kare — fulfills; ei — this; kārya — business; tāńra — Her.

TRANSLATION

"That supreme ecstasy of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is the essence of spiritual life. Her only business is to fulfill all the desires of Krishna.
