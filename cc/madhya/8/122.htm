Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.122

tomāra śikshāya padi yena śuka-pātha

sākshāt īśvara tumi, ke bujhe tomāra nāta

SYNONYMS

tomāra śikshāya — by Your instruction; padi — I recite; yena — like; śuka-pātha — the reciting of a parrot; sākshāt — directly; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; tumi — You; ke — who; bujhe — can understand; tomāra — Your; nāta — dramatic performance.

TRANSLATION

"I simply repeat like a parrot whatever instructions You have given me. You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. Who can understand Your dramatic performances?
