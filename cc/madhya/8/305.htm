Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.305

rādhā-krishna-līlā — tāte karpūra-milana

bhāgyavān yei, sei kare āsvādana

SYNONYMS

rādhā-krishna-līlā — the pastimes of Śrī Rādhā and Krishna; tāte — in that composition; karpūra — the camphor; milana — mixture; bhāgyavān — fortunate; yei — one who; sei — that person; kare — does; āsvādana — tasting.

TRANSLATION

Their meeting is exactly like a mixture of condensed milk and sugar candy. When they talk of the pastimes of Rādhā and Krishna, camphor is added. One who tastes this combined preparation is most fortunate.
