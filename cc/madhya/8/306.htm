Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.306

ye ihā eka-bāra piye karna-dvāre

tāra karna lobhe ihā chādite nā pāre

SYNONYMS

ye — anyone; ihā — this; eka-bāra — once; piye — drinks; karna-dvāre — through aural reception; tāra — his; karna — ears; lobhe — in greed; ihā — this; chādite — to give up; nā — not; pāre — are able.

TRANSLATION

This wonderful preparation has to be taken aurally. If one takes it, he becomes greedy to relish it even further.
