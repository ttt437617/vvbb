Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.119

'krishnera svarūpa' kaha 'rādhāra svarūpa'

'rasa' kon tattva, 'prema' — kon tattva-rūpa

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa — the transcendental features; kaha — speak; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; svarūpa — the transcendental features; rasa — mellows; kon — what; tattva — that truth; prema — love of Godhead; kon — what; tattva-rūpa — actual form.

TRANSLATION

"Kindly explain the transcendental features of Krishna and Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. Also explain the truth of transcendental mellows and the transcendental form of love of Godhead.
