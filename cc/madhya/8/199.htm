Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.199

tribhuvana-madhye aiche haya kon dhīra

ye tomāra māyā-nāte ha-ibeka sthira

SYNONYMS

tri-bhuvana-madhye — within the three worlds; aiche — so much; haya — there is; kon — who; dhīra — patient; ye — who; tomāra — Your; māyā-nāte — in the manipulation of different energies; ha-ibeka — will be; sthira — steady.

TRANSLATION

"Within these three worlds, who is so undisturbed that he can remain steady as You manipulate Your different energies?
