Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.203

sakhī vinā ei līlā pushta nāhi haya

sakhī līlā vistāriyā, sakhī āsvādaya

SYNONYMS

sakhī vinā — without the gopīs; ei līlā — these pastimes; pushta — nourished; nāhi haya — are never; sakhī — the gopīs; līlā — the pastimes; vistāriyā — expanding; sakhī — the gopīs; āsvādaya — taste this mellow.

TRANSLATION

"Without the gopīs, these pastimes between Rādhā and Krishna cannot be nourished. Only by their cooperation are such pastimes broadcast. It is their business to taste the mellows.
