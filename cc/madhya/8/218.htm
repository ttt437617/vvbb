Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.218

nijendriya-sukha-vāñchā nāhi gopikāra

krishne sukha dite kare sańgama-vihāra

SYNONYMS

nija-indriya-sukha — for personal sense gratification; vāñchā — the desire; nāhi — there is not; gopikāra — of the gopīs; krishne — unto Krishna; sukha — happiness; dite — to give; kare — do; sańgama-vihāra — mingling and enjoying with Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Among the gopīs, there is not a pinch of desire for sense gratification. Their only desire is to give pleasure to Krishna, and this is why they mingle with Him and enjoy with Him.
