Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.118

ebe se jāniluń sādhya-sādhana-nirnaya

āge āra āche kichu, śunite mana haya

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; se — that; jāniluń — I have understood; sādhya — of the ultimate goal; sādhana — and of the process; nirnaya — the ascertainment; āge — ahead; āra — more; āche — there is; kichu — something; śunite — to hear; mana — the mind; haya — it is.

TRANSLATION

"Now I have come to understand the sublime goal of life and the process of achieving it. Nevertheless, I think that there is something more ahead, and My mind is desiring to have it.
