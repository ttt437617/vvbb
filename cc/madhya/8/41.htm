Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.41

āmāra sańge brāhmanādi sahasreka jana

tomāra darśane sabāra dravī-bhūta mana

SYNONYMS

āmāra sańge — with me; brāhmana-ādi — brāhmanas and others; sahasreka — more than one thousand; jana — persons; tomāra — of You; darśane — in seeing; sabāra — of all of them; dravī-bhūta — became melted; mana — the hearts.

TRANSLATION

"Along with me there are about a thousand men — including the brāhmanas — and all of them appear to have had their hearts melted simply by seeing You.
