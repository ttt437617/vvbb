Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.21

tathāpi puchila, — tumi rāya rāmānanda?

teńho kahe, — sei hańa dāsa śūdra manda

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; puchila — He inquired; tumi — you; rāya rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; teńho kahe — he replied; sei hańa — I am that; dāsa — servant; śūdra — belonging to the śūdra community; manda — very low.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then inquired whether he was Rāmānanda Rāya, and he replied, "Yes, I am Your very low servant, and I belong to the śūdra community."
