Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.180

krishnake karāya śyāma-rasa-madhu pāna

nirantara pūrna kare krishnera sarva-kāma

SYNONYMS

krishnake — unto Krishna; karāya — She induces; śyāma-rasa — of the mellow of conjugal love; madhu — the honey; pāna — drinking; nirantara — constantly; pūrna — complete; kare — makes; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sarva-kāma — all kinds of lusty desires.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīmatī Rādhārānī induces Krishna to drink the honey of the conjugal relationship. She is therefore engaged in satisfying all the lusty desires of Krishna.
