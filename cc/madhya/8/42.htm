Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.42

'krishna' 'krishna' nāma śuni sabāra vadane

sabāra ańga — pulakita, aśru — nayane

SYNONYMS

krishna krishna — "Krishna," "Krishna"; nāma — the holy name; śuni — I hear; sabāra — of everyone; vadane — in the mouths; sabāra — of all; ańga — the bodies; pulakita — gladdened; aśru — tears; nayane — in the eyes.

TRANSLATION

"I hear everyone chanting the holy name of Krishna. Everyone's body is thrilled with ecstasy, and there are tears in everyone's eyes.
