Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.101

prabhu kahe — āge kaha, śunite pāi sukhe

apūrvāmrita-nadī vahe tomāra mukhe

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; āge — ahead; kaha — please speak; śunite — to hear; pāi — I get; sukhe — happiness; apūrva-amrita — of unprecedented nectar; nadī — a river; vahe — flows; tomāra mukhe — from your mouth.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please speak on. I am very happy to hear you because a river of unprecedented nectar is flowing from your mouth.
