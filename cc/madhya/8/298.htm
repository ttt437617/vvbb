Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.298

dui-jane nīlācale rahiba eka-sańge

sukhe gońāiba kāla krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

dui-jane — both of us; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; rahiba — shall stay; eka-sańge — together; sukhe — in happiness; gońāiba — shall pass; kāla — time; krishna-kathā-rańge — in the pleasure of discussing topics about Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"The two of us shall remain together at Jagannātha Purī and happily pass our time discussing Krishna."
