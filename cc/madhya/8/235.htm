Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.235

vidāya-samaye prabhura carane dhariyā

rāmānanda rāya kahe vinati kariyā

SYNONYMS

vidāya-samaye — at the point of departure; prabhura carane — the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhariyā — capturing; rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kahe — says; vinati kariyā — with great humility.

TRANSLATION

Before departing from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Rāmānanda Rāya fell to the ground and caught hold of the Lord's lotus feet. He then spoke submissively as follows.
