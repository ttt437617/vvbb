Madhya-līlāChapter 8: Talks Between Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 8.12

sei vane kata-kshana kari' nritya-gāna

godāvarī pāra hañā tāhāń kaila snāna

SYNONYMS

sei vane — in that forest; kata-kshana — for some time; kari' — performing; nritya-gāna — dancing and chanting; godāvarī — the river; pāra hañā — crossing; tāhāń — there; kaila — took; snāna — bath.

TRANSLATION

After performing His usual chanting and dancing for some time in this forest, the Lord crossed the river and took His bath on the other bank.
