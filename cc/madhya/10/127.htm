Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.127

jagadānanda, mukunda, śańkara, sārvabhauma

sabā-sańge yathā-yogya karila milana

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; mukunda — Mukunda; śańkara — Śańkara; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma; sabā-sańge — with all; yathā-yogya — as is befitting; karila — did; milana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

After worshiping Nityānanda Prabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara met Jagadānanda, Mukunda, Śańkara and Sārvabhauma, as was befitting.
