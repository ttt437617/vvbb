Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.187

yata nada nadī yaiche samudre milaya

aiche mahāprabhura bhakta yāhāń tāhāń haya

SYNONYMS

yata — all; nada nadī — rivers; yaiche — as; samudre — in the sea; milaya — meet; aiche — similarly; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta — devotees; yāhāń tāhāń — wherever; haya — they were.

TRANSLATION

As all the rivers flow into the sea, all the devotees throughout the country finally came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's shelter.
