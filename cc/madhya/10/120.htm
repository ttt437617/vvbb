Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.120

uthāñā mahāprabhu kaila ālińgana

dui-jane premāveśe haila acetana

SYNONYMS

uthāñā — after raising him; mahāprabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — made; ālińgana — embracing; dui-jane — two persons; prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love; haila — became; acetana — unconscious.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu raised Svarūpa Dāmodara to his feet and embraced him. They both became ecstatic in love and fell unconscious.
