Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.152

ājñā deha' yadi tāńre āniye ethāi

prabhu kahe, — guru teńha, yāba tāńra thāñi

SYNONYMS

ājñā deha' — order; yadi — if; tāńre — him; āniye — I can bring; ethāi — here; prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; guru teńha — he is My spiritual master; yāba — I shall go; tāńra thāñi — to his place.

TRANSLATION

Mukunda Datta then asked the Lord, "Shall I bring him here?"

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Brahmānanda Bhāratī is like My spiritual master. It is better that I go to him."
