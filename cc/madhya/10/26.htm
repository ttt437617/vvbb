Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.26

śuni' ānandita haila sabākāra mana

sabe āsi' sārvabhaume kaila nivedana

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; ānandita — happy; haila — were; sabākāra — of everyone; mana — the minds; sabe āsi' — everyone coming; sārvabhaume — unto Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kaila — did; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

Hearing of the Lord's return, everyone became very happy, and they all went to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and spoke to him as follows.
