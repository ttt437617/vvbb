Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.176

krishna-nāma sphure mukhe, mane netre krishna

tomāke tad-rūpa dekhi' hridaya — satrishna

SYNONYMS

krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; sphure — is manifest; mukhe — in the mouth; mane — in the mind; netre — before the eyes; krishna — the presence of Lord Krishna; tomāke — You; tat-rūpa — His form; dekhi' — I see; hridaya — my heart; sa-trishna — very eager.

TRANSLATION

Brahmānanda Bhāratī continued, "Since I have seen You, I have been feeling Lord Krishna's presence in my mind and have been seeing Him before my eyes. I now want to chant the holy name of Lord Krishna. Over and above this, within my heart I consider You to be Krishna, and I am therefore very eager to serve You.
