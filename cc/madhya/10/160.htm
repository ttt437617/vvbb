Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.160

āji haite nā pariba ei carmāmbara

prabhu bahirvāsa ānāilā jāniyā antara

SYNONYMS

āji haite — from today; nā pariba — I shall not put on; ei — this; carma-ambara — deerskin garment; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bahir-vāsa — the cloth of a sannyāsī; ānāilā — had someone bring; jāniyā — knowing; antara — his contemplation.

TRANSLATION

"From today on I shall not wear this deerskin." As soon as Brahmānanda Bhāratī decided this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, understanding his mind, immediately sent for the robes of a sannyāsī.
