Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.29

āra dina mahāprabhu bhattācāryera sańge

jagannātha daraśana kaila mahā-rańge

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhattācāryera sańge — with Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; jagannātha — of Lord Jagannātha; daraśana — visiting the temple; kaila — did; mahā-rańge — with great enthusiasm.

TRANSLATION

The next day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived and went with Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, with great enthusiasm, to see the temple of Lord Jagannātha.
