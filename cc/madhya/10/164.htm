Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.164

tumi — gaura-varna, teńha — śyāmala-varna

dui brahme kaila saba jagat-tārana

SYNONYMS

tumi — You; gaura-varna — having a golden or fair complexion; teńha — He; śyāmala-varna — having a blackish complexion; dui brahme — both Brahmans; kaila — performed; saba jagat — of the whole world; tārana — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"Of the two Brahmans, You are fair-complexioned, and the other, Lord Jagannātha, is blackish. Both of You are delivering the whole world."
