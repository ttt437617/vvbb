Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.62

bhattācārya saba loke vidāya karāila

tabe prabhu kālā-krishnadāse bolāila

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; saba loke — all persons; vidāya karāila — asked to leave; tabe — at that time; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kālā-krishnadāse — Kālā Krishnadāsa; bolāila — called for.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya then asked all the people to leave. Afterward, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called for Kālā Krishnadāsa, who had accompanied the Lord during His South Indian tour.
