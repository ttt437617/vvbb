Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.20

thākurera nikata, āra ha-ibe nirjane

e-mata nirnaya kari' deha' eka sthāne

SYNONYMS

thākurera nikata — near the place of Lord Jagannātha; āra — also; ha-ibe — must be; nirjane — secluded; e-mata — in this way; nirnaya kari' — considering carefully; deha' — please give; eka sthāne — one place.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Caitanya's residence should be very secluded and also near the temple of Jagannātha. Please consider this proposal and give me a nice place for Him."
