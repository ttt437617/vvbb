Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.75

tabe gauda-deśe āilā kālā-krishnadāsa

navadvīpe gela teńha śacī-āi-pāśa

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; gauda-deśe — to Bengal; āilā — came; kālā-krishnadāsa — Kālā Krishnadāsa; navadvīpe — to Navadvīpa; gela — went; teńha — he; śacī-āi-pāśa — before mother Śacī.

TRANSLATION

Thus Kālā Krishnadāsa went to Bengal, and he first went to Navadvīpa to see mother Śacī.
