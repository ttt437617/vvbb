Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.180

bhattācārya kahe, — dońhāra susatya vacana

āge yadi krishna dena sākshāt daraśana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said; dońhāra — of both; su-satya — correct; vacana — statements; āge — first; yadi — if; krishna — Lord Krishna; dena — gives; sākshāt — direct; daraśana — audience.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said, "The statements of both of you are correct. Krishna gives direct audience through His mercy.
