Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.38

tabe sārvabhauma prabhura dakshina-pārśve vasi'

milāite lāgilā saba purushottama-vāsī

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dakshina-pārśve — by the right side; vasi' — sitting; milāite — to introduce; lāgilā — began; saba — all; purushottama-vāsī — residents of Purushottama (Jagannātha Purī).

TRANSLATION

After this, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, sitting at the right hand of the Lord, began to introduce all the inhabitants of Purushottama, Jagannātha Purī.
