Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.130

āra dina sārvabhauma-ādi bhakta-sańge

vasiyā āchena mahāprabhu krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; sārvabhauma-ādi — headed by Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; bhakta-sańge — with the devotees; vasiyā āchena — was sitting; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-kathā-rańge — engaged in discussions of topics concerning Krishna.

TRANSLATION

The next day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat with all the devotees, headed by Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, and they discussed the pastimes of Krishna.
