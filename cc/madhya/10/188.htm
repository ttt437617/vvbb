Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.188

sabe āsi' mililā prabhura śrī-carane

prabhu kripā kari' sabāya rākhila nija sthāne

SYNONYMS

sabe — all; āsi' — coming; mililā — met; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrī-carane — under the shelter; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā kari' — showing mercy; sabāya — every one of them; rākhila — kept; nija sthāne — under His protection.

TRANSLATION

Since all the devotees came to Him for shelter, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed them all mercy and kept them under His protection.
