Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.11

tīrtha pavitra karite kare tīrtha-bhramana

sei chale nistāraye sāmsārika jana

SYNONYMS

tīrtha — holy places; pavitra karite — to purify; kare — does; tīrtha-bhramana — touring in places of pilgrimage; sei chale — on that plea; nistāraye — delivers; sāmsārika — conditioned; jana — souls.

TRANSLATION

"Great saints go to holy places of pilgrimage in order to purify them. For that reason Caitanya Mahāprabhu is visiting many tīrthas and delivering many, many conditioned souls.
