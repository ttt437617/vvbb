Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.121

kata-kshane dui jane sthira yabe hailā

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

kata-kshane — after some time; dui jane — both persons; sthira — patient; yabe — when; hailā — became; tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; kahite — to speak; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

After they had regained their patience, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to speak.
