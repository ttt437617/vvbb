Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.25

sarva-lokera utkanthā yabe atyanta bādila

mahāprabhu dakshina haite tabahi āila

SYNONYMS

sarva-lokera — of all people; utkanthā — anxieties; yabe — when; atyanta — very much; bādila — increased; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dakshina haite — from South India; tabahi — at that very time; āila — returned.

TRANSLATION

When all the residents of Jagannātha Purī became extremely anxious to meet the Lord again, He returned from South India.
