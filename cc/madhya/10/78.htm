Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.78

śuniyā sabāra haila parama ullāsa

advaita-ācārya-grihe gelā krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; sabāra — of all; haila — there was; parama — supreme; ullāsa — happiness; advaita-ācārya — of Advaita Ācārya Prabhu; grihe — to the home; gelā — went; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa.

TRANSLATION

Hearing of Lord Caitanya's return to Purī, everyone became very glad. Krishnadāsa next went to the house of Advaita Ācārya.
