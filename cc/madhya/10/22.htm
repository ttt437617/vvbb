Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.22

eta kahi' rājā rahe utkanthita hañā

bhattācārya kāśī-miśre kahila āsiyā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; rājā — the King; rahe — remained; utkanthita — very anxious; hañā — being; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kāśī-miśre — unto Kāśī Miśra; kahila — said; āsiyā — coming.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, the King became very anxious for the Lord to return. Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya then went to Kāśī Miśra to convey the King's desire.
