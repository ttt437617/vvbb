Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.106

parama virakta teńha parama pandita

kāya-mane āśriyāche śrī-krishna-carita

SYNONYMS

parama — very; virakta — renounced; teńha — he; parama — great; pandita — learned scholar; kāya-mane — with body and mind; āśriyāche — took shelter of; śrī-krishna-carita — the Personality of Godhead Śrī Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara was a great renunciant as well as a great learned scholar. With heart and soul he took shelter of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Śrī Krishna.
