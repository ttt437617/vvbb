Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.138

īśvarera kripā jāti-kulādi nā māne

vidurera ghare krishna karilā bhojane

SYNONYMS

īśvarera kripā — the mercy of the Lord; jāti — caste; kula-ādi — family, etc.; nā māne — does not obey; vidurera — of Vidura; ghare — at the home; krishna — Lord Krishna; karilā — did; bhojane — eating.

TRANSLATION

"The mercy of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is not restricted to the jurisdiction of caste and creed. Vidura was a śūdra, yet Krishna accepted lunch at his home.
