Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.6

tomāre bahu kripā kailā, kahe sarva-jana

kripā kari' karāha more tāńhāra darśana

SYNONYMS

tomāre — unto you; bahu kripā — great mercy; kailā — showed; kahe — says; sarva-jana — everyone; kripā kari' — being merciful; karāha — arrange; more — for me; tāńhāra — His; darśana — interview.

TRANSLATION

"I have also heard that this great personality has shown you great favor. At any rate, this is what I hear from many different people. Now, being merciful upon me, you should do me the favor of arranging an interview."
