Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.179

prabhu kahe, — krishne tomāra gādha premā haya

yāhāń netra pade, tāhāń śrī-krishna sphuraya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; krishne — unto Krishna; tomāra — your; gādha — deep; premā — love; haya — there is; yāhāń — wherever; netra — eyes; pade — fall; tāhāń — there; śrī-krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; sphuraya — becomes manifest.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You have a deep ecstatic love for Krishna; therefore wherever you turn your eyes, you simply heighten your Krishna consciousness."
