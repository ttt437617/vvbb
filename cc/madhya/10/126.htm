Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.126

tabe svarūpa kaila nitāira carana vandana

nityānanda-prabhu kaila prema-ālińgana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; kaila — did; nitāira — of Nityānanda Prabhu; carana — of the lotus feet; vandana — worship; nityānanda-prabhu — Lord Nityānanda; kaila — did; prema-ālińgana — embracing in love.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara then worshiped the lotus feet of Nityānanda Prabhu, and Nityānanda Prabhu in turn embraced him in the ecstasy of love.
