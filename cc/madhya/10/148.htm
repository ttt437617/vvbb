Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.148

prabhura priya bhritya kari' sabe kare māna

sakala vaishnavera govinda kare samādhāna

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; priya — dear; bhritya — servant; kari' — understanding; sabe — all; kare — do; māna — respect; sakala — all; vaishnavera — of devotees; govinda — Govinda; kare — does; samādhāna — service.

TRANSLATION

Everyone respected Govinda as the dearest servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and Govinda served all the Vaishnavas and saw to their needs.
