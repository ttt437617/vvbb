Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.84

rāghava-pandita, āra ācārya nandana

kateka kahiba āra yata prabhura gana

SYNONYMS

rāghava-pandita — Rāghava Pandita; āra — and; ācārya nandana — the son of Advaita Ācārya; kateka — how many; kahiba — shall I describe; āra — other; yata — all; prabhura gana — associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Rāghava Pandita, the son of Advaita Ācārya and all the devotees became very satisfied. How many can I describe?
