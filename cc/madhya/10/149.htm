Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.149

chota-bada-kīrtanīyā — dui haridāsa

rāmāi, nandāi rahe govindera pāśa

SYNONYMS

chota-bada — junior and senior; kīrtanīyā — musicians; dui — two; haridāsa — Haridāsas; rāmāi — Rāmāi; nandāi — Nandāi; rahe — stay; govindera pāśa — with Govinda.

TRANSLATION

Both Haridāsa senior and Haridāsa junior, who were musicians, as well as Rāmāi and Nandāi, used to stay with Govinda.
