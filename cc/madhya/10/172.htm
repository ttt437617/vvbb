Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.172

bhattācārya kahe, — bhāratī, dekhi tomāra jaya

prabhu kahe, — yei kaha, sei satya haya

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — the Bhattācārya said; bhāratī — O Brahmānanda Bhāratī; dekhi — I see; tomāra jaya — your victory; prabhu kahe — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; yei kaha — whatever you say; sei — that; satya — true; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

After hearing this, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya rendered his judgment, saying, "Brahmānanda Bhāratī, I see that you are victorious."

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately said, "I accept whatever Brahmānanda Bhāratī has said. It is quite all right with Me."
