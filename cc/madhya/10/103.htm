Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.103

'purushottama ācārya' tāńra nāma pūrvāśrame

navadvīpe chilā teńha prabhura carane

SYNONYMS

purushottama ācārya — Purushottama Ācārya; tāńra — his; nāma — name; pūrva-āśrame — in the previous āśrama; navadvīpe — at Navadvīpa; chilā — was; teńha — he; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the feet.

TRANSLATION

When Svarūpa Dāmodara was residing at Navadvīpa under the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, his name was Purushottama Ācārya.
