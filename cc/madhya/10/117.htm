Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.117

advaita-nityānandera parama priyatama

śrīvāsādi bhakta-ganera haya prāna-sama

SYNONYMS

advaita — of Advaita Ācārya; nityānandera — of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; parama — very; priya-tama — dear; śrīvāsa-ādi — beginning with Śrīvāsa; bhakta-ganera — of the devotees; haya — is; prāna-sama — exactly like the life and soul.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara was very dear to Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, and he was the life and soul of all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura.
