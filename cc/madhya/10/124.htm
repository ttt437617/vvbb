Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.124

tomāra carane mora nāhi prema-leśa

tomā chādi' pāpī muñi genu anya deśa

SYNONYMS

tomāra carane — for Your lotus feet; mora — my; nāhi — there is not; prema-leśa — a trace of love; tomā — You; chādi' — giving up; pāpī — sinful; muñi — I; genu — went; anya deśa — to another country.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, I do not possess even a trace of love for Your lotus feet. If I did, how could I have gone to another country? I am therefore a most sinful man.
