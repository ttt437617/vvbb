Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.147

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre kaila ańgīkāra

āpana-śrī-ańga-sevāya dila adhikāra

SYNONYMS

tabe — after that; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto Govinda; kaila — did; ańgīkāra — acceptance; āpana — personal; śrī-ańga — of the transcendental body; sevāya — in the service; dila — gave; adhikāra — responsibility.

TRANSLATION

After Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Govinda and engaged him in the service of His personal body.
