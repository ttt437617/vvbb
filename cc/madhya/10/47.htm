Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.47

e-saba vaishnava — ei kshetrera bhūshana

ekānta-bhāve cinte sabe tomāra carana

SYNONYMS

e-saba vaishnava — all these pure devotees; ei kshetrera — of this holy place; bhūshana — ornaments; ekānta-bhāve — without deviation; cinte — meditate; sabe — all; tomāra carana — on Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"All these pure devotees serve as ornaments to Jagannātha Purī. They are always undeviatingly meditating upon Your lotus feet."
