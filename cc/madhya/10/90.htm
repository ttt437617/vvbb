Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.90

mukunda, narahari, raghunandana khanda haite

ācāryera thāñi āilā nīlācala yāite

SYNONYMS

mukunda — Mukunda; narahari — Narahari; raghunandana — Raghunandana; khanda haite — from the place known as Khanda; ācāryera thāñi — to Advaita Ācārya; āilā — came; nīlācala yāite — to go to Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī).

TRANSLATION

Mukunda, Narahari, Raghunandana and all the others came from Khanda to Advaita Ācārya's home to accompany Him to Jagannātha Purī.
