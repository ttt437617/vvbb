Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.153

eta bali' mahāprabhu bhakta-gana-sańge

cali' āilā brahmānanda-bhāratīra āge

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana-sańge — with the devotees; cali' — walking; āilā — came; brahmānanda-bhāratīra — of Brahmānanda Bhāratī; āge — in the presence.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees came into the presence of Brahmānanda Bhāratī.
