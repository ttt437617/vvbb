Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.5

śunilāńa tomāra ghare eka mahāśaya

gauda ha-ite āilā, teńho mahā-kripāmaya

SYNONYMS

śunilāńa — I have heard; tomāra — your; ghare — at home; eka — one; mahāśaya — great personality; gauda ha-ite — from Bengal; āilā — has come; teńho — He; mahā-kripā-maya — very merciful.

TRANSLATION

The King said to the Bhattācārya, "I have heard that a great personality has come from Bengal and is staying at your home. I have also heard that He is very, very merciful.
