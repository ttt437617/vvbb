Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.56

ei vānīnātha rahibe tomara carane

yabe yei ājñā, tāhā karibe sevane

SYNONYMS

ei vānīnātha — this Vānīnātha; rahibe — will remain; tomāra carane — at Your lotus feet; yabe — when; yei — whatever; ājñā — order; tāhā — that; karibe — will execute; sevane — service.

TRANSLATION

"This son Vānīnātha will remain at Your lotus feet to always immediately attend to Your orders and serve You.
