Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.45

candaneśvara, simheśvara, murāri brāhmana

vishnudāsa, — ińha dhyāye tomāra carana

SYNONYMS

candaneśvara — Candaneśvara; simheśvara — Simheśvara; murāri brāhmana — the brāhmana named Murāri; vishnudāsa — Vishnudāsa; ińha — all of them; dhyāye — meditate; tomāra — Your; carana — on the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"Here are Candaneśvara, Simheśvara, Murāri Brāhmana and Vishnudāsa. They are all constantly engaged in meditating on Your lotus feet.
