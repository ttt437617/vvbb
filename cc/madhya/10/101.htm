Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.101

kāśī-miśrera āvāse nibhrite eka ghara

prabhu tāńre dila, āra sevāra kińkara

SYNONYMS

kāśī-miśrera — of Kāśī Miśra; āvāse — at the house; nibhrite — solitary; eka — one; ghara — room; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto Paramānanda Purī; dila — gave; āra — and; sevāra — to serve him; kińkara — one servant.

TRANSLATION

There was a solitary room at Kāśī Miśra's house, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave it to Paramānanda Purī. He also gave him a servant.
