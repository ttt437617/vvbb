Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.98

purī kahe, — tomā-sańge rahite vāñchā kari'

gauda haite cali' āilāńa nīlācala-purī

SYNONYMS

purī kahe — Paramānanda Purī replied; tomā-sańge — with You; rahite — to stay; vāñchā kari' — desiring; gauda haite — from Bengal; cali' — traveling; āilāńa — I have come; nīlācala-purī — to Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī replied, "I also wish to stay with You. Therefore I have come from Bengal, Gauda, to Jagannātha Purī.
