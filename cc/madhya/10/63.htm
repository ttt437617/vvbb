Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.63

prabhu kahe, — bhattācārya, śunaha ińhāra carita

dakshina giyāchila ińha āmāra sahita

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; bhattācārya — My dear Bhattācārya; śunaha — just hear; ińhāra carita — his character; dakshina giyāchila — went to South India; ińha — this man; āmāra sahita — with Me.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Bhattācārya, just consider the character of this man who went with Me to South India.
