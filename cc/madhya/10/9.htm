Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.9

tathāpi prakāre tomā karāitāma daraśana

samprati karilā teńho dakshina gamana

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — yet; prakāre — somehow or other; tomā — you; karāitāma — I would have arranged; daraśana — interview; samprati — recently; karilā — has done; teńho — He; dakshina — to the southern part of India; gamana — departure.

TRANSLATION

"Still, I would have tried to arrange your interview, but He has recently left to tour South India."
