Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.115

vidyāpati, candīdāsa, śrī-gīta-govinda

ei tina gīte karā'na prabhura ānanda

SYNONYMS

vidyāpati — an old Vaishnava poet from the province of Mithilā; candīdāsa — a Bengali Vaishnava poet born in the village of Nānnura, in the Birbhum district; śrī-gīta-govinda — a celebrated poem by Jayadeva Gosvāmī; ei — these; tina — three; gīte — songs; karā'na — cause; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda — happiness.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara used to read the poems of Vidyāpati and Candīdāsa and Jayadeva Gosvāmī's Śrī Gīta-govinda. He used to make Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very happy by singing these songs.
