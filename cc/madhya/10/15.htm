Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.15

bhattācārya kahe, — teńho svayam īśvara svatantra

sākshāt śrī-krishna, teńho nahe para-tantra

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — Sārvabhauma replied; teńho — He; svayam — personally; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; svatantra — independent; sākshāt — directly; śrī-krishna — Lord Krishna; teńho — He; nahe — is not; para-tantra — dependent on anyone.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself and is completely independent. Being Lord Krishna Himself, He is not dependent on anyone.
