Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.40

trishita cātaka yaiche kare hāhākāra

taiche ei saba, — sabe kara ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

trishita — thirsty; cātaka — the cātaka bird; yaiche — just as; kare — does; hāhā-kāra — vibration of disappointment; taiche — similarly; ei saba — all of these; sabe — all of them; kara ańgīkāra — kindly accept.

TRANSLATION

"In Your absence all these people have been exactly like thirsty cātaka birds crying in disappointment. Kindly accept them."
