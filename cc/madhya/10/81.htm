Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.81

haridāsa thākurera haila parama ānanda

vāsudeva datta, gupta murāri, sena śivānanda

SYNONYMS

haridāsa thākurera — of Haridāsa Thākura; haila — was; parama — topmost; ānanda — ecstasy; vāsudeva datta — Vāsudeva Datta; gupta murāri — Murāri Gupta; sena śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena.

TRANSLATION

Also hearing this auspicious news, Haridāsa Thākura became very pleased. So also did Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta and Śivānanda Sena.
