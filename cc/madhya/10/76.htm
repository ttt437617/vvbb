Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.76

mahā-prasāda diyā tāńre kaila namaskāra

dakshina haite āilā prabhu, — kahe samācāra

SYNONYMS

mahā-prasāda diyā — delivering the mahā-prasādam; tāńre — unto Śacīmātā; kaila namaskāra — he offered respects by bowing down; dakshina haite — from the South India tour; āilā — came back; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe samācāra — he delivered this news.

TRANSLATION

Upon reaching mother Śacī, Kālā Krishnadāsa first offered his obeisances and delivered the food remnants [mahā-prasādam]. He then informed her of the good news that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had returned from His South Indian tour.
