Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.68

gauda-deśe pāthāite cāhi eka-jana

'āi'ke kahibe yāi, prabhura āgamana

SYNONYMS

gauda-deśe — to Bengal; pāthāite — to send; cāhi — we want; eka-jana — one person; āike — mother Śacīdevī; kahibe — will inform; yāi — going; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āgamana — arrival.

TRANSLATION

The Lord's four devotees considered, "We want a person to go to Bengal just to inform Śacīmātā about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival at Jagannātha Purī.
