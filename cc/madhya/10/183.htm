Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.183

eta bali' bhāratīre lañā nija-vāsā āilā

bhāratī-gosāñi prabhura nikate rahilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; bhāratīre — Brahmānanda Bhāratī; lañā — taking with Him; nija-vāsā āilā — returned to His own residence; bhāratī-gosāñi — Brahmānanda Bhāratī; prabhura nikate — in the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahilā — remained.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took Brahmānanda Bhāratī with Him to His residence. From that time on, Brahmānanda Bhāratī remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
