Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.21

rājā kahe, — aiche kāśī-miśrera bhavana

thākurera nikata, haya parama nirjana

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King replied; aiche — exactly like that; kāśī-miśrera bhavana — the house of Kāśī Miśra; thākurera nikata — near Lord Jagannātha; haya — is; parama — very; nirjana — secluded.

TRANSLATION

The King replied, "Kāśī Miśra's house is exactly what you require. It is near the temple and is very secluded, calm and quiet."
