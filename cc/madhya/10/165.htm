Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.165

prabhu kahe, — satya kahi, tomara āgamane

dui brahma prakatila śrī-purushottame

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; satya kahi — I speak the truth; tomāra āgamane — by your presence; dui brahma — two Brahmans; prakatila — appeared; śrī-purushottame — at Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Actually, to tell you the truth, due to your presence there are now two Brahmans at Jagannātha Purī.
