Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.69

advaita-śrīvāsādi yata bhakta-gana

sabei āsibe śuni' prabhura āgamana

SYNONYMS

advaita — Advaita Prabhu; śrīvāsa-ādi — and all the devotees like Śrīvāsa; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees; sabei — all; āsibe — will come; śuni' — hearing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āgamana — arrival.

TRANSLATION

"After hearing news of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's arrival, devotees like Advaita and Śrīvāsa will certainly come to see Him.
