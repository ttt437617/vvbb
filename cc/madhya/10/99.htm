Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.99

dakshina haite śuni' tomāra āgamana

śacī ānandita, āra yata bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

dakshina haite — from South India; śuni' — hearing; tomāra āgamana — Your return; śacī — mother Śacī; ānandita — very happy; āra — and; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees.

TRANSLATION

"At Navadvīpa, mother Śacī and all the other devotees were very glad to hear about Your return from South India.
