Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.39

ei saba loka, prabhu, vaise nīlācale

utkanthita hañāche sabe tomā milibāre

SYNONYMS

ei saba loka — all these people; prabhu — my Lord; vaise — reside; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; utkanthita hañāche — they have become very anxious; sabe — all; tomā — You; milibāre — to meet.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya said, "My dear Lord, all these people who are residents of Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, have been very anxious to meet You.
