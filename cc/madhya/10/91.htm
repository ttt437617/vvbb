Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.91

se-kāle dakshina haite paramānanda-purī

gańgā-tīre-tīre āilā nadīyā nagarī

SYNONYMS

se-kāle — at that time; dakshina haite — from the South; paramānanda-purī — Paramānanda Purī; gańgā-tīre-tīre — along the bank of the Ganges; āilā — came; nadīyā nagarī — to the town of Nadia.

TRANSLATION

At that time Paramānanda Purī came from South India. Traveling along the banks of the Ganges, he ultimately reached the town of Nadia.
