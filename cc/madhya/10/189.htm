Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.189

ei ta' kahila prabhura vaishnava-milana

ihā yei śune, pāya caitanya-carana

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; kahila — I have described; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaishnava-milana — meeting with all the Vaishnavas; ihā — this narration; yei — anyone who; śune — hears; pāya — gets; caitanya-carana — the shelter of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described the meeting of all the Vaishnavas with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Whoever hears this description ultimately attains shelter at His lotus feet.
