Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.32

kāśī-miśra āsi' padila prabhura carane

griha-sahita ātmā tāńre kaila nivedane

SYNONYMS

kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; āsi' — coming; padila — fell down; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the lotus feet; griha-sahita — with his house; ātmā — his personal self; tāńre — unto Him; kaila — did; nivedane — submission.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived at his house, Kāśī Miśra immediately fell down at His lotus feet and surrendered himself and all his possessions.
