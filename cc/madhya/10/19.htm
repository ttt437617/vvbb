Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.19

bhattācārya kahe, — teńho āsibe alpa-kāle

rahite tāńre eka sthāna cāhiye virale

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied; teńho — He; āsibe — will come; alpa-kāle — very soon; rahite — to keep; tāńre — Him; eka — one; sthāna — place; cāhiye — I want; virale — secluded.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "His Holiness Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will return very soon. I wish to have a nice place ready for Him, a place solitary and peaceful.
