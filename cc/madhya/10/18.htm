Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.18

punarapi ihāń tāńra haile āgamana

eka-bāra dekhi' kari saphala nayana

SYNONYMS

punarapi — again; ihāń — here; tāńra — His; haile — when there is; āgamana — arrival; eka-bāra — once; dekhi' — seeing; kari — I make; sa-phala — fruitful; nayana — my eyes.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returns, I wish to see Him just once in order to make my eyes perfect."
