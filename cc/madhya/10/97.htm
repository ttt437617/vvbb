Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.97

prabhu kahe, — tomā-sańge rahite vāñchā haya

more kripā kari' kara nīlādri āśraya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; tomā-sańge — with you; rahite — to stay; vāñchā haya — I desire; more — unto Me; kripā kari' — doing a favor; kara — accept; nīlādri — at Jagannātha Purī; āśraya — shelter.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please stay with Me and thus show Me favor, accepting the shelter of Jagannātha Purī."
