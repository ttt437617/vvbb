Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.95

satvare āsiyā teńha mililā prabhure

prabhura ānanda haila pāñā tāńhāre

SYNONYMS

satvare — very soon; āsiyā — coming; teńha — he; mililā — met; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda — happiness; haila — was; pāñā — getting; tāńhāre — him.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī very soon arrived at Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's place. The Lord was very happy to see him.
