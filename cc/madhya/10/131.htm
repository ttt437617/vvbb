Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.131

hena-kāle govindera haila āgamana

dandavat kari' kahe vinaya-vacana

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at that time; govindera — of Govinda; haila — there was; āgamana — arrival; dandavat kari' — offering obeisances; kahe — says; vinaya-vacana — submissive words.

TRANSLATION

At that time Govinda appeared on the scene, offered his respectful obeisances and spoke submissively.
