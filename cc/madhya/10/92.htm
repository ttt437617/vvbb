Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.92

āira mandire sukhe karilā viśrāma

āi tāńre bhikshā dilā kariyā sammāna

SYNONYMS

āira mandire — at the house of Śacīmātā; sukhe — in happiness; karilā — took; viśrāma — lodging; āi — mother Śacī; tāńre — unto him; bhikshā dilā — gave boarding; kariyā sammāna — with great respect.

TRANSLATION

At Navadvīpa, Paramānanda Purī took his board and lodging at the house of Śacīmātā. She provided him with everything very respectfully.
