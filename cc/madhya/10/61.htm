Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.61

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre ghare pāthāila

vānīnātha-pattanāyake nikate rākhila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him (Bhavānanda Rāya); ghare — to his home; pāthāila — sent back; vānīnātha-pattanāyake — Vānīnātha Pattanāyaka; nikate — near; rākhila — kept.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then sent Bhavānanda Rāya back to his home, and He kept only Vānīnātha Pattanāyaka in His personal service.
