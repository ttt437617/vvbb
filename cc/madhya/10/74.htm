Madhya-līlāChapter 10: The Lord's Return to Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 10.74

tabe sei krishnadāse gaude pāthāila

vaishnava-sabāke dite mahā-prasāda dila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sei — that; krishnadāse — Krishnadāsa; gaude — to Bengal; pāthāila — sent; vaishnava-sabāke — to all the Vaishnavas; dite — to deliver; mahā-prasāda — the remnants of Jagannātha's food; dila — they gave.

TRANSLATION

In this way Kālā Krishnadāsa was sent to Bengal, and he was given sufficient quantities of Lord Jagannātha's food remnants to distribute there.
