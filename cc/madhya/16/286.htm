Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.286

sei dina gadādhara kaila nimantrana

tāhāń bhikshā kaila prabhu lañā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

sei dina — that day; gadādhara — Gadādhara Pandita; kaila nimantrana — gave an invitation; tāhāń — at his place; bhikshā kaila — took lunch; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — with; bhakta-gana — His devotees.

TRANSLATION

That day Gadādhara Pandita extended an invitation to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord took His lunch at his place with the other devotees.
