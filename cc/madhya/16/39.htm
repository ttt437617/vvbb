Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.39

dui mālā govinda dui-jane parāila

advaita, avadhūta-gosāñi bada sukha pāila

SYNONYMS

dui mālā — the two garlands; govinda — Govinda; dui-jane parāila — placed on the necks of two personalities; advaita — Advaita Ācārya; avadhūta-gosāñi — Nityānanda Prabhu; bada sukha pāila — became very happy.

TRANSLATION

Govinda offered the two garlands to Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu, and They both became very happy.
