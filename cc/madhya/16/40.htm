Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.40

tāhāñi ārambha kaila krishna-sańkīrtana

nācite nācite cali' āilā dui-jana

SYNONYMS

tāhāñi — on that very spot; ārambha kaila — began; krishna-sańkīrtana — chanting the holy name of Lord Krishna; nācite nācite — dancing and dancing; cali' — going; āilā — reached; dui-jana — both of Them.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, They began chanting the holy name of Krishna on that very spot, and in this way, dancing and dancing, Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu reached Jagannātha Purī.
