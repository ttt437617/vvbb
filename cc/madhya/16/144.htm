Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.144

tumi jāna, krishna nija-pratijñā chādilā

bhakta kripā-vaśe bhīshmera pratijñā rākhilā

SYNONYMS

tumi jāna — you know; krishna — Lord Krishna; nija-pratijñā — His own promise; chādilā — gave up; bhakta kripā-vaśe — being obliged by the devotional service of a devotee; bhīshmera — of Grandfather Bhīshma; pratijñā rākhilā — kept the promise.

TRANSLATION

"You should know that Lord Krishna Himself violated His own promise just to keep the promise of Grandfather Bhīshma.
