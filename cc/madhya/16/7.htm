Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.7

rāmānanda, sārvabhauma, dui-janā-sthāne

tabe yukti kare prabhu — 'yāba vrindāvane'

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda — Rāmānanda; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma; dui-janā-sthāne — before the two persons; tabe — then; yukti kare — consulted; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yāba vrindāvane — I shall go to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself consulted Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, saying, "I shall go to Vrindāvana."
