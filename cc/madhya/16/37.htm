Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.37

prabhuke milite sabāra utkanthā antare

śīghra kari' āilā sabe śrī-nīlācale

SYNONYMS

prabhuke milite — to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāra — of everyone; utkanthā — anxiety; antare — within the heart; śīghra kari' — making great haste; āilā — reached; sabe — all of them; śrī-nīlācale — Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Everyone in the party was very anxious at heart to see Caitanya Mahāprabhu; therefore they hastily went on to Jagannātha Purī.
