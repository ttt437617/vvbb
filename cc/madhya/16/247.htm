Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.247

sabāra sahita ihāń āmāra ha-ila milana

e varsha 'nīlādri' keha nā kariha gamana

SYNONYMS

sabāra sahita — with everyone; ihāń — here; āmāra — of Me; ha-ila — there was; milana — meeting; e varsha — this year; nīlādri — to Jagannātha Purī; keha — any of you; nā — not; kariha gamana — go.

TRANSLATION

Because He had met them all at Śāntipura, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested all the devotees not to go to Jagannātha Purī that year.
