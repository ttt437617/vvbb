Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.278

gadādhare chādi' genu, ińho duhkha pāila

sei hetu vrindāvana yāite nārila

SYNONYMS

gadādhare chādi' — leaving aside Gadādhara Pandita; genu — I went; ińho — Gadādhara Pandita; duhkha pāila — became unhappy; sei hetu — for that reason; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana-dhāma; yāite nārila — I was unable to go.

TRANSLATION

"I left Gadādhara Pandita here, and he became very unhappy. For this reason I could not go to Vrindāvana."
