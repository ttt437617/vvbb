Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.222

sei govardhanera putra — raghunātha dāsa

bālya-kāla haite teńho vishaye udāsa

SYNONYMS

sei — that; govardhanera putra — son of Govardhana Majumadāra; raghunātha dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa; bālya-kāla haite — from his very childhood; teńho — he; vishaye udāsa — indifferent to material happiness.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa was the son of Govardhana Majumadāra. From childhood, he was uninterested in material enjoyment.
