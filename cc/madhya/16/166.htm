Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.166

sei saba loka haya bāulera prāya

'krishna' kahi' nāce, kānde, gadāgadi yāya

SYNONYMS

sei saba loka — all those persons; haya — are; bāulera prāya — almost like madmen; krishna kahi' — chanting the holy name of Krishna; nāce — they dance; kānde — they cry; gadāgadi yāya — they roll on the ground.

TRANSLATION

"All these people become like madmen. They simply chant the holy name of Krishna and dance. Sometimes they even cry and roll on the ground.
