Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.227

prabhu tāńre vidāya diyā gelā nīlācala

teńho ghare āsi' hailā premete pāgala

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto Raghunātha dāsa; vidāya diyā — bidding farewell; gelā — went back; nīlācala — to Jagannātha Purī; teńho — he; ghare āsi' — returning home; hailā — became; premete pāgala — mad in ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

After bidding farewell to Raghunātha dāsa, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Jagannātha Purī. After returning home, Raghunātha dāsa became mad with ecstatic love.
