Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.288

ei mata gaura-līlā — ananta, apāra

sańkshepe kahiye, kahā nā yāya vistāra

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; gaura-līlā — pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ananta — unlimited; apāra — unfathomed; sańkshepe — in brief; kahiye — I describe; kahā — describing; nā yāya vistāra — no one can do so elaborately and completely.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performs His pastimes, which are unlimited and unfathomable. Somehow or other, these have briefly been described. It is not possible to describe them elaborately.
