Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.22

śrīvāsa pandita-sańge calilā mālinī

śivānanda-sańge cale tāńhāra grihinī

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa pandita-sańge — with Śrīvāsa Pandita; calilā — went; mālinī — his wife, Mālinī; śivānanda-sańge — with Śivānanda Sena; cale — goes; tāńhāra — his; grihinī — wife.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Pandita also took his wife, Mālinī, and the wife of Śivānanda Sena also went with her husband.
