Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.192

tabe sei mahāprabhura carana vandiyā

sabāra carana vandi' cale hrishta hañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sei — the governor; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carana vandiyā — after worshiping the lotus feet; sabāra carana vandi' — offering respect to the feet of all the other devotees; cale — departed; hrishta hañā — being very pleased.

TRANSLATION

After this, the Muslim governor offered prayers to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, as well as to the lotus feet of all His devotees. After that, the governor departed. Indeed, he was very pleased.
