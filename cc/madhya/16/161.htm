Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.161

sei kāle se yavanera eka anucara

'udiyā-katake' āila kari' veśāntara

SYNONYMS

sei kāle — at that time; se yavanera — of the Muslim governor; eka anucara — one follower; udiyā-katake — to the camp of Orissan soldiers; āila — came; kari' veśa-antara — changing the dress.

TRANSLATION

At that time, a follower of the Muslim governor arrived at the Orissa encampment dressed in disguise.
