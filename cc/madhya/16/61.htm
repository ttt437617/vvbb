Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.61

tāńra mukha dekhi' hāse śacīra nandana

ańgīkāra jāni' ācārya karena nartana

SYNONYMS

tāńra mukha — His face; dekhi' — seeing; hāse — smiles; śacīra nandana — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ańgīkāra jāni' — understanding the acceptance; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; karena — performed; nartana — dancing.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the face of Advaita Ācārya, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled. Understanding that the Lord had accepted the proposal, Advaita Ācārya started to dance.
