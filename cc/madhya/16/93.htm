Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.93

duńhe kahe, — ebe varshā, calite nāribā

vijayā-daśamī āile avaśya calibā

SYNONYMS

duńhe kahe — both of them said; ebe — now; varshā — rainy season; calite nāribā — You will not be able to go; vijayā-daśamī — the Vijayā-daśamī day; āile — when it arrives; avaśya — certainly; calibā — You will go.

TRANSLATION

They both said, "Now that the rainy season is here, it will be difficult for You to travel. It is better to wait for Vijayā-daśamī before departing for Vrindāvana."
