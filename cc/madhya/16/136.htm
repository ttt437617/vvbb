Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.136

eta bali' pandita-gosāñi prithak calilā

kataka āsi' prabhu tāńre sańge ānāilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; pandita-gosāñi — Gadādhara Pandita; prithak calilā — proceeded separately; kataka āsi' — when He came to Kataka; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; sańge — with Him; ānāilā — brought.

TRANSLATION

Thus Gadādhara Pandita Gosvāmī traveled alone, but when they all arrived at Kataka, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu called him, and he went into the Lord's company.
