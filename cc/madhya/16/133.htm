Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.133

prabhu kahe, — "sevā chādibe, āmāya lāge dosha

ińhā rahi' sevā kara, — āmāra santosha"

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; sevā chādibe — you will give up the service; āmāya — to Me; lāge — will attach; dosha — fault; ińhā rahi' — staying here; sevā kara — just be engaged in service; āmāra — My; santosha — satisfaction.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "If you abandon His service, it will be My fault. It is better that you remain here and render service. That will be My satisfaction."
