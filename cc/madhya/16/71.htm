Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.71

teńho kahe, — "ke vaishnava, ki tāńra lakshana?"

tabe hāsi' kahe prabhu jāni' tāńra mana

SYNONYMS

teńho kahe — he said; ke — who; vaishnava — a Vaishnava; ki — what; tāńra — of him; lakshana — the symptoms; tabe — thereafter; hāsi' — smiling; kahe — says; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jāni' — knowing; tāńra mana — his mind.

TRANSLATION

The inhabitant of Kulīna-grāma said, "Please let me know who is actually a Vaishnava and what his symptoms are." Understanding his mind, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and gave the following reply.
