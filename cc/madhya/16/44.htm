Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.44

sabā lañā kaila jagannātha-daraśana

sabā lañā āilā punah āpana-bhavana

SYNONYMS

sabā lañā — taking all of them; kaila — performed; jagannātha-daraśana — seeing Lord Jagannātha; sabā lañā — taking them all; āilā — went back; punah — again; āpana-bhavana — to His own residence.

TRANSLATION

Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all His devotees visited Lord Jagannātha. Finally, accompanied by them all, He returned to His own residence.
