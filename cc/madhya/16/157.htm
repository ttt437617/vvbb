Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.157

dina dui-cāri teńho karila sevana

āge calibāre sei kahe vivarana

SYNONYMS

dina dui-cāri — two or four days; teńho — he; karila sevana — served the Lord; āge — forward; calibāre — for going; sei — that officer; kahe — spoke; vivarana — detailed information.

TRANSLATION

For two or four days, the government officer served the Lord. He also gave the Lord detailed information of what was ahead.
