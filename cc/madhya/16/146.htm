Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.146

ei-mata prabhu tomāra viccheda sahiyā

tomāra pratijñā rakshā kaila yatna kariyā"

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tomāra — of you; viccheda sahiyā — tolerating the separation; tomāra pratijñā — your vow; rakshā kaila — protected; yatna kariyā — with great endeavor.

TRANSLATION

"Similarly, tolerating separation from you, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has protected your vow with great endeavor."
