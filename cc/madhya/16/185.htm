Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.185

ińhāra ye ei gati, ithe ki vismaya?

tomāra darśana-prabhāva ei-mata haya'

SYNONYMS

ińhāra — of this Muslim governor; ye — which; ei — this; gati — result; ithe — in this; ki — what; vismaya — the wonder; tomāra — of You; darśana-prabhāva — influence of seeing; ei-mata haya — is like this.

TRANSLATION

"It is no wonder that this Muslim governor has attained such results. Simply by seeing You, all this is possible.
