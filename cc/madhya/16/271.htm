Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.271

mādhavendra-purī tathā gelā 'ekeśvare'

dugdha-dāna-cchale krishna sākshāt dila tāńre

SYNONYMS

mādhavendra-purī — Mādhavendra Purī; tathā — there; gelā — went; ekeśvare — alone; dugdha-dāna-chale — on the plea of giving milk in charity; krishna — Lord Krishna; sākshāt — direct audience; dila — gave; tāńre — unto him.

TRANSLATION

"Mādhavendra Purī went to Vrindāvana alone, and Krishna, on the pretext of giving him milk, granted him an audience.
