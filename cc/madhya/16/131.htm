Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.131

pandita kahe, — "yāhāń tumi, sei nīlācala

kshetra-sannyāsa mora yāuka rasātala"

SYNONYMS

pandita kahe — Gadādhara Pandita said; yāhāń — wherever; tumi — You are situated; sei — that; nīlācala — Jagannātha Purī; kshetra-sannyāsa — vow to remain in a holy place of pilgrimage; mora — my; yāuka — let it go; rasātala — to hell.

TRANSLATION

When he was requested to return to Jagannātha Purī, Gadādhara Pandita told the Lord, "Wherever You are staying is Jagannātha Purī. Let my so-called kshetra-sannyāsa go to hell."
