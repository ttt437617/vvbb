Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.24

ācāryaratna-sańge cale tāńhāra grihinī

tāńhāra premera kathā kahite nā jāni

SYNONYMS

ācāryaratna-sańge — with Candraśekhara; cale — goes; tāńhāra — his; grihinī — wife; tāńhāra — his; premera kathā — the description of the ecstatic love; kahite — to speak; nā jāni — I do not know how.

TRANSLATION

The wife of Candraśekhara [Ācāryaratna] also went. I cannot describe the greatness of Candraśekhara's love for the Lord.
