Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.83

tāra madhye ye ye varshe āchaye viśesha

vistāriyā āge tāhā kahiba nihśesha

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — within those episodes; ye ye — whatever; varshe — in years; āchaye — there is; viśesha — particular occurrence; vistāriyā — elaborating; āge — ahead; tāhā — that; kahiba — I shall say; nihśesha — completely.

TRANSLATION

Whatever happened during those years that is worth noting shall be described later.
