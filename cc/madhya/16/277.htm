Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.277

nirvighne ebe kaiche yāiba vrindāvane

sabe meli' yukti deha' hañā parasanne

SYNONYMS

nirvighne — without obstacles; ebe — now; kaiche — how; yāiba — I shall go; vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; sabe meli' — altogether; yukti deha' — give Me consultation; hañā parasanne — being very pleased with Me.

TRANSLATION

"Now I wish that you all will be pleased with Me and give Me good consultation. Tell Me how I shall be able to go to Vrindāvana without impediments.
