Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.198

jala-dasyu-bhaye sei yavana calila

daśa naukā bhari' bahu sainya sańge nila

SYNONYMS

jala-dasyu-bhaye — because of fearing pirates; sei — that; yavana — Muslim governor; calila — went along; daśa naukā bhari' — filling ten boats; bahu — many; sainya — soldiers; sańge — with him; nila — took.

TRANSLATION

The Muslim governor then personally accompanied Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Because of pirates, the governor took ten boats full of many soldiers.
