Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.224

prabhura carane pade premāvishta hañā

prabhu pāda-sparśa kaila karunā kariyā

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the lotus feet; pade — fell down; prema-āvishta — absorbed in ecstatic love; hañā — becoming; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāda-sparśa kaila — touched with His feet; karunā — mercy; kariyā — showing.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha dāsa went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he fell at the Lord's lotus feet in ecstatic love. Showing him mercy, the Lord touched him with His feet.
