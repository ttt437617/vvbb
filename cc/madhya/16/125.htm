Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.125

svagana-sahite prabhu prasāda ańgīkari'

uthiyā calilā prabhu bali' 'hari' 'hari'

SYNONYMS

sva-gana-sahite — with His personal associates; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prasāda — the remnants of food; ańgīkari' — accepting; uthiyā — standing up; calilā — started; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bali' — uttering; hari hari — Hari, Hari.

TRANSLATION

After accepting the prasādam, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up and started to go, chanting the holy names, "Hari! Hari!"
