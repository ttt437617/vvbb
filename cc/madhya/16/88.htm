Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.88

bahuta utkanthā mora yāite vrindāvana

tomāra hathe dui vatsara nā kailuń gamana

SYNONYMS

bahuta utkanthā — great anxiety; mora — My; yāite vrindāvana — to go to Vrindāvana; tomāra hathe — by your tricks; dui vatsara — for two years; nā kailuń — I did not do; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My desire to go to Vrindāvana has very much increased. Because of your tricks, I have not been able to go there for the past two years.
