Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.62

kibā prārthanā, kibā ājñā — keha nā bujhila

ālińgana kari' prabhu tāńre vidāya dila

SYNONYMS

kibā — what; prārthanā — the request; kibā — what; ājnā — the order; keha — anyone; nā bujhila — did not know; ālińgana kari' — embracing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — to Him; vidāya dila — bade farewell.

TRANSLATION

No one knew what Advaita Ācārya requested or what the Lord ordered. After embracing the Ācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade Him farewell.
