Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.54

pūrvavat ratha-yātrā kaila daraśana

herā-pañcamī-yātrā dekhe lañā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as in the previous year; ratha-yātrā — the car festival; kaila daraśana — saw; herā-pañcamī-yātrā — the festival of Herā-pañcamī; dekhe — He sees; lañā bhakta-gana — with all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

As in the previous year, the Lord, with all the devotees, saw the Ratha-yātrā festival and the Herā-pañcamī festival as well.
