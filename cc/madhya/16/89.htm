Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.89

avaśya caliba, duńhe karaha sammati

tomā-duńhā vinā mora nāhi anya gati

SYNONYMS

avaśya — certainly; caliba — I shall go; duńhe — both of you; karaha sammati — kindly agree to this proposal; tomā-duńhā vinā — except you two; mora — My; nāhi — there is not; anya gati — other resort.

TRANSLATION

"This time I must go. Will you please give Me permission? Save for you two, I have no other resort.
