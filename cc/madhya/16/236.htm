Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.236

sarvajña gaurāńga-prabhu jāni' tāńra mana

śikshā-rūpe kahe tāńre āśvāsa-vacana

SYNONYMS

sarva-jña — omniscient; gaurāńga-prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jāni' — knowing; tāńra — his; mana — mind; śikshā-rūpe — as an instruction; kahe — says; tāńre — unto Raghunātha dāsa; āśvāsa-vacana — words of assurance.

TRANSLATION

Since Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was omniscient, He could understand Raghunātha dāsa's mind. The Lord therefore instructed him with the following reassuring words.
