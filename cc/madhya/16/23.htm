Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.23

śivānandera bālaka, nāma — caitanya-dāsa

teńho caliyāche prabhure dekhite ullāsa

SYNONYMS

śivānandera bālaka — the son of Śivānanda; nāma — named; caitanya-dāsa — Caitanya dāsa; teńho — he; caliyāche — was going; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhite — to see; ullāsa — jubilantly.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya dāsa, the son of Śivānanda Sena, also jubilantly accompanied them as they went to see the Lord.
