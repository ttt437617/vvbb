Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.206

tāhāń haite āge gelā śivānanda-ghara

vāsudeva-grihe pāche āilā īśvara

SYNONYMS

tāhāń haite — from there; āge — ahead; gelā — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu proceeded; śivānanda-ghara — to the house of Śivānanda Sena; vāsudeva-grihe — to the house of Vāsudeva Datta; pāche — after this; āilā — came; īśvara — the Lord.

TRANSLATION

From the house of Śrīvāsa Thākura, the Lord went to the house of Śivānanda Sena and then to the house of Vāsudeva Datta.
