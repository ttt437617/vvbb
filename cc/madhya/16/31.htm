Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.31

kshīra bāńti' sabāre dila prabhu-nityānanda

kshīra-prasāda pāñā sabāra bādila ānanda

SYNONYMS

kshīra — the condensed milk; bāńti' — dividing; sabāre — unto everyone; dila — gave; prabhu-nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; kshīra-prasāda — remnants of condensed milk from the Deity; pāñā — getting; sabāra — of everyone; bādila — increased; ānanda — the transcendental bliss.

TRANSLATION

When the condensed milk was placed before Nityānanda Prabhu, He distributed the prasādam to everyone, and thus everyone's transcendental bliss increased.
