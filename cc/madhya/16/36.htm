Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.36

sākshi-gopālera kathā kahe nityānanda

śuniyā vaishnava-mane bādila ānanda

SYNONYMS

sākshi-gopālera — of the Deity known as Sākshi-gopāla; kathā — the narration; kahe — describes; nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; śuniyā — hearing; vaishnava-mane — in the minds of all the Vaishnavas; bādila — increased; ānanda — the transcendental bliss.

TRANSLATION

When Nityānanda Prabhu described all the activities of Sākshi-gopāla, transcendental bliss increased in the minds of all the Vaishnavas.

PURPORT

For these activities refer to Madhya-līlā, Chapter Five, verses 8-138.
