Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.148

prabhu lāgi' dharma-karma chāde bhakta-gana

bhakta-dharma-hāni prabhura nā haya sahana

SYNONYMS

prabhu lāgi' — for the sake of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dharma-karma — all prescribed duties; chāde — give up; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; bhakta-dharma — of the duty of a devotee; hāni — the abandonment; prabhura — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā haya — is not; sahana — tolerable.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees would abandon all kinds of duties for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's sake, yet the Lord did not like the devotees' giving up their promised duties.
