Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.204

rāghava-pandita āsi' prabhu lañā gelā

pathe yāite loka-bhide kashte-srishtye āilā

SYNONYMS

rāghava-pandita — Rāghava Pandita; āsi' — coming; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; gelā — went to his place; pathe yāite — passing on the road; loka-bhide — in the crowd of men; kashte-srishtye — with great difficulty; āilā — reached.

TRANSLATION

At length Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was taken away by Rāghava Pandita. There was a great crowd assembled along the way, and the Lord reached Rāghava Pandita's residence with great difficulty.
