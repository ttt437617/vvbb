Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.108

aiche tāńhāre kripā kaila gaurarāya

"pratāparudra-santrātā" nāma haila yāya

SYNONYMS

aiche — such; tāńhāre — unto the King; kripā — mercy; kaila — showed; gaurarāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pratāparudra-santrātā — the deliverer of Mahārāja Pratāparudra; nāma — the name; haila — became; yāya — by which.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed such mercy to the King that from that day on the Lord became known as Pratāparudra-santrātā, the deliverer of Mahārāja Pratāparudra.
