Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.135

āi'ke dekhite yāiba, nā yāiba tomā lāgi'

'pratijñā'-'sevā'-tyāga-dosha, tāra āmi bhāgī"

SYNONYMS

āi'ke — mother Śacīdevī; dekhite — to see; yāiba — I shall go; nā yāiba — I shall not go; tomā lāgi' — for Your sake; pratijñā-sevā — the vow and service to Gopīnātha; tyāga-dosha — the fault of giving up; tāra — for that; āmi bhāgī — I am responsible.

TRANSLATION

"I shall go to see Śacīmātā, but I shall not go for Your sake. I shall be responsible for the abandoning of my vow and service to Gopīnātha."
