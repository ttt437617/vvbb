Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.280

tumi yāhāń-yāhāń raha, tāhāń 'vrindāvana'

tāhāń yamunā, gańgā, sarva-tīrtha-gana

SYNONYMS

tumi — You; yāhāń-yāhāń — wherever; raha — stay; tāhāń vrindāvana — that place is Vrindāvana; tāhāń — there; yamunā — the river Yamunā; gańgā — the river Gańgā; sarva-tīrtha-gana — all other holy places of pilgrimage.

TRANSLATION

Gadādhara Pandita said, "Wherever You stay is Vrindāvana, as well as the river Yamunā, the river Ganges and all other places of pilgrimage.
