Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.256

'vrindāvana yāba āmi gauda-deśa diyā

nija-mātāra, gańgāra carana dekhiyā

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana yāba — shall go to Vrindāvana; āmi — I; gauda-deśa diyā — through Bengal; nija-mātāra — of My own mother; gańgāra — of the river Ganges; carana — the feet; dekhiyā — seeing.

TRANSLATION

"It was My decision to go to Vrindāvana through Bengal in order to see My mother and the river Ganges.
