Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.59

cāturmāsya-ante punah nityānande lañā

kibā yukti kare nitya nibhrite vasiyā

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsya-ante — at the end of Cāturmāsya; punah — again; nityānande — Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; lañā — taking into confidence; kibā — what; yukti kare — They consult; nitya — daily; nibhrite — in a solitary place; vasiyā — sitting.

TRANSLATION

At the end of the four-month Cāturmāsya period, Caitanya Mahāprabhu again consulted with Nityānanda Prabhu daily in a solitary place. No one could understand what Their consultation was about.
