Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.260

kashte-srishtye kari' gelāńa rāmakeli-grāma

āmāra thāñi āilā 'rūpa' 'sanātana' nāma

SYNONYMS

kashte-srishtye — with great difficulty; kari' — doing; gelāńa — I went; rāmakeli-grāma — to the village of Rāmakeli; āmāra thāñi — before Me; āilā — came; rūpa sanātana nāma — the two brothers named Rūpa and Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

"With great difficulty I went to the town of Rāmakeli, where I met two brothers named Rūpa and Sanātana.
