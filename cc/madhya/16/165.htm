Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.165

laksha laksha loka āise tāhā dekhibāre

tāńre dekhi' punarapi yāite nāre ghare

SYNONYMS

laksha laksha — millions upon millions; loka — people; āise — come; tāhā — that; dekhibāre — to see; tāńre dekhi' — after seeing Him; punarapi — again; yāite — to go; nāre — are not able; ghare — home.

TRANSLATION

"Many millions upon millions of people come to see Him, and after they see Him, they cannot return home.
