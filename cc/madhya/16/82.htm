Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.82

ei-mata pratyabda āise gaudera bhakta-gana

prabhu-sańge rahi' kare yātrā-daraśana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; prati-abda — every year; āise — come; gaudera — of Bengal; bhakta-gana — the devotees; prabhu-sańge — with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahi' — residing; kare — do; yātrā-daraśana — observing the Ratha-yātrā festival.

TRANSLATION

Every year the devotees of Bengal would come and stay with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to see the Ratha-yātrā festival.
