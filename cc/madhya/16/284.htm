Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.284

śuni' saba bhakta kahe prabhura carane

sabākāra icchā pandita kaila nivedane

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; saba — all; bhakta — devotees; kahe — said; prabhura carane — unto the lotus feet of the Lord; sabākāra icchā — everyone's desire; pandita — Gadādhara Pandita; kaila — has made; nivedane — submission.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this statement, the devotees present at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stated that Gadādhara Pandita had properly presented their desire.
