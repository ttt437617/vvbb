Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.30

sei rātri saba mahānta tāhāñi rahilā

bāra kshīra āni' āge sevaka dharilā

SYNONYMS

sei rātri — that night; saba mahānta — all the great devotees; tāhāñi rahilā — remained there; bāra — twelve pots; kshīra — condensed milk; āni' — bringing; āge — in front of Nityānanda Prabhu; sevaka — the priests; dharilā — placed.

TRANSLATION

That night, all the great devotees remained in the temple, and the priests brought twelve pots of condensed milk, which they placed before Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.
