Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.77

svarūpa-sahita tāńra haya sakhya-prīti

dui-janāya krishna-kathāya ekatra-i sthiti

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-sahita — with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; tāńra — his; haya — there is; sakhya-prīti — very intimate friendship; dui-janāya — both of them; krishna-kathāya — in topics of Krishna; ekatra-i — on the same level; sthiti — position.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Pundarīka Vidyānidhi had a friendly, intimate relationship, and as far as discussing topics about Krishna, they were situated on the same platform.
