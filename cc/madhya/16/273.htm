Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.273

ekā yāiba, kibā sańge bhritya eka-jana

tabe se śobhaya vrindāvanera gamana

SYNONYMS

ekā yāiba — I shall go alone; kibā — or; sańge — with Me; bhritya — servant; eka-jana — one; tabe — in that way; se — that; śobhaya — is beautiful; vrindāvanera gamana — going to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"I have therefore resolved to go alone or, at the utmost, with one servant. In this way, My journey to Vrindāvana will be beautiful.
