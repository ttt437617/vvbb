Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.242

eta kahi' mahāprabhu tāńre vidāya dila

ghare āsi' mahāprabhura śikshā ācarila

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto Raghunātha dāsa; vidāya dila — bade farewell; ghare āsi' — returning home; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śikshā — the instruction; ācarila — practiced.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to Raghunātha dāsa, who returned home and did exactly what the Lord told him.
