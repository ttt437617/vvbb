Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.11

yadyapi svatantra prabhu nahe nivārana

bhakta-icchā vinā prabhu nā kare gamana

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; svatantra — fully independent; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nahe nivārana — there is no checking Him; bhakta-icchā vinā — without the permission of devotees; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā kare gamana — does not go.

TRANSLATION

Although the Lord is completely independent and no one can check Him, He still did not go without the permission of His devotees.
