Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.41

punah mālā diyā svarūpādi nija-gana

āgu bādi' pāthāila śacīra nandana

SYNONYMS

punah — again; mālā — garlands; diyā — offering; svarūpa-ādi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and others; nija-gana — personal associates; āgu bādi' — going forward; pāthāila — sent; śacīra nandana — the son of mother Śacī.

TRANSLATION

Then, for the second time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sent garlands through Svarūpa Dāmodara and other personal associates. Thus they went forward, sent by the son of mother Śacī.
