Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.96

jagannāthe ājñā māgi' prabhāte calilā

udiyā-bhakta-gana sańge pāche cali' āilā

SYNONYMS

jagannāthe — from Lord Jagannātha; ājñā māgi' — taking permission; prabhāte — early in the morning; calilā — departed; udiyā-bhakta-gana — all the devotees of Orissa; sańge — with Him; pāche — following; cali' āilā — went.

TRANSLATION

After taking Lord Jagannātha's permission early in the morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed, and all the devotees of Orissa began following Him.
