Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.202

sei naukā cadi' prabhu āilā 'pānihāti'

nāvikere parāila nija-kripā-sātī

SYNONYMS

sei naukā cadi' — boarding the same boat; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — reached; pānihāti — the place named Pānihāti; nāvikere — the captain of the boat; parāila — He put on; nija-kripā-sātī — His own used cloth as special mercy.

TRANSLATION

The Lord finally reached Pānihāti, and as an act of mercy He gave the captain of the boat one of His personal garments.
