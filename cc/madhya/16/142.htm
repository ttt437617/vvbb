Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.142

eta bali' mahāprabhu naukāte cadilā

mūrcchita hañā pandita tathāi padilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; naukāte cadilā — got on a boat; mūrcchita hañā — fainting; pandita — Gadādhara Pandita Gosvāmī; tathāi — there; padilā — fell down.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got into a boat, and Gadādhara Pandita immediately fell down unconscious.
