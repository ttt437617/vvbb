Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.182

'hindu' haile pāitāma tomāra carana-sannidhāna

vyartha mora ei deha, yāuka parāna"

SYNONYMS

hindu haile — if I had been born in a Hindu family; pāitāma — I would have gotten; tomāra — of You; carana — of the lotus feet; sannidhāna — proximity; vyartha — useless; mora — my; ei — this; deha — body; yāuka parāna — let me die immediately.

TRANSLATION

"If I had taken birth in a Hindu family, it would have been easy for me to remain near Your lotus feet. Since my body is now useless, let me die immediately."
