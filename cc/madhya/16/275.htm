Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.275

dhik, dhik āpanāke bali' ha-ilāńa asthira

nivritta hañā punah āilāńa gańgā-tīra

SYNONYMS

dhik dhik — fie! fie!; āpanāke — on Myself; bali' — saying; ha-ilāńa — I became; asthira — agitated; nivritta hañā — stopping such an action; punah — again; āilāńa — I came back; gańgā-tīra — to the bank of the Ganges.

TRANSLATION

"I therefore said, 'Fie upon Me!' and being very much agitated, I returned to the banks of the Ganges.
