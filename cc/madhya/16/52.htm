Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.52

ghata bhari' prabhura teńho abhisheka kaila

tāńra abhisheke prabhu mahā-tripta haila

SYNONYMS

ghata bhari' — filling a waterpot; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; teńho — he; abhisheka kaila — performed the bathing; tāńra — his; abhisheke — by the act of bathing the Lord; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahā-tripta haila — became very satisfied.

TRANSLATION

It was Krishnadāsa who filled a great waterpot and poured it over the Lord while He was taking His bath. The Lord was greatly satisfied by this.
