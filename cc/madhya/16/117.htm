Madhya-līlāChapter 16: The Lord's Attempt to Go to Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 16.117

sandhyāte calibe prabhu, — nripati śunila

hastī-upara tāmbu-grihe strī-gane cadāila

SYNONYMS

sandhyāte — in the evening; calibe prabhu — the Lord will start; nripati śunila — the King heard; hastī-upara — upon the backs of elephants; tāmbu-grihe — in tents; strī-gane — all the ladies; cadāila — made get up.

TRANSLATION

When the King heard that the Lord was leaving that evening, he immediately made arrangements for some elephants with small tents on their backs to be brought there. Then all the ladies of the palace got on the elephants.
