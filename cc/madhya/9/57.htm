Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.57

hāhākāra kari' kānde saba śishya-gana

sabe āsi' prabhu-pade la-ila śarana

SYNONYMS

hāhā-kāra — a roaring sound; kari' — making; kānde — cry; saba — all; śishya-gana — disciples; sabe — all of them; āsi' — coming; prabhu-pade — to the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; la-ila — took; śarana — shelter.

TRANSLATION

When the teacher fell unconscious, his Buddhist disciples cried aloud and ran to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for shelter.
