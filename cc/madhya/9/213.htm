Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.213

patra pāñā viprera haila ānandita mana

prabhura carane dhari' karaye krandana

SYNONYMS

patra pāñā — getting the leaves; viprera — of the brāhmana; haila — there was; ānandita — pleased; mana — mind; prabhura carane — the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhari' — taking; karaye — does; krandana — crying.

TRANSLATION

Rāmadāsa Vipra was very pleased to receive the original leaf manuscript of the Kūrma Purāna, and he immediately fell down before the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and began to cry.
