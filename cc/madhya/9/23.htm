Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.23

bhikshā kari' mahāprabhu tāńre praśna kaila

''kaha vipra, ei tomāra kon daśā haila

SYNONYMS

bhikshā kari' — after accepting lunch; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; praśna kaila — asked a question; kaha vipra — My dear brāhmana friend, please say; ei — this; tomāra — your; kon — what; daśā — situation; haila — became.

TRANSLATION

After finishing His lunch there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the brāhmana, "My dear friend, kindly tell Me what your position is now.
