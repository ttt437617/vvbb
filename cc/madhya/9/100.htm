Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.100

arjunere kahitechena hita-upadeśa

tāńre dekhi' haya mora ānanda-āveśa

SYNONYMS

arjunere — unto Arjuna; kahitechena — He is speaking; hita-upadeśa — good instruction; tāńre — Him; dekhi' — seeing; haya — there is; mora — my; ānanda — transcendental happiness; āveśa — ecstasy.

TRANSLATION

"While seeing Lord Krishna sitting in a chariot and instructing Arjuna, I am filled with ecstatic happiness.
