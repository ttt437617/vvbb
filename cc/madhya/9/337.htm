Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.337

yāhāń yāya, loka uthe hari-dhvani kari'

dekhi' ānandita-mana hailā gaurahari

SYNONYMS

yāhāń yāya — wherever He goes; loka uthe — people stand up; hari-dhvani kari' — vibrating the holy name of the Hare Krishna mantra; dekhi' — by seeing; ānandita — happy; mana — in mind; hailā — became; gaurahari — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Wherever Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went, the holy name of Śrī Hari was vibrated. Seeing this, the Lord became very happy.
