Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.217

eta bali' sei vipra sukhe pāka kaila

uttama prakāre prabhuke bhikshā karāila

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; sei vipra — that brāhmana; sukhe — in great happiness; pāka kaila — cooked; uttama prakāre — very nicely; prabhuke — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā — lunch; karāila — gave.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, the brāhmana very happily cooked food, and a first-class dinner was offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
