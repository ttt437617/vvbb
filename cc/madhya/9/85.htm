Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.85

cāturmāsye kripā kari' raha mora ghare

krishna-kathā kahi' kripāya uddhāra' āmāre

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsye — during this period of Cāturmāsya; kripā kari' — being merciful; raha — please stay; mora ghare — at my place; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; kahi' — speaking; kripāya — by Your mercy; uddhāra' āmāre — kindly deliver me.

TRANSLATION

Veńkata Bhatta said, "Please be merciful to me and stay at my house during Cāturmāsya. Speak about Lord Krishna's pastimes and kindly deliver me by Your mercy."
