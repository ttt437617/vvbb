Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.107

sei vipra mahāprabhura bada bhakta haila

cāri māsa prabhu-sańga kabhu nā chādila

SYNONYMS

sei vipra — that brāhmana; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bada — big; bhakta — devotee; haila — became; cāri māsa — for four months; prabhu-sańga — association of the Lord; kabhu — at any time; nā — did not; chādila — give up.

TRANSLATION

That brāhmana became a great devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and for four continuous months he did not give up the Lord's company.
