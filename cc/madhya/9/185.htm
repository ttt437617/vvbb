Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.185

prabhu bhikshā kaila dinera tritīya-prahare

nirvinna sei vipra upavāsa kare

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā kaila — took His luncheon; dinera — of the day; tritīya-prahare — at about three o'clock; nirvinna — sorrowful; sei — that; vipra — brāhmana; upavāsa kare — fasted.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His lunch at about three o'clock, but the brāhmana, being very sorrowful, fasted.
