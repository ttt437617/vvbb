Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.207

śuniñā prabhura ānandita haila mana

rāmadāsa-viprera kathā ha-ila smarana

SYNONYMS

śuniñā — hearing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita — very pleased; haila — became; mana — the mind; rāmadāsa-viprera — of the brāhmana known as Rāmadāsa; kathā — words; ha-ila smarana — He remembered.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this story, He was very pleased, and He remembered the words of Rāmadāsa Vipra.
