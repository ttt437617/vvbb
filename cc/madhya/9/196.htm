Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.196

prabhura vacane viprera ha-ila viśvāsa

bhojana karila, haila jīvanera āśa

SYNONYMS

prabhura vacane — in the words of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; viprera — of the brāhmana; ha-ila — was; viśvāsa — faith; bhojana karila — he took his lunch; haila — there was; jīvanera — for living; āśa — hope.

TRANSLATION

Although the brāhmana was fasting, he had faith in the words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and accepted food. In this way his life was saved.
