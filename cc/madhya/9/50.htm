Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.50

bauddhācārya 'nava praśna' saba uthāila

dridha yukti-tarke prabhu khanda khanda kaila

SYNONYMS

bauddha-ācārya — the teacher of the Buddhist cult; nava praśna — nine different types of questions; saba — all; uthāila — raised; dridha — strong; yukti — argument; tarke — with logic; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; khanda khanda kaila — broke into pieces.

TRANSLATION

The teacher of the Buddhist cult set forth the nine principles, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu broke them to pieces with His strong logic.
