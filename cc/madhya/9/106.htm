Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.106

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre karāila śikshana

ei bāt kāhāń nā kariha prakāśana

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto the brāhmana; karāila — made; śikshana — instruction; ei bāt — this version; kāhāń — anywhere; nā — do not; kariha — do; prakāśana — revelation.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then taught the brāhmana very thoroughly and requested him not to disclose the fact that He was Lord Krishna Himself.
