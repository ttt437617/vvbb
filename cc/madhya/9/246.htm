Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.246

nartaka gopāla dekhe parama-mohane

madhvācārye svapna diyā āilā tāńra sthāne

SYNONYMS

nartaka gopāla — dancing Gopāla; dekhe — saw; parama-mohane — most beautiful; madhva-ācārye — unto Madhvācārya; svapna diyā — appearing in a dream; āilā — came; tāńra — his; sthāne — to the place.

TRANSLATION

While at the Udupī monastery, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw "dancing Gopāla," a most beautiful Deity. This Deity appeared to Madhvācārya in a dream.
