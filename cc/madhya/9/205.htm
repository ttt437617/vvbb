Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.205

raghunātha āsi' yabe rāvane mārila

agni-parīkshā dite yabe sītāre ānila

SYNONYMS

raghunātha — Lord Rāmacandra; āsi' — coming; yabe — when; rāvane — Rāvana; mārila — killed; agni-parīkshā — test by fire; dite — to give; yabe — when; sītāre — Sītā; ānila — brought.

TRANSLATION

After Rāvana was killed by Lord Rāmacandra, Sītādevī was brought before the fire and tested.
