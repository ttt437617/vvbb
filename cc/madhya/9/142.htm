Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.142

krishnera vilāsa-mūrti — śrī-nārāyana

ataeva lakshmī-ādyera hare teńha mana

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; vilāsa-mūrti — form for enjoyment; śrī-nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; ataeva — therefore; lakshmī-ādyera — of the goddess of fortune and her followers; hare — attracts; teńha — He (Lord Nārāyana); mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Nārāyana, the opulent form of Krishna, attracts the minds of the goddess of fortune and her followers.
