Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.328

rātri-kāle rāya punah kaila āgamana

dui jane krishna-kathāya kaila jāgarana

SYNONYMS

rātri-kāle — at night; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; punah — again; kaila — did; āgamana — coming; dui jane — the two of them; krishna-kathāya — in discourses on topics of Krishna; kaila — did; jāgarana — keeping awake through the night.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya returned at night, and he and the Lord discussed topics concerning Krishna. Thus they passed the night.
