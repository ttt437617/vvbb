Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.81

premāveśe kaila bahuta gāna nartana

dekhi' camatkāra haila saba lokera mana

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love; kaila — did; bahuta — various; gāna — songs; nartana — dancing; dekhi' — seeing which; camatkāra — astonished; haila — were; saba — all; lokera — of persons; mana — minds.

TRANSLATION

In the temple of Rańganātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted and danced in ecstatic love of Godhead. Seeing His performance, everyone was struck with wonder.
