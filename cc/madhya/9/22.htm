Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.22

punah siddhavata āilā sei vipra-ghare

sei vipra krishna-nāma laya nirantare

SYNONYMS

punah — again; siddha-vata — to the place known as Siddhavata; āilā — returned; sei — that; vipra-ghare — in the house of the brāhmana; sei vipra — that brāhmana; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; laya — chants; nirantare — constantly.

TRANSLATION

After visiting the temple of Trivikrama, the Lord returned to Siddhavata, where He again visited the house of the brāhmana, who was now constantly chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
