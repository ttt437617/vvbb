Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.139

tāńhāra bhajana sarvopari-kakshā haya

śrī-vaishnave'ra bhajana ei sarvopari haya

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra bhajana — worship of Nārāyana; sarva-upari — topmost; kakshā — department; haya — is; śrī-vaishnavera — of the followers of Rāmānujācārya; bhajana — worship; ei — this; sarva-upari haya — is the topmost.

TRANSLATION

Thinking in this way, Veńkata Bhatta believed that worship of Nārāyana was the supreme form of worship, superior to all other processes of devotional service, for it was followed by the Śrī Vaishnava disciples of Rāmānujācārya.
