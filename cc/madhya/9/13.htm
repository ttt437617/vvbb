Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.13

rāma! rāghava! rāma! rāghava! rāma! rāghava! pāhi mām

krishna! keśava! krishna! keśava! krishna! keśava! raksha mām

SYNONYMS

rāma — O Rāma; rāghava — O descendant of Raghu; pāhi — please protect; mām — me; krishna — O Krishna; keśava — O killer of Keśī; raksha — protect; mām — me.

TRANSLATION

"'O Lord Rāmacandra, descendant of Mahārāja Raghu, kindly protect me! O Lord Krishna, killer of the Keśī demon, kindly protect me!'"
