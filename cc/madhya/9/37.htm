Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.37

sei krishna tumi sākshāt — ihā nirdhārila

eta kahi' vipra prabhura carane padila

SYNONYMS

sei — that; krishna — the Personality of Godhead, Krishna; tumi — You; sākshāt — directly; ihā — this; nirdhārila — concluded; eta kahi' — saying this; vipra — the brāhmana; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the lotus feet; padila — fell down.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, You are that Lord Krishna Himself. This is my conclusion." Saying this, the brāhmana fell down at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
