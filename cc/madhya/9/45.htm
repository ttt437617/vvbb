Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.45

hāri' hāri' prabhu-mate karena praveśa

ei-mate 'vaishnava' prabhu kaila dakshina deśa

SYNONYMS

hāri' hāri' — being defeated; prabhu-mate — into the cult of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena praveśa — enter; ei-mate — in this way; vaishnava — Vaishnava devotees; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — made; dakshina — South India; deśa — country.

TRANSLATION

Being thus defeated by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all these philosophers and their followers entered into His cult. In this way Lord Caitanya made South India into a country of Vaishnavas.
