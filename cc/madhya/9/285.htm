Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.285

mādhava-purīra śishya 'śrī-rańga-purī' nāma

sei grāme vipra-grihe karena viśrāma

SYNONYMS

mādhava-purīra śishya — a disciple of Mādhavendra Purī; śrī-rańga-purī — Śrī Rańga Purī; nāma — named; sei grāme — in that village; vipra-grihe — in the house of a brāhmana; karena viśrāma — rests.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu received word that Śrī Rańga Purī, one of the disciples of Śrī Mādhavendra Purī, was present in that village at the home of a brāhmana.
