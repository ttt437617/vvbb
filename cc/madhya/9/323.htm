Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.323

tīrtha-yātrā-kathā prabhu sakala kahilā

karnāmrita, brahma-samhitā, — dui puńthi dilā

SYNONYMS

tīrtha-yātrā-kathā — topics of His pilgrimage; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sakala kahilā — described everything; karnāmrita — the book named Krishna-karnāmrita; brahma-samhitā — the book named Brahma-samhitā; dui — two; puńthi — scriptures; dilā — delivered.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave Rāmānanda Rāya a vivid description of His travels to the holy places and told him how He had acquired the two books named Krishna-karnāmrita and Brahma-samhitā. The Lord delivered the books to Rāmānanda Rāya.
