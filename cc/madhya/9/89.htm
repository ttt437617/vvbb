Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.89

laksha laksha loka āila nānā-deśa haite

sabe krishna-nāma kahe prabhuke dekhite

SYNONYMS

laksha laksha — many hundreds of thousands; loka — of people; āila — came there; nānā-deśa — different countries; haite — from; sabe — all of them; krishna-nāma kahe — chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; prabhuke — the Lord; dekhite — seeing.

TRANSLATION

Many hundreds of thousands of people from various countries came to see the Lord, and after seeing Him they all chanted the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
