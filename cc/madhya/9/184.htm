Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.184

tāńra upāsanā śuni' prabhu tushta hailā

āste-vyaste sei vipra randhana karilā

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; upāsanā — method of worship; śuni' — hearing; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tushta hailā — was very pleased; āste-vyaste — with great haste; sei — that; vipra — brāhmana; randhana karilā — began to cook.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very satisfied to hear about the brāhmana's method of worship. Finally the brāhmana hastily made arrangements for cooking.
