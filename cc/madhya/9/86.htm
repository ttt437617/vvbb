Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.86

tāńra ghare rahilā prabhu krishna-kathā-rase

bhatta-sańge gońāila sukhe cāri māse

SYNONYMS

tāńra ghare — in his home; rahilā — stayed; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-kathā-rase — enjoying the transcendental mellow of discussing Lord Krishna's pastimes; bhatta-sańge — with Veńkata Bhatta; gońāila — passed; sukhe — in happiness; cāri māse — four months.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the house of Veńkata Bhatta for four continuous months. The Lord passed His days in great happiness, enjoying the transcendental mellow of discussing Lord Krishna's pastimes.
