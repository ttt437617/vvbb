Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.344

sārvabhauma mahāprabhura padilā carane

prabhu tāńre uthāñā kaila ālińgane

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; padilā — fell down; carane — at the feet; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; uthāñā — getting up; kaila ālińgane — embraced.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya fell down at the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord pulled him up and embraced him.
