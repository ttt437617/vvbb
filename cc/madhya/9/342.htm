Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.342

prabhu premāveśe sabāya kaila ālińgana

premāveśe sabe kare ānanda-krandana

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; sabāya — all of them; kaila ālińgana — embraced; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; sabe kare — all of them did; ānanda-krandana — crying in pleasure.

TRANSLATION

The Lord was also filled with ecstatic love, and He embraced them all. Out of their love, they began to cry with pleasure.
