Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.273

śuni' tattvācārya hailā antare lajjita

prabhura vaishnavatā dekhi, ha-ilā vismita

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; tattva-ācārya — the ācārya of the Tattvavāda sampradāya; hailā — became; antare — within the mind; lajjita — ashamed; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaishnavatā — devotion in Vaishnavism; dekhi — seeing; ha-ilā — became; vismita — struck with wonder.

TRANSLATION

After hearing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the ācārya of the Tattvavāda sampradāya became very much ashamed. Upon observing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's rigid faith in Vaishnavism, he was struck with wonder.
