Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.62

krishna bali' ācārya prabhure karena vinaya

dekhiyā sakala loka ha-ila vismaya

SYNONYMS

krishna bali' — chanting the holy name of Krishna; ācārya — the so-called spiritual master of the Buddhists; prabhure — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena — does; vinaya — submission; dekhiyā — seeing this; sakala loka — all the people; ha-ila — became; vismaya — astonished.

TRANSLATION

When the spiritual master of the Buddhists began to chant the holy name of Krishna and submitted to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the people who were gathered there were astonished.
