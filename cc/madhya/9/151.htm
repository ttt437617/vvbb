Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.151

eta kahi' prabhu tāńra garva cūrna kariyā

tāńre sukha dite kahe siddhānta phirāiyā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra — his (of Veńkata Bhatta); garva — pride; cūrna kariyā — smashing into pieces; tāńre — unto him; sukha dite — to give happiness; kahe — says; siddhānta phirāiyā — turning the whole conversation.

TRANSLATION

In this way Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu deflated the pride of Veńkata Bhatta, but just to make him happy again, He spoke as follows.
