Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.284

bahuta ādare prabhuke bhikshā karāila

bhikshā kari' tathā eka śubha-vārtā pāila

SYNONYMS

bahuta ādare — with great love; prabhuke — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā karāila — offered lunch; bhikshā kari' — after finishing His lunch; tathā — there; eka — one; śubha-vārtā — auspicious news; pāila — got.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu food with great respect and love. After finishing His lunch, the Lord received auspicious news.
