Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.105

krishna-sphūrtye tāńra mana hañāche nirmala

ataeva prabhura tattva jānila sakala

SYNONYMS

krishna-sphūrtye — by revelation of Lord Krishna; tāńra — his; mana — mind; hañāche — did become; nirmala — purified; ataeva — therefore; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tattva — truth; jānila — could understand; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

The mind of the brāhmana was purified by the revelation of Lord Krishna, and therefore he could understand the truth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in all details.
