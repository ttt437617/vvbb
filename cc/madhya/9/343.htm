Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.343

sārvabhauma bhattācārya ānande calilā

samudrera tīre āsi' prabhure mililā

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; ānande — in pleasure; calilā — went; samudrera tīre — on the beach by the ocean; āsi' — coming; prabhure mililā — met the Lord.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya also went to see the Lord with great pleasure, and he met Him on the beach by the sea.
