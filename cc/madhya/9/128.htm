Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.128

vraja-lokera bhāve pāiye tāńhāra carana

tāńre īśvara kari' nāhi jāne vraja-jana

SYNONYMS

vraja-lokera — of the inhabitants of Goloka Vrindāvana; bhāve — in the ecstasy; pāiye — one gets; tāńhāra — Lord Krishna's; carana — lotus feet; tāńre — unto Him; īśvara — the Supreme Person; kari' — accepting; nāhi — do not; jāne — know; vraja-jana — the inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi.

TRANSLATION

"By following in the footsteps of the inhabitants of the planet known as Vrajaloka or Goloka Vrindāvana, one can attain the shelter of the lotus feet of Śrī Krishna. However, in that planet the inhabitants do not know that Lord Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
