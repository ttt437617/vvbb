Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.192

sparśibāra kārya āchuka, nā pāya darśana

sītāra ākriti-māyā harila rāvana

SYNONYMS

sparśibāra — to touch; kārya — business; āchuka — let it be; nā — does not; pāya — get; darśana — sight; sītāra — of mother Sītā; ākriti-māyā — the form made of māyā; harila — took away; rāvana — the demon Rāvana.

TRANSLATION

"To say nothing of touching mother Sītā, a person with material senses cannot even see her. When Rāvana kidnapped her, he kidnapped only her material, illusory form.
