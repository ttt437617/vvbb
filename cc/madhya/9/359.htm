Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.359

ananta caitanya-līlā kahite nā jāni

lobhe lajjā khāñā tāra kari tānātāni

SYNONYMS

ananta — unlimited; caitanya-līlā — pastimes of Lord Caitanya; kahite — to speak; nā jāni — I do not know; lobhe — out of greed; lajjā khāñā — becoming shameless; tāra — of them; kari — I do; tānātāni — some attempt only.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Lord Caitanya are unlimited. No one can properly describe His activities, yet I make the attempt out of greed. This but reveals my shamelessness.
