Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.253

tāń-sabāra antare garva jāni gauracandra

tāń-sabā-sańge goshthī karilā ārambha

SYNONYMS

tāń-sabāra — of all of them; antare — within the mind; garva — pride; jāni — knowing; gaura-candra — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāń-sabā-sańge — with them; goshthī — discussion; karilā — made; ārambha — beginning.

TRANSLATION

Considering them very proud, Caitanya Mahāprabhu began His discussion.
