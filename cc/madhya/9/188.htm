Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.188

jagan-mātā mahā-lakshmī sītā-thākurānī

rākshase sparśila tāńre, — ihā kāne śuni

SYNONYMS

jagat-mātā — the mother of the universe; mahā-lakshmī — the supreme goddess of fortune; sītā-thākurānī — mother Sītā; rākshase — the demon Rāvana; sparśila — touched; tāńre — her; ihā — this; kāne śuni — I have heard.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sir, mother Sītā is the mother of the universe and the supreme goddess of fortune. She has been touched by the demon Rāvana, and I am troubled upon hearing this news.
