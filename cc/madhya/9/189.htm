Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.189

e śarīra dharibāre kabhu nā yuyāya

ei duhkhe jvale deha, prāna nāhi yāya

SYNONYMS

e śarīra — this body; dharibāre — to keep; kabhu — ever; nā — not; yuyāya — deserve; ei duhkhe — in this unhappiness; jvale deha — my body is burning; prāna — my life; nāhi yāya — does not go away.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, due to my unhappiness I cannot continue living. Although my body is burning, my life is not leaving."
