Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.70

premāveśe nritya-gīta bahuta karila

dina-dui rahi' loke 'krishna-bhakta' kaila

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; bahuta — much; karila — performed; dina-dui — for two days; rahi' — staying; loke — the people in general; krishna-bhakta — devotees of Lord Krishna; kaila — made.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed at Vishnu-kāñcī for two days, He danced and performed kīrtana in ecstasy. When all the people saw Him, they were converted into devotees of Lord Krishna.
