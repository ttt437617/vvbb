Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.24

pūrve tumi nirantara laite rāma-nāma

ebe kene nirantara lao krishna-nāma''

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; tumi — you; nirantara — constantly; laite — used to chant; rāma-nāma — the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; ebe — now; kene — why; nirantara — constantly; lao — you chant; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Formerly you were constantly chanting the holy name of Lord Rāma. Why are you now constantly chanting the holy name of Krishna?"
