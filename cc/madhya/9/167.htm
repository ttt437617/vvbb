Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.167

paramānanda-purī tāhāń rahe catur-māsa

śuni' mahāprabhu gelā purī-gosāñira pāśa

SYNONYMS

paramānanda-purī — Paramānanda Purī; tāhāń — there; rahe — remained; catur-māsa — four months; śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gelā — went; purī — Paramānanda Purī; gosāñira — the spiritual master; pāśa — near.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī had stayed at Rishabha Hill during the four months of the rainy season, and when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this, He immediately went to see him.
