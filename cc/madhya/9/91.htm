Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.91

śrī-rańga-kshetre vaise yata vaishnava-brāhmana

eka eka dina sabe kaila nimantrana

SYNONYMS

śrī-rańga-kshetre — in Śrī Rańga-kshetra; vaise — residing; yata — all; vaishnava-brāhmana — Vaishnava brāhmanas; eka eka dina — every day; sabe — all of them; kaila nimantrana — invited the Lord.

TRANSLATION

All the Vaishnava brāhmanas residing in Śrī Rańga-kshetra invited the Lord to their homes. Indeed, He had an invitation every day.
