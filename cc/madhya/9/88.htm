Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.88

saundaryādi premāveśa dekhi, sarva-loka

dekhibāre āise, dekhe, khande duhkha-śoka

SYNONYMS

saundarya-ādi — the beauty of the body, etc.; prema-āveśa — His ecstatic love; dekhi — seeing; sarva-loka — all men; dekhibāre — to see; āise — come there; dekhe — and see; khande duhkha-śoka — are relieved from all unhappiness and distress.

TRANSLATION

The beauty of Lord Caitanya's body and His ecstatic love of God were witnessed by everyone. Many people used to come see Him, and as soon as they saw Him, all their unhappiness and distress vanished.
