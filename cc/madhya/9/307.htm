Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.307

'karnāmrita'-sama vastu nāhi tribhuvane

yāhā haite haya krishne śuddha-prema-jñāne

SYNONYMS

karnāmrita — the Krishna-karnāmrita; sama — like; vastu nāhi — there is nothing; tri-bhuvane — in the three worlds; yāhā haite — from which; haya — there is; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; śuddha-prema-jñāne — knowledge of pure devotional service.

TRANSLATION

There is no comparison to the Krishna-karnāmrita within the three worlds. By studying this book, one is elevated to the knowledge of pure devotional service to Krishna.
