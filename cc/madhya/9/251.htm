Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.251

pāche premāveśa dekhi' haila camatkāra

vaishnava-jñāne bahuta karila satkāra

SYNONYMS

pāche — later; prema-āveśa — ecstatic love; dekhi' — seeing; haila camatkāra — became struck with wonder; vaishnava-jñāne — understanding as a Vaishnava; bahuta — much; karila — did; satkāra — reception.

TRANSLATION

Later, after seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in ecstatic love, they were struck with wonder. Then, considering Him a Vaishnava, they gave Him a nice reception.
