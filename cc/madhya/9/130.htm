Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.130

'vrajendra-nandana' bali' tāńre jāne vraja-jana

aiśvarya-jñāne nāhi kona sambandha-mānana

SYNONYMS

vrajendra-nandana — the son of Nanda Mahārāja, the King of Vrajabhūmi; bali' — as; tāńre — Him; jāne — know; vraja-jana — the inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi; aiśvarya-jñāne — in opulence; nāhi — there is not; kona — any; sambandha — relationship; mānana — regarding.

TRANSLATION

"The inhabitants of Vrajabhūmi know Krishna as the son of Mahārāja Nanda, the King of Vrajabhūmi, and they consider that they can have no relationship with the Lord in the rasa of opulence.
