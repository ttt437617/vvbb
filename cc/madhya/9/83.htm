Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.83

nija-ghare lañā kaila pāda-prakshālana

sei jala lañā kaila sa-vamśe bhakshana

SYNONYMS

nija-ghare — to his own home; lañā — bringing; kaila — did; pāda-prakshālana — washing of the feet; sei jala — that water; lañā — taking; kaila — did; sa-vamśe — with all the family members; bhakshana — drinking.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Veńkata Bhatta took Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his home. After he washed the Lord's feet, all the members of his family drank the water.
