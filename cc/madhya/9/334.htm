Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.334

dina-daśe ihā-sabāra kari' samādhāna

tomāra pāche pāche āmi kariba prayāna

SYNONYMS

dina-daśe — within ten days; ihā-sabāra — of all of this; kari' samādhāna — making adjustment; tomāra — You; pāche pāche — following; āmi — I; kariba — shall do; prayāna — going.

TRANSLATION

"I shall make arrangements within ten days. Following You, I shall go to Nīlācala without delay."
