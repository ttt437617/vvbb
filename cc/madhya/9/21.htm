Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.21

skanda-kshetra-tīrthe kaila skanda daraśana

trimatha āilā, tāhāń dekhi' trivikrama

SYNONYMS

skanda-kshetra-tīrthe — in the holy place known as Skanda-kshetra; kaila — did; skanda daraśana — visiting Lord Skanda (Kārttikeya, son of Lord Śiva); trimatha — at Trimatha; āilā — arrived; tāhāń — there; dekhi' — seeing; trivikrama — a form of Lord Vishnu, Trivikrama.

TRANSLATION

At the holy place known as Skanda-kshetra, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Skanda. From there He went to Trimatha, where He saw the Vishnu Deity Trivikrama.
