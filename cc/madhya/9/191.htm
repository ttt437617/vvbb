Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.191

īśvara-preyasī sītā — cid-ānanda-mūrti

prākrita-indriyera tāńre dekhite nāhi śakti

SYNONYMS

īśvara-preyasī — the dearmost wife of the Lord; sītā — mother Sītā; cit-ānanda-mūrti — spiritual blissful form; prākrita — material; indriyera — of the senses; tāńre — her; dekhite — to see; nāhi — there is not; śakti — power.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "Sītādevī, the dearmost wife of the Supreme Lord Rāmacandra, certainly has a spiritual form full of bliss. No one can see her with material eyes, for no materialist has such power.
