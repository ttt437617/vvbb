Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.44

sarvatra sthāpaya prabhu vaishnava-siddhānte

prabhura siddhānta keha nā pāre khandite

SYNONYMS

sarvatra — everywhere; sthāpaya — establishes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaishnava-siddhānte — the conclusion of the Vaishnavas; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; siddhānta — conclusion; keha — anyone; nā pāre — is not able; khandite — to defy.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu established the devotional cult everywhere. No one could defeat Him.
