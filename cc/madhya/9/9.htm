Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.9

dakshina deśera loka aneka prakāra

keha jñānī, keha karmī, pāshandī apāra

SYNONYMS

dakshina deśera — of South India; loka — people; aneka — many; prakāra — varieties; keha — someone; jñānī — philosophical speculator; keha — someone; karmī — fruitive worker; pāshandī — nondevotees; apāra — innumerable.

TRANSLATION

In South India there were many types of people. Some were philosophical speculators, and some were fruitive workers, but in any case there were innumerable nondevotees.
