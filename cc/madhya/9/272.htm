Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.272

sannyāsī dekhiyā more karaha vañcana

nā kahilā teñi sādhya-sādhana-lakshana

SYNONYMS

sannyāsī — a person in the renounced order of life; dekhiyā — seeing; more — unto Me; karaha — you do; vañcana — duplicity; nā kahilā — did not describe; teñi — therefore; sādhya — objective; sādhana — process of achievement; lakshana — symptoms.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued speaking to the Tattvavādī ācārya: "Seeing that I am a mendicant in the renounced order of life, you have been playing with Me in a duplicitous way. You have not actually described the process and ultimate objective."
