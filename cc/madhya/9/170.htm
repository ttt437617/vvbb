Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.170

purī-gosāñi bale, — āmi yāba purushottame

purushottama dekhi' gaude yāba gańgā-snāne

SYNONYMS

purī-gosāñi — Paramānanda Purī; bale — said; āmi — I; yāba — shall go; purushottame — to Jagannātha Purī; purushottama dekhi' — after visiting Jagannātha Purī; gaude yāba — I shall go to Bengal; gańgā-snāne — for bathing in the Ganges.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that he was going to see Purushottama at Jagannātha Purī. After seeing Lord Jagannātha there, he would go to Bengal to bathe in the Ganges.
