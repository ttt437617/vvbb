Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.183

vanya śāka-phala-mūla ānibe lakshmana

tabe sītā karibena pāka-prayojana

SYNONYMS

vanya — of the forest; śāka — vegetables; phala-mūla — fruits and roots; ānibe — will bring; lakshmana — Lakshmana; tabe — that time; sītā — mother Sītā; karibena — will do; pāka-prayojana — the necessary cooking.

TRANSLATION

"When Lakshmana brings all the vegetables, fruits and roots from the forest, Sītā will do the necessary cooking."
