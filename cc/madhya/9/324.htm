Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.324

prabhu kahe, — tumi yei siddhānta kahile

ei dui puńthi sei saba sākshī dile

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; tumi — you; yei — whatever; siddhānta — conclusion; kahile — informed; ei dui — these two; puńthi — books; sei — that; saba — everything; sākshī — evidence; dile — gave.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "Whatever you have told Me about devotional service is all supported by these two books."
