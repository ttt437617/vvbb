Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.288

dekhiyā vismita haila śrī-rańga-purīra mana

'uthaha śrīpāda' bali' balilā vacana

SYNONYMS

dekhiyā — seeing; vismita — astonished; haila — became; śrī-rańga-purīra — of Śrī Rańga Purī; mana — the mind; uthaha — get up; śrī-pāda — Your Holiness; bali' — saying; balilā vacana — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in such an ecstatic mood, Śrī Rańga Purī said, "Your Holiness, please get up.
