Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.315

saśarīre tāla gela śrī-vaikuntha-dhāma

aiche śakti kāra haya, vinā eka rāma

SYNONYMS

sa-śarīre — with the material body; tāla — the palm trees; gela — went; śrī-vaikuntha-dhāma — to the spiritual kingdom, known as Vaikuntha; aiche — such; śakti — power; kāra — whose; haya — is; vinā — without; eka — one; rāma — Lord Rāmacandra.

TRANSLATION

"Only Lord Rāmacandra has the power to send seven palm trees to the spiritual Vaikuntha planets."
