Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.322

kata-kshane dui janā susthira hañā

nānā ishta-goshthī kare ekatra vasiyā

SYNONYMS

kata-kshane — after some time; dui — two; janā — people; su-sthira hañā — coming to their senses; nānā — various; ishta-goshthī — discussions; kare — do; ekatra — together; vasiyā — sitting.

TRANSLATION

After some time they regained their senses and sat together to discuss various subjects.
