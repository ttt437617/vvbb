Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.127

prabhu kahe, — krishnera eka svabhāva vilakshana

sva-mādhurye sarva citta kare ākarshana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; eka — one; svabhāva — characteristic; vilakshana — special; sva-mādhurye — His conjugal love; sarva — all; citta — hearts; kare — does; ākarshana — attraction.

TRANSLATION

The Lord replied, "Lord Krishna has a special characteristic: He attracts everyone's heart by the mellow of His personal conjugal love.
