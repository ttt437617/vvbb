Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.265

sarva-dharmān parityajya

mām ekam śaranam vraja

aham tvām sarva-pāpebhyo

mokshayishyāmi mā śucah

SYNONYMS

sarva-dharmān — all kinds of occupational duties; parityajya — giving up; mām ekam — unto Me only; śaranam — as shelter; vraja — go; aham — I; tvām — unto you; sarva-pāpebhyah — from all the reactions of sinful life; mokshayishyāmi — will give liberation; mā — do not; śucah — worry.

TRANSLATION

"'Abandon all varieties of religion and just surrender unto Me. I shall deliver you from all sinful reactions. Do not fear. [Bg. 18.66]'
