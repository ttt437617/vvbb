Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.163

cāturmāsya pūrna haila, bhatta-ājñā lañā

dakshina calilā prabhu śrī-rańga dekhiyā

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsya — the period of Cāturmāsya; pūrna haila — became completed; bhatta-ājñā lañā — taking permission from Veńkata Bhatta; dakshina — south; calilā — proceeded; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrī-rańga dekhiyā — visiting Śrī Rańga.

TRANSLATION

When the period of Cāturmāsya was completed, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took permission to leave from Veńkata Bhatta, and after visiting Śrī Rańga, He proceeded further toward southern India.
