Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.99

arjunera rathe krishna haya rajju-dhara

vasiyāche hāte totra śyāmala sundara

SYNONYMS

arjunera — of Arjuna; rathe — in the chariot; krishna — Lord Krishna; haya — is; rajju-dhara — holding the reins; vasiyāche — He was sitting there; hāte — in the hand; totra — a bridle; śyāmala — blackish; sundara — very beautiful.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana continued, "Actually I only see Lord Krishna sitting on a chariot as Arjuna's charioteer. Taking the reins in His hands, He appears very beautiful and blackish.
