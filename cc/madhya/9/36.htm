Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.36

tomāra darśane yabe krishna-nāma āila

tāhāra mahimā tabe hridaye lāgila

SYNONYMS

tomāra darśane — by meeting You; yabe — when; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; āila — appeared; tāhāra — His; mahimā — glories; tabe — at that time; hridaye — in the heart; lāgila — became fixed.

TRANSLATION

"By Your appearance, Lord Krishna's holy name also appeared, and at that time the glories of Krishna's name awoke in my heart.
