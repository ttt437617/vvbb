Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.216

mano-duhkhe bhāla bhikshā nā dila sei dine

mora bhāgye punarapi pāiluń daraśane

SYNONYMS

mano-duhkhe — out of great mental distress; bhāla bhikshā — good lunch; nā dila — could not give You; sei dine — that day; mora bhāgye — because of my good fortune; punarapi — again; pāiluń — I have gotten; daraśane — visit.

TRANSLATION

"Due to my mental distress I could not give You a very nice lunch the other day. Now, by good fortune, You have come again to my home."
