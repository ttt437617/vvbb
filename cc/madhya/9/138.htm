Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.138

pūrve bhattera mane eka chila abhimāna

'śrī-nārāyana' hayena svayam-bhagavān

SYNONYMS

pūrve — before this; bhattera — of Veńkata Bhatta; mane — in the mind; eka — one; chila — there was; abhimāna — an impression; śrī-nārāyana — the form of the Lord as Nārāyana; hayena — is; svayam — personally; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Before this explanation was given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Veńkata Bhatta thought that Śrī Nārāyana was the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
