Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.330

rāmānanda kahe, — prabhu, tomāra ājñā pāñā

rājāke likhiluń āmi vinaya kariyā

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya said; prabhu — my dear Lord; tomāra ājñā — Your permission; pāñā — getting; rājāke likhiluń — have written a letter to the King; āmi — I; vinaya kariyā — with great humility.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya said, "My dear Lord, with Your permission I have already written a letter to the King with great humility.
