Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.172

tomāra nikate rahi, — hena vāñchā haya

nīlācale āsibe more hañā sadaya

SYNONYMS

tomāra nikate — with you; rahi — I may stay; hena — such; vāñchā haya — is My desire; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āsibe — please come; more — unto Me; hañā — being; sa-daya — merciful.

TRANSLATION

"It is My desire to stay with you, and therefore if you would return to Jagannātha Purī, you would show great mercy to Me."
