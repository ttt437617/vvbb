Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.35

ishta-deva rāma, tāńra nāme sukha pāi

sukha pāñā rāma-nāma rātri-dina gāi

SYNONYMS

ishta-deva — my worshipable Lord; rāma — Lord Śrī Rāmacandra; tāńra nāme — in His holy name; sukha pāi — I get happiness; sukha pāñā — getting such transcendental happiness; rāma-nāma — the holy name of Lord Rāma; rātri-dina — day and night; gāi — I chant.

TRANSLATION

"My worshipable Lord has been Lord Rāmacandra, and by chanting His holy name I received happiness. Because I received such happiness, I chanted the holy name of Lord Rāma day and night.
