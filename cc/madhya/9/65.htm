Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.65

tripati āsiyā kaila śrī-rāma daraśana

raghunātha-āge kaila pranāma stavana

SYNONYMS

tripati āsiyā — coming to Tirupati; kaila śrī-rāma daraśana — visited the temple of Rāmacandra; raghunātha-āge — before Lord Rāmacandra; kaila — did; pranāma — obeisances; stavana — offering prayers.

TRANSLATION

After arriving at Tirupati, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Lord Rāmacandra. He offered His prayers and obeisances before Rāmacandra, the descendant of King Raghu.
