Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.278

ei-mata tāńra ghare garva cūrna kari'

phalgu-tīrthe tabe cali āilā gaurahari

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; tāńra ghare — at his place; garva — pride; cūrna — broken; kari' — making; phalgu-tīrthe — to the holy place named Phalgu-tīrtha; tabe — then; cali — walking; āilā — came; gaurahari — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu broke the pride of the Tattvavādīs to pieces. He then went to the holy place known as Phalgu-tīrtha.
