Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.59

prabhu kahe, — sabe kaha 'krishna' 'krishna' 'hari'

guru-karne kaha krishna-nāma ucca kari'

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; sabe — all of you; kaha — chant; krishna krishna hari — the holy names of Lord Krishna and Hari; guru-karne — near the ear of your spiritual master; kaha — chant; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; ucca kari' — very loudly.

TRANSLATION

The Lord then replied to the Buddhist disciples, "You should all chant the names of Krishna and Hari very loudly near the ear of your spiritual master.
