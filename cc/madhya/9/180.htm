Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.180

kritamālāya snāna kari' āilā tāńra ghare

bhikshā ki dibena vipra, — pāka nāhi kare

SYNONYMS

kritamālāya — in the Kritamālā River; snāna kari' — bathing; āilā — came; tāńra — of the brāhmana; ghare — to the home; bhikshā — offering of alms; ki dibena — what shall give; vipra — the brāhmana; pāka — cooking; nāhi kare — did not do.

TRANSLATION

After bathing in the river Kritamālā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the brāhmana's house to take lunch, but He saw that the food was unprepared because the brāhmana had not cooked it.
