Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.327

loka dehki' rāmānanda gelā nija-ghare

madhyāhne uthilā prabhu bhikshā karibāre

SYNONYMS

loka dekhi' — seeing the people; rāmānanda — Rāya Rāmānanda; gelā — departed; nija-ghare — to his own home; madhyāhne — at noon; uthilā prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up; bhikshā karibāre — to take His lunch.

TRANSLATION

After seeing the people who gathered there, Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya returned to his own home. At noon, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up to take His lunch.
