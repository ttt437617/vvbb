Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.241

bahu yatne sei puńthi nila lekhāiyā

'ananta padmanābha' āilā harashita hañā

SYNONYMS

bahu yatne — with great attention; sei puńthi — that scripture; nila — took; lekhāiyā — having it copied; ananta-padmanābha — to Ananta Padmanābha; āilā — came; harashita — in great happiness; hañā — being.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu copied the Brahma-samhitā, and then with great pleasure He went to a place known as Ananta Padmanābha.

PURPORT

Concerning Ananta Padmanābha, one should refer to Madhya-līlā, Chapter One, text 115.
