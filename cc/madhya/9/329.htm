Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.329

dui jane krishna-kathā kahe rātri-dine

parama-ānande gela pāńca-sāta dine

SYNONYMS

dui jane — both of them; krishna-kathā — topics of Krishna; kahe — speak; rātri-dine — day and night; parama-ānande — in great happiness; gela — passed; pāńca-sāta dine — five to seven days.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu discussed Krishna day and night, and thus they passed from five to seven days in great happiness.
