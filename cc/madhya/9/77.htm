Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.77

deva-sthāne āsi' kaila vishnu daraśana

śrī-vaishnavera sańge tāhāń goshthī anukshana

SYNONYMS

deva-sthāne — to the place known as Devasthāna; āsi' — coming; kaila — did; vishnu daraśana — visiting the temple of Lord Vishnu; śrī-vaishnavera sańge — with the Vaishnavas in the disciplic succession of Rāmānuja; tāhāń — there; goshthī — discussion; anukshana — always.

TRANSLATION

At Devasthāna, Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited the temple of Lord Vishnu, and there He talked with the Vaishnavas in the disciplic succession of Rāmānujācārya. These Vaishnavas are known as Śrī Vaishnavas.
