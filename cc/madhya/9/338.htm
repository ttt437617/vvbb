Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.338

ālālanāthe āsi' krishnadāse pāthāila

nityānanda-ādi nija-gane bolāila

SYNONYMS

ālālanāthe — to the place known as Ālālanātha; āsi' — coming; krishnadāse — Krishnadāsa, His assistant; pāthāila — sent ahead; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; ādi — and others; nija-gane — personal associates; bolāila — called for.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord reached Ālālanātha, He sent His assistant Krishnadāsa ahead to call for Nityānanda Prabhu and other personal associates.
