Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.356

prabhu kahe, — eta tīrtha kailuń paryatana

tomā-sama vaishnava nā dekhiluń eka-jana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; eta tīrtha — to so many holy places; kailuń paryatana — I have traveled; tomā-sama — like you; vaishnava — devotee; nā — not; dekhiluń — I could see; eka-jana — one man.

TRANSLATION

The Lord told Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, "I have traveled to many holy places, but I could not find a Vaishnava as good as you anywhere."
