Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.149

'catur-bhuja-mūrti' dekhāya gopī-ganera āge

sei 'krishne' gopikāra nahe anurāge

SYNONYMS

catur-bhuja-mūrti — four-handed form; dekhāya — exhibits; gopī-ganera — of the gopīs; āge — in front; sei krishne — unto that Krishna; gopikāra — of the gopīs; nahe — not; anurāge — attraction.

TRANSLATION

"Although Krishna assumed the four-armed form of Nārāyana, He could not attract the serious attention of the gopīs in ecstatic love.
