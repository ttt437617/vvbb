Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.25

vipra bale, — ei tomāra darśana-prabhāve

tomā dekhi' gela mora ājanma svabhāve

SYNONYMS

vipra bale — the brāhmana replied; ei — this; tomāra darśana-prabhāve — by the influence of Your visit; tomā dekhi' — after seeing You; gela — went; mora — my; ā-janma — from childhood; svabhāve — nature.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana replied, "This is all due to Your influence, Sir. After seeing You, I have lost my lifelong practice.
