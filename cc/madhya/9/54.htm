Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.54

hena-kāle mahā-kāya eka pakshī āila

thońte kari' anna-saha thāli lañā gela

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; mahā-kāya — having a large body; eka — one; pakshī — bird; āila — appeared there; thońte kari' — by the beak; anna-saha — with food; thāli — the plate; lañā — taking; gela — went away.

TRANSLATION

When the contaminated food was offered to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, a very large bird appeared on the spot, picked up the plate in its beak and flew away.
