Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.41

gosāñira saundarya dekhi' tāte premāveśa

sabe 'krishna' kahe, 'vaishnava' haila sarva-deśa

SYNONYMS

gosāñira — of the Lord; saundarya — the beauty; dekhi' — seeing; tāte — in that; prema-āveśa — ecstatic love; sabe — everyone; krishna kahe — uttered the holy name of Krishna; vaishnava — Vaishnava devotees; haila — became; sarva-deśa — everyone.

TRANSLATION

The Lord's bodily features were very beautiful, and in addition He was always in the ecstasy of love of Godhead. Simply by seeing Him, everyone began chanting the holy name of Krishna, and thus everyone became a Vaishnava devotee.
