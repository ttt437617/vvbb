Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.203

'māyā-sītā' rāvana nila, śunilā ākhyāne

śuni' mahāprabhu haila ānandita mane

SYNONYMS

māyā-sītā — false, illusory Sītā; rāvana — the demon Rāvana; nila — took; śunilā — heard; ākhyāne — in the narration of the Kūrma Purāna; śuni' — hearing this; mahāprabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haila — became; ānandita — very happy; mane — within the mind.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing from the Kūrma Purāna how Rāvana had kidnapped a false form of mother Sītā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very satisfied.
