Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.157

bhatta kahe, — kāhāń āmi jīva pāmara

kāhāń tumi sei krishna, — sākshāt īśvara

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — Veńkata Bhatta said; kāhāń — whereas; āmi — I; jīva — an ordinary living being; pāmara — fallen; kāhāń — whereas; tumi — You; sei krishna — the same Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna; sākshāt īśvara — directly the Lord.

TRANSLATION

Veńkata Bhatta then said, "I am an ordinary fallen living entity, but You are Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself.
