Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.124

śruti pāya, lakshmī nā pāya, ithe ki kārana

bhatta kahe, — ihā praveśite nāre mora mana

SYNONYMS

śruti pāya — the Vedic authorities got admission; lakshmī nā pāya — and the goddess of fortune could not get admission; ithe ki kārana — what must be the reason for this; bhatta kahe — Veńkata Bhatta replied; ihā — this; praveśite — to enter; nāre — is not able; mora — my; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

Having been asked by Caitanya Mahāprabhu why the goddess of fortune could not enter into the rāsa dance whereas the authorities on Vedic knowledge could, Veńkata Bhatta replied, "I cannot enter into the mysteries of this behavior."
