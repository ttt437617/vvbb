Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.335

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre āsite ājñā diyā

nīlācale calilā prabhu ānandita hañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; āsite — to come; ājñā diyā — giving an order; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; calilā — departed; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita hañā — with great pleasure.

TRANSLATION

Giving orders to Rāmānanda Rāya to come to Nīlācala, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu departed for Jagannātha Purī with great pleasure.
