Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.232

tāra astra tāra ańge pade hāta haite

khanda khanda haila bhattathāri palāya cāri bhite

SYNONYMS

tāra astra — their weapons; tāra ańge — on their bodies; pade — fall; hāta haite — from their hands; khanda khanda — cut into pieces; haila — became; bhattathāri — the nomads; palāya — run away; cāri bhite — in the four directions.

TRANSLATION

However, their weapons fell from their hands and struck their own bodies. When some of the Bhattathāris were thus cut to pieces, the others ran away in the four directions.
