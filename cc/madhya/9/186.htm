Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.186

prabhu kahe, — vipra kāńhe kara upavāsa

kene eta duhkha, kene karaha hutāśa

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; vipra — My dear brāhmana; kāńhe — why; kara upavāsa — you are fasting; kene — why; eta — so much; duhkha — unhappiness; kene — why; karaha hutāśa — you express so much worry.

TRANSLATION

While the brāhmana was fasting, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked him, "Why are you fasting? Why are you so unhappy? Why are you so worried?"
