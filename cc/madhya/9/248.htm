Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.248

madhvācārya āni' tāńre karilā sthāpana

adyāvadhi sevā kare tattvavādi-gana

SYNONYMS

madhva-ācārya — Madhvācārya; āni' — bringing; tāńre — Him; karilā sthāpana — installed; adya-avadhi — to date; sevā kare — worship; tattvavādi-gana — the Tattvavādīs.

TRANSLATION

Madhvācārya brought this dancing Gopāla Deity to Udupī and installed Him in the temple. To date, the followers of Madhvācārya, known as Tattvavādīs, worship this Deity.
