Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.19

sei vipra rāma-nāma nirantara laya

'rāma' 'rāma' vinā anya vānī nā kahaya

SYNONYMS

sei vipra — that brāhmana; rāma-nāma — the holy name of Lord Rāmacandra; nirantara — constantly; laya — chants; rāma rāma — the holy names Rāma Rāma; vinā — without; anya — other; vānī — vibration; nā — does not; kahaya — speak.

TRANSLATION

That brāhmana constantly chanted the holy name of Rāmacandra. Indeed, but for chanting Lord Rāmacandra's holy name, that brāhmana did not speak a word.
