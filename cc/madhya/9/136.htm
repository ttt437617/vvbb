Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.136

lakshmī cāhe sei dehe krishnera sańgama

gopikā-anugā hañā nā kaila bhajana

SYNONYMS

lakshmī — the goddess of fortune; cāhe — wants; sei — that; dehe — in the body; krishnera sańgama — the association of Krishna; gopikā — of the gopīs; anugā — follower; hañā — becoming; nā — did not; kaila — perform; bhajana — worship.

TRANSLATION

"The goddess of fortune, Lakshmī, wanted to enjoy Krishna and at the same time retain her spiritual body in the form of Lakshmī. However, she did not follow in the footsteps of the gopīs in her worship of Krishna.
