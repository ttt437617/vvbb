Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.301

prabhu kahe, — pūrvāśrame teńha mora bhrātā

jagannātha miśra — pūrvāśrame mora pitā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; pūrva-āśrame — in My previous āśrama; teńha — He; mora bhrātā — My brother; jagannātha miśra — Jagannātha Miśra; pūrva-āśrame — in My previous āśrama; mora pitā — My father.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "In My previous āśrama, Śańkarāranya was My brother and Jagannātha Miśra was My father."
