Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.110

nirantara tāńra sańge haila sakhya-bhāva

hāsya-parihāse duńhe sakhyera svabhāva

SYNONYMS

nirantara — constantly; tāńra sańge — being associated with him; haila — there was; sakhya-bhāva — a friendly relationship; hāsya — laughing; parihāse — joking; duńhe — both of them; sakhyera — of fraternity; svabhāva — nature.

TRANSLATION

Constantly associating with each other, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Veńkata Bhatta gradually developed a friendly relationship. Indeed, sometimes they laughed and joked together.
