Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.190

prabhu kahe, — e bhāvanā nā kariha āra

pandita hañā kene nā karaha vicāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; e bhāvanā — this kind of thinking; nā — do not; kariha — do; āra — anymore; pandita hañā — being a learned pandita; kena — why; nā karaha — you do not make; vicāra — consideration.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Please do not think this way any longer. You are a learned pandita. Why don't you consider the case?"
