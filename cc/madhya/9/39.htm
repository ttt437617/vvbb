Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.39

tāhāń haite cali' āge gelā eka grāme

brāhmana-samāja tāhāń, karila viśrāme

SYNONYMS

tāhāń haite — from there; cali' — going; āge — forward; gelā — went; eka — one; grāme — to a village; brāhmana-samāja — assembly of brāhmanas; tāhāń — there; karila viśrāme — He rested.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then left Vriddhakāśī and proceeded further. In one village He saw that most of the residents were brāhmanas, and He took His rest there.
