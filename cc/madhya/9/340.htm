Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.340

jagadānanda, dāmodara-pandita, mukunda

nāciyā calilā, dehe nā dhare ānanda

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; dāmodara-pandita — Dāmodara Pandita; mukunda — Mukunda; nāciyā — dancing; calilā — departed; dehe — the body; nā dhare — does not hold; ānanda — happiness.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nityānanda Rāya, Jagadānanda, Dāmodara Pandita and Mukunda all became ecstatic in their happiness, and dancing along the way, they went to meet the Lord.
