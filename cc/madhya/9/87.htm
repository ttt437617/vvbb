Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.87

kāverīte snāna kari' śrī-rańga darśana

pratidina premāveśe karena nartana

SYNONYMS

kāverīte — in the river known as Kāverī; snāna kari' — taking a bath; śrī-rańga darśana — visiting the temple of Śrī Rańga; prati-dina — every day; prema-āveśe — in great happiness; karena — does perform; nartana — dancing.

TRANSLATION

While there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took His bath in the river Kāverī and visited the temple of Śrī Rańga. Every day the Lord also danced in ecstasy.
