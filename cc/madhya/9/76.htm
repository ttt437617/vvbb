Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.76

amritalińga-śiva dekhi' vandana karila

saba śivālaye śaiva 'vaishnava' ha-ila

SYNONYMS

amrita-lińga-śiva — the Lord Śiva deity named Amrita-lińga; dekhi' — seeing; vandana karila — offered obeisances; saba śiva-ālaye — in all the temples of Lord Śiva; śaiva — devotees of Lord Śiva; vaishnava ha-ila — became devotees of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the Śiva deity named Amrita-lińga, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances. Thus He visited all the temples of Lord Śiva and converted the devotees of Lord Śiva into Vaishnavas.
