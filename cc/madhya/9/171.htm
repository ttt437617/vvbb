Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.171

prabhu kahe, — tumi punah āisa nīlācale

āmi setubandha haite āsiba alpa-kāle

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; tumi — you; punah — again; āisa — come; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āmi — I; setubandha haite — from Rāmeśvara; āsiba — shall return; alpa-kāle — very soon.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told him, "Please return to Jagannātha Purī, for I will return there very soon from Rāmeśvara [Setubandha].
