Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.177

tāńra sańge mahāprabhu kari ishtagoshthī

tāńra ājñā lañā āilā purī kāmakoshthī

SYNONYMS

tāńra sańge — with him; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kari ishta-goshthī — discussing spiritual subject matter; tāńra — his; ājñā — order; lañā — taking; āilā — came; purī kāmakoshthī — to Kāmakoshthī-purī.

TRANSLATION

After talking with Lord Śiva, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took his permission to leave and went to Kāmakoshthī-purī.
