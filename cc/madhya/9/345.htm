Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.345

premāveśe sārvabhauma karilā rodane

sabā-sańge āilā prabhu īśvara-daraśane

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma; karilā rodane — cried; sabā-sańge — with all of them; āilā — came; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; īśvara-daraśane — to see the Jagannātha temple.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya cried in great ecstatic love. Then the Lord, accompanied by them all, went to the temple of Jagannātha.
