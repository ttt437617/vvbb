Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.12

sei saba vaishnava mahāprabhura darśane

krishna-upāsaka haila, laya krishna-nāme

SYNONYMS

sei saba — all those; vaishnava — devotees; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; darśane — by seeing; krishna-upāsaka — devotees of Lord Krishna; haila — became; laya — took; krishna-nāme — the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

After meeting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all those different Vaishnavas became devotees of Krishna and began chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
