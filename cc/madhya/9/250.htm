Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.250

tattvavādi-gana prabhuke 'māyāvādī' jñāne

prathama darśane prabhuke nā kaila sambhāshane

SYNONYMS

tattvavādi-gana — the Tattvavādīs; prabhuke — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; māyāvādī jñāne — considering as a Māyāvādī sannyāsī; prathama darśane — in the first meeting; prabhuke — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā — did not; kaila — do; sambhāshane — addressing.

TRANSLATION

When the Tattvavādī Vaishnavas first saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they considered Him a Māyāvādī sannyāsī. Therefore they did not talk to Him.
