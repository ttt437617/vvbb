Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.16

rāmadāsa mahādeve karila daraśana

ahovala-nrisimhere karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

rāma-dāsa — Rāmadāsa; mahā-deve — of Mahādeva; karila — did; daraśana — seeing; ahovala-nrisimhere — to Ahovala-nrisimha; karilā — did; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

There he saw Lord Mahādeva [Śiva], the servant of Lord Rāma. He then went to Ahovala-nrisimha.
