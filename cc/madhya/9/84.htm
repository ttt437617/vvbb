Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.84

bhikshā karāñā kichu kaila nivedana

cāturmāsya āsi' prabhu, haila upasanna

SYNONYMS

bhikshā karāñā — after offering lunch; kichu — some; kaila — did; nivedana — submission; cāturmāsya — the period of Cāturmāsya; āsi' — coming; prabhu — my Lord; haila upasanna — has already arrived.

TRANSLATION

After offering lunch to the Lord, Veńkata Bhatta submitted that the period of Cāturmāsya had already arrived.
