Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.215

mahā-duhkha ha-ite more karilā nistāra

āji mora ghare bhikshā kara ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

mahā-duhkha — great unhappiness; ha-ite — from; more — me; karilā nistāra — You delivered; āji — today; mora — my; ghare — at home; bhikshā — lunch; kara — do; ańgīkāra — accept.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sir, You have delivered me from a very unhappy condition. I request that You take Your lunch at my place. Please accept this invitation.
