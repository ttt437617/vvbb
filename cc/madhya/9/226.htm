Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.226

gosāñira sańge rahe krishnadāsa brāhmana

bhattathāri-saha tāhāń haila daraśana

SYNONYMS

gosāñira — the Lord; sańge — with; rahe — there was; krishnadāsa brāhmana — a brāhmana servant named Krishnadāsa; bhattathāri-saha — with the Bhattathāris; tāhāń — there; haila — there was; daraśana — a meeting.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was accompanied by His servant, Krishnadāsa. He was a brāhmana, but he met with the Bhattathāris there.
