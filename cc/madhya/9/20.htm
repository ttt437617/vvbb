Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.20

sei dina tāńra ghare rahi' bhikshā kari'

tāńre kripā kari' āge calilā gaurahari

SYNONYMS

sei dina — on that day; tāńra ghare — the house of that brāhmana; rahi' — staying; bhikshā kari' — accepting prasādam; tāńre — unto him; kripā kari' — showing mercy; āge — ahead; calilā — departed; gaura-hari — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

That day, Lord Caitanya remained there and accepted prasādam at his house. After bestowing mercy upon him in this way, the Lord proceeded ahead.
