Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.159

more pūrna kripā kaila lakshmī-nārāyana

tāńra kripāya pāinu tomāra carana-daraśana

SYNONYMS

more — unto me; pūrna — complete; kripā — mercy; kaila — did; lakshmī-nārāyana — the Deity of mother goddess of fortune and Nārāyana; tāńra kripāya — by Their mercy; pāinu — I have gotten; tomāra — Your; carana-daraśana — vision of the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"I have been engaged in the service of Lakshmī-Nārāyana, and it is due to Their mercy that I have been able to see Your lotus feet.
