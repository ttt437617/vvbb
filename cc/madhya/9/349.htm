Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.349

kāśī-miśra āsi' prabhura padilā carane

mānya kari' prabhu tāńre kaila ālińgane

SYNONYMS

kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; āsi' — coming; prabhura — of the Lord; padilā — fell down; carane — at the feet; mānya kari' — with great respect; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; kaila — did; ālińgane — embracing.

TRANSLATION

Afterward, Kāśī Miśra came and fell down at the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord respectfully embraced him.
