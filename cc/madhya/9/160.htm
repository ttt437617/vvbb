Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.160

kripā kari' kahile more krishnera mahimā

yāńra rūpa-gunaiśvaryera keha nā pāya sīmā

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — showing causeless mercy; kahile — You have spoken; more — unto me; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mahimā — the glories; yāńra — whose; rūpa-guna-aiśvaryera — of forms, qualities and opulence; keha — anyone; nā — not; pāya — gets; sīmā — the limit.

TRANSLATION

"Out of Your causeless mercy You have told me of the glories of Lord Krishna. No one can reach the end of the opulence, qualities and forms of the Lord.
