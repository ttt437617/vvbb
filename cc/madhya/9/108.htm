Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.108

ei-mata bhatta-grihe rahe gauracandra

nirantara bhatta-sańge krishna-kathānanda

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; bhatta-grihe — in the house of Veńkata Bhatta; rahe — remained; gauracandra — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nirantara — constantly; bhatta-sańge — with Veńkata Bhatta; krishna-kathā-ānanda — the transcendental bliss of talking about Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained at the house of Veńkata Bhatta and constantly talked with him about Lord Krishna. In this way He was very happy.
