Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.112

āmāra thākura krishna — gopa, go-cāraka

sādhvī hañā kene cāhe tāńhāra sańgama

SYNONYMS

āmāra thākura — My worshipable Deity; krishna — Lord Krishna; gopa — cowherd; go-cāraka — a tender of cows; sādhvī hañā — being so chaste; kene — why; cāhe — wants; tāńhāra — His; sańgama — association.

TRANSLATION

"However, my Lord is Lord Śrī Krishna, a cowherd boy who is engaged in tending cows. Why is it that Lakshmī, being such a chaste wife, wants to associate with My Lord?
