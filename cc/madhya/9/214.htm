Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.214

vipra kahe, — tumi sākshāt śrī-raghunandana

sannyāsīra veshe more dilā daraśana

SYNONYMS

vipra kahe — the brāhmana said; tumi — You; sākshāt — directly; śrī-raghunandana — Lord Śrī Rāmacandra; sannyāsīra veshe — in the dress of a mendicant; more — unto me; dilā — You gave; daraśana — audience.

TRANSLATION

After receiving the manuscript, the brāhmana, being very pleased, said, "Sir, You are Lord Rāmacandra Himself and have come in the dress of a sannyāsī to give me audience.
