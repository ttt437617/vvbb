Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.283

premāveśe kaila bahuta kīrtana-nartana

tāhāń eka vipra tāńre kaila nimantrana

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in the great ecstasy of love; kaila — performed; bahuta — much; kīrtana-nartana — chanting and dancing; tāhāń — there; eka — one; vipra — brāhmana; tāńre — unto Him; kaila — did; nimantrana — invitation.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted and danced in various ways as usual. A brāhmana, seeing Him in ecstatic love, was very pleased and invited the Lord to his home for lunch.
