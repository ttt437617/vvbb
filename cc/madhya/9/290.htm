Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.290

eta bali' prabhuke uthāñā kaila ālińgana

galāgali kari' duńhe karena krandana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhuke — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uthāñā — lifting up; kaila — did; ālińgana — embracing; galāgali — shoulder to shoulder; kari' — doing; duńhe — both of them; karena — do; krandana — crying.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Rańga Purī lifted Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu up and embraced Him. As they embraced shoulder to shoulder, they both began to cry in ecstasy.
