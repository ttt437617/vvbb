Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.140

ei tāńra garva prabhu karite khandana

parihāsa-dvāre uthāya eteka vacana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; tāńra — his (Veńkata Bhatta's); garva — pride; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karite khandana — to curb; parihāsa-dvāre — by joking; uthāya — raises; eteka — so many; vacana — words.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had understood this misconception of Veńkata Bhatta's, and to correct it the Lord talked so much in a joking way.
