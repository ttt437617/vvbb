Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.104

tomā dekhi' tāhā haite dvi-guna sukha haya

sei krishna tumi, — hena mora mane laya

SYNONYMS

tomā dekhi' — by seeing You; tāhā haite — than the vision of Lord Krishna; dvi-guna — twice as much; sukha — happiness; haya — there is; sei krishna — that Lord Krishna; tumi — You are; hena — such; mora — my; mane — in the mind; laya — takes.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana said, "Upon seeing You, my happiness is doubled. I take it that You are the same Lord Krishna."
