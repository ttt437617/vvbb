Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.31

param brahma dui-nāma samāna ha-ila

punah āra śāstre kichu viśesha pāila

SYNONYMS

param brahma — the Absolute Truth; dui-nāma — two names (Rāma and Krishna); samāna — on an equal level; ha-ila — were; punah — again; āra — further; śāstre — in revealed scriptures; kichu — some; viśesha — specification; pāila — is found.

TRANSLATION

"As far as the holy names of Rāma and Krishna are concerned, they are on an equal level, but for further advancement we receive some specific information from the revealed scriptures.
