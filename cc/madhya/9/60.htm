Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.60

tomā-sabāra 'guru' tabe pāibe cetana

saba bauddha mili' kare krishna-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

tomā-sabāra — all of you; guru — the spiritual master; tabe — then; pāibe — will get; cetana — consciousness; saba bauddha — all the Buddhist disciples; mili' — coming together; kare — do; krishna-sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra.

TRANSLATION

"By this method your spiritual master will regain his consciousness." Following Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's advice, all the Buddhist disciples began to chant the holy name of Krishna congregationally.
