Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.237

mahā-bhakta-gana-saha tāhāń goshthī kaila

'brahma-samhitādhyāya'-puńthi tāhāń pāila

SYNONYMS

mahā-bhakta-gana-saha — among highly advanced devotees; tāhāń — there; goshthī kaila — discussed; brahma-samhitā-adhyāya — one chapter of the Brahma-samhitā; puńthi — scripture; tāhāń — there; pāila — found.

TRANSLATION

In the temple of Ādi-keśava, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu discussed spiritual matters among highly advanced devotees. While there, He found a chapter of the Brahma-samhitā.
