Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.119

vinodinī lakshmīra haya krishne abhilāsha

ihāte ki dosha, kene kara parihāsa

SYNONYMS

vinodinī — the enjoyer; lakshmīra — of the goddess of fortune; haya — there is; krishne — for Lord Krishna; abhilāsha — desire; ihāte — in this; ki — what; dosha — fault; kene — why; kara — You do; parihāsa — joking.

TRANSLATION

Veńkata Bhatta further explained, "Mother Lakshmī, the goddess of fortune, is also an enjoyer of transcendental bliss; therefore if she wanted to enjoy herself with Krishna, what fault is there? Why are You joking so about this?"
