Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.173

eta bali' tāńra thāñi ei ājñā lañā

dakshine calilā prabhu harashita hañā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; tāńra thāñi — from him; ei ājñā lañā — taking permission; dakshine calilā — departed for southern India; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; harashita hañā — being very pleased.

TRANSLATION

After talking in this way with Paramānanda Purī, the Lord took his permission to leave and, very pleased, departed for southern India.
