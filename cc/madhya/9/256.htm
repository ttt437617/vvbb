Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.256

ācārya kahe, — 'varnāśrama-dharma, krishne samarpana'

ei haya krishna-bhaktera śreshtha 'sādhana'

SYNONYMS

ācārya kahe — the ācārya said; varna-āśrama-dharma — the institution of four castes and four āśramas; krishne — unto Krishna; samarpana — to dedicate; ei haya — this is; krishna-bhaktera — of the devotee of Krishna; śreshtha sādhana — the best means of achievement.

TRANSLATION

The ācārya replied, "When the activities of the four castes and the four āśramas are dedicated to Krishna, they constitute the best means whereby one can attain the highest goal of life.
