Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.164

sańgete calilā bhatta, nā yāya bhavane

tāńre vidāya dilā prabhu aneka yatane

SYNONYMS

sańgete — along with Him; calilā — began to go; bhatta — Veńkata Bhatta; nā yāya bhavane — does not return to his home; tāńre — unto him; vidāya dilā — gave farewell; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; aneka yatane — with great endeavor.

TRANSLATION

Veńkata Bhatta did not want to return home but also wanted to go with the Lord. It was with great endeavor that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade him farewell.
