Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.161

ebe se jāninu krishna-bhakti sarvopari

kritārtha karile, more kahile kripā kari'

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; se — that; jāninu — I understand; krishna-bhakti — devotional service to Lord Krishna; sarva-upari — above all; krita-artha — successful; karile — You have made; more — unto me; kahile — You have spoken; kripā kari' — by Your causeless mercy.

TRANSLATION

"I can now understand that devotional service unto Lord Krishna is the supreme form of worship. Out of Your causeless mercy You have made my life successful simply by explaining the facts."
