Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.141

prabhu kahe, — bhatta, tumi nā kariha samśaya

'svayam-bhagavān' krishna ei ta' niścaya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; bhatta — My dear Veńkata Bhatta; tumi — you; nā kariha — do not do; samśaya — doubt; svayam-bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna — is Lord Krishna; ei ta' niścaya — this is the conclusion.

TRANSLATION

The Lord then continued, "My dear Veńkata Bhatta, please do not continue doubting. Lord Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and this is the conclusion of the Vedic literatures.
