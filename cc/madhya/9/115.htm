Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.115

bhatta kahe, krishna-nārāyana — eka-i svarūpa

krishnete adhika līlā-vaidagdhyādi-rūpa

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — Veńkata Bhatta said; krishna-nārāyana — Krishna and Nārāyana; eka-i svarūpa — one and the same; krishnete — in Lord Krishna; adhika — more; līlā — pastimes; vaidagdhya-ādi-rūpa — sportive nature.

TRANSLATION

Veńkata Bhatta then said, "Lord Krishna and Lord Nārāyana are one and the same, but the pastimes of Krishna are more relishable due to their sportive nature.
