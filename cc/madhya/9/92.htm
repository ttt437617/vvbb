Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.92

eka eka dine cāturmāsya pūrna haila

kataka brāhmana bhikshā dite nā pāila

SYNONYMS

eka eka dine — day by day; cāturmāsya — the period of Cāturmāsya; pūrna haila — became filled; kataka brāhmana — some of the brāhmanas; bhikshā dite — to offer Him lunch; nā — did not; pāila — get the opportunity.

TRANSLATION

Each day the Lord was invited by a different brāhmana, but some of the brāhmanas did not get the opportunity to offer Him lunch because the period of Cāturmāsya came to an end.
