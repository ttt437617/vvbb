Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.48

yadyapi asambhāshya bauddha ayukta dekhite

tathāpi balilā prabhu garva khandāite

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; asambhāshya — not fit for discussion; bauddha — followers of Buddha's philosophy; ayukta — not fit; dekhite — to see; tathāpi — still; balilā — spoke; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; garva — pride; khandāite — to diminish.

TRANSLATION

Although the Buddhists are unfit for discussion and should not be seen by Vaishnavas, Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke to them just to decrease their false pride.
