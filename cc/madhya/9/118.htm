Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.118

krishna-sańge pativratā-dharma nahe nāśa

adhika lābha pāiye, āra rāsa-vilāsa

SYNONYMS

krishna-sańge — in the association of Lord Krishna; pati-vratā — of chastity; dharma — vow; nahe — is not; nāśa — lost; adhika — more; lābha — profit; pāiye — I get; āra — also; rāsa-vilāsa — the enjoyment in the rāsa dance.

TRANSLATION

"The goddess of fortune considered that her vow of chastity would not be damaged by her relationship with Krishna. Rather, by associating with Krishna she could enjoy the benefit of the rāsa dance."
