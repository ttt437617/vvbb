Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.302

ei-mata dui-jane ishta-goshthī kari'

dvārakā dekhite calilā śrī-rańga-purī

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; dui-jane — both of them; ishta-goshthī kari' — discussing many topics; dvārakā dekhite — to see Dvārakā; calilā — started; śrī-rańga-purī — Śrī Rańga Purī.

TRANSLATION

After finishing his talks with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrī Rańga Purī started for Dvārakā-dhāma.
