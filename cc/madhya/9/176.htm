Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.176

tina dina bhikshā dila kari' nimantrana

nibhrite vasi' gupta-vārtā kahe dui jana

SYNONYMS

tina dina — for three days; bhikshā dila — offered alms; kari' nimantrana — inviting Him; nibhrite — in a solitary place; vasi' — sitting together; gupta-vārtā — confidential talks; kahe — speak; dui jana — both of them.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śiva, dressed like a brāhmana, gave alms to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and invited Him to spend three days in a solitary place. Sitting there together, they talked very confidentially.
