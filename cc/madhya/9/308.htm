Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.308

saundarya-mādhurya-krishna-līlāra avadhi

sei jāne, ye 'karnāmrita' pade niravadhi

SYNONYMS

saundarya — beauty; mādhurya — sweetness; krishna-līlāra — of the pastimes of Lord Krishna; avadhi — limit; sei jāne — he knows; ye — one who; karnāmrita — the book Krishna-karnāmrita; pade — studies; niravadhi — constantly.

TRANSLATION

One who constantly reads the Krishna-karnāmrita can fully understand the beauty and melodious taste of the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
