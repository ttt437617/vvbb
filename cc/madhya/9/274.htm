Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.274

ācārya kahe, — tumi yei kaha, sei satya haya

sarva-śāstre vaishnavera ei suniścaya

SYNONYMS

ācārya kahe — the Tattvavādī ācārya said; tumi — You; yei — whatever; kaha — say; sei — that; satya — truth; haya — is; sarva-śāstre — in all revealed scriptures; vaishnavera — of the devotees of Lord Vishnu; ei — this; su-niścaya — conclusion.

TRANSLATION

The Tattvavādī ācārya replied, "What You have said is certainly factual. It is the conclusion of all the revealed scriptures of the Vaishnavas.
