Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.321

dui jane premāveśe karena krandana

premānande śithila haila duńhākāra mana

SYNONYMS

dui jane — both of them; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; karena — do; krandana — crying; prema-ānande — in ecstatic love; śithila haila — became slackened; duńhākāra — of both of them; mana — minds.

TRANSLATION

In great ecstatic love they both began to cry, and thus their minds were slackened.
