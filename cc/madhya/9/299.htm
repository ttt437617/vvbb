Madhya-līlāChapter 9: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Travels to the Holy Places

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 9.299

tāńra eka yogya putra kariyāche sannyāsa

'śańkarāranya' nāma tāńra alpa vayasa

SYNONYMS

tāńra — her; eka — one; yogya — deserving; putra — son; kariyāche — has accepted; sannyāsa — the renounced order of life; śańkarāranya — Śańkarāranya; nāma — named; tāńra — his; alpa — little; vayasa — age.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rańga Purī also remembered that one of her deserving sons had accepted the renounced order at a very young age. His name was Śańkarāranya.
