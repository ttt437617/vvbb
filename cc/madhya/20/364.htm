Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.364

sanātana kahe, — "yāte īśvara-lakshana

pīta-varna, kārya — prema-dāna-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

sanātana kahe — Sanātana said; yāte — in whom; īśvara-lakshana — the characteristics of the Lord are found; pīta-varna — yellowish color; kārya — activities; prema-dāna — distributing love of Godhead; sańkīrtana — and chanting the holy name of the Lord congregationally.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī, "The color of the personality in whom the characteristics of the Lord are found is yellowish. His activities include the distribution of love of Godhead and the chanting of the holy names of the Lord.
