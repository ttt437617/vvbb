Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.89

prabhu kahe, — 'tomāra bhota-kambala kothā gela?'

prabhu-pade saba kathā gosāñi kahila

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; tomāra — your; bhota-kambala — woolen blanket; kothā gela — where did it go; prabhu-pade — unto the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; saba — all; kathā — narration; gosāñi — Sanātana Gosvāmī; kahila — said.

TRANSLATION

When Sanātana Gosvāmī returned, the Lord asked, "Where is your woolen blanket?" Sanātana Gosvāmī then narrated the whole story to the Lord.
