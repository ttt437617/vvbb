Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.152

krishnera svarūpa-vicāra śuna, sanātana

advaya-jñāna-tattva, vraje vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa-vicāra — consideration of the eternal form; śuna — please hear; sanātana — My dear Sanātana; advaya-jñāna-tattva — the Absolute Truth without duality; vraje — in Vrindāvana; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"O Sanātana, please hear about the eternal form of Lord Krishna. He is the Absolute Truth, devoid of duality but present in Vrindāvana as the son of Nanda Mahārāja.
