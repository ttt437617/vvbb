Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.403

sańkshepe kahiluń krishnera svarūpa-vicāra

'ananta' kahite nāre ihāra vistāra

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; kahiluń — I have described; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa-vicāra — consideration of His different forms and features; ananta — Lord Ananta; kahite nāre — not able to describe; ihāra — of this; vistāra — the expanse.

TRANSLATION

"Thus I have briefly described Krishna's manifestation of transcendental forms. This subject matter is so large that even Lord Ananta cannot describe it fully.
