Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.73

pāda-prakshālana kari' bhikshāte vasilā

'sanātane bhikshā deha' — miśrere kahilā

SYNONYMS

pāda-prakshālana — washing the feet; kari' — doing; bhikshāte — to lunch; vasilā — sat down; sanātane bhikshā deha — give Sanātana lunch also; miśrere kahilā — He asked Tapana Miśra.

TRANSLATION

After washing His feet, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down for lunch. He asked Tapana Miśra to supply Sanātana Gosvāmī lunch also.
