Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.193

tāńhā haite punah catur-vyūha-parakāśa

āvarana-rūpe cāri-dike yāńra vāsa

SYNONYMS

tāńhā haite — from that original catur-vyūha; punah — again; catur-vyūha-parakāśa — manifestation of quadruple expansions; āvarana-rūpe — in the form of a covering; cāri-dike — in four directions; yāńra — whose; vāsa — residence.

TRANSLATION

"Thus the original quadruple forms again manifest Themselves in a second set of quadruple expansions. The residences of these second quadruple expansions cover the four directions.
