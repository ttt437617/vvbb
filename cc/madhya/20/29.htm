Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.29

bhūñā hāsi' kahe, — "āmi jāniyāchi pahile

ashta mohara haya tomāra sevaka-āńcale

SYNONYMS

bhūñā — the landlord; hāsi' — smiling; kahe — said; āmi — I; jāniyāchi — knew; pahile — before this; ashta mohara — eight golden coins; haya — there are; tomāra — your; sevaka-āńcale — in the pocket of the servant.

TRANSLATION

Smiling, the landlord said, "Before you offered them, I already knew that there were eight gold coins in your servant's possession.
