Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.144

vedādi sakala śāstre krishna — mukhya sambandha

tāńra jñāne ānushańge yāya māyā-bandha

SYNONYMS

veda-ādi — beginning with the Vedas; sakala — all; śāstre — in the revealed scriptures; krishna — Lord Krishna; mukhya — chief; sambandha — central point or central attraction; tāńra jñāne — by knowledge of Him; ānushańge — simultaneously; yāya — goes away; māyā-bandha — the bondage of material existence.

TRANSLATION

"In all revealed scriptures, beginning with the Vedas, the central point of attraction is Krishna. When complete knowledge of Him is realized, the bondage of māyā, the illusory energy, is automatically broken.
