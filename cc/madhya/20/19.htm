Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.19

'ińhāra thāñi suvarnera ashta mohara haya'

śuni' ānandita bhūñā sanātane kaya

SYNONYMS

ińhāra thāñi — in the possession of this man; suvarnera — of gold; ashta — eight; mohara — coins; haya — there are; śuni' — hearing; ānandita — pleased; bhūñā — the landlord; sanātane — to Sanātana; kaya — says.

TRANSLATION

The palmist said, "This man Sanātana possesses eight gold coins." Hearing this, the landlord was very pleased and spoke the following to Sanātana Gosvāmī.
