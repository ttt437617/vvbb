Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.205

vāsudevera vilāsa dui — adhokshaja, purushottama

sańkarshanera vilāsa — upendra, acyuta dui-jana

SYNONYMS

vāsudevera vilāsa — the pastime expansions of Vāsudeva; dui — two; adhokshaja — Adhokshaja; purushottama — Purushottama; sańkarshanera vilāsa — the pastime expansions of Sańkarshana; upendra — Upendra; acyuta — Acyuta; dui-jana — the two persons.

TRANSLATION

"Of these eight expansions, two are pastime forms of Vāsudeva. Their names are Adhokshaja and Purushottama. The two pastime forms of Sańkarshana are Upendra and Acyuta.
