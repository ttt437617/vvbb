Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.209

padmanābha, trivikrama, nrisimha, vāmana

hari, krishna ādi haya 'ākāre' vilakshana

SYNONYMS

padmanābha — Padmanābha; trivikrama — Trivikrama; nrisimha — Nrisimha; vāmana — Vāmana; hari — Hari; krishna — Krishna; ādi — and so on; haya — are; ākāre vilakshana — different in bodily features.

TRANSLATION

"Of Them, Padmanābha, Trivikrama, Nrisimha, Vāmana, Hari, Krishna and so on all have different bodily features.
