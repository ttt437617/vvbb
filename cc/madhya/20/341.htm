Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.341

dharma pravartana kare vrajendra-nandana

preme gāya nāce loka kare sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

dharma pravartana kare — introduces a particular type of religious activity; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna Himself; preme — in love; gāya — chants; nāce — dances; loka — all people; kare — perform; sańkīrtana — congregational chanting.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja, personally introduces the occupational duty of the Age of Kali. He personally chants and dances in ecstatic love, and thus the entire world chants congregationally.
