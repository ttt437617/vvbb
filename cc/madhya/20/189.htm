Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.189

ādi-catur-vyūha — ińhāra keha nāhi sama

ananta catur-vyūha-ganera prākatya-kārana

SYNONYMS

ādi-catur-vyūha — the original quadruple group; ińhāra — of this; keha nāhi — no one; sama — equal; ananta — unlimited; catur-vyūha-ganera — of the quadruple expansions; prākatya — of manifestation; kārana — the cause.

TRANSLATION

"The first expansion of the caturvyūha, quadruple forms, is unique. There is nothing to compare with Them. These quadruple forms are the source of unlimited quadruple forms.
