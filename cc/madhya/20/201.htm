Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.201

āśvine — padmanābha, kārtike dāmodara

'rādhā-dāmodara' anya vrajendra-końara

SYNONYMS

āśvine — in the month of Āśvina (September-October); padmanābha — Padmanābha; kārtike — in the month of Kārttika (October-November); dāmodara — Dāmodara; rādhā-dāmodara — the Dāmodara of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; anya — another; vrajendra-końara — the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

TRANSLATION

"In the month of Āśvina, the predominating Deity is Padmanābha, and in Kārttika it is Dāmodara. This Dāmodara is different from Rādhā-Dāmodara, the son of Nanda Mahārāja in Vrindāvana.
