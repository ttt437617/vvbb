Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.284

sei purusha ananta-koti brahmānda srijiyā

ekaika-mūrtye praveśilā bahu mūrti hañā

SYNONYMS

sei purusha — that Personality of Godhead, Mahā-Vishnu; ananta-koti brahmānda — millions and trillions of brahmāndas, or universes; srijiyā — after creating; eka-eka — in each one of them; mūrtye — in a form; praveśilā — entered; bahu mūrti hañā — becoming many forms.

TRANSLATION

"After creating the total number of universes, which are unlimited, Mahā-Vishnu expanded Himself into unlimited forms and entered into each of them.
