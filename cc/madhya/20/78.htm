Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.78

tabe miśra purātana eka dhuti dila

teńho dui bahirvāsa-kaupīna karila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; miśra — Tapana Miśra; purātana — old; eka — one; dhuti — dhotī; dila — delivered; teńho — he (Sanātana Gosvāmī); dui — two; bahirvāsa — outer coverings; kaupīna — underwear; karila — made.

TRANSLATION

When Tapana Miśra gave Sanātana Gosvāmī a used dhotī, Sanātana immediately tore it into pieces to make two sets of outer cloth and underwear.
