Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.385

'nitya-līlā' krishnera sarva-śāstre kaya

bujhite nā pāre līlā kemane 'nitya' haya

SYNONYMS

nitya-līlā — eternal pastimes; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sarva-śāstre kaya — described in every śāstra; bujhite nā pāre — not able to understand; līlā — pastimes; kemane — how; nitya haya — are eternal.

TRANSLATION

"Descriptions of Krishna's eternal pastimes are in all the revealed scriptures. But one cannot understand how they are continuing eternally.
