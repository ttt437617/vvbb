Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.25

śuni' sanātana tāre karilā bhartsana

'sańge kene āniyācha ei kāla-yama?'

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; tāre — him; karilā bhartsana — chastised; sańge — with you; kene — why; āniyācha — have you brought; ei — this; kāla-yama — death knell.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Sanātana Gosvāmī chastised his servant, saying, "Why have you brought this death knell with you?"
