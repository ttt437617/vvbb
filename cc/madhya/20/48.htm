Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.48

'dvārete vaishnava nāhi' — prabhure kahila

'keha haya' kari' prabhu tāhāre puchila

SYNONYMS

dvārete — at my door; vaishnava nāhi — there is no Vaishnava; prabhure kahila — he informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; keha haya — is there anyone; kari' — in this way; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāhāre puchila — inquired from him.

TRANSLATION

When Candraśekhara informed the Lord that no Vaishnava was at his door, the Lord asked him, "Is there anyone at all at your door?"
