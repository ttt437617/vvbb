Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.283

eita kahiluń prathama purushera tattva

dvitīya purushera ebe śunaha mahattva

SYNONYMS

eita — thus; kahiluń — I have explained; prathama purushera — of the first incarnation of the Personality of Godhead; tattva — the truth; dvitīya purushera — of the second incarnation of the Personality of Godhead; ebe — now; śunaha — please hear; mahattva — glories.

TRANSLATION

"I have thus explained the truth of the first Personality of Godhead, Mahā-Vishnu. I shall now explain the glories of the second Personality of Godhead.
