Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.203

ei cāri-janera vilāsa-mūrti āra ashta lana

tāń sabāra nāma kahi, śuna sanātana

SYNONYMS

ei cāri-janera — of the four personalities; vilāsa-mūrti — pastime forms; āra — more; ashta jana — eight personalities; tāń sabāra — of all of them; nāma — the holy names; kahi — I shall mention; śuna — hear; sanātana — O Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

"From Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana, Pradyumna and Aniruddha, there are eight additional pastime expansions. O Sanātana, please hear Me as I mention Their names.
