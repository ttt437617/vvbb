Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.238

keśava-bhede padma-śańkha-gadā-cakra-dhara

mādhava-bhede cakra-gadā-śańkha-padma-kara

SYNONYMS

keśava-bhede — according to the different opinion about Lord Keśava; padma — lotus; śańkha — conch; gadā — club; cakra — and disc; dhara — holding; mādhava-bhede — according to the different opinion about the bodily features of Lord Mādhava; cakra — disc; gadā — club; śańkha — conch; padma — lotus; kara — in the hands.

TRANSLATION

"Keśava is described differently as holding the lotus, conch, club and disc, and Mādhava is described as holding the disc, club, conch and lotus in His hands.
