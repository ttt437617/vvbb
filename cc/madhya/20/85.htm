Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.85

tāre kahe, — "ore bhāi, kara upakāre

ei bhota lañā ei kāńthā deha' more"

SYNONYMS

tāre kahe — he said to him; ore bhāi — O my brother; kara upakāre — kindly do a favor; ei bhota — this woolen blanket; lañā — taking; ei — this; kāńthā — quilt; deha' — give; more — to me.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī then told the Bengali mendicant, "My dear brother, please do me a favor. Trade me your quilt for this woolen blanket."
