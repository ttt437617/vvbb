Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.233

purushottama — cakra-padma-śańkha-gadā-dhara

śrī-acyuta — gadā-padma-cakra-śańkha-dhara

SYNONYMS

purushottama — Lord Purushottama; cakra — disc; padma — lotus; śańkha — conch; gadā — club; dhara — holding; śrī-acyuta — Lord Acyuta; gadā — club; padma — lotus; cakra — disc; śańkha — conch; dhara — holding.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Purushottama holds the disc, lotus, conch and club. Lord Acyuta holds the club, lotus, disc and conch.
