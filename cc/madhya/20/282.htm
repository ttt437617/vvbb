Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.282

samasta brahmānda-ganera ińho antaryāmī

kāranābdhiśāyī — saba jagatera svāmī

SYNONYMS

samasta brahmānda-ganera — of the aggregate of the brahmāndas, or universes; ińho — that Lord Mahā-Vishnu; antaryāmī — the Supersoul; kārana-abdhi-śāyī — Lord Mahā-Vishnu, lying on the Causal Ocean; saba jagatera — of all the universes; svāmī — the Supreme Lord.

TRANSLATION

"Mahā-Vishnu is the Supersoul of all the universes. Lying on the Causal Ocean, He is the master of all material worlds.
