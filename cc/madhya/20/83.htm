Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.83

sanātana jānila ei prabhure nā bhāya

bhota tyāga karibāre cintilā upāya

SYNONYMS

sanātana jānila — Sanātana Gosvāmī could understand; ei — this; prabhure — by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā bhāya — is not approved; bhota — the woolen blanket; tyāga — giving up; karibāre — to do; cintilā — considered; upāya — a means.

TRANSLATION

Because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was repeatedly glancing at this valuable woolen blanket, Sanātana Gosvāmī could understand that the Lord did not approve of it. He then began to consider a way to give it up.
