Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.101

kripā kari' yadi more kariyācha uddhāra

āpana-kripāte kaha 'kartavya' āmāra

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — by Your causeless mercy; yadi — if; more — unto me; kariyācha — You have done; uddhāra — deliverance; āpana-kripāte — by Your own mercy; kaha — please speak; kartavya āmāra — my duty.

TRANSLATION

"Out of Your causeless mercy, You have delivered me from the materialistic path. Now, by the same causeless mercy, please tell me what my duty is.
