Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.12

aneka dekhila, tāra lāg nā pāila

dādukā-sahita dubi kāhāń vahi' gela

SYNONYMS

aneka — for a long time; dekhila — I looked; tāra — of him; lāg — contact; nā pāila — could not obtain; dādukā-sahita — with the shackles; dubi — drowning; kāhāń — somewhere; vahi' gela — washed away.

TRANSLATION

"Tell him, 'I looked for him a long time, but I could not find any trace of him. He jumped in with his shackles, and therefore he was drowned and washed away by the waves.'
