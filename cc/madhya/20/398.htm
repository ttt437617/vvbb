Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.398

vraje krishna — sarvaiśvarya-prakāśe 'pūrnatama'

purī-dvaye, paravyome — 'pūrnatara', 'pūrna'

SYNONYMS

vraje — in Vrindāvana; krishna — Lord Krishna; sarva-aiśvarya-prakāśe — manifestation of His full opulence; pūrna-tama — most complete; purī-dvaye — in Dvārakā and Mathurā; para-vyome — and in the spiritual world; pūrna-tara — more complete; pūrna — complete.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is complete in the spiritual sky [Vaikuntha], He is more complete in Mathurā and Dvārakā, and He is most complete in Vrindāvana, Vraja, due to His manifesting all His opulences.

PURPORT

This is confirmed in the following three verses from the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (2.1.221-223).
