Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.80

"sanātana, tumi yāvat kāśīte rahibā

tāvat āmāra ghare bhikshā ye karibā"

SYNONYMS

sanātana — O Sanātana; tumi — you; yāvat — as long as; kāśīte — in Benares; rahibā — will remain; tāvat — so long; āmāra — my; ghare — at the home; bhikshā — lunch; ye — that; karibā — please accept.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana said, "My dear Sanātana, as long as you remain at Kāśī, please accept lunch at my place."
