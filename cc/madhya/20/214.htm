Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.214

eka 'krishnaloka' haya trividha-prakāra

gokulākhya, mathurākhya, dvārakākhya āra

SYNONYMS

eka — one; krishna-loka — the planet known as Krishnaloka; haya — there is; tri-vidha-prakāra — in three different divisions; gokula-ākhya — Gokula; mathurā-ākhya — Mathurā; dvārakā-ākhya — Dvārakā; āra — and.

TRANSLATION

"The planet of Krishnaloka is divided into three sections — Gokula, Mathurā and Dvārakā.
