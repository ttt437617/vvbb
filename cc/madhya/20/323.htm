Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.323

ananta brahmānde aiche karaha ganana

mahā-vishnu eka-śvāse brahmāra jīvana

SYNONYMS

ananta brahmānde — in innumerable universes; aiche — in that way; karaha ganana — just try to count; mahā-vishnu — Lord Mahā-Vishnu; eka-śvāse — by one exhalation; brahmāra jīvana — the duration of life of one Brahmā.

TRANSLATION

"The number of manvantara-avatāras for only one universe has been given. One can only imagine how many manvantara-avatāras exist in the innumerable universes. And all these universes and Brahmās exist only during one exhalation of Mahā-Vishnu.
