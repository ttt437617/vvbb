Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.94

prasanna hañā prabhu tāńre kripā kaila

tāńra kripāya praśna karite tāńra śakti haila

SYNONYMS

prasanna hañā — being very pleased; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; kripā kaila — offered His causeless mercy; tāńra kripāya — by His mercy; praśna karite — to inquire; tāńra — his; śakti haila — there was strength.

TRANSLATION

Being pleased with Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed His causeless mercy upon him. By the Lord's mercy, Sanātana Gosvāmī received the spiritual strength to inquire from Him.
