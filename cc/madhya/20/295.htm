Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.295

virāt vyashti-jīvera teńho antaryāmī

kshīrodakaśāyī teńho — pālana-kartā, svāmī

SYNONYMS

virāt — the universal form; vyashti-jīvera — of all other living entities; teńho — He; antaryāmī — the Supersoul; kshīra-udaka-śāyī — Lord Vishnu who lies down on the ocean of milk; teńho — He; pālana-kartā — the maintainer; svāmī — the master.

TRANSLATION

"Kshīrodakaśāyī Vishnu is the universal form of the Lord and is the Supersoul within every living entity. He is known as Kshīrodakaśāyī because He is the Lord who lies on the ocean of milk. He is the maintainer and master of the universe.
