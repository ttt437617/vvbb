Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.360

ei śloke 'param'-śabde 'krishna'-nirūpana

'satyam' śabde kahe tāńra svarūpa-lakshana

SYNONYMS

ei śloke — in this verse; param-śabde — by the word param, or supreme; krishna — of Lord Krishna; nirūpana — there is an indication; satyam śabde — by the word satyam, or Absolute Truth; kahe — indicates; tāńra — His; svarūpa-lakshana — personal characteristics.

TRANSLATION

"In this invocation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, the word 'param' indicates Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and the word 'satyam' indicates His personal characteristics.
