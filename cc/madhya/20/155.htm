Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.155

svayam bhagavān krishna, 'govinda' para nāma

sarvaiśvarya-pūrna yāńra goloka — nitya-dhāma

SYNONYMS

svayam — personally; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna — Krishna; govinda — Govinda; para nāma — another name; sarva-aiśvarya-pūrna — full of all opulences; yāńra — whose; goloka — Goloka Vrindāvana; nitya-dhāma — eternal abode.

TRANSLATION

"The original Supreme Personality of Godhead is Krishna. His original name is Govinda. He is full of all opulences, and His eternal abode is known as Goloka Vrindāvana.
