Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.39

tina laksha mudrā rājā diyāche tāra sthāne

ghodā mūlya lañā pāthāya pātsāra sthāne

SYNONYMS

tina laksha — 300,000; mudrā — golden coins; rājā — the king or nawab; diyāche — has given; tāra sthāne — in his custody; ghodā — of horses; mūlya lañā — taking the price; pāthāya — sends; pātsāra sthāne — to the care of the emperor.

TRANSLATION

Śrīkānta had 300,000 gold coins with him, which had been given to him by the emperor for the purchase of horses. Thus Śrīkānta was buying horses and dispatching them to the emperor.
