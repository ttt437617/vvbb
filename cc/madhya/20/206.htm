Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.206

pradyumnera vilāsa — nrisimha, janārdana

aniruddhera vilāsa — hari, krishna dui-jana

SYNONYMS

pradyumnera vilāsa — the pastime forms of Pradyumna; nrisimha — Nrisimha; janārdana — Janārdana; aniruddhera vilāsa — the pastime forms of Aniruddha; hari — Hari; krishna — Krishna; dui-jana — the two persons.

TRANSLATION

"The pastime forms of Pradyumna are Nrisimha and Janārdana, and the pastime forms of Aniruddha are Hari and Krishna.
