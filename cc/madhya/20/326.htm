Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.326

raivate 'vaikuntha', cākshushe 'ajita', vaivasvate 'vāmana'

sāvarnye 'sārvabhauma', daksha-sāvarnye 'rishabha' ganana

SYNONYMS

raivate — in the Raivata-manvantara; vaikuntha — the avatāra named Vaikuntha; cākshushe — in the Cākshusha-manvantara; ajita — the avatāra named Ajita; vaivasvate — in the Vaivasvata-manvantara; vāmana — the avatāra named Vāmana; sāvarnye — in the Sāvarnya-manvantara; sārvabhauma — the avatāra named Sārvabhauma; daksha-sāvarnye — in the Daksha-sāvarnya-manvantara; rishabha — the avatāra named Rishabha; ganana — named.

TRANSLATION

"In the Raivata-manvantara, the avatāra is named Vaikuntha, and in the Cākshusha-manvantara, He is named Ajita. In the Vaivasvata-manvantara, He is named Vāmana, and in the Sāvarnya-manvantara, He is named Sārvabhauma. In the Daksha-sāvarnya-manvantara, he is named Rishabha.
