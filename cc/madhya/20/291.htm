Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.291

brahmā, vishnu, śiva — tāńra guna-avatāra

srishti-sthiti-pralayera tinera adhikāra

SYNONYMS

brahmā — Lord Brahmā; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; śiva — Lord Śiva; tāńra — of Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu; guna-avatāra — incarnations of the material qualities; srishti-sthiti-pralayera — of the three functions, namely creation, maintenance and dissolution; tinera adhikāra — there is control by the three deities (Lord Brahmā, Lord Vishnu and Lord Śiva).

TRANSLATION

"Brahmā, Vishnu and Śiva are His three incarnations of the material qualities. Creation, maintenance and destruction respectively are under the charge of these three personalities.
