Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.224

vāsudeva — gadā-śańkha-cakra-padma-dhara

sańkarshana — gadā-śańkha-padma-cakra-kara

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; gadā — club; śańkha — conchshell; cakra — disc; padma — lotus flower; dhara — holding; sańkarshana — Sańkarshana; gadā — club; śańkha — conchshell; padma — lotus flower; cakra-kara — the disc in the hand.

TRANSLATION

"In His lower right hand, Lord Vāsudeva holds a club, in the upper right hand a conchshell, in the upper left hand a disc and in the lower left hand a lotus flower. In His lower right hand, Sańkarshana holds a club, in His upper right hand a conchshell, in His upper left hand a lotus flower and in His lower left hand a disc.
