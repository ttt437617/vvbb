Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.76

miśra sanātane dilā nūtana vasana

vastra nāhi nilā, teńho kaila nivedana

SYNONYMS

miśra — Tapana Miśra; sanātane — unto Sanātana; dilā — delivered; nūtana vasana — new cloth; vastra — the cloth; nāhi nilā — he did not accept; teńho — he; kaila — made; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

When Tapana Miśra offered Sanātana Gosvāmī a new cloth, he did not accept it. Instead, he spoke as follows.
