Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.325

svāyambhuve 'yajña', svārocishe 'vibhu' nāma

auttame 'satyasena', tāmase 'hari' abhidhāna

SYNONYMS

svāyambhuve — in the Svāyambhuva-manvantara; yajña — the avatāra named Yajña; svārocishe — in the Svārocisha-manvantara; vibhu — the avatāra Vibhu; nāma — named; auttame — in the Auttama-manvantara; satyasena — the avatāra named Satyasena; tāmase — in the Tāmasa-manvantara; hari — Hari; abhidhāna — named.

TRANSLATION

"In the Svāyambhuva-manvantara, the avatāra is named Yajña. In the Svārocisha-manvantara, he is named Vibhu. In the Auttama-manvantara, He is named Satyasena, and in the Tāmasa-manvantara, He is named Hari.
