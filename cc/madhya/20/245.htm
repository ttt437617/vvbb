Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.245

avatāra haya krishnera shad-vidha prakāra

purushāvatāra eka, līlāvatāra āra

SYNONYMS

avatāra — incarnations; haya — there are; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; shat-vidha prakāra — six kinds; purusha-avatāra — incarnations of Vishnu; eka — one; līlā-avatāra — incarnations for the execution of pastimes; āra — also.

TRANSLATION

"There are six types of incarnations [avatāras] of Krishna. One includes the incarnations of Vishnu [purusha-avatāras], and another includes the incarnations meant for the performance of pastimes [līlā-avatāras].
