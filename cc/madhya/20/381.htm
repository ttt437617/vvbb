Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.381

pūtanā-vadhādi yata līlā kshane kshane

saba līlā nitya prakata kare anukrame

SYNONYMS

pūtanā-vadha-ādi — killing of the demons like Pūtanā; yata — all; līlā — pastimes; kshane kshane — one moment after another; saba līlā — all these pastimes; nitya — eternally; prakata — manifesting; kare — does; anukrame — one after another.

TRANSLATION

"When Lord Krishna appears, from moment to moment He exhibits His different pastimes, beginning with the killing of Pūtanā. All these pastimes are eternally being demonstrated one after another.
