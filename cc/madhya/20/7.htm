Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.7

pūrve āmi tomāra kariyāchi upakāra

tumi āmā chādi' kara pratyupakāra

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; āmi — I; tomāra — your; kariyāchi — have done; upakāra — welfare; tumi — you; āmā — me; chādi' — releasing; kara — do; prati-upakāra — return welfare.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī continued, "Previously I have done much for you. Now I am in difficulty. Please return my goodwill by releasing me.
