Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.195

cakrādi-dhārana-bhede nāma-bheda saba

vāsudevera mūrti — keśava, nārāyana, mādhava

SYNONYMS

cakra-ādi — of the disc and other weapons; dhārana — of holding; bhede — by differences; nāma — of names; bheda — differences; saba — all; vāsudevera mūrti — the expansions of Vāsudeva; keśava — Keśava; nārāyana — Nārāyana; mādhava — Mādhava.

TRANSLATION

"Out of the catur-vyūha, there are three expansions of each and every form, and They are named differently according to the position of the weapons. The Vāsudeva expansions are Keśava, Nārāyana and Mādhava.
