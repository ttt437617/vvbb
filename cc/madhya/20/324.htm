Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.324

mahā-vishnura niśvāsera nāhika paryanta

eka manvantarāvatārera dekha lekhāra anta

SYNONYMS

mahā-vishnura — of Lord Mahā-Vishnu; niśvāsera — of the exhalations; nāhika paryanta — there is no limit; eka manvantara-avatārera — of only one feature of the Lord, namely the manvantara-avatāras; dekha — just see; lekhāra anta — it is beyond the power of writing.

TRANSLATION

"There is no limit to the exhalations of Mahā-Vishnu. Just see how impossible it is to speak or write of only the manvantara-avatāras!
