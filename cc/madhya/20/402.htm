Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.402

ei krishna — vraje 'pūrnatama' bhagavān

āra saba svarūpa — 'pūrnatara' 'pūrna' nāma

SYNONYMS

ei krishna — the same Krishna; vraje — Vrindāvana; pūrna-tama bhagavān — the most complete manifestation of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; āra — other; saba — all; svarūpa — forms; pūrna-tara — more complete; pūrna — complete; nāma — named.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna is the most complete Supreme Personality of Godhead in Vrindāvana. Elsewhere all His expansions are either complete or more complete.
