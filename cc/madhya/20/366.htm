Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.366

prabhu kahe, — caturāli chāda, sanātana

śaktyāveśāvatārera śuna vivarana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; caturāli — very intelligent argument; chāda — give up; sanātana — O Sanātana; śakti-āveśa-avatārera — of the especially empowered incarnations; śuna — hear; vivarana — the description.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "O Sanātana, you must give up your intelligent tricks. Now just try to understand the description of the śaktyāveśa-avatāras.
