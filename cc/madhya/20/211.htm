Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.211

ińhā-sabāra prithak vaikuntha — paravyoma-dhāme

pūrvādi ashta-dike tina tina krame

SYNONYMS

ińhā — of Them; sabāra — of all; prithak — separate; vaikuntha — a Vaikuntha planet; paravyoma-dhāme — in the spiritual world; pūrva-ādi — beginning from the east; ashta-dike — in the eight directions; tina tina — three in each; krame — in consecutive order.

TRANSLATION

"All these forms preside over different Vaikuntha planets in the spiritual world, beginning from the east in consecutive order. In each of the eight directions, there are three different forms.
