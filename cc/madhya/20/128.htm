Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.128

'tumi kene duhkhī, tomāra āche pitri-dhana

tomāre nā kahila, anyatra chādila jīvana"

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; kene — why; duhkhī — distressed; tomāra — your; āche — there is; pitri-dhana — the riches of your father; tomāre — unto you; nā kahila — he did not disclose; anyatra — somewhere else; chādila — gave up; jīvana — his life.

TRANSLATION

"The astrologer asked, 'Why are you unhappy? Your father was very wealthy, but he did not disclose his wealth to you because he died elsewhere.'
