Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.66

prabhu kahe, — "tomāra dui-bhāi prayāge mililā

rūpa, anupama — duńhe vrindāvana gelā"

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; tomāra — your; dui-bhāi — two brothers; prayāge mililā — met Me at Prayāga; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; anupama — his brother Anupama; duńhe — both of them; vrindāvana gelā — have gone to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I met your two brothers, Rūpa and Anupama, at Prayāga. They have now gone to Vrindāvana."
