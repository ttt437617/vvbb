Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.33

tabe bhūñā gosāñira sańge cāri pāika dila

rātrye rātrye vana-pathe parvata pāra kaila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; bhūñā — the landlord; gosāñira sańge — with Sanātana Gosvāmī; cāri pāika — four watchmen; dila — gave; rātrye rātrye — during the whole night; vana-pathe — on the jungle path; parvata — the hilly tract of land; pāra kaila — took him across.

TRANSLATION

After this settlement was made, the landlord gave Sanātana Gosvāmī four watchmen to accompany him. They went through the forest path for the whole night and thus brought him over the hilly tract of land.
