Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.287

tāńra nābhi-padma haite uthila eka padma

sei padme ha-ila brahmāra janma-sadma

SYNONYMS

tāńra nābhi-padma haite — from His lotus navel; uthila — grew; eka — one; padma — lotus flower; sei padme — on that lotus flower; ha-ila — there was; brahmāra — of Lord Brahmā; janma-sadma — the place of generation.

TRANSLATION

"A lotus flower then sprouted from the lotus navel of that Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu. That lotus flower became Lord Brahmā's birthplace.
