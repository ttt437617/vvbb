Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.49

teńho kahe, — eka 'daraveśa' āche dvāre

'tāńre āna' prabhura vākye kahila tāńhāre

SYNONYMS

teńho kahe — he replied; eka daraveśa — one Muslim mendicant; āche — there is; dvāre — at the door; tāńre āna — bring him; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vākye — the order; kahila — said; tāńhāre — unto him.

TRANSLATION

Candraśekhara replied, "There is a Muslim mendicant."

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately said, "Please bring him here." Candraśekhara then spoke to Sanātana Gosvāmī, who was still sitting beside the door.
