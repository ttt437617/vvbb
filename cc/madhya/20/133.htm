Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.133

'paścime' khudibe, tāhā 'yaksha' eka haya

se vighna karibe, — dhane hāta nā padaya

SYNONYMS

paścime — on the western side; khudibe — if you dig; tāhā — there; yaksha — ghost; eka — one; haya — there is; se — he; vighna karibe — will create disturbances; dhane — on the treasure; hāta — hand; nā — not; padaya — touches.

TRANSLATION

"'If you dig on the western side, there is a ghost who will create such a disturbance that your hands will not even touch the treasure.
