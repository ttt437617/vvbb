Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.346

dhyāyan krite yajan yajñais

tretāyām dvāpare 'rcayan

yad āpnoti tad āpnoti

kalau sańkīrtya keśavam

SYNONYMS

dhyāyan — meditating; krite — in Satya-yuga; yajan — worshiping; yajñaih — by the performance of great sacrifices; tretāyām — in Tretā-yuga; dvāpare — in Dvāpara-yuga; arcayan — worshiping the lotus feet; yat — whatever; āpnoti — is achieved; tat — that; āpnoti — is obtained; kalau — in the Age of Kali; sańkīrtya — simply by chanting; keśavam — the pastimes and qualities of Lord Keśava.

TRANSLATION

"'Whatever is achieved by meditation in Satya-yuga, by the performance of yajña in Tretā-yuga or by the worship of Krishna's lotus feet in Dvāpara-yuga is also obtained in the Age of Kali simply by chanting the glories of Lord Keśava.'
