Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.296

purushāvatārera ei kailuń nirūpana

līlāvatāra ebe śuna, sanātana

SYNONYMS

purusha-avatārera — of all the purusha-avatāras; ei — this; kailuń nirūpana — I have described; līlā-avatāra — incarnations for pastimes; ebe — now; śuna — please hear; sanātana — O Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

"O Sanātana, I have definitively described the three purusha-avatāras of Vishnu. Now please hear from Me about the pastime incarnations.
