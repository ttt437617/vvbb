Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.357

ākriti, prakriti, svarūpa, — svarūpa-lakshana

kārya-dvārā jñāna, — ei tatastha-lakshana

SYNONYMS

ākriti — bodily features; prakriti — nature; svarūpa — form; svarūpa-lakshana — personal symptoms; kārya-dvārā — by activities; jñāna — knowledge; ei — this; tatastha-lakshana — the marginal symptoms.

TRANSLATION

"Bodily features, nature and form are the personal characteristics. Knowledge of His activities provides the marginal characteristics.
