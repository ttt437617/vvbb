Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.386

drishtānta diyā kahi tabe loka yadi jāne

krishna-līlā — nitya, jyotiścakra-pramāne

SYNONYMS

drishtānta diyā — giving an example; kahi — let Me say; tabe — then; loka — people; yadi — if; jāne — can understand; krishna-līlā — pastimes of Lord Krishna; nitya — eternal; jyotih-cakra — of the zodiac; pramāne — by evidence.

TRANSLATION

"Let me give an example by which people may understand Lord Krishna's eternal pastimes. An example can be found in the zodiac.
