Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.255

kriyā-śakti-pradhāna sańkarshana balarāma

prākritāprākrita-srishti karena nirmāna

SYNONYMS

kriyā-śakti-pradhāna — the predominator of the creative energy; sańkarshana — Lord Sańkarshana; balarāma — Lord Balarāma; prākrita — material; aprākrita — spiritual; srishti — worlds; karena — does; nirmāna — creation.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Sańkarshana is Lord Balarāma. Being the predominator of the creative energy, He creates both the material and the spiritual world.
