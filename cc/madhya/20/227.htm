Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.227

śrī-keśava — padma-śańkha-cakra-gadā-dhara

nārāyana — śańkha-padma-gadā-cakra-dhara

SYNONYMS

śrī-keśava — Lord Keśava; padma — lotus; śańkha — conch; cakra — disc; gadā — club; dhara — holding; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; śańkha — conch; padma — lotus; gadā — club; cakra — disc; dhara — holding.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Keśava holds the lotus, conch, disc and club. Lord Nārāyana holds the conch, lotus, club and disc.
