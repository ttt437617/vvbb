Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.370

vaikunthe 'śesha' — dharā dharaye 'ananta'

ei mukhyāveśāvatāra — vistāre nāhi anta

SYNONYMS

vaikunthe — in the spiritual world; śesha — Lord Śesha; dharā dharaye — carries innumerable planets; ananta — Ananta; ei — these; mukhya-āveśa-avatāra — primary directly empowered incarnations; vistāre — in expanding them; nāhi — there is not; anta — limit.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Śesha in the spiritual world of Vaikuntha and, in the material world, Lord Ananta, who carries innumerable planets on His hoods, are two primary empowered incarnations. There is no need to count the others, for they are unlimited.
