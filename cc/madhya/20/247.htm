Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.247

bālya, pauganda haya vigrahera dharma

eta-rūpe līlā karena vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

bālya — childhood; pauganda — boyhood; haya — there are; vigrahera — of the Deity; dharma — characteristics; eta-rūpe — in so many forms; līlā — pastimes; karena — executes; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"Childhood and boyhood are the typical ages of the Deity. Krishna, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, performed His pastimes as a child and as a boy.
