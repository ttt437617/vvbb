Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.36

tāre vidāya diyā gosāñi calilā ekalā

hāte karońyā, chińdā kānthā, nirbhaya ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

tāre vidāya diyā — bidding him farewell; gosāñi — Sanātana Gosvāmī; calilā ekalā — began to travel alone; hāte — in the hand; karońyā — a beggar's pot; chińdā kānthā — a torn quilt; nirbhaya ha-ilā — he became free from all anxiety.

TRANSLATION

After departing from Īśāna, Sanātana Gosvāmī began traveling alone with a waterpot in his hand. Simply covered with a torn quilt, he thus lost all his anxiety.
