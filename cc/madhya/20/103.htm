Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.103

'sādhya'-'sādhana'-tattva puchite nā jāni

kripā kari' saba tattva kaha ta' āpani"

SYNONYMS

sādhya — of the goal of spiritual life; sādhana — of the process of obtaining that goal; tattva — truth; puchite — to inquire; nā jāni — I do not know; kripā kari' — by Your causeless mercy; saba tattva — all such truths; kaha ta' āpani — please personally explain to me.

TRANSLATION

"Actually I do not know how to inquire about the goal of life and the process for obtaining it. Being merciful upon me, please explain all these truths."
