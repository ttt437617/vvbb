Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.64

sanātana kahe, — 'krishna āmi nāhi jāni

āmāra uddhāra-hetu tomāra kripā māni'

SYNONYMS

sanātana kahe — Sanātana Gosvāmī said; krishna — Lord Krishna; āmi — I; nāhi jāni — do not know; āmāra — my; uddhāra-hetu — the cause of release; tomāra — Your; kripā — mercy; māni — I accept.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana replied, "I do not know who Krishna is. As far as I am concerned, I have been released from prison only by Your mercy."
