Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.236

adhokshaja — padma-gadā-śańkha-cakra-kara

upendra — śańkha-gadā-cakra-padma-kara

SYNONYMS

adhokshaja — Lord Adhokshaja; padma — lotus; gadā — club; śańkha — conch; cakra — disc; kara — in the hands; upendra — Lord Upendra; śańkha — conch; gadā — club; cakra — disc; padma — lotus; kara — in the hands.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Adhokshaja holds the lotus, club, conch and disc. Lord Upendra holds the conch, club, disc and lotus.
