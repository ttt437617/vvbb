Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.231

śrīdhara — padma-cakra-gadā-śańkha-kara

hrishīkeśa — gadā-cakra-padma-śańkha-dhara

SYNONYMS

śrīdhara — Lord Śrīdhara; padma — lotus; cakra — disc; gadā — club; śańkha — conch; kara — in the hands; hrishīkeśa — Lord Hrishīkeśa; gadā — club; cakra — disc; padma — lotus; śańkha — conch; dhara — holding.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Śrīdhara holds the lotus, disc, club and conch. Lord Hrishīkeśa holds the club, disc, lotus and conch.
