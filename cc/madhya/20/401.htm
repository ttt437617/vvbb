Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.401

krishnasya pūrnatamatā

vyaktābhūd gokulāntare

pūrnatā pūrnataratā

dvārakā-mathurādishu

SYNONYMS

krishnasya — of Lord Krishna; pūrna-tamatā — being most perfect; vyaktā — manifested; abhūt — became; gokula-antare — in the domain of Gokula Vrindāvana; pūrnatā — completeness; pūrna-taratā — more completeness; dvārakā — in Dvārakā; mathurā-ādishu — and Mathurā, and so on.

TRANSLATION

"'The most complete qualities of Krishna are manifested within Vrindāvana, and His complete and more complete qualities are manifested in Dvārakā and Mathurā.'
