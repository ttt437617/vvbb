Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.340

'pīta'-varna dhari' tabe kailā pravartana

prema-bhakti dilā loke lañā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

pīta-varna dhari' — assuming the color yellow; tabe — thereafter; kailā pravartana — introduced the sańkīrtana movement; prema-bhakti dilā — He distributed love of Krishna; loke — to the people in general; lañā bhakta-gana — accompanied by His devotees.

TRANSLATION

"In the Age of Kali, Lord Krishna assumes a golden color and, accompanied by His personal devotees, introduces hari-nāma-sańkīrtana, the chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra. By this process He delivers love for Krishna to the general populace.
