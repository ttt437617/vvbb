Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.126

krishna-mādhurya-sevānanda-prāptira kārana

krishna-sevā kare, āra krishna-rasa-āsvādana

SYNONYMS

krishna-mādhurya — of an intimate relationship with Krishna; sevā-ānanda — of pleasure from rendering service unto Him; prāptira — of achievement; kārana — because; krishna-sevā kare — one renders service to Krishna; āra — and; krishna-rasa — of the mellows of such service; āsvādana — tasting.

TRANSLATION

"When one attains the transcendental bliss of an intimate relationship with Krishna, he renders service to Him and tastes the mellows of Krishna consciousness.
