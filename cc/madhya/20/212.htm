Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.212

yadyapi paravyoma sabākāra nitya-dhāma

tathāpi brahmānde kāro kāńho sannidhāna

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; paravyoma — the spiritual sky; sabākāra — of all of Them; nitya-dhāma — the eternal abode; tathāpi — still; brahmānde — in the material universes; kāro — of some of Them; kāńho — somewhere; sannidhāna — the residential places.

TRANSLATION

"Although They all have Their residences eternally in the spiritual sky, some of Them are situated within the material universes.
