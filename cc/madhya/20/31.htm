Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.31

santushta ha-ilāńa āmi, mohara nā la-iba

punya lāgi' parvata tomā' pāra kari' diba"

SYNONYMS

santushta — satisfied; ha-ilāńa — have become; āmi — I; mohara — the golden coins; nā la-iba — I shall not take; punya lāgi' — simply for pious activity; parvata — the hilly tract of land; tomā' — you; pāra kari' diba — I shall get across.

TRANSLATION

"I am very satisfied with your behavior. I shall not accept these gold coins, but I shall get you across that hilly tract of land simply to perform a pious activity."
