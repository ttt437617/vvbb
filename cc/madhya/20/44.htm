Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.44

yatna kari' teńho eka bhota-kambala dila

gańgā pāra kari' dila — gosāñi calila

SYNONYMS

yatna kari' — with great care; teńho — he (Śrīkānta); eka — one; bhota-kambala — woolen blanket; dila — gave; gańgā pāra kari' dila — got him across the river Ganges; gosāñi calila — Sanātana Gosvāmī departed.

TRANSLATION

With great care, Śrīkānta gave him a woolen blanket and helped him cross the Ganges. Thus Sanātana Gosvāmī departed again.
