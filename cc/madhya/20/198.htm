Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.198

dvādaśa-māsera devatā — ei-bāra jana

mārgaśīrshe — keśava, paushe — nārāyana

SYNONYMS

dvādaśa-māsera — of the twelve months; devatā — predominating Deities; ei — these; bāra jana — twelve Personalities of Godhead; mārga-śīrshe — the month of Agrahāyana (November-December); keśava — Keśava; paushe — the month of Pausha (December-January); nārāyana — Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

"These twelve are the predominating Deities of the twelve months. Keśava is the predominating Deity of Agrahāyana, and Nārāyana is the predominating Deity of Pausha.
