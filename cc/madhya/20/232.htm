Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.232

padmanābha — śańkha-padma-cakra-gadā-kara

dāmodara — padma-cakra-gadā-śańkha-dhara

SYNONYMS

padmanābha — Lord Padmanābha; śańkha — conch; padma — lotus; cakra — disc; gadā — club; kara — in the hands; dāmodara — Lord Dāmodara; padma — lotus; cakra — disc; gadā — club; śańkha — conch; dhara — holding.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Padmanābha holds the conch, lotus, disc and club. Lord Dāmodara holds the lotus, disc, club and conch.
