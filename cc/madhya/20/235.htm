Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.235

śrī-hari — śańkha-cakra-padma-gadā-kara

śrī-krishna — śańkha-gadā-padma-cakra-kara

SYNONYMS

śrī-hari — Lord Hari; śańkha — conch; cakra — disc; padma — lotus; gadā — club; kara — in the hand; śrī-krishna — Lord Krishna; śańkha — conch; gadā — club; padma — lotus; cakra — disc; kara — in the hands.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Hari holds the conch, disc, lotus and club. Lord Śrī Krishna holds the conch, club, lotus and disc.
