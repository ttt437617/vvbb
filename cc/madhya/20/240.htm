Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.240

'svayam bhagavān', āra 'līlā-purushottama'

ei dui nāma dhare vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

svayam bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; āra — and; līlā-purushottama — the Lord Purushottama of pastimes; ei dui — these two; nāma — names; dhare — takes; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna, the original Supreme Personality of Godhead, indicated as the son of Mahārāja Nanda, has two names. One is svayam bhagavān, and the other is līlā-purushottama.
