Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.329

yugāvatāra ebe śuna, sanātana

satya-tretā-dvāpara-kali-yugera ganana

SYNONYMS

yuga-avatāra — incarnation of millenniums; ebe — now; śuna — hear; sanātana — O Sanātana Gosvāmī; satya-tretā-dvāpara-kali-yugera — of Satya-yuga, Tretā-yuga, Dvāpara-yuga and Kali-yuga; ganana — the chronological order.

TRANSLATION

"O Sanātana, now hear from Me about the yuga-avatāras, the incarnations for the millenniums. First of all, there are four yugas — Satya-yuga, Tretā-yuga, Dvāpara-yuga and Kali-yuga.
