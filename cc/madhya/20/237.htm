Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.237

hayaśīrsha-pañcarātre kahe shola-jana

tāra mate kahi ebe cakrādi-dhārana

SYNONYMS

hayaśīrsha-pañcarātre — the revealed scripture named the Hayaśīrsha-pañcarātra; kahe — says; shola-jana — sixteen personalities; tāra mate — according to this opinion; kahi — I shall describe; ebe — now; cakra-ādi-dhārana — the holding of weapons, beginning with the disc.

TRANSLATION

"According to the Hayaśīrsha-pañcarātra, there are sixteen personalities. I shall now describe that opinion of how They hold the weapons.

PURPORT

The sixteen personalities are as follows: (1) Vāsudeva, (2) Sańkarshana, (3) Pradyumna, (4) Aniruddha, (5) Keśava, (6) Nārāyana, (7) Mādhava, (8) Govinda, (9) Vishnu, (10) Madhusūdana, (11) Trivikrama, (12) Vāmana, (13) Śrīdhara, (14) Hrishīkeśa, (15) Padmanābha and (16) Dāmodara.
