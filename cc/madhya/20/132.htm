Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.132

'ei sthāne āche dhana' — yadi dakshine khudibe

'bhīmarula-barulī' uthibe, dhana nā pāibe

SYNONYMS

ei sthāne — at this place; āche — is; dhana — treasure; yadi — if; dakshine — on the southern side; khudibe — you will dig; bhīmarula-barulī — wasps and drones; uthibe — will rise; dhana — the riches; nā pāibe — you will not get.

TRANSLATION

"The astrologer said, 'The treasure is in this place, but if you dig toward the southern side, the wasps and drones will rise, and you will not get your treasure.
