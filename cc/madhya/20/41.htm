Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.41

dui-jana mili' tathā ishta-goshthī kaila

bandhana-mokshana-kathā gosāñi sakali kahila

SYNONYMS

dui-jana mili' — meeting together; tathā — there; ishta-goshthī — various types of conversation; kaila — did; bandhana-mokshana — of the arrest and release; kathā — the story; gosāñi — Sanātana Gosvāmī; sakali — everything; kahila — narrated.

TRANSLATION

When they met, they had many conversations. Sanātana Gosvāmī told him in detail about his arrest and release.
