Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.204

purushottama, acyuta, nrisimha, janārdana

hari, krishna, adhokshaja, upendra, — ashta-jana

SYNONYMS

purushottama — Purushottama; acyuta — Acyuta; nrisimha — Nrisimha; janārdana — Janārdana; hari — Hari; krishna — Krishna; adhokshaja — Adhokshaja; upendra — Upendra; ashta-jana — eight persons.

TRANSLATION

"The eight pastime expansions are Purushottama, Acyuta, Nrisimha, Janārdana, Hari, Krishna, Adhokshaja and Upendra.
