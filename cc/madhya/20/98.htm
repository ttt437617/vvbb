Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.98

tabe sanātana prabhura carane dhariyā

dainya vinati kare dante trina lañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — the lotus feet; dhariyā — catching; dainya — humility; vinati — bowing; kare — does; dante — in the teeth; trina — a straw; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

Putting a straw in his mouth and bowing down, Sanātana Gosvāmī clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and humbly spoke as follows.
