Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.43

gosāñi kahe, — 'eka-kshana ihā nā rahiba

gańgā pāra kari' deha' e-kshane caliba"

SYNONYMS

gosāñi kahe — Sanātana Gosvāmī said; eka-kshana — even for one moment; ihā — here; nā rahiba — I shall not stay; gańgā pāra kari' deha' — help me cross the river Ganges; e-kshane — immediately; caliba — I shall go.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "I shall not stay here even for a moment. Please help me cross the Ganges. I shall leave immediately."
