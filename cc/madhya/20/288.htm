Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.288

sei padma-nāle ha-ila caudda bhuvana

teńho 'brahmā' hañā srishti karila srijana

SYNONYMS

sei padma-nāle — within the stem of that lotus; ha-ila — was manifested; caudda — fourteen; bhuvana — planetary systems; teńho — He; brahmā — Lord Brahmā; hañā — having become; srishti — the material creation; karila srijana — created.

TRANSLATION

"In the stem of that lotus flower, the fourteen worlds were generated. Then He became Lord Brahmā and manifested the entire universe.
