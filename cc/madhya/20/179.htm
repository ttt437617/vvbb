Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.179

govindera mādhurī dekhi' vāsudevera kshobha

se mādhurī āsvādite upajaya lobha

SYNONYMS

govindera — of Lord Govinda; mādhurī — the sweetness; dekhi' — seeing; vāsudevera — of Vāsudeva; kshobha — agitation; se — that; mādhurī — sweetness; āsvādite — to taste; upajaya — awakens; lobha — greed.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, Vāsudeva is agitated just to see the sweetness of Govinda, and a transcendental greed awakens in Him to enjoy that sweetness.
