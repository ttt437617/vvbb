Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.265

sei māyā avalokite śrī-sańkarshana

purusha-rūpe avatīrna ha-ilā prathama

SYNONYMS

sei māyā — that material energy; avalokite — just to glance over; śrī-sańkarshana — Sańkarshana; purusha-rūpe — in the original form of Mahā-Vishnu; avatīrna — incarnated; ha-ilā — became; prathama — at first.

TRANSLATION

"To glance over that material energy and empower her, Lord Sańkarshana first incarnates as Lord Mahā-Vishnu.
