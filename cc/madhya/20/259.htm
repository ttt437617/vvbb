Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.259

māyā-dvāre srije teńho brahmāndera gana

jada-rūpā prakriti nahe brahmānda-kārana

SYNONYMS

māyā-dvāre — by the agency of the external energy; srije — creates; teńho — Lord Sańkarshana; brahmāndera gana — all the groups of universes; jada-rūpā — appearing dull; prakriti — the material energy; nahe — is not; brahmānda-kārana — the cause of the cosmic manifestation.

TRANSLATION

"By the agency of the material energy, this same Lord Sańkarshana creates all the universes. The dull material energy — known in modern language as nature — is not the cause of the material universe.
