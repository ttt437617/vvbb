Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.343

āra tina-yuge dhyānādite yei phala haya

kali-yuge krishna-nāme sei phala pāya

SYNONYMS

āra tina-yuge — in the three other yugas; dhyāna-ādite — by processes beginning with meditation; yei — whatever; phala — result; haya — there is; kali-yuge — in this Age of Kali; krishna-nāme — by chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; sei phala pāya — one gets the same achievement.

TRANSLATION

"In the other three yugas — Satya, Tretā and Dvāpara — people perform different types of spiritual activities. Whatever results they achieve in that way, they can achieve in Kali-yuga simply by chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
