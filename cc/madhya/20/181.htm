Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.181

mathurāya yaiche gandharva-nritya-daraśane

punah dvārakāte yaiche citra-vilokane

SYNONYMS

mathurāya — at Mathurā; yaiche — just as; gandharva-nritya — the dance of the Gandharvas; daraśane — by seeing; punah — again; dvārakāte — at Dvārakā; yaiche — just as; citra-vilokane — by seeing a picture of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"One instance of Vāsudeva's attraction to Krishna occurred when Vāsudeva saw the Gandharva dance at Mathurā. Another instance occurred in Dvārakā when Vāsudeva was surprised to see a picture of Krishna.
