Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.404

ananta svarūpa krishnera nāhika ganana

śākhā-candra-nyāye kari dig-daraśana

SYNONYMS

ananta — unlimited; svarūpa — forms; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; nāhika ganana — there is no counting; śākhā-candra-nyāye — by the logic of showing the moon through the branches of a tree; kari — I do; dik-daraśana — only partial showing.

TRANSLATION

"In this way Krishna's transcendental forms are expanded unlimitedly. No one can count them. Whatever I have explained is simply a little glimpse. It is like showing the moon through the branches of a tree."
