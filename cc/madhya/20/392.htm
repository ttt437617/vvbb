Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.392

saoyāśata vatsara krishnera prakata-prakāśa

tāhā yaiche vraja-pure karilā vilāsa

SYNONYMS

saoyāśata — 125; vatsara — years; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; prakata-prakāśa — manifestation of the appearance; tāhā — that; yaiche — like; vraja-pure — in Vrindāvana and Dvārakā; karilā vilāsa — enjoys the pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna remains within a universe for 125 years, and He enjoys His pastimes both in Vrindāvana and Dvārakā.
