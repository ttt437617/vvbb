Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.302

bhakti-miśra-krita-punye kona jīvottama

rajo-gune vibhāvita kari' tāńra mana

SYNONYMS

bhakti-miśra-krita-punye — because of pious activities mixed with devotional service; kona — someone; jīva-uttama — the best of the living entities; rajah-gune — by the mode of passion; vibhāvita — influenced; kari' — making; tāńra — his; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"Because of his past pious activities mixed with devotional service, the first-class living entity is influenced by the mode of passion within his mind.
