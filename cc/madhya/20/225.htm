Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.225

pradyumna — cakra-śańkha-gadā-padma-dhara

aniruddha — cakra-gadā-śańkha-padma-kara

SYNONYMS

pradyumna — Lord Pradyumna; cakra — disc; śańkha — conch; gadā — club; padma — lotus; dhara — holding; aniruddha — Lord Aniruddha; cakra — disc; gadā — club; śańkha — conch; padma-kara — lotus flower in hand.

TRANSLATION

"Pradyumna holds the disc, conch, club and lotus. Aniruddha holds the disc, club, conch and lotus.
