Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.285

praveśa kariyā dekhe, saba — andhakāra

rahite nāhika sthāna, karilā vicāra

SYNONYMS

praveśa kariyā — after entering; dekhe — He sees; saba — everywhere; andhakāra — complete darkness; rahite — to remain there; nāhika sthāna — there was no place; karilā vicāra — then He considered.

TRANSLATION

"When Mahā-Vishnu entered each of the limitless universes, He saw that there was darkness all around and that there was no place to stay. He therefore began to consider the situation.
