Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.349

cāri-yugāvatāre ei ta' ganana

śuni' bhańgi kari' tāńre puche sanātana

SYNONYMS

cāri-yuga-avatāre — of the incarnations in the four different yugas; ei ta' ganana — such enumeration; śuni' — hearing; bhańgi kari' — giving a hint; tāńre — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; puche — inquired; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

"Thus I have given a description of the incarnations of the four different yugas." After hearing all this, Sanātana Gosvāmī gave an indirect hint to the Lord.
