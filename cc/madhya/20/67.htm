Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.67

tapana-miśrere āra candraśekharere

prabhu-ājñāya sanātana mililā dońhāre

SYNONYMS

tapana-miśrere — unto Tapana Miśra; āra — and; candraśekharere — unto Candraśekhara; prabhu-ājñāya — by the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sanātana — Sanātana; mililā — met; dońhāre — both of them.

TRANSLATION

By the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Sanātana Gosvāmī met both Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara.
