Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.54

tabe prabhu tāńra hāta dhari' lañā gelā

pindāra upare āpana-pāśe vasāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; hāta dhari' — catching the hand; lañā gelā — took him inside; pindāra upare — on an elevated place; āpana-pāśe — near Him; vasāilā — made Sanātana Gosvāmī sit down.

TRANSLATION

Catching his hand, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took Sanātana Gosvāmī inside and made him sit on an elevated place next to Him.
