Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.68

tapana-miśra tabe tāńre kailā nimantrana

prabhu kahe, — 'kshaura karāha, yāha, sanātana'

SYNONYMS

tapana-miśra — Tapana Miśra; tabe — then; tāńre — unto him (Sanātana Gosvāmī); kailā — made; nimantrana — invitation; prabhu kahe — Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; kshaura karāha — get shaved; yāha — go; sanātana — My dear Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

Tapana Miśra then extended an invitation to Sanātana, and Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked Sanātana to go get a shave.
