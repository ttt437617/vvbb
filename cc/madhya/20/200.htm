Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.200

jyaishthe — trivikrama, āshādhe — vāmana deveśa

śrāvane — śrīdhara, bhādre — deva hrishīkeśa

SYNONYMS

jyaishthe — in the month of Jyaishtha (May-June); trivikrama — Trivikrama; āshādhe — in the month of Āshādha (June-July); vāmana deva-īśa — Lord Vāmana; śrāvane — in the month of Śrāvana (July-August); śrīdhara — Śrīdhara; bhādre — in the month of Bhādra (August-September); deva hrishīkeśa — Lord Hrishīkeśa.

TRANSLATION

"In the month of Jyaishtha, the predominating Deity is Trivikrama. In Āshādha the Deity is Vāmana, in Śrāvana the Deity is Śrīdhara, and in Bhādra the Deity is Hrishīkeśa.
