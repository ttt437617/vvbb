Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.4

patrī pāñā sanātana ānandita hailā

yavana-rakshaka-pāśa kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

patrī pāñā — receiving the note; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; ānandita hailā — became very pleased; yavana — meat-eater; rakshaka — the superintendent of the jail; pāśa — before; kahite lāgilā — began to say.

TRANSLATION

When Sanātana Gosvāmī received this note from Rūpa Gosvāmī, he became very pleased. He immediately went to the jail superintendent, who was a meat-eater, and spoke as follows.
