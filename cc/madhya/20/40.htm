Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.40

tuńgi upara vasi' sei gosāñire dekhila

rātrye eka-jana-sańge gosāñi-pāśa āila

SYNONYMS

tuńgi upara vasi' — sitting in an elevated place; sei — that Śrīkānta; gosāñire — Sanātana Gosvāmī; dekhila — saw; rātrye — at night; eka-jana-sańge — with a servant; gosāñi-pāśa — near Sanātana Gosvāmī; āila — he came.

TRANSLATION

When Śrīkānta was sitting in an elevated place, he could see Sanātana Gosvāmī. That night he took a servant and went to see Sanātana Gosvāmī.
