Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.293

ei ta' dvitīya-purusha — brahmāndera īśvara

māyāra 'āśraya' haya, tabu māyā-pāra

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — in this way; dvitīya-purusha — the second Personality of Godhead; brahmāndera īśvara — the master of the universe; māyāra — of the external, material energy; āśraya haya — becomes the shelter; tabu — still; māyā-pāra — is beyond the touch of the material energy.

TRANSLATION

"This second Personality of Godhead, known as Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu, is the master of each and every universe and the shelter of the external energy. Nonetheless, He remains beyond the touch of the external energy.
