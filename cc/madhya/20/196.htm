Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.196

sańkarshanera mūrti — govinda, vishnu, madhusūdana

e anya govinda — nahe vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

sańkarshanera mūrti — the expansions of Sańkarshana; govinda — Govinda; vishnu — Vishnu; madhusūdana — Madhusūdana; e — this; anya — another; govinda — Govinda; nahe vrajendra-nandana — not the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"The expansions of Sańkarshana are Govinda, Vishnu and Madhusūdana. This Govinda is different from the original Govinda, for He is not the son of Mahārāja Nanda.
