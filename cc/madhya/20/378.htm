Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.378

kiśora-śekhara-dharmī vrajendra-nandana

prakata-līlā karibāre yabe kare mana

SYNONYMS

kiśora-śekhara — topmost of youth; dharmī — whose natural position; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Mahārāja Nanda; prakata-līlā — manifested pastimes; karibāre — to perform; yabe — when; kare — makes; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"As the son of Mahārāja Nanda, Lord Krishna is by nature the paragon of kiśora [youth]. He chooses to exhibit His pastimes at that age.
