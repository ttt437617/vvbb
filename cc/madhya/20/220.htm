Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.220

ińhāra madhye kāro haya 'avatāre' ganana

yaiche vishnu, trivikrama, nrisimha, vāmana

SYNONYMS

ińhāra madhye — of Them; kāro — of some; haya — there is; avatāre — as incarnations; ganana — counting; yaiche — as; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; trivikrama — Lord Trivikrama; nrisimha — Lord Nrisimha; vāmana — Lord Vāmana.

TRANSLATION

"Of these forms, some are considered incarnations. Examples are Lord Vishnu, Lord Trivikrama, Lord Nrisimha and Lord Vāmana.
