Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.75

bhikshā kari' mahāprabhu viśrāma karila

miśra prabhura śesha-pātra sanātane dila

SYNONYMS

bhikshā kari' — after taking His lunch; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; viśrāma karila — took rest; miśra — Tapana Miśra; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha-pātra — the plate of remnants; sanātane dila — delivered to Sanātana.

TRANSLATION

After eating, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took rest for a while. Tapana Miśra then gave Sanātana Gosvāmī the remnants of food left by Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
