Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.134

'uttare' khudile āche krishna 'ajagare'

dhana nāhi pābe, khudite gilibe sabāre

SYNONYMS

uttare — on the northern side; khudile — if you dig; āche — there is; krishna — black; ajagare — snake; dhana — treasure; nāhi — not; pābe — you will get; khudite — digging; gilibe — will devour; sabāre — everyone.

TRANSLATION

"'If you dig on the northern side, there is a big black snake that will devour you if you attempt to dig up the treasure.
