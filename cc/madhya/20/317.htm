Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.317

brahmā, śiva — ājñā-kārī bhakta-avatāra

pālanārthe vishnu — krishnera svarūpa-ākāra

SYNONYMS

brahmā — Lord Brahmā; śiva — Lord Śiva; ājñā-kārī — order-carriers; bhakta-avatāra — incarnations of devotees; pālana-arthe — for maintenance; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa-ākāra — in the form of a personal feature.

TRANSLATION

"The conclusion is that Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva are simply devotee incarnations who carry out orders. However, Lord Vishnu, the maintainer, is the personal feature of Lord Krishna.
