Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.278

ińho mahat-srashtā purusha — 'mahā-vishnu' nāma

ananta brahmānda tāńra loma-kūpe dhāma

SYNONYMS

ińho — He; mahat-srashtā — the creator of the mahat-tattva, or total material energy; purusha — the person; mahā-vishnu nāma — called Lord Mahā-Vishnu; ananta — unlimited; brahmānda — universes; tāńra — of His body; loma-kūpe — within the hair holes; dhāma — are situated.

TRANSLATION

"The first form of Lord Vishnu is called Mahā-Vishnu. He is the original creator of the total material energy. The innumerable universes emanate from the pores of His body.
