Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.186

prābhava-vilāsa — vāsudeva, sańkarshana

pradyumna, aniruddha, — mukhya cāri-jana

SYNONYMS

prābhava-vilāsa — the prābhava-vilāsa forms; vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; sańkarshana — Sańkarshana; pradyumna — Pradyumna; aniruddha — Aniruddha; mukhya cāri-jana — the four chief expansions.

TRANSLATION

"The chief quadruple expansions are named Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana, Pradyumna and Aniruddha. These are called prābhava-vilāsa.
