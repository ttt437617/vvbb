Madhya-līlāChapter 20: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Instructs Sanātana Gosvāmī in the Science of the Absolute Truth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 20.79

mahārāshtrīya dvije prabhu milāilā sanātane

sei vipra tāńre kaila mahā-nimantrane

SYNONYMS

mahārāshtrīya — from Maharashtra; dvije — the brāhmana; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; milāilā — introduced; sanātane — unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; sei — that; vipra — brāhmana; tāńre — unto him; kaila — did; mahā — full; nimantrane — invitation.

TRANSLATION

When Caitanya Mahāprabhu introduced the Maharashtrian brāhmana to Sanātana, the brāhmana immediately invited Sanātana Gosvāmī for full meals.
