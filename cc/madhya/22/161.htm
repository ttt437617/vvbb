Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.161

dāsa-sakhā-pitrādi-preyasīra gana

rāga-mārge nija-nija-bhāvera ganana

SYNONYMS

dāsa — servants; sakhā — friends; pitri-ādi — parents; preyasīra gana — conjugal lovers; rāga-mārge — on the path of spontaneous loving service; nija-nija — of one's own choice; bhāvera — of the ecstasy; ganana — counting.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna has many types of devotees — some are servants, some are friends, some are parents, and some are conjugal lovers. Devotees who are situated in one of these attitudes of spontaneous love according to their choice are considered to be on the path of spontaneous loving service.
