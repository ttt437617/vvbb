Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.164

ei mata kare yebā rāgānugā-bhakti

krishnera carane tāńra upajaya 'prīti'

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; kare — executes; yebā — anyone who; rāgānugā-bhakti — spontaneous devotional service to Krishna; krishnera carane — for the lotus feet of Krishna; tāńra — his; upajaya — awakens; prīti — affection.

TRANSLATION

"If one engages in spontaneous loving service to the Lord, his affection for the lotus feet of Krishna gradually increases.
