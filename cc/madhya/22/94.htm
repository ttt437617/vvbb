Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.94

sarva-dharmān parityajya

mām ekam śaranam vraja

aham tvām sarva-pāpebhyo

mokshayishyāmi mā śucah

SYNONYMS

sarva-dharmān — all kinds of occupational duties; parityajya — giving up; mām ekam — unto Me only; śaranam — as shelter; vraja — go; aham — I; tvām — unto you; sarva-pāpebhyah — from all the reactions of sinful life; mokshayishyāmi — will give liberation; mā — don't; śucah — worry.

TRANSLATION

"'After giving up all kinds of religious and occupational duties, if you come to Me, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, and take shelter, I shall give you protection from all of life's sinful reactions. Do not worry. [Bg. 18.66]'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Bhagavad-gītā (18.66) spoken by Lord Krishna. For an explanation, refer to Madhya-līlā 8.63.
