Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.17

krishna-bhakti haya abhidheya-pradhāna

bhakti-mukha-nirīkshaka karma-yoga-jñāna

SYNONYMS

krishna-bhakti — devotional service to Lord Krishna; haya — is; abhidheya-pradhāna — the chief function of the living entity; bhakti-mukha — of the face of devotional service; nirīkshaka — observers; karma-yoga-jñāna — fruitive activities, mystic yoga and speculative knowledge.

TRANSLATION

"Devotional service to Krishna is the chief function of the living entity. There are different methods for the liberation of the conditioned soul — karma, jñāna, yoga and bhakti — but all are dependent on bhakti.
