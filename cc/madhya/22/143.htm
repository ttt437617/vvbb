Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.143

ajñāne vā haya yadi 'pāpa' upasthita

krishna tāńre śuddha kare, nā karāya prāyaścitta

SYNONYMS

ajñāne — by ignorance; vā — or; haya — there are; yadi — if; pāpa — sinful activities; upasthita — present; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāńre — him (the devotee); śuddha kare — purifies; nā karāya — does not cause; prāyaścitta — atonement.

TRANSLATION

"If, however, a devotee accidentally becomes involved in a sinful activity, Krishna purifies him. He does not have to undergo the regulative form of atonement.

PURPORT

Krishna purifies from within as the caittya-guru, the spiritual master within the heart. This is described in the following verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (11.5.42).
