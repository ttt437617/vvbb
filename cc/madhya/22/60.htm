Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.60

ei ājñā-bale bhaktera 'śraddhā' yadi haya

sarva-karma tyāga kari' se krishna bhajaya

SYNONYMS

ei ājñā-bale — on the strength of this supreme order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; bhaktera — of the devotees; śraddhā — faith; yadi — if; haya — there is; sarva-karma — all other activities, material and spiritual; tyāga kari' — leaving aside; se — he; krishna bhajaya — serves Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"If the devotee has faith in the strength of this order, he worships Lord Krishna and gives up all other activities.
