Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.127

sarvathā śaranāpatti, kārtikādi-vrata

'catuh-shashti ańga' ei parama-mahattva

SYNONYMS

sarvathā — in all respects; śarana-āpatti — surrender; kārtika-ādi-vrata — to observe special vows in the month of Kārttika; catuh-shashti ańga — sixty-four parts; ei — this; parama-mahattva — very important items.

TRANSLATION

"(34) One should surrender to Krishna in all respects. (35) One should observe particular vows like kārtika-vrata. These are some of the sixty-four important items of devotional service.
