Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.96

kah panditas tvad-aparam śaranam samīyād

bhakta-priyād rita-girah suhridah kritajñāt

sarvān dadāti suhrido bhajato 'bhikāmān

ātmānam apy upacayāpacayau na yasya

SYNONYMS

kah — what; panditah — learned man; tvat-aparam — other than Your Lordship; śaranam — shelter; samīyāt — would take; bhakta-priyāt — who are affectionate to Your devotees; rita-girah — who are truthful to the devotees; suhridah — who are the friend of the devotees; krita-jñāt — who are grateful to the devotees; sarvān — all; dadāti — gives; suhridah — to Your well-wishers; bhajatah — who worship You by devotional service; abhikāmān — desires; ātmānam — Yourself; api — even; upacaya — increase; apacayau — and diminution; na — not; yasya — of whom.

TRANSLATION

"'My dear Lord, You are very affectionate to Your devotees. You are also a truthful and grateful friend. Where is that learned man who would give You up and surrender to someone else? You fulfill all the desires of Your devotees, so much so that sometimes You even give Yourself to them. Still, You neither increase nor decrease by such activity.'

PURPORT

This is a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (10.48.26).
