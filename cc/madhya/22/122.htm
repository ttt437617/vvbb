Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.122

agre nritya, gīta, vijñapti, dandavan-nati

abhyutthāna, anuvrajyā, tīrtha-grihe gati

SYNONYMS

agre nritya — dancing before the Deity; gīta — songs; vijñapti — opening the mind; dandavat-nati — offering obeisances; abhyutthāna — stand up; anuvrajyā — following; tīrtha-grihe gati — going to temples and places of pilgrimage.

TRANSLATION

"One should also (10) dance before the Deity, (11) sing before the Deity, (12) open one's mind to the Deity, (13) offer obeisances to the Deity, (14) stand up before the Deity and the spiritual master just to show them respect, (15) follow the Deity or the spiritual master and (16) visit different places of pilgrimage or go see the Deity in the temple.
