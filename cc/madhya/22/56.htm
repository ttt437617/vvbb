Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.56

krishna kripālu arjunere lakshya kariyā

jagatere rākhiyāchena upadeśa diyā

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kripālu — merciful; arjunere — Arjuna; lakshya kariyā — aiming at; jagatere — the whole world; rākhiyāchena — has protected; upadeśa diyā — giving instructions.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is so merciful that simply by aiming His instructions at Arjuna, He has given protection to the whole world.
