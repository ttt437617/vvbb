Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.114

vividhāńga sādhana-bhaktira bahuta vistāra

sańkshepe kahiye kichu sādhanāńga-sāra

SYNONYMS

vividha-ańga — varieties of limbs (regulative principles); sādhana-bhaktira — of regulative devotional service; bahuta — many; vistāra — expansions; sańkshepe — in brief; kahiye — I shall speak; kichu — something; sādhana-ańga-sāra — the essential parts of the practice of devotional service.

TRANSLATION

"I shall say something about the various practices of devotional service, which is expanded in so many ways. I wish to speak briefly of the essential practices.
