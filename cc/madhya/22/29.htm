Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.29

jñānī jīvan-mukta-daśā pāinu kari' māne

vastutah buddhi 'śuddha' nahe krishna-bhakti vine

SYNONYMS

jñānī — the speculative philosophers; jīvan-mukta-daśā — the stage of liberation even while in this body; pāinu — I have gotten; kari' — taking; māne — considers; vastutah — factually; buddhi — intelligence; śuddha — purified; nahe — not; krishna-bhakti vine — without devotional service to Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"There are many philosophical speculators [jñānīs] belonging to the Māyāvāda school who consider themselves liberated and call themselves Nārāyana. But their intelligence is not purified unless they engage in Krishna's devotional service.
