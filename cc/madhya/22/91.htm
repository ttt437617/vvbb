Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.91

varam huta-vaha-jvālā-

pañjarāntar-vyavasthitih

na śauri-cintā-vimukha-

jana-samvāsa-vaiśasam

SYNONYMS

varam — better; huta-vaha — of fire; jvālā — in the flames; pañjara-antah — inside a cage; vyavasthitih — abiding; na — not; śauri-cintā — of Krishna consciousness, or thought of Krishna; vimukha — bereft; jana — of persons; samvāsa — of the association; vaiśasam — the calamity.

TRANSLATION

"'It is better to accept the miseries of being encaged within bars and surrounded by burning flames than to associate with those bereft of Krishna consciousness. Such association is a very great hardship.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Kātyāyana-samhitā.
