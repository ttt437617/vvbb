Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.107

nitya-siddha krishna-prema 'sādhya' kabhu naya

śravanādi-śuddha-citte karaye udaya

SYNONYMS

nitya-siddha — eternally established; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; sādhya — to be gained; kabhu — at any time; naya — not; śravana-ādi — by hearing, etc.; śuddha — purified; citte — in the heart; karaye udaya — awakens.

TRANSLATION

"Pure love for Krishna is eternally established in the hearts of the living entities. It is not something to be gained from another source. When the heart is purified by hearing and chanting, this love naturally awakens.
