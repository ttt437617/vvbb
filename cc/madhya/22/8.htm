Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.8

svāmśa-vibhinnāmśa-rūpe hañā vistāra

ananta vaikuntha-brahmānde karena vihāra

SYNONYMS

sva-amśa — of personal expansions; vibhinna-amśa — of separated expansions; rūpe — in the forms; hañā — becoming; vistāra — expanded; ananta — unlimited; vaikuntha — in the spiritual planets known as Vaikunthas; brahmānde — in the material universes; karena vihāra — performs His pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna expands Himself in many forms. Some of them are personal expansions, and some are separate expansions. Thus He performs pastimes in both the spiritual and the material worlds. The spiritual worlds are the Vaikuntha planets, and the material universes are the brahmāndas, gigantic globes governed by Lord Brahmā.
