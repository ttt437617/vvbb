Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.11

'nitya-mukta' — nitya krishna-carane unmukha

'krishna-pārishada' nāma, bhuñje sevā-sukha

SYNONYMS

nitya-mukta — eternally liberated; nitya — always; krishna-carane — the lotus feet of Lord Krishna; unmukha — turned toward; krishna-pārishada — associates of Lord Krishna; nāma — known as; bhuñje — enjoy; sevā-sukha — the happiness of service.

TRANSLATION

"Those who are eternally liberated are always awake to Krishna consciousness, and they render transcendental loving service at the feet of Lord Krishna. They are to be considered eternal associates of Krishna, and they are eternally enjoying the transcendental bliss of serving Krishna.
