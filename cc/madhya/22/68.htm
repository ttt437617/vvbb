Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.68

yah śāstrādishv anipunah

śraddhāvān sa tu madhyamah

SYNONYMS

yah — anyone who; śāstra-ādishu — in the revealed scriptures; anipunah — not very expert; śraddhāvān — full of faith; sah — he; tu — certainly; madhyamah — second-class or middle-class devotee.

TRANSLATION

"'He who does not know scriptural argument very well but who has firm faith is called an intermediate or second-class devotee.'

PURPORT

This verse appears in the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (1.2.18).
