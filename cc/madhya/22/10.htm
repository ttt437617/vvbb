Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.10

sei vibhinnāmśa jīva — dui ta' prakāra

eka — 'nitya-mukta', eka — 'nitya-samsāra'

SYNONYMS

sei vibhinna-amśa — that separated part and parcel of Krishna; jīva — the living entity; dui ta' prakāra — two categories; eka — one; nitya-mukta — eternally liberated; eka — one; nitya-samsāra — perpetually conditioned.

TRANSLATION

"The living entities [jīvas] are divided into two categories. Some are eternally liberated, and others are eternally conditioned.
