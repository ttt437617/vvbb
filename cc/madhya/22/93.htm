Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.93

eta saba chādi' āra varnāśrama-dharma

akiñcana hañā laya krishnaika-śarana

SYNONYMS

eta saba — all these; chādi' — giving up; āra — and; varna-āśrama-dharma — the regulative principle of four varnas and four āśramas; akiñcana — without any attachment for anything material; hañā — becoming; laya — he takes; krishna-eka-śarana — exclusive shelter at the lotus feet of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

"Without hesitation, one should take exclusive shelter of Lord Krishna with full confidence, giving up bad association and even neglecting the regulative principles of the four varnas and four āśramas. That is to say, one should abandon all material attachment.
