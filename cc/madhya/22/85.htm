Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.85

ata ātyantikam kshemam

pricchāmo bhavato 'naghāh

samsāre 'smin kshanārdho 'pi

sat-sańgah sevadhir nrinām

SYNONYMS

atah — therefore (due to the rarity of seeing pure devotees of the Lord); ātyantikam — supreme; kshemam — auspiciousness; pricchāmah — we are asking; bhavatah — you; anaghāh — O sinless ones; samsāre — in the material world; asmin — this; kshana-ardhah — lasting half a moment; api — even; sat-sańgah — association with devotees; sevadhih — a treasure; nrinām — for human society.

TRANSLATION

"'O devotees! O you who are free from all sins! Let me inquire from you about that which is supremely auspicious for all living entities. Association with a pure devotee for even half a moment in this material world is the greatest treasure for human society.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (11.2.30).
