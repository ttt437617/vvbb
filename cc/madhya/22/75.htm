Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.75

sarva mahā-guna-gana vaishnava-śarīre

krishna-bhakte krishnera guna sakali sañcāre

SYNONYMS

sarva — all; mahā — great; guna-gana — transcendental qualities; vaishnava-śarīre — in the bodies of Vaishnavas; krishna-bhakte — in the devotees of Lord Krishna; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; guna — the qualities; sakali — all; sañcāre — appear.

TRANSLATION

"A Vaishnava is one who has developed all good transcendental qualities. All the good qualities of Krishna gradually develop in Krishna's devotee.
