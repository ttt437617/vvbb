Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.66

śāstre yuktau ca nipunah

sarvathā dridha-niścayah

praudha-śraddho 'dhikārī yah

sa bhaktāv uttamo matah

SYNONYMS

śāstre — in the revealed scriptures; yuktau — in logic; ca — also; nipunah — expert; sarvathā — in all respects; dridha-niścayah — who is firmly convinced; praudha — deep; śraddhah — who has faith; adhikārī — eligible; yah — who; sah — he; bhaktau — in devotional service; uttamah — highest; matah — is considered.

TRANSLATION

"'One who is expert in logic and in understanding the revealed scriptures, and who always has firm conviction and deep faith that is not blind, is to be considered a topmost devotee in devotional service.'

PURPORT

This verse appears in the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (1.2.17), by Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
