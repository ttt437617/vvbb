Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.77

sei saba guna haya vaishnava-lakshana

saba kahā nā yāya, kari dig-daraśana

SYNONYMS

sei saba guna — all those transcendental qualities; haya — are; vaishnava-lakshana — the symptoms of a Vaishnava; saba — all; kahā nā yāya — cannot be explained; kari — I shall do; dik-daraśana — a general review.

TRANSLATION

"All these transcendental qualities are the characteristics of pure Vaishnavas, and they cannot be fully explained, but I shall try to point out some of the important qualities.
