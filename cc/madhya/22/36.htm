Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.36

akāmah sarva-kāmo vā

moksha-kāma udāra-dhīh

tīvrena bhakti-yogena

yajeta purusham param

SYNONYMS

akāmah — a pure devotee with no desire for material enjoyment; sarva-kāmah — one who has no end to his desires for material enjoyment; vā — or; moksha-kāmah — one who desires to merge into the existence of Brahman; udāra-dhīh — being very intelligent; tīvrena — firm; bhakti-yogena — by devotional service; yajeta — should worship; purusham — the person; param — supreme.

TRANSLATION

"'Whether one desires everything or nothing, or whether he desires to merge into the existence of the Lord, he is intelligent only if he worships Lord Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, by rendering transcendental loving service.'

PURPORT

This is a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (2.3.10).
