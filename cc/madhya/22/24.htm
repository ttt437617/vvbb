Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.24

'krishna-nitya-dāsa' — jīva tāhā bhuli' gela

ei doshe māyā tāra galāya bāndhila

SYNONYMS

krishna-nitya-dāsa — eternal servant of Krishna; jīva — the living entity; tāhā — that; bhuli' — forgetting; gela — went; ei doshe — for this fault; māyā — the material energy; tāra — his; galāya — on the neck; bāndhila — has bound.

TRANSLATION

"The living entity is bound around the neck by the chain of māyā because he has forgotten that he is eternally a servant of Krishna.
