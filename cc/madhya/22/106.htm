Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.106

śravanādi-kriyā — tāra 'svarūpa'-lakshana

'tatastha'-lakshane upajāya prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

śravana-ādi-kriyā — the process of hearing, chanting and so forth; tāra — of that; svarūpa-lakshana — symptoms of the nature; tatastha-lakshane — marginal symptoms; upajāya — awakens; prema-dhana — love of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"The spiritual activities of hearing, chanting, remembering and so forth are the natural characteristics of devotional service. The marginal characteristic is that it awakens pure love for Krishna.
