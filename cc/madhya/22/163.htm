Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.163

pati-putra-suhrid-bhrātri-

pitrivan mitravad dharim

ye dhyāyanti sadodyuktās

tebhyo 'pīha namo namah

SYNONYMS

pati — a husband; putra — a son; suhrit — a friend; bhrātri — a brother; pitri — a father; vat — like; mitra — an intimate friend; vat — like; harim — on the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ye — all those who; dhyāyanti — meditate; sadā — always; udyuktāh — full of eagerness; tebhyah — unto them; api — also; iha — here; namah namah — repeated respectful obeisances.

TRANSLATION

"'Let me offer my respectful obeisances again and again to those who always eagerly meditate upon the Supreme Personality of Godhead as a husband, son, friend, brother, father or intimate friend.'

PURPORT

This verse appears in the Bhakti-rasāmrita-sindhu (1.2.308).
