Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.38

krishna kahe, — 'āmā bhaje, māge vishaya-sukha

amrita chādi' visha māge, — ei bada mūrkha

SYNONYMS

krishna kahe — Krishna says; āmā bhaje — he worships Me; māge — but requests; vishaya-sukha — material happiness; amrita chādi' — giving up the nectar; visha māge — he begs for poison; ei bada mūrkha — he is a great fool.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna says, 'If one engages in My transcendental loving service but at the same time wants the opulence of material enjoyment, he is very, very foolish. Indeed, he is just like a person who gives up ambrosia to drink poison.
