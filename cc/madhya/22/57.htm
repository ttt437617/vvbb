Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.57-58

sarva-guhyatamam bhūyah

śrinu me paramam vacah

ishto 'si me dridham iti

tato vakshyāmi te hitam

man-manā bhava mad-bhakto

mad-yājī mām namaskuru

mām evaishyasi satyam te

pratijāne priyo 'si me

SYNONYMS

sarva-guhya-tamam — most confidential of all; bhūyah — again; śrinu — hear; me — My; paramam vacah — supreme instruction; ishtah — beloved; asi — you are; me — My; dridham iti — very firmly; tatah — therefore; vakshyāmi — I shall speak; te — to you; hitam — words of benediction; mat-manāh — whose mind is always on Me; bhava — become; mat-bhaktah — My devotee; mat-yājī — My worshiper; mām — unto Me; namaskuru — offer obeisances; mām eva — to Me only; eshyasi — you will come; satyam — truly; te — to you; pratijāne — I promise; priyah asi — you are dear; me — My.

TRANSLATION

"'Because you are My very dear friend, I am speaking to you My supreme instruction, the most confidential knowledge of all. Hear this from Me, for it is for your benefit. Always think of Me and become My devotee, worship Me and offer your homage unto Me. Thus you will come to Me without fail. I promise you this because you are My very dear friend.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Bhagavad-gītā (18.64-65).
