Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.13

sei doshe māyā-piśācī danda kare tāre

ādhyātmikādi tāpa-traya tāre jāri' māre

SYNONYMS

sei doshe — because of this fault; māyā-piśācī — the witch known as the external energy; danda kare — gives punishment; tāre — unto him; ādhyātmika-ādi — beginning with those pertaining to the body and mind; tāpa-traya — the threefold miseries; tāre — him; jāri' — burning; māre — gives pain.

TRANSLATION

"Due to his being opposed to Krishna consciousness, the conditioned soul is punished by the witch of the external energy, māyā. He is thus ready to suffer the threefold miseries — miseries brought about by the body and mind, the inimical behavior of other living entities and natural disturbances caused by the demigods.
