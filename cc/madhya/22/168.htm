Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.168

abhidheya sādhana-bhakti śune yei jana

acirāt pāya sei krishna-prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

abhidheya — necessary duty; sādhana-bhakti — devotional service in practice; śune — hears; yei jana — anyone who; acirāt — very soon; pāya — gets; sei — that person; krishna-prema-dhana — the treasure of love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Whoever hears this description of the process of practical devotional service very soon attains shelter at the lotus feet of Krishna in love and affection.
