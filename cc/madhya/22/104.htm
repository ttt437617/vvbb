Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.104

ebe sādhana-bhakti-lakshana śuna, sanātana

yāhā haite pāi krishna-prema-mahā-dhana

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; sādhana-bhakti — regulative principles for executing devotional service; lakshana — the symptoms; śuna — please hear; sanātana — My dear Sanātana; yāhā haite — from which; pāi — one can get; krishna-prema-mahā-dhana — the most valuable treasure of love for Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sanātana, please now hear about the regulative principles for the execution of devotional service. By this process, one can attain the highest perfection of love of Godhead, which is the most desirable treasure.
