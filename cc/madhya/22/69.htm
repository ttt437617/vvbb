Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.69

yāhāra komala śraddhā, se 'kanishtha' jana

krame krame teńho bhakta ha-ibe 'uttama'

SYNONYMS

yāhāra — whose; komala śraddhā — soft faith; se — such a person; kanishtha jana — a neophyte devotee; krame krame — by a gradual progression; teńho — he; bhakta — devotee; ha-ibe — will become; uttama — first class.

TRANSLATION

"One whose faith is soft and pliable is called a neophyte, but by gradually following the process he will rise to the platform of a first-class devotee.
