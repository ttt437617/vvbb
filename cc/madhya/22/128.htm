Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.128

sādhu-sańga, nāma-kīrtana, bhāgavata-śravana

mathurā-vāsa, śrī-mūrtira śraddhāya sevana

SYNONYMS

sādhu-sańga — association with devotees; nāma-kīrtana — chanting the holy name; bhāgavata-śravana — hearing Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; mathurā-vāsa — living at Mathurā; śrī-mūrtira śraddhāya sevana — worshiping the Deity with faith and veneration.

TRANSLATION

"One should associate with devotees, chant the holy name of the Lord, hear Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, reside at Mathurā and worship the Deity with faith and veneration.
