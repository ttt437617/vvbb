Madhya-līlāChapter 22: The Process of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 22.33

'krishna, tomāra hańa' yadi bale eka-bāra

māyā-bandha haite krishna tāre kare pāra

SYNONYMS

krishna — O my Lord Krishna; tomāra hańa — I am Yours; yadi — if; bale — someone says; eka-bāra — once; māyā-bandha haite — from the bondage of conditioned life; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāre — him; kare pāra — releases.

TRANSLATION

"One is immediately freed from the clutches of māyā if he seriously and sincerely says, 'My dear Lord Krishna, although I have forgotten You for so many long years in the material world, today I am surrendering unto You. I am Your sincere and serious servant. Please engage me in Your service.'
