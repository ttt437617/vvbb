Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.141

svarūpera ucca-gāna prabhure sadā bhāya

ānande uddanda nritya kare gaurarāya

SYNONYMS

svarūpera — of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; ucca-gāna — loud singing; prabhure — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sadā bhāya — always very pleasing; ānande — in jubilation; uddanda nritya — jumping high and dancing; kare — performs; gaurarāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always liked the loud chanting of Svarūpa Dāmodara. Therefore when Svarūpa Dāmodara sang, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced and jumped high in jubilation.
