Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.58

sundara, rājāra putra — śyāmala-varana

kiśora vayasa, dīrgha kamala-nayana

SYNONYMS

sundara — beautiful; rājāra putra — the son of the King; śyāmala-varana — blackish complexion; kiśora vayasa — the age just before youth; dīrgha — long; kamala-nayana — lotus eyes.

TRANSLATION

The prince, just entering upon his youth, was very beautiful. He was blackish in complexion and had large lotus eyes.
