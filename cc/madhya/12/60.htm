Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.60

tāńre dekhi, mahāprabhura krishna-smriti haila

premāveśe tāńre mili' kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

tāńre dekhi — seeing him; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-smriti — remembrance of Krishna; haila — there was; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; tāńre — him; mili' — meeting; kahite lāgila — began to say.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the boy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately remembered Krishna. Meeting the boy in ecstatic love, the Lord began to speak.
