Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.144

premāveśe nritya kari' ha-ilā mūrcchite

acetana hañā teńha padilā bhūmite

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; nritya kari' — dancing; ha-ilā mūrcchite — fainted; acetana hañā — being unconscious; teńha — he; padilā — fell; bhūmite — on the ground.

TRANSLATION

While dancing in ecstatic love, Śrī Gopāla fainted and fell to the ground unconscious.
