Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.148

tabe mahāprabhu tāńra buke hasta dila

'uthaha gopāla' bali' uccaihsvare kahila

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra buke — on his chest; hasta — hand; dila — placed; uthaha gopāla — stand up, Gopāla; bali' — saying; uccaih-svare — very loudly; kahila — said.

TRANSLATION

Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu placed His hand on the chest of Śrī Gopāla and said loudly, "Gopāla, stand up."
