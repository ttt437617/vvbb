Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.103

ghara dhui' pranālikāya jala chādi' dila

sei jale prāńgana saba bhariyā rahila

SYNONYMS

ghara dhui' — washing the room; pranālikāya — unto the outlet; jala — water; chādi' dila — let go; sei jale — by that water; prāńgana — the yard; saba — all; bhariyā — filled; rahila — remained.

TRANSLATION

After the room was washed, the water was let out through an outlet, and it then flowed and filled the yard outside.
