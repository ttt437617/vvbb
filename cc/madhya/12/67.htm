Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.67

putre ālińgana kari' premāvishta hailā

sākshāt paraśa yena mahāprabhura pāilā

SYNONYMS

putre — his son; ālińgana — embracing; kari' — doing; prema-āvishta hailā — he became ecstatic; sākshāt — directly; paraśa — touch; yena — as if; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāilā — he got.

TRANSLATION

Just by embracing his son, the King was filled with ecstatic love, just as if he had touched Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu directly.
