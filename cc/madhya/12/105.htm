Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.105

śata ghata jale haila mandira mārjana

mandira śodhiyā kaila — yena nija mana

SYNONYMS

śata — one hundred; ghata — of waterpots; jale — by the water; haila — became; mandira — the temple; mārjana — cleansed; mandira — the temple; śodhiyā — cleansing; kaila — did; yena — as if; nija mana — his own mind.

TRANSLATION

In this way all the rooms were cleansed with a hundred waterpots. After the rooms had been cleansed, the minds of the devotees were as clean as the rooms.
