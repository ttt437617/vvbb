Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.43

prabhu-pade prema-bhakti jānāila rājāra

prasańga pāñā aiche kahe bāra-bāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu-pade — unto the lotus feet of the Lord; prema-bhakti — ecstatic love; jānāila — informed; rājāra — of the King; prasańga — discussion; pāñā — getting; aiche — thus; kahe — says; bāra-bāra — again and again.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Rāmānanda Rāya informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about the ecstatic love of the King. Indeed, as soon as there was some opportunity, he repeatedly informed the Lord about the King.
