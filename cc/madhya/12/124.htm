Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.124

yadyapi gosāñi tāre hañāche santosha

dharma-samsthāpana lāgi' bāhire mahā-rosha

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; gosāñi — the Lord; tāre — with him; hañāche — became; santosha — satisfied; dharma-samsthāpana lāgi' — for establishing the etiquette of religious principles; bāhire — externally; mahā-rosha — very angry.

TRANSLATION

Although the Lord was certainly satisfied with him, He became angry externally in order to establish the etiquette of religious principles.
