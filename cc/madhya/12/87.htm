Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.87

bhoga-mandira śodhana kari' śodhila prāńgana

sakala āvāsa krame karila śodhana

SYNONYMS

bhoga-mandira — the place where food is placed; śodhana kari' — cleansing; śodhila prāńgana — cleansed the yard; sakala — all; āvāsa — residential places; krame — one after another; karila śodhana — cleansed.

TRANSLATION

After this, the place where the Deity's food was kept [bhoga-mandira] was cleansed. Then the yard was cleansed, and then all the residential quarters, one after the other.
