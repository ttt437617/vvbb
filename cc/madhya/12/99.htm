Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.99

śrī-haste karena simhāsanera mārjana

prabhu āge jala āni' deya bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

śrī-haste — with His own hand; karena — does; simhāsanera mārjana — washing of the sitting place of the Lord; prabhu āge — before the Lord; jala — water; āni' — bringing; deya — deliver; bhakta-gana — all devotees.

TRANSLATION

Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to wash the sitting place of Lord Jagannātha with His own hands, and all the devotees began to bring water to the Lord.
