Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.78

nūtana eka-śata ghata, śata sammārjanī

padichā āniyā dila prabhura icchā jāni'

SYNONYMS

nūtana — new; eka-śata — one hundred; ghata — waterpots; śata — hundred; sammārjanī — brooms; padichā — the superintendent; āniyā — bringing; dila — delivered; prabhura — of the Lord; icchā — the desire; jāni' — knowing.

TRANSLATION

As soon as the superintendent understood the desire of the Lord, he immediately delivered a hundred new waterpots and a hundred brooms for sweeping the temple.
