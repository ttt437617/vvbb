Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.91

sabāra jhyāńtāna bojhā ekatra karila

sabā haite prabhura bojhā adhika ha-ila

SYNONYMS

sabāra — of all; jhyāńtāna — the dirt collected; bojhā — load; ekatra — combined in one place; karila — made; sabā haite — than all of them; prabhura bojhā — the pile of dirt collected by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; adhika ha-ila — was greater.

TRANSLATION

Even though all the devotees collected dirt in one pile, the dirt collected by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was much greater.
