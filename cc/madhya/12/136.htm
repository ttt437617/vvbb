Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.136

nrisimha-mandira-bhitara-bāhira śodhila

kshaneka viśrāma kari' nritya ārambhila

SYNONYMS

nrisimha-mandira — the temple of Nrisimhadeva; bhitara — inside; bāhira — outside; śodhila — cleansed; kshaneka — for a few moments; viśrāma — rest; kari' — after taking; nritya — dancing; ārambhila — began.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu also cleansed the Nrisimha temple inside and outside. Finally, He rested a few minutes and then began dancing.

PURPORT

The Nrisimha temple is a nice temple just outside the Gundicā temple. In this temple there is a great festival on the day of Nrisimha-caturdaśī. There is also a Nrisimha temple at Navadvīpa where the same festival is observed, as described by Murāri Gupta in his book Caitanya-carita.
