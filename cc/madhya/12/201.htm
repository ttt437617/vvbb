Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.201

prabhura avaśesha govinda rākhila dhariyā

sei anna haridāse kichu dila lañā

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avaśesha — remnants; govinda — Govinda; rākhila — saved; dhariyā — keeping; sei anna — that prasādam; haridāse — unto Haridāsa Thākura; kichu — some; dila — delivered; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

Govinda saved some remnants of food left by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and kept them carefully. Later, one portion of these remnants was delivered to Haridāsa Thākura.
