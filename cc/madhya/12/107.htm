Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.107

śata śata jana jala bhare sarovare

ghāte sthāna nāhi, keha kūpe jala bhare

SYNONYMS

śata śata jana — hundreds of men; jala bhare — draw water; sarovare — from the lake; ghāte — on the bank; sthāna — place; nāhi — there is not; keha — someone; kūpe — from the well; jala bhare — draws the water.

TRANSLATION

Since hundreds of men were engaged in bringing water from the lake, there was no place to stand on the banks. Consequently someone began to draw water from a well.
