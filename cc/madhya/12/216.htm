Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.216

ei-mata mahāprabhu lañā bhakta-gana

madhyāhna paryanta kaila śrī-mukha daraśana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; bhakta-gana — His associates; madhyāhna paryanta — up to midday; kaila — performs; śrī-mukha daraśana — seeing the face of Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees enjoyed transcendental bliss upon seeing the face of Jagannātha. This continued to midday.
