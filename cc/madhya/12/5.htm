Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.5

kataka haite patrī dila sārvabhauma-thāñi

prabhura ājñā haya yadi, dekhibāre yāi

SYNONYMS

kataka haite — from Kataka, the capital of Orissa; patrī — a letter; dila — sent; sārvabhauma — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; thāñi — to the place; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā — order; haya — there is; yadi — if; dekhibāre yāi — I can go and see.

TRANSLATION

The King sent a letter from his capital, Kataka, to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, entreating him to obtain the Lord's permission so that he could go and see Him.
