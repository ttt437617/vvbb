Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.162

pāche more prasāda govinda dibe bahirdvāre

mana jāni' prabhu punah nā balila tāńre

SYNONYMS

pāche — at last; more — unto me; prasāda — remnants of food; govinda — the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dibe — will deliver; bahir-dvāre — outside the door; mana jāni' — understanding the mind; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; punah — again; nā — not; balila — called; tāńre — him.

TRANSLATION

"Govinda will give me prasādam later, outside the door." Understanding his mind, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not call him again.
