Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.146

nrisimhera mantra padi' māre jala-chāńti

huńkārera śabde brahmānda yāya phāti'

SYNONYMS

nrisimhera mantra — prayers to Nrisimhadeva; padi' — chanting; māre — throws; jala-chāńti — sprinkling of water; huńkārera śabde — by the sound of roaring; brahmānda — the whole universe; yāya — becomes; phāti' — cracking.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya and others began to chant the holy name of Lord Nrisimha and sprinkle water. The roaring of the chant was so great that it seemed to shake the entire universe.
