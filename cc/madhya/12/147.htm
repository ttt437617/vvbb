Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.147

aneka karila, tabu nā haya cetana

ācārya kāndena, kānde saba bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

aneka karila — much endeavor was done; tabu — still; nā haya — there was not; cetana — consciousness; ācārya kāndena — Advaita Ācārya began to cry; kānde — cried; saba bhakta-gana — all the other devotees.

TRANSLATION

When the boy did not regain consciousness after some time, Advaita Ācārya and the other devotees began to cry.
