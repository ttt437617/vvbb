Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.219

darśana-ānande prabhu saba pāsarilā

bhakta-gana madhyāhna karite prabhure lañā gelā

SYNONYMS

darśana-ānande — because of pleasure due to seeing the face of the Lord; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; saba — everything; pāsarilā — forgot; bhakta-gana — the devotees; madhyāhna — noontime lunch; karite — to accept; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā gelā — took.

TRANSLATION

Feeling such great pleasure upon seeing the face of Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu forgot everything. The devotees, however, took Him to His lunch at noontime.
