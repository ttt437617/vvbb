Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.173

svarūpa-gosāñi bhāla mishta-prasāda lañā

prabhuke nivedana kare āge dāndāñā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara; bhāla — first-class; mishta-prasāda — sweetmeats; lañā — taking; prabhuke — unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nivedana kare — offered; āge — in front of Him; dāndāñā — standing.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī then brought some excellent sweetmeats and, standing before the Lord, offered them to Him.
