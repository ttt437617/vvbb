Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.83

chota-bada-mandira kaila mārjana-śodhana

pāche taiche śodhila śrī-jagamohana

SYNONYMS

chota-bada-mandira — all the small and big temples; kaila — did; mārjana-śodhana — proper cleansing; pāche — thereafter; taiche — in the similar way; śodhila — cleansed; śrī-jagamohana — the place between the original temple and the kīrtana hall.

TRANSLATION

Thus the Lord and His companions cleansed and swept all the temple's buildings, big and small, and finally cleansed the area between the temple and the kīrtana hall.
