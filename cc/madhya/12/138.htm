Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.138

sveda, kampa, vaivarnyāśru pulaka, huńkāra

nija-ańga dhui' āge cale aśru-dhāra

SYNONYMS

sveda — perspiration; kampa — trembling; vaivarnya — fading; aśru — tears; pulaka — jubilation; huńkāra — roaring; nija-ańga — personal body; dhui' — washing; āge — forward; cale — goes; aśru-dhāra — a flow of tears.

TRANSLATION

As usual, when Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced, there were perspiration, trembling, fading, tears, jubilation and roaring. Indeed, the tears from His eyes washed His body and those before Him.
