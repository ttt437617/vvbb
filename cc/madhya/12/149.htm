Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.149

śunitei gopālera ha-ila cetana

'hari' bali' nritya kare sarva-bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

śunitei — upon hearing; gopālera — of Śrī Gopāla; ha-ila — there was; cetana — consciousness; hari bali' — chanting the holy name of Hari; nritya kare — danced; sarva-bhakta-gana — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Gopāla heard the voice of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he immediately came to his senses. All the devotees then began to dance, chanting the holy name of Hari.
