Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.21

dekhiba se mukha-candra nayana bhariyā

dhariba se pāda-padma hridaye tuliyā

SYNONYMS

dekhiba — I shall see; se — that; mukha-candra — moonlike face; nayana bhariyā — to the fulfillment of the eyes; dhariba — I shall catch; se — those; pāda-padma — lotus feet; hridaye — on my heart; tuliyā — raising.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu continued, "The King also expressed his desire to see the moonlike face of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his eyes' full satisfaction. He would like to raise the lotus feet of the Lord to his heart."
