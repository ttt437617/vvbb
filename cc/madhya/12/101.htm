Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.101

keha jala āni' deya mahāprabhura kare

keha jala deya tāńra carana-upare

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; jala āni' — bringing water; deya — delivers; mahāprabhura kare — to the hand of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; keha — someone; jala deya — pours water; tāńra — His; carana-upare — on the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Someone brought water to pour into the hands of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and someone poured water on His lotus feet.
