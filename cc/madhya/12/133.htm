Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.133

ei mata saba purī karila śodhana

śītala, nirmala kaila — yena nija-mana

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; saba purī — all of Gundicā Purī; karila śodhana — they cleansed; śītala — cool; nirmala — clean; kaila — made; yena — as; nija-mana — his own mind.

TRANSLATION

In this way all the quarters of the Gundicā temple were completely cleansed and cleared. All quarters were cool and spotless, like one's cleansed and pacified mind.
