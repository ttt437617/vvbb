Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.72

prathamei kāśī-miśre prabhu bolāila

padichā-pātra, sārvabhaume bolāñā ānila

SYNONYMS

prathamei — in the beginning; kāśī-miśre — Kāśī Miśra; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bolāila — called for; padichā-pātra — the superintendent of the temple; sārvabhaume — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; bolāñā — calling; ānila — brought.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu first of all called for Kāśī Miśra, then for the superintendent of the temple, then for Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
