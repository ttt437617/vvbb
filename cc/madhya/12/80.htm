Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.80

śrī-haste dila sabāre eka eka mārjanī

saba-gana lañā prabhu calilā āpani

SYNONYMS

śrī-haste — by His own hand; dila — delivered; sabāre — unto every one of them; eka eka — one by one; mārjanī — a broom; saba-gana — all the associates; lañā — taking; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calilā — went; āpani — personally.

TRANSLATION

He then gave each devotee a broom with His own hand, and taking all of them personally with Him, the Lord went to Gundicā.
