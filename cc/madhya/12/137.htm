Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.137

cāri-dike bhakta-gana karena kīrtana

madhye nritya karena prabhu matta-simha-sama

SYNONYMS

cāri-dike — all around; bhakta-gana — devotees; karena — performed; kīrtana — congregational chanting; madhye — in the middle; nritya — dancing; karena — does; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; matta-simha-sama — just like a maddened lion.

TRANSLATION

All around Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu all the devotees performed congregational chanting. The Lord, just like a maddened lion, danced in the middle.
