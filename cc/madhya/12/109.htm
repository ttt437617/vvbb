Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.109

nityānanda, advaita, svarūpa, bhāratī, purī

ińhā vinu āra saba āne jala bhari'

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; advaita — Advaita Ācārya; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; bhāratī — Brahmānanda Bhāratī; purī — Paramānanda Purī; ińha — these; vinu — except; āra — others; saba — all; āne — bring; jala — water; bhari' — filling up.

TRANSLATION

With the exception of Nityānanda Prabhu, Advaita Ācārya, Svarūpa Dāmodara, Brahmānanda Bhāratī and Paramānanda Purī, everyone was engaged in filling the waterpots and bringing them there.
