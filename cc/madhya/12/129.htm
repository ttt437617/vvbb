Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.129

punah āsi' prabhu pāya karila vinaya

'ajña-aparādha' kshamā karite yuyāya

SYNONYMS

punah āsi' — again coming back; prabhu pāya — at the lotus feet of the Lord; karila vinaya — made a submission; ajña-aparādha — offense by innocent person; kshamā karite — to be excused; yuyāya — deserves.

TRANSLATION

After Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī returned within the temple, he requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to excuse that innocent person.
