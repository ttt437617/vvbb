Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.39

rāmānanda rāya yabe 'dakshina' haite āilā

prabhu-sańge rahite rājāke nivedilā

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; yabe — when; dakshina — South India; haite — from; āilā — returned; prabhu-sańge — with Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahite — to stay; rājāke — unto the King; nivedilā — requested.

TRANSLATION

After returning from his service in South India, Rāmānanda Rāya requested the King to allow him to remain with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
