Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.98

khāparā bhariyā jala ūrdhve cālāila

sei jale ūrdhva śodhi bhitti prakshālila

SYNONYMS

khāparā — basin; bhariyā — filling; jala — water; ūrdhve — on the ceiling; cālāila — began to throw; sei jale — with that water; ūrdhva śodhi — washing the ceiling; bhitti — walls and floor; prakshālila — washed.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself and His devotees began to throw water onto the ceiling. When this water fell, it washed the walls and floor.
