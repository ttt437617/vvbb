Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.185

prabhu kahe, — pūrve siddha krishne tomāra prīti

tomā-sańge āmā-sabāra haila krishne mati

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; pūrve — previously; siddha — perfected; krishne — in Krishna consciousness; tomāra — your; prīti — love for Krishna; tomā-sańge — by your association; āmā-sabāra — of all of us; haila — there was; krishne — unto Krishna; mati — consciousness.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, "From your previous birth you have been in Krishna consciousness. Thus you love Krishna so much that simply by your association we are all developing Krishna consciousness."
