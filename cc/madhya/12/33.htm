Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.33

eka yukti āche, yadi kara avadhāna

tumi nā milileha tāńre, rahe tāńra prāna

SYNONYMS

eka yukti — one plan; āche — there is; yadi — if; kara avadhāna — You consider it; tumi — You; nā milileha — may not meet; tāńre — with him; rahe — remains; tāńra — his; prāna — life.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu then submitted one suggestion for the Lord's consideration. "There is a way," He suggested, "by which You need not meet the King but which would enable the King to continue living.
