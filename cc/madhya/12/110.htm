Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.110

ghate ghate theki' kata ghata bhāńgi' gela

śata śata ghata loka tāhāń lañā āila

SYNONYMS

ghate ghate theki' — when there was a collision between one pot and another; kata — so many; ghata — pots; bhāńgi' gelā — became broken; śata śata — hundreds of; ghata — pots; loka — people; tāhāń — there; lañā — bringing; āila — came.

TRANSLATION

Many of the waterpots were broken when people collided with one another, and hundreds of men had to bring new waterpots to fill.
