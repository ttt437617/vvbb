Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.118

e-kathā śuniyā sabe sańkucita hañā

bhāla-mate karma kare sabe mana diyā

SYNONYMS

e-kathā śuniyā — hearing these words; sabe — all; sańkucita hañā — being ashamed; bhāla-mate — very well; karma kare — do work; sabe — all; mana diyā — with attention.

TRANSLATION

As soon as they heard Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu say this, everyone became ashamed. Thus the devotees began to work with great attention.
