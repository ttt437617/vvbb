Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.197

tabe prabhu sarva-vaishnavera nāma lañā

mahā-prasāda dena mahā-amrita siñciyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sarva-vaishnavera — of all the Vaishnavas; nāma — names; lañā — calling; mahā-prasāda — the remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; dena — delivers; mahā-amrita — transcendental nectar; siñciyā — sprinkling.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, calling all the Vaishnavas, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu distributed mahā-prasādam as if sprinkling nectar. At that time the mock fight between Advaita Ācārya and Nityānanda Prabhu became more and more delicious.
