Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.115

śata-haste karena yena kshālana-mārjana

pratijana-pāśe yāi' karāna śikshana

SYNONYMS

śata-haste — with one hundred hands; karena — He does; yena — as if; kshālana-mārjana — rinsing and washing; pratijana-pāśe yāi' — going to the side of everyone; karāna śikshana — He was teaching them.

TRANSLATION

It appeared as though Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were cleansing and washing with a hundred hands. He approached every devotee just to teach him how to work.
