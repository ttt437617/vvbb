Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.114

premāveśe prabhu kahe 'krishna' 'krishna'-nāma

ekale premāveśe kare śata-janera kāma

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — says; krishna krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; ekale — alone; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; kare — does; śata-janera kāma — the work of hundreds of men.

TRANSLATION

As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was vibrating the holy name of Krishna in ecstatic love, He Himself was performing the work of hundreds of men.
