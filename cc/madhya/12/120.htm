Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.120

nātaśālā dhui' dhuila catvara-prāńgana

pākaśālā-ādi kari' karila prakshālana

SYNONYMS

nāta-śālā — the meeting place; dhui' — washing; dhuila — washed; catvara-prāńgana — the yard and the raised sitting place; pāka-śālā — the kitchen; ādi — and so on; kari' — making; karila prakshālana — washed.

TRANSLATION

In this way the meeting place was washed, the entire yard, the raised sitting places, the kitchen and every other room.
