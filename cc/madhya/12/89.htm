Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.89

ei-mata bhakta-gana kari' nija-vāse

trina, dhūli bāhire phelāya parama harishe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — similarly; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; kari' — doing; nija-vāse — in their own cloths; trina — straw; dhūli — dust; bāhire phelāya — throw outside; parama harishe — with great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

Following the example of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees, in great jubilation, began to gather straws and dust with their own cloths and throw them outside the temple.
