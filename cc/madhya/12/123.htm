Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.123

sei jala lañā āpane pāna kaila

tāhā dekhi' prabhura mane duhkha rosha haila

SYNONYMS

sei jala — that water; lañā — taking; āpane — personally; pāna kaila — drank; tāhā dekhi' — seeing that; prabhura — of the Lord; mane — in the mind; duhkha — unhappiness; rosha — anger; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

The Gaudīya Vaishnava then took that water and drank it himself. Seeing that, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt a little unhappy and was also outwardly angry.
