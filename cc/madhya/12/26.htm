Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.26

dāmodara kahe, — tumi svatantra īśvara

kartavyākartavya saba tomāra gocara

SYNONYMS

dāmodara kahe — Pandita Dāmodara said; tumi — You; svatantra — fully independent; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; kartavya — duty which is permissible; akartavya — duty which is not permissible; saba — all; tomāra — of You; gocara — within knowledge.

TRANSLATION

Dāmodara immediately replied, "My Lord, You are the fully independent Supreme Personality of Godhead. Since everything is known to You, You know what is permissible and what is not permissible.
