Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.54

yadyapi pratāparudra — sarva-gunavān

tāńhāre malina kaila eka 'rājā'-nāma

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; pratāparudra — the King; sarva-guna-vān — qualified in every respect; tāńhāre — unto him; malina kaila — makes impure; eka — one; rājā-nāma — the name ''king.''

TRANSLATION

"The King certainly possesses all good qualities, but simply by taking up the name 'king,' he has infected everything.
