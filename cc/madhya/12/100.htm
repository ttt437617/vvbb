Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.100

bhakta-gana kare griha-madhya prakshālana

nija nija haste kare mandira mārjana

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — devotees; kare — do; griha-madhya — within the room; prakshālana — washing; nija nija — each one of them; haste — in the hand; kare — does; mandira mārjana — cleansing of the temple.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees within the temple began to wash. Each one had a broom in his hand, and in this way they cleansed the temple of the Lord.
