Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.205

paksha-dina duhkhī loka prabhura adarśane

darśana kariyā loka sukha pāila mane

SYNONYMS

paksha-dina — for a fortnight; duhkhī — unhappy; loka — devotees; prabhura — of Lord Jagannātha; adarśane — without the sight; darśana kariyā — by seeing; loka — all the devotees; sukha — happiness; pāila — got; mane — in the mind.

TRANSLATION

Everyone was unhappy for a fortnight because they could not see the Deity of Lord Jagannātha. Upon seeing the Lord at the festival, the devotees were very happy.
