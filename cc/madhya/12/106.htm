Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.106

nirmala, śītala, snigdha karila mandire

āpana-hridaya yena dharila bāhire

SYNONYMS

nirmala — purified; śītala — cool; snigdha — pleasing; karila — made; mandire — the temple; āpana-hridaya — own heart; yena — as if; dharila — kept; bāhire — outside.

TRANSLATION

When the temple was cleansed, it was purified, cool and pleasing, just as if the Lord's own pure mind had appeared.
