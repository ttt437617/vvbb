Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.169

jagadānanda bedāya pariveśana karite

prabhura pāte bhāla-dravya dena ācambite

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; bedāya — walks; pariveśana — distribution of prasādam; karite — to do; prabhura pāte — on the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhāla-dravya — the first-class preparations; dena — puts; ācambite — suddenly.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda went to distribute prasādam, and suddenly he placed all the first-class preparations on the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
