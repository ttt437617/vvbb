Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.151

tabe mahāprabhu kshaneka viśrāma kariyā

snāna karibāre gelā bhakta-gana lañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kshaneka — for some time; viśrāma kariyā — taking rest; snāna karibāre — for bathing; gelā — went; bhakta-gana lañā — taking all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

After taking rest, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the devotees departed to take their baths.
