Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.92

ei-mata abhyantara karila mārjana

punah sabākāre dila kariyā vantana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; abhyantara — inside; karila — did; mārjana — cleansing; punah — again; sabākāre — to all of them; dila — gave; kariyā vantana — allotting areas.

TRANSLATION

After the inside of the temple was cleansed, the Lord again allotted areas for the devotees to cleanse.
