Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.119

tabe prakshālana kaila śrī-jagamohana

bhoga-mandira-ādi tabe kaila prakshālana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prakshālana — washing; kaila — performed; śrī-jagamohana — in front of the temple; bhoga-mandira — the place where food is offered; ādi — all such places; tabe — then; kaila prakshālana — washed.

TRANSLATION

They washed the Jagamohana area and then the place where food was kept. All other places were also washed.
