Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.22

yadyapi śuniyā prabhura komala haya mana

tathāpi bāhire kahe nishthura vacana

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; śuniyā — hearing; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; komala — softened; haya — becomes; mana — mind; tathāpi — still; bāhire — externally; kahe — He says; nishthura vacana — hard words.

TRANSLATION

Hearing all these statements, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind was certainly softened, but externally He wished to speak some harsh words.
