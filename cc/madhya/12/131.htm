Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.131

āpane vasiyā mājhe, āpanāra hāte

trina, kāńkara, kutā lāgilā kudāite

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; vasiyā mājhe — sitting in the middle; āpanāra hāte — with His own hand; trina — straw; kāńkara — grains of sand; kutā — dirt; lāgilā — began; kudāite — to pick up.

TRANSLATION

The Lord then personally sat down in the middle and picked up all kinds of straw, grains of sand and dirty things.
