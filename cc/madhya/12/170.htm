Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.170

yadyapi dile prabhu tāńre karena rosha

bale-chale tabu dena, dile se santosha

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; dile — by such deliverance; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; karena — does; rosha — anger; bale-chale — somehow or other (sometimes by tricks, sometimes by force); tabu — still; dena — delivers; dile — when he delivers; se santosha — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased.

TRANSLATION

When such nice prasādam was put on the plate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord was outwardly very angry. Nonetheless, when the preparations were placed on His plate sometimes by tricks and sometimes by force, the Lord was satisfied.
