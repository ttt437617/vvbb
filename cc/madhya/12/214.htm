Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.214

śrī-mukha-sundara-kānti bādhe kshane kshane

koti-bhakta-netra-bhrińga kare madhu-pāne

SYNONYMS

śrī-mukha — of His beautiful face; sundara-kānti — attractive luster; bādhe — increases; kshane kshane — at every moment; koti-bhakta — of millions of devotees; netra-bhrińga — eyes like bumblebees; kare — engaged; madhu-pāne — in drinking the honey.

TRANSLATION

The luster of His beautiful face increased at every moment, and the eyes of hundreds and thousands of devotees drank its honey like bumblebees.
