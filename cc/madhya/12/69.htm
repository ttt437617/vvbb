Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.69

ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gana-sańge

nirantara krīdā kare sańkīrtana-rańge

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana-sańge — in the society of His pure devotees; nirantara — constantly; krīdā kare — performs pastimes; sańkīrtana-rańge — in the course of His sańkīrtana movement.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu acted in the society of His pure devotees, performing His pastimes and spreading the sańkīrtana movement.
