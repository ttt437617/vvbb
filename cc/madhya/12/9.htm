Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.9

tāń-sabāra prasāde mile śrī-prabhura pāya

prabhu-kripā vinā mora rājya nāhi bhāya

SYNONYMS

tāń-sabāra prasāde — by the mercy of all of them; mile — one gets; śrī-prabhura pāya — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu-kripā — the mercy of the Lord; vinā — without; mora — my; rājya — kingdom; nāhi — does not; bhāya — appeal to me.

TRANSLATION

"By the mercy of all the devotees, one can attain the shelter of the lotus feet of the Lord. Without His mercy, my kingdom does not appeal to me.
