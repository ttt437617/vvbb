Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.153

udyāne vasilā prabhu bhakta-gana lañā

tabe vānīnātha āilā mahā-prasāda lañā

SYNONYMS

udyāne — in the garden; vasilā — sat down; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana lañā — with the devotees; tabe — at that time; vānīnātha — Vānīnātha Rāya; āilā — came; mahā-prasāda lañā — bringing all kinds of mahā-prasādam.

TRANSLATION

In the garden, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with the other devotees. Vānīnātha Rāya then came and brought all kinds of mahā-prasādam.
