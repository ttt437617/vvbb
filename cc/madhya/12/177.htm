Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.177

sārvabhaume prabhu vasāñāchena vāma-pāśe

dui bhaktera sneha dekhi' sārvabhauma hāse

SYNONYMS

sārvabhaume — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; prabhu — the Lord; vasāñāchena — made to sit; vāma-pāśe — on His left side; dui bhaktera — of the two devotees; sneha — the affection; dekhi' — seeing; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; hāse — smiles.

TRANSLATION

The Lord made Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya sit on His left side, and when Sārvabhauma saw the behavior of Svarūpa Dāmodara and Jagadānanda, he smiled.
