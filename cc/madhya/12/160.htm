Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.160

'haridāsa' bali' prabhu dāke ghane ghana

dūre rahi' haridāsa kare nivedana

SYNONYMS

haridāsa bali' — calling Haridāsa; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dāke — calls; ghane ghana — repeatedly; dūre rahi' — standing at a distance; haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; kare nivedana — submitted.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was repeatedly calling, "Haridāsa, Haridāsa," and at that time Haridāsa, standing at a distance, spoke as follows.
