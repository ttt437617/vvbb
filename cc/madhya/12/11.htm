Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.11

bhattācārya patrī dekhi' cintita hañā

bhakta-gana-pāśa gelā sei patrī lañā

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; patrī — the letter; dekhi' — seeing; cintita hañā — becoming very anxious; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; pāśa — near; gelā — went; sei — that; patrī — letter; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

When the Bhattācārya received this letter, he became very anxious. He then took the letter and went to the devotees of the Lord.
