Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.112

'krishna' 'krishna' kahi' kare ghatera prārthana

'krishna' 'krishna' kahi' kare ghata samarpana

SYNONYMS

krishna krishna kahi' — while chanting "Krishna, Krishna"; kare — do; ghatera — for the waterpots; prārthana — begging; krishna krishna — the holy name of Lord Krishna; kahi' — chanting; kare — does; ghata — of the waterpots; samarpana — delivery.

TRANSLATION

One person begged for a waterpot by chanting the holy names "Krishna, Krishna," and another delivered a pot while chanting "Krishna, Krishna."
