Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.108

pūrna kumbha lañā āise śata bhakta-gana

śūnya ghata lañā yāya āra śata jana

SYNONYMS

pūrna kumbha — a filled waterpot; lañā — taking; āise — come; śata bhakta-gana — hundreds of devotees; śūnya ghata — an empty waterpot; lañā — taking back; yāya — go; āra — another; śata jana — hundreds of men.

TRANSLATION

Hundreds of devotees brought water in the pots, and hundreds took the empty pots away to fill them up again.
