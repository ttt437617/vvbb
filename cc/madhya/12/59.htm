Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.59

pītāmbara, dhare ańge ratna-ābharana

śrī-krishna-smarane teńha hailā 'uddīpana'

SYNONYMS

pīta-ambara — dressed in yellow cloth; dhare — carries; ańge — on the body; ratna-ābharana — ornaments of jewels; śrī-krishna-smarane — for remembering Śrī Krishna; teńha — he; hailā — was; uddīpana — stimulation.

TRANSLATION

The prince was dressed in yellow cloth, and there were jeweled ornaments decorating his body. Therefore anyone who saw him would remember Lord Krishna.
