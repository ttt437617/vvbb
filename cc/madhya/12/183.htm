Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.183

tārkika-śrigāla-sańge bheu-bheu kari

sei mukhe ebe sadā kahi 'krishna' 'hari'

SYNONYMS

tārkika — logician; śrigāla — jackals; sańge — in the association of; bheu-bheu kari — barking; sei mukhe — in that very mouth; ebe — now; sadā — always; kahi — speak; krishna — the holy name of Lord Krishna; hari — Hari.

TRANSLATION

"In the association of the jackals known as logicians, I simply continued to bark a resounding 'bheu bheu.' Now, from the same mouth I am chanting the holy names 'Krishna' and 'Hari.'
