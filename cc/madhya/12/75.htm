Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.75

viśeshe rājāra ājñā hañāche āmāre

prabhura ājñā yei, sei śīghra karibāre

SYNONYMS

viśeshe — specifically; rājāra — of the King; ājñā — order; hañāche — there is; āmāre — upon me; prabhura — of Your Lordship; ājñā — order; yei — whatever; sei — that; śīghra karibāre — to execute without delay.

TRANSLATION

"The King gave a special order for me to do without delay whatever Your Lordship orders.
