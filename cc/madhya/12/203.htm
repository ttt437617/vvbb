Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.203

svatantra īśvara prabhu kare nānā khelā

'dhoyā-pākhalā' nāma kaila ei eka līlā

SYNONYMS

svatantra īśvara — the independent Personality of Godhead; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — performs; nānā — various; khelā — pastimes; dhoyā-pākhalā — washing and cleansing; nāma — named; kaila — performed; ei — this; eka — one; līlā — pastime.

TRANSLATION

The fully independent Supreme Personality of Godhead performs various types of pastimes. The pastime of washing and cleansing the Gundicā temple is but one of them.
