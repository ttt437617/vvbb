Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.64

'krishna' 'krishna' kahe, nāce, karaye rodana

tāńra bhāgya dekhi' ślāghā kare bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

krishna krishna — O Krishna, O Krishna; kahe — chants; nāce — dances; karaye — does; rodana — crying; tāńra — his; bhāgya — fortune; dekhi' — seeing; ślāghā — praise; kare — do; bhakta-gana — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

The boy began to cry and dance, and he chanted, "Krishna! Krishna!" Upon seeing his bodily symptoms and his chanting and dancing, all the devotees praised him for his great spiritual fortune.
