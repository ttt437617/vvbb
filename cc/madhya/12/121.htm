Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.121

mandirera catur-dik prakshālana kaila

saba antahpura bhāla-mate dhoyāila

SYNONYMS

mandirera — the temple; catur-dik — all around; prakshālana kaila — washed; saba — all; antahpura — inside the rooms; bhāla-mate — with great care; dhoyāila — washed.

TRANSLATION

Thus all places around the temple were thoroughly washed within and without.
