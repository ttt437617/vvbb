Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.81

gundicā-mandire gelā karite mārjana

prathame mārjanī lañā karila śodhana

SYNONYMS

gundicā-mandire — to the temple known as Gundicā; gelā — went; karite — to do; mārjana — washing; prathame — in the first instance; mārjanī — the brooms; lañā — taking; karila — did; śodhana — cleansing.

TRANSLATION

In this way the Lord and His associates went to cleanse the Gundicā temple. At first they cleansed the temple with the brooms.
