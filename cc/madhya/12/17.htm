Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.17

prabhu kahe, — ki kahite sabāra āgamana

dekhiye kahite cāha, — nā kaha, ki kārana?

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; ki — what; kahite — to speak; sabāra — of all of you; āgamana — there is the presence here; dekhiye — I see; kahite cāha — you want to speak; nā kaha — but do not speak; ki kārana — what is the reason.

TRANSLATION

After they arrived at Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's place, the Lord, seeing them, said, "What have you all come here to say? I see that you want to say something, but you do not speak. What is the reason?"
