Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.171

punarapi sei dravya kare nirīkshana

tāńra bhaye prabhu kichu karena bhakshana

SYNONYMS

punarapi — again; sei dravya — that very thing; kare nirīkshana — sees carefully; tāńra bhaye — out of fear of Jagadānanda; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kichu — something; karena — does; bhakshana — eating.

TRANSLATION

When the food was thus delivered, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu looked at it for some time. Being afraid of Jagadānanda, He finally ate something.
