Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.65

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre dhairya karāila

nitya āsi' āmāya miliha — ei ājñā dila

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — the boy; dhairya — patient; karāila — caused to be; nitya — daily; āsi' — coming; āmāya — Me; miliha — meet; ei ājñā — this order; dila — gave.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu calmed the youth and ordered him to come there daily to meet Him.
