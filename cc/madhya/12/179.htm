Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.179

gopīnāthācārya uttama mahā-prasāda āni'

sārvabhaume diyā kahe sumadhura vānī

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha-ācārya — Gopīnātha Ācārya; uttama — first-class; mahā-prasāda — remnants of food; āni' — bringing; sārvabhaume — to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; diyā — delivering; kahe — says; su-madhura — very sweet; vānī — words.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya also brought first-class food and offered it to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya while speaking sweet words.
