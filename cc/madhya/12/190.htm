Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.190

prabhu ta' sannyāsī, uńhāra nāhi apacaya

anna-doshe sannyāsīra dosha nāhi haya

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ta' — indeed; sannyāsī — in the renounced order of life; uńhāra — for Him; nāhi — there is not; apacaya — any discrepancy; anna-doshe — by contamination of food; sannyāsīra — of a person in the renounced order; dosha — fault; nāhi — not; haya — there is.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in the renounced order of life. Consequently He does not recognize discrepancies. As a matter of fact, a sannyāsī is not affected by eating food from anywhere and everywhere.
