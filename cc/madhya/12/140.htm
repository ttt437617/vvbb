Madhya-līlāChapter 12: The Cleansing of the Gundicā Temple

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 12.140

mahā-ucca-sańkīrtane ākāśa bharila

prabhura uddanda-nritye bhūmi-kampa haila

SYNONYMS

mahā-ucca-sańkīrtane — by a great and loud performance of chanting; ākāśa — the sky; bharila — became filled; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uddanda-nritye — by dancing and jumping high; bhūmi-kampa — earthquake; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

The sky was filled with the great and loud chanting of sańkīrtana, and the earth shook from the jumping and dancing of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
