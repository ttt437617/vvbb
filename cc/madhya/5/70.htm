Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.70

kanyā dite nāribe, habe asatya-vacana

punarapi kahe vipra kariyā yatana

SYNONYMS

kanyā — daughter; dite — to give; nāribe — you will not be able; habe — it will become; asatya-vacana — a false statement; punarapi — again; kahe — he says; vipra — the brāhmana; kariyā yatana — with great attention.

TRANSLATION

"'My dear sir, you will not be able to fulfill your promise. Your promise will be broken.' Yet, again and again the brāhmana emphasized his promise.
