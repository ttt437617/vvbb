Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.25

bada-vipra kahe, — "tumi nā kara samśaya

tomāke kanyā diba āmi, karila niścaya"

SYNONYMS

bada-vipra — the older brāhmana; kahe — replies; tumi — you; nā — not; kara — do; samśaya — doubt; tomāke — to you; kanyā — the daughter; diba — shall give; āmi — I; karila — made; niścaya — certainty.

TRANSLATION

The older brāhmana replied, "My dear boy, do not doubt me. I will give you my daughter in charity. I have already decided this."
