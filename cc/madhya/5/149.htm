Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.149

premāveśe padilā tumi, tomāre dharinu

tomā-saha sei danda-upare padinu

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in an ecstatic condition; padilā — fell down; tumi — You; tomāre — You; dharinu — I caught; tomā-saha — with You; sei — that; danda-upare — upon that staff; padinu — I fell down.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu said, "When You fell down in ecstasy, I caught You, but both of Us together fell upon the staff.
