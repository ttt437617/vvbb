Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.18

vipra bale — tumi mora bahu sevā kailā

sahāya hañā more tīrtha karāilā

SYNONYMS

vipra bale — the elderly brāhmana says; tumi — you; mora — my; bahu — various; sevā — service; kailā — have rendered; sahāya — assistant; hañā — being; more — to me; tīrtha — pilgrimage; karāilā — helped to do.

TRANSLATION

The older man told the younger, "You have rendered various types of service to me. You have assisted me in traveling to all these places of pilgrimage.
