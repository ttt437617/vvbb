Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.61

saba dhana lañā kahe — 'core la-ila dhana'

'kanyā dite cāhiyāche' — uthāila vacana

SYNONYMS

saba — all; dhana — money; lañā — taking; kahe — says; core — a thief; la-ila — took; dhana — all the money; kanyā — the daughter; dite — to give in charity; cāhiyāche — has promised; uthāila — has raised; vacana — a slogan.

TRANSLATION

"Having taken all my father's money, this rogue claimed that it was taken by some thief. Now he is claiming that my father has promised to give him his daughter in charity.
