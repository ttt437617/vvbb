Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.21

chota-vipra kahe, "śuna, vipra-mahāśaya

asambhava kaha kene, yei nāhi haya

SYNONYMS

chota-vipra — the younger brāhmana; kahe — replies; śuna — hear; vipra-mahāśaya — my dear brāhmana; asambhava — unlikely; kaha — you say; kene — why; yei — which; nāhi — not; haya — happens.

TRANSLATION

The younger brāhmana replied, "My dear sir, please hear me. You are saying something very unusual. Such a thing never happens.
