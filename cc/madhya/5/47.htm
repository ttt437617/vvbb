Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.47

'mora dharma rakshā pāya, nā mare nija-jana

dui rakshā kara, gopāla, lainu śarana'

SYNONYMS

mora — my; dharma — religious principles; rakshā pāya — spared; nā — not; mare — die; nija-jana — own kinsmen; dui — two; rakshā kara — You kindly protect; gopāla — my Lord Gopāla; lainu — I have taken; śarana — shelter under Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

The elderly brāhmana prayed, "My dear Lord Gopāla, I have taken shelter of Your lotus feet, and therefore I request You to please protect my religious principles from disturbance and at the same time save my kinsmen from dying."
