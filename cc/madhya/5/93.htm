Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.93

vipra bale, — "yadi hao caturbhuja-mūrti

tabu tomāra vākye kāru nā habe pratīti

SYNONYMS

vipra bale — the young brāhmana says; yadi — if; hao — You become; catuh-bhuja — four-handed; mūrti — Deity; tabu — still; tomāra — Your; vākye — in the word; kāru — of anyone; nā — not; habe — there will be; pratīti — belief.

TRANSLATION

The young brāhmana replied, "My dear sir, even if You appear there as a four-handed Vishnu Deity, still, none of those people will believe in Your words.
