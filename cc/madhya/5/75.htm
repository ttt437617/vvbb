Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.75

yadi ei vipra more nā dibe kanyā-dāna

sākshī bolāimu tomāya, ha-io sāvadhāna

SYNONYMS

yadi — if; ei — this; vipra — brāhmana; more — to me; nā — not; dibe — will give; kanyā-dāna — his daughter in charity; sākshī bolāimu — I shall call as a witness; tomāya — You; ha-io sāvadhāna — kindly be attentive.

TRANSLATION

"'If this brāhmana later hesitates to give me his daughter, my dear Lord, I shall call on You as a witness. Please note this with care and attention.'
