Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.49

āsiñā parama-bhaktye namaskāra kari'

vinaya kariñā kahe kara dui yudi'

SYNONYMS

āsiñā — coming; parama-bhaktye — in great devotion; namaskāra kari' — offering obeisances; vinaya kariñā — with great humility; kahe — says; kara — hands; dui — two; yudi' — folding.

TRANSLATION

The young brāhmana came to him and offered respectful obeisances. Then, very humbly folding his hands, he spoke as follows.
