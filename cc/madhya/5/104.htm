Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.104

'ebe muñi grāme āinu, yāimu bhavana

lokere kahiba giyā sākshīra āgamana

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; muñi — I; grāme — to the village; āinu — have come; yāimu — I shall go; bhavana — to my home; lokere — the people; kahiba — I shall tell; giyā — going there; sākshīra — of the witness; āgamana — about the arrival.

TRANSLATION

"I have now come to my village, and I shall go to my home and tell all the people that the witness has arrived."
