Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.112

sakala lokera āge gopāla sākshī dila

bada-vipra chota-vipre kanyā-dāna kaila

SYNONYMS

sakala — all; lokera — of the people; āge — in the presence; gopāla — Lord Gopāla; sākshī — witness; dila — gave; bada-vipra — the elderly brāhmana; chota-vipre — unto the young brāhmana; kanyā-dāna — giving the daughter in charity; kaila — did.

TRANSLATION

Thus in the presence of all the townspeople, Lord Gopāla bore witness that the elderly brāhmana had offered his daughter in charity to the young brāhmana.
