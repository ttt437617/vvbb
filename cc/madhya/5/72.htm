Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.72

tabe āmi kahilāń dridha kari' mana

gopālera āge kaha e-satya vacana

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; āmi — I; kahilāń — said; dridha kari' mana — fixing my mind; gopālera āge — in front of the Gopāla Deity; kaha — speak; e-satya vacana — this truthful statement.

TRANSLATION

"At that time I concentrated my mind and requested the brāhmana to make the promise before the Gopāla Deity.
