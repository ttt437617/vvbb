Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.55

'ińha more kanyā dite karyāche ańgīkāra

ebe ye nā dena, pucha ińhāra vyavahāra'

SYNONYMS

ińha — this gentleman; more — to me; kanyā — his daughter; dite — to give in charity; karyāche — has made; ańgīkāra — promise; ebe — now; ye — indeed; nā — not; dena — he gives; pucha — kindly ask; ińhāra — of him; vyavahāra — the behavior.

TRANSLATION

"This gentleman has promised to hand over his daughter to me, yet now he does not follow his promise. Please ask him about his behavior."
