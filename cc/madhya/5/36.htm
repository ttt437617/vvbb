Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.36

tīrthe vipre vākya diluń, — kemate satya haya

strī, putra, jñāti, bandhu jānibe niścaya

SYNONYMS

tīrthe — on pilgrimage; vipre — to a brāhmana; vākya — word of honor; diluń — I have given; kemate — how; satya — true; haya — it is; strī — wife; putra — sons; jñāti — relatives; bandhu — friends; jānibe — will know; niścaya — certainly.

TRANSLATION

He began to think, "I have given my word to a brāhmana in a holy place, and what I promised will certainly come to pass. I must now disclose this to my wife, sons, other relatives and friends."
