Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.50

'tumi more kanyā dite karyācha ańgīkāra

ebe kichu nāhi kaha, ki tomāra vicāra'

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; more — to me; kanyā — your daughter; dite — to give in charity; karyācha — have made; ańgīkāra — a promise; ebe — now; kichu — something; nāhi — not; kaha — you say; ki — what; tomāra — your; vicāra — conclusion.

TRANSLATION

"You have promised to give your daughter in charity to me. Now you do not say anything. What is your conclusion?"
