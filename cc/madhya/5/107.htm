Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.107

brāhmanere kahe, — "tumi yāha nija-ghara

ethāya rahiba āmi, nā yāba atahpara"

SYNONYMS

brāhmanere kahe — He asked the brāhmana; tumi — you; yāha — go; nija-ghara — to your own home; ethāya — here in this place; rahiba — shall stay; āmi — I; nā — not; yāba — shall go; atahpara — hereafter.

TRANSLATION

The Lord told the brāhmana, "Now you can go home. I shall stay here and shall not leave."
