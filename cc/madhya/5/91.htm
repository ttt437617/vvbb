Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.91

krishna kahe, — vipra, tumi yāha sva-bhavane

sabhā kari' more tumi kariha smarane

SYNONYMS

krishna kahe — Lord Krishna says; vipra — My dear brāhmana; tumi — you; yāha — go back; sva-bhavane — to your own home; sabhā kari' — calling a meeting of all the men; more — of Me; tumi — you; kariha — just do; smarane — remembering.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna replied, "My dear brāhmana, go back to your home and call a meeting of all the men. In that meeting, just try to remember Me.
