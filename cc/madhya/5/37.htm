Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.37

eka-dina nija-loka ekatra karila

tā-sabāra āge saba vrittānta kahila

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; nija-loka — all his relatives; ekatra — in one place; karila — assembled; tā-sabāra — of all of them; āge — in front; saba — all; vrittānta — narration; kahila — spoke.

TRANSLATION

Thus one day the elderly brāhmana called for a meeting of all his relatives and friends, and before them all he narrated what had taken place in front of Gopāla.
