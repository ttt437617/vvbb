Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.59

'tīrtha-yātrāya pitāra sańge chila bahu dhana

dhana dekhi ei dushtera laite haila mana

SYNONYMS

tīrtha-yātrāya — when touring the holy places; pitāra — my father; sańge — with; chila — there was; bahu — much; dhana — money; dhana — money; dekhi — seeing; ei — this; dushtera — of the rogue; laite — to take; haila — it was; mana — the intention.

TRANSLATION

"While touring various holy places of pilgrimage, my father carried much money. Seeing the money, this rogue decided to take it away.
