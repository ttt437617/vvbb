Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.15

gopāla-saundarya duńhāra mana nila hari'

sukha pāñā rahe tāhāń dina dui-cāri

SYNONYMS

gopāla-saundarya — the beauty of the Gopāla Deity; duńhāra — of both of them; mana — the minds; nila — took away; hari' — carrying; sukha pāñā — feeling this transcendental happiness; rahe — remained; tāhāń — in that temple; dina — days; dui-cāri — two or four.

TRANSLATION

The beauty of the Gopāla Deity stole away their minds, and feeling great happiness, they remained there for two or four days.
