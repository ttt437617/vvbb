Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.64

tabe chota-vipra kahe, "śuna, mahājana

nyāya jinibāre kahe asatya-vacana

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; chota-vipra — the young brāhmana; kahe — says; śuna — please hear; mahā-jana — all gentlemen; nyāya — the argument; jinibāre — to win; kahe — he says; asatya-vacana — untruthful statements.

TRANSLATION

At that time the young brāhmana said, "My dear gentlemen, please hear. Just to gain victory in an argument, this man is lying.
