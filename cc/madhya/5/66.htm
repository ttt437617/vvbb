Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.66

tabe muñi nishedhinu, — śuna, dvija-vara

tomāra kanyāra yogya nahi muñi vara

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; muñi — I; nishedhinu — forbade; śuna — hear; dvija-vara — O best of the brāhmanas; tomāra — your; kanyāra — for the daughter; yogya — suitable; nahi — not; muñi — I; vara — husband.

TRANSLATION

"At that time I forbade him to do this, telling him, 'O best of the brāhmanas, I am not a fit husband for your daughter.
