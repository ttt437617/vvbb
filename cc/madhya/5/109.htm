Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.109

āila sakala loka sākshī dekhibāre

gopāla dekhiñā loka dandavat kare

SYNONYMS

āila — came; sakala — all; loka — the people; sākshī — the witness; dekhibāre — to see; gopāla — Lord Gopāla; dekhiñā — seeing; loka — all the people; dandavat — offering obeisances; kare — do.

TRANSLATION

All the townspeople went to see the witness Gopāla, and when they saw the Lord actually standing there, they all offered their respectful obeisances.
