Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.80

putrera mane, — 'pratimā nā āsibe sākshī dite'

ei buddhye dui-jana ha-ilā sammate

SYNONYMS

putrera mane — in the mind of the son; pratimā — the Deity; nā — not; āsibe — will come; sākshī dite — to give witness; ei — this; buddhye — in understanding; dui-jana — both the father and the son; ha-ilā sammate — agreed.

TRANSLATION

The atheistic son thought, "It is not possible for Gopāla to come and bear witness." Thinking thus, the father and son agreed.
