Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.88

"brahmanya-deva tumi bada dayā-maya

dui viprera dharma rākha hañā sadaya

SYNONYMS

brahmanya-deva — O Lord of brahminical culture; tumi — You; bada — very; dayā-maya — merciful; dui — two; viprera — of brāhmanas; dharma — the religious principles; rākha — protect; hañā — becoming; sa-daya — merciful.

TRANSLATION

He said, "My Lord, You are the protector of brahminical culture, and You are also very merciful. Therefore, kindly show Your great mercy by protecting the religious principles of us two brāhmanas.
