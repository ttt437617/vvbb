Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.127

thākurera nāsāte yadi chidra thākita

tabe ei dāsī muktā nāsāya parāita

SYNONYMS

thākurera nāsāte — in the nose of the Deity; yadi — if; chidra — a hole; thākita — there were; tabe — then; ei — this; dāsī — maidservant; muktā — pearl; nāsāya — on the nose; parāita — could put on.

TRANSLATION

"If there were a hole in the Deity's nose, I could transfer the pearl to Him."
