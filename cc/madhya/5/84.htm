Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.84

sva-vākya chādite ińhāra nāhi kabhu mana

svajana-mrityu-bhaye kahe asatya-vacana

SYNONYMS

sva-vākya — his own promise; chādite — to give up; ińhāra — of this brāhmana; nāhi — not; kabhu — at any time; mana — the mind; sva-jana — of his own kinsmen; mrityu-bhaye — fearing the suicide; kahe — says; asatya-vacana — untruthful words.

TRANSLATION

"He had no desire to break his promise, but fearing that his kinsmen would commit suicide, he deviated from the truth.
