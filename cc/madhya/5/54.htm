Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.54

saba loka bada-vipre dākiyā ānila

tabe sei laghu-vipra kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

saba loka — all the village inhabitants; bada-vipre — to the senior brāhmana; dākiyā — calling; ānila — brought; tabe — then; sei laghu-vipra — the junior brāhmana; kahite lāgila — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

All the people of the village then called for the elderly brāhmana and brought him to their meeting place. The young brāhmana then began to speak before them as follows.
