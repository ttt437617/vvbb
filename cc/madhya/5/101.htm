Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.101

āra dina ājñā māgi' calilā brāhmana

tāra pāche pāche gopāla karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; ājñā — permission; māgi' — begging; calilā — started; brāhmana — the young brāhmana; tāra — him; pāche — behind; pāche — behind; gopāla — Lord Gopāla; karilā — began; gamana — following.

TRANSLATION

The next day, the brāhmana begged permission from Gopāla and started for his country. Gopāla followed him, step by step.
