Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.105

sākshāte nā dekhile mane pratīti nā haya

ihāń yadi rahena, tabu nāhi kichu bhaya'

SYNONYMS

sākshāte — directly; nā — not; dekhile — if seeing; mane — in the mind; pratīti — assurance; nā — not; haya — there is; ihāń — here; yadi — if; rahena — the Lord stays; tabu — still; nāhi — there is not; kichu — any; bhaya — fear.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana then began to think that if the people didn't directly see the Gopāla Deity, they would not believe that He had arrived. "But even if Gopāla stays here," he thought, "there is still nothing to fear."
