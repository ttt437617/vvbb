Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.35

deśe āsi' dui-jane gelā nija-ghare

kata dine bada-vipra cintita antare

SYNONYMS

deśe āsi' — after returning to their own country; dui-jane — both of them; gelā — went; nija-ghare — to their respective homes; kata dine — after some time; bada-vipra — the elderly brāhmana; cintita — very anxious; antare — within.

TRANSLATION

After returning to Vidyānagara, each brāhmana went to his respective home. After some time, the elderly brāhmana became very anxious.
