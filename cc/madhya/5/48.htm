Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.48

ei-mata vipra citte cintite lāgila

āra dina laghu-vipra tāńra ghare āila

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; vipra — the elderly brāhmana; citte — within the mind; cintite — to think; lāgila — began; āra dina — the next day; laghu-vipra — the young brāhmana; tāńra — his; ghare — to the home; āila — came.

TRANSLATION

The next day, the elderly brāhmana was thinking deeply about this matter when the young brāhmana came to his house.
