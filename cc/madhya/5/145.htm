Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.145

bhakta-gana āvishta hañā, sabe nāce gāya

premāveśe prabhu-sańge rāja-mārge yāya

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — the devotees; āvishta — ecstatic; hañā — being; sabe — all; nāce — dance; gāya — sing; prema-āveśe — absorbed in love of God; prabhu-sańge — with Lord Caitanya; rāja-mārge — on the pathway; yāya — going.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees became ecstatic in the association of Lord Caitanya, and thus absorbed in love of God, they were dancing and singing while going along the main road.
