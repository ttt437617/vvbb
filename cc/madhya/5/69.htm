Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.69

tabe āmi kahilāń — śuna, mahā-mati

tomāra strī-putra-jñātira nā habe sammati

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; āmi — I; kahilāń — said; śuna — please hear; mahā-mati — O intelligent brāhmana; tomāra — your; strī-putra — wife and children; jñātira — kinsmen; nā habe sammati — will not agree.

TRANSLATION

"I then said, 'Please hear. You are a learned brāhmana. Your wife, friends and relatives will never agree to this proposal.
