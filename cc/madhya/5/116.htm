Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.116

gopāla rahilā, duńhe karena sevana

dekhite āilā saba deśera loka-jana

SYNONYMS

gopāla — Lord Gopāla; rahilā — stayed; duńhe — both of them; karena — do; sevana — service; dekhite — to see; āilā — came; saba — all; deśera — of the countries; loka-jana — the people.

TRANSLATION

Lord Gopāla stayed, and the two brāhmanas engaged in His service. After hearing of the incident, many people from different countries began to come to see Gopāla.
