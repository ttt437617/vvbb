Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.114

duńhāra satye tushta ha-ilāń, duńhe māga' vara"

dui-vipra vara māge ānanda-antara

SYNONYMS

duńhāra satye — in the truthfulness of both of you; tushta ha-ilāń — I have be come satisfied; duńhe — both of you; māga' — ask; vara — some benediction; dui-vipra — both the brāhmanas; vara — a benediction; māge — beg; ānanda — pleased; antara — within.

TRANSLATION

The Lord continued, "I have become very much pleased by the truthfulness of you both. Now you can ask for a benediction." Thus with great pleasure the two brāhmanas begged for a benediction.
