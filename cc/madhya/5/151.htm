Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.151

mora aparādhe tomāra danda ha-ila khanda

ye ucita haya, mora kara tāra danda"

SYNONYMS

mora — My; aparādhe — by the offense; tomāra — Your; danda — sannyāsa staff; ha-ila — became; khanda — broken; ye — whatever; ucita — fitting; haya — is; mora — to Me; kara — do; tāra — for that; danda — punishment.

TRANSLATION

"It is certainly because of My offense that Your staff was broken. Now You can punish Me on this account as You think proper."
