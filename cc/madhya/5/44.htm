Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.44

nāhi kahi — nā kahio e mithyā-vacana

sabe kahibe — 'mora kichu nāhika smarana'

SYNONYMS

nāhi kahi — I did not say; nā kahio — do not say; e — this; mithyā-vacana — false statement; sabe — only; kahibe — you shall say; mora — my; kichu — anything; nāhika — not; smarana — remembrance.

TRANSLATION

"You do not have to flatly deny that you spoke such a thing. There is no need to make a false statement. Simply say that you do not remember what you said.
