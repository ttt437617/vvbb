Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.125

tāńhāra mahishī āilā gopāla-darśane

bhakti kari' bahu alańkāra kaila samarpane

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra mahishī — his Queen; āilā — came; gopāla-darśane — to see the Gopāla Deity; bhakti kari' — in great devotion; bahu — various; alańkāra — of ornaments; kaila — made; samarpane — presentation.

TRANSLATION

When the Gopāla Deity was installed at Kataka, the Queen of Purushottama-deva went to see Him and, with great devotion, presented various kinds of ornaments.
