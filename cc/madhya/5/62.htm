Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.62

tomarā sakala loka karaha vicāre

'mora pitāra kanyā dite yogya ki ihāre'

SYNONYMS

tomarā — you; sakala — all; loka — people; karaha — just make; vicāre — judgment; mora — my; pitāra — of the father; kanyā — the daughter; dite — to give in charity; yogya — befitting; ki — is it; ihāre — to him.

TRANSLATION

"All of you assembled here are gentlemen. Please judge whether it is befitting to offer this poor brāhmana my father's daughter."
