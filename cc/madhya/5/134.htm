Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.134

nityānanda-mukhe śuni' gopāla-carita

tushta hailā mahāprabhu svabhakta-sahita

SYNONYMS

nityānanda-mukhe — from the mouth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; śuni' — hearing; gopāla-carita — the narration of Gopāla; tushta hailā — became very pleased; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sva-bhakta-sahita — with His devotees.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard the narration of Gopāla's activities. Both He and His personal devotees became very pleased.
