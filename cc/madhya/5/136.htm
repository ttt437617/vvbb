Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.136

duńhe — eka varna, duńhe — prakānda-śarīra

duńhe — raktāmbara, duńhāra svabhāva — gambhīra

SYNONYMS

duńhe — both of Them; eka varna — one complexion; duńhe — both of Them; prakānda-śarīra — gigantic bodies; duńhe — both of Them; rakta-ambara — red clothes; duńhāra — of both; svabhāva — the natures; gambhīra — grave.

TRANSLATION

They were of the same complexion, and both had gigantic bodies. Both wore saffron cloth, and both were very grave.
