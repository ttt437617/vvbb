Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.160

śraddhā-yukta hañā ihā śune yei jana

acire milaye tāre gopāla-carana

SYNONYMS

śraddhā-yukta — with faith and love; hañā — being; ihā — this narration; śune — hears; yei — which; jana — person; acire — very soon; milaye — gets; tāre — he; gopāla-carana — the lotus feet of Lord Gopāla.

TRANSLATION

One who hears this narration of Lord Gopāla with faith and love very soon attains the lotus feet of Lord Gopāla.
