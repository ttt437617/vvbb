Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.100

eka-sera anna rāndhi' kariha samarpana

tāhā khāñā tomāra sańge kariba gamana

SYNONYMS

eka-sera — one kilogram; anna — of rice; rāndhi' — cooking; kariha — do; samarpana — offering; tāhā — that; khāñā — eating; tomāra — of you; sańge — in the company; kariba — I shall do; gamana — walking.

TRANSLATION

"Cook one kilogram of rice daily and offer it. I shall eat that rice and follow behind you."
