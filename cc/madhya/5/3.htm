Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.3

calite calite āilā yājapura-grāma

varāha-thākura dekhi' karilā pranāma

SYNONYMS

calite calite — walking on and on; āilā — reached; yājapura-grāma — the village of Yājapura-grāma; varāha-thākura — the temple of Varāhadeva; dekhi' — seeing; karilā — offered; pranāma — obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Walking and walking, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His party finally arrived at Yājapura, on the river Vaitaranī. There He saw the temple of Varāhadeva and offered His obeisances unto Him.
