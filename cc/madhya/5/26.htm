Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.26

chota-vipra bale, — "tomāra strī-putra saba

bahu jñāti-goshthī tomāra bahuta bāndhava

SYNONYMS

chota-vipra — the young brāhmana; bale — says; tomāra — your; strī-putra — wife and sons; saba — all; bahu — many; jñāti — of family relations; goshthī — group; tomāra — your; bahuta — many; bāndhava — friends.

TRANSLATION

The young brāhmana said, "You have a wife and sons, and you have a large circle of relatives and friends.
