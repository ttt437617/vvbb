Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.5

katake āilā sākshi-gopāla dekhite

gopāla-saundarya dekhi' hailā ānandite

SYNONYMS

katake — in the town of Kataka (Cuttak); āilā — arrived; sākshi-gopāla — the witness Gopāla; dekhite — to see; gopāla — of the Deity of Gopāla; saundarya — the beauty; dekhi' — seeing; hailā — became; ānandite — very much pleased.

TRANSLATION

Afterwards, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the town of Kataka to see the temple of the witness Gopāla. When He saw the Deity of Gopāla, He was very much pleased by His beauty.
