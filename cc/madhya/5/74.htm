Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.74

tabe āmi gopālere sākshī kariñā

kahilāń tāńra pade minati kariñā

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; āmi — I; gopālere — to the Gopāla Deity; sākshī — the witness; kariñā — making; kahilāń — said; tāńra pade — at His lotus feet; minati — humility; kariñā — making.

TRANSLATION

"Accepting the Gopāla Deity as my witness, I then submitted the following at His lotus feet.
