Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.120

utkalera rājā purushottama-deva nāma

sei deśa jini' nila kariyā sańgrāma

SYNONYMS

utkalera — of Orissa; rājā — the King; purushottamadeva — Purushottama-deva; nāma — named; sei deśa — this country; jini' — conquering; nila — took; kariyā — executing; sańgrāma — fight.

TRANSLATION

Later there was a fight, and this country was conquered by King Purushottama-deva of Orissa.
