Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.30

tomāke kanyā diba, sabāke kari' tiraskāra

samśaya nā kara tumi, karaha svīkāra"

SYNONYMS

tomāke — to you; kanyā — the daughter; diba — I shall offer; sabāke — all others; kari' — doing; tiraskāra — neglecting; samśaya — doubt; nā — not; kara — do; tumi — you; karaha — just do; svīkāra — acceptance.

TRANSLATION

"My dear boy, I will give my daughter to you in charity, and I will neglect the position of all others. Don't doubt me in this regard; just accept my proposal."
