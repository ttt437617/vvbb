Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.6

premāveśe nritya-gīta kaila kata-kshana

āvishta hañā kaila gopāla stavana

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love of God; nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; kaila — performed; kata-kshana — for some time; āvishta hañā — being overwhelmed; kaila — offered; gopāla stavana — prayers to Gopāla.

TRANSLATION

While there, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in chanting and dancing for some time, and being overwhelmed, He offered many prayers to Gopāla.
