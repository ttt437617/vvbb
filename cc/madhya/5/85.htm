Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.85

ińhāra punye krishne āni' sākshī bolāiba

tabe ei viprera satya-pratijñā rākhiba

SYNONYMS

ińhāra punye — by his piety; krishne — Lord Krishna; āni' — bringing; sākshī — witness; bolāiba — I shall call; tabe — at that time; ei viprera — of this brāhmana; satya — truthful; pratijñā — the promise; rākhiba — I shall keep.

TRANSLATION

"By the piety of the elderly brāhmana, I shall call the Supreme Personality of Godhead as a witness. Thus I shall keep his truthful promise intact."
