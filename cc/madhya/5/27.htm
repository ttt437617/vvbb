Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.27

tā'-sabāra sammati vinā nahe kanyā-dāna

rukminīra pitā bhīshmaka tāhāte pramāna

SYNONYMS

tā'-sabāra — of all of them; sammati — consent; vinā — without; nahe — not; kanyā-dāna — giving the daughter in charity; rukminīra — of Queen Rukminī; pitā — father; bhīshmaka — Bhīshmaka; tāhāte — of that; pramāna — evidence.

TRANSLATION

"Without the consent of all your friends and relatives, it is not possible to give me your daughter in charity. Just consider the story of Queen Rukminī and her father, Bhīshmaka.
