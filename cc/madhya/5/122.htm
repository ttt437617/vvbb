Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.122

purushottama-deva sei bada bhakta ārya

gopāla-carane māge, — 'cala mora rājya'

SYNONYMS

purushottama-deva — King Purushottama-deva; sei — that; bada — very great; bhakta — devotee; ārya — Āryan; gopāla-carane — at the lotus feet of Gopāla; māge — begs; cala — please come; mora — my; rājya — to the kingdom.

TRANSLATION

King Purushottama-deva was a great devotee and was advanced in the civilization of the Āryans. He begged at the lotus feet of Gopāla, "Please come to my kingdom."
