Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.121

sei rājā jini' nila tāńra simhāsana

'mānikya-simhāsana' nāma aneka ratana

SYNONYMS

sei rājā — that King (Mahārāja Purushottama-deva); jini' — conquering; nila — took; tāńra — his; simha-āsana — the throne; mānikya-simhāsana — the throne known as Mānikya-simhāsana; nāma — named; aneka — various; ratana — bedecked with jewels.

TRANSLATION

That King was victorious over the King of Vidyānagara, and he took possession of his throne, the Mānikya-simhāsana, which was bedecked with many jewels.
