Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.41

jñāti loka kahe, — 'morā tomāke chādiba'

strī-putra kahe, — 'visha khāiyā mariba'

SYNONYMS

jñāti loka — the relatives; kahe — answer; morā — we all; tomāke — you; chādiba — will give up; strī — wife; putra — sons; kahe — say; visha — poison; khāiyā — drinking; mariba — we shall die.

TRANSLATION

The relatives unanimously said, "If you give your daughter to that boy, we shall give up all connection with you." Indeed, his wife and sons declared, "If such a thing happens, we shall take poison and die."
