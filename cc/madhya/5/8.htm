Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.8

nityānanda-gosāñi yabe tīrtha bhramilā

sākshi-gopāla dekhibāre kataka āilā

SYNONYMS

nityānanda-gosāñi — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; yabe — when; tīrtha bhramilā — traveled to the places of pilgrimage; sākshi-gopāla — the witness Gopāla; dekhibāre — to see; kataka — to the town of Kataka; āilā — came.

TRANSLATION

Previously, when Nityānanda Prabhu had toured all over India to see different places of pilgrimage, He also had come to see Sākshi-gopāla at Kataka.
