Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.139

ei-mata mahā-rańge se rātri vañciyā

prabhāte calilā mańgala-ārati dekhiñā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahā-rańge — in great pleasure; se — that; rātri — night; vañciyā — passing; prabhāte — in the morning; calilā — departed; mańgala-ārati — the mańgala-ārati performance; dekhiñā — seeing.

TRANSLATION

Thus with great pleasure Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed that night in the temple. After seeing the mańgala-ārati ceremony in the morning, He started on His journey.
