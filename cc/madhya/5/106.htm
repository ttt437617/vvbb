Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.106

eta bhāvi' sei vipra phiriyā cāhila

hāsiñā gopāla-deva tathāya rahila

SYNONYMS

eta bhāvi' — thinking like this; sei — that; vipra — brāhmana; phiriyā — turning; cāhila — saw; hāsiñā — smiling; gopāla-deva — Lord Gopāladeva, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; tathāya — there; rahila — stayed.

TRANSLATION

Thinking this, the brāhmana turned to look back, and He saw that Gopāla, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, was standing there smiling.
