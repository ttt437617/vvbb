Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.79

bada-viprera mane, — 'krishna bada dayāvān

avaśya mora vākya teńho karibe pramāna'

SYNONYMS

bada-viprera mane — within the mind of the elderly brāhmana; krishna — Lord Krishna; bada — very; dayāvān — merciful; avaśya — certainly; mora — my; vākya — words; teńho — He; karibe — will make; pramāna — evidence.

TRANSLATION

The elderly brāhmana thought, "Since Lord Krishna is very merciful, He will certainly come to prove my statement."
