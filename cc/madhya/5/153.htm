Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.153

nīlācale āni' mora sabe hita kailā

sabe danda-dhana chila, tāhā nā rākhilā

SYNONYMS

nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; āni' — bringing; mora — My; sabe — all of you; hita — benefit; kailā — did; sabe — only; danda-dhana — one staff; chila — there was; tāhā — that also; nā — not; rākhilā — you kept.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You have all benefited Me by bringing Me to Nīlācala. However, My only possession was that one staff, and you have not kept it.
