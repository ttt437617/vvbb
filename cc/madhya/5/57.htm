Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.57

vipra kahe, — 'śuna, loka, mora nivedana

kabe ki baliyāchi, mora nāhika smarana'

SYNONYMS

vipra kahe — the senior brāhmana replied; śuna — please hear; loka — all people; mora — my; nivedana — submission; kabe — when; ki — what; baliyāchi — I have said; mora — my; nāhika — there is not; smarana — remembrance.

TRANSLATION

The elderly brāhmana said, "My dear friends, please hear what I have to submit. I do not exactly remember making a promise like that."
