Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.33

chota-vipra bale, — "thākura, tumi mora sākshī

tomā sākshī bolāimu, yadi anyathā dekhi"

SYNONYMS

chota-vipra bale — the younger brāhmana replied; thākura — my dear Lord Gopāla; tumi — You; mora — my; sākshī — witness; tomā — unto You; sākshī — witness; bolāimu — I shall call as; yadi — if; anyathā — otherwise; dekhi — I see.

TRANSLATION

Then the younger brāhmana addressed the Deity, saying, "My dear Lord, You are my witness. I shall call for You to testify if it is necessary later on."
