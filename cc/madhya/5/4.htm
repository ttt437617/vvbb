Madhya-līlāChapter 5: The Activities of Sākshi-gopāla

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 5.4

nritya-gīta kaila preme bahuta stavana

yājapure se rātri karilā yāpana

SYNONYMS

nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; kaila — executed; preme — in love of Godhead; bahuta — various; stavana — prayers; yājapure — in the village of Yājapura; se rātri — that night; karilā — did; yāpana — passing.

TRANSLATION

In the temple of Varāhadeva, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in chanting and dancing and offered prayers. He passed that night in the temple.
