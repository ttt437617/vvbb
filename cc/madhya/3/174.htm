Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.174

tomā-sabāra ājñā vinā calilāma vrindāvana

yāite nārila, vighna kaila nivartana

SYNONYMS

tomā-sabāra — of all of you; ājñā — order; vinā — without; calilāma — I started; vrindāvana — for Vrindāvana; yāite nārila — not able to go; vighna — some obstruction; kaila — did; nivartana — made to return.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed them all, "Without your order, I tried to go to Vrindāvana. There was some obstacle, however, and I had to return.
