Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.89

sei vyañjana ācārya punah karena pūrana

ei mata punah punah pariveśe vyañjana

SYNONYMS

sei vyañjana — that half-finished vegetable; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; punah — again; karena — does; pūrana — filling; ei mata — in this way; punah punah — again and again; pariveśe — distributes; vyañjana — vegetables.

TRANSLATION

As soon as half of the vegetable in the pot was finished, Advaita Ācārya filled it up again. In this way, as the Lord finished half of a preparation, Advaita Ācārya again and again filled it up.
