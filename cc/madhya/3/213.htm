Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.213

kata dūra giyā prabhu kari' yoda hāta

ācārye prabodhi' kahe kichu mishta bāta

SYNONYMS

kata dūra giyā — after going some distance; prabhu — the Lord; kari' — making; yoda hāta — folded hands; ācārye — Advaita Ācārya; prabodhi' — pacifying; kahe — says; kichu — something; mishta bāta — sweet words.

TRANSLATION

After He had followed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for some distance, Advaita Ācārya was petitioned by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with folded hands. The Lord spoke the following sweet words.
