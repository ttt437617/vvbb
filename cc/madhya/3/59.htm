Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.59

ārati kariyā krishne karā'la śayana

ācārya āsi' prabhure tabe kailā nivedana

SYNONYMS

ārati kariyā — after finishing the ārati; krishne — Lord Krishna; karā'la — made to do; śayana — lying down to rest; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; āsi' — coming; prabhure — unto Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tabe — then; kailā — made; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

After ārati was performed for the Deities in the temple, Lord Krishna was made to lie down to rest. Advaita Ācārya then came out to submit something to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
