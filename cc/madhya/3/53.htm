Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.53

saghrita-pāyasa nava-mrit-kundikā bhariñā

tina pātre ghanāvarta-dugdha rākheta dhariñā

SYNONYMS

sa-ghrita-pāyasa — sweet rice mixed with ghee; nava-mrit-kundikā — new earthen pots; bhariñā — filling; tina pātre — in three pots; ghana-āvarta-dugdha — finely condensed milk; rākheta — were kept; dhariñā — fixing.

TRANSLATION

Along with the various vegetables was sweet rice mixed with ghee. This was kept in new earthen pots. Earthen pots filled with highly condensed milk were placed in three places.
