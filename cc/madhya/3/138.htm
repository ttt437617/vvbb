Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.138

nadīyā-nagarera loka — strī-bālaka-vriddha

saba loka āilā, haila sańghatta samriddha

SYNONYMS

nadīyā — known as Nadia; nagarera — of the city; loka — the people; strī — women; bālaka — boys; vriddha — old men; saba loka — all people; āilā — came; haila — was; sańghatta — crowd; samriddha — increased.

TRANSLATION

In this way, all the people of the town of Nadia — including all women, boys and old men — came there. Thus the crowd increased.
