Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.195

muñi adhama tomāra nā pāba daraśana

kemate dhariba ei pāpishtha jīvana

SYNONYMS

muñi — I; adhama — the lowest of men; tomāra — Your; nā — not; pāba — will get; daraśana — seeing; kemate — how; dhariba — shall I maintain; ei — this; pāpishtha — sinful; jīvana — life.

TRANSLATION

"Because I am the lowest among men, I shall not be able to see You. How shall I maintain my sinful life?"
