Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.104

sugandhi candane lipta kaila kalevara

sugandhi pushpa-mālā āni' dila hridaya-upara

SYNONYMS

su-gandhi — fragrant; candane — in sandalwood; lipta — smeared; kaila — made; kalevara — the bodies; su-gandhi — very fragrant; pushpa-mālā — flower garlands; āni' — bringing; dila — gave; hridaya-upara — on the chests.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Ācārya then smeared the bodies of the Lords with sandalwood pulp and then placed very fragrant flower garlands on Their chests.
