Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.60

grihera bhitare prabhu karuna gamana

dui bhāi āilā tabe karite bhojana

SYNONYMS

grihera bhitare — within the room; prabhu — Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karuna — kindly do; gamana — entering; dui bhāi — the two brothers, Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu; āilā — came; tabe — then; karite bhojana — to partake of the prasādam.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Prabhu said, "My dear Lords, kindly enter this room." The two brothers, Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, then came forward to take the prasādam.
