Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.211

tāńre pradakshina kari' karila gamana

ethā ācāryera ghare uthila krandana

SYNONYMS

tāńre — mother Śacī; pradakshina kari' — circumambulating; karila — did; gamana — going; ethā — there; ācāryera — of Advaita Ācārya; ghare — in the house; uthila — there arose; krandana — crying.

TRANSLATION

When everything was arranged, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated His mother and then started for Jagannātha Purī. In the house of Advaita Ācārya there arose tumultuous crying.
