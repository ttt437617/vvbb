Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.111

āise yāya loka harshe, nāhi samādhāna

lokera sańghatte dina haila avasāna

SYNONYMS

āise — come; yāya — go; loka — all people; harshe — in great pleasure; nāhi — there is not; samādhāna — calculation; lokera — of the people; sańghatte — in crowds; dina — the day; haila — there was; avasāna — the end.

TRANSLATION

People came and went with great pleasure. There was no calculating how many people assembled there before the day was over.
