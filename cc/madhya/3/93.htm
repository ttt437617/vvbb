Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.93

nityānanda kahe — āmāra peta nā bharila

lañā yāha, tora anna kichu nā khāila

SYNONYMS

nityānanda kahe — Nityānanda Prabhu said; āmāra — My; peta — belly; nā — not; bharila — filled; lañā — taking away; yāha — go; tora — Your; anna — food; kichu nā khāila — I have not eaten anything.

TRANSLATION

Again Nityānanda Prabhu jokingly said, "My belly is not yet filled up. Please take away Your food. I have not taken the least of it."
