Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.34

prabhu kahe, — nityānanda āmāre vañcilā

gańgāke āniyā more yamunā kahilā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; āmāre — Me; vañcilā — has cheated; gańgāke — to the bank of the Ganges; āniyā — bringing; more — Me; yamunā — the river Yamunā; kahilā — informed.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "Nityānanda has cheated Me. He has brought Me to the bank of the Ganges and told Me that it was the Yamunā."
