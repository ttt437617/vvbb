Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.21

prabhu laye yāba āmi tāńhāra mandire

sāvadhāne rahena yena naukā lañā tīre

SYNONYMS

prabhu laye — taking the Lord; yāba — shall go; āmi — I; tāńhāra — of Him; mandire — to the house; sāvadhāne — very carefully; rahena — let Him stay; yena — there; naukā — boat; lañā — taking; tīre — on the bank.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nityānanda Gosvāmī told him, "I shall take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to the bank of the Ganges at Śāntipura, and Advaita Ācārya should carefully stay there on shore with a boat.
