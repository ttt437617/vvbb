Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.27

aho bhāgya, yamunāre pāiluń daraśana

eta bali' yamunāra karena stavana

SYNONYMS

aho bhāgya — oh, My great fortune; yamunāre — of the river Yamunā; pāiluń — I have gotten; daraśana — vision; eta bali' — after saying this; yamunāra — of the river Yamunā; karena — does; stavana — praising.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "Oh, what good fortune! Now I have seen the river Yamunā." Thus thinking the Ganges to be the river Yamunā, Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to offer prayers to it.
