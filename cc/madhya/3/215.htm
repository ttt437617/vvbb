Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.215

eta bali' prabhu tāńre kari' ālińgana

nivritti kariyā kaila svacchanda gamana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — unto Him; kari' — doing; ālińgana — embracing; nivritti — stop; kariyā — making; kaila — did; svacchanda — without anxiety; gamana — going toward Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Advaita Ācārya and stopped Him from following any further. Then, without anxiety, He proceeded to Jagannātha Purī.
