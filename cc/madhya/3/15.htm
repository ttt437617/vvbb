Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.15

tā'-sabāra stuti kare, — tomarā bhāgyavān

kritārtha karile more śunāñā hari-nāma

SYNONYMS

tā'-sabāra — of all of them; stuti kare — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu praised the behavior; tomarā — you; bhāgyavān — fortunate; krita-artha — successful; karile — you have made; more — to Me; śunāñā — by chanting; hari-nāma — the holy name of Lord Hari.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus blessed them all, saying that they were all fortunate. In this way He praised them, and He felt very successful because they chanted the holy name of Lord Hari.
