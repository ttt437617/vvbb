Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.14

śuni' tā-sabāra nikata gelā gaurahari

'bala' 'bala' bale sabāra śire hasta dhari'

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; tā-sabāra — of all of them; nikata — near; gelā — went; gaurahari — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bala bala — go on speaking, go on speaking; bale — He said; sabāra — of all of them; śire hasta dhari' — keeping His hand on their heads.

TRANSLATION

When He heard all the cowherd boys also chanting "Hari! Hari!" Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very pleased. He approached them, put His hand on their heads and said, "Go on chanting like that."
