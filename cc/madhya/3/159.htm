Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.159

ācārya-gosāñira bhāndāra — akshaya, avyaya

yata dravya vyaya kare tata dravya haya

SYNONYMS

ācārya-gosāñira — of Advaita Ācārya; bhāndāra — storehouse; akshaya — inexhaustible; avyaya — indestructible; yata — all; dravya — commodities; vyaya — expenditure; kare — does; tata — so much; dravya — commodity; haya — becomes filled.

TRANSLATION

The supplies of Advaita Ācārya were inexhaustible and indestructible. As many goods and commodities as He used, just as many again appeared.
