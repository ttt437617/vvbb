Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.125

rātri-dine pode mana soyāsti nā pāń

yāhāń gele kānu pāń, tāhāń udi' yāń

SYNONYMS

rātri-dine — day and night; pode — burns; mana — mind; soyāsti — rest; nā — not; pāń — I get; yāhāń — where; gele — if going; kānu pāń — I can get Krishna; tāhāń — there; udi' — flying; yāń — I go.

TRANSLATION

"'My feeling is like this: My mind burns day and night, and I can get no rest. If there were someplace I could go to meet Krishna, I would immediately fly there.'"
