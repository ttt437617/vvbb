Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.25

prabhu kahe, — kata dūre āche vrindāvana

teńho kahena, — kara ei yamunā daraśana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; kata dūre — how far; āche — there is; vrindāvana — Vrindāvana-dhāma; teńho kahena — He replied; kara — just do; ei — this; yamunā — Yamunā River; daraśana — seeing.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord asked Nityānanda Prabhu how far it was to Vrindāvana, Nityānanda replied, "Just see! Here is the river Yamunā."
