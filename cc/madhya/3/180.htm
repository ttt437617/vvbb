Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.180

prabhura nivedana tāńre sakala kahila

śuni' śacī jagan-mātā kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of the Lord; nivedana — submission; tāńre — unto her; sakala — all; kahila — told; śuni' — hearing this; śacī — mother Śacī; jagat-mātā — the mother of the universe; kahite — to say; lāgila — began.

TRANSLATION

When they submitted Lord Caitanya's statement, mother Śacī, who is the mother of the universe, began to speak.
