Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.65

aiche anna ye krishnake karāya bhojana

janme janme śire dharoń tāńhāra carana

SYNONYMS

aiche — in this way; anna — the eatables; ye — anyone who; krishnake — unto Krishna; karāya — made to do; bhojana — eating; janme janme — birth after birth; śire — on My head; dharoń — I keep; tāńhāra — his; carana — lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu approved of all the methods employed in cooking and offering food to Krishna. Indeed, He was so pleased that He said, "Frankly, I will personally take the lotus feet of anyone who can offer Krishna such nice food and place those lotus feet on My head birth after birth."
