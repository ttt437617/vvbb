Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.107

tabe ta' ācārya sańge lañā dui jane

karila icchāya bhojana, ye āchila mane

SYNONYMS

tabe ta' — thereafter; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; sańge — with; lañā — taking; dui jane — the two persons, namely Mukunda and Haridāsa; karila — did; icchāya — according to desire; bhojana — eating; ye āchila mane — whatever there was in mind.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon Advaita Ācārya took prasādam with Mukunda and Haridāsa, and they all wholeheartedly ate as much as they desired.
