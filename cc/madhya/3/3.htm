Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.3

cabbiśa vatsara-śesha yei māgha-māsa

tāra śukla-pakshe prabhu karilā sannyāsa

SYNONYMS

cabbiśa — twenty-fourth; vatsara — of the year; śesha — at the end; yei — that; māgha-māsa — the month of Māgha (January-February); tāra — of that; śukla-pakshe — in the waxing period of the moon; prabhu — the Lord; karilā — accepted; sannyāsa — the sannyāsa order of life.

TRANSLATION

At the end of His twenty-fourth year, in the month of Māgha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the sannyāsa order during the waxing period of the moon.
