Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.101

ācārya kahe — nā kariba sannyāsi-nimantrana

sannyāsī nāśila mora saba smriti-dharma

SYNONYMS

ācārya kahe — Advaita Ācārya said; nā kariba — I shall never do; sannyāsi-nimantrana — invitation to the sannyāsīs; sannyāsī — a sannyāsī; nāśila — has spoiled; mora — My; saba — all; smriti-dharma — regulative principles of the smriti-śāstra.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya replied, "I shall never again invite another sannyāsī, for it is a sannyāsī who has spoiled all My brahminical smriti regulations."
