Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.192

eta bali' sabākāre īshat hāsiñā

vidāya karila prabhu sammāna kariñā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; sabākāre — to all the devotees; īshat hāsiñā — smiling very mildly; vidāya karila — bid them farewell; prabhu — the Lord; sammāna kariñā — showing all respect.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, offering due respects to all the devotees and smiling very mildly, bade them farewell.
