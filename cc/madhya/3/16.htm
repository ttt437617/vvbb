Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.16

gupte tā-sabāke āni' thākura nityānanda

śikhāilā sabākāre kariyā prabandha

SYNONYMS

gupte — in confidence; tā-sabāke — unto all the cowherd boys; āni' — taking them; thākura nityānanda — Nityānanda Thākura; śikhāilā — instructed; sabākāre — all of them; kariyā prabandha — by making a reasonable story.

TRANSLATION

Calling all the boys in confidence and telling a reasonable story, Nityānanda Prabhu instructed them as follows.
