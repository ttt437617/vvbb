Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.32

tumi ta' ācārya-gosāñi, ethā kene āilā

āmi vrindāvane, tumi ke-mate jānilā

SYNONYMS

tumi — You are; ta' — certainly; ācārya-gosāñi — Advaita Ācārya; ethā — here; kene — why; āilā — You have come; āmi — I; vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; tumi — You; ke-mate — how; jānilā — knew.

TRANSLATION

Still in His ecstasy, the Lord asked Advaita Ācārya, "Why did You come here? How did You know that I was in Vrindāvana?"
