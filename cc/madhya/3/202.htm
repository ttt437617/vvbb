Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.202

ānandita hañā śacī karena randhana

sukhe bhojana kare prabhu lañā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

ānandita hañā — being pleased; śacī — mother Śacī; karena — does; randhana — cooking; sukhe — in happiness; bhojana — eating; kare — does; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — accompanied by; bhakta-gana — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

Mother Śacī cooked with great pleasure, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, along with the devotees, accepted the prasādam with great pleasure.
