Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.108

śāntipurera loka śuni' prabhura āgamana

dekhite āilā loka prabhura carana

SYNONYMS

śāntipurera loka — all the people of Śāntipura; śuni' — hearing; prabhura āgamana — the arrival of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhite āilā — came to see; loka — all the people; prabhura carana — the lotus feet of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

When the people of Śāntipura heard that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was staying there, they all immediately came to see His lotus feet.
