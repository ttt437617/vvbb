Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.103

lavańga elācī-bīja — uttama rasa-vāsa

tulasī-mañjarī saha dila mukha-vāsa

SYNONYMS

lavańga — cloves; elācī — cardamom; bīja — seeds; uttama — very nice; rasa-vāsa — tasteful spices; tulasī-mañjarī — the flowers of tulasī; saha — with; dila — gave; mukha-vāsa — perfume of the mouth.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Ācārya fed the two Lords cloves and cardamom mixed with tulasī flowers. Thus there was a good flavor within Their mouths.
