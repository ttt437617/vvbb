Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.100

śateka sannyāsī yadi karāha bhojana

tabe ei aparādha ha-ibe khandana

SYNONYMS

śateka sannyāsī — one hundred sannyāsīs; yadi — if; karāha — You make; bhojana — the eating; tabe — then; ei — this; aparādha — offense; ha-ibe — there will be; khandana — nullification.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu continued, "If you invite at least one hundred sannyāsīs to Your home and feed them sumptuously, Your offense will be nullified."
