Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.198

tabe ta' ācārya kahe vinaya kariñā

dina dui-cāri raha kripā ta' kariñā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; ta' — certainly; ācārya kahe — Advaita Ācārya says; vinaya kariñā — offering all respect; dina dui-cāri — another two or four days; raha — kindly remain; kripā — mercy; ta' — certainly; kariñā — showing.

TRANSLATION

After that, Advaita Ācārya respectfully requested Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu to show Him mercy by remaining another two or four days.
