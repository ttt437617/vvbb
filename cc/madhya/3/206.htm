Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.206

āra dina prabhu kahe saba bhakta-gane

nija-nija-grihe sabe karaha gamane

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; prabhu — the Lord; kahe — says; saba — all; bhakta-gane — to the devotees; nija-nija-grihe — to your respective homes; sabe — all; karaha — do; gamane — returning.

TRANSLATION

The next day, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested all the devotees to return to their respective homes.
