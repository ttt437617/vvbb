Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.128

jara-jara haila prabhu bhāvera prahāre

bhūmite padila, śvāsa nāhika śarīre

SYNONYMS

jara-jara — tottering; haila — there was; prabhu — the Lord; bhāvera — of the ecstatic moods; prahāre — in the onslaught; bhūmite — on the ground; padila — fell; śvāsa — breathing; nāhika — there was not; śarīre — in the body.

TRANSLATION

The entire body of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to totter due to the onslaught of various ecstatic symptoms. As a result, He immediately fell to the ground, and His breathing almost stopped.
