Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.131

nityānanda sańge bule prabhuke dhariñā

ācārya, haridāsa bule pāche ta' nāciñā

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; sańge — with; bule — walks; prabhuke — the Lord; dhariñā — catching; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; bule — walk; pāche — behind; ta — certainly; nāciñā — dancing.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda began to walk with Caitanya Mahāprabhu to see that He would not fall, and Advaita Ācārya and Haridāsa Thākura followed Them, dancing.
