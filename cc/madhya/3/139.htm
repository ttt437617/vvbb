Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.139

prātah-kritya kari' kare nāma-sańkīrtana

śacīmātā lañā āilā advaita-bhavana

SYNONYMS

prātah-kritya — the morning duties; kari' — finishing; kare — does; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting the Hare Krishna mantra; śacī-mātā — mother Śacī; lañā — with; āilā — came; advaita-bhavana — at the house of Advaita Ācārya.

TRANSLATION

In the morning, after regular duties were completed and the Lord was chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra, the people accompanied Śacīmātā to the house of Advaita Ācārya.
