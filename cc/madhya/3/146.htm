Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.146

tomāra pālita deha, janma tomā haite

koti janme tomāra rina nā pāri śodhite

SYNONYMS

tomāra — your; pālita — raised; deha — body; janma — birth; tomā — you; haite — from; koti — millions; janme — in births; tomāra — your; rina — debt; nā — not; pāri — I am able; śodhite — to repay.

TRANSLATION

"This body was raised by you, and it comes from you. I cannot repay this debt even in millions of births.
