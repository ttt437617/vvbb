Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.105

ācārya karite cāhe pāda-samvāhana

sańkucita hañā prabhu balena vacana

SYNONYMS

ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; karite — to do; cāhe — wants; pāda-samvāhana — massaging the feet; sańkucita — hesitant; hañā — becoming; prabhu — the Lord; balena — says; vacana — the words.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord lay down on the bed, Advaita Ācārya waited to massage His legs, but the Lord was very hesitant and spoke as follows to Advaita Ācārya.
