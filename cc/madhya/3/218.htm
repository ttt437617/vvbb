Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.218

advaita-grihe prabhura vilāsa śune yei jana

acire milaye tāńre krishna-prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

advaita-grihe — at the house of Advaita Ācārya; prabhura — of the Lord; vilāsa — the pastimes; śune — hears; yei — one who; jana — person; acire — very soon; milaye — meets; tāńre — him; krishna-prema-dhana — the riches of love of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

If one hears the activities of the Lord at the house of Advaita Ācārya, he will certainly very soon attain the riches of love of Krishna.
