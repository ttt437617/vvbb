Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.83

nityānanda bale — yabe kaile nimantrana

tata dite cāha, yata kariye bhojana

SYNONYMS

nityānanda bale — Lord Nityānanda said; yabe — when; kaile — You have done; nimantrana — invitation; tata — so much; dite cāha — You must supply; yata — as much as; kariye bhojana — I can eat.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda Prabhu replied, "Whatever I may be, You have invited Me. Therefore You must supply as much as I want to eat."
