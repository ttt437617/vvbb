Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.113

nityānanda gosāñi bule ācārya dhariñā

haridāsa pāche nāce harashita hañā

SYNONYMS

nityānanda gosāñi — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; bule — began to move, dancing; ācārya dhariñā — following Advaita Ācārya; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; pāche — behind; nāce — dances; harashita hañā — being pleased.

TRANSLATION

When Advaita Ācārya began to dance, Nityānanda Prabhu began dancing behind Him. Haridāsa Thākura, being very pleased, also began dancing behind Him.
