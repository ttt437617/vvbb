Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.133

tina dina upavāse kariyā bhojana

uddanda-nrityete prabhura haila pariśrama

SYNONYMS

tina dina — three days; upavāse — in fasting; kariyā — doing; bhojana — eating; uddanda — jumping high; nrityete — in dancing; prabhura — of the Lord; haila — there was; pariśrama — fatigue.

TRANSLATION

The Lord had been fasting for three days, and after that period He took eatables sumptuously. Thus when He danced and jumped high, He became a little fatigued.
