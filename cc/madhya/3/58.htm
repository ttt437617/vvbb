Madhya-līlāChapter 3: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Stay at the House of Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 3.58

āratira kāle dui prabhu bolāila

prabhu-sańge sabe āsi' ārati dekhila

SYNONYMS

āratira kāle — during the time of ārati; dui prabhu — Lord Nityānanda and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bolāila — He called; prabhu-sańge — with the Lords; sabe — all other people; āsi' — coming there; ārati — the ārati ceremony; dekhila — observed.

TRANSLATION

It is the system, after offering food, to perform bhoga-ārati. Advaita Prabhu asked the two brothers, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, to come see the ārati. The two Lords and all others present went to see the ārati ceremony.
