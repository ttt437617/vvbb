Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.223

bhattācārya kaila tabe pāda prakshālana

gharera bhitare gelā karite bhojana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kaila — performed; tabe — thereafter; pāda prakshālana — washing the feet; gharera bhitare — within the room; gelā — entered; karite bhojana — to take lunch.

TRANSLATION

After Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya washed the Lord's feet, the Lord entered the room to take His lunch.
