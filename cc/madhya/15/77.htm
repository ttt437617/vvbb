Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.77

śasya samarpana kari' bāhire dheyāna

śasya khāñā krishna kare śūnya bhājana

SYNONYMS

śasya — the pulp; samarpana kari' — offering; bāhire — outside the temple room; dheyāna — meditates; śasya khāñā — eating the pulp; krishna — Lord Krishna; kare — makes; śūnya — vacant; bhājana — the plate.

TRANSLATION

"After offering the pulp, he meditates outside the temple door. In the meantime, Lord Krishna, having eaten the pulp, leaves the plate empty.
