Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.242

govardhana-yajñe anna khāilā rāśi rāśi

tāra lekhāya ei anna nahe eka grāsī

SYNONYMS

govardhana-yajñe — in the Govardhana-pūjā sacrifice; anna — food; khāilā — You ate; rāśi rāśi — stacks; tāra — to that; lekhāya — in comparison; ei — this; anna — food; nahe — not; eka grāsī — one morsel.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed," Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya continued, "at the Govardhana-pūjā ceremony You ate stacks of rice. In comparison to that, this small quantity is not even a morsel for You.
