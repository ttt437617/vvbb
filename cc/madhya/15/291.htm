Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.291

prabhu kahe, — amogha śiśu, tomāra bālaka

bālaka-dosha nā laya pitā, tāhāte pālaka

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; amogha śiśu — Amogha is a child; tomāra bālaka — your son; bālaka-dosha — the offense of a child; nā laya — does not accept; pitā — the father; tāhāte — unto him; pālaka — the maintainer.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Amogha is a child and your son. The father does not take the faults of his son seriously, especially when he is maintaining him.
