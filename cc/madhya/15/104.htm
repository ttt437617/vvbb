Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.104

prabhu kahena, — 'krishna-sevā', 'vaishnava-sevana'

'nirantara kara krishna-nāma-sańkīrtana'

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — the Lord replied; krishna-sevā — serving Krishna; vaishnava-sevana — abiding by the orders of Vaishnavas; nirantara — without cessation; kara — do; krishna-nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy name of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Without cessation continue chanting the holy name of Lord Krishna. Whenever possible, serve Him and His devotees, the Vaishnavas."
