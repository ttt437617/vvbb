Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.276

sārvabhauma-sańge tomāra 'kalusha' haila kshaya

'kalmasha' ghucile jīva 'krishna-nāma' laya

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma-sańge — by the association of Sārvabhauma; tomāra — your; kalusha — contamination; haila kshaya — is now vanquished; kalmasha — contamination; ghucile — when dispelled; jīva — the living entity; krishna-nāma — the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; laya — can chant.

TRANSLATION

"However, due to the association of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, all your contamination is now vanquished. When a person's heart is cleansed of all contamination, he is able to chant the mahā-mantra, Hare Krishna.
