Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.61

kibā āmi anna-pātre bhrame nā bādila!'

eta cinti' pāka-pātra yāñā dekhila

SYNONYMS

kibā — or; āmi — I; anna-pātre — on the plate for food; bhrame — by mistake; nā bādila — did not put anything; eta cinti' — thinking this; pāka-pātra — the kitchen pots; yāñā dekhila — went and saw.

TRANSLATION

"She thought, 'Perhaps by mistake I did not put any food on the plate.' So thinking, she went into the kitchen and saw the pots.
