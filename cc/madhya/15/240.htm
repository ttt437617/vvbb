Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.240

dvārakāte shola-sahasra mahishī-mandire

ashtādaśa mātā, āra yādavera ghare

SYNONYMS

dvārakāte — at Dvārakā-dhāma; shola-sahasra — sixteen thousand; mahishī — queens; mandire — palaces; ashtādaśa mātā — eighteen mothers; āra — and; yādavera ghare — in the house of the Yadu dynasty.

TRANSLATION

"At Dvārakā, You keep sixteen thousand queens in sixteen thousand palaces. Also, there are eighteen mothers and numerous friends and relatives of the Yadu dynasty.
