Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.224

annādi dekhiyā prabhu vismita hañā

bhattācārye kahe kichu bhańgi kariyā

SYNONYMS

anna-ādi dekhiyā — seeing the arrangement of food; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vismita hañā — being astonished; bhattācārye kahe — said to the Bhattācārya; kichu — some; bhańgi — gesture; kariyā — making.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was a little astonished to see the gorgeous arrangement, and gesturing, He spoke to Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
