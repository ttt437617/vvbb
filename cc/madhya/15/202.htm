Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.202

bhattācāryera grihe saba dravya āche bhari'

yebā śāka-phalādika, ānāila āhari'

SYNONYMS

bhattācāryera grihe — at the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; saba dravya — all kinds of ingredients; āche — there are; bhari' — filling; yebā — whatever; śāka — spinach; phala-ādika — fruits and so on; ānāila — he brought; āhari' — collecting.

TRANSLATION

At Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya's house, there was always a full stock of food. Whatever spinach, vegetables, fruit and so on were required, he collected and brought back home.
