Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.162

jīvera duhkha dekhi' mora hridaya bidare

sarva-jīvera pāpa prabhu deha' mora śire

SYNONYMS

jīvera — of all conditioned souls; duhkha dekhi' — by seeing the sufferings; mora — my; hridaya — heart; bidare — breaks; sarva-jīvera — of all living entities; pāpa — the sinful reactions; prabhu — my dear Lord; deha' — just put; mora śire — upon my head.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, my heart breaks to see the sufferings of all the conditioned souls; therefore I request You to transfer the karma of their sinful lives upon my head.
