Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.286

prabhu dekhi' sārvabhauma dharilā carane

prabhu tāńre ālińgiyā vasilā āsane

SYNONYMS

prabhu dekhi' — seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; dharilā carane — caught hold of His feet; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; ālińgiyā — embracing; vasilā āsane — sat down on the seat.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing the Lord, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya immediately caught hold of His lotus feet. The Lord embraced him and then sat down.
