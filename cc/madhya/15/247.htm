Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.247

teńho yadi prasāda dite hailā āna-mana

amogha āsi' anna dekhi' karaye nindana

SYNONYMS

teńho — he (the Bhattācārya); yadi — when; prasāda dite — supplying the prasādam; hailā — became; āna-mana — inattentive; amogha — Amogha; āsi' — coming; anna dekhi' — seeing the food; karaye nindana — began blaspheming.

TRANSLATION

However, as soon as the Bhattācārya began distributing prasādam and was a little inattentive, Amogha came in. Seeing the quantity of food, he began to blaspheme.
