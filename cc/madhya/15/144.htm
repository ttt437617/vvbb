Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.144

āmāre kahena, — āmi tomāra kińkara

tomāra ājñākārī āmi nāhi svatantara

SYNONYMS

āmāre kahena — he said unto Me; āmi — I; tomāra kińkara — Your servant; tomāra ājñā-kārī — Your order-carrier; āmi — I; nāhi — am not; svatantara — independent.

TRANSLATION

"Murāri Gupta then replied, 'I am Your servant and Your order-carrier. I have no independent existence.'
