Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.117

śuni' harshe kahe prabhu — "kahile niścaya

yāńhā haite krishna-bhakti sei guru haya"

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; harshe — in great jubilation; kahe prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; kahile niścaya — you have spoken correctly; yāńhā haite — from whom; krishna-bhakti — devotion to Krishna; sei — that person; guru haya — is the spiritual master.

TRANSLATION

Hearing Mukunda dāsa give this proper decision, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu confirmed it, saying, "Yes, it is correct. One who awakens devotion to Krishna is certainly the spiritual master."
