Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.3

jaya śrī-caitanya-caritāmrita-srotā-gana

caitanya-caritāmrita — yāńra prāna-dhana

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; śrī-caitanya-caritāmrita-śrotā-gana — to the listeners of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita; caitanya-caritāmrita — Caitanya-caritāmrita; yāńra — of whom; prāna-dhana — the life and soul.

TRANSLATION

All glories to the listeners of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita who have accepted it as their life and soul!
