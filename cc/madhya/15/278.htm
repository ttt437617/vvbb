Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.278

śuni' 'krishna' 'krishna' bali' amogha uthilā

premonmāde matta hañā nācite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; krishna krishna — the holy name of Krishna; bali' — speaking; amogha uthilā — Amogha stood up; premonmāde — in ecstatic love of Krishna; matta hañā — becoming maddened; nācite lāgilā — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

After hearing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and being touched by Him, Amogha, who was on his deathbed, immediately stood up and began to chant the holy name of Krishna. Thus he became mad with ecstatic love and began to dance emotionally.
