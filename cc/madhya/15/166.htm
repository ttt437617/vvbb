Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.166

krishna sei satya kare, yei māge bhritya

bhritya-vāñchā-pūrti vinu nāhi anya kritya

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; sei — that; satya kare — fulfills as true; yei — whatever; māge — wants; bhritya — servant; bhritya-vāñchā — the desire of His servant; pūrti — fulfilling; vinu — without; nāhi — there is not; anya — other; kritya — duty.

TRANSLATION

"Whatever a pure devotee wants from his master, Lord Krishna doubtlessly grants because He has no duty other than to fulfill the desire of His devotee.
