Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.295

eta bali' prabhu gelā īśvara-daraśane

bhatta snāna darśana kari' karilā bhojane

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gelā — went; īśvara-daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; bhatta — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; snāna darśana kari' — finishing his bathing and seeing of Lord Jagannātha; karilā bhojane — accepted food.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to see Lord Jagannātha. Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya completed his bath, went to see Lord Jagannātha and then returned to his house to accept food.
