Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.81

dvārera upara bhite teńho hāta dila

sei hāte phala chuńila, pandita dekhila

SYNONYMS

dvārera upara — above the door; bhite — on the ceiling; teńho — he; hāta dila — brushed his hand; sei hāte — with that hand; phala chuńila — touched the fruit; pandita — Rāghava Pandita; dekhila — saw.

TRANSLATION

"Rāghava Pandita then saw that the servant touched the ceiling above the door and then touched the coconuts with the same hand.
