Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.71

vātite kata śata vrikshe laksha laksha phala

tathāpi śunena yathā mishta nārikela

SYNONYMS

vātite — in his garden; kata śata — how many hundreds of; vrikshe — trees; laksha laksha phala — millions of fruits; tathāpi — still; śunena — hears; yathā — where; mishta nārikela — sweet coconut.

TRANSLATION

"Although he already has hundreds of trees and millions of fruits, he is still very eager to hear about the place where sweet coconuts are available.
