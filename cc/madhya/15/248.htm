Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.248

ei anne tripta haya daśa bāra jana

ekelā sannyāsī kare eteka bhakshana!

SYNONYMS

ei anne — with so much food; tripta haya — can be satisfied; daśa bāra jana — at least ten to twelve men; ekelā — alone; sannyāsī — this person in the renounced order; kare — does; eteka — so much; bhakshana — eating.

TRANSLATION

"This much food is sufficient to satisfy ten or twelve men, but this sannyāsī alone is eating so much!"
