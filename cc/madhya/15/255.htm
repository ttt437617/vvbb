Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.255

sarvāńge parāila prabhura mālya-candana

dandavat hañā bale sadainya vacana

SYNONYMS

sarva-ańge — all over the body; parāila — put; prabhura — of the Lord; mālya-candana — a flower garland and sandalwood pulp; dandavat hañā — offering obeisances; bale — says; sa-dainya — humble; vacana — statement.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya then placed a flower garland over Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and smeared His body with sandalwood pulp. After offering obeisances, the Bhattācārya submitted the following humble statement.
