Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.249

śunitei bhattācārya ulati' cāhila

tāńra avadhāna dekhi' amogha palāila

SYNONYMS

śunitei — hearing; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; ulati' cāhila — turned his eyes upon him; tāńra — his; avadhāna — attention; dekhi' — seeing; amogha — Amogha; palāila — left.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Amogha said this, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya turned his eyes upon him. Seeing the Bhattācārya's attitude, Amogha immediately left.
