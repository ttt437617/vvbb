Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.138

pūrve āmi ińhāre lobhāila bāra bāra

parama madhura, gupta, vrajendra-kumāra

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; āmi — I; ińhāre — him; lobhāila — induced to be allured; bāra bāra — again and again; parama madhura — very sweet; gupta — O Gupta; vrajendra-kumāra — Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Previously I induced Murāri Gupta again and again to be allured by Lord Krishna. I said to him, 'My dear Gupta, Lord Śrī Krishna, Vrajendra-kumāra, is the supreme sweetness.
