Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.174

taiche eka brahmānda yadi mukta haya

tabu alpa-hāni krishnera mane nāhi laya

SYNONYMS

taiche — similarly; eka brahmānda — one universe; yadi — if; mukta haya — becomes liberated; tabu — still; alpa-hāni — very little loss; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mane — the mind; nāhi laya — does not take it very seriously.

TRANSLATION

"In the same way, if one universe is vacated due to the living entities' having been liberated, that is a very little thing for Krishna. He does not take it very seriously.
