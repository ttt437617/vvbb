Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.58

śīghra yāi' muñi saba karinu bhakshana

śūnya-pātra dekhi' aśru kariyā mārjana

SYNONYMS

śīghra — very soon; yāi' — going; muñi — I; saba — all; karinu bhakshana — ate; śūnya-pātra dekhi' — seeing the dish empty; aśru — tears; kariyā mārjana — smearing with her hands.

TRANSLATION

"While she was thus thinking and crying, I immediately went there with great haste and ate everything. Seeing the dish empty, she wiped her tears away.
