Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.256

nindā karāite tomā āninu nija-ghare

ei aparādha, prabhu, kshamā kara more

SYNONYMS

nindā karāite — just to cause blasphemy; tomā — You; āninu — I brought; nija-ghare — to my place; ei aparādha — this offense; prabhu — my Lord; kshamā kara — please pardon; more — me.

TRANSLATION

"I brought You to my home just to have You blasphemed. This is a great offense. Please excuse me. I beg Your pardon."
