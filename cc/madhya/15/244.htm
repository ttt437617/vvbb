Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.244

eta śuni' hāsi' prabhu vasilā bhojane

jagannāthera prasāda bhatta dena harsha-mane

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; hāsi' — smiling; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vasilā bhojane — sat down to eat; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; prasāda — remnants of food; bhatta — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; dena harsha-mane — delivers in great happiness.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled and sat down to eat. The Bhattācārya, with great pleasure, first offered Him the prasādam from the Jagannātha temple.
