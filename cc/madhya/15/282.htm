Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.282

cadāite cadāite gāla phulāila

hāte dhari' gopīnāthācārya nishedhila

SYNONYMS

cadāite cadāite — slapping over and over again; gāla — the cheeks; phulāila — he made them swollen; hāte dhari' — catching his hands; gopīnātha-ācārya — Gopīnātha Ācārya; nishedhila — forbade.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, Amogha continued slapping his face over and over until his cheeks were swollen. Finally Gopīnātha Ācārya stopped him by catching hold of his hands.
