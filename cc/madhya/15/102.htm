Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.102

tabe rāmānanda, āra satyarāja khāńna

prabhura carane kichu kaila nivedana

SYNONYMS

tabe — after this; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Vasu; āra — and; satyarāja khāńna — Satyarāja Khān; prabhura carane — at the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kichu — some; kaila — made; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

After this, Rāmānanda Vasu and Satyarāja Khān both submitted questions at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
