Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.27

pratāparudrera ājñāya padichā-tulasī

jagannāthera prasāda-vastra eka lañā āsi

SYNONYMS

pratāparudrera — of King Pratāparudra; ājñāya — on the order; padichā-tulasī — the temple superintendent named Tulasī; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; prasāda-vastra — used cloth; eka — one; lañā — taking; āsi — came.

TRANSLATION

Following the orders of Mahārāja Pratāparudra, the temple superintendent, named Tulasī, brought one of Lord Jagannātha's used cloths.
