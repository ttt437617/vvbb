Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.119

bhakta-gane kahe, — śuna mukundera prema

nigūdha nirmala prema, yena dagdha hema

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gane kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed His devotees; śuna — please hear; mukundera prema — Mukunda's love of Godhead; nigūdha — very deep; nirmala — pure; prema — ecstatic love; yena — as if; dagdha — clarified; hema — gold.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then informed all His devotees, "Please hear about Mukunda's love of Godhead. It is a very deep and pure love and can only be compared to purified gold.
