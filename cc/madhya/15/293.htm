Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.293

bhatta kahe, — cala, prabhu, īśvara-daraśane

snāna kari' tāńhā muñi āsichoń ekhane

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — the Bhattācārya said; cala — go; prabhu — my Lord; īśvara-daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha, the Personality of Godhead; snāna kari' — taking my bath; tāńhā — there; muñi — I; āsichoń — shall come back; ekhane — here.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said, "Please go, my Lord, to see Lord Jagannātha. After taking my bath, I shall go there and then return."
