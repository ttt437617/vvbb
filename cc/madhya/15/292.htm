Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.292

ebe 'vaishnava' haila, tāra gela 'aparādha'

tāhāra upare ebe karaha prasāda

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; vaishnava haila — has become a Vaishnava; tāra — his; gela — went away; aparādha — offenses; tāhāra upare — upon him; ebe — now; karaha prasāda — show mercy.

TRANSLATION

"Now that he has become a Vaishnava, he is offenseless. You can bestow your mercy upon him without hesitation."
