Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.297

aiche citra-līlā kare śacīra nandana

yei dekhe, śune, tāńra vismaya haya mana

SYNONYMS

aiche — in this way; citra-līlā — varieties of pastimes; kare — performs; śacīra nandana — the son of mother Śacī; yei dekhe — anyone who sees; śune — hears; tāńra — his; vismaya — astonished; haya — becomes; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His various pastimes. Whoever sees them or hears them recounted becomes truly astonished.
