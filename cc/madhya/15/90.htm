Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.90

kāśamdi, ācāra ādi aneka prakāra

gandha, vastra, alańkāra, sarva dravya-sāra

SYNONYMS

kāśamdi — a kind of pickle; ācāra — other pickles; ādi — and so on; aneka prakāra — of many varieties; gandha — scents; vastra — clothing; alańkāra — ornaments; sarva — all; dravya — of things; sāra — best.

TRANSLATION

"Rāghava Pandita also offers all kinds of pickles, such as kāśamdi. He offers various scents, garments, ornaments and the best of everything.
