Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.19

kānāñi-khutiyā āchena 'nanda'-veśa dhari'

jagannātha-māhāti hañāchena 'vrajeśvarī'

SYNONYMS

kānāñi-khutiyā — Kānāñi Khutiyā; āchena — is; nanda-veśa dhari' — in the dress of Nanda Mahārāja; jagannātha-māhāti — Jagannātha Māhāti; hañāchena — was; vrajeśvarī — mother Yaśodā.

TRANSLATION

Kānāñi Khutiyā dressed himself like Nanda Mahārāja, and Jagannātha Māhiti dressed himself as mother Yaśodā.
