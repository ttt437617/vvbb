Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.141

madhura-caritra krishnera madhura-vilāsa

cāturya-vaidagdhya kare yāńra līlā-rasa

SYNONYMS

madhura-caritra — pleasing character; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; madhura-vilāsa — melodious pastimes; cāturya — expertise; vaidagdhya — intelligence; kare — manifests; yāńra — whose; līlā — of pastimes; rasa — mellows.

TRANSLATION

"'His character is very sweet, and His pastimes are melodious. He is expert in intelligence, and thus He enjoys all His pastimes and mellows.'
