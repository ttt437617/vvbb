Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.274

sahaje nirmala ei 'brāhmana'-hridaya

krishnera vasite ei yogya-sthāna haya

SYNONYMS

sahaje — by nature; nirmala — without contamination; ei — this; brāhmana-hridaya — heart of a brāhmana; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; vasite — to sit down; ei — this; yogya-sthāna — proper place; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

"The heart of a brāhmana is by nature very clean; therefore it is a proper place for Krishna to sit.
