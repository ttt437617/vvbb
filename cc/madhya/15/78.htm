Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.78

kabhu śasya khāñā punah pātra bhare śāmse

śraddhā bāde panditera, prema-sindhu bhāse

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; śasya khāñā — eating the pulp; punah — again; pātra — the plate; bhare — fills; śāmse — with pulp; śraddhā — faith; bāde — increases; panditera — of Rāghava Pandita; prema-sindhu — in the ocean of love; bhāse — floats.

TRANSLATION

"Sometimes, after eating the pulp, Krishna fills the plate again with new pulp. In this way, the faith of Rāghava Pandita increases, and he floats in an ocean of love.
