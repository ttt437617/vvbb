Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.268

īśvare ta' aparādha phale tata-kshana

eta bali' pade dui śāstrera vacana

SYNONYMS

īśvare — unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ta' — indeed; aparādha — offense; phale — brings results; tata-kshana — immediately; eta bali' — saying this; pade — recites; dui — two; śāstrera vacana — quotations from the revealed scriptures.

TRANSLATION

"When one offends the Supreme Personality of Godhead, karma immediately takes effect." After saying this, he recited two verses from the revealed scripture.
