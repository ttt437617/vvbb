Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.124

rājāra jñāna, — rāja-vaidyera ha-ila marana

āpane nāmiyā tabe karāila cetana

SYNONYMS

rājāra jñāna — the King thought; rāja-vaidyera — of the royal physician; ha-ila marana — there was death; āpane — personally; nāmiyā — getting down; tabe — thereupon; karāila cetana — brought him to consciousness.

TRANSLATION

"The King, fearing that the royal physician had been killed, personally descended and brought him to consciousness.
