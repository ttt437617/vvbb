Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.260

ghare āsi' bhattācārya shāthīra mātā-sane

āpanā nindiyā kichu balena vacane

SYNONYMS

ghare āsi' — returning home; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; shāthīra mātā-sane — with the mother of Shāthī; āpanā nindiyā — condemning himself; kichu — some; balena vacane — speaks words.

TRANSLATION

After returning to his home, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya consulted with his wife, the mother of Shāthī. After personally condemning himself, he began to speak as follows.
