Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.230

tomāra bahuta bhāgya kata praśamsiba

āmi — bhāgyavān, ihāra avaśesha pāba

SYNONYMS

tomāra — your; bahuta — great; bhāgya — fortune; kata — how much; praśamsiba — shall I praise; āmi — I; bhāgyavān — fortunate; ihāra — of this; avaśesha — remnants; pāba — shall get.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Bhattācārya, your fortune is very great. How much shall I praise you? I also am very fortunate to be able to take the remnants of this food.
