Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.298

aiche bhatta-grihe kare bhojana-vilāsa

tāra madhye nānā citra-caritra-prakāśa

SYNONYMS

aiche — in this way; bhatta-grihe — in the house of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kare — performs; bhojana-vilāsa — pastime of eating; tāra madhye — within that pastime; nānā — various; citra-caritra — of varieties of activities; prakāśa — manifestation.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu enjoyed eating in Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya's house. Within that one pastime, many wonderful pastimes were manifested.
