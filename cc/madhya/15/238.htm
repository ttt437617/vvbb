Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.238

tathāpi eteka anna khāona nā yāya

bhatta kahe, — jāni, khāo yateka yuyāya

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; eteka — so much; anna — food; khāona — eating; nā yāya — is not possible; bhatta kahe — the Bhattācārya said; jāni — I know; khāo — You can eat; yateka — how much; yuyāya — is possible.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "There is so much food here that it is impossible to eat it all."

The Bhattācārya replied, "I know how much You can eat.
