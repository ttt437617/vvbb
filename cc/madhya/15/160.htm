Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.160

jagat tārite prabhu tomāra avatāra

mora nivedana eka karaha ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

jagat tārite — to deliver the whole world; prabhu — my Lord; tomāra — Your; avatāra — incarnation; mora — my; nivedana — petition; eka — one; karaha ańgīkāra — please accept.

TRANSLATION

Vāsudeva Datta told Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "My dear Lord, You incarnate just to deliver all conditioned souls. I now have one petition, which I wish You would accept.
