Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.44

madhye madhye āmi tomāra nikata yāiba

alakshite rahi' tomāra nritya dekhiba'

SYNONYMS

madhye madhye — at intervals; āmi — I; tomāra nikata — near You; yāiba — shall go; alakshite rahi' — keeping invisible; tomāra nritya — Your dancing; dekhiba — I shall see.

TRANSLATION

"I shall also go to see You at intervals. Keeping Myself invisible, I shall watch You dance."
