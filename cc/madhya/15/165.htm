Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.165

"tomāra vicitra nahe, tumi — sākshāt prahlāda

tomāra upare krishnera sampūrna prasāda

SYNONYMS

tomāra — in you; vicitra nahe — this is not extraordinary; tumi — you; sākshāt prahlāda — incarnation of Prahlāda Mahārāja; tomāra upare — upon you; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sampūrna — complete; prasāda — mercy.

TRANSLATION

Accepting Vāsudeva Datta as a great devotee, the Lord said, "Such a statement is not at all astonishing because you are the incarnation of Prahlāda Mahārāja. It appears that Lord Krishna has bestowed complete mercy upon you. There is no doubt about it.
