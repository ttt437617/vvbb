Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.167

brahmānda jīvera tumi vāñchile nistāra

vinā pāpa-bhoge habe sabāra uddhāra

SYNONYMS

brahmānda — of the universe; jīvera — of all living entities; tumi vāñchile — if you desire; nistāra — deliverance; vinā — without; pāpa-bhoge — undergoing tribulations of sinful activities; habe — there will be; sabāra — everyone's; uddhāra — liberation.

TRANSLATION

"If you desire the deliverance of all living entities within the universe, then all of them can be delivered even without your undergoing the tribulations of sinful activity.
