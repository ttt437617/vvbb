Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.28

bahu-mūlya vastra prabhu-mastake bāndhila

ācāryādi prabhura ganere parāila

SYNONYMS

bahu-mūlya — very valuable; vastra — cloth; prabhu-mastake — on the head of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāndhila — wrapped; ācārya-ādi — headed by Advaita Ācārya; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ganere — on the associates; parāila — put.

TRANSLATION

This valuable cloth was wrapped around the head of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The other devotees, headed by Advaita Ācārya, also had cloths wrapped about their heads.
