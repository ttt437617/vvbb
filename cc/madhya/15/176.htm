Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.176

tāte bhāse māyā lañā ananta brahmānda

gada-khāite bhāse yena rāi-pūrna bhānda

SYNONYMS

tāte — in that water; bhāse — floats; māyā — the material energy; lañā — taking; ananta — unlimited; brahmānda — universes; gada-khāite — in the surrounding water; bhāse — floats; yena — as if; rāi-pūrna bhānda — a pot filled with mustard seeds.

TRANSLATION

"Māyā and her unlimited material universes are situated in that Causal Ocean. Indeed, māyā appears to be floating like a pot filled with mustard seeds.
