Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.131

raghunandanera kārya — krishnera sevana

krishna-sevā vinā ińhāra anya nāhi mana

SYNONYMS

raghunandanera kārya — the duty of Raghunandana; krishnera sevana — worshiping Lord Krishna; krishna-sevā vinā — except for worshiping Krishna; ińhāra — of him; anya — other; nāhi — there is not; mana — intention.

TRANSLATION

"Furthermore, it is the duty of Raghunandana to always engage in Lord Krishna's service. He has no other intention but the service of Lord Krishna."
