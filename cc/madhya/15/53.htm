Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.53

nitya yāi' dekhi muñi tāńhāra carane

sphūrti-jñāne teńho tāhā satya nāhi māne

SYNONYMS

nitya yāi' — going daily; dekhi — see; muñi — I; tāńhāra carane — her lotus feet; sphūrti-jñāne — feeling My presence; teńho — she; tāhā — that; satya nāhi māne — does not accept as true.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, I go there daily to see her lotus feet. She is able to feel My presence, although she does not believe it to be true.
