Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.8

sugandhi-salile dena pādya, ācamana

sarvāńge lepaye prabhura sugandhi candana

SYNONYMS

su-gandhi-salile — with scented water; dena — offers; pādya — water for washing the feet; ācamana — washing the mouth; sarva-ańge — all over the body; lepaye — smears; prabhura — of the Lord; su-gandhi candana — fragrant sandalwood pulp.

TRANSLATION

While worshiping Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Advaita Ācārya would offer Him scented water to wash His mouth and feet. Then Advaita Ācārya would smear very fragrant sandalwood pulp all over His body.
