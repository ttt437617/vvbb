Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.133

sārvabhauma, vidyā-vācaspati, — dui bhāi

dui-jane kripā kari' kahena gosāñi

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; vidyā-vācaspati — Vidyā-vācaspati; dui bhāi — two brothers; dui-jane — unto the two; kripā kari' — out of His causeless mercy; kahena — says; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Out of His causeless mercy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave the following directions to the brothers Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and Vidyā-vācaspati.
