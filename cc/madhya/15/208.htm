Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.208

pīta-sugandhi-ghrite anna sikta kaila

cāri-dike pāte ghrita vahiyā calila

SYNONYMS

pīta — yellowish; su-gandhi — fragrant; ghrite — with clarified butter; anna — rice; sikta — mixed; kaila — made; cāri-dike — on all sides; pāte — the leaf; ghrita — the clarified butter; vahiyā calila — began to flood.

TRANSLATION

Then the whole stack of rice was mixed with so much yellowish and fragrant clarified butter that it began to overflow the leaf.
