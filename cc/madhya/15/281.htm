Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.281

ei chāra mukhe tomāra karinu nindane

eta bali' āpana gāle cadāya āpane

SYNONYMS

ei chāra mukhe — in this abominable mouth; tomāra — Your; karinu — I did; nindane — blaspheming; eta bali' — saying this; āpana — his own; gāle — cheeks; cadāya — he slapped; āpane — himself.

TRANSLATION

Not only did Amogha beg the Lord's pardon, but he also began slapping his own cheeks, saying, "By this mouth I have blasphemed You."
