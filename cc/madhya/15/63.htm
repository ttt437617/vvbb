Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.63

īśāne bolāñā punah sthāna lepāila

punarapi gopālake anna samarpila

SYNONYMS

īśāne — to Īśāna, the servant; bolāñā — calling; punah — again; sthāna — the place; lepāila — cleaned; punarapi — again; gopālake — unto Gopāla; anna — cooked rice and vegetables; samarpila — offered.

TRANSLATION

"Thus wondering, she called Īśāna, the servant, and had the place cleaned again. She then offered another plate to Gopāla.
