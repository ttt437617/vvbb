Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.285

aparādha' nāhi, sadā lao krishna-nāma

eta bali' prabhu āilā sārvabhauma-sthāna

SYNONYMS

aparādha' nāhi — do not commit offenses; sadā — always; lao — chant; krishna-nāma — the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; sārvabhauma-sthāna — to the place of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.

TRANSLATION

"Amogha, always chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra and do not commit any further offenses." After giving Amogha this instruction, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to Sārvabhauma's house.
