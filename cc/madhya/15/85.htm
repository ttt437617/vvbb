Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.85

tabe āra nārikela samskāra karāila

parama pavitra kari' bhoga lāgāila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; āra — other; nārikela — coconuts; samskāra karāila — had clipped and cleaned; parama pavitra kari' — with great attention to keep them pure; bhoga lāgāila — offered for eating.

TRANSLATION

"Thereafter, Rāghava Pandita had other coconuts gathered, cleansed and clipped, and with great attention he offered them to the Deity to eat.
