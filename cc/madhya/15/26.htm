Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.26

ei-mata nityānanda phirāya laguda

ke bujhibe tāńhā duńhāra gopa-bhāva gūdha

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; phirāya laguda — wheels a rod; ke — who; bujhibe — will understand; tāńhā — there; duńhāra — of both of Them; gopa-bhāva — the ecstasy of the cowherd boys; gūdha — very deep.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu also played at whirling the rod. Who can understand how They were ecstatically immersed in the deep emotions of the cowherd boys?
