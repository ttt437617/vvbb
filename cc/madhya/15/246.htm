Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.246

bhojana dekhite cāhe, āsite nā pāre

lāthi-hāte bhattācārya āchena duyāre

SYNONYMS

bhojana — the eating; dekhite cāhe — he wanted to see; āsite nā pāre — could not come; lāthi-hāte — with a stick in his hand; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; āchena — was; duyāre — on the threshold.

TRANSLATION

Amogha wanted to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eat, but he was not allowed to enter. Indeed, the Bhattācārya guarded the threshold of his house with a stick in his hand.
