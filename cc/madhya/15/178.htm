Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.178

saba brahmānda saha yadi 'māyā'ra haya kshaya

tathāpi nā māne krishna kichu apacaya

SYNONYMS

saba brahmānda — all the universes; saha — with; yadi — if; māyāra — of the material energy; haya kshaya — there is destruction; tathāpi — still; nā — not; māne — considers; krishna — Lord Krishna; kichu — any; apacaya — lost.

TRANSLATION

"To say nothing of one universal mustard seed, even if all the universes and the material energy [māyā] are destroyed, Krishna does not even consider the loss.
