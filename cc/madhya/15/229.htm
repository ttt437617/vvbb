Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.229

annera saurabhya, varna — ati manorama

rādhā-krishna sākshāt ihāń kariyāchena bhojana

SYNONYMS

annera saurabhya — the fragrance of the cooked rice; varna — color; ati manorama — very attractive; rādhā-krishna — Lord Krishna and Rādhārānī; sākshāt — directly; ihāń — all this; kariyāchena bhojana — have eaten.

TRANSLATION

"The color of the rice is so attractive and its aroma so good that it appears Rādhā and Krishna have directly taken it.
