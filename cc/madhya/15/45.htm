Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.45

śrīvāsa-pandite prabhu kari' ālińgana

kanthe dhari' kahe tāńre madhura vacana

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa-pandite — unto Śrīvāsa Pandita; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kari' — doing; ālińgana — embracing; kanthe dhari' — catching his neck; kahe — says; tāńre — unto him; madhura vacana — sweet words.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Śrīvāsa Pandita and, with His arm about his neck, began to speak to him in sweet words.
