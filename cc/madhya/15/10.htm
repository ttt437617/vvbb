Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.10

pūjā-pātre pushpa-tulasī śesha ye āchila

sei saba lañā prabhu ācārye pūjila

SYNONYMS

pūjā-pātre — on the dish that holds flowers and tulasī; pushpa-tulasī — flowers and tulasī; śesha — remaining; ye āchila — whatever there were; sei saba — all of them; lañā — taking; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ācārye pūjila — worshiped Advaita Ācārya.

TRANSLATION

After being worshiped by Advaita Ācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would take the dish containing flowers and tulasī and, with whatever paraphernalia remained, worship Advaita Ācārya.
