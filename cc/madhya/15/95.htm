Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.95

'grihastha' hayena ińho, cāhiye sañcaya

sañcaya nā kaile kutumba-bharana nāhi haya

SYNONYMS

grihastha — householder; hayena — is; ińho — he (Vāsudeva Datta); cāhiye sañcaya — requires to save some money; sañcaya nā kaile — without saving money; kutumba-bharana — maintenance of a family; nāhi haya — is not possible.

TRANSLATION

"Being a householder, Vāsudeva Datta needs to save some money. Because he is not doing so, it is very difficult for him to maintain his family.
