Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.74

bhogera samaya punah chuli' samskari'

krishne samarpana kare mukha chidra kari'

SYNONYMS

bhogera samaya — at the time of offering bhoga; punah — again; chuli' — clipping; samskari' — cleansing; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; samarpana — offering; kare — makes; mukha — at the top; chidra kari' — making a hole.

TRANSLATION

"At the time of offering bhoga, the coconuts are again clipped and cleansed. After holes are made in them at the top, they are offered to Lord Krishna.
