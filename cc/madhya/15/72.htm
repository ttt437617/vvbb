Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.72

eka eka phalera mūlya diyā cāri-cāri pana

daśa-krośa haite ānāya kariyā yatana

SYNONYMS

eka eka phalera — of each and every fruit; mūlya — price; diyā — paying; cāri-cāri pana — four panas each (one pana equals twenty gandās); daśa-krośa — twenty miles away; haite — from; ānāya — brings; kariyā yatana — with great endeavor.

TRANSLATION

"He collects coconuts with great endeavor from a place twenty miles away, and he pays four panas each for them.
