Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.67

eteka kahite prabhu vihvala ha-ilā

loka vidāya karite prabhu dhairya dharilā

SYNONYMS

eteka kahite — saying this; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vihvala ha-ilā — became overwhelmed; loka vidāya karite — to bid farewell to the devotees; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhairya dharilā — maintained patience.

TRANSLATION

While describing all this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became a little overwhelmed, but just to finish bidding farewell to the devotees, He remained patient.
