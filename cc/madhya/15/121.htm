Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.121

eka dina mleccha-rājāra ucca-tuńgite

cikitsāra bāt kahe tāńhāra agrete

SYNONYMS

eka dina — one day; mleccha-rājāra — of the Muslim King; ucca-tuńgite — on a high platform; cikitsāra bāt — talk of medical treatment; kahe — was speaking; tāńhāra agrete — before him.

TRANSLATION

"One day Mukunda dāsa, the royal physician, was seated with the Muslim King on a high platform and was telling the King about medical treatment.
