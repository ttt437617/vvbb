Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.213

nava-nimbapatra-saha bhrishta-vārtākī

phula-badī patola-bhājā, kushmānda-māna-cākī

SYNONYMS

nava — newly grown; nimba-patra — nimba leaves; saha — along with; bhrishta-vārtākī — fried eggplant; phula-badī — light badī; patola-bhājā — fried patola vegetable; kushmānda — of pumpkin; māna — of squash; cākī — rounds.

TRANSLATION

Other preparations included eggplant mixed with newly grown nimba leaves fried together, light badī, fried patola and fried rounds of squash and pumpkin.
