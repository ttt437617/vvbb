Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.186

prabhu-pāśa āsi' sārvabhauma eka dina

yoda-hāta kari' kichu kaila nivedana

SYNONYMS

prabhu-pāśa — in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āsi' — coming; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; eka dina — one day; yoda-hāta kari' — with folded hands; kichu — some; kaila — did; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

One day Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya came before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with folded hands and submitted a request.
