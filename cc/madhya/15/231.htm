Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.231

krishnera āsana-pītha rākhaha uthāñā

more prasāda deha' bhinna pātrete kariyā

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; āsana-pītha — the sitting place; rākhaha — keep aside; uthāñā — raising; more — unto Me; prasāda — prasādam; deha' — give; bhinna — separate; pātrete — on a plate; kariyā — putting.

TRANSLATION

"Take away Krishna's sitting place and put it aside. Then give Me prasādam on a different plate."
