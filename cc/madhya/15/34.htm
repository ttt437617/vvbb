Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.34

'kāhāńre rāvnā' prabhu kahe krodhāveśe

'jagan-mātā hare pāpī, mārimu savamśe'

SYNONYMS

kāhāńre rāvnā — where is the rascal Rāvana; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — says; krodha-āveśe — in great anger; jagat-mātā — the mother of the universe; hare — kidnaps; pāpī — sinful; mārimu — I shall kill; sa-vamśe — with all his family.

TRANSLATION

In the ecstasy of Hanumān, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu angrily said, "Where is that rascal Rāvana? He has kidnapped the universal mother, Sītā. Now I shall kill him and all his family."
