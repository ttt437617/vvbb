Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.252

śuni' shāthīra mātā śire-buke ghāta māre

'shāthī rāndī ha-uka' — ihā bale bāre bāre

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; shāthīra mātā — the mother of Shāthī; śire — on the head; buke — on the chest; ghāta māre — strikes; shāthī rāndī ha-uka — let Shāthī become a widow; ihā bale — says this; bāre bāre — again and again.

TRANSLATION

When Shāthī's mother, the Bhattācārya's wife, heard of this incident, she immediately began to strike her head and chest, saying again and again, "Let Shāthī become a widow!"
