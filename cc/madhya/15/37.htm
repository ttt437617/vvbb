Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.37

eka-dina mahāprabhu nityānande lañā

dui bhāi yukti kaila nibhrite vasiyā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nityānande lañā — taking Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; dui bhāi — two brothers; yukti kaila — consulted; nibhrite vasiyā — sitting in a solitary place.

TRANSLATION

One day the two brothers, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Nityānanda Prabhu, consulted with each other while sitting together in a solitary place.
