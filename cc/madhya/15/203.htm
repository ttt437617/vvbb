Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.203

āpani bhattācārya kare pākera saba karma

shāthīra mātā — vicakshanā, jāne pāka-marma

SYNONYMS

āpani — personally; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kare — arranges; pākera — of cooking; saba karma — all activities; shāthīra mātā — the mother of Shāthī; vicakshanā — very experienced; jāne — knows; pāka-marma — how to cook.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya personally began to help Shāthīra Mātā cook. She was very experienced, and she knew how to cook nicely.
