Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.156

sākshāt hanumān tumi śrī-rāma-kińkara

tumi kene chādibe tāńra carana-kamala

SYNONYMS

sākshāt — directly; hanumān — Hanumān; tumi — you; śrī-rāma-kińkara — the servant of Śrī Rāma; tumi — you; kene — why; chādibe — should give up; tāńra — His; carana-kamala — lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, I congratulated Murāri Gupta, saying, 'Indeed, you are the incarnation of Hanumān. Consequently you are the eternal servant of Lord Rāmacandra. Why should you give up the worship of Lord Rāmacandra and His lotus feet?'"
