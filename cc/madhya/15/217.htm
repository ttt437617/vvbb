Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.217

ghrita-sikta paramānna, mrit-kundikā bhari'

cāńpākalā-ghanadugdha-āmra tāhā dhari

SYNONYMS

ghrita-sikta parama-anna — sweet rice mixed with ghee; mrit-kundikā bhari' — filling an earthen pot; cāńpā-kalā — a kind of banana; ghana-dugdha — condensed milk; āmra — mango pulp; tāhā — that; dhari — including.

TRANSLATION

Sweet rice mixed with ghee was poured into an earthen pot and mixed with cāńpā-kalā, condensed milk and mango.
