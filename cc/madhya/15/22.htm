Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.22

advaita kahe, — satya kahi, nā kariha kopa

laguda phirāite pāra, tabe jāni gopa

SYNONYMS

advaita kahe — Advaita Ācārya says; satya kahi — I speak the truth; nā kariha kopa — please do not be angry; laguda — stick, staff; phirāite pāra — if You can wheel around; tabe jāni — then I shall understand; gopa — cowherd boy.

TRANSLATION

At this time Śrīla Advaita Ācārya said, "Please do not be angry. I speak the truth. I shall know that You are a cowherd boy only if You can wheel this rod about."
