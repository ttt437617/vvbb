Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.152

eta śuni' āmi bada mane sukha pāiluń

ińhāre uthāñā tabe ālińgana kailuń

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; āmi — I; bada — very great; mane — in the mind; sukha — happiness; pāiluń — got; ińhāre — him; uthāñā — raising; tabe — at that time; ālińgana kailuń — I embraced.

TRANSLATION

"Hearing this, I became very happy. I then raised Murāri Gupta and embraced him.
