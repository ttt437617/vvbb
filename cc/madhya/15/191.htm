Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.191

tabe sārvabhauma prabhura carane dhariyā

'daśa-dina bhikshā kara' kahe vinati kariyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane dhariyā — catching the lotus feet; daśa-dina — for ten days; bhikshā kara — accept lunch; kahe — says; vinati kariyā — with great submission.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya then caught hold of the Lord's lotus feet and submissively begged, "Please accept lunch for at least ten days."
