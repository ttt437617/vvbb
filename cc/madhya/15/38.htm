Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.38

kibā yukti kaila duńhe, keha nāhi jāne

phale anumāna pāche kaila bhakta-gane

SYNONYMS

kibā yukti kaila — what consultation They had; duńhe — the two of Them; keha nāhi jāne — no one knows; phale — by the result; anumāna — guess; pāche — later; kaila — did; bhakta-gane — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

No one could understand what the brothers discussed between Themselves, but later all the devotees could guess what the subject matter was.
