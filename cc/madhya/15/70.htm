Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.70

āra dravya rahu — śuna nārikelera kathā

pāńca gandā kari' nārikela vikāya tathā

SYNONYMS

āra dravya rahu — apart from the other commodities; śuna — just hear; nārikelera kathā — the incident of offering coconuts; pāńca gandā kari' — at the price of five gandās; nārikela — coconut; vikāya — is sold; tathā — there.

TRANSLATION

"Apart from other commodities, just hear about his offering of coconuts. A coconut is sold at the rate of five gandās each.
