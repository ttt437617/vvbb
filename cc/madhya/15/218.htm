Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.218

rasālā-mathita dadhi, sandeśa apāra

gaude utkale yata bhakshyera prakāra

SYNONYMS

rasālā — delicious; mathita — churned; dadhi — curd; sandeśa — a sweetmeat; apāra — unlimited; gaude — in Bengal; utkale — in Orissa; yata — all; bhakshyera — of eatables; prakāra — kinds.

TRANSLATION

Other preparations included a very delicious churned curd and a variety of sandeśa sweetmeats. Indeed, all the various eatables available in Bengal and Orissa were prepared.
