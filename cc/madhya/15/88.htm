Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.88

ei mata vyañjanera śāka, mūla, phala

ei mata cidā, huduma, sandeśa sakala

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; vyañjanera — of vegetables; śāka — spinach; mūla — radishes; phala — fruits; ei mata — in this way; cidā — chipped rice; huduma — powdered rice; sandeśa — sweetmeats; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

"Thus with great care and attention Rāghava Pandita prepares spinach, other vegetables, radishes, fruits, chipped rice, powdered rice and sweetmeats.
