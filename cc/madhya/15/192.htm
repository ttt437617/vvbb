Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.192

prabhu krame krame pāńca-dina ghātāila

pāńca-dina tāńra bhikshā niyama karila

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krame krame — gradually; pāńca-dina — to five days; ghātāila — reduced; pāñca-dina — for five days; tāńra — his; bhikshā — invitation for lunch; niyama karila — accepted regularly.

TRANSLATION

In this way, by and by, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu reduced the duration to five days. Thus for five days He regularly accepted the Bhattācārya's invitation to lunch.
