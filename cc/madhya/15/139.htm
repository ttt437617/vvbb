Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.139

svayam bhagavān krishna — sarvāmśī, sarvāśraya

viśuddha-nirmala-prema, sarva-rasamaya

SYNONYMS

svayam bhagavān krishna — Lord Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sarva-amśī — the source of all others; sarva-āśraya — the reservoir of all energies; viśuddha — transcendental; nirmala — free from all material contamination; prema — love; sarva-rasa-maya — the reservoir of all pleasure.

TRANSLATION

"'Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the origin of all incarnations and the source of everything. He is pure transcendental love itself, and He is the reservoir of all pleasure.
