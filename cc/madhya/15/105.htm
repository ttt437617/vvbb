Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.105

satyarāja bale, — vaishnava ciniba kemane?

ke vaishnava, kaha tāńra sāmānya lakshane

SYNONYMS

satyarāja bale — Satyarāja Khān said; vaishnava — a Vaishnava; ciniba kemane — how shall I recognize; ke vaishnava — who is a Vaishnava; kaha — please say; tāńra — of him; sāmānya lakshane — common symptoms.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this, Satyarāja said, "How can I recognize a Vaishnava? Please let me know what a Vaishnava is. What are his common symptoms?"
