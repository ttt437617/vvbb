Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.187

ebe saba vaishnava gauda-deśe cali' gela

ebe prabhura nimantrane avasara haila

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; saba — all; vaishnava — devotees; gauda-deśe — to Bengal; cali' gela — have returned; ebe — now; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nimantrane — for invitations; avasara haila — there is a chance.

TRANSLATION

Since all the Vaishnavas had returned to Bengal, there was a good chance that the Lord would accept an invitation.
