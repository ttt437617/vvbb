Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.296

sei amogha haila prabhura bhakta 'ekānta'

preme nāce, krishna-nāma laya mahā-śānta

SYNONYMS

sei amogha — that same Amogha; haila — became; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta — devotee; ekānta — unflinching; preme nāce — dances in ecstasy; krishna-nāma laya — chants the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; mahā-śānta — very peaceful.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, Amogha became an unalloyed devotee of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He danced in ecstasy and peacefully chanted the holy name of Lord Krishna.
