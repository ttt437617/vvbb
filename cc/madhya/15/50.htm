Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.50

vātula bālakera mātā nāhi laya dosha

ei jāni' mātā more nā karaya rosha

SYNONYMS

vātula bālakera — of a mad son; mātā — mother; nāhi — does not; laya — accept; dosha — fault; ei jāni' — knowing this; mātā — mother; more — unto Me; nā karaya rosha — is not at all angry.

TRANSLATION

"A mother is not offended by her mad son, and knowing this, My mother is not offended by Me.
