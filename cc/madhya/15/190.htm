Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.190

sārvabhauma kahe punah, — dina 'pañca-daśa'

prabhu kahe, — tomāra bhikshā 'eka' divasa

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said; punah — again; dina pañca-daśa — fifteen days; prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; tomāra bhikshā — lunch at your place; eka divasa — only one day.

TRANSLATION

When Sārvabhauma requested Caitanya Mahāprabhu to accept lunch for fifteen days, the Lord said, "I shall accept lunch at your place for one day only."
