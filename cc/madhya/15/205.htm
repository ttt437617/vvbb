Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.205

āra ghara mahāprabhura bhikshāra lāgiyā

nibhrite kariyāche bhatta nūtana kariyā

SYNONYMS

āra ghara — the other room; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshāra lāgiyā — for taking lunch; nibhrite kariyāche — constructed in a solitary place; bhatta — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; nūtana kariyā — newly done.

TRANSLATION

The other room was for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's lunch. The Lord's lunchroom was very secluded, and it was newly constructed by the Bhattācārya.
