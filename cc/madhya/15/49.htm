Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.49

tāńra prema-vaśa āmi, tāńra sevā — dharma

tāhā chādi' kariyāchi vātulera karma

SYNONYMS

tāńra prema-vaśa — subordinate to her love; āmi — I; tāńra sevā — her service; dharma — My religion; tāhā chādi' — giving that up; kariyāchi — I performed; vātulera karma — the acts of a madman.

TRANSLATION

"I am subordinate to the love of My mother, and it is My duty to serve her in return. Instead of doing so, I have accepted the renounced order. Certainly this is the act of a madman.
