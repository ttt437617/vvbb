Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.250

bhattācārya lāthi lañā mārite dhāila

palāila amogha, tāra lāga nā pāila

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; lāthi lañā — taking a stick; mārite — to strike; dhāila — ran; palāila — fled; amogha — Amogha; tāra — him; lāga nā pāila — could not catch.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya ran after him to strike him with a stick, but Amogha fled so fast that the Bhattācārya could not catch him.
