Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.222

hena-kāle mahāprabhu madhyāhna kariyā

ekale āila tāńra hridaya jāniyā

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; madhyāhna kariyā — finishing His midday duties; ekale — alone; āila — came; tāńra — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; hridaya — the heart; jāniyā — knowing.

TRANSLATION

When everything was ready, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came there alone after finishing His midday duties. He knew the heart of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
