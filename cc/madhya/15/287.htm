Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.287

prabhu kahe, — amogha śiśu, kibā tāra dosha

kene upavāsa kara, kene kara rosha

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; amogha śiśu — Amogha is a child; kibā — what; tāra dosha — his fault; kene — why; upavāsa kara — are you fasting; kene — why; kara rosha — are you angry.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu pacified Sārvabhauma, saying, "After all, Amogha, your son-in-law, is a child. So what is his fault? Why are you fasting, and why are you angry?
