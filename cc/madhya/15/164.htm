Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.164

eta śuni' mahāprabhura citta dravilā

aśru-kampa-svarabhańge kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; citta — heart; dravilā — became softened; aśru — tears; kampa — trembling; svara-bhańge — with faltering of the voice; kahite — to speak; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard Vāsudeva Datta's statement, His heart became very soft. Tears flowed from His eyes, and He began to tremble. In a faltering voice He spoke as follows.
