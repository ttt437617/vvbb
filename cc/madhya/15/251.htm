Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.251

tabe gāli, śāpa dite bhattācārya āilā

nindā śuni' mahāprabhu hāsite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; gāli — calling by ill names; śāpa dite — cursing; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; āilā — came back; nindā śuni' — hearing the criticism; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hāsite lāgilā — began to laugh.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya then began to curse his son-in-law and call him ill names. When the Bhattācārya returned, he saw that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was laughing to hear him criticize Amogha.
