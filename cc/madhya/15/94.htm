Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.94

parama udāra ińho, ye dina ye āise

sei dine vyaya kare, nāhi rākhe śeshe

SYNONYMS

parama udāra — very liberal; ińho — he; ye dina — every day; ye āise — whatever he gets; sei dine — on that very day; vyaya kare — spends; nāhi — does not; rākhe — keep; śeshe — any balance.

TRANSLATION

"Vāsudeva Datta is very liberal. Every day, whatever income he receives, he spends. He does not keep any balance.
