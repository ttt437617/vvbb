Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.89

ei-mata pithā-pānā, kshīra-odana

parama pavitra, āra kare sarvottama

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; pithā-pānā — cakes and sweet rice; kshīra-odana — condensed milk; parama pavitra — highly purified; āra — and; kare — he makes; sarva-uttama — first class, tasteful.

TRANSLATION

"He prepares cakes, sweet rice, condensed milk and everything else with great attention, and the cooking conditions are purified so that the food is first class and delicious.
