Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.182

prabhura vicchede bhakta karena rodana

bhaktera vicchede prabhura vishanna haila mana

SYNONYMS

prabhura — from Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vicchede — by separation; bhakta — all the devotees; karena — do; rodana — crying; bhaktera — of the devotees; vicchede — by the separation; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vishanna — morose; haila — became; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

Due to the impending separation from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, all the devotees began to cry. The Lord was also morose due to separation from the devotees.
