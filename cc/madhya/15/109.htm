Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.109

anushańga-phale kare samsārera kshaya

citta ākarshiyā karāya krishne premodaya

SYNONYMS

anushańga-phale — as a simultaneous result; kare — does; samsārera kshaya — annihilation of entanglement in the material world; citta — thought; ākarshiyā — attracting; karāya — causes; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; prema-udaya — arousal of transcendental love.

TRANSLATION

"By chanting the holy name of the Lord, one dissolves his entanglement in material activities. After this, one becomes very much attracted to Krishna, and thus dormant love for Krishna is awakened.
