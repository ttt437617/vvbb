Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.66

ei vijayā-daśamīte haila ei rīti

tāńhāke puchiyā tāńra karāiha pratīti

SYNONYMS

ei vijayā-daśamīte — on the previous Vijayā-daśamī day; haila — there was; ei rīti — such an incident; tāńhāke — unto her; puchiyā — asking; tāńra — her; karāiha — make; pratīti — belief.

TRANSLATION

"Such an incident took place on the last Vijayā-daśamī day. You can ask her about this incident and thus make her believe that I actually go there."
