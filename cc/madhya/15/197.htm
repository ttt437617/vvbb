Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.197

bahuta sannyāsī yadi āise eka thāñi

sammāna karite nāri, aparādha pāi

SYNONYMS

bahuta sannyāsī — many sannyāsīs; yadi — if; āise — come; eka thāñi — together; sammāna karite nāri — I cannot receive them properly; aparādha pāi — I shall be an offender.

TRANSLATION

"If all the sannyāsīs came together, it would not be possible for me to pay them proper respects. Therefore I would be an offender.
