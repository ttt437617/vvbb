Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.91

ei-mata premera sevā kare anupama

yāhā dekhi' sarva-lokera judāya nayana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; premera sevā — service in love; kare — performs; anupama — without comparison; yāhā dekhi' — seeing which; sarva-lokera — of all people; judāya — become pleased; nayana — the eyes.

TRANSLATION

"Thus Rāghava Pandita serves the Lord in an incomparable way. Everyone is very much satisfied just to see him."
