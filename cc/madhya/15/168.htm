Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.168

asamartha nahe krishna, dhare sarva bala

tomāke vā kene bhuñjāibe pāpa-phala?

SYNONYMS

asamartha nahe — is not unable; krishna — Lord Krishna; dhare — possesses; sarva bala — all potencies; tomāke — you; vā — then; kene — why; bhuñjāibe — would cause to suffer; pāpa-phala — results of sinful reactions.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is not incapable, for He has all potencies. Why would He induce you to suffer the sinful reactions of other living entities?
