Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.198

tumiha nija-chāye āsibe mora ghara

kabhu sańge āsibena svarūpa-dāmodara

SYNONYMS

tumiha — You; nija-chāye — alone; āsibe — will come; mora ghara — to my place; kabhu — sometimes; sańge — with You; āsibena — will come; svarūpa-dāmodara — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

"Sometimes You will come alone to my place, and sometimes You will be accompanied by Svarūpa Dāmodara."
