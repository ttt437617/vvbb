Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.17

krishna-janma-yātrā-dine nanda-mahotsava

gopa-veśa hailā prabhu lañā bhakta saba

SYNONYMS

krishna-janma-yātrā — observance of the birth of Lord Krishna; dine — on the day of; nanda-mahotsava — the festival observed by Nanda Mahārāja, the father of Krishna; gopa-veśa hailā — dressed Himself as a cowherd boy; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; bhakta saba — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

The devotees celebrated the festival of Janmāshtamī, Krishna's birthday, which is also called Nanda-mahotsava, the festival of Nanda Mahārāja. At that time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees dressed themselves as cowherd boys.
