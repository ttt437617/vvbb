Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.212

vriddha-kushmānda-badīra vyañjana apāra

phulabadī-phala-mūla vividha prakāra

SYNONYMS

vriddha-kushmānda-badīra — of small pieces of fried dhal mixed with ripe pumpkin; vyañjana — vegetables; apāra — unlimited; phula-badī — small fried pieces of another kind of dhal; phala — fruits; mūla — roots; vividha prakāra — of different varieties.

TRANSLATION

There were unlimited quantities of vriddha-kushmānda-badī, phula-badī, fruits and various roots.
