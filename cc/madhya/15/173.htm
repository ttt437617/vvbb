Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.173

tāra eka phala padi' yadi nashta haya

tathāpi vriksha nāhi jāne nija-apacaya

SYNONYMS

tāra — of the tree; eka phala — one fruit; padi' — falling down; yadi — if; nashta haya — becomes destroyed; tathāpi — still; vriksha — the tree; nāhi jāne — does not know; nija-apacaya — its loss.

TRANSLATION

"The udumbara tree is filled with millions of fruits, and if one falls down and is destroyed, the tree does not even consider the loss.
