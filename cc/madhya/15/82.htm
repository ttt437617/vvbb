Madhya-līlāChapter 15: The Lord Accepts Prasādam at the House of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 15.82

pandita kahe, — dvāre loka kare gatāyāte

tāra pada-dhūli udi' lāge upara bhite

SYNONYMS

pandita kahe — Rāghava Pandita said; dvāre — through the door; loka — people in general; kare — do; gatāyāte — coming and going; tāra — their; pada-dhūli — dust of the feet; udi' — being blown; lāge — touches; upara — upward; bhite — the ceiling.

TRANSLATION

"Rāghava Pandita then said, 'People are always coming and going through that door. The dust from their feet blows up and touches the ceiling.
