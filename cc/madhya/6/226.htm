Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.226

na deśa-niyamas tatra

na kāla-niyamas tathā

prāptam annam drutam śishtair

bhoktavyam harir abravīt

SYNONYMS

na — not; deśa — of the country; niyamah — regulation; tatra — in that; na — not; kāla — of time; niyamah — regulation; tathā — so also; prāptam — received; annam — prasādam; drutam — hastily; śishtaih — by gentlemen; bhoktavyam — to be eaten; harih — the Lord; abravīt — has said.

TRANSLATION

"'The prasādam of Lord Krishna is to be eaten by gentlemen as soon as it is received; there should be no hesitation. There are no regulative principles concerning time and place. This is the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead.'"

PURPORT

These verses are quoted from the Padma Purāna.
