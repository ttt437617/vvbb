Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.74

bhattācārya kahe, — 'ińhāra praudha yauvana

kemate sannyāsa-dharma ha-ibe rakshana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied; ińhāra — His; praudha — full; yauvana — youth; kemate — how; sannyāsa-dharma — principles of a sannyāsī; ha-ibe — there will be; rakshana — protection.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya inquired, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in His full-fledged youthful life. How can He keep the principles of sannyāsa?
