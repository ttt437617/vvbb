Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.274

yadyapi tomāra artha ei śabde kaya

tathāpi 'āślishya-doshe' kahana nā yāya

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; tomāra — Your; artha — meaning; ei — this; śabde — by the word; kaya — is said; tathāpi — still; āślishya-doshe — by the fault of ambiguity; kahana — to say; nā — not; yāya — possible.

TRANSLATION

"Although Your explanation is correct, it should not be used, because there is ambiguity in the word 'mukti-pada.'
