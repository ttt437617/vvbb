Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.280

bhattācāryera vaishnavatā dekhi' sarva-jana

prabhuke jānila — 'sākshāt vrajendra-nandana'

SYNONYMS

bhattācāryera — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; vaishnavatā — clear understanding of Vaishnava philosophy; dekhi' — seeing; sarva-jana — all persons; prabhuke — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jānila — knew; sākshāt — directly; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna, the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing transcendental Vaishnavism in Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, everyone could understand that Lord Caitanya was none other than Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.
