Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.27

tomāra milane yabe āmāra haila mana

daive sei kshane pāiluń tomāra daraśana

SYNONYMS

tomāra — of you; milane — in meeting; yabe — when; āmāra — of me; haila — there was; mana — the mind; daive — by chance; sei kshane — at that very moment; pāiluń — got; tomāra — your; daraśana — meeting.

TRANSLATION

"Just as I was thinking of meeting you, by chance we have actually met.
