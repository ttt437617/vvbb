Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.37

ucca kari' kare sabe nāma-sańkīrtana

tritīya prahare haila prabhura cetana

SYNONYMS

ucca — very loudly; kari' — doing; kare — began; sabe — all; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; tritīya prahare — in the forenoon; haila — there was; prabhura — of Lord Caitanya; cetana — consciousness.

TRANSLATION

All of the devotees then began to loudly chant the Hare Krishna mantra. Just before noon the Lord regained His consciousness.
