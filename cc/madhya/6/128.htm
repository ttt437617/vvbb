Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.128

bhattācārya kahe, — nā bujhi', hena jñāna yāra

bujhibāra lāgi' seha puche punarbāra

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied; nā bujhi' — not understanding; hena — this; jñāna — the knowledge; yāra — of someone; bujhibāra lāgi' — just to understand; seha — he also; puche — inquires; punah-bāra — again.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "I accept that You do not understand, yet even one who does not understand inquires about the subject matter.
