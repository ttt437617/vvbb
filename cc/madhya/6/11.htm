Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.11

vasi' bhattācārya mane karena vicāra

ei krishna-mahāpremera sāttvika vikāra

SYNONYMS

vasi' — sitting down; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; mane — in his mind; karena — does; vicāra — consideration; ei — this; krishna-mahā-premera — of ecstatic love for Krishna; sāttvika — transcendental; vikāra — transformation.

TRANSLATION

Sitting beside Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he thought, "This is a transcendental ecstatic transformation brought about by love of Krishna."
