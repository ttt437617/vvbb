Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.47

ājñā māgi' gelā gopīnātha ācāryake lañā

prabhura nikata āilā bhojana kariñā

SYNONYMS

ājñā māgi' — taking permission; gelā — went; gopīnātha ācāryake lañā — taking Gopīnātha Ācārya; prabhura — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nikata — near; āilā — went; bhojana kariñā — after taking lunch.

TRANSLATION

Begging permission from Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya then went with Gopīnātha Ācārya to take lunch. After finishing their lunch, they returned to Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
