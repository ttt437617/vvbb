Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.176

ei-mate kalpita bhāshye śata dosha dila

bhattācārya pūrva-paksha apāra karila

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; kalpita — imagined; bhāshye — in the commentary; śata — hundreds; dosha — of faults; dila — gave; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; pūrva-paksha — opposing elements; apāra — unlimitedly; karila — manifested.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu criticized Śańkarācārya's Śārīraka-bhāshya as imaginary, and He pointed out hundreds of faults in it. To defend Śańkarācārya, however, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya presented unlimited opposition.
