Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.202

nija-rūpa prabhu tāńre karāila darśana

catur-bhuja-rūpa prabhu hā-ilā takhana

SYNONYMS

nija-rūpa — personal form; prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — unto him; karāila — made; darśana — seeing; catur-bhuja — four-handed; rūpa — form; prabhu — the Lord; ha-ilā — became; takhana — at that time.

TRANSLATION

To show him mercy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu allowed him to see His Vishnu form. Thus He immediately assumed four hands.
