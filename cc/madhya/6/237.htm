Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.237

caitanya-carana vine nāhi jāne āna

bhakti vinu śāstrera āra nā kare vyākhyāna

SYNONYMS

caitanya-carana — the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; vine — except; nāhi — not; jāne — knows; āna — other; bhakti — devotional service; vinu — except; śāstrera — of the scripture; āra — any other; nā — not; kare — does; vyākhyāna — explanation.

TRANSLATION

From that day on, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya did not know anything but the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and from that day he could explain the revealed scriptures only in accordance with the process of devotional service.
