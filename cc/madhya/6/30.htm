Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.30

sārvabhauma-sthāne giyā prabhuke dekhila

prabhu dekhi' ācāryera duhkha-harsha haila

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma-sthāne — to the place of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; giyā — going there; prabhuke — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhila — all of them saw; prabhu dekhi' — seeing the Lord; ācāryera — of Gopīnātha Ācārya; duhkha — unhappiness; harsha — happiness; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

Arriving at the home of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, everyone saw the Lord lying unconscious. Seeing Him in this condition, Gopīnātha Ācārya became very unhappy, but at the same time he was happy just to see the Lord.
