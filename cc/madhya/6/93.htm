Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.93

ishta-goshthī vicāra kari, nā kariha rosha

śāstra-drishtye kahi, kichu nā la-iha dosha

SYNONYMS

ishta-goshthī — discussion among friends; vicāra — consideration; kari — we do; nā — not; kariha — make; rosha — anger; śāstra-drishtye — according to the conclusion of scriptures; kahi — we speak; kichu — any; nā — not; la-iha — take; dosha — fault.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya said, "We are just having a discussion among friends and considering the points described in the scriptures. Do not become angry. I am simply speaking on the strength of the śāstras. Please don't take any offense.
