Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.105

tomāra āge eta kathāra nāhi prayojana

ūshara-bhūmite yena bījera ropana

SYNONYMS

tomāra āge — before you; eta — so many; kathāra — of words; nāhi — there is not; prayojana — necessity; ūshara-bhūmite — in barren land; yena — like; bījera — of the seed; ropana — sowing.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya then said, "There is no need to quote so much evidence from the śāstras, for you are a very dry speculator. There is no need to sow seeds in barren land.
