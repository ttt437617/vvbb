Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.213

'jagat nistārile tumi, — seha alpa-kārya

āmā uddhārile tumi, — e śakti āścarya

SYNONYMS

jagat — the whole world; nistārile — have delivered; tumi — You; seha — that; alpa-kārya — minor activity; āmā — me; uddhārile — have delivered; tumi — You; e — this; śakti — power; āścarya — wonderful.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said, "My dear Sir, You have delivered the entire world, but that is not a very great task. However, You have also delivered me, and that is certainly the work of very wonderful powers.
