Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.267

'sālokyādi' cāri yadi haya sevā-dvāra

tabu kadācit bhakta kare ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

sālokya-ādi — beginning with sālokya; cāri — four kinds of liberation; yadi — if; haya — are; sevā-dvāra — a means of rendering service to the Lord; tabu — still; kadācit — occasionally; bhakta — a pure devotee; kare — makes; ańgīkāra — acceptance.

TRANSLATION

"If there is a chance to serve the Supreme Personality of Godhead, a pure devotee sometimes accepts the sālokya, sārūpya, sāmīpya or sārshti forms of liberation, but never sāyujya.
