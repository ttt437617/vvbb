Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.113

ācāryera siddhānte mukundera haila santosha

bhattācāryera vākya mane haila duhkha-rosha

SYNONYMS

ācāryera — of Gopīnātha Ācārya; siddhānte — with the conclusions; mukundera — of Mukunda Datta; haila — there was; santosha — satisfaction; bhattācāryera — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; vākye — by the words; mane — in the mind; haila — there was; duhkha — unhappiness; rosha — and anger.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Mukunda Datta felt very satisfied to hear the conclusive statements of Gopīnātha Ācārya, but he became very unhappy and angry to hear the statements put forward by Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya.
