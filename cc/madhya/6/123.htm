Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.123

sāta dina paryanta aiche karena śravane

bhāla-manda nāhi kahe, vasi' mātra śune

SYNONYMS

sāta dina — seven days; paryanta — up to; aiche — in this way; karena — does; śravane — hearing; bhāla — right; manda — wrong; nāhi — not; kahe — says; vasi' — sitting; mātra — only; śune — hears.

TRANSLATION

Thus for seven days continually, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu listened to the Vedānta philosophy expounded by Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya. However, Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not say anything and did not indicate whether it was right or wrong. He simply sat there and listened to the Bhattācārya.
