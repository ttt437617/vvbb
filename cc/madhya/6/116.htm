Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.116

śuni mahāprabhu kahe aiche mat kaha

āmā prati bhattācāryera haya anugraha

SYNONYMS

śuni — hearing them; mahāprabhu — Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — says; aiche — such; mat kaha — do not speak; āmā prati — toward Me; bhattācāryera — of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; haya — there is; anugraha — mercy.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Do not speak like that. Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya has shown great affection and mercy toward Me.
