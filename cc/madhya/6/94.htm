Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.94

mahā-bhāgavata haya caitanya-gosāñi

ei kali-kāle vishnura avatāra nāi

SYNONYMS

mahā-bhāgavata — a great devotee; haya — is; caitanya-gosāñi — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — this; kali-kāle — in the Age of Kali; vishnura — of Lord Vishnu; avatāra — incarnation; nāi — there is not.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is certainly a great, uncommon devotee, but we cannot accept Him as an incarnation of Lord Vishnu because, according to śāstra, there is no incarnation in this Age of Kali.
