Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.248

jagadānanda dāmodara, — dui sańge lañā

ghare āila bhattācārya jagannātha dekhiyā

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; dāmodara — Dāmodara; dui — two persons; sańge — with him; lañā — taking; ghare — to his home; āila — returned; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; dekhiyā — seeing in the temple.

TRANSLATION

After visiting the temple of Lord Jagannātha, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya returned home with Jagadānanda and Dāmodara.
