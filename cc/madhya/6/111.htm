Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.111

prasāda āni' tāńre karāha āge bhikshā

paścāt āsi' āmāre karāiha śikshā

SYNONYMS

prasāda āni' — bringing jagannātha-prasādam; tāńre — unto Him; karāha — make; āge — first; bhikshā — acceptance; paścāt — afterwards; āsi' — coming here; āmāre — unto me; karāiha — cause; śikshā — teaching.

TRANSLATION

"Take jagannātha-prasādam and first give it to Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His associates. After that, come back here and teach me well."
