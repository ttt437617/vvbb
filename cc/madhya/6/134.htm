Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.134

mukhyārtha chādiyā kara gaunārtha kalpanā

'abhidhā'-vritti chādi' kara śabdera lakshanā

SYNONYMS

mukhya-artha — direct meaning; chādiyā — giving up; kara — you do; gauna-artha — indirect meaning; kalpanā — imagining; abhidhā-vritti — the meaning that is understood immediately; chādi' — giving up; kara — you do; śabdera — of the words; lakshanā — interpretation.

TRANSLATION

"For each sūtra the direct meaning must be accepted without interpretation. However, you simply abandon the direct meaning and proceed with your imaginative interpretation.
