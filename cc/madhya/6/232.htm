Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.232

āji tumi nishkapate hailā krishnāśraya

krishna āji nishkapate tomā haila sadaya

SYNONYMS

āji — today; tumi — you; nishkapate — without a doubt; hailā — have become; krishna-āśraya — under the shelter of Lord Krishna; krishna — Lord Krishna; āji — today; nishkapate — without reservation; tomā — unto you; haila — has become; sa-daya — very merciful.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, today you have undoubtedly taken shelter of the lotus feet of Krishna, and Krishna, without reservation, has become very merciful toward you.
