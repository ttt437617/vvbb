Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.63

prabhu kahe, — 'mandira bhitare nā yāiba

garudera pāśe rahi' darśana kariba'

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya replied; mandira — the temple; bhitare — inside; nā — never; yāiba — I shall go; garudera — of the column known as the Garuda-stambha; pāśe — by the side; rahi' — staying; darśana — seeing; kariba — I shall do.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "I shall never enter the temple but shall always view the Lord from the side of the Garuda-stambha."
