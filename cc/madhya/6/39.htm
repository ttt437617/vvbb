Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.39

sārvabhauma kahe, — śīghra karaha madhyāhna

muñi bhikshā dimu āji mahā-prasādānna

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kahe — says; śīghra — very soon; karaha — do; madhya-ahna — midday duties; muñi — I; bhikshā — alms; dimu — shall offer; āji — today; mahā-prasāda-anna — remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya informed all of them, "Please take your midday baths immediately. Today I shall offer you mahā-prasādam, the remnants of food offered to Lord Jagannātha."
