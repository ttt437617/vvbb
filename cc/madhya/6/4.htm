Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.4

jagannātha ālińgite calilā dhāñā

mandire padilā preme āvishta hañā

SYNONYMS

jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; ālińgite — to embrace; calilā — went; dhāñā — very swiftly; mandire — in the temple; padilā — fell down; preme — in ecstasy; āvishta — overwhelmed; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went swiftly to embrace Lord Jagannātha, but when He entered the temple, He was so overwhelmed with love of Godhead that He fainted to the floor.
