Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.38

huńkāra kariyā uthe 'hari' 'hari' bali'

ānande sārvabhauma tāńra laila pada-dhūli

SYNONYMS

huńkāra kariyā — making a loud sound; uthe — got up; hari hari bali' — chanting Hari, Hari; ānande — in pleasure; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; tāńra — His; laila — took; pada-dhūli — the dust of the feet.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu got up and very loudly chanted, "Hari! Hari!" Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya was very pleased to see the Lord regain consciousness, and he took the dust of the Lord's lotus feet.
