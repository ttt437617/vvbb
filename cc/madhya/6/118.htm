Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.118

āra dina mahāprabhu bhattācārya-sane

ānande karilā jagannātha daraśane

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhattācārya-sane — along with Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; ānande — in great pleasure; karilā — did; jagannātha — to Lord Jagannātha; daraśane — visit.

TRANSLATION

The next morning, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya together visited the temple of Lord Jagannātha. Both of them were in a very pleasant mood.
