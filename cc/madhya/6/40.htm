Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.40

samudra-snāna kari' mahāprabhu śīghra āilā

carana pākhāli' prabhu āsane vasilā

SYNONYMS

samudra-snāna — a bath in the sea; kari' — taking; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śīghra — very soon; āilā — returned; carana — feet; pākhāli' — washing; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āsane — on a seat; vasilā — sat.

TRANSLATION

After bathing in the sea, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees returned very soon. The Lord then washed His feet and sat down on a carpet to take lunch.
