Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.24

āmā-sabā chādi' āge gelā daraśane

āmi-saba pāche āilāń tāńra anveshane

SYNONYMS

āmā-sabā — all of us; chādi' — leaving; āge — ahead; gelā — went; daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; āmi-saba — all of us; pāche — behind; āilāń — came; tāńra — of Him; anveshane — in search.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu left our company and walked ahead to see Lord Jagannātha. We have just arrived and are now looking for Him.
