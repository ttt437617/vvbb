Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.200

'ińho ta' sākshāt krishna, — muñi nā jāniyā

mahā-aparādha kainu garvita ha-iyā'

SYNONYMS

ińho — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ta' — indeed; sākshāt — directly; krishna — Lord Krishna; muñi — I; nā — not; jāniyā — knowing; mahā-aparādha — a great offense; kainu — did; garvita — proud; ha-iyā — being.

TRANSLATION

"Caitanya Mahāprabhu is certainly Lord Krishna Himself. Because I could not understand Him and was very proud of my own learning, I have committed many offenses."
