Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.19

mukunda-sahita pūrve āche paricaya

mukunda dekhiyā tāńra ha-ila vismaya

SYNONYMS

mukunda-sahita — with Mukunda Datta; pūrve — previously; āche — there was; paricaya — acquaintance; mukunda — Mukunda Datta; dekhiyā — seeing; tāńra — of him (Gopīnātha Ācārya); ha-ila — there was; vismaya — astonishment.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya had previously been acquainted with Mukunda Datta, and when the Ācārya saw him at Jagannātha Purī, he was very much astonished.
