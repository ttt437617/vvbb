Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.233

āji se khandila tomāra dehādi-bandhana

āji tumi chinna kaile māyāra bandhana

SYNONYMS

āji — today; se — that; khandila — dismantled; tomāra — your; deha-ādi-bandhana — material bondage due to the bodily concept of life; āji — today; tumi — you; chinna — cut to pieces; kaile — did; māyāra — of illusory energy; bandhana — the shackles.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Bhattācārya, today you have been released from material bondage in the bodily conception of life; you have cut to pieces the shackles of the illusory energy.
