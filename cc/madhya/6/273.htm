Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.273

dui-arthe 'krishna' kahi, kene pātha phiri

sārvabhauma kahe, — o-pātha kahite nā pāri

SYNONYMS

dui-arthe — by two interpretations; krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; kahi — I accept; kene — why; pātha — reading; phiri — changing; sārvabhauma kahe — Sārvabhauma replied; o-pātha — such a reading; kahite — to say; nā — not; pāri — am able.

TRANSLATION

"Since I can understand Krishna according to these two meanings," Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "what point is there in changing the verse?"

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "I was not able to give that reading to the verse.
