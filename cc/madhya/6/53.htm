Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.53

sārvabhauma kahe, — nīlāmbara cakravartī

viśāradera samādhyāyī, — ei tāńra khyāti

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma kahe — Sārvabhauma said; nīlāmbara cakravartī — the gentleman named Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; viśāradera — of Maheśvara Viśārada (Sārvabhauma's father); samādhyāyī — class friend; ei — this; tāńra — of him; khyāti — acquaintance.

TRANSLATION

The Bhattācārya said, "Nīlāmbara Cakravartī was a classmate of my father, Maheśvara Viśārada. I knew him as such.
