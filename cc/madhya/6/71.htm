Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.71

gopīnātha kahe, — nāma śrī-krishna-caitanya

guru ińhāra keśava-bhāratī mahā-dhanya

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha kahe — Gopīnātha Ācārya replied; nāma — His name; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Śrī Krishna Caitanya; guru — sannyāsa-guru; ińhāra — His; keśava-bhāratī — of the name Keśava Bhāratī; mahā-dhanya — the greatly fortunate personality.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Ācārya replied, "The Lord's name is Śrī Krishna Caitanya, and His sannyāsa preceptor is the greatly fortunate Keśava Bhāratī."
