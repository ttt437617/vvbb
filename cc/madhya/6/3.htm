Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.3

āveśe calilā prabhu jagannātha-mandire

jagannātha dekhi' preme ha-ilā asthire

SYNONYMS

āveśe — in ecstasy; calilā — went; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jagannātha-mandire — to the temple of Jagannātha; jagannātha dekhi' — seeing the Jagannātha Deity; preme — in ecstasy; ha-ilā — became; asthire — restless.

TRANSLATION

In ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went from Āthāranālā to the temple of Jagannātha. After seeing Lord Jagannātha, He became very restless due to love of Godhead.
