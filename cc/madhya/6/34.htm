Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.34

jagannātha dekhi' sabāra ha-ila ānanda

bhāvete āvishta hailā prabhu nityānanda

SYNONYMS

jagannātha dekhi' — seeing Lord Jagannātha; sabāra — of everyone; ha-ila — there was; ānanda — pleasure; bhāvete — in ecstasy; āvishta — overwhelmed; hailā — became; prabhu nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda.

TRANSLATION

Everyone was then very pleased to see the Deity of Lord Jagannātha. Lord Nityānanda in particular was overwhelmed with ecstasy.
