Madhya-līlāChapter 6: The Liberation of Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 6.166

īśvarera śrī-vigraha sac-cid-ānandākāra

se-vigrahe kaha sattva-gunera vikāra

SYNONYMS

īśvarera — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; śrī-vigraha — the form; sat-cit-ānanda-ākāra — complete in eternity, cognizance and bliss; se-vigrahe — about that form of the Lord; kaha — you say; sattva-gunera — of the quality of material goodness; vikāra — transformation.

TRANSLATION

"The transcendental form of the Supreme Personality of Godhead is complete in eternity, cognizance and bliss. However, you describe this transcendental form as a product of material goodness.
