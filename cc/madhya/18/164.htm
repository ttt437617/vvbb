Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.164

prabhure dekhiyā mleccha karaye vicāra

ei yati-pāśa chila suvarna apāra

SYNONYMS

prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhiyā — seeing; mleccha — the Muslims; karaye vicāra — considered; ei yati-pāśa — within the possession of this sannyāsī; chila — there was; suvarna apāra — a large quantity of gold.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the Lord unconscious, the soldiers thought, "This sannyāsī must have possessed a large quantity of gold.
