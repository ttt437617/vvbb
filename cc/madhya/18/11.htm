Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.11

kundera 'mādhurī' — yena rādhāra 'madhurimā'

kundera 'mahimā' — yena rādhāra 'mahimā'

SYNONYMS

kundera — of the lake; mādhurī — sweetness; yena — as if; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; madhurimā — sweetness; kundera — of the lake; mahimā — glories; yena — as if; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; mahimā — glories.

TRANSLATION

"The attraction of Rādhā-kunda is as sweet as that of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. Similarly, the glories of the kunda [lake] are as great as Śrīmatī Rādhārānī's.
