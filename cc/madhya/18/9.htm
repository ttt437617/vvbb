Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.9

yei kunde nitya krishna rādhikāra sańge

jale jala-keli kare, tīre rāsa-rańge

SYNONYMS

yei kunde — in which lake; nitya — daily; krishna — Lord Krishna; rādhikāra sańge — accompanied by Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; jale — in the water; jala-keli — sporting in the water; kare — performs; tīre — on the bank; rāsa-rańge — His rāsa dance.

TRANSLATION

"In that lake, Lord Krishna and Śrīmatī Rādhārānī used to sport daily in the water and have a rāsa dance on the bank.
