Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.107

vrindāvane 'krishna' āilā, — sei 'satya' haya

krishnere dekhila loka, — ihā 'mithyā' naya

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; krishna — Lord Krishna; āilā — has come back; sei — that; satya haya — is true; krishnere — Krishna; dekhila — saw; loka — the people; ihā mithyā naya — this is not false.

TRANSLATION

"Actually Lord Krishna has returned to Vrindāvana. That is the truth, and it is also true that people have seen Him.
