Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.189

prabhu kahe, — tomāra śāstra sthāpe 'nirviśeshe'

tāhā khandi' 'saviśesha' sthāpiyāche śeshe

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued to speak; tomāra śāstra — your scripture (the Koran); sthāpe — establishes; nirviśeshe — impersonalism; tāhā khandi' — refuting that; sa-viśesha — personal God; sthāpiyāche — established; śeshe — at the end.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "The Koran certainly establishes impersonalism, but at the end it refutes that impersonalism and establishes the personal God.
