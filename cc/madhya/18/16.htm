Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.16

govardhana dekhi' prabhu ha-ilā dandavat

'eka śilā' ālińgiyā ha-ilā unmatta

SYNONYMS

govardhana dekhi' — seeing Govardhana Hill; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ilā dandavat — offered obeisances like a straight rod; eka śilā — one piece of stone; ālińgiyā — embracing; ha-ilā — became; unmatta — maddened.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord saw Govardhana Hill, He immediately offered obeisances, falling down on the ground like a rod. He embraced one piece of rock from Govardhana Hill and became mad.
