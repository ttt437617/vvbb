Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.228

caitanya-caritra ei — 'amritera sindhu'

jagat ānande bhāsāya yāra eka-bindu

SYNONYMS

caitanya-caritra — pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — these; amritera sindhu — ocean of nectar; jagat — the whole world; ānande — with bliss; bhāsāya — inundates; yāra — of which; eka-bindu — one drop.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are an ocean of nectar. Even a drop of this ocean can inundate the whole world with transcendental bliss.
