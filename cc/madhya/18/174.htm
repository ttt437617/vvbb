Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.174

ekhani āsibe saba, āmi yadi phukāri

ghodā-pidā luti' labe tomā-sabā māri'

SYNONYMS

ekhani — immediately; āsibe saba — all of them will come; āmi — I; yadi — if; phu-kāri — call loudly; ghodā-pidā — horses and their saddles; luti' — plundering; labe — will take; tomā-sabā māri' — after killing all of you.

TRANSLATION

"If I call loudly, they will come immediately to kill you and plunder your horses and saddles.
