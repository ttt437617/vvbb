Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.221

dakshina yāite yaiche śakti prakāśilā

sei-mata paścima deśa, preme bhāsāilā

SYNONYMS

dakshina yāite — while touring in the southern part of India; yaiche — as; śakti prakāśilā — manifested His spiritual energy; sei-mata — in that way; paścima deśa — the western part of India; preme bhāsāilā — inundated with love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Just as the Lord inundated South India on His tour there, He also inundated the western part of the country with love of Godhead.

PURPORT

According to some opinions, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu visited Kurukshetra while going to Prayāga from Vrindāvana. There is a temple of Bhadra-kālī in Kurukshetra, and near that temple there is a temple containing the Deity of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
