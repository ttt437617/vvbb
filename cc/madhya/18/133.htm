Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.133

kānyakubja-dākshinātyera vaidika brāhmana

dainya kari, kare mahāprabhura nimantrana

SYNONYMS

kānyakubja — brāhmanas from Kānyakubja; dākshinātyera — certain brāhmanas from South India; vaidika — followers of the Vedic religion; brāhmana — brāhmanas; dainya kari — with great humility; kare — do; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nimantrana — invitation.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmanas from different places, such as Kānyakubja and South India, who were all strict followers of the Vedic religion, offered invitations to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great humility.
