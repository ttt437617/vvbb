Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.149

prātah-kāle āise loka, tomāre nā pāya

tomāre nā pāñā loka mora māthā khāya

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāle — in the morning; āise — come; loka — people; tomāre — You; nā pāya — cannot see; tomāre nā pāñā — not getting You; loka — people; mora māthā khāya — tax my brain.

TRANSLATION

"Early in the morning people come here, and not seeing You present, they simply tax my brain.
