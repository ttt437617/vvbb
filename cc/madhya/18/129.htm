Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.129

mādhava-purīra śishya seita brāhmana

mathurāra ghare-ghare karā'na nimantrana

SYNONYMS

mādhava-purīra — of Mādhavendra Purī; śishya — disciple; seita — that; brāhmana — brāhmana; mathurāra — of Mathurā City; ghare-ghare — home to home; karā'na — causes to make; nimantrana — invitation.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana disciple of Mādhavendra Purī went from house to house in Mathurā and inspired other brāhmanas to invite Caitanya Mahāprabhu to their homes.
