Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.104

loka kahe, — rātrye kaivartya naukāte cadiyā

kālīya-dahe matsya māre, deutī jvāliyā

SYNONYMS

loka kahe — the sensible respectable persons said; rātrye — at night; kaivartya — a fisherman; naukāte — on a boat; cadiyā — getting up; kālīya-dahe — in the lake of Kālīya; matsya māre — catches fish; deutī jvāliyā — lighting a torch.

TRANSLATION

These respectable gentlemen replied, "At night in Kālīya Lake a fisherman lights a torch in his boat and catches many fish.
