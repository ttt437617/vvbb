Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.82

hena-kāle āila vaishnava 'krishnadāsa' nāma

rājaputa-jāti, — grihastha, yamunā-pāre grāma

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; āila — came; vaishnava — a devotee; krishnadāsa nāma — of the name Krishnadāsa; rājaputa-jāti — belonging to the kshatriya class; grihastha — householder; yamunā-pāre grāma — his residence on the other side of the Yamunā.

TRANSLATION

During this time, a Vaishnava named Krishnadāsa came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He was a householder belonging to the kshatriya caste, and his house was located on the other side of the Yamunā.
