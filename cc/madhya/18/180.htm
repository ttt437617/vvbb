Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.180

bhattācārya āsi' prabhure dhari' vasāila

mleccha-gana dekhi' mahāprabhura 'bāhya' haila

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — the Bhattācārya; āsi' — immediately coming near; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhari' — taking; vasāila — made to sit; mleccha-gana dekhi' — seeing the Muslim soldiers; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhya — external consciousness; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Balabhadra Bhattācārya went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and made Him sit down. Seeing the Muslim soldiers, the Lord regained His normal senses.
