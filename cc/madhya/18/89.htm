Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.89

prabhu-sańge madhyāhne akrūra tīrthe āilā

prabhura avaśishta-pātra-prasāda pāilā

SYNONYMS

prabhu-sańge — with the Lord; madhyāhne — in the afternoon; akrūra tīrthe — to Akrūra-tīrtha; āilā — came; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avaśishta-pātra-prasāda — remnants of food; pāilā — got.

TRANSLATION

Krishnadāsa returned to Akrūra-tīrtha with the Lord, and remnants of the Lord's food were given to him.
