Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.54

eka-māsa rahi' gopāla gelā nija-sthāne

śrī-rūpa-gosāñi āilā śrī-vrindāvane

SYNONYMS

eka-māsa rahi' — staying for one month; gopāla — the Gopāla Deity; gelā — went; nija-sthāne — to His own place; śrī-rūpa-gosāñi — Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; āilā — came back; śrī-vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

After staying at Mathurā for one month, the Gopāla Deity returned to His own place, and Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Vrindāvana.
