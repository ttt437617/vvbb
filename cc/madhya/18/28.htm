Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.28

āji rātrye palāha, nā rahiha eka-jana

thākura lañā bhāga', āsibe kāli yavana'

SYNONYMS

āji rātrye — this night; palāha — go away; nā rahiha — do not remain; eka-jana — one person; thākura — the Deity; lañā — taking; bhāga' — go away; āsibe — will come; kāli — tomorrow; yavana — the Muslim soldiers.

TRANSLATION

"Flee this village tonight, and do not allow one person to remain. Take the Deity with you and leave, for the Muslim soldiers will come tomorrow."
