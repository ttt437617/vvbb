Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.110

vrindāvane ha-ilā tumi krishna-avatāra

tomā dekhi' sarva-loka ha-ila nistāra

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; ha-ilā — became; tumi — You; krishna-avatāra — incarnation of Krishna; tomā dekhi' — by seeing You; sarva-loka — all people; ha-ila nastāra — become liberated.

TRANSLATION

The people then said, "You have appeared in Vrindāvana as an incarnation of Krishna. Just by seeing You, everyone is now liberated."
