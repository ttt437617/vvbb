Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.183

prabhu kahena, — thak nahe, mora 'sańgī' jana

bhikshuka sannyāsī, mora nāhi kichu dhana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; thak nahe — they are not rogues; mora sańgī jana — My associates; bhikshuka — beggar; sannyāsī — sannyāsī; mora — My; nāhi — are not; kichu — any; dhana — riches.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "These are not rogues. They are My associates. Being a sannyāsī beggar, I do not possess anything.
