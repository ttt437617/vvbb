Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.175

gaudiyā — 'bātapāda' nahe, tumi — 'bātapāda'

tīrtha-vāsī lutha', āra cāha' māribāra

SYNONYMS

gaudiyā — the Bengalis; bātapāda nahe — are not rogues; tumi — you; bātapāda — rogues; tīrtha-vāsī — persons visiting places of pilgrimage; lutha' — you plunder; āra — and; cāha' — you want; māribāra — to kill.

TRANSLATION

"The Bengali pilgrims are not rogues. You are rogues, for you want to kill the pilgrims and plunder them."
