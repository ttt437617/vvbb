Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.13

ei-mata stuti kare premāvishta hañā

tīre nritya kare kunda-līlā sańariyā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; stuti kare — offers prayers; prema-āvishta — overwhelmed by ecstatic love; hañā — becoming; tīre — on the bank; nritya kare — dances; kunda-līlā — pastimes of Rādhā-kunda; sańariyā — remembering.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus offered prayers to Rādhā-kunda. Overwhelmed by ecstatic love, He danced on the bank, remembering the pastimes Lord Krishna performed on the bank of Rādhā-kunda.
