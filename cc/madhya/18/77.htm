Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.77

nikate yamunā vahe śītala samīra

vrindāvana-śobhā dekhe yamunāra nīra

SYNONYMS

nikate — near the Teńtulī-talā, or Āmli-talā; yamunā — the Yamunā; vahe — flows; śītala samīra — very cool breeze; vrindāvana-śobhā — the beauty of Vrindāvana; dekhe — sees; yamunāra — of the river Yamunā; nīra — water.

TRANSLATION

Since the river Yamunā flowed near Teńtulī-talā, a very cool breeze blew there. While there, the Lord saw the beauty of Vrindāvana and the water of the river Yamunā.
