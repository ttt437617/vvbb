Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.154

ye tomāra icchā, āmi seita kariba

yāhāń lañā yāha tumi, tāhāńi yāiba"

SYNONYMS

ye tomāra icchā — whatever you like; āmi — I; seita kariba — must act accordingly; yāhāń — wherever; lañā yāha — take; tumi — you; tāhāńi yāiba — I shall go there.

TRANSLATION

"Whatever you desire, I must do. Wherever you take Me, I shall go."
