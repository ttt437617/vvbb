Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.198

nija-śāstra dekhi' tumi vicāra kariyā

ki likhiyāche śeshe kaha nirnaya kariyā

SYNONYMS

nija-śāstra — your own scripture; dekhi' — seeing; tumi — you; vicāra kariyā — deliberating; ki likhiyāche — what was written; śeshe — at the end; kaha — say; nirnaya kariyā — ascertaining.

TRANSLATION

"Seeing your own Koran and deliberating over what is written there, what is your conclusion?"
