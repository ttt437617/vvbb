Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.45

parvate nā cade dui — rūpa-sanātana

ei-rūpe tāń-sabāre diyāchena daraśana

SYNONYMS

parvate — on the hill; nā cade — do not ascend; dui — two; rūpa-sanātana — Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī; ei-rūpe — in this way; tāń-sabāre — unto them; diyāchena — has given; daraśana — interview.

TRANSLATION

The two brothers Rūpa and Sanātana did not climb the hill. To them also Lord Gopāla granted an interview.
