Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.29

śuniyā grāmera loka cintita ha-ila

prathame gopāla lañā gāńthuli-grāme khuila

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; grāmera loka — all the people in the village; cintita ha-ila — became very anxious; prathame — first; gopāla lañā — taking Gopāla; gāńthuli-grāme — in the village known as Gāńthuli; khuila — kept Him hidden.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, all the villagers became very anxious. They first took Gopāla and moved Him to a village known as Gāńthuli.
