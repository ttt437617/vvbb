Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.10

sei kunde yei eka-bāra kare snāna

tāńre rādhā-sama 'prema' krishna kare dāna

SYNONYMS

sei kunde — in that lake; yei — anyone who; eka-bāra — once; kare snāna — takes a bath; tāńre — unto him; rādhā-sama — like Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; prema — ecstatic love; krishna — Lord Krishna; kare dāna — gives as charity.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, Lord Krishna gives ecstatic love like that of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī to whoever bathes in that lake even once in his life.
