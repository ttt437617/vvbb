Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.73

cetana pāñā punah gadāgadi yāya

hāse, kānde, nāce, pade, uccaih-svare gāya

SYNONYMS

cetana pāñā — getting His senses back; punah — again; gadāgadi yāya — rolls on the ground; hāse — laughs; kānde — cries; nāce — dances; pade — falls down; uccaih-svare gāya — sings very loudly.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord regained His senses, He began to roll on the ground. He would sometimes laugh, cry, dance and fall down. He would also chant very loudly.
