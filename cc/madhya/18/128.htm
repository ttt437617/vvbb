Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.128

ei-mata kata-dina 'akrūre' rahilā

krishna-nāma-prema diyā loka nistārilā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; kata-dina — for some days; akrūre rahilā — stayed at Akrūra-tīrtha; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; prema — ecstatic love; diyā — distributing; loka — everyone; nistārilā — delivered.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained for some days in Akrūra-tīrtha. He delivered everyone there simply by distributing the holy name of Krishna and ecstatic love for the Lord.
