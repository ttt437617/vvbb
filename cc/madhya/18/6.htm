Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.6

dekhi' saba grāmya-lokera vismaya haila mana

preme prabhu kare rādhā-kundera stavana

SYNONYMS

dekhi' — seeing; saba grāmya-lokera — of all the people of the village; vismaya haila — became astonished; mana — the minds; preme — in ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — does; rādhā-kundera — of Rādhā-kunda; stavana — prayers.

TRANSLATION

When the people of the village saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu taking His bath in those two ponds in the middle of the paddy fields, they were very much astonished. The Lord then offered His prayers to Śrī Rādhā-kunda.
