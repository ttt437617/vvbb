Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.56

prabhura gamana-rīti pūrve ye likhila

sei-mata vrindāvane tāvat dekhila

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gamana-rīti — method of touring; pūrve — formerly; ye — which; likhila — I have written; sei-mata — similarly; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; tāvat dekhila — saw all the places.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's touring Vrindāvana has been previously described. In the same ecstatic way, He traveled all over Vrindāvana.
