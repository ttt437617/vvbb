Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.62

vrajendra-vrajeśvarīra kaila carana vandana

premāveśe krishnera kaila sarvāńga-sparśana

SYNONYMS

vraja-indra — of the King of Vraja, Nanda Mahārāja; vraja-īśvarīra — and of the Queen of Vraja, mother Yaśodā; kaila — did; carana vandana — worshiping the lotus feet; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; kaila — did; sarva-ańga-sparśana — touching the whole body.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His respects to Nanda Mahārāja and mother Yaśodā, and with great ecstatic love He touched the body of Lord Krishna.
