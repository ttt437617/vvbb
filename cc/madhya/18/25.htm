Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.25

anārurukshave śailam

svasmai bhaktābhimānine

avaruhya gireh krishno

gaurāya svam adarśayat

SYNONYMS

anārurukshave — who was unwilling to climb up; śailam — the mountain; svasmai — unto Himself; bhakta-abhimānine — considering Himself a devotee of Lord Krishna; avaruhya — getting down; gireh — from the hill; krishnah — Lord Krishna; gaurāya — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svam — Himself; adarśayat — showed.

TRANSLATION

Coming down from Govardhana Hill, Lord Gopāla granted an interview to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who was unwilling to climb the hill, thinking Himself a devotee of Lord Krishna.
