Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.17

preme matta cali' āilā govardhana-grāma

'harideva' dekhi' tāhāń ha-ilā pranāma

SYNONYMS

preme — in ecstatic love; matta — maddened; cali' — proceeding; āilā — came; govardhana-grāma — to the village known as Govardhana; harideva — the Deity named Harideva installed there; dekhi' — seeing; tāhāń — there; ha-ilā pranāma — offered obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Mad with ecstatic love, the Lord came to the village known as Govardhana. There He saw the Deity named Harideva and offered His obeisances unto Him.
