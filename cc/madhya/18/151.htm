Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.151

udvigna ha-ila prāna, sahite nā pāri

prabhura ye ājñā haya, sei śire dhari"

SYNONYMS

udvigna — agitated; ha-ila — has become; prāna — my mind; sahite — to bear; nā pāri — I am unable; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ye — what; ājñā — order; haya — there is; sei śire dhari — I accept that.

TRANSLATION

"My mind has become very much agitated, and I cannot bear this anxiety. Now everything rests on the permission of Your Lordship. I will accept whatever You want to do."
