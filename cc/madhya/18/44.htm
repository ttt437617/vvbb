Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.44

kabhu kuñje rahe, kabhu rahe grāmāntare

sei bhakta, tāhāń āsi' dekhaye tāńhāre

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; kuñje — in the bushes; rahe — remains; kabhu — sometimes; rahe — He stays; grāma-antare — in a different village; sei bhakta — that devotee; tāhāń āsi' — coming there; dekhaye tāńhāre — sees Him.

TRANSLATION

In this way, giving some excuse, Gopāla sometimes remains in the bushes of the forest, and sometimes He stays in a village. One who is a devotee comes to see the Deity.
