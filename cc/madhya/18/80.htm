Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.80

vrindāvane āsi' prabhu vasiyā ekānta

nāma-sańkīrtana kare madhyāhna-paryanta

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane āsi' — coming to Vrindāvana; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vasiyā — sitting; ekānta — in a solitary place; nāma-sańkīrtana kare — performs chanting of the holy name; madhyāhna-paryanta — until noon.

TRANSLATION

Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go to Vrindāvana and sit in a solitary place, where He would chant the holy name until noon.
