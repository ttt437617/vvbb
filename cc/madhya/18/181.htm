Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.181

mleccha-gana āsi' prabhura vandila carana

prabhu-āge kahe, — ei thak cāri-jana

SYNONYMS

mleccha-gana — the Muslim soldiers; āsi' — after coming there; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vandila carana — worshiped the lotus feet; prabhu-āge kahe — said before the Lord; ei thak cāri-jana — these four persons are rogues.

TRANSLATION

All the Muslim soldiers then came before the Lord, worshiped His lotus feet and said, "Here are four rogues.
