Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.141

lokera sańghatta, āra nimantranera jañjāla

nirantara āveśa prabhura nā dekhiye bhāla

SYNONYMS

lokera sańghatta — crowds of people; āra — and; nimantranera jañjāla — the disturbance of invitations; nirantara — always; āveśa — ecstatic love; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā dekhiye bhāla — I do not see any good in this.

TRANSLATION

"Now there is a crowd of people here, and these invitations are causing much disturbance. In addition, the Lord is always ecstatic and emotional. I do not find the situation here very good.
