Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.223

vrindāvana-gamana, prabhu-caritra ananta

'sahasra-vadana' yāńra nāhi pā'na anta

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-gamana — going to Vrindāvana; prabhu-caritra — pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ananta — unlimited; sahasra-vadana — Lord Śesha, who has thousands of hoods; yāńra — whose; nāhi — does not; pā'na — get; anta — limit.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's visit to Vrindāvana and His activities there are unlimited. Even Lord Śesha, who has thousands of hoods, cannot reach the end of His activities.
