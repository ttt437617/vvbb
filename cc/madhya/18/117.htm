Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.117

loka kahe, — tomāte kabhu nahe 'jīva'-mati

krishnera sadriśa tomāra ākriti-prakriti

SYNONYMS

loka kahe — the people said; tomāte — unto You; kabhu — at any time; nahe — there is not; jīva-mati — considering an ordinary living being; krishnera sadriśa — like Lord Krishna; tomāra — Your; ākriti — bodily features; prakriti — characteristics.

TRANSLATION

After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu explained the difference between an ordinary living being and the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the people said, "No one considers You an ordinary human being. You are like Krishna in every respect, in both bodily features and characteristics.
