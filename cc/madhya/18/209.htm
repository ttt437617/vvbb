Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.209

'krishna' bali' pade sei mahāprabhura pāya

prabhu śrī-carana dila tāńhāra māthāya

SYNONYMS

krishna bali' — chanting the holy name of Krishna; pade — falls down; sei — that Vijulī Khān; mahāprabhura pāya — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrī-carana dila — placed His foot; tāńhāra māthāya — on his head.

TRANSLATION

Vijulī Khān also fell down at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord placed His foot on his head.
