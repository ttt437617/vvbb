Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.177

huńkāra kariyā uthe, bale 'hari' 'hari'

premāveśe nritya kare ūrdhva-bāhu kari'

SYNONYMS

huń-kāra kariyā — resounded very loudly; uthe — stands up; bale hari hari — chants "Hari, Hari"; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; nritya kare — dances; ūrdhva-bāhu kari' — raising His arms upward.

TRANSLATION

Coming to His senses, the Lord very loudly began chanting the holy name, "Hari! Hari!" The Lord raised His arms upward and began to dance in ecstatic love.
