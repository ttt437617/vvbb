Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.173

krishnadāsa kahe, — āmāra ghara ei grāme

dui-śata turkī āche, śateka kāmāne

SYNONYMS

krishnadāsa kahe — Rājaputa Krishnadāsa said; āmāra ghara — my home; ei grāme — in this village; dui-śata turkī — two hundred Turks; āche — I have; śateka kāmāne — one hundred cannons.

TRANSLATION

Rājaputa Krishnadāsa said, "I have my home here, and I also have about two hundred Turkish soldiers and about one hundred cannons.
