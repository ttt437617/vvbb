Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.138

dekhi' krishnadāsa kāndi' phukāra karila

bhattācārya śīghra āsi' prabhure uthāila

SYNONYMS

dekhi' — seeing; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa; kāndi' — crying; phu-kāra karila — called loudly; bhattācārya — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; śīghra — hastily; āsi' — coming; prabhure uthāila — raised Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When Krishnadāsa saw that Caitanya Mahāprabhu was drowning, he cried and shouted very loudly. Balabhadra Bhattācārya immediately came and pulled the Lord out.
