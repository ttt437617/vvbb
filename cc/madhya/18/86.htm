Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.86

rājaputa-jāti muñi, o-pāre mora ghara

mora icchā haya — 'hańa vaishnava-kińkara'

SYNONYMS

rājaputa-jāti — belong to the Rājaputa caste; muñi — I; o-pāre — on the other side of the Yamunā; mora ghara — my residence; mora icchā haya — I wish; hańa — to become; vaishnava-kińkara — the servant of a Vaishnava.

TRANSLATION

"I belong to the Rājaputa caste, and my home is just on the other side of the river Yamunā. But I wish to be the servant of a Vaishnava.
