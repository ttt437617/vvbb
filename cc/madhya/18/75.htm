Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.75

prāte vrindāvane kailā 'cīra-ghāte' snāna

teńtulī-talāte āsi' karilā viśrāma

SYNONYMS

prāte — in the morning; vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; kailā — performed; cīra-ghāte snāna — bathing at Cīra-ghāta; teńtulī-talāte — underneath the Teńtulī tree; āsi' — coming; karilā viśrāma — took rest.

TRANSLATION

The next morning Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Vrindāvana and took His bath at Cīra-ghāta. He then went to Teńtulī-talā, where He took rest.
