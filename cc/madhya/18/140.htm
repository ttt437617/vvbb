Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.140

āji āmi āchilāńa uthāiluń prabhure

vrindāvane dubena yadi, ke uthābe tāńre?

SYNONYMS

āji — today; āmi — I; āchilāńa — was present; uthāiluń — raised; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; dubena yadi — if He drowns; ke uthābe tāńre — who will raise Him.

TRANSLATION

Balabhadra Bhattācārya said, "Since I was present today, it was possible for me to pull the Lord up. But if He starts to drown at Vrindāvana, who will help Him?
