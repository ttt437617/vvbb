Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.120

alaukika 'prakriti' tomāra — buddhi-agocara

tomā dekhi' krishna-preme jagat pāgala

SYNONYMS

alaukika — uncommon; prakriti — characteristics; tomāra — Your; buddhi-agocara — beyond our imagination; tomā dekhi' — by seeing You; krishna-preme — in ecstatic love for Krishna; jagat — the whole world; pāgala — mad.

TRANSLATION

"Indeed, Your characteristics are uncommon and beyond the imagination of an ordinary living being. Simply by seeing You, the entire universe becomes mad with ecstatic love for Krishna.
