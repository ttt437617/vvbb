Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.144

'soro-kshetre, āge yāñā kari' gańgā-snāna

sei pathe prabhu lañā kariye payāna

SYNONYMS

soro-kshetre — to the holy place named Soro-kshetra; āge — first, beyond; yāñā — going; kari' gańgā-snāna — having taken a bath in the Ganges; sei pathe — that way; prabhu lañā — taking Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariye payāna — let us go.

TRANSLATION

"After going to the holy place named Soro-kshetra and bathing in the Ganges, let us take Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu that way and go.
