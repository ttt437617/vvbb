Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.142

vrindāvana haite yadi prabhure kādiye

tabe mańgala haya, — ei bhāla yukti haye

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana haite — from Vrindāvana; yadi — if; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kādiye — I take away; tabe — then; mańgala haya — there is auspiciousness; ei — this; bhāla — good; yukti — plan; haye — is.

TRANSLATION

"It would be good if we could get Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu out of Vrindāvana. That is my final conclusion."
