Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.178

premāveśe prabhu yabe karena citkāra

mlecchera hridaye yena lāge śeladhāra

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yabe — when; karena citkāra — loudly shouts; mlecchera hridaye — in the hearts of the Muslim soldiers; yena — as if; lāge — strikes; śela-dhāra — a thunderbolt.

TRANSLATION

When the Lord shouted very loudly in ecstatic love, it appeared to the Muslim soldiers that their hearts were struck by thunderbolts.
