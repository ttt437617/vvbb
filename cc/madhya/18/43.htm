Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.43

dekhite utkanthā haya, nā cade govardhane

kona chale gopāla āsi' utare āpane

SYNONYMS

dekhite — to see; utkanthā haya — there was great anxiety; nā cade — does not go up; govardhane — on the hill known as Govardhana; kona chale — by some trick; gopāla — the Gopāla Deity; āsi' — coming; utare — descends; āpane — personally.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very eager to see Gopāla, but He did not want to climb Govardhana Hill. Therefore by some trick the Gopāla Deity personally descended.
