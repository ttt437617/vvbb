Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.90

prāte prabhu-sańge āilā jala-pātra lañā

prabhu-sańge rahe griha-strī-putra chādiyā

SYNONYMS

prāte — in the morning; prabhu-sańge — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; jala-pātra lañā — carrying a waterpot; prabhu-sańge rahe — remains with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; griha — home; strī — wife; putra — children; chādiyā — leaving aside.

TRANSLATION

The next morning, Krishnadāsa went with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Vrindāvana and carried His waterpot. Krishnadāsa thus left his wife, home and children in order to remain with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
