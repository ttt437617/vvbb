Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.74

ei-rańge sei-dina tathā gońāilā

sandhyā-kāle akrūre āsi' bhikshā nirvāhilā

SYNONYMS

ei-rańge — in this amusement; sei-dina — that day; tathā gońāilā — passed the day there; sandhyā-kāle — in the evening; akrūre āsi' — returning to Akrūra-tīrtha; bhikshā nirvāhilā — took His meal.

TRANSLATION

Being thus transcendentally amused, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed that day happily at Keśī-tīrtha. In the evening He returned to Akrūra-tīrtha, where He took His meal.
