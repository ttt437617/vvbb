Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.137

eta bali' jhāńpa dilā jalera upare

dubiyā rahilā prabhu jalera bhitare

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; jhāńpa dilā — jumped; jalera upare — above the water; dubiyā — sinking; rahilā — remained; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jalera bhitare — within the water.

TRANSLATION

While considering how Akrūra remained within the water, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately jumped in and stayed under water for some time.
