Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.92

eka-dina akrūrete loka prātah-kāle

vrindāvana haite āise kari' kolāhale

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; akrūrete — at Akrūra-tīrtha; loka — people; prātah-kāle — in the morning; vrindāvana haite — from Vrindāvana; āise — came; kari' — making; kolāhale — tumult.

TRANSLATION

One morning many people came to Akrūra-tīrtha. As they came from Vrindāvana, they made a tumultuous sound.
