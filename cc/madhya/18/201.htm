Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.201

seita 'gosāñi' tumi — sākshāt 'īśvara'

more kripā kara, muñi — ayogya pāmara

SYNONYMS

seita — that; gosāñi — Personality of Godhead; tumi — You; sākshāt — directly; īśvara — the Personality of Godhead; more — upon me; kripā kara — kindly be merciful; muñi — I; ayogya pāmara — very fallen and unfit.

TRANSLATION

"Since You are that very same Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself, please be merciful upon me. I am fallen and unfit.
