Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.20

prabhu-prema-saundarya dekhi' loke camatkāra

haridevera bhritya prabhura karila satkāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-saundarya — ecstatic love and beauty; dekhi' — seeing; loke — people; camatkāra — astonished; haridevera — of Lord Harideva; bhritya — servants; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karila satkāra — offered a good reception.

TRANSLATION

The people were astonished when they saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic love and personal beauty. The priests who served the Harideva Deity offered the Lord a good reception.
