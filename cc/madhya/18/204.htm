Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.204

kripā kari' bala more 'sādhya-sādhane'

eta bali' pade mahāprabhura carane

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — by Your causeless mercy; bala — speak; more — to me; sādhya-sādhane — the ultimate object of life and the process to achieve it; eta bali' — saying this; pade — falls down; mahāprabhura carane — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, the saintly Muslim fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and requested Him to speak of life's ultimate goal and the process by which it could be obtained.
