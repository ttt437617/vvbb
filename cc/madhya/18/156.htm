Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.156

bāhya vikāra nāhi, premāvishta mana

bhattācārya kahe, — cala, yāi mahāvana

SYNONYMS

bāhya — external; vikāra — symptoms; nāhi — there were not; prema-āvishta mana — the mind was full of ecstatic love; bhattācārya kahe — the Bhattācārya said; cala — let us go; yāi mahāvana — let us go to Mahāvana.

TRANSLATION

Although the Lord did not exhibit any external symptoms, His mind was filled with ecstatic love. At that time, Balabhadra Bhattācārya said, "Let us go to Mahāvana [Gokula]."
