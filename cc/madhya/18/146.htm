Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.146

āpanāra duhkha kichu kari' nivedana

'makara-pańcasi prayāge' kariha sūcana

SYNONYMS

āpanāra — personal; duhkha — unhappiness; kichu — some; kari' — doing; nivedana — submission; makara-pańcasi — the full-moon day in the month of Māgha; prayāge — to Prayāga; kariha sūcana — kindly inform.

TRANSLATION

The Sanodiyā brāhmana continued, "Kindly submit to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the unhappiness you are feeling within yourself. Then propose that we all go to Prayāga on the full-moon day of the month of Māgha.
