Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.64

līlā-sthala dekhi' tāhāń gelā 'śeshaśāyī'

'lakshmī' dekhi' ei śloka padena gosāñi

SYNONYMS

līlā-sthala dekhi' — seeing the places of pastimes; tāhāń — there; gelā — departed; śesha-śāyī — for seeing Śeshaśāyī; lakshmī — the goddess of fortune; dekhi' — seeing; ei — this; śloka — verse; padena — recites; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

After seeing the places of Lord Krishna's pastimes, Śrī Caitanya went to Śeshaśāyī, where He saw Lakshmī and recited the following verse.
