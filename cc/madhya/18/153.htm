Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.153

"tumi āmāya āni' dekhāilā vrindāvana

ei 'rina' āmi nāriba karite śodhana

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; āmāya — Me; āni' — bringing; dekhāilā — showed; vrindāvana — the holy place named Vrindāvana; ei rina — this debt; āmi nāriba — I shall not be able; karite śodhana — to repay.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "You have brought Me here to show Me Vrindāvana. I am very much indebted to you, and I shall not be able to repay this debt.
