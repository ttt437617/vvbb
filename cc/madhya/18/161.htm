Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.161

ācambite eka gopa vamśī bājāila

śuni' mahāprabhura mahā-premāveśa haila

SYNONYMS

ācambite — suddenly; eka gopa — one cowherd boy; vamśī — flute; bājāila — blew; śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahā-prema-āveśa — absorption in great ecstatic love; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

Suddenly a cowherd boy blew on his flute, and immediately the Lord was struck with ecstatic love.
