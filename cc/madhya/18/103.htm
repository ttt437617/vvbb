Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.103

prātah-kāle bhavya-loka prabhu-sthāne āilā

'krishna dekhi' āilā?' — prabhu tāńhāre puchilā

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāle — the next morning; bhavya-loka — respectable gentlemen; prabhu-sthāne — at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; krishna dekhi' — seeing Lord Krishna; āilā — have you come; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńhāre puchilā — inquired from them.

TRANSLATION

The next morning some respectable gentlemen came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord asked them, "Have you seen Krishna?"
