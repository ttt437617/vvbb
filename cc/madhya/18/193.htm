Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.193

sarva-śreshtha, sarvārādhya, kāranera kārana

tāńra bhaktye haya jīvera samsāra-tārana

SYNONYMS

sarva-śreshtha — the Supreme Truth; sarva-ārādhya — worshipable by everyone; kāranera kārana — the cause of all causes; tāńra — His; bhaktye — by devotional service; haya — becomes; jīvera — of the living entity; samsāra-tārana — deliverance from material existence.

TRANSLATION

"The Lord is the Supreme Truth, worshipable by everyone. He is the cause of all causes. By engaging in His devotional service, the living entity is relieved from material existence.
