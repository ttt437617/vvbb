Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.167

krishnadāsa — rājaputa, nirbhaya se bada

sei vipra — nirbhaya, se — mukhe bada dada

SYNONYMS

krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa; rājaputa — belonging to the Rājaputa race; nirbhaya — fearless; se — he; bada — very; sei vipra — the Sanodiyā brāhmana; nirbhaya — also fearless; se — he; mukhe — in the mouth; bada dada — very brave.

TRANSLATION

The devotee Krishnadāsa, who belonged to the Rājaputa race, was very fearless. The Sanodiyā brāhmana was also fearless, and he spoke very bravely.
