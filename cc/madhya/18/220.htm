Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.220

tāńra sańge anyonye, tāńra sańge āna

ei-mata 'vaishnava' kailā saba deśa-grāma

SYNONYMS

tāńra sańge — with Him; anyonye — other; tāńra sańge — and with him; āna — another; ei-mata — in this way; vaishnava — Vaishnava; kailā — made; saba — all; deśa-grāma — villages and towns.

TRANSLATION

Whoever met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became a Vaishnava, and whoever met that Vaishnava also became a Vaishnava. In this way, all the towns and villages became Vaishnava, one after the other.
