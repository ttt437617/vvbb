Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.136

ei ghāte akrūra vaikuntha dekhila

vrajavāsī loka 'goloka' darśana kaila

SYNONYMS

ei ghāte — in this bathing place; akrūra — Akrūra; vaikuntha dekhila — saw the spiritual world; vrajavāsī loka — the inhabitants of Vrindāvana; goloka darśana kaila — saw Goloka.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thought, "At this bathing place, Akrūra saw Vaikuntha, the spiritual world, and all the inhabitants of Vraja saw Goloka Vrindāvana."
