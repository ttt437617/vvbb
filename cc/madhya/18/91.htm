Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.91

vrindāvane punah 'krishna' prakata ha-ila

yāhāń tāhāń loka saba kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana; punah — again; krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; prakata ha-ila — became manifested; yāhāń tāhāń — everywhere; loka — people; saba — all; kahite lāgila — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Everywhere the Lord went, all the people said, "Krishna has again manifested at Vrindāvana."
