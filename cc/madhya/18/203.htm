Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.203

tomā dekhi' jihvā mora bale 'krishna-nāma'

'āmi — bada jñānī' — ei gela abhimāna

SYNONYMS

tomā dekhi' — by seeing You; jihvā — tongue; mora — my; bale krishna-nāma — chants the Hare Krishna mantra; āmi — I; bada jñānī — very learned scholar; ei — this; gela abhimāna — false prestige has gone away.

TRANSLATION

"Now that I have seen You, my tongue is chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra. The false prestige I felt from being a learned scholar is now gone."
