Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.1

vrindāvane sthira-carān

nandayan svāvalokanaih

ātmānam ca tad-ālokād

gaurāńgah parito 'bhramat

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; sthira-carān — to the living entities, both moving and not moving; nandayan — giving pleasure; sva-avalokanaih — by His personal glances; ātmānam — to Himself; ca — also; tat-ālokāt — by seeing them; gaurāńgah — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; paritah — all around; abhramat — traveled.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu traveled all over Vrindāvana and pleased all living entities, moving and nonmoving, with His glances. The Lord took much personal pleasure in seeing everyone. In this way Lord Gaurāńga traveled in Vrindāvana.
