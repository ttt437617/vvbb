Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.139

tabe bhattācārya sei brāhmane lañā

yukti karilā kichu nibhrite vasiyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; bhattācārya — Bhattācārya; sei brāhmane — the Sanodiyā brāhmana; lañā — taking; yukti karilā — consulted; kichu — something; nibhrite vasiyā — sitting in a solitary place.

TRANSLATION

After this, Balabhadra Bhattācārya took the Sanodiyā brāhmana to a secluded place and consulted with him.
