Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.102

'vātula' nā ha-io, ghare rahata vasiyā

'krishna' daraśana kariha kāli rātrye yāñā"

SYNONYMS

vātula — mad; nā ha-io — do not become; ghare — at home; rahata — keep; vasiyā — sitting; krishna — Lord Krishna; daraśana — seeing; kariha — you may do; kāli — tomorrow; rātrye — at night; yāñā — going.

TRANSLATION

"Do not become mad. Simply sit down here, and tomorrow night you will go see Krishna."
