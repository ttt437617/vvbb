Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.206

'krishna' kaha, 'krishna' kaha, — kailā upadeśa

sabe 'krishna' kahe, sabāra haila premāveśa

SYNONYMS

krishna kaha — just chant "Krishna"; krishna kaha — just chant "Krishna"; kailā upadeśa — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu instructed; sabe — all; krishna kahe — chant the holy name of Krishna; sabāra — of all of them; haila — there was; prema-āveśa — ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then told all the Muslims there, "Chant the holy name of Krishna! Chant the holy name of Krishna!" As they all began to chant, they were overwhelmed by ecstatic love.
