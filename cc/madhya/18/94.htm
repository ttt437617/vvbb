Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.94

loke kahe, — krishna prakata kālīya-dahera jale!

kālīya-śire nritya kare, phanā-ratna jvale

SYNONYMS

loke kahe — all the people replied; krishna prakata — Krishna is again manifest; kālīya-dahera jale — in the water of Lake Kālīya; kālīya-śire — on the head of the serpent Kālīya; nritya kare — dances; phanā-ratna jvale — the jewels on the hoods blaze.

TRANSLATION

The people replied, "Krishna has again manifested Himself on the waters of Kālīya Lake. He dances on the hoods of the serpent Kālīya, and the jewels on those hoods are blazing.
