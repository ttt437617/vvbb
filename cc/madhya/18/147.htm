Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.147

gańgā-tīra-pathe sukha jānāiha tāńre

bhattācārya āsi' tabe kahila prabhure

SYNONYMS

gańgā-tīra — on the bank of the Ganges; pathe — on the path; sukha — happiness; jānāiha — kindly let know; tāńre — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhattācārya — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; āsi' — coming; tabe — thereafter; kahila prabhure — informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

"Tell the Lord of the happiness you will feel in traveling via the banks of the Ganges." Balabhadra Bhattācārya therefore submitted this prayer to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
