Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.131

eka-dina 'daśa' 'biśa' āise nimantrana

bhattācārya ekera mātra karena grahana

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — in one day; daśa biśa — ten to twenty; āise — come; nimantrana — the invitations; bhattācārya — Balabhadra Bhattācārya; ekera — of one of them; mātra — only; karena grahana — accepts.

TRANSLATION

In one day, ten to twenty invitations were received, but Balabhadra Bhattācārya would accept only one of them.
