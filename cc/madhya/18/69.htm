Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.69

'gokula' dekhiyā āilā 'mathurā'-nagare

janma-sthāna' dekhi' rahe sei vipra-ghare

SYNONYMS

gokula dekhiyā — seeing Gokula; āilā — came; mathurā-nagare — in the city of Mathurā; janma-sthāna — the birthplace of Lord Krishna; dekhi' — seeing; rahe — stays; sei vipra-ghare — in the house of the Sanodiyā brāhmana.

TRANSLATION

After seeing Gokula, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to Mathurā, where He saw the birthplace of the Lord. While there, He stayed at the house of the Sanodiyā brāhmana.
