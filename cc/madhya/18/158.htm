Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.158

premī krishnadāsa, āra seita brāhmana

gańgā-tīra-pathe yāibāra vijña dui-jana

SYNONYMS

premī krishnadāsa — the devotee Rājaputa Krishnadāsa; āra — and; seita brāhmana — that Sanodiyā brāhmana; gańgā-tīra-pathe — on the path by the bank of the Ganges; yāibāra — to go; vijña — experienced; dui-jana — two persons.

TRANSLATION

Both Rājaputa Krishnadāsa and the Sanodiyā brāhmana knew the path along the Ganges bank very well.
