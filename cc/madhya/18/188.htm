Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.188

yei yei kahila, prabhu sakali khandila

uttara nā āise mukhe, mahā-stabdha haila

SYNONYMS

yei yei kahila — whatever he spoke; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sakali khandila — refuted everything; uttara — answer; nā āise — could not come; mukhe — in his mouth; mahā-stabdha haila — he became greatly stunned.

TRANSLATION

Whatever arguments he put forward, the Lord refuted them all. Finally the person became stunned and could not speak.
