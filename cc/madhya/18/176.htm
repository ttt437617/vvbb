Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.176

śuniyā pāthāna mane sańkoca ha-ila

hena-kāle mahāprabhu 'caitanya' pāila

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; pāthāna — the Muslim soldiers; mane — in the mind; sańkoca ha-ila — there was a little hesitation; hena-kāle — at this time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; caitanya pāila — came to his senses.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this challenge, the Pāthāna soldiers became hesitant. Then suddenly Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regained consciousness.
