Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.205

prabhu kahe, — utha, krishna-nāma tumi la-ilā

koti-janmera pāpa gela, 'pavitra' ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; utha — please get up; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; tumi — you; la-ilā — have taken; koti-janmera — of many millions of births; pāpa gela — your sinful reactions have gone; pavitra ha-ilā — you have become pure.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Please get up. You have chanted the holy name of Krishna; therefore the sinful reactions you have accrued for many millions of lives are now gone. You are now pure."
