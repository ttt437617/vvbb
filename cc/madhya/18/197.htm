Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.197

tomāra pandita-sabāra nāhi śāstra-jñāna

pūrvāpara-vidhi-madhye 'para' — balavān

SYNONYMS

tomāra pandita-sabāra — of the learned scholars of your community; nāhi — there is not; śāstra-jñāna — knowledge of revealed scripture; pūrva-apara — former and latter; vidhi — regulative principles; madhye — among; para — the conclusion at the end; balavān — most powerful.

TRANSLATION

"The scholars of the Koran are not very advanced in knowledge. Although there are many methods prescribed, they do not know that the ultimate conclusion should be considered the most powerful.
