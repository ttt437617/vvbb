Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.84

prabhura rūpa-prema dekhi' ha-ila camatkāra

premāveśe prabhure karena namaskāra

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rūpa-prema — personal beauty and ecstatic love; dekhi' — seeing; ha-ila camatkāra — became astonished; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; prabhure — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena namaskāra — offers obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing the Lord's personal beauty and ecstatic love, Krishnadāsa was very much astonished. Out of ecstatic love, he offered his respectful obeisances unto the Lord.
