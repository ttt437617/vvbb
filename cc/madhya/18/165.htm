Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.165

ei cāri bātoyāra dhuturā khāoyāñā

māri' dāriyāche, yatira saba dhana lañā

SYNONYMS

ei — these; cāri — four; bātoyāra — rogues; dhuturā — dhuturā; khāoyāñā — making Him eat; māri' dāriyāche — killed; yatira — of the sannyāsī; saba — all; dhana — wealth; lañā — taking away.

TRANSLATION

"These four rogues here must have taken away that sannyāsī's riches after killing Him by making Him take the poison dhuturā."
