Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.97

prabhu-āge kahe loka, — śrī-krishna dekhila

'sarasvatī' ei vākye 'satya' kahāila

SYNONYMS

prabhu-āge — in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe loka — all the people began to say; śrī-krishna dekhila — that they have seen Lord Krishna; sarasvatī — the goddess of learning; ei vākye — this statement; satya — true; kahāila — caused the people to speak.

TRANSLATION

Everyone came before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and said, "Now we have directly seen Lord Krishna." Thus by the mercy of the goddess of learning they were made to speak the truth.
