Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.192

srishti, sthiti, pralaya tāńhā haite haya

sthūla-sūkshma-jagatera teńho samāśraya

SYNONYMS

srishti — creation; sthiti — maintenance; pralaya — dissolution; tāńhā — Him; haite — from; haya — becomes possible; sthūla — gross; sūkshma — subtle; jagatera — of the cosmic manifestation; teńho — He; samāśraya — the only shelter.

TRANSLATION

"Creation, maintenance and dissolution come from Him. He is the original shelter of all gross and subtle cosmic manifestations.
