Madhya-līlāChapter 18: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Visit to Śrī Vrindāvana

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 18.98

mahāprabhu dekhi' 'satya' krishna-daraśana

nijājñāne satya chādi' 'asatye satya-bhrama'

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu dekhi' — by seeing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; satya — truly; krishna-daraśana — seeing Krishna; nija-ajñāne — by their personal lack of knowledge; satya chādi' — giving up the real truth; asatye — untruth; satya-bhrama — mistaking for the truth.

TRANSLATION

When the people saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they actually saw Krishna, but because they were following their own imperfect knowledge, they accepted the wrong thing as Krishna.
