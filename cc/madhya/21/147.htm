Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.147

tabe mahāprabhu kshaneka mauna kari' rahe

mane eka kari' punah sanātane kahe

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kshaneka — for a moment; mauna — silence; kari' — making; rahe — remained; mane — within His mind; eka kari' — adjusting things; punah — again; sanātane — unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; kahe — instructs.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then remained silent for a moment. Finally, adjusting things within His mind, He again spoke to Sanātana Gosvāmī.
