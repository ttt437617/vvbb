Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.38

e sāmānya, tryadhīśvarera śuna artha āra

jagat-kārana tina purushāvatāra

SYNONYMS

e sāmānya — this is a general description; tri-adhīśvarera — of the master of the three worlds; śuna — please hear; artha — meaning; āra — another; jagat-kārana — the cause of the material creation; tina — three; purusha-avatāra — purusha incarnations of Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

"This is only a general description. Please try to understand another meaning of tryadhīśa. The three purusha incarnations of Vishnu are the original cause of the material creation.
