Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.47

'madhyama-āvāsa' krishnera — shad-aiśvarya-bhāndāra

ananta svarūpe yāhāń karena vihāra

SYNONYMS

madhyama-āvāsa — the middle residence; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; shat-aiśvarya-bhāndāra — the storehouse of six opulences; ananta svarūpe — in unlimited forms; yāhāń — where; karena vihāra — enjoys His pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"The spiritual sky, which is full in all six opulences, is the intermediate residence of Lord Krishna. It is there that an unlimited number of forms of Krishna enjoy Their pastimes.
