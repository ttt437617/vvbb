Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.3

sarva svarūpera dhāma — paravyoma-dhāme

prithak prithak vaikuntha saba, nāhika ganane

SYNONYMS

sarva — all; svarūpera — of the personal forms; dhāma — abode; para-vyoma-dhāme — in the spiritual sky; prithak prithak — separate; vaikuntha — Vaikuntha planets; saba — all; nāhika ganane — there is no counting.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "All the transcendental forms of the Lord are situated in the spiritual sky. They preside over spiritual planets in that abode, but there is no counting those Vaikuntha planets.
