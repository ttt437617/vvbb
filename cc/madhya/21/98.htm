Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.98

krishnera aiśvarya — apāra amritera sindhu

avagāhite nāri, tāra chuilań eka bindu

SYNONYMS

krishnera aiśvarya — the opulence of Krishna; apāra — unlimited; amritera sindhu — an ocean of nectar; avagāhite — to bathe; nāri — I am unable; tāra — of that; chuilań — I touched; eka bindu — only one drop.

TRANSLATION

"The unlimited potencies of Krishna are just like an ocean of nectar. Since one cannot bathe within that ocean, I have only touched a drop of it."
