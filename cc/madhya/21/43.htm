Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.43

'antahpura' — goloka-śrī-vrindāvana

yāhāń nitya-sthiti mātā-pitā-bandhu-gana

SYNONYMS

antah-pura — the internal abode; goloka-śrī-vrindāvana — Goloka Vrindāvana; yāhāń — where; nitya-sthiti — eternal residence; mātā-pitā — mother and father; bandhu-gana — and friends.

TRANSLATION

"The internal abode is called Goloka Vrindāvana. It is there that Lord Krishna's personal friends, associates, father and mother live.
