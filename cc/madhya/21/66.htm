Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.66

śuni' hāsi' krishna tabe karilena dhyāne

asańkhya brahmāra gana āilā tata-kshane

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; hāsi' — smilingly; krishna — Lord Krishna; tabe — then; karilena — did; dhyāne — meditation; asańkhya — unlimited; brahmāra — of Lord Brahmās; gana — the group; āilā — arrived; tata-kshane — at that time.

TRANSLATION

"Upon hearing this, Śrī Krishna smiled and immediately meditated. Unlimited Brahmās arrived instantly.
