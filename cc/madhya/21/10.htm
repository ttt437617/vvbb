Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.10

ei-mata krishnera divya sad-guna ananta

brahmā-śiva-sanakādi nā pāya yāńra anta

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; divya — transcendental; sat-guna — spiritual qualities; ananta — unlimited; brahmā — Lord Brahmā; śiva — Lord Śiva; sanaka-ādi — the four Kumāras and so on; nā — not; pāya — obtain; yāńra — of which; anta — the limit.

TRANSLATION

"The spiritual qualities of Krishna are also unlimited. Great personalities like Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva and the four Kumāras cannot estimate the spiritual qualities of the Lord.
