Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.48

ananta vaikuntha yāhāń bhāndāra-kothari

pārishada-gane shad-aiśvarye āche bhari'

SYNONYMS

ananta — unlimited; vaikuntha — Vaikuntha planets; yāhāń — where; bhāndāra-kothari — like rooms of a treasure-house; pārishada-gane — eternal associates; shat-aiśvarye — with the six opulences; āche — are; bhari' — filling.

TRANSLATION

"Innumerable Vaikuntha planets, which are just like different rooms of a treasure-house, are all there, filled with all opulences. Those unlimited planets house the Lord's eternal associates, who are also enriched with the six opulences.
