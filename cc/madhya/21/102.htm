Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.102

krishnera madhura rūpa, śuna, sanātana

ye rūpera eka kana, dubāya saba tribhuvana,

sarva prānī kare ākarshana

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; madhura — sweet; rūpa — form; śuna — please hear; sanātana — O My dear Sanātana; ye rūpera — of which form; eka kana — even a fraction; dubāya — floods; saba — all; tri-bhuvana — the three worlds; sarva prānī — all living entities; kare — does; ākarshana — attracting.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sanātana, the sweet, attractive transcendental form of Krishna is so nice. Just try to understand it. Even a fractional understanding of Krishna's beauty can merge all three worlds in the ocean of love. He attracts all living entities within the three worlds.
