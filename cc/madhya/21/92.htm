Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.92

antarańga-pūrnaiśvarya-pūrna tina dhāma

tinera adhīśvara — krishna svayam bhagavān

SYNONYMS

antarańga — internal; pūrna-aiśvarya-pūrna — full of all opulences; tina dhāma — three abodes; tinera adhīśvara — the Lord of all three; krishna — Lord Krishna; svayam bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"These three places are full of internal potencies, and Krishna, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, is their sole proprietor.
