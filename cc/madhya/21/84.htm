Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.84

krishna kahe, "ei brahmānda pañcāśat koti yojana

ati kshudra, tāte tomāra cāri vadana

SYNONYMS

krishna kahe — Krishna said; ei brahmānda — this universe; pañcāśat koti yojana — four billion miles; ati kshudra — very small; tāte — therefore; tomāra — your; cāri vadana — four faces.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna said, 'Your particular universe has a diameter of four billion miles; therefore it is the smallest of all the universes. Consequently you have only four heads.

PURPORT

Śrīla Bhaktisiddhānta Sarasvatī Thākura, one of the greatest astrologers of his time, gives information from the Siddhānta-śiromani that this universe measures 18,712,069,200,000,000 x 8 miles. This is the circumference of this universe. According to some, this is only half the circumference.
