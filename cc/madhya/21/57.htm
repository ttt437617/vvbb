Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.57

tripāda-vibhūti krishnera — vākya-agocara

eka-pāda vibhūtira śunaha vistāra

SYNONYMS

tri-pāda-vibhūti krishnera — three fourths of the energy of Lord Krishna; vākya-agocara — beyond words; eka-pāda vibhūtira — of one fourth of the energy; śunaha — please hear; vistāra — breadth.

TRANSLATION

"The three-fourths part of Lord Krishna's energy is beyond our speaking power. Let us therefore hear elaborately about the remaining one fourth of His energy.
