Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.85

kona brahmānda śata-koti, kona laksha-koti

kona niyuta-koti, kona koti-koti

SYNONYMS

kona brahmānda — some universes; śata-koti — one billion yojanas; kona — some; laksha-koti — one trillion yojanas; kona — some; niyuta-koti — ten trillion; kona — some; koti-koti — one hundred trillion.

TRANSLATION

"'Some of the universes are one billion yojanas in diameter, some one trillion, some ten trillion and some one hundred trillion yojanas. Thus they are almost unlimited in area.

PURPORT

A yojana equals eight miles.
