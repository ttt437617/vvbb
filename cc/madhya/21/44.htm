Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.44

madhuraiśvarya-mādhurya-kripādi-bhāndāra

yogamāyā dāsī yāhāń rāsādi līlā-sāra

SYNONYMS

madhura-aiśvarya — of sweetness and opulence; mādhurya — of conjugal love; kripā-ādi — and of mercy and so on; bhāndāra — storehouse; yoga-māyā — the spiritual energy; dāsī — maidservant; yāhāń — where; rāsa-ādi — the rāsa dance and other pastimes; līlā-sāra — the quintessence of all pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Vrindāvana is the storehouse of Krishna's mercy and the sweet opulences of conjugal love. That is where the spiritual energy, working as a maidservant, exhibits the rāsa dance, the quintessence of all pastimes.
