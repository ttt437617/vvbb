Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.25

"ye kahe — 'krishnera vaibhava muñi saba jānoń'

se jānuka, — kāya-mane muñi ei mānoń

SYNONYMS

ye kahe — if anyone says; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; vaibhava — opulences; muñi — I; saba — all; jānoń — know; se jānuka — let him know; kāya-mane — by my body and mind; muñi — myself; ei — this; mānoń — accept.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Brahmā said, 'If someone says that he knows everything about Krishna's opulence, let him think that way. But as far as I am concerned, with my body and mind I consider it in this way.
