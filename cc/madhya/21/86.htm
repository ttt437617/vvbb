Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.86

brahmāndānurūpa brahmāra śarīra-vadana

ei-rūpe pāli āmi brahmāndera gana

SYNONYMS

brahmānda-anurūpa — according to the size of a universe; brahmāra — of Lord Brahmā; śarīra-vadana — heads on the body; ei-rūpe — in this way; pāli āmi — I maintain; brahmāndera gana — all the innumerable groups of universes.

TRANSLATION

"'According to the size of the universe, there are so many heads on the body of Brahmā. In this way I maintain innumerable universes [brahmāndas].
