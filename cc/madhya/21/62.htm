Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.62

krishne jānāñā dvārī brahmāre lañā gelā

krishnera carane brahmā dandavat kailā

SYNONYMS

krishne jānāñā — informing Lord Krishna; dvārī — the doorman; brahmāre — Lord Brahmā; lañā — taking; gelā — went; krishnera carane — at the lotus feet of Krishna; brahmā — Lord Brahmā; dandavat kailā — offered obeisances.

TRANSLATION

"The doorman then informed Lord Krishna of Lord Brahmā's description, and Lord Krishna gave him permission to enter. The doorman escorted Lord Brahmā in, and as soon as Brahmā saw Lord Krishna, he offered obeisances at His lotus feet.
