Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.23

eka krishna-deha haite sabāra prakāśe

kshaneke sabāi sei śarīre praveśe

SYNONYMS

eka — one; krishna-deha — transcendental body of Krishna; haite — from; sabāra — of everyone; prakāśe — the manifestation; kshaneke — in a second; sabāi — every one of Them; sei śarīre — in that body of Krishna; praveśe — enter.

TRANSLATION

"All these transcendental bodies emanated from the body of Krishna, and within a second They all entered again into His body.
