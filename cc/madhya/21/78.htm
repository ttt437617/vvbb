Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.78

dvārakādi — vibhu, tāra ei ta pramāna

'āmāra-i brahmānde krishna' sabāra haila jñāna

SYNONYMS

dvārakā-ādi — Dvārakā-dhāma and other abodes; vibhu — transcendental abode; tāra ei ta pramāna — this is the evidence of that; āmāra-i brahmānde — in my brahmānda; krishna — Krishna is now present; sabāra — of all of them; haila jñāna — there was this knowledge.

TRANSLATION

"This is the proof of Dvārakā's opulence: all the Brahmās thought, 'Krishna is now staying in my jurisdiction.'
