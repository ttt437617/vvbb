Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.71

krishnera acintya-śakti lakhite keha nāre

yata brahmā, tata mūrti eka-i śarīre

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; acintya-śakti — inconceivable potencies; lakhite — to observe; keha — anyone; nāre — not able; yata brahmā — all the Brahmās; tata mūrti — so many forms; eka-i śarīre — in the same body.

TRANSLATION

"No one can estimate the inconceivable potency of Krishna. All the Brahmās who were there were resting in the one body of Krishna.
