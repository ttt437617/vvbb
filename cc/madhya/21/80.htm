Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.80

tabe krishna sarva-brahmā-gane vidāya dilā

dandavat hañā sabe nija ghare gelā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; krishna — Lord Krishna; sarva-brahmā-gane — unto all the Brahmās; vidāya dilā — bade farewell; dandavat hañā — offering obeisances; sabe — all of them; nija ghare gelā — returned to their respective homes.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna then bade farewell to all the Brahmās there, and after offering their obeisances, they all returned to their respective homes.
