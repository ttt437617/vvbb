Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.111

kahite krishnera rase, śloka pade premāveśe,

preme sanātana-hāta dhari'

gopī-bhāgya, krishna guna, ye karila varnana,

bhāvāveśe mathurā-nāgarī

SYNONYMS

kahite — to describe; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; rase — the different types of mellows; śloka — a verse; pade — recites; prema-āveśe — absorbed in ecstatic love; preme — in such love; sanātana-hāta dhari' — catching the hand of Sanātana Gosvāmī; gopī-bhāgya — the fortune of the gopīs; krishna guna — the transcendental qualities of Krishna; ye — which; karila varnana — described; bhāva-āveśe — in ecstatic love; mathurā-nāgarī — the women of the city of Mathurā.

TRANSLATION

Just as the women of Mathurā ecstatically described the fortune of the gopīs of Vrindāvana and the transcendental qualities of Krishna, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu described the different mellows of Krishna and became overwhelmed with ecstatic love. Grasping the hand of Sanātana Gosvāmī, He recited the following verse.
