Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.19

"krishna-vatsair asańkhyātaih" — śukadeva-vānī

krishna-sańge kata gopa — sańkhyā nāhi jāni

SYNONYMS

krishna-vatsaih asańkhyātaih — Krishna was accompanied by an unlimited number of calves and cowherd boys; śukadeva-vānī — the words of Śukadeva Gosvāmī; krishna-sańge — with Lord Krishna; kata gopa — how many cowherd boys; sańkhyā — the count; nāhi jāni — we do not know.

TRANSLATION

"According to Śukadeva Gosvāmī, Krishna had unlimited calves and cowherd boys with Him. No one could count their actual number.
