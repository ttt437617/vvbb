Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21 Summary

Śrīla Bhaktivinoda Thākura gives the following summary of the Twenty-first Chapter. In this chapter Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fully describes Krishnaloka, the spiritual sky, the Causal Ocean and the material world, which consists of innumerable universes. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then describes Lord Brahmā's interview with Krishna at Dvārakā and the Lord's curbing the pride of Brahmā. There is also a description of one of Krishna's pastimes with Brahmā. In this chapter the author of the Caitanya-caritāmrita has presented some nice poems about the pastimes of Krishna and Krishna's superexcellent beauty. Throughout the rest of the chapter, our intimate relationship (sambandha) with Krishna is described.
