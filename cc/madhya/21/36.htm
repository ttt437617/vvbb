Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.36

brahmā, vishnu, hara, — ei srishtyādi-īśvara

tine ājñākārī krishnera, krishna — adhīśvara

SYNONYMS

brahmā — Lord Brahmā; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; hara — and Lord Śiva; ei — they; srishti-ādi-īśvara — the masters of material creation, maintenance and dissolution; tine — all three of them; ājñā-kārī — order carriers; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; krishna — Lord Krishna; adhīśvara — their master.

TRANSLATION

"The primary predominating deities of this material creation are Lord Brahmā, Lord Śiva and Lord Vishnu. Nonetheless, they simply carry out the orders of Lord Krishna, who is master of them all.
