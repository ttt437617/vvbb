Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.77

samprati prithivīte yebā haiyāchila bhāra

avatīrna hañā tāhā karilā samhāra'

SYNONYMS

samprati — presently; prithivīte — upon the earth; yebā — whatever; haiyāchila — there was; bhāra — burden; avatīrna hañā — descending; tāhā — that; karilā samhāra — You have taken away.

TRANSLATION

"'Whatever burden was upon the earth You have taken away by descending to that planet.'
