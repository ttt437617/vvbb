Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.5

saba vaikuntha — vyāpaka, ānanda-cinmaya

pārishada-shadaiśvarya-pūrna saba haya

SYNONYMS

saba — all; vaikuntha — the spiritual planets; vyāpaka — vast; ānanda-cit-maya — made of spiritual bliss; pārishada — associates; shat-aiśvarya — six kinds of opulence; pūrna — in full; saba — all; haya — are.

TRANSLATION

"Each Vaikuntha planet is very large, and each is made of spiritual bliss. The inhabitants are all associates of the Supreme Lord, and they have full opulence like the Lord Himself. This is how the Vaikuntha planets are all situated.
