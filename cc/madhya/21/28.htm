Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.28

krishnera mahimā rahu — kebā tāra jñātā

vrindāvana-sthānera dekha āścarya vibhutā

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mahimā — glories; rahu — let be; kebā — who; tāra — of those; jñātā — a knower; vrindāvana-sthānera — of the abode of Krishna, Vrindāvana; dekha — just see; āścarya — wonderful; vibhutā — opulences.

TRANSLATION

"Let the glories of Lord Krishna be! Who could be aware of all of them? His abode, Vrindāvana, has many wonderful opulences. Just try to see them all.
