Madhya-līlāChapter 21: The Opulence and Sweetness of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 21.22

sabe hailā caturbhuja vaikunthera pati

prithak prithak brahmāndera brahmā kare stuti

SYNONYMS

sabe — all of them: hailā — became; catur-bhuja — four-handed; vaikunthera pati — predominating Deities of the Vaikuntha planets; prithak prithak — separately; brahmāndera — of the universes; brahmā — the predominating deities known as Lord Brahmā; kare stuti — offer prayers.

TRANSLATION

"The cowherd boys then became four-handed Nārāyanas, predominating Deities of Vaikuntha planets. All the separate Brahmās from different universes began to offer their prayers unto the Lords.
