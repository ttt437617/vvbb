Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.247

kāli haite tumi yei mrigādi māribā

prathamei māribā, ardha-mārā nā karibā"

SYNONYMS

kāli haite — from tomorrow; tumi — you; yei — whatever; mriga-ādi — animals; māribā — you will kill; prathamei māribā — kill them in the beginning; ardha-mārā — half-killing; nā karibā — do not do.

TRANSLATION

"'I beg you that from this day on you will kill animals completely and not leave them half-dead.'
