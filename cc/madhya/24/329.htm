Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.329

tathāpi ei sūtrera śuna dig-daraśana

sakārana likhi ādau guru-āśrayana

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; ei sūtrera — of the synopsis of this book; śuna — hear; dik-daraśana — an indication; sakārana — the cause; likhi — we should write; ādau — in the beginning; guru-āśrayana — accepting a bona fide spiritual master.

TRANSLATION

"Because you asked Me for a synopsis, please hear these few indications. In the beginning describe how one must take shelter of a bona fide spiritual master.
