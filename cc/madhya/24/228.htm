Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.228

'krishnārāmāś ca' eva — haya krishna-manana

vyādha hañā haya pūjya bhāgavatottama

SYNONYMS

krishna-ārāmāh ca — one who takes pleasure in Krishna; eva — certainly; haya — there is; krishna-manana — meditation on Krishna; vyādha hañā — being a hunter; haya — is; pūjya — worshipable; bhāgavata-uttama — the best of the devotees.

TRANSLATION

"The words 'krishnārāmaś ca' refer to one who takes pleasure in thinking of Krishna. Even though such a person may be a hunter, he is still worshipable and is the best of devotees.
