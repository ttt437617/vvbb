Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.322

ei mata kahiluń eka ślokera vyākhyāna

vātulera pralāpa kari' ke kare pramāna?

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; kahiluń — I have spoken; eka — one; ślokera — of a verse; vyākhyāna — the explanation; vātulera pralāpa — the talking of a madman; kari' — doing; ke kare pramāna — who will accept this as evidential proof.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, like a madman, I have explained the meaning of just one verse. I do not know who will take this as evidence.
