Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.295

itaretara 'ca' diyā samāsa kariye

'ātānna'-bāra ātmārāma nāma la-iye

SYNONYMS

itaretara — in different varieties; ca — the word ca; diyā — adding; samāsa kariye — make a compound word; ātānna-bāra — fifty-eight times; ātmārāma — the ātmārāma devotees; nāma la-iye — I take their different names.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, as I add the word 'ca' to one word after another, I make a compound. Thus the different names of ātmārāmas can be taken fifty-eight times.
