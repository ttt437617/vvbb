Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.328

prabhu kahe, — "ye karite karibā tumi mana

krishna sei sei tomā karābe sphurana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; ye — whatever; karite — to do; karibā tumi mana — you want; krishna — Lord Krishna; sei sei — that; tomā — to you; karābe sphurana — will manifest.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Whatever you want to do you will be able to do correctly by Lord Krishna's favor. He will manifest the real purport.

PURPORT

Sanātana Gosvāmī was a pure devotee of Krishna. A pure devotee has no business other than serving Krishna; consequently Krishna is always ready to help him. This benediction was given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to Sanātana Gosvāmī, who was authorized to write Vaishnava smriti. Sanātana Gosvāmī was a pure devotee of the Lord, and through the blessings of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu he was able to write the book perfectly.
