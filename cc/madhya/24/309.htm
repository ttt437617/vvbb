Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.309

"kshetrajña ātmā purushah

pradhānam prakritih striyām"

SYNONYMS

kshetra-jñah — the word kshetrajña; ātmā — the living entity; purushah — the enjoyer; pradhānam — the chief; prakritih — the material nature; striyām — in the feminine gender.

TRANSLATION

"'The word 'kshetrajña' refers to the living entity, the enjoyer, the chief and material nature.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Svarga-varga (7) of the Amara-kośa dictionary.
