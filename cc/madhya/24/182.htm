Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.182

krishna-bhakta — duhkha-hīna, vāñchāntara-hīna

krishna-prema-sevā-pūrnānanda-pravīna

SYNONYMS

krishna-bhakta — a devotee of Lord Krishna; duhkha-hīna — not under miserable material conditions; vāñchā-antara-hīna — he has no desire other than to serve Krishna; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; sevā — service; pūrna-ānanda — full in transcendental bliss; pravīna — and very expert or experienced in all subject matters.

TRANSLATION

"A devotee of Krishna is never in a miserable condition, nor does he have any desire other than to serve Krishna. He is experienced and advanced. He feels the transcendental bliss of love of Krishna and always engages in His service in full perfection.
