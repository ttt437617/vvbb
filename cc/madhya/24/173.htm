Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.173

teshām satata-yuktānām

bhajatām prīti-pūrvakam

dadāmi buddhi-yogam tam

yena mām upayānti te

SYNONYMS

teshām — to them; satata-yuktānām — always engaged; bhajatām — in devotional service; prīti-pūrvakam — in loving ecstasy; dadāmi — I give; buddhi-yogam — real intelligence; tam — that; yena — by which; mām — unto Me; upayānti — come; te — they.

TRANSLATION

"'To those who are constantly devoted to serving Me with love, I give the understanding by which they can come to Me.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Bhagavad-gītā (10.10). For an explanation, see Ādi-līlā 1.49
