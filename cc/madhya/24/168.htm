Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.168

'ātmā'-śabde 'yatna' kahe — yatna kariyā

"munayo 'pi" krishna bhaje gunākrishta hañā

SYNONYMS

ātmā-śabde — by the word ātmā; yatna — endeavor; kahe — one means; yatna kariyā — by great endeavor; munayah api — even great saintly persons; krishna bhaje — take to the devotional service of Krishna; guna-ākrishta hañā — being attracted by His transcendental qualities.

TRANSLATION

"'Ātmā' also means 'endeavor.' Being attracted by Krishna's transcendental qualities, some saints make a great endeavor to come to the point of rendering service to Him.
