Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.14

'muni'-ādi śabdera artha śuna, sanātana

prithak prithak artha pāche kariba milana

SYNONYMS

muni — the word muni; ādi — and the other; śabdera — of the words; artha — the meaning; śuna — hear; sanātana — My dear Sanātana; prithak prithak — separately; artha — meaning; pāche — after; kariba milana — I shall combine.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sanātana, first hear the meanings of the other words, beginning with the word 'muni.' I shall first explain their separate meanings, then combine them.
