Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.296

'ātmārāmāś ca ātmārāmāś ca' ātānna-bāra

śeshe saba lopa kari' rākhi eka-bāra

SYNONYMS

ātmārāmāh ca ātmārāmāh ca — repeating ātmārāmāh; ātānna-bāra — fifty-eight times; śeshe — at the end; saba lopa kari' — rejecting all; rākhi — we keep; eka-bāra — only one.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, one can repeat the word 'ātmārāmāh' with 'ca' for each of the fifty-eight meanings. By following the rule previously stated and rejecting all but the last, we retain that which represents all the meanings.
