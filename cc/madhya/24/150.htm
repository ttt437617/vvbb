Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.150

eka 'ātmārāma'-śabda avaśesha rahe

eka 'ātmārāma'-śabde chaya-jana kahe

SYNONYMS

eka — one; ātmārāma — ātmārāma; śabda — vibration; avaśesha rahe — remains at last; eka ātmārāma — one ātmārāma; śabde — by vibrating; chaya-jana — six persons; kahe — are indicated.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore there is no need to repeat the word 'ātmārāma.' One is sufficient, and that one word indicates six persons.
