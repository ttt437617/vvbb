Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.335

śrī-mūrti-lakshana, āra śālagrāma-lakshana

krishna-kshetra-yātrā, krishna-mūrti-daraśana

SYNONYMS

śrī-mūrti-lakshana — characteristics of the Deity; āra — and; śālagrāma-lakshana — characteristics of the śālagrāma-śilā; krishna-kshetra-yātrā — visiting places like Vrindāvana, Dvārakā and Mathurā; krishna-mūrti-daraśana — visiting the Deity in the temple.

TRANSLATION

"The characteristics of the Deities should be discussed, as well as the characteristics of the śālagrāma-śilā. You should also discuss visiting the Deities in the temple and touring holy places like Vrindāvana, Mathurā and Dvārakā.
