Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.57

guru-tulya strī-ganera vātsalye ākarshana

dāsya-sakhyādi-bhāve purushādi gana

SYNONYMS

guru-tulya — on the level of a superior guardian; strī-ganera — of the ladies of Vrindāvana; vātsalye — in parental affection; ākarshana — attracting; dāsya-sakhya-ādi — servants, friends and others; bhāve — in the mood of; purusha-ādi gana — all the males of Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"The women of Vrindāvana who are on the level of superior guardians are attracted to Lord Krishna maternally. The men of Vrindāvana are attracted as servants, friends and fathers.
