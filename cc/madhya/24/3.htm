Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.3

tabe sanātana prabhura carane dhariyā

punarapi kahe kichu vinaya kariyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; prabhura carane dhariyā — catching the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; punarapi — again; kahe — says; kichu — something; vinaya kariyā — with great humility.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, Sanātana Gosvāmī clasped the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and humbly submitted the following petition.
