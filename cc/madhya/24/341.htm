Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.341

ekādaśī, janmāshtamī, vāmana-dvādaśī

śrī-rāma-navamī, āra nrisimha-caturdaśī

SYNONYMS

ekādaśī — the eleventh day of the fortnight; janmāshtamī — the birthday ceremony of Lord Krishna; vāmana-dvādaśī — the birthday or appearance day of Lord Vāmana; śrī-rāma-navamī — the birthday ceremony of Lord Rāmacandra; āra — and; nrisimha-caturdaśī — the appearance day of Lord Nrisimha.

TRANSLATION

"Ekādaśī, Janmāshtamī, Vāmana-dvādaśī, Rāma-navamī and Nrisimha-caturdaśī — all these should be described.
