Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.191

vicāra kariyā yabe bhaje krishna-pāya

sei buddhi dena tāńre, yāte krishna pāya

SYNONYMS

vicāra — consideration; kariyā — doing; yabe — when; bhaje — one worships; krishna-pāya — at the lotus feet of Krishna; sei buddhi — that intelligence; dena — gives; tāńre — to him; yāte — by which; krishna pāya — one gets the shelter of the lotus feet of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Considering all these points, when one engages in the service of Krishna's lotus feet, Krishna gives one the intelligence by which he can gradually progress toward perfection in service to the Lord.
