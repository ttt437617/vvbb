Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.116

vyāsa-kripāya śukadevera līlādi-smarana

krishna-gunākrishta hañā karena bhajana

SYNONYMS

vyāsa-kripāya — by the mercy of Śrīla Vyāsadeva; śukadevera — of Śukadeva Gosvāmī; līlā-ādi-smarana — remembrance of the transcendental pastimes of Krishna; krishna-guna-ākrishta — attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna; hañā — becoming; karena — performed; bhajana — loving service.

TRANSLATION

"By the mercy of Śrīla Vyāsadeva, Śukadeva Gosvāmī was attracted by the pastimes of Lord Krishna. Being thus attracted by Krishna's transcendental qualities, he also became a devotee and engaged in His service.
