Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.122

'mumukshu' jagate aneka samsārī jana

'mukti' lāgi' bhaktye kare krishnera bhajana

SYNONYMS

mumukshu — desiring to be liberated; jagate — in this world; aneka — many; samsārī jana — engaged in material activities; mukti lāgi' — for the sake of liberation; bhaktye — in devotional service; kare — perform; krishnera bhajana — the worship of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"There are many people within this material world who desire liberation, and for this purpose they render devotional service to Lord Krishna.
