Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.85

rāga-bhaktye vraje svayam-bhagavāne pāya

SYNONYMS

rāga-bhaktye — by the discharge of spontaneous devotional service; vraje — in Vrindāvana; svayam — Himself; bhagavāne — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; pāya — one gets.

TRANSLATION

"By executing spontaneous devotional service in Vrindāvana, one attains the original Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna.
