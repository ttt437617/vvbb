Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.162

ca-śabde 'api'ra artha ihāńo kahaya

'muni', 'nirgrantha'-śabdera pūrvavat artha haya

SYNONYMS

ca-śabde — by the word ca; 'api'ra — of the word api; artha — the meaning; ihāńo — here also; kahaya — is applicable; muni — muni; nirgrantha — nirgrantha; śabdera — of the words; pūrva-vat — as mentioned above; artha haya — there are the meanings.

TRANSLATION

"The meanings of the words 'ca' and 'api' can be applied here. The meanings of the words 'muni' and 'nirgrantha' are the same as before.
