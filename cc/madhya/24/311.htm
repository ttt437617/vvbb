Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.311

shāti artha kahiluń, saba — krishnera bhajane

sei artha haya ei saba udāharane

SYNONYMS

shāti — sixty; artha — imports; kahiluń — I explained; saba — all; krishnera bhajane — aiming at rendering transcendental loving service to Krishna; sei artha haya — that is the only meaning; ei saba — all these; udāharane — examples.

TRANSLATION

"I have thus explained sixty different meanings, and all of them aim at the service of Lord Krishna. After giving so many examples, that is the only meaning.
