Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.53

vamśī-gīte hare krishna lakshmy-ādira mana

SYNONYMS

vamśī-gīte — by the vibration of His flute; hare — attracts; krishna — Lord Krishna; lakshmī-ādira — of the goddess of fortune and others; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna even attracts the mind of the goddess of fortune simply by vibrating His transcendental flute.
