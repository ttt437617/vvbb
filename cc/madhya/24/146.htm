Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.146

"ātmārāmāś ca api" kare krishne ahaitukī bhakti

"munayah santah" iti krishna-manane āsakti

SYNONYMS

ātmārāmāh ca api — self-realized persons also; kare — do; krishne — unto Krishna; ahaitukī bhakti — unmotivated devotional service; munayah santah — great saintly persons and transcendentalists; iti — thus; krishna-manane — in meditation on Krishna; āsakti — attraction.

TRANSLATION

"The six kinds of ātmārāmas render devotional service to Krishna without ulterior motives. The words 'munayah' and 'santah' indicate those who are very much attached to meditating upon Krishna.
