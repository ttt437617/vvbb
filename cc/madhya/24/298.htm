Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.298

ātānna ca-kārera saba lopa haya

eka ātmārāma-śabde ātānna artha kaya

SYNONYMS

ātānna — fifty-eight; ca-kārera — of the additions of the word ca; saba lopa haya — all of them are rejected; eka — one; ātmārāma — ātmārāma; śabde — by the word; ātānna artha kaya — fifty-eight imports are automatically derived.

TRANSLATION

"When all the ca-kāras, or additions of the word 'ca,' are taken away, fifty-eight different meanings can still be understood by the one word 'ātmārāma.'
