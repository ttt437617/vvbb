Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.26

"svarita-ñitah kartr-abhiprāye kriyā-phale"

SYNONYMS

svarita-ñ-itah — of verbs having an indicatory ñ or a svarita accent; kartri-abhiprāye — is meant for the agent; kriyā-phale — when the fruit of the action.

TRANSLATION

"'The terminations of the ātmane-pada are employed when the fruit of the action accrues to the agent of verbs having an indicatory ñ or a svarita accent.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from Pānini's sūtras (1.3.72).
