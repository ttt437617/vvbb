Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.324

punah sanātana kahe yudi' dui kare

"prabhu ājñā dilā 'vaishnava-smriti' karibāre

SYNONYMS

punah — again; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; kahe — says; yudi' dui kare — folding his two hands; prabhu ājñā dilā — Your Lordship has ordered me; vaishnava-smriti karibāre — to write a directory of Vaishnava activities.

TRANSLATION

Folding his hands, Sanātana Gosvāmī said, "My Lord, You ordered me to write a directory about the activities of Vaishnavas.
