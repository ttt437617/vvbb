Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.96

ei cāri sukriti haya mahā-bhāgyavān

tat-tat-kāmādi chādi' haya śuddha-bhaktimān

SYNONYMS

ei cāri — these four persons; sukriti — pious men; haya — are; mahā-bhāgyavān — highly fortunate; tat-tat — those respective; kāma-ādi — aspirations; chādi' — giving up; haya — become; śuddha-bhaktimān — pure devotees.

TRANSLATION

"Because they have a pious background, all four types of people are to be considered greatly fortunate. Such people gradually give up material desires and become pure devotees.
