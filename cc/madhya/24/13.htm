Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.13

ei sāte rame yei, sei ātmārāma-gana

ātmārāma-ganera āge kariba ganana

SYNONYMS

ei sāte — in these seven items; rame — enjoy; yei — those who; sei — they; ātmārāma-gana — ātmārāmas; ātmārāma-ganera — of the ātmārāmas; āge — later; kariba ganana — shall make a count.

TRANSLATION

"The word 'ātmārāma' refers to one who enjoys these seven items [the Absolute Truth, body, mind and so on]. Later, I shall enumerate the ātmārāmas.
