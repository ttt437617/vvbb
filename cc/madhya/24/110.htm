Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.110

bhaktira svabhāva, — brahma haite kare ākarshana

divya deha diyā karāya krishnera bhajana

SYNONYMS

bhaktira — of devotional service; svabhāva — nature; brahma — impersonal Brahman realization; haite — from; kare — does; ākarshana — attracting; divya — transcendental; deha — body; diyā — offering; karāya — causes to perform; krishnera bhajana — the service of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Characteristically, one in devotional service is attracted away from the impersonal Brahman platform. He is offered a transcendental body to engage in Lord Krishna's service.
