Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.152

tabe ye ca-kāra, sei 'samuccaya' kaya

"ātmārāmāś ca munayaś ca" krishnere bhajaya

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; ye — that; ca-kāra — syllable ca; sei — that; samuccaya — aggregation; kaya — is said; ātmārāmāh ca — all those who enjoy in the self; munayah ca — all saintly persons; krishnere bhajaya — worship Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"By the aggregate use of the word 'ca,' it is indicated that all the ātmārāmas and saints serve and worship Krishna.
