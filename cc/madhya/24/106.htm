Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.106

śloka-vyākhyā lāgi' ei kariluń ābhāsa

ebe kari ślokera mūlārtha prakāśa

SYNONYMS

śloka-vyākhyā — of the explanation of the verse; lāgi' — for the matter; ei — this; kariluń — I did; ābhāsa — indication; ebe — now; kari — let Me do; ślokera — of the verse; mūla-artha — the real meaning; prakāśa — the manifestation.

TRANSLATION

"I have given all these explanations just to give some indication of the verse's meaning. Now let Me explain the real meaning of the verse.
