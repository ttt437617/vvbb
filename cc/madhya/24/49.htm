Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.49

śrī-ańga-rūpe hare gopikāra mana

SYNONYMS

śrī-ańga — of His transcendental body; rūpe — by the beauty; hare — attracts; gopikāra mana — the minds of the gopīs.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Śrī Krishna attracts the minds of all the gopīs with His beautiful, transcendental bodily features.
