Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.78

ātatatvāc ca mātritvād

ātmā hi paramo harih

SYNONYMS

ātatatvāt — due to being all-pervading; ca — and; mātritvāt — due to being the progenitor; ātmā — the soul; hi — certainly; paramah — supreme; harih — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"'Hari, the Personality of Godhead, is the all-pervasive original source of everything; He is therefore the Supersoul of everything.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Bhāvārtha-dīpikā, Śrīdhara Svāmī's commentary on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
