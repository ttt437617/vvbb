Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.294

batriśe chābbiśe mili, ashta-pañcāśa

āra eka bheda śuna arthera prakāśa

SYNONYMS

batriśe — these thirty-two varieties; chābbiśe — the formerly described twenty-six varieties; mili' — added together; ashta-pañcāśa — they become fifty-eight; āra — another; eka — one; bheda — different; śuna — please hear; arthera prakāśa — manifestation of import.

TRANSLATION

"When we add the twenty-six types of devotees to these thirty-two, they altogether number fifty-eight. You may now hear from Me of further manifestations of meanings.
