Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.51

rūpa-guna-śravane rukminy-ādira ākarshana

SYNONYMS

rūpa — beauty; guna — qualities; śravane — by hearing; rukminī-ādira — of the queens, headed by Rukminī; ākarshana — attracting.

TRANSLATION

"The queens in Dvārakā, headed by Rukminī, are also attracted to Krishna simply by hearing about His transcendental beauty and qualities.
