Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.102

sakāma-bhakte 'ajña' jāni' dayālu bhagavān

sva-carana diyā kare icchāra pidhāna

SYNONYMS

sakāma-bhakte — to devotees who still have material desires to fulfill; ajña — foolish; jāni' — knowing; dayālu — merciful; bhagavān — Śrī Krishna; sva-carana — His own lotus feet; diyā — giving; kare — does; icchāra pidhāna — the covering of other desires.

TRANSLATION

"When merciful Lord Krishna understands that a foolish devotee desires material prosperity, He gratefully gives him the shelter of His lotus feet. In this way, the Lord covers the devotee's undesirable ambitions.
