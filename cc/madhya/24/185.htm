Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.185

'ca' — avadhārane, ihā 'api' — samuccaye

dhritimanta hañā bhaje pakshi-mūrkha-caye

SYNONYMS

ca — the word ca; avadhārane — in emphasis; ihā — here; api — the word api; samuccaye — in the sense of an aggregate; dhritimanta — fully saturated; hañā — becoming; bhaje — worship; pakshi-mūrkha-caye — dull creatures like the birds, the most foolish.

TRANSLATION

"The word 'ca' is for emphasis, and the word 'api' is used to indicate an aggregate. Thus it is to be understood that even dull creatures [birds and illiterates] can also be situated in endurance and engage in Krishna's devotional service.
