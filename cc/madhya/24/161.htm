Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.161

ei chaya yogī sādhu-sańgādi-hetu pāñā

krishna bhaje krishna-gune ākrishta hañā

SYNONYMS

ei — this; chaya — six; yogī — mystics; sādhu — of devotees; sańga-ādi — the association; hetu — because of; pāñā — getting; krishna bhaje — render service to Krishna; krishna-gune — by the transcendental qualities of Krishna; ākrishta — attracted; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

"When a purified yogī associates with devotees, he engages in Lord Krishna's devotional service, being attracted by the Lord's transcendental qualities.
