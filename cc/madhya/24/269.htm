Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.269

tabe dui rishi āilā sei vyādha-sthāne

dūra haite vyādha pāila gurura daraśane

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; dui rishi — two saintly persons; āilā — came; sei vyādha-sthāne — to the place of that hunter; dūra haite — from a distant place; vyādha — the hunter; pāila — got; gurura daraśane — vision of his spiritual master.

TRANSLATION

"As the saintly sages approached the hunter's place, the hunter could see them from a distance.
