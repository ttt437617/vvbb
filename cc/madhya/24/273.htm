Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.273

ete na hy adbhutā vyādha

tavāhimsādayo gunāh

hari-bhaktau pravrittā ye

na te syuh para-tāpinah

SYNONYMS

ete — all these; na — not; hi — certainly; adbhutāh — wonderful; vyādha — O hunter; tava — your; ahimsā-ādayah — nonviolence and others; gunāh — qualities; hari-bhaktau — in devotional service; pravrittāh — engaged; ye — those who; na — not; te — they; syuh — are; para-tāpinah — envious of other living entities.

TRANSLATION

"'O hunter, good qualities like nonviolence, which you have developed, are not very astonishing, for those engaged in the Lord's devotional service are never inclined to give pain to others because of envy.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Skanda Purāna.
