Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.344

'sāmānya' sad-ācāra, āra 'vaishnava'-ācāra

kartavyākartavya saba 'smārta' vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

sāmānya — general; sat-ācāra — good behavior; āra — and; vaishnava — of devotees of Lord Vishnu; ācāra — etiquette; kartavya-akartavya — things which are to be done and which are not to be done; saba — all; smārta — connected with regulative principles; vyavahāra — business.

TRANSLATION

"You should give general and specific descriptions of the behavior and activities of a Vaishnava. You should outline things that are to be done and things that are not to be done. All this should be described as regulations and etiquette.
