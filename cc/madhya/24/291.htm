Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.291

ajāta-rati sādhaka-bhakta, — e cāri prakāra

vidhi-mārge bhakte shodaśa bheda pracāra

SYNONYMS

ajāta-rati sādhaka-bhakta — immature devotees engaged in devotional service; e cāri prakāra — there are also the same four varieties; vidhi-mārge — on the path of regulative devotional service; bhakte — devotees; shodaśa bheda pracāra — there are sixteen varieties.

TRANSLATION

"Within the category of regulative devotional service, there are also immature devotees. These are also of four varieties. Thus in regulative devotional service there are altogether sixteen varieties.
