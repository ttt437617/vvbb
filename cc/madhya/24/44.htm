Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.44

sanakādira mana harila saurabhādi gune

SYNONYMS

sanaka-ādira mana — the minds of saintly sages like Sanaka and Sanātana; harila — attracted; saurabha-ādi — such as the transcendental aroma of His lotus feet; gune — by the quality.

TRANSLATION

"The minds of the four boy sages [Sanaka, Sanātana, Sanandana and Sanat-kumāra] were attracted to the lotus feet of Krishna by the aroma of the tulasī that had been offered to the Lord.
