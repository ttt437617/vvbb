Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.240

pathe ye śūkara-mriga, jāni tomāra haya"

vyādha kahe, — "yei kaha, sei ta' niścaya"

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the path; ye — those; śūkara-mriga — boars and other animals; jāni — I can understand; tomāra haya — all belong to you; vyādha kahe — the hunter replied; yei kaha — whatever you are saying; sei ta' niścaya — it is a fact.

TRANSLATION

"'I was wondering whether all the boars and other animals that are half-killed belong to you.'

"The hunter replied, 'Yes, what you are saying is so.'
