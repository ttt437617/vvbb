Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.130

'bhaktye jīvan-mukta' gunākrishta hañā krishna bhaje

śushka-jñāne jīvan-mukta aparādhe adho maje

SYNONYMS

bhaktye jīvan-mukta — persons liberated in this life by discharging devotional service; guna-ākrishta hañā — being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna; krishna bhaje — engage in the devotional service of the Lord; śushka-jñāne jīvan-mukta — so-called liberated in this life by dry, speculative knowledge; aparādhe — by offenses; adho maje — fall down.

TRANSLATION

"Those who are liberated by devotional service become more and more attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna. Thus they engage in His service. Those who are liberated by the speculative process eventually fall down again due to offensive activity.
