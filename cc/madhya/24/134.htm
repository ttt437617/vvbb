Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.134

bhakti-bale 'prāpta-svarūpa' divya-deha pāya

krishna-gunākrishta hañā bhaje krishna-pā'ya

SYNONYMS

bhakti-bale — by the strength of devotional service; prāpta-svarūpa — attaining his original status; divya-deha — a transcendental body; pāya — one gets; krishna-guna-ākrishta — attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna; hañā — being; bhaje — takes to devotional service; krishna-pā'ya — at Krishna's lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"One who has attained his constitutional position by the strength of devotional service attains a transcendental body even in this lifetime. Being attracted by Lord Krishna's transcendental qualities, he fully engages in service at His lotus feet.
