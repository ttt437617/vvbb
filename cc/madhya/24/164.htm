Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.164

ei saba śānta yabe bhaje bhagavān

'śānta' bhakta kari' tabe kahi tāńra nāma

SYNONYMS

ei saba — all these; śānta — neutral; yabe — when; bhaje — worship; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; śānta bhakta — devotees in the neutral stage of devotional service; kari' — describing as; tabe — that time; kahi — I speak; tāńra — their; nāma — name.

TRANSLATION

"These thirteen types of yogīs and munis are called śānta-bhaktas, for they render transcendental loving service to the Supreme Personality of Godhead in the neutral stage.
