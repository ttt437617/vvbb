Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.338

puraścarana-vidhi, krishna-prasāda-bhojana

anivedita-tyāga, vaishnava-nindādi-varjana

SYNONYMS

puraścarana-vidhi — ritualistic ceremonies; krishna-prasāda-bhojana — eating the remnants of food offered to the Lord; anivedita-tyāga — not touching anything not offered to the Lord; vaishnava-nindā-ādi-varjana — completely avoiding blaspheming a Vaishnava.

TRANSLATION

"Other items you should describe are the method of performing puraścarana, taking krishna-prasādam, giving up unoffered food and not blaspheming the Lord's devotees.

PURPORT

Regarding the vaishnava-nindā, see Madhya-līlā 15.261.
