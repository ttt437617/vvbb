Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.12

"ātmā deha-mano-brahma-svabhāva-dhriti-buddhishu, prayatne ca" iti

SYNONYMS

ātmā — the word ātmā; deha — the body; manah — the mind; brahma — the Absolute Truth; svabhāva — nature; dhriti — firmness; buddhishu — in the sense of intelligence; prayatne — in endeavor; ca — and; iti — thus.

TRANSLATION

"'The following are synonyms of the word "ātmā": the body, mind, Absolute Truth, natural characteristics, firmness, intelligence and endeavor.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Viśva-prakāśa dictionary.
