Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.127

krishnera darśane, kāro krishnera kripāya

mumukshā chādiyā gune bhaje tāńra pā'ya

SYNONYMS

krishnera darśane — simply by meeting Krishna; kāro — someone; krishnera kripāya — by the favor of Krishna; mumukshā chādiyā — giving up the desire for liberation; gune — being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna; bhaje — engages in service; tāńra pā'ya — at the lotus feet of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Simply by meeting Krishna or receiving Krishna's special favor, one can give up the desire for liberation. Being attracted by the transcendental qualities of Krishna, one can engage in His service.
