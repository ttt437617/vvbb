Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.212

dehārāmī dehe bhaje 'dehopādhi brahma'

sat-sańge seha kare krishnera bhajana

SYNONYMS

dehārāmī — persons who have accepted the body as the self and are interested only in sense gratification; dehe — in the body; bhaje — worships; deha-upādhi brahma — Brahman having the body as a designation; sat-sańge — in the association of devotees; seha — such a person; kare — does; krishnera bhajana — service to Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"One in the bodily conception worships his own body as Brahman, but when he comes in contact with a devotee, he gives up this mistaken idea and engages himself in the devotional service of Lord Krishna.
