Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.289

vidhi-bhaktye nitya-siddha pārishada — 'dāsa'

'sakhā' 'guru', 'kāntā-gana', — cāri-vidha prakāśa

SYNONYMS

vidhi-bhaktye — by regulative devotional service; nitya-siddha pārishada — eternally perfect associate; dāsa — the servant; sakhā guru kāntā-gana — friends, superiors and beloved damsels; cāri-vidha prakāśa — they are manifested in four varieties.

TRANSLATION

"By executing regulative devotional service, one is elevated to the platform of an eternally perfect associate, such as a servant, friend, superior or beloved woman. Each of these is of four varieties.
