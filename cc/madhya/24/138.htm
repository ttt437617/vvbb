Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.138

daivī hy eshā guna-mayī

mama māyā duratyayā

mām eva ye prapadyante

māyām etām taranti te

SYNONYMS

daivī — belonging to the Supreme Lord; hi — certainly; eshā — this; guna-mayī — made of the three modes; mama — My; māyā — external energy; duratyayā — very difficult to surpass; mām — unto Me; eva — certainly; ye — those who; prapadyante — surrender fully; māyām — the illusory energy; etām — this; taranti — cross over; te — they.

TRANSLATION

"'This divine energy of Mine, consisting of the three modes of material nature, is difficult to overcome. But those who have surrendered unto Me can easily cross beyond it.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Bhagavad-gītā (7.14).
