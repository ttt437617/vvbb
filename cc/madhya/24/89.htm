Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.89

sei upāsaka haya trividha prakāra

akāma, moksha-kāma, sarva-kāma āra

SYNONYMS

sei upāsaka — those devotees; haya — are; tri-vidha prakāra — three varieties; akāma — without material desires; moksha-kāma — desiring to become liberated; sarva-kāma — filled with all material desires; āra — and.

TRANSLATION

"The devotees are divided into three categories — akāma [desireless], moksha-kāma [desiring liberation] and sarva-kāma [desiring material perfection].
