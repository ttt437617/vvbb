Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.310

bhramite bhramite yadi sādhu-sańga pāya

saba tyaji' tabe tińho krishnere bhajaya

SYNONYMS

bhramite bhramite — wandering in different forms in different manners; yadi — if; sādhu-sańga pāya — one obtains the association of devotees; saba tyaji' — giving up everything; tabe — then; tińho — he; krishnere bhajaya — engages himself in the service of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"The living entities are wandering in different species of life on different planets, but if by chance they get the association of a pure devotee [sādhu], they give up all other engagements and engage in the service of Lord Krishna.
