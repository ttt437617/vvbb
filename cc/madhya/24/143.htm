Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.143

bhaktye mukti pāileha avaśya krishnere bhajaya

SYNONYMS

bhaktye — by devotional service; mukti — liberation; pāileha — if one gets; avaśya — certainly; krishnere — unto Lord Krishna; bhajaya — renders service.

TRANSLATION

"When one is actually liberated by executing devotional service, he always engages in the transcendental loving service of the Lord.
