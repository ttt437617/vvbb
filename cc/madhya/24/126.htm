Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.126

nāradera sańge śaunakādi muni-gana

mumukshā chādiyā kailā krishnera bhajana

SYNONYMS

nāradera sańge — by the association of the great saintly person Nārada; śaunaka-ādi muni-gana — the great sages headed by Śaunaka Muni; mumukshā chādiyā — giving up the desire for liberation; kailā — performed; krishnera bhajana — devotional service to Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"By associating with the great saint Nārada, Śaunaka and other great sages gave up the desire for liberation and engaged in Krishna's devotional service.
