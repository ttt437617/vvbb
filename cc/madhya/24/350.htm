Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.350

kālena vrindāvana-keli-vārtā

lupteti tām khyāpayitum viśishya

kripāmritenābhishisheca devas

tatraiva rūpam ca sanātanam ca

SYNONYMS

kālena — in the course of time; vrindāvana-keli-vārtā — topics concerning the transcendental mellows of the pastimes of Lord Krishna in Vrindāvana; luptā — almost lost; iti — thus; tām — all those; khyāpayitum — to enunciate; viśishya — making specific; kripā-amritena — with the nectar of mercy; abhishisheca — sprinkled; devah — the Lord; tatra — there; eva — indeed; rūpam — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; ca — and; sanātanam — Sanātana Gosvāmī; ca — as well as.

TRANSLATION

"In the course of time, the transcendental news of Krishna's pastimes in Vrindāvana was almost lost. To enunciate explicitly those transcendental pastimes, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu empowered Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī with the nectar of His mercy to carry out this work in Vrindāvana."
