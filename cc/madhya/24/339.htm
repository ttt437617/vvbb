Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.339

sādhu-lakshana, sādhu-sańga, sādhu-sevana

asat-sańga-tyāga, śrī-bhāgavata-śravana

SYNONYMS

sādhu-lakshana — the symptoms of a devotee; sādhu-sańga — association with devotees; sādhu-sevana — offering service to devotees; asat-sańga-tyāga — giving up the company of nondevotees; śrī-bhāgavata-śravana — regularly hearing the recitation of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.

TRANSLATION

"You should describe the symptoms of a devotee, how to associate with devotees, how to satisfy a devotee by rendering service, and how to give up the association of nondevotees. You should also explain the value of regularly hearing the recitation of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
