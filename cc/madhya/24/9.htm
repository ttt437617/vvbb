Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.9

sahaje āmāra kichu artha nāhi bhāse

tomā-sabāra sańga-bale ye kichu prakāśe

SYNONYMS

sahaje — generally; āmāra — My; kichu — any; artha — meaning; nāhi bhāse — does not manifest; tomā-sabāra sańga-bale — by the strength of your association; ye — which; kichu — something; prakāśe — manifests.

TRANSLATION

"Generally by Myself I cannot give an explanation, but by the strength of your association something may manifest itself.
