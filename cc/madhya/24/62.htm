Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.62

tabe kare bhakti-bādhaka karma, avidyā nāśa

śravanādyera phala 'premā' karaye prakāśa

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; kare — does; bhakti-bādhaka — impediments on the path of devotional service; karma — activities; avidyā — ignorance; nāśa — vanquishing; śravana-ādyera — of hearing, chanting and so forth; phala — the result; premā — love of Godhead; karaye prakāśa — causes a manifestation of.

TRANSLATION

"In this way, when all sinful activities are vanquished by the grace of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, one gradually vanquishes all kinds of impediments on the path of devotional service, as well as the ignorance resulting from these impediments. After this, one totally manifests his original love of Godhead through devotional service in nine different ways — hearing, chanting and so forth.
