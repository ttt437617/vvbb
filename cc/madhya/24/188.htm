Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.188

krishna-kripāya sādhu-sańge rati-buddhi pāya

saba chādi' śuddha-bhakti kare krishna-pāya

SYNONYMS

krishna-kripāya — by the mercy of Krishna; sādhu-sańge — in the association of devotees; rati-buddhi — devotional attraction and intelligence; pāya — one obtains; saba chādi' — giving up everything; śuddha-bhakti — pure devotional service; kare — performs; krishna-pāya — at the lotus feet of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"By the mercy of Krishna and by the association of devotees, one increases his attraction to and intelligence for pure devotional service; therefore one gives up everything and engages himself at the lotus feet of Krishna and His pure devotees.
