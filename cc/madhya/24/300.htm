Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.300

"asmin vane vrikshāh phalanti" yaiche haya

taiche saba ātmārāma krishne bhakti karaya

SYNONYMS

asmin vane — in this forest; vrikshāh phalanti — different varieties of trees bear fruit; yaiche haya — just as in this sentence; taiche — similarly; saba — all; ātmārāma — self-realized souls; krishne bhakti karaya — engage themselves in loving devotional service to Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"The ātmārāma verse is like the sentence 'In this forest many different trees bear fruit.' All ātmārāmas render devotional service to Lord Krishna.
