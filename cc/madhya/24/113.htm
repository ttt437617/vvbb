Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.113

janma haite śuka-sanakādi 'brahmamaya'

krishna-gunākrishta hañā krishnere bhajaya

SYNONYMS

janma haite — from birth; śuka — Śukadeva Gosvāmī; sanaka-ādi — the four Kumāras; brahma-maya — absorbed in the thought of impersonal Brahman; krishna-guna-ākrishta — attracted by the transcendental pastimes of the Lord; hañā — becoming; krishnere bhajaya — worshiped Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Although Śukadeva Gosvāmī and the four Kumāras were always absorbed in the thought of impersonal Brahman and were thus Brahmavādīs, they were nonetheless attracted by the transcendental pastimes and qualities of Krishna. Therefore they later became devotees of Krishna.
