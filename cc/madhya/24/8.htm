Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.8

kibā pralāpilāńa, kichu nāhika smarane

tomāra sańga-bale yadi kichu haya mane

SYNONYMS

kibā — what; pralāpilāńa — I have said; kichu — anything; nāhika — there is not; smarane — in memory; tomāra — of you; sańga-bale — by the strength of association; yadi — if; kichu — something; haya — there is; mane — in My mind.

TRANSLATION

"I do not recall what I spoke in that connection, but if something comes to My mind due to association with you, I shall explain it.
