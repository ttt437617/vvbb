Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.69

api sambhāvanā-praśna-

śańkā-garhā-samuccaye

tathā yukta-padārtheshu

kāma-cāra-kriyāsu ca

SYNONYMS

api — the word api; sambhāvanā — possibility; praśna — question; śańkā — doubt; garhā — censure or abuse; samuccaye — aggregation; tathā — as well as; yukta-pada-artheshu — the appropriate application of things; kāma-cāra-kriyāsu — of extravagance; ca — and.

TRANSLATION

"'The word "api" is used in the sense of possibility, question, doubt, censure, aggregation, appropriate application of things, and extravagance.'

PURPORT

This is another quotation from the Viśva-prakāśa.
