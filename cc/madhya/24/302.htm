Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.302

'nirgranthā eva' hañā, 'api' — nirdhārane

ei 'ūnashashti' prakāra artha kariluń vyākhyāne

SYNONYMS

nirgranthāh — the word nirgranthāh; eva — also the word eva; hañā — becoming; api — the word api; nirdhārane — in fixation; ei ūnashashti prakāra artha — in this way fifty-nine imports; kariluń vyākhyāne — I have described.

TRANSLATION

"Then taking the word 'nirgranthāh' and considering 'api' in the sense of sustenance, I have tried to explain a fifty-ninth meaning of the verse.
