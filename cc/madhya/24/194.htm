Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.194

ei-pañca-madhye eka 'svalpa' yadi haya

subuddhi janera haya krishna-premodaya

SYNONYMS

ei — these; pañca-madhye — out of the five; eka — of only one; svalpa — a small quantity; yadi — if; haya — there is; su-buddhi — intelligent; janera — of the person; haya — there is; krishna-prema-udaya — awakening of dormant love for Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"One's dormant love for Krishna gradually awakens if one is a little advanced in one of these five items and is intelligent.
