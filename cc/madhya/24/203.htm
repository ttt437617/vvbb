Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.203

ei jīva — sanakādi saba muni-jana

'nirgrantha' — mūrkha, nīca, sthāvara-paśu-gana

SYNONYMS

ei jīva — these living entities; sanaka-ādi saba muni-jana — all the great personalities, such as Sanaka and Sanātana; nirgrantha — down to the illiterate; mūrkha — foolish person; nīca — lowborn; sthāvara — the trees and plants; paśu-gana — the beasts and birds.

TRANSLATION

"Living entities include great personalities like the four Kumāras, and also low-class foolish people, trees, plants, birds and beasts.
