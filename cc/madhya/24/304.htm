Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.304

'api'-śabda — avadhārane, seha cāri bāra

cāri-śabda-sańge evera karibe uccāra

SYNONYMS

api-śabda — the word api; avadhārane — in the matter of ascertaining; seha cāri bāra — those four times; cāri-śabda — four words; sańge — with; evera — of the word eva; karibe — one can do; uccāra — pronouncing.

TRANSLATION

"The word 'api' is then used in the sense of ascertainment, and then the word 'eva' can be uttered four times with four words.
