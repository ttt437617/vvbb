Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.201

jīvera svabhāva — krishna-'dāsa'-abhimāna

dehe ātma-jñāne ācchādita sei 'jñāna'

SYNONYMS

jīvera svabhāva — the original characteristic of all living entities; krishna-dāsa — servant of Krishna; abhimāna — the conception; dehe — in the material body; ātma-jñāne — by the conception of the self; ācchādita — covered; sei jñāna — that original consciousness.

TRANSLATION

"The original nature of every living entity is to consider himself the eternal servant of Krishna. But under the influence of māyā he thinks himself to be the body, and thus his original consciousness is covered.
