Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.55

yogya-bhāve jagate yata yuvatīra gana

SYNONYMS

yogya-bhāve — by proper behavior; jagate — within the three worlds; yata — all; yuvatīra gana — the groups of young girls.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna attracts not only the minds of the gopīs and the goddesses of fortune but the minds of all the young girls in the three worlds as well.
