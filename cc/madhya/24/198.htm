Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.198

bhakti-prabhāva, — sei kāma chādāñā

krishna-pade bhakti karāya gune ākarshiyā

SYNONYMS

bhakti-prabhāva — the influence of devotional service; sei — that; kāma — material desire; chādāñā — causing to give up; krishna-pade — unto the lotus feet of Krishna; bhakti karāya — engages in devotional service; gune — by transcendental qualities; ākarshiyā — attracting.

TRANSLATION

"Devotional service is so strong that when one engages in it, he gradually gives up all material desires and becomes fully attracted to the lotus feet of Krishna. All this is brought about by attraction for the transcendental qualities of the Lord.
