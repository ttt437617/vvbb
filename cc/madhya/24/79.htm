Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.79

sei krishna-prāpti-hetu trividha 'sādhana'

jñāna, yoga, bhakti, — tinera prithak lakshana

SYNONYMS

sei — those; krishna-prāpti — of achieving the lotus feet of Krishna; hetu — causes; tri-vidha sādhana — the three kinds of execution; jñāna — knowledge; yoga — mystic yoga practice; bhakti — and devotional service; tinera — of these three; prithak lakshana — the symptoms are different.

TRANSLATION

"There are three ways to attain the lotus feet of the Absolute Truth, Krishna. There is the process of philosophical speculation, the practice of mystic yoga and the execution of devotional service. Each of these has its different characteristics.
