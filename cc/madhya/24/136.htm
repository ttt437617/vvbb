Madhya-līlāChapter 24: The Sixty-One Explanations of the Ātmārāma Verse

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 24.136

krishna-bahirmukha-doshe māyā haite bhaya

krishnonmukha bhakti haite māyā-mukta haya

SYNONYMS

krishna-bahir-mukha — of going against Krishna consciousness; doshe — by the fault; māyā haite — from the illusory energy; bhaya — fear; krishna-unmukha — in favor of Krishna consciousness; bhakti — devotional service; haite — from; māyā-mukta — liberated from māyā; haya — one becomes.

TRANSLATION

"By opposing Krishna consciousness, one becomes conditioned and fearful due to the influence of māyā. By executing devotional service faithfully, one is liberated from māyā.
