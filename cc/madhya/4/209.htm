Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.209

nāma-sańkīrtane sei rātri gońāilā

mańgala-ārati dekhi' prabhāte calilā

SYNONYMS

nāma-sańkīrtane — in congregational chanting; sei — that; rātri — night; gońāilā — passed; mańgala-ārati — the early ārati; dekhi' — after seeing; prabhāte — in the morning; calilā — departed.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu passed that night at the temple engaged in congregational chanting. In the morning, after seeing the mańgala-ārati performance, He departed.
