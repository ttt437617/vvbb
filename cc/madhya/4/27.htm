Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.27

purī kahe, — ke tumi, kāhāń tomāra vāsa

ke-mate jānile, āmi kari upavāsa

SYNONYMS

purī kahe — Mādhavendra Purī inquired from the boy; ke tumi — who are You; kāhāń tomāra vāsa — where do You reside; ke-mate — how; jānile — You know; āmi kari upavāsa — I am fasting.

TRANSLATION

Mādhavendra Purī said, "Who are You? Where do You reside? And how did You know that I was fasting?"
