Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.21

pūrve śrī-mādhava-purī āilā vrindāvana

bhramite bhramite gelā giri govardhana

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; śrī-mādhava-purī — Śrīla Mādhavendra Purī; āilā — came; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; bhramite bhramite — while traveling; gelā — went; giri govardhana — to the hill known as Govardhana.

TRANSLATION

Once, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī traveled to Vrindāvana, where he came upon the hill known as Govardhana.
