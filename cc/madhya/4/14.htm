Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.14

tāńra pāda-padma nikata pranāma karite

tāńra pushpa-cūdā padila prabhura māthāte

SYNONYMS

tāńra pāda-padma — the lotus feet of Gopīnātha; nikata — near; pranāma — obeisances; karite — while offering; tāńra — His; pushpa-cūdā — helmet of flowers; padila — fell down; prabhura — of the Lord; māthāte — on the head.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances at the lotus feet of the Gopīnātha Deity, the helmet of flowers on the head of Gopīnātha fell down and landed on the head of Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
