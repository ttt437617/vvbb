Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.24

gopāla-bālaka eka dugdha-bhānda lañā

āsi' āge dhari' kichu balila hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

gopāla-bālaka — cowherd boy; eka — one; dugdha-bhānda lañā — taking a pot of milk; āsi' — coming; āge dhari' — holding it in front; kichu — something; balila — said; hāsiyā — smiling.

TRANSLATION

While he was sitting beneath a tree, an unknown cowherd boy came with a pot of milk, placed it before Mādhavendra Purī and, smiling, addressed him as follows.
