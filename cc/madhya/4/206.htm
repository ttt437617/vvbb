Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.206

kshīra dekhi' mahāprabhura ānanda bādila

bhakta-gane khāoyāite pañca kshīra laila

SYNONYMS

kshīra — the sweet rice; dekhi' — seeing; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda — the pleasure; bādila — increased; bhakta-gane — the devotees; khāoyāite — to feed them; pañca — five pots; kshīra — sweet rice; laila — accepted.

TRANSLATION

When all the pots of sweet rice, remnants left by Gopīnātha, were placed before Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, He became very pleased. In order to feed the devotees, He accepted five of them.
