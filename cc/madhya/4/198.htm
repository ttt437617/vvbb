Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.198

ei śloka padite prabhu ha-ilā mūrcchite

premete vivaśa hañā padila bhūmite

SYNONYMS

ei śloka — this verse; padite — reciting; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ilā — became; mūrcchite — unconscious; premete — in ecstatic love; vivaśa — uncontrolled; hañā — becoming; padila — fell down; bhūmite — on the ground.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited this verse, He immediately fell to the ground unconscious. He was overwhelmed and had no control over Himself.
