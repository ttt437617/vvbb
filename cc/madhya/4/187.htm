Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.187

ei tāra gādha premā loke dekhāite

gopāla tāńre ājñā dila candana ānite

SYNONYMS

ei — this; tāra — of Mādhavendra Purī; gādha — intense; premā — love of Godhead; loke — unto the people; dekhāite — to show; gopāla — Lord Gopāla; tāńre — to him; ājñā — order; dila — gave; candana — sandalwood; ānite — to bring.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Gopāla wanted to show how intensely Mādhavendra Purī loved Krishna; therefore He asked him to go to Nīlācala to fetch sandalwood and camphor.
