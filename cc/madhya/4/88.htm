Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.88

punah dina-śeshe prabhura karāila utthāna

kichu bhoga lāgāila karāila jala-pāna

SYNONYMS

punah — again; dina-śeshe — at the end of the day; prabhura — of the Lord; karāila — caused to do; utthāna — rising; kichu — some; bhoga — food; lāgāila — offered; karāila — caused to do; jala — water; pāna — drinking.

TRANSLATION

After taking rest, the Deity must be awakened at the end of the day, and immediately some food and some water must be offered to Him.

PURPORT

This offering is called vaikāli-bhoga, food offered at the end of the day.
