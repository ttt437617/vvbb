Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.152

eka vipra, eka sevaka, candana vahite

purī-gosāñira sańge dila sambala-sahite

SYNONYMS

eka vipra — one brāhmana; eka sevaka — one servant; candana — the sandalwood; vahite — to carry; purī-gosāñira — Mādhavendra Purī; sańge — with; dila — gave; sambala-sahite — with the necessary expenditure.

TRANSLATION

One brāhmana and one servant were given to Mādhavendra Purī just to carry the sandalwood. He was also given the necessary traveling expenses.
