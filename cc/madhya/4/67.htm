Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.67

grāmera yateka tandula, dāli godhūma-cūrna

sakala āniyā dila parvata haila pūrna

SYNONYMS

grāmera — of the village; yateka — all; tandula — the rice; dāli — dhal; godhūma-cūrna — wheat flour; sakala — all; āniyā — bringing; dila — offered; parvata — the top of the hill; haila — became; pūrna — filled.

TRANSLATION

As soon as the people of the village had understood that the Deity was going to be installed, they had brought their entire stocks of rice, dhal and wheat flour. They brought such large quantities that the entire surface of the top of the hill was filled.
