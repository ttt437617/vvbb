Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.76

aneka ghata bhari' dila suvāsita jala

bahu-dinera kshudhāya gopāla khāila sakala

SYNONYMS

aneka ghata — many waterpots; bhari' — filled; dila — offered; suvāsita — scented; jala — water; bahu-dinera — of many days; kshudhāya — by hunger; gopāla — Gopāla; khāila — ate; sakala — everything.

TRANSLATION

Many waterpots were filled with scented water for drinking, and Lord Śrī Gopāla, who had been hungry for many days, ate everything offered to Him.
