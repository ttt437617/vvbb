Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.150

gopāla candana māge, — śuni' bhakta-gana

ānande candana lāgi' karila yatana

SYNONYMS

gopāla — Lord Gopāla at Vrindāvana; candana — sandalwood; māge — wants; śuni' — hearing; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; ānande — in great pleasure; candana lāgi' — for sandalwood; karila — made; yatana — endeavor.

TRANSLATION

When all the devotees at Jagannātha Purī heard that the Gopāla Deity wanted sandalwood, in great pleasure they all endeavored to collect it.
