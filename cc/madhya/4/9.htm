Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.9

ataeva tāńra pāye kari namaskāra

tāńra pāya aparādha nā ha-uk āmāra

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; tāńra pāye — at his lotus feet; kari — I do; namaskāra — obeisances; tāńra pāya — to the lotus feet of Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; aparādha — offense; nā — not; ha-uk — let it happen; āmāra — my.

TRANSLATION

I thus offer my respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura. I hope that I will not offend his lotus feet by this action.
