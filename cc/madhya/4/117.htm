Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.117

sandhyāya bhoga lāge kshīra — 'amrita-keli'-nāma

dvādaśa mrit-pātre bhari' amrita-samāna

SYNONYMS

sandhyāya — in the evening; bhoga — foods; lāge — they offer; kshīra — sweet rice; amrita-keli-nāma — named amrita-keli; dvādaśa — twelve; mrit-pātre — earthen pots; bhari' — filling; amrita-samāna — just like nectar.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana priest said, "In the evening the Deity is offered sweet rice in twelve earthen pots. Because the taste is as good as nectar [amrita], it is named amrita-keli.
