Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.38

eka matha kari' tāhāń karaha sthāpana

bahu śītala jale kara śrī-ańga mārjana

SYNONYMS

eka — one; matha — temple; kari' — constructing; tāhāń — there; karaha — do; sthāpana — installment; bahu — much; śītala — cold; jale — in water; kara — do; śrī-ańga — My transcendental body; mārjana — washing.

TRANSLATION

"Please construct a temple on top of that hill," the boy continued, "and install Me in that temple. After this, wash Me with large quantities of cold water so that My body may be cleansed.
