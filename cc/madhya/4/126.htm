Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.126

nija kritya kari' pūjārī karila śayana

svapane thākura āsi' balilā vacana

SYNONYMS

nija kritya — his own duty; kari' — finishing; pūjārī — the brāhmana priest in the temple; karila — took; śayana — rest; svapane — in a dream; thākura — the Deity; āsi' — coming there; balilā — said; vacana — the words.

TRANSLATION

Finishing his daily duties, the priest went to take rest. In a dream he saw the Gopīnātha Deity come to talk to him, and He spoke as follows.
