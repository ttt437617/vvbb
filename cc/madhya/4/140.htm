Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.140

prati-dina eka-khāni karena bhakshana

khāile premāveśa haya, — adbhuta kathana

SYNONYMS

prati-dina — every day; eka-khāni — one piece; karena — does; bhakshana — eating; khāile — by eating; prema-āveśa — ecstasy; haya — there is; adbhuta — wonderful; kathana — narrations.

TRANSLATION

Each day, Mādhavendra Purī would eat one piece of that earthen pot, and after eating it he would immediately be overwhelmed with ecstasy. These are wonderful stories.
