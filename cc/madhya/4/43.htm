Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.43

sei haite rahi āmi ei kuñja-sthāne

bhāla haila āilā āmā kādha sāvadhāne

SYNONYMS

sei haite — from that time; rahi — reside; āmi — I; ei — this; kuñja-sthāne — in the bush; bhāla haila — it was very good; āilā — you have come; āmā — Me; kādha — take out; sāvadhāne — with care.

TRANSLATION

"Since the priest went away, I have been staying in this bush. It is very good that you have come here. Now just remove Me with care."
