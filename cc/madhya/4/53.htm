Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.53

mahā-mahā-balishtha loka ekatra kariñā

parvata-upari gela purī thākura lañā

SYNONYMS

mahā-mahā-balishtha — who are very strong; loka — persons; ekatra kariñā — assembling; parvata-upari — to the top of the hill; gela — went; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; thākura lañā — taking the Deity.

TRANSLATION

Since the Deity was very heavy, some of the stronger men assembled to carry Him to the top of the hill. Mādhavendra Purī also went there.
