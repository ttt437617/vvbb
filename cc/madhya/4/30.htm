Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.30

jala nite strī-gana tomāre dekhi' gela

strī-saba dugdha diyā āmāre pāthāila

SYNONYMS

jala nite — for bringing water; strī-gana — the women; tomāre — you; dekhi' gela — saw you and went; strī-saba — all the women; dugdha — milk; diyā — giving; āmāre — Me; pāthāila — have sent.

TRANSLATION

"The women who come here to take water saw you, and they supplied Me with this milk and sent Me to you."
