Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.199

āste-vyaste kole kari' nila nityānanda

krandana kariyā tabe uthe gauracandra

SYNONYMS

āste-vyaste — in great haste; kole — on the lap; kari' — making; nila — took; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; krandana — crying; kariyā — doing; tabe — at that time; uthe — got up; gauracandra — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell to the ground in ecstatic love, Lord Nityānanda quickly took Him on His lap. Crying, Caitanya Mahāprabhu then got up again.
