Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.192

ghashite ghashite yaiche malayaja-sāra

gandha bāde, taiche ei ślokera vicāra

SYNONYMS

ghashite ghashite — rubbing and rubbing; yaiche — just as; malayaja-sāra — sandalwood; gandha — the fragrance; bāde — increases; taiche — similarly; ei — this; ślokera — of the verse; vicāra — the consideration.

TRANSLATION

Continuous rubbing increases the aroma of Malaya sandalwood. Similarly, consideration of this verse increases one's understanding of its importance.
