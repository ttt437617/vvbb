Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.100

svarna, raupya, vastra, gandha, bhakshya-upahāra

asańkhya āise, nitya bādila bhāndāra

SYNONYMS

svarna — gold; raupya — silver; vastra — garments; gandha — scents; bhakshya-upahāra — presentations for eating; asańkhya — countless; āise — came; nitya — daily; bādila — increased; bhāndāra — the store.

TRANSLATION

Thus countless presentations of gold, silver, garments, scented articles and eatables arrived. The store of Gopāla increased daily.
