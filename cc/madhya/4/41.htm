Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.41

'śrī-gopāla' nāma mora, — govardhana-dhārī

vajrera sthāpita, āmi ihāń adhikārī

SYNONYMS

śrī-gopāla nāma — the name Śrī Gopāla; mora — My; govardhana-dhārī — the lifter of Govardhana Hill; vajrera — by Vajra, the grandson of Krishna; sthāpita — installed; āmi — I; ihāń — here; adhikārī — the authority.

TRANSLATION

"My name is Gopāla. I am the lifter of Govardhana Hill. I was installed by Vajra, and here I am the authority.
