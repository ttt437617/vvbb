Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.120

ayācita kshīra prasāda alpa yadi pāi

svāda jāni' taiche kshīra gopāle lāgāi

SYNONYMS

ayācita — without asking; kshīra — sweet rice; prasāda — remnants of food; alpa — a little; yadi — if; pāi — I get; svāda — the taste; jāni' — knowing; taiche — similar; kshīra — sweet rice; gopāle — to my Gopāla; lāgāi — I can offer.

TRANSLATION

"If, without my asking, a little sweet rice is given to me, I can then taste it and make a similar preparation to offer my Lord Gopāla."
