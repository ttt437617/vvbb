Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.45

śrī-krishnake dekhinu muñi nārinu cinite

eta bali' premāveśe padilā bhūmite

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishnake dekhinu — saw Lord Krishna personally; muñi — I; nārinu — was unable; cinite — to identify; eta bali' — saying this; prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love; padilā — fell down; bhūmite — on the ground.

TRANSLATION

Mādhavendra Purī began to lament, "I saw Lord Krishna directly, but I could not recognize Him!" Thus he fell down on the ground in ecstatic love.
