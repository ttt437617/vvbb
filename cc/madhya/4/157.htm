Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.157

sei rātre devālaye karila śayana

śesha-rātri haile purī dekhila svapana

SYNONYMS

sei rātre — on that night; deva-ālaye — in the temple; karila — did; śayana — sleeping; śesha-rātri — at the end of the night; haile — when it was; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; dekhila — saw; svapana — a dream.

TRANSLATION

Mādhavendra Purī took rest that night in the temple, but toward the end of the night he had another dream.
