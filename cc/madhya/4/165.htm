Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.165

grīshma-kāle gopīnātha paribe candana

śuni' ānandita haila sevakera mana

SYNONYMS

grīshma-kāle — in the summer; gopīnātha — Lord Gopīnātha; paribe — will put on; candana — sandalwood pulp; śuni' — hearing; ānandita — pleased; haila — became; sevakera — of the servants; mana — the minds.

TRANSLATION

The servants of Gopīnātha became very pleased to hear that in the summer all the sandalwood pulp would be used to anoint the body of Gopīnātha.
