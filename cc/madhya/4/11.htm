Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.11

bhikshā lāgi' eka-dina eka grāma giyā

āpane bahuta anna ānila māgiyā

SYNONYMS

bhikshā lāgi' — for prasādam; eka-dina — in one day; eka grāma — to one village; giyā — going; āpane — personally; bahuta — a great quantity; anna — of rice and other eatables; ānila — brought; māgiyā — begging.

TRANSLATION

Each day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally went to a village and collected a great quantity of rice and other grains for the preparation of prasādam.
