Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.92

prātah-kāle punah taiche karila sevana

anna lañā eka-grāmera āila loka-gana

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāle — in the morning; punah — again; taiche — just as before; karila — rendered; sevana — service; anna lañā — with food grains; eka-grāmera — of one village; āila — came; loka-gana — the people.

TRANSLATION

The next morning, the rendering of service to the Deity began again, and people from one village arrived with all kinds of food grains.
