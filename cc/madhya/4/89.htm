Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.89

gopāla prakata haila, — deśe śabda haila

āśa-pāśa grāmera loka dekhite āila

SYNONYMS

gopāla — Lord Gopāla; prakata haila — has appeared; deśe — throughout the country; śabda haila — the news spread; āśa-pāśa — neighboring; grāmera — of the villages; loka — the people; dekhite āila — came to see.

TRANSLATION

When it was advertised throughout the country that Lord Gopāla had appeared atop Govardhana Hill, all the people from neighboring villages came to see the Deity.
