Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.119

hena-kāle sei bhoga thākure lāgila

śuni' purī-gosāñi kichu mane vicārila

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; sei bhoga — that wonderful food; thākure — in front of the Deity; lāgila — was placed; śuni' — hearing; purī-gosāñi — Mādhavendra Purī; kichu — something; mane — within the mind; vicārila — considered.

TRANSLATION

While Mādhavendra Purī was talking with the brāhmana priest, the sweet rice was placed before the Deity as an offering. Hearing this, Mādhavendra Purī thought as follows.
