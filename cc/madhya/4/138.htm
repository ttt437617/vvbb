Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.138

eta bali' namaskari' karilā gamana

āveśe karilā purī se kshīra bhakshana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; namaskari' — offering obeisances; karilā gamana — returned; āveśe — in ecstasy; karilā — did; purī — Purī Gosāñi; se — that; kshīra — sweet rice; bhakshana — eating.

TRANSLATION

The priest offered his obeisances to Mādhavendra Purī and returned to the temple. Then, in ecstasy, Mādhavendra Purī ate the sweet rice offered to him by Krishna.
