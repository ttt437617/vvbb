Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.78

ihā anubhava kaila mādhava gosāñi

tāńra thāñi gopālera lukāna kichu nāi

SYNONYMS

ihā — this; anubhava kaila — perceived; mādhava gosāñi — Mādhavendra Purī Gosvāmī; tāńra thāñi — before him; gopālera — of Lord Gopāla; lukāna — secret; kichu — anything; nāi — there is not.

TRANSLATION

How Gopāla ate everything while the food remained the same was transcendentally perceived by Mādhavendra Purī Gosvāmī; nothing remains a secret to the devotees of the Lord.
