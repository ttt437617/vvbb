Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.201

'ayi dīna, 'ayi dīna' bale bāra-bāra

kanthe nā nihsare vānī, netre aśru-dhāra

SYNONYMS

ayi dīna — O My Lord, master of the poor; ayi dīna — O My Lord, master of the poor; bale — says; bāra-bāra — repeatedly; kanthe — in the throat; nā — not; nihsare — comes out; vānī — the voice; netre — in the eyes; aśru-dhāra — torrents of tears.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu could not recite the whole verse. He simply said, "Ayi dīna! Ayi dīna!" repeatedly. Thus He could not speak, and profuse tears were in His eyes.
