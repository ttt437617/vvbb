Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.17

nānā-rūpe prītye kaila prabhura sevana

sei rātri tāhāń prabhu karilā vañcana

SYNONYMS

nānā-rūpe — in various ways; prītye — on account of love; kaila — did; prabhura — of the Lord; sevana — service; sei rātri — that night; tāhāń — there; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karilā — did; vañcana — passing.

TRANSLATION

Because of their love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they served Him in many ways, and that night the Lord stayed at the temple of Gopīnātha.
