Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.159

karpūra-sahita ghashi' e-saba candana

gopīnāthera ańge nitya karaha lepana

SYNONYMS

karpūra-sahita — with the camphor; ghashi' — grinding; e-saba — all this; candana — sandalwood; gopīnāthera — of Śrī Gopīnātha; ańge — on the body; nitya — daily; karaha — do; lepana — smearing.

TRANSLATION

"Now just grind all the sandalwood together with the camphor and then smear the pulp on the body of Gopīnātha daily until it is finished.
