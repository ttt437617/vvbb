Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.116

ei lāgi' puchilena brāhmanera sthāne

brāhmana kahila saba bhoga-vivarane

SYNONYMS

ei lāgi' — for this matter; puchilena — he inquired; brāhmanera sthāne — from the brāhmana; brāhmana — the priest; kahila — informed; saba — everything; bhoga — foods; vivarane — in description.

TRANSLATION

When the brāhmana priest was questioned about this matter, he explained in detail what kinds of foods were offered to the Deity of Gopīnātha.
