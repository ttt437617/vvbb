Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.7

caitanya-mańgale yāhā karila varnana

sūtra-rūpe sei līlā kariye sūcana

SYNONYMS

caitanya-mańgale — in the book named Caitanya-mańgala; yāhā — whatever; karila varnana — has described; sūtra-rūpe — in the form of a synopsis; sei līlā — those pastimes; kariye sūcana — I shall present.

TRANSLATION

I am therefore presenting only a synopsis of those events already described elaborately by Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura in his Caitanya-mańgala [now known as Caitanya-bhāgavata].
