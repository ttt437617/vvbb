Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.49

atyanta nivida kuñja, — nāri praveśite

kuthāri kodāli laha dvāra karite

SYNONYMS

atyanta — very; nivida — dense; kuñja — bushes; nāri — we are not able; praveśite — to enter; kuthāri — choppers; kodāli — spades; laha — take; dvāra karite — to make a way.

TRANSLATION

"The bushes are very dense, and we will not be able to enter the jungle. Therefore take choppers and spades to clear the way."
