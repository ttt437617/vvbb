Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.83

purī-gosāñi ājñā dila sakala brāhmane

ā-bāla-vriddha grāmera loka karāha bhojane

SYNONYMS

purī-gosāñi — Mādhavendra Purī; ājñā — order; dila — gave; sakala brāhmane — to all the brāhmanas; ā-bāla-vriddha — beginning from the children up to the old persons; grāmera — of the village; loka — the people; karāha — make; bhojane — take prasādam.

TRANSLATION

After the Lord was laid down to rest on the bed, Mādhavendra Purī gathered all the brāhmanas who had prepared the prasādam and said to them, "Now feed everyone sumptuously, from the children on up to the aged!"
