Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.56

nava śata-ghata jala kaila upanīta

nānā vādya-bherī bāje, strī-gana gāya gīta

SYNONYMS

nava — nine; śata-ghata — hundreds of waterpots; jala — water; kaila — made; upanīta — brought; nānā — various; vādya — musical sounds; bherī — bugles; bāje — vibrate; strī-gana — all the women; gāya — chant; gīta — various songs.

TRANSLATION

When the Deity was being installed, nine hundred pots of water were brought from Govinda-kunda. There were musical sounds of bugles and drums and the singing of women.
