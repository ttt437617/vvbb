Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.57

keha gāya, keha nāce, mahotsava haila

dadhi, dugdha, ghrita āila grāme yata chila

SYNONYMS

keha gāya — some sing; keha nāce — some dance; mahotsava haila — there was a festival; dadhi — yogurt; dugdha — milk; ghrita — clarified butter; āila — was brought; grāme — in the village; yata — as much; chila — as there was.

TRANSLATION

During the festival at the installation ceremony, some people sang and some danced. All the milk, yogurt and clarified butter in the village was brought to the festival.
