Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.113

nritya-gīta kari' jaga-mohane vasilā

'kyā kyā bhoga lāge?' brāhmane puchilā

SYNONYMS

nritya-gīta kari' — after performing dancing and chanting; jaga-mohane — in the corridor of the temple; vasilā — he sat down; kyā kyā — what; bhoga — foods; lāge — they offer; brāhmane — from the brāhmana priest; puchilā — inquired.

TRANSLATION

In the corridor of the temple, from which people generally viewed the Deity, Mādhavendra Purī chanted and danced. Then he sat down there and asked a brāhmana what kinds of foods they offered to the Deity.
