Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.47

prātah-snāna kari' purī grāma-madhye gelā

saba loka ekatra kari' kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

prātah-snāna — morning bath; kari' — finishing; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; grāma-madhye — within the village; gelā — entered; saba loka — all the people; ekatra kari' — assembling; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

After taking his morning bath, Mādhavendra Purī entered the village and assembled all the people. Then he spoke as follows.
