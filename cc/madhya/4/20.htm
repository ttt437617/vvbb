Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.20

pūrve mādhava-purīra lāgi' kshīra kaila curi

ataeva nāma haila 'kshīra-corā hari'

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; mādhava-purīra lāgi' — for Mādhavendra Purī; kshīra — sweet rice; kaila — did; curi — steal; ataeva — therefore; nāma — the name; haila — became; kshīra-corā hari — the Lord who stole a pot of sweet rice.

TRANSLATION

Formerly the Deity had stolen a pot of sweet rice for Mādhavendra Purī; therefore He became very famous as the Lord who stole the sweet rice.
