Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.48

grāmera īśvara tomāra — govardhana-dhārī

kuñje āche, cala, tāńre bāhira ye kari

SYNONYMS

grāmera — of the village; īśvara — the proprietor; tomāra — your; govardhana-dhārī — the lifter of Govardhana Hill; kuñje āche — in the bushes in the jungle; cala — let us go; tāńre — Him; bāhira ye kari — take out.

TRANSLATION

"The proprietor of this village, Govardhana-dhārī, is lying in the bushes. Let us go there and rescue Him from that place.
