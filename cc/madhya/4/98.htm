Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.98

gopāla-prakata śuni' nānā deśa haite

nānā dravya lañā loka lāgila āsite

SYNONYMS

gopāla — of the Deity of Gopāla; prakata — appearance; śuni' — hearing; nānā — various; deśa — countries; haite — from; nānā — various; dravya — things; lañā — bringing; loka — people; lāgila — began; āsite — to come.

TRANSLATION

In this way not only the neighboring villages but all the other provinces came to know of Gopāla's appearance. Thus people came from all over, bringing a variety of presentations.
