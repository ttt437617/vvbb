Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.15

cūdā pāñā mahāprabhura ānandita mana

bahu nritya-gīta kaila lañā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

cūdā pāñā — getting the helmet; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita — pleased; mana — the mind; bahu — various kinds; nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; kaila — performed; lañā — with; bhakta-gana — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

When the Deity's helmet fell upon His head, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very pleased, and thus He chanted and danced in various ways with His devotees.
