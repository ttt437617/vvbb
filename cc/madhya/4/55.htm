Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.55

grāmera brāhmana saba nava ghata lañā

govinda-kundera jala ānila chāniñā

SYNONYMS

grāmera — of the village; brāhmana — brāhmana priests; saba — all; nava — nine; ghata — waterpots; lañā — bringing; govinda-kundera — of the lake known as Govinda-kunda; jala — the water; ānila — brought; chāniñā — filtering.

TRANSLATION

All the brāhmana priests of the village gathered together with nine waterpots, and water from Govinda-kunda lake was brought there and filtered.
