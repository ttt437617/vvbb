Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.19

'kshīra-corā gopīnātha' prasiddha tāńra nāma

bhakta-gane kahe prabhu sei ta' ākhyāna

SYNONYMS

kshīra-corā gopīnātha — the Gopīnātha who stole the pot of sweet rice; prasiddha — very famous; tāńra nāma — His name; bhakta-gane — to all the devotees; kahe — tells; prabhu — the Lord; sei ta' ākhyāna — that narration.

TRANSLATION

That Deity was known widely as Kshīra-corā-gopīnātha, and Caitanya Mahāprabhu told His devotees the story of how the Deity became so famous.
