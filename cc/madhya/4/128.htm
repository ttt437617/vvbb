Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.128

dhadāra añcale dhākā eka kshīra haya

tomarā nā jānilā tāhā āmāra māyāya

SYNONYMS

dhadāra — of the cloth curtain; añcale — by the skirt; dhākā — covered; eka — one; kshīra — pot of sweet rice; haya — there is; tomarā — you; nā — not; jānilā — knew; tāhā — that; āmāra — of Me; māyāya — by the trick.

TRANSLATION

"This pot of sweet rice is just behind My cloth curtain. You did not see it because of My tricks.
