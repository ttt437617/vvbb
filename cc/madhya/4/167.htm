Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.167

ei mata candana deya pratyaha ghashiyā

parāya sevaka saba ānanda kariyā

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; candana — sandalwood; deya — gives; pratyaha — daily; ghashiyā — grinding; parāya — caused to put on; sevaka — servants; saba — all of it; ānanda — pleasure; kariyā — feeling.

TRANSLATION

In this way Gopīnāthajī was supplied ground sandalwood pulp daily. The servants of Gopīnātha were very pleased with this.
