Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.94

pūrva-dina-prāya vipra karila randhana

taiche anna-kūta gopāla karila bhojana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-dina-prāya — almost as on the previous day; vipra — all the brāhmanas; karila — did; randhana — cooking; taiche — similarly; anna-kūta — heaps of food; gopāla — the Deity of Lord Gopāla; karila — did; bhojana — eating.

TRANSLATION

The next day, almost as before, there was an Annakūta ceremony. All the brāhmanas prepared foods and Gopāla accepted them.
