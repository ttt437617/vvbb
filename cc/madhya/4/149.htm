Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.149

jagannāthera sevaka yata, yateka mahānta

sabāke kahila purī gopāla-vrittānta

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera sevaka — the servants of Lord Jagannātha; yata — all; yateka mahānta — all respectable devotees; sabāke — unto everyone; kahila — told; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; gopāla-vrittānta — the narration of Gopāla.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Mādhavendra Purī told all the servants of Lord Jagannātha and all the great devotees there the story of the appearance of Śrī Gopāla.
