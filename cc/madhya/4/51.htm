Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.51

thākura dekhila mātī-trine ācchādita

dekhi' saba loka haila ānande vismita

SYNONYMS

thākura — the Deity; dekhila — they saw; mātī — with dirt; trine — and grass; ācchādita — covered; dekhi' — seeing; saba loka — all the people; haila — became; ānande — with pleasure; vismita — amazed.

TRANSLATION

When they saw the Deity covered with dirt and grass, they were all struck with wonder and pleasure.
