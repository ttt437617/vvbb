Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.204

lokera sańghatta dekhi' prabhura bāhya haila

thākurera bhoga sari' ārati bājila

SYNONYMS

lokera — of people; sańghatta — a crowd; dekhi' — seeing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhya — external consciousness; haila — appeared; thākurera — of the Deity; bhoga — offering; sari' — finishing; ārati — performance of ārati; bājila — resounded.

TRANSLATION

When many people crowded around Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, He regained His external senses. In the meantime, the offering to the Deity had been finished, and there was a resounding ārati performance.
