Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.194

ei śloka kahiyāchena rādhā-thākurānī

tāńra kripāya sphuriyāche mādhavendra-vānī

SYNONYMS

ei — this; śloka — verse; kahiyāchena — has spoken; rādhā-thākurānī — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; tāńra — Her; kripāya — by the mercy; sphuriyāche — has manifested; mādhavendra — of Mādhavendra Purī; vānī — the words.

TRANSLATION

Actually this verse was spoken by Śrīmatī Rādhārānī Herself, and by Her mercy only was it manifest in the words of Mādhavendra Purī.
