Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.82

trina-tāti diyā cāri-dik āvarila

uparete eka tāti diyā ācchādila

SYNONYMS

trina-tāti — straw mattress; diyā — with; cāri-dik — all around; āvarila — covered; uparete — on top; eka — one; tāti — similar straw mattress; diyā — with; ācchādila — covered.

TRANSLATION

A temporary temple was constructed by covering the bed all around with a straw mattress. Thus there was a bed and a straw mattress to cover it.
