Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.8

tāńra sūtre āche, teńha nā kaila varnana

yathā-kathañcit kari' se līlā kathana

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; sūtre — in the synopsis; āche — there are; teńha — he; nā kaila varnana — did not describe; yathā-kathañcit — something of them; kari' — doing; se — these; līlā — of pastimes; kathana — narration.

TRANSLATION

Some of the incidents he did not describe elaborately but only summarized, and these I shall try to describe in this book.
