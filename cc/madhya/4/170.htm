Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.170

śrī-mukhe mādhava-purīra amrita-carita

bhakta-gane śunāñā prabhu kare āsvādita

SYNONYMS

śrī-mukhe — from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mādhava-purīra — of Mādhavendra Purī; amrita-carita — nectarean characteristics; bhakta-gane — the devotees; śunāñā — making hear; prabhu — the Lord; kare — does; āsvādita — relished.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally praised the nectarean characteristics of Mādhavendra Purī, and while He related all this to the devotees, He personally relished it.
