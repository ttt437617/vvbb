Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.54

pātharera simhāsane thākura vasāila

bada eka pāthara prishthe avalamba dila

SYNONYMS

pātharera — of stone; simha-āsane — on a throne; thākura — the Deity; vasāila — installed; bada — big; eka — one; pāthara — stone; prishthe — at the back; avalamba — support; dila — gave.

TRANSLATION

A big stone was made into a throne, and the Deity was installed upon it. Another big stone was placed behind the Deity for support.
