Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.155

gopīnātha-carane kaila bahu namaskāra

premāveśe nritya-gīta karilā apāra

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha-carane — at the lotus feet of Lord Gopīnātha; kaila — made; bahu — many; namaskāra — obeisances; prema-āveśe — in the ecstasy of love; nritya-gīta — dancing and chanting; karilā — performed; apāra — unlimitedly.

TRANSLATION

When Mādhavendra Purī reached the temple of Gopīnātha, he offered his respectful obeisances many times at the lotus feet of the Lord. In the ecstasy of love, he began to dance and sing without cessation.
