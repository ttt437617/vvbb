Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.196

śesha-kāle ei śloka pathite pathite

siddhi-prāpti haila purīra ślokera sahite

SYNONYMS

śesha-kāle — at the end; ei śloka — this verse; pathite pathite — by reciting repeatedly; siddhi-prāpti — attainment of perfection; haila — there was; purīra — of Mādhavendra Purī; ślokera — this verse; sahite — with.

TRANSLATION

Mādhavendra Purī recited this verse again and again at the end of his material existence. Thus uttering this verse, he attained the ultimate goal of life.
