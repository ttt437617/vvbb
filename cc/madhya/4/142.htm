Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.142

sei bhaye rātri-śeshe calilā śrī-purī

sei-khāne gopīnāthe dandavat kari'

SYNONYMS

sei bhaye — fearing that; rātri-śeshe — at the end of the night; calilā — left; śrī-purī — Śrī Mādhavendra Purī; sei-khāne — on that spot; gopīnāthe — to Lord Gopīnātha; dandavat — obeisances; kari' — doing.

TRANSLATION

Thinking this, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī offered his obeisances to Gopīnātha on the spot and left Remunā before morning.
