Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.112

remunāte kaila gopīnātha daraśana

tāńra rūpa dekhiñā haila vihvala-mana

SYNONYMS

remunāte — in the village of Remunā; kaila — did; gopīnātha — the Deity of Gopīnātha; daraśana — seeing; tāńra — His; rūpa — beauty; dekhiñā — seeing; haila — became; vihvala — bewildered; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

Going into South India, Śrī Mādhavendra Purī visited Remunā, where Gopīnātha is situated. Upon seeing the beauty of the Deity, Mādhavendra Purī was overwhelmed.
