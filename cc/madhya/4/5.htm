Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.5

sahaje vicitra madhura caitanya-vihāra

vrindāvana-dāsa-mukhe amritera dhāra

SYNONYMS

sahaje — naturally; vicitra — wonderful; madhura — sweet; caitanya — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vihāra — the activities; vrindāvana-dāsa — of Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; mukhe — from the mouth; amritera — of nectar; dhāra — shower.

TRANSLATION

By nature all the activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are very wonderful and sweet, and when they are described by Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura, they become like a shower of nectar.
