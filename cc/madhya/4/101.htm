Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.101

eka mahā-dhanī kshatriya karāila mandira

keha pāka-bhāndāra kaila, keha ta' prācīra

SYNONYMS

eka — one; mahā-dhanī — very rich man; kshatriya — of the royal order; karāila — constructed; mandira — a temple; keha — someone; pāka-bhāndāra — clay pots for cooking; kaila — made; keha — someone; ta' — certainly; prācīra — the boundary walls.

TRANSLATION

One very rich kshatriya of the royal order constructed a temple, someone made cooking utensils, and someone constructed boundary walls.
