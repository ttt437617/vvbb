Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.156

purī dekhi' sevaka saba sammāna karila

kshīra-prasāda diyā tāńre bhikshā karāila

SYNONYMS

purī dekhi' — by seeing Mādhavendra Purī; sevaka — the priest or servant; saba sammāna — all respects; karila — offered; kshīra-prasāda — sweet rice prasādam; diyā — offering; tāńre — him; bhikshā karāila — made to eat.

TRANSLATION

When the priest of Gopīnātha saw Mādhavendra Purī again, he offered all respects to him and, giving him the sweet rice prasādam, made him eat.
