Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.127

uthaha, pūjārī, kara dvāra vimocana

kshīra eka rākhiyāchi sannyāsi-kārana

SYNONYMS

uthaha — please get up; pūjārī — O priest; kara — just do; dvāra — the door; vimocana — opening; kshīra — sweet rice; eka — one pot; rākhiyāchi — I have kept; sannyāsi — of the mendicant, Mādhavendra Purī; kārana — for the reason.

TRANSLATION

"O priest, please get up and open the door of the temple. I have kept one pot of sweet rice for the sannyāsī Mādhavendra Purī.
