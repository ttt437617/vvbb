Madhya-līlāChapter 4: Śrī Mādhavendra Puri's Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 4.174

yāńra lāgi' gopīnātha kshīra kaila curi

ataeva nāma haila 'kshīra-corā' kari'

SYNONYMS

yāńra — whom; lāgi' — on account of; gopīnātha — Lord Gopīnātha; kshīra — sweet rice; kaila — did; curi — steal; ataeva — therefore; nāma — the name; haila — became; kshīra-corā — the thief of sweet rice; kari' — making.

TRANSLATION

"On account of Mādhavendra Purī, Lord Gopīnātha stole the pot of sweet rice. Thus He became famous as Kshīra-corā [the thief who stole the sweet rice].
