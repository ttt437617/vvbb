Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.242

ei ta' kahiluń prabhura kīrtana-vilāsa

yebā ihā śune, haya caitanyera dāsa

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have explained; prabhura — of the Lord; kīrtana-vilāsa — pastimes in sańkīrtana; yebā — anyone who; ihā — this; śune — listens to; haya — becomes; caitanyera dāsa — a servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

In this way I have explained the Lord's pastime of sańkīrtana, and I bless everyone with this benediction: By listening to this description, one will surely become a servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
