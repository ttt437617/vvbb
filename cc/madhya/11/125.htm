Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.125

simha-dvāra dāhine chādi' saba vaishnava-gana

kāśī-miśra-griha-pathe karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

simha-dvāra dāhine — on the right side of the lion gate; chādi' — leaving aside; saba — all; vaishnava-gana — devotees of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kāśī-miśra-griha — to the house of Kāśī Miśra; pathe — on the way; karilā gamana — began to proceed.

TRANSLATION

Beginning from the right side of the lion gate, or the main gate of the temple, all the Vaishnavas began to proceed toward the house of Kāśī Miśra.
