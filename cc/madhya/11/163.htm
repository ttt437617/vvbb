Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.163

milana-sthāne āsi' prabhure nā mililā

rājapatha-prānte dūre padiyā rahilā

SYNONYMS

milana-sthāne — in the meeting place; āsi' — coming; prabhure — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā — not; mililā — did meet; rājapatha-prānte — on the side of the common road; dūre — at a distant place; padiyā — falling flat; rahilā — remained.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura did not come to the Lord's meeting place but remained fallen flat on the common road at a distance.
