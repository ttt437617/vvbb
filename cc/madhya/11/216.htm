Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.216

ashta mridańga bāje, batriśa karatāla

hari-dhvani kare sabe, bale — bhāla, bhāla

SYNONYMS

ashta mridańga — eight mridańgas; bāje — sounded; batriśa — thirty-two; karatāla — cymbals; hari-dhvani — vibrating the transcendental sound; kare — does; sabe — every one of them; bale — says; bhāla bhāla — very good, very good.

TRANSLATION

In the four groups there were eight mridańgas and thirty-two cymbals. All together they began to vibrate the transcendental sound, and everyone said, "Very good! Very good!"
