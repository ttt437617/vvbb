Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.111

rājā kahe, — upavāsa, kshaura — tīrthera vidhāna

tāhā nā kariyā kene khāiba anna-pāna

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; upavāsa — fasting; kshaura — shaving; tīrthera vidhāna — this is the regulation for visiting a holy place; tāhā — that; nā kariyā — without performing; kene — why; khāiba — they shall eat; anna-pāna — solid and liquid food.

TRANSLATION

The King then asked the Bhattācārya, "Why have the devotees not observed the regulations for visiting the pilgrimage place, such as fasting, shaving and so on? Why have they first eaten prasādam?"
