Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.55

premāveśe pushpodyāne karibena praveśa

sei-kāle ekale tumi chādi' rāja-veśa

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; pushpa-udyāne — into the garden at Gundicā where the Lord stays; karibena praveśa — will enter; sei-kāle — at that time; ekale — alone; tumi — you; chādi' — giving up; rāja-veśa — the royal dress.

TRANSLATION

"On that Ratha-yātrā festival day, after dancing before the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will enter the Gundicā garden. At that time you should go there alone, without your royal dress.
