Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.40

prabhu ājñā pāñā rāya calilā daraśane

rāyera prema-bhakti-rīti bujhe kon jane

SYNONYMS

prabhu ājñā — the Lord's permission; pāñā — getting; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; calilā — departed; daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; rāyera — of Rāmānanda Rāya; prema-bhakti — of ecstatic love for Krishna; rīti — process; bujhe — understands; kon jane — what person.

TRANSLATION

Having received Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's permission, Rāmānanda Rāya hastily went to the temple of Lord Jagannātha. Who can understand the devotional service of Rāya Rāmānanda?
