Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.158

eta bali' prabhu tāńre kaila ālińgana

nikate vasāñā kare ańga sammārjana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — the Lord; tāńre — him; kaila — did; ālińgana — embrace; nikate — nearby; vasāñā — making sit down; kare — does; ańga — of his body; sammārjana — cleansing.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, the Lord embraced Murāri and had him sit down by His side. The Lord then began to cleanse his body with His own hands.
