Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.226

bahu-kshana nritya kari' prabhu sthira hailā

cāri mahāntere tabe nācite ājñā dilā

SYNONYMS

bahu-kshana — for a long period; nritya kari' — dancing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sthira hailā — became still; cāri mahāntere — to four great personalities; tabe — then; nācite — to dance; ājñā dilā — ordered.

TRANSLATION

After dancing for a long time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became still and ordered four great personalities to begin to dance.
