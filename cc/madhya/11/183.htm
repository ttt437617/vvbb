Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.183

samudra-snāna kari' kara cūdā daraśana

tabe āji ihań āsi' karibe bhojana

SYNONYMS

samudra-snāna — bathing in the sea; kari' — finishing; kara — just do; cūdā daraśana — observing the top of the temple; tabe — thereafter; āji — today; ihań — here; āsi' — coming back; karibe bhojana — take your lunch.

TRANSLATION

"Go to the sea and bathe and look at the top of the temple. After so doing, please come back here and take your lunch."
