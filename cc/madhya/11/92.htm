Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.92

mukunda-dāsa, narahari, śrī-raghunandana

khanda-vāsī cirañjīva, āra sulocana

SYNONYMS

mukunda-dāsa — Mukunda dāsa; narahari — Narahari; śrī-raghunandana — Śrī Raghunandana; khanda-vāsī — residents of Khanda; cirañjīva — Cirañjīva; āra — and; sulocana — Sulocana.

TRANSLATION

"Here are Mukunda dāsa, Narahari, Śrī Raghunandana, Cirañjīva and Sulocana, all residents of Khanda.
