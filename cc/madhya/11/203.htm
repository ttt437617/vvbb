Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.203

tomā-sańge rahe yata sannyāsīra gana

gopīnāthācārya tāńre kariyāche nimantrana

SYNONYMS

tomā-sańge — along with You; rahe — remain; yata — as many as; sannyāsīra gana — rank of sannyāsīs; gopīnātha-ācārya — Gopīnātha Ācārya; tāńre — all of them; kariyāche — has done; nimantrana — invitation.

TRANSLATION

"Gopīnātha Ācārya has invited all the sannyāsīs who remain with You to come and take prasādam.
