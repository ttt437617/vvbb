Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.76

bhattācārya kahe, — ei svarūpa-dāmodara

mahāprabhura haya ińha dvitīya kalevara

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya kahe — the Bhattācārya said; ei — this gentleman; svarūpa-dāmodara — his name is Svarūpa Dāmodara; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haya — is; ińha — he; dvitīya — the second; kalevara — expansion of the body.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "Here is Svarūpa Dāmodara, who is practically the second expansion of the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
