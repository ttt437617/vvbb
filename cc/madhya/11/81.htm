Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.81

prabhura sevā karite purī ājñā dila

ataeva prabhu ińhāke nikate rākhila

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sevā — the service; karite — to perform; purī — Īśvara Purī; ājñā dila — ordered; ataeva — therefore; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ińhāke — him; nikate — by His side; rākhila — kept.

TRANSLATION

"Īśvara Purī ordered Govinda to serve Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Thus the Lord keeps him by His side."
