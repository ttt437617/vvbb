Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.167

jagannātha-sevakera mora sparśa nāhi haya

tāhāń padi' rahoń, — mora ei vāñchā haya

SYNONYMS

jagannātha-sevakera — of the servants of Lord Jagannātha; mora — my; sparśa — touching; nāhi — not; haya — takes place; tāhāń — there; padi' rahoń — I stay; mora — my; ei — this; vāñchā — desire; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

"I do not wish the servants of Lord Jagannātha to touch me. I would remain there in the garden alone. That is my desire."
