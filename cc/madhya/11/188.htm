Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.188

haridāsa kahe, — prabhu, nā chuńio more

muñi — nīca, aspriśya, parama pāmare

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kahe — Haridāsa Thākura said; prabhu — my dear Lord; nā chuńio more — please do not touch me; muñi — I; nīca — most fallen; aspriśya — untouchable; parama pāmare — the lowest of mankind.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura said, "My dear Lord, please do not touch me, for I am most fallen and untouchable and am the lowest among men."
