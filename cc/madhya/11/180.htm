Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.180

gopīnāthe dekhāila saba vāsā-ghara

vānīnātha-thāñi dila prasāda vistara

SYNONYMS

gopīnāthe — unto Gopīnātha Ācārya; dekhāila — showed; saba — all; vāsā-ghara — residential places; vānīnātha-thāñi — unto Vānīnātha Rāya; dila — delivered; prasāda vistara — remnants of food in large quantities.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha was then shown all the residential places, and Vānīnātha was given large quantities of food [mahā-prasādam] left by Lord Jagannātha.
