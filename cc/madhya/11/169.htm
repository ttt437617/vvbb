Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.169

hena-kāle kāśī-miśra, padichā, — dui jana

āsiyā karila prabhura carana vandana

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; padichā — the superintendent; dui jana — two persons; āsiyā — coming; karila — did; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carana vandana — worshiping the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

At this time, Kāśī Miśra, along with the superintendent of the temple, came and offered his respects unto the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
