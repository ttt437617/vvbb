Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.170

sarva vaishnava dekhi' sukha bada pāilā

yathā-yogya sabā-sane ānande mililā

SYNONYMS

sarva vaishnava — all the Vaishnavas; dekhi' — seeing; sukha — happiness; bada — very much; pāilā — got; yathā-yogya — as is befitting; sabā-sane — along with everyone; ānande — in happiness; mililā — met.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing all the Vaishnavas together, Kāśī Miśra and the superintendent became very happy. With great happiness they met with the devotees in a befitting manner.
