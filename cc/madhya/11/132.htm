Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.132

āpana-nikate prabhu sabā vasāilā

āpani śrī-haste sabāre mālya-gandha dilā

SYNONYMS

āpana-nikate — by His own side; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabā — all of them; vasāilā — made sit; āpani — personally Himself; śrī-haste — with His hand; sabāre — unto everyone; mālya — garland; gandha — sandalwood pulp; dilā — offered.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made all the devotees sit at His side, and with His own hand He offered them garlands and sandalwood pulp.
