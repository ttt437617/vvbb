Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.130

eke eke sarva-bhakte kaila sambhāshana

sabā lañā abhyantare karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

eke eke — one after another; sarva-bhakte — to every devotee; kaila — did; sambhāshana — address; sabā lañā — taking all of them; abhyantare — inside; karilā gamana — entered.

TRANSLATION

The Lord addressed all the devotees one after another and took all of them with Him into the house.
