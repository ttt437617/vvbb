Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.88

govinda, mādhava ghosha, ei vāsu-ghosha

tina bhāira kīrtane prabhu pāyena santosha

SYNONYMS

govinda — Govinda Ghosha; mādhava ghosha — Mādhava Ghosha; ei — this; vāsu-ghosha — Vāsudeva Ghosha; tina bhāira — of the three brothers; kīrtane — in the sańkīrtana; prabhu — the Lord; pāyena santosha — gets very much pleasure.

TRANSLATION

"Here also are Govinda Ghosha, Mādhava Ghosha and Vāsudeva Ghosha. They are three brothers, and their sańkīrtana, congregational chanting, pleases the Lord very much.

PURPORT

Govinda Ghosha belonged to the kāyastha dynasty of the Uttara-rādhīya section, and he was known as Ghosha Thākura. Even to the present day there is a place named Agradvīpa, near Katwa, where a fair takes place and is named after Ghosha Thākura. As far as Vāsudeva Ghosha is concerned, he composed many nice songs about Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and these are all authorized Vaishnava songs, like the songs of Narottama dāsa Thākura, Bhaktivinoda Thākura, Locana dāsa Thākura, Govinda dāsa Thākura and other great Vaishnavas.
