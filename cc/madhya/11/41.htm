Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.41

kshetre āsi' rājā sārvabhaume bolāilā

sārvabhaume namaskari' tāńhāre puchilā

SYNONYMS

kshetre — to Jagannātha Purī; āsi' — coming; rājā — the King; sārvabhaume — for Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; bolāilā — called; sārvabhaume — unto Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; namaskari' — offering obeisances; tāńhāre puchilā — he asked him.

TRANSLATION

When King Pratāparudra returned to Jagannātha Purī, he called for Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya. When the Bhattācārya went to see the King, the King offered him respects and made the following inquiries.
