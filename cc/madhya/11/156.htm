Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.156

more nā chuńiha, prabhu, muñi ta' pāmara

tomāra sparśa-yogya nahe pāpa kalevara

SYNONYMS

more — me; nā chuńiha — do not touch; prabhu — my Lord; muñi — I; ta' — certainly; pāmara — most abominable; tomāra — of You; sparśa-yogya — fit to be touched; nahe — not; pāpa — sinful; kalevara — body.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, please do not touch me. I am most abominable and am not fit for You to touch because my body is sinful."
