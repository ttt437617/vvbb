Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.33

purī, bhāratī-gosāñi, svarūpa, nityānanda

jagadānanda, mukundādi yata bhakta-vrinda

SYNONYMS

purī — Paramānanda Purī; bhāratī — Brahmānanda Bhāratī; gosāñi — on the level of the spiritual master; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; mukunda — Mukunda; ādi — and others; yata — all; bhakta-vrinda — devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī, Brahmānanda Bhāratī Gosāñi, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi, Lord Nityānanda, Jagadānanda, Mukunda and others were present before the Lord at that time.
