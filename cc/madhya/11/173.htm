Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.173

prabhu kahe, — gopīnātha, yāha' vaishnava lañā

yāhāń yāhāń kahe vāsā, tāhāń deha' lañā

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; gopīnātha — My dear Gopīnātha; yāha' — please go; vaishnava lañā — taking all the Vaishnavas; yāhāń yāhāń — wherever; kahe — they say; vāsā — staying place; tāhāń — there; deha' — give; lañā — accepting.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately told Gopīnātha Ācārya, "Please go with the Vaishnavas and accommodate them in whatever residences Kāśī Miśra and the temple superintendent offer."
