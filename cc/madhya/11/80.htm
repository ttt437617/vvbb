Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.80

dāmodara kahe, — ihāra 'govinda' nāma

īśvara-purīra sevaka ati gunavān

SYNONYMS

dāmodara kahe — Dāmodara said; ihāra — of him; govinda — Govinda; nāma — the name; īśvara-purīra sevaka — servant of Īśvara Purī; ati gunavān — very much qualified.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara informed Him, "Govinda was the servant of Īśvara Purī. He is very highly qualified.
