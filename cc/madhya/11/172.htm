Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.172

sabāra kariyāchi vāsā-griha-sthāna

mahā-prasāda sabākāre kari samādhāna

SYNONYMS

sabāra — for all of them; kariyāchi — we have arranged; vāsā-griha-sthāna — residential places for staying; mahā-prasāda — remnants of food offered to Jagannātha; sabākāre — to all of them; kari — let us do; samādhāna — distribution.

TRANSLATION

"Accommodations have been arranged for all the Vaishnavas. Now let us distribute mahā-prasādam to all of them."
