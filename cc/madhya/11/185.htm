Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.185

mahāprabhu āilā tabe haridāsa-milane

haridāsa kare preme nāma-sańkīrtane

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; tabe — thereafter; haridāsa-milane — to meet Thākura Haridāsa; haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; kare — does; preme — in ecstatic love; nāma-sańkīrtane — chanting of the holy name.

TRANSLATION

After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to meet Haridāsa Thākura, and He saw him engaged in chanting the mahā-mantra with ecstatic love. Haridāsa chanted, "Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare/ Hare Rāma, Hare Rāma, Rāma Rāma, Hare Hare."
