Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.162

dūra haite haridāsa gosāñe dekhiyā

rājapatha-prānte padi' āche dandavat hañā

SYNONYMS

dūra haite — from a distance; haridāsa gosāñe — Haridāsa Thākura; dekhiyā — seeing; rājapatha-prānte — at the side of the common road; padi' — falling down; āche — he was; dandavat hañā — offering obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then saw in the distance that Haridāsa Thākura was lying down flat on the road offering obeisances.
