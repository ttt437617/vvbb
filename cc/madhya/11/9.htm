Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.9

sārvabhauma kahe, — satya tomāra vacana

jagannātha-sevaka rājā kintu bhaktottama

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied; satya — true; tomāra — Your; vacana — statement; jagannātha-sevaka — servant of Lord Jagannātha; rājā — the King; kintu — but; bhakta-uttama — a great devotee.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "My dear Lord, what You have said is correct, but this King is not an ordinary king. He is a great devotee and servant of Lord Jagannātha."
