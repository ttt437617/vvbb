Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.129

śrīvāsādi karila prabhura carana vandana

pratyeke karila prabhu prema-ālińgana

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa-ādi — devotees headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura; karila — did; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carana vandana — worshiping the lotus feet; pratyeke — to everyone; karila — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-ālińgana — embracing in love.

TRANSLATION

After this, all the devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura, offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord embraced each and every one of them in great love and ecstasy.
