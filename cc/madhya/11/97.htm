Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.97

bhattācārya kahe ei madhura vacana

caitanyera srishti — ei prema-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; kahe — replied; ei — this; madhura vacana — transcendental sweetness of the voice; caitanyera srishti — the creation of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — this; prema-sańkīrtana — chanting in the ecstasy of love of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya replied, "This sweet transcendental sound is a special creation of the Lord known as prema-sańkīrtana, congregational chanting in love of Godhead.
