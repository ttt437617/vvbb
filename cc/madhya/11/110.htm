Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.110

bhatta kahe, — bhakta-gana āila jāniñā

prabhura ińgite prasāda yāya tāńrā lañā

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; āila — have come; jāniñā — knowing; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ińgite — by the indication; prasāda — remnants of the food offered to Jagannātha; yāya — go; tāńrā — all of them; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said, "Understanding that all the devotees have come, Lord Caitanya gave the sign, and therefore Vānīnātha and the others have brought such great quantities of mahā-prasādam."
