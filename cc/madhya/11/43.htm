Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.43

tathāpi nā kare teńha rāja-daraśana

kshetra chādi' yābena punah yadi kari nivedana

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — yet; nā kare — does not do; teńha — He; rāja-daraśana — visiting a king; kshetra chādi' — leaving Jagannātha-kshetra; yābena — He will go away; punah — again; yadi — if; kari nivedana — I request.

TRANSLATION

"Yet despite my great endeavor, the Lord would not agree to see a king. Indeed, He said that if He were asked again, He would quit Jagannātha Purī and go elsewhere."
