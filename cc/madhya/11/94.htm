Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.94

rājā kahe — dekhi' mora haila camatkāra

vaishnavera aiche teja dekhi nāhi āra

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; dekhi' — after seeing; mora — my; haila — there is; camatkāra — astonishment; vaishnavera — of the devotees of the Lord; aiche — such; teja — effulgence; dekhi — I see; nāhi — not; āra — anyone else.

TRANSLATION

The King said, "Upon seeing all these devotees, I am very much astonished, for I have never seen such an effulgence.
