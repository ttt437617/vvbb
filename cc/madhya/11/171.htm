Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.171

prabhu-pade dui jane kaila nivedane

ājñā deha', — vaishnavera kari samādhāne

SYNONYMS

prabhu-pade — unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dui jane — both of them; kaila — did; nivedane — submission; ājñā deha' — please order; vaishnavera — of all the Vaishnavas; kari — let us do; samādhāne — accommodation.

TRANSLATION

Both submitted to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Please give us orders so that we may make proper arrangements to accommodate all the Vaishnavas.
