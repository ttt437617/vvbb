Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.134

advaitere kahena prabhu madhura vacane

āji āmi pūrna ha-ilāńa tomāra āgamane

SYNONYMS

advaitere — unto Advaita Ācārya Prabhu; kahena — says; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; madhura vacane — in sweet language; āji — today; āmi — I; pūrna — perfect; ha-ilāńa — became; tomāra — Your; āgamane — on arrival.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu addressed Advaita Ācārya Prabhu, saying sweetly, "My dear Sir, today I have become perfect because of Your arrival."
