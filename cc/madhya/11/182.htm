Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.182

mahāprabhu kahe, — śuna, sarva vaishnava-gana

nija-nija-vāsā sabe karaha gamana

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; śuna — kindly listen; sarva vaishnava-gana — all Vaishnavas; nija-nija-vāsā — to the respective residential quarters; sabe — all of you; karaha — make; gamana — departure.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then addressed all the Vaishnavas and requested that they listen to Him. He said, "Now you can go to your respective residential quarters.
