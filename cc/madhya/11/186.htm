Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.186

prabhu dekhi' pade āge dandavat hañā

prabhu ālińgana kaila tāńre uthāñā

SYNONYMS

prabhu dekhi' — after seeing the Lord; pade — fell down; āge — in front of Him; dandavat — flat like a stick; hañā — becoming; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ālińgana kaila — embraced; tāńre — him; uthāñā — raising him up.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Haridāsa Thākura saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he immediately fell down like a stick to offer Him obeisances, and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu raised him up and embraced him.
