Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.61

rājāre prabodhiyā bhatta gelā nijālaya

snāna-yātrā-dine prabhura ānanda hridaya

SYNONYMS

rājāre — the King; prabodhiyā — encouraging; bhatta — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; gelā — departed; nija-ālaya — to his own home; snāna-yātrā-dine — on the day of the bathing ceremony of Lord Jagannātha; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda — full of happiness; hridaya — heart.

TRANSLATION

After thus encouraging the King, Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya returned home. On the day of Lord Jagannātha's bathing ceremony, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very happy at heart.
