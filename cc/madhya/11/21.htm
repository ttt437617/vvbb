Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.21

tomāra nāma śuni' haila mahā-premāveśa

mora hāte dhari' kare pirīti viśesha

SYNONYMS

tomāra — Your; nāma — name; śuni' — hearing; haila — became; mahā — great; prema-āveśa — ecstasy of love; mora hāte — my hand; dhari' — catching; kare — does; pirīti — loving symptoms; viśesha — specific.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, as soon as the King heard Your holy name, he was immediately overwhelmed by great ecstatic love. Catching my hand, he displayed all the symptoms of love.
