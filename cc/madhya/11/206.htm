Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.206

tabe prabhu prasādānna govinda-hāte dilā

yatna kari' haridāsa-thākure pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prasāda-anna — remnants of Jagannātha's food; govinda-hāte — in the hand of Govinda; dilā — delivered; yatna kari' — with great attention; haridāsa-thākure — unto Haridāsa Thākura; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

After this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu carefully delivered some prasādam into the hands of Govinda to be given to Haridāsa Thākura.
