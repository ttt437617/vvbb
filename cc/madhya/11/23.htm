Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.23

āmi — chāra, yogya nahi tāńra daraśane

tāńre yei bhaje tāńra saphala jīvane

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; chāra — very much fallen; yogya — fit; nahi — not; tāńra — His; daraśane — for interviewing; tāńre — Him; yei — anyone who; bhaje — worships; tāńra — his; saphala — successful; jīvane — life.

TRANSLATION

"Then Mahārāja Pratāparudra very humbly said, 'I am most fallen and abominable, and I am unfit to receive an interview with the Lord. One's life is successful if one engages in His service.'
