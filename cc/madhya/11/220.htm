Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.220

tabe prabhu jagannāthera mandira bediyā

pradakshina kari' bulena nartana kariyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; mandira — temple; bediyā — walking all around; pradakshina — circumambulation; kari' — doing; bulena — walks; nartana kariyā — dancing.

TRANSLATION

At this time Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu circumambulated the temple of Jagannātha and continuously danced about the whole area.
