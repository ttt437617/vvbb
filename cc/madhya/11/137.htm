Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.137

vāsudeva dekhi' prabhu ānandita hañā

tāńre kichu kahe tāńra ańge hasta diyā

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; dekhi' — seeing; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānandita hañā — becoming very pleased; tāńre — unto him; kichu kahe — says something; tāńra ańge — on his body; hasta diyā — placing His hand.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Vāsudeva Datta, the older brother of Mukunda Datta, He immediately became very happy and, placing His hand on his body, began to speak.
