Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.26

prabhu kahe, — tumi krishna-bhakata-pradhāna

tomāke ye prīti kare, sei bhāgyavān

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; tumi — you; krishna-bhakata-pradhāna — the chief of the devotees of Lord Krishna; tomāke — unto you; ye — anyone who; prīti kare — shows love; sei — such a person; bhāgyavān — most fortunate.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "My dear Rāmānanda Rāya, you are the foremost of all the devotees of Krishna; therefore whoever loves you is certainly a very fortunate person.
