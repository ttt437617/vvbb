Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.119

tabe rājā attālikā haite talete āilā

kāśī-miśra, padichā-pātra, duńhe ānāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; rājā — the King; attālikā haite — from the top of the palace; talete — to the ground; āilā — came down; kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; padichā-pātra — the inspector of the temple; duńhe — both of them; ānāilā — called for.

TRANSLATION

After this, King Pratāparudra came down from the top of his palace to the ground and called for Kāśī Miśra and the inspector of the temple.
