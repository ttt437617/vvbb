Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.191

nirantara kara cāri veda adhyayana

dvija-nyāsī haite tumi parama-pāvana

SYNONYMS

nirantara — constantly; kara — you do; cāri — four; veda — of the Vedas; adhyayana — study; dvija — brāhmana; nyāsī — sannyāsī; haite — than; tumi — you; parama-pāvana — supremely pure.

TRANSLATION

"You are constantly studying the four Vedas, and you are far better than any brāhmana or sannyāsī."
