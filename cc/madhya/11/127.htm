Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.127

advaita karila prabhura carana vandana

ācāryere kaila prabhu prema-ālińgana

SYNONYMS

advaita — Advaita Ācārya; karila — did; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carana — of the lotus feet; vandana — worship; ācāryere — unto Advaita Ācārya; kaila — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-ālińgana — embracing in ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

First Advaita Ācārya offered prayers to the lotus feet of the Lord, and the Lord immediately embraced Him in ecstatic love.
