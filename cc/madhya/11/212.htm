Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.212

hena-kāle rāmānanda āilā prabhu-sthāne

prabhu milāila tāńre saba vaishnava-gane

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda; āilā — came; prabhu-sthāne — at the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; milāila — caused to meet; tāńre — him (Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya); saba — all; vaishnava-gane — the devotees of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

At this time Rāmānanda Rāya also came to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord took the opportunity to introduce him to all the Vaishnavas.
