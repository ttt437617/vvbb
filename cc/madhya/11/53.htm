Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.53

tathāpi kahiye āmi eka upāya

ei upāya kara' prabhu dekhibe yāhāya

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; kahiye — say; āmi — I; eka upāya — one means; ei upāya — this means; kara' — try to adopt; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhibe — will see you; yāhāya — by that.

TRANSLATION

Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya then suggested, "There is one means by which you can directly see Him.
