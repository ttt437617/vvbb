Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.56

'krishna-rāsa-pañcādhyāya' karite pathana

ekale yāi' mahāprabhura dharibe carana

SYNONYMS

krishna-rāsa-pañca-adhyāya — the five chapters in the Tenth Canto of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam in which Lord Krishna's pastimes of the rāsa dance are described; karite pathana — to recite; ekale yāi' — going alone; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dharibe carana — catch hold of the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu enters the Gundicā garden, you should also go there and read the five chapters of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam about Lord Krishna's dancing with the gopīs. In this way you can catch hold of the Lord's lotus feet.
