Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.87

ei hari-bhatta, ei śrī-nrisimhānanda

ei vāsudeva datta, ei śivānanda

SYNONYMS

ei — this; hari-bhatta — Hari Bhatta; ei — this; śrī-nrisimhānanda — Śrī Nrisimhānanda; ei — this; vāsudeva datta — Vāsudeva Datta; ei — this; śivānanda — Śivānanda.

TRANSLATION

"Here is Hari Bhatta, and there is Nrisimhānanda. Here are Vāsudeva Datta and Śivānanda Sena.
