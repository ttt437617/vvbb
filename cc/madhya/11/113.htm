Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.113

īśvarera paroksha ājñā — kshaura, uposhana

prabhura sākshāt ājñā — prasāda-bhojana

SYNONYMS

īśvarera — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; paroksha — indirect; ājñā — order; kshaura — shaving; uposhana — fasting; prabhura — of the Lord; sākshāt — direct; ājñā — order; prasāda-bhojana — to take the prasādam.

TRANSLATION

"The scriptural injunctions for shaving and fasting are indirect orders of the Supreme Personality of Godhead. However, when there is a direct order from the Lord to take prasādam, naturally the devotees take prasādam as their first duty.
