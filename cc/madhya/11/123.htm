Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.123

eta bali' vidāya dila sei dui-jane

sārvabhauma dekhite āila vaishnava-milane

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; vidāya dila — granted permission to go; sei dui-jane — to those two persons; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; dekhite — to see; āila — came; vaishnava-milane — in the meeting of all the Vaishnavas.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, the King gave them permission to leave. Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya also went to see the assembly of all the Vaishnavas.
