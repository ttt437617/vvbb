Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.205

nityānanda lañā bhikshā karite vaisa tumi

vaishnavera pariveśana karitechi āmi

SYNONYMS

nityānanda lañā — taking along Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; bhikshā — luncheon; karite — to take; vaisa — sit down; tumi — You; vaishnavera — to all the devotees; pariveśana — distribution of prasādam; karitechi — am doing; āmi — I.

TRANSLATION

"You may sit down and accept the luncheon with Nityānanda Prabhu, and I shall distribute the prasādam to all the Vaishnavas."
