Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.131

miśrera āvāsa sei haya alpa sthāna

asańkhya vaishnava tāhāń haila parimāna

SYNONYMS

miśrera āvāsa — the residence of Kāśī Miśra; sei — that; haya — is; alpa sthāna — insufficient place; asańkhya — unlimited; vaishnava — devotees; tāhāń — there; haila — were; parimāna — overcrowded.

TRANSLATION

Since the residence of Kāśī Miśra was insufficient, all the assembled devotees were very much overcrowded.
