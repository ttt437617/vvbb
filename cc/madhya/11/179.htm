Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.179

eta kahi' dui jane vidāya la-ila

gopīnātha, vānīnātha — duńhe sańge nila

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; dui jane — both of them; vidāya la-ila — took leave; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha Ācārya; vānīnātha — Vānīnātha Rāya; duńhe sańge nila — took both of them with them.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Kāśī Miśra and the temple inspector took their leave, and Gopīnātha and Vānīnātha went with them.
