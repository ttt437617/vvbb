Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.85

ācāryaratna ińha, pandita-purandara

gańgādāsa pandita ińha, pandita-śańkara

SYNONYMS

ācāryaratna — Candraśekhara; ińha — here; pandita-purandara — Purandara Pandita; gańgādāsa pandita — Gańgādāsa Pandita; ińha — here; pandita-śańkara — Śańkara Pandita.

TRANSLATION

"Here are Ācāryaratna, Purandara Pandita, Gańgādāsa Pandita and Śańkara Pandita.
