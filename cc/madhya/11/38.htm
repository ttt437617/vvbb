Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.38

āmi ki kariba, mana ihāń lañā āila

jagannātha-daraśane vicāra nā kaila

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; ki — what; kariba — shall do; mana — my mind; ihāń — here; lañā — taking; āila — arrived; jagannātha-daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; vicāra — consideration; nā — did not; kaila — make.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya continued, "What shall I do? My mind has brought me here. I could not consider going first to Lord Jagannātha's temple."
