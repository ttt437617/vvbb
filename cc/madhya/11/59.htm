Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.59

śuni' gajapatira mane sukha upajila

prabhure milite ei mantranā dridha kaila

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; gajapatira — of King Pratāparudra; mane — in the mind; sukha — happiness; upajila — awakened; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; milite — to meet; ei — this; mantranā — instruction; dridha kaila — decided to accept rigidly.

TRANSLATION

Mahārāja Pratāparudra took the Bhattācārya's advice and firmly decided to follow his instructions. Thus he felt transcendental happiness.
