Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.19

āmi kahi, — āmā haite nā haya 'vishaya'

caitanya-carane rahoń, yadi ājñā haya

SYNONYMS

āmi kahi — I said; āmā haite — by me; nā — not; haya — is possible; vishaya — government service; caitanya-carane — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahoń — I may stay; yadi ājñā haya — if you kindly give me permission.

TRANSLATION

"I said, 'Your Majesty, I am now not willing to engage in political activities. I desire only to stay at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Kindly give me permission.'
