Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.141

punah prabhu kahe — āmi tomāra nimitte

dui pustaka āniyāchi 'dakshina' ha-ite

SYNONYMS

punah — again; prabhu kahe — the Lord said; āmi — I; tomāra nimitte — for your sake; dui — two; pustaka — books; āniyāchi — have brought; dakshina ha-ite — from South India.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "For your sake only, I have brought two books from South India.
