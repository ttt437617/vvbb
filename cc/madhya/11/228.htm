Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.228

āra sampradāye nāce pandita-vakreśvara

śrīvāsa nāce āra sampradāya-bhitara

SYNONYMS

āra sampradāye — in another sampradāya, or group; nāce — dances; pandita-vakreśvara — Vakreśvara Pandita; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; nāce — dances; āra — another; sampradāya-bhitara — in the middle of a group.

TRANSLATION

Vakreśvara Pandita began to dance in another group, and in yet another group Śrīvāsa Thākura began to dance.
