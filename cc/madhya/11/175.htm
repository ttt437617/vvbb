Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.175

āmāra nikate ei pushpera udyāne

eka-khāni ghara āche parama-nirjane

SYNONYMS

āmāra nikate — nearby My place; ei — this; pushpera udyāne — in a garden of flowers; eka-khāni — one; ghara — room; āche — there is; parama-nirjane — in a very solitary place.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "Nearby My place, in this garden of flowers, is a single room that is very solitary.
