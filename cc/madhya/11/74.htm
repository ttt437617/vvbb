Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.74

dāmodara-svarūpa, govinda, — dui jana

mālā-prasāda lañā yāya, yāhāń vaishnava-gana

SYNONYMS

dāmodara-svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; govinda — Govinda; dui jana — two persons; mālā-prasāda — flower garlands and remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food; lañā — taking; yāya — went; yāhāń — where; vaishnava-gana — the Vaishnavas.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara and Govinda, taking the flower garlands and prasādam of Lord Jagannātha, proceeded to where all the Vaishnavas were standing.
