Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.70

mahāprabhura gana yata āila gauda haite

bhattācārya, eke eke dekhāha āmāte

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gana — associates; yata — all; āila — who have come; gauda haite — from Bengal; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; eke eke — one after another; dekhāha — please show; āmāte — to me.

TRANSLATION

"Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, please show me, one after another, all of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's devotees who are coming from Bengal."
