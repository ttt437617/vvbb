Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.149

śivānande kahe prabhu, — tomāra āmāte

gādha anurāga haya, jāni āge haite

SYNONYMS

śivānande — unto Śivānanda Sena; kahe — says; prabhu — the Lord; tomāra — your; āmāte — upon Me; gādha anurāga — deep affection; haya — there is; jāni — I know; āge haite — from the very beginning.

TRANSLATION

Then turning toward Śivānanda Sena, the Lord said, "I know that from the very beginning your affection for Me has been very great."
