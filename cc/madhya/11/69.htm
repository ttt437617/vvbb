Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.69

rājā kahe, — padichāke āmi ājñā diba

vāsā ādi ye cāhiye, — padichā saba diba

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; padichāke — unto the attendant; āmi — I; ājñā diba — shall give orders; vāsā — residential quarters; ādi — and other arrangements; ye cāhiye — whatever you want; padichā — the attendant; saba — everything; diba — will supply.

TRANSLATION

The King replied, "I shall give orders to the attendant in the temple. He will arrange for everyone's residential quarters and prasādam, as you desire.
