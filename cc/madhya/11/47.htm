Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.47

adarśanīyān api nīca-jātīn

samvīkshate hanta tathāpi no mām

mad-eka-varjam kripayishyatīti

nirnīya kim so 'vatatāra devah

SYNONYMS

adarśanīyān — upon those who are unfit to be seen; api — although; nīca-jātīn — the lower class of men; samvīkshate — puts His merciful glance; hanta — alas; tathā api — still; na u — not; mām — upon me; mat — myself; eka — alone; varjam — rejecting; kripayishyati — He will bestow His mercy; iti — thus; nirnīya — deciding; kim — whether; sah — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avatatāra — has descended; devah — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"'Alas, has Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made His advent deciding that He will deliver all others with the exception of me? He bestows His merciful glance upon many lower-class men who are usually not even to be seen.'"

PURPORT

This verse is found in the Śrī Caitanya-candrodaya-nātaka (8.28).
