Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.235

mahā-nritya, mahā-prema, mahā-sańkīrtana

dekhi' premāveśe bhāse nīlācala-jana

SYNONYMS

mahā-nritya — great dancing; mahā-prema — great love; mahā-sańkīrtana — great congregational chanting; dekhi' — seeing; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; bhāse — float; nīlācala-jana — all the residents of Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

Upon seeing the great dancing, great love and great sańkīrtana, all the people of Jagannātha Purī floated in an ecstatic ocean of love.
