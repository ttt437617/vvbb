Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.105

rājā kahe, — sabe jagannātha nā dekhiyā

caitanyera vāsā-grihe calilā dhāñā

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; sabe — all of them; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; nā dekhiyā — without visiting; caitanyera — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vāsā-grihe — to the residential place; calilā — they went; dhāñā — running.

TRANSLATION

The King said, "Instead of visiting the temple of Lord Jagannātha, all the devotees are running toward the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."
