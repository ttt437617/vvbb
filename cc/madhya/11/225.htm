Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.225

cāri-dike cāri sampradāya uccaihsvare gāya

madhye tāndava-nritya kare gaurarāya

SYNONYMS

cāri-dike — on four sides; cāri sampradāya — the four groups; uccaih-svare — very loudly; gāya — chant; madhye — in the middle; tāndava-nritya — jumping and dancing; kare — performs; gaurarāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

On all four sides the four sańkīrtana groups chanted very loudly, and in the middle Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced, jumping high.
