Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.231

cāri janera nritya dekhite prabhura abhilāsha

sei abhilāshe kare aiśvarya prakāśa

SYNONYMS

cāri janera — of the four persons; nritya — dancing; dekhite — to see; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; abhilāsha — desire; sei abhilāshe — for that purpose; kare — does; aiśvarya prakāśa — exhibition of a miracle.

TRANSLATION

Wanting to see the dancing of the four great personalities, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu exhibited this miracle of seeing everyone simultaneously.
