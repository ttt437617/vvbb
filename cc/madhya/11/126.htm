Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.126

hena-kāle mahāprabhu nija-gana-sańge

vaishnave mililā āsi' pathe bahu-rańge

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija-gana-sańge — in the association of His personal assistants; vaishnave — all the Vaishnavas; mililā — met; āsi' — coming; pathe — on the road; bahu-rańge — in great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

In the meantime, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, accompanied by His personal associates, met all the Vaishnavas on the road with great jubilation.
