Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.202

svarūpa-gosāñi prabhuke kaila nivedana

tumi nā vasile keha nā kare bhojana

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; prabhuke — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — did; nivedana — submission; tumi — You; nā vasile — if not sitting; keha — anyone; nā — not; kare — does; bhojana — eating.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī then informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Unless You sit and take prasādam, no one will accept it.
