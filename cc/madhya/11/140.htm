Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.140

chota hañā mukunda ebe haila āmāra jyeshtha

tomāra kripā-pātra tāte sarva-gune śreshtha

SYNONYMS

chota hañā — being junior; mukunda — Mukunda; ebe — now; haila — has become; āmāra — my; jyeshtha — senior; tomāra — Your; kripā-pātra — favorite; tāte — therefore; sarva-gune — in all good qualities; śreshtha — superior.

TRANSLATION

Thus Vāsudeva Datta admitted his inferiority to Mukunda, his younger brother. "Although Mukunda is my junior," he said, "he first received Your favor. Consequently he became transcendentally senior to me. Besides that, You very much favored Mukunda. Thus he is superior in all good qualities."
