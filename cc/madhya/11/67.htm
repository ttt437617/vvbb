Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.67

gauda haite vaishnava āsitechena dui-śata

mahāprabhura bhakta saba — mahā-bhāgavata

SYNONYMS

gauda haite — from Bengal; vaishnava — devotees; āsitechena — are coming; dui-śata — numbering about two hundred; mahāprabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta — the devotees; saba — all; mahā-bhāgavata — greatly advanced devotees.

TRANSLATION

"About two hundred devotees are coming from Bengal. All of them are greatly advanced and specifically devoted to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
