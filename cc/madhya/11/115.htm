Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.115

viśeshe śrī-haste prabhu kare pariveśana

eta lābha chādi' kon kare uposhana

SYNONYMS

viśeshe — especially; śrī-haste — with His transcendental hands; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — does; pariveśana — distribution; eta — so much; lābha — profit; chādi' — giving up; kon — who; kare — does; uposhana — fasting.

TRANSLATION

"When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is distributing prasādam with His transcendental hand, who will neglect such an opportunity and accept the regulative principle of fasting?
