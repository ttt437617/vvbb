Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.142

svarūpera thāńi āche, laha tā likhiyā

vāsudeva ānandita pustaka pāñā

SYNONYMS

svarūpera thāńi — in the possession of Svarūpa Dāmodara; āche — they are; laha — you take; tā — them; likhiyā — copying; vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; ānandita — very glad; pustaka — the books; pāñā — getting.

TRANSLATION

"The books are being kept with Svarūpa Dāmodara, and you can get them copied." Hearing this, Vāsudeva became very glad.
