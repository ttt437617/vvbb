Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.208

svarūpa gosāñi, dāmodara, jagadānanda

vaishnavere pariveśe tina jane — ānanda

SYNONYMS

svarūpa gosāñi — Svarūpa Gosāñi; dāmodara — Dāmodara; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; vaishnavere pariveśe — distributed to the Vaishnavas; tina jane — the three persons; ānanda — very jubilant.

TRANSLATION

Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, Dāmodara Pandita and Jagadānanda all began to distribute prasādam to the devotees with great pleasure.
