Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.4

prabhu kahe, — kaha tumi, nāhi kichu bhaya

yogya haile kariba, ayogya haile naya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; kaha tumi — yes, you can speak; nāhi — there is not; kichu — any; bhaya — fear; yogya — befitting; haile — if it is; kariba — I shall grant; ayogya — not befitting; haile — if it is; naya — then I shall not.

TRANSLATION

The Lord gave the Bhattācārya assurance that he could speak without fear, but added that if his statement were suitable He would accept it, and if it were not, He would reject it.
