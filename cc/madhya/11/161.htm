Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.161

sabāre sammāni' prabhura ha-ila ullāsa

haridāse nā dekhiyā kahe, — kāhāń haridāsa

SYNONYMS

sabāre sammāni' — respecting everyone; prabhura — of the Lord; ha-ila — there was; ullāsa — jubilation; haridāse — Haridāsa Thākura; nā dekhiyā — without seeing; kahe — says; kāhāń haridāsa — where is Haridāsa.

TRANSLATION

After thus offering respect to each and every devotee, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu became very jubilant. However, not seeing Haridāsa Thākura, He inquired, "Where is Haridāsa?"
