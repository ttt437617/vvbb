Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.101

rājā kahe, — śāstra-pramāne caitanya hana krishna

tabe kene pandita saba tāńhāte vitrishna?

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; śāstra-pramāne — by the evidence of revealed scripture; caitanya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hana — is; krishna — the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Krishna; tabe — therefore; kene — why; pandita — so-called learned scholars; saba — all; tāńhāte — unto Him; vitrishna — indifferent.

TRANSLATION

The King said, "According to evidence given in the revealed scriptures, it is concluded that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is Lord Krishna Himself. Why, then, are learned scholars sometimes indifferent to Him?"
