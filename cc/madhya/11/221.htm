Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.221

āge-pāche gāna kare cāri sampradāya

āchādera kāle dhare nityānanda rāya

SYNONYMS

āge-pāche — in front and in the rear; gāna — singing; kare — do; cāri — four; sampradāya — groups; āchādera — of falling down; kāle — at the time; dhare — captures; nityānanda rāya — Lord Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

As the circumambulation was performed, the four kīrtana parties sang in front and in the rear. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down to the ground, Śrī Nityānanda Rāya Prabhu lifted Him up.
