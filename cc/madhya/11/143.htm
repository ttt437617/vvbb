Madhya-līlāChapter 11: The Bedā-kīrtana Pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 11.143

pratyeka vaishnava sabe likhiyā la-ila

krame krame dui grantha sarvatra vyāpila

SYNONYMS

pratyeka — each and every; vaishnava — devotee; sabe — all; likhiyā — copying; la-ila — took; krame krame — by and by; dui grantha — the two books; sarvatra — everywhere; vyāpila — become broadcast.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, each and every Vaishnava copied the two books. By and by, the two books [the Brahma-samhitā and Śrī Krishna-karnāmrita] were broadcast all over India.
