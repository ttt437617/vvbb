Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.4

śrī-rādhikāra ceshtā yena uddhava-darśane

ei-mata daśā prabhura haya rātri-dine

SYNONYMS

śrī-rādhikāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; ceshtā — the activities; yena — just like; uddhava-darśane — in seeing Uddhava at Vrindāvana; ei-mata — in this way; daśā — the condition; prabhura — of the Lord; haya — is; rātri-dine — day and night.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's state of mind, day and night, was practically identical to Rādhārānī's state of mind when Uddhava came to Vrindāvana to see the gopīs.
