Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.38

punah yadi kona kshana, karāya krishna daraśana

tabe sei ghatī-kshana-pala

diyā mālya-candana, nānā ratna-ābharana,

alańkrita karimu sakala

SYNONYMS

punah — again; yadi — if; kona — some; kshana — moment; karāya — helps; krishna — Lord Krishna; daraśana — seeing; tabe — then; sei — that; ghatī-kshana-pala — seconds, moments and hours; diyā — offering; mālya-candana — garlands and sandalwood pulp; nānā — various; ratna — jewels; ābharana — ornaments; alańkrita — decorated; karimu — I shall make; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

"If by chance such a moment comes when I can once again see Krishna, then I shall worship those seconds, moments and hours with flower garlands and pulp of sandalwood and decorate them with all kinds of jewels and ornaments."
