Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.65

he deva he dayita he bhuvanaika-bandho

he krishna he capala he karunaika-sindho

he nātha he ramana he nayanābhirāma

hā hā kadā nu bhavitāsi padam driśor me

SYNONYMS

he deva — O Lord; he dayita — O most dear; he bhuvana-eka-bandho — O only friend of the universe; he krishna — O Lord Krishna; he capala — O restless one; he karunā-eka-sindho — O only ocean of mercy; he nātha — O My Lord; he ramana — O My enjoyer; he nayana-abhirāma — O most beautiful to My eyes; hā hā — alas; kadā — when; nu — certainly; bhavitā asi — will You be; padam — the dwelling place; driśoh me — of My vision.

TRANSLATION

"'O My Lord! O dearest one! O only friend of the universe! O Krishna, O restless one, O only ocean of mercy! O My Lord, O My enjoyer, O beloved to My eyes! Alas, when will You again be visible to Me?'"

PURPORT

This is text 40 of the Krishna-karnāmrita.
