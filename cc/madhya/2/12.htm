Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.12

hasta-padera sandhi saba vitasti-pramāne

sandhi chādi' bhinna haye, carma rahe sthāne

SYNONYMS

hasta-padera — of the hands and legs; sandhi — joints; saba — all; vitasti — about eight inches; pramāne — in length; sandhi — joints; chādi' — dislocated; bhinna — separated; haye — become; carma — skin; rahe — remains; sthāne — in the place.

TRANSLATION

The joints of His hands and legs would sometimes become separated by eight inches, and they remained connected only by the skin.
