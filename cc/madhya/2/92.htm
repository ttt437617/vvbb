Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.92

sańkshepe ei sūtra kaila, yei ihāń nā likhila,

āge tāhā kariba vistāra

yadi tata dina jiye, mahāprabhura kripā haye,

icchā bhari' kariba vicāra

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; ei sūtra — these notes; kaila — I have made; yei — whatever; ihāń — in this; nā likhila — I could not write; āge — in the future; tāhā — that; kariba — I shall make; vistāra — expansion; yadi — if; tata — so many; dina — days; jiye — I live; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā — the mercy; haye — there is; icchā bhari' — satisfying the desire; kariba — I shall do; vicāra — consideration.

TRANSLATION

In this chapter I have briefly described the antya-līlā. Whatever I have not described I shall describe extensively in the future. If by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy I live for so many days that I can fulfill my desires, I will give full consideration to these pastimes.
