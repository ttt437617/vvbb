Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.15

kāhāń mora prāna-nātha muralī-vadana

kāhāń karoń kāhāń pāń vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — where; mora — My; prāna-nātha — Lord of the life; muralī-vadana — playing the flute; kāhāń — what; karoń — shall I do; kāhāń — where; pāń — I shall get; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Mahārāja Nanda.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to express His mind in this way: "Where is the Lord of My life, who is playing His flute? What shall I do now? Where should I go to find the son of Mahārāja Nanda?
