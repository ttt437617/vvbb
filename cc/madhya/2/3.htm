Madhya-līlāChapter 2: The Ecstatic Manifestations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Madhya 2.3

śesha ye rahila prabhura dvādaśa vatsara

krishnera viyoga-sphūrti haya nirantara

SYNONYMS

śesha — at the end; ye — those; rahila — remained; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dvādaśa vatsara — twelve years; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; viyoga — of separation; sphūrti — manifestation; haya — is; nirantara — always.

TRANSLATION

During His last twelve years, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always manifested all the symptoms of ecstasy in separation from Krishna.
