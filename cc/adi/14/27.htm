Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.27

kāndiyā balena śiśu, — kene kara rosha

tumi māti khāite dile, mora kibā dosha

SYNONYMS

kāndiyā — while crying; balena — says; śiśu — the child; kene — why; kara — you become; rosha — angry; tumi — you; māti — dirt; khāite — to eat; dile — gave Me; mora — My; kibā — what is; dosha — fault.

TRANSLATION

Crying, the child inquired from His mother, "Why are you angry? You have already given Me dirt to eat. What is My fault?
