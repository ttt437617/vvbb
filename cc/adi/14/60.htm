Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.60

āniyā naivedya tārā sammukhe dharila

khāiyā naivedya tāre ishta-vara dila

SYNONYMS

āniyā — bringing; naivedya — offering; tārā — all of them; sammukhe — in front; dharila — held; khāiyā — eating; naivedya — offering; tāre — them; ishta-vara — desired benediction; dila — gave.

TRANSLATION

The girls then brought the offerings before the Lord, who ate them all and blessed the girls to their satisfaction.
