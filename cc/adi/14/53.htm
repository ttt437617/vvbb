Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.53

āmā sabākāra pakshe ihā karite nā yuyāya

nā laha devatā sajja, nā kara anyāya

SYNONYMS

āmā sabākāra — of all of us; pakshe — on the behalf; ihā — this; karite — to do; nā — not; yuyāya — is suitable; nā — don't; laha — take; devatā — demigods; sajja — worshipable paraphernalia; nā — don't; kara — do; anyāya — mischief.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore it does not behoove You to act like this. Don't take our paraphernalia for worship of the demigods. Don't create a disturbance in this way."
