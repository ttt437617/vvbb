Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.11

stana piyāite putrera carana dekhila

sei cihna pāye dekhi' miśre bolāila

SYNONYMS

stana — her breast; piyāite — while letting Him suck; putrera — of her son; carana — lotus feet; dekhila — observed; sei — those very; cihna — marks; pāye — on the sole; dekhi' — seeing; miśre — Jagannātha Miśra; bolāila — called for.

TRANSLATION

While mother Śacī was feeding the child from her breast, she saw on His lotus feet all the marks that were visible on the floor of the room, and she called for Jagannātha Miśra.
