Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.79

miśra kahe, — ei bada adbhuta kāhinī

śiśura śūnya-pade kene nūpurera dhvani

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Jagannātha Miśra said; ei bada — this is very; adbhuta — wonderful; kāhinī — incident; śiśura — of the child; śūnya-pade — on the naked legs; kene — why; nūpurera — of the ankle bells; dhvani — sound.

TRANSLATION

Jagannātha Miśra said, "This is a very wonderful incident. Why is there a sound of ankle bells from the bare feet of my child?"
