Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.57

kona kanyā palāila naivedya la-iyā

tāre dāki' kahe prabhu sakrodha ha-iyā

SYNONYMS

kona kanyā — some of the girls; palāila — fled; naivedya — the plate of worshipable things; la-iyā — taking away; tāre — unto them; dāki' — calling; kahe — says; prabhu — the Lord; sakrodha — angry; ha-iyā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

When some of the girls fled, the Lord called them in anger and advised them as follows:
