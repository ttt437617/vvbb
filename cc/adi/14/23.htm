Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.23

tabe kata dine kaila pada-cańkramana

śiśu-gane mili' kaila vividha khelana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; kata dine — in a few days; kaila — did; pada — legs; cańkramana — movement; śiśu-gane — all the children; mili' — mingling together; kaila — executed; vividha — varieties; khelana — sporting.

TRANSLATION

After some days the Lord began to move His legs and walk. He mixed with other children and exhibited various sports.
