Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.14

batriśa lakshana — mahāpurusha-bhūshana

ei śiśu ańge dekhi se saba lakshana

SYNONYMS

batriśa — thirty-two; lakshana — symptoms; mahā-purusha — great personality; bhūshana — ornament; ei śiśu — this child; ańge — on the body; dekhi — I see; se — those; saba — all; lakshana — symptoms.

TRANSLATION

"There are thirty-two bodily marks that symptomize a great personality, and I see all those marks on the body of this child.
