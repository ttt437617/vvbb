Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.93

ei mata śiśu-līlā kare gauracandra

dine dine pitā-mātāra bādāya ānanda

SYNONYMS

ei — this; mata — like; śiśu-līlā — childhood pastimes; kare — does; gauracandra — Śrī Gaurahari; dine dine — day after day; pitā-mātāra — of His parents; bādāya — He increases; ānanda — the pleasure.

TRANSLATION

In this way Gaurahari performed His childhood pastimes and day after day increased the pleasure of His parents.
