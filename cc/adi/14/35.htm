Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.35

eta bali' jananīra kolete cadiyā

stana pāna kare prabhu īshat hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; jananīra — of the mother; kolete — on the lap; cadiyā — rising; stana pāna — sucking the nipple; kare — does; prabhu — the Lord; īshat — slightly; hāsiyā — smiling.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, the Lord, smiling slightly, climbed on the lap of His mother and sucked her breast.
