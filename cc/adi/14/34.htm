Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.34

ebe se jānilāń, āra māti nā khāiba

kshudhā lāge yabe, tabe tomāra stana piba"

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; se — that; jānilāń — I understand; āra — more; māti — dirt; nā — not; khāiba — I shall eat; kshudhā — hunger; lāge — arises; yabe — when; tabe — at that time; tomāra — your; stana — breast; piba — I shall suck.

TRANSLATION

"Now that I can understand this philosophy, no more shall I eat dirt. Whenever I am hungry I shall suck your breast and drink your breast milk."
