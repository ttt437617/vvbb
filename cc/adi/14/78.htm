Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.78

calite carane nūpura bāje jhanjhan

śuni' camakita haila pitā-mātāra mana

SYNONYMS

calite — while going; carane — on the lotus feet; nūpura — ankle bells; bāje — sounded; jhanjhan — tinkling; śuni' — hearing; camakita — struck with wonder; haila — became; pitā — of His father; mātāra — and mother; mana — the minds.

TRANSLATION

When the child was going out, there was a tinkling of ankle bells from His lotus feet. Hearing this, His father and mother were struck with wonder.
