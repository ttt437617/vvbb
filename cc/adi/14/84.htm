Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.84

rātre svapna dekhe, — eka āsi' brāhmana

miśrere kahaye kichu sa-rosha vacana

SYNONYMS

rātre — at night; svapna dekhe — he dreamt; eka — one; āsi' — coming; brāhmana — brāhmana; miśrere — unto Jagannātha Miśra; kahaye — spoke; kichu — something; sa-rosha — with anger; vacana — words.

TRANSLATION

On that very night, Jagannātha Miśra dreamt that a brāhmana had come before him speaking these words in great anger:
