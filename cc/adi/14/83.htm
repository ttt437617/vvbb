Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.83

eka-dina miśra putrera cāpalya dekhiyā

dharma-śikshā dila bahu bhartsanā kariyā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; miśra — Jagannātha Miśra; putrera — of his son; cāpalya — the mischievous behavior; dekhiyā — seeing; dharma-śikshā — religious teaching; dila — gave; bahu — much; bhartsanā — rebuking; kariyā — doing.

TRANSLATION

On another occasion, Jagannātha Miśra, seeing the mischievous acts of his son, gave Him lessons in morality after rebuking Him greatly.
