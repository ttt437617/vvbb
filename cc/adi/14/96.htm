Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.96

ataeva ei-līlā sańkshepe sūtra kaila

punarukti-bhaye vistāriyā nā kahila

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; ei-līlā — these pastimes; sańkshepe — in brief; sūtra — synopsis; kaila — made; punar-ukti — repetition; bhaye — being afraid of; vistārirā — elaborate explanation; nā — did not; kahila — say.

TRANSLATION

I have therefore made only a brief summary. Being afraid of repetition, I have not elaborated upon this subject matter.
