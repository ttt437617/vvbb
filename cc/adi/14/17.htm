Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.17

ei ta' karibe vaishnava-dharmera pracāra

ihā haite habe dui kulera nistāra

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — this child; karibe — will do; vaishnava — of Vaishnavism, or devotional service; dharmera — of the religion; pracāra — preaching; ihā haite — from this; habe — there will be; dui — two; kulera — dynasties; nistāra — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"This child will preach the Vaishnava cult and deliver both His maternal and paternal families.

PURPORT

Only Nārāyana Himself or His bona fide representative can preach the cult of Vaishnavism, or devotional service. When a Vaishnava is born, he delivers both his maternal and paternal families simultaneously.
