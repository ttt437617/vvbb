Ādi-līlāChapter 14: Lord Caitanya's Childhood Pastimes

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 14.25

eta bali' gelā śacī grihe karma karite

lukāñā lāgilā śiśu mrittikā khāite

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; gelā — returned; śacī — mother Śacī; grihe — in the house; karma — duties; karite — to execute; lukāñā — hiding; lāgilā — began; śiśu — the child; mrittikā — dirt; khāite — to eat.

TRANSLATION

But when she returned to her household duties, the child hid from His mother and began to eat dirt.
