Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.188

tāń sabāra nāhi nija-sukha-anurodha

tathāpi bādhaye sukha, padila virodha

SYNONYMS

tāń sabāra — of all of them; nāhi — not; nija-sukha — for their own happiness; anurodha — entreaty; tathāpi — still; bādhaye — increases; sukha — happiness; padila — happened; virodha — contradiction.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs have no inclination for their own enjoyment, and yet their joy increases. That is indeed a contradiction.
