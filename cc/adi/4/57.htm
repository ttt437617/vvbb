Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.57

sei dui eka ebe caitanya gosāñi

rasa āsvādite dońhe hailā eka-thāńi

SYNONYMS

sei — these; dui — two; eka — one; ebe — now; caitanya gosāñi — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rasa — mellow; āsvādite — to taste; dońhe — the two; hailā — have become; eka-thāńi — one body.

TRANSLATION

Now, to enjoy rasa, They have appeared in one body as Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
