Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.276

mańgalācaranam krishna-

caitanya-tattva-lakshanam

prayojanam cāvatāre

śloka-shatkair nirūpitam

SYNONYMS

mańgala-ācaranam — invoking auspiciousness; krishna-caitanya — of Lord Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tattva-lakshanam — symptoms of the truth; prayojanam — necessity; ca — also; avatāre — in the matter of His incarnation; śloka — verses; shatkaih — by six; nirūpitam — ascertained.

TRANSLATION

Thus the auspicious invocation, the essential nature of the truth of Lord Caitanya, and the need for His appearance have been set forth in six verses.
