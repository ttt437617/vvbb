Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.112

pūrve vraje krishnera tri-vidha vayo-dharma

kaumāra, pauganda, āra kaiśora atimarma

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; vraje — in Vraja; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; tri-vidha — three sorts; vayah-dharma — characteristics of age; kaumāra — childhood; pauganda — boyhood; āra — and; kaiśora — adolescence; ati-marma — the very core.

TRANSLATION

Formerly in Vraja Lord Krishna displayed three ages, namely childhood, boyhood and adolescence. His adolescence is especially significant.
