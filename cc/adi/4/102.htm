Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.102

avatari' prabhu pracārila sańkīrtana

eho bāhya hetu, pūrve kariyāchi sūcana

SYNONYMS

avatari' — incarnating; prabhu — the Lord; pracārila — propagated; sańkīrtana — the congregational chanting of the holy name; eho — this; bāhya — external; hetu — reason; pūrve — previously; kariyāchi — I have given; sūcana — indication.

TRANSLATION

The Lord came to propagate sańkīrtana. That is an external purpose, as I have already indicated.
