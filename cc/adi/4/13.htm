Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.13

ataeva vishnu takhana krishnera śarīre

vishnu-dvāre kare krishna asura-samhāre

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; takhana — at that time; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; śarīre — in the body; vishnu-dvāre — by Lord Vishnu; kare — does; krishna — Lord Krishna; asura-samhāre — killing the demons.

TRANSLATION

At that time, therefore, Lord Vishnu is present in the body of Lord Krishna, and Lord Krishna kills the demons through Him.
