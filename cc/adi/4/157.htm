Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.157

apūrva mādhurī krishnera, apūrva tāra bala

yāhāra śravane mana haya talamala

SYNONYMS

apūrva — unprecedented; mādhurī — sweetness; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; apūrva — unprecedented; tāra — of that; bala — the strength; yāhāra — of which; śravane — in hearing; mana — the mind; haya — becomes; talamala — unsteady.

TRANSLATION

The sweetness of Lord Krishna is unprecedented, and its strength is also unprecedented. Simply by one's hearing of such beauty, the mind becomes unsteady.
