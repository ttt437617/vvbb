Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.252

krishna-ālińgana pāinu, janama saphale

krishna-sukhe magna rahe vriksha kari' kole

SYNONYMS

krishna — of Lord Krishna; ālińgana — the embrace; pāinu — I have gotten; janama sa-phale — My birth is now fulfilled; krishna-sukhe — in the matter of pleasing Krishna; magna — immersed; rahe — She remains; vriksha — the tree; kari' — taking; kole — on the lap.

TRANSLATION

"'I have gotten the embrace of Śrī Krishna,' She thinks, 'so now My life is fulfilled.' Thus She remains immersed in pleasing Krishna, taking the tree in Her arms.
