Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.64

sandhinīra sāra amśa — 'śuddha-sattva' nāma

bhagavānera sattā haya yāhāte viśrāma

SYNONYMS

sandhinīra — of the existence potency; sāra — essence; amśa — portion; śuddha-sattva — śuddha-sattva (pure existence); nāma — named; bhagavānera — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sattā — the existence; haya — is; yāhāte — in which; viśrāma — the resting place.

TRANSLATION

The essential portion of the sandhinī potency is śuddha-sattva. Lord Krishna's existence rests upon it.
