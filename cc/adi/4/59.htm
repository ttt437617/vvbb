Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.59

rādhikā hayena krishnera pranaya-vikāra

svarūpa-śakti — 'hlādinī' nāma yāńhāra

SYNONYMS

rādhikā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; hayena — is; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; pranaya-vikāra — transformation of love; svarūpa-śakti — personal energy; hlādinī — hlādinī; nāma — name; yāńhāra — whose.

TRANSLATION

Śrīmatī Rādhikā is the transformation of Krishna's love. She is His internal energy called hlādinī.
