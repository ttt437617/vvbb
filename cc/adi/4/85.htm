Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.85

krishna-mayī — krishna yāra bhitare bāhire

yāńhā yāńhā netra pade tāńhā krishna sphure

SYNONYMS

krishna-mayī — the word krishna-mayī; krishna — Lord Krishna; yāra — of whom; bhitare — the within; bāhire — the without; yāńhā yāńhā — wherever; netra — the eyes; pade — fall; tāńhā — there; krishna — Lord Krishna; sphure — manifests.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna-mayī" means "one whose within and without are Lord Krishna." She sees Lord Krishna wherever She casts Her glance.
