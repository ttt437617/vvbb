Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.162

gopī-ganera premera 'rūdha-bhāva' nāma

viśuddha nirmala prema, kabhu nahe kāma

SYNONYMS

gopī-ganera — of the gopīs; premera — of the love; rūdha-bhāva — rūdha-bhāva; nāma — named; viśuddha — pure; nirmala — spotless; prema — love; kabhu — at anytime; nahe — is not; kāma — lust.

TRANSLATION

The love of the gopīs is called rūdha-bhāva. It is pure and spotless. It is not at any time lust.

PURPORT

As already explained, the position of the gopīs in their loving dealings with Krishna is transcendental. Their emotion is called rūdha-bhāva. Although it is apparently like mundane sex, one should not confuse it with mundane sexual love, for it is pure and unadulterated love of Godhead.
