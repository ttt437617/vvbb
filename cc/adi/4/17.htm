Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.17

aiśvarya-jñānete saba jagat miśrita

aiśvarya-śithila-preme nahi mora prīta

SYNONYMS

aiśvarya jñānete — with knowledge of majesty; saba — all; jagat — the universe; miśrita — mixed; aiśvarya-śithila — weakened by majesty; preme — in love; nāhi — there is not; mora — My; prīta — pleasure.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Krishna thought:] "All the universe is filled with the conception of My majesty, but love weakened by that sense of majesty does not satisfy Me.
