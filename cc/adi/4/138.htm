Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.138

adbhuta, ananta, pūrna mora madhurimā

tri-jagate ihāra keha nāhi pāya sīmā

SYNONYMS

adbhuta — wonderful; ananta — unlimited; pūrna — full; mora — My; madhurimā — sweetness; tri-jagate — in the three worlds; ihāra — of this; keha — someone; nāhi — not; pāya — obtains; sīmā — limit.

TRANSLATION

"My sweetness is wonderful, infinite and full. No one in the three worlds can find its limit.
