Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.140

yadyapi nirmala rādhāra sat-prema-darpana

tathāpi svacchatā tāra bādhe kshane kshana

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; nirmala — pure; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; sat-prema — of real love; darpana — the mirror; tathāpi — still; svacchatā — transparency; tāra — of that; bādhe — increases; kshane kshana — every moment.

TRANSLATION

"Although Rādhā's love is pure like a mirror, its purity increases at every moment.
