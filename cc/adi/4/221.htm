Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.221

sei bhāve nija-vāñchā karila pūrana

avatārera ei vāñchā mūla-kārana

SYNONYMS

sei — that; bhāve — in the mood; nija-vāñchā — His own desires, karila — did; pūrana — fulfilling; avatārera — of the incarnation; ei — this; vāñchā — desire; mūla — root; kārana — cause.

TRANSLATION

In the mood of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, He also fulfilled His own desires. This is the principal reason for His appearance.
