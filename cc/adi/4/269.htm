Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.269

sarva-bhāve kaila krishna ei ta' niścaya

hena-kāle āila yugāvatāra-samaya

SYNONYMS

sarva-bhāve — in all respects; kaila — made; krishna — Lord Krishna; ei — this; ta' — certainly; niścaya — decision; hena-kāle — at this time; āila — came; yuga-avatāra — of the incarnation according to the age; samaya — the time.

TRANSLATION

In this way Lord Krishna came to a decision. Simultaneously, the time came for the incarnation of the age.
