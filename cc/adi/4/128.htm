Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.128

rādhā-premā vibhu — yāra bādite nāhi thāñi

tathāpi se kshane kshane bādaye sadāi

SYNONYMS

rādhā-premā — the love of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; vibhu — all-pervading; yāra — of which; bādite — to increase; nāhi — there is not; thāñi — space; tathāpi — still; se — that; kshane kshane — every second; bādaye — increases; sadāi — always.

TRANSLATION

"Rādhā's love is all-pervading, leaving no room for expansion. But still it is expanding constantly.
