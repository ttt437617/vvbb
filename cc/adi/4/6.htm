Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.6

satya ei hetu, kintu eho bahirańga

āra eka hetu, śuna, āche antarańga

SYNONYMS

satya — true; ei — this; hetu — reason; kintu — but; eho — this; bahirańga — external; āra — another; eka — one; hetu — reason; śuna — please hear; āche — is; antarańga — internal.

TRANSLATION

Although this is true, this is but the external reason for the Lord's incarnation. Please hear one other reason — the confidential reason — for the Lord's appearance.

PURPORT

In the Third Chapter, fourth verse, it has been clearly said that Lord Caitanya appeared in order to distribute love of Krishna and the chanting of His transcendental holy name, Hare Krishna. That was the secondary purpose of Lord Caitanya's appearance. The real reason is different, as we shall see in this chapter.
