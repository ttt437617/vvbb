Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.79

ākāra svabhāva-bhede vraja-devī-gana

kāya-vyūha-rūpa tāńra rasera kārana

SYNONYMS

ākāra — of features; svabhāva — of natures; bhede — with differences; vraja-devī-gana — the gopīs; kāya — of Her body; vyūha — of expansions; rūpa — having the form; tāńra — of Her; rasera — of mellows; kārana — instruments.

TRANSLATION

The Vraja-devīs have diverse bodily features. They are Her expansions and are the instruments for expanding rasa.
