Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.121

tāńhāra prathama vāñchā kariye vyākhyāna

krishna kahe, — 'āmi ha-i rasera nidāna

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra — His; prathama — first; vāñchā — desire; kariye — I do; vyākhyāna — explanation; krishna — Lord Krishna; kahe — says; āmi — I; ha-i — am; rasera — of mellow; nidāna — primary cause.

TRANSLATION

I shall explain His first desire. Krishna says, "I am the primary cause of all rasas.
