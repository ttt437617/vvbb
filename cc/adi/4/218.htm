Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.218

krishnera vallabhā rādhā krishna-prāna-dhana

tāńhā vinu sukha-hetu nahe gopī-gana

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; vallabhā — beloved; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; krishna-prāna-dhana — the wealth of the life of Lord Krishna; tāńhā — Her; vinu — without; sukha-hetu — cause of happiness; nahe — are not; gopī-gana — the gopīs.

TRANSLATION

Rādhā is the beloved consort of Krishna, and She is the wealth of His life. Without Her, the gopīs cannot give Him pleasure.
