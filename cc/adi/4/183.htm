Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.183

e-deha-darśana-sparśe krishna-santoshana'

ei lāgi' kare dehera mārjana-bhūsana

SYNONYMS

e-deha — of this body; darśana — by sight; sparśe — and touch; krishna — of Lord Krishna; santoshana — the satisfaction; ei lāgi' — for this; kare — they do; dehera — of the body; mārjana — cleaning; bhūshana — decorating.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna finds joy in seeing and touching this body." It is for this reason that they cleanse and decorate their bodies.
