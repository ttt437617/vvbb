Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.46

ataeva madhura rasa kahi tāra nāma

svakīyā-parakīyā-bhāve dvi-vidha samsthāna

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; madhura — sweet; rasa — mellow; kahi — I say; tāra — of that; nāma — the name; svakīyā — svakīyā (own); parakīyā — and named parakīyā (another's); bhāve — in the moods; dvi-vidha — two types; samsthāna — positions.

TRANSLATION

Therefore I call it madhura-rasa. It has two further divisions, namely wedded and unwedded love.
