Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.177

krishnera pratijñā eka āche pūrva haite

ye yaiche bhaje, krishna tāre bhaje taiche

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; pratijñā — promise; eka — one; āche — there is; pūrva haite — from before; ye — whoever; yaiche — just as; bhaje — he worships; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāre — to him; bhaje — reciprocates; taiche — just so.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna has a promise from before to reciprocate with His devotees according to the way they worship Him.
