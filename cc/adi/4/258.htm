Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.258

anyera sańgame āmi yata sukha pāi

tāhā haite rādhā-sukha śata adhikāi

SYNONYMS

anyera — others; sańgame — by meeting; āmi — I; yata — all; sukha — happiness; pāi — get; tāhā haite — than that; rādhā-sukha — happiness by association with Rādhārānī; śata — one hundred times; adhikāi — increased.

TRANSLATION

"The happiness I feel when meeting Rādhārānī is a hundred times greater than the happiness I get from meeting others.
