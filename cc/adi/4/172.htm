Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.172

ataeva gopī-ganera nāhi kāma-gandha

krishna-sukha lāgi mātra, krishna se sambandha

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; gopī-ganera — of the gopīs; nāhi — not; kāma-gandha — the slightest bit of lust; krishna-sukha — the happiness of Lord Krishna; lāgi — for; mātra — only; krishna — Lord Krishna; se — that; sambandha — the relationship.

TRANSLATION

Thus there is not the slightest taint of lust in the gopīs' love. Their relationship with Krishna is only for the sake of His enjoyment.
