Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.113

vātsalya-āveśe kaila kaumāra saphala

pauganda saphala kaila lañā sakhāvala

SYNONYMS

vātsalya — of parental love; āveśe — in the attachment; kaila — made; kaumāra — childhood; sa-phala — fruitful; pauganda — boyhood; sa-phala — fruitful; kaila — made; lañā — taking along; sakhā-āvala — friends.

TRANSLATION

Parental affection made His childhood fruitful. His boyhood was successful with His friends.
