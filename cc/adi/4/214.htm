Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.214

sei gopī-gana-madhye uttamā rādhikā

rūpe, gune, saubhāgye, preme sarvādhikā

SYNONYMS

sei — those; gopī-gana — the gopīs; madhye — among; uttamā — the highest; rādhikā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; rūpe — in beauty; gune — in qualities; saubhāgye — in good fortune; preme — in love; sarva-adhikā — above all.

TRANSLATION

Among the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhikā is the foremost. She surpasses all in beauty, in good qualities, in good fortune and, above all, in love.

PURPORT

Among all the gopīs, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is the most exalted. She is the most beautiful, the most qualified and, above all, the greatest lover of Krishna.
