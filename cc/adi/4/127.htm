Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.127

āmi yaiche paraspara viruddha-dharmāśraya

rādhā-prema taiche sadā viruddha-dharma-maya

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; yaiche — just as; paraspara — mutually; viruddha-dharma — of conflicting characteristics; āśraya — the abode; rādhā-prema — the love of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; taiche — just so; sadā — always; viruddha-dharma-maya — consists of conflicting characteristics.

TRANSLATION

"Just as I am the abode of all mutually contradictory characteristics, so Rādhā's love is always full of similar contradictions.
