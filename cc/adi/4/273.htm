Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.273

ei ta' kariluń shashtha ślokera vyākhyāna

śrī-rūpa-gosāñira pāda-padma kari' dhyāna

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; kariluń — I have made; shashtha ślokera — of the sixth verse; vyākhyāna — explanation; śrī-rūpa — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; gosāñira — of the master; pāda-padma — lotus feet; kari' — doing; dhyāna — meditation.

TRANSLATION

Meditating on the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, I have thus explained the sixth verse.
