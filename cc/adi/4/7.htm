Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.7

pūrve yena prithivīra bhāra haribāre

krishna avatīrna hailā śāstrete pracāre

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yena — as; prithivīra — of the earth; bhāra — burden; haribāre — to take away; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatīrna — incarnated; hailā — was; śāstrete — the scriptures; pracāre — proclaim.

TRANSLATION

The scriptures proclaim that Lord Krishna previously descended to take away the burden of the earth.
