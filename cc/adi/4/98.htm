Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.98

rādhā-krishna aiche sadā eka-i svarūpa

līlā-rasa āsvādite dhare dui-rūpa

SYNONYMS

rādhā-krishna — Rādhā and Krishna; aiche — in this way; sadā — always; eka-i — one; svarūpa — nature; līlā-rasa — the mellows of a pastime; āsvādite — to taste; dhare — manifest; dui-rūpa — two forms.

TRANSLATION

Thus Rādhā and Lord Krishna are one, yet They have taken two forms to enjoy the mellows of pastimes.
