Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.137

ei eka, śuna āra lobhera prakāra

sva-mādhurya dekhi' krishna karena vicāra

SYNONYMS

ei — this; eka — one; śuna — please hear; āra — another; lobhera — of eager desire; prakāra — type; sva-mādhurya — own sweetness; dekhi' — seeing; krishna — Lord Krishna; karena — does; vicāra — consideration.

TRANSLATION

That is one desire. Now please hear of another. Seeing His own beauty, Lord Krishna began to consider.
