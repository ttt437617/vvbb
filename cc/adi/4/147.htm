Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.147

krishna-mādhuryera eka svābhāvika bala

krishna-ādi nara-nārī karaye cañcala

SYNONYMS

krishna — of Lord Krishna; mādhuryera — of the sweetness; eka — one; svābhāvika — natural; bala — strength; krishna — Lord Krishna; ādi — beginning with; nara-nārī — men and women; karaye — makes; cañcala — perturbed.

TRANSLATION

The beauty of Krishna has one natural strength: it thrills the hearts of all men and women, beginning with Lord Krishna Himself.
