Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.126

nija-premāsvāde mora haya ye āhlāda

tāhā ha'te koti-guna rādhā-premāsvāda

SYNONYMS

nija — own; prema — love; āsvāde — in tasting; mora — My; haya — there is; ye — whatever; āhlāda — pleasure; tāhā ha'te — than that; koti-guna — ten million times greater; rādhā — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; prema-āsvāda — the tasting of love.

TRANSLATION

"Whatever pleasure I get from tasting My love for Śrīmatī Rādhārānī, She tastes ten million times more than Me by Her love.
