Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.249

ei mata anubhava āmāra pratīta

vicāri' dekhiye yadi, saba viparīta

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; anubhava — affectionate feelings; āmāra — My; pratīta — understood; vicāri' — by consideration; dekhiye — I see; yadi — if; saba — everything; viparīta — contrary.

TRANSLATION

"In this way My affectionate feelings for Śrīmatī Rādhārānī may be understood, but on analysis I find them contradictory.
