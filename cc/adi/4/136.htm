Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.136

eta cinti' rahe krishna parama-kautukī

hridaye bādaye prema-lobha dhakdhaki

SYNONYMS

eta cinti' — thinking this; rahe — remains; krishna — Lord Krishna; parama-kautukī — the supremely curious; hridaye — in the heart; bādaye — increases; prema-lobha — eager desire for love; dhakdhaki — blazing.

TRANSLATION

Thinking in this way, Lord Krishna was curious to taste that love. His eager desire for that love increasingly blazed in His heart.
