Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.95

jagat-mohana krishna, tāńhāra mohinī

ataeva samastera parā thākurānī

SYNONYMS

jagat-mohana — enchanting the universe; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāńhāra — of Him; mohinī — the enchantress; ataeva — therefore; samastera — of all; parā — foremost; thākurānī — goddess.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna enchants the world, but Śrī Rādhā enchants even Him. Therefore She is the supreme goddess of all.
