Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.148

śravane, darśane ākarshaye sarva-mana

āpanā āsvādite krishna karena yatana

SYNONYMS

śravane — in hearing; darśane — in seeing; ākarshaye — attracts; sarva-mana — all minds; āpanā — Himself; āsvādite — to taste; krishna — Lord Krishna; karena — makes; yatana — efforts.

TRANSLATION

All minds are attracted by hearing His sweet voice and flute, or by seeing His beauty. Even Lord Krishna Himself makes efforts to taste that sweetness.
