Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.91

kimvā, 'sarva-lakshmī' — krishnera shad-vidha aiśvarya

tāńra adhishthātrī śakti — sarva-śakti-varya

SYNONYMS

kimvā — or; sarva-lakshmī — the word sarva-lakshmī; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; shat-vidha — six kinds; aiśvarya — opulences; tāńra — of Him; adhishthātrī — chief; śakti — energy; sarva-śakti — of all energies; varya — the best.

TRANSLATION

Or "sarva-lakshmī" indicates that She fully represents the six opulences of Krishna. Therefore She is the supreme energy of Lord Krishna.
