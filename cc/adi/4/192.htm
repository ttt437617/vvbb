Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.192

gopī-śobhā dekhi' krishnera śobhā bādhe yata

krishna-śobhā dekhi' gopīra śobhā bādhe tata

SYNONYMS

gopī-śobhā — the beauty of the gopīs; dekhi' — seeing; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; śobhā — the beauty; bādhe — increases; yata — as much as; krishna-śobhā — the beauty of Lord Krishna; dekhi' — seeing; gopīra — of the gopīs; śobhā — the beauty; bādhe — increases; tata — that much.

TRANSLATION

The beauty of Lord Krishna increases at the sight of the beauty of the gopīs. And the more the gopīs see Lord Krishna's beauty, the more their beauty increases.
