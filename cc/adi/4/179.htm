Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.179

se pratijñā bhańga haila gopīra bhajane

tāhāte pramāna krishna-śrī-mukha-vacane

SYNONYMS

se — that; pratijñā — promise; bhańga haila — was broken; gopīra — of the gopīs; bhajane — by the worship; tāhāte — in that; pramāna — the proof; krishna — of Lord Krishna; śrī-mukha-vacane — by the words from the mouth.

TRANSLATION

That promise has been broken by the worship of the gopīs, as Lord Krishna Himself admits.
