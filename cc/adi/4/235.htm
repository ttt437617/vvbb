Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.235

abhakta-ushtrera ithe nā haya praveśa

tabe citte haya mora ānanda-viśesha

SYNONYMS

abhakta — nondevotee; ushtrera — of a camel; ithe — in this; nā — not; haya — is there; praveśa — entrance; tabe — then; citte — in my heart; haya — there is; mora — my; ānanda-viśesha — special jubilation.

TRANSLATION

The camellike nondevotees cannot enter into these topics. Therefore there is special jubilation in my heart.
