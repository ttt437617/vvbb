Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.171

ataeva kāma-preme bahuta antara

kāma — andha-tamah, prema — nirmala bhāskara

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; kāma-preme — in lust and love; bahuta — much; antara — space between; kāma — lust; andha-tamah — blind darkness; prema — love; nirmala — pure; bhāskara — sun.

TRANSLATION

Therefore lust and love are quite different. Lust is like dense darkness, but love is like the bright sun.
