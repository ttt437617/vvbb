Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.151

koti netra nāhi dila, sabe dila dui

tāhāte nimesha, — krishna ki dekhiba muñi

SYNONYMS

koti — ten million; netra — eyes; nāhi dila — did not give; sabe — to all; dila — gave; dui — two; tāhāte — in that; nimesha — a blink; krishna — Lord Krishna; ki — how; dekhiba — shall see; muñi — I.

TRANSLATION

He has not given millions of eyes to see the beauty of Krishna. He has given only two eyes, and even those eyes blink. How then shall I see the lovely face of Krishna?
