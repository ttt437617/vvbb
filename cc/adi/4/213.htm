Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.213

man-māhātmyam mat-saparyām

mac-chraddhām man-mano-gatam

jānanti gopikāh pārtha

nānye jānanti tattvatah

SYNONYMS

mat-māhātmyam — My greatness; mat-saparyām — My service; mat-śraddhām — respect for Me; mat-manah-gatam — the intention of My mind; jānanti — they know; gopikāh — the gopīs; pārtha — O Arjuna; na — not; anye — others; jānanti — know; tattvatah — factually.

TRANSLATION

"O Pārtha, the gopīs know My greatness, My loving service, respect for Me, and My mentality. Others cannot really know these."

PURPORT

This verse was spoken by Lord Krishna to Arjuna in the Ādi Purāna.
