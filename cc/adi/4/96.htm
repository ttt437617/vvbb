Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.96

rādhā — pūrna-śakti, krishna — pūrna-śaktimān

dui vastu bheda nāi, śāstra-paramāna

SYNONYMS

rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; pūrna-śakti — the complete energy; krishna — Lord Krishna; pūrna-śaktimān — the complete possessor of energy; dui — two; vastu — things; bheda — difference; nāi — there is not; śāstra-paramāna — the evidence of revealed scripture.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Rādhā is the full power, and Lord Krishna is the possessor of full power. The two are not different, as evidenced by the revealed scriptures.
