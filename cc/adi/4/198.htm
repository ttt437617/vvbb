Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.198

gopī-preme kare krishna-mādhuryera pushti

mādhurye bādhāya prema hañā mahā-tushti

SYNONYMS

gopī-preme — the love of the gopīs; kare — does; krishna-mādhuryera — of the sweetness of Lord Krishna; pushti — nourishment; mādhurye — the sweetness; bādhāya — causes to increase; prema — the love; hañā — being; mahā-tushti — greatly pleased.

TRANSLATION

The love of the gopīs nourishes the sweetness of Lord Krishna. That sweetness in turn increases their love, for they are greatly satisfied.
