Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.212

gopikā jānena krishnera manera vāñchita

prema-sevā-paripātī, ishta-samīhita

SYNONYMS

gopikā — the gopīs; jānena — know; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; manera — of the mind; vāñchita — the desired object; prema-sevā — of service in love; paripātī — perfection; ishta-samīhita — achievement of the desired goal of life.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs know Krishna's desires, and they know how to render perfect loving service for His enjoyment. They perform their service expertly for the satisfaction of their beloved.
