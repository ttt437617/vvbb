Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.186

gopī-gana kare yabe krishna-daraśana

sukha-vāñchā nāhi, sukha haya koti-guna

SYNONYMS

gopī-gana — the gopīs; kare — do; yabe — when; krishna-daraśana — seeing Lord Krishna; sukha-vāñchā — desire for happiness; nāhi — there is not; sukha — the happiness; haya — there is; koti-guna — ten million times.

TRANSLATION

When the gopīs see Lord Krishna, they derive unbounded bliss, although they have no desire for such pleasure.
