Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.160

atyanta-nigūdha ei rasera siddhānta

svarūpa-gosāñi mātra jānena ekānta

SYNONYMS

atyanta — extremely; nigūdha — deep; ei — this; rasera — of mellow; siddhānta — conclusion; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; mātra — only; jānena — knows; ekānta — much.

TRANSLATION

This conclusion of rasa is extremely deep. Only Svarūpa Dāmodara knows much about it.
