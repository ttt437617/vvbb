Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.149

e mādhuryāmrita pāna sadā yei kare

trishnā-śānti nahe, trishnā bādhe nirantare

SYNONYMS

e — this; mādhurya-amrita — nectar of sweetness; pāna — drinks; sadā — always; yei — that person who; kare — does; trishnā-śānti — satisfaction of thirst; nahe — there is not; trishnā — thirst; bādhe — increases; nirantare — constantly.

TRANSLATION

The thirst of one who always drinks the nectar of that sweetness is never satisfied. Rather, that thirst increases constantly.
