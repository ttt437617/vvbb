Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.223

sei rasa āsvādite kaila avatāra

ānusańge kaila saba rasera pracāra

SYNONYMS

sei — that; rasa — mellow; āsvādite — to taste; kaila — made; avatāra — incarnation; ānusańge — as a secondary motive; kaila — did; saba — all; rasera — of mellows; pracāra — broadcasting.

TRANSLATION

He made His appearance to taste that conjugal mellow and incidentally to broadcast all the rasas.
