Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.124

rādhikāra prema — guru, āmi — śishya nata

sadā āmā nānā nritye nācāya udbhata

SYNONYMS

rādhikāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; prema — the love; guru — teacher; āmi — I; śishya — disciple; nata — dancer; sadā — always; āmā — Me; nānā — various; nritye — in dances; nācāya — causes to dance; udbhata — novel.

TRANSLATION

"The love of Rādhikā is My teacher, and I am Her dancing pupil. Her prema makes Me dance various novel dances."
