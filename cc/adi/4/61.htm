Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.61

sac-cid-ānanda, pūrna, krishnera svarūpa

eka-i cic-chakti tāńra dhare tina rūpa

SYNONYMS

sat-cit-ānanda — eternity, knowledge and bliss; pūrna — full; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sva-rūpa — own form; eka-i — one; cit-śakti — spiritual energy; tāńra — of Him; dhare — manifests; tina — three; rūpa — forms.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna's body is eternal [sat], full of knowledge [cit] and full of bliss [ānanda]. His one spiritual energy manifests three forms.
