Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.109

rātre pralāpa kare svarūpera kantha dhari'

āveśe āpana bhāva kahaye ughādi'

SYNONYMS

rātre — at night; pralāpa — delirium; kare — does; svarūpera — of Svarūpa Dāmodara; kantha dhari' — embracing the neck; āveśe — in ecstasy; āpana — His own; bhāva — mood; kahaye — speaks; ughādi' — exuberantly.

TRANSLATION

At night He talked incoherently in grief with His arms around Svarūpa Dāmodara's neck. He spoke out His heart in ecstatic inspiration.
