Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.193

ei-mata paraspara pade hudāhudi

paraspara bādhe, keha mukha nāhi mudi

SYNONYMS

ei mata — like this; paraspara — reciprocal; pade — happens; hudāhudi — jostling; paraspara — mutually; bādhe — increases; keha — someone; mukha — face; nāhi — not; mudi — covering.

TRANSLATION

In this way a competition takes place between them in which no one acknowledges defeat.
