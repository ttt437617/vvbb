Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.43

nija nija bhāva sabe śreshtha kari' māne

nija-bhāve kare krishna-sukha āsvādane

SYNONYMS

nija nija — each his own; bhāva — mood; sabe — all; śreshtha kari' — making the best; māne — accepts; nija-bhāve — in his own mood; kare — does; krishna-sukha — happiness with Lord Krishna; āsvādane — tasting.

TRANSLATION

Each kind of devotee feels that his sentiment is the most excellent, and thus in that mood he tastes great happiness with Lord Krishna.
