Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.123

nā jāni rādhāra preme āche kata bala

ye bale āmāre kare sarvadā vihvala

SYNONYMS

nā jāni — I do not know; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; preme — in the love; āche — there is; kata — how much; bala — strength; ye — which; bale — strength; āmāre — Me; kare — makes; sarvadā — always; vihvala — overwhelmed.

TRANSLATION

"I do not know the strength of Rādhā's love, with which She always overwhelms Me.
