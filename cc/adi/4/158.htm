Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.158

krishnera mādhurye krishne upajaya lobha

samyak āsvādite nāre, mane rahe kshobha

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mādhurye — in the sweetness; krishne — in Lord Krishna; upajaya — arises; lobha — eager desire; samyak — fully; āsvādite — to taste; nāre — is not able; mane — in the mind; rahe — remains; kshobha — sorrow.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna's own beauty attracts Lord Krishna Himself. But because He cannot fully enjoy it, His mind remains full of sorrow.
