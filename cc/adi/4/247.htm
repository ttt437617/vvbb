Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.247

yadyapi āmāra sparśa kotīndu-śītala

rādhikāra sparśe āmā kare suśītala

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; āmāra — My; sparśa — touch; koti-indu — like millions upon millions of moons; śītala — cool; rādhikāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; sparśe — the touch; āmā — Me; kare — makes; su-śītala — very, very cool.

TRANSLATION

"And although My touch is cooler than ten million moons, I am refreshed by the touch of Śrīmatī Rādhikā.
