Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.237

ataeva bhakta-gane kari namaskāra

nihśańke kahiye, tāra hauk camatkāra

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; bhakta-gane — unto the devotees; kari — I offer; namaskāra — obeisances; nihśańke — without any doubt; kahiye — I say; tāra — of the devotees; hauk — let there be; camatkāra — astonishment.

TRANSLATION

Therefore after offering obeisances to the devotees, for their satisfaction I shall speak without hesitating.
