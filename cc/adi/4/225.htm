Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.225

śrī-krishna-caitanya gosāñi rasera sadana

aśesha-viśeshe kaila rasa āsvādana

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya gosāñi — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rasera — of mellow; sadana — the residence; aśesha-viśeshe — unlimited varieties of enjoyment; kaila — did; rasa — mellow; āsvādana — tasting.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya is the abode of rasa. He Himself tasted the sweetness of rasa in endless ways.
