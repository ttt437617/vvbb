Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.77

vaibhava-gana yena tāńra ańga-vibhūti

bimba-pratibimba-rūpa mahishīra tati

SYNONYMS

vaibhava-gana — the expansions; yena — as it were; tāńra — of Her; ańga — of the body; vibhūti — powerful expansions; bimba — reflections; pratibimba — counterreflections; rūpa — having the form; mahishīra — of the queens; tati — the expansion.

TRANSLATION

The goddesses of fortune are partial manifestations of Śrīmatī Rādhikā, and the queens are reflections of Her image.
