Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.248

ei mata jagatera sukhe āmi hetu

rādhikāra rūpa-guna āmāra jīvātu

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; jagatera — of the whole world; sukhe — in the matter of happiness; āmi — I am; hetu — the cause; rādhikāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; rūpa-guna — beauty and attributes; āmāra — My; jīvātu — life and soul.

TRANSLATION

"Thus although I am the source of happiness for the entire world, the beauty and attributes of Śrī Rādhikā are My life and soul.
