Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.141

āmāra mādhurya nāhi bādhite avakāśe

e-darpanera āge nava nava rūpe bhāse

SYNONYMS

āmāra — of Me; mādhurya — sweetness; nāhi — not; bādhite — to increase; avakāśe — opportunity; e-darpanera āge — in front of this mirror; nava nava — newer and newer; rūpe — in beauty; bhāse — shines.

TRANSLATION

"My sweetness also has no room for expansion, yet it shines before that mirror in newer and newer beauty.
