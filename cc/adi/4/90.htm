Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.90

'sarva-lakshmī'-śabda pūrve kariyāchi vyākhyāna

sarva-lakshmī-ganera tińho hana adhishthāna

SYNONYMS

sarva-lakshmī-śabda — the word sarva-lakshmī; pūrve — previously; kariyāchi — I have done; vyākhyāna — explanation; sarva-lakshmī-ganera — of all the goddesses of fortune; tińho — She; hana — is; adhishthāna — abode.

TRANSLATION

I have already explained the meaning of "sarva-lakshmī." Rādhā is the original source of all the goddesses of fortune.
