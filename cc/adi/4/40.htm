Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.40

sei dvāre ācandāle kīrtana sañcāre

nāma-prema-mālā gāńthi' parāila samsāre

SYNONYMS

sei dvāre — by that; ā-candāle — even among the candālas; kīrtana — the chanting of the holy names; sañcāre — He infuses; nāma — of the holy names; prema — and of love of God; mālā — a garland; gāńthi' — stringing together; parāila — He put it on; samsāre — the whole material world.

TRANSLATION

Thus He spread kīrtana even among the untouchables. He wove a wreath of the holy name and prema, with which He garlanded the entire material world.
