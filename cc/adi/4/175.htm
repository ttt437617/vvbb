Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.175

krishna lāgi' āra saba kare parityāga

krishna-sukha-hetu kare śuddha anurāga

SYNONYMS

krishna lāgi' — for Lord Krishna; āra — other; saba — all; kare — do; parityāga — give up; krishna-sukha-hetu — for the purpose of Lord Krishna's happiness; kare — do; śuddha — pure; anurāga — attachments.

TRANSLATION

They renounced everything for Krishna. They have pure attachment to giving Krishna pleasure.
