Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.58

ithi lāgi' āge kari tāra vivarana

yāhā haite haya gaurera mahimā-kathana

SYNONYMS

ithi lāgi' — for this; āge — first; kari — I shall do; tāra — of that; vivarana — description; yāhā haite — from which; haya — there is; gaurera — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahimā — the glory; kathana — relating.

TRANSLATION

Therefore I shall first delineate the position of Rādhā and Krishna. From that description the glory of Lord Caitanya will be known.
