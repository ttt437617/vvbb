Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.48

vraja-vadhū-ganera ei bhāva niravadhi

tāra madhye śrī-rādhāya bhāvera avadhi

SYNONYMS

vraja-vadhū-ganera — of the young wives of Vraja; ei — this; bhāva — mood; niravadhi — unbounded; tāra madhye — among them; śrī-rādhāya — in Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; bhāvera — of the mood; avadhi — the highest limit.

TRANSLATION

This mood is unbounded in the damsels of Vraja, but among them it finds its perfection in Śrī Rādhā.
