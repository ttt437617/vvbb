Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.234

e saba siddhānta haya āmrera pallava

bhakta-gana-kokilera sarvadā vallabha

SYNONYMS

e — these; saba — all; siddhānta — transcendental conclusions; haya — are; āmrera — of mango; pallava — twigs; bhakta-gana — the devotees; kokilera — to those who are just like cuckoo birds; sarvadā — always; vallabha — pleasing.

TRANSLATION

All these conclusions are like the newly grown twigs of a mango tree; they are always pleasing to the devotees, who in this way resemble cuckoo birds.
