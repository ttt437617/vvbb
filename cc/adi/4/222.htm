Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.222

śrī-krishna-caitanya gosāñi vrajendra-kumāra

rasa-maya-mūrti krishna sākshāt śrińgāra

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vrajendra-kumāra — the child of King Nanda; rasa-maya — consisting of mellows; mūrti — the form; krishna — Lord Krishna; sākshāt — directly; śrińgāra — amorous love.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya is Krishna [Vrajendra-kumāra], the embodiment of rasas. He is amorous love personified.
