Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.270

sei-kāle śrī-advaita karena ārādhana

tāńhāra huńkāre kaila krishne ākarshana

SYNONYMS

sei-kāle — at that time; śrī-advaita — Advaita Ācārya; karena — performs; ārādhana — worship; tāńhāra — of Him; huńkāre — by the tumultuous call; kaila — did; krishne — to Lord Krishna; ākarshana — attraction.

TRANSLATION

At that time Śrī Advaita was earnestly worshiping Him. Advaita attracted Him with His loud calls.
