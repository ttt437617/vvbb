Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.139

ei prema-dvāre nitya rādhikā ekali

āmāra mādhuryāmrita āsvāde sakali

SYNONYMS

ei — this; prema-dvāre — by means of the love; nitya — always; rādhikā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; ekali — only; āmāra — of Me; mādhurya-amrita — the nectar of the sweetness; āsvāde — tastes; sakali — all.

TRANSLATION

"Only Rādhikā, by the strength of Her love, tastes all the nectar of My sweetness.
