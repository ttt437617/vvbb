Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.53

bhāva-grahanera hetu kaila dharma-sthāpana

tāra mukhya hetu kahi, śuna sarva-jana

SYNONYMS

bhāva-grahanera — of accepting the mood; hetu — the reason; kaila — did; dharma — religion; sthāpana — establishing; tāra — of that; mukhya — principal; hetu — reason; kahi — I say; śuna — please hear; sarva-jana — everyone.

TRANSLATION

To accept ecstatic love is the main reason He appeared and reestablished the religious system for this age. I shall now explain that reason. Everyone please listen.
