Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.232

ataeva kahi kichu kariñā nigūdha

bujhibe rasika bhakta, nā bujhibe mūdha

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; kahi — I speak; kichu — something; kariñā — squeezing; nigūdha — essence; bujhibe — can understand; rasika — humorous; bhakta — devotees; nā — not; bujhibe — will understand; mūdha — rascals.

TRANSLATION

Therefore I shall mention them, revealing only their essence, so that loving devotees will understand them but fools will not.
