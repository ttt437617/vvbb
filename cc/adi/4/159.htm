Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.159

ei ta' dvitīya hetura kahila vivarana

tritīya hetura ebe śunaha lakshana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; ta' — certainly; dvitīya — second; hetura — of the reason; kahila — has been said; vivarana — description; tritīya — the third; hetura — of the reason; ebe — now; śunaha — please hear; lakshana — the characteristic.

TRANSLATION

This is a description of His second desire. Now please listen as I describe the third.
