Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.144

darpanādye dekhi' yadi āpana mādhurī

āsvādite haya lobha, āsvādite nāri

SYNONYMS

darpana-ādye — beginning in a mirror; dekhi' — seeing; yadi — if; āpana — own; mādhurī — sweetness; āsvādite — to taste; haya — there is; lobha — desire; āsvādite — to taste; nāri — I am not able.

TRANSLATION

"If I see My sweetness in a mirror, I am tempted to taste it, but nevertheless I cannot.
