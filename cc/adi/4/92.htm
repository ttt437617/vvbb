Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.92

sarva-saundarya-kānti vaisaye yāńhāte

sarva-lakshmī-ganera śobhā haya yāńhā haite

SYNONYMS

sarva-saundarya — of all beauty; kānti — the splendor; vaisaye — sits; yāńhāte — in whom; sarva-lakshmī-ganera — of all the goddesses of fortune; śobhā — the splendor; haya — is; yāńhā haite — from whom.

TRANSLATION

The word "sarva-kānti" indicates that all beauty and luster rest in Her body. All the lakshmīs derive their beauty from Her.
