Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.101

shashtha ślokera artha karite prakāśa

prathame kahiye sei ślokera ābhāsa

SYNONYMS

shashtha — sixth; ślokera — of the verse; artha — meaning; karite — to do; prakāśa — manifestation; prathame — first; kahiye — I shall speak; sei — that; ślokera — of the verse; ābhāsa — hint.

TRANSLATION

To explain the sixth verse, I shall first give a hint of its meaning.
