Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.268

rādhā-bhāva ańgīkari' dhari' tāra varna

tina-sukha āsvādite haba avatīrna

SYNONYMS

rādhā-bhāva — the moods of Rādhārānī; ańgīkari' — accepting; dhari' — taking; tāra varna — Her bodily complexion; tina — three; sukha — happiness; āsvādite — to taste; haba — I shall; avatīrna — descend as an incarnation.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore, assuming Rādhārānī's sentiments and bodily complexion, I shall descend to fulfill these three desires."
