Ādi-līlāChapter 4: The Confidential Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 4.78

lakshmī-gana tāńra vaibhava-vilāsāmśa-rūpa

mahishī-gana vaibhava-prakāśa-svarūpa

SYNONYMS

lakshmī-gana — the goddesses of fortune; tāńra — Her; vaibhava-vilāsa — as vaibhava-vilāsa; amśa — of plenary portions; rūpa — having the form; mahishī-gana — the queens; vaibhava-prakāśa — of vaibhava-prakāśa; svarūpa — having the nature.

TRANSLATION

The goddesses of fortune are Her plenary portions, and they display the forms of vaibhava-vilāsa. The queens are of the nature of Her vaibhava-prakāśa.
