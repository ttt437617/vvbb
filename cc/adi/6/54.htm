Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.54

ihāra pramāna śuna — śāstrera vyākhyāna

mahad-anubhava yāte sudridha pramāna

SYNONYMS

ihāra — of this; pramāna — evidence; śuna — please hear; śāstrera vyākhyāna — the description in the revealed scriptures; mahat-anubhava — the conception of great souls; yāte — by which; su-dridha — strong; pramāna — evidence.

TRANSLATION

For evidence, please listen to the examples described in the revealed scriptures, which are corroborated by the realization of great souls.
