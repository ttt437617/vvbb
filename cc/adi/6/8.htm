Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.8

ye purusha srishti-sthiti karena māyāya

ananta brahmānda srishti karena līlāya

SYNONYMS

ye purusha — that personality who; srishti-sthiti — creation and maintenance; karena — performs; māyāya — through the external energy; ananta brahmānda — unlimited universes; srishti — creation; karena — does; līlāya — by pastimes.

TRANSLATION

That purusha creates and maintains with His external energy. He creates innumerable universes in His pastimes.
