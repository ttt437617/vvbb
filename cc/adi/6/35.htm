Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.35

yāńra dvārā kaila prabhu kīrtana pracāra

yāńra dvārā kaila prabhu jagat nistāra

SYNONYMS

yāńra dvārā — by whom; kaila — did; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kīrtana pracāra — spreading of the sańkīrtana movement; yāńra dvārā — by whom; kaila — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jagat nistāra — deliverance of the entire world.

TRANSLATION

It is through Him [Advaita Ācārya] that Lord Caitanya spread the sańkīrtana movement and through Him that He delivered the world.
