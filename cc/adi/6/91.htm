Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.91

sańkarshana-avatāra kāranābdhi-śāyī

tāńhāra hridaye bhakta-bhāva anuyāyī

SYNONYMS

sańkarshana-avatāra — an incarnation of Lord Sańkarshana; kārana-abdhi-śāyī — Lord Vishnu lying on the Causal Ocean; tāńhāra — His; hridaye — in the heart; bhakta-bhāva — the emotion of being a devotee; anuyāyī — accordingly.

TRANSLATION

The Vishnu who lies on the Causal Ocean is an incarnation of Lord Sańkarshana, and, accordingly, the emotion of being a devotee is always present in His heart.
