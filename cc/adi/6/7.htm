Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.7

mahā-vishnu srishti karena jagad-ādi kārya

tāńra avatāra sākshāt advaita ācārya

SYNONYMS

mahā-vishnu — the original Vishnu; srishti — creation; karena — does; jagat-ādi — the material world; kārya — the occupation; tāńra — His; avatāra — incarnation; sākshāt — directly; advaita ācārya — Prabhu Advaita Ācārya.

TRANSLATION

Mahā-Vishnu performs all the functions for the creation of the universes. Śrī Advaita Ācārya is His direct incarnation.
