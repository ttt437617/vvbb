Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.102

na tathā me priya-tama

ātma-yonir na śańkarah

na ca sańkarshano na śrīr

naivātmā ca yathā bhavān

SYNONYMS

na tathā — not so much; me — My; priya-tamah — dearmost; ātma-yonih — Lord Brahmā; na śańkarah — nor Śańkara (Lord Śiva); na ca — nor; sańkarshanah — Lord Sańkarshana; na — nor; śrīh — the goddess of fortune; na — nor; eva — certainly; ātmā — My self; ca — and; yathā — as; bhavān — you.

TRANSLATION

"O Uddhava! Neither Brahmā, nor Śańkara, nor Sańkarshana, nor Lakshmī, nor even My own self is as dear to Me as you."

PURPORT

This text is from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (11.14.15).
