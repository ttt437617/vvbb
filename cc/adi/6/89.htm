Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.89

tāńra avatāra eka śrī-sańkarshana

bhakta bali' abhimāna kare sarva-kshana

SYNONYMS

tāńra avatāra — His incarnation; eka — one; śrī-sańkarshana — Lord Sańkarshana; bhakta bali' — as a devotee; abhimāna — conception; kare — does; sarva-kshana — always.

TRANSLATION

Lord Sańkarshana, who is one of His incarnations, always considers Himself a devotee.
