Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.37

ācārya gosāñi caitanyera mukhya ańga

āra eka ańga tāńra prabhu nityānanda

SYNONYMS

ācārya gosāñi — Advaita Ācārya; caitanyera — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mukhya — primary; ańga — part; āra — another; eka — one; ańga — part; tāńra — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Ācārya is a principal limb of Lord Caitanya. Another limb of the Lord is Nityānanda Prabhu.
