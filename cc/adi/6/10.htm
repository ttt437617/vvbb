Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.10

se purushera amśa — advaita, nāhi kichu bheda

śarīra-viśesha tāńra — nāhika viccheda

SYNONYMS

se — that; purushera — of the Lord; amśa — part; advaita — Advaita Ācārya; nāhi — not; kichu — any; bheda — difference; śarīra-viśesha — another specific transcendental body; tāńra — of Him; nāhika viccheda — there is no separation.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Ācārya is a plenary part of that purusha and so is not different from Him. Indeed, Śrī Advaita Ācārya is not separate but is another form of that purusha.
