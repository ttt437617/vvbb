Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.81

krishna-preme unmatta, vihvala digambara

krishna-guna-līlā gāya, nāce nirantara

SYNONYMS

krishna-preme — in ecstatic love of Krishna; unmatta — almost mad; vihvala — overwhelmed; digambara — without any dress; krishna — of Lord Krishna; guna — attributes; līlā — pastimes; gāya — chants; nāce — dances; nirantara — constantly.

TRANSLATION

Intoxicated by ecstatic love for Lord Krishna, he becomes overwhelmed and incessantly dances without clothing and sings about Lord Krishna's qualities and pastimes.
