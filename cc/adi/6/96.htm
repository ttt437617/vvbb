Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.96

ei saba haya śrī-krishnera avatāra

nirantara dekhi sabāra bhaktira ācāra

SYNONYMS

ei saba — all of them; haya — are; śrī-krishnera avatāra — incarnations of Lord Krishna; nirantara — constantly; dekhi — I see; sabāra — of all; bhaktira ācāra — behavior as devotees.

TRANSLATION

These are all incarnations of Lord Krishna, yet we always find that they act as devotees.
