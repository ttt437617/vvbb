Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.95

prithivī dharena yei śesha-sańkarshana

kāya-vyūha kari' karena krishnera sevana

SYNONYMS

prithivī — planets; dharena — holds; yei — that one who; śesha-sańkarshana — Lord Śesha Sańkarshana; kāya-vyūha kari' — expanding Himself in different bodies; karena — does; krishnera sevana — service to Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Śesha Sańkarshana, who holds all the planets on His heads, expands Himself in different bodies to render service to Lord Krishna.
