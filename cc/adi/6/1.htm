Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.1

vande tam śrīmad-advaitā-

cāryam adbhuta-ceshtitam

yasya prasādād ajño 'pi

tat-svarūpam nirūpayet

SYNONYMS

vande — I offer my respectful obeisances; tam — unto Him; śrīmat — with all opulences; advaita-ācāryam — Śrī Advaita Ācārya; adbhuta-ceshtitam — whose activities are wonderful; yasya — of whom; prasādāt — by the mercy; ajñah api — even a foolish person; tat-svarūpam — His characteristics; nirūpayet — may describe.

TRANSLATION

I offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Advaita Ācārya, whose activities are all wonderful. By His mercy, even a foolish person can describe His characteristics.
