Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.44

krishna-dāsa-abhimāne ye ānanda-sindhu

kotī-brahma-sukha nahe tāra eka bindu

SYNONYMS

krishna-dāsa-abhimāne — under this impression of being a servant of Krishna; ye — that; ānanda-sindhu — ocean of transcendental bliss; kotī-brahma-sukha — ten million times the transcendental bliss of becoming one with the Absolute; nahe — not; tāra — of the ocean of transcendental bliss; eka — one; bindu — drop.

TRANSLATION

The conception of servitude to Śrī Krishna generates such an ocean of joy in the soul that even the joy of oneness with the Absolute, if multiplied ten million times, could not compare to a drop of it.
