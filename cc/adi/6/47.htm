Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.47

dāsya-bhāve ānandita pārishada-gana

vidhi, bhava, nārada āra śuka, sanātana

SYNONYMS

dāsya-bhāve — in the conception of being a servant; ānandita — very pleased; pārishada-gana — all the associates; vidhi — Lord Brahmā; bhava — Lord Śiva; nārada — the great sage Nārada; āra — and; śuka — Śukadeva Gosvāmī; sanātana — and Sanātana Kumāra.

TRANSLATION

All the associates of Lord Krishna, such as Brahmā, Śiva, Nārada, Śuka and Sanātana Kumāra, are very pleased in the sentiment of servitude.
