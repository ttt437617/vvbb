Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.80

teńho karena krishnera dāsya-pratyāśa

nirantara kahe śiva, 'muñi krishna-dāsa'

SYNONYMS

teńho — he; karena — does; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; dāsya-pratyāśa — expectation of being a servant; nirantara — constantly; kahe — says; śiva — Lord Śiva; muñi — I; krishna-dāsa — a servant of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

He also desires only to be a servant of Lord Krishna. Śrī Sadāśiva always says, "I am a servant of Lord Krishna."
