Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.90

tāńra avatāra āna śrī-yuta lakshmana

śrī-rāmera dāsya tińho kaila anukshana

SYNONYMS

tāńra avatāra — His incarnation; āna — another; śrī-yuta — with all beauty and opulence; lakshmana — Lord Lakshmana; śrī-rāmera — of Rāmacandra; dāsya — servitude; tińho — He; kaila — did; anukshana — always.

TRANSLATION

Another of His incarnations, Lakshmana, who is very beautiful and opulent, always serves Lord Rāma.
