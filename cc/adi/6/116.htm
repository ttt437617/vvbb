Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.116

ācārya-carane mora koti namaskāra

ithe kichu aparādha nā labe āmāra

SYNONYMS

ācārya-carane — at the lotus feet of Advaita Ācārya; mora — my; koti namaskāra — offering obeisances ten million times; ithe — in this connection; kichu — some; aparādha — offense; nā labe — please do not take; āmāra — my.

TRANSLATION

I offer my obeisances ten million times to the lotus feet of Śrī Advaita Ācārya. Please do not take offense at this.
