Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.107

anyera āchuk kārya, āpane śrī-krishna

āpana-mādhurya-pāne ha-ilā satrishna

SYNONYMS

anyera — of others; āchuk — let be; kārya — the business; āpane — personally; śrī-krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; āpana-mādhurya — personal sweetness; pāne — in drinking; ha-ilā — became; sa-trishna — very eager.

TRANSLATION

What to speak of others, even Lord Krishna Himself becomes thirsty to taste His own sweetness.
