Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.78

sahasra-vadane yeńho śesha-sańkarshana

daśa deha dhari' kare krishnera sevana

SYNONYMS

sahasra-vadane — with thousands of mouths; yeńho — one who; śesha-sańkarshana — Lord Śesha, the incarnation of Sańkarshana; daśa — ten; deha — bodies; dhari' — accepting; kare — does; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sevana — service.

TRANSLATION

He who is Śesha, Sańkarshana, with His thousands of mouths, serves Śrī Krishna by assuming ten forms.
