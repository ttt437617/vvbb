Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.82

pitā-mātā-guru-sakhā-bhāva kene naya

krishna-premera svabhāve dāsya-bhāva se karaya

SYNONYMS

pitā — father; mātā — mother; guru — superior teacher; sakhā — friend; bhāva — the emotion; kene naya — let it be; krishna-premera — of love of Krishna; svabhāve — in a natural inclination; dāsya bhāva — the emotion of becoming a servant; se — that; karaya — does.

TRANSLATION

All the emotions, whether those of father, mother, teacher or friend, are full of sentiments of servitude. That is the nature of love of Krishna.
