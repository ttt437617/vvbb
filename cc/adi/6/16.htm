Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.16

āpane purusha — viśvera 'nimitta'-kārana

advaita-rūpe 'upādāna' hana nārāyana

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; purusha — Lord Vishnu; viśvera — of the entire material world; nimitta kārana — the original cause; advaita-rūpe — in the form of Advaita; upādāna — the material cause; hana — becomes; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

Lord Vishnu Himself is the efficient [nimitta] cause of the material world, and Nārāyana in the form of Śrī Advaita is the material cause [upādāna].
