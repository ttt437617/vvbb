Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.84

sei krishna avatīrna — caitanya-īśvara

ataeva āra saba, — tāńhāra kińkara

SYNONYMS

sei — that; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatīrna — descended; caitanya-īśvara — Lord Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ataeva — therefore; āra — others; saba — all; tāńhāra kińkara — His servants.

TRANSLATION

That same Lord Krishna has descended as Lord Caitanya, the Supreme Personality of Godhead. Everyone, therefore, is His servant.
