Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.57

teńho rati-mati māge krishnera carane

tāhāra śrī-mukha-vānī tāhāte pramāne

SYNONYMS

teńho — he also; rati-mati — affection and attraction; māge — begs; krishnera carane — unto the lotus feet of Krishna; tāhāra — his; śrī-mukha-vānī — words from his mouth; tāhāte — in that; pramāne — evidence.

TRANSLATION

He too prays for attachment and devotion to the lotus feet of Lord Krishna, as the words from his own mouth give evidence.
