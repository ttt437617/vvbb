Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.60

manaso vrittayo nah syuh

krishna-pādāmbujāśrayāh

vāco 'bhidhāyinīr nāmnām

kāyas tat-prahvanādishu

SYNONYMS

manasah — of the mind; vrittayah — activities (thinking, feeling and willing); nah — of us; syuh — let there be; krishna — of Lord Krishna; pāda-ambuja — the lotus feet; āśrayāh — those sheltered by; vācah — the words; abhidhāyinīh — speaking; nāmnām — of His holy names; kāyah — the body; tat — to Him; prahvana-ādishu — bowing down to Him, etc.

TRANSLATION

"May our minds be attached to the lotus feet of your Lord Krishna, may our tongues chant His holy names, and may our bodies lie prostrate before Him.
