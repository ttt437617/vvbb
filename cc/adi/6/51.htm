Ādi-līlāChapter 6: The Glories of Śrī Advaita Ācārya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 6.51

ei mata gāya, nāce, kare attahāsa

loke upadeśe, — 'hao caitanyera dāsa'

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; gāya — chant; nāce — dance; kare — do; atta-hāsa — laughing like madmen; loke — unto the people in general; upadeśe — instruct; hao — just become; caitanyera dāsa — servants of Śrī Caitanya.

TRANSLATION

Thus they dance, sing and laugh like madmen, and they instruct everyone, "Just be loving servants of Lord Caitanya."
