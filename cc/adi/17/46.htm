Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.46

sarvāńga bedila kīte, kāte nirantara

asahya vedanā, duhkhe jvalaye antara

SYNONYMS

sarva-ańga — all over the body; bedila — became covered; kīte — by insects; kāte — biting; nirantara — always; asahya — unbearable; vedanā — pain; duhkhe — in unhappiness; jvalaye — burns; antara — without cessation.

TRANSLATION

Incessantly covered with germs and insects biting him all over his body, Gopāla Cāpāla felt unbearable pain. His entire body burned in distress.
