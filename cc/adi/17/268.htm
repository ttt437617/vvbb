Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.268

ei dridha yukti kari' prabhu āche ghare

keśava bhāratī āilā nadīyā-nagare

SYNONYMS

ei — this; dridha — firm; yukti — consideration; kari' — making; prabhu — the Lord; āche — was; ghare — in His home; keśava bhāratī — Keśava Bhāratī; āilā — came; nadīyā-nagare — to the town of Nadia.

TRANSLATION

After coming to this firm conclusion, the Lord continued to stay at home. In the meantime Keśava Bhāratī came to the town of Nadia.
