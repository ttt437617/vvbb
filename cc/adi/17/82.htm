Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.82

śata dui phala prabhu śīghra pādāila

prakshālana kari' krishne bhoga lāgāila

SYNONYMS

śata — hundred; dui — two; phala — fruits; prabhu — the Lord; śīghra — very soon; pādāila — caused to be picked up; prakshālana — washing; kari' — doing; krishne — to Lord Krishna; bhoga — offering; lāgāila — made it so.

TRANSLATION

The Lord immediately picked about two hundred fruits, and after washing them He offered them to Krishna to eat.
