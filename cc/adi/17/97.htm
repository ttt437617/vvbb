Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.97

aparādha nāhi, kaile lokera nistāra

ye tomā' dekhila, tāra chutila samsāra

SYNONYMS

aparādha — offense; nāhi — did not; kaile — committed; lokera — of the people; nistāra — liberation; ye — anyone who; tomā' — You; dekhila — saw; tāra — his; chutila — became free; samsāra — material bondage.

TRANSLATION

"There was no offense in Your appearing as Nrisimhadeva. Rather, any man who saw You in that mood was immediately liberated from the bondage of material existence."
