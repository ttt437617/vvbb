Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.39

kalāra pāta upare thuila oda-phula

haridrā, sindūra āra rakta-candana, tandula

SYNONYMS

kalāra pāta — a banana leaf; upare — upon it; thuila — placed; oda-phula — a particular type of flower; haridrā — turmeric; sindūra — vermilion; āra — and; rakta-candana — red sandalwood; tandula — rice.

TRANSLATION

On the upper portion of a plantain leaf he placed such paraphernalia for worship as oda-phula, turmeric, vermilion, red sandalwood and rice.
