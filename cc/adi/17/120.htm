Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.120

ei-mata nritya ha-ila cāri prahara

sandhyāya gańgā-snāna kari' sabe gelā ghara

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; nritya — dancing; ha-ila — was performed; cāri — four; prahara — a period of time lasting three hours; sandhyāya — in the evening; gańgā-snāna — taking bath in the Ganges; kari' — finishing; sabe — all; gelā — returned; ghara — home.

TRANSLATION

In this way they danced continuously for twelve hours, and in the evening they all took a bath in the Ganges and then returned to their homes.
