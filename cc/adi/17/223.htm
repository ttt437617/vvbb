Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.223

śuni' prabhu 'hari' bali' uthilā āpani

uthila vaishnava saba kari' hari-dhvani

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; prabhu — the Lord; hari — the holy name of the Lord; bali' — chanting; uthilā — got up; āpani — personally; uthila — got up; vaishnava — other devotees; saba — all; kari' — making; hari-dhvani — vibration of the holy name, "Hari Hari."

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, the Lord got up, chanting "Hari! Hari!" Following Him, all the other Vaishnavas also got up, chanting the vibration of the holy name.
