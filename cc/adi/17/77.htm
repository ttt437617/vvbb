Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.77

murārike kahe tumi krishna vaśa kailā

śuniyā murāri śloka kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

murārike — unto Murāri; kahe — says; tumi — you; krishna — Lord Krishna; vaśa — satisfied; kaila — made; śuniyā — hearing; murāri — Murāri; śloka — verse; kahite — to speak; lāgila — began.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya then praised Murāri Gupta, saying, "You have satisfied Lord Krishna." Hearing this, Murāri Gupta quoted a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
