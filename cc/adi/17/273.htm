Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.273

sańge nityānanda, candraśekhara ācārya

mukunda-datta, — ei tina kaila sarva kārya

SYNONYMS

sańge — in His company; nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; candraśekhara ācārya — Candraśekhara Ācārya; mukunda-datta — Mukunda Datta; ei tina — these three; kaila — performed; sarva — all; kārya — necessary activities.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted sannyāsa, three personalities were with Him to perform all the necessary activities. They were Nityānanda Prabhu, Candraśekhara Ācārya and Mukunda Datta.
