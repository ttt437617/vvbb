Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.87

ei saba līlā kare śacīra nandana

anya loka nāhi jāne vinā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

ei saba — all these; līlā — pastimes; kare — performed; śacīra — of mother Śacī; nandana — son; anya loka — other people; nāhi — do not; jāne — know; vinā — except; bhakta-gana — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

These are confidential pastimes of the son of Śacī. Other than devotees, no one knows of this incident.

PURPORT

Nondevotees cannot believe this incident, yet the place where the tree grew still exists in Māyāpur. It is called Āmra-ghatta or Āma-ghātā.
