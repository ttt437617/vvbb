Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.318

pañcame 'śrī-nityānanda'-tattva nirūpana

nityānanda hailā rāma rohinī-nandana

SYNONYMS

pañcame — in the Fifth Chapter; śrī-nityānanda — of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; tattva — the truth; nirūpana — description; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; hailā — was; rāma — Balarāma; rohinī-nandana — the son of Rohinī.

TRANSLATION

The Fifth Chapter describes the truth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, who is none other than Balarāma, the son of Rohinī.
