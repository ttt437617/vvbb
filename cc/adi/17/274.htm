Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.274

ei ādi-līlāra kaila sūtra ganana

vistāri varnilā ihā dāsa vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; ādi-līlāra — of the ādi-līlā (the first portion of Lord Caitanya's pastimes); kaila — made; sūtra — synopsis; ganana — enumeration; vistāri — elaborately; varnilā — described; ihā — this; dāsa vrindāvana — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have summarized the incidents of the ādi-līlā. Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has described them elaborately [in his Caitanya-bhāgavata].
