Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.93

nrisīmha-āveśa dekhi' mahā-tejomaya

patha chādi' bhāge loka pāñā bada bhaya

SYNONYMS

nrisimha-āveśa — the ecstasy of Lord Nrisimhadeva; dekhi' — seeing; mahā-tejo-maya — very fierce; patha chādi' — giving up the road; bhāge — run away; loka — all people; pāñā — getting; bada — very much; bhaya — afraid.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Him appearing very fierce in the ecstasy of Lord Nrisimha, people ran from the street and fled here and there, afraid of His anger.
