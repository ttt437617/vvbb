Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.214

tabe āmi prīti-vākya kahila sabāre

sabe ghare yāha, āmi nishedhiba tāre

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; āmi — I; prīti-vākya — sweet words; kahila — said; sabāre — unto all of them; sabe — all of you; ghare — back home; yāha — go; āmi — I; nishedhiba — shall prohibit; tāre — Him (Nimāi Pandita).

TRANSLATION

"After hearing their complaints, in sweet words I told them, 'Please go back home. I shall certainly prohibit Nimāi Pandita from continuing His Hare Krishna movement.'
