Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.252

prabhure śānta kari' ānila nija ghare

paduyā palāyā gela paduyā-sabhāre

SYNONYMS

prabhure — the Lord; śānta kari' — pacifying; ānila — brought; nija — His own; ghare — to the house; paduyā — the student; palāyā — running away; gela — went; paduyā — of students; sabhāre — to the assembly.

TRANSLATION

The devotees pacified the Lord and brought Him home, and the student ran away to an assembly of other students.
