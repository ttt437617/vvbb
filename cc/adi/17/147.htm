Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.147

ebe tumi śāntā haile, āsi, mililāń

bhāgya mora, — tomā hena atithi pāilāń

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; tumi — You; śānta — pacified; haile — have become; āsi' — coming; mililāń — I have met (You); bhāgya mora — it is my great fortune; toma — You; hena — like; atithi — guest; pāilāń — I have received.

TRANSLATION

"Now that You have become pacified, I have come to You. It is my good fortune to receive a guest like Your Honor.
