Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.132

tā-sabhāra antare bhaya prabhu mane jāni

kahite lāgilā loke śīghra dāki' āni'

SYNONYMS

tā-sabhāra — of all of them; antare — in the mind; bhaya — fear; prabhu — the Lord; mane — in the mind; jāni — understanding; kahite — to speak; lāgilā — began; loke — to the people; śīghra — very soon; dāki' — calling; āni' — bringing them.

TRANSLATION

Understanding the anxiety within the people's minds, the Lord called them together and spoke to them as follows.
