Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.208

nā jāni, — ki khāñā matta hañā nāce, gāya

hāse, kānde, pade, uthe, gadāgadi yāya

SYNONYMS

nā jāni — we do not know; ki — what; khāñā — eating; matta — mad; hañā — becoming; nāce — He dances; gāya — chants; hāse — laughs; kānde — cries; pade — falls down; uthe — gets up; gadāgadi yāya — goes rolling on the ground.

TRANSLATION

"'We do not know what He eats that makes Him become mad, dancing, singing, sometimes laughing, crying, falling down, jumping up and rolling on the ground.
