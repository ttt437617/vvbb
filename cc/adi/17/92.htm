Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.92

nrisimha-āveśe prabhu hāte gadā lañā

pāshandī mārite yāya nagare dhāiyā

SYNONYMS

nrisimha-āveśe — in the ecstatic mood of Lord Nrisimha; prabhu — the Lord; hāte — in His hand; gadā — club; lañā — taking; pāshandī — the atheists; mārite — to kill; yāya — goes; nagare — in the city; dhāiyā — running.

TRANSLATION

In the mood of Lord Nrisimhadeva, Lord Caitanya ran through the city streets, club in hand, ready to kill all the atheists.
