Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.319

shashtha paricchede 'advaita-tattve'ra vicāra

advaita-ācārya — mahā-vishnu-avatāra

SYNONYMS

shashtha paricchede — in the Sixth Chapter; advaita — of Advaita Ācārya; tattvera — of the truth; vicāra — consideration; advaita-ācārya — Advaita Prabhu; mahā-vishnu-avatāra — incarnation of Mahā-Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

The Sixth Chapter considers the truth of Advaita Ācārya. He is an incarnation of Mahā-Vishnu.
