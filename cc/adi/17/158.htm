Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.158

tomāra vedete āche go-vadhera vānī

ataeva go-vadha kare bada bada muni

SYNONYMS

tomāra vedete — in Your Vedic literatures; āche — there is; go-vadhera — for cow-killing; vānī — injunction; ataeva — therefore; go-vadha — cow-killing; kare — does; bada bada — very, very great; muni — sages.

TRANSLATION

As a learned scholar, the Kazi challenged Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "In Your Vedic scriptures there is an injunction for killing a cow. On the strength of this injunction, great sages performed sacrifices involving cow-killing."
