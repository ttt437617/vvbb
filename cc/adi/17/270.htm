Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.270

tumi ta' īśvara bata, — sākshāt nārāyana

kripā kari' kara mora samsāra mocana

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; ta' — certainly; īśvara — the Lord; bata — are; sākshāt — directly; nārāyana — the Supreme Lord, Nārāyana; kripā kari' — showing mercy; kara — please do; mora — My; samsāra — material life; mocana — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, you are directly Nārāyana. Therefore please be merciful unto Me. Deliver Me from this material bondage."
