Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.209

nagariyāke pāgala kaila sadā sańkīrtana

rātre nidrā nāhi yāi, kari jāgarana

SYNONYMS

nagariyāke — all the citizens; pāgala — mad; kaila — He has made; sadā — always; sańkīrtana — congregational chanting; rātre — at night; nidrā — sleep; nāhi yāi — we do not get; kari — observe; jāgarana — wakefulness.

TRANSLATION

"'He has made all the people practically mad by always performing congregational chanting. At night we cannot get any sleep; we are always kept awake.
