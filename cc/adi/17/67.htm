Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.67

bhańgī kari' jñāna-mārga karila vyākhyāna

krodhāveśe prabhu tāre kaila avajñāna

SYNONYMS

bhańgī kari' — doing it in fun; jñāna-mārga — the path of philosophical speculation; karila — did; vyākhyāna — explanation; krodha-āveśe — in the mood of anger; prabhu — the Lord; tāre — to Him; kaila — did; avajñāna — disrespect.

TRANSLATION

Thus He whimsically began to explain the path of philosophical speculation, and the Lord, in His anger, seemingly disrespected Him.
