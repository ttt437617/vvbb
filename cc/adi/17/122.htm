Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.122

'haraye namah, krishna yādavāya namah

gopāla govinda rāma śrī-madhusūdana'

SYNONYMS

haraye namah — I offer my respectful obeisances to Lord Hari; krishna — O Krishna; yādavāya — unto the descendant of the Yadu dynasty; namah — all obeisances; gopāla — Gopāla; govinda — Govinda; rāma — Rāma; śrī-madhusūdana — Śrī Madhusūdana.

TRANSLATION

[All the devotees sang this popular song along with the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.] "Haraye namah, krishna yādavāya namah/ gopāla govinda rāma śrī-madhusūdana."
