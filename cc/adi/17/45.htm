Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.45

tina dina rahi' sei gopāla-cāpāla

sarvāńge ha-ila kushtha, vahe rakta-dhāra

SYNONYMS

tina dina — three days; rahi' — remaining in that way; sei — that; gopāla-cāpāla — Gopāla Cāpāla; sarva-ańge — all over the body; ha-ila — became visible; kushtha — leprosy; vahe — discharging; rakta-dhāra — a flow of blood.

TRANSLATION

After three days, leprosy attacked Gopāla Cāpāla, and blood oozed from sores all over his body.
