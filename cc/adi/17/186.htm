Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.186

eta kahi' simha gela, āmāra haila bhaya

ei dekha, nakha-cihna amora hridaya

SYNONYMS

eta — thus; kahi' — saying; simha — the lion; gela — returned; āmāra — my; haila — there was; bhaya — fear; ei dekha — just see this; nakha-cihna — the nail marks; amora hridaya — on my heart.

TRANSLATION

"After saying this, the lion left, but I was very much afraid of Him. Just see the marks of His nails on my heart!"
