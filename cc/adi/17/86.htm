Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.86

ei-mata pratidina phale bāra māsa

vaishnava khāyena phala, — prabhura ullāsa

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; prati-dina — every day; phale — fruit grew; bāra — twelve; māsa — months; vaishnava — the Vaishnavas; khāyena — eat; phala — the fruits; prabhura — the Lord's; ullāsa — satisfaction.

TRANSLATION

In this way, fruits grew on the tree every day throughout the twelve months of the year, and the Vaishnavas used to eat them, to the Lord's great satisfaction.
