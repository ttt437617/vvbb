Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.216

eta śuni' mahāprabhu hāsiyā hāsiyā

kahite lāgilā kichu kājire chuńiyā

SYNONYMS

eta — this; śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hāsiyā hāsiya — smiling; kahite — to speak; lāgilā — began; kichu — something; kājire — unto the Kazi; chuńiyā — touching.

TRANSLATION

After hearing the Kazi speak so nicely, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu touched him and smilingly spoke as follows.
