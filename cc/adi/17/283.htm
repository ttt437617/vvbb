Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.283

nibhrita-nikuñje vasi' dekhe rādhāra bāta

anveshite āilā tāhāń gopikāra thāta

SYNONYMS

nibhrita — solitary; nikuñje — in a bush; vasi' — sitting; dekhe — waiting to see; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; bāta — the passing; anveshite — while searching; āilā — came; tāhāń — there; gopikāra — of the gopīs; thāta — the phalanx.

TRANSLATION

Krishna was sitting in a solitary bush, waiting for Śrīmatī Rādhārānī to pass by. But while He was searching, the gopīs arrived there, like a phalanx of soldiers.
