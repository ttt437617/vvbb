Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.22

kali-kāle nāma-rūpe krishna-avatāra

nāma haite haya sarva-jagat-nistāra

SYNONYMS

kali-kāle — in this Age of Kali; nāma-rūpe — in the form of the holy name; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatāra — incarnation; nāma — holy name; haite — from; haya — becomes; sarva — all; jagat — of the world; nistāra — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"In this Age of Kali, the holy name of the Lord, the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra, is the incarnation of Lord Krishna. Simply by chanting the holy name, one associates with the Lord directly. Anyone who does this is certainly delivered.
