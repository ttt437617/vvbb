Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.280

ihā chādi' krishna yadi haya anyākāra

gopikāra bhāva nāhi yāya nikata tāhāra

SYNONYMS

ihā — this; chādi' — giving up; krishna — Krishna; yadi — if; haya — takes; anya-ākāra — another form; gopikāra — of the gopīs; bhāva — the ecstasy; nāhi — does not; yāya — arise; nikata — near; tāhāra — that (form).

TRANSLATION

If Lord Krishna gives up this original form and assumes another Vishnu form, nearness to Him cannot invoke the ecstatic mood of the gopīs.
