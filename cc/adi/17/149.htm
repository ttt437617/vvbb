Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.149

nīlāmbara cakravartī haya tomāra nānā

se-sambandhe hao tumi āmāra bhāginā

SYNONYMS

nīlāmbara cakravartī — Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; haya — becomes; tomāra — Your; nānā — maternal grandfather; se-sambandhe — by such a relationship; hao — become; tumi — You; āmāra — my; bhāginā — nephew (the son of my sister).

TRANSLATION

"Nīlāmbara Cakravartī is Your maternal grandfather, and by this relationship You are thus my nephew.
