Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.221

prabhu kahe, — eka dāna māgiye tomāya

sańkīrtana vāda yaiche nahe nadīyāya

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord said; eka — one; dāna — charity; māgiye — I beg; tomāya — from you; sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; vāda — opposition; yaiche — as it may be; nahe — not be; nadīyāya — in the district of Nadia.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "I wish to beg you for one favor in charity. You must pledge that this sańkīrtana movement will not be checked, at least in the district of Nadia."
