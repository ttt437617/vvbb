Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.203

eta śuni' tā'-sabhāre ghare pāthāila

hena-kāle pāshandī hindu pāńca-sāta āila

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — after hearing all this; tā'-sabhāre — all of them; ghare — back home; pāthāila — sent; hena-kāle — at that time; pāsandī — nonbeliever; hindu — Hindus; pāńca-sāta — five or seven; āila — came.

TRANSLATION

"After hearing all this, I sent all the mlecchas back to their homes. Five or seven nonbelieving Hindus then approached me.

PURPORT

The word pāshandī refers to nonbelievers engaged in fruitive activities and to idolatrous worshipers of many demigods. Pāshandīs do not believe in one God, the Supreme Personality, Lord Vishnu; they think that all the demigods have the same potency as He. The definition of a pāshandī is given in the tantra-śāstra:

yas tu nārāyanam devam brahma-rudrādi-daivataih

samatvenaiva vīksheta sa pāshandī bhaved dhruvam

"A pāshandī is one who considers the great demigods such as Lord Brahmā and Lord Śiva equal to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana." (Hari-bhakti-vilāsa, 1.17)

The Supreme Personality of Godhead is asamaurdhva; in other words, no one can be equal to or greater than Him. But pāshandīs do not believe this. They worship any kind of demigod, thinking it all right to accept whomever they please as the Supreme Lord. The pāshandīs were against the Hare Krishna movement of Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and now we see practically that they also do not like our humble attempts to spread Krishna consciousness all over the world. On the contrary, these pāshandīs say that we are spoiling the Hindu religion because people all over the world are accepting Lord Krishna as the Supreme Personality of Godhead according to the version of Bhagavad-gītā As It Is. The pāshandīs condemn this movement, and sometimes they accuse Vaishnavas from foreign countries of being not bona fide. Even so-called Vaishnavas — pseudo followers of the Vaishnava cult — do not agree with our activities in making Vaishnavas in the Western countries. Such pāshandīs existed even during the time of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and they continue to exist. Despite all the activities of these pāshandīs, however, the prediction of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu will triumph: prithivīte āche yata nagarādi grāma/ sarvatra pracāra haibe mora nāma. "In every town and village, the chanting of My name will be heard." No one can check the spread of the Krishna consciousness movement because upon this movement is the benediction of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
