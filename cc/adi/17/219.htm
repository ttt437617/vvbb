Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.219

eta śuni' kājīra dui cakshe pade pāni

prabhura carana chuńi' bale priya-vānī

SYNONYMS

eta — this; śuni' — hearing; kājīra — of the Kazi; dui — two; cakshe — in the eyes; pade — flow down; pāni — tears; prabhura — of the Lord; carana — lotus feet; chuńi' — touching; bale — says; priya-vānī — pleasing words.

TRANSLATION

After the Kazi heard this, tears flowed down from his eyes. He immediately touched the lotus feet of the Lord and spoke the following sweet words.
