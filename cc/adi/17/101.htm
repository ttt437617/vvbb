Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.101

āra dina eka bhikshuka āilā māgite

prabhura nritya dekhi nritya lāgila karite

SYNONYMS

āra — another; dina — day; eka — one; bhikshuka — beggar; āilā — came; māgite — to beg; prabhura — of the Lord; nritya — dancing; dekhi — seeing; nritya — dancing; lāgila — began; karite — to perform.

TRANSLATION

On another day a mendicant came to beg alms from the Lord's house, but when he saw the Lord dancing, he also began to dance.
