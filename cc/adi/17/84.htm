Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.84

dekhiyā santushta hailā śacīra nandana

sabāke khāoyāla āge kariyā bhakshana

SYNONYMS

dekhiyā — seeing this; santushta — satisfied; hailā — became; śacīra — of mother Śacī; nandana — son; sabāke — everyone; khāoyāla — made to eat; āge — in the beginning; kariyā — doing; bhakshana — eating Himself.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the quality of the mangoes, the Lord was greatly satisfied, and thus after eating first, He fed all the other devotees.
