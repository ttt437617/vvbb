Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.322

navamete 'bhakti-kalpa-vrikshera varnana'

śrī-caitanya-mālī kailā vriksha āropana

SYNONYMS

navamete — in the Ninth Chapter; bhakti-kalpa-vrikshera — of the desire tree of devotional service; varnana — the description; śrī-caitanya-mālī — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as the gardener; kailā — did; vriksha — the tree; āropana — implantation.

TRANSLATION

The Ninth Chapter describes the desire tree of devotional service. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Himself is the gardener who planted it.
