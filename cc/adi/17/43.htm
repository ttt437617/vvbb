Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.43

tabe saba śishta-loka kare hāhākāra

aiche karma hethā kaila kon durācāra

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; saba — all; śishta-loka — gentlemen; kare — exclaimed; hāhā-kāra — alas, alas; aiche — such; karma — activities; hethā — here; kaila — did; kon — who; durācāra — sinful person.

TRANSLATION

Then all the assembled gentlemen exclaimed, "What is this? What is this? Who has performed such mischievous activities? Who is that sinful man?"
