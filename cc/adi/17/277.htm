Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.277

gopī-bhāva yāte prabhu dhariyāche ekānta

vrajendra-nandane māne āpanāra kānta

SYNONYMS

gopī-bhāva — the mood of the gopīs; yāte — in which; prabhu — the Lord; dhariyāche — accepted; ekānta — positively; vrajendra-nandane — Lord Krishna; māne — they accept; āpanāra — own; kānta — lover.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the mood of the gopīs, who accept Vrajendranandana, Śrī Krishna, as their lover.
