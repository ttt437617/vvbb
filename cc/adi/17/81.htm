Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.81

dekhite dekhite vriksha ha-ila phalita

pākila aneka phala, sabei vismita

SYNONYMS

dekhite dekhite — as people were seeing; vriksha — the tree; ha-ila — became; phalita — fully grown with fruits; pākila — ripened; aneka — many; phala — fruits; sabei — every one of them; vismita — struck with wonder.

TRANSLATION

As people looked on, the tree became fully grown, with fruits that fully ripened. Thus everyone was struck with wonder.
