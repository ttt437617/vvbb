Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.336

śrī-svarūpa-śrī-rūpa-śrī-sanātana

śrī-raghunātha-dāsa, āra śrī-jīva-carana

śire dhari vandoń, nitya karoń tāńra āśa

caitanya-caritāmrita kahe krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

śrī-svarūpa — Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara; śrī-rūpa — Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī; śrī-sanātana — Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī; śrī-raghunātha-dāsa — Śrī Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; āra — and; śrī-jīva-carana — the lotus feet of Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī; śire — on the head; dhari — placing; vandoń — I worship; nitya — always; karoń — I do; tāńra — their; āśa — hope to serve; caitanya-caritāmrita — the book named Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita; kahe — describes; krishnadāsa — Śrīla Krishnadāsa Kavirāja Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

I wish to place the lotus feet of the Gosvāmīs on my head. Their names are Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara, Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrī Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī and Śrī Jīva Gosvāmī. Placing their lotus feet on my head, always hoping to serve them, I, Krishnadāsa, narrate Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita, following in their footsteps.

Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports to Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita, Ādi-līlā, Seventeenth Chapter, describing the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His youth.

END OF THE ĀDI-LĪLĀ
