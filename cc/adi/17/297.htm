Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.297

prema-bhakti diyā teńho bhāsā'la jagate

tāńra caritra loke nā pāre bujhite

SYNONYMS

prema-bhakti — devotional service; diyā — giving; teńho — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; bhāsā'la — overflooded; jagate — in the world; tāńra — His; caritra — character; loke — people; nā — not; pāre — able; bujhite — to understand.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu overflooded the entire world by distributing transcendental loving service. No one can understand His character and activities.
