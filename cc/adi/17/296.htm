Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.296

vātsalya, dāsya, sakhya — tina bhāvamaya

sei nityānanda — krishna-caitanya-sahāya

SYNONYMS

vātsalya — paternity; dāsya — servitude; sakhya — fraternity; tina — three; bhāva-maya — emotional ecstasies; sei — that; nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; krishna-caitanya — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sahāya — the assistant.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu always feels the ecstatic emotions of paternity, servitude and friendship. He always assists Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in that way.
