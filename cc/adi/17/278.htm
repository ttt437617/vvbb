Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.278

gopikā-bhāvera ei sudridha niścaya

vrajendra-nandana vinā anyatra nā haya

SYNONYMS

gopikā-bhāvera — of the ecstasy of the gopīs; ei — this; sudridha — firm; niścaya — confirmation; vrajendra-nandana — Lord Śrī Krishna; vinā — without; anyatra — anyone else; nā — not; haya — is possible.

TRANSLATION

It is firmly concluded that the ecstatic mood of the gopīs is possible only before Krishna, and no one else.
