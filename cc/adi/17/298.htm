Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.298

advaita-ācārya-gosāñi bhakta-avatāra

krishna avatāriyā kailā bhaktira pracāra

SYNONYMS

advaita-ācārya — Śrī Advaita Prabhu; gosāñi — spiritual master; bhakta-avatāra — the incarnation of a devotee; krishna — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; avatāriyā — descending; kailā — did; bhaktira — of devotional service; pracāra — propagation.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Advaita Ācārya Prabhu appeared as an incarnation of a devotee. He is in the category of Krishna, but He descended to this earth to propagate devotional service.
