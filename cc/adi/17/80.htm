Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.80

eka āmra-bīja prabhu ańgane ropila

tat-kshane janmila vriksha bādite lāgila

SYNONYMS

eka — one; āmra-bīja — seed of a mango; prabhu — the Lord; ańgane — in the yard; ropila — sowed; tat-kshane — immediately; janmila — fructified; vriksha — a tree; bādite — to grow; lāgila — began.

TRANSLATION

The Lord then sowed a mango seed in the yard, and immediately the seed fructified into a tree and began to grow.
