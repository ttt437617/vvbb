Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.151

ei mata duńhāra kathā haya thāre-thore

bhitarera artha keha bujhite nā pāre

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; duńhāra — of both of them; kathā — conversation; haya — took place; thāre-thore — with different indications; bhitarera — inner; artha — meaning; keha — anyone; bujhite — to understand; nā pāre — is not able.

TRANSLATION

In this way the Kazi and the Lord talked with each other with various indications, but no outsider could understand the inner meaning of their conversation.
