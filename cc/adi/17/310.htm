Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.310

prasańge kahila ei siddhāntera sāra

ihā yei śune, śuddha-bhakti haya tāra

SYNONYMS

prasańge — in the course of discussion; kahila — it was said; ei — this; siddhāntera — of the conclusion; sāra — the essence; ihā — this; yei — anyone who; śune — hears; śuddha-bhakti — unalloyed devotional service; haya — becomes; tāra — his.

TRANSLATION

In this discourse I have explained the essence of the devotional conclusion. Anyone who hears this develops unalloyed devotional service to the Lord.
