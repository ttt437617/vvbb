Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.247

eka-dina gopī-bhāve grihete vasiyā

'gopī' 'gopī' nāma laya vishanna hañā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; gopī-bhāve — in the ecstasy of the gopīs; grihete — at home; vasiyā — sitting; gopī gopī — gopī gopī; nāma — the name; laya — chants; vishanna — morose; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

One day the Lord, in the ecstasy of the gopīs, was sitting in His house. Very morose in separation, He was calling, "Gopī! Gopī!"
