Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.205

mańgalacandī vishahari kari' jāgarana

tā'te vādya, nritya, gīta, — yogya ācarana

SYNONYMS

mańgala-candī — of the religious performance for worship of Mańgalacandī; vishahari — of the religious performance for worship of Vishahari; kari' — observing; jāgarana — night vigil; tā'te — in that ceremony; vādya — musical performance; nritya — dancing; gīta — chanting; yogya — suitable; ācarana — custom.

TRANSLATION

"'When we keep a night-long vigil to observe religious performances for the worship of Mańgalacandī and Vishahari, playing on musical instruments, dancing and chanting are certainly fitting customs.
