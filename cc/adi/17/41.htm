Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.41

bada bada loka saba ānila bolāiyā

sabāre kahe śrīvāsa hāsiyā hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

bada bada — respectable; loka — persons; saba — all; ānila — brought them; bolāiyā — causing to be called; sabāre — to everyone; kahe — addresses; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; hāsiyā hāsiyā — while smiling.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura called for all the respectable gentlemen of the neighborhood and smilingly addressed them as follows.
