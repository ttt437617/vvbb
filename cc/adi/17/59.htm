Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.59

tabe vipra la-ila āsi śrīvāsa śarana

tāńhāra kripāya haila pāpa-vimocana

SYNONYMS

tabe — after that; vipra — the brāhmana (Gopāla Cāpāla); la-ila — took shelter; āsi — coming; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; śarana — shelter of his lotus feet; tāńhāra kripāya — by his mercy; haila — became; pāpa-vimocana — free from all sinful reaction.

TRANSLATION

Then the brāhmana, Gopāla Cāpāla, went to Śrīvāsa Thākura and took shelter of his lotus feet, and by Śrīvāsa Thākura's mercy he was freed from all sinful reactions.
