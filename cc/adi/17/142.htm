Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.142

uddhata loka bhāńge kājīra ghara-pushpavana

vistāri' varnilā ihā dāsa-vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

uddhata — agitated; loka — persons; bhāńge — break; kājīra — of the Kazi; ghara — house; pushpa-vana — flower garden; vistāri' — elaborately; varnilā — described; ihā — this; dāsa-vrindāvana — Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura.

TRANSLATION

Naturally some of the people who were very much agitated began to retaliate the Kazi's actions by wrecking his house and flower garden. Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has elaborately described this incident.
