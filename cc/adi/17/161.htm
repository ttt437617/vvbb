Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.161

ataeva jarad-gava māre muni-gana

veda-mantre siddha kare tāhāra jīvana

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; jarad-gava — old cows; māre — killed; muni-gana — sages; veda-mantre — by the power of Vedic hymns; siddha — rejuvenated; kare — makes; tāhāra — his; jīvana — life.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore the great sages sometimes killed old cows, and by chanting Vedic hymns they brought them back to life for perfection.
