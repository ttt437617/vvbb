Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.96

śrīvāsa balena, — ye tomāra nāma laya

tāra koti aparādha saba haya kshaya

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa balena — Śrīvāsa Pandita said; ye — anyone who; tomara — Your; nāma — holy name; laya — takes; tāra — his; koti — ten million; aparādha — offenses; saba — all; haya — become; kshaya — vanquished.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura replied, "Anyone who takes Your holy name vanquishes ten million of his offenses immediately.
