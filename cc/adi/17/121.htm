Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.121

nagariyā loke prabhu yabe ājñā dilā

ghare ghare sańkīrtana karite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

nagariyā — citizens; loke — all the people; prabhu — the Lord; yabe — when; ājñā — order; dilā — gave; ghare ghare — in each and every home; sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; karite — to perform; lāgilā — began.

TRANSLATION

The Lord ordered all the citizens of Navadvīpa to chant the Hare Krishna mantra, and in each and every home they began performing sańkīrtana regularly.
