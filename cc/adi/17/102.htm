Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.102

prabhu-sańge nritya kare parama ullāse

prabhu tāre prema dila, prema-rase bhāse

SYNONYMS

prabhu-sańge — along with the Lord; nritya kare — was dancing; parama — very much; ullāse — in satisfaction; prabhu — the Lord; tāre — him; prema — love of Godhead; dila — delivered; prema-rase — in the mellows of love of God; bhāse — began to float.

TRANSLATION

He danced with the Lord because he was favored by love of Krishna. Thus he flowed in the mellows of love of Godhead.
