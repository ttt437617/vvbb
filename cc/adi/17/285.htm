Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.285

gopī-gana dekhi' krishnera ha-ila sādhvasa

lukāite nārila, bhaye hailā vibaśa

SYNONYMS

gopī-gana — all the gopīs combined together; dekhi' — seeing; krishnera — of Krishna; ha-ila — there were; sādhvasa — some emotional feelings; lukāite — to hide; nārila — was unable; bhaye — out of fear; hailā — became; vibaśa — motionless.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Krishna saw all the gopīs, He was struck with emotion. Thus He could not hide Himself, and out of fear He became motionless.
