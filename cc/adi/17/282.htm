Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.282

vasanta-kāle rāsa-līlā kare govardhane

antardhāna kailā sańketa kari' rādhā-sane

SYNONYMS

vasanta-kāle — during the season of spring; rāsa-līlā — the rāsa dance; kare — does; govardhane — near the Govardhana Hill; antardhāna — disappearance; kailā — did; sańketa — indication; kari' — making; rādhā-sane — with Rādhārānī.

TRANSLATION

During the season of springtime, when the rāsa dance was going on, suddenly Krishna disappeared from the scene, indicating that He wanted to be alone with Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
