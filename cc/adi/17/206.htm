Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.206

pūrve bhāla chila ei nimāi pandita

gayā haite āsiyā cālāya viparīta

SYNONYMS

pūrve — before this; bhāla — very good; chila — was; ei — this; nimāi pandita — Nimāi Pandita; gayā — Gayā (a place of pilgrimage); haite — from; āsiyā — coming; cālāya — conducts; viparīta — just the opposite.

TRANSLATION

"'Nimāi Pandita was previously a very good boy, but since He has returned from Gayā He conducts Himself differently.
