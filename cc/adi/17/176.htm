Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.176

śuna, gaurahari, ei praśnera kārana

nibhrita hao yadi, tabe kari nivedana

SYNONYMS

śuna — kindly hear; gaurahari — O Gaurahari; ei praśnera — of this question; kārana — reason; nibhrita — solitary; hao — You become; yadi — if; tabe — then; kari — I shall make; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

"Kindly listen, O Gaurahari! If You come to a private place, I shall then explain the reason."
