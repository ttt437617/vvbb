Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.136

āge sampradāye nritya kare haridāsa

madhye nāce ācārya-gosāñi parama ullāsa

SYNONYMS

āge — in front; sampradāye — in the party; nritya — dancing; kare — does; haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; madhye — in the middle; nāce — dances; ācārya-gosāñi — Śrī Advaita Ācārya; parama — very; ullāsa — happy.

TRANSLATION

In the front party danced Thākura Haridāsa, and in the middle party danced Advaita Ācārya with great jubilation.
