Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.198

mleccha kahe, — hindure āmi kari parihāsa

keha keha — krishnadāsa, keha — rāmadāsa

SYNONYMS

mleccha — the meat-eater; kahe — says; hindure — unto a Hindu; āmi — I; kari — do; parihāsa — joking; keha keha — some of them; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa; keha — some of them; rāmadāsa — Rāmadāsa.

TRANSLATION

"The meat-eater replied, 'Sometimes I joke with the Hindus. Some of them are called Krishnadāsa, and some are called Rāmadāsa.
