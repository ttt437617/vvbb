Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.116

nityānanda-gosāñi prabhura āveśa jānila

gańgā-jala-pātra āni' sammukhe dharila

SYNONYMS

nityānanda-gosāñi — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; prabhura — of the Lord; āveśa — ecstasy; jānila — could understand; gańgā-jala — Ganges water; pātra — pot; āni' — bringing; sammukhe — in front; dharila — placed it.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu Gosāñi, understanding the ecstatic mood of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, brought a pot of Ganges water as a token and put it before Him.
