Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.33

prabhu-ājñāya kara ei śloka ācarana

avaśya pāibe tabe śrī-krishna-carana

SYNONYMS

prabhu — of the Lord; ājñāya — on the order; kara — do; ei śloka — of this verse; ācarana — practice; avaśya — certainly; pāibe — he will get; tabe — afterwards; śrī-krishna-carana — the lotus feet of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

One m7ust strictly follow the principles given by Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in this verse. If one simply follows in the footsteps of Lord Caitanya and the Gosvāmīs, certainly he will achieve the ultimate goal of life, the lotus feet of Śrī Krishna.
