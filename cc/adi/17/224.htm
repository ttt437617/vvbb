Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.224

kīrtana karite prabhu karilā gamana

sańge cali' āise kājī ullasita mana

SYNONYMS

kīrtana — chanting; karite — to perform; prabhu — the Lord; karilā — made; gamana — departure; sańge — accompanying Him; cali' — walking; āise — comes; kājī — the Kazi; ullasita — jubilant; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went back to perform kīrtana, and the Kazi, his mind jubilant, went with Him.
