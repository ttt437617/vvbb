Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.156

sei śāstre kahe, — pravritti-nivritti-mārga-bheda

nivritti-mārge jīva-mātra-vadhera nishedha

SYNONYMS

sei śāstre — in the scripture (the Koran); kahe — it is ordered; pravritti — of attachment; nivritti — of detachment; mārga — ways; bheda — difference; nivritti — of detachment; mārge — on the path; jīva-mātra — of any living entity; vadhera — of killing; nishedha — prohibition.

TRANSLATION

"According to the Koran, there are two ways of advancement — through increasing the propensity to enjoy, and through decreasing the propensity to enjoy. On the path of decreasing attachment [nivritti-mārga], the killing of animals is prohibited.
