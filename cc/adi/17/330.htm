Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.330

vrindāvana-dāsa ihā 'caitanya-mańgale'

vistāri' varnilā nityānanda-ājñā-bale

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-dāsa — Thākura Vrindāvana dāsa; ihā — this; caitanya-mańgale — in his book Caitanya-mańgala; vistāri' — expanding; varnilā — described; nityānanda — of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu; ājñā — of the order; bale — on the strength.

TRANSLATION

By the order and strength of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has elaborately described in his Caitanya-mańgala all that I have not.
