Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.306

acintya, adbhuta krishna-caitanya-vihāra

citra bhāva, citra guna, citra vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

acintya — inconceivable; adbhuta — wonderful; krishna-caitanya — of Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vihāra — pastimes; citra — wonderful; bhāva — ecstasy; citra — wonderful; guna — qualities; citra — wonderful; vyavahāra — behavior.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu are inconceivable and wonderful. His ecstasy is wonderful, His qualities are wonderful, and His behavior is wonderful.
