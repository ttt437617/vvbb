Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.61

phiri' gela vipra ghare mane duhkha pāñā

āra dina prabhuke kahe gańgāya lāga pāñā

SYNONYMS

phiri' gela — went back; vipra — the brāhmana; ghare — to his home; mane — within his mind; duhkha — unhappiness; pāñā — getting; āra dina — the next day; prabhuke — unto the Lord; kahe — says; gańgāya — on the bank of the Ganges; lāga — touch; pāñā — getting.

TRANSLATION

He returned home with an unhappy mind, but on the next day he met Lord Caitanya on the bank of the Ganges and spoke to Him.
