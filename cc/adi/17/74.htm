Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.74

sagane sacele giyā kaila gańgā-snāna

bhaktira mahimā tāhāń karila vyākhyāna

SYNONYMS

sa-gane — with His followers; sa-cele — without leaving the clothes; giyā — going; kaila — did; gańgā-snāna — bathing in the Ganges; bhaktira — of devotional service; mahimā — glories; tāhāń — there; karila — did; vyākhyāna — explanation.

TRANSLATION

Without even removing His garments, Lord Caitanya took a bath in the Ganges with His companions. There He explained the glories of devotional service.
