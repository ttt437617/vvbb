Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.57-58

śrīvāsa panditera sthāne āche aparādha

tathā yāha, teńho yadi karena prasāda

tabe tora habe ei pāpa-vimocana

yadi punah aiche nāhi kara ācarana

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa panditera — of Śrīvāsa Thākura; sthāne — at the lotus feet; āche — there is; aparādha — offense; tathā — there; yāha — go; teńho — he; yadi — if; karena — does; prasāda — blessings; tabe — then; tora — your; habe — there will be; ei — this; pāpa-vimocana — immunity from sinful reaction; yadi — if; punah — again; aiche — such; nāhi kara — you do not commit; ācarana — behavior.

TRANSLATION

"You have committed an offense at the lotus feet of Śrīvāsa Thākura," the Lord said. "First you must go there and beg for his mercy, and then if he gives you his blessings and you do not commit such sins again, you will be freed from these reactions."
