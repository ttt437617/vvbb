Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.210

'nimāñi' nāma chādi' ebe bolāya 'gaurahari'

hindura dharma nashta kaila pāshanda sañcāri'

SYNONYMS

nimāñi — Nimāi; nāma — the name; chādi' — giving up; ebe — now; bolāya — calls; gaurahari — Gaurahari; hindura — of the Hindus; dharma — the religious principles; nashta kaila — spoiled; pāshanda — irreligion; sañcāri' — introducing.

TRANSLATION

"'Now He has given up His own name Nimāi and introduced Himself by the name Gaurahari. He has spoiled the Hindu religious principles and introduced the irreligion of nonbelievers.
