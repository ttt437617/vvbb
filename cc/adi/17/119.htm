Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.119

vanamālī ācārya dekhe sonāra lāńgala

sabe mili' nritya kare āveśe vihvala

SYNONYMS

vanamālī ācārya — Vanamālī Ācārya; dekhe — sees; sonāra — made of gold; lāńgala — plow; sabe — all; mili' — meeting together; nritya — dance; kare — perform; āveśe — in ecstasy; vihvala — overwhelmed.

TRANSLATION

Vanamālī Ācārya saw a golden plow in the hand of Balarāma, and the devotees all assembled together and danced, overwhelmed by ecstasy.
