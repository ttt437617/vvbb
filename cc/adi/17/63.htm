Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.63

samsāra-sukha tomāra ha-uka vināśa

śāpa śuni' prabhura citte ha-ila ullāsa

SYNONYMS

samsāra-sukha — material happiness; tomāra — Your; ha-uka — may it become; vināśa — all vanquished; śāpa śuni' — hearing this curse; prabhura — of the Lord; citte — within His mind; ha-ila — there was; ullāsa — jubilation.

TRANSLATION

The brāhmana cursed the Lord, "You shall be bereft of all material happiness!" When the Lord heard this, He felt great jubilation within Himself.
