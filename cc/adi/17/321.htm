Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.321

ashtame 'caitanya-līlā-varnana'-kārana

eka krishna-nāmera mahā-mahimā-kathana

SYNONYMS

ashtame — in the Eighth Chapter; caitanya-līlā-varnana-kārana — the reason for describing Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes; eka — one; krishna-nāmera — of the holy name of Lord Krishna; mahā-mahimā-kathana — description of great glories.

TRANSLATION

The Eighth Chapter gives the reason for describing Lord Caitanya's pastimes. It also describes the greatness of Lord Krishna's holy name.
