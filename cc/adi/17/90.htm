Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.90

eka-dina prabhu śrīvāsere ājñā dila

'brihat sahasra-nāma' pada, śunite mana haila

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; prabhu — the Lord; śrīvāsere — unto Śrīvāsa Thākura; ājñā — order; dila — gave; brihat — great; sahasra-nāma — one thousand names; pada — read; śunite — to hear; mana — mind; haila — wanted.

TRANSLATION

One day the Lord ordered Śrīvāsa Thākura to read the Brihat-sahasra-nāma [the thousand names of Lord Vishnu], for He wanted to hear them at that time.
