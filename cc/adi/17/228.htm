Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.228

śrīvāsa-putrera tāhāń haila paraloka

tabu śrīvāsera citte nā janmila śoka

SYNONYMS

śrīvāsa — of Śrīvāsa Thākura; putrera — of the son; tāhāń — there; haila — took place; paraloka — death; tabu — still; śrīvāsera — of Śrīvāsa Thākura; citte — in the mind; nā — not; janmila — there was; śoka — lamentation.

TRANSLATION

At that time a calamity took place — Śrīvāsa Thākura's son died. Yet Śrīvāsa Thākura was not at all sorry.
