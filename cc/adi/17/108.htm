Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.108

pūrva janme chilā tumi jagat-āśraya

paripūrna bhagavān — sarvaiśvaryamaya

SYNONYMS

pūrva janme — in the previous birth; chilā — were; tumi — You; jagat — universe; āśraya — shelter; paripūrna — with full potencies; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sarva-aiśvarya-maya — full of all opulences.

TRANSLATION

"My dear sir, in Your previous birth You were the shelter of all creation, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, full of all opulences.
