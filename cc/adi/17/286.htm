Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.286

catur-bhuja mūrti dhari' āchena vasiyā

krishna dekhi' gopī kahe nikate āsiyā

SYNONYMS

catur-bhuja — four-armed; mūrti — form; dhari' — accepting; āchena — was; vasiyā — sitting; krishna — Lord Krishna; dekhi' — seeing; gopī — the gopīs; kahe — say; nikate — nearby; āsiyā — coming there.

TRANSLATION

Krishna assumed His four-armed Nārāyana form and sat there. When all the gopīs came, they looked at Him and spoke as follows.
