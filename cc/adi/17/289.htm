Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.289

eta bali namaskari' gelā gopī-gana

hena-kāle rādhā āsi' dilā daraśana

SYNONYMS

eta bali — saying this; namaskari' — offering obeisances; gelā — went away; gopī-gana — all the gopīs; hena-kāle — at this time; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; āsi' — coming there; dilā — gave; daraśana — audience.

TRANSLATION

After saying this and offering obeisances, all the gopīs dispersed. Then Śrīmatī Rādhārānī came and appeared before Lord Krishna.
