Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.193

nagare hindura dharma bādila apāra

'hari' 'hari' dhvani ba-i nāhi śuni āra

SYNONYMS

nagare — in the city; hindura — of the Hindus; dharma — religion; bādila — has increased; apāra — unlimitedly; hari hari — of the Lord's name, Hari, Hari; dhvani — the vibration; ba-i — except; nāhi — do not; śuni — we hear; āra — anything else.

TRANSLATION

"'The religion of the Hindus has increased unlimitedly. There are always vibrations of "Hari! Hari!" We do not hear anything but this.'
