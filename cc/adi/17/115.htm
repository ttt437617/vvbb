Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.115

eka dina prabhu vishnu-mandape vasiyā

'madhu āna', 'madhu āna' balena dākiyā

SYNONYMS

eka dina — one day; prabhu — the Lord; vishnu-mandape — in the corridor of a Vishnu temple; vasiyā — sitting; madhu āna — bring honey; madhu āna — bring honey; balena — says; dākiyā — calling loudly.

TRANSLATION

One day the Lord sat down in the corridor of a Vishnu temple and began calling very loudly, "Bring some honey! Bring some honey!"
