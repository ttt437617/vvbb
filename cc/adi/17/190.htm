Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.190

pudila sakala dādi, mukhe haila vrana

yei peyādā yāya, tāra ei vivarana

SYNONYMS

pudila — burned; sakala — all; dādi — beard; mukhe — on the face; haila — there was; vrana — blisters; yei — any; peyādā — orderly; yāya — goes; tāra — his; ei — this; vivarana — description.

TRANSLATION

"'My beard was burned, and there were blisters on my cheeks.' Every orderly who went gave the same description.
