Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.4

vidyā-saundarya-sad-veśa-

sambhoga-nritya-kīrtanaih

prema-nāma-pradānaiś ca

gauro dīvyati yauvane

SYNONYMS

vidyā — education; saundarya — beauty; sat-veśa — nice dress; sambhoga — enjoyment; nritya — dancing; kīrtanaih — by chanting; prema-nāma — the holy name of the Lord, which induces one to become a devotee; pradānaih — by distributing; ca — and; gaurah — Lord Śrī Gaurasundara; dīvyati — illuminated; yauvane — in His youth.

TRANSLATION

Exhibiting His scholarship, beauty and fine dress, Lord Caitanya danced and chanted as He distributed the holy name of the Lord to awaken dormant love of Krishna. Thus Lord Śrī Gaurasundara shone in His youthful pastimes.
