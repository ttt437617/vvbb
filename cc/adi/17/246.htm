Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.246

vijaya ācāryera ghare se rātre rahilā

prātah-kāle bhakta sabe ghare lañā gelā

SYNONYMS

vijaya — named Vijaya; ācāryera — of the teacher; ghare — at the home; se — that; rātre — on the night; rahilā — remained; prātah-kāle — in the morning; bhakta — the devotees; sabe — all; ghare — home; lañā — taking them; gelā — went.

TRANSLATION

That night the Lord stayed at the house of Vijaya Ācārya. In the morning the Lord took all His devotees and returned home.
