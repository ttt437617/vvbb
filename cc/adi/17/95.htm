Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.95

śrīvāse kahena prabhu kariyā vishāda

loka bhaya pāya, — mora haya aparādha

SYNONYMS

śrīvāse — unto Śrīvāsa Thākura; kahena — says; prabhu — the Lord; kariyā — becoming; vishāda — morose; loka — people; bhaya pāya — become afraid; mora — My; haya — there is; aparādha — offense.

TRANSLATION

The Lord became morose and said to Śrīvāsa Thākura, "When I adopted the mood of Lord Nrisimhadeva, people were greatly afraid. Therefore I stopped, since causing fear among people is an offense."
