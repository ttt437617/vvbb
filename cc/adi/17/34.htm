Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.34

tabe prabhu śrīvāsera grihe nirantara

rātre sańkīrtana kaila eka samvatsara

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; prabhu — the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrīvāsera — of Śrīvāsa Thākura; grihe — in the home; nirantara — always; rātre — at night; sańkīrtana — congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; kaila — performed; eka samvatsara — one full year.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu regularly led congregational chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra in the house of Śrīvāsa Thākura every night for one full year.
