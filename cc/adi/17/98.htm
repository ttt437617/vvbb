Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.98

eta bali' śrīvāsa karila sevana

tushta hañā prabhu āilā āpana-bhavana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; karila — did; sevana — worship; tushta — satisfied; hañā — becoming; prabhu — the Lord; āilā — came back; āpana-bhavana — to His own home.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrīvāsa Thākura worshiped the Lord, who was then greatly satisfied and returned to His own home.
