Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.248

eka paduyā āila prabhuke dekhite

'gopī' 'gopī' nāma śuni' lāgila balite

SYNONYMS

eka paduyā — one student; āila — came there; prabhuke — the Lord; dekhite — to see; gopī gopī — gopī gopī; nāma — the name; śuni' — hearing; lāgila — began; balite — to say.

TRANSLATION

A student who came to see the Lord was astonished that the Lord was chanting "Gopī! Gopī!" Thus he spoke as follows.
