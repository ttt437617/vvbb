Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.36

kīrtana śuni' bāhire tārā jvali' pudi' mare

śrīvāsere duhkha dite nānā yukti kare

SYNONYMS

kīrtana śuni' — after hearing the chanting; bāhire — outside; tārā — the nonbelievers; jvali' — burned; pudi' — to ashes; mare — die; śrīvāsere — unto Śrīvāsa Thākura; duhkha — troubles; dite — to give; nānā — various; yukti — plans; kare — do.

TRANSLATION

Thus the nonbelievers almost burned to ashes and died out of envy. To retaliate, they planned various ways to give trouble to Śrīvāsa Thākura.
