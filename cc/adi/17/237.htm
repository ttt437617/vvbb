Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.237

vamśī-vādye gopī-ganera vane ākarshana

tāń-sabāra sańge yaiche vana-viharana

SYNONYMS

vamśī-vādye — on hearing the sound of the flute; gopī-ganera — of all the gopīs; vane — in the forest; ākarshana — the attraction; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; sańge — in the company; yaiche — in what way; vana — in the forest; viharana — wandering.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvāsa Thākura extensively explained how the gopīs were attracted to the forests of Vrindāvana by the vibration of Krishna's flute and how they wandered together in the forest.
