Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.267

e-saba pāshandīra tabe ha-ibe nistāra

āra kona upāya nāhi, ei yukti sāra

SYNONYMS

e-saba — all these; pāshandīra — of the demons; tabe — then; ha-ibe — there will be; nistāra — deliverance; āra — alternative; kona — some; upāya — means; nāhi — there is not; ei — this; yukti — of the argument; sāra — essence.

TRANSLATION

"All the unfaithful rogues of this world can be delivered by this process. There is no alternative. This is the essence of the argument."
