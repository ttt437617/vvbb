Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.194

āra mleccha kahe, — hindu 'krishna krishna' bali'

hāse, kānde, nāce, gāya, gadi yāya dhūli

SYNONYMS

āra — another; mleccha — meat-eater; kahe — said; hindu — Hindus; krishna krishna bali' — saying "Krishna, Krishna"; hāse — laugh; kānde — cry; nāce — dance; gāya — chant; gadi yāya dhūli — roll in the dust.

TRANSLATION

"One meat-eater said, 'The Hindus say, "Krishna, Krishna," and they laugh, cry, dance, chant and fall on the ground, smearing their bodies with dirt.
