Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.60

āra eka vipra āila kīrtana dekhite

dvāre kapāta, — nā pāila bhitare yāite

SYNONYMS

āra — another; eka — one; vipra — brāhmana; āila — came; kīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; dekhite — to see; dvāre — in the gateway; kapāta — the door (being closed); nā pāila — did not get; bhitare — inside; yāite — to go.

TRANSLATION

Another brāhmana came to see the kīrtana performance, but the door was closed, and he could not enter the hall.
