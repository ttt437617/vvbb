Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.182

mora kīrtana mānā karis, karimu tora kshaya

āńkhi mudi' kāńpi āmi pāñā bada bhaya

SYNONYMS

mora — My; kīrtana — congregational chanting; mānā karis — you are forbidding; karimu — I shall do; tora — your; kshaya — destruction; āńkhi — eyes; mudi' — closing; kāńpi — I was trembling; āmi — I; pāñā — getting; bada — very great; bhaya — fear.

TRANSLATION

"'You have forbidden the performance of My congregational chanting. Therefore I must destroy you!' Being very much afraid of Him, I closed my eyes and trembled.
