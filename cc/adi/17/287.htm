Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.287

'ihoń krishna nahe, ihoń nārāyana mūrti'

eta bali' tāńre sabhe kare nati-stuti

SYNONYMS

ihoń — this; krishna — Lord Krishna; nahe — is not; ihoń — this is; nārāyana — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; mūrti — the form; eta bali' — saying this; tāńre — unto Him; sabhe — all the gopīs; kare — make; nati-stuti — obeisances and prayers.

TRANSLATION

"He is not Krishna! He is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Nārāyana." After saying this, they offered obeisances and the following respectful prayers.
