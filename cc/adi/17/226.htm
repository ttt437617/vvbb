Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.226

ei mate kājīre prabhu karilā prasāda

ihā yei śune tāra khande aparādha

SYNONYMS

ei mate — in this way; kājīre — unto the Kazi; prabhu — the Lord; karilā — did; prasāda — mercy; ihā — this; yei — anyone who; śune — hears; tāra — his; khande — vanquishes; aparādha — offenses.

TRANSLATION

This is the incident concerning the Kazi and the Lord's mercy upon him. Anyone who hears this is also freed from all offenses.
