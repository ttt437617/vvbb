Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.94

loka-bhaya dekhi' prabhura bāhya ha-ila

śrīvāsa-grihete giyā gadā phelāila

SYNONYMS

loka-bhaya — the fearful people; dekhi' — seeing this; prabhura — of the Lord; bāhya — external sense; ha-ila — appeared; śrīvāsa-grihete — in the house of Śrīvāsa Pandita; giyā — going there; gadā — the club; phelāila — threw away.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the people so afraid, the Lord came to His external senses and thus returned to the house of Śrīvāsa Thākura and threw away the club.
