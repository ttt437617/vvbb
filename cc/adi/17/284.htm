Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.284

dūra haite krishne dekhi' bale gopī-gana

"ei dekha kuñjera bhitara vrajendra-nandana"

SYNONYMS

dūra haite — from a distance; krishne — unto Krishna; dekhi' — seeing; bale — said; gopī-gana — all the gopīs; ei dekha — just see here; kuñjera — the bush; bhitara — within; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"Just see!" the gopīs said, seeing Krishna from a distant place. "Here within a bush is Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja."
