Ādi-līlāChapter 17: The Pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 17.40

madya-bhānda-pāśe dhari' nija-ghare gela

prātah-kāle śrīvāsa tāhā ta' dekhila

SYNONYMS

madya-bhānda — a pot of wine; pāśe — by the side of; dhari' — placing; nija-ghare — to his own home; gela — went; prātah-kāle — in the morning; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; tāhā — all those things; ta' — certainly; dekhila — saw.

TRANSLATION

He placed a pot of wine beside all this, and in the morning when Śrīvāsa Thākura opened his door he saw this paraphernalia.
