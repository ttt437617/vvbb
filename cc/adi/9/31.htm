Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.31

mālākāra kahe, — śuna, vriksha-parivāra

mūlaśākhā-upaśākhā yateka prakāra

SYNONYMS

mālā-kāra — the gardener; kahe — said; śuna — hear; vriksha-parivāra — the family of this transcendental tree of devotional service; mūla-śākhā — chief branches; upaśākhā — subbranches; yateka — as many; prakāra — varieties.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya thus addressed the multifarious varieties of branches and subbranches of the tree of devotional service:
