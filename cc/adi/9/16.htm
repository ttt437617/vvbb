Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.16

madhya-mūla paramānanda purī mahā-dhīra

ashta dike ashta mūla vriksha kaila sthira

SYNONYMS

madhya-mūla — the middle root; paramānanda purī — Paramānanda Purī; mahā-dhīra — most sober; ashta dike — in the eight directions; ashta mūla — eight roots; vriksha — the tree; kaila sthira — fixed.

TRANSLATION

With the sober and grave Paramānanda Purī as the central root and the other eight roots in the eight directions, the tree of Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood firmly.
