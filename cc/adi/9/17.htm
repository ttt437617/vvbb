Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.17

skandhera upare bahu śākhā upajila

upari upari śākhā asańkhya ha-ila

SYNONYMS

skandhera upare — upon the trunk; bahu śākhā — many branches; upajila — grew; upari upari — over and above them; śākhā — other branches; asańkhya — innumerable; ha-ila — fructified.

TRANSLATION

From the trunk grew many branches, and above them innumerable others.
