Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.52

sarva-loke matta kailā āpana-samāna

preme matta loka vinā nāhi dekhi āna

SYNONYMS

sarva-loke — all people; matta — maddened; kailā — He made; āpana — Himself; samāna — like; preme — in love of God; matta — maddened; loka — people in general; vinā — without; nāhi — do not; dekhi — we see; āna — anything else.

TRANSLATION

With His sańkīrtana movement the Lord made everyone mad like Himself. We do not find anyone who was not intoxicated by His sańkīrtana movement.
