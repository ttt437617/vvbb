Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.33

e vrikshera ańga haya saba sa-cetana

bādiyā vyāpila sabe sakala bhuvana

SYNONYMS

e — this; vrikshera — of the Caitanya tree; ańga — parts; haya — are; saba — all; sa-cetana — spiritually cognizant; bādiyā — increasing; vyāpila — overflooded; sabe — all the parts; sakala — all; bhuvana — the world.

TRANSLATION

"All the parts of this tree are spiritually cognizant, and thus as they grow they spread all over the world.
