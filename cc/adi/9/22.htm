Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.22

sei dui-skandhe bahu śākhā upajila

tāra upaśākhā-gane jagat chāila

SYNONYMS

sei — that; dui-skandhe — in two trunks; bahu — many; śākhā — branches; upajila — grew; tāra — of them; upaśākhā-gane — subbranches; jagat — the whole world; chāila — covered.

TRANSLATION

From these two trunks grew many branches and subbranches that covered the entire world.
