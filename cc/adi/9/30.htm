Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.30

añjali añjali bhari' phele caturdiśe

daridra kudāñā khāya, mālākāra hāse

SYNONYMS

añjali — handful; añjali — handful; bhari' — filling; phele — distributes; catur-diśe — in all directions; daridra — poor; kudāñā — picking up; khāya — eats; mālā-kāra — the gardener; hāse — smiles.

TRANSLATION

The transcendental gardener, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, distributed handful after handful of fruit in all directions, and when the poor, hungry people ate the fruit, the gardener smiled with great pleasure.
