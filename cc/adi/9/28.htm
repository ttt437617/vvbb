Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.28

tri-jagate yata āche dhana-ratnamani

eka-phalera mūlya kari' tāhā nāhi gani

SYNONYMS

tri-jagate — in the three worlds; yata — as much as; āche — there is; dhana-ratna-mani — wealth and riches; eka-phalera — one fruit's; mūlya — price; kari' — calculated; tāhā — that; nāhi — do not; gani — count.

TRANSLATION

All the wealth in the three worlds cannot equal the value of one such nectarean fruit of devotional service.
