Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.21

vrikshera upare śākhā haila dui skandha

eka 'advaita' nāma, āra 'nityānanda'

SYNONYMS

vrikshera — of the tree; upare — on the top; śākhā — branch; haila — became; dui — two; skandha — trunks; eka — one; advaita — Śrī Advaita Prabhu; nāma — of the name; āra — and; nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

At the top of the tree the trunk branched into two. One trunk was named Śrī Advaita Prabhu and the other Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu.
