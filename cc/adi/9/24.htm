Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.24

śishya, praśishya, āra upaśishya-gana

jagat vyāpila tāra nāhika ganana

SYNONYMS

śishya — disciples; praśishya — granddisciples; āra — and; upaśishya-gana — admirers; jagat — the whole world; vyāpila — spread; tāra — of that; nāhika — there is none; ganana — enumeration.

TRANSLATION

Thus the disciples and the granddisciples and their admirers spread throughout the entire world, and it is not possible to enumerate them all.
