Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.40

jagat vyāpiyā mora habe punya khyāti

sukhī ha-iyā loka mora gāhibeka kīrti

SYNONYMS

jagat vyāpiyā — spreading all over the world; mora — My; habe — there will be; punya — pious; khyāti — reputation; sukhī — happy; ha-iyā — becoming; loka — all the people; mora — My; gāhibeka — glorify; kīrti — reputation.

TRANSLATION

"If the fruits are distributed all over the world, My reputation as a pious man will be known everywhere, and thus all people will glorify My name with great pleasure.

PURPORT

This prediction of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's is now actually coming to pass. The Krishna consciousness movement is being distributed all over the world through the chanting of the holy name of the Lord, the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra, and people who were leading confused, chaotic lives are now feeling transcendental happiness. They are finding peace in sańkīrtana, and therefore they are acknowledging the supreme benefit of this movement. This is the blessing of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. His prediction is now factually being fulfilled, and those who are sober and conscientious are appreciating the value of this great movement.
