Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.20

mukhya mukhya śākhā-ganera nāma aganana

āge ta' kariba, śuna vrikshera varnana

SYNONYMS

mukhya mukhya — the foremost of them all; śākhā-ganera — of the branches; nāma — name; aganana — uncountable; āge — subsequently; ta' kariba — I shall do; śuna — please hear; vrikshera varnana — the description of the Caitanya tree.

TRANSLATION

I shall try to name the foremost of the innumerable branches. Please hear the description of the Caitanya tree.
