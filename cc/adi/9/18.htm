Ādi-līlāChapter 9: The Desire Tree of Devotional Service

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 9.18

viśa viśa śākhā kari' eka eka mandala

mahā-mahā-śākhā chāila brahmānda sakala

SYNONYMS

viśa viśa — twenty, twenty; śākhā — branches; kari' — making a group; eka eka mandala — form a society; mahā-mahā-śākhā — big branches; chāila — covered; brahmānda — the whole universe; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

Thus the branches of the Caitanya tree formed a cluster or society, with great branches covering all the universe.

PURPORT

Our International Society for Krishna Consciousness is one of the branches of the Caitanya tree.
