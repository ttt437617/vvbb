Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.55

'avimrishta-vidheyāmśa' — dui thāñi cihna

'viruddha-mati', 'bhagna-krama', 'punar-ātta', — dosha tina

SYNONYMS

avimrishta-vidheyāmśa — unclean composition; dui thāñi — in two places; cihna — symptoms; viruddha-mati — a contradictory conception; bhagna-krama — broken order; punar-ātta — redundancy (also called punar-ukti); dosha — faults; tina — three.

TRANSLATION

"In this verse the fault of avimrishta-vidheyāmśa occurs twice, and the faults of viruddha-mati, bhagna-krama and punar-ātta occur once each.
