Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.56

'gańgāra mahattva' — śloke mūla 'vidheya'

idam śabde 'anuvāda' — pāche avidheya

SYNONYMS

gańgāra mahattva — glorification of mother Ganges; śloke — in the verse; mūla — chief; vidheya — unknown; idam śabde — by the word idam ("this"); anuvāda — the known; pāche — at the end; avidheya — improper.

TRANSLATION

"The glorification of the Ganges [mahattvam gańgāyāh] is the principal unknown subject matter in this verse, and the known subject matter is indicated by the word 'idam,' which has been placed after the unknown.
