Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.101

bhavabhūti, jayadeva, āra kālidāsa

tāń-sabāra kavitve āche doshera prakāśa

SYNONYMS

bhavabhūti — Bhavabhūti; jayadeva — Jayadeva; āra — and; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; kavitve — in the poetic power; āche — there is; doshera — of faults; prakāśa — manifestation.

TRANSLATION

"Even in the poetic compositions of such great poets as Bhavabhūti, Jayadeva and Kālidāsa there are many examples of faults.
