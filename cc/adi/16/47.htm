Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.47

prabhu kahena, — kahi, yadi na karaha rosha

kaha tomāra ei śloke kibā āche dosha

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — the Lord replied; kahi — let Me say; yadi — if; nā — do not; karaha — you become; rosha — angry; kaha — please tell Me; tomāra — your; ei śloke — in this verse; kiba — what; āche — there is; dosha — fault.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "My dear sir, I may say something to you if you will not become angry. Can you explain the faults in this verse?
