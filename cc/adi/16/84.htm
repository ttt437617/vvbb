Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.84

sthūla ei pañca dosha, pañca alańkāra

sūkshma vicāriye yadi āchaye apāra

SYNONYMS

sthūla — gross; ei — these; pañca — five; dosha — faults; pañca — five; alańkāra — literary ornaments; sūkshma — in detail; vicāriye — we consider; yadi — if; āchaye — there are; apāra — unlimited.

TRANSLATION

"I have simply discussed the five gross faults and five literary embellishments of this verse, but if we consider it in fine detail we will find unlimited faults.
