Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.109

e-saba līlā varniyāchena vrindāvana-dāsa

ye kichu viśesha ihāń karila prakāśa

SYNONYMS

e-saba — all these; līlā — pastimes; varniyāchena — has described; vrindāvana-dāsa — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; ye kichu — whatever; viśesha — specifics; ihāń — in this connection; karila — I have made; prakāśa — presentation.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has described all these incidents elaborately. I have only presented the specific incidents he has not described.
