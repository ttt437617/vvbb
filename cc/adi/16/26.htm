Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.26

vrindāvana-dāsa ihā kariyāchena vistāra

sphuta nāhi kare dosha-gunera vicāra

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-dāsa — Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; ihā — this; kariyāchena — has made; vistāra — elaborate description ; sphuta — what was clear; nahi — not; kare — does; dosha-gunera — of both the faults and the virtues; vicāra — analysis.

TRANSLATION

Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has previously elaborately described this. That which is clear need not be scrutinized for good qualities and faults.
