Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.80

'ihāń vishnu-pāda-padme gańgāra utpatti'

virodhālańkāra ihā mahā-camatkriti

SYNONYMS

ihāń — in this connection; vishnu-pāda-padme — in the lotus feet of Lord Vishnu; gańgāra — of mother Ganges; utpatti — beginning; virodha — contradiction; alańkāra — literary decoration; iha — it; mahā — very great; camatkriti — wonder.

TRANSLATION

"The existence of mother Ganges begins from the lotus feet of the Lord. Although this statement that water comes from a lotus flower is a contradiction, in connection with Lord Vishnu it is a great wonder.
