Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.12

svapne eka vipra kahe, — śunaha tapana

nimāñi-pandita pāśe karaha gamana

SYNONYMS

svapne — in a dream; eka — one; vipra — brāhmana; kahe — says; śunaha — just hear; tapana — Tapana Miśra; nimāñi-pandita — Nimāi Pandita; pāśe — unto Him; karaha gamana — go.

TRANSLATION

Tapana Miśra, being thus bewildered, was directed by a brāhmana in a dream to go to Nimāi Pandita [Caitanya Mahāprabhu].
