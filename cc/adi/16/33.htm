Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.33

prabhu kahe, vyākarana padāi — abhimāna kari

śishyete nā bujhe, āmi bujhāite nāri

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — the Lord replied; vyākarana padāi — yes, I teach grammar; abhimāna kari — I am supposed to do so; śishyete — amongst My disciples; nā — do not; bujhe — understand; āmi — I also; bujhāite — to make them understand; nāri — am not able.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "Yes, I am known as a teacher of grammar, but factually I cannot impress My students with grammatical knowledge, nor can they understand Me very well.

PURPORT

Since Keśava Kāśmīrī was a little puffed up, the Lord increased his artificial pride by presenting Himself as subordinate to him. Thus He flattered him as follows.
