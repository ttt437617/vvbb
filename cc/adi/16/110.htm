Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.110

caitanya-gosāñira līlā — amritera dhāra

sarvendriya tripta haya śravane yāhāra

SYNONYMS

caitanya-gosāñira līlā — the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; amritera dhāra — drops of nectar; sarva-indriya — all senses; tripta — satisfied; haya — become; śravane — by hearing; yāhāra — of them all.

TRANSLATION

The nectarean drops of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes can satisfy the senses of everyone who hears them.
