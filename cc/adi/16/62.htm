Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.62

'bhavānī-bhartri'-śabda dile pāiyā santosha

'viruddha-mati-krit' nāma ei mahā dosha

SYNONYMS

bhavānī-bhartri śabda — the word bhavānī-bhartri ("the husband of Bhavānī"); dile — you have placed; pāiyā — getting; santosha — very much satisfaction; viruddha-mati-krit — a statement of opposing elements; nāma — named; ei — this; mahā — great; dosha — fault.

TRANSLATION

"Here is another great fault. You have arranged the word 'bhavānī-bhartri' to your great satisfaction, but this betrays the fault of contradiction.
