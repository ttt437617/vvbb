Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.17

tāhāń āmā-sańge tomāra habe daraśana

ājñā pāñā miśra kaila kāśīte gamana

SYNONYMS

tāhāń — there; āmā-sańge — with Me; tomāra — your; habe — there will be; daraśana — meeting; ājñā pāñā — receiving this order; miśra — Tapana Miśra; kaila — did; kāśīte — to Benares; gamana — going.

TRANSLATION

The Lord assured Tapana Miśra that they would meet again in Vārānasī. Receiving this order, Tapana Miśra went there.
