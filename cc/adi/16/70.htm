Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.70

sundara śarīra yaiche bhūshane bhūshita

eka śveta-kushthe yaiche karaye vigīta

SYNONYMS

sundara — beautiful; śarīra — body; yaiche — as; bhūshane — with ornaments; bhūshita — decorated; eka — one; śveta-kushthe — with a white spot of leprosy; yaiche — as; karaye — is made; vigīta — abominable.

TRANSLATION

"One's beautiful body may be decorated with jewels, but one spot of white leprosy makes the entire body abominable.
