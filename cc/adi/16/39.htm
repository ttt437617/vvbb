Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.39

eka ślokera artha yadi kara nija-mukhe

śuni' saba loka tabe pāiba bada-sukhe

SYNONYMS

eka — one; ślokera — of a verse; artha — the meaning; yadi — if; kara — you do; nija-mukhe — by your own mouth; śuni' — hearing; saba — all; loka — persons; tabe — thereafter; pāiba — we shall get; bada-sukhe — with great happiness.

TRANSLATION

"But if you explain the meaning of one verse, we can all hear it from your own mouth and thus be very happy."
