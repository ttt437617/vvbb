Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.29

hena-kāle digvijayī tāhāńi āilā

gańgāre vandana kari' prabhure mililā

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; dig-vijayī — Keśava Kāśmīrī; tāhāńi — there; āilā — reached; gańgāre — to mother Ganges; vandana — prayers; kari' — offering; prabhure — the Lord; mililā — met.

TRANSLATION

Coincidentally, Keśava Kāśmīrī Pandita came there. While offering his prayers to mother Ganges, he met Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
