Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.83

gańgāra mahattva — sādhya, sādhana tāhāra

vishnu-pādotpatti — 'anumāna' alańkāra

SYNONYMS

gańgāra — of the Ganges; mahattva — opulences; sādhya — subject matter; sādhana — means; tāhāra — of that; vishnu-pāda-utpatti — her origin from the lotus feet of the Lord; anumāna — called anumāna (hypothesis); alańkāra — an ornament.

TRANSLATION

"The real glory of mother Ganges is that she has grown from the lotus feet of Lord Vishnu. Such a hypothesis is another ornament, called anumāna.
