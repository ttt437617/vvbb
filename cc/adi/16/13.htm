Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.13

teńho tomāra sādhya-sādhana karibe niścaya

sākshāt īśvara teńho, — nāhika samśaya

SYNONYMS

teńho — He; tomāra — your; sādhya — objective of life; sādhana — process; karibe — will do; niścaya — ascertain; sākshāt — direct; īśvara — the Lord; teńho — He is; nāhika — there is no; samśaya — doubt.

TRANSLATION

"Because He is the Lord [īśvara]," the brāhmana told him, "undoubtedly He can give you proper direction."
