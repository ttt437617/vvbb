Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.60

'dvitīya' śabda — vidheya tāhā padila samāse

'lakshmīra samatā' artha karila vināśe

SYNONYMS

dvitīya śabda — the word dvitīya ("second"); vidheya — the unknown; tāhā — that; padila — joined; samāse — in the compound word; lakshmīra — with Lakshmī; samatā — equality; artha — meaning; karila — became; vināśe — lost.

TRANSLATION

"Because the word 'dvitīya' ['second'] is the unknown, in its combination in this compound word the intended meaning of equality with Lakshmī is lost.
