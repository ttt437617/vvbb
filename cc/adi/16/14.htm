Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.14

svapna dekhi' miśra āsi' prabhura carane

svapnera vrittānta saba kaila nivedane

SYNONYMS

svapna dekhi' — by seeing the dream; miśra — Tapana Miśra; āsi' — coming; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the shelter of the lotus feet; svapnera — of the dream; vrittānta — details; saba — all; kaila — did; nivedane — inform Him.

TRANSLATION

After seeing the dream, Tapana Miśra came to the shelter of Lord Caitanya's lotus feet, and he described all the details of the dream to the Lord.
