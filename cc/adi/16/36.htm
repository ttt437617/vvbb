Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.36

śuniyā brāhmana garve varnite lāgilā

ghatī eke śata śloka gańgāra varnilā

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing this; brāhmana — the pandita, Keśava Kāśmīrī; garve — in pride; varnite — to describe; lāgilā — began; ghatī — hour; eke — one; śata — hundred; śloka — verses; gańgāra — of the Ganges; varnilā — described.

TRANSLATION

When the brāhmana, Keśava Kāśmīrī, heard this, he became still more puffed up, and within one hour he composed one hundred verses describing mother Ganges.
