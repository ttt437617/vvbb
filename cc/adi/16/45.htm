Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.45

ślokera artha kaila vipra pāiyā santosha

prabhu kahe — kaha ślokera kibā guna-dosha

SYNONYMS

ślokera — of the verse; artha — explanation; kaila — made; vipra — the brāhmana; pāiyā — obtaining; santosha — satisfaction; prabhu — the Lord; kahe — said; kaha — kindly speak; ślokera — of the verse; kibā — what are; guna — qualities; dosha — faults.

TRANSLATION

Satisfied by the statement of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the brāhmana [Keśava Kāśmīrī] explained the quoted verse. Then the Lord said, "Now kindly explain the special qualities and faults in the verse."

PURPORT

Not only did Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu pick out this one among the one hundred verses and remember it although the brāhmana had recited them like the blowing wind, but He also analyzed its qualities and faults. Not only did He hear the verse, but He immediately made a critical study of it.
