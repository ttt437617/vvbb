Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.5

śata śata śishya sańge sadā adhyāpana

vyākhyā śuni sarva-lokera camakita mana

SYNONYMS

śata śata — very many; śishya — disciples; sańge — along with Him; sadā — always; adhyāpana — studying; vyākhyā — explanation; śuni — hearing; sarva-lokera — of all people; camakita — astonished; mana — minds.

TRANSLATION

As soon as the Lord became a teacher, many, many students came to Him, every one of them astonished to hear His mode of explanation.
