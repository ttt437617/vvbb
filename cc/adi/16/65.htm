Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.65

'brāhmana-patnīra bhartāra haste deha dāna'

śabda śunitei haya dvitīya-bhartā jñāna

SYNONYMS

brāhmana-patnīra — of the wife of a brāhmana; bhartāra — of the husband; haste — in the hand; deha — give; dāna — charity; śabda — these words; śunitei — hearing; haya — there is; dvitīya-bhartā — another husband; jñāna — knowledge.

TRANSLATION

"If someone says, 'Place this charity in the hand of the husband of the wife of the brāhmana,' when we hear these contradictory words we immediately understand that the brāhmana's wife has another husband.
