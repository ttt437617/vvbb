Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.69

daśa alańkāre yadi eka śloka haya

eka doshe saba alańkāra haya kshaya

SYNONYMS

daśa alańkāre — with ten instances of literary ornamentation; yadi — if; eka — one; śloka — verse; haya — there is; eka doshe — by one fault; saba — all; alańkāra — ornaments; haya kshaya — become null and void.

TRANSLATION

"If there are ten literary ornaments in a verse but even one faulty expression, the entire verse is nullified.
