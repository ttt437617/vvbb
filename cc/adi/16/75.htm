Ādi-līlāChapter 16: The Pastimes of the Lord in His Childhood and Youth

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 16.75

caturtha-carane cāri 'bha'-kāra-prakāśa

ataeva śabdālańkāra anuprāsa

SYNONYMS

caturtha-carane — in the fourth line; cāri — four; bha-kāra — of the letter bha; prakāśa — manifestations; ataeva — therefore; śabda-alańkāra — ornamental use of different sounds; anuprāsa — alliteration.

TRANSLATION

"In the fourth line the letter 'bha' occurs four times. This arrangement of alliteration is a pleasing ornamental use of sounds.
