Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.17

ei dui janera sūtra dekhiyā śuniyā

varnanā karena vaishnava krama ye kariyā

SYNONYMS

ei — of these; dui — two; janera — persons; sūtra — notes; dekhiyā — after looking at; śuniyā — and hearing; varnanā — description; karena — does; vaishnava — the devotee; krama — chronological; ye — which; kariyā — making.

TRANSLATION

By seeing and hearing the notes recorded by these two great personalities, a Vaishnava, a devotee of the Lord, can know these pastimes one after another.
