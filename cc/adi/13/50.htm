Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.50

prabhura līlāmrita teńho kaila āsvādana

tāńra bhukta-śesha kichu kariye carvana

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of the Lord; līlāmrita — the nectar of the pastimes; teńho — he (Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura); kaila — did; āsvādana — taste; tāńra — his; bhukta — of food; śesha — remnants; kichu — something; kariye — I do; carvana — chew.

TRANSLATION

The transcendental pastimes of Lord Caitanya have actually been relished by Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura. I am simply trying to chew the remnants of food left by him.
