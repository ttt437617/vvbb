Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.59

jagannātha miśravara — padavī 'purandara'

nanda-vasudeva-rūpa sadguna-sāgara

SYNONYMS

jagannātha miśra-vara — Jagannātha Miśra, who was the chief among the seven; padavī — designation; purandara — another name of Vasudeva; nanda — Nanda, the father of Krishna; vasudeva — the father of Krishna; rūpa — like; sat-guna — good qualities; sāgara — ocean.

TRANSLATION

Jagannātha Miśra was designated as Purandara. Exactly like Nanda Mahārāja and Vasudeva, he was an ocean of all good qualities.
