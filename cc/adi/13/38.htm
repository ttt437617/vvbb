Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.38

tāra madhye chaya vatsara bhaktagana-sańge

prema-bhakti laoyāila nritya-gīta-rańge

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — out of that; chaya vatsara — six years; bhakta-gana-sańge — along with devotees; prema-bhakti — love of Krishna; laoyāila — induced; nritya — dancing; gīta — chanting; rańge — in transcendental bliss.

TRANSLATION

For six of the eighteen years He continuously stayed in Jagannātha Purī, He regularly performed kīrtana, inducing all the devotees to love Krishna simply by chanting and dancing.
