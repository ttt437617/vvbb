Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.62

asańkhya bhaktera karāilā avatāra

śeshe avatīrna hailā vrajendra-kumāra

SYNONYMS

asańkhya — unlimited; bhaktera — of devotees; karāilā — made into being; avatāra — incarnation; śeshe — at last; avatīrna — descended; hailā — became; vrajendra-kumāra — Lord Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna, Vrajendra-kumāra, first caused countless devotees to appear, and at last He appeared Himself.
