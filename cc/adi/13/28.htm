Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.28

pauganda-vayase padena, padāna śishyagane

sarvatra karena krishna-nāmera vyākhyāne

SYNONYMS

pauganda-vayase — in the age of pauganda; padena — studies; padāna — teaches; śishya-gane — disciples; sarvatra — everywhere; karena — does; krishna-nāmera — the holy name of Lord Krishna; vyākhyāne — description.

TRANSLATION

During His pauganda age He became a serious student and also taught disciples. In this way He used to explain the holy name of Krishna everywhere.
