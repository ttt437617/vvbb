Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.76

tāńhā ba-i viśve kichu nāhi dekhi āra

ataeva 'viśvarūpa' nāma ye tāńhāra

SYNONYMS

tāńhā ba-i — except Him; viśve — within this cosmic manifestation; kichu — something; nāhi — there is none; dekhi — I see; āra — further; ataeva — therefore; viśvarūpa — universal form; nāma — name; ye — that; tāńhāra — His.

TRANSLATION

The gigantic universal form is called the Viśvarūpa incarnation of Mahā-sańkarshana. Thus we do not find anything within this cosmic manifestation except the Lord Himself.
