Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.33

cabbiśa vatsara aiche navadvīpa-grāme

laoyāilā sarva-loke krishna-prema-nāme

SYNONYMS

cabbiśa — twenty-four; vatsara — years; aiche — in that way; navadvīpa — Navadvīpa; grāme — in the village; laoyāilā — induced; sarva-loke — every man; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; nāme — in the holy name.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu lived in the Navadvīpa area for twenty-four years, and He induced every person to chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra and thus merge in love of Krishna.
