Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.71

krishnera āhvāna kare saghana huńkāra

huńkāre ākrishta hailā vrajendra-kumāra

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; āhvāna — invitation; kare — does; saghana — with great gravity; huńkāra — vibration; huńkāre — and by such loud cries; ākrishta — attracted; hailā — became; vrajendra-kumāra — the son of Vrajendra, Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

By loud cries He invited Krishna to appear, and this repeated invitation attracted Lord Krishna to descend.
