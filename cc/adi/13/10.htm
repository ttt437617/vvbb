Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.10

cabbiśa vatsara prabhu kaila griha-vāsa

nirantara kaila krishna-kīrtana-vilāsa

SYNONYMS

cabbiśa — twenty-four; vatsara — years; prabhu — the Lord; kaila — did; griha-vāsa — residing at home; nirantara — always; kaila — did; krishna-kīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; vilāsa — pastimes.

TRANSLATION

For twenty-four years Lord Caitanya lived in the grihastha-āśrama [household life], always engaging in the pastimes of the Hare Krishna movement.
