Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.48

caitanya-līlāra vyāsa, — dāsa vrindāvana

madhura kariyā līlā karilā racana

SYNONYMS

caitanya-līlāra — of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; vyāsa — the authorized writer Vyāsadeva; dāsa vrindāvana — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; madhura — sweet; kariyā — making it; līlā — pastimes; karilā — did; racana — compilation.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura, the authorized writer of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, is as good as Śrīla Vyāsadeva. He has described the pastimes in such a way as to make them sweeter and sweeter.
