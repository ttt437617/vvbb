Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.16

prabhura ye śesha-līlā svarūpa-dāmodara

sūtra kari' granthilena granthera bhitara

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of the Lord; ye — whatever; śesha-līlā — pastimes at the end; svarūpa-dāmodara — Svarūpa Dāmodara; sūtra kari' — in the form of notes; granthilena — recorded; granthera — a book; bhitara — within.

TRANSLATION

His later pastimes [the madhya-līlā and antya-līlā] were recorded in the form of notes by His secretary, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, and thus kept within a book.
