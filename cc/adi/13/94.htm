Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.94

jagat bhariyā loka bale — 'hari' 'hari'

sei-kshane gaurakrishna bhūme avatari

SYNONYMS

jagat — the whole world; bhariyā — fulfilling; loka — people; bale — said; hari hari — the holy name of the Lord; sei-kshane — at that time; gaurakrishna — Lord Krishna in the form of Gaurahari; bhūme — on the earth; avatari — advented.

TRANSLATION

When the whole world was thus chanting the holy name of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna in the form of Gaurahari advented Himself on the earth.
