Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.68

lokera nistāra-hetu karena cintana

kemate e saba lokera ha-ibe tārana

SYNONYMS

lokera — of all people; nistāra-hetu — for the matter of deliverance; karena — does; cintana — contemplation; kemate — how; e — these; saba — all; lokera — of people in general; ha-ibe — will become; tārana — liberation.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the condition of the world, He began to think seriously of how all these people could be delivered from the clutches of māyā.
