Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.25

'gaurahari' bali' tāre hāse sarva nārī

ataeva haila tāńra nāma 'gaurahari'

SYNONYMS

gaurahari — Gaurahari; bali' — addressing Him thus; tāre — unto the Lord; hāse — laugh; sarva nārī — all the ladies; ataeva — therefore; haila — became; tāńra — His; nāma — name; gaurahari — Gaurahari.

TRANSLATION

When all the ladies saw this fun, they enjoyed laughing and called the Lord "Gaurahari." From then on, Gaurahari became another of His names.
