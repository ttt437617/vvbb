Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.53

āge avatārilā ye ye guru-parivāra

sańkshepe kahiye, kahā nā yāya vistāra

SYNONYMS

āge — first of all; avatārilā — allowed to descend; ye ye — all those; guru-parivāra — family of spiritual masters; sańkshepe — in brief; kahiye — I describe; kahā — to describe; nā — not; yāya — possible; vistāra — expansively.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna therefore first allowed His family of superiors to descend to the earth. I shall try to describe them in brief because it is not possible to describe them fully.
