Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.36

setubandha, āra gauda-vyāpi vrindāvana

prema-nāma pracāriyā karilā bhramana

SYNONYMS

setubandha — Cape Comorin; āra — and; gauda — Bengal; vyāpi — extending; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; prema-nāma — love of Krishna and the holy name of Krishna; pracāriyā — distributing; karilā — performed; bhramana — touring.

TRANSLATION

Beginning from Cape Comorin and extending through Bengal to Vrindāvana, during these six years He toured all of India, chanting, dancing and distributing love of Krishna.
