Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.23

bālya-bhāva chale prabhu karena krandana

'krishna' 'hari' nāma śuni' rahaye rodana

SYNONYMS

bālya-bhāva chale — as if in His childhood state; prabhu — the Lord; karena — does; krandana — crying; krishna — Lord Krishna; hari — Lord Hari; nāma — names; śuni' — hearing; rahaye — stops; rodana — crying.

TRANSLATION

In His childhood, when the Lord was crying He would stop immediately upon hearing the holy names Krishna and Hari.
