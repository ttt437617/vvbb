Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.40

rātri-divase krishna-viraha-sphurana

unmādera ceshtā kare pralāpa-vacana

SYNONYMS

rātri-divase — day and night; krishna-viraha — feelings of separation from Krishna; sphurana — awakening; unmādera — of a madman; ceshtā — activities; kare — performs; pralāpa — talking inconsistently; vacana — words.

TRANSLATION

Day and night Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt separation from Krishna. Manifesting symptoms of this separation, He cried and talked very inconsistently, like a madman.
