Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.73

apatya-virahe miśrera duhkhī haila mana

putra lāgi' ārādhila vishnura carana

SYNONYMS

apatya — of children; virahe — in separation; miśrera — of Jagannātha Miśra; duhkhī — unhappy; haila — became; mana — mind; putra — son; lāgi' — for the matter of; ārādhila — worshiped; vishnura — of Lord Vishnu; carana — lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Jagannātha Miśra was very unhappy at the death of his children one after another. Therefore, desiring a son, he worshiped the lotus feet of Lord Vishnu.
