Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.26

bālya vayasa — yāvat hāte khadi dila

pauganda vayasa — yāvat vivāha nā kaila

SYNONYMS

bālya vayasa — childhood age; yāvat — until the time; hāte — in His hand; khadi — chalk; dila — was given; pauganda vayasa — the part of boyhood known as pauganda; yāvat — until; vivāha — marriage; nā — not; kaila — did take place.

TRANSLATION

His childhood lasted until the date of hāte khadi, the beginning of His education, and His age from the end of His childhood until He married is called pauganda.
