Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.9

caudda-śata sāta śake janmera pramāna

caudda-śata pañcānne ha-ila antardhāna

SYNONYMS

caudda-śata-sata — 1407; śake — in the Śaka Era; janmera — of birth; pramāna — evidence; caudda-śata pañcānne — in the year 1455; ha-ila — became; antardhāna — disappearance.

TRANSLATION

In the year 1407 of the Śaka Era (A.D. 1486), Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared, and in the year 1455 (A.D. 1534) He disappeared from this world.
