Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.75

baladeva-prakāśa — parama-vyome 'sańkarshana'

teńha — viśvera upādāna-nimitta-kārana

SYNONYMS

baladeva-prakāśa — manifestation of Baladeva; parama-vyome — in the spiritual sky; sańkarshana — Sańkarshana; teńha — He; viśvera — the cosmic manifestation; upādāna — ingredient; nimitta-kārana — immediate cause.

TRANSLATION

The expansion of Baladeva known as Sańkarshana in the spiritual world is the ingredient and immediate cause of this material cosmic manifestation.
