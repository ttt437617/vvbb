Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.31

kiśora vayase ārambhilā sańkīrtana

rātra-dine preme nritya, sańge bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

kiśora vayase — just before the beginning of His youthful life; ārambhilā — began; sańkīrtana — the sańkīrtana movement; rātra-dine — night and day; preme — in ecstasy; nritya — dancing; sańge — along with; bhakta-gana — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

Just prior to His youthful life, He began the sańkīrtana movement. Day and night He used to dance in ecstasy with His devotees.
