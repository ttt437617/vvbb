Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.21

'hari' 'hari' bale loka harashita hañā

janmilā caitanya-prabhu 'nāma' janmāiyā

SYNONYMS

hari hari — the holy names of the Lord; bale — speak; loka — the people; harashita — jubilant; hañā — becoming; janmilā — took birth; caitanya-prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāma — the holy name; janmāiyā — after causing to appear.

TRANSLATION

In jubilation everyone was chanting the holy name of the Lord — "Hari! Hari!" — and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then appeared, after first causing the appearance of the holy name.
