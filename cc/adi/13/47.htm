Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.47

sei, anusāre likhi līlā-sūtragana

vistāri' varniāchena tāhā dāsa-vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

sei — that; anusāre — following; likhi — I write; līlā — pastimes; sūtra-gana — notes; vistāri' — very explicitly; varniyāchena — has described; tāhā — that; dāsa-vrindāvana — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura.

TRANSLATION

The notes kept by Śrī Svarūpa Dāmodara and Murāri Gupta are the basis of this book. Following those notes, I write of all the pastimes of the Lord. The notes have been described elaborately by Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura.
