Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.12

tāra madhye chaya vatsara — gamanāgamana

kabhu dakshina, kabhu gauda, kabhu vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — out of that; chaya vatsara — six years; gamana-āgamana — touring; kabhu — sometimes; dakshina — in South India; kabhu — sometimes; gauda — in Bengal; kabhu — sometimes; vrindāvana — in Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Of these last twenty-four years, He spent the first six continuoually touring India, sometimes in South India, sometimes in Bengal and sometimes in Vrindāvana.
