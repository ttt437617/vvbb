Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.88

nīlāmbara cakravartī kahila ganiyā

ei māse putra habe śubha-kshana pāñā

SYNONYMS

nīlāmbara cakravartī — Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; kahila — said; ganiyā — by astrological calculation; ei māse — in this month; putra — son; habe — will take birth; śubha-kshana — auspicious moment; pāñā — taking advantage of.

TRANSLATION

Nīlāmbara Cakravartī [the grandfather of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu] then did an astrological calculation and said that in that very month, taking advantage of an auspicious moment, the child would take birth.
