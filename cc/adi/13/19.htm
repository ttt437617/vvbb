Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.19

sarva-sad-guna-pūrnām tām

vande phālguna-pūrnimām

yasyām śrī-krishna-caitanyo

'vatīrnah krishna-nāmabhih

SYNONYMS

sarva — all; sat — auspicious; guna — qualities; pūrnām — filled with; tām — that; vande — I offer obeisances; phālguna — of the month of Phālguna; pūrnimām — the full-moon evening; yasyām — in which; śrī-krishna-caitanyah — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avatīrnah — advented; krishna — Lord Krishna's; nāmabhih — with the chanting of the holy names.

TRANSLATION

I offer my respectful obeisances unto the full-moon evening in the month of Phālguna, an auspicious time full of auspicious symptoms, when Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu advented Himself with the chanting of the holy name, Hare Krishna.
