Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.52

kona vāñchā pūrana lāgi' vrajendra-kumāra

avatīrna haite mane karilā vicāra

SYNONYMS

kona — some; vāñchā — desire; pūrana — fulfillment; lāgi' — for the matter of; vrajendra-kumāra — Lord Krishna; avatīrna haite — to descend as an incarnation; mane — in the mind; karilā — did; vicāra — consideration.

TRANSLATION

To fulfill a particular desire within His mind, Lord Krishna, Vrajendra-kumāra, decided to descend to this planet after mature contemplation.
