Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.4

jaya dāmodara-svarūpa jaya murāri gupta

ei saba candrodaye tamah kaila lupta

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; dāmodara-svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; jaya — all glories; murāri gupta — Murāri Gupta; ei saba — of all these; candra-udaye — such moons having arisen; tamah — darkness; kaila — made; lupta — dissipated.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Svarūpa Dāmodara and Murāri Gupta! All these brilliant moons have together dissipated the darkness of this material world.
