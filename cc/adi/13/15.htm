Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.15

ādi-līlā-madhye prabhura yateka carita

sūtra-rūpe murāri gupta karilā grathita

SYNONYMS

ādi-līlā — the original pastimes; madhye — within; prabhura — of the Lord; yateka — whatever; carita — activities; sūtra-rūpe — in the form of notes; murāri gupta — Murāri Gupta; karilā — has; grathita — recorded.

TRANSLATION

All the pastimes enacted by Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in His ādi-līlā were recorded in summary form by Murāri Gupta.
