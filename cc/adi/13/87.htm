Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.87

haite haite haila garbha trayodaśa māsa

tathāpi bhūmishtha nahe, — miśrera haila trāsa

SYNONYMS

haite haite — thus becoming; haila — it so became; garbha — pregnancy; trayodaśa — thirteenth; māsa — month; tathāpi — still; bhūmishtha — delivery; nahe — there was no sign; miśrera — of Jagannātha Miśra; haila — became; trāsa — apprehension.

TRANSLATION

In this way the pregnancy approached its thirteenth month, but still there was no sign of the delivery of the child. Thus Jagannātha Miśra became greatly apprehensive.
