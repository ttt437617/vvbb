Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.8

śrī-krishna-caitanya navadvīpe avatari

āta-calliśa vatsara prakata vihari

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; navadvīpe — at Navadvīpa; avatari — adventing Himself; āta-calliśa — forty-eight; vatsara — years; prakata — visible; vihari — enjoying.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, adventing Himself in Navadvīpa, was visible for forty-eight years, enjoying His pastimes.
