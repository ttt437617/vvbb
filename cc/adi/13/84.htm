Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.84

jagannātha miśra kahe, — svapna ye dekhila

jyotirmaya-dhāma mora hridaye paśila

SYNONYMS

jagannātha miśra kahe — Jagannātha Miśra replied; svapna — dream; ye — that; dekhila — I have seen; jyotir-maya — with a brilliant effulgence; dhāma — abode; mora — my; hridaye — in the heart; paśila — entered.

TRANSLATION

Jagannātha Miśra then replied, "In a dream I saw the effulgent abode of the Lord enter my heart.
