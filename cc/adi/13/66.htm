Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.66

tāńra sańge ānanda kare vaishnavera gana

krishna-kathā, krishna-pūjā, nāma-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

tāńra sańge — with Him (Advaita Ācārya); ānanda — pleasure; kare — takes; vaishnavera — of the devotees; gana — assembly; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; krishna-pūjā — worship of Krishna; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.

TRANSLATION

In the house of Advaita Ācārya, all the Vaishnavas took pleasure in always talking of Krishna, always worshiping Krishna and always chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.

PURPORT

On these principles only does the Krishna consciousness movement go on. We have no business other than to talk of Krishna, worship Krishna and chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
