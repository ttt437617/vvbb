Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.64

gītā-bhhāgavata kahe ācārya-gosāñi

jñāna-karma nindi' kare bhaktira badāi

SYNONYMS

gītā — the Bhagavad-gītā; bhāgavata — Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; kahe — recites; ācārya-gosāñi — Advaita Ācārya; jñāna — the path of philosophical speculation; karma — fruitive activity; nindi' — decrying; kare — establishes; bhaktira — of devotional service; badāi — excellence.

TRANSLATION

In these meetings of the Vaishnavas, Advaita Ācārya used to recite the Bhagavad-gītā and Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, decrying the paths of philosophical speculation and fruitive activity and establishing the superexcellence of devotional service.
