Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.72

jagannāthamiśra-patnī śacīra udare

ashta kanyā krame haila, janmi' janmi' mare

SYNONYMS

jagannātha-miśra — Jagannātha Miśra; patnī — his wife; śacīra — of Śacīmātā; udare — within the womb; ashta — eight; kanyā — daughters; krame — one after another; haila — appeared; janmi' — after taking birth; janmi' — after taking birth; mare — all died.

TRANSLATION

Before the birth of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, eight daughters took birth one after another from the womb of Śacīmātā, the wife of Jagannātha Miśra. But just after their birth, they all died.
