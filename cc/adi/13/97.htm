Ādi-līlāChapter 13: The Advent of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 13.97

prasanna haila daśa dik, prasanna nadījala

sthāvara-jańgama haila ānande vihvala

SYNONYMS

prasanna — jubilant; haila — became; daśa — ten; dik — directions; prasanna — satisfied; nadī-jala — the water of the rivers; sthāvara — immovable; jańgama — movable; haila — became; ānande — in joy; vihvala — overwhelmed.

TRANSLATION

In this atmosphere, all the ten directions became jubilant, as did the waves of the rivers. Moreover, all beings, moving and nonmoving, were overwhelmed with transcendental bliss.
