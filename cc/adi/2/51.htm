Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.51

hiranya-garbhera ātmā garbhodaka-śāyī

vyashti-jīva-antaryāmī kshīrodaka-śāyī

SYNONYMS

hiranya-garbhera — of the total of the living entities; ātmā — the Supersoul; garbha-udaka-śāyī — Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu; vyashti — the individual; jīva — of the living entity; antah-yāmī — Supersoul; kshīra-udaka-śāyī — Kshīrodakaśāyī Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

"Garbhodakaśāyī Vishnu is the Supersoul of the aggregate of living entities, and Kshīrodakaśāyī Vishnu is the Supersoul of each individual living being.
