Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.90

taiche saba avatārera krishna se kārana

āra eka śloka śuna, kuvyākhyā-khandana

SYNONYMS

taiche — in a similar way; saba — all; avatārera — of the incarnations; krishna — Lord Krishna; se — He; kārana — the cause; āra — another; eka — one; śloka — verse; śuna — please hear; ku-vyākhyā — fallacious explanations; khandana — refuting.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna, in the same way, is the cause of all causes and all incarnations. Please hear another verse to defeat all misinterpretations.
