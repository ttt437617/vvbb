Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.105

yadyapi brahmānda-ganera purusha āśraya

sei purushādi sabhāra krishna mūlāśraya

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; brahma-anda-ganera — of the multitude of universes; purusha — the purusha-avatāra; āśraya — the shelter; sei — that; purusha-ādi — of the purusha-avatāras, etc.; sabhāra — of the assembly; krishna — Lord Krishna; mūla-āśraya — original source.

TRANSLATION

"Although the three purushas are the shelter of all the universes, Lord Krishna is the original source of the purushas.
