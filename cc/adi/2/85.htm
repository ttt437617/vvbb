Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.85

nārāyana amśī yei svayam-bhagavān

teńha śrī-krishna — aiche karita vyākhyāna

SYNONYMS

nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; amśī — the source of all incarnations; yei — who; svayam-bhagavān — Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; teńha — He; śrī-krishna — Lord Krishna; aiche — in such away; karita — would have made; vyākhyāna — explanation.

TRANSLATION

"Thus he would have said, 'Nārāyana, the source of all incarnations, is the original Personality of Godhead. He has appeared as Śrī Krishna.'
