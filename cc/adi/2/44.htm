Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.44

ithe yata jīva, tāra trai-kālika karma

tāhā dekha, sākshī tumi, jāna saba marma

SYNONYMS

ithe — in these; yata — as many; jīva — living beings; tāra — of them; trai-kālika — past, present and future; karma — the activities; tāhā — that; dekha — You see; sākshi — witness; tumi — You; jāna — You know; saba — of everything; marma — the essence.

TRANSLATION

"Both in this material world and in the transcendental world, You see all the deeds of all living beings, in the past, present and future. Since You are the witness of all such deeds, You know the essence of everything.
