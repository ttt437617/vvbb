Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.69

tabe sūta gosāñi mane pāñā bada bhaya

yāra ye lakshana tāhā karila niścaya

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; sūta gosāñi — Sūta Gosvāmī; mane — in the mind; pāñā — obtaining; bada — great; bhaya — fear; yāra — of whom; ye — which; lakshana — symptoms; tāhā — that; karila — he made; niścaya — certainly.

TRANSLATION

This made Sūta Gosvāmī greatly apprehensive. Therefore he distinguished each incarnation by its specific symptoms.
