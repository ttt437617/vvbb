Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.23

para-vyomete vaise nārāyana nāma

shad-aiśvarya-pūrna lakshmī-kānta bhagavān

SYNONYMS

para-vyomete — in the transcendental world; vaise — sits; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; nāma — of the name; shat-aiśvarya — of six kinds of opulences; pūrna — full; lakshmī-kānta — the husband of the goddess of opulence; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nārāyana, who dominates the transcendental world, is full in six opulences. He is the Personality of Godhead, the Lord of the goddess of fortune.
