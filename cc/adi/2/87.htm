Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.87

viruddhārtha kaha tumi, kahite kara rosha

tomāra arthe avimrishta-vidheyāmśa-dosha

SYNONYMS

viruddha-artha — contrary meaning; kaha — say; tumi — you; kahite — pointing out; kara — you do; rosha — anger; tomāra — your; arthe — in the meaning; avimrishta-vidheya-amśa — of the unconsidered predicate portion; dosha — the fault.

TRANSLATION

"You say something contradictory and become angry when this is pointed out. Your explanation has the defect of a misplaced object. This is an unconsidered adjustment.
