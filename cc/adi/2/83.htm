Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.83

krishnera svayam-bhagavattā — ihā haila sādhya

svayam-bhagavānera krishnatva haila bādhya

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svayam-bhagavattā — the quality of being Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; ihā — this; haila — was; sādhya — to be established; svayam-bhagavānera — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishnatva — the quality of being Lord Krishna; haila — was; bādhya — obligatory

TRANSLATION

"This establishes that Śrī Krishna is the original Personality of Godhead. The original Personality of Godhead is therefore necessarily Krishna.
