Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.62

ei-mate nānā-rūpa kare pūrva-paksha

tāhāre nirjite bhāgavata-padya daksha

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — thus; nānā — many; rūpa — forms; kare — takes; pūrva-paksha — the objections; tāhāre — them; nirjite — overcoming; bhāgavata — of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; padya — poetry; daksha — expert.

TRANSLATION

In this way their arguments appear in various forms, but the poetry of the Bhāgavatam expertly refutes them all.
