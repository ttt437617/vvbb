Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.34

krishna kahena — brahmā, tomāra pitā nārāyana

āmi gopa, tumi kaiche āmāra nandana

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kahena — says; brahmā — O Lord Brahmā; tomāra — your; pitā — father; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; āmi — I (am); gopa — cowherd boy; tumi — you; kaiche — how; āmāra — My; nandana — son.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Krishna said, "O Brahmā, your father is Nārāyana. I am but a cowherd boy. How can you be My son?"
