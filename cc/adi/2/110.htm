Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.110

ataeva caitanya gosāñi paratattva-sīmā

tāńre kshīroda-śāyī kahi, ki tāńra mahimā

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; caitanya gosāñi — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; para-tattva-sīmā — the highest limit of the Absolute Truth; tāńre — Him; kshīroda-śāyī — Kshīrodakaśāyī Vishnu; kahi — if I say; ki — what; tāńra — of Him; mahimā — glory.

TRANSLATION

Therefore Lord Caitanya is the Supreme Absolute Truth. To call Him Kshīrodakaśāyī Vishnu does not add to His glory.
