Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.120

caitanya-gosāñira ei tattva-nirūpana

svayam-bhagavān krishna vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

caitanya-gosāñira — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — this; tattva — of the truth; nirūpana — settling; svayam-bhagavān — Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna — Lord Krishna; vrajendra-nandana — the son of the King of Vraja.

TRANSLATION

The conclusion is that Lord Caitanya is the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna, the son of the King of Vraja.
