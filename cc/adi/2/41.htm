Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.41

ataeva adhīśvara tumi sarva pitā

tomāra śaktite tāńrā jagat-rakshitā

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; adhīśvara — primeval Lord; tumi — You; sarva — of all; pitā — father; tomāra — Your; śaktite — by the energy; tāńrā — They; jagat — of the cosmic creations; rakshitā — protectors.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore You are the primeval Lord, the original father of everyone. They [the purushas] are protectors of the universes by Your power.
