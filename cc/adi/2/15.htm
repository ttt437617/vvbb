Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.15

kotī kotī brahmānde ye brahmera vibhūti

sei brahma govindera haya ańga-kānti

SYNONYMS

kotī — tens of millions; kotī — tens of millions; brahma-ande — in universes; ye — which; brahmera — of Brahman; vibhūti — opulences; sei — that; brahma — Brahman; govindera — of Lord Govinda; haya — is; ańga-kānti — bodily effulgence.

TRANSLATION

[Lord Brahmā said:] "The opulences of the impersonal Brahman spread throughout the millions and millions of universes. That Brahman is but the bodily effulgence of Govinda."
