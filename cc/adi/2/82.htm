Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.82

ataeva 'krishna'-śabda āge anuvāda

'svayam-bhagavattā' piche vidheya-samvāda

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; krishna-śabda — the word krishna; āge — first; anuvāda — the subject; svayam-bhagavattā — being Himself the Supreme Personality of Godhead; piche — afterwards; vidheya — of the predicate; samvāda — the message

TRANSLATION

"Therefore first the word 'krishna' appears as the subject, followed by the predicate, describing Him as the original Personality of Godhead.
