Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.58

ataeva brahma-vākye — paravyoma-nārāyana

teńho krishnera vilāsa — ei tattva-vivarana

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; brahma — of Lord Brahmā; vākye — in the speech; para-vyoma — in the spiritual sky; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; teńho — He; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; vilāsa — pastime incarnation; ei — this; tattva — of the truth; vivarana — description.

TRANSLATION

Therefore according to the authority of Brahmā, the Nārāyana who is the predominating Deity in the transcendental world is but the vilāsa feature of Krishna. This has now been conclusively proved.
