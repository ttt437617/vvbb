Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.88

yāńra bhagavattā haite anyera bhagavattā

'svayam-bhagavān'-śabdera tāhātei sattā

SYNONYMS

yāńra — of whom; bhagavattā — the quality of being the Supreme Personality of Godhead; haite — from; anyera — of others; bhagavattā — the quality of being the Supreme Personality of Godhead; svayam-bhagavān-śabdera — of the word svayam-bhagavān; tāhātei — in that; sattā — the presence.

TRANSLATION

"Only the Personality of Godhead, the source of all other Divinities, is eligible to be designated svayam bhagavān, or the primeval Lord.
