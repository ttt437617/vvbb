Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.31

śiśu vatsa hari' brahmā kari aparādha

aparādha kshamāite māgena prasāda

SYNONYMS

śiśu — playmates; vatsa — calves; hari' — stealing; brahmā — Lord Brahmā; kari — making; aparādha — offense; aparādha — offense; kshamāite — to pardon; māgena — begged; prasāda — mercy.

TRANSLATION

After Brahmā had offended Krishna by stealing His playmates and calves, he begged the Lord's pardon for his offensive act and prayed for the Lord's mercy.
