Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.20

atha vā bahunaitena

kim jñātena tavārjuna

vishtabhyāham idam kritsnam

ekāmśena sthito jagat

SYNONYMS

atha vā — or; bahunā — much; etena — with this; kim — what use; jñātena — being known; tava — by you; arjuna — O Arjuna; vishtabhya — pervading; aham — I; idam — this; kritsnam — entire; eka-amśena — with one portion; sthitah — situated; jagat — universe.

TRANSLATION

[The Personality of Godhead, Śrī Krishna, said:] "What more shall I say to you? I live throughout this cosmic manifestation merely by My single plenary portion."

PURPORT

Describing His own potencies to Arjuna, the Personality of Godhead Śrī Krishna spoke this verse of the Bhagavad-gītā (10.42).
