Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.57

sei tinera amśī paravyoma-nārāyana

teńha tomāra vilāsa, tumi mūla-nārāyana

SYNONYMS

sei — these; tinera — of the three; amśī — source; para-vyoma — in the spiritual sky; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; teńha — He; tomāra — Your; vilāsa — pastime expansion; tumi — You; mūla — original; nārāyana — Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

"The source of these three features is the Nārāyana in the spiritual sky. He is Your vilāsa expansion. Therefore You are the ultimate Nārāyana."
