Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.106

svayam bhagavān krishna, krishna sarvāśraya

parama īśvara krishna sarva-śāstre kaya

SYNONYMS

svayam — Himself; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna — Lord Krishna; krishna — Lord Krishna; sarva-āśraya — the shelter of all; parama — Supreme; īśvara — Lord; krishna — Lord Krishna; sarva-śāstre — all scriptures; kaya — say.

TRANSLATION

"Thus the Personality of Godhead Śrī Krishna is the original, primeval Lord, the source of all other expansions. All the revealed scriptures accept Śrī Krishna as the Supreme Lord.
