Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.109

sei krishna avatārī vrajendra-kumāra

āpane caitanya-rūpe kaila avatāra

SYNONYMS

sei — that; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatārī — the source of all incarnations; vrajendra-kumāra — the son of the King of Vraja; āpane — personally; caitanya-rūpe — in the form of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — made; avatāra — incarnation.

TRANSLATION

That same Lord Krishna, the fountainhead of all incarnations, is known as the son of the King of Vraja. He has descended personally as Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
