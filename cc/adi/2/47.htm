Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.47

krishna kahena — brahmā, tomāra nā bujhi vacana

jīva-hridi, jale vaise sei nārāyana

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kahena — says; brahmā — O Brahmā; tomāra — your; nā — not; bujhi — I understand; vacana — speech; jīva — of the living entity; hridi — in the heart; jale — in the water; vaise — sits; sei — that; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

Krishna said, "Brahmā, I cannot understand what you are saying. Lord Nārāyana is He who sits in the hearts of all living beings and lies down in the waters of the Kārana Ocean."
