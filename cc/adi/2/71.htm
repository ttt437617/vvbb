Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.71

pūrva-paksha kahe — tomāra bhāla ta' vyākhyāna

paravyoma-nārāyana svayam-bhagavān

SYNONYMS

pūrva-paksha — opposing side; kahe — says; tomāra — your; bhāla — nice; ta' — certainly; vyākhyāna — exposition; para-vyoma — situated in the spiritual sky; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; svayam — Himself; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

An opponent may say, "This is your interpretation, but actually the Supreme Lord is Nārāyana, who is in the transcendental realm.
