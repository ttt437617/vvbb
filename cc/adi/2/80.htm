Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.80

'ete'-śabde avatārera āge anuvāda

'purushera amśa' pāche vidheya-samvāda

SYNONYMS

ete-śabde — in the word ete (these); avatārera — of the incarnations; āge — first; anuvāda — the subject; purushera — of the purusha-avatāras; amśa — plenary portions; pāche — afterwards; vidheya — of the predicate; samvāda — message.

TRANSLATION

"First the word 'ete' ['these'] establishes the subject [the incarnations]. Then 'plenary portions of the purusha-avatāras' follows as the predicate.
