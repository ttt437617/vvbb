Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.50

sei tina jala-śāyī sarva-antaryāmī

brahmānda-vrindera ātmā ye purusha-nāmī

SYNONYMS

sei — these; tina — three; jala-śāyī — lying in the water; sarva — of all; antah yāmī — the Supersoul; brahma-anda — of universes; vrindera — of the multitude; ātmā — Supersoul; ye — who; purusha — purusha; nāmī — named.

TRANSLATION

"These three Vishnus lying in the water are the Supersoul of everything. The Supersoul of all the universes is known as the first purusha.
