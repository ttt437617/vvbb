Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.81

taiche krishna avatāra-bhitare haila jñāta

tāńhāra viśesha-jñāna sei avijñāta

SYNONYMS

taiche — in the same way; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatāra-bhitare — among the incarnations; haila — was; jñāta — known; tāńhāra — of Him; viśesha-jñāna — specific knowledge; sei — that; avijñāta — unknown.

TRANSLATION

"In the same way, when Krishna was first counted among the incarnations, specific knowledge about Him was still unknown.
