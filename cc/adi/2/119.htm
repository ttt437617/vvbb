Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.119

caitanya-prabhura mahimā kahibāra tare

krishnera mahimā kahi kariyā vistāre

SYNONYMS

caitanya-prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahimā — the glories; kahibāra tare — for the purpose of speaking; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mahimā — the glories; kahi — I speak; kariyā — doing; vistāre — in expansion.

TRANSLATION

Just to enunciate the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, I have tried to describe the glories of Śrī Krishna in detail.
