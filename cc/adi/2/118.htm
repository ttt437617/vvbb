Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.118

caitanya-mahimā jāni e saba siddhānte

citta dridha hañā lāge mahimā-jñāna haite

SYNONYMS

caitanya-mahimā — the glory of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jāni — I know; e — these; saba — all; siddhānte — by the conclusions; citta — the mind; dridha — firm; hañā — becoming; lāge — becomes fixed; mahimā-jñāna — knowledge of the greatness; haite — from.

TRANSLATION

By such conclusive studies I know the glories of Lord Caitanya. Only by knowing these glories can one become strong and fixed in attachment to Him.

PURPORT

One can know the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu only by reaching, in knowledge, a conclusive decision about Śrī Krishna, strengthened by bona fide study of the conclusions of the ācāryas.
