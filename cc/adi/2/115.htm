Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.115

keho kahe, para-vyome nārāyana hari

sakala sambhave krishne, yāte avatārī

SYNONYMS

keho — someone; kahe — says; para-vyome — in the transcendental world; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; hari — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sakala sambhave — all possibilities; krishne — in Lord Krishna; yāte — since; avatārī — the source of all incarnations.

TRANSLATION

Some call Him Hari, or the Nārāyana of the transcendental world. Everything is possible in Krishna, for He is the primeval Lord.
