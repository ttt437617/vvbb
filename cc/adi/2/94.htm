Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.94

krishna eka sarvāśraya, krishna sarva-dhāma

krishnera śarīre sarva-viśvera viśrāma

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; eka — one; sarva-āśraya — shelter of all; krishna — Lord Krishna; sarva-dhāma — the abode of all; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; śarīre — in the body; sarva-viśvera — of all the universes; viśrāma — resting place.

TRANSLATION

"The Personality of Godhead Śrī Krishna is the shelter and abode of everything. All the universes rest in His body.
