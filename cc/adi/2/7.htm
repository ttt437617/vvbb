Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.7

anuvāda āge, pāche vidheya sthāpana

sei artha kahi, śuna śāstra-vivarana

SYNONYMS

anuvāda — the subject; āge — first; pāche — afterwards; vidheya — the predicate; sthāpana — placing; sei — this; artha — the meaning; kahi — I speak; śuna — please listen; śāstra-vivarana — to the description of the scriptures.

TRANSLATION

A predicate always follows its subject. Now I shall explain the meaning of this verse according to the revealed scriptures.
