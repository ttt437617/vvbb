Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.84

krishna yadi amśa haita, amśī nārāyana

tabe viparīta haita sūtera vacana

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — if; amśa — plenary portion; haita — were; amśī — the source of all expansions; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; tabe — then; viparīta — the reverse; haita — would have been; sūtera — of Sūta Gosvāmī; vacana — the statement.

TRANSLATION

"Had Krishna been the plenary portion and Nārāyana the primeval Lord, the statement of Sūta Gosvāmī would have been reversed.
