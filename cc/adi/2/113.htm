Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.113

krishnake kahaye keha — nara-nārāyana

keho kahe, krishna haya sākshāt vāmana

SYNONYMS

krishnake — Lord Krishna; kahaye — says; keha — someone; nara-nārāyana — Nara-Nārāyana; keho — someone; kahe — says; krishna — Lord Krishna; haya — is; sākshāt — directly; vāmana — Lord Vāmana.

TRANSLATION

Some say that Śrī Krishna is directly Nara-Nārāyana. Others say that He is directly Vāmana.
