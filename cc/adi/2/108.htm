Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.108

e saba siddhānta tumi jāna bhāla-mate

tabu pūrva-paksha kara āmā cālāite

SYNONYMS

e — these; saba — all; siddhānta — conclusions; tumi — you; jāna — know; bhāla-mate — in a good way; tabu — still; pūrva-paksha — objection; kara — you make; āmā — to me; cālāite — to give useless anxiety.

TRANSLATION

"You know all the conclusions of the scriptures very well. You create these logical arguments just to agitate me."

PURPORT

A learned man who has thoroughly studied the scriptures cannot hesitate to accept Śrī Krishna as the Supreme Personality of Godhead. If such a man argues about this matter, certainly he must be doing so to agitate the minds of his opponents.
