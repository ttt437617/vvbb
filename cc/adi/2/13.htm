Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.13

carma-cakshe dekhe yaiche sūrya nirviśesha

jñāna-mārge laite nāre krishnera viśesha

SYNONYMS

carma-cakshe — by the naked eye; dekhe — one sees; yaiche — just as; sūrya — the sun; nirviśesha — without variegatedness; jñāna-mārge — by the path of philosophical speculation; laite — to accept; nāre — not able; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; viśesha — the variety.

TRANSLATION

As with the naked eye one cannot know the sun except as a glowing substance, merely by philosophical speculation one cannot understand Lord Krishna's transcendental varieties.
