Ādi-līlāChapter 2: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Is the Supreme Personality of Godhead

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 2.4

tritīya ślokera artha kari vivarana

vastu-nirdeśa-rūpa mańgalācarana

SYNONYMS

tritīya — third; ślokera — of the verse; artha — the meaning; kari — I do; vivarana — description; vastu — of the Absolute Truth; nirdeśa-rūpa — in the form of delineation; mańgala — auspicious; ācarana — conduct.

TRANSLATION

Let me describe the meaning of the third verse [of the first fourteen]. It is an auspicious vibration that describes the Absolute Truth.
