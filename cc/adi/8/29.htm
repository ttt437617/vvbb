Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.29-30

hena krishna-nāma yadi laya bahu-bāra

tabu yadi prema nahe, nahe aśrudhāra

tabe jāni, aparādha tāhāte pracura

krishna-nāma-bīja tāhe nā kare ańkura

SYNONYMS

hena — such; krishna-nāma — holy name of the Lord; yadi — if; laya — one takes; bahu-bāra — again and again; tabu — still; yadi — if; prema — love of Godhead; nahe — is not visible; nahe aśru-dhāra — there are no tears in the eyes; tabe — then; jāni — I understand; aparādha — offense; tāhāte — there (in that process); pracura — enough; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; bīja — seed; tāhe — in those activities; nā — does not; kare — do; ańkura — sprout.

TRANSLATION

If one chants the exalted holy name of the Lord again and again and yet his love for the Supreme Lord does not develop and tears do not appear in his eyes, it is evident that because of his offenses in chanting, the seed of the holy name of Krishna does not sprout.

PURPORT

If one chants the Hare Krishna mantra offensively, one does not achieve the desired result. Therefore one should carefully avoid the offenses, which have already been described in connection with verse 24.
