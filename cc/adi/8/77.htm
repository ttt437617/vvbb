Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.77

ājñā-mālā pāñā āmāra ha-ila ānanda

tāhāńi karinu ei granthera ārambha

SYNONYMS

ājñā-mālā — the garland of order; pāñā — receiving; āmāra — my; ha-ila — became; ānanda — great pleasure; tāhāńi — then and there; karinu — attempted; ei — this; granthera — of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita; ārambha — beginning.

TRANSLATION

I was greatly pleased to have the garland signifying the order of the Lord, and then and there I commenced to write this book.
