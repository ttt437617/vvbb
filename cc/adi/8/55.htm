Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.55

suśīla, sahishnu, śānta, vadānya, gambhīra

madhura-vacana, madhura-ceshtā, mahā-dhīra

SYNONYMS

suśīla — well behaved; sahishnu — tolerant; śānta — peaceful; vadānya — magnanimous; gambhīra — grave; madhura-vacana — sweet words; madhura-ceshtā — sweet endeavor; mahā-dhīra — completely sober.

TRANSLATION

He was gentle, tolerant, peaceful, magnanimous, grave, sweet in his words and very sober in his endeavors.
