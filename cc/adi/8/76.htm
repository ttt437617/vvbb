Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.76

saba vaishnava-gana hari-dhvani dila

gosāñi-dāsa āni' mālā mora gale dila

SYNONYMS

saba — all; vaishnava — devotees; gana — group; hari-dhvani — chanting Hare Krishna; dila — made; gosāñi-dāsa — Gosāñi dāsa; āni' — bringing; mālā — garland; mora — my; gale — on the neck; dila — gave it.

TRANSLATION

As soon as this happened, the Vaishnavas standing there all loudly chanted, "Haribol!" and the priest, Gosāñi dāsa, brought me the garland and put it around my neck.
