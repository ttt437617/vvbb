Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.69

tāńra śishya — govinda pūjaka caitanya-dāsa

mukundānanda cakravartī, premī krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

tāńra śishya — his disciple; govinda — the Govinda Deity; pūjaka — priest; caitanya-dāsa — Caitanya dāsa; mukundānanda cakravartī — Mukundānanda Cakravartī; premī — a great lover of God; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa.

TRANSLATION

Among his disciples were Caitanya dāsa, who was a priest of the Govinda Deity, as well as Mukundānanda Cakravartī and the great devotee Krishnadāsa.
