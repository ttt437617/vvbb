Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.83

mūrkha, nīca, kshudra muñi vishaya-lālasa

vaishnavājñā-bale kari eteka sāhasa

SYNONYMS

mūrkha — foolish; nīca — lowborn; kshudra — very insignificant; muñi — I; vishaya — material; lālasa — desires; vaishnava — of the Vaishnavas; ājñā — order; bale — on the strength of; kari — I do; eteka — so much; sāhasa — energy.

TRANSLATION

I am foolish, lowborn and insignificant, and I always desire material enjoyment; yet by the order of the Vaishnavas I am greatly enthusiastic to write this transcendental literature.
