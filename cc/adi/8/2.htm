Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.2

jaya jaya śrī-krishna-caitanya gauracandra

jaya jaya paramānanda jaya nityānanda

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gaura-candra — whose name is Lord Gaurāńga; jaya jaya — all glories; paramānanda — most joyful; jaya — all glories; nityānanda — unto Nityānanda Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is known as Gaurasundara. I also offer my respectful obeisances unto Nityānanda Prabhu, who is always very joyful.
