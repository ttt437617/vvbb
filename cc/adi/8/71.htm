Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.71

āra yata vrindāvane baise bhakta-gana

śesha-līlā śunite sabāra haila mana

SYNONYMS

āra yata — there are many others; vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; baise — residents; bhakta-gana — great devotees; śesha-līlā — the last portions of Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes; śunite — to hear; sabāra — of everyone; haila — became; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

In Vrindāvana there were also many other great devotees, all of whom desired to hear the last pastimes of Lord Caitanya.
