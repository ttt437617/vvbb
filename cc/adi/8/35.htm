Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.35

vrindāvana-dāsa kaila 'caitanya-mańgala'

yāńhāra śravane nāśe sarva amańgala

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-dāsa — Vrindāvana dāsa; kaila — compiled; caitanya-mańgala — the book named Caitanya-mańgala; yāńhāra — of which; śravane — by hearing; nāśe — annihilated; sarva — all; amańgala — inauspiciousness.

TRANSLATION

Thākura Vrindāvana dāsa has composed Śrī Caitanya-mańgala. Hearing this book annihilates all misfortune.
