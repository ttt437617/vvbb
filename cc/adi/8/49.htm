Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.49

sei saba līlāra śunite vivarana

vrindāvana-vāsī bhaktera utkanthita mana

SYNONYMS

sei — those; saba — all; līlāra — of the pastimes; śunite — to hear; vivarana — description; vrindāvana-vāsī — the inhabitants of Vrindāvana; bhaktera — of devotees; utkanthita — in anxiety; mana — minds.

TRANSLATION

The devotees of Vrindāvana were all very anxious to hear those pastimes.
