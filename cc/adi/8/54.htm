Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.54

sevāra adhyaksha — śrī-pandita haridāsa

tāńra yaśah-guna sarva-jagate prakāśa

SYNONYMS

sevāra — of the service; adhyaksha — commander; śrī-pandita haridāsa — Haridāsa Pandita; tāńra — of his; yaśah — fame; guna — quality; sarva-jagate — all over the world; prakāśa — known.

TRANSLATION

In that temple the chief servitor was Śrī Haridāsa Pandita. His qualities and fame are known all over the world.

PURPORT

Śrī Haridāsa Pandita was a disciple of Śrī Ananta Ācārya, who was a disciple of Gadādhara Pandita.
