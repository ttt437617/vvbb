Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.64

kathāya sabhā ujjvala kare yena pūrna-candra

nija-gunāmrite bādāya vaishnava-ānanda

SYNONYMS

kathāya — by words; sabhā — assembly; ujjvala — illuminated; kare — does; yena — as; pūrna-candra — full moon; nija — own; guna-amrite — nectar of qualities; bādāya — increases; vaishnava — of the devotees; ānanda — pleasure.

TRANSLATION

Like the full moon, he illuminated the entire assembly of Vaishnavas by speaking Śrī Caitanya-mańgala, and by the nectar of his qualities he increased their transcendental bliss.
