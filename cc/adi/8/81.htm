Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.81

vrindāvana-dāsera pāda-padma kari' dhyāna

tāńra ājñā lañā likhi yāhāte kalyāna

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-dāsera — of Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; pāda-padma — lotus feet; kari' — doing; dhyāna — meditation; tāńra — his; ājñā — order; lañā — receiving; likhi — I write; yāhāte — in which permission; kalyāna — all auspiciousness.

TRANSLATION

I took permission from Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura by praying at his lotus feet, and upon receiving his order I have attempted to write this auspicious literature.

PURPORT

Śrīla Krishnadāsa Kavirāja Gosvāmī took permission not only from the Vaishnavas and Madana-mohana but also from Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura, who is understood to be the Vyāsa of the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
