Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.48

nityānanda-līlā-varnane ha-ila āveśa

caitanyera śesha-līlā rahila avaśesha

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; līlā — pastimes; varnane — in the matter of description; ha-ila — there was; āveśa — ecstasy; caitanyera — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha-līlā — pastimes in the last portion of His life; rahila — remained; avaśesha — supplement.

TRANSLATION

He ecstatically described the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda, but the later pastimes of Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained untold.
