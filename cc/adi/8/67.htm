Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.67

yādavācārya gosāñi śrī-rūpera sańgī

caitanya-carite teńho ati bada rańgī

SYNONYMS

yādavācārya — Yādavācārya; gosāñi — spiritual master; śrī-rūpera — of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; sańgī — associate; caitanya-carite — in the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; teńho — he; ati — very; bada — great; rańgī — enthusiastic.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Yādavācārya Gosāñi, a constant associate of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, was also very enthusiastic in hearing and chanting about Lord Caitanya's pastimes.
