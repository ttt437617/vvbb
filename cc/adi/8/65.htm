Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.65

teńho ati kripā kari' ājñā kailā more

gaurāńgera śesha-līlā varnibāra tare

SYNONYMS

teńho — he; ati — very much; kripā — mercy; kari' — showing; ājñā — order; kailā — made it; more — unto me; gaurāńgera — of Lord Caitanya; śesha-līlā — last portion of the pastimes; varnibāra — describing; tare — for the matter of.

TRANSLATION

By his causeless mercy he ordered me to write about the last pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
