Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.51

tāte vasi' āche sadā vrajendra-nandana

'śrī-govinda-deva' nāma sākshāt madana

SYNONYMS

tāte — on that throne; vasi' — sitting; āche — there is; sadā — always; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Mahārāja Nanda; śrī-govinda-deva — whose name is Govinda; nāma — name; sākshāt — direct; madana — transcendental Cupid.

TRANSLATION

On that throne sits the son of Nanda Mahārāja, Śrī Govindadeva, the transcendental Cupid.
