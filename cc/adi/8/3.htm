Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.3

jaya jayādvaita ācārya kripāmaya

jaya jaya gadādhara pandita mahāśaya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; advaita — unto Advaita Prabhu; ācārya — teacher; kripāmaya — very merciful; jaya jaya — all glories to; gadādhara — Gadādhara; pandita — learned scholar; mahāśaya — great personality.

TRANSLATION

Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Advaita Ācārya, who is very merciful, and also to that great personality Gadādhara Pandita, the learned scholar.
