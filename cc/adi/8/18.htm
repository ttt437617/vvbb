Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.18

krishna yadi chute bhakte bhukti mukti diyā

kabhu prema-bhakti nā dena rākhena lukāiyā

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — of course; chute — goes away; bhakte — unto the devotee; bhukti — material enjoyment; mukti — liberation; diyā — offering; kabhu — at any time; prema-bhakti — love of Godhead; nā — does not; dena — give; rākhena — keeps; lukāiyā — hiding.

TRANSLATION

If a devotee wants liberation or material sense gratification from the Lord, Krishna immediately delivers it, but pure devotional service He keeps hidden.
