Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.47

vistāra dekhiyā kichu sańkoca haila mana

sūtra-dhrita kona līlā nā kaila varnana

SYNONYMS

vistāra — expansive; dekhiyā — seeing; kichu — some; sańkoca — with hesitation; haila — became; mana — mind; sūtra-dhrita — taking the synopsis; kona — some; līlā — pastimes; nā — did not; kaila — make; varnana — description.

TRANSLATION

He saw them to be so extensive that he later felt that some had not been properly described.
