Ādi-līlāChapter 8: The Author Receives the Orders of Krishna and Guru

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 8.34

krishna-līlā bhāgavate kahe veda-vyāsa

caitanya-līlāra vyāsa — vrindāvana-dāsa

SYNONYMS

krishna-līlā — the pastimes of Lord Krishna; bhāgavate — in the book Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; kahe — tells; veda-vyāsa — Vyāsadeva, the editor of the Vedic literatures; caitanya-līlāra — of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; vyāsa — compiler; vrindāvana-dāsa — is Vrindāvana dāsa.

TRANSLATION

As Vyāsadeva has compiled all the pastimes of Lord Krishna in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, Thākura Vrindāvana dāsa has depicted the pastimes of Lord Caitanya.
