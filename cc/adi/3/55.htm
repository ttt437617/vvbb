Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.55

krishna-varna-śabdera artha dui ta pramāna

krishna vinu tāńra mukhe nāhi āise āna

SYNONYMS

krishna-varna-śabdera — of the word krishna-varna; artha — the meaning; dui — two; ta — certainly; pramāna — examples; krishna — Krishna; vinu — except for; tāńra — of Him; mukhe — in the mouth; nāhi āise — does not come; āna — anything else.

TRANSLATION

These are two meanings of the word "krishna-varna." Indeed, nothing else but Krishna issues from His mouth.
