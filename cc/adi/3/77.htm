Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.77

sańkīrtana-pravartaka śrī-krishna-caitanya

sańkīrtana-yajñe tāńre bhaje, sei dhanya

SYNONYMS

sańkīrtana-pravartaka — the initiator of congregational chanting; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sańkīrtana — of congregational chanting; yajñe — by the sacrifice; tāńre — Him; bhaje — worships; sei — he; dhanya — fortunate.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya is the initiator of sańkīrtana [congregational chanting of the holy name of the Lord]. One who worships Him through sańkīrtana is fortunate indeed.
