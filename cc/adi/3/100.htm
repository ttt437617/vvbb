Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.100

nāma vinu kali-kāle dharma nāhi āra

kali-kāle kaiche habe krishna avatāra

SYNONYMS

nāma vinu — except for the holy name; kali-kāle — in the Age of Kali; dharma — religion; nāhi — there is not; āra — another; kali-kāle — in the Age of Kali; kaiche — how; habe — there will be; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatāra — incarnation.

TRANSLATION

"In this Age of Kali there is no religion other than the chanting of the holy name of the Lord, but how in this age will the Lord appear as an incarnation?
