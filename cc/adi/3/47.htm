Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.47

ei saba guna lañā muni vaiśampāyana

sahasra-nāme kaila tāńra nāma-ganana

SYNONYMS

ei — these; saba — all; guna — qualities; lañā — taking; muni — the sage; vaiśampāyana — named Vaiśampāyana; sahasra-nāme — in the Vishnu-sahasra-nāma; kaila — did; tāńra — of Him; nāma-ganana — counting of the name.

TRANSLATION

Recording all these qualities of Lord Caitanya, the sage Vaiśampāyana included His name in the Vishnu-sahasra-nāma.
