Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.108

gańgā-jala, tulasī-mañjarī anukshana

krishna-pāda-padma bhāvi' kare samarpana

SYNONYMS

gańgā-jala — the water of the Ganges; tulasī-mañjarī — buds of the tulasī plant; anukshana — constantly; krishna — of Lord Krishna; pāda-padma — lotus feet; bhāvi' — thinking of; kare — does; samarpana — offering.

TRANSLATION

Thinking of the lotus feet of Śrī Krishna, He constantly offered tulasī buds in water from the Ganges.
