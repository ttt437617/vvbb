Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.42

dairghya-vistāre yei āpanāra hāta

cāri hasta haya 'mahā-purusha' vikhyāta

SYNONYMS

dairghya — in length; vistāre — and in breadth; yei — who; āpanāra — of his own; hāta — hand; cāri — four; hasta — cubits; haya — is; mahā-purusha — as a great personality; vikhyāta — celebrated.

TRANSLATION

One who measures four cubits in height and in breadth by his own hand is celebrated as a great personality.
