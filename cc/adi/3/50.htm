Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.50

vyakta kari' bhāgavate kahe bāra bāra

kali-yuge dharma — nāma-sańkīrtana sāra

SYNONYMS

vyakta — evident; kari' — making; bhāgavate — in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; kahe — they say; bāra bāra — time and time again; kali-yuge — in the Age of Kali; dharma — the religion; nāma-sańkīrtana — congregational chanting of the holy name; sāra — the essence.

TRANSLATION

In Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam it is repeatedly and clearly said that the essence of religion in the Age of Kali is the chanting of the holy name of Krishna.
