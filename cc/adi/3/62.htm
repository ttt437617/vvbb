Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.62

bāhu tuli' hari bali' prema-drishtye cāya

kariyā kalmasha nāśa premete bhāsāya

SYNONYMS

bāhu tuli' — raising the arms; hari bali' — chanting the holy name; prema-drishtye — with His glance of deep love; cāya — He looks; kariyā — causing; kalmasha — to sins; nāśa — destruction; premete — in love of God; bhāsāya — He floods.

TRANSLATION

Raising His arms, chanting the holy name and looking upon all with deep love, He drives away all sins and floods everyone with love of Godhead.
