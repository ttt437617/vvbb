Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.46

candanera ańgada-bālā, candana-bhūshana

nritya-kāle pari' karena krishna-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

candanera — of sandalwood; ańgada — and armlets; bālā — bangles; candana — of sandalwood pulp; bhūshana — decorations; nritya-kāle — at the time of dancing; pari' — putting on; karena — does; krishna-sańkīrtana — congregational chanting of the name of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

He is decorated with sandalwood bangles and armlets and anointed with the pulp of sandalwood. He especially wears these decorations to dance in śrī-krishna-sańkīrtana.
