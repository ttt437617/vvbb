Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.28

tāhāte āpana bhakta-gana kari' sańge

prithivīte avatari' karimu nānā rańge

SYNONYMS

tāhāte — in that; āpana — My own; bhakta-gana — with devotees; kari' — doing; sańge — in association; prithivīte — on the earth; avatari' — descending; karimu — I shall perform; nānā — various; rańge — colorful pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore in the company of My devotees I shall appear on earth and perform various colorful pastimes."
