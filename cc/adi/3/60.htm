Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.60

jīvera kalmasha-tamo nāśa karibāre

ańga-upāńga-nāma nānā astra dhare

SYNONYMS

jīvera — of the living entity; kalmasha — of sinful activities; tamah — the darkness; nāśa karibāre — for destroying; ańga — associates; upāńga — devotees; nāma — holy names; nānā — various; astra — weapons; dhare — He holds.

TRANSLATION

The sinful life of the living beings results from ignorance. To destroy that ignorance, He has brought various weapons, such as His plenary associates, His devotees and the holy name.
