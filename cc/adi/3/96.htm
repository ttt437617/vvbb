Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.96

prakatiyā dekhe ācārya sakala samsāra

krishna-bhakti gandha-hīna vishaya-vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

prakatiyā — manifesting; dekhe — He saw; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; sakala — all; samsāra — material existence; krishna-bhakti — of devotion to Lord Krishna; gandha-hīna — without a trace; vishaya — of the sense objects; vyavahāra — affairs.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya having appeared, He found the world devoid of devotional service to Śrī Krishna because people were engrossed in material affairs.
