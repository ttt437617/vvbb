Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.109

krishnera āhvāna kare kariyā huńkāra

e-mate krishnere karāila avatāra

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; āhvāna — invitation; kare — makes; kariyā — making; huńkāra — loud shouts; e-mate — in this way; krishnere — Lord Krishna; karāila — caused to make; avatāra — incarnation.

TRANSLATION

He appealed to Śrī Krishna with loud calls and thus made it possible for Krishna to appear.
