Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.65

anya avatāre saba sainya-śastra sańge

caitanya-krishnera sainya ańga-upāńge

SYNONYMS

anya — other; avatāre — in incarnations; saba — all; sainya — soldiers; śastra — and weapons; sańge — along with; caitanya-krishnera — of Lord Krishna as Lord Caitanya; sainya — soldiers; ańga — plenary parts; upāńge — and associates.

TRANSLATION

In other incarnations the Lord descended with armies and weapons, but in this incarnation His soldiers are His plenary parts and associates.
