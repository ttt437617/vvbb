Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.85

pratyakshe dekhaha nānā prakata prabhāva

alaukika karma, alaukika anubhāva

SYNONYMS

pratyakshe — directly; dekhaha — just see; nānā — various; prakata — manifested; prabhāva — influence; alaukika — uncommon; karma — activities; alaukika — uncommon; anubhāva — realizations in Krishna consciousness.

TRANSLATION

One can also directly see Lord Caitanya's manifest influence in His uncommon deeds and uncommon Krishna conscious realization.
