Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.37

śukla, rakta, pīta-varna — ei tina dyuti

satya-tretā-kali-kāle dharena śrī-pati

SYNONYMS

śukla — white; rakta — red; pīta-varna — the color yellow; ei — these; tina — three; dyuti — lusters; satya — in Satya-yuga; tretā — in Tretā-yuga; kali-kāle — in the Age of Kali; dharena — manifests; śrī-pati — the husband of the goddess of fortune.

TRANSLATION

White, red and yellow — these are the three bodily lusters that the Lord, the husband of the goddess of fortune, assumes in the ages of Satya, Tretā and Kali respectively.
