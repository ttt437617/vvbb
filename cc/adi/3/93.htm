Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.93

krishna yadi prithivīte karena avatāra

prathame karena guru-vargera sañcāra

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — if; prithivīte — on the earth; karena — makes; avatāra — incarnation; prathame — first; karena — makes; guru-vargera — of the group of respectable predecessors; sañcāra — the advent.

TRANSLATION

Whenever Śrī Krishna desires to manifest His incarnation on earth, first He creates the incarnations of His respectable predecessors.
