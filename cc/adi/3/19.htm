Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.19

yuga-dharma pravartāimu nāma-sańkīrtana

cāri bhāva-bhakti diyā nācāmu bhuvana

SYNONYMS

yuga-dharma — the religion of the age; pravartāimu — I shall inaugurate; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy name; cāri — four; bhāva — of the moods; bhakti — devotion; diyā — giving; nācāmu — I shall cause to dance; bhuvana — the world.

TRANSLATION

"I shall personally inaugurate the religion of the age — nāma-sańkīrtana, the congregational chanting of the holy name. I shall make the world dance in ecstasy, realizing the four mellows of loving devotional service.
