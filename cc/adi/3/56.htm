Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.56

keha tāńre bale yadi krishna-varana

āra viśeshane tāra kare nivārana

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; tāńre — to Him; bale — ascribes; yadi — if; krishna — black; varana — the color; āra — another; viśeshane — in the adjective; tāra — of that; kare — does; nivārana — prevention.

TRANSLATION

If someone tries to describe Him as being of blackish complexion, the next adjective [tvishā akrishnam] immediately restricts him.
