Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.53

śuna, bhāi, ei saba caitanya-mahimā

ei śloke kahe tāńra mahimāra sīmā

SYNONYMS

śuna — please hear; bhāi — O brothers; ei — this; saba — all; caitanya — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahimā — the glories; ei — this; śloke — verse; kahe — says; tāńra — of Him; mahimāra — of the glories; sīmā — the limit.

TRANSLATION

My dear brothers, please hear all these glories of Lord Caitanya. This verse clearly summarizes His activities and characteristics.
