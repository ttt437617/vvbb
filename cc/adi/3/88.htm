Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.88

āpanā lukāite krishna nānā yatna kare

tathāpi tāńhāra bhakta jānaye tāńhāre

SYNONYMS

āpanā — Himself; lukāite — to hide; krishna — Lord Krishna; nānā — various; yatna — efforts; kare — makes; tathāpi — still; tāńhāra — His; bhakta — devotees; jānaye — know; tāńhāre — Him.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna tries to hide Himself in various ways, but nevertheless His pure devotees know Him as He is.
