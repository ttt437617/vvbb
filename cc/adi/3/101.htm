Ādi-līlāChapter 3: The External Reasons for the Appearance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 3.101

śuddha-bhāve kariba krishnera ārādhana

nirantara sadainye kariba nivedana

SYNONYMS

śuddha-bhāve — in a purified state of mind; kariba — I shall do; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; ārādhana — worship; nirantara — constantly; sa-dainye — in humility; kariba — I shall make; nivedana — request.

TRANSLATION

"I shall worship Krishna in a purified state of mind. I shall constantly petition Him in humbleness.
