Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.18

krishna-miśra-nāma āra ācārya-tanaya

caitanya-gosāñi baise yāńhāra hridaya

SYNONYMS

krishna-miśra — Krishna Miśra; nāma — of the name; āra — and; ācārya-tanaya — the son of Advaita Ācārya; caitanya-gosāñi — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; baise — sits; yāńhāra — in whose; hridaya — heart.

TRANSLATION

Krishna Miśra was a son of Advaita Ācārya. Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu always sat in his heart.
