Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.23

duhkhita ha-ilā ācārya putra kole lañā

rakshā kare nrisimhera mantra padiyā

SYNONYMS

duhkhita — unhappy; ha-ilā — became; ācārya — Advaita Prabhu; putra — His son; kole — on the lap; lañā — taking; rakshā — protection; kare — does; nrisimhera — of Lord Nrisimha; mantra — the hymn; padiyā — by chanting.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya Prabhu became very unhappy. Taking His son on His lap, He began to chant the Nrisimha mantra for his protection.
