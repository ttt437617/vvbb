Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.48

śuniyā prabhura mana prasanna ha-ila

duńhāra antara-kathā duńhe se jānila

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing this; prabhura — of Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mana — mind; prasanna — satisfaction; ha-ila — felt; duńhāra — of both of Them; antara-kathā — confidential talks; duńhe — both of Them; se — that; jānila — could understand.

TRANSLATION

When Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this, His mind was satisfied. Only They could understand each other's minds.
