Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.53

ei śikshā sabākāre, sabe mane kaila

ācārya-gosāñi mane ānanda pāila

SYNONYMS

ei — this; śikshā — instruction; sabākāre — for all; sabe — all present; mane — in the mind; kaila — took it; ācārya-gosāñi — Advaita Ācārya; mane — within the mind; ānanda — pleasure; pāila — felt.

TRANSLATION

When Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave this instruction to Kamalākānta, all present considered it to be meant for everyone. Thus Advaita Ācārya was greatly pleased.
