Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.94

gaura-līlāmrita-sindhu — apāra agādha

ke karite pāre tāhāń avagāha-sādha

SYNONYMS

gaura-līlāmrita-sindhu — the ocean of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya; apāra — immeasurable; agādha — unfathomable; ke — who; karite — to do; pāre — is able; tāhāń — in that ocean; avagāha — taking a dip; sādha — execution.

TRANSLATION

The ocean of the pastimes of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu is immeasurable and unfathomable. Who can have the courage to measure that great ocean?
