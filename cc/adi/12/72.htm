Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.72

ki pandita, ki tapasvī, kibā grihī, yati

caitanya-vimukha yei, tāra ei gati

SYNONYMS

ki pandita — whether a learned scholar; ki tapasvī — whether a great ascetic; kibā — or; grihī — householder; yati — or sannyāsī; caitanya-vimukha — one who is against the cult of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yei — anyone; tāra — his; ei — this; gati — destination.

TRANSLATION

Be he a learned scholar, a great ascetic, a successful householder or a famous sannyāsī, one who is against the cult of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is destined to suffer the punishment meted out by Yamarāja.
