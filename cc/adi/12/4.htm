Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.4

vrikshera dvitīya skandha — ācārya-gosāñi

tāńra yata śākhā ha-ila, tāra lekhā nāñi

SYNONYMS

vrikshera — of the tree; dvitīya skandha — the second big branch; ācārya-gosāñi — Śrī Advaita Ācārya Gosvāmī; tāńra — His; yata — all; śākhā — branches; ha-ila — became; tāra — of that; lekhā — description; nāñi — there is not.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Advaita Prabhu was the second big branch of the tree. There are many subbranches, but it is impossible to mention them all.
