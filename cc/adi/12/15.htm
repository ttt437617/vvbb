Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.15

jagad-gurute tumi kara aiche upadeśa

tomāra ei upadeśe nashta ha-ila deśa

SYNONYMS

jagat-gurute — on the spiritual master of the universe; tumi — You; kara — do; aiche — such; upadeśa — instruction; tomāra — Your; ei upadeśe — by this instruction; nashta — spoiled; ha-ila — will become; deśa — the country.

TRANSLATION

He told his father, "Your instruction that Keśava Bhāratī is the spiritual master of Caitanya Mahāprabhu will spoil the entire country.
