Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.32

kintu tāńra daive kichu ha-iyāche rina

rina śodhibāre cāhi tańkā śata-tina

SYNONYMS

kintu — but; tāńra — His; daive — in due course of time; kichu — some; ha-iyāche — there was; rina — debt; rina — debt; śodhibāre — to liquidate; cāhi — I want; tańkā — rupees; śata-tina — about three hundred.

TRANSLATION

But it also mentioned that Advaita Ācārya had recently incurred a debt of about three hundred rupees that Kamalākānta Viśvāsa wanted to liquidate.
