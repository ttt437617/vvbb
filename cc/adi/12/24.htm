Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.24

nānā mantra padena ācārya, nā haya cetana

ācāryera duhkhe vaishnava karena krandana

SYNONYMS

nānā — various; mantra — hymns; padena — chants; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; nā — not; haya — became; cetana — conscious; ācāryera — of Advaita Ācārya; duhkhe — in unhappiness; vaishnava — all the Vaishnavas; karena — do; krandana — cry.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya chanted various mantras, but Gopāla did not come to consciousness. Thus all the Vaishnavas present cried in sorrow at His plight.
