Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.2

jaya jaya mahāprabhu śrī-krishna-caitanya

jaya jaya nityānanda jayādvaita dhanya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; mahāprabhu — Mahāprabhu; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Śrī Krishna Caitanya; jaya jaya — all glories; nityānanda — to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; jaya advaita — all glories to Advaita Prabhu; dhanya — who are all very glorious.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! All glories to Lord Nityānanda! All glories to Śrī Advaita Prabhu! All of Them are glorious.
