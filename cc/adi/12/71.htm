Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.71

kevala e gana-prati nahe ei danda

caitanya-vimukha yei sei ta' pāshanda

SYNONYMS

kevala — only; e — this; gana — group; prati — unto them; nahe — it is not; ei — this; danda — punishment; caitanya-vimukha — against Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yei — anyone; sei — he; ta' — but; pāshanda — atheist.

TRANSLATION

Not only the misguided descendants of Advaita Ācārya but anyone who is against the cult of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu should be considered an atheist subject to be punished by Yamarāja.
