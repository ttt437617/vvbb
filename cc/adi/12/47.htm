Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.47

ācārya kahe, ihāke kene dile daraśana

dui prakārete kare more vidambana

SYNONYMS

ācārya kahe — Śrī Advaita Ācārya said; ihāke — unto him; kene — why; dile — You gave; daraśana — audience; dui — two; prakārete — in ways; kare — does; more — unto Me; vidambana — cheating.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya then said to Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Why have You called back this man and allowed him to see You? He has cheated Me in two ways."
