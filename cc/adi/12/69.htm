Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.69

kruddha hañā skandha tāre jala nā sañcāre

jalābhāve kriśa śākhā śukāiyā mare

SYNONYMS

kruddha hañā — being angry; skandha — the trunk; tāre — onto them; jala — water; nā — did not; sañcāre — sprinkle; jala-abhāve — for want of water; kriśa — thinner; śākhā — branch; śukāiyā — dried up; mare — died.

TRANSLATION

Thus Lord Caitanya did not sprinkle upon them the water of His mercy, and they gradually withered and died.
