Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.13

acyutānanda — bada śākhā, ācārya-nandana

ājanma sevilā teńho caitanya-carana

SYNONYMS

acyutānanda — Acyutānanda; bada śākhā — a big branch; ācārya-nandana — the son of Advaita Ācārya; ājanma — from the very beginning of life; sevilā — served; teńho — he; caitanya-carana — the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya.

TRANSLATION

A big branch of Advaita Ācārya was His son Acyutānanda. From the beginning of his life he engaged in the service of the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya.
