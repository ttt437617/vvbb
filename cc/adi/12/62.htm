Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.62

śrīvatsa pandita, brahmacārī haridāsa

purushottama brahmacārī, āra krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

śrīvatsa pandita — Śrīvatsa Pandita; brahmacārī haridāsa — Haridāsa Brahmacārī; purushottama brahmacārī — Purushottama Brahmacārī; āra — and; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa.

TRANSLATION

Śrīvatsa Pandita, Haridāsa Brahmacārī, Purushottama Brahmacārī and Krishnadāsa were the twenty-fifth, twenty-sixth, twenty-seventh and twenty-eighth branches of Advaita Ācārya.
