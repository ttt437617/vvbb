Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.63

purushottama pandita, āra raghunātha

vanamālī kavicandra, āra vaidyanātha

SYNONYMS

purushottama pandita — Purushottama Pandita; āra raghunātha — and Raghunātha; vanamālī kavicandra — Vanamālī Kavicandra; āra — and; vaidyanātha — Vaidyanātha.

TRANSLATION

Purushottama Pandita, Raghunātha, Vanamālī Kavicandra and Vaidyanātha were the twenty-ninth, thirtieth, thirty-first and thirty-second branches of Advaita Ācārya.
