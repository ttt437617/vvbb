Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.5

caitanya-mālīra kripā-jalera secane

sei jale pushta skandha bāde dine dine

SYNONYMS

caitanya-mālīra — of the gardener named Caitanya; kripā-jalera — of the water of His mercy; secane — by sprinkling; sei jale — by that water; pushta — nourished; skandha — branches; bāde — increased; dine dine — day after day.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also the gardener, and as He poured the water of His mercy on the tree, all the branches and subbranches grew, day after day.
