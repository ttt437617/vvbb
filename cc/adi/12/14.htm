Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.14

caitanya-gosāñira guru — keśava bhāratī

ei pitāra vākya śuni' duhkha pāila ati

SYNONYMS

caitanya — Lord Caitanya; gosāñira — the spiritual master; guru — His spiritual master; keśava bhāratī — Keśava Bhāratī; ei — these; pitāra — his father's; vākya — words; śuni' — hearing; duhkha — unhappiness; pāila — got; ati — very much.

TRANSLATION

When Acyutānanda heard from his father that Keśava Bhāratī was the spiritual master of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was very unhappy.
