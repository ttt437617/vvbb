Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.74

sei sei, — ācāryera kripāra bhājana

anāyāse pāila sei caitanya-carana

SYNONYMS

sei sei — whoever; ācāryera — of Advaita Ācārya; kripāra — of the mercy; bhājana — eligible candidate; anāyāse — without difficulty; pāila — got; sei — he; caitanya-carana — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

By the mercy of Advaita Ācārya, the devotees who strictly followed the path of Caitanya Mahāprabhu attained the shelter of Lord Caitanya's lotus feet without difficulty.
