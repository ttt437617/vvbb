Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.90

panditera gana saba, — bhāgavata dhanya

prāna-vallabha — sabāra śrī-krishna-caitanya

SYNONYMS

panditera — of Gadādhara Pandita; gana — followers; saba — all; bhāgavata dhanya — glorious devotees; prāna-vallabha — the heart and soul; sabāra — of all of them; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

All the followers of Gadādhara Pandita are considered great devotees because they have Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as their life and soul.
