Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.39

pūrve mahāprabhu more karena sammāna

duhkha pāi' mane āmi kailuń anumāna

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; mahāprabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; more — unto Me; karena — does; sammāna — respect; duhkha — unhappy; pāi' — becoming; mane — in the mind; āmi — I; kailuń — made; anumāna — a plan.

TRANSLATION

"Formerly Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu always respected Me as His senior, but I did not like such respect. Therefore, My mind being afflicted by unhappiness, I made a plan.
