Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.76

sei ācārya-gane mora koti namaskāra

acyutānanda-prāya, caitanya — jīvana yāńhāra

SYNONYMS

sei — those; ācārya-gane — unto the spiritual masters; mora — my; koti — millions; namaskāra — obeisances; acyutānanda-prāya — almost as good as Acyutānanda; caitanya — Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jīvana — life; yāńhāra — whose.

TRANSLATION

I therefore offer my respectful obeisances millions of times to Acyutānanda's actual followers, whose life and soul was Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
