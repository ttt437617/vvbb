Ādi-līlāChapter 12: The Expansions of Advaita Ācārya and Gadādhara Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 12.83

vānīnātha brahmacārī — bada mahāśaya

vallabha-caitanya-dāsa — krishna-premamaya

SYNONYMS

vānīnātha brahmacārī — Vānīnātha Brahmacārī; bada mahāśaya — very great personality; vallabha-caitanya-dāsa — Vallabha-caitanya dāsa; krishna-prema-maya — always filled with love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

The thirteenth branch was Vānīnātha Brahmacārī, and the fourteenth was Vallabha-caitanya dāsa. Both of these great personalities were always filled with love of Krishna.

PURPORT

Śrī Vānīnātha Brahmacārī is described in the Tenth Chapter, verse 114, of the Ādi-līlā. A disciple of Vallabha-caitanya named Nalinī-mohana Gosvāmī established a temple of Madana-gopāla in Navadvīpa.
