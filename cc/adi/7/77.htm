Ādi-līlāChapter 7: Lord Caitanya in Five Features

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 7.77

ei ājñā pāñā nāma la-i anukshana

nāma laite laite mora bhrānta haila mana

SYNONYMS

ei — this; ājñā — order; pāñā — receiving; nāma — the holy name; la-i — chant; anukshana — always; nāma — the holy name; laite — accepting; laite — accepting; mora — My; bhrānta — bewilderment; haila — taking place; mana — in the mind.

TRANSLATION

"Since I received this order from My spiritual master, I always chant the holy name, but I thought that by chanting and chanting the holy name I had been bewildered.
