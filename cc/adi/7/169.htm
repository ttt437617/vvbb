Ādi-līlāChapter 7: Lord Caitanya in Five Features

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 7.169

śrī-caitanya, nityānanda, advaita, — tina jana

śrīvāsa-gadādhara-ādi yata bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

śrī-caitanya, nityānanda, advaita — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Nityānanda Prabhu and Advaita Prabhu; tina — these three; jana — personalities; śrīvāsa-gadādhara — Śrīvāsa and Gadādhara; ādi — etc.; yata — all; bhakta-gana — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

While chanting the Pañca-tattva mahā-mantra, one must chant the names of Śrī Caitanya, Nityānanda, Advaita, Gadādhara and Śrīvāsa with their many devotees. This is the process.
