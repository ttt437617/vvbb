Ādi-līlāChapter 7: Lord Caitanya in Five Features

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 7.9

sei krishna avatīrna śrī-krishna-caitanya

sei parikara-gana sańge saba dhanya

SYNONYMS

sei krishna — that very Lord Krishna; avatīrna — has advented; śrī-krishna-caitanya — in the form of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sei — those; parikara-gana — associates; sańge — with Him; saba — all; dhanya — glorious.

TRANSLATION

The selfsame Lord Krishna advented Himself as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with all His eternal associates, who are also equally glorious.
