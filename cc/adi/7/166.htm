Ādi-līlāChapter 7: Lord Caitanya in Five Features

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 7.166

āpane dakshina deśa karilā gamana

grāme grāme kailā krishna-nāma pracārana

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; dakshina deśa — South India; karilā — went; gamana — traveling; grāme grāme — in each and every village; kailā — He did; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; pracārana — broadcasting.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally went to South India, and He broadcast the holy name of Lord Krishna in every village and town.
