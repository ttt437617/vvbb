Ādi-līlāChapter 7: Lord Caitanya in Five Features

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 7.156

laksha laksha loka āise prabhuke dekhite

mahā-bhida haila dvāre, nāre praveśite

SYNONYMS

laksha laksha — hundreds of thousands; loka — people; āise — came; prabhuke — unto the Lord; dekhite — to see; mahā-bhida — a great crowd; haila — there happened; dvāre — at the door; nāre — may not; praveśite — to enter.

TRANSLATION

The crowd at the door of His residence was so great that it numbered hundreds of thousands.
