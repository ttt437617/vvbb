Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.117

prabhura ājñāya nityānanda gaude calilā

tāńra sańge tina-jana prabhu-ājñāya āilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura ājñāya — under the order of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; gaude — to Bengal; calilā — went back; tāńra sańge — in His company; tina jana — three men; prabhu-ājñāya — under the order of the Lord; āilā — went.

TRANSLATION

By the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, three devotees accompanied Lord Nityānanda Prabhu when He returned to Bengal to preach.
