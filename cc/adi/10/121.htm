Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.121

gauda-deśa-bhaktera kaila sańkshepa kathana

ananta caitanya-bhakta nā yāya ganana

SYNONYMS

gauda-deśa — in Bengal; bhaktera — of the devotees; kaila — I have described; sańkshepa — in brief; kathana — narration; ananta — unlimited; caitanya-bhakta — devotees of Lord Caitanya; nā — not; yāya — can be; ganana — counted.

TRANSLATION

I have given a brief description of the devotees of Lord Caitanya in Bengal. Actually His devotees are innumerable.
