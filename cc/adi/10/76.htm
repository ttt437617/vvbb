Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.76

gopīnātha simha — eka caitanyera dāsa

akrūra bali' prabhu yāńre kailā parihāsa

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha simha — Gopīnātha Simha; eka — one; caitanyera dāsa — servant of Lord Caitanya; akrūra bali' — famous as Akrūra; prabhu — the Lord; yāńre — whom; kaila — did; parihāsa — joking.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Simha, the thirty-eighth branch of the tree, was a faithful servant of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord jokingly addressed him as Akrūra.

PURPORT

Actually he was Akrūra, as stated in text 117 of the Gaura-ganoddeśa-dīpikā.
