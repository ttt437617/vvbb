Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.31

dāmodara-pandita śākhā premete pracanda

prabhura upare yeńho kaila vākya-danda

SYNONYMS

dāmodara-pandita — Dāmodara Pandita; śākhā — another branch (the tenth branch); premete — in affection; pracanda — very advanced; prabhura — the Lord; upare — upon; yeńho — he who; kaila — did; vākya-danda — chastisement by speaking.

TRANSLATION

Dāmodara Pandita, the tenth branch of the Caitanya tree, was so elevated in love of Lord Caitanya that he once unhesitatingly chastised the Lord with strong words.
