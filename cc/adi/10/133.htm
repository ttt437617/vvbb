Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.133

rāmānanda rāya, pattanāyaka gopīnātha

kalānidhi, sudhānidhi, nāyaka vānīnātha

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; pattanāyaka gopīnātha — Pattanāyaka Gopīnātha; kalānidhi — Kalānidhi; sudhānidhi — Sudhānidhi; nāyaka vānīnātha — Nāyaka Vānīnātha.

TRANSLATION

The five sons of Bhavānanda Rāya were Rāmānanda Rāya, Pattanāyaka Gopīnātha, Kalānidhi, Sudhānidhi and Nāyaka Vānīnātha.
