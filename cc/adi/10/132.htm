Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.132

ālińgana kari' tāńre balila vacana

tumi pāndu, pañca-pāndava — tomāra nandana

SYNONYMS

ālińgana kari' — embracing; tāńre — unto him; balila — said; vacana — those words; tumi — you; pāndu — were Pāndu; pañca — five; pāndava — the Pāndavas; tomāra — your; nandana — sons.

TRANSLATION

Embracing Rāya Bhavānanda, the Lord declared to him, "You formerly appeared as Pāndu, and your five sons appeared as the five Pāndavas."
