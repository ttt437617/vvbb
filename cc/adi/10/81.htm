Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.81

vānīnātha vasu ādi yata grāmī jana

sabei caitanya-bhritya, — caitanya-prānadhana

SYNONYMS

vānīnātha vasu — Vānīnātha Vasu; ādi — heading the list; yata — all; grāmī — of the village; jana — inhabitants; sabei — all of them; caitanya-bhritya — servants of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; caitanya-prāna-dhana — their life and soul was Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

All the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma village, headed by Vānīnātha Vasu, were servants of Lord Caitanya, who was their only life and wealth.
