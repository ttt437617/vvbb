Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.129

nīlācale prabhu-saha prathama milana

sei bhakta-ganera ebe kariye ganana

SYNONYMS

nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; prabhu-saha — with the Lord; prathama — first; milana — meeting; sei — that; bhakta-ganera — of the devotees; ebe — now; kariye — I do; ganana — count.

TRANSLATION

Now let me enumerate the devotees of Bengal who first came to see the Lord at Jagannātha Purī.
