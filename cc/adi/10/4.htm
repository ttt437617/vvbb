Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.4

caitanya-gosāñira yata pārishada-caya

guru-laghu-bhāva tāńra nā haya niścaya

SYNONYMS

caitanya — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gosāñira — of the supreme spiritual master; yata — all; pārishada-caya — groups of associates; guru-laghu-bhāva — conceptions of high and low; tāńra — of them; nā — never; haya — become; niścaya — ascertained.

TRANSLATION

The associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were many, but none of them should be considered lower or higher. This cannot be ascertained.
