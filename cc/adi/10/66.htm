Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.66

'ratnabāhu' bali' prabhu thuila tāńra nāma

akiñcana prabhura priya krishnadāsa-nāma

SYNONYMS

ratnabāhu — the title Ratnabāhu; bali' — calling him; prabhu — the Lord; thuila — kept; tāńra — his; nāma — name; akiñcana — unalloyed; prabhura — of the Lord; priya — dear; krishnadāsa — Krishnadāsa; nāma — name.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave Vijaya dāsa the name Ratnabāhu ["jewel-handed"] because he copied many manuscripts for Him. The twenty-eighth branch was Krishnadāsa, who was very dear to the Lord. He was known as Akiñcana Krishnadāsa.

PURPORT

Akiñcana means "one who possesses nothing in this world."
