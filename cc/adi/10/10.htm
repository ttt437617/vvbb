Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.10

dui śākhāra upaśākhāya tāń-sabāra ganana

yāńra grihe mahāprabhura sadā sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

dui śākhāra — of the two branches; upaśākhāya — on the subbranches; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; ganana — counting; yāńra grihe — in whose house; mahāprabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sadā — always; sańkīrtana — congregational chanting.

TRANSLATION

There is no counting the subbranches of these two branches. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu held congregational chanting daily at the house of Śrīvāsa Pandita.
