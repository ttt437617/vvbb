Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.29

prabhura atyanta priya — pandita gańgādāsa

yāńhāra smarane haya sarva-bandha-nāśa

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of the Lord; atyanta — very; priya — dear; pandita gańgādāsa — Pandita Gańgādāsa; yāńhāra — who; smarane — by remembering; haya — it becomes; sarva-bandha-nāśa — freedom from all kinds of bondage.

TRANSLATION

Pandita Gańgādāsa was the eighth dear branch of the tree of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. One who remembers his activities attains freedom from all bondage.
