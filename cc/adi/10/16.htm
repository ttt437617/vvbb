Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.16

tāńra śishya-upaśishya, — tāńra upaśākhā

eimata saba śākhā-upaśākhāra lekhā

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; śishya — disciples; upaśishya — granddisciples and admirers; tāńra — his; upaśākhā — subbranches; eimata — in this way; saba — all; śākhā — branches; upaśākhāra — subbranches; lekhā — to describe by writing.

TRANSLATION

His disciples and granddisciples are his subbranches. To describe them all would be difficult.
