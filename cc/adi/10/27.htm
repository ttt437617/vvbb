Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.27

vāra-māsa tāhā prabhu karena ańgīkāra

'rāghavera jhāli' bali' prasiddhi yāhāra

SYNONYMS

vāra-māsa — the whole year; tāhā — all those foods; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena — did; ańgīkāra — accept; rāghavera jhāli — the bags of Rāghava Pandita; bali' — so called; prasiddhi — celebrated; yāhāra — of which.

TRANSLATION

The Lord accepted these foods throughout the entire year. Those bags are still celebrated as rāghavera jhāli ["the bags of Rāghava Pandita"].
