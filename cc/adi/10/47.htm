Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.47

tāńra līlā varniyāchena vrindāvana-dāsa

yebā avaśishta, āge kariba prakāśa

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; līlā — pastimes; varniyāchena — described; vrindāvana-dāsa — Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; yebā — whatever; avaśishta — remained undescribed; āge — later in the book; kariba — I shall make; prakāśa — manifest.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has vividly described the pastimes of Haridāsa Thākura in his Caitanya-bhāgavata. Whatever has remained undescribed I shall try to explain later in this book.
