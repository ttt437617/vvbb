Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.159

ei-mata sańkhyātīta caitanya-bhakta-gana

dińmātra likhi, samyak nā yāya kathana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; sańkhyā-atīta — innumerable; caitanya-bhakta-gana — devotees of Lord Caitanya; diń-mātra — only a fractional part; likhi — I write; samyak — full; nā — cannot; yāya — be possible; kathana — to explain.

TRANSLATION

I list in this way only a portion of the innumerable devotees of Lord Caitanya. To describe them all fully is not possible.
