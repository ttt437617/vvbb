Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.96

tabe dui bhāi tāńre marite nā dila

nija tritīya bhāi kari' nikate rākhila

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; dui bhāi — the two brothers (Śrīla Rūpa and Sanātana); tāńre — him; marite — to die; nā dila — did not allow; nija — own; tritīya — third; bhāi — brother; kari' — accepting; nikate — near; rākhila — kept him.

TRANSLATION

These two brothers, however, did not allow him to die. They accepted him as their third brother and kept him in their company.
