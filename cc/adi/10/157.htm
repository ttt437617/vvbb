Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.157

prabhura ājñā pāñā vrindāvanere āilā

āsiyā śrī-rūpa-gosāñira nikate rahilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā — order; pāñā — receiving; vrindāvanere — to Vrindāvana; āilā — he came; āsiyā — coming there; śrī-rūpa-gosāñira — of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; nikate — at his shelter; rahilā — remained.

TRANSLATION

Later, by the order of Lord Caitanya, Raghunātha went to Vrindāvana and remained there under the shelter of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī.
