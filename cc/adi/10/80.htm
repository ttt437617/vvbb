Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.80

kulīnagrāma-vāsī satyarāja, rāmānanda

yadunātha, purushottama, śańkara, vidyānanda

SYNONYMS

kulīna-grāma-vāsī — the inhabitants of Kulīna-grāma; satyarāja — Satyarāja; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda; yadunātha — Yadunātha; purushottama — Purushottama; śańkara — Śańkara; vidyānanda — Vidyānanda.

TRANSLATION

Satyarāja, Rāmānanda, Yadunātha, Purushottama, Śańkara and Vidyānanda all belonged to the twentieth branch. They were inhabitants of the village known as Kulīna-grāma.
