Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.33

tāńhāra anuja śākhā — śańkara-pandita

'prabhu-pādopādhāna' yāńra nāma vidita

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra — his (Dāmodara Pandita's); anuja — younger brother; śākhā — the eleventh branch; śańkara-pandita — Śańkara Pandita; prabhu — the Lord's; pāda-upadhāna — shoes; yāńra — whose; nāma — name; vidita — celebrated.

TRANSLATION

The eleventh branch, the younger brother of Dāmodara Pandita, was known as Śańkara Pandita. He was celebrated as the shoes of the Lord.
