Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.37

śrīmān-pandita śākhā — prabhura nija bhritya

deuti dharena, yabe prabhu karena nritya

SYNONYMS

śrīmān-pandita — Śrīmān Pandita; śākhā — branch; prabhura — of the Lord; nija — own; bhritya — servant; deuti — torch light; dharena — carries; yabe — while; prabhu — Lord Caitanya; karena — does; nritya — dance.

TRANSLATION

The fifteenth branch was Śrīmān Pandita, who was a constant servitor of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He used to carry a torch while the Lord danced.

PURPORT

Śrīmān Pandita was among the companions of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu when the Lord performed sańkīrtana. When Lord Caitanya dressed Himself in the form of the goddess Lakshmī and danced in the streets of Navadvīpa, Śrīmān Pandita carried a torch to light the way.
