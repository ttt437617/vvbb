Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.3

ei mālīra — ei vrikshera akathya kathana

ebe śuna mukhya-śākhāra nāma-vivarana

SYNONYMS

ei mālīra — of this gardener; ei vrikshera — of this tree; akathya kathana — inconceivable description; ebe — now; śuna — hear; mukhya — chief; śākhāra — branches; nāma — of the names; vivarana — description.

TRANSLATION

The description of Lord Caitanya as the gardener and the tree is inconceivable. Now hear with attention about the branches of this tree.
