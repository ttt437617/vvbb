Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.36

nārāyana-pandita eka bada-i udāra

caitanya-carana vinu nāhi jāne āra

SYNONYMS

nārāyana-pandita — Nārāyana Pandita; eka — one; badai — very; udāra — liberal; caitanya-carana — the lotus feet of Lord Caitanya; vinu — except; nāhi — not; jāne — know; āra — anything else.

TRANSLATION

Nārāyana Pandita, the fourteenth branch, a great and liberal devotee, did not know any shelter but Lord Caitanya's lotus feet.

PURPORT

Nārāyana Pandita was one of the associates of Śrīvāsa Thākura. It is mentioned in the Caitanya-bhāgavata, Antya-khanda, Eighth Chapter, verse 36, that he went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī with the Thākura's brother Śrī Rāma Pandita.
