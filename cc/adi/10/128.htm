Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.128

āra yata bhakta-gana gauda-deśa-vāsī

pratyabde prabhure dekhe nīlācale āsi'

SYNONYMS

āra — others; yata — all; bhakta-gana — devotees; gauda-deśa-vāsī — residents of Bengal; prati-abde — each year; prabhure — the Lord; dekhe — see; nīlācale — in Jagannātha Purī; āsi' — coming there.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees who resided in Bengal used to visit Jagannātha Purī every year to see the Lord.
