Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.6

ataeva tāń-sabāre kari' namaskāra

nāma-mātra kari, dosha nā labe āmāra

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; tāń-sabāre — to all of them; kari' — doing; namaskāra — offer my obeisances; nāma-mātra — that is also a token; kari — I do; dosha — fault; nā — do not; labe — take; āmāra — of me.

TRANSLATION

I offer my obeisances unto them as a token of respect. I request them not to consider my offenses.
