Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.93

shodaśa vatsara kaila antarańga-sevana

svarūpera antardhāne āilā vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

shodaśa — sixteen; vatsara — years; kaila — did; antarańga — confidential; sevana — service; svarūpera — of Svarūpa Dāmodara; antardhāne — disappearance; āilā — came; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

He rendered confidential service to the Lord for sixteen years at Jagannātha Purī, and after the disappearance of both the Lord and Svarūpa Dāmodara, he left Jagannātha Purī and went to Vrindāvana.
