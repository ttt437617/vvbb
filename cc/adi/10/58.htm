Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.58

'pradyumna brahmacārī' tāńra āge nāma chila

'nrisimhānanda' nāma prabhu pāche ta' rākhila

SYNONYMS

pradyumna brahmacārī — Pradyumna Brahmacārī; tāńra — his; āge — previously; nāma — name; chila — was; nrisimhānanda — Nrisimhānanda; nāma — the name; prabhu — the Lord; pāche — afterward; ta' — certainly; rākhila — kept it.

TRANSLATION

The former Pradyumna Brahmacārī was given the name Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
