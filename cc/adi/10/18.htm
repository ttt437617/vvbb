Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.18

āpane mahāprabhu gāya yāńra nritya-kāle

prabhura carana dhari' vakreśvara bale

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gāya — sang; yāńra — whose; nritya-kāle — at the time of dancing; prabhura — of the Lord; carana — lotus feet; dhari' — embracing; vakreśvara — Vakreśvara Pandita; bale — said.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally sang while Vakreśvara Pandita danced, and thus Vakreśvara Pandita fell at the lotus feet of the Lord and spoke as follows.
