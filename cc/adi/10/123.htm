Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.123

kevala nīlācale prabhura ye ye bhakta-gana

sańkshepe kariye kichu se saba kathana

SYNONYMS

kevala — only; nīlācale — in Jagannātha Purī; prabhura — of the Lord; ye ye — all those; bhakta-gana — devotees; sańkshepe — in brief; kariye — I do; kichu — some; se saba — all those; kathana — narration.

TRANSLATION

Let me briefly describe some of the devotees of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu in Jagannātha Purī.
