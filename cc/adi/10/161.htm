Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.161

sakala bhariyā āche prema-phula-phale

bhāsāila tri-jagat krishna-prema-jale

SYNONYMS

sakala — all; bhariyā — filled; āche — there is; prema — love of Godhead; phula — flowers; phale — fruits; bhāsāila — inundated; tri-jagat — the whole world; krishna-prema — of love of Krishna; jale — with water.

TRANSLATION

Every branch and subbranch of the tree is full of innumerable fruits and flowers. They inundate the world with the waters of love of Krishna.
