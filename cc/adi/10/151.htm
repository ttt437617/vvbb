Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.151

nirloma gańgādāsa, āra vishnudāsa

ei sabera prabhu-sańge nīlācale vāsa

SYNONYMS

nirloma gańgādāsa — Nirloma Gańgādāsa; āra — and; vishnudāsa — Vishnudāsa; ei sabera — of all of them; prabhu-sańge — with Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; vāsa — residence.

TRANSLATION

Nirloma Gańgādāsa and Vishnudāsa were the thirty-sixth and thirty-seventh among the devotees who lived at Jagannātha Purī as servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
