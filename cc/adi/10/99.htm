Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.99

sahasra dandavat kare, laya laksha nāma

dui sahasra vaishnavere nitya paranāma

SYNONYMS

sahasra — thousand; dandavat — obeisances; kare — does; laya — takes; laksha — one hundred thousand; nāma — holy names; dui — two; sahasra — thousand; vaishnavere — unto the devotees; nitya — daily; paranāma — obeisances.

TRANSLATION

As a daily duty, he regularly offered one thousand obeisances to the Lord, chanted at least one hundred thousand holy names and offered obeisances to two thousand Vaishnavas.
