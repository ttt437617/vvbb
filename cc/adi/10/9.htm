Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.9

śrīpati, śrīnidhi — tāńra dui sahodara

cāri bhāira dāsa-dāsī, griha-parikara

SYNONYMS

śrīpati — Śrīpati; śrīnidhi — Śrīnidhi; tāńra — their; dui — two; sahodara — own brothers; cāri — four; bhāira — brothers; dāsa-dāsī — family members, menservants and maidservants; griha-parikara — all counted in one family.

TRANSLATION

Their two brothers were named Śrīpati and Śrīnidhi. These four brothers and their servants and maidservants are considered one big branch.
