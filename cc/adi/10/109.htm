Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.109

krishnadāsa vaidya, āra pandita-śekhara

kavicandra, āra kīrtanīyā shashthīvara

SYNONYMS

krishnadāsa vaidya — Krishnadāsa Vaidya; āra — and; pandita-śekhara — Pandita Śekhara; kavicandra — Kavicandra; āra — and; kīrtanīyā — kīrtana performer; shashthīvara — Shashthīvara.

TRANSLATION

The fifty-first branch of the Caitanya tree was Krishnadāsa Vaidya, the fifty-second was Pandita Śekhara, the fifty-third was Kavicandra, and the fifty-fourth was Shashthīvara, who was a great sańkīrtana performer.

PURPORT

In the Gaura-ganoddeśa-dīpikā (171) it is mentioned that Śrīnātha Miśra was Citrāńgī and that Kavicandra was Manoharā-gopī.
