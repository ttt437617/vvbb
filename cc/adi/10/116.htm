Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.116

rāmadāsa abhirāma — sakhya-premarāśi

sholasāńgera kāshtha tuli' ye karila vāńśī

SYNONYMS

rāmadāsa abhirāma — Rāmadāsa Abhirāma; sakhya-prema — friendship; rāśi — great volume; sholasa-ańgera — of sixteen knots; kashtha — wood; tuli' — lifting; ye — one who; karila — made; vāńśī — flute.

TRANSLATION

Rāmadāsa Abhirāma was fully absorbed in the mellow of friendship. He made a flute of a bamboo stick with sixteen knots.

PURPORT

Abhirāma was an inhabitant of Khānākula-krishna-nagara.
