Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.141

ańga-sevā govindere dilena īśvara

jagannātha dekhite calena āge kāśīśvara

SYNONYMS

ańga-sevā — taking care of the body; govindere — unto Govinda; dilena — He gave; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; jagannātha — the Jagannātha Deity; dekhite — while going to visit; calena — goes; āge — in front; kāśīśvara — Kāśīśvara.

TRANSLATION

Govinda cared for the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, whereas Kāśīśvara went in front of the Lord when He went to see Jagannātha in the temple.
