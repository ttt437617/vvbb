Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.156

bada haile nīlācale gelā prabhura sthāne

ashta-māsa rahila bhikshā dena kona dine

SYNONYMS

bada haile — when he grew to be a young man; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; gelā — went; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sthāne — at the place; ashta-māsa — eight months; rahila — stayed; bhikshā — prasādam; dena — gave; kona dine — some days.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha grew to be a young man, he visited Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī and stayed there for eight months. Sometimes he offered prasādam to the Lord.
