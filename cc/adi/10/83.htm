Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.83

kulīnagrāmīra bhāgya kahane nā yāya

śūkara carāya doma, seha krishna gāya

SYNONYMS

kulīna-grāmīra — the residents of Kulīna-grāma; bhāgya — fortune; kahane — to speak; nā — not; yāya — is possible; śūkara — hogs; carāya — tending; doma — sweeper; seha — he also; krishna — Lord Krishna; gāya — chants.

TRANSLATION

"No one can describe the fortunate position of Kulīna-grāma. It is so sublime that even sweepers who tend their hogs there also chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra."
