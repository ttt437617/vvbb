Ādi-līlāChapter 10: The Trunk, Branches and Subbranches of the Caitanya Tree

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 10.23

dui-jane khatmati lāgāya kondala

tāńra prītyera kathā āge kahiba sakala

SYNONYMS

dui-jane — two persons; khatmati — fighting over trifles; lāgāya — continued; kondala — quarrel; tāńra — his; prītyera — affection; kathā — narration; āge — ahead; kahiba — I shall speak; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

They sometimes appeared to fight over trifles, but these quarrels were based on their affection, of which I shall speak later.
