Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.49

śrīmanta, gokula-dāsa hariharānanda

śivāi, nandāi, avadhūta paramānanda

SYNONYMS

śrī-manta — Śrīmanta; gokula-dāsa — Gokula dāsa; hariharānanda — Hariharānanda; śivāi — Śivāi; nandāi — Nandāi; avadhūta paramānanda — Avadhūta Paramānanda.

TRANSLATION

Śrīmanta was the forty-fifth, Gokula dāsa the forty-sixth, Hariharānanda the forty-seventh, Śivāi the forty-eighth, Nandāi the forty-ninth and Paramānanda the fiftieth.
