Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.56

sarvaśākhā-śreshtha vīrabhadra gosāñi

tāńra upaśākhā yata, tāra anta nāi

SYNONYMS

sarva-śākhā-śreshtha — the best of all the branches; vīrabhadra gosāñi — Vīrabhadra Gosāñi; tāńra upaśākhā — His subbranches; yata — all; tāra — of them; anta — limit; nāi — there is not.

TRANSLATION

Among all the branches of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, Vīrabhadra Gosāñi was the topmost. His subbranches were unlimited.
