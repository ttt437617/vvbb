Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.5

śrī-nityānanda-vrikshera skandha gurutara

tāhāte janmila śākhā-praśākhā vistara

SYNONYMS

śrī-nityānanda-vrikshera — of the tree known as Śrī Nityānanda; skandha — main branch; gurutara — extremely heavy; tāhāte — from that branch; janmila — grew; śākhā — branches; praśākhā — subbranches; vistara — expansively.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu is an extremely heavy branch of the Śrī Caitanya tree. From that branch grow many branches and subbranches.
