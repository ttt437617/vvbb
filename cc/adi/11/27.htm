Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.27

nityānande samarpila jāti-kula-pāńti

śrī-caitanya-nityānande kari prānapati

SYNONYMS

nityānande — to Lord Nityānanda; samarpila — he offered; jāti — caste distinction; kula — family; pāńti — fellowship; śrī-caitanya — Lord Caitanya; nityānande — in Lord Nityānanda; kari — making; prāna-pati — the Lords of his life.

TRANSLATION

Making Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda the Lords of his life, Gaurīdāsa Pandita sacrificed everything for the service of Lord Nityānanda, even the fellowship of his own family.
