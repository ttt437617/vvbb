Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.19

vāsudeva gīte kare prabhura varnane

kāshtha-pāshāna drave yāhāra śravane

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; gīte — while singing; kare — does; prabhura — of Nityānanda Prabhu and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; varnane — in description; kāshtha — wood; pāshāna — stone; drave — melt; yāhāra — whose; śravane — by hearing.

TRANSLATION

When Vāsudeva Ghosha described Lord Caitanya and Nityānanda while performing kīrtana, even wood and stone would melt upon hearing it.
