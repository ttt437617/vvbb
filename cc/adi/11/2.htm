Ādi-līlāChapter 11: The Expansions of Lord Nityānanda

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 11.2

jaya jaya mahāprabhu śrī-krishna-caitanya

tāńhāra caranāśrita yei, sei dhanya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; mahāprabhu — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrī-krishna-caitanya — known as Krishna Caitanya; tāńhāra carana-āśrita — all who have taken shelter at His lotus feet; yei — anyone; sei — he is; dhanya — glorious.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! Anyone who has taken shelter at His lotus feet is glorious.
