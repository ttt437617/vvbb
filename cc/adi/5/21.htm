Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.21

prema-netre dekhe tāra svarūpa-prakāśa

gopa-gopī-sańge yāńhā krishnera vilāsa

SYNONYMS

prema-netre — with the eyes of love of Godhead; dekhe — one sees; tāra — its; svarūpa-prakāśa — manifestation of identity; gopa — cowherd boys; gopī-sańge — with the cowherd damsels; yāńhā — where; krishnera vilāsa — the pastimes of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

But with the eyes of love of Godhead one can see its real identity as the place where Lord Krishna performs His pastimes with the cowherd boys and cowherd girls.
