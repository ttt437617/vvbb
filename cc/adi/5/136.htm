Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.136

vrisha hañā krishna-sane māthā-māthi rana

kabhu krishna kare tāńra pāda-samvāhana

SYNONYMS

vrisha hañā — becoming a bull; krishna-sane — with Krishna; māthā-māthi rana — fighting head to head; kabhu — sometimes; krishna — Krishna; kare — does; tāńra — His; pāda-samvāhana — massaging the feet.

TRANSLATION

Playing like a bull, Lord Balarāma fights with Krishna head to head. And sometimes Lord Krishna massages the feet of Lord Balarāma.
