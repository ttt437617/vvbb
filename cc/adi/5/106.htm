Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.106

hiranya-garbha, antaryāmī, jagat-kārana

yāńra amśa kari' kare virāta-kalpana

SYNONYMS

hiranya-garbha — Hiranyagarbha; antah-yāmī — the Supersoul; jagat-kārana — the cause of the material world; yāńra amśa kari' — taking as His expansion; kare — does; virāta-kalpana — conception of the universal form.

TRANSLATION

He is the Supersoul, Hiranyagarbha, the cause of the material world. The universal form is conceived as His expansion.
