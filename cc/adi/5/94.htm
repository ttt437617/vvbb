Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.94

sei ta' purusha ananta-brahmānda srijiyā

saba ande praveśilā bahu-mūrti hañā

SYNONYMS

sei — that; ta' — certainly; purusha — incarnation; ananta-brahmānda — innumerable universes; srijiyā — creating; saba — all; ande — in the egglike universes; praveśilā — entered; bahu-mūrti hañā — taking multifarious forms.

TRANSLATION

After creating millions of universes, the first purusha entered into each of them in a separate form, as Śrī Garbhodakaśāyī.
