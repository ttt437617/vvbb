Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.227

hena ye govinda prabhu, pāinu yāńhā haite

tāńhāra carana-kripā ke pāre varnite

SYNONYMS

hena — thus; ye govinda — this Lord Govinda; prabhu — master; pāinu — I got; yāńhā haite — from whom; tāńhāra — His; carana-kripā — mercy of the lotus feet; ke — who; pāre — is able; varnite — to describe.

TRANSLATION

Therefore who can describe the mercy of the lotus feet of Him [Lord Nityānanda] by whom I have attained the shelter of this Lord Govinda?
