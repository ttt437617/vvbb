Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.178

kruddha haiyā vamśī bhāńgi' cale rāmadāsa

tat-kāle āmāra bhrātāra haila sarva-nāśa

SYNONYMS

kruddha haiyā — being very angry; vamśī — the flute; bhāńgi' — breaking; cale — departs; rāma-dāsa — Rāmadāsa; tat-kāle — at that time; āmāra — my; bhrātāra — of the brother; haila — there was; sarva-nāśa — downfall.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Rāmadāsa broke his flute in anger and went away, and at that time my brother fell down.
