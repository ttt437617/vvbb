Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.180

bhāike bhartsinu muñi, lañā ei guna

sei rātre prabhu more dilā daraśana

SYNONYMS

bhāike — my brother; bhartsinu — chastised; muñi — I; lañā — taking; ei — this; guna — as a good quality; sei rātre — on that night; prabhu — my Lord; more — unto me; dilā — gave; daraśana — appearance.

TRANSLATION

That night Lord Nityānanda appeared to me in a dream because of my good quality in chastising my brother.
