Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.154

sei amśa lañā jyeshtha-kanishthābhimāna

amśāmśi-rūpe śāstre karaye vyākhyāna

SYNONYMS

sei amśa lañā — taking that plenary portion; jyeshtha-kanishtha-abhimāna — considering Themselves the elder or younger; amśa-amśi-rūpe — as the expansion and the original Supreme Personality of Godhead; śāstre — in the revealed scriptures; karaye — does; vyākhyāna — explanation.

TRANSLATION

Krishna and Balarāma present Themselves as younger brother and elder brother, but in the scriptures They are described as the original Supreme Personality of Godhead and His expansion.
