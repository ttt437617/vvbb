Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.129

keho kahe, krishna sākshāt nara-nārāyana

keho kahe, krishna haya sākshāt vāmana

SYNONYMS

keho kahe — someone says; krishna — Lord Krishna; sākshāt — directly; nara-nārāyana — Lord Nara-Nārāyana; keho kahe — someone says; krishna haya — Krishna is; sākshāt vāmana — Lord Vāmanadeva.

TRANSLATION

Some said that Krishna was directly Lord Nara-Nārāyana, and some called Him Lord Vāmanadeva incarnate.
