Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.31

brahma-sāyujya-muktera tāhā nāhi gati

vaikuntha-bāhire haya tā'-sabāra sthiti

SYNONYMS

brahma-sāyujya — of merging into the Supreme Brahman; muktera — of the liberation; tāhā — there (in Vaikuntha); nāhi — not; gati — entrance; vaikuntha-bāhire — outside the Vaikuntha planets; haya — there is; tā'-sabāra sthiti — the residence of all of them.

TRANSLATION

Those who attain brahma-sāyujya liberation cannot gain entrance into Vaikuntha; their residence is outside the Vaikuntha planets.
