Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.123

chatra, pādukā, śayyā, upādhāna, vasana

ārāma, āvāsa, yajña-sūtra, simhāsana

SYNONYMS

chatra — umbrella; pādukā — slippers; śayyā — bed; upādhāna — pillow; vasana — garments; ārāma — resting chair; āvāsa — residence; yajña-sūtra — sacred thread; simha-āsana — throne.

TRANSLATION

He serves Lord Krishna, assuming all the following forms: umbrella, slippers, bedding, pillow, garments, resting chair, residence, sacred thread and throne.
