Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.9

āpane karena krishna-līlāra sahāya

srishti-līlā-kārya kare dhari' cāri kāya

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; karena — performs; krishna-līlāra sahāya — assistance in the pastimes of Lord Krishna; srishti-līlā — of the pastimes of creation; kārya — the work; kare — does; dhari' — accepting; cāri kāya — four bodies.

TRANSLATION

He Himself helps in the pastimes of Lord Krishna, and He does the work of creation in four other forms.
