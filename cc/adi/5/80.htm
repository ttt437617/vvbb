Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.80

sei purusha srishti-sthiti-pralayera kartā

nānā avatāra kare, jagatera bhartā

SYNONYMS

sei — that; purusha — the Personality of Godhead; srishti-sthiti-pralayera — of creation, maintenance and annihilation; kartā — creator; nānā — various; avatāra — incarnations; kare — makes; jagatera — of the material world; bhartā — maintainer.

TRANSLATION

That purusha [Kāranodakaśāyī Vishnu] is the performer of creation, maintenance and destruction. He manifests Himself in many incarnations, for He is the maintainer of the world.
