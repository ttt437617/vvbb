Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.200

jaya jaya nityānanda, nityānanda-rāma

yāńhāra kripāte pāinu vrindāvana-dhāma

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; nityānanda — to Lord Nityānanda; nityānanda-rāma — to Lord Balarāma, who appeared as Nityānanda; yāńhāra kripāte — by whose mercy; pāinu — I got; vrindāvana-dhāma — shelter at Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

All glory, all glory to Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, by whose mercy I have attained shelter in the transcendental abode of Vrindāvana!
