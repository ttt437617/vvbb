Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.230

se vaishnavera pada-renu, tāra pada-chāyā

adhamere dila prabhu-nityānanda-dayā

SYNONYMS

se vaishnavera — of all those Vaishnavas; pada-renu — the dust of the feet; tāra — their; pada-chāyā — the shade of the feet; adhamere — unto this fallen soul; dila — gave; prabhu-nityānanda-dayā — the mercy of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

The dust and shade of the lotus feet of the Vaishnavas have been granted to this fallen soul by the mercy of Lord Nityānanda.
