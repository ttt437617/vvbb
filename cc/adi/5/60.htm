Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.60

krishna-śaktye prakriti haya gauna kārana

agni-śaktye lauha yaiche karaye jārana

SYNONYMS

krishna-śaktye — by the energy of Krishna; prakriti — the material nature; haya — becomes; gauna — indirect; kārana — cause; agni-śaktye — by the energy of fire; lauha — iron; yaiche — just as; karaye — becomes; jārana — powerful or red-hot.

TRANSLATION

Thus prakriti, by the energy of Lord Krishna, becomes the secondary cause, just as iron becomes red-hot by the energy of fire.
