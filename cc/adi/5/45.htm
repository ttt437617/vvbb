Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.45

'jīva'-nāma tatasthākhya eka śakti haya

mahā-sańkarshana — saba jīvera āśraya

SYNONYMS

jīva — the living entity; nāma — named; tata-sthā-ākhya — known as the marginal potency; eka — one; śakti — energy; haya — is; mahā-sańkarshana — Mahā-sańkarshana; saba — all; jīvera — of living entities; āśraya — the shelter.

TRANSLATION

There is one marginal potency, known as the jīva. Mahā-sańkarshana is the shelter of all jīvas.
