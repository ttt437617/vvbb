Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.151

nishedha karite nāre, yāte chota bhāi

mauna dhari' rahe lakshmana mane duhkha pāi'

SYNONYMS

nishedha karite nāre — unable to prohibit Lord Rāmacandra; yāte — because; chota bhāi — younger brother; mauna dhari' — becoming silent; rahe — remains; lakshmana — Lakshmana; mane — in the mind; duhkha — unhappiness; pāi' — getting.

TRANSLATION

As a younger brother He could not stop Lord Rāma from His resolution, and so He remained silent, although unhappy in His mind.
