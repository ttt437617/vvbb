Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.182

dandavat haiyā āmi padinu pāyete

nija-pāda-padma prabhu dilā mora māthe

SYNONYMS

dandavat haiyā — offering obeisances; āmi — I; padinu — fell down; pāyete — at His lotus feet; nija-pāda-padma — His own lotus feet; prabhu — the Lord; dilā — placed; mora — my; māthe — on the head.

TRANSLATION

I fell at His feet, offering my obeisances, and He then placed His own lotus feet upon my head.
