Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.205

jagāi mādhāi haite muñi se pāpishtha

purīshera kīta haite muñi se laghishtha

SYNONYMS

jagāi mādhāi — the two brothers Jagāi and Mādhāi; haite — than; muñi — I; se — that; pāpishtha — more sinful; purīshera — in stool; kīta — the worms; haite — than; muñi — I am; se — that; laghishtha — lower.

TRANSLATION

I am more sinful than Jagāi and Mādhāi and even lower than the worms in the stool.
