Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.152

krishna-avatāre jyeshtha hailā sevāra kārana

krishnake karāila nānā sukha āsvādana

SYNONYMS

krishna-avatāre — in the incarnation of Lord Krishna; jyeshtha hailā — He became the elder brother; sevāra kārana — for the purpose of service; krishnake — to Krishna; karāila — made; nānā — various; sukha — happinesses; āsvādana — tasting.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Krishna appeared, He [Balarāma] became His elder brother to serve Him to His heart's content and make Him enjoy all sorts of happiness.
