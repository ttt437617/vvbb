Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.233

āpanāra kathā likhi nirlajja ha-iyā

nityānanda-gune lekhāya unmatta kariyā

SYNONYMS

āpanāra — personal; kathā — description; likhi — I write; nirlajja ha-iyā — being shameless; nityānanda-gune — the attributes of Nityānanda; lekhāya — cause to write; unmatta kariyā — making like a madman.

TRANSLATION

I have described my own story without reservations. The attributes of Lord Nityānanda, making me like a madman, force me to write these things.
