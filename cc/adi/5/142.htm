Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.142

ekale īśvara krishna, āra saba bhritya

yāre yaiche nācāya, se taiche kare nritya

SYNONYMS

ekale — alone; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna — Krishna; āra — others; saba — all; bhritya — servants; yāre — unto whom; yaiche — as; nācāya — He causes to dance; se — He; taiche — in that way; kare nritya — dances.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna alone is the supreme controller, and all others are His servants. They dance as He makes them do so.
