Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.81

srishty-ādi-nimitte yei amśera avadhāna

sei ta' amśere kahi 'avatāra' nāma

SYNONYMS

srishti-ādi-nimitte — for the cause of creation, maintenance and annihilation; yei — which; amśera avadhāna — manifestation of the part; sei ta' — that certainly; amśere kahi — I speak about that plenary expansion; avatāra nāma — by the name "incarnation."

TRANSLATION

That fragment of the Supreme Lord, known as the Mahā-purusha, appears for the purpose of creation, maintenance and annihilation and is called an incarnation.
