Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.65

dūra haite purusha kare māyāte avadhāna

jīva-rūpa vīrya tāte karena ādhāna

SYNONYMS

dūra haite — from a distance; purusha — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; kare — does; māyāte — unto the material energy; avadhāna — glancing over; jīva-rūpa — the living entities; vīrya — seed; tāte — in her; karena — does; ādhāna — impregnation.

TRANSLATION

The first purusha casts His glance at māyā from a distance, and thus He impregnates her with the seed of life in the form of the living entities.
