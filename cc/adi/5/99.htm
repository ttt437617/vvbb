Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.99

tāńhāi prakata kaila vaikuntha nija-dhāma

śesha-śayana-jale karila viśrāma

SYNONYMS

tāńhāi — there; prakata — manifestation; kaila — did; vaikuntha — the spiritual world; nija-dhāma — His own abode; śesha — of Lord Śesha; śayana — on the bed; jale — on the water; karila — did; viśrāma — rest.

TRANSLATION

There He manifested Vaikuntha as His own abode and rested in the waters on the bed of Lord Śesha.
