Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.234

nityānanda-prabhura guna-mahimā apāra

'sahasra-vadane' śesha nāhi pāya yāńra

SYNONYMS

nityānanda-prabhura — of Lord Nityānanda; guna-mahimā — glories of transcendental attributes; apāra — unfathomable; sahasra-vadane — in thousands of mouths; śesha — ultimate end; nāhi — does not; pāya — get; yāńra — whose.

TRANSLATION

The glories of Lord Nityānanda's transcendental attributes are unfathomable. Even Lord Śesha, with His thousands of mouths, cannot find their limit.
