Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.176

ekete viśvāsa, anye nā kara sammāna

"ardha-kukkutī-nyāya" tomāra pramāna

SYNONYMS

ekete viśvāsa — faith in one; anye — in the other; nā — not; kara — do; sammāna — respect; ardha-kukkutī-nyāya — the logic of accepting half of a hen; tomāra — your; pramāna — evidence.

TRANSLATION

"If you have faith in one but disrespect the other, your logic is like the logic of accepting half a hen.
