Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.52

vaikuntha bediyā eka āche jala-nidhi

ananta, apāra — tāra nāhika avadhi

SYNONYMS

vaikuntha — the spiritual planets of Vaikuntha; bediyā — surrounding; eka — one; āche — there is; jala-nidhi — ocean of water; ananta — unlimited; apāra — unfathomed; tāra — of that; nāhika — no; avadhi — limitation.

TRANSLATION

Surrounding Vaikuntha is a mass of water that is endless, unfathomed and unlimited.
