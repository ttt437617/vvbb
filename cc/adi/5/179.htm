Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.179

ei ta' kahila tāńra sevaka-prabhāva

āra eka kahi tāńra dayāra svabhāva

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; kahila — explained; tāńra — of Him; sevaka-prabhāva — the power of the servant; āra — other; eka — one; kahi — I say; tāńra — His; dayāra — of mercy; svabhāva — characteristic.

TRANSLATION

I have thus described the power of the servants of Lord Nityānanda. Now I shall describe another characteristic of His mercy.
