Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.128

avatāra-avatārī — abheda, ye jāne

pūrve yaiche krishnake keho kāho kari' māne

SYNONYMS

avatāra-avatārī — an incarnation and the source of all incarnations; abheda — identical; ye jāne — anyone who knows; pūrve — formerly; yaiche — just as; krishnake — unto Lord Krishna; keho — somebody; kāho — somewhere; kari' — making; māne — accepts.

TRANSLATION

They know that there is no difference between the incarnation and the source of all incarnations. Previously Lord Krishna was regarded in the light of different principles by different people.
