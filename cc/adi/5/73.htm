Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.73

amśera amśa yei, 'kalā' tāra nāma

govindera pratimūrti śrī-balarāma

SYNONYMS

amśera — of the part; amśa — part; yei — that which; kalā — a kalā, or part of the plenary portion; tāra — its; nāma — name; govindera — of Lord Govinda; prati-mūrti — counterform; śrī-balarāma — Lord Balarāma.

TRANSLATION

A part of a part of a whole is called a kalā. Śrī Balarāma is the counterform of Lord Govinda.
