Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.88

ei mata gītāteha punah punah kaya

sarvadā īśvara-tattva acintya-śakti haya

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; gītāteha — in the Bhagavad-gītā; punah punah — again and again; kaya — it is said; sarvadā — always; īśvara-tattva — the truth of the Absolute Truth; acintya-śakti haya — is inconceivable.

TRANSLATION

Thus the Bhagavad-gītā also states again and again that the Absolute Truth always possesses inconceivable power.
