Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.208

preme matta nityānanda kripā-avatāra

uttama, adhama, kichu nā kare vicāra

SYNONYMS

preme — in ecstatic love; matta — mad; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; kripā — merciful; avatāra — incarnation; uttama — good; adhama — bad; kichu — any; nā — not; kare — makes; vicāra — consideration.

TRANSLATION

Because He is intoxicated by ecstatic love and is an incarnation of mercy, He does not distinguish between the good and the bad.
