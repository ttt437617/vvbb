Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.158

āra eka śuna tāńra kripāra mahimā

adhama jīvere cadhāila ūrdhva-sīmā

SYNONYMS

āra — another; eka — one; śuna — please hear; tāńra kripāra mahimā — glory of His mercy; adhama jīvere — the downtrodden living being; cadhāila — He elevated; ūrdhva-sīmā — to the topmost limit.

TRANSLATION

Please listen to another glory of His mercy. He made a fallen living entity climb to the highest limit.
