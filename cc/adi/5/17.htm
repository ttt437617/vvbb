Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.17

sarvopari śrī-gokula — vrajaloka-dhāma

śrī-goloka, śvetadvīpa, vrindāvana nāma

SYNONYMS

sarva-upari — above all of them; śrī-gokula — the place known as Gokula; vraja-loka-dhāma — the place of Vraja; śrī-goloka — the place named Goloka; śveta-dvīpa — the white island; vrindāvana nāma — also named Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Gokula, the highest of all, is also called Vraja, Goloka, Śvetadvīpa and Vrindāvana.
