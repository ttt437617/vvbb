Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.24

vāsudeva-sańkarshana-pradyumnāniruddha

sarva-catur-vyūha-amśī, turīya, viśuddha

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Lord Vāsudeva; sańkarshana — Lord Sańkarshana; pradyumna — Lord Pradyumna; aniruddha — and Lord Aniruddha; sarva-catuh-vyūha — of all other quadruple expansions; amśī — source; turīya — transcendental; viśuddha — pure.

TRANSLATION

Vāsudeva, Sańkarshana, Pradyumna and Aniruddha are the primary quadruple forms, from whom all other quadruple forms are manifested. They are all purely transcendental.
