Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.103

sei padma-nāle haila caudda-bhuvana

teńho brahmā hañā srishti karila srijana

SYNONYMS

sei padma-nāle — within the stem of that lotus flower; haila — were; caudda-bhuvana — the fourteen worlds; teńho — He Himself; brahmā hañā — appearing as Brahmā; srishti — the creation; karila srijana — created.

TRANSLATION

Within the stem of that lotus were the fourteen worlds. Thus the Supreme Lord, as Brahmā, created the entire creation.
