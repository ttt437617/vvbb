Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.196

eta bali' prerilā more hātasāni diyā

antardhāna kaila prabhu nija-gana lañā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prerilā — dispatched; more — me; hātasāni — indication of the hand; diyā — giving; antardhāna kaila — disappeared; prabhu — my Lord; nija-gana lañā — taking His personal associates.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, He directed me toward Vrindāvana by waving His hand. Then He disappeared with His associates.
