Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.170

'ei ta' dvitīya sūta romaharashana

baladeva dekhi' ye nā kaila pratyudgama'

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — this; dvitīya — second; sūta romaharashana — Romaharshana-sūta; baladeva dekhi' — seeing Lord Balarāma; ye — who; nā — not; kaila — did; pratyudgama — stand up.

TRANSLATION

"Here I find the second Romaharshana-sūta, who did not stand to show honor when he saw Lord Balarāma."
