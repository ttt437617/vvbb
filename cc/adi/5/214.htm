Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.214

tāsām āvirabhūc chaurih

smayamāna-mukhāmbujah

pītāmbara-dharah sragvī

sākshān manmatha-manmathah

SYNONYMS

tāsām — among them; āvirabhūt — appeared; śaurih — Lord Krishna; smayamāna — smiling; mukha-ambujah — lotus face; pīta-ambara-dharah — dressed with yellow garments; sragvī — decorated with a flower garland; sākshāt — directly; manmatha — of Cupid; manmathah — Cupid.

TRANSLATION

"Wearing yellow garments and decorated with a flower garland, Lord Krishna, appearing among the gopīs with His smiling lotus face, looked directly like the charmer of the heart of Cupid."

PURPORT

This is a quotation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (10.32.2).
