Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.137

āpanāke bhritya kari' krishne prabhu jāne

krishnera kalāra kalā āpanāke māne

SYNONYMS

āpanāke — Himself; bhritya kari' — considering a servant; krishna — Krishna; prabhu — master; jāne — He knows; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; kalāra kalā — as a plenary portion of a plenary portion; āpanāke — Himself; māne — He accepts.

TRANSLATION

He considers Himself a servant and knows Krishna to be His master. Thus He regards Himself as a fragment of His plenary portion.
