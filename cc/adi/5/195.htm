Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.195

āre āre krishnadāsa, nā karaha bhaya

vrindāvane yāha, — tāńhā sarva labhya haya

SYNONYMS

āre āre — O! O!; krishna-dāsa — Krishnadāsa; nā — not; karaha — make; bhaya — fear; vrindāvane yāha — go to Vrindāvana; tāńhā — there; sarva — everything; labhya — available; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

"O my dear Krishnadāsa, do not be afraid. Go to Vrindāvana, for there you will attain all things."
