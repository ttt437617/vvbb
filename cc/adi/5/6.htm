Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.6

sei krishna — navadvīpe śrī-caitanya-candra

sei balarāma — sańge śrī-nityānanda

SYNONYMS

sei krishna — that original Krishna; navadvīpe — at Navadvīpa; śrī-caitanya-candra — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sei balarāma — that Lord Balarāma; sańge — with Him; śrī-nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda.

TRANSLATION

That original Lord Krishna appeared in Navadvīpa as Lord Caitanya, and Balarāma appeared with Him as Lord Nityānanda.
