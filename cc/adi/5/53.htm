Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.53

vaikunthera prithivy-ādi sakala cinmaya

māyika bhūtera tathi janma nāhi haya

SYNONYMS

vaikunthera — of the spiritual world; prithivī-ādi — earth, water, etc.; sakala — all; cit-maya — spiritual; māyika — material; bhūtera — of elements; tathi — there; janma — generation; nāhi haya — there is not.

TRANSLATION

The earth, water, fire, air and ether of Vaikuntha are all spiritual. Material elements are not found there.
