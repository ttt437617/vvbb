Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.67

aganya, ananta yata anda-sanniveśa

tata-rūpe purusha kare sabāte prakāśa

SYNONYMS

aganya — innumerable; ananta — unlimited; yata — all; anda — universes; sanniveśa — groups; tata-rūpe — in as many forms; purusha — the Lord; kare — does; sabāte — in every one of them; prakāśa — manifestation.

TRANSLATION

The purusha enters each and every one of the countless universes. He manifests Himself in as many separate forms as there are universes.
