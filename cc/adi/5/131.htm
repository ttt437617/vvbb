Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.131

krishna yabe avatare sarvāmśa-āśraya

sarvāmśa āsi' tabe krishnete milaya

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; yabe — when; avatare — descends; sarva-amśa-āśraya — the shelter of all other vishnu-tattvas; sarva-amśa — all plenary portions; āsi' — coming; tabe — at that time; krishnete — in Krishna; milaya — join.

TRANSLATION

When the Supreme Personality of Godhead Krishna appears, He is the shelter of all plenary parts. Thus at that time all His plenary portions join in Him.
