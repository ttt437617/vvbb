Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.212

vrindāvana-purandara śrī-madana-gopāla

rāsa-vilāsī sākshāt vrajendra-kumāra

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-purandara — the chief Deity of Vrindāvana; śrī-madana-gopāla — Lord Madana Gopāla; rāsa-vilāsī — the enjoyer of the rāsa dance; sākshāt — directly; vrajendra-kumāra — the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

Lord Madana Gopāla, the chief Deity of Vrindāvana, is the enjoyer of the rāsa dance and is directly the son of the King of Vraja.
