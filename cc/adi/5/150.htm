Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.150

rāmera caritra saba, — duhkhera kārana

svatantra līlāya duhkha sahena lakshmana

SYNONYMS

rāmera caritra saba — all the activities of Lord Rāmacandra; duhkhera kārana — causes of suffering; sva-tantra — although independent; līlāya — in the pastimes; duhkha — unhappiness; sahena lakshmana — Lakshmana tolerates.

TRANSLATION

The activities of Lord Rāma were full of suffering, but Lakshmana, of His own accord, tolerated that suffering.
