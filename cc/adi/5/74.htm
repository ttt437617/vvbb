Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.74

tāńra eka svarūpa — śrī-mahā-sańkarshana

tāńra amśa 'purusha' haya kalāte ganana

SYNONYMS

tāńra — His; eka — one; svarūpa — manifestation; śrī-mahā-sańkarshana — the great Lord Mahā-sańkarshana; tāńra — His; amśa — part; purusha — the Mahā-Vishnu incarnation; haya — is; kalāte ganana — counted as a kalā.

TRANSLATION

Balarāma's own expansion is called Mahā-sańkarshana, and His fragment, the purusha, is counted as a kalā, or a part of a plenary portion.
