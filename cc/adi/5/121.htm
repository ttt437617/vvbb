Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.121

sahasra-vadane kare krishna-guna gāna

niravadhi guna gā'na, anta nāhi pā'na

SYNONYMS

sahasra-vadane — in thousands of mouths; kare — does; krishna-guna gāna — chanting of the holy attributes of Krishna; niravadhi — continuously; guna gā'na — chanting of the transcendental qualities; anta nāhi pā'na — does not reach the end.

TRANSLATION

With His thousands of mouths He sings the glories of Lord Krishna, but although He always sings in that way, He does not find an end to the qualities of the Lord.
