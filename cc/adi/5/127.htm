Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.127

athavā bhaktera vākya māni satya kari'

sakala sambhave tāńte, yāte avatārī

SYNONYMS

athavā — otherwise; bhaktera vākya — anything spoken by a pure devotee; māni — I accept; satya kari' — as truth; sakala — everything; sambhave — possible; tāńte — in Him; yāte — since; avatārī — the original source of all incarnations.

TRANSLATION

But I accept it as the truth because it has been said by devotees. Since He is the source of all incarnations, everything is possible in Him.
