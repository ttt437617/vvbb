Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.49

ashtama ślokera kaila sańkshepe vivarana

navama ślokera artha śuna diyā mana

SYNONYMS

ashtama — eighth; ślokera — of the verse; kaila — I have done; sańkshepe — in brief; vivarana — description; navama — the ninth; ślokera — of the verse; artha — the meaning; śuna — please hear; diyā mana — with mental attention.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly explained the eighth verse. Now please listen with attention as I explain the ninth verse.
