Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.55

sei ta' kāranārnave sei sańkarshana

āpanāra eka amśe karena śayana

SYNONYMS

sei — that; ta' — certainly; kārana-arnave — in the ocean of cause, or Causal Ocean; sei — that; sańkarshana — Lord Sańkarshana; āpanāra — of His own; eka — one; amśe — by the part; karena śayana — lies down.

TRANSLATION

In that ocean lies a plenary portion of Lord Sańkarshana.
