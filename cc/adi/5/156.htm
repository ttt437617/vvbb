Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.156

śrī-caitanya — sei krishna, nityānanda — rāma

nityānanda pūrna kare caitanyera kāma

SYNONYMS

śrī-caitanya — Lord Śrī Caitanya; sei krishna — that original Krishna; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; rāma — Balarāma; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; pūrna kare — fulfills; caitanyera kāma — all the desires of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya is the same Lord Krishna, and Lord Nityānanda is Lord Balarāma. Lord Nityānanda fulfills all of Lord Caitanya's desires.
