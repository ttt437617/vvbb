Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.159

veda-guhya kathā ei ayogya kahite

tathāpi kahiye tāńra kripā prakāśite

SYNONYMS

veda — like the Vedas; guhya — very confidential; kathā — incident; ei — this; ayogya kahite — not fit to disclose; tathāpi — still; kahiye — I speak; tāńra — His; kripā — mercy; prakāśite — to manifest.

TRANSLATION

To disclose it is not proper, for it should be kept as confidential as the Vedas, yet I shall speak of it to make His mercy known to all.
