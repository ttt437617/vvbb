Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.8

śrī-balarāma gosāñi mūla-sańkarshana

pañca-rūpa dhari' karena krishnera sevana

SYNONYMS

śrī-balarāma — Balarāma; gosāñi — the Lord; mūla-sańkarshana — the original Sańkarshana; pañca-rūpa dhari' — accepting five bodies; karena — does; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; sevana — service.

TRANSLATION

Lord Balarāma is the original Sańkarshana. He assumes five other forms to serve Lord Krishna.
