Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.48

turīya, viśuddha-sattva, 'sańkarshana' nāma

tińho yāńra amśa, sei nityānanda-rāma

SYNONYMS

turīya — transcendental; viśuddha-sattva — pure existence; sańkarshana nāma — named Sańkarshana; tińho yāńra amśa — of whom that Sańkarshana is also a partial expansion; sei nityānanda-rāma — that person is known as Balarāma or Nityānanda.

TRANSLATION

That Sańkarshana, who is transcendental pure goodness, is a partial expansion of Nityānanda Balarāma.
