Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.64

krishna — kartā, māyā tāńra karena sahāya

ghatera kārana — cakra-dandādi upāya

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; kartā — the creator; māyā — material energy; tāńra — His; karena — does; sahāya — assistance; ghatera kārana — the cause of the earthen pot; cakra-danda-ādi — the wheel, the rod, and so on; upāya — instruments.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna is the creator, and māyā only helps Him as an instrument, just like the potter's wheel and other instruments, which are the instrumental causes of a pot.
