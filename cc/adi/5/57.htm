Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.57

māyā-śakti rahe kāranābdhira bāhire

kārana-samudra māyā paraśite nāre

SYNONYMS

māyā-śakti — material energy; rahe — remains; kārana-abdhira — to the Causal Ocean; bāhire — external; kārana-samudra — the Causal Ocean; māyā — material energy; paraśite nāre — cannot touch.

TRANSLATION

Māyā-śakti resides outside the Kārana Ocean. Māyā cannot touch its waters.
