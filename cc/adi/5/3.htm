Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.3

ei shat-śloke kahila krishna-caitanya-mahimā

pañca-śloke kahi nityānanda-tattva-sīmā

SYNONYMS

ei — this; shat-śloke — in six verses; kahila — described; krishna-caitanya-mahimā — the glories of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pañca-śloke — in five verses; kahi — let me explain; nityānanda — of Lord Nityānanda; tattva — of the truth; sīmā — the limitation.

TRANSLATION

I have described the glory of Śrī Krishna Caitanya in six verses. Now, in five verses I shall describe the glory of Lord Nityānanda.
