Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.163

mahā-prema-maya tińho vasilā ańgane

sakala vaishnava tāńra vandilā carane

SYNONYMS

mahā-prema-maya — absorbed in emotional love; tińho — he; vasilā — sat; ańgane — in the courtyard; sakala vaishnava — all other Vaishnavas; tāńra — his; vandilā — worshiped; carane — lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Absorbed in emotional love, he sat in my courtyard, and all the Vaishnavas bowed down at his feet.
