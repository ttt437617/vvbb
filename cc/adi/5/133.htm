Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.133

ataeva śrī-krishna-caitanya gosāñi

sarva avatāra-līlā kari' sabāre dekhāi

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gosāñi — the Lord; sarva — all; avatāra-līlā — the pastimes of different incarnations; kari' — exhibiting; sabāre — to everyone; dekhāi — He showed.

TRANSLATION

Therefore Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu has exhibited to everyone all the pastimes of all the various incarnations.
