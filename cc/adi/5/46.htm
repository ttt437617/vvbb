Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.46

yāńhā haite viśvotpatti, yāńhāte pralaya

sei purushera sańkarshana samāśraya

SYNONYMS

yāńhā haite — from whom; viśva-utpatti — the creation of the material cosmic manifestation; yāńhāte — in whom; pralaya — merging; sei purushera — of that Supreme Personality of Godhead; sańkarshana — Sańkarshana; samāśraya — the original shelter.

TRANSLATION

Sańkarshana is the original shelter of the purusha, from whom this world is created and in whom it is dissolved.
