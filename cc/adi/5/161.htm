Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.161

avadhūta gosāñira eka bhritya prema-dhāma

mīnaketana rāmadāsa haya tāńra nāma

SYNONYMS

avadhūta — the mendicant; gosāñira — of Lord Nityānanda; eka — one; bhritya — servant; prema-dhāma — reservoir of love; mīnaketana — Mīnaketana; rāma-dāsa — Rāmadāsa; haya — is; tāńra — his; nāma — name.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda Prabhu had a servant named Śrī Mīnaketana Rāmadāsa, who was a reservoir of love.
