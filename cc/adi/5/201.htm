Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.201

jaya jaya nityānanda, jaya kripā-maya

yāńhā haite pāinu rūpa-sanātanāśraya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; nityānanda — to Lord Nityānanda; jaya kripā-maya — all glories to the most merciful Lord; yāńhā haite — from whom; pāinu — I got; rūpa-sanātana-āśraya — shelter at the lotus feet of Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

All glory, all glory to the merciful Lord Nityānanda, by whose mercy I have attained shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa and Śrī Sanātana!
