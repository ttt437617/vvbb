Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.138

vrishāyamānau nardantau

yuyudhāte parasparam

anukritya rutair jantūmś

ceratuh prākritau yathā

SYNONYMS

vrishāyamānau — becoming like bulls; nardantau — making roaring sounds; yuyudhāte — both used to fight; parasparam — each other; anukritya — imitating; rutaih — with cries; jantūn — the animals; ceratuh — used to play; prākritau — ordinary boys; yathā — just like.

TRANSLATION

"Acting just like ordinary boys, They played like roaring bulls as They fought each other, and They imitated the calls of various animals."

PURPORT

This and the following quotation are from the Bhāgavatam (10.11.40 and 10.15.14).
