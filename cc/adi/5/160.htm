Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.160

ullāsa-upari lekhoń tomāra prasāda

nityānanda prabhu, mora kshama aparādha

SYNONYMS

ullāsa-upari — on account of great ecstasy; lekhoń — I write; tomāra prasāda — Your mercy; nityānanda prabhu — Lord Nityānanda; mora — my; kshama — please excuse; aparādha — offenses.

TRANSLATION

O Lord Nityānanda, I write of Your mercy out of great exultation. Please forgive me for my offenses.
