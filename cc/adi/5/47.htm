Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.47

sarvāśraya, sarvādbhuta, aiśvarya apāra

'ananta' kahite nāre mahimā yāńhāra

SYNONYMS

sarva-āśraya — the shelter of everything; sarva-adbhuta — wonderful in every respect; aiśvarya — opulences; apāra — unfathomed; ananta — Ananta Śesha; kahite nāre — cannot speak; mahimā yāńhāra — the glories of whom.

TRANSLATION

He [Sańkarshana] is the shelter of everything. He is wonderful in every respect, and His opulences are infinite. Even Ananta cannot describe His glory.
