Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.143

ei mata caitanya-gosāñi ekale īśvara

āra saba pārishada, keha vā kińkara

SYNONYMS

ei mata — in this way; caitanya-gosāñi — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ekale — alone; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; āra saba — all others; pārishada — associates; keha — someone; vā — or; kińkara — servants.

TRANSLATION

Thus Lord Caitanya is also the only controller. All others are His associates or servants.
