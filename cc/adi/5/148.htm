Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.148

ācārya-gosāñira tattva nā yāya kathana

krishna avatāri yeńho tārila bhuvana

SYNONYMS

ācārya-gosāñira — of Advaita Ācārya; tattva — the truth; nā yāya kathana — cannot be described; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatāri — making descend; yeńho — who; tārila — delivered; bhuvana — all the world.

TRANSLATION

I cannot describe the truth of Advaita Ācārya. He has delivered the entire world by making Lord Krishna descend.
