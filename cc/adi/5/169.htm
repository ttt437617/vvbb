Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.169

ańgane āsiyā teńho nā kaila sambhāsha

tāhā dekhi' kruddha hañā bale rāmadāsa

SYNONYMS

ańgane — to the courtyard; āsiyā — coming; teńho — he; nā — not; kaila — did; sambhāsha — address; tāhā dekhi' — seeing this; kruddha hañā — becoming angry; bale — says; rāma-dāsa — Śrī Rāmadāsa.

TRANSLATION

When Mīnaketana was seated in the yard, this brāhmana did not offer him respect. Seeing this, Śrī Rāmadāsa became angry and spoke.
