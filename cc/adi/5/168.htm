Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.168

gunārnava miśra nāme eka vipra ārya

śrī-mūrti-nikate teńho kare sevā-kārya

SYNONYMS

gunārnava miśra — of Gunārnava Miśra; nāme — by the name; eka — one; vipra — brāhmana; ārya — very respectable; śrī-mūrti-nikate — by the side of the Deity; teńho — he; kare — does; sevā-kārya — activities in devotion.

TRANSLATION

One respectable brāhmana named Śrī Gunārnava Miśra was serving the Deity.
