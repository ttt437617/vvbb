Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.207

emana nirghrina more kebā kripā kare

eka nityānanda vinu jagat bhitare

SYNONYMS

emana — such; nirghrina — abominable; more — unto me; kebā — who; kripā — mercy; kare — shows; eka — one; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; vinu — but; jagat — world; bhitare — within.

TRANSLATION

Who in this world but Nityānanda could show His mercy to such an abominable person as me?
