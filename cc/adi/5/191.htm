Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.191

pārishada-gane dekhi' saba gopa-veśe

'krishna' 'krishna' kahe sabe saprema āveśe

SYNONYMS

pārishada-gane — associates; dekhi' — seeing; saba — all; gopa-veśe — in the dress of cowherd boys; krishna krishna — Krishna, Krishna; kahe — says; sabe — all; sa-prema — of ecstatic love; āveśe — in absorption.

TRANSLATION

His devotees, dressed like cowherd boys, surrounded His feet like so many bees and also chanted "Krishna, Krishna," absorbed in ecstatic love.
