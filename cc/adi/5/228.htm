Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.228

vrindāvane vaise yata vaishnava-mandala

krishna-nāma-parāyana, parama-mańgala

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; vaise — there are; yata — all; vaishnava-mandala — groups of devotees; krishna-nāma-parāyana — addicted to the name of Lord Krishna; parama-mańgala — all-auspicious.

TRANSLATION

All the groups of Vaishnavas who live in Vrindāvana are absorbed in chanting the all-auspicious name of Krishna.
