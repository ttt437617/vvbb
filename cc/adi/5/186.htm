Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.186

suvarna-kundala karne, svarnāńgada-vālā

pāyete nūpura bāje, kanthe pushpa-mālā

SYNONYMS

suvarna-kundala — gold earrings; karne — on the ears; svarna-ańgada — golden armlets; vālā — and bangles; pāyete — on the feet; nūpura — ankle bells; bāje — tinkle; kanthe — on the neck; pushpa-mālā — flower garland.

TRANSLATION

He wore golden earrings on His ears, and golden armlets and bangles. He wore tinkling anklets on His feet and a garland of flowers around His neck.
