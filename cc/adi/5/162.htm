Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.162

āmāra ālaye aho-rātra-sańkīrtana

tāhāte āilā teńho pāñā nimantrana

SYNONYMS

āmāra ālaye — at my house; ahah-rātra — day and night; sańkīrtana — chanting the Hare Krishna mantra; tāhāte — on account of this; āilā — came; teńho — he; pāñā nimantrana — getting an invitation.

TRANSLATION

At my house there was sańkīrtana day and night, and therefore he visited there, having been invited.
