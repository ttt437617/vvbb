Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.107

hena nārāyana, — yāńra amśera amśa

sei prabhu nityānanda — sarva-avatamsa

SYNONYMS

hena — such; nārāyana — Lord Nārāyana; yāńra — of whom; amśera — of the plenary part; amśa — apart; sei — that; prabhu — the Lord; nityānanda — Nityānanda; sarva-avatamsa — the source of all incarnations.

TRANSLATION

That Lord Nārāyana is a part of a plenary part of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma, who is the source of all incarnations.
