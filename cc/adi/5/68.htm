Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.68

purusha-nāsāte yabe bāhirāya śvāsa

niśvāsa sahite haya brahmānda-prakāśa

SYNONYMS

purusha-nāsāte — in the nostrils of the Lord; yabe — when; bāhirāya — expels; śvāsa — breath; niśvāsa sahite — with that exhalation; haya — there is; brahmānda-prakāśa — manifestation of universes.

TRANSLATION

When the purusha exhales, the universes are manifested with each outward breath.
