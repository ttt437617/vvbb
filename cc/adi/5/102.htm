Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.102

tāńra nābhi-padma haite uthila eka padma

sei padme haila brahmāra janma-sadma

SYNONYMS

tāńra — His; nābhi-padma — lotus navel; haite — from; uthila — grew; eka — one; padma — lotus flower; sei padme — on that lotus; haila — there was; brahmāra — of Lord Brahmā; janma-sadma — the place of birth.

TRANSLATION

From His navel grew a lotus flower, which became the birthplace of Lord Brahmā.
