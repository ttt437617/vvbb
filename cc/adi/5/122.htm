Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.122

sanakādi bhāgavata śune yāńra mukhe

bhagavānera guna kahe, bhāse prema-sukhe

SYNONYMS

sanaka-ādi — the great sages headed by Sanaka, Sananda, etc.; bhāgavata — Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; śune — hear; yāńra mukhe — from whose mouth; bhagavānera — of the Personality of Godhead; guna — attributes; kahe — say; bhāse — float; prema-sukhe — in the transcendental bliss of love of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

The four Kumāras hear Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam from His lips, and they in turn repeat it in the transcendental bliss of love of Godhead.
