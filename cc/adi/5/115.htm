Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.115

tabe avatari' kare jagat pālana

ananta vaibhava tāńra nāhika ganana

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; avatari' — descending; kare — does; jagat pālana — maintenance of the material world; ananta — unlimited; vaibhava — the opulences; tāńra — of Him; nāhika — there is not; ganana — counting.

TRANSLATION

He then descends to maintain the material world. His unlimited opulences cannot be counted.
