Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.209

ye āge padaye, tāre karaye nistāra

ataeva nistārilā mo-hena durācāra

SYNONYMS

ye — whoever; āge — in front; padaye — falls down; tāre — unto him; karaye — does; nistāra — deliverance; ataeva — therefore; nistārilā — delivered; mo — as me; hena — such; durācāra — sinful and fallen person.

TRANSLATION

He delivers all those who fall down before Him. Therefore He has delivered such a sinful and fallen person as me.
