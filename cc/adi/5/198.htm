Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.198

ki dekhinu ki śuninu, kariye vicāra

prabhu-ājñā haila vrindāvana yāibāra

SYNONYMS

ki dekhinu — what did I see; ki śuninu — what did I hear; kariye vicāra — I began to consider; prabhu-ājñā — the order of my Lord; haila — there was; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; yāibāra — to go.

TRANSLATION

I thought about what I had seen and heard and concluded that the Lord had ordered me to proceed to Vrindāvana at once.
