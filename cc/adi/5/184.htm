Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.184

śyāma-cikkana kānti, prakānda śarīra

sākshāt kandarpa, yaiche mahā-malla-vīra

SYNONYMS

śyāma — blackish; cikkana — glossy; kānti — luster; prakānda — heavy; śarīra — body; sākshāt — directly; kandarpa — Cupid; yaiche — like; mahā-malla — very stout and strong; vīra — hero.

TRANSLATION

He had a glossy blackish complexion, and His tall, strong, heroic stature made Him seem like Cupid himself.
