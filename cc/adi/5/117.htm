Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.117

sei vishnu 'śesha'-rūpe dharena dharanī

kāńhā āche mahī, śire, hena nāhi jāni

SYNONYMS

sei — that; vishnu — Lord Vishnu; śesha-rūpe — in form of Lord Śesha; dharena — carries; dharanī — the planets; kāńhā — where; āche — are; mahī — the planets; śire — on the head; hena nāhi jāni — I cannot understand.

TRANSLATION

That same Lord Vishnu, in the form of Lord Śesha, holds the planets upon His heads, although He does not know where they are, for He cannot feel their existence upon His heads.
