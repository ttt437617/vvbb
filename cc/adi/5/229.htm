Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.229

yāńra prāna-dhana — nityānanda-śrī-caitanya

rādhā-krishna-bhakti vine nāhi jāne anya

SYNONYMS

yāńra — whose; prāna-dhana — life and soul; nityānanda-śrī-caitanya — Lord Nityānanda and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rādhā-krishna — to Krishna and Rādhārānī; bhakti — devotional service; vine — except; nāhi jāne anya — do not know anything else.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda are the life and soul of those Vaishnavas, who do not know anything but devotional service to Śrī Śrī Rādhā-Krishna.
