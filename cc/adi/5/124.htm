Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.124

eta mūrti-bheda kari' krishna-sevā kare

krishnera śeshatā pāñā 'śesha' nāma dhare

SYNONYMS

eta — so many; mūrti-bheda — different forms; kari' — taking; krishna-sevā kare — serves Lord Krishna; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; śeshatā — ultimate end; pāñā — having reached; śesha nāma dhare — assumes the name Śesha Nāga.

TRANSLATION

He is thus called Lord Śesha, for He has attained the ultimate end of servitude to Krishna. He takes many forms for the service of Krishna, and thus He serves the Lord.
