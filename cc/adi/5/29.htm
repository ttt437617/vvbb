Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.29

yadyapi kevala tāńra krīdā-mātra dharma

tathāpi jīvere kripāya kare eka karma

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; kevala — only; tāńra — His; krīdā-mātra — pastime only; dharma — characteristic function; tathāpi — still; jīvere — to the fallen souls; kripāya — by the causeless mercy; kare — does; eka — one; karma — activity.

TRANSLATION

Although His pastimes are His only characteristic functions, by His causeless mercy He performs one activity for the fallen souls.
