Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.206

mora nāma śune yei tāra punya kshaya

mora nāma laya yei tāra pāpa haya

SYNONYMS

mora nāma — my name; śune — hears; yei — anyone who; tāra — his; punya kshaya — destruction of piety; mora nāma — my name; laya — takes; yei — anyone; tāra — his; pāpa — sin; haya — is.

TRANSLATION

Anyone who hears my name loses the results of his pious activities. Anyone who utters my name becomes sinful.
