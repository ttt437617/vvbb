Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.199

sei kshane vrindāvane karinu gamana

prabhura kripāte sukhe āinu vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

sei kshane — that very second; vrindāvane — toward Vrindāvana; karinu — I did; gamana — starting; prabhura kripāte — by the mercy of Lord Nityānanda; sukhe — in great happiness; āinu — arrived; vrindāvana — at Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

That very second I started for Vrindāvana, and by His mercy I reached there in great happiness.
