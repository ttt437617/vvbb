Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.130

keho kahe, krishna kshīroda-śāyī avatāra

asambhava nahe, satya vacana sabāra

SYNONYMS

keho kahe — someone says; krishna — Lord Krishna; kshīroda-śāyī avatāra — an incarnation of Lord Vishnu lying in the ocean of milk; asambhava nahe — there is not impossibility; satya — true; vacana sabāra — everyone's statement.

TRANSLATION

Some called Lord Krishna an incarnation of Lord Kshīrodakaśāyī. All these names are true; nothing is impossible.
