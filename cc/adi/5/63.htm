Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.63

ghatera nimitta-hetu yaiche kumbhakāra

taiche jagatera kartā — purushāvatāra

SYNONYMS

ghatera — of the earthen pot; nimitta-hetu — original cause; yaiche — just as; kumbhakāra — the potter; taiche — similarly; jagatera kartā — the creator of the material world; purusha-avatāra — the purusha incarnation, or Kāranārnavaśāyī Vishnu.

TRANSLATION

Just as the original cause of an earthen pot is the potter, so the creator of the material world is the first purusha incarnation [Kāranārnavaśāyī Vishnu].
