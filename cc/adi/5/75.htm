Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.75

yāńhāke ta' kalā kahi, tińho mahā-vishnu

mahā-purushāvatārī teńho sarva-jishnu

SYNONYMS

yāńhāke — unto whom; ta' — certainly; kalā kahi — I say kalā; tińho — He; mahā-vishnu — Lord Mahā-Vishnu; mahā-purushāvatārī — Mahā-Vishnu, the source of other purusha incarnations; teńho — He; sarva-jishnu — all-pervading.

TRANSLATION

I say that this kalā is Mahā-Vishnu. He is the Mahā-purusha, who is the source of the other purushas and who is all-pervading.
