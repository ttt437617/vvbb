Ādi-līlāChapter 5: The Glories Of Lord Nityānanda Balarāma

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 5.210

mo-pāpishthe ānilena śrī-vrindāvana

mo-hena adhame dilā śrī-rūpa-carana

SYNONYMS

mo-pāpishthe — unto me, who am so sinful; ānilena — He brought; śrī-vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; mo-hena — such as me; adhame — to the lowest of mankind; dilā — delivered; śrī-rūpa-carana — the lotus feet of Rūpa Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Although I am sinful and I am the most fallen, He has conferred upon me the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī.
