Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.41

gadādhara-panditādi — prabhura nija-śakti

tāń'-sabāra carane mora sahasra pranati

SYNONYMS

gadādhara-pandita-ādi — headed by Śrī Gadādhara Pandita; prabhura — of the Supreme Lord; nija-śakti — internal potencies; tāń'-sabāra — of all of them; carane — unto the lotus feet; mora — my; sahasra — thousands; pranati — respectful obeisances.

TRANSLATION

I offer my respectful obeisances unto the internal potencies of the Lord, of whom Śrī Gadādhara Prabhu is the foremost.
