Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.32

krishna, guru, bhakta, śakti, avatāra, prakāśa

krishna ei chaya-rūpe karena vilāsa

SYNONYMS

krishna — the Supreme Lord, Śrī Krishna; guru — the spiritual masters; bhakta — the devotees; śakti — the potencies; avatāra — the incarnations; prakāśa — plenary portions; krishna — Lord Krishna; ei chaya-rūpe — in these six features; karena vilāsa — enjoys.

TRANSLATION

Lord Krishna enjoys by manifesting Himself as the spiritual masters, the devotees, the diverse energies, the incarnations and the plenary portions. They are all six in one.
