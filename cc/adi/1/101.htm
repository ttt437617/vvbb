Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.101

eka adbhuta — sama-kāle dońhāra prakāśa

āra adbhuta — citta-guhāra tamah kare nāśa

SYNONYMS

eka — one; adbhuta — wonderful thing; sama-kāle — at the same time; dońhāra — of both; prakāśa — the manifestation; āra — the other; adbhuta — wonderful thing; citta-guhāra — of the core of the heart; tamah — darkness; kare — do; nāśa — destruction.

TRANSLATION

The first wonder is that both brothers appear simultaneously, and the other is that They illuminate the innermost depths of the heart.
