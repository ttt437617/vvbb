Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.90

ajñāna-tamera nāma kahiye 'kaitava'

dharma-artha-kāma-moksha-vāñchā ādi saba

SYNONYMS

ajñāna-tamera — of the darkness of ignorance; nāma — name; kahiye — I call; kaitava — cheating process; dharma — religiosity; artha — economic development; kāma — sense gratification; moksha — liberation; vāñchā — desire for; ādi — and so on; saba — all.

TRANSLATION

The darkness of ignorance is called kaitava, the way of cheating, which begins with religiosity, economic development, sense gratification and liberation.
