Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.24

tritīya ślokete kari vastura nirdeśa

yāhā ha-ite jāni para-tattvera uddeśa

SYNONYMS

tritīya ślokete — in the third verse; kari — I make; vastura — of the object; nirdeśa — indication; yāhā ha-ite — from which; jāni — I understand; para-tattvera — of the Absolute Truth; uddeśa — identification.

TRANSLATION

In the third verse I indicate the Absolute Truth, who is the ultimate substance. With such a description, one can visualize the Supreme Truth.
