Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.81

svayam-rūpa krishnera kāya-vyūha — tāńra sama

bhakta sahite haya tāńhāra āvarana

SYNONYMS

svayam-rūpa — His own original form (two-handed Krishna); krishnera — of Lord Krishna; kāya-vyūha — personal expansions; tāńra — with Him; sama — equal; bhakta — the devotees; sahite — associated with; haya — are; tāńhāra — His; āvarana — covering.

TRANSLATION

The personal associates of the primeval Lord, Śrī Krishna, are His devotees, who are identical with Him. He is complete with His entourage of devotees.

PURPORT

Śrī Krishna and His various personal expansions are nondifferent in potential power. These expansions are associated with further, secondary expansions, or servitor expansions, who are called devotees.
