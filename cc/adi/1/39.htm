Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.39

advaita ācārya — prabhura amśa-avatāra

tāńra pāda-padme koti pranati āmāra

SYNONYMS

advaita ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; prabhura — of the Supreme Lord; amśa — partial; avatāra — incarnation; tāńra — of Him; pāda-padme — unto the lotus feet; koti — ten million; pranati — respectful obeisances; āmāra — my.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Ācārya is the Lord's partial incarnation, and therefore I offer my obeisances millions of times at His lotus feet.
