Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.104

ei dui śloke kaila mańgala-vandana

tritīya ślokera artha śuna sarva-jana

SYNONYMS

ei — these; dui — two; śloke — in the verses; kaila — I did; mańgala — auspicious; vandana — obeisance; tritīya — third; ślokera — of the verse; artha — meaning; śuna — please hear; sarva-jana — everyone.

TRANSLATION

I have invoked the benediction of the Lords with these two verses [texts 1 and 2 of this chapter]. Now please hear attentively the purport of the third verse.
