Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.98

dui bhāi hridayera kshāli' andhakāra

dui bhāgavata-sańge karāna sākshātkāra

SYNONYMS

dui — two; bhāi — brothers; hridayera — of the heart; kshāli' — purifying; andhakāra — darkness; dui bhāgavata — of the two bhāgavatas; sańge — by the association; karāna — cause; sākshāt-kāra — a meeting.

TRANSLATION

But these two brothers [Lord Caitanya and Lord Nityānanda] dissipate the darkness of the inner core of the heart, and thus They help one meet the two kinds of bhāgavatas [persons or things in relationship with the Personality of Godhead].
