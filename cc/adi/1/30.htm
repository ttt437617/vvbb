Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.30

saba śrotā-vaishnavere kari' namaskāra

ei saba ślokera kari artha-vicāra

SYNONYMS

saba — all; śrotā — hearers or audience; vaishnavere — unto the Vaishnavas; kari' — offering; namaskāra — obeisances; ei saba ślokera — of all these (fourteen) verses; kari — I make; artha — of the meaning; vicāra — analysis.

TRANSLATION

I offer my obeisances unto all my Vaishnava readers as I begin to explain the intricacies of all these verses.
