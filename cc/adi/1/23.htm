Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.23

prathama dui śloke ishta-deva-namaskāra

sāmānya-viśesha-rūpe dui ta' prakāra

SYNONYMS

prathama — in the first; dui — two; śloke — verses; ishta-deva — worshipable Deity; namaskāra — obeisances; sāmānya — generally; viśesha-rūpe — and specifically; dui — two; ta — certainly; prakāra — ways.

TRANSLATION

The first two verses offer respectful obeisances, generally and specifically, to the Lord, who is the object of worship.
