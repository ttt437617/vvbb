Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.106

"mitam ca sāram ca vaco hi vāgmitā" iti

SYNONYMS

mitam — concise; ca — and; sāram — essential; ca — and; vacah — speech; hi — certainly; vāgmitā — eloquence; iti — thus.

TRANSLATION

"Essential truth spoken concisely is true eloquence."
