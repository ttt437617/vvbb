Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.97

sūrya candra bāhirera tamah se vināśe

bahir-vastu ghata-pata-ādi se prakāśe

SYNONYMS

sūrya — the sun; candra — the moon; bāhirera — of the external world; tamah — darkness; se — they; vināśe — destroy; bahih-vastu — external things; ghata — waterpots; pata-ādi — plates, etc.; se — they; prakāśe — reveal.

TRANSLATION

The sun and moon dissipate the darkness of the external world and thus reveal external material objects like pots and plates.
