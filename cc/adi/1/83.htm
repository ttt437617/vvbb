Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.83

prathama śloke kahi sāmānya mańgalācarana

dvitīya ślokete kari viśesha vandana

SYNONYMS

prathama — first; śloke — in the verse; kahi — I express; sāmānya — general; mańgala-ācarana — invocation of benediction; dvitīya — second; ślokete — in the verse; kari — I do; viśesha — particular; vandana — offering of prayers.

TRANSLATION

In the first verse I have invoked a general benediction, but in the second I have prayed to the Lord in a particular form.
