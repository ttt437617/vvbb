Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.87

śrī-krishna-caitanya āra prabhu nityānanda

yāńhāra prakāśe sarva jagat ānanda

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya; āra — and; prabhu nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; yāńhāra — of whom; prakāśe — on the appearance; sarva — all; jagat — the world; ānanda — full of happiness.

TRANSLATION

The appearance of Śrī Krishna Caitanya and Prabhu Nityānanda has surcharged the world with happiness.
