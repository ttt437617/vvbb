Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.29

ei caudda śloke kari mańgalācarana

tańhi madhye kahi saba vastu-nirūpana

SYNONYMS

ei caudda śloke — in these fourteen verses; kari — I make; mańgala-ācarana — auspicious invocation; tańhi — therefore in that; madhye — within; kahi — I speak; saba — all; vastu — object; nirūpana — description.

TRANSLATION

These fourteen verses, therefore, offer auspicious invocations and describe the Supreme Truth.
