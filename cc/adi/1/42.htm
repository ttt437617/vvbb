Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.42

śrī-krishna-caitanya prabhu svayam-bhagavān

tāńhāra padāravinde ananta pranāma

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu — the Supreme Lord; svayam-bhagavān — is the original Personality of Godhead; tāńhāra — His; pada-aravinde — unto the lotus feet; ananta — innumerable; pranāma — respectful obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Personality of Godhead Himself, and therefore I offer innumerable prostrations at His lotus feet.
