Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.105

vaktavya-bāhulya, grantha-vistārera dare

vistāre nā varni, sārārtha kahi alpākshare

SYNONYMS

vaktavya — of words to be spoken; bāhulya — elaboration; grantha — of the book; vistārera — of the big volume; dare — in fear; vistāre — in expanded form; nā — not; varni — I describe; sāra-artha — essential meaning; kahi — I say; alpa-akshare — in few words.

TRANSLATION

I purposely avoid extensive description for fear of increasing the bulk of this book. I shall describe the essence as concisely as possible.
