Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.21

tinera smarane haya vighna-vināśana

anāyāse haya nija vāñchita-pūrana

SYNONYMS

tinera — of these three; smarane — by remembrance; haya — there is; vighna-vināśana — the destruction of all difficulties; anāyāse — very easily; haya — there is; nija — our own; vāñchita — of the desired object; pūrana — fulfillment.

TRANSLATION

Such remembrance destroys all difficulties and very easily enables one to fulfill his own desires.
