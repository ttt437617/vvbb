Ādi-līlāChapter 1: The Spiritual Masters

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 1.25

caturtha ślokete kari jagate āśīrvāda

sarvatra māgiye krishna-caitanya-prasāda

SYNONYMS

caturtha — fourth; ślokete — in the verse; kari — I make; jagate — for the world; āśīh-vāda — benediction; sarvatra — everywhere; māgiye — I am begging; krishna-caitanya — of Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prasāda — the mercy.

TRANSLATION

In the fourth verse I have invoked the benediction of the Lord upon all the world, praying to Lord Caitanya for His mercy upon all.
