Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.33

ataeva dińmātra ihāń dekhāila

'caitanya-mańgale' sarva-loke khyāta haila

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; diń-mātra — only in the matter of indication; ihāń — here; dekhāila — I have exhibited; caitanya-mańgale — in the book of the name Caitanya-mańgala; sarva-loke — all over the world; khyāta — famous; haila — became.

TRANSLATION

I have given but a single hint of these pastimes, for Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura, in his book Caitanya-mańgala [now Caitanya-bhāgavata], has described them all vividly.
