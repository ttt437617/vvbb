Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.8

eka dina mātāra pade kariyā pranāma

prabhu kahe, — mātā, more deha eka dāna

SYNONYMS

eka dina — one day; mātāra — of the mother; pade — on the feet; kariyā — doing; pranāma — obeisances; prabhu — the Lord; kahe — said; mātā — My dear mother; more — unto Me; deha — give; eka — one; dāna — gift.

TRANSLATION

One day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fell down at the feet of His mother and requested her to give Him one thing in charity.
