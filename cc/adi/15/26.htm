Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.26

grihinī vinā griha-dharma nā haya śobhana

eta cinti' vivāha karite haila mana

SYNONYMS

grihinī — wife; vinā — without; griha-dharma — duties of family life; nā — not; haya — become; śobhana — beautiful; eta cinti' — thinking thus; vivāha — marriage; karite — to execute; haila — became; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"Without a wife," Lord Caitanya considered, "there is no meaning to householder life." Thus the Lord decided to marry.
