Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.27

na griham griham ity āhur

grihinī griham ucyate

tayā hi sahitah sarvān

purushārthān samaśnute

SYNONYMS

na — not; griham — the home; griham — the house; iti — thus; āhuh — said; grihinī — the wife; griham — home; ucyate — it is said; tayā — with her; hi — certainly; sahitah — together; sarvān — all; purusha-arthān — goals of human life; samaśnute — perfects.

TRANSLATION

"Merely a house is not a home, for it is a wife who gives a home its meaning. If one lives at home with his wife, together they can fulfill all the interests of human life."
