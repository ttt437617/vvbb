Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.25

kata dine prabhu citte karilā cintana

grihastha ha-ilāma, ebe cāhi griha-dharma

SYNONYMS

kata dine — after some days; prabhu — the Lord; citte — within His mind; karilā — made; cintana — consideration; grihastha ha-ilāma — I remained in householder life; ebe — now; cāhi — I want; griha-dharma — activities of family life.

TRANSLATION

After some days the Lord thought, "I did not take sannyāsa, and since I am remaining at home it is My duty to act as a grihastha.
