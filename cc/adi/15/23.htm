Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.23

kata dina rahi' miśra gelā para-loka

mātā-putra duńhāra bādila hridi śoka

SYNONYMS

kata dina — some days; rahi' — remaining; miśra — Jagannātha Miśra; gelā — passed away; para-loka — for the transcendental world; mātā — mother; putra — son; duńhāra — of both of them; bādila — increased; hridi — in the hearts; śoka — lamentation.

TRANSLATION

After some days, Jagannātha Miśra passed away from this world to the transcendental world, and both mother and son were very much aggrieved in their hearts.
