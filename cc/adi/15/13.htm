Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.13

śuni, śacī-miśrera duhkhī haila mana

tabe prabhu mātā-pitāra kaila āśvāsana

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing this; śacī — of mother Śacī; miśrera — and of Jagannātha Miśra; duhkhī — very unhappy; haila — became; mana — minds; tabe — at that time; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mātā-pitāra — of the parents; kaila — did; āśvāsana — pacification.

TRANSLATION

When Śacīmātā and Jagannātha Miśra heard of the departure of their elder son, Viśvarūpa, they were very unhappy, but Lord Caitanya tried to console them.
