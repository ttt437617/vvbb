Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.20

grihastha ha-iyā kariba pitā-mātāra sevana

ihāte-i tushta habena lakshmī-nārāyana

SYNONYMS

grihastha — a householder; ha-iyā — becoming; kariba — I shall do; pitā-mātāra — of the parents; sevana — service; ihāte-i — in this; tushta — satisfied; habena — will become; lakshmī-nārāyana — the goddess of fortune and Nārāyana.

TRANSLATION

"'Later I shall become a householder and thus serve My parents, for this action will very much satisfy Lord Nārāyana and His wife, the goddess of fortune.'
