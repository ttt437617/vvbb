Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.31

vistāriyā varnilā tāhā vrindāvana-dāsa

ei ta' pauganda-līlāra sūtra-prakāśa

SYNONYMS

vistāriyā — having elaborated; varnilā — has described; tāhā — that; vrindāvana-dāsa — Thākura Vrindāvana dāsa; ei ta' — this is; pauganda-līlāra — of the pastimes of His early age; sūtra-prakāśa — manifestation of the synopsis.

TRANSLATION

Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has elaborately described all these pastimes of the Lord's early age. What I have given is but a condensed presentation of the same pastimes.
