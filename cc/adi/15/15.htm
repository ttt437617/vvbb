Ādi-līlāChapter 15: The Lord's Pauganda-līlā

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Ādi 15.15

āmi ta' kariba tomā' duńhāra sevana

śuniyā santushta haila pitā-mātāra mana

SYNONYMS

āmi ta' — I; kariba — shall do; tomā — for you; duńhāra — both; sevana — service; śuniyā — after hearing; santushta — pleased; haila — became; pitā-mātāra mana — the parents' minds.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu assured His parents that He would serve them, and thus the minds of His father and mother were satisfied.
