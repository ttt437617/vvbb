Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.128

hasta tuli' rahena prabhu, nā karena bhojana

tabe pandita kahena kichu saprema vacana

SYNONYMS

hasta tuli' — raising His hands; rahena prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu remained; nā karena bhojana — did not eat; tabe — at that time; pandita kahena — Jagadānanda said; kichu — some; sa-prema vacana — words with great affection and love.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu kept His hands raised and would not accept the prasādam until Jagadānanda Pandita, with great affection and love, spoke the following words.
