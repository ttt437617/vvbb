Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.127

prabhu kahe, — "dvitīya-pāte bāda' anna-vyañjana

tomāya āmāya āji ekatra kariba bhojana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; dvitīya-pāte — on a second leaf; bāda' — deliver; anna-vyañjana — cooked rice and vegetables; tomāya āmāya — both you and I; āji — today; ekatra — together; kariba bhojana — will take lunch.

TRANSLATION

The Lord said, "Spread another leaf with a helping of rice and vegetables so that today you and I may take lunch together."
