Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.142

pandita kahe, — "prabhu yāi' karuna viśrāma

mui, ebe la-iba prasāda kari' samādhāna

SYNONYMS

pandita kahe — Jagadānanda Pandita said; prabhu — my Lord; yāi' — going; karuna viśrāma — take rest; mui — I; ebe — now; la-iba prasāda — shall take prasādam; kari' samādhāna — after arranging.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda replied, "My Lord, You go take rest. I shall take prasādam after I finish making some arrangements.
