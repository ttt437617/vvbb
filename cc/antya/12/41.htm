Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.41

śivānande lāthi mārilā, — ihā nā kahilā

ethā saba vaishnava-gana āsiyā mililā

SYNONYMS

śivānande — Śivānanda Sena; lāthi mārilā — (Lord Nityānanda) has kicked; ihā — this; nā kahilā — he did not say; ethā — here; saba — all; vaishnava-gana — devotees; āsiyā — coming; mililā — met.

TRANSLATION

As he described the Vaishnavas, therefore, he did not mention Lord Nityānanda's kicking Śivānanda Sena. Meanwhile, all the devotees arrived and went to meet the Lord.
