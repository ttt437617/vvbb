Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.76

prabhu sabāra galā dhari' karena rodana

kāndite kāndite sabāya kailā ālińgana

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāra — of all of them; galā — necks; dhari' — catching; karena rodana — began to cry; kāndite kāndite — crying and crying; sabāya — all of them; kailā ālińgana — He embraced.

TRANSLATION

Catching hold of His devotees, the Lord embraced them all and began to cry and cry.
