Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.87

āira carana yāi' karilā vandana

jagannāthera vastra-prasāda kailā nivedana

SYNONYMS

āira — of Śacīmātā; carana — to the lotus feet; yāi' — going; karilā vandana — offered prayers; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; vastra-prasāda — cloth and prasādam; kailā nivedana — offered.

TRANSLATION

When he arrived, he offered prayers at her lotus feet and then offered her the cloth and prasādam of Lord Jagannātha.
