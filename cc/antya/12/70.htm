Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.70

āilena ācārya-gosāñi more kripā kari'

prema-rine baddha āmi, śudhite nā pāri

SYNONYMS

āilena — has come; ācārya-gosāñi — Advaita Ācārya; more — to Me; kripā kari' — giving mercy; prema — of love; rine — by the debt; baddha āmi — I am bound; śudhite — to pay back; nā pāri — I am unable.

TRANSLATION

"Out of His causeless mercy upon Me, Advaita Ācārya has also come here. I am indebted to Him for His affectionate behavior. This debt is impossible for Me to liquidate.
