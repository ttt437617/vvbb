Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.64

dine nānā krīdā kare lañā bhakta-gana

rātrye krishna-vicchede prabhu karena rodana

SYNONYMS

dine — during the day; nānā — various; krīdā kare — performed pastimes; lañā bhakta-gana — with His devotees; rātrye — at night; krishna-vicchede — because of separation from Krishna; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena rodana — cries.

TRANSLATION

During the day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu engaged in various activities with His devotees, but at night He felt great separation from Krishna and used to cry.
