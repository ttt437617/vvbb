Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.62

cāturmāsya saba yātrā kailā daraśana

mālinī-prabhriti prabhure kailā nimantrana

SYNONYMS

cāturmāsya — for four months; saba yātrā — all the festivals; kailā daraśana — saw; mālinī-prabhriti — ladies like Mālinī, the wife of Śrīvāsa Thākura; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā nimantrana — made invitations.

TRANSLATION

For four consecutive months, the devotees observed all the festivals. The wives, such as Mālinī, extended invitations for lunch to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
