Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.14

śacīmātā dekhi' sabe tāńra ājñā lañā

ānande calilā krishna-kīrtana kariyā

SYNONYMS

śacī-mātā dekhi' — seeing Śacīmātā; sabe — all of them; tāńra ājñā lañā — taking her permission; ānande — with great jubilation; calilā — they proceeded; krishna-kīrtana kariyā — performing congregational chanting.

TRANSLATION

The devotees first saw Śacīmātā and took her permission. Then in great happiness they started for Jagannātha Purī, congregationally chanting the holy name of the Lord.
