Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.26

carane dhariyā prabhure vāsāya lañā gelā

vāsā diyā hrishta hañā kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

carane — the feet; dhariyā — catching; prabhure — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; vāsāya — to His residence; lañā — taking; gelā — went; vāsā diyā — after giving His residential quarters; hrishta hañā — being very pleased; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Śivānanda Sena touched the lotus feet of Nityānanda Prabhu and led Him to His residence. After giving the Lord His quarters, Śivānanda Sena, being very pleased, spoke as follows.
