Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.56

prabhu-vishaye sneha tāra bālaka-kāla haite

se vatsara seha āila prabhure dekhite

SYNONYMS

prabhu-vishaye — in regard to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sneha — affection; tāra — of Parameśvara Modaka; bālaka-kāla haite — since He was a boy; se vatsara — that year; seha — he also; āila — came; prabhure dekhite — to see the Lord.

TRANSLATION

Parameśvara Modaka had been affectionate toward the Lord since His childhood, and he was one of those who came that year to see the Lord at Jagannātha Purī.
