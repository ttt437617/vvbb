Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.30

āji mora saphala haila janma, kula, karma

āji pāinu krishna-bhakti, artha, kāma, dharma"

SYNONYMS

āji — today; mora — my; sa-phala — successful; haila — became; janma — birth; kula — family; karma — activities; āji — today; pāinu — I have gotten; krishna-bhakti — devotional service to Lord Krishna; artha — economic development; kāma — satisfaction of the senses; dharma — religion.

TRANSLATION

"Today my birth, my family and my activities have all become successful. Today I have achieved the fulfillment of religious principles, economic development, satisfaction of the senses and ultimately devotional service to Lord Krishna."
