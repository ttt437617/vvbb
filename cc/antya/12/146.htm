Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.146

"tumi śīghra yāha karite pāda-samvāhane

kahiha, — 'pandita ebe vasila bhojane'

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; śīghra — hastily; yāha — go; karite — to perform; pāda-samvāhane — massaging the feet; kahiha — say; pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; ebe — just now; vasila bhojane — sat down to eat.

TRANSLATION

"Go quickly and massage the Lord's feet," he said. "You may tell Him, 'The Pandita has just sat down to take his meal.'
