Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.22

śivānandera patnī tāńre kahena kāndiyā

'putre śāpa dichena gosāñi vāsā nā pāñā'

SYNONYMS

śivānandera — of Śivānanda Sena; patnī — the wife; tāńre — unto him; kahena — says; kāndiyā — crying; putre — on our sons; śāpa — curse; dichena — awarded; gosāñi — Nityānanda Prabhu; vāsā nā pāñā — not getting His residential quarters.

TRANSLATION

Crying, his wife informed him, "Lord Nityānanda has cursed our sons to die because His quarters have not been provided."
