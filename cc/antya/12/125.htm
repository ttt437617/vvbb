Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.125

saghrita śālyanna kalā-pāte stūpa kailā

kalāra dońgā bhari' vyañjana caudike dharilā

SYNONYMS

sa-ghrita — mixed with ghee; śāli-anna — very fine rice; kalā-pāte — on a banana leaf; stūpa kailā — stacked; kalāra dońgā — pots made of the bark of a banana tree; bhari' — filling; vyañjana — vegetables; cau-dike — all around; dharilā — placed.

TRANSLATION

He had cooked fine rice, mixed it with ghee and piled it high on a banana leaf. There were also varieties of vegetables, placed all around in pots made of banana tree bark.
