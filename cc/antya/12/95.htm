Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.95

ei-mata jagadānanda śacīmātā-sane

caitanyera sukha-kathā kahe rātri-dine

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; śacīmātā-sane — with mother Śacī; caitanyera — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sukha-kathā — words of happiness; kahe — says; rātri-dine — day and night.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Jagadānanda Pandita and mother Śacī talked day and night about the happiness of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
