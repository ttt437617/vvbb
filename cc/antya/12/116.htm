Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.116

prabhu kahe, — "pandita, taila ānilā gauda ha-ite

āmi ta' sannyāsī, — taila nā pāri la-ite

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; pandita — My dear Pandita; taila — oil; ānilā — you have brought; gauda ha-ite — from Bengal; āmi — I; ta' — but; sannyāsī — a sannyāsī; taila — oil; nā pāri la-ite — I cannot accept.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Jagadānanda Pandita, "My dear Pandita, you have brought Me some oil from Bengal, but since I am in the renounced order, I cannot accept it.
