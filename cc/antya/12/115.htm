Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.115

śuni prabhura vākya govinda mauna karilā

prātah-kāle jagadānanda prabhu-sthāne āilā

SYNONYMS

śuni — hearing; prabhura vākya — the statement of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; govinda — Govinda; mauna karilā — remained silent; prātah-kāle — in the morning; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; prabhu-sthāne — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came.

TRANSLATION

Hearing these words of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda remained silent. The next morning, Jagadānanda went to see the Lord.
