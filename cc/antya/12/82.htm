Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.82

cale saba bhakta-gana rodana kariyā

mahāprabhu rahilā ghare vishanna hañā

SYNONYMS

cale — proceed; saba — all; bhakta-gana — the devotees; rodana kariyā — crying; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahilā — remained; ghare — at His place; vishanna hañā — being very morose.

TRANSLATION

The devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began their journey crying, while the Lord remained morosely at His residence.
