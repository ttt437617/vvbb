Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.147

tomāre prabhura 'śesha' rākhimu dhariyā

prabhu nidrā gele, tumi khāiha āsiyā"

SYNONYMS

tomāre — for you; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha — remnants of food; rākhimu — I shall keep; dhariyā — taking; prabhu nidrā gele — when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is asleep; tumi — you; khāiha āsiyā — come and eat.

TRANSLATION

"I shall keep some remnants of the Lord's food for you. When He is asleep, come and take your portion."
