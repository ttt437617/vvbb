Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.35

"caitanyera pārishada mora mātulera khyāti

'thākurālī' karena gosāñi, tāńre māre lāthi"

SYNONYMS

caitanyera pārishada — associate of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mora — my; mātulera — of the maternal uncle; khyāti — reputation; thākurālī — superiority; karena — exhibits; gosāñi — Nityānanda Prabhu; tāńre — him; māre lāthi — kicks.

TRANSLATION

"My uncle is well known as one of the associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but Lord Nityānanda Prabhu asserts His superiority by kicking him."
