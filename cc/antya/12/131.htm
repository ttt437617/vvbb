Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.131

"krodhāveśera pākera haya aiche svāda!

ei ta' jāniye tomāya krishnera 'prasāda'

SYNONYMS

krodha-āveśera — in an angry mood; pākera — of cooking; haya — is; aiche — such; svāda — taste; ei ta' — for this reason; jāniye — I can understand; tomāya — unto you; krishnera prasāda — the mercy of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Even when you cook in an angry mood," He said, "the food is very delicious. This shows how pleased Krishna is with you.
