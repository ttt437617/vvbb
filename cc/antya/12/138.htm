Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.138

kichu balite nārena prabhu, khāyena tarāse

nā khāile jagadānanda karibe upavāse

SYNONYMS

kichu — anything; balite nārena — could not say; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; khāyena — eats; tarāse — out of fear; nā khāile — if He did not eat; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; karibe upavāse — would fast.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dared not forbid him to feed Him more. He just continued eating, fearful that Jagadānanda would fast if He stopped.
