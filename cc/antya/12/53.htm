Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.53

"śivānandera 'prakriti', putra — yāvat ethāya

āmāra avaśesha-pātra tārā yena pāya"

SYNONYMS

śivānandera — of Śivānanda Sena; prakriti — wife; putra — sons; yāvat — as long as; ethāya — here; āmāra — My; avaśesha-pātra — plate of the remnants of food; tārā — all of them; yena — must; pāya — get.

TRANSLATION

"As long as Śivānanda Sena's wife and children stay in Jagannātha Purī," He said, "they must be given the remnants of My food."
