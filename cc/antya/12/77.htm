Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.77

sabāi rahila, keha calite nārila

āra dina pāńca-sāta ei-mate gela

SYNONYMS

sabāi rahila — all of them stayed; keha calite nārila — no one could move; āra — further; dina pāńca-sāta — five to seven days; ei-mate — in this way; gela — passed.

TRANSLATION

Unable to leave, everyone remained there, and five to seven more days thus passed by.
