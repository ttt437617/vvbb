Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.38

prabhu kahe, — "śrīkānta āsiyāche pāñā mano-duhkha

kichu nā baliha, karuka, yāte ihāra sukha"

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; śrīkānta — Śrīkānta; āsiyāche — has come; pāñā — getting; manah-duhkha — distress in the mind; kichu — anything; nā baliha — do not say; karuka — let him do; yāte — by which; ihāra — his; sukha — happiness.

TRANSLATION

As Govinda was warning Śrīkānta, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Don't bother him. Let Śrīkānta do whatever he likes, for he has come here in a distressed state of mind."
