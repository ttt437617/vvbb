Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.6

rātri-dina ei daśā svasti nāhi mane

kashte rātri gońāya svarūpa-rāmānanda-sane

SYNONYMS

rātri-dina — day and night; ei daśā — this situation; svasti nāhi mane — no peace of mind; kashte — with great difficulty; rātri gońāya — passes the night; svarūpa-rāmānanda-sane — in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya.

TRANSLATION

This was His situation day and night. Unable to find peace of mind, He passed His nights with great difficulty in the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya.
