Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.135

punah punah pandita nānā vyañjana pariveśe

bhaye kichu nā balena prabhu, khāyena harishe

SYNONYMS

punah punah — again and again; pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; nānā vyañjana — various vegetables; pariveśe — administered; bhaye — out of fear; kichu — anything; nā balena — does not speak; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; khāyena — eats; harishe — very happily.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita continued to offer the Lord varieties of vegetables. Out of fear, the Lord said nothing but continued eating happily.
