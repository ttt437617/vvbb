Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.152

jagadānande-prabhute prema cale ei-mate

satyabhāmā-krishne yaiche śuni bhāgavate

SYNONYMS

jagadānande-prabhute — between Jagadānanda Pandita and the Lord; prema — affection; cale — goes on; ei-mate — in this way; satyabhāmā-krishne — between Satyabhāmā and Krishna; yaiche — as; śuni — we learn; bhāgavate — in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.

TRANSLATION

The affectionate loving exchanges between Jagadānanda Pandita and Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued in this manner, exactly like the exchanges between Satyabhāmā and Lord Krishna related in Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
