Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.136

āgraha kariyā pandita karāilā bhojana

āra dina haite bhojana haila daśa-guna

SYNONYMS

āgraha kariyā — with great eagerness; pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; karāilā bhojana — fed; āra dina — other days; haite — than; bhojana — the eating; haila — was; daśa-guna — ten times greater.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita eagerly forced the Lord to eat so much that He ate ten times more than on other days.
