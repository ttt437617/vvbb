Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.51

śivānandera bhāgya-sindhu ke pāibe pāra?

yāńra saba goshthīke prabhu kahe 'āpanāra'

SYNONYMS

śivānandera — of Śivānanda Sena; bhāgya-sindhu — the ocean of good fortune; ke — who; pāibe pāra — can cross over; yāńra — whose; saba goshthīke — whole family; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — says; āpanāra — His own.

TRANSLATION

No one can cross over the ocean of Śivānanda Sena's good fortune, for the Lord considered Śivānanda's whole family His own.
