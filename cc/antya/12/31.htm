Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.31

śuni' nityānanda-prabhura ānandita mana

uthi' śivānande kailā prema-ālińgana

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; nityānanda-prabhura — of Lord Nityānanda; ānandita — very pleased; mana — mind; uthi' — standing up; śivānande — unto Śivānanda Sena; kailā — performed; prema — in love; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

When Lord Nityānanda heard this, He was very happy. He rose and embraced Śivānanda Sena in great love.
