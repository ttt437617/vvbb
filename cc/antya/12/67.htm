Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.67

"prati-varshe āisa sabe āmāre dekhite

āsite yāite duhkha pāo bahu-mate

SYNONYMS

prati-varshe — every year; āisa — come; sabe — all of you; āmāre dekhite — to see Me; āsite — to come; yāite — to return; duhkha pāo — you get much trouble; bahu-mate — in various ways.

TRANSLATION

"All of you come to see Me every year," the Lord said. "To come here and then return must certainly give you great trouble.
