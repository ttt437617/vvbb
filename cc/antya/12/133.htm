Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.133

aiche amrita-anna krishne kara samarpana

tomāra bhāgyera sīmā ke kare varnana?"

SYNONYMS

aiche — such; amrita-anna — nectarean rice; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; kara samarpana — you offer; tomāra — your; bhāgyera — of fortune; sīmā — limit; ke — who; kare varnana — can describe.

TRANSLATION

"You offer such nectarean rice to Krishna. Who can estimate the limit of your fortune?"
