Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.132

āpane khāibe krishna, tāhāra lāgiyā

tomāra haste pāka karāya uttama kariyā

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; khāibe — will eat; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāhāra lāgiyā — for that reason; tomāra haste — by your hands; pāka karāya — causes to cook; uttama kariyā — so nicely.

TRANSLATION

"Because He will personally eat the food, Krishna makes you cook so nicely.
