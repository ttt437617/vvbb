Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.78

advaita avadhūta kichu kahe prabhu-pāya

"sahaje tomāra gune jagat vikāya

SYNONYMS

advaita — Advaita Prabhu; avadhūta — Nityānanda Prabhu; kichu — something; kahe — said; prabhu-pāya — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sahaje — naturally; tomāra — Your; gune — because of transcendental attributes; jagat vikāya — the whole world is obligated to You.

TRANSLATION

Advaita Prabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu submitted these words at the lotus feet of the Lord: "The entire world is naturally obligated to You for Your transcendental attributes.
