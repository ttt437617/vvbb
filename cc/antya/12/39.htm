Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.39

vaishnavera samācāra gosāñi puchilā

eke eke sabāra nāma śrīkānta jānāilā

SYNONYMS

vaishnavera — of all the Vaishnavas; samācāra — news; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; puchilā — inquired; eke eke — one after another; sabāra — of all of them; nāma — names; śrīkānta — the nephew of Śivānanda Sena; jānāilā — informed.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from Śrīkānta about all the Vaishnavas, and the boy informed the Lord about them, naming them one after another.
