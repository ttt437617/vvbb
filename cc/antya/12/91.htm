Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.91

bhojana kariyā kahe ānandita hañā

mātā āji khāoyāilā ākantha pūriyā

SYNONYMS

bhojana kariyā — after eating; kahe — says; ānandita hañā — being very pleased; mātā — Mother; āji — today; khāoyāilā — fed; ākantha — up to the neck; pūriyā — filling.

TRANSLATION

"After eating the food, the Lord says, 'Today Mother has fed Me up to My neck.
