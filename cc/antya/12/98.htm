Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.98

vāsudeva, murāri-gupta jagadānande pāñā

ānande rākhilā ghare, nā dena chādiyā

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva — Vāsudeva; murāri-gupta — Murāri Gupta; jagadānande pāñā — getting Jagadānanda; ānande — in great happiness; rākhilā — kept; ghare — at home; nā dena chādiyā — did not allow to go out.

TRANSLATION

Vāsudeva Datta and Murāri Gupta were so pleased to see Jagadānanda Pandita that they kept him at their homes and would not allow him to leave.
