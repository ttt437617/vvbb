Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.110

ei kathā govinda jagadānandere kahila

mauna kari' rahila pandita, kichu nā kahila

SYNONYMS

ei kathā — this message; govinda — Govinda; jagadānandere kahila — informed Jagadānanda; mauna kari' — keeping silent; rahila — remained; pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; kichu — anything; nā kahila — did not reply.

TRANSLATION

When Govinda informed Jagadānanda Pandita of this message, Jagadānanda remained silent, not saying even a word.
