Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.65

ei-mata nānā-līlāya cāturmāsya gela

gauda-deśe yāite tabe bhakte ājñā dila

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; nānā-līlāya — in various pastimes; cāturmāsya gela — the four months of the rainy season passed; gauda-deśe yāite — to return to Bengal; tabe — at that time; bhakte — all the devotees; ājñā dila — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered.

TRANSLATION

In this way the Lord spent the four months of the rainy season in various pastimes, and then He ordered the Bengali devotees to return to their homes.
