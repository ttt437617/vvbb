Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.40

'duhkha pāñā āsiyāche' — ei prabhura vākya śuni'

jānilā 'sarvajña prabhu' — eta anumāni'

SYNONYMS

duhkha — unhappiness; pāñā — getting; āsiyāche — he has come; ei — this; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vākya — statement; śuni' — hearing; jānilā — could understand; sarvajña prabhu — the Lord is omniscient; eta — this; anumāni' — guessing.

TRANSLATION

When Śrīkānta Sena heard the Lord say "He is distressed," he could understand that the Lord is omniscient.
