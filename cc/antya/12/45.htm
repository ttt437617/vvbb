Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.45

chota-putre dekhi' prabhu nāma puchilā

'paramānanda-dāsa'-nāma sena jānāilā

SYNONYMS

chota-putre — the youngest son; dekhi' — seeing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāma puchilā — inquired about his name; paramānanda-dāsa — Paramānanda dāsa; nāma — name; sena — Śivānanda Sena; jānāilā — informed.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya asked the youngest son's name, and Śivānanda Sena informed the Lord that his name was Paramānanda dāsa.
