Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.134

pandita kahe, — "ye khāibe, sei pāka-kartā

āmi-saba — kevala-mātra sāmagrī-āhartā"

SYNONYMS

pandita kahe — the Pandita said; ye khāibe — He who will eat; sei — He; pāka-kartā — the cook; āmi-saba — as far as I am concerned; kevala-mātra — only; sāmagrī — of ingredients; āhartā — collector.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita replied, "He who will eat has cooked this. As far as I am concerned, I simply collect the ingredients."
