Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.103

sugandhi kariyā taila gāgarī bhariyā

nīlācale lañā āilā yatana kariyā

SYNONYMS

su-gandhi kariyā — making aromatic; taila — oil; gāgarī — a big pot; bhariyā — filling; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; lañā — taking; āilā — came; yatana kariyā — with great care.

TRANSLATION

They filled a large earthen pot with the aromatic oil, and with great care Jagadānanda Pandita brought it to Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī.
