Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.150

"dekha, — jagadānanda prasāda pāya ki nā pāya

śīghra āsi' samācāra kahibe āmāya"

SYNONYMS

dekha — see; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; prasāda — prasādam; pāya — gets; ki — or; nā — not; pāya — gets; śīghra āsi' — coming hastily; samācāra — the news; kahibe — inform; āmāya — to Me.

TRANSLATION

The Lord told him, "Go see whether Jagadānanda Pandita is eating. Then quickly return and let Me know."
