Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.121

tritīya divase prabhu tāńra dvāre yāñā

'uthaha' pandita' — kari' kahena dākiyā

SYNONYMS

tritīya divase — on the third day; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra — of Jagadānanda Pandita; dvāre — to the door; yāñā — going; uthaha' — please get up; pandita — My dear Jagadānanda Pandita; kari' — saying; kahena — said; dākiyā — calling.

TRANSLATION

Three days later, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to the door of his room and said, "My dear Jagadānanda Pandita, please get up.
