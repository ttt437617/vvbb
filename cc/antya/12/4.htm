Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.4

atahpara mahāprabhura vishanna-antara

krishnera viyoga-daśā sphure nirantara

SYNONYMS

atahpara — thereafter; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vishanna-antara — morose mind; krishnera — of Krishna; viyoga-daśā — feeling of separation; sphure — manifests; nirantara — continuously.

TRANSLATION

The mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was always morose because of a continuously manifested feeling of separation from Krishna.
