Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.73

sannyāsī mānusha mora, nāhi kona dhana

ki diyā tomāra rina karimu śodhana?

SYNONYMS

sannyāsī mānusha — in the renounced order of life; mora — My; nāhi — there is not; kona — any; dhana — money; ki — what; diyā — giving; tomāra rina — the debt to you; karimu śodhana — shall I repay.

TRANSLATION

"I am a mendicant and have no money. How can I clear My debt for the favor you have shown Me?
