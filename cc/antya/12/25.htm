Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.25

ānandita hailā śivāi pāda-prahāra pāñā

śīghra vāsā-ghara kailā gauda-ghare giyā

SYNONYMS

ānandita hailā — became very pleased; śivāi — Śivānanda Sena; pāda-prahāra pāñā — being kicked; śīghra — very soon; vāsā-ghara — residential place; kailā — arranged; gauda-ghare — to a milkman's house; giyā — going.

TRANSLATION

Very pleased at being kicked, Śivānanda Sena quickly arranged for a milkman's house to be the Lord's residence.
