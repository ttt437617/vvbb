Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.1

śrūyatām śrūyatām nityam

gīyatām gīyatām mudā

cintyatām cintyatām bhaktāś

caitanya-caritāmritam

SYNONYMS

śrūyatām — let it be heard; śrūyatām — let it be heard; nityam — always; gīyatām — let it be chanted; gīyatām — let it be chanted; mudā — with great happiness; cintyatām — let it be meditated upon; cintyatām — let it be meditated upon; bhaktāh — O devotees; caitanya-caritāmritam — the transcendental life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

O devotees, may the transcendental life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always be heard, chanted and meditated upon with great happiness.
