Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.123

eta bali' prabhu gelā, pandita uthilā

snāna kari' nānā vyañjana randhana karilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu gelā — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu left; pandita uthilā — Jagadānanda Pandita got up; snāna kari' — taking his bath; nānā — various; vyañjana — vegetables; randhana karilā — cooked.

TRANSLATION

After Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said this and left, Jagadānanda Pandita got up from his bed, bathed and began to cook varieties of vegetables.
