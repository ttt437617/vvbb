Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.92

āmi yāi' bhojana kari — mātā nāhi jāne

sākshāte khāi āmi' teńho 'svapna' hena māne"

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; yāi' — going; bhojana kari — eat; mātā — mother; nāhi jāne — cannot understand; sākshāte — directly; khāi āmi' — I eat; teńho — she; svapna — a dream; hena — as; māne — thinks.

TRANSLATION

"'I go there and eat the food My mother offers, but she cannot understand that I am eating it directly. She thinks that this is a dream.'"
