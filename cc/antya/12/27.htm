Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.27

"āji more bhritya kari' ańgīkāra kailā

yemana aparādha bhrityera, yogya phala dilā

SYNONYMS

āji — today; more — me; bhritya — servant; kari' — as; ańgīkāra — acceptance; kailā — You have done; yemana — as; aparādha — offense; bhrityera — of the servant; yogya — proper; phala — result; dilā — You have given.

TRANSLATION

"Today You have accepted me as Your servant and have properly punished me for my offense.
