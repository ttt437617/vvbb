Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.118

pandita kahe, — 'ke tomāre kahe mithyā vānī

āmi gauda haite taila kabhu nāhi āni'

SYNONYMS

pandita kahe — Jagadānanda Pandita said; ke — who; tomāre — unto You; kahe — says; mithyā vānī — false stories; āmi — I; gauda haite — from Bengal; taila — oil; kabhu nāhi āni — never brought.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita replied, "Who tells You all these false stories? I never brought any oil from Bengal."
