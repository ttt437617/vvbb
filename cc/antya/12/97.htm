Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.97

ācārya milite tabe gelā jagadānanda

jagadānande pāñā haila ācārya ānanda

SYNONYMS

ācārya milite — to meet Advaita Ācārya; tabe — thereafter; gelā — went; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; jagadānande pāñā — getting Jagadānanda; haila — became; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; ānanda — very happy.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita thereafter went to meet Advaita Ācārya, who also was very happy to have him.
