Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.29

brahmāra durlabha tomāra śrī-carana-renu

hena carana-sparśa pāila mora adhama tanu

SYNONYMS

brahmāra — by Lord Brahmā; durlabha — almost unattainable; tomāra — Your; śrī-carana-renu — dust of the lotus feet; hena — such; carana-sparśa — touch of the lotus feet; pāila — got; mora — my; adhama — most fallen; tanu — body.

TRANSLATION

"The dust of Your lotus feet is not attainable even by Lord Brahmā, yet Your lotus feet have touched my wretched body.
