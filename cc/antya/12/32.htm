Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.32

ānandita śivānanda kare samādhāna

ācāryādi-vaishnavere dilā vāsā-sthāna

SYNONYMS

ānandita — pleased; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; kare samādhāna — began to arrange things; ācārya-ādi-vaishnavere — unto all the Vaishnavas, headed by Advaita Ācārya; dilā — gave; vāsā-sthāna — residential places.

TRANSLATION

Being very much pleased by Nityānanda Prabhu's behavior, Śivānanda Sena began to arrange residential quarters for all the Vaishnavas, headed by Advaita Ācārya.
