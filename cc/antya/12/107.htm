Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.107

eka-kalasa sugandhi taila gaudete kariyā

ihāń āniyāche bahu yatana kariyā"

SYNONYMS

eka-kalasa — one big full jug; su-gandhi taila — scented oil; gaudete kariyā — manufacturing in Bengal; ihāń — here; āniyāche — has brought; bahu yatana kariyā — with great care.

TRANSLATION

"He prepared a large jug of it in Bengal, and with great care he has brought it here."
