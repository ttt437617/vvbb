Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.86

pūrva-varshe jagadānanda 'āi' dekhibāre

prabhu-ājñā lañā āilā nadīyā-nagare

SYNONYMS

pūrva-varshe — in the previous year; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; āi — Śacīmātā; dekhibāre — to see; prabhu-ājñā lañā — taking the permission of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; nadīyā-nagare — to the city of Nadia.

TRANSLATION

The previous year, Jagadānanda Pandita, following the Lord's order, had returned to the city of Nadia to see Śacīmātā.
