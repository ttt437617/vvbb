Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.120

taila bhāńgi' sei pathe nija-ghara giyā

śuiyā rahilā ghare kapāta khiliyā

SYNONYMS

taila bhāńgi' — breaking the pot of oil; sei — he; pathe — by the path; nija-ghara — to his room; giyā — going; śuiyā rahilā — lay down; ghare — in the room; kapāta — the door; khiliyā — bolting closed.

TRANSLATION

After breaking the jug, Jagadānanda Pandita returned to his residence, bolted the door and lay down.
