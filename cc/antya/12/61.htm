Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.61

pūrvavat sabā lañā gundicā-mārjana

ratha-āge pūrvavat karilā nartana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as previously; sabā — all the devotees; lañā — taking; gundicā-mārjana — the cleaning of the Gundicā temple; ratha-āge — in front of the chariot; pūrva-vat — as previously; karilā nartana — danced.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees engaged in the cleansing ceremony of the Gundicā temple and danced in front of the Ratha-yātrā chariot, just as they had done in the past.
