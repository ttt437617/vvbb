Antya-līlāChapter 12: The Loving Dealings Between Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Jagadānanda Pandita

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 12.88

prabhura nāme mātāre dandavat kailā

prabhura vinati-stuti mātāre kahilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura nāme — in the name of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mātāre — to His mother; dandavat kailā — he offered obeisances; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vinati-stuti — very submissive prayers; mātāre — to His mother; kahilā — he informed.

TRANSLATION

He offered obeisances to Śacīmātā in the name of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and informed her of all the Lord's submissive prayers to her.
