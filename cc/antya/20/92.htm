Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.92

'āmi likhi', — eha mithyā kari anumāna

āmāra śarīra kāshtha-putalī-samāna

SYNONYMS

āmi likhi — I write; eha mithyā — this is false; kari anumāna — I can infer; āmāra śarīra — my body; kāshtha-putalī-samāna — is exactly like a wooden doll.

TRANSLATION

I infer that "I have written" is a false understanding, for my body is like a wooden doll.
