Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.123

caturdaśe — divyonmāda-ārambha varnana

'śarīra' ethā prabhura, 'mana' gelā vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

caturdaśe — in the Fourteenth Chapter; divya-unmāda-ārambha — the beginning of the spiritual trance of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; varnana — describing; śarīra — the body; ethā — here; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mana — the mind; gelā — went; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

The Fourteenth Chapter describes the beginning of the Lord's spiritual trance, in which His body was at Jagannātha Purī but His mind was in Vrindāvana.
