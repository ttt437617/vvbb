Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.58

"krishna — mora jīvana, krishna — mora prāna-dhana,

krishna — mora prānera parāna

hridaya-upare dharoń, sevā kari' sukhī karoń,

ei mora sadā rahe dhyāna

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; mora jīvana — My life and soul; krishna — Lord Krishna; mora prāna-dhana — the wealth of My life; krishna — Lord Krishna; mora prānera parāna — the life of My life; hridaya-upare — on My heart; dharoń — I hold; sevā kari' — serving; sukhī karoń — I make happy; ei — this; mora — My; sadā — always; rahe — remains; dhyāna — meditation.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna is My life and soul. Krishna is the treasure of My life. Indeed, Krishna is the very life of My life. I therefore keep Him always in My heart and try to please Him by rendering service. That is My constant meditation.
