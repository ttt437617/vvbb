Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.154

caritam amritam etac chrīla-caitanya-vishnoh

śubha-dam aśubha-nāśi śraddhayāsvādayed yah

tad-amala-pada-padme bhrińgatām etya so 'yam

rasayati rasam uccaih prema-mādhvīka-pūram

SYNONYMS

caritam — the character and activities; amritam — nectarean; etat — this; śrīla — most opulent; caitanya — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vishnoh — of Him who is Lord Vishnu Himself, the Supreme Personality of Godhead; śubha-dam — giving auspiciousness; aśubha-nāśi — destroying all inauspiciousness; śraddhayā — with faith and love; āsvādayet — should taste; yah — anyone who; tat-amala-pada-padme — at the spotless lotus feet of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; bhrińgatām etya — becoming like a bumblebee; sah — that person; ayam — this; rasayati — tastes; rasam — transcendental mellow; uccaih — a large quantity; prema-mādhvīka — of the wine of ecstatic love; pūram — full.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita is filled with the activities of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. It invokes all good fortune and destroys everything inauspicious. If one tastes the nectar of Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita with faith and love, I become like a bumblebee tasting the honey of transcendental love from his lotus feet.
