Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.65

prabhura 'śikshāshtaka'-śloka yei pade, śune

krishne prema-bhakti tāra bāde dine-dine

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śikshā-ashtaka — of the eight instructions; śloka — the verses; yei — anyone who; pade — recites; śune — or hears; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; prema-bhakti — ecstatic love and devotion; tāra — his; bāde — increases; dine-dine — day after day.

TRANSLATION

If anyone recites or hears these eight verses of instruction by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, his ecstatic love and devotion for Krishna increase day by day.
