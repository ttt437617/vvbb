Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.110

pañcame — pradyumna-miśre prabhu kripā karilā

rāya-dvārā krishna-kathā tāńre śunāilā

SYNONYMS

pañcame — in the Fifth Chapter; pradyumna-miśre — unto Pradyumna Miśra; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā karilā — showed mercy; rāya-dvārā — with the help of Rāmānanda Rāya; krishna-kathā — topics of Krishna; tāńre śunāilā — made him hear.

TRANSLATION

The Fifth Chapter tells how the Lord showed His favor to Pradyumna Miśra and made him hear topics of Krishna from Rāmānanda Rāya.
