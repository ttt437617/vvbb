Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.117

daśame — kahiluń bhakta-datta-āsvādana

rāghava-panditera tāhāń jhālira sājana

SYNONYMS

daśame — in the Tenth Chapter; kahiluń — I have described; bhakta-datta-āsvādana — the tasting of the food given by the devotees; rāghava-panditera — of Rāghava Pandita; tāhāń — therein; jhālira sājana — the assortment in the bags.

TRANSLATION

In the Tenth Chapter I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tasted the food given by His devotees, and I have also described the assortment of prasādam in the bags of Rāghava Pandita.
