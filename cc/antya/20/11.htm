Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.11

nāma-sańkīrtana haite sarvānartha-nāśa

sarva-śubhodaya, krishna-premera ullāsa

SYNONYMS

nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy names of the Lord; haite — from; sarva-anartha-nāśa — destruction of all undesirable things; sarva-śubha-udaya — awakening of all good fortune; krishna-premera ullāsa — the beginning of the flow of love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Simply by chanting the holy name of Lord Krishna, one can be freed from all undesirable habits. This is the means of awakening all good fortune and initiating the flow of waves of love for Krishna.
