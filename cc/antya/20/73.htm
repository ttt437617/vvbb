Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.73

vrindāvana-dāsa prathama ye līlā varnila

sei-saba līlāra āmi sūtra-mātra kaila

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-dāsa — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; prathama — at first; ye — whatever; līlā — pastimes; varnila — described; sei-saba — all of those; līlāra — of the pastimes; āmi — I; sūtra-mātra kaila — prepared only the synopsis.

TRANSLATION

Whatever pastimes Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has first described I have merely summarized.
