Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.80

aiche mahāprabhura līlā — nāhi ora-pāra

'jīva' hañā kebā samyak pāre varnibāra?

SYNONYMS

aiche — similarly; mahāprabhura līlā — the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāhi ora-pāra — there is no limit above or below; jīva hañā — being an ordinary living entity; kebā — who; samyak — fully; pāre — is able; varnibāra — to describe.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are like the unlimited sky. How, then, can an ordinary living being describe them all?
