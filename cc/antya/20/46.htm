Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.46

sei bhāve prabhu sei śloka uccārilā

śloka uccārite tad-rūpa āpane ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

sei bhāve — in that ecstatic mood; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sei — that; śloka — verse; uccārilā — recited; śloka — the verse; uccārite — by reciting; tat-rūpa — like Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; āpane — Himself; ha-ilā — became.

TRANSLATION

In the same spirit of ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited that verse, and as soon as He did so, He felt like Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
