Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.94

nānā-roga-grasta, — calite vasite nā pāri

pañca-roga-pīdā-vyākula, rātri-dine mari

SYNONYMS

nānā-roga-grasta — infected with so many diseases; calite — to walk; vasite — to sit down; nā pāri — I am not properly able; pañca-roga-pīdā-vyākula — always disturbed by five kinds of disease; rātri-dine — day or night; mari — I can die any time.

TRANSLATION

I am infected with so many diseases that I can neither properly walk nor properly sit. Indeed, I am always exhausted by five kinds of diseases. I may die at any time of the day or night.
