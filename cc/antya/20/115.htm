Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.115

ashtame — rāmacandra-purīra āgamana

tāńra bhaye kailā prabhu bhikshā sańkocana

SYNONYMS

ashtame — in the Eighth Chapter; rāmacandra-purīra āgamana — the arrival of Rāmacandra Purī; tāńra bhaye — because of fear of him; kailā — did; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā sańkocana — minimizing His eating.

TRANSLATION

The Eighth Chapter describes the arrival of Rāmacandra Purī and how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu minimized His eating due to fear of him.
