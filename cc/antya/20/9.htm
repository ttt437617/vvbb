Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.9

sańkīrtana-yajñe kalau krishna-ārādhana

sei ta' sumedhā pāya krishnera carana

SYNONYMS

sańkīrtana-yajñe — performing the yajña of chanting the Hare Krishna mantra; kalau — in this Age of Kali; krishna-ārādhana — the process of worshiping Krishna; sei ta' — such persons; su-medhā — greatly intelligent; pāya — get; krishnera carana — shelter at the lotus feet of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"In this Age of Kali, the process of worshiping Krishna is to perform sacrifice by chanting the holy name of the Lord. One who does so is certainly very intelligent, and he attains shelter at the lotus feet of Krishna.

PURPORT

For further information, one may refer to Ādi-līlā, Chapter Three, texts 77-78
