Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.84

ye kichu varniluń, seha sańkshepa kariyā

likhite nā pārena, tabu rākhiyāchena likhiyā

SYNONYMS

ye kichu varniluń — whatever I have described; seha — them; sańkshepa — briefly; kariyā — doing; likhite nā pārena — Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura was not able to describe; tabu — still; rākhiyāchena — has kept; likhiyā — recording in writing.

TRANSLATION

What I have described was left aside by Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura, but although he could not describe these pastimes, he gave us a synopsis.
