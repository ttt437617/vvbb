Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.88

caitanya-līlāmrita-sindhu — dugdhābdhi-samāna

trishnānurūpa jhārī bhari' teńho kailā pāna

SYNONYMS

caitanya-līlā-amrita-sindhu — the ocean of nectarean pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dugdha-abdhi-samāna — exactly like the ocean of milk; trishnā-anurūpa — according to one's thirst; jhārī — the pitcher; bhari' — filling; teńho — he; kailā pāna — drank.

TRANSLATION

The ocean of nectarean pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is like the ocean of milk. According to his thirst, Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura filled his pitcher and drank from that ocean.
