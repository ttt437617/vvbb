Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.105

dvitīye — chota-haridāse karāilā śikshana

tāra madhye śivānandera āścarya darśana

SYNONYMS

dvitīye — in the Second Chapter; chota-haridāse — Junior Haridāsa; karāilā śikshana — He taught very strictly; tāra madhye — within that chapter; śivānandera — of Śivānanda Sena; āścarya darśana — the wonderful vision.

TRANSLATION

The Second Chapter tells how the Lord instructively punished Junior Haridāsa. Also in that chapter is a description of the wonderful vision of Śivānanda Sena.
