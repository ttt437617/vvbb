Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.86

sańkshepe kahiluń, vistāra nā yāya kathane

vistāriyā veda-vyāsa kariba varnane

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe kahiluń — I have described very briefly; vistāra nā yāya kathane — it is not possible to describe them in full; vistāriyā — elaborating; veda-vyāsa — a representative of Vyāsadeva; kariba — will do; varnane — describing.

TRANSLATION

I have described the pastimes very briefly, for it is impossible for me to describe them in full. In the future, however, Vedavyāsa will describe them elaborately.
