Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.89

tāńra jhārī-śeshāmrita kichu more dilā

tateke bharila peta, trishnā mora gelā

SYNONYMS

tāńra jhārī-śesha-amrita — the remnants of the milk of Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura's pitcher; kichu — some; more dilā — has given to me; tateke — by those remnants; bharila peta — my abdomen is filled; trishnā mora gelā — now my thirst has gone.

TRANSLATION

Whatever remnants of milk Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has given me are sufficient to fill my belly. Now my thirst is completely satiated.
