Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.122

raghunātha-bhattācāryera tāhāńi milana

prabhu tāńre kripā kari' pāthāilā vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

raghunātha-bhattācāryera — of Raghunātha Bhatta; tāhāńi — there; milana — meeting; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — to him; kripā kari' — showing causeless mercy; pāthāilā vrindāvana — sent to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Also in the Thirteenth Chapter is an account of how Raghunātha Bhatta met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who by His causeless mercy sent him to Vrindāvana.
