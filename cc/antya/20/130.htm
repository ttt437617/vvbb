Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.130

mahā-prasādera tāhāń mahimā varnilā

krishnādharāmritera phala-śloka āsvādilā

SYNONYMS

mahā-prasādera — of the remnants of the food of the Lord, mahā-prasādam; tāhāń — there; mahimā — the glories; varnilā — described; krishna-adhara-amritera — of the nectar from the lips of Krishna; phala-śloka — the verse mentioning the result; āsvādilā — tasted.

TRANSLATION

Also in that chapter, the glories of mahā-prasādam are explained, and a verse is tasted describing the effect of nectar from the lips of Krishna.
