Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.35

punah ati-utkanthā, dainya ha-ila udgama

krishna-thāñi māge prema-nāma-sańkīrtana

SYNONYMS

punah — again; ati-utkanthā — great eagerness; dainya — humility; ha-ila udgama — were awakened; krishna-thāñi — from Lord Krishna; māge — prays for; prema — in ecstatic love; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting the mahā-mantra.

TRANSLATION

Natural humility and eagerness then awoke in Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He prayed to Krishna to be able to chant the mahā-mantra in ecstatic love.
