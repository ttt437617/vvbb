Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.127

tāra madhye prabhura pañcendriya-ākarshana

tāra madhye karilā rāse krishna-anveshana

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — within that; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pañca-indriya-ākarshana — the attraction of the five senses; tāra madhye — within that chapter; karilā — did; rāse — in the rāsa dance; krishna-anveshana — searching for Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Also in that chapter is a description of the attraction of Lord Caitanya's five senses to Krishna and how He searched for Krishna in the rāsa dance.
