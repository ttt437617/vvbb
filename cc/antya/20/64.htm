Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.64

pūrve ashta-śloka kari' loke śikshā dilā

sei ashta-ślokera artha āpane āsvādilā

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; ashta-śloka kari' — composing eight verses; loke śikshā dilā — gave instruction to the people in general; sei — those; ashta-ślokera — of the eight stanzas; artha — the meaning; āpane āsvādilā — personally tasted.

TRANSLATION

The Lord had formerly composed these eight verses to teach people in general. Now He personally tasted the meaning of the verses, which are called the Śikshāshtaka.
