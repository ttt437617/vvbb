Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.34

kripā kari' kara more pada-dhūli-sama

tomāra sevaka karoń tomāra sevana"

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — being merciful; kara — make; more — Me; pada-dhūli-sama — like a particle of dust at Your lotus feet; tomāra sevaka — as I am Your eternal servant; karoń — let Me be engaged; tomāra sevana — in Your service.

TRANSLATION

"Be causelessly merciful to Me by giving Me a place with the particles of dust at Your lotus feet so that I may engage in the service of Your Lordship as Your eternal servant."
