Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.45

eta bhāve rādhāra mana asthira ha-ilā

sakhī-gana-āge praudhi-śloka ye padilā

SYNONYMS

eta bhāve — in that mood; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; mana — the mind; asthira ha-ilā — became agitated; sakhī-gana-āge — before the friends, the gopīs; praudhi-śloka — advanced devotional verse; ye — which; padilā — She recited.

TRANSLATION

In that mood, the mind of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī was agitated, and therefore She spoke a verse of advanced devotion to Her gopī friends.
