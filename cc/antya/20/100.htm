Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.100

nā kahile haya mora krita-ghnatā-dosha

dambha kari bali' śrotā, nā kariha rosha

SYNONYMS

nā kahile — if I do not say; haya — there is; mora — my; krita-ghnatā-dosha — fault of ingratitude; dambha kari — I am proud; bali' — taking as; śrotā — O readers; nā kariha rosha — do not be angry.

TRANSLATION

If I did not disclose this fact, I would be guilty of ingratitude to the Lord. Therefore, my dear readers, please do not consider me too proud and be angry at me.
