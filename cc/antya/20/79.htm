Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.79

ākāśa — ananta, tāte yaiche pakshi-gana

yāra yata śakti, tata kare ārohana

SYNONYMS

ākāśa — the sky; ananta — unlimited; tāte — in that sky; yaiche — just as; pakshi-gana — all types of birds; yāra — of someone; yata śakti — whatever power; tata — that much; kare ārohana — rises up and up.

TRANSLATION

The sky is unlimited, but many birds fly higher and higher according to their own abilities.
