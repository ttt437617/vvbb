Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.42

krishna udāsīna ha-ilā karite parīkshana

sakhī saba kahe, — 'krishne kara upekshana'

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; udāsīna — indifferent; ha-ilā — became; karite — to make; parīkshana — testing; sakhī saba kahe — all the friends say; krishne — unto Krishna; kara — just do; upekshana — neglecting.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Krishna has become indifferent to Me just to test My love, and My friends say, 'Better to disregard Him.'"
