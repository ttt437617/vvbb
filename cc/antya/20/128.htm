Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.128

shodaśe — kālidāse prabhu kripā karilā

vaishnavocchishta khāibāra phala dekhāilā

SYNONYMS

shodaśe — in the Sixteenth Chapter; kālidāse — unto Kālidāsa; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā karilā — showed favor; vaishnava-ucchishta khāibāra — of eating the remnants of food left by Vaishnavas; phala dekhāilā — showed the result.

TRANSLATION

The Sixteenth Chapter tells how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu showed His mercy to Kālidāsa and thus demonstrated the result of eating the remnants of the food of Vaishnavas.
