Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.152

śrotāra pada-renu karoń mastaka-bhūshana

tomarā e-amrita pile saphala haila śrama

SYNONYMS

śrotāra — of the audience; pada-renu — the dust of the lotus feet; karoń — I make; mastaka-bhūshana — a decoration on my head; tomarā — all of you; e-amrita — this nectar; pile — having drunk; sa-phala — successful; haila — has become; śrama — my labor.

TRANSLATION

I decorate my head with the dust of the lotus feet of my audience. Now you have all drunk this nectar, and therefore my labor is successful.
