Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.129

śivānandera bālake śloka karāilā

simha-dvāre dvārī prabhure krishna dekhāilā

SYNONYMS

śivānandera — of Śivānanda Sena; bālake — the son; śloka karāilā — made to compose a verse; simha-dvāre — at the Simha-dvāra gate of the Jagannātha temple; dvārī — the doorkeeper; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna dekhāilā — showed Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

It also describes how Śivānanda's son composed a verse and how the doorkeeper of the Simha-dvāra showed Krishna to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
