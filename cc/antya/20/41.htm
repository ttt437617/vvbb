Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.41

govinda-virahe śūnya ha-ila tribhuvana

tushānale pode, — yena nā yāya jīvana

SYNONYMS

govinda-virahe — by separation from Govinda; śūnya — void; ha-ila — became; tri-bhuvana — the three worlds; tusha-anale — in the slow fire; pode — burns; yena — just like; nā yāya — does not go; jīvana — life.

TRANSLATION

"The three worlds have become void because of separation from Govinda. I feel as if I were burning alive in a slow fire.
