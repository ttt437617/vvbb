Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.26

ei-mata hañā yei krishna-nāma laya

śrī-krishna-carane tāńra prema upajaya

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; hañā — becoming; yei — anyone who; krishna-nāma laya — chants the holy name of Krishna; śrī-krishna-carane — at the lotus feet of Lord Krishna; tāńra — his; prema upajaya — love of Krishna awakens.

TRANSLATION

"If one chants the holy name of Lord Krishna in this manner, he will certainly awaken his dormant love for Krishna's lotus feet."
