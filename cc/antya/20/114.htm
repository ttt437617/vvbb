Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.114

saptama-paricchede — vallabha bhattera milana

nānā-mate kailā tāńra garva khandana

SYNONYMS

saptama-paricchede — in the Seventh Chapter; vallabha bhattera milana — the meeting of Vallabha Bhatta with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nānā-mate — in various ways; kailā — did; tāńra — his; garva — pride; khandana — dismantling.

TRANSLATION

The Seventh Chapter tells how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met Vallabha Bhatta and dismantled his false pride in various ways.
