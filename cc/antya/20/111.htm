Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.111

tāra madhye 'bāńgāla'-kavira nātaka-upekshana

svarūpa-gosāñi kailā vigrahera mahimā-sthāpana

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — within that chapter; bāńgāla-kavira — of a poet from Bengal; nātaka-upekshana — the rejection of the drama; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; kailā — did; vigrahera — of the Deity; mahimā-sthāpana — the establishment of the glories.

TRANSLATION

That chapter also describes how Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī rejected the drama of a poet from Bengal and established the glories of the Deity.
