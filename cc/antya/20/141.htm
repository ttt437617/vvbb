Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.141

eka eka paricchedera kathā — aneka-prakāra

mukhya-mukhya kahiluń, kathā nā yāya vistāra

SYNONYMS

eka eka paricchedera — of every chapter; kathā — description; aneka-prakāra — different varieties; mukhya-mukhya kahiluń — I have repeated only the principal ones; kathā — discussions; nā yāya — not possible; vistāra — expansion.

TRANSLATION

In every chapter there are various topics, but I have repeated only those that are principal, for not all of them could be described again.
