Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.27

kahite kahite prabhura dainya bādilā

'śuddha-bhakti' krishna-thāñi māgite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

kahite kahite — speaking like this; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dainya — humility; bādilā — increased; śuddha-bhakti — pure devotional service; krishna-thāñi — from Krishna; māgite lāgilā — began to pray for.

TRANSLATION

As Lord Caitanya spoke in this way, His humility increased, and He began praying to Krishna that He could discharge pure devotional service.
