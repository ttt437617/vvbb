Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.137

vasanta-rajanīte pushpodyāne viharana

krishnera saurabhya-ślokera artha-vivarana

SYNONYMS

vasanta-rajanīte — on a spring night; pushpa-udyāne — in a flower garden; viharana — the wandering; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; saurabhya-ślokera — of a verse about the bodily fragrance; artha-vivarana — describing the meaning.

TRANSLATION

That chapter also describes Krishna's wandering in a garden on a spring night, and it fully describes the meaning of a verse about the scent of Krishna's body.
