Antya-līlāChapter 20: The Śikshāshtaka Prayers

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 20.63

ei-mata mahāprabhu bhāvāvishta hañā

pralāpa karilā tat-tat śloka padiyā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhāva-āvishta hañā — being overwhelmed by ecstatic love; pralāpa karilā — said crazy words; tat-tat — appropriate; śloka padiyā — by reciting verses.

TRANSLATION

Thus overwhelmed by ecstatic love, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spoke like a madman and recited suitable verses.
