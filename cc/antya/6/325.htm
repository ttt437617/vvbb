Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.325

ei-mata mahāprabhu nānā līlā kare

raghunāthera vairāgya dekhi' santosha antare

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nānā līlā — many pastimes; kare — performs; raghunāthera — of Raghunātha dāsa; vairāgya — renunciation; dekhi' — by seeing; santosha antare — satisfied within.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed many pastimes at Jagannātha Purī. Seeing the severe penances performed by Raghunātha dāsa in the renounced order, the Lord was greatly satisfied.
