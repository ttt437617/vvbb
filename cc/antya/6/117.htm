Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.117

sugandhi sundara prasāda — mādhuryera sāra

dui bhāi tāhā khāñā santosha apāra

SYNONYMS

su-gandhi — fragrant; sundara — beautiful; prasāda — food; mādhuryera sāra — the essence of all sweetness; dui bhāi — the two brothers; tāhā — that; khāñā — eating; santosha apāra — very, very happy.

TRANSLATION

Aromatic and pleasing to see, the food was the essence of all sweetness. Thus the two brothers, Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, ate it with great satisfaction.
