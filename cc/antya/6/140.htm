Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.140

kripā kari' kailā cidā-dugdha bhojana

nritya dekhi' rātrye kailā prasāda bhakshana

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — by His causeless mercy; kailā — did; cidā-dugdha bhojana — eating of the chipped rice and milk; nritya dekhi' — after seeing the dancing; rātrye — at night; kailā prasāda bhakshana — ate the prasādam.

TRANSLATION

"By His causeless mercy He ate the chipped rice and milk. Then, after seeing the dancing of the devotees at night, He took His supper.
