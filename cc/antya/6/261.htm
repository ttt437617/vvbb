Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.261

ebe ghara yāha, yabe āmi saba calimu

tabe tomā sabākāre sańge lañā yāmu

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; ghara yāha — go home; yabe — when; āmi — we; saba — all; calimu — will go; tabe — then; tomā sabākāre — all of you; sańge — with; lañā — taking; yāmu — I shall go.

TRANSLATION

"Now go home. When all of us go, I shall take all of you with me."
