Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.10

pūrve yaiche rādhāra lalitā sahāya-pradhāna

taiche svarūpa-gosāñi rākhe mahāprabhura prāna

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yaiche — just as; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; lalitā — Her companion named Lalitā; sahāya-pradhāna — the best helper; taiche — similarly; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; rākhe — keeps; mahāprabhura prāna — the life of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Previously, when Śrīmatī Rādhārānī felt the pangs of separation from Krishna, Her constant companion Lalitā kept Her alive by helping Her in many ways. Similarly, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt Rādhārānī's emotions, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī helped Him maintain His life.
