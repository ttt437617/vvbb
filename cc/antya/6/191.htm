Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.191

prabhu kahena, — 'āisa', teńho dharilā carana

uthi' prabhu kripāya tāńre kailā ālińgana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — the Lord said; āisa — come here; teńho — he; dharilā carana — caught His lotus feet; uthi' — standing up; prabhu — the Lord; kripāya — out of mercy; tāńre — him; kailā ālińgana — embraced.

TRANSLATION

As soon as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard these words, He immediately welcomed Raghunātha dāsa. "Come here," He said. Raghunātha dāsa then clasped the lotus feet of the Lord, but the Lord stood up and embraced him out of His causeless mercy.
