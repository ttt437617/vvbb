Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.300

ei-mata raghunātha karena pūjana

pūjā-kāle dekhe śilāya 'vrajendra-nandana'

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; karena pūjana — worships; pūjā-kāle — while worshiping; dekhe — he sees; śilāya — in the stone from Govardhana; vrajendra-nandana — the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

Thus Raghunātha dāsa began worshiping the stone from Govardhana, and as he worshiped he saw the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja, directly in the stone.
