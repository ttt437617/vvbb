Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.82

ki kariyā bedāya, — ihā keha nāhi jāne

mahāprabhura darśana pāya kona bhāgyavāne

SYNONYMS

ki kariyā — doing what; bedāya — walks through; ihā — this; keha nāhi jāne — no one could understand; mahāprabhura darśana pāya — see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kona bhāgyavāne — some fortunate men.

TRANSLATION

No one could understand what Nityānanda Prabhu was doing as He walked about. Some, however, who were very fortunate, could see that Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also present.
