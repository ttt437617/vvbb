Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.178

tāńra pitā kahe, — "gaudera saba bhakta-gana

prabhu-sthāne nīlācale karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; pitā — father; kahe — said; gaudera — of Bengal; saba — all; bhakta gana — the devotees; prabhu-sthāne — to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; karilā gamana — have gone.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa's father said, "Now all the devotees from Bengal have gone to Jagannātha Purī to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
