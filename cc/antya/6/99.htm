Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.99

ānandita raghunātha prabhura 'śesha' pāñā

āpanāra gana-saha khāilā bāńtiyā

SYNONYMS

ānandita — being very happy; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; prabhura śesha pāñā — after getting the remnants left by Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; āpanāra gana — his own associates; saha — with; khāilā — ate; bāńtiyā — distributing.

TRANSLATION

After receiving the remnants of food left by Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, Raghunātha dāsa, who was greatly happy, ate some and distributed the rest among his own associates.
