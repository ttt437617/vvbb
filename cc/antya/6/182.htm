Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.182

patrī diyā śivānande vārtā puchila

śivānanda kahe, — 'teńha ethā nā āila'

SYNONYMS

patrī — letter; diyā — delivering; śivānande — unto Śivānanda Sena; vārtā — news; puchila — inquired; śivānanda kahe — Śivānanda said; teńha — he; ethā — here; nā āila — did not come.

TRANSLATION

After delivering the letter, the men inquired from Śivānanda Sena about Raghunātha dāsa, but Śivānanda Sena replied, "He did not come here."
