Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.322

'kāńhā vastu khāo sabe, more nā deha' kene?'

eta bali' eka grāsa karilā bhakshane

SYNONYMS

kāńhā — what; vastu — things; khāo — you eat; sabe — all; more — to Me; nā deha' kene — why do you not give; eta bali' — saying this; eka grāsa — one morsel; karilā bhakshane — ate.

TRANSLATION

"What nice things are you eating? Why don't you give anything to Me?" Saying this, He forcibly took a morsel and began to eat.
