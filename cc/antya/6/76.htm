Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.76

rāghave vasāñā dui kundī deoyāilā

rāghava dvividha cidā tāte bhijāilā

SYNONYMS

rāghave — Rāghava Pandita; vasāñā — making sit down; dui — two; kundī — earthen pots; deoyāilā — arranged to be delivered to him; rāghava — Rāghava Pandita; dvi-vidha — two kinds; cidā — chipped rice; tāte — in them; bhijāilā — soaked.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda made Rāghava Pandita sit down and had two pots delivered to him also. There were two kinds of chipped rice soaked in them.
