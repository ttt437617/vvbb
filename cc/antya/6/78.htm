Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.78

mahāprabhu āilā dekhi' nitāi uthilā

tāńre lañā sabāra cidā dekhite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; dekhi' — seeing; nitāi — Lord Nityānanda; uthilā — stood up; tāńre lañā — with Him; sabāra — of everyone; cidā — chipped rice; dekhite lāgilā — began to see.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu arrived, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu stood up. They then saw how the others were enjoying the chipped rice with yogurt and condensed milk.
