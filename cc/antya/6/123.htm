Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.123

kahilā, — "caitanya gosāñi kariyāchena bhojana

tāńra śesha pāile, tomāra khandila bandhana"

SYNONYMS

kahilā — he said; caitanya gosāñi — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariyāchena bhojana — has eaten; tāńra śesha — His remnants; pāile — if you take; tomāra — your; khandila — will cease; bandhana — bondage.

TRANSLATION

He said, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has eaten this food. If you take His remnants, you will be released from the bondage of your family."
