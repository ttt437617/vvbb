Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.80

hāsi' mahāprabhu āra eka grāsa lañā

tāńra mukhe diyā khāoyāya hāsiyā hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

hāsi' — smiling; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āra — another; eka grāsa — one morsel; lañā — taking; tāńra mukhe — in the mouth of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; diyā — putting; khāoyāya — makes eat; hāsiyā hāsiyā — laughing.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, also smiling, took a morsel of food, pushed it into the mouth of Nityānanda and laughed as He made Lord Nityānanda eat it.
