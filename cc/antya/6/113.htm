Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.113

prati-dina mahāprabhu karena bhojana

madhye madhye prabhu tāńre dena daraśana

SYNONYMS

prati-dina — daily; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena bhojana — eats; madhye madhye — sometimes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; dena daraśana — gives His audience.

TRANSLATION

Every day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would eat at the house of Rāghava Pandita. Sometimes He would give Rāghava Pandita the opportunity to see Him.
