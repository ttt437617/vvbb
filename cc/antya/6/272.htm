Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.272

māsa-dui yabe raghunātha nā kare nimantrana

svarūpe puchilā tabe śacīra nandana

SYNONYMS

māsa-dui — for two months; yabe — when; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; nā kare nimantrana — does not invite; svarūpe puchilā — inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara; tabe — at that time; śacīra nandana — the son of mother Śacī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha dāsa neglected to invite Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for two consecutive months, the Lord, the son of Śacī, questioned Svarūpa Dāmodara.
