Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.86

ājñā dilā, — 'hari bali' karaha bhojana'

'hari' 'hari'-dhvani uthi' bharila bhuvana

SYNONYMS

ājñā dilā — He ordered; hari bali' — saying "Hari"; karaha bhojana — all of you eat; hari hari-dhvani — the resounding of "Hari, Hari"; uthi' — rising; bharila bhuvana — filled the universe.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda Prabhu ordered, "All of you eat, chanting the holy name of Hari." Immediately the holy names "Hari, Hari" resounded, filling the entire universe.
