Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.194

raghunātha mane kahe, — 'krishna nāhi jāni

tava kripā kādila āmā, — ei āmi māni'

SYNONYMS

raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; mane kahe — answered within his mind; krishna — Lord Krishna; nāhi jāni — I do not know; tava — Your; kripā — mercy; kādila — has delivered; āmā — me; ei — this; āmi māni — I accept.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa answered within his mind, "I do not know who Krishna is. I simply know that Your mercy, O my Lord, has saved me from my family life."
