Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.71

hena-kāle āilā tathā rāghava pandita

hāsite lāgilā dekhi' hañā vismita

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; āilā — arrived; tathā — there; rāghava pandita — the great scholar named Rāghava Pandita; hāsite lāgilā — began to laugh; dekhi' — seeing; hañā vismita — being astonished.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Rāghava Pandita arrived there. Seeing the situation, he began to laugh in great surprise.
