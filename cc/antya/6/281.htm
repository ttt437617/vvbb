Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.281

kata dine raghunātha simha-dvāra chādilā

chatre yāi' māgiyā khāite ārambha karilā

SYNONYMS

kata dine — after some days; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; simha-dvāra chādilā — gave up standing at the gate known as Simha-dvāra; chatre yāi' — going to an alms booth; māgiyā — begging; khāite — to eat; ārambha karilā — he began.

TRANSLATION

After some days, Raghunātha dāsa gave up standing near the Simha-dvāra gate and instead began eating by begging alms from a booth for free distribution of food.
