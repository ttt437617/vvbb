Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.38

"putra 'bātula' ha-ila, ihāya rākhaha bāndhiyā"

tāńra pitā kahe tāre nirvinna hañā

SYNONYMS

putra — son; bātula ha-ila — has become mad; ihāya — him; rākhaha bāndhiyā — just keep by binding; tāńra pitā — his father; kahe — says; tāre — to her; nirvinna hañā — being very unhappy.

TRANSLATION

"Our son has become mad," she said. "Just keep him by binding him with ropes." His father, being very unhappy, replied to her as follows.
