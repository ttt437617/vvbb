Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.108

mahāprabhu āsi' sei āsane vasila

dekhi' rāghavera mane ānanda bādila

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āsi' — coming; sei āsane — on that seat; vasila — sat down; dekhi' — seeing; rāghavera mane — in the mind of Rāghava Pandita; ānanda — great happiness; bādila — increased.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came there and sat down at His place. Seeing this, Rāghava Pandita felt increasing happiness.
