Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.254

parama vairāgya tāra, nāhi bhakshya-paridhāna

yaiche taiche āhāra kari' rākhaye parāna

SYNONYMS

parama — supreme; vairāgya — renunciation; tāra — his; nāhi — not; bhakshya — eating; paridhāna — dressing; yaiche taiche — somehow or other; āhāra kari' — eating; rākhaye parāna — keeps life.

TRANSLATION

"He is in the supreme order of renounced life. Indeed, he does not care about eating or dressing. Somehow or other he eats and maintains his life.
