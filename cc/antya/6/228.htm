Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.228

āra dina raghunātha svarūpa-carane

āpanāra kritya lāgi' kailā nivedane

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; svarūpa-carane — unto the lotus feet of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; āpanāra — his; kritya — duty; lāgi' — for; kailā nivedane — submitted.

TRANSLATION

The next day, Raghunātha dāsa inquired at the lotus feet of Svarūpa Dāmodara about his duty.
