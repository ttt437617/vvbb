Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.118

bhojane vasite raghunāthe kahe sarva-jana

pandita kahe, — ińha pāche karibe bhojana'

SYNONYMS

bhojane — to eat; vasite — to sit down; raghunāthe — unto Raghunātha dāsa; kahe — requested; sarva-jana — everyone; pandita kahe — Rāghava Pandita said; ińha — this; pāche — later; karibe bhojana — will take food.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees present requested Raghunātha dāsa to sit down and take prasādam, but Rāghava Pandita told them, "He will take prasādam later."
