Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.139

"tumi ye karāilā ei pulina-bhojana

tomāya kripā kari' gaura kailā āgamana

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; ye — that; karāilā — caused to do; ei — this; pulina-bhojana — picnic on the bank of the Ganges; tomāya — unto you; kripā kari' — being merciful; gaura — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā āgamana — came.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Raghunātha dāsa," He said, "since you arranged the feast on the bank of the Ganges, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came here just to show you His mercy.
