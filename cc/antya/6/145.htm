Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.145

prabhu-ājñā lañā vaishnavera ājñā la-ilā

rāghava-sahite nibhrite yukti karilā

SYNONYMS

prabhu-ājñā — the order of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; lañā — taking; vaishnavera ājñā — the permission of all the Vaishnavas; la-ilā — he took; rāghava-sahite — with Rāghava Pandita; nibhrite — in a solitary place; yukti karilā — he consulted.

TRANSLATION

After taking leave of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu and then all the other Vaishnavas, Śrī Raghunātha dāsa consulted secretly with Rāghava Pandita.
