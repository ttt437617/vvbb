Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.207

"pathe ińha kariyāche bahuta lańghana

kata-dina kara ihāra bhāla santarpana"

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the way; ińha — this Raghunātha dāsa; kariyāche — has done; bahuta — much; lańghana — fasting and difficult endeavor; kata-dina — for some days; kara — do; ihāra — of him; bhāla — good; santarpana — attention.

TRANSLATION

"On the way, Raghunātha dāsa has fasted and undergone hardships for many days. Therefore, take good care of him for some days so that he may eat to his satisfaction."
