Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.58

āra ardheka ghanāvrita-dugdhete chānila

cāńpā-kalā, cini, ghrita, karpūra tāte dila

SYNONYMS

āra ardheka — the other half; ghana-āvrita — condensed; dugdhete — in milk; chānila — mixed; cāńpā-kalā — a special type of banana; cini — sugar; ghrita — clarified butter, ghee; karpūra — camphor; tāte dila — put into that.

TRANSLATION

The other half was mixed with condensed milk and a special type of banana known as cāńpā-kalā. Then sugar, clarified butter and camphor were added.
