Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.147

tāńre nishedhilā, — "prabhure ebe nā kahibā

nija-ghare yābena yabe tabe nivedibā"

SYNONYMS

tāńre — him; nishedhilā — he forbade; prabhure — unto Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; ebe — now; nā kahibā — do not speak; nija-ghare — to His home; yābena — will return; yabe — when; tabe — then; nivedibā — kindly inform Him.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa admonished the treasurer, "Do not speak about this to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu now, but when He returns home, kindly inform Him about this presentation."
