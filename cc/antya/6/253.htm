Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.253

rātri-dina kare teńho nāma-sańkīrtana

kshana-mātra nāhi chāde prabhura carana

SYNONYMS

rātri-dina — all day and night; kare — performs; teńho — he; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; kshana-mātra — even for a moment; nāhi chāde — does not give up; prabhura carana — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

"He chants the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra all day and night. He never gives up the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, not even for a moment.
