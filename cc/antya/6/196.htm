Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.196

cakravartīra duhe haya bhrātri-rūpa dāsa

ataeva tāre āmi kari parihāsa

SYNONYMS

cakravartīra — of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī; duhe — both; haya — are; bhrātri-rūpa dāsa — servants as younger brothers; ataeva — therefore; tāre — unto them; āmi — I; kari parihāsa — say something jokingly.

TRANSLATION

"Since your father and his elder brother are younger brothers of Nīlāmbara Cakravartī, I may joke about them in this way.
