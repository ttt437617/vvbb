Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.131

tomāra kripā vinā keha 'caitanya' nā pāya

tumi kripā kaile tāńre adhameha pāya

SYNONYMS

tomāra kripā — Your mercy; vinā — without; keha — anyone; caitanya — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā pāya — cannot get; tumi kripā kaile — if You are merciful; tāńre — Him; adhameha — even the fallen soul; pāya — can get.

TRANSLATION

"No one can attain the shelter of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu without Your mercy, but if You are merciful, even the lowest of men can attain shelter at His lotus feet.
