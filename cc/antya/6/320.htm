Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.320

svarūpa kahe, — "aiche amrita khāo niti-niti

āmā-sabāya nāhi deha', — ki tomāra prakriti?"

SYNONYMS

svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara said; aiche — such; amrita — nectar; khāo — you eat; niti-niti — daily; āmā-sabāya — to us; nāhi deha' — you do not offer; ki — what; tomāra — your; prakriti — nature.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara said, "You eat such nectar every day, but you never offer it to us. What is your character?"
