Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.149

aneka 'prasāda' dilā pathe khāibāre

tabe punah raghunātha kahe panditere

SYNONYMS

aneka prasāda — much prasādam; dilā — delivered; pathe khāibāre — to eat on his way; tabe — then; punah — again; raghunātha kahe — Raghunātha dāsa said; panditere — to Rāghava Pandita.

TRANSLATION

He gave Raghunātha dāsa a large quantity of prasādam to eat on his way home. Then Raghunātha dāsa again spoke to Rāghava Pandita.
