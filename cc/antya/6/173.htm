Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.173

grāme-grāmera patha chādi' yāya vane vane

kāya-mano-vākye cinte caitanya-carane

SYNONYMS

grāme-grāmera — from village to village; patha — the general path; chādi' — giving up; yāya — goes; vane vane — through the jungles; kāya-manah-vākye — with body, mind and words; cinte — thinks; caitanya carane — of the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Giving up the general path from village to village, he passed through the jungles, thinking with heart and soul about the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
