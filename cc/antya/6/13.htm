Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.13

pūrve śāntipure raghunātha yabe āilā

mahāprabhu kripā kari' tāńre śikhāilā

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; śāntipure — to Śāntipura; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; yabe āilā — when he came; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā kari' — showing causeless mercy; tāńre śikhāilā — gave him lessons.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha dāsa, during his family life, went to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Śāntipura, the Lord gave him worthy instructions by His causeless mercy.
