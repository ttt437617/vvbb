Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.111

rāghava-thākurera prasāda amritera sāra

mahāprabhu yāhā khāite āise bāra bāra

SYNONYMS

rāghava-thākurera — of Rāghava Pandita; prasāda — food offered to the Deity; amritera sāra — the essence of nectar; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yāhā — which; khāite — to eat; āise — came; bāra bāra — again and again.

TRANSLATION

The food prepared and offered to the Deity by Rāghava Pandita was like the essence of nectar. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu came there again and again to eat such prasādam.
