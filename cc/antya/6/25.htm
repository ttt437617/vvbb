Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.25

"āmāra pitā, jyethā haya tomāra dui bhāi

bhāi-bhāiye tomarā kalaha kara sarvadāi

SYNONYMS

āmāra pitā — my father; jyethā — and his elder brother; haya — are; tomāra — your; dui bhāi — two brothers; bhāi-bhāiye — between brother and brother; tomarā — all of you; kalaha kara — fight; sarvadāi — always.

TRANSLATION

"My dear sir, my father and his elder brother are your brothers. All brothers always fight about something.
