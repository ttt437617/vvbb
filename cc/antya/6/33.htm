Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.33

yāha tumi, tomāra jyethāre milāha āmāre

ye-mate bhāla haya karuna, bhāra diluń tāńre

SYNONYMS

yāha — go; tumi — you; tomāra — your; jyethāre — the elder brother of your father; milāha āmāre — arrange to meet with me; ye-mate — in whatever way; bhāla — good; haya — is; karuna — let him do; bhāra diluń tāńre — I shall completely depend upon him.

TRANSLATION

"Now you go arrange a meeting between me and your uncle. Let him do whatever he thinks best. I shall completely depend on his decision."
