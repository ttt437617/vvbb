Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.100

ei ta' kahiluń nityānandera vihāra

'cidā-dadhi-mahotsava'-nāme khyāti yāra

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — in this way; kahiluń — I have described; nityānandera vihāra — the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; cidā-dadhi-mahotsava — the festival of eating chipped rice and yogurt; nāme — of the name; khyāti — the fame; yāra — of which.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described the pastimes of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu in relation to the celebrated festival of chipped rice and yogurt.
