Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.180

śivānande patrī dila vinaya kariyā

'āmāra putrere tumi dibā bāhudiyā'

SYNONYMS

śivānande — unto Śivānanda Sena; patrī — a letter; dila — sent; vinaya kariya — with great humility; āmāra putrere — my son; tumi — you; dibā — please give; bāhudiyā — returning.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa's father wrote a letter to Śivānanda Sena, asking him with great humility, "Please return my son."
