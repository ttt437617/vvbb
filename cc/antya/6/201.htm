Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.201

raghunāthera kshīnatā-mālinya dekhiyā

svarūpere kahena prabhu kripārdra-citta hañā

SYNONYMS

raghunāthera — of Raghunātha dāsa; kshīnatā — thinness; mālinya — dirty condition of the body; dekhiyā — seeing; svarūpere kahena — said to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; prabhu — Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā — out of mercy; ārdra — melted; citta — heart; hañā — being.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Raghunātha dāsa skinny and dirty because of having traveled for twelve days and fasted, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His heart melting due to causeless mercy, spoke to Svarūpa Dāmodara.
