Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.243

sabā lañā kailā prabhu gundicā-mārjana

sabā lañā kailā prabhu vanya-bhojana

SYNONYMS

sabā lañā — taking all of them; kailā — performed; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gundicā-mārjana — washing of the Gundicā temple; sabā lañā — with all of them; kailā — performed; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya; vanya-bhojana — eating in the garden.

TRANSLATION

As He had previously done, He cleansed the Gundicā temple and held a picnic feast in the garden with the devotees.
