Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.188

bāra dine cali' gelā śrī-purushottama

pathe tina-dina mātra karilā bhojana

SYNONYMS

bāra dine — for twelve days; cali' — traveling; gelā — reached; śrī-purushottama — Jagannātha Purī, or Nīlācala, the place of Purushottama; pathe — on the path; tina-dina — on three days; mātra — only; karilā bhojana — he ate.

TRANSLATION

He reached Jagannātha Purī in twelve days but could eat only for three days on the way.
