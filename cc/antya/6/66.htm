Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.66

āra yata loka saba cautarā-talāne

mandalī-bandhe vasilā, tāra nā haya ganane

SYNONYMS

āra — other; yata — as many as; loka — people; saba — all; cautarā-talāne — at the base of the platform; mandalī-bandhe — in groups; vasilā — sat down; tāra — of them; nā haya ganane — there was no counting.

TRANSLATION

All the other people sat in groups around the platform. No one could count how many people there were.
