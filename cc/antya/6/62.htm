Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.62

dhanañjaya, jagadīśa, parameśvara-dāsa

maheśa, gaurīdāsa, hoda-krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

dhanañjaya — Dhanañjaya; jagadīśa — Jagadīśa; parameśvara-dāsa — Parameśvara dāsa; maheśa — Maheśa; gaurīdāsa — Gaurīdāsa; hoda-krishnadāsa — Hoda Krishnadāsa.

TRANSLATION

Dhanañjaya, Jagadīśa, Parameśvara dāsa, Maheśa, Gaurīdāsa and Hoda Krishnadāsa were also there.
