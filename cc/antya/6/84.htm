Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.84

āsana diyā mahāprabhure tāhāń vasāilā

dui bhāi tabe cidā khāite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

āsana diyā — offering a sitting place; mahāprabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāhāń — there; vasāilā — made sit; dui bhāi — the two brothers; tabe — at that time; cidā — chipped rice; khāite lāgilā — began to eat.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda offered Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu a place and had Him sit down. Then together the two brothers began eating chipped rice.
