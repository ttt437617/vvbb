Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.98

mālā-candana-tāmbūla śesha ye āchila

śrī-haste prabhu tāhā sabākāre bāńti' dila

SYNONYMS

mālā-candana-tāmbūla — the flower garlands, sandalwood pulp and betel; śesha ye āchila — whatever remained; śrī-haste — in His own hand; prabhu — Nityānanda Prabhu; tāhā — that; sabākāre — to all; bāńti' dila — distributed.

TRANSLATION

With His own hands Lord Nityānanda Prabhu distributed to all the devotees whatever flower garlands, sandalwood pulp and betel nuts remained.
