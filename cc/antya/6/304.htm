Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.304

"ashta-kaudira khājā-sandeśa kara samarpana

śraddhā kari' dile, sei amritera sama"

SYNONYMS

ashta-kaudira — costing eight kaudis; khājā-sandeśa — khājā and sandeśa sweetmeats; kara samarpana — offer; śraddhā kari' — with love and faith; dile — if you offer; sei — that; amritera sama — just like nectar.

TRANSLATION

"Offer the Govardhana stone eight kaudis worth of the first-class sweetmeats known as khājā and sandeśa. If you offer them with faith and love, they will be just like nectar."
