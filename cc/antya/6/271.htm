Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.271

ei-mata nimantrana varsha dui kailā

pāche raghunātha nimantrana chādi' dilā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; nimantrana — invitation; varsha dui — for two years; kailā — continued; pāche — at the end; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; nimantrana — invitation; chādi' dilā — gave up.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa continued to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in this way for two years, but at the end of the second year he stopped.
