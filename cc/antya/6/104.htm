Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.104

nityānandera nritya, — yena tāńhāra nartane

upamā dibāra nāhi e-tina bhuvane

SYNONYMS

nityānandera nritya — the dancing of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; yena — as; tāńhāra nartane — with the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; upamā dibāra nāhi — there cannot be any comparison; e-tina bhuvane — within these three worlds.

TRANSLATION

The dancing of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, like the dancing of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, cannot be compared to anything within these three worlds.
