Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.116

durvāsāra thāñi teńho pāñāchena vara

amrita ha-ite pāka tāńra adhika madhura

SYNONYMS

durvāsāra thāñi — from Durvāsā Muni; teńho — She; pāñāchena vara — got the benediction; amrita ha-ite — than nectar; pāka — cooking; tāńra — Her; adhika madhura — more sweet.

TRANSLATION

Śrīmatī Rādhārānī received from Durvāsā Muni the benediction that whatever She cooked would be sweeter than nectar. That is the special feature of Her cooking.
