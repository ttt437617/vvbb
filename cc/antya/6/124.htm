Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.124

bhakta-citte bhakta-grihe sadā avasthāna

kabhu gupta, kabhu vyakta, svatantra bhagavān

SYNONYMS

bhakta-citte — in the heart of a devotee; bhakta-grihe — at the house of a devotee; sadā avasthāna — always resides; kabhu gupta — sometimes hidden; kabhu vyakta — sometimes manifest; svatantra — fully independent; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead always resides either in the heart or in the home of a devotee. This fact is sometimes hidden and sometimes manifest, for the Supreme Personality of Godhead is fully independent.
