Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.65

dui dui mrit-kundikā sabāra āge dila

eke dugdha-cidā, āre dadhi-cidā kaila

SYNONYMS

dui dui — two and two; mrit-kundikā — earthen pots; sabāra āge — before everyone; dila — offered; eke — in one; dugdha-cidā — chipped rice with condensed milk; āre — in the other; dadhi-cidā — chipped rice with yogurt; kaila — put.

TRANSLATION

Everyone was offered two earthen pots. In one was put chipped rice with condensed milk, and in the other chipped rice with yogurt.
