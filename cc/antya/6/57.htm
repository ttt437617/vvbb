Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.57

eka-thāñi tapta-dugdhe cidā bhijāñā

ardheka chānila dadhi, cini, kalā diyā

SYNONYMS

eka-thāñi — in one place; tapta-dugdhe — in hot milk; cidā — the chipped rice; bhijāñā — soaking; ardheka — half of it; chānila — mixed; dadhi — yogurt; cini — sugar; kalā — bananas; diyā — putting in.

TRANSLATION

In one place, chipped rice was soaked in hot milk in each of the large pots. Then half the rice was mixed with yogurt, sugar and bananas.
