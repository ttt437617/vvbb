Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.87

'hari' 'hari' bali' vaishnava karaye bhojana

pulina-bhojana sabāra ha-ila smarana

SYNONYMS

hari hari bali' — chanting "Hari, Hari"; vaishnava — all the Vaishnavas; karaye bhojana — eat; pulina-bhojana — eating on the bank of the Yamunā; sabāra ha-ila smarana — everyone could remember.

TRANSLATION

When all the Vaishnavas were chanting the holy names "Hari, Hari" and eating, they remembered how Krishna and Balarāma ate with Their companions the cowherd boys on the bank of the Yamunā.
