Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.8

tāńra sukha-hetu sańge rahe dui janā

krishna-rasa-śloka-gīte karena sāntvanā

SYNONYMS

tāńra sukha-hetu — for His happiness; sańge — in His association; rahe — remain; dui janā — two personalities; krishna-rasa — of the transcendental mellows of Krishna; śloka — verses; gīte — by songs; karena sāntvanā — they pacified.

TRANSLATION

Two people — Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī — stayed with the Lord to pacify Him by reciting various verses about Krishna's pastimes and by singing appropriate songs for His satisfaction.
