Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.9

subala yaiche pūrve krishna-sukhera sahāya

gaura-sukha-dāna-hetu taiche rāma-rāya

SYNONYMS

subala — Subala, one of the cowherd boyfriends of Krishna; yaiche — just as; pūrve — previously; krishna-sukhera — to give happiness to Krishna; sahāya — helper; gaura-sukha-dāna-hetu — for giving happiness to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; taiche — similarly; rāma-rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya.

TRANSLATION

Previously, when Lord Krishna was personally present, Subala, one of His cowherd boyfriends, gave Him happiness when He felt separation from Rādhārānī. Similarly, Rāmānanda Rāya helped give happiness to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
