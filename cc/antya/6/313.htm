Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.313

prāna-rakshā lāgi' yebā karena bhakshana

tāhā khāñā āpanāke kahe nirveda-vacana

SYNONYMS

prāna-rakshā lāgi' — to maintain life; yebā — whatever; karena bhakshana — he ate; tāhā khāñā — eating that; āpanāke — to himself; kahe — said; nirveda-vacana — words of reproach.

TRANSLATION

Whatever he ate was only to keep his body and soul together, and when he ate he would reproach himself thus.
