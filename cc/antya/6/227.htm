Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.227

jihvāra lālase yei iti-uti dhāya

śiśnodara-parāyana krishna nāhi pāya"

SYNONYMS

jihvāra — of the tongue; lālase — because of greed; yei — anyone who; iti-uti — here and there; dhāya — goes; śiśna — genitals; udara — belly; parāyana — devoted to; krishna — Lord Krishna; nāhi pāya — does not get.

TRANSLATION

"One who is subservient to the tongue and who thus goes here and there, devoted to the genitals and the belly, cannot attain Krishna."
