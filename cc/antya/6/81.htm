Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.81

ei-mata nitāi bule sakala mandale

dāndāñā rańga dekhe vaishnava sakale

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; nitāi bule — Lord Nityānanda was walking; sakala mandale — through all the groups; dāndāñā — standing; rańga dekhe — see the fun; vaishnava sakale — all the Vaishnavas.

TRANSLATION

In this way Lord Nityānanda was walking through all the groups of eaters, and all the Vaishnavas standing there were seeing the fun.
