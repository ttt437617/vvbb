Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.52

sei-kshane nija-loka pāthāilā grāme

bhakshya-dravya loka saba grāma haite āne

SYNONYMS

sei-kshane — immediately; nija-loka — his servants; pāthāilā grāme — he sent to the nearby village; bhakshya-dravya — eatables; loka saba — all the persons; grāma haite — from the village; āne — bring.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa immediately sent his own men to the village to purchase all kinds of eatables and bring them back.
