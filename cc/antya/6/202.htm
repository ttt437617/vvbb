Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.202

"ei raghunāthe āmi sańpinu tomāre

putra-bhritya-rūpe tumi kara ańgīkāre

SYNONYMS

ei raghunāthe — this Raghunātha dāsa; āmi — I; sańpinu tomāre — am entrusting to you; putra — son; bhritya — servant; rūpe — as; tumi — you (Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī); kara ańgīkāre — please accept.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Svarūpa," He said, "I entrust this Raghunātha dāsa to you. Please accept him as your son or servant.
