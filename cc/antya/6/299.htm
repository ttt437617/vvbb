Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.299

eka-vitasti dui-vastra, pińdā eka-khāni

svarūpa dilena kuńjā ānibāre pāni

SYNONYMS

eka-vitasti — about six inches long; dui-vastra — two cloths; pińdā eka-khāni — one wooden platform; svarūpa dilena — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī delivered; kuńjā — a jug; ānibāre pāni — for bringing water.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara gave Raghunātha dāsa two cloths, each about six inches long, a wooden platform and a jug in which to keep water.
