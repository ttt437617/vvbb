Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.148

tabe rāghava-pandita tāńre ghare lañā gelā

thākura darśana karāñā mālā-candana dilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; rāghava-pandita — Rāghava Pandita; tāńre — him; ghare lañā gelā — took to his home; thākura darśana karāñā — after inducing him to see the Deity; mālā-candana — a garland and sandalwood pulp; dilā — delivered.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon, Rāghava Pandita took Raghunātha dāsa to his home. After inducing him to see the Deity, he gave Raghunātha dāsa a garland and sandalwood pulp.
