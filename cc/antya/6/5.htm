Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.5

utkata viraha-duhkha yabe bāhirāya

tabe ye vaikalya prabhura varnana nā yāya

SYNONYMS

utkata — severe; viraha-duhkha — unhappiness of separation; yabe — when; bāhirāya — is manifested; tabe — at that time; ye — what; vaikalya — transformations; prabhura — of the Lord; varnana nā yāya — cannot be described.

TRANSLATION

The transformations undergone by the Lord when He manifested severe unhappiness due to separation from Krishna cannot be described.
