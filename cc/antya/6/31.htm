Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.31

ujire kahiyā raghunāthe chādāila

prīti kari' raghunāthe kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

ujire — unto the minister; kahiyā — speaking; raghunāthe chādāila — released Raghunātha dāsa; prīti kari' — with great affection; raghunāthe — unto Raghunātha dāsa; kahite lāgila — began to say.

TRANSLATION

After informing the minister, the caudhurī released Raghunātha dāsa and then began to speak to him with great affection.
