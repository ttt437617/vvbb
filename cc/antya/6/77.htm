Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.77

sakala-lokera cidā pūrna yabe ha-ila

dhyāne tabe prabhu mahāprabhure ānila

SYNONYMS

sakala-lokera — of everyone; cidā — chipped rice; pūrna — full; yabe — when; ha-ila — was; dhyāne — in meditation; tabe — at that time; prabhu — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; mahāprabhure ānila — brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When chipped rice had been served to everyone, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, in meditation, brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
