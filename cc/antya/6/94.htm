Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.94

bhojana kari' nityānanda ācamana kailā

cāri kundīra avaśesha raghunāthe dilā

SYNONYMS

bhojana kari' — after finishing eating; nityānanda — Nityānanda Prabhu; ācamana kailā — washing His hands and mouth; cāri kundīra — of the four pots; avaśesha — what was remaining; raghunāthe dilā — delivered to Raghunātha dāsa.

TRANSLATION

After Lord Nityānanda Prabhu finished eating, He washed His hands and mouth and gave Raghunātha dāsa the food remaining in the four pots.
