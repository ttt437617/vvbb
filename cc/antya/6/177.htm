Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.177

teńha kahe, 'ājñā māgi' gelā nija-ghara'

'palāila raghunātha' — uthila kolāhala

SYNONYMS

teńha kahe — he said; ājñā māgi' — asking my permission; gelā — went; nija ghara — to his home; palāila raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa has gone away; uthila — arose; kolāhala — a tumultuous sound.

TRANSLATION

Yadunandana Ācārya said, "He has already asked my permission and returned home." Thus there arose a tumultuous sound, as everyone cried, "Now Raghunātha has gone away!"
