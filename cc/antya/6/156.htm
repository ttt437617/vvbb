Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.156

tāńhā jāgi' rahe saba rakshaka-gana

palāite karena nānā upāya cintana

SYNONYMS

tāńhā — there; jāgi' — keeping awake; rahe — stay; saba — all; rakshaka-gana — the watch men; palāite — to go away; karena — does; nānā — various; upāya — means; cintana — thinking of.

TRANSLATION

There, however, the watchmen alertly kept guard. Raghunātha dāsa was thinking of various means by which to escape their vigilance.
