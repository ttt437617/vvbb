Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.291

govardhana-śilā prabhu hridaye-netre dhare

kabhu nāsāya ghrāna laya, kabhu śire kare

SYNONYMS

govardhana-śilā — the stone from Govardhana Hill; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hridaye — on the heart; netre — on the eyes; dhare — puts; kabhu — sometimes; nāsāya — with the nose; ghrāna laya — smells; kabhu — sometimes; śire kare — He puts on His head.

TRANSLATION

The Lord would put the stone to His heart or sometimes to His eyes. Sometimes He would smell it with His nose and sometimes place it on His head.
