Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.158

tāń-sabāra sańge raghunātha yāite nā pāre

prasiddha prakata sańga, tabahiń dharā pade

SYNONYMS

tāń-sabāra — all of them; sańge — with; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; yāite nā pāre — could not go; prasiddha — famous; prakata — known; sańga — group; tabahiń — immediately; dharā pade — he would be caught.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa could not accompany them, for they were so famous that he would have been caught immediately.
