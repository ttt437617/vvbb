Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.302

jala-tulasīra sevāya tāńra yata sukhodaya

shodaśopacāra-pūjāya tata sukha naya

SYNONYMS

jala-tulasīra sevāya — by worshiping with water and tulasī; tāńra — his; yata — as much as; sukha-udaya — rise of transcendental happiness; shodaśa-upacāra-pūjāya — by worshiping with sixteen kinds of paraphernalia; tata — so much; sukha — happiness; naya — is not.

TRANSLATION

The amount of transcendental bliss that Raghunātha dāsa enjoyed simply by offering water and tulasī is impossible to achieve even if one worships the Deity with sixteen kinds of paraphernalia.
