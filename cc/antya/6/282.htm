Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.282

govinda-pāśa śuni' prabhu puchena svarūpere

'raghu bhikshā lāgi' thāda kene nahe simha-dvāre'?

SYNONYMS

govinda-pāśa — from Govinda; śuni' — hearing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; puchena svarūpere — inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; raghu — Raghunātha dāsa; bhikshā lāgi' — for begging; thāda kene nahe — why does he not stand; simha-dvāre — at the Simha-dvāra gate.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu heard this news from Govinda, He inquired from Svarūpa Dāmodara, "Why does Raghunātha dāsa no longer stand at the Simha-dvāra gate to beg alms?"
