Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.204

eta kahi' raghunāthera hasta dharilā

svarūpera haste tāńre samarpana kailā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; raghunāthera — of Raghunātha dāsa; hasta dharilā — caught the hand; svarūpera haste — in the hands of Svarūpa Dāmodara; tāńre — him; samarpana kailā — entrusted.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu grasped the hand of Raghunātha dāsa and entrusted him to the hands of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.
