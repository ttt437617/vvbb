Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.323

āra grāsa laite svarūpa hātete dharilā

'tava yogya nahe' bali' bale kādi' nilā

SYNONYMS

āra — another; grāsa — morsel; laite — taking; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; hātete — the hand; dharilā — caught; tava — for You; yogya — fit; nahe — is not; bali' — saying; bale — by force; kādi' — snatching; nilā — he took.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was taking another morsel of food, Svarūpa Dāmodara caught Him by the hand and said, "It is not fit for You." Thus he forcibly took the food away.
