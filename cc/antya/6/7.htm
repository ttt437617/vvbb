Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.7

dine prabhu nānā-sańge haya anya mana

rātri-kāle bāde prabhura viraha-vedana

SYNONYMS

dine — during the daytime; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nānā-sańge — by various associations; haya — becomes; anya — diverted; mana — His mind; rātri-kāle — at night; bāde — increase; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; viraha-vedana — pangs of separation.

TRANSLATION

Because the Lord associated with various devotees during the day, His mind was somewhat diverted, but at night the pangs of separation from Krishna increased very rapidly.
