Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.154

tāńra pada-dhūli lañā svagrihe āilā

nityānanda-kripā pāñā kritārtha mānilā

SYNONYMS

tāńra — his; pada-dhūli — the dust of the feet; lañā — taking; sva-grihe āilā — returned to his home; nityānanda-kripā — the mercy of Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; pāñā — getting; kritārtha mānilā — he felt greatly obligated.

TRANSLATION

After taking dust from the feet of Rāghava Pandita, Raghunātha dāsa returned to his home, feeling greatly obligated to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu because of having received His merciful benediction.
