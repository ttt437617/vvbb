Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.216

simha-dvāre annārthī vaishnave dekhiyā

pasārira thāñi anna dena kripā ta' kariyā

SYNONYMS

simha-dvāre — at the Simha gate; anna-arthī — in need of some eatables; vaishnave — Vaishnavas; dekhiyā — seeing; pasārira thāñi — from the shopkeepers; anna dena — deliver some eatables; kripā ta' kariyā — out of mercy.

TRANSLATION

If they see a Vaishnava standing at the Simha-dvāra begging alms, out of mercy they arrange with the shopkeepers to give him something to eat.
