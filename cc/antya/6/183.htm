Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.183

bāhudiyā sei daśa jana āila ghara

tāńra mātā-pitā ha-ila cintita antara

SYNONYMS

bāhudiyā — returning; sei — those; daśa jana — ten men; āila ghara — came back home; tāńra — his; mātā-pitā — mother and father; ha-ila — became; cintita — full of anxiety; antara — within themselves.

TRANSLATION

The ten men returned home, and Raghunātha dāsa's father and mother were filled with anxiety.
