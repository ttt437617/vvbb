Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.245

raghunātha-dāsa yabe sabāre mililā

advaita-ācārya tāńre bahu kripā kailā

SYNONYMS

raghunātha-dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa; yabe — when; sabāre mililā — met all the devotees; advaita-ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; tāńre — unto him; bahu — much; kripā — mercy; kailā — did.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha dāsa met all the devotees, Advaita Ācārya showed him great mercy.
