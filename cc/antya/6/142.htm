Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.142

svarūpera sthāne tomā karibe samarpane

'antarańga' bhritya bali' rākhibe carane

SYNONYMS

svarūpera sthāne — unto Svarūpa Dāmodara; tomā — you; karibe samarpane — He will give; antarańga — very confidential; bhritya — servant; bali' — as; rākhibe carane — will keep under His lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu will accept you and place you under the charge of His secretary, Svarūpa Dāmodara. You will thus become one of the most confidential internal servants and will attain shelter at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
