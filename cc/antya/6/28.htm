Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.28

pālaka hañā pālyere tādite nā yuyāya

tumi sarva-śāstra jāna 'jindā-pīra'-prāya"

SYNONYMS

pālaka hañā — being a maintainer; pālyere — the person who is maintained; tādite — to punish; nā yuyāya — is not good; tumi — you; sarva-śāstra — all scriptures; jāna — know; jindā-pīra — a living saintly person; prāya — just like.

TRANSLATION

"For a maintainer to punish the person he maintains is not good. You are expert in all the scriptures. Indeed, you are like a living saint."
