Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.75

gopa-jāti āmi bahu gopa-gana sańge

āmi sukha pāi ei pulina-bhojana-rańge"

SYNONYMS

gopa-jāti — belonging to the community of cowherd boys; āmi — I; bahu — many; gopa-gana — cowherd boys; sańge — with; āmi — I; sukha pāi — become very happy; ei — this; pulina — by the riverside; bhojana-rańge — in the enjoyment of eating.

TRANSLATION

"I belong to a community of cowherd boys, and therefore I generally have many cowherd associates with Me. I am happy when we eat together in a picnic like this by the sandy bank of the river."
