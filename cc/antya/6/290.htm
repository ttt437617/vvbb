Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.290

dui apūrva-vastu pāñā prabhu tushta hailā

smaranera kāle gale pare guñjā-mālā

SYNONYMS

dui — two; apūrva-vastu — uncommon things; pāñā — getting; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tushta hailā — became very happy; smaranera kāle — at the time of remembering (when He was chanting Hare Krishna); gale — on the neck; pare — wears; guñjā-mālā — the garland of small conchshells.

TRANSLATION

Upon receiving these two uncommon items, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was extremely happy. While chanting, He would put the garland around His neck.
