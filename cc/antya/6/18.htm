Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.18

hiranya-dāsa muluka nila 'makrari' kariyā

tāra adhikāra gela, mare se dekhiyā

SYNONYMS

hiranya-dāsa — the uncle of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; muluka nila — took charge of the country; makrari kariyā — by some agreement; tāra adhikāra gela — the Muslim caudhurī lost his position; mare se dekhiyā — became extremely envious of Hiranya dāsa.

TRANSLATION

When Hiranya dāsa, Raghunātha dāsa's uncle, made an agreement with the government to collect taxes, the Muslim caudhurī, or tax collector, having lost his position, became extremely envious of him.
