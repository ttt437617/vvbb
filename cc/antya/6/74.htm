Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.74

prabhu kahe, — "e-dravya dine kariye bhojana

rātrye tomāra ghare prasāda karimu bhakshana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu said; e-dravya — this food; dine — during the daytime; kariye bhojana — let Me eat; rātrye — at night; tomāra ghare — in your house; prasāda — the prasādam; karimu bhakshana — I shall eat.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda replied, "Let Me eat all this food here during the day, and I shall eat at your home at night.
