Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.247

tomāre pāthāite patrī pāthāila more

jhāńkarā ha-ite tomā nā pāñā gela ghare

SYNONYMS

tomāre — you; pāthāite — to send back; patrī — letter; pāthāila more — sent to me; jhāńkarā ha-ite — from Jhāńkarā; tomā — you; nā pāñā — not getting; gela ghare — returned home.

TRANSLATION

"He wrote me a letter asking me to send you back, but when those ten men received no information about you, they returned home from Jhāńkarā."
