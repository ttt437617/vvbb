Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.268

raghunātha-dāsa ańgīkāra nā karila

dravya lañā dui-jana tāhāńi rahila

SYNONYMS

raghunātha-dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa; ańgīkāra nā karila — did not accept; dravya lañā — taking the wealth; dui-jana — two persons; tāhāńi rahila — remained there.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa did not accept the money and men sent by his father. Therefore the brāhmana and one of the servants stayed there with the money.
