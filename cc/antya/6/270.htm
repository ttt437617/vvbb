Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.270

dui nimantrane lāge kaudi ashta-pana

brāhmana-bhritya-thāñi karena eteka grahana

SYNONYMS

dui nimantrane — these two invitations; lāge — cost; kaudi ashta-pana — 640 kaudis; brāhmana-bhritya-thāñi — from the brāhmana and the servant; karena — does; eteka — so much; grahana — accepting.

TRANSLATION

The cost for these two occasions was 640 kaudis. Therefore he would take that much from the servant and the brāhmana.
