Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.166

eta kahi' raghunāthe lañā calilā

rakshaka saba śesha-rātre nidrāya padilā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; raghunāthe lañā — taking Raghunātha dāsa; calilā — he went out; rakshaka saba — all the watchmen; śesha-rātre — at the end of night; nidrāya padilā — fell asleep.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Yadunandana Ācārya took Raghunātha dāsa with him and went out. By that time all the watchmen were deeply asleep because it was the end of the night.
