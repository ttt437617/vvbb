Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.210

raghunāthe prabhura kripā dekhi, bhakta-gana

vismita hañā kare tāńra bhāgya-praśamsana

SYNONYMS

raghunāthe — unto Raghunātha dāsa; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā — mercy; dekhi — seeing; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; vismita — struck with wonder; hañā — being; kare — do; tāńra — his; bhāgya — fortune; praśamsana — praise.

TRANSLATION

Having seen the causeless mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu upon Raghunātha dāsa, all the devotees, struck with wonder, praised his good fortune.
