Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.238

ei ta' sańkshepe āmi kailuń upadeśa

svarūpera thāñi ihāra pāibe viśesha

SYNONYMS

ei — this; ta' — certainly; sańkshepe — in brief; āmi — I; kailuń upadeśa — have given instruction; svarūpera thāñi — from Svarūpa Dāmodara; ihāra — of this instruction; pāibe — you will get; viśesha — all details.

TRANSLATION

"I have briefly given you My instructions. Now you will get all details about them from Svarūpa Dāmodara.
