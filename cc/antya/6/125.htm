Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.125

sarvatra 'vyāpaka' prabhura sadā sarvatra vāsa

ihāte samśaya yāra, sei yāya nāśa

SYNONYMS

sarvatra — everywhere; vyāpaka — pervading; prabhura — of the Supreme Personality of Godhead; sadā — always; sarvatra — everywhere; vāsa — residence; ihāte — about this; samśaya — doubt; yāra — of whom; sei — he; yāya nāśa — becomes annihilated.

TRANSLATION

The Supreme Personality of Godhead is all-pervasive, and therefore He resides everywhere. Anyone who doubts this will be annihilated.
