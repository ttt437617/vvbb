Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.240

eta śuni' raghunātha vandilā carana

mahāprabhu kailā tāńre kripā-ālińgana

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; vandilā carana — offered prayers to the lotus feet; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā — did; tāńre — him; kripā-ālińgana — embracing out of mercy.

TRANSLATION

Having heard this, Raghunātha dāsa offered prayers at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord, out of great mercy, embraced him.
