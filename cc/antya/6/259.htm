Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.259

cāri-śata mudrā, dui bhritya, eka brāhmana

śivānandera thāñi pāthāila tata-kshana

SYNONYMS

cāri-śata mudrā — four hundred coins; dui bhritya — two servants; eka brāhmana — one brāhmana; śivānandera thāñi — to Śivānanda Sena; pāthāila — sent; tata-kshana — immediately.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa's father immediately sent four hundred coins, two servants and one brāhmana to Śivānanda Sena.
