Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.60

cabutarā-upare yata prabhura nija-gane

bada bada loka vasilā mandalī-racane

SYNONYMS

cabutarā-upare — on the raised platform; yata — all; prabhura nija-gane — very intimate associates of the Lord; bada bada loka — big, big people; vasilā — sat down; mandalī-racane — in a circle.

TRANSLATION

On that platform, all the most important associates of Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu, as well as other important men, sat down in a circle around the Lord.
