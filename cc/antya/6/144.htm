Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.144

saba bhakta-dvāre tāńre āśīrvāda karāilā

tāń-sabāra carana raghunātha vandilā

SYNONYMS

saba — all; bhakta-dvāre — by the devotees; tāńre āśīrvāda karāilā — had him blessed; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; carana — the lotus feet; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; vandilā — worshiped.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda had Raghunātha dāsa blessed by all the devotees, and Raghunātha dāsa offered his respects to their lotus feet.
