Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.167

ācāryera ghara ihāra pūrva-diśāte

kahite śunite duńhe cale sei pathe

SYNONYMS

ācāryera ghara — the house of Yadunandana Ācārya; ihāra — of this; pūrva-diśāte — to the east; kahite — talking; śunite — listening; duńhe — both of them; cale — go; sei pathe — on that path.

TRANSLATION

East of the house of Raghunātha dāsa was the house of Yadunandana Ācārya. Yadunandana Ācārya and Raghunātha dāsa talked together as they went toward that house.
