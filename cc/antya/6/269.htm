Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.269

tabe raghunātha kari' aneka yatana

māse dui-dina kailā prabhura nimantrana

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; kari' aneka yatana — with great attention; māse — every month; dui-dina — two days; kailā — he made; prabhura nimantrana — invitation to Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

At that time, Raghunātha dāsa began inviting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to his house with great attention for two days every month.
