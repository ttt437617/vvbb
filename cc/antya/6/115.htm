Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.115

kata upahāra āne, hena nāhi jāni

rāghavera ghare rāndhe rādhā-thākurānī

SYNONYMS

kata upahāra — many presentations; āne — brings; hena — such; nāhi jāni — I cannot understand; rāghavera ghare — at the house of Rāghava Pandita; rāndhe — cooks; rādhā-thākurānī — the supreme mother, Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.

TRANSLATION

He brought so many presentations that no one could know them perfectly. Indeed, it was a fact that the supreme mother, Rādhārānī, personally cooked in the house of Rāghava Pandita.
