Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.187

kabhu carvana, kabhu randhana, kabhu dugdha-pāna

yabe yei mile, tāhe rākhe nija prāna

SYNONYMS

kabhu carvana — sometimes chewing; kabhu randhana — sometimes cooking; kabhu dugdha-pāna — sometimes drinking milk; yabe — when; yei — whoever; mile — meets; tāhe — in that way; rākhe — keeps; nija prāna — his life.

TRANSLATION

Sometimes he chewed fried grains, sometimes he cooked, and sometimes he drank milk. In this way he kept his life and soul together with whatever was available wherever he went.
