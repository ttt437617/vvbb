Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.153

eka-śata mudrā āra sonā tolā-dvaya

panditera āge dila kariyā vinaya

SYNONYMS

eka-śata mudrā — one hundred coins; āra — and; sonā — gold; tolā-dvaya — two tolās; panditera āge — before Rāghava Pandita; dila — presented; kariyā vinaya — with great humility.

TRANSLATION

With great humility, Raghunātha dāsa placed one hundred gold coins and about two tolās of gold before Rāghava Pandita for all the other devotees.
