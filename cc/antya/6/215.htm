Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.215

jagannāthera sevaka yata — 'vishayīra gana'

sevā sāri' rātrye kare grihete gamana

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; sevaka — servants; yata — all; vishayīra gana — generally known as vishayīs; sevā sāri' — after finishing their service; rātrye — at night; kare — do; grihete gamana — returning home.

TRANSLATION

After finishing their prescribed duties, the many servants of Lord Jagannātha, who are known as vishayīs, return home at night.
