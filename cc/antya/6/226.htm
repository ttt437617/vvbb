Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.226

vairāgīra kritya — sadā nāma-sańkīrtana

śāka-patra-phala-mūle udara-bharana

SYNONYMS

vairāgīra — of a person in the renounced order; kritya — duty; sadā — always; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting the holy name of the Lord; śāka — vegetables; patra — leaves; phala — fruit; mūle — by roots; udara-bharana — filling the belly.

TRANSLATION

"The duty of a person in the renounced order is to chant the Hare Krishna mantra always. He should satisfy his belly with whatever vegetables, leaves, fruits and roots are available.
