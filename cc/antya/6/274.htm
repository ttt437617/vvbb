Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.274

vishayīra dravya lañā kari nimantrana

prasanna nā haya ihāya jāni prabhura mana

SYNONYMS

vishayīra dravya — things supplied by materialistic men; lañā — accepting; kari nimantrana — I invite; prasanna — satisfied; nā haya — is not; ihāya — in this connection; jāni — I can understand; prabhura mana — the mind of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

"'I invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu by accepting goods from materialistic people. I know that the Lord's mind is not satisfied by this.
