Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.273

'raghu kene āmāya nimantrana chādi' dila?'

svarūpa kahe, — "mane kichu vicāra karila

SYNONYMS

raghu — Raghunātha dāsa; kene — why; āmāya — to Me; nimantrana — invitation; chādi' dila — has stopped; svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara replied; mane — within his mind; kichu — something; vicāra karila — he has thought.

TRANSLATION

The Lord asked, "Why has Raghunātha dāsa stopped inviting Me?"

Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "He must have reconsidered something in his mind.
