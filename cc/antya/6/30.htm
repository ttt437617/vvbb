Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.30

mleccha bale, — "āji haite tumi — mora 'putra'

āji chādāimu tomā' kari' eka sūtra"

SYNONYMS

mleccha bale — the Muslim said; āji haite — from this day; tumi — you; mora putra — my son; āji — today; chādāimu tomā' — I shall get you released; kari' eka sūtra — by some means.

TRANSLATION

The Muslim caudhurī told Raghunātha dāsa, "You are my son from this day on. Today, by some means, I shall have you released."
