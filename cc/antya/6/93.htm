Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.93

kautuka dekhite āila yata yata jana

sei cidā, dadhi, kalā karila bhakshana

SYNONYMS

kautuka — these funny things; dekhite — to see; āila — arrived; yata yata jana — all kinds of men; sei — they; cidā — chipped rice; dadhi — yogurt; kalā — bananas; karila bhakshana — ate.

TRANSLATION

Anyone who came to see how these funny things were going on was also fed chipped rice, yogurt and bananas.
