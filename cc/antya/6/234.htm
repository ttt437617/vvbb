Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.234

'sādhya'-'sādhana'-tattva śikha ińhāra sthāne

āmi tata nāhi jāni, ińho yata jāne

SYNONYMS

sādhya — duty; sādhana — how to execute it; tattva — truth; śikha — learn; ińhāra sthāne — from him; āmi — I; tata — so much; nāhi jāni — do not know; ińho — he; yata — as much as; jāne — knows.

TRANSLATION

"You may learn from him what your duty is and how to discharge it. I do not know as much as he.
