Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.308

ānande raghunāthera bāhya vismarana

kāya-mane sevilena gaurāńga-carana

SYNONYMS

ānande — in transcendental bliss; raghunāthera — of Raghunātha dāsa; bāhya vismarana — forgetting everything external; kāya-mane — by mind and body; sevilena — served; gaurāńga-carana — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa's transcendental bliss was boundless. Forgetting everything external, he served the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with his body and mind.
