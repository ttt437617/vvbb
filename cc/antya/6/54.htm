Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.54

'mahotsava'-nāma śuni' brāhmana-sajjana

āsite lāgila loka asańkhya-ganana

SYNONYMS

mahotsava — festival; nāma — name; śuni — hearing; brāhmana-sat-jana — brāhmanas and other gentlemen; āsite lāgila — began to pour in; loka — people; asańkhya-ganana — innumerable.

TRANSLATION

As soon as they heard that a festival was going to be held, all kinds of brāhmanas and other gentlemen began to arrive. Thus there were innumerable people.
