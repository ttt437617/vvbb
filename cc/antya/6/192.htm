Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.192

svarūpādi saba bhaktera carana vandilā

prabhu-kripā dekhi' sabe ālińgana kailā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-ādi — headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara; saba bhaktera — of all the devotees; carana vandilā — offered prayers to the lotus feet; prabhu-kripā — the mercy of Lord Caitanya; dekhi' — seeing; sabe — all of them; ālińgana kailā — embraced.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa offered prayers at the lotus feet of all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī. Seeing the special mercy Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had bestowed upon Raghunātha dāsa, they embraced him also.
