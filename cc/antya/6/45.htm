Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.45

tale upare bahu-bhakta hañāche veshtita

dekhi' prabhura prabhāva raghunātha — vismita

SYNONYMS

tale — the surface; upare — upon; bahu-bhakta — many devotees; hañāche veshtita — He was surrounded; dekhi' — seeing; prabhura prabhāva — the influence of Nityānanda Prabhu; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; vismita — astonished.

TRANSLATION

Many devotees sat on the ground surrounding Him. Seeing the influence of Nityānanda Prabhu, Raghunātha dāsa was astonished.
