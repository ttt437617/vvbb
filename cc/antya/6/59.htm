Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.59

dhuti pari' prabhu yadi pindāte vasilā

sāta-kundī vipra tāńra āgete dharilā

SYNONYMS

dhuti pari' — putting on a new cloth; prabhu — Lord Nityānanda; yadi — when; pindāte vasilā — sat on a high platform; sāta-kundī — the seven big, big earthen pots; vipra — the brāhmana; tāńra āgete — in front of Him; dharilā — placed.

TRANSLATION

After Nityānanda Prabhu had changed His cloth for a new one and sat on a raised platform, the brāhmana brought before Him the seven huge pots.
