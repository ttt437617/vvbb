Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.105

nrityera mādhurī kebā varnibāre pāre

mahāprabhu āise yei nritya dekhibāre

SYNONYMS

nrityera mādhurī — the sweetness of the dancing; kebā — who; varnibāre pāre — can describe; mahāprabhu āise — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu comes; yei — that; nritya — dancing; dekhibāre — to see.

TRANSLATION

No one can properly describe the sweetness of Lord Nityānanda's dancing. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu personally comes to see it.
