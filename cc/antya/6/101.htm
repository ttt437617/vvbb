Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.101

prabhu viśrāma kailā, yadi dina-śesha haila

rāghava-mandire tabe kīrtana ārambhila

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Nityānanda Prabhu; viśrāma kailā — took rest; yadi — when; dina-śesha haila — the day was ended; rāghava-mandire — at the temple of Rāghava Pandita; tabe — at that time; kīrtana ārambhila — began congregational chanting of the holy name.

TRANSLATION

Nityānanda Prabhu rested for the day, and when the day ended He went to the temple of Rāghava Pandita and began congregational chanting of the holy name of the Lord.
