Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.211

raghunātha samudre yāñā snāna karilā

jagannātha dekhi' punah govinda-pāśa āilā

SYNONYMS

raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; samudre — to the sea; yāñā — going; snāna karilā — took a bath; jagannātha dekhi' — after seeing Lord Jagannātha; punah — again; govinda-pāśa āilā — came to Govinda.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa took his bath in the sea and saw Lord Jagannātha. Then he returned to Govinda, the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
