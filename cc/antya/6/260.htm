Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.260

śivānanda kahe, — "tumi saba yāite nāribā

āmi yāi yabe, āmāra sańge yāibā

SYNONYMS

śivānanda kahe — Śivānanda Sena said; tumi — you; saba — all; yāite nāribā — cannot go; āmi yāi — I go; yabe — when; āmāra sańge — with me; yāibā — you will go.

TRANSLATION

Śivānanda Sena informed them, "You cannot go to Jagannātha Purī directly. When I go there, you may accompany me.
