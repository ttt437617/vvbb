Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.248

cāri māsa rahi' bhakta-gana gaude gelā

śuni' raghunāthera pitā manushya pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

cāri māsa — for four months; rahi' — remaining; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; gaude gelā — returned to Bengal; śuni' — hearing; raghunāthera pitā — the father of Raghunātha dāsa; manushya — a man; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

When all the devotees from Bengal returned home after staying at Jagannātha Purī for four months, Raghunātha dāsa's father heard about their arrival and therefore sent a man to Śivānanda Sena.
