Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.176

ethā tāńra sevaka rakshaka tāńre nā dekhiyā

tāńra guru-pāśe vārtā puchilena giyā

SYNONYMS

ethā — here, at his home; tāńra — his; sevaka — servant; rakshaka — watchman; tāńre — him; nā dekhiyā — not seeing; tāńra guru-pāśe — from his spiritual master; vārtā — news; puchilena — inquired; giyā — going.

TRANSLATION

At the house of Raghunātha dāsa, the servant and watchman, not seeing him there, immediately went to inquire about him from his spiritual master, Yadunandana Ācārya.
