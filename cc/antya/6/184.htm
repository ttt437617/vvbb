Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.184

ethā raghunātha-dāsa prabhāte uthiyā

pūrva-mukha chādi' cale dakshina-mukha hañā

SYNONYMS

ethā — here; raghunātha-dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa; prabhāte — early in the morning; uthiyā — rising; pūrva-mukha — facing the east; chādi' — giving up; cale — began to proceed; dakshina-mukha — facing south; hañā — being.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa, who had been resting at the milkman's house, got up early in the morning. Instead of going to the east, he turned his face south and proceeded.
