Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.47

śuni' prabhu kahe, — "corā dili daraśana

āya, āya, āji tora karimu dandana"

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; prabhu kahe — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu said; corā — thief; dili daraśana — you have come to see Me; āya āya — come here, come here; āji — today; tora — your; karimu — I shall do; dandana — punishment.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Lord Nityānanda Prabhu said, "You are a thief. Now you have come to see Me. Come here, come here. Today I shall punish you!"
