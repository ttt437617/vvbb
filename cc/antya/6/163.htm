Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.163

ańgane āsiyā teńho yabe dāndāilā

raghunātha āsi' tabe dandavat kailā

SYNONYMS

ańgane — in the courtyard; āsiyā — entering; teńho — Yadunandana Ācārya; yabe — when; dāndāilā — stood up; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa; āsi' — coming; tabe — at that time; dandavat kailā — offered his respects, falling down.

TRANSLATION

When Yadunandana Ācārya entered the house of Raghunātha dāsa and stood in the courtyard, Raghunātha dāsa went there and fell down to offer his obeisances.
