Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.72

ni-sakdi nānā-mata prasāda ānila

prabhure āge diyā bhakta-gane bāńti dila

SYNONYMS

ni-sakdi — food cooked in ghee; nānā-mata — various types; prasāda — remnants of the Lord's food; ānila — he brought; prabhure āge — in front of Lord Nityānanda; diyā — placing; bhakta-gane — to all the devotees; bāńti dila — distributed.

TRANSLATION

He brought many kinds of food cooked in ghee and offered to the Lord. This prasādam he first placed before Lord Nityānanda and then distributed among the devotees.
