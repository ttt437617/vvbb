Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.114

dui bhāire rāghava āni' pariveśe

yatna kari' khāoyāya, nā rahe avaśeshe

SYNONYMS

dui bhāire — to the two brothers; rāghava — Rāghava Pandita; āni' — bringing; pariveśe — distributed; yatna kari' — with great attention; khāoyāya — fed Them; nā rahe avaśeshe — there were no remnants.

TRANSLATION

Rāghava Pandita would bring and distribute prasādam to the two brothers, feeding Them with great attention. They ate everything, and therefore there were no remnants left.
