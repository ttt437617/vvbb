Antya-līlāChapter 6: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Raghunatha dasa Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 6.305

tabe ashta-kaudira khājā kare samarpana

svarūpa-ājñāya govinda tāhā kare samādhāna

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; ashta-kaudira — costing eight kaudis; khājā — the sweetmeat named khājā; kare samarpana — offers; svarūpa-ājñāya — by the order of Svarūpa Dāmodara; govinda — the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāhā — that; kare samādhāna — arranges.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha dāsa then began offering the costly sweetmeats known as khājā, which Govinda, following the order of Svarūpa Dāmodara, would supply.
