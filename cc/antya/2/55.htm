Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.55

kāli madhyāhne teńho āsibena tomāra ghare

pāka-sāmagrī ānaha, āmi bhikshā dimu tāńre

SYNONYMS

kāli madhyāhne — tomorrow at noon; teńho — He; āsibena — will come; tomāra ghare — to your place; pāka-sāmagrī ānaha — please bring everything necessary for cooking; āmi — I; bhikshā dimu — shall cook and offer food; tāńre — to Him.

TRANSLATION

"Tomorrow at noon He will come to your home. Therefore please bring all kinds of cooking ingredients. I shall personally cook and offer Him food.
