Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.42

śivānande kahiha, — āmi ei pausha-māse

ācambite avaśya āmi yāiba tāńra pāśe

SYNONYMS

śivānande kahiha — speak to Śivānanda Sena; āmi — I; ei — this; pausha-māse — in the month of Pausha (December-January); ācambite — all of a sudden; avaśya — certainly; āmi — I; yāiba — shall go; tāńra pāśe — to his place.

TRANSLATION

"Please inform Śivānanda Sena that in the month of Pausha [December-January] I shall certainly go to his home.
