Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.52

tāńhāra prabhāva-prema jāne dui-jane

ānibe prabhure ebe niścaya kailā mane

SYNONYMS

tāńhāra — his; prabhāva — influence; prema — love of Godhead; jāne — know; dui-jane — both of them; ānibe prabhure — he will bring Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ebe — now; niścaya kailā mane — they were greatly assured within their minds.

TRANSLATION

Śivānanda and Jagadānanda knew of Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī's influence and love of Godhead. Therefore they now felt assured that he would certainly bring Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
