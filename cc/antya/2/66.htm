Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.66

bhojana dekhi' yadyapi tāńra hridaye ullāsa

nrisimha lakshya kari' bāhye kichu kare duhkhābhāsa

SYNONYMS

bhojana dekhi' — seeing the eating; yadyapi — although; tāńra hridaye — within his heart; ullāsa — jubilation; nrisimha — Lord Nrisimhadeva; lakshya kari' — for the sake of; bāhye — externally; kichu — some; kare — does; duhkha-ābhāsa — expression of disappointment.

TRANSLATION

Although Nrisimha Brahmacārī felt jubilation within his heart to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu eating everything, for the sake of Lord Nrisimhadeva he externally expressed disappointment.
