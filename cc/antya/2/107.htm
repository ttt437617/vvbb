Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.107

tāńra thāñi tandula māgi' ānila haridāsa

tandula dekhi' ācāryera adhika ullāsa

SYNONYMS

tāńra thāñi — from her; tandula māgi' — begging rice; ānila haridāsa — Haridāsa brought; tandula dekhi' — seeing the rice; ācāryera — of Bhagavān Ācārya; adhika ullāsa — very great satisfaction.

TRANSLATION

After begging the rice from her, Junior Haridāsa brought it to Bhagavān Ācārya, who was very pleased to see its quality.
