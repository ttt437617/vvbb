Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.8

gauda-deśera bhakta-gana pratyabda āsiyā

punah gauda-deśe yāya prabhure miliyā

SYNONYMS

gauda-deśera — of Bengal; bhakta-gana — devotees; prati-abda — every year; āsiyā — coming; punah — again; gauda-deśe — to Bengal; yāya — return; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; miliyā — after meeting.

TRANSLATION

Every year, devotees from Bengal would go to Jagannātha Purī to meet Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and after the meeting they would return to Bengal.
