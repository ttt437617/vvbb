Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.56

tabe tāńre ethā āmi āniba satvara

niścaya kahilāńa, kichu sandeha nā kara

SYNONYMS

tabe — in this way; tāńre — Him; ethā — here; āmi — I; āniba satvara — shall bring very soon; niścaya — with certainty; kahilāńa — I spoke; kichu sandeha nā kara — do not be doubtful.

TRANSLATION

"In this way I shall bring Him here very soon. Be assured that I am telling you the truth. Do not be doubtful.
