Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.23

parīkshā karite tāńra yabe icchā haila

bāhire rahiyā tabe vicāra karila

SYNONYMS

parīkshā karite — to test; tāńra — of Śivānanda Sena; yabe — when; icchā — desire; haila — there was; bāhire rahiyā — staying outside; tabe — at that time; vicāra karila — considered.

TRANSLATION

Desiring to test the authenticity of Nakula Brahmacārī, he stayed outside, thinking as follows.
