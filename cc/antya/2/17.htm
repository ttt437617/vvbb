Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.17

gauda-deśera loka nistārite mana haila

nakula-hridaye prabhu 'āveśa' karila

SYNONYMS

gauda-deśera loka — the people of Bengal; nistārite — to deliver; mana haila — wanted; nakula-hridaye — in the heart of Nakula Brahmacārī; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āveśa karila — entered.

TRANSLATION

Desiring to deliver all the people of Bengal, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered the heart of Nakula Brahmacārī.
