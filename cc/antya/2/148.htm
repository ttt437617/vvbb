Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.148

sei-kshane divya-dehe prabhu-sthāne āilā

prabhu-kripā pāñā antardhānei rahilā

SYNONYMS

sei-kshane — immediately thereupon; divya-dehe — in a spiritual body; prabhu-sthāne āilā — came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu-kripā — the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāñā — getting; antardhānei rahilā — remained invisible.

TRANSLATION

Immediately after committing suicide in this way, he went in his spiritual body to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and received the mercy of the Lord. However, he still remained invisible.
