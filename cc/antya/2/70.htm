Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.70

śivānanda kahe, — 'kene karaha phutkāra?'

teńha kahe, — "dekha tomāra prabhura vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

śivānanda kahe — Śivānanda Sena said; kene karaha phut-kāra — why are you expressing dismay; teńha kahe — he replied; dekha — see; tomāra prabhura — of your Lord; vyayahāra — the behavior.

TRANSLATION

Śivānanda said to Nrisimhānanda, "Why are you expressing dismay?"

Nrisimhānanda replied, "Just see the behavior of your Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
