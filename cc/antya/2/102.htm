Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.102

'chota-haridāsa' nāma prabhura kīrtanīyā

tāhāre kahena ācārya dākiyā āniyā

SYNONYMS

chota-haridāsa nāma — a devotee named Chota Haridāsa; prabhura kīrtanīyā — a chanter of songs for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāhāre — unto him; kahena — says; ācārya — the ācārya; dākiyā āniyā — calling him to his place.

TRANSLATION

A devotee named Chota Haridāsa used to sing for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Bhagavān Ācārya called him to his home and spoke as follows.
