Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.41

e-vatsara tāńhā āmi yāimu āpane

tāhāi milimu saba advaitādi sane

SYNONYMS

e-vatsara — this year; tāńhā — there (to Bengal); āmi — I; yāimu — shall go; āpane — personally; tāhāi — there; milimu — I shall meet; saba — all; advaita-ādi — beginning with Advaita Ācārya; sane — with.

TRANSLATION

"This year I shall personally go to Bengal and meet all the devotees there, headed by Advaita Ācārya.
