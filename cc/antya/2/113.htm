Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.113

'āji haite ei mora ājñā pālibā

chota haridāse ihāń āsite nā dibā'

SYNONYMS

āji haite — from today; ei — this; mora — My; ājñā — order; pālibā — you should carry out; chota haridāse — Chota Haridāsa; ihāń — here; āsite — to come; nā dibā — do not allow.

TRANSLATION

"From this day forward, do not allow Chota Haridāsa to come here."
