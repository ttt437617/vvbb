Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.152

śuni' mahāprabhu īshat hāsiyā rahilā

saba bhakta-gana mane vismaya ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; īshat — slightly; hāsiyā rahilā — remained smiling; saba bhakta-gana — all the devotees; mane — in the minds; vismaya ha-ilā — became surprised.

TRANSLATION

While hearing the devotees lament, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was mildly smiling. Thus all the devotees were very much astonished.
