Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.84

purushottame prabhu-pāśe bhagavān ācārya

parama vaishnava teńho supandita ārya

SYNONYMS

purushottame — at Jagannātha Purī; prabhu-pāśe — in the association of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhagavān ācārya — Bhagavān Ācārya; parama vaishnava — pure devotee; teńho — he; su-pandita — very learned scholar; ārya — gentleman.

TRANSLATION

At Jagannātha Purī, in the association of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, lived Bhagavān Ācārya, who was certainly a gentleman, a learned scholar and a great devotee.

PURPORT

For a description of Bhagavān Ācārya, one may refer to Ādi-līlā, Tenth Chapter, verse 136.
