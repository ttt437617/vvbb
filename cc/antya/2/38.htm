Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.38

eka vatsara teńho prathama ekeśvara

prabhu dekhibāre āilā utkanthā-antara

SYNONYMS

eka vatsara — one year; teńho — Śrīkānta Sena; prathama — first; ekeśvara — alone; prabhu dekhibāre — to see the Lord; āilā — came; utkanthā-antara — with great eagerness in the mind.

TRANSLATION

One year, Śrīkānta Sena came alone to Jagannātha Purī in great eagerness to see the Lord.
