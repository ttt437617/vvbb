Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.21

yāre dekhe tāre kahe, — 'kaha krishna-nāma'

tāńhāra darśane loka haya premoddāma

SYNONYMS

yāre dekhe — to whomever he saw; tāre kahe — he addresses him; kaha krishna-nāma — my dear friend, chant Krishna's holy name; tāńhāra darśane — by seeing him; loka haya — people became; prema-uddāma — highly elevated in love of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

He advised whomever he met to chant the holy names Hare Krishna. Thus upon seeing him, people were overwhelmed with love of Godhead.
