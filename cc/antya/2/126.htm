Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.126

eta śuni' sabe nija-karne hasta diyā

nija nija kārye sabe gela ta' uthiyā

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; sabe — all the devotees; nija-karne — over their ears; hasta diyā — putting their hands; nija nija kārye — to their respective duties; sabe — all of them; gela — went; ta' — certainly; uthiyā — getting up.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, all the devotees covered their ears with their hands, got up and went about their respective duties.
