Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.133

eta bali' prabhu yadi govinde bolāilā

purīre namaskāra kari' uthiyā calilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yadi — when; govinde bolāilā — called for Govinda; purīre — to Paramānanda Purī; namaskāra kari' — offering respect; uthiyā calilā — got up and began to go away.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, the Lord called for Govinda. Offering obeisances to Paramānanda Purī, He got up and began to leave.
