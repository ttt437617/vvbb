Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.61

ishta-deva nrisimha lāgi' prithak bādila

tina-jane samarpiyā bāhire dhyāna kaila

SYNONYMS

ishta-deva — the worshipable Deity; nrisimha — Lord Nrisimhadeva; lāgi' — for; prithak — separately; bādila — arranged; tina-jane — to the three Deities; samarpiyā — offering; bāhire — outside; dhyāna kaila — meditated.

TRANSLATION

He also separately offered dishes to Nrisimhadeva, his worshipable Deity. Thus he divided all the food into three offerings. Then, outside the temple, he began to meditate upon the Lord.
