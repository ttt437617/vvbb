Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.112

anna praśamsiyā prabhu bhojana karilā

nija-grihe āsi' govindere ājñā dilā

SYNONYMS

anna praśamsiyā — praising the rice; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhojana karilā — partook of the prasādam; nija-grihe — to His own residence; āsi' — coming back; govindere — to Govinda; ājñā dilā — He gave an order.

TRANSLATION

Praising the quality of the rice, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu partook of the prasādam. Then, after returning to His residence, He gave the following order to Govinda, His personal assistant.
