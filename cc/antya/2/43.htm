Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.43

jagadānanda haya tāhāń, teńho bhikshā dibe

sabāre kahiha, — e vatsara keha nā āsibe"

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; haya — is; tāhāń — there; teńho — he; bhikshā dibe — will give offerings of food; sabāre kahiha — inform all of them; e vatsara — this year; keha nā āsibe — no one should come.

TRANSLATION

"Jagadānanda is there, and he will give Me offerings of food. Inform them all that no one should come to Jagannātha Purī this year."
