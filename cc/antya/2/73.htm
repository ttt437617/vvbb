Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.73

tabe śivānande kichu kahe brahmacārī

'sāmagrī āna nrisimha lāgi punah pāka kari'

SYNONYMS

tabe — upon this; śivānande — unto Śivānanda; kichu — something; kahe — says; brahmacārī — Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī; sāmagrī āna — bring more ingredients; nrisimha lāgi' — for Lord Nrisimhadeva; punah — again; pāka kari' — let me cook.

TRANSLATION

When Śivānanda Sena was thus perplexed, Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī said to him, "Bring more food. Let me cook again for Lord Nrisimhadeva."
