Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.63

ānande vihvala pradyumna, pade aśru-dhāra

"hāhā kibā kara" bali' karaye phutkāra

SYNONYMS

ānande vihvala — overwhelmed by transcendental ecstasy; pradyumna — Pradyumna Brahmacārī; pade aśru-dhāra — tears fell from his eyes; hāhā — alas, alas; kibā kara — what are You doing; bali' — saying; karaye phut-kāra — began to express disappointment.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Brahmacārī was overwhelmed by transcendental ecstasy upon seeing Caitanya Mahāprabhu eating everything. Thus tears flowed from his eyes. Nevertheless, he expressed dismay, saying, "Alas, alas! My dear Lord, what are You doing? You are eating everyone's food!
