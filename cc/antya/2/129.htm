Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.129

tabe purī-gosāñi ekā prabhu-sthāne āilā

namaskari' prabhu tāńre sambhrame vasāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; purī-gosāñi — Paramānanda Purī; ekā — alone; prabhu-sthāne — to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; namaskari' — after offering obeisances; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; sambhrame — with great respect; vasāilā — got to sit down.

TRANSLATION

Paramānanda Purī thereupon went alone to the residence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. The Lord, after offering him obeisances, seated him by His side with great respect.
