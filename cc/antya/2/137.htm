Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.137

eta bali' purī-gosāñi gelā nija-sthāne

haridāsa-sthāne gelā saba bhakta-gane

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; purī-gosāñi — Paramānanda Gosāñi; gelā — left; nija-sthāne — for his own place; haridāsa-sthāne — unto the place of Junior Haridāsa; gelā — went; saba bhakta-gane — all the other devotees.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Paramānanda Purī Gosāñi left for his own home. Then all the devotees went to see Junior Haridāsa.
