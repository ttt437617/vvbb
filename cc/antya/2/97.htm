Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.97

ācārya kahe, — 'āmā sabāra krishna-nishtha-citte

āmā sabāra mana bhāshya nāre phirāite'

SYNONYMS

ācārya kahe — Bhagavān Ācārya replied; āmā sabāra — of all of us; krishna-nishtha — devoted to Krishna; citte — hearts; āmā sabāra — of all of us; mana — minds; bhāshya — Śārīraka-bhāshya; nāre phirāite — cannot change.

TRANSLATION

In spite of Svarūpa Dāmodara's protest, Bhagavān Ācārya continued, "We are all fixed at the lotus feet of Krishna with our hearts and souls. Therefore the Śārīraka-bhāshya cannot change our minds."
