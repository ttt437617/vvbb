Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.156

'vishādi khāñā haridāsa ātma-ghāta kaila

sei pāpe jāni 'brahma-rākshasa' haila

SYNONYMS

visha-ādi khāñā — by drinking poison; haridāsa — Junior Haridāsa; ātma-ghāta kaila — has committed suicide; sei pāpe — on account of that sinful activity; jāni — we understand; brahma-rākshasa — a brāhmana ghost; haila — he has become.

TRANSLATION

"Haridāsa must have committed suicide by drinking poison, and because of this sinful act, he has now become a brāhmana ghost.
