Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.75

varshāntare śivānanda lañā bhakta-gana

nīlācale dekhe yāñā prabhura carana

SYNONYMS

varsha-antare — the next year; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; lañā — taking; bhakta-gana — all the devotees; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; dekhe — sees; yāñā — going; prabhura carana — the lotus feet of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

The next year, Śivānanda went to Jagannātha Purī with all the other devotees to see the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
