Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.62

dekhe, śīghra āsi' vasilā caitanya-gosāñi

tina bhoga khāilā, kichu avaśishta nāi

SYNONYMS

dekhe — he sees; śīghra āsi' — coming quickly; vasilā — sat down; caitanya-gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tina bhoga — the three separate offerings; khāilā — He ate; kichu avaśishta nāi — there were no remnants left.

TRANSLATION

In his meditation he saw Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu quickly come, sit down and eat all three offerings, leaving behind no remnants.
