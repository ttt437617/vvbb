Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.40

tabe prabhu tāńre ājñā kailā gaude yāite

"bhakta-gane nishedhiha ethāke āsite

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; ājñā kailā — ordered; gaude yāite — returning to Bengal; bhakta-gane — the devotees; nishedhiha — forbid; ethāke āsite — to come to this place.

TRANSLATION

When he was about to return to Bengal, the Lord told him, "Forbid the devotees of Bengal to come to Jagannātha Purī this year.
