Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.3

sarva-loka uddhārite gaura-avatāra

nistārera hetu tāra trividha prakāra

SYNONYMS

sarva-loka — all the worlds; uddhārite — to deliver; gaura-avatāra — the incarnation of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nistārera hetu — causes of the deliverance of all people; tāra — His; tri-vidha prakāra — three kinds.

TRANSLATION

In His incarnation as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Lord Śrī Krishna descended to deliver all the living beings in the three worlds, from Brahmaloka down to Pātālaloka. He caused their deliverance in three ways.
