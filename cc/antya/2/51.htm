Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.51

śuni' brahmacārī kahe, — 'karaha santoshe

āmi ta' āniba tāńre tritīya divase'

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; brahmacārī — Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī; kahe — said; karaha santoshe — become happy; āmi — I; ta' — certainly; āniba — shall bring; tāńre — Him (Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu); tritīya divase — on the third day.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī replied, "Please be satisfied. I assure you that I shall bring Him here three days from now."
