Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.160

prayāga ha-ite eka vaishnava navadvīpa āila

haridāsera vārtā teńho sabāre kahila

SYNONYMS

prayāga ha-ite — from Prayāga; eka — one; vaishnava — devotee of Lord Krishna; navadvīpa āila — came to Navadvīpa; haridāsera vārtā — the news of Haridāsa; teńho — he; sabāre kahila — informed everyone.

TRANSLATION

A devotee returned to Navadvīpa from Prayāga and told everyone the details of Junior Haridāsa's suicide.
