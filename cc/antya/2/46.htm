Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.46

pausha-māse āila duńhe sāmagrī kariyā

sandhyā-paryanta rahe apekshā kariyā

SYNONYMS

pausha-māse — the month of Pausha (December-January); āila — came; duńhe — Śivānanda Sena and Jagadānanda; sāmagrī kariyā — making all arrangements; sandhyā-paryanta — until the evening; rahe — remain; apekshā kariyā — waiting.

TRANSLATION

When the month of Pausha arrived, Jagadānanda and Śivānanda collected all kinds of paraphernalia for the Lord's reception. Every day, they would wait until evening for the Lord to come.
