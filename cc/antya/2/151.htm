Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.151

sabe kahe, — "haridāsa varsha-pūrna dine

rātre uthi kāńhā gelā, keha nāhi jāne"

SYNONYMS

sabe kahe — everyone spoke; haridāsa — Haridāsa; varsha-pūrna dine — at the end of one full year; rātre — at night; uthi — getting up; kāńhā gelā — where he went; keha nāhi jāne — no one knows.

TRANSLATION

The devotees all replied, "One night at the end of a full year, Junior Haridāsa got up and went away. No one knows where he has gone."
