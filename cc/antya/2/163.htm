Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.163

'haridāsa kāńhā?' yadi śrīvāsa puchilā

"sva-karma-phala-bhuk pumān" — prabhu uttara dilā

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kāńhā — where is Junior Haridāsa; yadi — when; śrīvāsa puchilā — Śrīvāsa Thākura inquired; sva-karma-phala-bhuk — sure to accept the resultant action of his fruitive activities; pumān — a person; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uttara dilā — replied.

TRANSLATION

When Śrīvāsa Thākura inquired from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Where is Junior Haridāsa?" the Lord replied, "A person is sure to achieve the results of his fruitive activities."
