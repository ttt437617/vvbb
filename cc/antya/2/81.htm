Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.81

prema-vaśa gaura-prabhu, yāhāń premottama

prema-vaśa hañā tāhā dena daraśana

SYNONYMS

prema-vaśa — subdued by loving service; gaura-prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Gaurasundara; yāhāń prema-uttama — wherever there is pure love; prema-vaśa hañā — being subdued by such love; tāhā — there; dena daraśana — appears personally.

TRANSLATION

Lord Gaurasundara is greatly influenced by the love of His devotees. Therefore wherever there is pure devotion to the Lord, the Lord Himself, subdued by such love, appears, and His devotees see Him.
