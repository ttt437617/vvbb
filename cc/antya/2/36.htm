Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.36

nrisimhānandera āge āvirbhūta hañā

bhojana karilā, tāhā śuna mana diyā

SYNONYMS

nrisimhānandera — the brahmacārī known as Nrisimhānanda; āge — before; āvirbhūta hañā — appearing; bhojana karilā — He accepted offerings of food; tāhā — that; śuna — hear; mana diyā — with attention.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu appeared before Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī and ate his offerings. Please hear about this with attention.
