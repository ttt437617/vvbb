Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.104

māhitira bhaginī sei, nāma — mādhavī-devī

vriddhā tapasvinī āra paramā vaishnavī

SYNONYMS

māhitira bhaginī — sister of Śikhi Māhiti; sei — that; nāma — named; mādhavī-devī — Mādhavī-devī; vriddhā — an elderly lady; tapasvinī — very strict in executing devotional service; āra — and; paramā vaishnavī — a first-class devotee.

TRANSLATION

Śikhi Māhiti's sister was named Mādhavī-devī. She was an elderly lady who always performed austerities. She was very advanced in devotional service.
