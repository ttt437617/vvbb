Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.168

āpana kārunya, loke vairāgya-śikshana

sva-bhaktera gādha-anurāga-prakatī-karana

SYNONYMS

āpana — personal; kārunya — favor; loke — to the people in general; vairāgya-śikshana — teaching about the order of renunciation; sva-bhaktera — of His devotees; gādha — deep; anurāga — of attachment; prakatī — manifestation; karana — causing.

TRANSLATION

This incident manifests the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His teaching that a sannyāsī should remain in the renounced order, and the deep attachment to Him felt by His faithful devotees.
