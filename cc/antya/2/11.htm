Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.11

prabhure dekhiyā yāya 'vaishnava' hañā

krishna bali' nāce saba premāvishta hañā

SYNONYMS

prabhure dekhiyā — by seeing the Lord; yāya — they return; vaishnava hañā — having become devotees of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna; krishna bali' — chanting Krishna; nāce — dance; saba — all of them; prema-āvishta hañā — overwhelmed by ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

Having seen the Lord, they all became Vaishnavas. Thus in ecstatic love of Godhead they chanted the Hare Krishna mantra and danced.
