Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.65

nrisimhera haila jāni āji upavāsa

thākura upavāsī rahe, jiye kaiche dāsa?'

SYNONYMS

nrisimhera — of Lord Nrisimha; haila — there was; jāni — I understand; āji — today; upavāsa — fasting; thākura upavāsī rahe — the master remains fasting; jiye kaiche dāsa — how can the servant sustain his life.

TRANSLATION

"I think that Nrisimhadeva could not eat anything today, and therefore He is fasting. If the master fasts, how can the servant live?"
