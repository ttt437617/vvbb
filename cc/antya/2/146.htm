Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.146

rātri avaśeshe prabhure dandavat hañā

prayāgete gela kāreha kichu nā baliyā

SYNONYMS

rātri avaśeshe — at the end of one night; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dandavat hañā — offering obeisances; prayāgete — to the holy place known as Prayāga (Allahabad); gela — went; kāreha — to anyone; kichu — anything; nā baliyā — not saying.

TRANSLATION

Thus at the end of one night, Junior Haridāsa, after offering Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu his respectful obeisances, departed for Prayāga without saying anything to anyone.
