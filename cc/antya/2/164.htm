Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.164

tabe śrīvāsa tāra vrittānta kahila

yaiche sańkalpa, yaiche trivenī praveśila

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; śrīvāsa — Śrīvāsa Thākura; tāra — of Junior Haridāsa; vrittānta — story; kahila — spoke; yaiche — how; sańkalpa — he decided; yaiche — how; trivenī praveśila — he entered the waters at the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā.

TRANSLATION

Then Śrīvāsa Thākura related the details of Haridāsa's decision and his entering the waters at the confluence of the Ganges and Yamunā.
