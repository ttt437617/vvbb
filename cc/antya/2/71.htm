Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.71

tina janāra bhoga teńho ekelā khāilā

jagannātha-nrisimha upavāsī ha-ilā"

SYNONYMS

tina janāra — of the three Deities; bhoga — offerings; teńho — He; ekelā — alone; khāilā — ate; jagannātha-nrisimha — Lord Jagannātha and Lord Nrisimhadeva; upavāsī ha-ilā — remained fasting.

TRANSLATION

"He alone has eaten the offerings for all three Deities. Because of this, both Jagannātha and Nrisimhadeva remain fasting."
