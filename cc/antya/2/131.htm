Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.131

śuniyā kahena prabhu, — "śunaha, gosāñi

saba vaishnava lañā tumi raha ei thāñi

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; kahena prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; śunaha — please hear; gosāñi — My lord; saba vaishnava — all the Vaishnavas; lañā — taking; tumi — your lordship; raha — stay; ei thāñi — in this place.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this request, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My dear lord, please hear Me. It is better for you to stay here with all the Vaishnavas.
