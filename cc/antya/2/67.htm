Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.67

svayam bhagavān krishna-caitanya-gosāñi

jagannātha-nrisimha-saha kichu bheda nāi

SYNONYMS

svayam — personally; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; krishna-caitanya-gosāñi — Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jagannātha-nrisimha-saha — with Lord Jagannātha and Nrisimhadeva; kichu bheda — any difference; nāi — there is not.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself. Therefore there is no difference between Him, Lord Jagannātha and Lord Nrisimhadeva.
