Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.39

mahāprabhu tāre dekhi' bada kripā kailā

māsa-dui teńho prabhura nikate rahilā

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāre — him; dekhi' — seeing; bada kripā kailā — bestowed great mercy; māsa-dui — for two months; teńho — Śrīkānta Sena; prabhura nikate — near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahilā — stayed.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Śrīkānta Sena, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bestowed causeless mercy upon him. Śrīkānta Sena stayed near Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for about two months at Jagannātha Purī.
