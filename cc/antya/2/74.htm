Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.74

tabe śivānanda bhoga-sāmagrī ānilā

pāka kari' nrisimhera bhoga lāgāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; bhoga-sāmagrī — ingredients for preparing food; ānilā — brought; pāka kari' — after cooking; nrisimhera — of Lord Nrisimhadeva; bhoga lāgāilā — offered the food.

TRANSLATION

Then Śivānanda Sena again brought the ingredients with which to cook, and Pradyumna Brahmacārī again cooked and offered the food to Nrisimhadeva.
