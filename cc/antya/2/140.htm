Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.140

tumi hatha kaile tāńra hatha se bādibe

snāna bhojana kara, āpane krodha yābe"

SYNONYMS

tumi hatha kaile — if you go on persisting; tāńra — His; hatha — persistence; se — that; bādibe — will increase; snāna bhojana kara — take your bath and take prasādam; āpane krodha yābe — automatically His anger will subside.

TRANSLATION

"The Lord is persisting, and if you also persist, His persistence will increase. It is better for you to bathe and take prasādam. In due course, His anger will automatically subside."
