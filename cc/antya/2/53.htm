Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.53

'pradyumna brahmacārī' — tāńra nija-nāma

'nrisimhānanda' nāma tāńra kailā gaura-dhāma

SYNONYMS

pradyumna brahmacārī — Pradyumna Brahmacārī; tāńra — his; nija-nāma — real name; nrisimhānanda — Nrisimhānanda; nāma — name; tāńra — his; kailā gaura-dhāma — was given by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

His real name was Pradyumna Brahmacārī. The name Nrisimhānanda had been given to him by Lord Gaurasundara Himself.
