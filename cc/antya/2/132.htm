Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.132

more ājñā haya, muñi yāńa ālālanātha

ekale rahiba tāhāń, govinda-mātra sātha"

SYNONYMS

more — to Me; ājñā haya — please give permission; muñi — I; yāńa — go; ālālanātha — to the place known as Ālālanātha; ekale rahiba — I shall remain alone; tāhāń — there; govinda-mātra sātha — with only Govinda.

TRANSLATION

"Please give Me permission to go to Ālālanātha. I shall remain there alone; only Govinda will go with Me."
