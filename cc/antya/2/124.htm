Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.124

prabhu kahe, — "mora vaśa nahe mora mana

prakriti-sambhāshī vairāgī nā kare darśana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; mora vaśa — under My control; nahe — is not; mora — My; mana — mind; prakriti-sambhāshī — one who talks with women; vairāgī — a person in the renounced order; nā kare darśana — does not see.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My mind is not under My control. It does not like to see anyone in the renounced order who talks intimately with women.
