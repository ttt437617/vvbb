Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.158

ājanma krishna-kīrtana, prabhura sevana

prabhu-kripā-pātra, āra kshetrera marana

SYNONYMS

ājanma — throughout the whole life; krishna-kīrtana — chanting of the Hare Krishna mantra; prabhura sevana — service to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu-kripā-pātra — very dear to the Lord; āra — and; kshetrera marana — his death in a holy place.

TRANSLATION

"Junior Haridāsa chanted the Hare Krishna mantra throughout his entire life and served the Supreme Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Moreover, he is dear to the Lord and has died in a holy place.
