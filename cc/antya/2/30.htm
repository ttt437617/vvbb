Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.30

brahmacārī bale, — "tumi karilā samśaya

eka-manā hañā śuna tāhāra niścaya

SYNONYMS

brahmacārī bale — Nakula Brahmacārī said; tumi — you; karilā samśaya — have doubted; eka-manā hañā — with great attention; śuna — please hear; tāhāra — for that; niścaya — settlement.

TRANSLATION

Nakula Brahmacārī said, "I know that you are doubtful. Now please hear this evidence with great attention.
