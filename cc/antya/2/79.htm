Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.79

ei-mata śacī-grihe satata bhojana

śrīvāsera grihe karena kīrtana-darśana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; śacī-grihe — at the house of Śacīmātā; satata — always; bhojana — eating; śrīvāsera grihe — in the house of Śrīvāsa Thākura; karena — performs; kīrtana-darśana — visiting the kīrtana performances.

TRANSLATION

In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu used to eat at the temple of Śacīmātā every day and also visit the house of Śrīvāsa Thākura when kīrtana was performed.
