Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.29

śuni', śivānanda sena tāńhā śīghra āila

namaskāra kari' tāńra nikate vasila

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; śivānanda sena — Śivānanda Sena; tāńhā — there; śīghra — quickly; āila — came; namaskāra kari' — offering obeisances; tāńra nikate — near him; vasila — sat down.

TRANSLATION

Hearing these calls, Śivānanda Sena quickly went there, offered obeisances to Nakula Brahmacārī, and sat down near him.
