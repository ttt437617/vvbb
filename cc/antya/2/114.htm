Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.114

dvāra mānā haila, haridāsa duhkhī haila mane

ki lāgiyā dvāra-mānā keha nāhi jāne

SYNONYMS

dvāra mānā — closed door; haila — there was; haridāsa — Chota Haridāsa; duhkhī — very unhappy; haila mane — became within his mind; ki lāgiyā — for what reason; dvāra-mānā — the door was closed; keha nāhi jāne — no one could understand.

TRANSLATION

When Junior Haridāsa heard that he had been ordered not to approach Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he was very unhappy. No one could understand why he had been ordered not to come.
