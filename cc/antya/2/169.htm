Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.169

tīrthera mahimā, nija bhakte ātmasāt

eka līlāya karena prabhu kārya pāńca-sāta

SYNONYMS

tīrthera mahimā — the glories of a holy place; nija bhakte ātmasāt — accepting His devotee again; eka līlāya — by one pastime; karena — executes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kārya pāńca-sāta — five to seven different purposes.

TRANSLATION

It also demonstrates the glories of holy places and shows how the Lord accepts His faithful devotee. Thus the Lord fulfilled five or seven purposes by performing one pastime.
