Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.159

durgati nā haya tāra, sad-gati se haya

prabhu-bhańgī ei, pāche jānibā niścaya"

SYNONYMS

durgati — a bad result; nā haya tāra — is not his; sat-gati se haya — he must have achieved liberation; prabhu-bhańgī — a sport of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — this; pāche — later; jānibā — you will understand; niścaya — the real fact.

TRANSLATION

"Haridāsa cannot have been degraded; he must have attained liberation. This is a pastime of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's. You will all understand it later."
