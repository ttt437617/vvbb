Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.64

'jagannāthe-tomāya aikya, khāo tāńra bhoga

nrisimhera bhoga kene kara upayoga?

SYNONYMS

jagannāthe — with Lord Jagannātha; tomāya — and You; aikya — oneness; khāo tāńra bhoga — You may eat His offering; nrisimhera bhoga — the offering of Nrisimhadeva; kene kara upayoga — why are You eating.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You are one with Jagannātha; therefore I have no objection to Your eating His offering. But why are You touching the offering for Lord Nrisimhadeva?
