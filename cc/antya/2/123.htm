Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.123

"alpa aparādha, prabhu karaha prasāda

ebe śikshā ha-ila nā karibe aparādha"

SYNONYMS

alpa aparādha — the offense is not very great; prabhu — O Lord; karaha prasāda — be merciful; ebe — now; śikshā ha-ila — he has gotten a sufficient lesson; nā karibe — he will not do; aparādha — offense.

TRANSLATION

"Haridāsa has committed a small offense," they said. "Therefore, O Lord, please be merciful to him. Now he has received a sufficient lesson. In the future he will not commit such an offense."
