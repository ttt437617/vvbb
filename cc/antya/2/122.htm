Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.122

āra dine sabe meli' prabhura carane

haridāsa lāgi, kichu kailā nivedane

SYNONYMS

āra dine — the next day; sabe meli' — all the devotees, coming together; prabhura carane — unto the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haridāsa lāgi — on behalf of Junior Haridāsa; kichu — some; kailā nivedane — submitted a petition.

TRANSLATION

The next day, all the devotees together approached the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to submit an appeal on behalf of Junior Haridāsa.
