Antya-līlāChapter 2: The Chastisement of Junior Haridāsa

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 2.76

eka-dina sabhāte prabhu bāta cālāilā

nrisimhānandera guna kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; sabhāte — in the presence of all the devotees; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāta cālāilā — raised the topic (of eating at Nrisimhānanda's house); nrisimhānandera — of Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī; guna — transcendental qualities; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

One day, in the presence of all the devotees, the Lord raised these topics concerning Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī and praised his transcendental qualities.
