Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.63

pranāma kari' prabhu kailā carana vandana

prabhure kahaye kichu hāsiyā vacana

SYNONYMS

pranāma kari' — offering obeisances; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā carana vandana — offered respect at his feet; prabhure — to Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahaye — he says; kichu — some; hāsiyā — smiling; vacana — words.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu offered His obeisances to Rāmacandra Purī, worshiping his feet. Then Rāmacandra Purī smiled and spoke to the Lord.
