Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.41

pratyaha prabhura bhikshā iti-uti haya

keha yadi mūlya āne, cāri-pana-nirnaya

SYNONYMS

prati-aha — daily; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā — alms; iti-uti — here and there; haya — is; keha — someone; yadi — if; mūlya āne — pays; cāri-pana — four times eighty small conchshells; nirnaya — as a fixed amount.

TRANSLATION

Every day the Lord would take His meal at a different place, and if someone was prepared to pay for a meal, the price was fixed at only four panas.
