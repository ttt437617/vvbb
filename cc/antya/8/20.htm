Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.20

rāmacandra-purī tabe upadeśe tāńre

śishya hañā guruke kahe, bhaya nāhi kare

SYNONYMS

rāmacandra-purī — Rāmacandra Purī; tabe — then; upadeśe tāńre — instructed him; śishya hañā — being a disciple; guruke kahe — said to his spiritual master; bhaya nāhi kare — without fear.

TRANSLATION

Then Rāmacandra Purī was so foolish that he fearlessly dared to instruct his spiritual master.
