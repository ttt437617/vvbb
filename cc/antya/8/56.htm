Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.56

rāmacandra-purīke sabāya deya tiraskāra

'ei pāpishtha āsi' prāna la-ila sabāra'

SYNONYMS

rāmacandra-purīke — unto Rāmacandra Purī; sabāya — all the devotees; deya tiraskāra — offer chastisement; ei pāpishtha — this sinful man; āsi' — coming; prāna — life; la-ila — has taken; sabāra — of everyone.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees condemned Rāmacandra Purī, saying, "This sinful man has come here and taken our lives."
