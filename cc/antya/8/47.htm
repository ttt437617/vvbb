Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.47

yata nindā kare tāhā prabhu saba jāne

tathāpi ādara kare bada-i sambhrame

SYNONYMS

yata — whatever; nindā — blasphemy; kare — does; tāhā — that; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; saba — all; jāne — knows; tathāpi — still; ādara kare — offers respect; bada-i sambhrame — with great attention.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu knew that Rāmacandra Purī was criticizing Him before everyone, but whenever Rāmacandra Purī came to see Him, the Lord offered him respects with great attention.
