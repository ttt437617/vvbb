Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.99

guru upekshā kaile, aiche phala haya

krame īśvara-paryanta aparādhe thekaya

SYNONYMS

guru upekshā kaile — if one's spiritual master rejects him; aiche — such; phala — result; haya — there is; krame — gradually; īśvara-paryanta — up to the point of the Personality of Godhead; aparādhe thekaya — commits offenses.

TRANSLATION

If one's spiritual master rejects him, one becomes so fallen that he, like Rāmacandra Purī, commits offenses even to the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
