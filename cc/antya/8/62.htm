Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.62

ei-rūpa mahā-duhkhe dina kata gela

śuni' rāmacandra-purī prabhu-pāśa āila

SYNONYMS

ei-rūpa — in this way; mahā-duhkhe — in great unhappiness; dina kata — some days; gela — passed; śuni' — hearing; rāmacandra-purī — Rāmacandra Purī; prabhu-pāśa āila — came to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

In this way, some days passed in great unhappiness. Hearing of all this, Rāmacandra Purī went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
