Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.76

ke kaiche vyavahāre, kebā kaiche khāya

ei anusandhāna teńho karaya sadāya

SYNONYMS

ke — who; kaiche — how; vyavahāre — behaves; kebā — who; kaiche — how; khāya — eats; ei anusandhāna — this inquiry; teńho — he; karaya — does; sadāya — always.

TRANSLATION

"It is Rāmacandra Purī's business to inquire always about how others are eating and conducting their daily affairs.
