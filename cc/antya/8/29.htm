Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.29

nirantara krishna-nāma karāya smarana

krishna-nāma, krishna-līlā śunāya anukshana

SYNONYMS

nirantara — always; krishna-nāma — the name of Lord Krishna; karāya smarana — was reminding; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; krishna-līlā — pastimes of Krishna; śunāya anukshana — was always causing to hear.

TRANSLATION

Īśvara Purī was always chanting the holy name and pastimes of Lord Krishna for Mādhavendra Purī to hear. In this way he helped Mādhavendra Purī remember the holy name and pastimes of Lord Krishna at the time of death.
