Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.103

śrī-rūpa-raghunātha-pade yāra āśa

caitanya-caritāmrita kahe krishnadāsa

SYNONYMS

śrī-rūpa — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; raghunātha — Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; pade — at the lotus feet; yāra — whose; āśa — expectation; caitanya-caritāmrita — the book named Caitanya-caritāmrita; kahe — describes; krishnadāsa — Śrīla Krishnadāsa Kavirāja Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Praying at the lotus feet of Śrī Rūpa and Śrī Raghunātha, always desiring their mercy, I, Krishnadāsa, narrate Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita, following in their footsteps.

Thus end the Bhaktivedanta purports to Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita, Antya-līlā, Eighth Chapter, describing how the Lord reduced His eating in fear of the criticism of Rāmacandra Purī.
