Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.28

īśvara-purī gosāñi kare śrīpāda-sevana

svahaste karena mala-mūtrādi mārjana

SYNONYMS

īśvara-purī — Īśvara Purī; gosāñi — Gosvāmī; kare — performs; śrīpāda-sevana — service to Mādhavendra Purī; sva-haste — with his own hand; karena — performs; mala-mūtra-ādi — stool, urine, and so on; mārjana — cleaning.

TRANSLATION

Īśvara Purī, the spiritual master of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, performed service to Mādhavendra Purī, cleaning up his stool and urine with his own hand.
