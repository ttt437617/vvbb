Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.23

'krishna nā pāinu, nā pāinu 'mathurā'

āpana-duhkhe maroń — ei dite āila jvālā

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; nā pāinu — I did not get; nā pāinu — did not get; mathurā — Mathurā; āpana-duhkhe — in my own unhappiness; maroń — I am dying; ei — this person; dite āila jvālā — has come to give more pain.

TRANSLATION

"O my Lord Krishna, I could not reach You, nor could I reach Your abode, Mathurā. I am dying in my unhappiness, and now this rascal has come to give me more pain.
