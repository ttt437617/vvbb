Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.100

yadyapi guru-buddhye prabhu tāra dosha nā la-ila

tāra phala-dvārā loke śikshā karāila

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; guru-buddhye — due to considering him a spiritual master; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāra — his; dosha — offense; nā la-ila — did not accept; tāra — his; phala — result; dvārā — by; loke — the general populace; śikshā karāila — He instructed.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not consider the offenses of Rāmacandra Purī, for the Lord considered him His spiritual master. However, his character instructed everyone about the result of offending the spiritual master.
