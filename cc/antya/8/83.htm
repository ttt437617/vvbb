Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.83

ińhāra vacane kene anna tyāga kara?

pūrvavat nimantrana māna', — sabāra bola dhara"

SYNONYMS

ińhāra vacane — by his words; kene — why; anna — food; tyāga kara — You give up; pūrva-vat — as before; nimantrana māna' — please accept the invitation; sabāra — of everyone; bola — the words; dhara — accept.

TRANSLATION

"Why have You given up proper eating due to the criticism of Rāmacandra Purī? Please accept invitations as before. This is the request of us all."
