Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.52

śuni' tāhā prabhura sańkoca-bhaya mane

govinde bolāñā kichu kahena vacane

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; tāhā — that; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya; sańkoca — doubt; bhaya — fear; mane — in the mind; govinde bolāñā — calling for Govinda; kichu — some; kahena — spoke; vacane — words.

TRANSLATION

After hearing this criticism, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was doubtful and apprehensive. Therefore He called Govinda and instructed him as follows.
