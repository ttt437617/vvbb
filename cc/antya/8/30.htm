Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.30

tushta hañā purī tāńre kailā ālińgana

vara dilā — 'krishne tomāra ha-uka prema-dhana'

SYNONYMS

tushta hañā — being pleased; purī — Mādhavendra Purī; tāńre — him; kailā ālińgana — embraced; vara dilā — gave the benediction; krishne — unto Krishna; tomāra — your; ha-uka — let there be; prema-dhana — the wealth of love.

TRANSLATION

Pleased with Īśvara Purī, Mādhavendra Purī embraced him and gave him the benediction that he would be a great devotee and lover of Krishna.
