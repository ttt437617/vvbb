Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.70

eta śuni' rāmacandra-purī uthi' gelā

bhakta-gana ardhāśana kare, — purī gosāñi śunilā

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; rāmacandra-purī — Rāmacandra Purī; uthi' gelā — got up and left; bhakta-gana — the devotees; ardha-aśana kare — were taking half the quantity of food; purī gosāñi — Rāmacandra Purī; śunilā — heard.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Rāmacandra Purī got up and left. He also heard from various sources that all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu were eating half as much as usual.
