Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.87

dui-pana kaudi lāge prabhura nimantrane

kabhu dui-jana bhoktā, kabhu tina-jane

SYNONYMS

dui-pana kaudi — two panas of kaudis (160 small conchshells); lāge — costs; prabhura nimantrane — to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kabhu — sometimes; dui-jana — two men; bhoktā — eating; kabhu — sometimes; tina-jane — three men.

TRANSLATION

The cost for the food needed to invite Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was fixed at two panas of kaudis [160 conchshells], and that food would be taken by two men and sometimes three.
