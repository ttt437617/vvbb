Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.14

āgraha kariyā punah punah khāoyāila

ācamana kaile nindā karite lāgila

SYNONYMS

āgraha kariyā — with eagerness; punah punah — again and again; khāoyāila — fed; ācamana kaile — when he had washed his hands and mouth; nindā karite lāgila — began to criticize.

TRANSLATION

Encouraging him again and again, Rāmacandra Purī fed him sumptuously, but when Jagadānanda had washed his hands and mouth, Rāmacandra Purī began criticizing him.
