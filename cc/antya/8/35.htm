Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.35

ei śloke krishna-prema kare upadeśa

krishnera virahe bhaktera bhāva-viśesha

SYNONYMS

ei śloke — in this verse; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; kare upadeśa — teaches; krishnera virahe — by feeling separation from Krishna; bhaktera — of the devotee; bhāva-viśesha — transcendental situation.

TRANSLATION

In this verse Mādhavendra Purī teaches how to achieve ecstatic love for Krishna. By feeling separation from Krishna, one becomes spiritually situated.
