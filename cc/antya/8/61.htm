Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.61

govinda-kāśīśvare prabhu kailā ājñāpana

'duńhe anyatra māgi' kara udara bharana'

SYNONYMS

govinda-kāśīśvare — unto Govinda and Kāśīśvara; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā — made; ājñāpana — order; duńhe — both of you; anyatra — elsewhere; māgi' — begging; kara udara bharana — fill your bellies.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ordered Govinda and Kāśīśvara, "You may both take alms elsewhere to fill your bellies."
