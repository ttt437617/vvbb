Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.94

kabhu rāmacandra-purīra haya bhritya-prāya

kabhu tāre nāhi māne, dekhe trina-prāya

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; rāmacandra-purīra — of Rāmacandra Purī; haya — was; bhritya-prāya — exactly like a servant; kabhu — sometimes; tāre — for him; nāhi māne — He did not care; dekhe — sees; trina-prāya — just like a straw.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sometimes accepted Rāmacandra Purī as His master and considered Himself a servant, and sometimes the Lord, not caring for him, would see him as being just like a straw.
