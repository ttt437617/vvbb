Antya-līlāChapter 8: Rāmacandra Purī Criticizes the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 8.1

tam vande krishna-caitanyam

rāmacandra-purī-bhayāt

laukikāhāratah svam yo

bhikshānnam samakocayat

SYNONYMS

tam — to Him; vande — I offer my respectful obeisances; krishna-caitanyam — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rāmacandra-purī-bhayāt — due to fear of Rāmacandra Purī; laukika — ordinary; āhāratah — from eating; svam — His own; yah — who; bhikshā-annam — quantity of food; samakocayat — reduced.

TRANSLATION

Let me offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who reduced His eating due to fear of the criticism of Rāmacandra Purī.
