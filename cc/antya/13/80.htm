Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.80

dūre gāna śuni' prabhura ha-ila āveśa

strī, purusha, ke gāya, — nā jāne viśesha

SYNONYMS

dūre — from a distant place; gāna — song; śuni' — hearing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ila — there was; āveśa — ecstatic emotion; strī — woman; purusha — man; ke gāya — who sings; nā jāne — could not understand; viśesha — particularly.

TRANSLATION

Hearing the song from a distance, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately became ecstatic. He did not know whether it was a man or a woman singing.
