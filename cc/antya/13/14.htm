Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.14

prabhu kahena, — "khāta eka ānaha pādite

jagadānanda cāhe āmāya vishaya bhuñjāite

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; khāta — bedstead; eka — one; ānaha — bring; pādite — to lie down; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; cāhe — wants; āmāya — Me; vishaya bhuñjāite — to cause to enjoy material happiness.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "You might as well bring a bedstead here for Me to lie on. Jagadānanda wants Me to enjoy material happiness.
