Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.7

sūkshma vastra āni' gaurika diyā rāńgāilā

śimulīra tūlā diyā tāhā pūrāilā

SYNONYMS

sūkshma vastra — fine cloth; āni' — bringing; gaurika — red oxide; diyā — with the help of; rāńgāilā — made reddish; śimulīra — of the śimula tree; tūlā — cotton; diyā — with; tāhā — that; pūrāilā — filled.

TRANSLATION

He acquired some fine cloth and colored it with red oxide. Then he filled it with cotton from a śimula tree.
