Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.53

"kāhāń pāilā tumi ei rātula vasana?"

'mukunda-sarasvatī' dila, — kahe sanātana

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — where; pāilā — did get; tumi — you; ei — this; rātula vasana — red cloth; mukunda-sarasvatī dila — Mukunda Sarasvatī gave; kahe sanātana — Sanātana replied.

TRANSLATION

"Where did you get that reddish cloth on your head?" Jagadānanda asked.

Sanātana Gosvāmī replied, "Mukunda Sarasvatī gave it to me."
