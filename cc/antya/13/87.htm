Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.87

prabhu kahe, — "govinda, mora sańge rahibā

yāhāń tāhāń mora rakshāya sāvadhāna ha-ibā"

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; govinda — My dear Govinda; mora sańge rahibā — you should always remain with Me; yāhāń tāhāń — anywhere and everywhere; mora — My; rakshāya — for protection; sāvadhāna ha-ibā — you should be very careful.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "My dear Govinda, you should stay with Me always. There is danger anywhere and everywhere; therefore you should protect Me very carefully."
