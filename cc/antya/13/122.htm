Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.122

eta bali' prabhu tāńre ālińgana kailā

prabhura kripāte krishna-preme matta hailā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — Raghunātha Bhatta; ālińgana kailā — embraced; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripāte — by the mercy; krishna-preme — in love of Krishna; matta hailā — became enlivened.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced Raghunātha Bhatta, and by the Lord's mercy Raghunātha was enlivened with ecstatic love for Krishna.
