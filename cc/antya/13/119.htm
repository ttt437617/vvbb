Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.119

pūrvavat ashta-māsa prabhu-pāśa chilā

ashta-māsa rahi' punah prabhu ājñā dilā

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as previously; ashta-māsa — for eight months; prabhu-pāśa chilā — remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ashta-māsa rahi' — after staying for eight months; punah — again; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā dilā — ordered him.

TRANSLATION

As previously, Raghunātha remained continuously with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for eight months. Then the Lord gave him the following order.
