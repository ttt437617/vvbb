Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.118

pitā-mātā kāśī pāile udāsīna hañā

punah prabhura thāñi āilā grihādi chādiyā

SYNONYMS

pitā-mātā — the father and mother; kāśī pāile — when they passed away at Kāśī (Vārānasī); udāsīna hañā — being indifferent; punah — again; prabhura thāñi — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — returned; griha-ādi chādiyā — leaving all relationships with home.

TRANSLATION

Then his parents died at Kāśī [Vārānasī], and he became detached. He therefore returned to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, giving up all relationships with his home.
