Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.107

raghunātha-bhatta — pāke ati sunipuna

yei rāndhe, sei haya amritera sama

SYNONYMS

raghunātha-bhatta — Raghunātha Bhatta; pāke — in cooking; ati su-nipuna — very expert; yei rāndhe — whatever he cooked; sei — that; haya — is; amritera sama — like nectar.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha Bhatta was an expert cook. Whatever he prepared tasted just like nectar.
