Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.20

tāte śayana karena prabhu, — dekhi' sabe sukhī

jagadānanda — bhitare krodha bāhire mahā-duhkhī

SYNONYMS

tāte — on that; śayana karena — lies down; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhi' — seeing; sabe sukhī — everyone became happy; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; bhitare — within his mind; krodha — angry; bāhire — externally; mahā-duhkhī — very unhappy.

TRANSLATION

Everyone was happy to see the Lord lie down on that bed, but Jagadānanda was inwardly angry, and externally he appeared very unhappy.
