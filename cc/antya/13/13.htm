Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.13

svarūpa kahe, — 'tomāra icchā, ki kahite pāri?

śayyā upekshile pandita duhkha pābe bhārī'

SYNONYMS

svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī said; tomāra icchā — Your will; ki — who; kahite pāri — can say; śayyā upekshile — if You do not accept the bedding; pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; duhkha — unhappiness; pābe — will feel; bhārī — great.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara said to the Lord, "I cannot contradict Your supreme will, my Lord, but if You do not accept the bedding, Jagadānanda Pandita will feel great unhappiness."
