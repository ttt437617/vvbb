Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.29

sahajei mora tāhāń yāite mana haya

prabhu-ājñā lañā deha', kariye vinaya"

SYNONYMS

sahejei — naturally; mora — my; tāhāń — there; yāite — to go; mana — mind; haya — is; prabhu-ājñā — permission from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā deha' — kindly get; kariye vinaya — humbly requesting.

TRANSLATION

"Naturally I have a desire to go to Vrindāvana; therefore please humbly request Him to grant His permission."
