Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.38

sanātana-sańge kariha vana daraśana

sanātanera sańga nā chādibā eka-kshana

SYNONYMS

sanātana-sańge — with Sanātana Gosvāmī; kariha — do; vana daraśana — visiting the twelve forests; sanātanera — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; sańga — association; nā chādibā — do not leave; eka-kshana — even for a moment.

TRANSLATION

"Visit all twelve forests of Vrindāvana in the company of Sanātana Gosvāmī. Do not leave his association for even a moment.
