Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.46

sanātanera gophāte duńhe rahe eka-thāñi

pandita pāka karena devālaye yāi'

SYNONYMS

sanātanera gophāte — in the cave where Sanātana Gosvāmī stayed; duńhe — both; rahe — stay; eka-thāñi — in one place; pandita — Jagadānanda; pāka karena — cooks; devālaye yāi' — going to a temple.

TRANSLATION

They stayed in Sanātana Gosvāmī's cave, but Jagadānanda Pandita would go to a nearby temple and cook for himself.
