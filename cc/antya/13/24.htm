Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.24

jagadānanda kahe prabhura dhariyā carana

"pūrva haite icchā mora yāite vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; kahe — said; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhariyā carana — grasping the lotus feet; pūrva haite — for a very long time; icchā — desire; mora — my; yāite vrindāvana — to go to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Grasping the Lord's feet, Jagadānanda Pandita then said, "For a long time I have desired to go to Vrindāvana.
