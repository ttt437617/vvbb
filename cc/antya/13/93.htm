Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.93

ashta-prahara rāma-nāma japena rātri-dine

sarva tyaji' calilā jagannātha-daraśane

SYNONYMS

ashta-prahara — twenty-four hours a day; rāma-nāma — the holy name of Lord Rāma; japena — chants; rātri-dine — day and night; sarva — all; tyaji' — giving up; calilā — went; jagannātha-daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

Rāmadāsa had renounced everything and was going to see Lord Jagannātha. While traveling, he chanted the holy name of Lord Rāma twenty-four hours a day.
