Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.32

āire dekhite yaiche gauda-deśe yāya

taiche eka-bāra vrindāvana dekhi' āya"

SYNONYMS

āire — mother Śacī; dekhite — to see; yaiche — as; gauda-deśe — to Bengal; yāya — he went; taiche — similarly; eka-bāra — once; vrindāvana dekhi' — after seeing Vrindāvana; āya — he can come back.

TRANSLATION

"You permitted him to go see mother Śacī in Bengal, and You may similarly permit him to go see Vrindāvana and then return here."
