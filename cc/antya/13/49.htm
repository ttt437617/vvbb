Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.49

eka-dina sanātane pandita nimantrilā

nitya-kritya kari' teńha pāka cadāilā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; sanātane — Sanātana Gosvāmī; pandita nimantrilā — Jagadānanda Pandita invited; nitya-kritya kari' — after finishing his routine duties; teńha — he; pāka cadāilā — began to cook.

TRANSLATION

One day Jagadānanda Pandita, having invited Sanātana to the nearby temple for lunch, finished his routine duties and began to cook.
