Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.45

sanātana karāilā tāńre dvādaśa vana daraśana

gokule rahilā duńhe dekhi' mahāvana

SYNONYMS

sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; karāilā — made; tāńre — him; dvādaśa — twelve; vana — forests; daraśana — visiting; gokule — at Gokula; rahilā — remained; duńhe — both; dekhi' — after seeing; mahā-vana — Mahāvana.

TRANSLATION

After Sanātana Gosvāmī had taken Jagadānanda to see all twelve forests of Vrindāvana, concluding with Mahāvana, the two of them remained in Gokula.
