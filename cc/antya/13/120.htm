Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.120

"āmāra ājñāya, raghunātha, yāha vrindāvane

tāhāń yāñā raha rūpa-sanātana-sthāne

SYNONYMS

āmāra ājñāya — upon My order; raghunātha — My dear Raghunātha; yāha vrindāvane — go to Vrindāvana; tāhāń yāñā — going there; raha — remain; rūpa-sanātana-sthāne — in the care of Rūpa Gosvāmī and Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Raghunātha, on My order go to Vrindāvana and live there under the care of Rūpa and Sanātana Gosvāmīs.
