Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.16

svarūpa-gosāñi āsi' pandite kahilā

śuni' jagadānanda mane mahā-duhkha pāilā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; āsi' — returning; pandite kahilā — told Jagadānanda Pandita; śuni' — hearing; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; mane — within the mind; mahā-duhkha pāilā — felt great unhappiness.

TRANSLATION

When Svarūpa Dāmodara returned and related all these incidents, Jagadānanda Pandita felt very unhappy.
