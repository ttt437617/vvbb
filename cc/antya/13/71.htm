Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.71

śīghra cali' nīlācale gelā jagadānanda

bhakta saha gosāñi hailā parama ānanda

SYNONYMS

śīghra — very quickly; cali' — going; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; gelā — arrived; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; bhakta saha — with His devotees; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hailā — became; parama ānanda — very happy.

TRANSLATION

Meanwhile, traveling very quickly, Jagadānanda Pandita soon arrived in Jagannātha Purī, much to the joy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees.
