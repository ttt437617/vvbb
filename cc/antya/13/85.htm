Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.85

prabhu kahe, — "govinda, āji rākhilā jīvana

strī-paraśa haile āmāra ha-ita marana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; govinda — My dear Govinda; āji — today; rākhilā jīvana — you saved My life; strī-paraśa haile — if I had touched a woman; āmāra — My; ha-ita — there would have been; marana — death.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Govinda," He said, "you have saved My life. If I had touched the body of a woman, I would certainly have died.
