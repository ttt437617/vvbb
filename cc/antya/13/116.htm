Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.116

svarūpa-ādi bhakta-thāñi ājñā māgiyā

vārānasī āilā bhatta prabhura ājñā pāñā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-ādi — headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; bhakta-thāñi — from the devotees; ājñā māgiyā — asking permission; vārānasī āilā — returned to Vārānasī; bhatta — Raghunātha Bhatta; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā pāñā — getting permission.

TRANSLATION

After taking permission from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara, Raghunātha Bhatta returned to Vārānasī.
