Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.19

ei-mata dui kailā odana-pādane

ańgīkāra kailā prabhu aneka yatane

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; dui — two pieces; kailā — made; odana-pādane — one for bedding, one for the pillow; ańgīkāra kailā — did accept; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; aneka yatane — after much endeavor.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Svarūpa Dāmodara made some bedding and a pillow, and after much endeavor by the devotees, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted them.
