Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.17

svarūpa-gosāñi tabe srijilā prakāra

kadalīra śushka-patra ānilā apāra

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; tabe — thereafter; srijilā prakāra — devised a means; kadalīra — of banana; śushka-patra — dry leaves; ānilā — brought; apāra — in great quantity.

TRANSLATION

Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī devised another method. First he secured a large quantity of dry banana leaves.
