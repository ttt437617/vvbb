Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.111

rāmadāsa kailā tabe nīlācale vāsa

pattanāyaka-goshthīke padāya 'kāvya-prakāśa'

SYNONYMS

rāmadāsa — Rāmadāsa Viśvāsa; kailā — did; tabe — then; nīlācale vāsa — residence at Jagannātha Purī; pattanāyaka-goshthīke — to the Pattanāyaka family (the descendants of Bhavānanda Rāya); padāya — teaches; kāvya-prakāśa — on the book Kāvya-prakāśa.

TRANSLATION

Rāmadāsa Viśvāsa then took up residence in Jagannātha Purī and taught the Kāvya-prakāśa to the Pattanāyaka family [the descendants of Bhavānanda Rāya].
