Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.48

sanātana panditera kare samādhāna

mahāvane dena āni' māgi' anna-pāna

SYNONYMS

sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; panditera — of Jagadānanda Pandita; kare samādhāna — gave all kinds of service; mahā-vane — at Mahāvana; dena — gives; āni' — bringing; māgi' — by begging; anna-pāna — food and drink.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī attended to all of Jagadānanda Pandita's needs. He begged in the area of Mahāvana and brought Jagadānanda all kinds of things to eat and drink.
