Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.68

jagadānanda-pandita calilā saba lañā

vyākula hailā sanātana tāńre vidāya diyā

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda-pandita — Jagadānanda Pandita; calilā — proceeded; saba — all; lañā — taking; vyākula hailā — became very much agitated; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; tāńre — to him; vidāya diyā — bidding farewell.

TRANSLATION

Thus Jagadānanda Pandita, bearing all these gifts, started on his journey. Sanātana Gosvāmī, however, was very much agitated after bidding him farewell.
