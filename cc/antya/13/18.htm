Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.18

nakhe ciri' ciri' tāhā ati sūkshma kailā

prabhura bahirvāsa duite se saba bharilā

SYNONYMS

nakhe — with the nails; ciri' ciri' — tearing and tearing; tāhā — them; ati — very; sūkshma — fine; kailā — made; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bahirvāsa — covering cloths; duite — in two; se saba — all those; bharilā — filled.

TRANSLATION

He then tore the leaves into very fine fibers with his nails and filled two of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's outer garments with the fibers.
