Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.135

mahāprabhura kripāya krishna-prema anargala

ei ta' kahiluń tāte caitanya-kripā-phala

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura kripāya — by the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-prema anargala — incessantly overwhelmed with ecstatic love of Krishna; ei ta' — thus; kahiluń — I have described; tāte — thereby; caitanya-kripā-phala — the result of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mercy.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described the powerful mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, by which Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī remained constantly overwhelmed with ecstatic love for Krishna.
