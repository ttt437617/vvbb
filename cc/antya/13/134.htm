Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.134

mahāprabhura datta mālā mananera kāle

prasāda-kadāra saha bāndhi lena gale

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura — by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; datta — given; mālā — tulasī garland; mananera — of remembering; kāle — at the time; prasāda-kadāra — the remnants of Lord Jagannātha; saha — with; bāndhi — binding together; lena — takes; gale — on his neck.

TRANSLATION

When Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī was absorbed in remembrance of Lord Krishna, he would take the tulasī garland and the prasādam of Lord Jagannātha given to him by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, bind them together and wear them on his neck.
