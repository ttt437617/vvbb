Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.6

dekhi' saba bhakta-gana mahā-duhkha pāya

sahite nāre jagadānanda, srijilā upāya

SYNONYMS

dekhi' — seeing; saba bhakta-gana — all the devotees; mahā-duhkha — great unhappiness; pāya — get; sahite — to tolerate; nāre — was unable; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; srijilā upāya — devised a means.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees felt very unhappy to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in pain. Indeed, they could not tolerate it. Then Jagadānanda Pandita devised a remedy.
