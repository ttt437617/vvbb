Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.10

śayanera kāle svarūpa tāhāńi rahilā

tūlī-bālisa dekhi' prabhu krodhāvishta ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

śayanera kāle — at bedtime; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; tāhāńi rahilā — remained there; tūlī — quilt; bālisa — pillow; dekhi' — seeing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krodha-āvishta ha-ilā — became very angry.

TRANSLATION

When it was time for the Lord to go to bed, Svarūpa Dāmodara stayed nearby, but when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw the quilt and pillow, He was immediately very angry.
