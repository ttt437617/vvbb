Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.82

ańge kāńtā lāgila, kichu nā jānilā!

āste-vyaste govinda tāńra pāchete dhāilā

SYNONYMS

ańge — on the body; kāńtā — thorns; lāgila — touched; kichu — anything; nā jānilā — did not perceive; āste-vyaste — very hastily; govinda — His personal servant; tāńra — Him; pāchete — behind; dhāilā — ran.

TRANSLATION

Govinda ran very quickly behind the Lord, who did not feel any pain from the pricking of the thorns.
