Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.97

rāmadāsa kahe, — "āmi śūdra adhama!

'brāhmanera sevā', — ei mora nija-dharma

SYNONYMS

rāmadāsa kahe — Rāmadāsa said; āmi — I; śūdra — a śūdra; adhama — most fallen; brāhmanera sevā — to serve a brāhmana; ei — this; mora nija-dharma — my own religious duty.

TRANSLATION

Rāmadāsa replied, "I am a śūdra, a fallen soul. To serve a brāhmana is my duty and religious principle.
