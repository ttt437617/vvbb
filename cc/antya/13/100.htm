Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.100

ei-mate raghunātha āilā nīlācale

prabhura carane yāñā mililā kutūhale

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; raghunātha — Raghunātha Bhatta; āilā — came; nīlācale — to Jagannātha Purī; prabhura carane — to the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yāñā — going; mililā — met; kutūhale — in great delight.

TRANSLATION

Traveling in this way, Raghunātha Bhatta soon arrived at Jagannātha Purī. There he met Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with great delight and fell at His lotus feet.
