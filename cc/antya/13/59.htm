Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.59

aiche caitanya-nishthā yogya tomāte

tumi nā dekhāile ihā śikhiba ke-mate?

SYNONYMS

aiche — such; caitanya-nishthā — faith in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yogya — just befitting; tomāte — in you; tumi nā dekhāile — if you do not show; ihā — this; śikhiba — I shall learn; ke-mate — how.

TRANSLATION

"This faith in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu quite befits you. Unless you demonstrate it, how could I learn such faith?
