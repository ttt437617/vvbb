Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.104

govindere kahi' eka vāsā deoyāilā

svarūpādi bhakta-gana-sane milāilā

SYNONYMS

govindere — to Govinda; kahi' — speaking; eka — one; vāsā — residential place; deoyāilā — caused to be given; svarūpa-ādi — headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; bhakta-gana-sane — with the devotees; milāilā — introduced.

TRANSLATION

The Lord asked Govinda to arrange for Raghunātha Bhatta's accommodations and then introduced him to all the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī.
