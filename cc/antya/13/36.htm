Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.36

mathurā gele sanātana-sańgei rahibā

mathurāra svāmī sabera carana vandibā

SYNONYMS

mathurā gele — when you go to Mathurā; sanātana-sańgei — in the association of Sanātana Gosvāmī; rahibā — remain; mathurāra svāmī — the leading men of Mathurā; sabera — of all; carana vandibā — worship the feet.

TRANSLATION

"When you reach Mathurā, you should remain with Sanātana Gosvāmī and offer respectful obeisances to the feet of all the leading men there.
