Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.3

hena-mate mahāprabhu jagadānanda-sańge

nānā-mate āsvādaya premera tarańge

SYNONYMS

hena-mate — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jagadānanda-sańge — in the company of Jagadānanda Pandita; nānā-mate — in various ways; āsvādaya — tastes; premera tarańge — the waves of spiritual loving affairs.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would taste various transcendental relationships of pure love in the company of Jagadānanda Pandita.
