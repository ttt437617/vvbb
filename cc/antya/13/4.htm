Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.4

krishna-vicchede duhkhe kshīna mana-kāya

bhāvāveśe prabhu kabhu praphullita haya

SYNONYMS

krishna-vicchede — because of separation from Krishna; duhkhe — in unhappiness; kshīna — thin; mana-kāya — mind and body; bhāva-āveśe — by ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kabhu — sometimes; praphullita haya — becomes healthy and developed.

TRANSLATION

The unhappiness of separation from Krishna exhausted the Lord's mind and reduced the structure of His body, but when He felt emotions of ecstatic love, He again became developed and healthy.
