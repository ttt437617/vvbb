Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.131

nija śishye kahi' govindera mandira karāilā

vamśī, makara kundalādi 'bhūshana' kari' dilā

SYNONYMS

nija śishye — to his own disciples; kahi' — by speaking; govindera — of Lord Govinda; mandira karāilā — constructed a temple; vamśī — flute; makara kundala-ādi — earrings shaped like sharks, etc.; bhūshana — ornaments; kari' — preparing; dilā — gave.

TRANSLATION

Subsequently Raghunātha Bhatta ordered his disciples to construct a temple for Govinda. He prepared various ornaments for Govinda, including a flute and shark-shaped earrings.
