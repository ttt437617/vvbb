Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.96

"tumi bada loka, pandita, mahā-bhāgavate

sevā nā kariha, sukhe cala mora sāthe"

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; bada loka — a great personality; pandita — a learned scholar; mahā-bhāgavate — a great devotee; sevā nā kariha — please do not serve; sukhe — happily; cala — go; mora sāthe — with me.

TRANSLATION

"You are a respectable gentleman, a learned scholar and a great devotee," Raghunātha Bhatta said. "Please do not try to serve me. Just come with me in a happy mood."
