Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.108

parama santoshe prabhu karena bhojana

prabhura avaśishta-pātra bhattera bhakshana

SYNONYMS

parama santoshe — in great satisfaction; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena bhojana — eats; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avaśishta-pātra — the plate of remnants; bhattera — of Raghunātha Bhatta; bhakshana — the eatables.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would accept with great satisfaction all the food he prepared. After the Lord was satisfied, Raghunātha Bhatta would eat His remnants.
