Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.40

āmiha āsitechi, — kahiha sanātane

āmāra tare eka-sthāna yena kare vrindāvane"

SYNONYMS

āmiha — I also; āsitechi — am coming; kahiha sanātane — inform Sanātana Gosvāmī; āmāra tare — for Me; eka-sthāna — one place; yena — so; kare — he may make; vrindāvane — at Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"Inform Sanātana Gosvāmī that I am coming to Vrindāvana for a second time and that he should therefore arrange a place for Me to stay."
