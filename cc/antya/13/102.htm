Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.102

miśra āra śekharera dandavat jānāilā

mahāprabhu tāń-sabāra vārtā puchilā

SYNONYMS

miśra — of Tapana Miśra; āra — and; śekharera — of Candraśekhara; dandavat — obeisances; jānāilā — he informed; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; vārtā — news; puchilā — inquired.

TRANSLATION

Raghunātha offered respectful obeisances to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu on behalf of Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara, and the Lord also inquired about them.
