Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.117

cāri-vatsara ghare pitā-mātāra sevā kailā

vaishnava-pandita-thāñi bhāgavata padilā

SYNONYMS

cāri-vatsara — for four years; ghare — at home; pitā-mātāra — of the father and mother; sevā kailā — rendered service; vaishnava-pandita-thāñi — from a self-realized, advanced Vaishnava; bhāgavata padilā — he studied Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.

TRANSLATION

In accordance with the instructions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he continuously rendered service to his mother and father for four years. He also regularly studied Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam from a self-realized Vaishnava.
