Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.43

tapana-miśra, candraśekhara, — dońhāre mililā

tāńra thāñi prabhura kathā sakala-i śunilā

SYNONYMS

tapana-miśra — Tapana Miśra; candraśekhara — Candraśekhara; dońhāre mililā — he met both; tāńra thāñi — from him; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kathā — topics; sakala-i — all; śunilā — they heard.

TRANSLATION

When he met Tapana Miśra and Candraśekhara in Vārānasī, they heard from him about topics concerning Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
