Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.11

govindere puchena, — 'ihā karāila kon jana?'

jagadānandera nāma śuni' sańkoca haila mana

SYNONYMS

govindere puchena — He inquired from Govinda; ihā — this; karāila — has made; kon jana — what person; jagadānandera — of Jagadānanda Pandita; nāma — name; śuni' — hearing; sańkoca — afraid; haila — was; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

The Lord inquired from Govinda, "Who has made this?" When Govinda named Jagadānanda Pandita, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was somewhat fearful.
