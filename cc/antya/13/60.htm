Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.60

yāhā dekhibāre vastra mastake bāndhila

sei apūrva prema ei pratyaksha dekhila

SYNONYMS

yāhā — which; dekhibāre — to see; vastra — the cloth; mastake bāndhila — I bound on my head; sei — that; apūrva prema — uncommon love; ei — this; pratyaksha — by direct experience; dekhila — I have seen.

TRANSLATION

"My purpose in binding my head with the cloth has now been fulfilled because I have personally seen your uncommon love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
