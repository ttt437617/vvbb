Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.42

saba bhakta-gana-thāñi ājñā māgilā

vana-pathe cali' cali' vārānasī āilā

SYNONYMS

saba bhakta-gana-thāñi — from all the devotees; ājñā māgilā — asked permission; vana-pathe cali' cali' — traversing the forest path; vārānasī āilā — he reached Vārānasī.

TRANSLATION

He took permission from all the devotees and then departed. Traveling on the forest path, he soon reached Vārānasī.
