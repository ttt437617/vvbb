Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.22

bhitarera krodha-duhkha prakāśa nā kaila

mathurā yāite prabhu-sthāne ājñā māgila

SYNONYMS

bhitarera — internal; krodha-duhkha — anger and unhappiness; prakāśa nā kaila — did not disclose; mathurā yāite — to go to Mathurā; prabhu-sthāne — from Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā māgila — asked for permission.

TRANSLATION

Now, concealing his anger and unhappiness, Jagadānanda Pandita again asked Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu for permission to go to Mathurā.
