Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.127

aśru, kampa, gadgada prabhura kripāte

netra kantha rodhe bāshpa, nā pāre padite

SYNONYMS

aśru — tears; kampa — trembling; gadgada — faltering of the voice; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripāte — by the mercy; netra — eyes; kantha — neck; rodhe — choked up; bāshpa — tears; nā pāre padite — could not recite.

TRANSLATION

By the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he experienced the symptoms of ecstatic love — tears, trembling and faltering of the voice. His eyes filled with tears and his throat became choked, and thus he could not recite Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
