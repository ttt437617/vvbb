Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.50

'mukunda sarasvatī' nāma sannyāsī mahājane

eka bahirvāsa teńho dila sanātane

SYNONYMS

mukunda sarasvatī — Mukunda Sarasvatī; nāma — named; sannyāsī — a sannyāsī; mahā-jane — a great personality; eka — one; bahirvāsa — outward covering; teńho — he; dila — gave; sanātane — to Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Previously, a great sannyāsī named Mukunda Sarasvatī had given Sanātana Gosvāmī an outer garment.
