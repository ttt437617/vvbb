Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.98

sańkoca nā kara tumi, āmi — tomāra 'dāsa'

tomāra sevā karile haya hridaye ullāsa"

SYNONYMS

sańkoca — hesitation; nā — not; kara — do; tumi — you; āmi — I; tomāra — your; dāsa — servant; tomāra — your; sevā — service; karile — by rendering; haya — there is; hridaye — within the heart; ullāsa — jubilation.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore please do not be hesitant. I am your servant, and when I serve you my heart becomes jubilant."
