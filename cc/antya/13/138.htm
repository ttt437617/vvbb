Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.138

ye ei-sakala kathā śune śraddhā kari'

tāńre krishna-prema-dhana dena gaurahari

SYNONYMS

ye — one who; ei-sakala — all these; kathā — topics; śune — hears; śraddhā kari' — with faith and love; tāńre — unto him; krishna-prema-dhana — the wealth of ecstatic love for Lord Krishna; dena — delivers; gaurahari — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu [Gaurahari] bestows ecstatic love for Krishna upon anyone who hears all these topics with faith and love.
