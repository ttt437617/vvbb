Antya-līlāChapter 13: Pastimes with Jagadānanda Pandita and Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 13.21

pūrve jagadānandera icchā vrindāvana yāite

prabhu ājñā nā dena tāńre, nā pāre calite

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; jagadānandera — of Jagadānanda Pandita; icchā — desire; vrindāvana yāite — to go to Vrindāvana; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā — permission; nā dena — did not give; tāńre — to him; nā pāre calite — he could not go.

TRANSLATION

Formerly, when Jagadānanda Pandita had desired to go to Vrindāvana, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had not given His permission, and therefore he could not go.
