Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.132

"veśyā hañā muñi pāpa kariyāchoń apāra

kripā kari' kara mo-adhame nistāra"

SYNONYMS

veśyā hañā — being a prostitute; muñi — I; pāpa — sinful activities; kariyāchoń — have done; apāra — unlimited; kripā kari' — being merciful; kara — please do; mo-adhame — unto me, the most fallen; nistāra — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"Because I have taken the profession of a prostitute," she said, "I have performed unlimited sinful acts. My lord, be merciful to me. Deliver my fallen soul."
