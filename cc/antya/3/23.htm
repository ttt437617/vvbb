Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.23

tomā sama 'nirapeksha' nāhi mora gane

'nirapeksha' nahile 'dharma' nā yāya rakshane

SYNONYMS

tomā sama — like you; nirapeksha — neutral; nāhi — there is not; mora gane — among My associates; nirapeksha — neutral; nahile — without being; dharma — religious principles; nā yāya rakshane — cannot be protected.

TRANSLATION

"You are the most neutral among My associates. This is very good, for without being neutral one cannot protect religious principles.
