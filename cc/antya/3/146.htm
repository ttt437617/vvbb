Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.146

sahajei avaishnava rāmacandra-khāńna

haridāsera aparādhe haila asura-samāna

SYNONYMS

sahajei — naturally; avaishnava — nondevotee; rāmacandra-khāńna — Rāmacandra Khān; haridāsera — at the lotus feet of Haridāsa; aparādhe — by offenses; haila — was; asura-samāna — exactly like a demon.

TRANSLATION

Rāmacandra Khān was naturally a nondevotee. Now, having offended the lotus feet of Haridāsa Thākura, he became just like a demoniac atheist.
