Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.203

śuni' haridāsa tabe uthiyā calilā

majumadāra sei vipre tyāga karilā

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; tabe — then; uthiyā calilā — got up and began to go away; majumadāra — Hiranya and Govardhana Majumadāra; sei vipre — this brāhmana; tyāga karilā — kicked out.

TRANSLATION

Then Haridāsa Thākura got up to leave, and the Majumadāras, the masters of Gopāla Cakravartī, immediately kicked him out and dismissed him from their service.
