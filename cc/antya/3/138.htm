Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.138

eta bali' tāre 'nāma' upadeśa kari'

uthiyā calilā thākura bali' 'hari' 'hari'

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; tāre — her; nāma upadeśa kari' — instructing about the process of chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; uthiyā — standing up; calilā — left; thākura — Haridāsa Thākura; bali' — chanting; hari hari — the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.

TRANSLATION

After thus instructing the prostitute about the process of chanting the Hare Krishna mantra, Haridāsa Thākura stood up and left, continuously chanting "Hari, Hari."
