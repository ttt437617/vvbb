Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.75

jagat nistārite ei tomāra avatāra

bhakta-bhāva āge tāte kailā ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

jagat nistārite — to deliver the whole world; ei — this; tomāra avatāra — Your incarnation; bhakta-bhāva — the mood of a devotee; āge — previously; tāte — therefore; kailā ańgīkāra — You accepted.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You have accepted the form of a devotee just to deliver all the fallen souls of this world.
