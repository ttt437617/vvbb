Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.81

pūrve yena raghunātha saba ayodhyā lañā

vaikunthake gelā, anya-jīve ayodhyā bharāñā

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yena — as; raghunātha — Rāmacandra; saba — all; ayodhyā — the population of Ayodhyā; lañā — taking with Him; vaikunthake gelā — went back to Vaikunthaloka; anya-jīve — other living entities; ayodhyā — Ayodhyā; bharāñā — filling.

TRANSLATION

"Previously, when Lord Rāmacandra left this world, He took with Him all the living entities of Ayodhyā. Then He filled Ayodhyā again with other living entities.
