Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.112

"thākura, tumi — parama-sundara, prathama yauvana

tomā dekhi' kon nārī dharite pāre mana?

SYNONYMS

thākura — O great devotee ācārya; tumi — you; parama-sundara — very beautifully constructed; prathama yauvana — the beginning of youth; tomā dekhi' — seeing you; kon nārī — what woman; dharite pāre — can control; mana — her mind.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Thākura, O great preacher, great devotee, you are so beautifully built, and your youth is just beginning. Who is the woman who could control her mind after seeing you?
