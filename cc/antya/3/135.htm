Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.135

veśyā kahe, — "kripā kari' karaha upadeśa

ki mora kartavya, yāte yāya bhava-kleśa"

SYNONYMS

veśyā kahe — the prostitute said; kripā kari' — being merciful; karaha upadeśa — please give instructions; ki — what; mora kartavya — my duty; yāte — by which; yāya — go away; bhava-kleśa — all material tribulations.

TRANSLATION

The prostitute said, "Kindly act as my spiritual master. Instruct me in my duty, by which I can get relief from material existence."
