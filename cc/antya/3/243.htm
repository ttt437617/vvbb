Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.243

eta bali' karena teńho nāma-sańkīrtana

sei nārī vasi' kare śrī-nāma-śravana

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; karena — performs; teńho — he; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy name; sei nārī — that woman; vasi' — sitting; kare — does; śrī-nāma-śravana — hearing the holy name.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Haridāsa Thākura continued to chant the holy name of the Lord. Thus the woman sitting before him began to hear the chanting of the holy name.
