Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.111

ańga ughādiyā dekhāi vasilā duyāre

kahite lāgilā kichu sumadhura svare

SYNONYMS

ańga ughādiyā — exposing part of her body; dekhāi — visible; vasilā — sat down; duyāre — on the threshold of the door; kahite lāgilā — began to speak; kichu — something; su-madhura svare — in very sweet language.

TRANSLATION

Exposing part of her body to his view, she sat down on the threshold of the door and spoke to him in very sweet words.
