Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.240

"sańkhyā-nāma-sańkīrtana — ei 'mahā-yajña' manye

tāhāte dīkshita āmi ha-i prati-dine

SYNONYMS

sańkhyā-nāma-sańkīrtana — numerical chanting of the holy name; ei — this; mahā-yajña — great sacrifice; manye — I have vowed; tāhāte dīkshita — initiated in that; āmi — I; ha-i — am; prati-dine — every day.

TRANSLATION

"I have been initiated into a vow to perform a great sacrifice by chanting the holy name a certain number of times every day.
