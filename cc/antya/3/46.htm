Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.46

ei-ta kahila dāmodarera vākya-danda

yāhāra śravane bhāge 'ajñāna pāshanda'

SYNONYMS

ei-ta — in this way; kahila — I have described; dāmodarera — of Dāmodara Pandita; vākya-danda — chastisement by words; yāhāra śravane — by hearing which; bhāge — goes away; ajñāna pāshanda — the atheist of ignorance.

TRANSLATION

In this way I have described Dāmodara Pandita's verbal chastisements. As one hears about this, atheistic principles and ignorance depart.
