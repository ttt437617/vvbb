Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.270

sei saba līlā kahi sańkshepa kariyā

caitanya-kripāte likhi kshudra-jīva hañā

SYNONYMS

sei saba — all those; līlā — pastimes; kahi — I say; sańkshepa kariyā — in brief; caitanya-kripāte — by the mercy of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; likhi — I write; kshudra-jīva hañā — being a very insignificant living entity.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly described those pastimes. Whatever I have written is by the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, since I am an insignificant living being.
