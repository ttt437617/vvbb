Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.83

pūrve yena vraje krishna kari' avatāra

sakala brahmānda-jīvera khandāilā samsāra

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yena — as; vraje — in Vrindāvana; krishna — Lord Krishna; kari' avatāra — descending as an incarnation; sakala — all; brahmānda-jīvera — of living entities within this universe; khandāilā — destroyed; samsāra — the material existence.

TRANSLATION

"Formerly, when Lord Krishna descended in Vrindāvana, He freed all living entities in the universe from material existence in the same way.
