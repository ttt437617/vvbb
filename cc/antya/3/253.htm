Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.252-253

mahā-bhāgavata tumi, — tomāra darśane

tomāra krishna-nāma-kīrtana-śravane

citta śuddha haila, cāhe krishna-nāma laite

krishna-nāma upadeśi' kripā kara mote

SYNONYMS

mahā-bhāgavata — the foremost devotee; tumi — you; tomāra darśane — by seeing you; tomāra — your; krishna-nāma — of the holy name of Krishna; kīrtana — chanting; śravane — by hearing; citta — consciousness; śuddha haila — became purified; cāhe — wants; krishna-nāma laite — to chant the holy name of Lord Krishna; krishna-nāma upadeśi' — instructing about chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; kripā kara — show mercy; mote — unto me.

TRANSLATION

"My dear sir, you are the foremost devotee. Simply seeing you and hearing you chant the holy name of Krishna has purified my consciousness. Now I want to chant the holy name of the Lord. Please be kind to me by instructing me about the ecstasy of chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.
