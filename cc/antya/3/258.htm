Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.258

krishna-nāma deha' tumi more kara dhanyā

āmāre bhāsāya yaiche ei prema-vanyā

SYNONYMS

krishna nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; deha' — please give; tumi — you; more — me; kara dhanyā — make fortunate; āmāre — me; bhāsāya — may cause to float; yaiche — so that; ei — this; prema-vanyā — inundation of ecstatic love of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Please give me the holy name of Krishna and thus make me fortunate, so that I also may float in the flood of love of Godhead inaugurated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."
