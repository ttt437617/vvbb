Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.120

'kāli duhkha pāilā, aparādha nā la-ibā mora

avaśya karimu āmi tomāya ańgīkāra

SYNONYMS

kāli — yesterday; duhkha pāilā — you were disappointed; aparādha — offense; nā la-ibā — please do not take; mora — my; avaśya — certainly; karimu — shall do; āmi — I; tomāya — unto you; ańgīkāra — acceptance.

TRANSLATION

"Last night you were disappointed. Please excuse my offense. I shall certainly accept you.
