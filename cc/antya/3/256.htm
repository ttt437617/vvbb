Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.256

pūrve āmi rāma-nāma pāñāchi 'śiva' haite

tomāra sańge lobha haila krishna-nāma laite

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; āmi — I; rāma-nāma — the holy name of Lord Rāma; pāñāchi — got; śiva haite — from Lord Śiva; tomāra sańge — by your association; lobha haila — I became greedy; krishna-nāma laite — to chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra.

TRANSLATION

"Formerly I received the holy name of Lord Rāma from Lord Śiva, but now, due to your association, I am greatly eager to chant the holy name of Lord Krishna.
