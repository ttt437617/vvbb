Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.86

taiche tumi navadvīpe kari' avatāra

sakala-brahmānda-jīvera karilā nistāra

SYNONYMS

taiche — in that way; tumi — You; navadvīpe — at Navadvīpa; kari' avatāra — descending as an incarnation; sakala — all; brahmānda — of the universe; jīvera — the living entities; karilā nistāra — have delivered.

TRANSLATION

"By descending as an incarnation at Navadvīpa, You, just like Krishna, have already delivered all the living entities of the universe.
