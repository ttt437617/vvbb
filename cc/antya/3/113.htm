Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.113

tomāra sańgama lāgi' lubdha mora mana

tomā nā pāile prāna nā yāya dhārana"

SYNONYMS

tomāra sańgama — union with you; lāgi' — for the sake of; lubdha — greedy; mora mana — my mind; tomā — you; nā pāile — if I do not get; prāna — my life; nā — not; yāya — can be; dhārana — maintained.

TRANSLATION

"I am eager to be united with you. My mind is greedy for this. If I don't obtain you, I shall not be able to keep my body and soul together."
