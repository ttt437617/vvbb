Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.225

haridāsa kare gońphāya nāma-sańkīrtana

krishna avatīrna ha-ibena, — ei tāńra mana

SYNONYMS

haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; kare — performed; gońphāya — in the cave; nāma-sańkīrtana — chanting of the holy name of the Lord; krishna — Lord Krishna; avatīrna ha-ibena — will descend; ei — this; tāńra mana — his mind.

TRANSLATION

Similarly, Haridāsa Thākura chanted in his cave on the bank of the Ganges with the intention of causing Krishna's descent.
