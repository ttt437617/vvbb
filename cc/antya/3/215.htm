Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.215

ācārye miliyā kailā dandavat pranāma

advaita ālińgana kari' karilā sammāna

SYNONYMS

ācārye miliyā — meeting Advaita Ācārya; kailā — offered; dandavat pranāma — obeisances and respects; advaita — Advaita Ācārya; ālińgana kari' — embracing; karilā sammāna — showed respect.

TRANSLATION

Upon meeting Advaita Ācārya, Haridāsa Thākura offered Him respect and obeisances. Advaita Ācārya embraced him and showed respect to him in return.
