Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.71

'pratidhvani' nahe, sei karaye 'kīrtana'

tomāra kripāra ei akathya kathana

SYNONYMS

prati-dhvani nahe — that sound vibration is not an echo; sei — they; karaye kīrtana — are chanting; tomāra kripāra — of Your mercy; ei — this; akathya kathana — inconceivable incident.

TRANSLATION

"Actually, however, it is not an echo: it is the kīrtana of the nonmoving living entities. All this, although inconceivable, is possible by Your mercy.
