Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.233

tāńra ańga-gandhe daśa dik āmodita

bhūshana-dhvanite karna haya camakita

SYNONYMS

tāńra — her; ańga-gandhe — the scent of the body; daśa dik — ten directions; āmodita — perfumed; bhūshana-dhvanite — by the tinkling of her ornaments; karna — the ear; haya — becomes; camakita — startled.

TRANSLATION

The scent of her body perfumed all directions, and the tinkling of her ornaments startled the ear.
