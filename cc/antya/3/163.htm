Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.163

jāti-dhana-jana khānera sakala la-ila

bahu-dina paryanta grāma ujāda rahila

SYNONYMS

jāti — birthright; dhana — riches; jana — followers; khānera — of Rāmacandra Khān; sakala — everything; la-ila — he took away; bahu-dina — a long time; paryanta — for; grāma — the village; ujāda rahila — remained deserted.

TRANSLATION

The Muslim minister took away Rāmacandra Khān's position, wealth and followers. For many days the village remained deserted.
