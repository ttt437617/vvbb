Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.84

na caivam vismayah kāryo

bhavatā bhagavaty aje

yogeśvareśvare krishne

yata etad vimucyate

SYNONYMS

na — not; ca — also; evam — thus; vismayah — wonder; kāryah — to be done; bhavatā — by You; bhagavati — unto the Supreme Personality of Godhead; aje — the unborn; yoga-īśvara-īśvare — the master of all masters of mystic power; krishne — unto Lord Krishna; yatah — by whom; etat — all living entities; vimucyate — are delivered.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna, the unborn Supreme Personality of Godhead, master of all masters of mystic power, delivers all living entities, moving and nonmoving. Nothing is astonishing in the activities of the Lord.'

PURPORT

This is a quotation from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (10.29.16).
