Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.195

haridāsa kahena, — kene karaha samśaya?

śāstre kahe, — nāmābhāsa-mātre 'mukti' haya

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kahena — Haridāsa Thākura said; kene — why; karaha samśaya — are you doubtful; śāstre kahe — it is stated in the revealed scriptures; nāma-ābhāsa mātre — simply by a glimpse of the chanting of the holy name; mukti haya — there is liberation.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura said, "Why are you doubtful? The revealed scriptures say that one can attain liberation simply by a glimpse of offenseless chanting of the holy name.
