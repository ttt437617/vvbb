Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.43

ācāryādi vaishnavere mahā-prasāda dilā

prabhura yaiche ājñā, pandita tāhā ācarilā

SYNONYMS

ācārya-ādi — headed by Advaita Ācārya; vaishnavere — to all the Vaishnavas; mahā-prasāda dilā — delivered all the prasādam of Lord Jagannātha; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yaiche — as; ājñā — the order; pandita — Dāmodara Pandita; tāhā — that; ācarilā — performed.

TRANSLATION

He delivered all the prasādam to such great Vaishnavas as Advaita Ācārya. Thus he stayed there and behaved according to the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
