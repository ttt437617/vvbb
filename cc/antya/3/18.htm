Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.18

eta bali' dāmodara mauna ha-ilā

antare santosha prabhu hāsi' vicārilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; dāmodara — Dāmodara Pandita; mauna ha-ilā — became silent; antare — within Himself; santosha — pleased; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hāsi' — smiling; vicārilā — considered.

TRANSLATION

Having said this, Dāmodara Pandita became silent. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smiled, pleased within Himself, and considered the impudence of Dāmodara Pandita.
