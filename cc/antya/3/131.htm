Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.131

dandavat hañā pade thākura-carane

rāmacandra-khāńnera kathā kaila nivedane

SYNONYMS

dandavat hañā — offering obeisances; pade — she fell down; thākura-carane — at the lotus feet of Haridāsa Thākura; rāmacandra-khāńnera — of Rāmacandra Khān; kathā — policy; kaila — did; nivedane — submission.

TRANSLATION

The prostitute, now purified, fell at the lotus feet of Haridāsa Thākura and confessed that Rāmacandra Khān had appointed her to pollute him.
