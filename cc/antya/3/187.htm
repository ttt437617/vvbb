Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.187

mriyamāno harer nāma

grinan putropacāritam

ajāmilo 'py agād dhāma

kim uta śraddhayā grinan

SYNONYMS

mriyamānah — dying; hareh nāma — the holy name of the Supreme Lord; grinan — chanting; putra-upacāritam — though spoken for his son; ajāmilah — Ajāmila; api — also; agāt — attained; dhāma — the spiritual world; kim uta — what to speak of; śraddhayā — with faith and reverence; grinan — chanting.

TRANSLATION

"'While dying, Ajāmila chanted the holy name of the Lord, intending to call his son Nārāyana. Nevertheless, he attained the spiritual world. What then to speak of those who chant the holy name with faith and reverence?'

PURPORT

This is a verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (6.2.49).
