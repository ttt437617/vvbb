Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.47

caitanyera līlā — gambhīra, koti-samudra haite

ki lāgi' ki kare, keha nā pāre bujhite

SYNONYMS

caitanyera līlā — the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gambhīra — very deep; koti-samudra haite — more than millions of seas; ki lāgi' — for what reason; ki kare — what He does; keha — anyone; nā — not; pāre bujhite — can understand.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are deeper than millions of seas and oceans. Therefore no one can understand what He does or why He does it.
