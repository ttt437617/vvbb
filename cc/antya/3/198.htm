Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.198

vipra kahe, — "nāmābhāse yadi 'mukti' naya

tabe tomāra nāka kāti' karaha niścaya"

SYNONYMS

vipra kahe — the brāhmana said; nāma-ābhāse — simply by the awakening of offenseless chanting of the holy name; yadi — if; mukti naya — liberation is not attainable; tabe — then; tomāra — your; nāka — nose; kāti' — I shall cut off; karaha niścaya — take it as certain.

TRANSLATION

Gopāla Cakravartī said, "If one is not liberated by nāmābhāsa, then you may be certain that I shall cut off your nose."
