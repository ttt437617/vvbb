Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.40

ei-mata bāra bāra karāiha smarana

mora nāma lañā tāńra vandiha carana"

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; bāra bāra — again and again; karāiha — cause; smarana — remembrance; mora — My; nāma — name; lañā — taking; tāńra — her; vandiha — worship; carana — feet.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Dāmodara Pandita, "Remind mother Śacī in this way again and again and worship her lotus feet in My name."
