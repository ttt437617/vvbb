Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.188

ye mukti bhakta nā laya, se krishna cāhe dite"

SYNONYMS

ye — which; mukti — liberation; bhakta — a devotee; nā laya — does not take; se — that; krishna — Lord Krishna; cāhe dite — wants to offer.

TRANSLATION

"Liberation, which is unacceptable for a pure devotee, is always offered by Krishna without difficulty.
