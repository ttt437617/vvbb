Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.37

pāka-pātre dekhilā saba anna āche bhari'

punah bhoga lāgāilā sthāna-samskāra kari'

SYNONYMS

pāka-pātre — the cooking pots; dekhilā — she saw; saba — all; anna — food; āche bhari' — were filled with; punah — again; bhoga lāgāilā — offered the food; sthāna — the place for offering; samskāra kari' — cleansing.

TRANSLATION

"'Then you went to see the cooking pots and found that every pot was filled with food. Therefore you again offered the food, after cleansing the place for the offering.
