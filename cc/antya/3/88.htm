Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.88

tomāra ye līlā mahā-amritera sindhu

mora mano-gocara nahe tāra eka bindu"

SYNONYMS

tomāra — Your; ye — whatever; līlā — pastimes; mahā-amritera sindhu — a great ocean of nectar; mora — for me; manah-gocara nahe — it is not possible to conceive; tāra — of it; eka bindu — one drop.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, Your pastimes are just like an ocean of nectar. It is not possible for me to conceive how great that ocean is or even to understand a drop of it."
