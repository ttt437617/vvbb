Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.103

haridāse loke pūje, sahite nā pāre

tāńra apamāna karite nānā upāya kare

SYNONYMS

haridāse — unto Haridāsa Thākura; loke — people; pūje — offer respect; sahite nā pāre — he could not tolerate; tāńra — his; apamāna — dishonor; karite — to do; nānā — various; upāya — means; kare — plans.

TRANSLATION

Unable to tolerate that such respect was being offered to Haridāsa Thākura, Rāmacandra Khān planned in various ways to dishonor him.
