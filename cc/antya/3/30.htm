Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.30

'bāre bāre āsi' āmi tomāra bhavane

mishtānna vyañjana saba kariye bhojane

SYNONYMS

bāre bāre — again and again; āsi' — coming; āmi — I; tomāra bhavane — at your place; mishtānna — sweetmeats; vyañjana — vegetables; saba — all; kariye — do; bhojane — eating.

TRANSLATION

"'I come to your home again and again to eat all the sweetmeats and vegetables you offer.
