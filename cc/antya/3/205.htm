Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.205

"tomā-sabāra dosha nāhi, ei ajña brāhmana

tāra dosha nāhi, tāra tarka-nishtha mana

SYNONYMS

tomā-sabāra — of all of you; dosha — fault; nāhi — there is not; ei — this; ajña — ignorant; brāhmana — so-called brāhmana; tāra dosha nāhi — he is also not at fault; tāra — his; tarka-nishtha — accustomed to speculation; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"None of you are at fault," he said. "Indeed, even this ignorant so-called brāhmana is not at fault, for he is accustomed to dry speculation and logic.
