Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.110

tulasī namaskari' haridāsera dvāre yāñā

gosāñire namaskari' rahilā dāndāñā

SYNONYMS

tulasī namaskari' — after offering obeisances to the tulasī plant; haridāsera — of Thākura Haridāsa; dvāre — at the door; yāñā — going; gosāñire — unto the ācārya; namaskari' — offering obeisances; rahilā dāndāñā — remained standing.

TRANSLATION

After offering obeisances to the tulasī plant, she went to the door of Haridāsa Thākura, offered him obeisances and stood there.
