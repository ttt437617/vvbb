Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.20

eteka vicāri' prabhu madhyāhne calilā

āra dine dāmodare nibhrite bolāilā

SYNONYMS

eteka vicāri' — considering like this; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; madhyāhne calilā — went to perform His noon duties; āra dine — the next day; dāmodare — unto Dāmodara Pandita; nibhrite — in a solitary place; bolāilā — called.

TRANSLATION

Thinking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to perform His noon duties. The next day, He called Dāmodara Pandita to a solitary place.
