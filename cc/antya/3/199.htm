Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.199

haridāsa kahena, — "yadi nāmābhāse 'mukti' naya

tabe āmāra nāka kātimu, — ei suniścaya"

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kahena — Haridāsa Thākura said; yadi — if; nāma-ābhāse — simply by the awakening of the holy name of the Lord; mukti naya — mukti is not available; tabe — then; āmāra — my; nāka — nose; kātimu — I shall cut off; ei — this; suniścaya — certain.

TRANSLATION

Then Haridāsa Thākura accepted the challenge offered by Gopāla Cakravartī. "If by nāmābhāsa liberation is not available," he said, "certainly I shall cut off my nose."
