Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.24

āmā haite ye nā haya, se tomā haite haya

āmāre karilā danda, āna kebā haya

SYNONYMS

āmā haite — from Me; ye — whatever; nā haya — is not; se — that; tomā haite — from you; haya — becomes possible; āmāre — Me; karilā danda — punished; āna — others; kebā haya — what to speak of.

TRANSLATION

"You can do whatever I cannot. Indeed, you can chastise even Me, what to speak of others.
