Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.118

'āji āmā ańgīkāra kariyāche vacane

kāli avaśya tāhāra sańge ha-ibe sańgame'

SYNONYMS

āji — today; āmā — me; ańgīkāra — acceptance; kariyāche — he has done; vacane — by word; kāli — tomorrow; avaśya — certainly; tāhāra sańge — with him; ha-ibe — there will be; sańgame — union.

TRANSLATION

"Today Haridāsa Thākura has promised to enjoy with me. Tomorrow certainly I shall have union with him."
