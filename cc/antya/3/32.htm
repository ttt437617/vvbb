Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.32

ei māgha-sańkrāntye tumi randhana karilā

nānā vyañjana, kshīra, pithā, pāyasa rāndhilā

SYNONYMS

ei — this; māgha-sańkrāntye — on the occasion of the Māgha-sańkrānti festival; tumi — you; randhana karilā — cooked; nānā vyañjana — varieties of vegetables; kshīra — condensed milk; pithā — cakes; pāyasa — sweet rice; rāndhilā — cooked.

TRANSLATION

"'During the last Māgha-sańkrānti festival, you cooked varieties of vegetables, condensed milk, cakes and sweet rice for Me.
