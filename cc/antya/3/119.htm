Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.119

āra dina rātri haile veśyā āila

haridāsa tāre bahu āśvāsa karila

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; rātri — night; haile — when there was; veśyā — the prostitute; āila — came; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; tāre — unto her; bahu — many; āśvāsa karila — gave assurances.

TRANSLATION

The next night, when the prostitute came again, Haridāsa Thākura gave her many assurances.
