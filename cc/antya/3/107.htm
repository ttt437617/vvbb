Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.107

khāńna kahe, — "mora pāika yāuka tomāra sane

tomāra sahita ekatra tāre dhari' yena āne"

SYNONYMS

khāńna kahe — Rāmacandra Khān said; mora pāika — my constable; yāuka — let him go; tomāra sane — with you; tomāra sahita — with you; ekatra — together; tāre — him; dhari' — arresting; yena — so that; āne — can bring.

TRANSLATION

Rāmacandra Khān said to the prostitute, "My constable will go with you so that as soon as he sees you with Haridāsa Thākura, immediately he will arrest him and bring both of you to me."
