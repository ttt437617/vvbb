Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.96

caitanya-mańgale śrī-vrindāvana-dāsa

haridāsera guna kichu kariyāchena prakāśa

SYNONYMS

caitanya-mańgale — in the book known as Caitanya-mańgala (Caitanya-bhāgavata); śrī-vrindāvana-dāsa — Śrī Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura; haridāsera — of Haridāsa Thākura; guna — qualities; kichu — some; kariyāchena prakāśa — manifested.

TRANSLATION

In the Caitanya-mańgala, Śrīla Vrindāvana dāsa Thākura has described the attributes of Haridāsa Thākura to some extent.
