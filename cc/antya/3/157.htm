Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.157

ihāń rāmacandra khāna sevake ājñā dila

gosāñi yāhāń vasilā, tāra mātī khodāila

SYNONYMS

ihāń — here; rāmacandra khāna — Rāmacandra Khān; sevake — to the servant; ājñā dila — ordered; gosāñi — Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; yāhāń — where; vasilā — sat down; tāra — of that place; mātī — earth; khodāila — caused to dig.

TRANSLATION

Rāmacandra Khān ordered the servant to dig up the dirt in the place where Nityānanda Prabhu had sat.
