Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.161

strī-putra-sahita rāmacandrere bāndhiyā

tāra ghara-grāma lute tina-dina rahiyā

SYNONYMS

strī-putra — his wife and children; sahita — with; rāmacandrere bāndhiyā — arresting Rāmacandra Khān; tāra — his; ghara-grāma — house and village; lute — plundered; tina-dina rahiyā — staying three days.

TRANSLATION

He arrested Rāmacandra Khān, along with his wife and sons, and then he continuously plundered the house and village for three days.
