Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.219

mahā-mahā-vipra ethā kulīna-samāja

nīce ādara kara, nā vāsaha bhaya lāja!!

SYNONYMS

mahā-mahā-vipra — great, great brāhmanas; ethā — here; kulīna-samāja — aristocratic society; nīce — to a low-class person; ādara kara — You show honor; nā vāsaha — You do not care for; bhaya lāja — fear or shame.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, You are living within a society of great, great brāhmanas and aristocrats, but without fear or shame You adore a lower-class man like me.
