Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.38

ei-mata bāra bāra kariye bhojana

tomāra śuddha-preme more kare ākarshana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; bāra bāra — again and again; kariye bhojana — I eat; tomāra — your; śuddha-preme — pure love; more — Me; kare ākarshana — attracts.

TRANSLATION

"'Thus I again and again eat everything you offer Me, for I am attracted by your pure love.
