Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.173

thākura dekhi' dui bhāi kailā abhyutthāna

pāya padi' āsana dilā kariyā sammāna

SYNONYMS

thākura dekhi' — seeing Haridāsa Thākura; dui bhāi — the two brothers; kailā abhyutthāna — stood up; pāya padi' — falling at the lotus feet; āsana dilā — offered a sitting place; kariyā sammāna — with great respect.

TRANSLATION

Seeing Haridāsa Thākura, the two brothers immediately stood up and fell at his lotus feet. Then with great respect they offered him a place to sit.
