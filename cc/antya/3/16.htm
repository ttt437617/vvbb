Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.16

yadyapi brāhmanī sei tapasvinī satī

tathāpi tāhāra dosha — sundarī yuvatī

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; brāhmanī — wife of a brāhmana; sei — that; tapasvinī — austere; satī — chaste; tathāpi — still; tāhāra — her; dosha — fault; sundarī — very beautiful; yuvatī — young girl.

TRANSLATION

"Although the boy's mother is completely austere and chaste, she has one natural fault — she is a very beautiful young girl.
