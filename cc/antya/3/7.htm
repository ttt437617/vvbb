Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.7

nitya āise, prabhu tāre kare mahā-prīta

yāńhā prīti tāńhā āise, — bālakera rīta

SYNONYMS

nitya āise — he comes daily; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāre — to him; kare — does; mahā-prīta — very affectionate behavior; yāńhā prīti — wherever there is love; tāńhā āise — one comes there; bālakera rīta — the nature of a small boy.

TRANSLATION

The boy came every day to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who treated him with great affection. It is the nature of any boy to go see a man who loves him.
