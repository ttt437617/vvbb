Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.160

āsi' sei durgā-mandape vāsā kaila

avadhya vadha kari' māmsa se-ghare rāndhāila

SYNONYMS

āsi' — coming; sei durgā-mandape — at that very place of the Durgā-mandapa; vāsā kaila — made his residence; avadhya — a cow or calf, which is not to be killed; vadha kari' — killing; māmsa — meat; se-ghare — in that place; rāndhāila — cooked.

TRANSLATION

The Muslim minister made his residence in the Durgā-mandapa of Rāmacandra Khān. He killed a cow and cooked the meat at that very place.
