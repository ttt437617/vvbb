Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.264

lakshmī-ādi kari' krishna-preme lubdha hañā

nāma-prema āsvādilā manushye janmiyā

SYNONYMS

lakshmī-ādi — the goddess of fortune and others; kari' — in this way; krishna-preme — for love of Krishna; lubdha hañā — being greedy; nāma-prema — the holy name of Krishna in love; āsvādilā — tasted; manushye janmiyā — taking birth in human society.

TRANSLATION

The goddess of fortune and others, allured by love of Krishna, also came down in the form of human beings and tasted the holy name of the Lord in love.
