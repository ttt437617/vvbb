Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.176

tina-laksha nāma thākura karena kīrtana

nāmera mahimā uthāila pandita-gana

SYNONYMS

tina-laksha — 300,000; nāma — holy names of the Lord; thākura — Haridāsa Thākura; karena kīrtana — used to chant; nāmera — of the holy name; mahimā — glories; uthāila — raised; pandita-gana — all the learned scholars.

TRANSLATION

It was mentioned in the assembly that Haridāsa Thākura chanted the holy names of Krishna 300,000 times a day. Thus all the learned scholars began to discuss the glories of the holy name.
