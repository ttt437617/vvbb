Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.247

tritīya divasera rātri-śesha yabe haila

thākurera sthāne nārī kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

tritīya divasera — of the third day; rātri-śesha — the end of the night; yabe — when; haila — there was; thākurera — of Haridāsa Thākura; sthāne — at the place; nārī — the woman; kahite lāgila — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

At the end of the night of the third day, the woman spoke to Haridāsa Thākura as follows.
