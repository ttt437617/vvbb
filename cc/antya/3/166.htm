Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.166

hiranya, govardhana — dui mulukera majumadāra

tāra purohita — 'balarāma' nāma tāńra

SYNONYMS

hiranya — Hiranya; govardhana — Govardhana; dui — two; mulukera — of that country; majumadāra — treasurers of the government; tāra — their; purohita — priest; balarāma — Balarāma; nāma — name; tāńra — his.

TRANSLATION

Hiranya and Govardhana were the two governmental treasurers in that division of the country. Their priest was named Balarāma Ācārya.

PURPORT

The word majumadāra refers to a treasurer who keeps accounts of revenue.
