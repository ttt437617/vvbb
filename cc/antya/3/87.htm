Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.87

ye kahe, — 'caitanya-mahimā mora gocara haya'

se jānuka, mora punah ei ta' niścaya

SYNONYMS

ye kahe — whoever says; caitanya-mahimā — the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mora gocara — known to me; haya — are; se jānuka — he may know; mora — of me; punah — again; ei ta' niścaya — this is the decision.

TRANSLATION

"One may say that he understands the glories of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He may know whatever he may know, but as far as I am concerned, this is my conclusion.
