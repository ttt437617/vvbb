Antya-līlāChapter 3: The Glories of Śrīla Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 3.117

prātah-kāla dekhi' veśyā uthiyā calilā

saba samācāra yāi khāńnere kahilā

SYNONYMS

prātah-kāla dekhi' — seeing the morning; veśyā — the prostitute; uthiyā calilā — stood up and left; saba samācāra — all information; yāi — going; khāńnere kahilā — she spoke to Rāmacandra Khān.

TRANSLATION

When she saw that it was morning, the prostitute stood up and left. Coming before Rāmacandra Khān, she informed him of all the news.
