Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.85

prati-vriksha-vallī aiche bhramite bhramite

aśokera tale krishne dekhena ācambite

SYNONYMS

prati-vriksha-vallī — around each and every tree and creeper; aiche — in that way; bhramite bhramite — wandering; aśokera tale — underneath an aśoka tree; krishne — Lord Krishna; dekhena — He sees; ācambite — suddenly.

TRANSLATION

As He thus wandered around every tree and creeper, He came beneath an aśoka tree and suddenly saw Lord Krishna.
