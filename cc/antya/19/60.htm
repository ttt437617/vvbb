Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.60

sarva-rātri karena bhāve mukha sańgharshana

goń-goń-śabda karena, — svarūpa śunilā takhana

SYNONYMS

sarva-rātri — the whole night; karena — does; bhāve — in emotion; mukha sańgharshana — rubbing of the face; goń-goń-śabda karena — makes a peculiar sound, goń-goń; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; śunilā — heard; takhana — then.

TRANSLATION

In ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rubbed His face against the walls all night long, making a peculiar sound, "goń-goń," which Svarūpa Dāmodara could hear through the door.
