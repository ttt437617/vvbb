Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.66

svarūpa-gosāñi tabe cintā pāilā mane

bhakta-gana lañā vicāra kailā āra dine

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; tabe — thereafter; cintā — an anxiety or thought; pāilā mane — got in his mind; bhakta-gana lañā — among all the devotees; vicāra kailā — considered; āra dine — the next day.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara was very anxious, but then he had an idea. The following day, he and the other devotees considered it together.
