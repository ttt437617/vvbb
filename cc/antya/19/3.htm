Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.3

ei-mate mahāprabhu krishna-premāveśe

unmāda-pralāpa kare rātri-divase

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-prema-āveśe — in ecstatic emotional love of Krishna; unmāda — madness; pralāpa — and crazy talk; kare — performs; rātri-divase — throughout the entire day and night.

TRANSLATION

In the ecstasy of love of Krishna, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu thus behaved like a madman, talking insanely all day and night.
