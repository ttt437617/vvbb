Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.88

krishnera śrī-ańga-gandhe bhariche udyāne

sei gandha pāñā prabhu hailā acetane

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; śrī-ańga-gandhe — the aroma of the transcendental body; bhariche — filled; udyāne — in the garden; sei gandha pāñā — getting that aroma; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hailā — became; acetane — unconscious.

TRANSLATION

The entire garden was filled with the scent of Lord Śrī Krishna's transcendental body. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu smelled it, He fell unconscious at once.
