Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.103

alaukika krishna-līlā, divya-śakti tāra

tarkera gocara nahe caritra yāhāra

SYNONYMS

alaukika — uncommon; krishna-līlā — pastimes of Lord Krishna; divya-śakti — transcendental potency; tāra — of that; tarkera — of argument; gocara — within the purview; nahe — is not; caritra — the characteristics; yāhāra — of which.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Lord Krishna are uncommonly full of transcendental potency. It is a characteristic of such pastimes that they do not fall within the jurisdiction of experimental logic and arguments.
