Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.77

ei-mata mahāprabhu rātri-divase

prema-sindhu-magna rahe, kabhu dube, bhāse

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rātri-divase — day and night; prema-sindhu-magna rahe — remains merged in the ocean of love of Krishna; kabhu dube — sometimes drowns; bhāse — floats.

TRANSLATION

In this way Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed immersed day and night in an ocean of ecstatic love for Krishna. Sometimes He was submerged, and sometimes He floated.
