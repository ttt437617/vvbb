Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.64

dvāra nāhi' pāñā mukha lāge cāri-bhite

kshata haya, rakta pade, nā pāi yāite"

SYNONYMS

dvāra nāhi' pāñā — not finding the door; mukha lāge — My face strikes; cāri-bhite — on the four walls; kshata haya — there was injury; rakta pade — blood came out; nā pāi yāite — still I could not get out.

TRANSLATION

"Unable to find the door, I kept hitting the four walls with My face. My face was injured, and it bled, but I still could not get out."
