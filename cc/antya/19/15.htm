Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.15

jagadānanda nadīyā giyā mātāre mililā

prabhura yata nivedana, sakala kahilā

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda — Jagadānanda; nadīyā — to Navadvīpa; giyā — going; mātāre — mother Śacī; mililā — met; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yata nivedana — all kinds of salutations; sakala — everything; kahilā — he told.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita thus went to Nadia, and when he met Śacīmātā, he conveyed to her all the Lord's salutations.
