Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.104

ei prema sadā jāge yāhāra antare

panditeha tāra ceshtā bujhite nā pāre

SYNONYMS

ei — this; prema — love of Godhead; sadā — always; jāge — awakens; yāhāra — of whom; antare — within the heart; panditeha — even a learned scholar; tāra — his; ceshtā — activities; bujhite — to understand; nā pāre — cannot.

TRANSLATION

When transcendental love of Krishna awakens in someone's heart, even a learned scholar cannot comprehend his activities.
