Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.72

ughāda-ańge padiyā śańkara nidrā yāya

prabhu uthi' āpana-kāńthā tāhāre jadāya

SYNONYMS

ughāda-ańge — without a covering over the body; padiyā — lying down; śańkara — Śańkara; nidrā yāya — goes to sleep; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uthi' — getting up; āpana-kāńthā — His own quilt; tāhāre jadāya — wraps him with.

TRANSLATION

He would lie asleep without a covering on his body, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would get up and wrap him with His own quilt.
