Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.73

nirantara ghumāya śańkara śīghra-cetana

vasi' pāda cāpi' kare rātri-jāgarana

SYNONYMS

nirantara — incessantly; ghumāya — sleeps; śańkara — Śańkara; śīghra — very quickly; cetana — waking up; vasi' — sitting; pāda cāpi' — massaging the legs; kare — does; rātri-jāgarana — keeps awake at night.

TRANSLATION

Śańkara Pandita would always fall asleep, but he would quickly awaken, sit up and again begin massaging the legs of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. In this way he would stay awake the entire night.
