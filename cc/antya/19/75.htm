Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.75

ei līlā mahāprabhura raghunātha-dāsa

gaurāńga-stava-kalpavrikshe kariyāche prakāśa

SYNONYMS

ei līlā — this pastime; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; raghunātha-dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; gaurāńga-stava-kalpa-vrikshe — in his book known as Gaurāńga-stava-kalpavriksha; kariyāche prakāśa — has described very nicely.

TRANSLATION

This pastime of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's has been described very nicely by Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī in his book known as Gaurāńga-stava-kalpavriksha.
