Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.50

āpanāra karma-dosha, tore kibā kari rosha,

toya-moya sambandha vidūra

ye āmāra prāna-nātha, ekatra rahi yāńra sātha,

sei krishna ha-ilā nithura!

SYNONYMS

āpanāra karma-dosha — it is the result of My own fate; tore — unto you; kibā — what; kari rosha — I give accusations; toya-moya — between you and Me; sambandha — the relationship; vidūra — very far; ye — the one who is; āmāra — My; prāna-nātha — life and soul; ekatra — together; rahi — We remain; yāńra sātha — with whom; sei krishna — that Krishna; ha-ilā nithura — has become so cruel.

TRANSLATION

"But this is the fault of My own destiny. Why should I needlessly accuse you? There is no intimate relationship between you and Me. Krishna, however, is My life and soul. It is We who live together, and it is He who has become so cruel.
