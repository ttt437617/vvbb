Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.111

caitanya-caritāmrita — nitya-nūtana

śunite śunite judāya hridaya-śravana

SYNONYMS

caitanya-caritāmrita — the book named Caitanya-caritāmrita; nitya-nūtana — always fresh; śunite śunite — by hearing and hearing; judāya — becomes pacified; hridaya-śravana — the ear and heart.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya-caritāmrita is ever-increasingly fresh. For one who hears it again and again, the heart and ear become pacified.
