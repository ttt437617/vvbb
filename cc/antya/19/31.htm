Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.31

unmāda-pralāpa-ceshtā kare rātri-dine

rādhā-bhāvāveśe viraha bāde anukshane

SYNONYMS

unmāda — madness; pralāpa — craziness; ceshtā — activities; kare rātri-dine — He performed day and night; rādhā-bhāva-āveśe — in the ecstatic emotion of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; viraha — separation; bāde — increases; anukshane — every moment.

TRANSLATION

As His feelings of separation in the ecstasy of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī increased at every moment, the Lord's activities, both day and night, were now wild, insane performances.
