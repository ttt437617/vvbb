Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.19

"prabhure kahiha āmāra koti namaskāra

ei nivedana tāńra carane āmāra

SYNONYMS

prabhure kahiha — just inform Lord Caitanya; āmāra — My; koti namaskāra — hundreds and thousands of obeisances; ei nivedana — this is the submission; tāńra — His; carane — unto the lotus feet; āmāra — My.

TRANSLATION

In His sonnet, Advaita Prabhu first offered His obeisances hundreds and thousands of times unto the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. He then submitted the following statement at His lotus feet.
