Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.20

bāulake kahiha, — loka ha-ila bāula

bāulake kahiha, — hāte nā vikāya cāula

SYNONYMS

bāulake kahiha — please inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is playing the part of a madman in ecstatic love; loka — the people in general; ha-ila — have become; bāula — also mad in ecstatic love; bāulake kahiha — again inform Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the bāula; hāte — in the market; nā — not; vikāya — sells; cāula — rice.

TRANSLATION

"Please inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is acting like a madman, that everyone here has become mad like Him. Inform Him also that in the marketplace rice is no longer in demand.
