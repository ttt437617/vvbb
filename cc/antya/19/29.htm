Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.29

śuniyā vismita ha-ilā saba bhakta-gana

svarūpa-gosāñi kichu ha-ilā vimana

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; vismita — astonished; ha-ilā — became; saba — all; bhakta-gana — the devotees; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; kichu — somewhat; ha-ilā — became; vimana — morose.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, all the devotees were astonished, especially Svarūpa Dāmodara, who became somewhat morose.
