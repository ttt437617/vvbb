Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.62

prabhure śayyāte āni' susthira karāilā

'kāńhe kailā ei tumi?' — svarūpa puchilā

SYNONYMS

prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śayyāte — to the bed; āni' — bringing; su-sthira karāilā — made Him pacified; kāńhe — why; kailā — have done; ei — this; tumi — You; svarūpa puchilā — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī inquired.

TRANSLATION

They brought the Lord to His bed, calmed Him and then asked, "Why have You done this to Yourself?"
