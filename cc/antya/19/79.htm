Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.79

'jagannātha-vallabha' nāma udyāna-pradhāne

praveśa karilā prabhu lañā bhakta-gane

SYNONYMS

jagannātha-vallabha — Jagannātha-vallabha; nāma — named; udyāna-pradhāne — one of the best gardens; praveśa karilā — entered; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; bhakta-gane — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

The Lord, along with His devotees, entered one of the nicest gardens, called Jagannātha-vallabha.
