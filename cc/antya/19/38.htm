Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.38

ei vrajera ramanī, kāmārka-tapta-kumudinī,

nija-karāmrita diyā dāna

praphullita kare yei, kāhāń mora candra sei,

dekhāha, sakhi, rākha mora prāna

SYNONYMS

ei — these; vrajera ramanī — women of Vrindāvana; kāma-arka-tapta-kumudinī — exactly like lilies becoming very hot in the sun of lusty desire; nija — own; kara-amrita — nectar of the hands; diyā — giving; dāna — charity; praphullita — jubilant; kare — makes; yei — one who; kāhāń — where; mora — My; candra — moon; sei — that; dekhāha — please show; sakhi — O My dear friend; rākha — please save; mora prāna — My life.

TRANSLATION

"The women of Vrindāvana are just like lilies growing hot in the sun of lusty desires. But moonlike Krishna makes them all jubilant by bestowing upon them the nectar of His hands. O My dear friend, where is My moon now? Save My life by showing Him to Me!
