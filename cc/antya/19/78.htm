Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.78

eka-kāle vaiśākhera paurnamāsī-dine

rātri-kāle mahāprabhu calilā udyāne

SYNONYMS

eka-kāle — once; vaiśākhera — of the month of Vaiśākha (April-May); paurnamāsī-dine — on the full-moon night; rātri-kāle — at night; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calilā — went; udyāne — to a garden.

TRANSLATION

One full-moon night in the month of Vaiśākha [April-May], Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to a garden.
