Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.59

mukhe, gande, nāke kshata ha-ila apāra

bhāvāveśe nā jānena prabhu, pade rakta-dhāra

SYNONYMS

mukhe — on the mouth; gande — on the cheeks; nāke — on the nose; kshata — injuries; ha-ila — there were; apāra — many; bhāva-āveśe — in ecstatic emotion; nā jānena — could not understand; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pade — oozes out; rakta-dhāra — a flow of blood.

TRANSLATION

Blood oozed from the many injuries on His mouth, nose and cheeks, but due to His ecstatic emotions, the Lord did not know it.
