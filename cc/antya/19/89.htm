Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.89

nirantara nāsāya paśe krishna-parimala

gandha āsvādite prabhu ha-ilā pāgala

SYNONYMS

nirantara — incessantly; nāsāya — in the nostrils; paśe — enters; krishna-parimala — the scent of Krishna's body; gandha āsvādite — to relish the fragrance; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ilā pāgala — became mad.

TRANSLATION

But the scent of Krishna's body incessantly entered His nostrils, and the Lord became mad to relish it.
