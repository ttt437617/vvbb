Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.7

kahiha tāńhāre — 'tumi karaha smarana

nitya āsi 'āmi tomāra vandiye carana

SYNONYMS

kahiha tāńhāre — inform her; tumi karaha smarana — please remember; nitya āsi' — coming daily; āmi — I; tomāra — your; vandiye carana — offer respect to the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"Tell her for Me, 'Please remember that I come here every day and offer My respects to your lotus feet.
