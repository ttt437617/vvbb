Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.65

unmāda-daśāya prabhura sthira nahe mana

yei kare, yei bole saba, — unmāda-lakshana

SYNONYMS

unmāda-daśāya — in such a state of madness; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sthira nahe mana — the mind is not steady; yei kare — whatever He does; yei bole — whatever He says; saba — all; unmāda-lakshana — simply symptoms of madness.

TRANSLATION

In this state of madness, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind was unsteady. Whatever He said or did was all symptomatic of madness.
