Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.74

tāńra bhaye nārena prabhu bāhire yāite

tāńra bhaye nārena bhittye mukhābja ghashite

SYNONYMS

tāńra bhaye — because of fear of him; nārena — was not able; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhire yāite — to go out; tāńra bhaye — because of fear of him; nārena — was not able; bhittye — on the walls; mukha-abja ghashite — to rub His lotuslike face.

TRANSLATION

Out of fear of Śańkara, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu could neither leave His room nor rub His lotuslike face against the walls.
