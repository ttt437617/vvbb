Antya-līlāChapter 19: The Inconceivable Behavior of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 19.32

ācambite sphure krishnera mathurā-gamana

udghūrnā-daśā haila unmāda-lakshana

SYNONYMS

ācambite — suddenly; sphure — there awoke; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; mathurā-gamana — the departure for Mathurā; udghūrnā-daśā — the ecstatic condition known as udghūrnā; haila — there was; unmāda-lakshana — the symptom of madness.

TRANSLATION

Suddenly there awoke within Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the scene of Lord Krishna's departure to Mathurā, and He began exhibiting the symptom of ecstatic madness known as udghūrnā.
