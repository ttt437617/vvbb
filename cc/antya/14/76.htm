Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.76

eta bali' prabhure dhari' ghare lañā gelā

tāńhāra avasthā saba kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; prabhure dhari' — supporting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ghare — home; lañā gelā — took back; tāńhāra avasthā — His condition; saba — all of them; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Thus all the devotees, supporting Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, took Him back to His residence. Then they all described to Him what had happened.
