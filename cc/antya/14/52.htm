Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.52

krishnera viyoge gopīra daśa daśā haya

sei daśa daśā haya prabhura udaya

SYNONYMS

krishnera viyoge — by separation from Krishna; gopīra — of the gopīs; daśa daśā — ten kinds of transcendental transformations of the body; haya — there are; sei — those; daśa daśā — ten kinds of transcendental transformations; haya — are; prabhura udaya — visible in the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

When the gopīs felt separation from Krishna, they experienced ten kinds of bodily transformations. These same symptoms appeared in the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
