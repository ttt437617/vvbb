Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.78

sabe dekhi — haya mora krishna vidyamāna

vidyut-prāya dekhā diyā haya antardhāna'

SYNONYMS

sabe — only this; dekhi — I know; haya — there is; mora — My; krishna — Lord Krishna; vidyamāna — brightly present; vidyut-prāya — like lightning; dekhā diyā — appearing; haya — there is; antardhāna — disappearance.

TRANSLATION

"All I can remember is that I saw My Krishna, but only for an instant. He appeared before Me and then, like lightning, immediately disappeared."
