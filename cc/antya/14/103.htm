Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.103

uthi' mahāprabhu vismita, iti uti cāya

ye dekhite cāya, tāhā dekhite nā pāya

SYNONYMS

uthi' — standing up; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vismita — astonished; iti uti — here and there; cāya — looks; ye — what; dekhite cāya — He wanted to see; tāhā — that; dekhite nā pāya — He could not see.

TRANSLATION

Astonished, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stood up and began looking here and there, trying to see something. But He could not catch sight of it.
