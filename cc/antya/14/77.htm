Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.77

śuni' mahāprabhu bada hailā camatkāra

prabhu kahe, — 'kichu smriti nāhika āmāra

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bada — very much; hailā camatkāra — became astonished; prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; kichu — any; smriti — remembrance; nāhika — is not; āmāra — Mine.

TRANSLATION

Hearing the description of His condition while He had lain near the Simha-dvāra, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very much astonished. He said, "I do not remember any of these things.
