Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.23

yāvat kāla darśana karena garudera pāche

prabhura āge darśana kare loka lākhe lākhe

SYNONYMS

yāvat kāla — as long as; darśana — seeing; karena — performs; garudera pāche — from the back of the Garuda column; prabhura āge — in front of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; darśana kare — see; loka — people; lākhe lākhe — in hundreds and thousands.

TRANSLATION

As He viewed Lord Jagannātha from behind the Garuda column, hundreds and thousands of people in front of Him were seeing the Deity.
