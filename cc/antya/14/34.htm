Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.34

kurukshetre dekhi' krishne aiche haila mana

'kāhāń kurukshetre āilāńa, kāhāń vrindāvana?'

SYNONYMS

kurukshetre — in Kurukshetra; dekhi' — seeing; krishne — Lord Krishna; aiche — in that way; haila mana — became His mind; kāhāń — where; kurukshetre āilāńa — I have come to Kurukshetra; kāhāń — where; vrindāvana — Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

When He saw the deities, Lord Caitanya thought He was seeing Krishna in Kurukshetra. He wondered, "Have I come to Kurukshetra? Where is Vrindāvana?"
