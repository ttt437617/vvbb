Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.58

rāmānanda-rāya tabe gelā nija ghare

svarūpa-govinda duńhe śuilena dvāre

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda-rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; tabe — thereupon; gelā — returned; nija ghare — to his home; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; govinda — and Govinda; duńhe — both; śuilena — lay down; dvāre — at the door.

TRANSLATION

Then Rāmānanda Rāya returned home, and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Govinda lay down in front of the door to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's room.
