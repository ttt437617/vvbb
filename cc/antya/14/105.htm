Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.105

"govardhana haite more ke ihāń ānila?

pāñā krishnera līlā dekhite nā pāila

SYNONYMS

govardhana haite — from Govardhana Hill; more — Me; ke — who; ihāń — here; ānila — brought; pāñā — getting; krishnera līlā — pastimes of Krishna; dekhite nā pāila — I could not see.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Who has brought Me here from Govardhana Hill? I was seeing Lord Krishna's pastimes, but now I cannot see them.
