Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.1

krishna-viccheda-vibhrāntyā

manasā vapushā dhiyā

yad yad vyadhatta gaurāńgas

tal-leśah kathyate 'dhunā

SYNONYMS

krishna-viccheda — of separation from Krishna; vibhrāntyā — by the bewilderment; manasā — by the mind; vapushā — by the body; dhiyā — by the intelligence; yat yat — whatever; vyadhatta — performed; gaurāńgah — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tat — of that; leśah — a very small fragment; kathyate — is being described; adhunā — now.

TRANSLATION

I shall now describe a very small portion of the activities performed by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu with His mind, intelligence and body when He was bewildered by strong feelings of separation from Krishna.
