Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.24

udiyā eka strī bhīde darśana nā pāñā

garude cadi' dekhe prabhura skandhe pada diyā

SYNONYMS

udiyā — a native of Orissa; eka — one; strī — woman; bhīde — in the crowd; darśana nā pāñā — being unable to see; garude cadi' — climbing up the column of Garuda; dekhe — sees; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; skandhe — on the shoulder; pada — her foot; diyā — placing.

TRANSLATION

Suddenly, a woman from Orissa, unable to see Lord Jagannātha because of the crowd, climbed the column of Garuda, placing her foot on Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's shoulder.
