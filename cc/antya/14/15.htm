Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.15

divyonmāde aiche haya, ki ihā vismaya?

adhirūdha-bhāve divyonmāda-pralāpa haya

SYNONYMS

divya-unmāde — in transcendental madness; aiche — such; haya — is; ki ihā vismaya — what is the wonder; adhirūdha-bhāve — in highly elevated love of Krishna; divya-unmāda — in transcendental madness; pralāpa — talking; haya — there is.

TRANSLATION

Such is the state of transcendental madness. Why is it difficult to understand? When one is highly elevated in love of Krishna, he becomes transcendentally mad and talks like a madman.
