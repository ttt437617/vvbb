Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.10

svarūpa — 'sūtra-kartā', raghunātha — 'vrittikāra'

tāra bāhulya varni — pāńji-tīkā-vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; sūtra-kartā — maker of short notes; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; vritti-kāra — maker of elaborate explanations; tāra — of those; bāhulya — more elaborately; varni — I shall describe; pāńji — fluffing out (as of cotton); tīkā — explanation; vyavahāra — behavior.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara wrote short notes, whereas Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī wrote elaborate descriptions. I shall now describe Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's activities more elaborately, as if fluffing out compressed cotton.

PURPORT

Pāńji-tīkā means further explanations of a subject. Writing such explanations is likened to the process of fluffing out cotton.
