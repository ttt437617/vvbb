Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.97

karańgera jale kare sarvāńga siñcana

bahirvāsa lañā kare ańga samvījana

SYNONYMS

karańgera jale — with water from a karańga waterpot; kare — does; sarva-ańga — all parts of the body; siñcana — sprinkling; bahirvāsa — covering cloth; lañā — taking; kare — does; ańga — the body; samvījana — fanning.

TRANSLATION

Govinda sprinkled water from a karańga waterpot all over the Lord's body, and then, taking His own outer garment, he began to fan Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
