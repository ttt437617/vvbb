Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.98

svarūpādi-gana tāhāń āsiyā mililā

prabhura avasthā dekhi' kāndite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-ādi-gana — the devotees, headed by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; tāhāń — there; āsiyā — coming; mililā — met; prabhura — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; avasthā — condition; dekhi' — seeing; kāndite lāgilā — began to cry.

TRANSLATION

When Svarūpa Dāmodara and the other devotees reached the spot and saw the condition of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, they began to cry.
