Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.107

govardhane cadi' krishna bājāilā venu

govardhanera caudike care saba dhenu

SYNONYMS

govardhane — on Govardhana Hill; cadi' — going up; krishna — Lord Krishna; bājāilā venu — played the flute; govardhanera — of Govardhana Hill; cau-dike — in four directions; care — graze; saba — all; dhenu — cows.

TRANSLATION

"I saw Lord Krishna climbing Govardhana Hill and playing His flute, surrounded on all sides by grazing cows.
