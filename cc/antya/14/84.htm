Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.84

eka-dina mahāprabhu samudre yāite

'cataka'-parvata dekhilena ācambite

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; samudre yāite — while going to the sea; cataka-parvata — the Cataka-parvata sand dune; dekhilena — saw; ācambite — suddenly.

TRANSLATION

One day, while Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was going to the sea to bathe, He suddenly saw a sand dune named Cataka-parvata.
