Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.106

ihāń haite āji mui genu govardhane

dekhoń, — yadi krishna karena godhana-cārane

SYNONYMS

ihāń haite — from here; āji — today; mui — I; genu — went; govardhane — to Govardhana Hill; dekhoń — I was searching; yadi — if; krishna — Lord Krishna; karena — does; godhana-cārane — tending the cows.

TRANSLATION

"Today I went from here to Govardhana Hill to find out if Krishna was tending His cows there.
