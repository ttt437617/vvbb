Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.11

tāte viśvāsa kari' śuna bhāvera varnana

ha-ibe bhāvera jñāna, pāibā prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

tāte — therefore; viśvāsa kari' — having faith; śuna — please hear; bhāvera varnana — description of ecstatic emotions; ha-ibe — there will be; bhāvera — of ecstatic emotions; jñāna — knowledge; pāibā — you will get; prema-dhana — love of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Please hear faithfully this description of Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic emotions. Thus you will come to know of His ecstatic love, and ultimately you will achieve love of Godhead.
