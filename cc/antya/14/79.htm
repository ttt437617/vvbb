Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.79

hena-kāle jagannāthera pāni-śańkha bājilā

snāna kari' mahāprabhu daraśane gelā

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; pāni-śańkha — a conchshell that can be held in the hand; bājilā — vibrated; snāna kari' — after bathing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; daraśane gelā — went to see Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

Just then, everyone heard the blowing of the conchshell at the Jagannātha temple. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately took His bath and went to see Lord Jagannātha.
