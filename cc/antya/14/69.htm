Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.69

svarūpa-gosāñi tabe ucca kariyā

prabhura kāne krishna-nāma kahe bhakta-gana lañā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; tabe — at that time; ucca kariyā — very loudly; prabhura kāne — in the ear of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; kahe — began to chant; bhakta-gana lañā — with all the other devotees.

TRANSLATION

When they saw this, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and all the other devotees began to chant the holy name of Krishna very loudly into Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ear.
