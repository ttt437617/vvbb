Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.12

krishna mathurāya gele, gopīra ye daśā haila

krishna-vicchede prabhura se daśā upajila

SYNONYMS

krishna mathurāya gele — when Lord Krishna departed for Mathurā; gopīra — of the gopīs; ye daśā — which condition; haila — was; krishna-vicchede — by separation from Krishna; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; se daśā — that situation; upajila — happened.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu felt separation from Krishna, His condition exactly corresponded to that of the gopīs in Vrindāvana after Krishna's departure for Mathurā.
