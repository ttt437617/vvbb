Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.57

ei-mata ardha-rātri kailā niryāpana

bhitara-prakoshthe prabhure karāilā śayana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; ardha-rātri — half the night; kailā niryāpana — passed; bhitara-prakoshthe — in the inner room; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karāilā śayana — they made to lie down.

TRANSLATION

After half the night had passed in this way, Rāmānanda Rāya and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lie down on His bed in the inner room.
