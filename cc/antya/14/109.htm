Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.109

rādhā lañā krishna praveśilā kandarāte

sakhī-gana kahe more phula uthāite

SYNONYMS

rādhā lañā — taking Śrīmatī Rādhārānī along; krishna — Lord Krishna; praveśilā — entered; kandarāte — a cave; sakhī-gana — the gopīs; kahe — said; more — unto Me; phula — flowers; uthāite — to pick up.

TRANSLATION

"When Krishna and Śrīmatī Rādhārānī entered a cave together, the other gopīs asked Me to pick some flowers.
