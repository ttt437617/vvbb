Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.35

prāpta-ratna hārāñā aiche vyagra ha-ilā

vishanna hañā prabhu nija-vāsā āilā

SYNONYMS

prāpta-ratna — the achieved jewel; hārāñā — having lost; aiche — in that way; vyagra ha-ilā — became very much agitated; vishanna hañā — being morose; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija — own; vāsā — to His residential place; āilā — returned.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya grew very much agitated, like a person who has just lost a recently acquired jewel. Then He became very morose and returned home.
