Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.13

uddhava-darśane yaiche rādhāra vilāpa

krame krame haila prabhura se unmāda-vilāpa

SYNONYMS

uddhava-darśane — by seeing Uddhava; yaiche — as; rādhāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; vilāpa — lamentation; krame krame — gradually; haila — became; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; se — that; unmāda-vilāpa — lamentation in madness.

TRANSLATION

The lamentation of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī when Uddhava visited Vrindāvana gradually became a feature of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's transcendental madness.
