Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.56

svarūpa-gosāñi kare krishna-līlā gāna

dui jane kichu kailā prabhura bāhya jñāna

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; kare — does; krishna-līlā — of the pastimes of Krishna; gāna — singing; dui jane — both of them; kichu — some; kailā — revived; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhya jñāna — external consciousness.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya recited verses from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, and Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī sang of Krishna's pastimes. In this way, they brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to external consciousness.
