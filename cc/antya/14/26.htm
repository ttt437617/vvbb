Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.26

'ādi-vasyā' ei strīre nā kara varjana

karuka yatheshta jagannātha daraśana

SYNONYMS

ādi-vasyā — uncivilized man, aborigine; ei — this; strīre — woman; nā kara varjana — do not forbid; karuka — let her do; yathā-ishta — as desired; jagannātha daraśana — seeing Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Govinda, "O ādi-vasyā [uncivilized man], do not forbid this woman to climb the Garuda-stambha. Let her see Lord Jagannātha to her satisfaction."

PURPORT

For another explanation of the word ādi-vasyā, refer to Antya-līlā, Chapter Ten, verse 116.
