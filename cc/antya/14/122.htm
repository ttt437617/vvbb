Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.122

sańkshepe kahiyā kari dik daraśana

yei ihā śune, pāya krishnera carana

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; kahiyā — describing; kari dik daraśana — I show an indication; yei — anyone who; ihā — this; śune — hears; pāya — gets; krishnera carana — the shelter of the lotus feet of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly described them just to give an indication of His transcendental pastimes. Nevertheless, anyone who hears this will certainly attain the shelter of Lord Krishna's lotus feet.
