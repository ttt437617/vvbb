Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.40

rātri haile svarūpa-rāmānande lañā

āpana manera bhāva kahe ughādiyā

SYNONYMS

rātri haile — when there was night; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; rāmānande — Rāmānanda Rāya; lañā — taking together; āpana manera — of His own mind; bhāva — the ecstatic feelings; kahe — speaks; ughādiyā — revealing.

TRANSLATION

At night, Lord Caitanya would reveal to Svarūpa Dāmodara and Rāmānanda Rāya the ecstatic feelings of His mind.
