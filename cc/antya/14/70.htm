Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.70

bahu-kshane krishna-nāma hridaye paśilā

'hari-bola' bali' prabhu garjiyā uthilā

SYNONYMS

bahu-kshane — after a long time; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Lord Krishna; hridaye paśilā — entered the heart; hari-bola bali' — saying "Haribol"; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; garjiyā — making a loud sound; uthilā — got up.

TRANSLATION

After they had chanted in this way for a long time, the holy name of Krishna entered the heart of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and He suddenly arose with a loud shout of "Haribol!"
