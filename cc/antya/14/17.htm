Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.17

eka-dina mahāprabhu kariyāchena śayana

krishna rāsa-līlā kare, — dekhilā svapana

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariyāchena śayana — was taking rest; krishna — Lord Krishna; rāsa-līlā kare — performs rāsa-līlā dance; dekhilā — He saw; svapana — a dream.

TRANSLATION

One day while He was resting, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu dreamed He saw Krishna performing His rāsa dance.
