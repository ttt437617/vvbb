Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.62

simha-dvārera uttara-diśāya āche eka thāñi

tāra madhye padi' āchena caitanya-gosāñi

SYNONYMS

simha-dvārera — of the gate known as Simha-dvāra; uttara-diśāya — on the northern side; āche — there is; eka thāñi — a place; tāra madhye — within that place; padi' — lying down; āchena — was; caitanya-gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

After searching for some time, they came upon Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lying in a corner by the northern side of the Simha-dvāra gate.
