Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.118

ei ta' kahiluń prabhura divyonmāda-bhāva

brahmāo kahite nāre yāhāra prabhāva

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; kahiluń — I have described; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; divya-unmāda-bhāva — transcendental ecstatic emotions; brahmāo — even Lord Brahmā; kahite nāre — cannot speak; yāhāra — of which; prabhāva — the influence.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described the transcendental ecstatic emotions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Even Lord Brahmā cannot describe their influence.
