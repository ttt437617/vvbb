Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.22

dehābhyāse nitya-kritya kari' samāpana

kāle yāi' kailā jagannātha daraśana

SYNONYMS

deha-abhyāse — as a habit; nitya-kritya — the daily duties; kari' samāpana — finishing; kāle — at the proper time; yāi' — going; kailā — performed; jagannātha daraśana — seeing Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His customary daily duties, and at the usual time He went to see Lord Jagannātha in the temple.
