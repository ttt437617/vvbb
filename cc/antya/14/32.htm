Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.32

svapnera darśanāveśe tad-rūpa haila mana

yāhāń tāhāń dekhe sarvatra muralī-vadana

SYNONYMS

svapnera — of the dream; darśana-āveśe — by being fully absorbed in the vision; tat-rūpa — like that; haila mana — the mind became; yāhāń tāhāń — anywhere and everywhere; dekhe — sees; sarvatra — all around; muralī-vadana — Krishna with His flute to His mouth.

TRANSLATION

Becoming fully absorbed in that vision, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had assumed the mood of the gopīs, so much so that everywhere He looked He saw Krishna standing with His flute to His lips.
