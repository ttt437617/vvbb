Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.61

cintita ha-ila sabe prabhure nā dekhiyā

prabhu cāhi' bule sabe deutī jvāliyā

SYNONYMS

cintita ha-ila — became very anxious; sabe — all the devotees; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā dekhiyā — not seeing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; cāhi' — searching for; bule — wander; sabe — all of them; deutī — lamp; jvāliyā — lighting.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees were very anxious when they saw that the Lord was not in His room. They wandered about searching for Him with a warning lamp.
