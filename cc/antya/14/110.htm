Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.110

hena-kāle tumi-saba kolāhala kailā

tāhāń haite dhari' more ihāń lañā āilā

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; tumi-saba — all of you; kolāhala kailā — made a tumultuous sound; tāhāń haite — from there; dhari' — catching; more — Me; ihāń — here; lañā āilā — you have brought.

TRANSLATION

"Just then, all of you made a tumultuous sound and carried Me from there to this place.
