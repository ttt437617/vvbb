Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.4

jaya svarūpa, śrīvāsādi prabhu-bhakta-gana

śakti deha', — kari yena caitanya-varnana

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; svarūpa — to Svarūpa Dāmodara; śrīvāsa-ādi — headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura; prabhu-bhakta-gana — to the devotees of the Lord; śakti deha' — please give strength; kari — I can make; yena — so that; caitanya-varnana — description of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Svarūpa Dāmodara and all the other devotees, headed by Śrīvāsa Thākura! Please give me the strength to describe the character of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
