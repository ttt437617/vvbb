Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.87

ei śloka padi' prabhu calena vāyu-vege

govinda dhāila pāche, nāhi pāya lāge

SYNONYMS

ei śloka — this verse; padi' — reciting; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calena — goes; vāyu-vege — at the speed of the wind; govinda — Govinda; dhāila — ran; pāche — behind; nāhi pāya lāge — could not catch.

TRANSLATION

Reciting this verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ran toward the sand dune as fast as the wind. Govinda ran after Him, but he could not approach Him.
