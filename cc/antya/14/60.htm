Antya-līlāChapter 14: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's Feelings of Separation from Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 14.60

śabda nā pāñā svarūpa kapāta kailā dūre

tina-dvāra deoyā āche, prabhu nāhi ghare!

SYNONYMS

śabda — sound; nā — not; pāñā — hearing; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; kapāta — the door; kailā dūre — opened; tina-dvāra — three doors; deoyā āche — were locked; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāhi ghare — was not within the room.

TRANSLATION

After some time, Svarūpa Dāmodara could no longer hear Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanting. When he entered the room, he found the three doors locked, but Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was gone.
