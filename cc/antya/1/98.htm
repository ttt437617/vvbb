Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.98

sei patre prabhu eka śloka ye dekhilā

paditei śloka, preme āvishta ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

sei patre — on that palm leaf; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; eka śloka — one verse; ye — which; dekhilā — He saw; paditei — by reading; śloka — the verse; preme — ecstatic love; āvishta ha-ilā — was overwhelmed.

TRANSLATION

While reading the manuscript, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw a verse on that page, and as soon as He read it He was overwhelmed by ecstatic love.
