Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.130

prabhu kahe, — "kaha, kene kara sańkoca-lāje?

granthera phala śunāibā vaishnava-samāje?"

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; kaha — speak up; kene — why; kara — you do; sańkoca-lāje — in shame and embarrassment; granthera — of the book; phala — the fruit; śunāibā — you should make heard; vaishnava-samāje — in the society of pure devotees.

TRANSLATION

The Lord, however, encouraged Rūpa Gosvāmī, saying, "Why are you embarrassed? You should recite it so the devotees can hear the good fruit of your writing."
