Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.199

sabe kripā kari' ińhāre deha' ei vara

vraja-līlā-prema-rasa yena varne nirantara

SYNONYMS

sabe — all of you; kripā kari' — showing your mercy; ińhāre — unto Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; deha' — give; ei vara — this benediction; vraja-līlā-prema-rasa — the transcendental mellows of the pastimes of Vrindāvana; yena — so that; varne — he can describe; nirantara — without cessation.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu requested all His personal associates to bless Rūpa Gosvāmī so that he might continuously describe the pastimes of Vrindāvana, which are full of emotional love of Godhead.
