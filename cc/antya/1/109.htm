Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.109

bhakta-sańge prabhu āilā, dekhi' dui jana

dandavat hañā kailā carana vandana

SYNONYMS

bhakta-sańge — accompanied by other devotee associates; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; dekhi' — seeing this; dui jana — Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa Thākura; dandavat hañā — falling flat like logs; kailā — did; carana vandana — prayers to their lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

When Haridāsa Thākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī saw that Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu had come with His intimate devotees, they both immediately fell down like logs and offered prayers to their lotus feet.
