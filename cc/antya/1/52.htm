Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.52

prayāge śuniluń, — teńho gelā vrindāvane

anupamera gańgā-prāpti kaila nivedane"

SYNONYMS

prayāge — in Prayāga; śuniluń — I heard; teńho — he; gelā vrindāvane — has gone to Vrindāvana; anupamera — of Anupama; gańgā-prāpti — getting the mercy of the Ganges (death); kaila nivedane — he informed.

TRANSLATION

"In Prayāga I heard that he had already gone to Vrindāvana." Rūpa Gosvāmī next informed the Lord about the death of Anupama.
