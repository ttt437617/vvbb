Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.147

rāya kahe, — "kaha dekhi bhāvera svabhāva?"

rūpa kahe, — "aiche haya krishna-vishayaka 'bhāva'"

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya says; kaha — kindly recite; dekhi — so that I may see; bhāvera svabhāva — the characteristic of emotional love; rūpa kahe — Rūpa Gosvāmī replies; aiche — such; haya — is; krishna-vishayaka — concerning Krishna; bhāva — emotional love.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya inquired, "What are the characteristics of emotional love?"

Rūpa Gosvāmī replied, "This is the nature of emotional love for Krishna:
