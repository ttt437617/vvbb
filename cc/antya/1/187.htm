Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.187

rāya kahe, — "kaha āge ańgera viśesha"

śrī-rūpa kahena kichu sańkshepa-uddeśa

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya says; kaha — please tell me; āge — further; ańgera viśesha — particular portions; śrī-rūpa kahena — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī says; kichu — something; sańkshepa — in brief; uddeśa — reference.

TRANSLATION

When Rāmānanda Rāya requested Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī to speak further about various portions of the play, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī briefly quoted his Lalita-mādhava.
