Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.43

svapna dekhi' rūpa-gosāñi karilā vicāra

satyabhāmāra ājñā — prithak nātaka karibāra

SYNONYMS

svapna dekhi' — after dreaming; rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; karilā vicāra — considered; satyabhāmāra ājñā — the order of Śrīmatī Satyabhāmā; prithak nātaka karibāra — to write a separate drama.

TRANSLATION

After having that dream, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī considered, "It is the order of Satyabhāmā that I write a separate drama for her.
