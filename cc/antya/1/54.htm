Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.54

āra dina mahāprabhu saba bhakta lañā

rūpe milāilā sabāya kripā ta' kariyā

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; saba — all; bhakta lañā — taking the devotees; rūpe milāilā — introduced Rūpa Gosvāmī; sabāya — to all of them; kripā ta' kariyā — showing His mercy.

TRANSLATION

On the next day, Caitanya Mahāprabhu again met Rūpa Gosvāmī, and with great mercy the Lord introduced him to all the devotees.
