Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.106

sārvabhauma-rāmānande parīkshā karite

śrī-rūpera guna duńhāre lāgilā kahite

SYNONYMS

sārvabhauma-rāmānande — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya; parīkshā karite — to examine; śrī-rūpera guna — the transcendental qualities of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; duńhāre — unto both of them; lāgilā kahite — He began to praise.

TRANSLATION

Just to examine Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya and Rāmānanda Rāya, the Lord began to praise the transcendental qualities of Śrī Rūpa Gosvāmī before them.
