Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.194

prema-paripātī ei adbhuta varnana

śuni' citta-karnera haya ānanda-ghūrnana

SYNONYMS

prema-paripātī — a first-class arrangement to express loving affairs; ei — this; adbhuta varnana — wonderful description; śuni' — hearing; citta-karnera — of the heart and the ear; haya — there is; ānanda-ghūrnana — a whirlpool of transcendental bliss.

TRANSLATION

"The wonderful descriptions of Rūpa Gosvāmī are superb arrangements to express loving affairs. Hearing them will plunge the heart and ears of everyone into a whirlpool of transcendental bliss.
