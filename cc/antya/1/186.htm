Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.186

padāni tv agatārthāni

tad-artha-gataye narāh

yojayanti padair anyaih

sa udghātyaka ucyate

SYNONYMS

padāni — words; tu — but; agata-arthāni — having an unclear meaning; tat — that; artha-gataye — to understand the meaning; narāh — men; yojayanti — join; padaih — with words; anyaih — other; sah — that; udghātyakah — udghātyaka; ucyate — is called.

TRANSLATION

"'To explain an unclear word, men generally join it with other words. Such an attempt is called udghātyaka.'"

PURPORT

This verse is quoted from the Sāhitya-darpana (6.289).
