Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.23

cāhiyā nā pāila kukkura, loka saba āilā

duhkhī hañā śivānanda upavāsa kailā

SYNONYMS

cāhiyā — looking; nā — not; pāila — found; kukkura — the dog; loka saba āilā — all the men returned; duhkhī hañā — being unhappy; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; upavāsa — fast; kailā — observed.

TRANSLATION

When the men returned without success, Śivānanda Sena became very unhappy and fasted for the night.
