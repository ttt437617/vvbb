Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.39

anupamera lāgi' tāńra kichu vilamba ha-ila

bhakta-gana-pāśa āilā, lāg nā pāila

SYNONYMS

anupamera lāgi' — on account of the passing away of Anupama; tāńra — of Rūpa Gosvāmī; kichu — some; vilamba — delay; ha-ila — there was; bhakta-gana-pāśa — to the devotees in Bengal; āilā — came; lāg nā pāila — he could not contact them.

TRANSLATION

There was some delay because of the death of Anupama, and therefore when Rūpa Gosvāmī went to Bengal to see the devotees there, he could not get in touch with them because they had already left.
