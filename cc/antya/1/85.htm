Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.85

sei śloka lañā prabhu svarūpe dekhāilā

svarūpera parīkshā lāgi' tāńhāre puchilā

SYNONYMS

sei śloka — that verse; lañā — taking; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svarūpe dekhāilā — showed to Svarūpa Dāmodara; svarūpera — of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; parīkshā lāgi' — for the examination; tāńhāre puchilā — He inquired from him.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took that verse and showed it to Svarūpa Dāmodara for him to examine. Then the Lord questioned him.
