Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.192

eta śuni' rāya kahe prabhura carane

rūpera kavitva praśamsi' sahasra-vadane

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kahe — says; prabhura carane — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rūpera — of Rūpa Gosvāmī; kavitva — poetic art; praśamsi' — glorifying; sahasra-vadane — as if with a thousand mouths.

TRANSLATION

After hearing this, Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya submitted at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu the superexcellence of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's poetic expression and began to praise it as if he had thousands of mouths.
