Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.50

sanātanera vārtā yabe gosāñi puchila

rūpa kahe, — 'tāra sańge dekhā nā ha-ila

SYNONYMS

sanātanera vārtā — news of Sanātana Gosvāmī; yabe — when; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; puchila — inquired; rūpa kahe — Rūpa Gosvāmī says; tāra sańge — with him; dekhā nā ha-ila — there was no meeting.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired about Sanātana Gosvāmī, Rūpa Gosvāmī replied, "I did not meet him.
