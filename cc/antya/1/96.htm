Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.96

'kyā puńthi likha?' bali' eka-patra nilā

akshara dekhiyā prabhu mane sukhī hailā

SYNONYMS

kyā — what; puńthi — book; likha — you are writing; bali' — saying this; eka-patra nilā — took one page written on a palm leaf; akshara — the good handwriting; dekhiyā — seeing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mane — in the mind; sukhī hailā — became very happy.

TRANSLATION

The Lord inquired, "What kind of book are you writing?" He held up a palm leaf that was a page of the manuscript, and when He saw the fine handwriting, His mind was very pleased.
