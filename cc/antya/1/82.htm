Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.82

śloka padi' prabhu sukhe premāvishta hailā

hena-kāle rūpa-gosāñi snāna kari' āilā

SYNONYMS

śloka padi' — reading this verse; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sukhe — in great happiness; prema-āvishta hailā — became overwhelmed by ecstatic love; hena-kāle — at that time; rūpa-gosāñi — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; snāna kari' — after taking his bath; āilā — came back.

TRANSLATION

After reading the verse, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed by ecstatic love. At that very time, Rūpa Gosvāmī returned, having finished bathing in the sea.
