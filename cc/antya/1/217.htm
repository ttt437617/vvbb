Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.217

"vrindāvane yāha' tumi, rahiha vrindāvane

ekabāra ihāń pāthāiha sanātane

SYNONYMS

vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; yāha' — now go; tumi — you; rahiha — stay; vrindāvane — in Vrindāvana; eka-bāra — once; ihāń — here; pāthāiha — send; sanātane — your elder brother, Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

"Now go to Vrindāvana and stay there," the Lord said. "You may send here your elder brother, Sanātana.
