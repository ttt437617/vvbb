Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.115

rāya, bhattācārya bale, — "tomāra prasāda vine

tomāra hridaya ei jānila kemane

SYNONYMS

rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; bhattācārya — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; bale — say; tomāra prasāda vine — without Your special mercy; tomāra hridaya — Your mind; ei — this Rūpa Gosvāmī; jānila — understood; kemane — how.

TRANSLATION

After hearing this verse, Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya said to Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "Without Your special mercy, how could this Rūpa Gosvāmī have understood Your mind?"
