Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.88

prabhu kahe, — "ińho āmāya prayāge milila

yogya-pātra jāni ińhāya mora kripā ta' ha-ila

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replies; ińho — Rūpa Gosvāmī; āmāya — with Me; prayāge — at Prayāga; milila — met; yogya-pātra jāni — knowing him to be a suitable person; ińhāya — unto him; mora — My; kripā ta' ha-ila — there was mercy.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "Rūpa Gosvāmī met Me at Prayāga. Knowing him to be a suitable person, I naturally bestowed My mercy upon him.
