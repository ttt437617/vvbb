Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.201

tomāra yaiche vishaya-tyāga, taiche tāńra rīti

dainya-vairāgya-pāndityera tāńhātei sthiti

SYNONYMS

tomāra — your; yaiche — just as; vishaya-tyāga — renunciation of material connections; taiche — similarly; tāńra rīti — his manner of activity; dainya — humbleness; vairāgya — renunciation; pāndityera — of learned scholarship; tāńhātei — in him; sthiti — existing.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Rāmānanda Rāya, "Sanātana Gosvāmī's renunciation of material connections is just like yours. Humility, renunciation and excellent learning exist in him simultaneously.
