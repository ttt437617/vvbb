Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.26

sabā lañā kailā jagannātha daraśana

sabā lañā mahāprabhu karena bhojana

SYNONYMS

sabā lañā — taking all of them; kailā — did; jagannātha daraśana — visiting the Jagannātha temple; sabā lañā — with all of them; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena bhojana — took prasādam.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went with them to see the Lord in the temple, and on that day He also took lunch in the company of all those devotees.
