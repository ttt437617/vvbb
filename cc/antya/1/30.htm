Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.30

śasya khāya kukkura, 'krishna' kahe bāra bāra

dekhiyā lokera mane haila camatkāra

SYNONYMS

śasya khāya — eats the pulp of green coconut; kukkura — the dog; krishna — the holy name of Krishna; kahe — chants; bāra bāra — again and again; dekhiyā — seeing this; lokera — of all the people; mane — in the minds; haila — there was; camatkāra — astonishment.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the dog eating the green coconut pulp and chanting "Krishna, Krishna" again and again, all the devotees present were very surprised.
