Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.44

vraja-pura-līlā ekatra kariyāchi ghatanā

dui bhāga kari' ebe karimu racanā

SYNONYMS

vraja-pura-līlā — Lord Krishna's pastimes in Vraja and Dvārakā; ekatra — in one place; kariyāchi — I have collected; ghatanā — all the events; dui bhāga kari' — dividing into two different parts; ebe — now; karimu racanā — I shall write.

TRANSLATION

"I have brought together in one work all the pastimes performed by Lord Krishna in Vrindāvana and in Dvārakā. Now I shall have to divide them into two dramas."
