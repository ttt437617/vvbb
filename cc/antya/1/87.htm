Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.87

anyathā e artha kāra nāhi haya jñāna

tumi pūrve kripā kailā, kari anumāna"

SYNONYMS

anyathā — otherwise; e artha — this confidential meaning; kāra — of anyone; nāhi — not; haya — is; jñāna — the knowledge; tumi — You; pūrve — before this; kripā kailā — bestowed mercy; kari anumāna — I can conjecture.

TRANSLATION

"No one could otherwise understand this meaning. I can therefore guess that previously You bestowed upon him Your causeless mercy."
