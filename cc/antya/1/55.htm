Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.55

sabāra carana rūpa karilā vandana

kripā kari' rūpe sabe kailā ālińgana

SYNONYMS

sabāra — of all the devotees; carana — to the lotus feet; rūpa — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; karilā vandana — offered prayers; kripā kari' — showing great mercy; rūpe — Rūpa Gosvāmī; sabe — all the devotees; kailā — did; ālińgana — embracing.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī offered his respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of them all, and all the devotees, by their mercy, embraced him.
