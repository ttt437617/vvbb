Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.64

govinda-dvārā prabhura śesha-prasāda pāilā

preme matta dui-jana nācite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

govinda-dvārā — through Govinda; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha-prasāda — remnants of food; pāilā — they got; preme matta — overwhelmed by ecstasy; dui-jana — both of them; nācite lāgilā — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

When they received the remnants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's prasādam through Govinda, they respected it, and then they both began to dance in ecstasy.
