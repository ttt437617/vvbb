Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.91

phalena phala-kāranam anumīyate

SYNONYMS

phalena — by the result; phala-kāranam — the origin of the result; anumīyate — one can guess.

TRANSLATION

"'By seeing a result, one can understand the cause of that result.'

PURPORT

This verse is from the doctrines of Nyāya, or logic.
