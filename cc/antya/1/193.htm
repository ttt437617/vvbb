Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.193

"kavitva nā haya ei amritera dhāra

nātaka-lakshana saba siddhāntera sāra

SYNONYMS

kavitva — poetic art; nā haya — is not; ei — this; amritera dhāra — constant shower of nectar; nātaka — a drama; lakshana — appearing as; saba — all; siddhāntera sāra — essences of ultimate realization.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya said, "This is not a poetic presentation; it is a continuous shower of nectar. Indeed, it is the essence of all ultimate realizations, appearing in the form of plays.
