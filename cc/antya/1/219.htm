Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.219

krishna-sevā, rasa-bhakti kariha pracāra

āmiha dekhite tāhāń yāimu ekabāra"

SYNONYMS

krishna-sevā — the service of Lord Krishna; rasa-bhakti — devotional service; kariha pracāra — preach; āmiha — I also; dekhite — to see; tāhāń — there to Vrindāvana; yāimu — I shall go; eka-bāra — once more.

TRANSLATION

"Establish the service of Lord Krishna and preach the mellows of Lord Krishna's devotional service. I shall also go to Vrindāvana once more."
