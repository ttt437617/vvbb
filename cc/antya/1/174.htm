Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.174

tomāra āge dhārshtya ei mukha-vyādāna"

eta bali' nāndī-śloka karilā vyākhyāna

SYNONYMS

tomāra āge — before you; dhārishtya — impudence; ei — this; mukha-vyādāna — simply opening the mouth; eta bali' — saying this; nāndī-śloka — the introductory verses; karilā vyākhyāna — explained.

TRANSLATION

"It is even impudent for me to open my mouth before you." Then, having said this, he recited the introductory verse of the Lalita-mādhava.
