Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.100

śloka śuni haridāsa ha-ilā ullāsī

nācite lāgilā ślokera artha praśamsi'

SYNONYMS

śloka śuni' — hearing this verse; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; ha-ilā ullāsī — became very jubilant; nācite lāgilā — he began to dance; ślokera — of the verse; artha praśamsi' — praising the meaning.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu chanted this verse, Haridāsa Thākura, upon hearing the vibration, became jubilant and began to dance while praising its meaning.
