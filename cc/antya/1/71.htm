Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.71

dui 'nāndī' 'prastāvanā', dui 'samghatanā'

prithak kariyā likhi kariyā bhāvanā

SYNONYMS

dui nāndī — two invocations of good fortune; prastāvanā — introductions; dui — two; samghatanā — chains of events; prithak kariyā — making separate; likhi — I shall write; kariyā bhāvanā — thinking about them.

TRANSLATION

"I shall write two separate invocations of good fortune and two different introductions. Let me think deeply about the matter and then describe two different sets of incidents."

PURPORT

The two works are the Vidagdha-mādhava and the Lalita-mādhava. The Vidagdha-mādhava describes pastimes in Vrindāvana, and the Lalita-mādhava describes pastimes in Dvārakā and Mathurā.
