Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.84

'gūdha mora hridaya tuñi jānilā kemane?'

eta kahi' rūpe kailā dridha ālińgane

SYNONYMS

gūdha — very confidential; mora — My; hridaya — heart; tuñi — you; jānilā — knew; kemane — how; eta kahi' — saying this; rūpe — to Rūpa Gosvāmī; kailā — did; dridha ālińgane — firm embracing.

TRANSLATION

"My heart is very confidential. How did you know My mind in this way?" After saying this, He firmly embraced Rūpa Gosvāmī.
