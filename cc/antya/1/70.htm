Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.70

pūrve dui nātaka chila ekatra racanā

dui-bhāga kari ebe karimu ghatanā

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; dui nātaka — two dramas; chila — there was; ekatra — together; racanā — composition; dui-bhāga kari — dividing into two; ebe — now; karimu ghatanā — I shall write the incidents.

TRANSLATION

"Formerly I wrote the two dramas as one composition. Now I shall divide it and describe the incidents in two separate works.
