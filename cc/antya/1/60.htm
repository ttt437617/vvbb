Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.60

ishta-goshthī duńhā sane kari' kata-kshana

madhyāhna karite prabhu karilā gamana

SYNONYMS

ishta-goshthī — conversation; duńhā sane — with both Rūpa Gosvāmī and Haridāsa; kari' — doing; kata-kshana — for some time; madhya-ahna karite — to execute daily noontime duties; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karilā gamana — left that place.

TRANSLATION

He would talk for some time with them both and then leave to perform His noontime duties.
