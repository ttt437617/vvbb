Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.94

eka-dina rūpa karena nātaka likhana

ācambite mahāprabhura haila āgamana

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; karena — does; nātaka — drama; likhana — writing; ācambite — all of a sudden; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; haila — there was; āgamana — the coming.

TRANSLATION

One day while Rūpa Gosvāmī was writing his book, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly appeared.
