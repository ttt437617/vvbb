Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.29

prasāda nārikela-śasya dena phelāñā

'rāma' 'krishna' 'hari' kaha' — balena hāsiyā

SYNONYMS

prasāda — food; nārikela-śasya — pulp of green coconut; dena — gives; phelāñā — throwing; rāma — Lord Rāmacandra; krishna — Śrī Krishna; hari — the holy name of Hari; kaha' — say; balena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; hāsiyā — smiling.

TRANSLATION

Furthermore, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was throwing remnants of green coconut pulp to the dog. Smiling in His own way, He was saying to the dog, "Chant the holy names 'Rāma,' 'Krishna' and 'Hari.'"
