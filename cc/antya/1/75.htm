Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.75

sāmānya eka śloka prabhu padena kīrtane

kene śloka pade — ihā keha nāhi jāne

SYNONYMS

sāmānya — generally; eka — one; śloka — verse; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; padena — recites; kīrtane — while chanting; kene — why; śloka — that verse; pade — He recites; ihā — this; keha nāhi jāne — no one knows.

TRANSLATION

Generally Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu recited a verse while dancing and chanting before the ratha, but no one knew why He was reciting that particular verse.
