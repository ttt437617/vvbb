Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.221

prabhura bhakta-gana-pāśe vidāya la-ilā

punarapi gauda-pathe vrindāvane āilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana-pāśe — from the devotees; vidāya la-ilā — took leave; punarapi — again; gauda-pathe — by the way through Bengal; vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; āilā — returned.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī took leave of all the devotees of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and returned to Vrindāvana by the path through Bengal.
