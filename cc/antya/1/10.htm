Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.10

madhya-līlā-madhye antya-līlā-sūtra-gana

pūrva-granthe sańkshepete kariyāchi varnana

SYNONYMS

madhya-līlā-madhye — within the madhya-līlā chapters; antya-līlā-sūtra-gana — a synopsis of the antya-līlā; pūrva-granthe — in the previous chapter; sańkshepete — in brief; kariyāchi varnana — I have described.

TRANSLATION

I have briefly described the antya-līlā within the description of the madhya-līlā.
