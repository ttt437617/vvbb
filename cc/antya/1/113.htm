Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.113

svarūpa-gosāñi tabe sei śloka padila

śuni' sabākāra citte camatkāra haila

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; tabe — then; sei — that; śloka padila — recited the verse; śuni' — hearing this; sabākāra — of all of them; citte — in the minds; camatkāra haila — there was great wonder.

TRANSLATION

Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī recited the verse, and when all the devotees heard it, their minds were struck with wonder.
