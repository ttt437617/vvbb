Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.89

tabe śakti sañcāri' āmi kailuń upadeśa

tumiha kahio ihāńya rasera viśesha"

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; śakti sañcāri' — empowering him with My transcendental potency; āmi — I; kailuń upadeśa — gave instruction; tumiha — you also; kahio — inform; ihāńya — unto him; rasera viśesha — particular information about transcendental mellows.

TRANSLATION

"I thereupon also bestowed upon him My transcendental potency. Now you also should give him instructions. In particular, instruct him in transcendental mellows."
