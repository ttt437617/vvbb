Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.156

rāya kahe, — "vrindāvana, muralī-nihsvana

krishna, rādhikāra kaiche kariyācha varnana?

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya says; vrindāvana — the place named Vrindāvana; muralī-nihsvana — the vibration of Krishna's flute; krishna — Lord Krishna; rādhikāra — of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; kaiche — how; kariyācha varnana — you have described.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya further inquired, "How have you described Vrindāvana, the vibration of the transcendental flute, and the relationship between Krishna and Rādhikā?
