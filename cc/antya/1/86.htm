Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.86

'mora antara-vārtā rūpa jānila kemane?'

svarūpa kahe — "jāni, kripā kariyācha āpane

SYNONYMS

mora antara-vārtā — My internal intentions; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; jānila — knew; kemane — how; svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa replied; jāni — I can understand; kripā kariyācha — You have bestowed Your mercy; āpane — personally.

TRANSLATION

"How could Rūpa Gosvāmī have understood My heart?" the Lord asked.

Svarūpa Dāmodara replied, "I can understand that You have already bestowed Your causeless mercy upon him.
