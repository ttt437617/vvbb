Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.12

pūrva-likhita grantha-sūtra-anusāre

yei nāhi likhi, tāhā likhiye vistāre

SYNONYMS

pūrva-likhita — previously mentioned; grantha-sūtra — the synopsis of the pastimes; anusāre — according to; yei — whatever; nāhi likhi — I have not mentioned; tāhā — that; likhiye — I shall write; vistāre — elaborately.

TRANSLATION

In accordance with the synopsis previously written, I shall describe in detail whatever I have not mentioned.
