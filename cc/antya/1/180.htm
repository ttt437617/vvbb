Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.180

rāya kahe, — "rūpera kāvya amritera pūra

tāra madhye eka bindu diyāche karpūra"

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya says; rūpera kāvya — the poetic expression of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; amritera pūra — filled with all nectar; tāra madhye — within that; eka bindu — one drop; diyāche — he has given; karpūra — camphor.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya objected, "It is not alkali at all. It is a particle of camphor he has put into the nectar of his exalted poetic expression."
