Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.17

eka kukkura cale śivānanda-sane

bhakshya diyā lañā cale kariyā pālane

SYNONYMS

eka — one; kukkura — dog; cale — goes; śivānanda-sane — with Śivānanda Sena; bhakshya — food; diyā — giving; lañā — taking; cale — goes; kariyā pālane — maintaining the dog.

TRANSLATION

While going to Jagannātha Purī, Śivānanda Sena allowed a dog to go with him. He supplied it food to eat and maintained it.
