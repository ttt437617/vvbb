Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.173

rūpa kahe, — "kāhāń tumi sūryopama bhāsa

muñi kon kshudra, — yena khadyota-prakāśa

SYNONYMS

rūpa kahe — Rūpa Gosvāmī says; kāhāń — where; tumi — you; sūrya-upama — like the sun; bhāsa — brilliance; muñi — I; kon — some; kshudra — insignificant; yena — exactly like; khadyota-prakāśa — the brilliance of the glowworm.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī said, "In your presence, which is just like brilliant sunshine, I am as insignificant as the light of a glowworm.
