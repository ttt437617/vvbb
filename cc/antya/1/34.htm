Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.34

ethā prabhu-ājñāya rūpa āilā vrindāvana

krishna-līlā-nātaka karite haila mana

SYNONYMS

ethā — on the other side; prabhu-ājñāya — upon the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu: rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; āilā — went; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; krishna-līlā-nātaka — a drama on Lord Krishna's pastimes; karite — to compose; haila — it was; mana — the mind.

TRANSLATION

Meanwhile, following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī returned to Vrindāvana. He desired to write dramas concerning the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
