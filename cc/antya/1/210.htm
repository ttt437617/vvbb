Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.210

haridāsa kahe, — "tomāra bhāgyera nāhi sīmā

ye saba varnilā, ihāra ke jāne mahimā?"

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kahe — Haridāsa Thākura says; tomāra — your; bhāgyera — of fortune; nāhi sīmā — there is no limit; ye — whatever; saba — all; varnilā — you have described; ihāra — of this; ke jāne — who can understand; mahimā — the glories.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura told him, "There is no limit to your good fortune. No one can understand the glories of what you have described."
