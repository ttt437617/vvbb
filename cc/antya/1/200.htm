Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.200

ińhāra ye jyeshtha-bhrātā, nāma — 'sanātana'

prithivīte vijña-vara nāhi tāńra sama

SYNONYMS

ińhāra — of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; ye — who; jyeshtha-bhrātā — the elder brother; nāma — named; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; prithivīte — on the surface of the world; vijña-vara — most learned; nāhi — there is none; tāńra sama — equal to him.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's elder brother, whose name is Sanātana Gosvāmī, is such a wise and learned scholar that no one is equal to him."
