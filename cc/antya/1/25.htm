Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.25

utkanthāya cali' sabe āilā nīlācale

pūrvavat mahāprabhu mililā sakale

SYNONYMS

utkanthāya — in great anxiety; cali' — walking; sabe — all the devotees; āilā — came to Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala; nīlācale — as usual; pūrvavat — as usual; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mililā sakale — met all of them.

TRANSLATION

Thus in great anxiety they all walked to Jagannātha Purī, where Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu met them as usual.
