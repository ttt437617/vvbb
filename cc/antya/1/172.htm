Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.172

rāya kahe, — "tomāra kavitva amritera dhāra

dvitīya nātakera kaha nāndī-vyavahāra"

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya says; tomāra — you; kavitva — superexcellence in poetic presentation; amritera dhāra — a continuous shower of nectar; dvitīya nātakera — of the second drama; kaha — please tell; nāndī-vyavahāra — treatment of the introduction.

TRANSLATION

Having heard these verses recited by Rūpa Gosvāmī, Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya said, "Your poetic expressions are like continuous showers of nectar. Kindly let me hear the introductory portion of the second drama."
