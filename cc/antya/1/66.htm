Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.66

'krishnere bāhira nāhi kariha vraja haite

vraja chādi' krishna kabhu nā yāna kāhāńte

SYNONYMS

krishnere — Krishna; bāhira — outside; nāhi — do not; kariha — take; vraja haite — from Vrindāvana; vraja chādi' — leaving Vrindāvana; krishna — Lord Krishna; kabhu — at any time; nā — not; yāna — goes; kāhāńte — anywhere.

TRANSLATION

"Do not try to take Krishna out of Vrindāvana, for He does not go anywhere else at any time.
