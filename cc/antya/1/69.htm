Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.69

"prithak nātaka karite satyabhāmā ājñā dila

jānilu, prithak nātaka karite prabhu-ājñā haila

SYNONYMS

prithak nātaka — different dramas; karite — to write; satyabhāmā — Satyabhāmā; ājñā dila — ordered; jānilu — now I understand; prithak nātaka — different dramas; karite — to write; prabhu-ājñā — the order of the Lord; haila — there was.

TRANSLATION

"Satyabhāmā ordered me to write two different dramas," Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī thought. "Now I understand that this order has been confirmed by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
