Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.57

tomā-duńhāra kripāte ińhāra ha-u taiche śakti

yāte vivarite pārena krishna-rasa-bhakti

SYNONYMS

tomā-duńhāra kripāte — by the mercy of both of You; ińhāra — of Rūpa Gosvāmī; ha-u — let there be; taiche — such; śakti — power; yāte — by which; vivarite — to describe; pārena — is able; krishna-rasa-bhakti — the transcendental mellows of devotional service.

TRANSLATION

"May Rūpa Gosvāmī, by Your mercy, become so powerful that he will be able to describe the transcendental mellows of devotional service."
