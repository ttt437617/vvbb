Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.46

haridāsa-thākura tāńre bahu-kripā kailā

'tumi āsibe, — more prabhu ye kahilā'

SYNONYMS

haridāsa-thākura — Haridāsa Thākura; tāńre — unto him; bahu-kripā kailā — showed much affection because of love and mercy; tumi āsibe — you will come; more — me; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ye — that; kahilā — informed.

TRANSLATION

Out of affectionate love and mercy, Haridāsa Thākura told Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has already informed me that you would come here."
