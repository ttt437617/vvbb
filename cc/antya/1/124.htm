Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.124

svarūpa kahe, — "krishna-līlāra nātaka karite

vraja-līlā-pura-līlā ekatra varnite

SYNONYMS

svarūpa kahe — Svarūpa Dāmodara replied on behalf of Rūpa Gosvāmī; krishna-līlāra — of the pastimes of Lord Krishna; nātaka karite — composing a drama; vraja-līlā-pura-līlā — His pastimes in Vrindāvana and His pastimes in Mathurā and Dvārakā; ekatra — in one book; varnite — to describe.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara replied for Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī: "He wanted to compose a drama about the pastimes of Lord Krishna. He planned to describe in one book both the pastimes of Vrindāvana and those of Dvārakā and Mathurā.
