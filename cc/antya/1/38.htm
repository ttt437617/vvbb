Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.38

rūpa-gosāñi prabhu-pāśe karilā gamana

prabhure dekhite tāńra utkanthita mana

SYNONYMS

rūpa-gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; prabhu-pāśe — the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karilā gamana — departed for; prabhure dekhite — to see Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra — his; utkanthita — full of anxiety; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

Rūpa Gosvāmī then departed to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, for he was very eager to see Him.
