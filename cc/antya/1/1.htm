Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.1

pańgum lańghayate śailam

mūkam āvartayec chrutim

yat-kripā tam aham vande

krishna-caitanyam īśvaram

SYNONYMS

pańgum — one who is lame; lańghayate — causes to cross over; śailam — a mountain; mūkam — one who is dumb; āvartayet — can cause to recite; śrutim — Vedic literature; yat-kripā — the mercy of whom; tam — unto Him; aham — I; vande — offer obeisances; krishna-caitanyam — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who is Krishna Himself; īśvaram — the Lord.

TRANSLATION

I offer my respectful obeisances to Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu, by whose mercy even a lame man can cross over a mountain and a dumb man recite Vedic literature.
