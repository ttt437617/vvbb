Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.42

"āmāra nātaka prithak karaha racana

āmāra kripāte nātaka haibe vilakshana"

SYNONYMS

āmāra nātaka — my drama; prithak karaha racana — write separately; āmāra kripāte — by my mercy; nātaka — the drama; haibe — will be; vilakshana — extraordinarily beautiful.

TRANSLATION

"Write a separate drama about me," she said. "By my mercy it will be extraordinarily beautiful."
