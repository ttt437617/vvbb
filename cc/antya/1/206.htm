Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.206

tabe mahāprabhu kailā rūpe ālińgana

tāńre karāilā sabāra carana vandana

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā — did; rūpe — unto Rūpa Gosvāmī; ālińgana — embracing; tāńre — him; karāilā — induced to do; sabāra — of all of them; carana vandana — worshiping the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then embraced Rūpa Gosvāmī and asked him to offer prayers at the lotus feet of all the devotees present.
