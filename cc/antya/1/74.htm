Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.74

pūrve sei saba kathā kariyāchi varnana

tathāpi kahiye kichu sańkshepe kathana

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; sei — these; saba — all; kathā — words; kariyāchi varnana — I have described; tathāpi — still; kahiye — let me say; kichu — something; sańkshepe — in brief; kathana — telling.

TRANSLATION

I have already described all these incidents, but I still wish to add briefly something more.
