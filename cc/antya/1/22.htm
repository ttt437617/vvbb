Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.22

kukkura nāhi pāya bhāta śuni' duhkhī hailā

kukkura cāhite daśa-manushya pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

kukkura — the dog; nāhi — did not; pāya — get; bhāta — rice; śuni' — hearing; duhkhī hailā — Śivānanda Sena became very unhappy; kukkura cāhite — to look for the dog; daśa-manushya — ten men; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

When he learned that the dog had not been supplied food in his absence, he was very unhappy. He then immediately sent ten men to find the dog.
