Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.20

eka-dina śivānande ghātiyāle rākhilā

kukkurake bhāta dite sevaka pāsarilā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; śivānande — Śivānanda Sena; ghātiyāle — tollman; rākhilā — detained; kukkurake — unto the dog; bhāta dite — to supply rice; sevaka — the servant; pāsarilā — forgot.

TRANSLATION

One day while Śivānanda was detained by a tollman, his servant forgot to give the dog its cooked rice.
