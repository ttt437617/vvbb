Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.61

ei-mata pratidina prabhura vyavahāra

prabhu-kripā pāñā rūpera ānanda apāra

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; pratidina — daily; prabhura vyavahāra — the dealings of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu-kripā — the mercy of Lord Caitanya; pāñā — getting; rūpera — of Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; ānanda apāra — unlimited happiness.

TRANSLATION

In this way Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dealings with them continued every day. Thus receiving the transcendental favor of the Lord, Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī felt unlimited pleasure.
