Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.31

śivānanda kukkura dekhi' dandavat kailā

dainya kari' nija aparādha kshamāilā

SYNONYMS

śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; kukkura — the dog; dekhi' — seeing there; dandavat kailā — offered obeisances; dainya kari' — exhibiting humbleness; nija — personal; aparādha — offenses; kshamāilā — asked to be forgiven.

TRANSLATION

When he saw the dog sitting in that way and chanting the name of Krishna, Śivānanda, because of his natural humility, immediately offered his obeisances to the dog just to counteract his offenses to it.
