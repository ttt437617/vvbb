Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.16

śivānanda kare sabāra ghāti samādhāna

sabāre pālana kare, deya vāsā-sthāna

SYNONYMS

śivānanda — Śivānanda; kare — does; sabāra — of everyone; ghāti — the camp; samādhāna — arrangement; sabāre — of everyone; pālana — maintenance; kare — performs; deya — gives; vāsā-sthāna — residential quarters.

TRANSLATION

Śivānanda Sena arranged for the journey. He maintained everyone and provided residential quarters.
