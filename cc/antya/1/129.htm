Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.129

rāya kahe, — 'kaha ishta-devera varnana'

prabhura sańkoce rūpa nā kare pathana

SYNONYMS

rāya kahe — Rāmānanda Rāya says; kaha — now speak; ishta-devera varnana — description of your worshipable Deity; prabhura sańkoce — embarrassment in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; nā kare — does not do; pathana — recitation.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya said, "Now please recite the description of the glories of your worshipable Deity." Rūpa Gosvāmī, however, hesitated due to embarrassment because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was present.
