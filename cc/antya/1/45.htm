Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.45

bhāvite bhāvite śīghra āilā nīlācale

āsi' uttarilā haridāsa-vāsā-sthale

SYNONYMS

bhāvite bhāvite — thinking and thinking; śīghra — very soon; āilā nīlācale — reached Nīlācala (Jagannātha Purī); āsi' — coming; uttarilā — approached; haridāsa-vāsā-sthale — the place where Haridāsa Thākura was residing.

TRANSLATION

Thus absorbed in thought, he quickly reached Jagannātha Purī. When he arrived, he approached the hut of Haridāsa Thākura.
