Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.68

eta kahi' mahāprabhu madhyāhne calilā

rūpa-gosāñi mane kichu vismaya ha-ilā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; madhya-ahne calilā — left to execute noon duties; rūpa-gosāñi — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; mane — in the mind; kichu — some; vismaya ha-ilā — there was surprise.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to perform His noontime duties, leaving Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī somewhat surprised.
