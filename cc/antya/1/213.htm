Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.213

ei-mata dui-jana krishna-kathā-rańge

sukhe kāla gońāya rūpa haridāsa-sańge

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; dui-jana — Haridāsa Thākura and Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; krishna-kathā-rańge — in the pleasure of discussing topics about Krishna; sukhe — in happiness; kāla — time; gońāya — passes; rūpa — Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī; haridāsa-sańge — in the company of Haridāsa Thākura.

TRANSLATION

In this way Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī passed his time in close association with Haridāsa Thākura by discussing the pastimes of Lord Krishna in great happiness.
