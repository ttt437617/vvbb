Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.2

durgame pathi me 'ndhasya

skhalat-pāda-gater muhuh

sva-kripā-yashti-dānena

santah santv avalambanam

SYNONYMS

durgame — very difficult; pathi — on the path; me — of me; andhasya — one who is blind; skhalat — slipping; pāda — on feet; gateh — whose manner of moving; muhuh — again and again; sva-kripā — of their own mercy; yashti — the stick; dānena — by giving; santah — those saintly persons; santu — let that become; avalambanam — my support.

TRANSLATION

My path is very difficult. I am blind, and my feet are slipping again and again. Therefore, may the saints help me by granting me the stick of their mercy as my support.
