Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.63

prasāda khāya, 'hari' bale sarva-bhakta-jana

dekhi' haridāsa-rūpera harashita mana

SYNONYMS

prasāda khāya — eat the prasādam; hari bale — chant the holy name of Hari; sarva-bhakta-jana — all the devotees; dekhi' — seeing this; haridāsa — of Haridāsa Thākura; rūpera — and of Rūpa Gosvāmī; harashita — jubilant; mana — the minds.

TRANSLATION

When Haridāsa Thākura and Rūpa Gosvāmī saw that all the devotees were accepting prasādam and chanting the holy name of Hari, they both were greatly pleased.
