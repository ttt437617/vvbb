Antya-līlāChapter 1: Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī's Second Meeting With the Lord

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 1.15

kulīna-grāmī bhakta āra yata khanda-vāsī

ācārya śivānanda sane mililā sabe āsi'

SYNONYMS

kulīna-grāmī — residents of the village known as Kulīna-grāma; bhakta — devotees; āra — and; yata — all; khanda-vāsī — the residents of Śrī Khanda; ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; sane — with; mililā — met; sabe āsi' — all coming together.

TRANSLATION

Thus all the devotees of Kulīna-grāma and Śrī Khanda, as well as Advaita Ācārya, came together to meet Śivānanda Sena.
