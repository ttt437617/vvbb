Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.50

krishne dekhi' ei saba karena namaskāra

krishna-gamana puche tāre kariyā nirdhāra

SYNONYMS

krishne dekhi' — seeing Krishna; ei — these; saba — all; karena namaskāra — offer respectful obeisances; krishna-gamana — the passing of Krishna; puche — inquire; tāre — from them; kariyā nirdhāra — making certain.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs thought that because all the trees must have seen Krishna pass by they were offering respectful obeisances to Him. To be certain, the gopīs inquired from the trees.
