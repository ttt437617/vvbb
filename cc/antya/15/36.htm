Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.36

krishna tomāra ihāń āilā, pāilā daraśana?

krishnera uddeśa kahi' rākhaha jīvana

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; tomāra — your; ihāń — here; āilā — came; pāilā daraśana — you have seen; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; uddeśa — the direction; kahi' — by telling; rākhaha jīvana — kindly save our lives.

TRANSLATION

"'Have you seen Krishna coming this way? Kindly tell us which way He has gone and save our lives.'"
