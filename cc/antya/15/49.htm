Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.49

āge vriksha-gana dekhe pushpa-phala-bhare

śākhā saba padiyāche prithivī-upare

SYNONYMS

āge — in front; vriksha-gana — the trees; dekhe — see; pushpa-phala-bhare — because of the heavy burden of flowers and fruits; śākhā saba — all the branches; padiyāche — have bent down; prithivī-upare — to the ground.

TRANSLATION

The gopīs then came upon many trees so laden with fruits and flowers that their branches were bent down to the ground.
