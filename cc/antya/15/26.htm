Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.26

sei dui-jana prabhure kare āśvāsana

svarūpa gāya, rāya kare śloka pathana

SYNONYMS

sei — those; dui-jana — two persons; prabhure — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kare — do; āśvāsana — pacification; svarūpa gāya — Svarūpa Dāmodara sings; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kare — does; śloka pathana — recitation of verses.

TRANSLATION

To enhance the ecstatic mood of the Lord, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī would sing appropriate songs and Rāmānanda Rāya would recite suitable verses. In this way they were able to pacify Him.
