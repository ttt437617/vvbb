Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.84

rāse harim iha vihita-vilāsam

smarati mano mama krita-parihāsam

SYNONYMS

rāse — in the rāsa dance; harim — Śrī Krishna; iha — here; vihita-vilāsam — performing pastimes; smarati — remembers; manah — mind; mama — My; krita-parihāsam — fond of making jokes.

TRANSLATION

"'Here in the arena of the rāsa dance, I remember Krishna, who is always fond of joking and performing pastimes.'"

PURPORT

This verse (Gīta-govinda 2.3) is sung by Śrīmatī Rādhārānī.
