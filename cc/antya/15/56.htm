Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.56

koti-manmatha-mohana muralī-vadana

apāra saundarye hare jagan-netra-mana

SYNONYMS

koti — ten million; manmatha — Cupids; mohana — enchanting; muralī-vadana — with His flute to His lips; apāra — unlimited; saundarye — by the beauty; hare — enchants; jagat — of the whole world; netra-mana — the eyes and mind.

TRANSLATION

Standing there with His flute to His lips, Krishna, who enchants millions upon millions of Cupids, attracted the eyes and minds of all the world with His unlimited beauty.
