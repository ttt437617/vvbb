Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.28

eka-dina mahāprabhu samudra-tīre yāite

pushpera udyāna tathā dekhena ācambite

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; samudra-tīre — to the seashore; yāite — while going; pushpera udyāna — a flower garden; tathā — there; dekhena — sees; ācambite — suddenly.

TRANSLATION

One day, while going to the beach by the sea, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly saw a flower garden.
