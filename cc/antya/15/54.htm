Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.54

krishnera viyoge ei sevaka duhkhita

kibā uttara dibe? ihāra nāhika samvit"

SYNONYMS

krishnera viyoge — by separation from Krishna; ei — these; sevaka — servants; duhkhita — very unhappy; kibā — what; uttara — reply; dibe — will they give; ihāra — of these; nāhika — there's not; samvit — consciousness.

TRANSLATION

"'Separation from Krishna has made these servants very unhappy. Having lost consciousness, how can they answer us?'"
