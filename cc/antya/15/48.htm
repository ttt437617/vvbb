Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.48

krishna ihāń chādi' gelā, ihoń — virahinī

kibā uttara dibe ei — nā śune kāhinī"

SYNONYMS

krishna — Lord Krishna; ihāń — here; chādi' gelā — has left; ihoń — the deer; virahinī — feeling separation; kibā — what; uttara — reply; dibe — will they give; ei — these; nā śune — do not hear; kāhinī — our words.

TRANSLATION

"'Lord Krishna has left this place, and therefore the deer are feeling separation. They do not hear our words; therefore how can they reply?'"
