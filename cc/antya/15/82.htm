Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.82

svarūpa-gosāñire kahena, — "gāo eka gīta

yāte āmāra hridayera haye ta' 'samvit'"

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñire — to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; kahena — said; gāo — sing; eka — one; gīta — song; yāte — by which; āmāra — My; hridayera — of the heart; haye — there is; ta' — certainly; samvit — consciousness.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said to Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, "Please sing a song that will bring consciousness to My heart."
