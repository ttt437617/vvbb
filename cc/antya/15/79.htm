Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.79

prabhu kahe, — "krishna muñi ekhana-i pāinu

āpanāra durdaive punah hārāinu

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; krishna — Lord Krishna; muñi — I; ekhana-i — just now; pāinu — had; āpanāra — My own; durdaive — by misfortune; punah — again; hārāinu — I have lost.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu then said, "I just now had Krishna, but unfortunately I have lost Him again.
