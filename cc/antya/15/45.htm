Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.45

"kaha, mrigi, rādhā-saha śrī-krishna sarvathā

tomāya sukha dite āilā? nāhika anyathā

SYNONYMS

kaha — please say; mrigi — O she-deer; rādhā-saha — with Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; śrī-krishna — Lord Śrī Krishna; sarvathā — in all respects; tomāya — to you; sukha dite — to give pleasure; āilā — did come; nāhika anyathā — it is certain.

TRANSLATION

"'O dear doe, Śrī Krishna is always very pleased to give you pleasure. Kindly inform us whether He passed this way in the company of Śrīmatī Rādhārānī. We think They must certainly have come this way.
