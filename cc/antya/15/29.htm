Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.29

vrindāvana-bhrame tāhāń paśilā dhāñā

premāveśe bule tāhāń krishna anveshiyā

SYNONYMS

vrindāvana-bhrame — taking it for Vrindāvana; tāhāń — there; paśilā — entered; dhāñā — running; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love of Krishna; bule — wanders; tāhāń — there; krishna — Lord Krishna; anveshiyā — searching for.

TRANSLATION

Lord Caitanya mistook that garden for Vrindāvana and very quickly entered it. Absorbed in ecstatic love of Krishna, He wandered throughout the garden, searching for Him.
