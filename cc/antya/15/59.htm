Antya-līlāChapter 15: The Transcendental Madness of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 15.59

pūrvavat sabe mili' karāilā cetana

uthiyā caudike prabhu karena darśana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-vat — as before; sabe — all; mili' — coming together; karāilā cetana — brought to consciousness; uthiyā — standing up; cau-dike — all around; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena darśana — was looking.

TRANSLATION

Once again all the devotees brought Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu back to consciousness by a concerted effort. Then the Lord got up and began wandering here and there, looking all around.
