Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.108

keha kona prasāda āni' deya govinda-thāñi

'ihā yena avaśya bhakshana karena gosāñi'

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; kona prasāda — some variety of prasādam; āni' — bringing; deya — delivers; govinda-thāñi — to Govinda; ihā — this; yena — that; avaśya — certainly; bhakshana karena — eats; gosāñi — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Each devotee would bring a certain type of prasādam. He would entrust it to Govinda and request him, "Please arrange that the Lord will surely eat this prasādam."
