Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.159

tāra madhye rāghavera jhāli-vivarana

tāra madhye pari-mundā-nritya-kathana

SYNONYMS

tāra madhye — in the midst of that; rāghavera — of Rāghava Pandita; jhāli-vivarana — description of the bags of food; tāra madhye — along with that; pari-mundā-nritya-kathana — the description of dancing in the temple of Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

In the midst of that narration are descriptions of Rāghava Pandita's bags of food and the dancing in the temple of Jagannātha.
