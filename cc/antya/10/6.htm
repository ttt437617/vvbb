Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.6

anurāgera lakshana ei, — 'vidhi' nāhi māne

tāńra ājñā bhāńge tāńra sańgera kārane

SYNONYMS

anurāgera — of real affection; lakshana — symptom; ei — this; vidhi — the regulation; nāhi māne — does not care for; tāńra — His; ājñā — order; bhāńge — neglects; tāńra — His; sańgera — association; kārane — for the purpose of.

TRANSLATION

Indeed, it is a symptom of real affection that one breaks the order of the Supreme Personality of Godhead, not caring for the regulative principles, to associate with Him.
