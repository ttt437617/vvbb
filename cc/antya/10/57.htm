Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.57

āra dina mahāprabhu nija-gana lañā

jagannātha dekhilena śayyotthāne yāñā

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija-gana lañā — accompanied by His personal devotees; jagannātha dekhilena — saw Lord Jagannātha; śayyā-utthāne — at the time of rising early from bed; yāñā — going.

TRANSLATION

The next day, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went with His personal devotees to see Lord Jagannātha when Lord Jagannātha arose early in the morning.
