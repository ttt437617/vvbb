Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.127

śata-janera bhakshya prabhu dandeke khāilā!

'āra kichu āche?' bali' govinde puchilā

SYNONYMS

śata-janera — of one hundred persons; bhakshya — eatables; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dandeke khāilā — ate within twenty-four minutes; āra kichu āche — is there anything more; bali' — saying; govinde — unto Govinda; puchilā — inquired.

TRANSLATION

Within a very short time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate enough for a hundred people. Then He asked Govinda, "Is there anything more left?"
