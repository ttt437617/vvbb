Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.77

tabe nityānanda prabhu srijilā upāya

krame-krame kīrtanīyā rākhila sabāya

SYNONYMS

tabe — at that time; nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; prabhu — the Lord; srijilā upāya — made a device; krame-krame — by and by; kīrtanīyā — the chanters; rākhila — stopped; sabāya — all.

TRANSLATION

Then Lord Nityānanda found a way to end the kīrtana. He gradually stopped all the chanters.
