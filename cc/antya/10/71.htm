Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.71

prabhu padi' mūrchā yāya, śvāsa nāhi āra

ācambite uthe prabhu kariyā huńkāra

SYNONYMS

prabhu — the Lord; padi' — falling down; mūrchā yāya — became unconscious; śvāsa nāhi — there was no breathing; āra — and; ācambite — suddenly; uthe — stands up; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariyā huńkāra — making a loud sound.

TRANSLATION

The Lord fell to the ground unconscious, not even breathing. Then suddenly He stood up, making a loud sound.
