Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.40

jhālira upara 'munsiba' makaradhvaja-kara

prāna-rūpe jhāli rākhe hañā tatpara

SYNONYMS

jhālira upara — upon the bags; munsiba — the superintendent; makaradhvaja-kara — Makaradhvaja Kara; prāna-rūpe — like his life; jhāli rākhe — he keeps the bags; hañā tatpara — with great attention.

TRANSLATION

The superintendent for all those bags was Makaradhvaja Kara, who kept them with great attention like his very life.
