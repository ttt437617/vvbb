Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.113

kāhāń kichu kahi' govinda kare vañcana

āra dina prabhure kahe nirveda-vacana

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — to someone; kichu — something; kahi' — saying; govinda — Govinda; kare vañcana — told lies; āra dina — one day; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — said; nirveda-vacana — a statement of disappointment.

TRANSLATION

When the devotees questioned Govinda, he had to tell them lies. Therefore one day he spoke to the Lord in disappointment.
