Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.9-11

vāsudeva-datta, murāri-gupta, gańgādāsa

śrīmān-sena, śrīmān-pandita, akiñcana krishnadāsa

murāri, garuda-pandita, buddhimanta-khāńna

sañjaya-purushottama, pandita-bhagavān

śuklāmbara, nrisimhānanda āra yata jana

sabāi calilā, nāma nā yāya likhana

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva-datta — Vāsudeva Datta; murāri-gupta — Murāri Gupta; gańgādāsa — Gańgādāsa; śrīmān-sena — Śrīmān Sena; śrīmān-pandita — Śrīmān Pandita; akiñcana krishnadāsa — Akiñcana Krishnadāsa; murāri — Murāri Gupta; garuda-pandita — Garuda Pandita; buddhimanta-khāńna — Buddhimanta Khān; sañjaya purushottama — Sañjaya Purushottama; pandita-bhagavān — Bhagavān Pandita; śuklāmbara — Śuklāmbara; nrisimhānanda — Nrisimhānanda; āra — and; yata — as many; jana — persons; sabāi — all; calilā — went; nāma — names; nā yāya likhana — it is not possible to mention.

TRANSLATION

Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta, Gańgādāsa, Śrīmān Sena, Śrīmān Pandita, Akiñcana Krishnadāsa, Murāri Gupta, Garuda Pandita, Buddhimanta Khān, Sañjaya Purushottama, Bhagavān Pandita, Śuklāmbara Brahmacārī, Nrisimhānanda Brahmacārī and many others joined together to go to Jagannātha Purī. It would be impossible to mention the names of them all.
