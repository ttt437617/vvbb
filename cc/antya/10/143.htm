Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.143

prabhure milāite tāńre sańgei ānilā

milāile, prabhu tāńra nāma ta' puchilā

SYNONYMS

prabhure milāite — to introduce to the Lord; tāńre — him, Caitanya dāsa; sańgei — along; ānilā — brought; milāile — when he introduced him; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńra — his; nāma — name; ta' — thereupon; puchilā — inquired.

TRANSLATION

When Śivānanda brought his son, Caitanya dāsa, to be introduced to the Lord, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired about his name.
