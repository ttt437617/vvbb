Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.86

'eka-pāśa hao, more deha' bhitara yāite'

prabhu kahe, — 'śakti nāhi ańga cālāite'

SYNONYMS

eka-pāśa hao — kindly turn on one side; more — me; deha' — allow; bhitara — within; yāite — to go; prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; śakti — strength; nāhi — there is not; ańga cālāite — to move My body.

TRANSLATION

Govinda said, "Kindly turn on one side. Let me pass to enter the room."

However, the Lord replied, "I don't have the strength to move My body."
