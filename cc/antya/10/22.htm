Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.22

dhaniyā-mauharīra tandula gundā kariyā

nādu bāndhiyāche cini-pāka kariyā

SYNONYMS

dhaniyā — of coriander seeds; mauharīra — of anise seeds; tandula — grains; gundā kariyā — grinding to a powder; nādu bāndhiyāche — rendered into lāddus; cini-pāka kariyā — cooking with sugar.

TRANSLATION

Damayantī powdered coriander and anise seeds, cooked them with sugar and made them into sweetmeats in the shape of small balls.
