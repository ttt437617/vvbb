Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.39

sańkshepe kahiluń ei jhālira vicāra

'rāghavera jhāli' bali' vikhyāti yāhāra

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; kahiluń — l have spoken; ei jhālira — of these bags; vicāra — the description; rāghavera jhāli — the bags of Rāghava; bali' — as; vikhyāti — the fame; yāhāra — of which.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have briefly described the bags that have become famous as rāghavera jhāli.
