Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.54

ishta-goshthī sabā lañā kata-kshana kailā

nija nija pūrva-vāsāya sabāya pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

ishta-goshthī — discussion of spiritual matters; sabā lañā — taking all the devotees; kata-kshana — for some time; kailā — did; nija nija — respective; pūrva-vāsāya — to the former residences; sabāya — all; pāthāilā — He sent.

TRANSLATION

After talking with all the devotees for some time, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked them to occupy the individual residences in which they had lived the previous year.
