Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.41

ei-mate vaishnava saba nīlācale āilā

daive jagannāthera se dina jala-līlā

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; vaishnava saba — all the Vaishnavas; nīlācale āilā — came to Nīlācala; daive — by chance; jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; se dina — that day; jala-līlā — pastimes in the water.

TRANSLATION

Thus all the Vaishnavas from Bengal went to Jagannātha Purī. By chance, they arrived on the day when Lord Jagannātha performs pastimes in the water.
