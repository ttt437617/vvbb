Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.58

bedā-sańkīrtana tahāń ārambha karilā

sāta-sampradāya tabe gāite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

bedā-sańkīrtana — surrounding congregational chanting; tāhāń — there; ārambha karilā — began; sāta-sampradāya — seven groups; tabe — thereupon; gāite lāgilā — began to chant.

TRANSLATION

After seeing Lord Jagannātha, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began His all-encompassing sańkīrtana. He formed seven groups, which then began to chant.

PURPORT

For an explanation of the bedā-sańkīrtana, one may refer to Madhya-līlā, Chapter Eleven, verses 215-238.
