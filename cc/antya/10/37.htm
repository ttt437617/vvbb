Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.37

sāmānya jhāli haite dviguna jhāli kailā

pāripāti kari' saba jhāli bharāilā

SYNONYMS

sāmānya — small; jhāli — bags; haite — from; dvi-guna — twice as big; jhāli — bags; kailā — made; pāripāti kari' — with great attention; saba jhāli — all the bags; bharāilā — she filled.

TRANSLATION

From small bags Damayantī made bags that were twice as large. Then with great attention she filled all the large ones with the small ones.
