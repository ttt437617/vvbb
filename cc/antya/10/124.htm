Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.124

aiche sabāra nāma lañā prabhura āge dhare

santushta hañā prabhu saba bhojana kare

SYNONYMS

aiche — in this way; sabāra nāma — everyone's name; lañā — taking; prabhura āge — before the Lord; dhare — he places; santushta hañā — being very satisfied; prabhu — the Lord; saba — all; bhojana kare — began to eat.

TRANSLATION

In this way, Govinda gave everyone's name as he put the food before the Lord. Being very satisfied, the Lord began to eat it all.
