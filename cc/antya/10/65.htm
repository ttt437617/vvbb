Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.65

ei-mata kata-kshana karāilā kīrtana

āpane nācite tabe prabhura haila mana

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; kata-kshana — for some time; karāilā kīrtana — caused kīrtana to be performed; āpane — personally; nācite — to dance; tabe — then; prabhura haila mana — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu desired.

TRANSLATION

In this way the Lord had congregational chanting performed for some time, and then He Himself desired to dance.
