Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.107

pūrve yadi gauda ha-ite bhakta-gana āila

prabhure kichu khāoyāite sabāra icchā haila

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; yadi — when; gauda ha-ite — from Bengal; bhakta-gana āila — the devotees arrived; prabhure — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kichu — something; khāoyāite — to feed; sabāra icchā haila — everyone desired.

TRANSLATION

Formerly, when all the devotees had arrived from Bengal, they all desired to give Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu something to eat.
