Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.89

tabe govinda bahirvāsa tāńra upare diyā

bhitara-ghare gelā mahāprabhure lańghiyā

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; govinda — Govinda; bahirvāsa — outward wrapper; tāńra upare — over Him; diyā — spreading; bhitara-ghare — within the room; gelā — went; mahāprabhure lańghiyā — crossing Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Then Govinda spread the Lord's wrapper over His body and in this way entered the room by crossing over the Lord.
