Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.13

rāghava-pandita cale jhāli sājāiyā

damayantī yata dravya diyāche kariyā

SYNONYMS

rāghava-pandita — Rāghava Pandita; cale — goes; jhāli sājāiyā — after preparing his bag of food; damayantī — his sister; yata dravya — all the goods; diyāche kariyā — cooked and prepared.

TRANSLATION

Rāghava Pandita came with bags full of food prepared very nicely by his sister, Damayantī.
