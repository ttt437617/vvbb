Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.85

saba dvāra yudi' prabhu kariyāchena śayana

bhitare yāite nāre, govinda kare nivedana

SYNONYMS

saba dvāra — the whole door; yudi' — occupying the space of; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kariyāchena śayana — was lying down; bhitare — within; yāite nāre — could not go; govinda — Govinda; kare nivedana — he requested.

TRANSLATION

This time when the Lord lay down, He occupied the entire doorway. Govinda could not enter the room, and therefore he made the following request.
