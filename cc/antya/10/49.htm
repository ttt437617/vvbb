Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.49

saba bhakta lañā prabhu nāmilena jale

sabā lañā jala-krīdā karena kutūhale

SYNONYMS

saba bhakta — all the devotees; lañā — taking; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāmilena jale — got down in the water; sabā lañā — taking all of them; jala-krīdā — activities in the water; karena — performs; kutūhale — in great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu entered the water with His devotees and began His pastimes with them in great jubilation.
