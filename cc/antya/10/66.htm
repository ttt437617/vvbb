Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.66

sāta-dike sāta-sampradāya gāya, bājāya

madhye mahā-premāveśe nāce gaura-rāya

SYNONYMS

sāta-dike — in seven directions; sāta-sampradāya — the seven groups; gāya — chant; bājāya — play on the mridańga; madhye — in the center; mahā-premāveśe — in great ecstatic love of Krishna; nāce — dances; gaura-rāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

The seven groups began chanting and beating their drums in seven directions, and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began dancing in the center in great ecstatic love.
