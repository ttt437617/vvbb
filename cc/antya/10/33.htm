Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.33

kahite nā jāni nāma e-janme yāhāra

aiche nānā bhakshya-dravya sahasra-prakāra

SYNONYMS

kahite nā jāni — I cannot speak; nāma — the names; e-janme — in this life; yāhāra — of which; aiche — similar; nānā — many; bhakshya-dravya — eatables; sahasra-prakāra — hundreds and thousands of varieties.

TRANSLATION

I could not mention the names of all these wonderful eatables, even in a lifetime. Damayantī made hundreds and thousands of varieties.
