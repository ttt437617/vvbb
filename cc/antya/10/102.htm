Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.102

sańkshepe kahiluń ei pari-mundā-nritya

adyāpiha gāya yāhā caitanyera bhritya

SYNONYMS

sańkshepe — in brief; kahiluń — I have described; ei — this; pari-mundā-nritya — dancing in the dancing hall of Jagannātha's temple; adyāpiha — even up to the present day; gāya — sing about; yāhā — which; caitanyera bhritya — the servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have briefly described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's dancing in the hall of the Jagannātha temple. The servants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sing about this dancing even now.
