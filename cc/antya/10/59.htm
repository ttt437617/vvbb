Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.59

sāta-sampradāye nritya kare sāta jana

advaita ācārya, āra prabhu-nityānanda

SYNONYMS

sāta-sampradāye — in the seven groups; nritya kare — danced; sāta jana — seven persons; advaita ācārya — Advaita Ācārya; āra — and; prabhu-nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda.

TRANSLATION

In each of the seven groups was a principal dancer, such as Advaita Ācārya or Lord Nityānanda.
