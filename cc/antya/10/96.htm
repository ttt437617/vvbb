Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.96

'sevā' lāgi' koti 'aparādha' nāhi gani

sva-nimitta 'aparādhābhāse' bhaya māni"

SYNONYMS

sevā lāgi' — for the matter of service; koti aparādha — ten million offenses; nāhi gani — I do not care for; sva-nimitta — for my personal self; aparādha-ābhāse — by a glimpse of an offense; bhaya māni — I am afraid.

TRANSLATION

"I would not mind committing hundreds and thousands of offenses for the service of the Lord, but I greatly fear committing even a glimpse of an offense for my own self."
