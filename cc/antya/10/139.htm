Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.139

ācāryaratna, ācāryanidhi, nandana, rāghava

śrīvāsa-ādi yata bhakta, vipra saba

SYNONYMS

ācāryaratna — Ācāryaratna; ācāryanidhi — Ācāryanidhi; nandana — Nandana Ācārya; rāghava — Rāghava Pandita; śrīvāsa-ādi — headed by Śrīvāsa; yata bhakta — all devotees; vipra saba — all brāhmanas.

TRANSLATION

Devotees like Ācāryaratna, Ācāryanidhi, Nandana Ācārya, Rāghava Pandita and Śrīvāsa were all of the brāhmana caste.
