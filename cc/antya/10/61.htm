Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.61

sāta-sampradāye prabhu karena bhramana

'mora sampradāye prabhu' — aiche sabāra mana

SYNONYMS

sāta-sampradāye — in the seven groups; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena bhramana — wanders; mora sampradāye prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is in our group; aiche — in this way; sabāra mana — everyone was thinking.

TRANSLATION

As Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu walked from one group to another inspecting them, the men in each group thought, "The Lord is within our group."
