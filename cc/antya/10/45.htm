Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.45

bhakta-gana pade āsi' prabhura carane

uthāñā prabhu sabāre kailā ālińgane

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — the devotees; pade — fell; āsi' — coming; prabhura carane — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uthāñā — getting them up; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāre — all of them; kailā ālińgane — embraced.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees immediately fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and the Lord lifted and embraced every one of them.
