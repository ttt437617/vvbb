Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.92

govinde dekhiyā prabhu bale kruddha hañā

'āji kene eta-kshana āchis vasiyā?

SYNONYMS

govinde dekhiyā — seeing Govinda; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bale — said; kruddha hañā — in an angry mood; āji — today; kene — why; eta-kshana — for so long; āchis — have you been; vasiyā — sitting.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu saw Govinda sitting by His side, He was somewhat angry. "Why have you been sitting here for so long today?" the Lord asked.
