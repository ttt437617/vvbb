Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.51

punah ihāń varnile punarukti haya

vyartha likhana haya, āra grantha bādaya

SYNONYMS

punah — again; ihāń — here; varnile — if I describe; punah-ukti haya — it will be repetition; vyartha — useless; likhana — writing; haya — is; āra — and; grantha bādaya — increases the volume of the book.

TRANSLATION

There is no use in again describing here the activities of the Lord. It would simply be repetitious and would increase the size of this book.
