Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.90

pāda-samvāhana kaila, kati-prishtha cāpila

madhūra-mardane prabhura pariśrama gela

SYNONYMS

pāda-samvāhana — massaging of the legs; kaila — he performed; kati — waist; prishtha — back; cāpila — pressed; madhūra-mardane — by mild pressing; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pariśrama — fatigue; gela — went away.

TRANSLATION

Govinda massaged the Lord's legs as usual. He pressed the Lord's waist and back very softly, and thus all the Lord's fatigue went away.
