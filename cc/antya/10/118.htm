Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.118

"ācāryera ei paida, pānā-sara-pūpī

ei amrita-gutikā, mandā, karpūra-kūpī

SYNONYMS

ācāryera — of Advaita Ācārya; ei — these; paida — coconut preparation; pānā — sweet rice; sara-pūpī — cakes made with cream; ei — these; amrita-gutikā — sweetballs; mandā — a type of round sweetmeat; karpūra-kūpī — a pot of camphor.

TRANSLATION

"These preparations — paida, sweet rice, cakes made with cream, and also amrita-gutikā, mandā and a pot of camphor — have been given by Advaita Ācārya.
