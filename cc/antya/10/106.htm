Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.106

cāri-māsa varshāya rahilā saba bhakta-gana

janmāshtamī ādi yātrā kailā daraśana

SYNONYMS

cāri-māsa — for four months; varshāya — of the rainy season; rahilā — stayed; saba bhakta-gana — all the devotees; janmāshtamī ādi yātrā — festivals like Lord Krishna's birth ceremony; kailā daraśana — observed.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees from Bengal stayed in Jagannātha Purī for the four months of the rainy season and observed many other ceremonies, such as the anniversary of Lord Krishna's birth.
