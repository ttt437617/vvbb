Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.99

yāiteha patha nāhi, yāibe kemane?

mahā-aparādha haya prabhura lańghane

SYNONYMS

yāiteha — to go; patha nāhi — there was no passage; yāibe kemane — how would he go away; mahā-aparādha — a great offense; haya — there would be; prabhura lańghane — to cross over the body of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

There was no way to go. How could he leave? When he thought of crossing over the Lord's body, he considered it a great offense.
