Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.78

svarūpera sańge mātra eka sampradāya

svarūpera sańge seha manda-svara gāya

SYNONYMS

svarūpera sańge — with Svarūpa Dāmodara; mātra — only; eka — one; sampradāya — group; svarūpera sańge — with Svarūpa Dāmodara; seha — they; manda-svara — very softly; gāya — chanted.

TRANSLATION

Thus only one group continued chanting with Svarūpa Dāmodara, and they chanted very softly.
