Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.62

sańkīrtana-kolāhale ākāśa bhedila

saba jagannātha-vāsī dekhite āila

SYNONYMS

sańkīrtana-kolāhale — tumultuous roaring of congregational chanting; ākāśa bhedila — filled the sky; saba — all; jagannātha-vāsī — the inhabitants of Jagannātha Purī; dekhite āila — came to see.

TRANSLATION

The congregational chanting made a tumultuous roar that filled the sky. All the inhabitants of Jagannātha Purī came to see the kīrtana.
