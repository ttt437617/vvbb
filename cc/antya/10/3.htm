Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.3

varshāntare saba bhakta prabhure dekhite

parama-ānande sabe nīlācala yāite

SYNONYMS

varsha-antare — the next year; saba bhakta — all the devotees; prabhure dekhite — to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; parama-ānande — in great happiness; sabe — all of them; nīlācala yāite — to go to Jagannātha Purī, Nīlācala.

TRANSLATION

The next year, all the devotees were very pleased to go to Jagannātha Purī [Nīlācala] to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
