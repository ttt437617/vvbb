Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.64

kīrtana-ātope prithivī kare talamala

'hari-dhvani' kare loka, haila kolāhala

SYNONYMS

kīrtana-ātope — by the force of congregational chanting; prithivī — the whole world; kare talamala — trembles; hari-dhvani kare — chanted the transcendental sound Hari; loka — people in general; haila — there was; kolāhala — a tumultuous sound.

TRANSLATION

Due to the forceful vibration of kīrtana, the entire world began trembling. When everyone chanted the holy name, they made a tumultuous sound.
