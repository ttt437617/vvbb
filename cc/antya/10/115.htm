Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.115

tumi se nā khāo, tāńrā puche bāra bāra

kata vañcanā karimu, kemane āmāra nistāra?"

SYNONYMS

tumi — You; se — that; nā khāo — do not eat; tāńrā — they; puche — inquire; bāra bāra — again and again; kata vañcanā karimu — how long shall I cheat; kemane — how; āmāra — my; nistāra — deliverance.

TRANSLATION

"You do not eat it, but they ask me again and again. How long shall I go on cheating them? How shall I be freed from this responsibility?"
