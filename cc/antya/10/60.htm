Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.60

vakreśvara, acyutānanda, pandita-śrīvāsa

satyarāja-khāńna, āra narahari-dāsa

SYNONYMS

vakreśvara — Vakreśvara; acyutānanda — Acyutānanda; pandita-śrīvāsa — Pandita Śrīvāsa; satyarāja-khāńna — Satyarāja Khān; āra — and; narahari-dāsa — Narahari dāsa.

TRANSLATION

The dancers in the other groups were Vakreśvara Pandita, Acyutānanda, Pandita Śrīvāsa, Satyarāja Khān and Narahari dāsa.
