Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.109

keha paida, keha nādu, keha pithā-pānā

bahu-mūlya uttama-prasāda-prakāra yāra nānā

SYNONYMS

keha — someone; paida — a coconut preparation; keha — someone; nādu — sweetballs; keha — someone; pithā — cakes; pānā — sweet rice; bahu-mūlya — costly; uttama-prasāda — very palatable food; prakāra yāra nānā — of different varieties.

TRANSLATION

Some brought paida [a coconut preparation], some brought sweetballs, and some brought cakes and sweet rice. The prasādam was of different varieties, all very costly.
