Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.133

ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gana-sańge

cāturmāsya gońāilā krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana-sańge — with His personal devotees; cāturmāsya gońāilā — passed the four months of the rainy season; krishna-kathā-rańge — in the happiness of discussing topics about Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu spent the entire period of Cāturmāsya [the four months of the rainy season] in the happiness of discussing topics of Krishna with His devotees.
