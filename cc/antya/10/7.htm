Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.7

rāse yaiche ghara yāite gopīre ājñā dilā

tāńra ājñā bhāńgi' tāńra sańge se rahilā

SYNONYMS

rāse — at the time of the rāsa dance; yaiche — as; ghara yāite — to return home; gopīre — the gopīs; ājñā dilā — Lord Krishna ordered; tāńra — His; ājñā — order; bhāńgi' — breaking; tāńra sańge — in His association; se — they; rahilā — kept themselves.

TRANSLATION

During the rāsa dance, Krishna asked all the gopīs to return home, but they neglected His order and stayed there for His association.
