Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.75

kshane kshane bāde prabhura ānanda-āveśa

tritīya prahara ha-ila, nritya nahe śesha

SYNONYMS

kshane kshane — at every moment; bāde — increases; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ānanda-āveśa — transcendental bliss; tritīya prahara — midafternoon; ha-ila — there was; nritya — the dancing; nahe śesha — did not end.

TRANSLATION

His transcendental bliss increased at every moment. Therefore even by midafternoon the dancing had not ended.
