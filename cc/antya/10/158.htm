Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.158

ei ta' kahiluń prabhura bhikshā-nimantrana

bhakta-datta vastu yaiche kailā āsvādana

SYNONYMS

ei ta' — thus; kahiluń — I have described; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhikshā-nimantrana — the invitation to dine; bhakta-datta — offered by the devotees; vastu — things; yaiche — as; kailā āsvādana — He tasted.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described how Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted invitations and how He accepted and tasted the prasādam offered by His devotees.
