Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.138

jagannāthera prasāda āne karite miśrita

kāhāń ekā yāyena, kāhāń ganera sahita

SYNONYMS

jagannāthera — of Lord Jagannātha; prasāda — remnants of food; āne — bring; karite miśrita — mixing; kāhāń — somewhere; ekā yāyena — goes alone; kāhāń — somewhere; ganera sahita — with associates.

TRANSLATION

They would mix these preparations with the remnants of food from Lord Jagannātha. When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu accepted the invitations, He went sometimes alone and sometimes with His associates.
