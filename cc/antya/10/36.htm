Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.36

pātala mrit-pātre sandhānādi bhari'

āra saba vastu bhare vastrera kuthalī

SYNONYMS

pātala — thin; mrit-pātre — in pots of earth; sandhāna-ādi — condiments and other items; bhari' — filling; āra — other; saba — all; vastu — things; bhare — filled; vastrera kuthalī — small bags of cloth.

TRANSLATION

The condiments and similar items were put into thin earthen pots, and everything else was put into small cloth bags.
