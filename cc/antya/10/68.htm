Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.68

"jagamohana-pari-mundā yāu"

SYNONYMS

jagamohana — the kīrtana hall known as Jagamohana; pari — in; mundā — my head; yāu — let it be offered.

TRANSLATION

"Let my head fall at the feet of Jagannātha in the kīrtana hall known as Jagamohana."
