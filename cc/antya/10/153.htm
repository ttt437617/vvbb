Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.153

gadādhara-pandita, bhattācārya sārvabhauma

ińhā sabāra āche bhikshāra divasa-niyama

SYNONYMS

gadādhara-pandita — Pandita Gadādhara; bhattācārya sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; ińhā sabāra — of all these persons; āche — there is; bhikshāra — for accepting invitations; divasa-niyama — a fixed date in every month.

TRANSLATION

Every month Gadādhara Pandita and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya had fixed dates on which Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would accept their invitations.
