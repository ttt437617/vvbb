Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.121

vāsudeva-dattera ei murāri-guptera āra

buddhimanta-khāńnera ei vividha prakāra

SYNONYMS

vāsudeva-dattera — of Vāsudeva Datta; ei — these; murāri-guptera — of Murāri Gupta; āra — and; buddhimanta-khāńnera — of Buddhimanta Khān; ei — these; vividha prakāra — of different varieties.

TRANSLATION

"And all these varieties of food have been given by Vāsudeva Datta, Murāri Gupta and Buddhimanta Khān.
