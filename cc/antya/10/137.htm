Antya-līlāChapter 10: Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Accepts Prasādam from His Devotees

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 10.137

bhrishta phula-badī, āra mudga-dāli-sūpa

vividha vyañjana rāndhe prabhura ruci-anurūpa

SYNONYMS

bhrishta — fried; phula-badī — a hot dhal preparation; āra — and; mudga-dāli-sūpa — a liquid preparation made from mung dhal; vividha vyañjana — varieties of vegetables; rāndhe — used to cook; prabhura ruci-anurūpa — very tasteful for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

They also offered phula-badī, liquid mung dhal and many vegetables, all cooked according to the Lord's taste.
