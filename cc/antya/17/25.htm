Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.25

tāńra pāche pāche āmi karinu gamana

tāńra bhūshā-dhvanite āmāra harila śravana

SYNONYMS

tāńra pāche pāche — just behind Him; āmi — I; karinu gamana — went; tāńra — His; bhūshā-dhvanite — by the sound of ornaments; āmāra — My; harila — became captivated; śravana — ears.

TRANSLATION

"I entered the bower just behind Krishna, My ears captivated by the sound of His ornaments.
