Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.3

ei-mata mahāprabhu rātri-divase

unmādera ceshtā, pralāpa kare premāveśe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rātri-divase — night and day; unmādera — of a madman; ceshtā — activities; pralāpa kare — talks insanely; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love.

TRANSLATION

Absorbed in ecstasy, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu acted and talked like a madman day and night.
