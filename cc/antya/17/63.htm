Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.63

ei-mata mahāprabhu prati-rātri-dine

unmāda ceshtita haya pralāpa-vacane

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prati-rātri-dine — every night and day; unmāda — mad; ceshtita — activities; haya — are; pralāpa-vacane — talking like a madman.

TRANSLATION

Each day and night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would become deranged in this way and talk like a madman.
