Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.19

aneka karilā yatna, nā haya cetana

prabhure uthāñā ghare ānilā bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

aneka — many; karilā — made; yatna — endeavors; nā haya — there was not; cetana — consciousness; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; uthāñā — lifting; ghare — home; ānilā — brought; bhakta-gana — the devotees.

TRANSLATION

The devotees tried to rouse the Lord by various means, but His consciousness did not return. Therefore they all lifted Him and brought Him back home.
