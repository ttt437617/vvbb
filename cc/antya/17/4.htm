Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.4

eka-dina prabhu svarūpa-rāmānanda-sańge

ardha-rātri gońāilā krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svarūpa-rāmānanda-sańge — with Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya; ardha-rātri — half the night; gońāilā — passed; krishna-kathā — of discussing Krishna's pastimes; rańge — in the matter.

TRANSLATION

In the company of Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu once passed half the night talking about the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
