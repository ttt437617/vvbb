Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.28

śunite nā pāinu sei amrita-sama vānī

śunite nā pāinu bhūshana-muralīra dhvani"

SYNONYMS

śunite nā pāinu — I could not hear; sei — those; amrita-sama — exactly like nectar; vānī — voices; śunite nā pāinu — I could not hear; bhūshana — of ornaments; muralīra — of the flute; dhvani — vibration.

TRANSLATION

"Because you brought Me back here, I could no longer hear the nectarean voices of Krishna and the gopīs, nor could I hear the sounds of their ornaments or the flute."
