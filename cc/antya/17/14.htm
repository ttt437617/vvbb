Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.14

tabe svarūpa-gosāñi sańge lañā bhakta-gana

deuti jvāliyā karena prabhura anveshana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; sańge — with him; lañā — taking; bhakta-gana — the devotees; deuti — lamp; jvāliyā — burning; karena — does; prabhura — for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; anveshana — searching.

TRANSLATION

Then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī lit a torch and went out with all the devotees to search for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
