Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.70

ei ta' kahiluń 'kūrmākriti'-anubhāva

unmāda-ceshtita tāte unmāda-pralāpa

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have described; kūrma-ākriti — of becoming like a tortoise; anubhāva — the ecstatic symptom; unmāda-ceshtita — enacted in madness; tāte — in that; unmāda-pralāpa — talking like a madman.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's ecstatic transformation of becoming like a tortoise. In that ecstasy, He talked and acted like a madman.
