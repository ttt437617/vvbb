Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.7

madhye madhye āpane prabhu śloka padiyā

ślokera artha karena prabhu vilāpa kariyā

SYNONYMS

madhye madhye — at intervals; āpane — personally; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śloka — a verse; padiyā — reciting; ślokera — of the verse; artha — meaning; karena — gives; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vilāpa kariyā — lamenting.

TRANSLATION

At intervals, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would also recite a verse. Then, in great lamentation, He would explain it.
