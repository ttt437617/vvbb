Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.30

svarūpa-gosāñi prabhura bhāva jāniyā

bhāgavatera śloka pade madhura kariyā

SYNONYMS

svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhāva — the emotion; jāniyā — understanding; bhāgavatera — of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; śloka — a verse; pade — recites; madhura kariyā — in a sweet voice.

TRANSLATION

Understanding the ecstatic emotions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Svarūpa Dāmodara, in a sweet voice, recited the following verse from Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam.
