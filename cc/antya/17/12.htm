Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.12

simha-dvāra-dakshine āche tailańgī-gābhī-gana

tāhāń yāi' padilā prabhu hañā acetana

SYNONYMS

simha-dvāra — of the gate named Simha-dvāra; dakshine — on the southern side; āche — there are; tailańgī-gābhī-gana — cows belonging to the Tailańga district; tāhāń — there; yāi' — going; padilā — fell down; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; hañā acetana — becoming unconscious.

TRANSLATION

He went to a cow shed on the southern side of the Simha-dvāra. There the Lord fell down unconscious among cows from the district of Tailańga.
