Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.65

jīva dīna ki karibe tāhāra varnana?

śākhā-candra-nyāya kari' dig-daraśana

SYNONYMS

jīva — a living entity; dīna — very poor; ki — what; karibe — will do; tāhāra — of that; varnana — description; śākhā-candra-nyāya — the logic of showing the moon through the branches of a tree; kari' — I make; dik-daraśana — seeing the direction.

TRANSLATION

What can a poor creature like me describe of those transformations? I can give only a hint of them, as if showing the moon through the branches of a tree.
