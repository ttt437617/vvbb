Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.71

ei līlā sva-granthe raghunātha-dāsa

gaurāńga-stava-kalpavrikshe kairāchena prakāśa

SYNONYMS

ei līlā — this pastime; sva-granthe — in his book; raghunātha-dāsa — Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; gaurāńga-stava-kalpa-vrikshe — named Gaurāńga-stava-kalpavriksha; kairāchena prakāśa — has fully described.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī has fully described this pastime in his book Gaurāńga-stava-kalpavriksha.
