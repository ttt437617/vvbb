Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.24

sańketa-venu-nāde rādhā āni' kuñja-ghare

kuñjere calilā krishna krīdā karibāre

SYNONYMS

sańketa-venu-nāde — by the signal of the vibration of the flute; rādhā — Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; āni' — bringing; kuñja-ghare — to a bower; kuñjere — within the bower; calilā — went; krishna — Lord Krishna; krīdā karibāre — to perform pastimes.

TRANSLATION

"He brought Śrīmatī Rādhārānī to a bower by signaling with His flute. Then He entered within that bower to perform pastimes with Her.
