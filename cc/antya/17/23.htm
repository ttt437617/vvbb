Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.23

venu-śabda śuni' āmi gelāńa vrindāvana

dekhi, — goshthe venu bājāya vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

venu-śabda — the vibration of the flute; śuni' — after hearing; āmi — I; gelāńa — went; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; dekhi — I saw; goshthe — in the pasturing field; venu — the flute; bājāya — played; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"After hearing the vibration of a flute, I went to Vrindāvana, and there I saw that Krishna, the son of Mahārāja Nanda, was playing on His flute in the pasturing grounds.
