Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.27

hena-kāle tumi-saba kolāhala kari'

āmā ińhā lañā āilā balātkāra kari'

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; tumi-saba — all of you; kolāhala kari' — making a tumultuous sound; āmā — Me; ińhā — here; lañā āilā — brought back; balātkāra kari' — by force.

TRANSLATION

"Just then, all of you made a tumultuous sound and brought Me back here by force.
