Antya-līlāChapter 17: The Bodily Transformations of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 17.1

likhyate śrīla-gaurendor

aty-adbhutam alaukikam

yair drishtam tan-mukhāc chrutvā

divyonmāda-viceshtitam

SYNONYMS

likhyate — they are being written; śrīla — most opulent; gaura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; indoh — moonlike; ati — very; adbhutam — wonderful; alaukikam — uncommon; yaih — by whom; drishtam — personally seen; tat-mukhāt — from their mouths; śrutvā — after hearing; divya-unmāda — in transcendental madness; viceshtitam — activities.

TRANSLATION

I am simply trying to write about Lord Gauracandra's transcendental activities and spiritual madness, which are very wonderful and uncommon. I dare to write of them only because I have heard from the mouths of those who have personally seen the Lord's activities.
