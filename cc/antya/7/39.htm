Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.39

'śuddha-prema' vraja-devīra — kāma-gandha-hīna

'krishna-sukha-tātparya', — ei tāra cihna

SYNONYMS

śuddha-prema — unalloyed love; vraja-devīra — of the gopīs or Śrīmatī Rādhārānī; kāma-gandha-hīna — without a scent of material lust; krishna — of Krishna; sukha — happiness; tātparya — the purpose; ei — this; tāra — of that; cihna — the symptom.

TRANSLATION

"The unalloyed love of the gopīs and Śrīmatī Rādhārānī is without any trace of material lust. The criterion of such transcendental love is that its only purpose is to satisfy Krishna.
