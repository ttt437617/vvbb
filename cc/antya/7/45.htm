Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.45

aiśvarya-jñāna haite kevalā-bhāva — pradhāna

prithivīte bhakta nāhi uddhava-samāna

SYNONYMS

aiśvarya-jñāna haite — than transcendental love in opulence; kevalā-bhāva — pure love; pradhāna — more prominent; prithivīte — on the surface of the world; bhakta nāhi — there is no devotee; uddhava-samāna — like Uddhava.

TRANSLATION

"Completely distinct from love of Krishna in opulence, pure love of Krishna is on the highest level. On the surface of the world there is no devotee greater than Uddhava.
