Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.84

bhatta kahe, "krishna-nāmera artha-vyākhyāne

vistāra kairāchi, tāhā karaha śravane"

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — Vallabha Bhatta said; krishna-nāmera — of the holy name of Krishna; artha-vyākhyāne — description of the meaning; vistāra — very elaborately; kairāchi — I have made; tāhā — that; karaha śravane — kindly hear.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta said, "I have tried to describe elaborately the meaning of Krishna's holy name. Kindly hear the explanation."
