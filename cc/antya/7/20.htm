Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.20

nityānanda-avadhūta — 'sākshāt īśvara'

bhāvonmāde matta krishna-premera sāgara

SYNONYMS

nityānanda — Lord Nityānanda; avadhūta — paramahamsa; sākshāt īśvara — directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead; bhāva-unmāde — by the madness of ecstatic love; matta — overwhelmed, intoxicated; krishna-premera — of love of Krishna; sāgara — the ocean.

TRANSLATION

"Lord Nityānanda Prabhu, the avadhūta, is also directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead. He is always intoxicated with the madness of ecstatic love. Indeed, He is an ocean of love of Krishna.
