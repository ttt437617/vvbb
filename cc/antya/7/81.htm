Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.81

"bhāgavatera tīkā kichu kariyāchi likhana

āpane mahāprabhu yadi karena śravana"

SYNONYMS

bhāgavatera — on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; tīkā — commentary; kichu — some; kariyāchi likhana — I have written; āpane — You; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yadi — if; karena śravana — would hear.

TRANSLATION

"I have written some commentary on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam," he said. "Would Your Lordship kindly hear it?"
