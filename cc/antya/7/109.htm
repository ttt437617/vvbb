Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.109

śuniyā vallabha-bhatta haila nirvacana

ghare yāi' mane duhkhe karena cintana

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; vallabha-bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; haila — became; nirvacana — speechless; ghare yāi' — returning home; mane — in the mind; duhkhe — unhappy; karena cintana — began to consider.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Vallabha Bhatta was speechless. He returned home greatly unhappy and began to consider thus.
