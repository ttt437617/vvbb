Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.65

advaita, nityānanda-rāya — pārśve dui-jana

madhye mahāprabhu vasilā, āge-pāche bhakta-gana

SYNONYMS

advaita — Advaita Ācārya; nityānanda-rāya — Lord Nityānanda; pārśve — on the sides; dui-jana — two personalities; madhye — in the middle; mahāprabhu vasilā — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down; āge — in front; pāche — behind; bhakta-gana — all the devotees.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat in the midst of the devotees. Advaita Ācārya and Lord Nityānanda each sat on one side of the Lord. The other devotees sat in front of the Lord and behind Him.
