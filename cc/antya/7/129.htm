Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.129

tomāra kripā-añjane ebe garva-āndhya gela

tumi eta kripā kailā, — ebe 'jñāna' haila

SYNONYMS

tomāra kripā-añjane — by the eye ointment of Your mercy; ebe — now; garva-āndhya — the blindness of false pride; gela — has gone; tumi — You; eta — such; kripā — mercy; kailā — have shown; ebe — now; jñāna — knowledge; haila — has become.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You have cured the blindness of my false pride by smearing my eyes with the ointment of Your mercy. You have bestowed so much mercy upon me that my ignorance is now gone.
