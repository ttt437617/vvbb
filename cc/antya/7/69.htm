Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.69

mahā-prasāda vallabha-bhatta bahu ānāila

prabhu-saha sannyāsi-gana bhojane vasila

SYNONYMS

mahā-prasāda — food offered to Śrī Jagannātha; vallabha-bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; bahu — a large quantity; ānāila — had brought; prabhu-saha — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sannyāsi-gana — all the sannyāsīs; bhojane vasila — sat down to accept the prasādam.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta had brought a large quantity of mahā-prasādam offered to Lord Jagannātha. Thus all the sannyāsīs sat down to eat with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
