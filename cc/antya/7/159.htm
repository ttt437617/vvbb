Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.159

eta bali' pandita prabhura sthāne āilā

rodana kariyā prabhura carane padilā

SYNONYMS

eta bali' — saying this; pandita — Gadādhara Pandita; prabhura sthāne — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āilā — came; rodana kariyā — crying; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — at the lotus feet; padilā — fell down.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Gadādhara Pandita went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and fell down crying at the lotus feet of the Lord.
