Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.97

ābhijātye pandita karite nāre nishedhana

"e sańkate rākha, krishna la-ilāńa śarana

SYNONYMS

ābhijātye — because of his aristocracy; pandita — Gadādhara Pandita; karite nāre nishedhana — could not forbid; e sańkate — in this danger; rākha — please protect; krishna — O Lord Krishna; la-ilāńa — I have taken; śarana — shelter.

TRANSLATION

Because Vallabha Bhatta was a learned brāhmana, Gadādhara Pandita could not forbid him. Thus he began to think of Lord Krishna. "My dear Lord Krishna," he requested, "please protect me in this danger. I have taken shelter of You.
