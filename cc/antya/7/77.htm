Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.77

tabe mahāprabhu sabāra nritya rākhilā

pūrvavat āpane nritya karite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāra — of all of them; nritya rākhilā — stopped the dancing; pūrvavat — as previously; āpane — personally; nritya — dancing; karite lāgilā — began to perform.

TRANSLATION

Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stopped the dancing of the others, and as He had done previously, He personally began to dance.
