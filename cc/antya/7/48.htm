Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.48

haridāsa-thākura — mahā-bhāgavata-pradhāna

prati dina laya teńha tina-laksha nāma

SYNONYMS

haridāsa-thākura — Haridāsa Thākura; mahā-bhāgavata-pradhāna — the topmost of all pure devotees; prati dina — every day; laya — chants; teńha — he; tina-laksha nāma — 300,000 holy names of the Lord.

TRANSLATION

"Haridāsa Thākura, the teacher of the holy name, is the most exalted of all pure devotees. Every day he chants 300,000 holy names of the Lord.
