Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.160

īshat hāsiyā prabhu kailā ālińgana

sabāre śunāñā kahena madhura vacana

SYNONYMS

īshat hāsiyā — smiling slightly; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā ālińgana — embraced; sabāre — all others; śunāñā — causing to hear; kahena — began to say; madhura vacana — sweet words.

TRANSLATION

Smiling slightly, the Lord embraced him and spoke sweet words so that others would also hear.
