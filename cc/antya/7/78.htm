Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.78

prabhura saundarya dekhi āra premodaya

'ei ta' sākshāt krishna' bhattera ha-ila niścaya

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; saundarya — the beauty; dekhi — seeing; āra — also; prema-udaya — arousing of ecstatic love; ei — this; ta' — certainly; sākshāt — directly; krishna — Lord Krishna; bhattera — of Vallabha Bhatta; ha-ila — was; niścaya — certainty.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the beauty of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and the awakening of His ecstatic love, Vallabha Bhatta concluded, "Here is Lord Krishna, without a doubt."
