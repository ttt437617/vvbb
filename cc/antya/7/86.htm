Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.86

tamāla-śyāmala-tvishi

śrī-yaśodā-stanan-dhaye

krishna-nāmno rūdhir iti

sarva-śāstra-vinirnayah

SYNONYMS

tamāla-śyāmala-tvishi — whose complexion is dark blue, resembling a tamāla tree; śrī-yaśodā-stanam-dhaye — sucking the breast of mother Yaśodā; krishna-nāmnah — of the name Krishna; rūdhih — the chief meaning; iti — thus; sarva-śāstra — of all revealed scriptures; vinirnayah — the conclusion.

TRANSLATION

"The only purport of the holy name of Krishna is that He is dark blue like a tamāla tree and is the son of mother Yaśodā. This is the conclusion of all the revealed scriptures.'

PURPORT

This is a verse from the Nāma-kaumudī
