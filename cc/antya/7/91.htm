Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.91

prabhura upekshāya saba nīlācalera jana

bhattera vyākhyāna kichu nā kare śravana

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; upekshāya — because of neglect; saba — all; nīlācalera jana — people in Jagannātha Purī; bhattera vyākhyāna — explanation of Vallabha Bhatta; kichu — any; nā kare śravana — do not hear.

TRANSLATION

Because Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu did not take Vallabha Bhatta very seriously, none of the people in Jagannātha Purī would hear any of his explanations.
