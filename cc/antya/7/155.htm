Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.155

pathe panditere svarūpa kahena vacana

"parīkshite prabhu tomāre kailā upekshana

SYNONYMS

pathe — on the way; panditere — unto Gadādhara Pandita; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara; kahena vacana — said some words; parīkshite — to test; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tomāre — you; kailā upekshana — neglected.

TRANSLATION

On the way, Svarūpa Dāmodara said to Gadādhara Pandita, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wanted to test you. Therefore He neglected you.
