Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.147

pūrve yena krishna yadi parihāsa kaila

śuni' rukminīra mane trāsa upajila

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yena — as; krishna — Lord Krishna; yadi — when; parihāsa kaila — play a joke; śuni' — hearing; rukminīra mane — in the mind of Rukminīdevī; trāsa — fear; upajila — arose.

TRANSLATION

Previously, in krishna-līlā, when Lord Krishna joked with Rukminīdevī, she took His words seriously, and fear awoke within her mind.
