Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.166

panditera saujanya, brahmanyatā-guna

dridha prema-mudrā loke karilā khyāpana

SYNONYMS

panditera saujanya — the gentle behavior of Gadādhara Pandita; brahmanyatā-guna — the attributes of a perfect brāhmana; dridha — firm; prema-mudrā — characteristic of love; loke — people; karilā khyāpana — proclaimed.

TRANSLATION

Gadādhara Pandita is celebrated all over the world for his gentle behavior, his brahminical attributes and his steady love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
