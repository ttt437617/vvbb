Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.146

ei lakshya pāñā prabhu kailā roshābhāsa

śuni' panditera citte upajila trāsa

SYNONYMS

ei — this; lakshya — aim; pāñā — taking; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā rosha-ābhāsa — made a semblance of anger; śuni' — hearing; panditera — of Gadādhara Pandita; citte — in the heart; upajila — arose; trāsa — fear.

TRANSLATION

For this purpose Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sometimes showed His apparent anger. Hearing of this anger inspired great fear in the heart of Gadādhara Pandita.
