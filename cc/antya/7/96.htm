Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.96

yadyapi pandita āra nā kailā ańgīkāra

bhatta yāi' tabu pade kari' balātkāra

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; pandita — Gadādhara Pandita; āra — also; nā kailā ańgīkāra — did not accept; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; yāi' — going; tabu — still; pade — reads; kari' balātkāra — forcibly.

TRANSLATION

Although Gadādhara Pandita Gosāñi did not want to hear it, Vallabha Bhatta began to read his explanation with great force.
