Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.75

caudda mādala bāje ucca sańkīrtana

eka eka nartakera preme bhāsila bhuvana

SYNONYMS

caudda mādala — fourteen mridańgas; bāje — were being played; ucca sańkīrtana — loud congregational chanting; eka eka — of each group; nartakera — of the dancer; preme — in ecstatic love; bhāsila bhuvana — inundated the entire world.

TRANSLATION

Fourteen mridańgas resounded with the loud congregational chanting, and in each group was a dancer whose dance of ecstatic love inundated the entire world.
