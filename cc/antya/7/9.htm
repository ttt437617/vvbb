Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.9

tomāre ye smarana kare, se haya pavitra

darśane pavitra habe, — ithe ki vicitra?

SYNONYMS

tomāre — You; ye — anyone who; smarana kare — remembers; se — he; haya — becomes; pavitra — purified; darśane — by seeing; pavitra — purified; habe — will be; ithe — in this; ki vicitra — what astonishment.

TRANSLATION

"Since one who remembers You is purified, why should it be astonishing that one becomes purified by seeing You?
