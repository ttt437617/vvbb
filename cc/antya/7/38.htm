Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.38

dāmodara-svarūpa — 'prema-rasa' mūrtimān

yāńra sańge haila vraja-madhura-rasa-jñāna

SYNONYMS

dāmodara-svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; prema-rasa — the transcendental mellows of ecstatic love; mūrtimān — personified; yāńra sańge — by whose association; haila — there was; vraja — of Vraja; madhura-rasa — of the mellow of conjugal love; jñāna — knowledge.

TRANSLATION

"The transcendental mellows of ecstatic love are personified by Svarūpa Dāmodara. By his association I have understood Vrindāvana's transcendental mellow of conjugal love.
