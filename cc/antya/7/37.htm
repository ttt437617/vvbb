Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.37

kahana nā yāya rāmānandera prabhāva

rāya-prasāde jāniluń vrajera 'śuddha' bhāva

SYNONYMS

kahana nā yāya — cannot be described; rāmānandera prabhāva — the influence of Rāmānanda Rāya; rāya — of Rāmānanda Rāya; prasāde — by the mercy; jāniluń — I have understood; vrajera — of the inhabitants of Vraja; śuddha bhāva — unalloyed love.

TRANSLATION

"It is impossible to describe the influence and knowledge of Rāmānanda Rāya, for only by his mercy have I understood the unalloyed love of the residents of Vrindāvana.
