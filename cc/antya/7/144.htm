Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.144

gadādhara-panditera śuddha gādha bhāva

rukminī-devīra yaiche 'dakshina-svabhāva'

SYNONYMS

gadādhara-panditera — of Gadādhara Pandita; śuddha — pure; gādha — deep; bhāva — ecstatic love; rukminī-devīra — of Rukminīdevī; yaiche — as; dakshina-svabhāva — submissive nature.

TRANSLATION

Gadādhara Pandita's pure ecstatic love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was also very deep. It was like that of Rukminīdevī, who was always especially submissive to Krishna.
