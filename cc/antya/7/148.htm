Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.148

vallabha-bhattera haya vātsalya-upāsana

bāla-gopāla-mantre teńho karena sevana

SYNONYMS

vallabha-bhattera — of Vallabha Bhatta; haya — there is; vātsalya-upāsana — worship as a parent; bāla-gopāla-mantre — with the mantra of Bāla-gopāla, child Krishna; teńho — he; karena — practices; sevana — worship.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta was accustomed to worshiping the Lord as child Krishna. Therefore he had been initiated into the Bāla-gopāla mantra and was thus worshiping the Lord.
