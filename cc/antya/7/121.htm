Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.121

svagana-sahite mora mānilā nimantrana

ebe kene prabhura mote phiri' gela mana?

SYNONYMS

sva-gana-sahite — accompanied by His personal associates; mora — my; mānilā — accepted; nimantrana — invitation; ebe — now; kene — why; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mote — unto me; phiri' gela — has changed; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"He accepted my invitation with His other devotees, and He was kind to me. Why has He now changed so much here at Jagannātha Purī?
