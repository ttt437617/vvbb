Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.89

vimanā hañā bhatta gelā nija-ghara

prabhu-vishaye bhakti kichu ha-ila antara

SYNONYMS

vimanā hañā — feeling morose; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; gelā — went; nija-ghara — to his home; prabhu-vishaye — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakti — devotion; kichu — somewhat; ha-ila — became; antara — different.

TRANSLATION

When Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu rigidly declined to hear his explanations, Vallabha Bhatta went home feeling morose. His faith in the Lord and devotion to Him changed.
