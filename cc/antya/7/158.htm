Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.158

yei kahe, sei sahi nija-śire dhari'

āpane karibena kripā guna-dosha vicāri'"

SYNONYMS

yei kahe — whatever He says; sei sahi — I tolerate that; nija-śire — on my head; dhari' — bearing; āpane — automatically; karibena kripā — He will be merciful; guna-dosha — attributes and faults; vicāri' — after considering.

TRANSLATION

"I can tolerate whatever He says, bearing it upon my head. He will automatically be merciful to me after considering my faults and attributes."
