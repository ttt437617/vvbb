Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.100

pratyaha vallabha-bhatta āise prabhu-sthāne

'udgrāhādi' prāya kare ācāryādi-sane

SYNONYMS

prati-aha — daily; vallabha-bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; āise — comes; prabhu-sthāne — to the place of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; udgrāha-ādi prāya — unnecessary argument; kare — does; ācārya-ādi-sane — with Advaita Ācārya and others.

TRANSLATION

Every day, Vallabha Bhatta would come to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to engage in unnecessary arguments with Advaita Ācārya and other great personalities, such as Svarūpa Dāmodara.
