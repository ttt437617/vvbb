Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.162

āmāra bhańgīte tomāra mana nā calilā

sudridha sarala-bhāve āmāre kinilā"

SYNONYMS

āmāra bhańgīte — by My trick; tomāra mana — your mind; nā calilā — did not become disturbed; sudridha — firm; sarala-bhāve — by simplicity; āmāre — Me; kinilā — you have purchased.

TRANSLATION

"Your mind was not disturbed by My tricks. Rather, you stayed fixed in your simplicity. In this way you have purchased Me."
