Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.13

jagate karilā tumi krishna-nāma prakāśe

yei tomā dekhe, sei krishna-preme bhāse

SYNONYMS

jagate — throughout the entire world; karilā — have done; tumi — You; krishna-nāma prakāśe — manifestation of the holy name of Lord Krishna; yei — anyone who; tomā dekhe — sees You; sei — he; krishna-preme — in ecstatic love of Krishna; bhāse — floats.

TRANSLATION

"You have manifested the holy name of Krishna throughout the entire world. Anyone who sees You is immediately absorbed in ecstatic love of Krishna.
