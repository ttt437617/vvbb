Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.99

yadyapi vicāre panditera nāhi kichu dosha

tathāpi prabhura gana tāńre kare pranaya-rosha

SYNONYMS

yadyapi — although; vicāre — conclusively; panditera — of Gadādhara Pandita; nāhi kichu dosha — there was no fault; tathāpi — still; prabhura gana — associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — unto him; kare pranaya-rosha — showed affectionate anger.

TRANSLATION

Although Gadādhara Pandita Gosāñi was not in the least at fault, some of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's devotees showed affectionate anger toward him.
