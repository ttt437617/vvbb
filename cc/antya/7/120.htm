Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.120

ghare āsi' rātrye bhatta cintite lāgila

"pūrve prayāge more mahā-kripā kaila

SYNONYMS

ghare āsi' — coming home; rātrye — at night; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; cintite lāgila — began to think; pūrve — previously; prayāge — at Prayāga; more — unto me; mahā-kripā kaila — showed great mercy.

TRANSLATION

Returning home that night, Vallabha Bhatta thought, "Previously, at Prayāga, Lord Caitanya was very kind to me.
