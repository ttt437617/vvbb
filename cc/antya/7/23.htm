Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.23

rāmānanda-rāya krishna-rasera 'nidhāna'

teńha jānāilā — krishna — svayam bhagavān

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda-rāya — Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya; krishna-rasera — of the transcendental mellows of Krishna's devotional service; nidhāna — the mine; teńha — he; jānāilā — has given instruction; krishna — Lord Krishna; svayam — Himself; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya is the ultimate knower of the transcendental mellows of Lord Krishna's devotional service. He has instructed Me that Lord Krishna is the Supreme Personality of Godhead.
