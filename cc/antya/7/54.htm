Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.54

"āmi se 'vaishnava', — bhakti-siddhānta saba jāni

āmi se bhāgavata-artha uttama vākhāni"

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; se — that; vaishnava — Vaishnava; bhakti-siddhānta — conclusions of devotional service; saba — all; jāni — I know; āmi — I; se — that; bhāgavata-artha — meaning of the Bhāgavatam; uttama — very well; vākhāni — can explain.

TRANSLATION

[Vallabha Bhatta was thinking:] "I am a great Vaishnava. Having learned all the conclusions of Vaishnava philosophy, I can understand the meaning of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and explain it very well."
