Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.60

tabe bhatta kahe bahu vinaya vacana

bahu dainya kari' prabhure kaila nimantrana

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; bhatta kahe — Vallabha Bhatta said; bahu — very; vinaya — humble; vacana — words; bahu dainya kari' — in all humility; prabhure — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila nimantrana — invited to dine.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, with great submission and humility, Vallabha Bhatta invited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to dine at his home.
