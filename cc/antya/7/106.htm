Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.106

śuni' prabhu kahena, — "tumi nā jāna dharma-marma

svāmi-ājñā pāle, — ei pati-vratā-dharma

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; prabhu kahena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; tumi — you; nā jāna — do not know; dharma-marma — real religious principles; svāmi — of the husband; ājñā — order; pāle — obeys; ei — this; pati-vratā-dharma — the religious principle of a chaste woman.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Vallabha Bhatta, you do not know religious principles. Actually, the first duty of a chaste woman is to carry out the order of her husband.
