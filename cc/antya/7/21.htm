Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.21

shad-darśana-vettā bhattācārya-sārvabhauma

shad-darśane jagad-guru bhāgavatottama

SYNONYMS

shat-darśana — of the six philosophical theses; vettā — the knower; bhattācārya-sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; shat-darśane — in six philosophical theses; jagat-guru — the spiritual master of the entire world; bhāgavata-uttama — the best of the devotees.

TRANSLATION

"Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya perfectly knows the six philosophical theses. He is therefore the spiritual master of the entire world in teaching the six paths of philosophy. He is the best of devotees.
