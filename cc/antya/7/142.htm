Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.142

jagadānanda-panditera śuddha gādha bhāva

satyabhāmā-prāya prema 'vāmya-svabhāva'

SYNONYMS

jagadānanda-panditera — of Jagadānanda Pandita; śuddha — pure; gādha — deep; bhāva — ecstatic love; satyabhāmā-prāya — like Satyabhāmā; prema — his love for the Lord; vāmya-svabhāva — quarrelsome nature.

TRANSLATION

Jagadānanda Pandita's pure ecstatic love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was very deep. It can be compared to the love of Satyabhāmā, who always quarreled with Lord Krishna.
