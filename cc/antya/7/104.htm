Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.104

pati-vratā hañā patira nāma nāhi laya

tomarā krishna-nāma laha, — kon dharma haya?"

SYNONYMS

pati-vratā — devoted to the husband; hañā — being; patira — of the husband; nāma — name; nāhi laya — does not utter; tomarā — all of you; krishna-nāma-laha — chant the name of Krishna; kon — what; dharma — religious principle; haya — is it.

TRANSLATION

"It is the duty of a chaste wife, devoted to her husband, not to utter her husband's name, but all of you chant the name of Krishna. How can this be called a religious principle?"
