Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.107

patira ājñā, — nirantara tāńra nāma la-ite

patira ājñā pati-vratā nā pāre lańghite

SYNONYMS

patira ājñā — the husband's order; nirantara — always; tāńra — His; nāma — name; la-ite — to chant; patira ājñā — the order of the husband; pati-vratā — a chaste, devoted wife; nā pāre lańghite — cannot deny.

TRANSLATION

"The order of Krishna is to chant His name incessantly. Therefore one who is chaste and adherent to the husband Krishna must chant the Lord's name, for she cannot deny the husband's order.
