Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.172

ei ta' kahiluń vallabha-bhattera milana

yāhāra śravane pāya gaura-prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have explained; vallabha-bhattera milana — the meeting of Vallabha Bhatta; yāhāra śravane — by hearing which; pāya — one can get; gaura-prema-dhana — the treasure of love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

I have thus explained the Lord's meeting with Vallabha Bhatta. By hearing of this incident, one can achieve the treasure of love for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
