Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.103

eka-dina bhatta puchila ācāryere

"jīva-'prakriti' 'pati' kari' mānaye krishnere

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; puchila ācāryere — inquired from Advaita Ācārya; jīva — the living entity; prakriti — female; pati — husband; kari' — as; mānaye krishnere — accepts Krishna.

TRANSLATION

One day Vallabha Bhatta said to Advaita Ācārya, "Every living entity is female [prakriti] and considers Krishna her husband [pati].
