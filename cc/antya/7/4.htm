Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.4

ei-mata vilāsa prabhura bhakta-gana lañā

hena-kāle vallabha-bhatta milila āsiyā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; vilāsa — pastimes; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana lañā — with His devotees; hena-kāle — at this time; vallabha-bhatta — the greatly learned scholar named Vallabha Bhatta; milila — met; āsiyā — coming.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu performed His pastimes with His devotees. Then a learned scholar named Vallabha Bhatta went to Jagannātha Purī to meet the Lord.

PURPORT

For a description of Vallabha Bhatta, one may refer to Madhya-līlā, Chapter Nineteen, text 61.
