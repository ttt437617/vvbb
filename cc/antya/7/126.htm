Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.126

"āmi ajña jīva, — ajñocita karma kailuń

tomāra āge mūrkha āmi pānditya prakāśiluń

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; ajña jīva — a foolish living being; ajña-ucita — fit for a fool; karma — activity; kailuń — I have done; tomāra āge — before You; mūrkha — a fool; āmi — I; pānditya prakāśiluń — have tried to demonstrate learning.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta admitted, "I am a great fool, and indeed I have acted like a fool by trying to demonstrate my learning to You.
