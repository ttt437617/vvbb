Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.114

sei vyākhyā karena yāhāń yei pade āni'

eka-vākyatā nāhi, tāte 'svāmī' nāhi māni"

SYNONYMS

sei — he; vyākhyā karena — explains; yāhāń — wherever; yei — whatever; pade — reads; āni' — accepting; eka-vākyatā — consistency; nāhi — there is not; tāte — therefore; svāmī — Śrīdhara Svāmī; nāhi māni — I cannot accept.

TRANSLATION

"Whatever Śrīdhara Svāmī reads he explains according to the circumstances. Therefore he is inconsistent in his explanations and cannot be accepted as an authority."
