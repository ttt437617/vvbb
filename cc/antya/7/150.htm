Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.150

panditera thāñi cāhe mantrādi śikhite

pandita kahe, — "ei karma nahe āmā haite

SYNONYMS

panditera thāñi — from Gadādhara Pandita; cāhe — wanted; mantra-ādi śikhite — to be initiated; pandita kahe — Gadādhara Pandita said; ei karma — this work; nahe āmā haite — is not possible for me.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta wanted to be initiated by Gadādhara Pandita, but Gadādhara Pandita refused, saying, "The work of acting as a spiritual master is not possible for me.
