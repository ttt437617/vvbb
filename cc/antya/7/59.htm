Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.59

ihāńi rahena sabe, vāsā — nānā-sthāne

ihāńi pāibā tumi sabāra darśane"

SYNONYMS

ihāńi — here; rahena sabe — all of them are living; vāsā — their residential places; nānā-sthāne — in various quarters; ihāńi — here; pāibā — will get; tumi — you; sabāra darśane — everyone's audience.

TRANSLATION

"At present they are all living here. Their residences are in various quarters. Here you will get the audience of them all."
