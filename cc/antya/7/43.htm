Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.43

sarvottama bhajana ei sarva-bhakti jini'

ataeva krishna kahe, — 'āmi tomāra rinī'

SYNONYMS

sarva-uttama — above all; bhajana — devotional service; ei — this; sarva-bhakti — all types of bhakti; jini' — conquering; ataeva — therefore; krishna kahe — Lord Krishna says; āmi — I; tomāra — your; rinī — debtor.

TRANSLATION

"The conjugal love of the gopīs is the most exalted devotional service, surpassing all other methods of bhakti. Therefore Lord Krishna is obliged to say, 'My dear gopīs, I cannot repay you. Indeed, I am always indebted to you.'
