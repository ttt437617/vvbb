Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.136

śrīdharānugata kara bhāgavata-vyākhyāna

abhimāna chādi' bhaja krishna bhagavān

SYNONYMS

śrīdhara-anugata — following in the footsteps of Śrīdhara Svāmī; kara — put forth; bhāgavata-vyākhyāna — an explanation of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; abhimāna chādi' — giving up false pride or false conceptions; bhaja — worship; krishna bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Put forth your explanation of Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam following in the footsteps of Śrīdhara Svāmī. Giving up your false pride, worship the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna.
