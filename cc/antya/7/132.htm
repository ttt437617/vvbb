Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.132

śrīdhara-svāmī nindi' nija-tīkā kara!

śrīdhara-svāmī nāhi māna', — eta 'garva' dhara!

SYNONYMS

śrīdhara-svāmī — a great commentator on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; nindi' — blaspheming; nija-tīkā — your own commentary; kara — you make; śrīdhara-svāmī — Śrīdhara Svāmī; nāhi māna' — you do not accept; eta — this; garva — pride; dhara — you bear.

TRANSLATION

"You have dared criticize Śrīdhara Svāmī, and you have begun your own commentary on Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, not accepting his authority. That is your false pride.
