Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.138

bhatta kahe, — "yadi more ha-ilā prasanna

eka-dina punah mora māna' nimantrana"

SYNONYMS

bhatta kahe — Vallabha Bhatta said; yadi — if; more — with me; ha-ilā prasanna — You are pleased; eka-dina — one day; punah — again; mora — my; māna' — accept; nimantrana — invitation.

TRANSLATION

Vallabha Bhatta Ācārya requested Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, "If You are actually pleased with me, please accept my invitation once again."
