Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.46

teńha yāńra pada-dhūli karena prārthana

svarūpera sańge pāiluń e saba śikshana

SYNONYMS

teńha — he; yāńra — whose; pada-dhūli — dust of the lotus feet; karena prārthana — desires; svarūpera sańge — from Svarūpa Dāmodara; pāiluń — I have gotten; e saba — all these; śikshana — instructions.

TRANSLATION

"Uddhava desires to take on his head the dust of the gopīs' lotus feet. I have learned about all these transcendental loving affairs of Lord Krishna from Svarūpa Dāmodara.
