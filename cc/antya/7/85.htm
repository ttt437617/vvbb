Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.85

prabhu kahe, — "krishna-nāmera bahu artha nā māni

'śyāma-sundara' 'yaśodā-nandana,' — ei-mātra jāni

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; krishna-nāmera — of the holy name of Krishna; bahu artha — many meanings; nā māni — I do not accept; śyāma-sundara — Śyāmasundara; yaśodā-nandana — Yaśodānandana; ei-mātra — only this; jāni — I know.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "I do not accept many different meanings for the holy name of Krishna. I know only that Lord Krishna is Śyāmasundara and Yaśodānandana. That's all I know.
