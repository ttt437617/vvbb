Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.18

sarva-śāstre krishna-bhaktye nāhi yāńra sama

ataeva 'advaita-ācārya' tāńra nāma

SYNONYMS

sarva-śāstre — in all revealed scriptures; krishna-bhaktye — in the devotional service of Lord Krishna; nāhi — is not; yāńra — of whom; sama — equal; ataeva — therefore; advaita — without a competitor; ācārya — ācārya; tāńra nāma — His name.

TRANSLATION

"He is unparalleled in His understanding of all the revealed scriptures and the devotional service of Lord Krishna. Therefore He is called Advaita Ācārya.
