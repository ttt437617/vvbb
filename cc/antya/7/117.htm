Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.117

jagatera hita lāgi' gaura-avatāra

antarera abhimāna jānena tāhāra

SYNONYMS

jagatera — of the entire world; hita lāgi' — for the benefit; gaura-avatāra — the incarnation of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; antarera abhimāna — internal pride; jānena — understands; tāhāra — his.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu descended as an incarnation for the benefit of the entire world. Thus He knew the mind of Vallabha Bhatta very well.
