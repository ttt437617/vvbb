Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.3

varshāntare yata gaudera bhakta-gana āilā

pūrvavat mahāprabhu sabāre mililā

SYNONYMS

varsha-antare — the next year; yata — all; gaudera — of Bengal; bhakta-gana — devotees; āilā — came; pūrva-vat — as previously; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sabāre mililā — met every one of them.

TRANSLATION

The next year, all the devotees of Bengal went to visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and as previously, the Lord met each and every one of them.
