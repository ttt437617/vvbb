Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.94

krishna-nāma-vyākhyā yadi karaha śravana

tabe mora lajjā-pańka haya prakshālana"

SYNONYMS

krishna-nāma — of the name of Lord Krishna; vyākhyā — explanation; yadi — if; karaha śravana — you hear; tabe — then; mora — my; lajjā-pańka — the mud of shame; haya — there is; prakshālana — washing.

TRANSLATION

"Please hear my explanation of the meaning of Lord Krishna's name. In that way the mud of the shame that has come upon me will be washed off."
