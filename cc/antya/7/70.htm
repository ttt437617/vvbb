Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.70

prasāda pāya vaishnava-gana bale, 'hari' 'hari'

hari hari dhvani uthe saba brahmānda bhari'

SYNONYMS

prasāda — the prasādam; pāya — accept; vaishnava-gana — all the Vaishnavas; bale — chant; hari hari — "Hari, Hari"; hari hari dhvani — the vibration of Hari, Hari; uthe — rises; saba brahmānda — the entire universe; bhari' — filling.

TRANSLATION

Accepting the prasādam, all the Vaishnavas chanted the holy names "Hari! Hari!" The rising vibration of the holy name of Hari filled the entire universe.
