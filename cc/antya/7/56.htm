Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.56

prabhura mukhe vaishnavatā śuniyā sabāra

bhattera icchā haila tāń-sabāre dekhibāra

SYNONYMS

prabhura mukhe — from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vaishnavatā — the standard of Vaishnavism; śuniyā sabāra — hearing of all the devotees; bhattera — of Vallabha Bhatta; icchā — desire; haila — was; tāń-sabāre — all of them; dekhibāra — to see.

TRANSLATION

When Vallabha Bhatta heard from the mouth of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu about the pure Vaishnavism of all these devotees, he immediately desired to see them.
