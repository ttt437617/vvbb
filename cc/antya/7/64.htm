Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.64

paramānanda purī-sańge sannyāsīra gana

eka-dike vaise saba karite bhojana

SYNONYMS

paramānanda purī-sańge — with Paramānanda Purī; sannyāsīra gana — all the sannyāsī associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; eka-dike — on one side; vaise — sat down; saba — all; karite bhojana — to accept the prasādam.

TRANSLATION

All the sannyāsī associates of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, headed by Paramānanda Purī, sat on one side and thus partook of the prasādam.
