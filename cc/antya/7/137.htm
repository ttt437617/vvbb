Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.137

aparādha chādi' kara krishna-sańkīrtana

acirāt pābe tabe krishnera carana"

SYNONYMS

aparādha chādi' — giving up offenses; kara krishna-sańkīrtana — chant the holy name of the Lord; acirāt — very soon; pābe — you will get; tabe — thereupon; krishnera carana — shelter at the lotus feet of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Abandoning your offenses, chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra, the holy names of the Lord. Then very soon you will achieve shelter at the lotus feet of Krishna."
