Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.108

ataeva nāma laya, nāmera 'phala' pāya

nāmera phale krishna-pade 'prema' upajāya"

SYNONYMS

ataeva — therefore; nāma laya — chants the holy name; nāmera — of the name; phala — result; pāya — gets; nāmera phale — as a result of chanting the holy name; krishna-pade — at the lotus feet of Krishna; prema — ecstatic love; upajāya — develops.

TRANSLATION

"Following this religious principle, a pure devotee of Lord Krishna always chants the holy name. As a result of this, he gets the fruit of ecstatic love for Krishna."
