Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.22

teńha dekhāilā more bhakti-yoga-pāra

tāńra prasāde jāniluń 'krishna-bhakti-yoga' sāra

SYNONYMS

teńha — he; dekhāilā — has shown; more — to Me; bhakti-yoga — of devotional service; pāra — the limit; tāńra prasāde — by his mercy; jāniluń — I have understood; krishna-bhakti — of devotional service to Lord Krishna; yoga — of the yoga system; sāra — the essence.

TRANSLATION

"Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya has shown Me the limit of devotional service. Only by his mercy have I understood that devotional service to Krishna is the essence of all mystic yoga.
