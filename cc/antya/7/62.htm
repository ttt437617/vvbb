Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.62

'vaishnave'ra teja dekhi' bhattera camatkāra

tāń-sabāra āge bhatta — khadyota-ākāra

SYNONYMS

vaishnavera — of the Vaishnavas; teja — the brilliance; dekhi' — seeing; bhattera — of Vallabha Bhatta; camatkāra — surprise; tāń-sabāra — of all of them; āge — in front; bhatta — Vallabha Bhatta; khadyota-ākāra — like a glowworm.

TRANSLATION

He was surprised to see the brilliance of their faces. Indeed, among them Vallabha Bhatta seemed just like a glowworm.
