Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.41

gopī-ganera śuddha-prema aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna

premete bhartsanā kare ei tāra cihna

SYNONYMS

gopī-ganera — of the gopīs; śuddha-prema — unalloyed love; aiśvarya-jñāna-hīna — devoid of knowledge of opulences; premete — of pure love; bhartsanā — chastisement; kare — do; ei — this; tāra — of that; cihna — the symptom.

TRANSLATION

"Obsessed with pure love, without knowledge of opulences, the gopīs sometimes chastise Krishna. That is a symptom of pure ecstatic love.
