Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.123

āpanā jānāite āmi kari abhimāna

se garva khandāite mora karena apamāna

SYNONYMS

āpanā jānāite — advertising myself; āmi — I; kari abhimāna — am falsely proud; se garva — that pride; khandāite — to cut down; mora karena apamāna — He insults me.

TRANSLATION

"I am falsely proud, advertising myself as a learned scholar. Therefore Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu insults me just to favor me by cutting down this false pride.
