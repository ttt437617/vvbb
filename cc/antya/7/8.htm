Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.8

tomāra darśana ye pāya sei bhāgyavān

tomāke dekhiye, — yena sākshāt bhagavān

SYNONYMS

tomāra darśana — Your audience; ye pāya — anyone who gets; sei — he; bhāgyavān — very fortunate; tomāke dekhiye — I see You; yena — as if; sākshāt bhagavān — directly the Supreme Personality of Godhead.

TRANSLATION

"One who receives Your audience is fortunate indeed, for You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead Himself.
