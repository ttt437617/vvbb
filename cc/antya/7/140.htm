Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.140

jagatera 'hita' ha-uka — ei prabhura mana

danda kari' kare tāra hridaya śodhana

SYNONYMS

jagatera — of the entire world; hita — welfare; ha-uka — let there be; ei — this; prabhura mana — the mind of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; danda kari' — punishing; kare — does; tāra — his; hridaya — heart; śodhana — purifying.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is always eager to see everyone in the material world happy. Therefore sometimes He chastises someone just to purify his heart.
