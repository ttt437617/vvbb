Antya-līlāChapter 7: The Meeting of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and Vallabha Bhatta

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 7.157

pandita kahena, — prabhu svatantra sarvajña-śiromani

tāńra sane 'hatha' kari, — bhāla nāhi māni

SYNONYMS

pandita kahena — Gadādhara Pandita said; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; svatantra — independent; sarvajña-śiromani — the best of the omniscient; tāńra sane — with Him; hatha kari — if I talk on an equal level; bhāla — good; nāhi māni — I do not think it is.

TRANSLATION

Gadādhara Pandita said, "Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu is completely independent. He is the topmost omniscient personality. It would not look well for me to talk to Him as if I were His equal.
