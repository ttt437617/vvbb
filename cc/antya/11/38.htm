Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.38

kintu āmāra ye kichu sukha, saba tomā lañā

tomāra yogya nahe, — yābe āmāre chādiyā"

SYNONYMS

kintu — but; āmāra — My; ye — whatever; kichu — any; sukha — happiness; saba — all; tomā lañā — because of your association; tomāra — for you; yogya nahe — it is not fit; yābe — you will go away; āmāre chādiyā — leaving Me behind.

TRANSLATION

"But whatever happiness is Mine is all due to your association. It is not fitting for you to go away and leave Me behind."
