Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.99

sabe gāya, — "jaya jaya jaya haridāsa

nāmera mahimā yeńha karilā prakāśa"

SYNONYMS

sabe gāya — everyone chanted; jaya jaya jaya — all glories; haridāsa — to Haridāsa Thākura; nāmera mahimā — the glories of chanting the holy name; yeńha — who; karilā prakāśa — revealed.

TRANSLATION

Everyone began to chant, "All glories to Haridāsa Thākura, who revealed the importance of chanting the holy name of the Lord!"
