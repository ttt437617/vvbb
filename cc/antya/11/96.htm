Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.96

icchā-mātre kailā nija-prāna nishkrāmana

pūrve yena śuniyāchi bhīshmera marana

SYNONYMS

icchā-mātre — just by desire; kailā — performed; nija-prāna — of his life; nishkrāmana — going away; pūrve — formerly; yena — as; śuniyāchi — we have heard; bhīshmera marana — the death of Bhīshmadeva.

TRANSLATION

"Simply by his will, Haridāsa Thākura could give up his life and go away, exactly like Bhīshma, who previously died simply by his own desire, as we have heard from śāstra.
