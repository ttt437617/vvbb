Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.32

sei līlā prabhu more kabhu nā dekhāibā

āpanāra āge mora śarīra pādibā

SYNONYMS

sei līlā — that pastime; prabhu — my Lord; more — unto me; kabhu — ever; nā dekhāibā — do not show; āpanāra āge — before You; mora śarīra — my body; pādibā — let fall down.

TRANSLATION

"I wish that You not show me this closing chapter of Your pastimes. Before that time comes, kindly let my body fall down in Your presence.
