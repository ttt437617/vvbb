Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.94

kripā kari' krishna more diyāchilā sańga

svatantra krishnera icchā, — kailā sańga-bhańga

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — being merciful; krishna — Lord Krishna; more — unto Me; diyāchilā sańga — gave the association; svatantra — independent; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; icchā — desire; kailā sańga-bhańga — He has broken My association.

TRANSLATION

"Being merciful upon Me, Krishna gave Me the association of Haridāsa Thākura. Being independent in His desires, He has now broken that association.
