Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.80

vānīnātha pattanāyaka prasāda ānilā

kāśī-miśra aneka prasāda pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

vānīnātha pattanāyaka — Vānīnātha Pattanāyaka; prasāda — prasādam; ānilā — brought in; kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; aneka prasāda — varieties of prasādam; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

Not only did Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī bring prasādam, but Vānīnātha Pattanāyaka and Kāśī Miśra also sent large quantities.
