Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.81

saba vaishnave prabhu vasāilā sāri sāri

āpane pariveśe prabhu lañā janā cāri

SYNONYMS

saba vaishnave — all the Vaishnavas; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; vasāilā — made to sit down; sāri sāri — in lines; āpane — personally; pariveśe — distributes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lañā — taking; janā cāri — four men.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu made all the devotees sit in rows and personally began to distribute the prasādam, assisted by four other men.
