Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.55

'śrī-krishna-caitanya' śabda balena bāra bāra

prabhu-mukha-mādhurī piye, netre jala-dhāra

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya; śabda — vibration; balena — speaks; bāra bāra — again and again; prabhu-mukha-mādhurī — the sweetness of the face of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; piye — he drinks; netre — through the eyes; jala-dhāra — a continuous flow of water.

TRANSLATION

He began to chant the holy name of Śrī Krishna Caitanya again and again. As he drank the sweetness of the face of the Lord, tears constantly glided down from his eyes.
