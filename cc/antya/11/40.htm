Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.40

mora śiromani kata kata mahāśaya

tomāra līlāra sahāya koti-bhakta haya

SYNONYMS

mora — my; śiromani — crown jewels; kata kata — many, many; mahāśaya — great persons; tomāra līlāra — in Your pastimes; sahāya — helpers; koti-bhakta — millions of devotees; haya — there are.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, there are many respectable personalities, millions of devotees, who are fit to sit on my head. They are all helpful in Your pastimes.
