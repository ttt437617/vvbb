Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.106

caitanya-caritra ei amritera sindhu

karna-mana tripta kare yāra eka bindu

SYNONYMS

caitanya-caritra — the life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ei — this; amritera sindhu — the ocean of nectar; karna — ear; mana — mind; tripta kare — pleases; yāra — of which; eka — one; bindu — drop.

TRANSLATION

The life and characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are exactly like an ocean of nectar, one drop of which can please the mind and ear.
