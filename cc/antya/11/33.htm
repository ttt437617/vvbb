Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.33

hridaye dharimu tomāra kamala carana

nayane dekhimu tomāra cāńda vadana

SYNONYMS

hridaye — upon my heart; dharimu — I shall catch; tomāra — Your; kamala carana — lotuslike feet; nayane — with my eyes; dekhimu — I shall see; tomāra — Your; cāńda vadana — face like the moon.

TRANSLATION

"I wish to catch Your lotuslike feet upon my heart and see Your moonlike face.
