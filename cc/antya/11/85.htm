Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.85

prabhu nā khāile keha nā kare bhojana

prabhure se dine kāśī-miśrera nimantrana

SYNONYMS

prabhu nā khāile — as long as the Lord does not eat; keha nā kare bhojana — no one would eat; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; se dine — on that day; kāśī-miśrera — of Kāśī Miśra; nimantrana — the invitation.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees who sat down would not eat the prasādam as long as the Lord had not eaten. On that day, however, Kāśī Miśra had extended an invitation to the Lord.
