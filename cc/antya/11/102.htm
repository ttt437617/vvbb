Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.102

caitanyera bhakta-vātsalya ihātei jāni

bhakta-vāñchā pūrna kailā nyāsi-śiromani

SYNONYMS

caitanyera — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-vātsalya — affection for His devotees; ihātei — from this; jāni — one can understand; bhakta-vāñchā — the desire of the devotee; pūrna kailā — fully satisfied; nyāsi-śiromani — the crown jewel of the sannyāsīs, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

From the incident of Haridāsa Thākura's passing away and the great care Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu took in commemorating it, one can understand just how affectionate He is toward His devotees. Although He is the topmost of all sannyāsīs, He fully satisfied the desire of Haridāsa Thākura.
