Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.82

mahāprabhura śrī-haste alpa nā āise

eka-eka pāte pañca-janāra bhakshya pariveśe

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śrī-haste — in the transcendental hands; alpa — a small quantity; nā āise — did not come; eka-eka pāte — on each and every plate; pañca-janāra — of five men; bhakshya — eatables; pariveśe — He administered.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was not accustomed to taking prasādam in small quantities. He therefore put on each plate what at least five men could eat.
