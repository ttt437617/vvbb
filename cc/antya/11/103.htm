Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.103

śesha-kāle dilā tāńre darśana-sparśana

tāńre kole kari' kailā āpane nartana

SYNONYMS

śesha-kāle — at the last stage of his life; dilā — gave; tāńre — to Haridāsa Thākura; darśana-sparśana — interview and touching; tāńre — him; kole kari' — taking on the lap; kailā — performed; āpane — personally; nartana — dancing.

TRANSLATION

When Haridāsa Thākura was at the last stage of his life, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu gave him His company and allowed him to touch Him. Thereafter, He took the body of Thākura Haridāsa on His lap and personally danced with it.
