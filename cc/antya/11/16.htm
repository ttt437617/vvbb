Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.16

eka-dina govinda mahā-prasāda lañā

haridāse dite gelā ānandita hañā

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; govinda — the personal servant of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahā-prasāda lañā — taking mahā-prasādam; haridāse dite — to deliver to Haridāsa; gelā — went; ānandita hañā — in great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

One day Govinda, the personal servant of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, went in great jubilation to deliver the remnants of Lord Jagannātha's food to Haridāsa Thākura.
