Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.13

ei-mata mahāprabhura sukhe kāla yāya

krishnera viraha-vikāra ańge nānā haya

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sukhe — in happiness; kāla yāya — time passes; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; viraha — from separation; vikāra — transformations; ańge — on the body; nānā — various; haya — there are.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu very happily passed His days in this way at Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī. Feeling separation from Krishna, He exhibited many transcendental symptoms all over His body.
