Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.6

jaya nityānanda-candra jaya caitanyera prāna

tomāra caranāravinde bhakti deha' dāna

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; nityānanda-candra — to Lord Nityānanda Prabhu; jaya — all glories; caitanyera prāna — to the life and soul of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tomāra carana-aravinde — at Your lotus feet; bhakti — devotional service; deha' — please give; dāna — the gift.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Lord Nityānanda, who is the life and soul of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu! My dear Lord, kindly give me engagement in devotional service at Your lotus feet.
