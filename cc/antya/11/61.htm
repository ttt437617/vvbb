Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.61

ei-mate nritya prabhu kailā kata-kshana

svarūpa-gosāñi prabhure karāila sāvadhāna

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; nritya — dancing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kailā — performed; kata-kshana — for some time; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; prabhure — unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karāila — caused to do; sāvadhāna — care of other rituals.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced for some time, and then Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī informed Him of other rituals for the body of Thākura Haridāsa.
