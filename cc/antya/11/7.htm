Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.7

jaya jayādvaita-candra caitanyera ārya

sva-carane bhakti deha' jayādvaitācārya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; advaita-candra — to Advaita Ācārya; caitanyera ārya — respected by the Lord; sva-carane — at Your lotus feet; bhakti deha' — please give devotional service; jaya — all glories; advaita-ācārya — to Advaita Ācārya.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Advaita Ācārya, who is treated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as superior due to His age and respectability! Please give me engagement in devotional service at Your lotus feet.
