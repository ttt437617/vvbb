Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.47

prabhu kahe, — 'haridāsa, kaha samācāra'

haridāsa kahe, — 'prabhu, ye kripā tomāra'

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; haridāsa — My dear Haridāsa; kaha samācāra — what is the news; haridāsa kahe — Haridāsa replied; prabhu — my Lord; ye — whatever; kripā — mercy; tomāra — Your.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired, "My dear Haridāsa, what is the news?"

Haridāsa Thākura replied, "My Lord, whatever mercy You can bestow upon me."
