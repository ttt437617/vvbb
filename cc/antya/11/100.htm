Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.100

tabe mahāprabhu saba bhakte vidāya dilā

harsha-vishāde prabhu viśrāma karilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; saba bhakte — to all the devotees; vidāya dilā — bade farewell; harsha-vishāde — in mixed happiness and distress; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; viśrāma karilā — took His rest.

TRANSLATION

Thereafter, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu bade farewell to all the devotees, and He Himself, with mixed feelings of happiness and distress, took rest.
