Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.26

ebe alpa sańkhyā kari' kara sańkīrtana"

haridāsa kahe, — "śuna mora satya nivedana

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; alpa sańkhyā — a reduced amount of chanting; kari' — doing; kara sańkīrtana — chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; haridāsa kahe — Haridāsa Thākura replied; śuna — kindly hear; mora — my; satya — real; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

The Lord concluded, "Now, therefore, please reduce the fixed number of times you chant the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra."

Haridāsa Thākura replied, "Kindly hear my real plea.
