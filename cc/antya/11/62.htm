Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.62

haridāsa-thākure tabe vimāne cadāñā

samudre lañā gelā tabe kīrtana kariyā

SYNONYMS

haridāsa-thākure — Haridāsa Thākura; tabe — then; vimāne — on a carrier like an airship; cadāñā — raising; samudre — to the seashore; lañā gelā — took; tabe — then; kīrtana kariyā — performing congregational chanting.

TRANSLATION

The body of Haridāsa Thākura was then raised onto a carrier that resembled an airship and taken to the sea, accompanied by congregational chanting.
