Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.15

svarūpa gosāñi, āra rāmānanda-rāya

rātri-dine kare dońhe prabhura sahāya

SYNONYMS

svarūpa gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; āra — and; rāmānanda-rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; rātri-dine — day and night; kare — do; dońhe — both of them; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sahāya — help.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī and Rāmānanda Rāya, the chief assistants in Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's pastimes, remained with Him both day and night.
