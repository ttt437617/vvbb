Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.75

śuniyā pasāri saba cāńgadā uthāñā

prasāda dite āse tārā ānandita hañā

SYNONYMS

śuniyā — hearing; pasāri — the shopkeepers; saba — all; cāńgadā uthāñā — taking big baskets; prasāda dite — to deliver the prasādam; āse — come forward; tārā — they; ānandita hañā — in great jubilation.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, all the shopkeepers immediately came forward with big baskets of prasādam, which they jubilantly delivered to Lord Caitanya.
