Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.36

ei nīca deha mora paduka tava āge

ei vāñchā-siddhi mora tomātei lāge"

SYNONYMS

ei — this; nīca — lowborn; deha — body; mora — my; paduka — let it fall down; tava āge — in front of You; ei — this; vāñchā-siddhi — perfection of desire; mora — my; tomātei — by You; lāge — can become possible.

TRANSLATION

"Let this lowborn body fall down before You. You can make possible this perfection of all my desires."
