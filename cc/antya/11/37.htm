Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.37

prabhu kahe, — "haridāsa, ye tumi māgibe

krishna kripāmaya tāhā avaśya karibe

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; haridāsa — My dear Haridāsa; ye — whatever; tumi — you; māgibe — request; krishna — Lord Krishna; kripā-maya — all-merciful; tāhā — that; avaśya — certainly; karibe — will execute.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "My dear Haridāsa, Krishna is so merciful that He must execute whatever you want.
