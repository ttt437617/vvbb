Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.84

svarūpa, jagadānanda, kāśīśvara, śańkara

cāri-jana pariveśana kare nirantara

SYNONYMS

svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; jagadānanda — Jagadānanda Pandita; kāśīśvara — Kāśīśvara; śańkara — Śańkara; cāri-jana — four men; pariveśana kare — administer; nirantara — continuously.

TRANSLATION

The four men — Svarūpa Dāmodara, Jagadānanda, Kāśīśvara and Śańkara — distributed the prasādam continuously.
