Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.28

adriśya, aspriśya more ańgīkāra kailā

raurava ha-ite kādi' more vaikunthe cadāilā

SYNONYMS

adriśya — unseeable; aspriśya — untouchable; more — me; ańgīkāra kailā — You have accepted; raurava ha-ite — from a hellish condition; kādi' — taking away; more — me; vaikunthe cadāilā — have raised to the Vaikuntha platform.

TRANSLATION

"I am unseeable and untouchable, but You have accepted me as Your servant. This means that You have delivered me from a hellish condition and raised me to the Vaikuntha platform.
