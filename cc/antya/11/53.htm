Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.53

haridāsa nijāgrete prabhure vasāilā

nija-netra — dui bhrińga — mukha-padme dilā

SYNONYMS

haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; nija-agrete — in front of himself; prabhure vasāilā — made the Lord sit down; nija-netra — his eyes; dui bhrińga — as if two bumblebees; mukha-padme — on the lotus face; dilā — he fixed.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura made Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sit down in front of him, and then he fixed his eyes, like two bumblebees, on the lotus face of the Lord.
