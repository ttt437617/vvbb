Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.56

'śrī-krishna-caitanya' śabda karite uccārana

nāmera sahita prāna kaila utkrāmana

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya — Śrī Krishna Caitanya; śabda — the sound vibration; karite uccārana — chanting; nāmera sahita — with the name; prāna — life; kaila utkrāmana — went away.

TRANSLATION

While chanting the holy name of Śrī Krishna Caitanya, he gave up his air of life and left his body.
