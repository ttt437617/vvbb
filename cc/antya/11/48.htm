Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.48

ańgane ārambhilā prabhu mahā-sańkīrtana

vakreśvara-pandita tāhāń karena nartana

SYNONYMS

ańgane — in the courtyard; ārambhilā — began; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mahā-sańkīrtana — great congregational chanting; vakreśvara-pandita — Vakreśvara Pandita; tāhāń — there; karena nartana — danced.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu immediately began great congregational chanting in the courtyard. Vakreśvara Pandita was the chief dancer.
