Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.9

jaya rūpa, sanātana, jīva, raghunātha

raghunātha, gopāla, — chaya mora nātha

SYNONYMS

jaya — all glories; rūpa — to Rūpa Gosvāmī; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; jīva — Jīva Gosvāmī; raghunātha — Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; raghunātha — Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī; gopāla — Gopāla Bhatta Gosvāmī; chaya — six; mora — my; nātha — lords.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Rūpa Gosvāmī, Sanātana Gosvāmī, Jīva Gosvāmī, Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī, Raghunātha Bhatta Gosvāmī and Gopāla Bhatta Gosvāmī, the six Gosvāmīs of Vrindāvana! They are all my masters.
