Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.63

āge mahāprabhu calena nritya karite karite

pāche nritya kare vakreśvara bhakta-gana-sāthe

SYNONYMS

āge — in front; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; calena — goes; nritya — dancing; karite karite — performing; pāche — behind; nritya kare — dances; vakreśvara — Vakreśvara; bhakta-gana-sāthe — with other devotees.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu danced in front of the procession, and Vakreśvara Pandita, along with the other devotees, chanted and danced behind Him.
