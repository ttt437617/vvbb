Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.74

'haridāsa-thākurera mahotsavera tare

prasāda māgiye bhikshā deha' ta' āmāre'

SYNONYMS

haridāsa-thākurera — of Haridāsa Thākura; mahotsavera tare — for holding a festival; prasāda māgiye — I am begging prasādam; bhikshā deha' — please give alms; ta' — certainly; āmāre — unto Me.

TRANSLATION

"I am begging prasādam for a festival honoring the passing away of Haridāsa Thākura," the Lord said. "Please give Me alms."
