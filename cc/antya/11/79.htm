Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.79

ei-mate nānā prasāda bojhā bāndhāñā

lañā āilā cāri janera mastake cadāñā

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; nānā — various; prasāda — prasādam; bojhā — load; bāndhāñā — packing; lañā āilā — brought; cāri janera — of the four persons; mastake — on the heads; cadāñā — mounting.

TRANSLATION

In this way varieties of prasādam were collected, then packed up in different loads and carried on the heads of the four servants.
