Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.97

haridāsa āchila prithivīra 'śiromani'

tāhā vinā ratna-śūnyā ha-ila medinī

SYNONYMS

haridāsa — Thākura Haridāsa; āchila — was; prithivīra — of this world; śiromani — the crown jewel; tāhā vinā — without him; ratna-śūnyā — without the valuable jewel; ha-ila — becomes; medinī — this world.

TRANSLATION

"Haridāsa Thākura was the crown jewel on the head of this world; without him, this world is now bereft of its valuable jewel."
