Antya-līlāChapter 11: The Passing of Haridāsa Thākura

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 11.50

rāmānanda, sārvabhauma, sabāra agrete

haridāsera guna prabhu lāgilā kahite

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; sārvabhauma — Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya; sabāra — of all; agrete — in front; haridāsera — of Haridāsa Thākura; guna — attributes; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; lāgilā kahite — began to describe.

TRANSLATION

In front of all the great devotees like Rāmānanda Rāya and Sārvabhauma Bhattācārya, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to describe the holy attributes of Haridāsa Thākura.
