Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.52

āmiha rāyera sthāne śuni krishna-kathā

śunite icchā haya yadi, punah yāha tathā

SYNONYMS

āmiha — I also; rāyera sthāne — from Rāmānanda Rāya; śuni — hear; krishna-kathā — talks of Krishna; śunite — to hear; icchā — desire; haya — there is; yadi — if; punah — again; yāha — go; tathā — there.

TRANSLATION

"I also hear topics about Krishna from Rāmānanda Rāya. If you want to hear such topics, go to him again.
