Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.83

āra eka 'svabhāva' gaurera śuna, bhakta-gana

aiśvarya-svabhāva gūdha kare prakatana

SYNONYMS

āra — another; eka — one; svabhāva — characteristic; gaurera — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śuna — hear; bhakta-gana — O devotees; aiśvarya-svabhāva — opulences and characteristics; gūdha — very deep; kare — does; prakatana — manifestation.

TRANSLATION

There is yet another characteristic of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. O devotees, listen carefully to how He manifests His opulence and characteristics, although they are exceptionally deep.
