Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.56

miśra kahe, — 'mahāprabhu pāthāilā more

tomāra sthāne krishna-kathā śunibāra tare'

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Pradyumna Miśra said; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; pāthāilā more — has sent me; tomāra sthāne — from you; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; śunibāra tare — to hear.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra answered, "Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu has sent me to hear topics about Lord Krishna from you."
