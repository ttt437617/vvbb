Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.159

ei ta' kahiluń pradyumna-miśra-vivarana

prabhura ājñāya kaila krishna-kathāra śravana

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have described; pradyumna-miśra-vivarana — the descriptive narration of Pradyumna Miśra; prabhura ājñāya — on the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaila — did; krishna-kathāra śravana — listening to discourses on topics concerning Krishna.

TRANSLATION

I have thus described the narration concerning Pradyumna Miśra and how, following the order of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he listened to discourses about Krishna spoken by Rāmānanda Rāya.
