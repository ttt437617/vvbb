Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.34

tabe miśra rāmānandera vrittānta kahilā

śuni' mahāprabhu tabe kahite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; rāmānandera — of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; vrittānta kahilā — described the activities; śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tabe — then; kahite lāgilā — began to speak.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra thereupon described the activities of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya. After hearing about these activities, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu began to speak.
