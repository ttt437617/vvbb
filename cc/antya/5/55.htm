Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.55

rāya-pāśa gela, rāya pranati karila

'ājñā kara, ye lāgi' āgamana haila'

SYNONYMS

rāya-pāśa — to Rāmānanda Rāya; gela — he went; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; pranati karila — offered his respects; ājñā kara — please order me; ye lāgi' — for what purpose; āgamana haila — have you come.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra went to Rāmānanda Rāya, who offered him respectful obeisances and said, "Please order me. For what purpose have you come?"
