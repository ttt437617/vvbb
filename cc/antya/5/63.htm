Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.63

tabe rāmānanda krame kahite lāgilā

krishna-kathā-rasāmrita-sindhu uthalilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; krame — gradually; kahite lāgilā — began to speak; krishna-kathā — of the topics of Krishna; rasāmrita-sindhu — the ocean of transcendental mellows; uthalilā — became agitated.

TRANSLATION

Thereupon Rāmānanda Rāya gradually began speaking on topics of Krishna. Thus the ocean of the transcendental mellows of those topics became agitated.
