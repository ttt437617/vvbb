Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.68

ghare giyā miśra kaila snāna, bhojana

sandhyā-kāle dekhite āila prabhura carana

SYNONYMS

ghare giyā — returning home; miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; kaila — performed; snāna — bathing; bhojana — eating; sandhyā-kāle — in the evening; dekhite — to see; āila — came; prabhura carana — the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

After returning home, Pradyumna Miśra bathed and ate his meal. In the evening he came to see the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
