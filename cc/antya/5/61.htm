Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.61

ānera ki kathā, tumi — prabhura upadeshtā!

āmi ta' bhikshuka vipra, tumi — mora poshtā

SYNONYMS

ānera ki kathā — what to speak of others; tumi — you; prabhura upadeshtā — an instructor of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āmi — I; ta' — certainly; bhikshuka — beggar; vipra — brāhmana; tumi — you; mora — my; poshtā — maintainer.

TRANSLATION

"You are an instructor even for Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, what to speak of others. I am but a beggar brāhmana, and you are my maintainer.
