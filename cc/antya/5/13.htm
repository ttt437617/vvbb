Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.13

"dui deva-kanyā haya parama-sundarī

nritya-gīte sunipunā, vayase kiśorī

SYNONYMS

dui — two; deva-kanyā — dancing girls; haya — are; parama-sundarī — very, very beautiful; nritya-gīte — in singing and dancing; su-nipunā — very expert; vayase — in age; kiśorī — very young.

TRANSLATION

"There are two dancing girls who are extremely beautiful. They are very youthful, and they are expert in dancing and singing.
