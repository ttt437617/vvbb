Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.155

krishne gāli dite kare nāma uccārana

sei nāma haya tāra 'muktira' kārana"

SYNONYMS

krishne — Lord Krishna; gāli dite — to blaspheme or chastise; kare nāma uccārana — chants the name of Krishna; sei nāma — that holy name; haya — becomes; tāra — his; muktira kārana — the cause of liberation.

TRANSLATION

"Sometimes it so happens that one who wants to chastise Krishna utters the holy name, and thus the holy name becomes the cause of his liberation."
