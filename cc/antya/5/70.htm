Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.70

miśra kahe, — "prabhu, more kritārtha karilā

krishna-kathāmritārnave more dubāilā

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Pradyumna Miśra said; prabhu — my dear Lord; more — me; kritārtha — satisfied; karilā — You have made; krishna-kathā — of talks about Krishna; amrita-arnave — in the ocean of the nectar; more — me; dubāilā — You have drowned.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra said, "My dear Lord, You have made me extremely obliged to You because You have drowned me in a nectarean ocean of talks about Krishna.
