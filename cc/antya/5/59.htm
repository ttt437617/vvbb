Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.59

eta kahi tāre lañā nibhrite vasilā

'ki kathā śunite cāha?' miśrere puchilā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi — saying this; tāre — him; lañā — taking; nibhrite vasilā — sat in a secluded place; ki kathā — what kind of topics; śunite cāha — do you want to hear; miśrere puchilā — he inquired from Pradyumna Miśra.

TRANSLATION

Saying this, Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya took Pradyumna Miśra to a secluded place and inquired from him, "What kind of krishna-kathā do you want to hear from me?"
