Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.108

rūpa yaiche dui nātaka kariyāche ārambhe

śunite ānanda bāde yāra mukha-bandhe"

SYNONYMS

rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; yaiche — as; dui — two; nātaka — dramas; kariyāche ārambhe — has compiled; śunite — to hear; ānanda bāde — transcendental happiness increases; yāra — of which; mukha-bandhe — even the introductory portion.

TRANSLATION

"The standard for writing dramas has been set by Rūpa Gosvāmī. If a devotee hears the introductory portions of his two dramas, they enhance his transcendental pleasure."
