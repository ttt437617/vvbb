Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.51

rāgānuga-mārge jāni rāyera bhajana

siddha-deha-tulya, tāte 'prākrita' nahe mana

SYNONYMS

rāgānuga-mārge — on the path of spontaneous love of Krishna; jāni — we can understand; rāyera bhajana — the devotional service of Rāmānanda Rāya; siddha-deha — spiritual body; tulya — equal to; tāte — therefore; prākrita — material; nahe — is not; mana — mind.

TRANSLATION

"Śrīla Rāmānanda Rāya is situated on the path of spontaneous love of Godhead. Therefore he is in his spiritual body, and his mind is not materially affected.
