Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.111

sabā lañā svarūpa gosāñi śunite vasilā

tabe sei kavi nāndī-śloka padilā

SYNONYMS

sabā lañā — in the company of other devotees; svarūpa gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; śunite vasilā — sat down to hear; tabe — thereafter; sei kavi — that poet; nāndī-śloka — the introductory verse; padilā — read.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī sat down with other devotees to hear the poetry, and then the poet began to read the introductory verse.
