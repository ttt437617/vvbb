Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.60

teńho kahe, — "ye kahilā vidyānagare

sei kathā krame tumi kahibā āmāre

SYNONYMS

teńho kahe — he replied; ye — what; kahilā — you spoke; vidyānagare — at Vidyānagara; sei kathā — those topics; krame — according to the order; tumi — you; kahibā — please speak; āmāre — to me.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra replied, "Kindly tell me about the same topics you spoke about at Vidyānagara.
