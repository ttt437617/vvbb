Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.15

tumi ihāń vasi' raha, kshaneke āsibena

tabe yei ājñā deha, sei karibena"

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; ihāń — here; vasi' — sitting; raha — just wait; kshaneke āsibena — he will come within a moment; tabe — then; yei — whatever; ājñā — order; deha — you give; sei — he; karibena — will do.

TRANSLATION

"Please sit here and wait for a few moments. As soon as he comes, he will execute whatever order you give him."
