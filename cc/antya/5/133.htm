Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.133

tabeta pānditya tomāra ha-ibe saphala

krishnera svarūpa-līlā varnibā nirmala

SYNONYMS

tabeta — then only; pānditya — learning; tomāra — your; ha-ibe — will become; sa-phala — successful; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; svarūpa-līlā — the transcendental pastimes; varnibā — you will describe; nirmala — without material contamination.

TRANSLATION

"Only if you follow the principles of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and His devotees will your learning be successful. Then you will be able to write about the transcendental pastimes of Krishna without material contamination.
