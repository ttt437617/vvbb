Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.57

śuni' rāmānanda rāya hailā premāveśe

kahite lāgilā kichu manera harishe

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; rāmānanda rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; hailā — became; prema-āveśe — absorbed in ecstatic love; kahite lāgilā — began to speak; kichu — something; manera harishe — in transcendental pleasure.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this, Rāmānanda Rāya became absorbed in ecstatic love and began to speak with great transcendental pleasure.
