Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.4

eka-dina pradyumna-miśra prabhura carane

dandavat kari' kichu kare nivedane

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; pradyumna-miśra — the devotee named Pradyumna Miśra; prabhura carane — at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dandavat kari' — offering his respects; kichu — something; kare nivedane — submits as a petition.

TRANSLATION

One day Pradyumna Miśra came to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, offering his respects and inquiring from Him with great submission.
