Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.31

miśra kahe, — "tomā dekhite haila āgamane

āpanā pavitra kailuń tomāra daraśane"

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Pradyumna Miśra replied; tomā — you; dekhite — to see; haila āgamane — I came; āpanā — myself; pavitra kailuń — I have purified; tomāra daraśane — by seeing you.

TRANSLATION

Pradyumna Miśra replied, "I came simply to see you. Now I have purified myself by seeing Your Honor."
