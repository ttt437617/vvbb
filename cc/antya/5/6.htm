Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.6

krishna-kathā śunibāre mora icchā haya

krishna-kathā kaha more hañā sadaya"

SYNONYMS

krishna-kathā — discussions on the subject of Lord Krishna; śunibāre — to hear; mora — my; icchā — desire; haya — is; krishna-kathā — talks about Lord Śrī Krishna; kaha — kindly speak; more — unto me; hañā — being; sa-daya — kind.

TRANSLATION

"I wish to hear topics concerning Lord Krishna constantly. Be merciful unto me and kindly tell me something about Krishna."
