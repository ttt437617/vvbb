Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.29

"bahu-kshana āilā, more keha nā kahila

tomāra carane mora aparādha ha-ila

SYNONYMS

bahu-kshana — long ago; āilā — you came; more — me; keha nā kahila — no one informed; tomāra carane — unto your lotus feet; mora — my; aparādha — offense; ha-ila — there was.

TRANSLATION

"Sir, you came here long ago, but no one informed me. Therefore I have certainly become an offender at your lotus feet.
