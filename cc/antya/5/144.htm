Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.144

yāhā haite anya purusha-sakala — 'adhama'

sei haya 'purushādhama' — sarasvatīra mana

SYNONYMS

yāhā haite — from whom; anya — other; purusha — persons; sakala — all; adhama — subordinate; sei — he; haya — is; purusha-adhama — the person under whom all others remain; sarasvatīra mana — the explanation of mother Sarasvatī.

TRANSLATION

"Mother Sarasvatī takes 'purushādhama' to mean 'purushottama,' 'He to whom all men are subordinate.'
