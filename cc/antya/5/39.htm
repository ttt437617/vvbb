Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.39

snānādi karāya, parāya vāsa-vibhūshana

guhya ańgera haya tāhā darśana-sparśana

SYNONYMS

snāna-ādi karāya — he performs their bathing and so on; parāya vāsa-vibhūshana — dresses and decorates the body with various types of ornaments; guhya ańgera — of the private parts of the body; haya — there is; tāhā — that; darśana-sparśana — seeing and touching.

TRANSLATION

"He personally bathes and dresses them and decorates them with ornaments. In this way, he naturally sees and touches the private parts of their bodies.
