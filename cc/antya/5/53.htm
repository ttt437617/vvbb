Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.53

mora nāma la-iha, — 'teho pāthāilā more

tomāra sthāne krishna-kathā śunibāra tare'

SYNONYMS

mora — My; nāma — name; la-iha — take; teho — He; pāthāilā — sent; more — me; tomāra sthāne — from you; krishna-kathā — topics of Krishna; śunibāra tare — to hear.

TRANSLATION

"You can mention My name before him, saying, 'He has sent me to hear about Lord Krishna from you.'
