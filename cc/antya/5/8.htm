Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.8

bhāgye tomāra krishna-kathā śunite haya mana

rāmānanda-pāśa yāi' karaha śravana

SYNONYMS

bhāgye — by fortune; tomāra — your; krishna-kathā — topics about Lord Krishna; śunite — to hear; haya mana — there is an inclination; rāmānanda-pāśa — to Rāmānanda Rāya; yāi' — going; karaha śravana — hear.

TRANSLATION

"It is your good fortune that you are inclined to hear topics regarding Krishna. The best course for you would be to go to Rāmānanda Rāya and hear these topics from him.
