Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.149

tāńhā-saha ātmatā eka-rūpa hañā

krishna eka-tattva-rūpa — dui rūpa hañā

SYNONYMS

tāńhā-saha — with Him; ātmatā — the quality of being the self; eka-rūpa hañā — being one form; krishna — Lord Krishna; eka-tattva-rūpa — one principle; dui — two; rūpa — forms; hañā — becoming.

TRANSLATION

"Thus Lord Jagannātha and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, although appearing as two, are one because They are both Krishna, who is one alone.
