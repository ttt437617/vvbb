Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.40

tabu nirvikāra rāya-rāmānandera mana

nānā-bhāvodgāra tāre karāya śikshana

SYNONYMS

tabu — still; nirvikāra — unchanged; rāya-rāmānandera mana — the mind of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya; nānā-bhāva-udgāra — all the symptoms and transformations of ecstasy; tāre — unto them; karāya śikshana — he teaches.

TRANSLATION

"Nevertheless, the mind of Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya never changes, although he teaches the girls how to physically express all the transformations of ecstasy.
