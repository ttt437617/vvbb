Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.134

ei śloka kariyācha pāñā santosha

tomāra hridayera arthe duńhāya lāge 'dosha'

SYNONYMS

ei śloka — this verse; kariyācha — you have composed; pāñā santosha — getting satisfaction; tomāra hridayera — of your heart; arthe — by the meaning; duńhāya — to both; lāge dosha — there is an offense.

TRANSLATION

"You have composed this introductory verse to your great satisfaction, but the meaning you have expressed is contaminated by offenses to both Lord Jagannātha and Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
