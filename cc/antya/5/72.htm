Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.72

āra eka kathā rāya kahilā āmāre

'krishna-kathā-vaktā kari' nā jāniha more

SYNONYMS

āra — another; eka — one; kathā — topic; rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kahilā āmāre — said to me; krishna-kathā-vaktā — the speaker on the talks of Krishna; kari' — as; nā jāniha more — do not consider me.

TRANSLATION

"There is one other thing Rāmānanda Rāya said to me: 'Do not consider me the speaker in these talks about Krishna.
