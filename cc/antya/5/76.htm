Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.76

hena 'rasa' pāna more karāilā tumi

janme janme tomāra pāya vikāilāńa āmi

SYNONYMS

hena rasa — such transcendental mellows; pāna — to drink; more — me; karāilā tumi — You have caused; janme janme — life after life; tomāra pāya — at Your feet; vikāilāńa āmi — I have become sold.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You have made me drink this transcendental nectar of krishna-kathā. Therefore I am sold to Your lotus feet, life after life."
