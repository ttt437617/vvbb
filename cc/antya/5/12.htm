Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.12

darśana nā pāñā miśra sevake puchila

rāyera vrittānta sevaka kahite lāgila

SYNONYMS

darśana — audience; nā — not; pāñā — getting; miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; sevake — unto the servant; puchila — inquired; rāyera — of Rāmānanda Rāya; vrittānta — activity; sevaka — the servant; kahite lāgila — began to describe.

TRANSLATION

Unable to see Rāmānanda Rāya immediately, Pradyumna Miśra inquired from the servant, who then described what Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya was doing.
