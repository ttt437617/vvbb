Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.86

haridāsa-dvārā nāma-māhātmya-prakāśa

sanātana-dvārā bhakti-siddhānta-vilāsa

SYNONYMS

haridāsa-dvārā — through Haridāsa Thākura; nāma-māhātmya — of the glories of chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; prakāśa — manifestation; sanātana-dvārā — through Sanātana Gosvāmī; bhakti-siddhānta-vilāsa — spreading the essence of devotional life.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu exhibited the glories of the holy name of the Lord through Haridāsa Thākura, who was born in a Muslim family. Similarly, He exhibited the essence of devotional service through Sanātana Gosvāmī, who had almost been converted into a Muslim.
