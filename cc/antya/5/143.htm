Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.143

jarāsandha kahe, — "krishna — purusha-adhama

tora sańge nā yujhimu, "yāhi bandhu-han"

SYNONYMS

jarāsandha kahe — Jarāsandha says; krishna — Krishna; purusha-adhama — the lowest of human beings; tora sańge — with You; nā yujhimu — I shall not fight; yāhi — because; bandhu-han — killer of Your own relatives.

TRANSLATION

"The demon Jarāsandha chastised Krishna, saying, 'You are the lowest of human beings. I shall not fight with You, for You killed Your own relatives.'

PURPORT

In this verse also, mother Sarasvatī offers prayers to Krishna. The word purusha-adhama refers to the Personality of Godhead, under whom all other persons remain, or, in other words, purusha-uttama, the best of all living beings. Similarly, the word bandhu-han means "the killer of māyā." In the conditioned state of life, one is closely related with māyā as a friend, but when one comes in contact with Krishna one is freed from that relationship.
