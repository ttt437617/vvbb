Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.25

tabe sei dui-jane prasāda khāoyāilā

nibhrite duńhāre nija-ghare pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; sei — to those; dui-jane — two girls; prasāda khāoyāilā — gave prasādam to eat; nibhrite — without being exposed; duńhāre — both of them; nija-ghare — their homes; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

Then Rāmānanda Rāya fed the two girls sumptuous prasādam and sent them to their homes unexposed.
