Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.9

krishna-kathāya ruci tomāra — bada bhāgyavān

yāra krishna-kathāya ruci, sei bhāgyavān

SYNONYMS

krishna-kathāya — in talking of Krishna; ruci — taste; tomāra — your; bada bhāgyavān — very fortunate; yāra — of whom; krishna-kathāya — in hearing about Krishna; ruci — taste; sei bhāgyavān — he is very fortunate.

TRANSLATION

"I see that you have acquired a taste for hearing talks regarding Krishna. Therefore you are extremely fortunate. Not only you but anyone who has awakened such a taste is considered most fortunate.
