Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.2

jaya jaya śacī-suta śrī-krishna-caitanya

jaya jaya kripā-maya nityānanda dhanya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; śacī-suta — to the son of mother Śacī; śrī-krishna-caitanya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; jaya jaya — all glories; kripā-maya — to the most merciful; nityānanda dhanya — the glorious Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the son of mother Śacī! All glories to Śrī Nityānanda Prabhu! Indeed, He is the most glorious and merciful.
