Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.106

krishna-līlā, gaura-līlā se kare varnana

gaura-pāda-padma yāńra haya prāna-dhana

SYNONYMS

krishna-līlā — the pastimes of Lord Krishna; gaura-līlā — the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; se — he; kare varnana — describes; gaura-pāda-padma — the lotus feet of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yāńra — whose; haya — is; prāna-dhana — the life and soul.

TRANSLATION

"One who has accepted the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu as his life and soul can describe the pastimes of Lord Krishna or the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
