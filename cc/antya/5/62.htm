Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.62

bhāla, manda — kichu āmi puchite nā jāni

'dīna' dekhi' kripā kari' kahibā āpani"

SYNONYMS

bhāla — good; manda — bad; kichu — something; āmi — I; puchite — to inquire; nā jāni — do not know; dīna — very poor in knowledge; dekhi' — seeing (me); kripā kari' — very mercifully; kahibā — please; āpani — by your own good will.

TRANSLATION

"I do not know how to inquire, for I do not know what is good and what is bad. Seeing me to be poor in knowledge, kindly speak whatever is good for me by your own good will."
