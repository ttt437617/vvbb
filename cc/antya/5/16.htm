Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.16

tabe pradyumna-miśra tāhāń rahila vasiyā

rāmānanda nibhrite sei dui-jana lañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — then; pradyumna-miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; tāhāń — there; rahila vasiyā — remained seated; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; nibhrite — in a solitary place; sei — those; dui-jana — two girls; lañā — taking.

TRANSLATION

While Pradyumna Miśra remained seated there, Rāmānanda Rāya took the two girls to a solitary place.
