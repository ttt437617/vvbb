Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.67

bahu-sammāna kari' miśre vidāya dilā

'kritārtha ha-ilāńa' bali' miśra nācite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

bahu-sammāna — much respectful behavior; kari' — doing; miśre — unto Pradyumna Miśra; vidāya dilā — bade farewell; kritārtha ha-ilāńa — I have become very satisfied; bali' — saying; miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; nācite lāgilā — began to dance.

TRANSLATION

Rāmānanda Rāya paid great respect to Pradyumna Miśra and bade him farewell. Pradyumna Miśra said, "I have become very satisfied." He then began to dance.
