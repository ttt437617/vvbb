Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.91

bańga-deśī eka vipra prabhura carite

nātaka kari' lañā āila prabhuke śunāite

SYNONYMS

bańga-deśī — from Bengal; eka vipra — one brāhmana; prabhura carite — about Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's characteristics; nātaka kari' — writing a drama; lañā — taking; āila — came; prabhuke śunāite — to induce Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to hear.

TRANSLATION

A brāhmana from Bengal wrote a drama about the characteristics of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and came with his manuscript to induce the Lord to hear it.
