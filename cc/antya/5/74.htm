Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.74

mora mukhe kahāya kathā, kare paracāra

prithivīte ke jānibe e-līlā tāńhāra?'

SYNONYMS

mora mukhe — in my mouth; kahāya — causes to speak; kathā — words; kare paracāra — preaches; prithivīte — in this world; ke jānibe — who will understand; e-līlā — this pastime; tāńhāra — His.

TRANSLATION

"'In this way the Lord speaks through my mouth to preach the cult of Krishna consciousness. Within the world, who will understand this pastime of the Lord's?'
