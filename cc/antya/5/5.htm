Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.5

"śuna, prabhu, muñi dīna grihastha adhama!

kona bhāgye pāñāchoń tomāra durlabha carana

SYNONYMS

śuna — please hear; prabhu — my Lord; muñi — I; dīna — very fallen; grihastha — householder; adhama — the lowest of men; kona bhāgye — by some good fortune; pāñāchoń — I have gotten; tomāra — Your; durlabha — rarely achieved; carana — lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord," he said, "kindly hear me. I am a cripple-minded householder, the most fallen of men, but somehow, by my good fortune, I have received the shelter of Your lotus feet, which are rarely to be seen.
