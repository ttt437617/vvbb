Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.58

"prabhura ājñāya krishna-kathā śunite āilā ethā

ihā va-i mahā-bhāgya āmi pāba kothā?"

SYNONYMS

prabhura ājñāya — under the instruction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; śunite — to hear; āilā ethā — you have come here; ihā va-i — without this; mahā-bhāgya — great fortune; āmi — I; pāba — will get; kothā — where.

TRANSLATION

"Following the instruction of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, you have come to hear about Krishna. This is my great fortune. How else would I get such an opportunity?"
