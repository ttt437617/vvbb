Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.148

jagannātha hana krishnera 'ātma-svarūpa'

kintu ihāń dāru-brahma — sthāvara-svarūpa

SYNONYMS

jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; hana — is; krishnera ātma-svarūpa — identical with Krishna; kintu — but; ihāń — here, at Jagannātha Purī; dāru-brahma — the Absolute appearing as wood; sthāvara-svarūpa — nonmoving identity.

TRANSLATION

"There is no difference between Lord Jagannātha and Krishna, but here Lord Jagannātha is fixed as the Absolute Person appearing in wood. Therefore He does not move.
