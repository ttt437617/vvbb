Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.75

ye-saba śuniluń, krishna-rasera sāgara

brahmādi-devera e saba nā haya gocara

SYNONYMS

ye-saba — all that; śuniluń — I heard; krishna-rasera — of the nectar of Lord Krishna; sāgara — the ocean; brahmā-ādi-devera — of the demigods, beginning with Lord Brahmā; e saba — all this; nā haya gocara — is not possible to be understood.

TRANSLATION

"What I have heard from Rāmānanda Rāya is like a nectarean ocean of discourses about Krishna. Even the demigods, beginning with Lord Brahmā, cannot understand all these topics.
