Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.162

śrī-krishna-caitanya-līlā — amritera sāra

eka-līlā-pravāhe vahe śata-śata dhāra

SYNONYMS

śrī-krishna-caitanya-līlā — the pastimes of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; amritera sāra — the essence of nectar; eka-līlā — of one pastime; pravāhe — by the stream; vahe — flow; śata-śata dhāra — hundreds and hundreds of branches.

TRANSLATION

The pastimes of Lord Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu are the essence of nectar. From the stream of one of His pastimes flow hundreds and thousands of branches.
