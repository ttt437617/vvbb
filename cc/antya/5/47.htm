Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.47

ujjvala madhura prema-bhakti sei pāya

ānande krishna-mādhurye vihare sadāya

SYNONYMS

ujjvala — illuminated; madhura — sweet; prema-bhakti — ecstatic love of Krishna; sei — he; pāya — gets; ānande — in transcendental bliss; krishna-mādhurye — the sweetness of Krishna's pastimes; vihare — enjoys; sadāya — always.

TRANSLATION

"Tasting transcendental, effulgent, sweetly ecstatic love of Krishna, such a person can enjoy life twenty-four hours a day in the transcendental bliss of the sweetness of Krishna's pastimes.
