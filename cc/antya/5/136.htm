Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.136

yaiche indra, daityādi kare krishnera bhartsana

sei-śabde sarasvatī karena stavana

SYNONYMS

yaiche — just like; indra — Lord Indra, the King of heaven; daitya — demons; ādi — and others; kare — do; krishnera bhartsana — chastisement to Krishna; sei-śabde — by those words; sarasvatī — the goddess of learning; karena stavana — offers prayers.

TRANSLATION

"Sometimes demons, and even Lord Indra, the King of heaven, chastised Krishna, but mother Sarasvatī, taking advantage of their words, offered prayers to the Lord.
