Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.33

āra dina miśra āila prabhu-vidyamāne

prabhu kahe, — 'krishna-kathā śunilā rāya-sthāne'?

SYNONYMS

āra dina — the next day; miśra — Pradyumna Miśra; āila — came; prabhu-vidyamāne — in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired; krishna-kathā — talks about Krishna; śunilā — have you heard; rāya-sthāne — from Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya.

TRANSLATION

The next day, when Pradyumna Miśra arrived in the presence of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the Lord inquired, "Have you heard talks about Krishna from Śrī Rāmānanda Rāya?"
