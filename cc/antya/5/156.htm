Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.156

tabe sei kavi sabāra carane padiyā

sabāra śarana laila dante trina lañā

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereupon; sei — that; kavi — poet; sabāra — of all; carane — at the feet; padiyā — falling down; sabāra — of all the devotees; śarana laila — took shelter; dante — in the mouth; trina lañā — taking a straw.

TRANSLATION

Upon hearing this proper explanation by Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, the Bengali poet fell down at the feet of all the devotees and took shelter of them with a straw in his mouth.
