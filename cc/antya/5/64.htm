Antya-līlāChapter 5: How Pradyumna Miśra Received Instructions from Rāmānanda Rāya

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 5.64

āpane praśna kari' pāche karena siddhānta

tritīya prahara haila, nahe kathā-anta

SYNONYMS

āpane — personally; praśna kari' — asking the question; pāche — after that; karena siddhānta — gives the conclusion; tritīya prahara haila — it became afternoon; nahe kathā-anta — there was no end to such topics.

TRANSLATION

He began personally posing questions and then answering them with conclusive statements. When afternoon came, the topics still did not end.
