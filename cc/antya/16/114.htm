Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.114

aneka 'sukrite' ihā hañāche samprāpti

sabe ei āsvāda kara kari' mahā-bhakti"

SYNONYMS

aneka — many; sukrite — by pious activities; ihā — this; hañāche samprāpti — has become available; sabe — all of you; ei — this prasādam; āsvāda kara — taste; kari' mahā-bhakti — with great devotion.

TRANSLATION

"This prasādam has been made available only as a result of many pious activities. Now taste it with great faith and devotion."
