Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.102

madhyāhna kariyā kailā bhikshā nirvāhana

krishnādharāmrita sadā antare smarana

SYNONYMS

madhyāhna kariyā — after finishing His noon duties; kailā bhikshā nirvāhana — completed His lunch; krishna-adhara-amrita — the nectar from the lips of Krishna; sadā — always; antare — within Himself; smarana — remembering.

TRANSLATION

After finishing His noon duties, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu ate His lunch, but He constantly remembered the remnants of Krishna's food.
