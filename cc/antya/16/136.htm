Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.136

yogya hañā keha karite nā pāya pāna

tathāpi se nirlajja, vrithā dhare prāna

SYNONYMS

yogya — competent; hañā — being; keha — anyone; karite — to do; nā pāya — does not get; pāna — drinking; tathāpi — still; se — that person; nirlajja — shameless; vrithā — uselessly; dhare prāna — continues life.

TRANSLATION

"When a person competent to drink that nectar does not do so, that shameless person continues his life uselessly.
