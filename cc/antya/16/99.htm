Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.99

sāmānya bhāgya haite tāra prāpti nāhi haya

krishnera yāńte pūrna-kripā, sei tāhā pāya

SYNONYMS

sāmānya — ordinary; bhāgya — fortune; haite — from; tāra — of that; prāpti — attainment; nāhi — not; haya — there is; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; yāńte — unto whom; pūrna-kripā — full mercy; sei — he; tāhā — that; pāya — can get.

TRANSLATION

"One who is only ordinarily fortunate cannot obtain such mercy. Only persons who have the full mercy of Krishna can receive such remnants.
