Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.77

bhakta-gana prabhu-sańge rahe cāri-māse

prabhu ājñā dilā sabe gelā gauda-deśe

SYNONYMS

bhakta-gana — all the devotees; prabhu-sańge — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahe — remained; cāri-māse — for four months; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā dilā — gave the order; sabe — unto everyone; gelā — returned; gauda-deśe — to Bengal.

TRANSLATION

All the devotees remained with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continuously for four months. Then the Lord ordered them back to Bengal, and therefore they returned.
