Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.120

eta kahi' gaura-prabhu bhāvāvishta hañā

dui ślokera artha kare pralāpa kariyā

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; gaura-prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhāva-āvishta — overwhelmed by ecstatic loving emotions; hañā — becoming; dui ślokera — of the two verses; artha — meaning; kare — makes; pralāpa kariyā — talking like a madman.

TRANSLATION

After saying this, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was overwhelmed by ecstatic loving emotions. Talking like a madman, He began to explain the meaning of the two verses.
