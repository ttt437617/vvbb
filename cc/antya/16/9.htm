Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.9

gauda-deśe haya yata vaishnavera gana

sabāra ucchishta teńho karila bhojana

SYNONYMS

gauda-deśe — in Bengal; haya — are; yata — as many; vaishnavera gana — Vaishnavas; sabāra — of all; ucchishta — remnants of food; teńho — he; karila bhojana — ate.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa ate the remnants of food of as many Vaishnavas as there were in Bengal.
