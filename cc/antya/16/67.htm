Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.67

'krishna kaha' bali' prabhu balena bāra bāra

tabu krishna-nāma bālaka nā kare uccāra

SYNONYMS

krishna kaha — say Krishna; bali' — saying; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; balena — said; bāra bāra — again and again; tabu — still; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; bālaka — the boy; nā kare uccāra — did not utter.

TRANSLATION

Again and again Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu asked the boy to chant the name of Krishna, but the boy would not utter the holy name.
