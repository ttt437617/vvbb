Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.33

jhadu-thākura ghara yāi' dekhi' āmra-phala

mānasei krishna-candre arpilā sakala

SYNONYMS

jhadu-thākura — Jhadu Thākura; ghara yāi' — returning home; dekhi' āmra-phala — seeing the mangoes; mānasei — within his mind; krishna-candre — unto Krishna; arpilā — offered; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

Upon returning home, Jhadu Thākura saw the mangoes Kālidāsa had presented. Within his mind he offered them to Krishna-candra.
