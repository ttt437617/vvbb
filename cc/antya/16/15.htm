Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.15

āmra bheta diyā tāńra carana vandilā

tāńra patnīre tabe namaskāra kailā

SYNONYMS

āmra — mangoes; bheta — gift; diyā — presenting; tāńra — his; carana — feet; vandilā — offered respects to; tāńra patnīre — to his wife; tabe — thereafter; namaskāra kailā — offered respectful obeisances.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa presented the mangoes to Jhadu Thākura and offered him respectful obeisances. Then he also offered respectful obeisances to the Thākura's wife.
