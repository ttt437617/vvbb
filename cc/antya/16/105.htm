Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.105

prabhura ińgite govinda prasāda ānilā

purī-bhāratīre prabhu kichu pāthāilā

SYNONYMS

prabhura ińgite — by the indication of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; govinda — Govinda; prasāda ānilā — brought the remnants of the food of Lord Jagannātha; purī — to Paramānanda Purī; bhāratīre — to Brahmānanda Bhāratī; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kichu — some; pāthāilā — sent.

TRANSLATION

Following the indications of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, Govinda brought the prasādam of Lord Jagannātha. The Lord sent some to Paramānanda Purī and Brahmānanda Bhāratī.
