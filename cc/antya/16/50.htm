Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.50

bāiśa 'pāhāca'-pāche upara dakshina-dike

eka nrisimha-mūrti āchena uthite vāma-bhāge

SYNONYMS

bāiśa pāhāca — of the twenty-two steps; pāche — toward the back; upara — above; dakshina-dike — on the southern side; eka — one; nrisimha-mūrti — Deity of Lord Nrisimha; āchena — there is; uthite — while stepping upward; vāma-bhāge — on the left side.

TRANSLATION

On the southern side, behind and above the twenty-two steps, is a Deity of Lord Nrisimhadeva. It is on the left as one goes up the steps toward the temple.
