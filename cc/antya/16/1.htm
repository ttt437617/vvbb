Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.1

vande śrī-krishna-caitanyam

krishna-bhāvāmritam hi yah

āsvādyāsvādayan bhaktān

prema-dīkshām aśikshayat

SYNONYMS

vande — I offer my respectful obeisances; śrī-krishna-caitanyam — unto Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; krishna-bhāva-amritam — the nectar of ecstatic love of Krishna; hi — certainly; yah — He who; āsvādya — tasting; āsvādayan — causing to taste; bhaktān — the devotees; prema — in love of Krishna; dīkshām — initiation; aśikshayat — instructed.

TRANSLATION

Let me offer my respectful obeisances unto Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, who personally tasted the nectar of ecstatic love for Krishna and then instructed His devotees how to taste it. Thus He enlightened them about ecstatic love of Krishna to initiate them into transcendental knowledge.
