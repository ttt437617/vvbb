Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.38

ei-mata yata vaishnava vaise gauda-deśe

kālidāsa aiche sabāra nilā avaśeshe

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; yata — as many as; vaishnava — Vaishnavas; vaise — reside; gauda-deśe — in Bengal; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; aiche — in that way; sabāra — of all of them; nilā — took; avaśeshe — the remnants.

TRANSLATION

In this way Kālidāsa ate the remnants of food left by all the Vaishnavas residing in Bengal.
