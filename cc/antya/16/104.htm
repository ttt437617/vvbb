Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.104

sandhyā-kritya kari' punah nija-gana-sańge

nibhrite vasilā nānā-krishna-kathā-rańge

SYNONYMS

sandhyā-kritya — the evening duties; kari' — after performing; punah — again; nija-gana-sańge — along with His personal associates; nibhrite — in a solitary place; vasilā — sat down; nānā — various; krishna-kathā — of topics of Krishna; rańge — in the jubilation.

TRANSLATION

After finishing His evening duties, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu sat down with His personal associates in a secluded place and discussed the pastimes of Krishna in great jubilation.
