Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.61

ei tina-sevā haite krishna-premā haya

punah punah sarva-śāstre phukāriyā kaya

SYNONYMS

ei tina-sevā — rendering service to these three; haite — from; krishna-prema — ecstatic love for Krishna; haya — there is; punah punah — again and again; sarva-śāstre — all the revealed scriptures; phu-kāriyā kaya — declare loudly.

TRANSLATION

By rendering service to these three, one attains the supreme goal of ecstatic love for Krishna. In all the revealed scriptures this is loudly declared again and again.
