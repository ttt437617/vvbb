Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.134

kahite kahite prabhura mana phiri' gela

krodha-amśa śānta haila, utkanthā bādila

SYNONYMS

kahite kahite — talking and talking; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mana — mind; phiri' gela — became changed; krodha-amśa — the part of anger; śānta haila — became pacified; utkanthā — agitation of the mind; bādila — increased.

TRANSLATION

While Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was talking like this, His mind changed. His anger subsided, but His mental agitation increased.
