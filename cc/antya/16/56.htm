Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.56

mahāprabhura ińgita govinda saba jāne

kālidāsere dila prabhura śesha-pātra-dāne

SYNONYMS

mahāprabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ińgita — indications; govinda — His personal servant; saba — all; jāne — knows; kālidāsere — unto Kālidāsa; dila — delivered; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; śesha-pātra — the remnants of food; dāne — presentation.

TRANSLATION

Govinda understood all the indications of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Therefore he immediately delivered the remnants of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's food to Kālidāsa.
