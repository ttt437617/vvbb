Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.17

ishtagoshthī kata-kshana kari' tāńra sane

jhadu-thākura kahe tāńre madhura vacane

SYNONYMS

ishta-goshthī — discussion; kata-kshana — for some time; kari' — performing; tāńra sane — with him; jhadu-thākura — Jhadu Thākura; kahe — says; tāńre — unto him (Kālidāsa); madhura vacane — in sweet words.

TRANSLATION

After a discussion with Kālidāsa that lasted for some time, Jhadu Thākura spoke the following sweet words.
