Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.97

prabhu kahe, — "ei ye dilā krishnādharāmrita

brahmādi-durlabha ei nindaye 'amrita'

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; ei — this; ye — which; dilā — you have given; krishna — of Lord Krishna; adhara-amrita — nectar from the lips; brahmā-ādi — by the demigods, headed by Lord Brahmā; durlabha — difficult to obtain; ei — this; nindaye — defeats; amrita — nectar.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied, "These are remnants of food that Krishna has eaten and thus turned to nectar with His lips. It surpasses heavenly nectar, and even such demigods as Lord Brahmā find it difficult to obtain.
