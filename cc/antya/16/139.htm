Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.139

'kaha rāma-rāya, kichu śunite haya mana'

bhāva jāni' pade rāya gopīra vacana

SYNONYMS

kaha — speak; rāma-rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya; kichu — something; śunite — to hear; haya mana — I wish; bhāva — the situation; jāni' — understanding; pade rāya — Rāmānanda Rāya recites; gopīra vacana — the words of the gopīs.

TRANSLATION

Again Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said to Rāmānanda Rāya, "Please say something. I want to hear." Understanding the situation, Rāmānanda Rāya recited the following words of the gopīs.
