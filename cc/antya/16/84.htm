Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.84

seha bale, — 'ei dekha śrī-purushottama

netra bhariyā tumi karaha daraśana'

SYNONYMS

seha bale — he also said; ei — this; dekha — just see; śrī-purusha-uttama — Lord Krishna, the best of all Personalities of Godhead; netra bhariyā — to the full satisfaction of Your eyes; tumi — You; karaha daraśana — see.

TRANSLATION

"Just see!" the doorkeeper said. "Here is the best of the Personalities of Godhead. From here You may see the Lord to the full satisfaction of Your eyes."
