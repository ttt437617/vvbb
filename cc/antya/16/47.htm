Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.47

"atahpara āra nā kariha punar-bāra

etāvatā vāñchā-pūrana kariluń tomāra"

SYNONYMS

atahpara — hereafter; āra — any more; nā kariha — do not do; punah-bāra — again; etāvatā — so far; vāñchā-pūrana — fulfilling the desire; kariluń — I have done; tomāra — of you.

TRANSLATION

"Do not act in this way any more. I have fulfilled your desire as far as possible."
