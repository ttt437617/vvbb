Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.98

krishnera ye bhukta-śesha, tāra 'phelā'-nāma

tāra eka 'lava' ye pāya, sei bhāgyavān

SYNONYMS

krishnera — of Lord Krishna; ye — whatever; bhukta-śesha — remnants of food; tāra — of that; phelā-nāma — the name is phelā; tāra — of that; eka — one; lava — fragment; ye — one who; pāya — gets; sei — he; bhāgyavān — fortunate.

TRANSLATION

"Remnants left by Krishna are called phelā. Anyone who obtains even a small portion must be considered very fortunate.
