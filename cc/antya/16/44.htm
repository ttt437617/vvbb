Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.44

prāni-mātra la-ite nā pāya sei jala

antarańga bhakta laya kari' kona chala

SYNONYMS

prāni-mātra — all living beings; la-ite — to take; nā pāya — do not get; sei jala — that water; antarańga — very intimate; bhakta — devotees; laya — take; kari' — doing; kona chala — some trick.

TRANSLATION

Because of the Lord's strict order, no living being could take the water. Some of His intimate devotees, however, would take it by some trick.
