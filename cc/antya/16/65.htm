Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.65

se vatsara śivānanda patnī lañā āilā

'purī-dāsa'-chota-putre sańgete ānilā

SYNONYMS

se vatsara — that year; śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; patnī — the wife; lañā — bringing; āilā — came; purī-dāsa — Purī dāsa; chota-putre — the youngest son; sańgete ānilā — he brought with him.

TRANSLATION

That year, Śivānanda Sena brought with him his wife and youngest son, Purī dāsa.
