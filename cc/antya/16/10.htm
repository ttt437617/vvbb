Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.10

brāhmana-vaishnava yata — chota, bada haya

uttama-vastu bheta lañā tāńra thāñi yāya

SYNONYMS

brāhmana-vaishnava — Vaishnavas coming from brāhmana families; yata — all; chota — neophyte; bada — highly advanced; haya — are; uttama-vastu — first-class eatables; bheta lañā — taking as gifts; tāńra thāñi — to them; yāya — goes.

TRANSLATION

He would go to all the Vaishnavas born in brāhmana families, be they neophyte or advanced devotees, and present them with gifts of first-class eatables.
