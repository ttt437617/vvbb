Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.6

mahā-bhāgavata teńho sarala udāra

krishna-nāma-'sańkete' cālāya vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

mahā-bhāgavata — a highly advanced devotee; teńho — he; sarala udāra — very simple and liberal; krishna-nāma-sańkete — with chanting of the holy name of Krishna; cālāya — performs; vyavahāra — ordinary dealings.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa was a very advanced devotee, yet he was simple and liberal. He would chant the holy name of Krishna while performing all his ordinary dealings.
