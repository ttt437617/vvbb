Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.68

śivānanda bālakere bahu yatna karilā

tabu sei bālaka krishna-nāma nā kahilā

SYNONYMS

śivānanda — Śivānanda Sena; bālakere — unto the boy; bahu — much; yatna — endeavor; karilā — did; tabu — still; sei bālaka — that boy; krishna-nāma — the name of Krishna; nā kahilā — did not utter.

TRANSLATION

Although Śivānanda Sena tried with much endeavor to get his boy to speak Krishna's holy name, the boy would not utter it.
