Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.8

raghunātha-dāsera teńho haya jñāti-khudā

vaishnavera ucchishta khāite teńho haila budā

SYNONYMS

raghunātha-dāsera — of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī; teńho — he (Kālidāsa); haya — is; jñāti — relative; khudā — uncle; vaishnavera — of the Vaishnavas; ucchishta — remnants of food; khāite — eating; teńho — he; haila — became; budā — aged.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa was an uncle of Raghunātha dāsa Gosvāmī. Throughout his entire life, even in his old age, he tried to eat the remnants of food left by Vaishnavas.
