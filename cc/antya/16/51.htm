Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.51

prati-dina tāńre prabhu karena namaskāra

namaskari' ei śloka pade bāra-bāra

SYNONYMS

prati-dina — every day; tāńre — unto the Deity of Lord Nrisimhadeva; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; karena — does; namaskāra — obeisances; namaskari' — offering obeisances; ei śloka — these verses; pade — recites; bāra-bāra — again and again.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, His left side toward the Deity, offered obeisances to Lord Nrisimha as He proceeded toward the temple. He recited the following verses again and again while offering obeisances.
