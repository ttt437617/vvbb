Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.138

tāte jāni, — kona tapasyāra āche bala

ayogyere deoyāya krishnādharāmrita-phala

SYNONYMS

tāte — therefore; jāni — I can understand; kona — some; tapasyāra — of austerity; āche — there is; bala — strength; ayogyere — unto the unfit; deoyāya — delivers; krishna-adhara-amrita — the nectar of Krishna's lips; phala — the result.

TRANSLATION

"It is therefore to be understood that such an unfit person must have obtained the nectar of Krishna's lips on the strength of some austerity."
