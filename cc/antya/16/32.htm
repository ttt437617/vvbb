Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.32

sei dhūli lañā kālidāsa sarvāńge lepilā

tāńra nikata eka-sthāne lukāñā rahilā

SYNONYMS

sei dhūli — that dust; lañā — taking; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; sarva-ańge — all over his body; lepilā — smeared; tāńra nikata — near his place; eka-sthāne — in one place; lukāñā rahilā — remained hidden.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa smeared the dust from those footprints all over his body. Then he hid in a place near Jhadu Thākura's home.
