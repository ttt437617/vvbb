Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.46

eka añjali, dui añjali, tina añjali pilā

tabe mahāprabhu tāńre nishedha karilā

SYNONYMS

eka añjali — one palmful; dui añjali — two palmfuls; tina añjali — three palmfuls; pilā — he drank; tabe — at that time; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; nishedha karilā — forbade.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa drank one palmful and then a second and a third. Then Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu forbade him to drink more.
