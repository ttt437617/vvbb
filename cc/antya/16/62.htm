Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.62

tāte bāra bāra kahi, — śuna bhakta-gana

viśvāsa kariyā kara e-tina sevana

SYNONYMS

tāte — therefore; bāra bāra — again and again; kahi — I say; śuna — hear; bhakta-gana — devotees; viśvāsa kariyā — keeping faith; kara — do; e-tina sevana — rendering service to these three.

TRANSLATION

Therefore, my dear devotees, please hear from me, for I insist again and again: please keep faith in these three and render service to them without hesitation.
