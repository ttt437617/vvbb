Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.40

prati-dina prabhu yadi yā'na daraśane

jala-karańga lañā govinda yāya prabhu-sane

SYNONYMS

prati-dina — every day; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yadi — when; yā'na — goes; daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; jala-karańga — a waterpot; lañā — taking; govinda — the personal servant of the Lord (Govinda); yāya — goes; prabhu-sane — with Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would regularly visit the temple of Jagannātha every day, and at that time Govinda, His personal servant, used to carry His waterpot and go with Him.
