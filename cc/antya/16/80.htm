Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.80

eka-dina prabhu gelā jagannātha-daraśane

simha-dvāre dala-i āsi' karila vandane

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gelā — went; jagannātha-daraśane — to see Lord Jagannātha; simha-dvāre — at the gate known as Simha-dvāra; dala-i — the gatekeeper; āsi' — coming; karila vandane — offered respectful obeisances.

TRANSLATION

One day, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu went to visit the temple of Lord Jagannātha, the gatekeeper at the Simha-dvāra approached Him and offered respectful obeisances.
