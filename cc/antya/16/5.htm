Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.5

tāń-sabāra sańge āila kālidāsa nāma

krishna-nāma vinā teńho nāhi kahe āna

SYNONYMS

tāń-sabāra sańge — with all of them; āila — came; kāli-dāsa nāma — a man named Kālidāsa; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; vinā — besides; teńho — he; nāhi — does not; kahe — say; āna — anything else.

TRANSLATION

Along with the devotees from Bengal came a gentleman named Kālidāsa. He never uttered anything but the holy name of Krishna.
