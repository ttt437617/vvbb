Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.20

kālidāsa kahe, — "thākura, kripā kara more

tomāra darśane āinu mui patita pāmare

SYNONYMS

kālidāsa kahe — Kālidāsa replied; thākura — my dear saintly person; kripā kara — bestow your mercy; more — upon me; tomāra darśane — to see you; āinu — have come; mui — I; patita pāmare — very fallen and sinful.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa replied, "My dear sir, please bestow your mercy upon me. I have come to see you, although I am very fallen and sinful.
