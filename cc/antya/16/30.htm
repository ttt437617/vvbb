Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.30

tāre namaskari' kālidāsa vidāya māgilā

jhadu-thākura tabe tāńra anuvraji' āilā

SYNONYMS

tāre — unto him (Jhadu Thākura); namaskari' — offering obeisances; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; vidāya māgilā — asked permission to go; jhadu-thākura — Jhadu Thākura; tabe — at that time; tāńra — him; anuvraji' — following; āilā — went.

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa again offered his obeisances to Jhadu Thākura and asked his permission to go. The saint Jhadu Thākura followed him as he left.
