Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.85

garudera pāche rahi' karena daraśana

dekhena, — jagannātha haya muralī-vadana

SYNONYMS

garudera pāche — behind the Garuda column; rahi' — staying; karena daraśana — He was seeing; dekhena — He saw; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; haya — was; muralī-vadana — Lord Krishna with His flute to the mouth.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu stayed behind the huge column called the Garuda-stambha and looked upon Lord Jagannātha, but as He looked He saw that Lord Jagannātha had become Lord Krishna, with His flute to His mouth.
