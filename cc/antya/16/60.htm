Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.60

bhakta-pada-dhūli āra bhakta-pada-jala

bhakta-bhukta-avaśesha, — tina mahā-bala

SYNONYMS

bhakta-pada-dhūli — the dust of the lotus feet of a devotee; āra — and; bhakta-pada-jala — the water that washed the feet of a devotee; bhakta-bhukta-avaśesha — and the remnants of food eaten by a devotee; tina — three; mahā-bala — very powerful.

TRANSLATION

The dust of the feet of a devotee, the water that has washed the feet of a devotee, and the remnants of food left by a devotee are three very powerful substances.
