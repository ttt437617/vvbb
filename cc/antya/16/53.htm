Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.53

ito nrisimhah parato nrisimho

yato yato yāmi tato nrisimhah

bahir nrisimho hridaye nrisimho

nrisimham ādim śaranam prapadye

SYNONYMS

itah — here; nrisimhah — Lord Nrisimha; paratah — on the opposite side; nrisimhah — Lord Nrisimha; yatah yatah — wherever; yāmi — I go; tatah — there; nrisimhah — Lord Nrisimha; bahih — outside; nrisimhah — Lord Nrisimha; hridaye — in my heart; nrisimhah — Lord Nrisimha; nrisimham — Lord Nrisimha; ādim — the original Supreme Personality; śaranam prapadye — I take shelter of.

TRANSLATION

"'Lord Nrisimhadeva is here, and He is also there on the opposite side. Wherever I go, there I see Lord Nrisimhadeva. He is outside and within my heart. Therefore I take shelter of Lord Nrisimhadeva, the original Supreme Personality of Godhead.'"
