Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.45

eka-dina prabhu tāńhā pāda prakshālite

kālidāsa āsi' tāhāń pātilena hāte

SYNONYMS

eka-dina — one day; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńhā — there; pāda prakshālite — washing His feet; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; āsi' — coming; tāhāń — there; pātilena — spread; hāte — his palm.

TRANSLATION

One day as Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu was washing His feet in that place, Kālidāsa came and extended his palm to take the water.
