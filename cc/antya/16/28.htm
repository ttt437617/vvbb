Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.28

śuni' thākura kahe, — "śāstra ei satya kaya

sei śreshtha, aiche yāńte krishna-bhakti haya

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; thākura kahe — Jhadu Thākura said; śāstra — revealed scripture; ei — this; satya — truth; kaya — says; sei — he; śreshtha — best; aiche — in such a way; yāńte — in whom; krishna-bhakti — devotion to Krishna; haya — there is.

TRANSLATION

Hearing these quotations from the revealed scripture Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam, Jhadu Thākura replied, "Yes, this is true, for it is the version of śāstra. It is true, however, for one who is genuinely advanced in devotion to Krishna.
