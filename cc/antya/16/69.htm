Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.69

prabhu kahe, — "āmi nāma jagate laoyāiluń

sthāvare paryanta krishna-nāma kahāiluń

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; āmi — I; nāma — the holy name; jagate — throughout the whole world; laoyāiluń — induced to take; sthāvare — the unmovable; paryanta — up to; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; kahāiluń — I induced to chant.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "I have induced the whole world to take to the holy name of Krishna. I have induced even the trees and immovable plants to chant the holy name.
