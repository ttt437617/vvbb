Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.135

parama durlabha ei krishnādharāmrita

tāhā yei pāya, tāra saphala jīvita

SYNONYMS

parama — supremely; durlabha — difficult to obtain; ei — this; krishna — of Krishna; adhara-amrita — the nectar from the lips; tāhā — that; yei — one who; pāya — gets; tāra — his; sa-phala — successful; jīvita — life.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu continued, "This nectar from Krishna's lips is supremely difficult to obtain, but if one gets some, his life becomes successful.
