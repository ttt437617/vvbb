Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.78

tāń-sabāra sańge prabhura chila bāhya-jñāna

tāńrā gele punah hailā unmāda pradhāna

SYNONYMS

tāń-sabāra — all of them; sańge — with; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; chila — there was; bāhya-jñāna — external consciousness; tāńrā gele — when they departed; punah — again; hailā — there was; unmāda — madness; pradhāna — the chief business.

TRANSLATION

As long as the devotees were in Nīlācala, Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu maintained His external consciousness, but after their departure His chief engagement was again the madness of ecstatic love for Krishna.
