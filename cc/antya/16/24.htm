Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.24

tabe kālidāsa śloka padi' śunāila

śuni' jhadu-thākurera bada sukha ha-ila

SYNONYMS

tabe — thereafter; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa; śloka — verses; padi' — reciting; śunāila — caused to hear; śuni' — hearing; jhadu-thākurera — of Jhadu Thākura; bada — very great; sukha — happiness; ha-ila — there was,

TRANSLATION

Kālidāsa then recited some verses, which Jhadu Thākura was very happy to hear.
