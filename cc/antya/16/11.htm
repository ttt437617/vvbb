Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.11

tāńra thāñi śesha-pātra layena māgiyā

kāhāń nā pāya, tabe rahe lukāñā

SYNONYMS

tāńra thāñi — from them; śesha-pātra — plates of remnants; layena — takes; māgiyā — begging; kāhāń — where; nā pāya — does not get; tabe — then; rahe — remains; lukāñā — hiding.

TRANSLATION

He would beg remnants of food from such Vaishnavas, and if he did not receive any, he would hide.
