Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.59

krishnera ucchishta haya 'mahā-prasāda' nāma

'bhakta-śesha' haile 'mahā-mahā-prasādākhyāna'

SYNONYMS

krishnera ucchishta — remnants of the food of Krishna; haya — are; mahā-prasāda nāma — called mahā-prasādam; bhakta-śesha — the remnants of a devotee; haile — when it becomes; mahā-mahā-prasāda — great mahā-prasādam; ākhyāna — named.

TRANSLATION

The remnants of food offered to Lord Krishna are called mahā-prasādam. After this same mahā-prasādam has been taken by a devotee, the remnants are elevated to mahā-mahā-prasādam.
