Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.70

ihāre nāriluń krishna-nāma kahāite!"

śuniyā svarūpa-gosāñi lāgilā kahite

SYNONYMS

ihāre — this boy; nāriluń — I could not; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; kahāite — cause to speak; śuniyā — hearing; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; lāgilā — began; kahite — to say.

TRANSLATION

"But I could not induce this boy to chant the holy name of Krishna." Hearing this, Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī began to speak.
