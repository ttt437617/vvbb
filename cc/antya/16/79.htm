Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.79

rātri-dine sphure krishnera rūpa-gandha-rasa

sākshād-anubhave, — yena krishna-upasparśa

SYNONYMS

rātri-dine — night and day; sphure — appears; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; rūpa — the beauty; gandha — fragrance; rasa — taste; sākshāt-anubhave — directly experienced; yena — as if; krishna-upasparśa — touching Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Throughout the entire day and night, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu directly relished Krishna's beauty, fragrance and taste as if He were touching Krishna hand to hand.
