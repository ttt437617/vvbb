Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.57

vaishnavera śesha-bhakshanera eteka mahimā

kālidāse pāoyāila prabhura kripā-sīmā

SYNONYMS

vaishnavera — of Vaishnavas; śesha-bhakshanera — of eating the remnants of food; eteka mahimā — so much value; kālidāse — Kālidāsa; pāoyāila — caused to get; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā-sīmā — the supreme mercy.

TRANSLATION

Taking the remnants of the food of Vaishnavas is so valuable that it induced Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to offer Kālidāsa His supreme mercy.
