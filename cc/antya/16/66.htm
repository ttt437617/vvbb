Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.66

putra sańge lañā teńho āilā prabhu-sthāne

putrere karāilā prabhura carana vandane

SYNONYMS

putra — the son; sańge — along; lañā — taking; teńho — he; āilā — came; prabhu-sthāne — to the place of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; putrere — his son; karāilā — made to do; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carana vandane — worshiping the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Taking his son, Śivānanda Sena went to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at His residence. He made his son offer respectful obeisances at the lotus feet of the Lord.
