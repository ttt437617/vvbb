Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.16

patnī-sahita teńho āchena vasiyā

bahu sammāna kailā kālidāsere dekhiyā

SYNONYMS

patnī-sahita — with his wife; teńho — he (Jhadu Thākura); āchena vasiyā — was sitting; bahu — much; sammāna — respect; kailā — offered; kālidāsere dekhiyā — seeing Kālidāsa.

TRANSLATION

When Kālidāsa went to Jhadu Thākura, he saw that saintly person sitting with his wife. As soon as Jhadu Thākura saw Kālidāsa, he likewise offered his respectful obeisances unto him.
