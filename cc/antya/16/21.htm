Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.21

pavitra ha-inu mui pāinu daraśana

kritārtha ha-inu, mora saphala jīvana

SYNONYMS

pavitra ha-inu — have become purified; mui — I; pāinu daraśana — have gotten your interview; krita-artha — obliged; ha-inu — I have become; mora — my; saphala — successful; jīvana — life.

TRANSLATION

"Simply by seeing you, I have become purified. I am very much obligated to you, for my life is now successful.
