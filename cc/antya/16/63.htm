Antya-līlāChapter 16: Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu Tastes Nectar from the Lips of Lord Śrī Krishna

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 16.63

tina haite krishna-nāma-premera ullāsa

krishnera prasāda, tāte 'sākshī' kālidāsa

SYNONYMS

tina haite — from these three; krishna-nāma — of the holy name of Lord Krishna; premera ullāsa — awakening of ecstatic love; krishnera prasāda — the mercy of Lord Krishna; tāte — in that; sākshī — evidence; kālidāsa — Kālidāsa.

TRANSLATION

From these three one achieves the highest goal of life — ecstatic love of Krishna. This is the greatest mercy of Lord Krishna. The evidence is Kālidāsa himself.
