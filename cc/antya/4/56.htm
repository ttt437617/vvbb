Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.56

deha-tyāge krishna nā paī, pāiye bhajane

krishna-prāptyera upāya kona nāhi 'bhakti' vine

SYNONYMS

deha-tyāge — by giving up the body; krishna — Lord Krishna; nā pāi — I do not get; pāiye — I get; bhajane — by devotional service; krishna-prāptyera — to get the shelter of Krishna; upāya — means; kona — any; nāhi — there is not; bhakti vine — without devotional service.

TRANSLATION

"You should know that one cannot attain Krishna simply by giving up the body. Krishna is attainable by devotional service. There is no other means for attaining Him.
