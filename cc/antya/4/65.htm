Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.65

kubuddhi chādiyā kara śravana-kīrtana

acirāt pābe tabe krishnera carana

SYNONYMS

ku-buddhi — intelligence not favorable to discharging devotional service; chādiyā — giving up; kara — just do; śravana-kīrtana — hearing and chanting; acirāt — very soon; pābe — you will get; tabe — then; krishnera carana — the lotus feet of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Caitanya Mahāprabhu told Sanātana Gosvāmī, "Give up all your nonsensical desires, for they are unfavorable for getting shelter at the lotus feet of Krishna. Engage yourself in chanting and hearing. Then you will soon achieve the shelter of Krishna without a doubt.
