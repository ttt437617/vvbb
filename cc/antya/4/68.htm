Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.68

dīnere adhika dayā kare bhagavān

kulīna, pandita, dhanīra bada abhimāna

SYNONYMS

dīnere — to the humble; adhika — more; dayā — mercy; kare — shows; bhagavān — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; kulīna — aristocratic; pandita — learned scholar; dhanīra — of a rich man; bada abhimāna — great pride.

TRANSLATION

"The Supreme Personality of Godhead, Krishna, is always favorable to the humble and meek, but aristocrats, learned scholars and the wealthy are always proud of their positions.
