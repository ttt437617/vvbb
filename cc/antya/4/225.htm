Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.225

'vidagdha-mādhava', 'lalita-mādhava, — nātaka-yugala

krishna-līlā-rasa tāhāń pāiye sakala

SYNONYMS

vidagdha-mādhava — Vidagdha-mādhava; lalita-mādhava — Lalita-mādhava; nātaka-yugala — two dramas; krishna-līlā-rasa — the mellows derived from the pastimes of Lord Krishna; tāhāń — there; pāiye sakala — we can understand all.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī also compiled two important dramas named Vidagdha-mādhava and Lalita-mādhava, from which one can understand all the mellows derived from the pastimes of Lord Krishna.
