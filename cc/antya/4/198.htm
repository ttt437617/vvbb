Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.198

vastutah prabhu yabe kailā ālińgana

tāńra sparśe gandha haila candanera sama

SYNONYMS

vastutah — in fact; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; yabe — when; kailā — did; ālińgana — embracing; tāńra sparśe — by His touch; gandha haila — there was a fragrance; candanera sama — exactly like that of sandalwood pulp.

TRANSLATION

In fact, however, when Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu embraced the body of Sanātana Gosvāmī, by the Lord's touch alone there was manifested a fragrance exactly like that of sandalwood pulp.
