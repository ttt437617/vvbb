Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.80

krishna-bhakti, krishnaprema-sevā-pravartana

lupta-tīrtha-uddhāra, āra vairāgya-śikshana

SYNONYMS

krishna-bhakti — devotional service to Krishna; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; sevā — service; pravartana — establishment of; lupta-tīrtha — the lost places of pilgrimage; uddhāra — excavating; āra — and; vairāgya-śikshana — instruction on the renounced order of life.

TRANSLATION

"You will also have to explain Krishna's devotional service, establish centers for cultivation of love of Krishna, excavate lost places of pilgrimage and teach people how to adopt the renounced order.
