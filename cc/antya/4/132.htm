Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.132

maryādā rākhile, tushta kaile mora mana

tumi aiche nā karile kare kon jana?"

SYNONYMS

maryādā rākhile — since you have observed the etiquette; tushta kaile — you have satisfied; mora mana — My mind; tumi — you; aiche — like that; nā karile — without doing; kare — would do; kon jana — who.

TRANSLATION

"By observing the etiquette, you have satisfied My mind. Who else but you could show this example?"
