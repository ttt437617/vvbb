Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.124

tapta-vālukāya tomāra pāya haila vrana

calite nā pāra, kemane karilā sahana?"

SYNONYMS

tapta-vālukāya — by the hot sand; tomāra — your; pāya — on the soles; haila — there were; vrana — blisters; calite nā pāra — you cannot walk; kemane — how; karilā sahana — did you tolerate.

TRANSLATION

"The hot sand must have blistered your soles. Now you cannot walk. How did you tolerate it?"
