Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.216

saba manah-kathā gosāñi kari' nirvāhana

niścinta hañā śīghra āilā vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

saba — all; manah-kathā — decisions; gosāñi — Rūpa Gosvāmī; kari' nirvāhana — executing properly; niścinta hañā — being freed from all anxiety; śīghra āilā — very soon returned; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

Thus after finishing all the tasks he had on his mind, he returned to Vrindāvana fully satisfied.
