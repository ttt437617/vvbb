Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.38

eta kahi' rātri-kāle karena cintana

kemane chādimu raghunāthera carana

SYNONYMS

eta kahi' — saying this; rātri-kāle — at night; karena cintana — began to think; kemane — how; chādimu — shall I give up; raghunāthera carana — the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha.

TRANSLATION

"After saying this, at night he began to think, 'How shall I give up the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha?'
