Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.182

āmā-saba adhame ye kariyācha ańgīkāra

dīna-dayālu-guna tomāra tāhāte pracāra"

SYNONYMS

āmā-saba — all of us; adhame — most fallen; ye — that; kariyācha — You have done; ańgīkāra — acceptance; dīna-dayālu — merciful unto the fallen souls; guna — attribute; tomāra — of You; tāhāte — in that; pracāra — proclaiming.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, we are all fallen, but You have accepted us due to Your attribute of being merciful to the fallen. This is well known all over the world."
