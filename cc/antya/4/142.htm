Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.142

prabhura ājñā hañāche tomā' dui bhāye

vrindāvane vaisa, tāhāń sarva-sukha pāiye

SYNONYMS

prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ājñā — order; hañāche — has been; tomā' — you; dui bhāye — to the two brothers; vrindāvane vaisa — sit down at Vrindāvana; tāhāń — there; sarva-sukha — all happiness; pāiye — you will get.

TRANSLATION

"The Lord has already ordered both of you brothers to situate yourselves in Vrindāvana. There you will achieve all happiness.
