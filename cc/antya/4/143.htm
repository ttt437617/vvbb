Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.143

ye-kārye āilā, prabhura dekhilā carana

rathe jagannātha dekhi' karaha gamana"

SYNONYMS

ye-kārye — for which business; āilā — you have come; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhilā — you have seen; carana — the feet; rathe — on the car; jagannātha — Lord Jagannātha; dekhi' — after seeing; karaha gamana — go.

TRANSLATION

"Your purpose in coming has been fulfilled, for you have seen the lotus feet of the Lord. Therefore, after seeing Lord Jagannātha on the Ratha-yātrā car, you can leave."
