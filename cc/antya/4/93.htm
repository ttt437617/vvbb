Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.93

sanātane kahe haridāsa kari' ālińgana

"tomāra bhāgyera sīmā nā yāya kathana

SYNONYMS

sanātane — unto Sanātana Gosvāmī; kahe — said; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; kari' ālińgana — embracing; tomāra — your; bhāgyera — of fortune; sīmā — limitation; nā yāya kathana — cannot be described.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sanātana," Haridāsa Thākura said, embracing him, "no one can find the limits of your good fortune.
