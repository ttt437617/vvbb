Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.72

eta śuni' sanātanera haila camatkāra

prabhure nā bhāya mora marana-vicāra

SYNONYMS

eta śuni' — hearing this; sanātanera — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; haila camatkāra — there was astonishment; prabhure nā bhāya — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu does not approve; mora — my; marana-vicāra — decision to commit suicide.

TRANSLATION

After hearing this, Sanātana Gosvāmī was exceedingly astonished. He could understand, "My decision to commit suicide has not been greatly appreciated by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu."
