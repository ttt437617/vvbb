Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.20

"more nā chuńiha, prabhu, padoń tomāra pāya

eke nīca-jāti adhama, āra kandu-rasā gāya"

SYNONYMS

more — me; nā chuńiha — please do not touch; prabhu — my Lord; padoń — I fall down; tomāra pāya — at Your feet; eke — on one side; nīca-jāti — of a low caste; adhama — the lowest of mankind; āra — and; kandu-rasā — a disease of wet, itching infections; gāya — on the body.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, please do not touch me. I fall at Your lotus feet. I am the lowest of men, having been born of a low caste. Besides that, I have infections on my body."
