Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.52

prabhu āsi' prati-dina milena dui-jane

ishta-goshthī, krishna-kathā kahe kata-kshane

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; āsi' — coming; prati-dina — every day; milena dui-jane — meets both of them; ishta-goshthī — discussion; krishna-kathā — topics of Lord Krishna; kahe — speaks; kata-kshane — for some time.

TRANSLATION

Every day Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go there to meet these two stalwart devotees and discuss topics of Krishna with them for some time.
