Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.47

durdaive sevaka yadi yāya anya sthāne

sei thākura dhanya tāre cule dhari' āne

SYNONYMS

durdaive — by chance; sevaka — the servant; yadi — if; yāya — goes; anya sthāne — to another place; sei thākura — that master; dhanya — glorified; tāre — him; cule — by the hair; dhari' — capturing; āne — brings back.

TRANSLATION

"If by chance a servant falls down and goes somewhere else, glorious is that master who captures him and brings him back by the hair.
