Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.195

sanātanera dehe krishna kandu upajāñā

āmā parīkshite ihāń dilā pāthāñā

SYNONYMS

sanātanera — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; dehe — in the body; krishna — Lord Krishna; kandu — itches; upajāñā — manifesting; āmā — Me; parīkshite — to test; ihāń — here; dilā pāthāñā — has sent.

TRANSLATION

"Krishna somehow or other manifested these itching sores on the body of Sanātana Gosvāmī and sent him here to test Me.
