Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.219

sanātana grantha kailā 'bhāgavatāmrite'

bhakta-bhakti-krishna-tattva jāni yāhā haite

SYNONYMS

sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; grantha — books; kailā — compiled; bhāgavatāmrite — in the Brihad-bhāgavatāmrita; bhakta — devotee; bhakti — devotional service; krishna-tattva — Krishna, the Absolute Truth; jāni — we know; yāhā haite — from which.

TRANSLATION

Śrīla Sanātana Gosvāmī compiled the Brihad-bhāgavatāmrita. From this book one can understand who is a devotee, what is the process of devotional service, and who is Krishna, the Absolute Truth.
