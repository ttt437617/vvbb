Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.29

hena vamśa ghrinā chādi' kailā ańgīkāra

tomāra kripāya vamśe mańgala āmāra

SYNONYMS

hena — such; vamśa — family; ghrinā — hatred; chādi' — giving up; kailā — You have done; ańgīkāra — acceptance; tomāra — Your; kripāya — by mercy; vamśe — in the family; mańgala — auspiciousness; āmāra — my.

TRANSLATION

"My Lord, without hatred for my family You have accepted me as Your servant. Only by Your mercy is there good fortune in my family.
