Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.228

sarva tyaji' teńho pāche āilā vrindāvana

teńha bhakti-śāstra bahu kailā pracārana

SYNONYMS

sarva tyaji' — renouncing everything; teńho — he (Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī); pāche — later; āilā vrindāvana — came to Vrindāvana; teńha — he; bhakti-śāstra — books on devotional service; bahu — many; kailā — did; pracārana — spreading.

TRANSLATION

After renouncing everything, Śrīla Jīva Gosvāmī went to Vrindāvana. Later he also wrote many books on devotional service and expanded the work of preaching.
