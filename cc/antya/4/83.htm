Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.83

eta saba karma āmi ye-dehe karimu

tāhā chādite cāha tumi, kemane sahimu?"

SYNONYMS

eta saba — all this; karma — work; āmi — I; ye-dehe — by which body; karimu — will do; tāhā — that; chādite — to give up; cāha tumi — you want; kemane — how; sahimu — shall I tolerate.

TRANSLATION

"I have to do all this work through your body, but you want to give it up. How can I tolerate this?"
