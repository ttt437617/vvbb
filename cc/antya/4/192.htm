Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.192

dīkshā-kāle bhakta kare ātma-samarpana

sei-kāle krishna tāre kare ātma-sama

SYNONYMS

dīkshā-kāle — at the time of initiation; bhakta — the devotee; kare — does; ātma — of himself; samarpana — full dedication; sei-kāle — at that time; krishna — Lord Krishna; tāre — him; kare — makes; ātma-sama — as spiritual as Himself.

TRANSLATION

"At the time of initiation, when a devotee fully surrenders unto the service of the Lord, Krishna accepts him to be as good as Himself.
