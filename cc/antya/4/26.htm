Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.26

prabhu kahe, — "ihāń rūpa chila daśa-māsa

ihāń haite gaude gelā, haila dina daśa

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; ihāń — here; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; chila — was; daśa-māsa — ten months; ihāń haite — from here; gaude gelā — has gone to Bengal; haila — it was; dina — days; daśa — ten.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu informed Sanātana Gosvāmī, "Śrīla Rūpa Gosvāmī was here for ten months. He left for Bengal just ten days ago.
