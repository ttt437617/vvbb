Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.193

sei deha kare tāra cid-ānanda-maya

aprākrita-dehe tāńra carana bhajaya

SYNONYMS

sei deha — that body; kare — makes; tāra — his; cit-ānanda-maya — full of transcendental bliss; aprākrita-dehe — in that transcendental body; tāńra — His; carana — feet; bhajaya — worships.

TRANSLATION

"When the devotee's body is thus transformed into spiritual existence, the devotee, in that transcendental body, renders service to the lotus feet of the Lord.
