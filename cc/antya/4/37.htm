Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.37

"tomā-duńhāra ājñā āmi kemane lańghimu?

dīkshā-mantra deha' krishna-bhajana karimu"

SYNONYMS

tomā — of you; duńhāra — of both; ājñā — the order; āmi — I; kemane — how; lańghimu — shall disobey; dīkshā — initiation; mantra — mantra; deha' — just give; krishna-bhajana — devotional service to Krishna; karimu — I shall perform.

TRANSLATION

"Vallabha replied, 'My dear brothers, how can I disobey your orders? Initiate me into the Krishna mantra so that I may perform devotional service to Lord Krishna.'
