Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.190

ālińgiyā kailā tāra kandarpa-sama ańga

bujhite nā pāri tomāra kripāra tarańga"

SYNONYMS

ālińgiyā — by embracing; kailā — You made; tāra — his; kandarpa-sama — as beautiful as Cupid; ańga — body; bujhite nā pāri — we cannot understand; tomāra — Your; kripāra tarańga — waves of mercy.

TRANSLATION

"By embracing him You made his body as beautiful as that of Cupid. We cannot understand the waves of Your mercy."
