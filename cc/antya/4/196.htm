Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.196

ghrinā kari' ālińgana nā karitāma yabe

krishna-thāñi aparādha-danda pāitāma tabe

SYNONYMS

ghrinā kari' — hating; ālińgana — embracing; nā karitāma — I would not do; yabe — when; krishna-thāñi — unto Lord Krishna; aparādha-danda — punishment for offenses; pāitāma — I would have gotten; tabe — then.

TRANSLATION

"If I had hated Sanātana Gosvāmī and had not embraced him, I would certainly have been chastised for offenses to Krishna.
