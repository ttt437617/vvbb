Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.45

gosāñi kahena, — "ei-mata murāri-gupta

pūrve āmi parīkshiluń tāra ei rīta

SYNONYMS

gosāñi kahena — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu replied; ei-mata — in this way; murāri-gupta — Murāri Gupta; pūrve — formerly; āmi — I; parīkshiluń — examined; tāra — of him; ei — this; rīta — manner.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "There was a similar incident concerning Murāri Gupta. Formerly I examined him, and his determination was similar.
