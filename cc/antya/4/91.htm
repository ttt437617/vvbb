Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.91

etādriśa tumi ińhāre kariyācha ańgīkāra

eta saubhāgya ihāń nā haya kāhāra"

SYNONYMS

etādriśa — such; tumi — You; ińhāre — him; kariyācha ańgīkāra — have accepted; eta saubhāgya — so much fortune; ihāń — upon him; nā haya — is not possible; kāhāra — by anyone else.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Sir, since You, a great personality, have accepted Sanātana Gosvāmī, he is greatly fortunate; no one can be as fortunate as he."
