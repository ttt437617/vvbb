Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.35

krishna-bhajana kara tumi āmā-duńhāra sańge

tina bhāi ekatra rahimu krishna-kathā-rańge"

SYNONYMS

krishna-bhajana — devotional service to Lord Krishna; kara — engage in; tumi — you; āmā-duńhāra — the two of us; sańge — with; tina bhāi — three brothers; ekatra — in one place; rahimu — we shall stay; krishna-kathā — of the pastimes of Lord Krishna; rańge — in enjoyment.

TRANSLATION

"'Engage yourself in devotional service to Krishna with the two of us. We three brothers shall stay together and enjoy discussing the pastimes of Lord Krishna.'
