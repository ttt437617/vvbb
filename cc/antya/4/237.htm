Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.237

ei ta' kahiluń punah sanātana-sańgame

prabhura āśaya jāni yāhāra śravane

SYNONYMS

ei ta' kahiluń — thus I have described; punah — again; sanātana-sańgame — the meeting with Sanātana Gosvāmī; prabhura āśaya — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's desire; jāni — I can understand; yāhāra śravane — by hearing of which.

TRANSLATION

Thus I have described how the Lord again met with Sanātana Gosvāmī. By hearing this I can understand the Lord's desire.
