Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.46

sei bhakta dhanya, ye nā chāde prabhura carana

sei prabhu dhanya, ye nā chāde nija-jana

SYNONYMS

sei bhakta — that devotee; dhanya — glorious; ye — who; nā — not; chāde — gives up; prabhura carana — the lotus feet of the Lord; sei prabhu — that Personality of Godhead; dhanya — glorious; ye — who; nā — not; chāde — gives up; nija-jana — His servant.

TRANSLATION

"Glorious is that devotee who does not give up the shelter of his Lord, and glorious is that Lord who does not abandon His servant.
