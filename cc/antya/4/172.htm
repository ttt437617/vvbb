Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.172

tomāra deha tumi kara bībhatsa-jñāna

tomāra deha āmāre lāge amrita-samāna

SYNONYMS

tomāra deha — your body; tumi — you; kara bībhatsa-jñāna — consider horrible; tomāra deha — your body; āmāre — unto Me; lāge — appears; amrita-samāna — as if made of nectar.

TRANSLATION

"You consider your body dangerous and awful, but I think that your body is like nectar.
