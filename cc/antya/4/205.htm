Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.205

duńhe ālińgiyā prabhu gelā nijālaya

prabhura guna kahe duńhe hañā prema-maya

SYNONYMS

duńhe — both of them; ālińgiyā — embracing; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; gelā — departed; nija-ālaya — for His place; prabhura guna — attributes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kahe — discussed; duńhe — both of them; hañā — being; prema-maya — overwhelmed by ecstasy.

TRANSLATION

After embracing both Haridāsa Thākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu returned to His residence. Then Haridāsa Thākura and Sanātana Gosvāmī, in great ecstatic love, began to describe the Lord's transcendental attributes.
