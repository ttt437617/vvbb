Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.155

tāte ihāń rahile mora nā haya 'kalyāna'

ājñā deha' — ratha dekhi' yāńa vrindāvana

SYNONYMS

tāte — because of this; ihāń — here; rahile — if I remain; mora — my; nā — not; haya — there is; kalyāna — auspiciousness; ājñā deha' — kindly give the order; ratha dekhi' — after witnessing the Ratha-yātrā festival; yāńa vrindāvana — I may return to Vrindāvana.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore I see that I will get nothing auspicious by staying here. Kindly give me orders allowing me to return to Vrindāvana after the Ratha-yātrā festival.
