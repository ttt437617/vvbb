Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.232

jīva-gosāñi gauda haite mathurā calilā

nityānanda-prabhu-thāñi ājñā māgilā

SYNONYMS

jīva-gosāñi — Śrīpāda Jīva Gosvāmī; gauda haite — from Bengal; mathurā calilā — started for Mathurā; nityānanda-prabhu-thāñi — from Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu; ājñā māgilā — he asked permission.

TRANSLATION

When Jīva Gosvāmī wanted to go to Mathurā from Bengal, he requested permission from Śrīla Nityānanda Prabhu.
