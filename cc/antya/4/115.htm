Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.115

pūrve vaiśākha-māse sanātana yabe āilā

jyaishtha-māse prabhu tāńre parīkshā karilā

SYNONYMS

pūrve — formerly; vaiśākha-māse — during the month of April-May; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; yabe — when; āilā — came; jyaishtha-māse — in the month of May-June; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tāńre — him; parīkshā karilā — tested.

TRANSLATION

Sanātana Gosvāmī had come to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Jagannātha Purī during the month of April-May, and during the month of May-June Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu tested him.
