Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.14

haridāsera kailā teńha carana vandana

jāni' haridāsa tāńre kailā ālińgana

SYNONYMS

haridāsera — of Haridāsa Thākura; kailā — did; teńha — he; carana vandana — worshiping the lotus feet; jāni' — knowing; haridāsa — Haridāsa Thākura; tāńre — him; kailā ālińgana — embraced.

TRANSLATION

He offered his respects to the lotus feet of Haridāsa Thākura, who knew him and thus embraced him.
