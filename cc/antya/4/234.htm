Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.234

ājñā dilā, — "śīghra tumi yāha vrindāvane

tomāra vamśe prabhu diyāchena sei-sthāne

SYNONYMS

ājñā dilā — He gave orders; śīghra — very soon; tumi — you; yāha — go; vrindāvane — to Vrindāvana; tomāra — your; vamśe — to the family; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; diyāchena — has given; sei-sthāne — that place.

TRANSLATION

Lord Nityānanda Prabhu ordered, "Yes, go soon to Vrindāvana. That place has been awarded to your family, to your father and uncles, by Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, and therefore you must go there immediately."
