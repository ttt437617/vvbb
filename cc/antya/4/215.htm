Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.215

gaude ye artha chila, tāhā ānāilā

kutumba-brāhmana-devālaye bāńti' dilā

SYNONYMS

gaude — in Bengal; ye — whatever; artha — money; chila — there was; tāhā — that; ānāilā — collected; kutumba — to relatives; brāhmana — brāhmanas; devālaye — temples; bāńti' dilā — divided and distributed.

TRANSLATION

He collected whatever money he had accumulated in Bengal and divided it among his relatives, the brāhmanas and the temples.
