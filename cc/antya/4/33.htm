Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.33

āmā-sabā-sańge krishna-kathā, bhāgavata śune

tāhāra parīkshā kailuń āmi-dui-jane

SYNONYMS

āmā-sabā — all of us; sańge — with; krishna-kathā — talks about Lord Krishna; bhāgavata śune — hears Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam; tāhāra — his; parīkshā — examination; kailuń — did; āmi-dui-jane — both of us.

TRANSLATION

"He heard Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam and talks about Lord Krishna with us, and both of us examined him.
