Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.24

kuśala-vārtā mahāprabhu puchena sanātane

teńha kahena, — 'parama mańgala dekhinu carane'

SYNONYMS

kuśala — of well-being; vārtā — news; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; puchena — inquires; sanātane — from Sanātana Gosvāmī; teńha kahena — he said; parama mańgala — everything is auspicious; dekhinu carane — I have seen Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from Sanātana about news of his well-being. Sanātana replied, "Everything is auspicious because I have seen Your lotus feet."
