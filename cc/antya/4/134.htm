Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.134

bāra bāra nishedhena, tabu kare ālińgana

ańge rasā lāge, duhkha pāya sanātana

SYNONYMS

bāra bāra — again and again; nishedhena — forbids; tabu — still; kare ālińgana — He embraces; ańge — on the body; rasā lāge — oozing moisture touches; duhkha — unhappiness; pāya — gets; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī.

TRANSLATION

Although Sanātana Gosvāmī repeatedly forbade Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu to embrace him, the Lord still did so. Thus His body was smeared with the moisture from Sanātana's body, and Sanātana became greatly distressed.
