Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.39

saba rātri krandana kari' kaila jāgarana

prātah-kāle āmā-duńhāya kaila nivedana

SYNONYMS

saba rātri — throughout the whole night; krandana — crying; kari' — doing; kaila jāgarana — remained awake; prātah-kāle — in the morning; āmā-duńhāya — to the two of us; kaila — made; nivedana — submission.

TRANSLATION

"He stayed up all night and cried. In the morning, he came to us and submitted the following plea.
