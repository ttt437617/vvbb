Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.137

"ihāń āilāńa prabhure dekhi' duhkha khandāite

yebā mane, tāhā prabhu nā dilā karite

SYNONYMS

ihāń — here (to Jagannātha Purī); āilāńa — I have come; prabhure — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dekhi' — by seeing; duhkha khandāite — to diminish my unhappiness; yebā mane — what was in my mind; tāhā — that; prabhu — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nā dilā karite — did not allow me to do.

TRANSLATION

"I came here to diminish my unhappiness by seeing Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, but the Lord did not allow me to execute what was in my mind.
