Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.41

kripā kari' more ājñā deha' dui-jana

janme-janme sevoń raghunāthera carana

SYNONYMS

kripā kari' — being merciful; more — unto me; ājñā deha' — give the order; dui-jana — both of you; janme-janme — life after life; sevoń — let me serve; raghunāthera carana — the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha.

TRANSLATION

"'The two of you please be merciful to me and order me in such a way that life after life I may serve the lotus feet of Lord Raghunātha.
