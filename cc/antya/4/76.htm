Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.76

prabhu kahe, — "tomāra deha mora nija-dhana

tumi more kariyācha ātma-samarpana

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu says; tomāra deha — your body; mora — My; nija-dhana — personal property; tumi — you; more — to Me; kariyācha — have done; ātma-samarpana — complete surrender.

TRANSLATION

Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "Your body is My property. You have already surrendered unto Me. Therefore you no longer have any claim to your body.
