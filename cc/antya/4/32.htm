Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.32

āmi āra rūpa — tāra jyeshtha-sahodara

āmā-dońhā-sańge teńha rahe nirantara

SYNONYMS

āmi — I; āra — and; rūpa — Rūpa Gosvāmī; tāra — his; jyeshtha-sahodara — elder brothers; āmā-dońhā — the two of us; sańge — with; teńha — he; rahe — remains; nirantara — continuously.

TRANSLATION

"Rūpa and I are his elder brothers. He stayed with us continuously.
