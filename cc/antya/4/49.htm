Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.49

krishna-bhakti-rase duńhe parama pradhāna

krishna-rasa āsvādana kara, laha krishna-nāma"

SYNONYMS

krishna — of Lord Krishna; bhakti-rase — in the transcendental mellow of devotional service; duńhe — both of you; parama pradhāna — highly expert; krishna-rasa — the transcendental taste of Krishna; āsvādana — relishing; kara — do; laha krishna-nāma — chanting the holy name of Krishna.

TRANSLATION

"Both of you are expert in understanding the mellows of Lord Krishna's devotional service. Therefore you should both continue relishing the taste for such activities and chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra."
