Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.203

sei jhārikhandera pānī tumi khāoyāilā

sei pānī-lakshye ińhāra kandu upajāilā

SYNONYMS

sei — that; jhārikhandera — of Jhārikhanda; pānī — water; tumi — You; khāoyāilā — made to drink; sei pānī-lakshye — on account of this water; ińhāra — of Sanātana Gosvāmī; kandu upajāilā — You generated the itching.

TRANSLATION

"My dear Lord, You made Sanātana Gosvāmī drink the water of Jhārikhanda, and You actually generated the consequent itching sores on his body.
