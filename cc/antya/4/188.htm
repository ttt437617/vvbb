Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.188

haridāsa kahe, — "tumi īśvara dayā-maya

tomāra gambhīra hridaya bujhana nā yāya

SYNONYMS

haridāsa kahe — Haridāsa Thākura said; tumi — You; īśvara — the Supreme Personality of Godhead; dayā-maya — merciful; tomāra — Your; gambhīra — deep; hridaya — heart; bujhana nā yāya — cannot be understood.

TRANSLATION

Haridāsa Thākura said, "My dear Sir, You are the Supreme Personality of Godhead and are most merciful toward us. No one can understand what is within Your deeply affectionate heart.
