Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.79

bhakta-bhakti-krishnaprema-tattvera nidhāra

vaishnavera kritya, āra vaishnava-ācāra

SYNONYMS

bhakta — devotee; bhakti — devotional service; krishna-prema — love of Krishna; tattvera — of the truth of; nidhāra — ascertainment; vaishnavera kritya — duties of a Vaishnava; āra — and; vaishnava-ācāra — characteristics of a Vaishnava.

TRANSLATION

"You shall have to ascertain the basic principles of a devotee, devotional service, love of Godhead, Vaishnava duties and Vaishnava characteristics.
