Antya-līlāChapter 4: Sanātana Gosvāmī Visits the Lord at Jagannātha Purī

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 4.22

saba bhakta-gane prabhu milāilā sanātane

sanātana kailā sabāra carana vandane

SYNONYMS

saba — all; bhakta-gane — devotees; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; milāilā — introduced; sanātane — to Sanātana Gosvāmī; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; kailā — did; sabāra — of all of them; carana vandane — worshiping the lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

The Lord introduced all the devotees to Sanātana Gosvāmī, who offered his respectful obeisances unto the lotus feet of them all.
