Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.101

miśra kahe, "kaudi chādibā, — nahe prabhura mane

kaudi chādile prabhu kadācit duhkha māne"

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Kāśī Miśra said; kaudi chādibā — you will excuse all dues; nahe — is not; prabhura mane — the thought of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kaudi chādile — if you excuse all the dues; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kadācit — certainly; duhkha māne — will be sorry.

TRANSLATION

Kāśī Miśra said, "Excusing Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka of all his debts will make the Lord unhappy, for that is not His intention."
