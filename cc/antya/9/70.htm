Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.70

tomā lāgi' rāmānanda rājya tyāga kailā

tomā lāgi' sanātana 'vishaya' chādilā

SYNONYMS

tomā lāgi' — for Your sake; rāmānanda — Rāmānanda Rāya; rājya — the kingdom; tyāga kailā — gave up; tomā lāgi' — for Your sake; sanātana — Sanātana Gosvāmī; vishaya — material life; chādilā — gave up.

TRANSLATION

"It is only for Your sake that Rāmānanda Rāya resigned from the governorship of South India and Sanātana Gosvāmī gave up his post as minister.
