Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.73

rāmānandera bhāi gopīnātha-mahāśaya

tomā haite vishaya-vāñchā, tāra icchā naya

SYNONYMS

rāmānandera — of Rāmānanda; bhāi — brother; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; mahāśaya — a great gentleman; tomā haite — from You; vishaya-vāñchā — desire for material advantage; tāra icchā — his desire; naya — is not.

TRANSLATION

"Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka is a good gentleman. He does not desire material benefits from You.
