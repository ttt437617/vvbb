Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.126

tāńhā lāgi' dravya chādi' — ihā māt jāne

'sahajei mora prīti haya tāhā-sane'"

SYNONYMS

tāńhā lāgi' — for them; dravya chādi' — I remit the debt; ihā — this; māt jāne — He does not know; sahajei — naturally; mora prīti — my affection; haya — is; tāhā-sane — with them all.

TRANSLATION

"'Because of my intimate relationship with them, I have absolved Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka of all his debts. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu does not know this fact. Whatever I have done is because of my intimate relationship with the family of Bhavānanda Rāya.'"
