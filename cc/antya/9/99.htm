Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.99

purushottama-jānāre teńha kaila parihāsa

sei 'jānā' tāre dekhāila mithyā trāsa

SYNONYMS

purushottama-jānāre — at Purushottama Jānā, the prince; teńha — he; kaila parihāsa — made a joke; sei jānā — that prince; tāre — unto him; dekhāila — showed; mithyā — false; trāsa — scare.

TRANSLATION

"He sneered at Purushottama Jānā. Therefore the prince tried to scare him as a punishment.
