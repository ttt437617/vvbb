Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.132

'netadhatī'-māthe gopīnātha carane padilā

rājāra kripā-vrittānta sakala kahilā

SYNONYMS

netadhatī-māthe — with the silken cover on the head; gopīnātha — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; carane padilā — fell down at the lotus feet; rājāra — of the King; kripā-vrittānta — story of the mercy; sakala — all; kahilā — narrated.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka, his head covered with the silken wrapper, fell at the lotus feet of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and described in detail the King's mercy toward him.
