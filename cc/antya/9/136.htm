Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.136

loke camatkāra mora e saba dekhiyā

praśamse tomāra kripā-mahimā gāñā

SYNONYMS

loke — among the people; camatkāra — great wonder; mora — my; e saba — all these; dekhiyā — by seeing; praśamse — they glorify; tomāra — Your; kripā — of mercy; mahimā — greatness; gāñā — chanting.

TRANSLATION

"Struck with wonder by my affairs, the populace is glorifying the greatness of Your mercy.
