Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.47

viśesha tāhāra thāñi kaudi bākī haya

prāna nile kibā lābha? nija dhana-kshaya

SYNONYMS

viśesha — particular; tāhāra thāñi — from him; kaudi — of kaudis; bākī — debt; haya — there is; prāna nile — if he is killed; kibā — what; lābha — profit; nija — own; dhana — wealth; kshaya — loss.

TRANSLATION

"His only fault is that he owes some money to the government. If he is killed, however, what profit will there be? The government will be the loser, for it will not get the money.
