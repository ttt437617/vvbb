Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.112

prabhura icchā nāhi, tāre kaudi chādāibe

dviguna vartana kari' punah 'vishaya' dibe

SYNONYMS

prabhura icchā — the wish of the Lord; nāhi — was not; tāre kaudi chādāibe — he be excused from the dues; dvi-guna — twice as much; vartana kari' — increasing the salary; punah — again; vishaya dibe — he should be appointed to the post.

TRANSLATION

It was not the desire of Lord Caitanya Mahāprabhu that Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka be excused of his debt to the government, nor was it His desire that his salary be doubled or that he be reappointed collector at the same place.
