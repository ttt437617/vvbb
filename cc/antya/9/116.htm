Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.116

ethā kāśī-miśra āsi' prabhura carane

rājāra caritra saba kailā nivedane

SYNONYMS

ethā — here; kāśī-miśra — Kāśī Miśra; āsi' — coming; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; carane — to the feet; rājāra — of the King; caritra saba — all the behavior; kailā nivedane — informed.

TRANSLATION

Kāśī Miśra went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and informed Him in detail of all the King's intentions.
