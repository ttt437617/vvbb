Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.55

ethā prabhu sei manushyere praśna kaila

"vānīnātha ki kare, yabe bāndhiyā ānila?"

SYNONYMS

ethā — here; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; sei manushyere — unto that person who brought the message; praśna kaila — inquired; vānīnātha ki kare — what was Vānīnātha doing; yabe — when; bāndhiyā ānila — he was arrested and brought there.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu inquired from the messenger, "What was Vānīnātha doing when he was arrested and brought there?"
