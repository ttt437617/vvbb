Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.82

nitya āsi' kare miśrera pāda samvāhana

jagannātha-sevāra kare bhiyāna śravana

SYNONYMS

nitya āsi' — coming daily; kare — performs; miśrera — of Kāśī Miśra; pāda — the feet; samvāhana — massaging; jagannātha-sevāra — for the service of Lord Jagannātha; kare — does; bhiyāna — arrangements; śravana — hearing.

TRANSLATION

He would come daily to the house of Kāśī Miśra to massage his lotus feet. The King would also hear from him about how opulently Lord Jagannātha was being served.
