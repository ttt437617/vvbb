Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.134

kāhāń cāńgera upara sei marana-pramāda!

kāhāń 'neta-dhatī' punah, — e-saba prasāda!

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — on one hand; cāńgera upara — on the cāńga; sei — that; marana-pramāda — danger of death; kāhāń — on the other hand; neta-dhatī — the silken cloth; punah — again; e-saba — all this; prasāda — mercy.

TRANSLATION

"I was lifted upon the cāńga to be killed, but instead I was honored with this silken cloth. This is all Your mercy.
