Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.7

trijagatera loka āsi' karena daraśana

yei dekhe, sei pāya krishna-prema-dhana

SYNONYMS

tri-jagatera — of the three worlds; loka — people; āsi' — coming; karena daraśana — visited; yei dekhe — anyone who saw; sei pāya — he got; krishna-prema-dhana — the transcendental treasure of ecstatic love for Krishna.

TRANSLATION

People from the three worlds used to come visit Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu. Anyone who saw Him received the transcendental treasure of love for Krishna.
