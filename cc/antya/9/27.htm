Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.27

śuni' rājaputra-mane krodha upajila

rājāra thāñi yāi' bahu lāgāni karila

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; rāja-putra — of the prince; mane — in the mind; krodha — anger; upajila — arose; rājāra thāñi — before the King; yāi' — going; bahu lāgāni karila — made many false allegations.

TRANSLATION

"Hearing this criticism, the prince became very angry. Going before the King, he made some false allegations against Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka.
