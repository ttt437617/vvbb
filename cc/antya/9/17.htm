Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.17

"gopīnātha-pattanāyaka — rāmānanda-bhāi

sarva-kāla haya teńha rāja-vishayī

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha-pattanāyaka — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; rāmānanda-bhāi — a brother of Rāmānanda Rāya; sarva-kāla — always; haya — is; teńha — he; rāja-vishayī — treasurer of the King.

TRANSLATION

They said, "Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka, the brother of Rāmānanda Rāya, has always been a treasurer for the government.
