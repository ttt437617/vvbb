Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.66

vishayīra vārtā śuni' kshubdha haya mana

tāte ihāń rahi' mora nāhi prayojana"

SYNONYMS

vishayīra — of materialistic persons; vārtā — news; śuni' — hearing; kshubdha — agitated; haya — becomes; mana — the mind; tāte — therefore; ihāń rahi' — to stay here; mora — My; nāhi prayojana — there is no necessity.

TRANSLATION

"If I hear about the activities of materialistic persons, My mind becomes agitated. There is no need for Me to stay here and be disturbed in that way."
