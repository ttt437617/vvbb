Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.92

rājā-kadi nā deya, āmāre phukāre

ei mahā-duhkha ihāń ke sahite pāre?

SYNONYMS

rājā-kadi — the revenue of the King; nā deya — does not pay; āmāre — to Me; phu-kāre — cries; ei — this; mahā-duhkha — great unhappiness; ihāń — here; ke sahite pāre — who can tolerate.

TRANSLATION

"'He does not pay the revenue to the King, but he wants My help for release. This is a greatly sinful affair. I cannot tolerate it here.
