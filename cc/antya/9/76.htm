Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.76

tomāra anukampā cāhe, bhaje anukshana

acirāt mile tāńre tomāra carana

SYNONYMS

tomāra — Your; anukampā — mercy; cāhe — desires; bhaje anukshana — engages in devotional service twenty-four hours a day; acirāt — very soon; mile — meet; tāńre — him; tomāra carana — Your lotus feet.

TRANSLATION

"One who engages in Your devotional service twenty-four hours a day, desiring only Your mercy, will very soon attain shelter at Your lotus feet.
