Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.86

"gopīnātha-pattanāyake yabe cāńge cadāilā

tāra sevaka saba āsi' prabhure kahilā

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha-pattanāyake — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; yabe — when; cāńge — on the cāńga; cadāilā — they lifted; tāra sevaka — his servants; saba — all; āsi' — coming; prabhure kahilā — informed Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

"When Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka was lifted onto the cāńga," he said, "all his servants went to inform Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.
