Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.22

eka rāja-putra ghodāra mūlya bhāla jāne

tāre pāthāila rājā pātra-mitra sane

SYNONYMS

eka — one; rāja-putra — prince; ghodāra mūlya — the price of horses; bhāla — well; jāne — knows; tāre pāthāila — sent for him; rājā — the King; pātra-mitra sane — accompanied by ministers and friends.

TRANSLATION

"One of the princes knew how to estimate the price of horses very well. Thus the King sent for him to come with his ministers and friends.
