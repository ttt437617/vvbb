Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.61

bhavānanda-rāyera goshthī kare rāja-vishaya

nānā-prakāre kare tārā rāja-dravya vyaya

SYNONYMS

bhavānanda-rāyera — of Bhavānanda Rāya; goshthī — the family; kare — engages; rāja-vishaya — in government service; nānā-prakāre — in various ways; kare — do; tārā — all of them; rāja-dravya vyaya — spending the government's revenues.

TRANSLATION

"All the family members of Bhavānanda Rāya are engaged in government service, but they spend the government's revenue in various ways.
