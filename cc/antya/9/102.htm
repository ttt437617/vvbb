Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.102

rājā kahe, "kaudi chādimu, — ihā nā kahibā

sahaje mora priya tā'rā, — ihā jānāibā

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; kaudi chādimu — I shall excuse all the dues; ihā — this; nā kahibā — do not speak; sahaje — naturally; mora priya — my dear friends; tā'rā — they; ihā — this; jānāibā — let Him know.

TRANSLATION

The King said, "I shall absolve Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka of all his debts, but don't speak of this to the Lord. Simply let Him know that all the family members of Bhavānanda Rāya, including Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka, are naturally my dear friends.
