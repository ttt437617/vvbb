Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.106

āra bāra aiche nā khāiha rāja-dhana

āji haite diluń tomāya dviguna vartana"

SYNONYMS

āra bāra — another time; aiche — like this; nā khāiha — do not misappropriate; rāja-dhana — government revenue; āji haite — from today; diluń — I award; tomāya — unto you; dvi-guna vartana — twice the salary.

TRANSLATION

"Do not again misappropriate the revenue of the government. In case you think your salary insufficient, henceforward it will be doubled."
