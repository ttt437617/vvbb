Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.97

miśra kahe, "kaudi chādibā, — nahe prabhura mana

tārā duhkha pāya, — ei nā yāya sahana"

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Kāśī Miśra said; kaudi chādibā — you shall abandon the money; nahe — is not; prabhura mana — the desire of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; tārā — they; duhkha pāya — get unhappiness; ei — this; nā yāya sahana — is intolerable.

TRANSLATION

Kāśī Miśra hinted to the King, "It is not the Lord's desire that you forfeit the payment. He is unhappy only because the whole family is troubled."
