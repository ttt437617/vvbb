Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.125

'jānā' eta kailā, — ihā mui nāhi jānoń

bhavānandera putra-sabe ātma-sama mānoń

SYNONYMS

jānā — the prince; eta — such; kailā — has done; ihā — this; mui — I; nāhi jānoń — did not know; bhavānandera putra — the sons of Bhavānanda Rāya; sabe — all; ātma-sama mānoń — I considered like my relatives.

TRANSLATION

"'The prince created this situation without my knowledge, but actually I consider all the sons of Bhavānanda Rāya to be like my relatives.
