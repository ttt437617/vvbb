Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.109

'rājya-vishaya'-phala ei — kripāra 'ābhāse'!

tāhāra gananā kāro mane nāhi āise!

SYNONYMS

rājya-vishaya — kingly opulence; phala — result; ei — this; kripāra ābhāse — by only a glimpse of such mercy; tāhāra — of that; gananā — calculation; kāro — of anyone; mane — within the mind; nāhi āise — does not come.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka achieved the result of kingly opulence due to but a glimpse of the Lord's mercy. Therefore no one can calculate the full value of His mercy.
