Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.93

ālālanātha yāi' tāhāń niścinte rahimu

vishayīra bhāla manda vārtā nā śunimu'"

SYNONYMS

ālālanātha yāi' — going to Ālālanātha; tāhāń — there; niścinte rahimu — I shall live peacefully; vishayīra — of materialistic persons; bhāla manda — good and bad; vārtā — news; nā śunimu — I shall not hear.

TRANSLATION

"'Therefore I shall leave Jagannātha Purī and go to Ālālanātha, where I shall live peacefully and not hear about all these affairs of materialistic people.'"
