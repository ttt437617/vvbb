Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.83

rājā miśrera carana yabe cāpite lāgilā

tabe miśra tāńre kichu bhańgīte kahilā

SYNONYMS

rājā — the King; miśrera — of Kāśī Miśra; carana — the lotus feet; yabe — when; cāpite lāgilā — began to press; tabe — at that opportunity; miśra — Kāśī Miśra; tāńre — unto him; kichu — something; bhańgīte — by a hint; kahilā — informed.

TRANSLATION

When the King began pressing his lotus feet, Kāśī Miśra informed him about something through hints.
