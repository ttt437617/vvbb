Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.79

yadi vā tomāra tāre rākhite haya mana

āji ye rākhila, sei karibe rakshana"

SYNONYMS

yadi vā — if somehow or other; tomāra — of You; tāre — him; rākhite — to protect; haya — is; mana — mind; āji — today; ye — He who; rākhila — protected; sei — he; karibe rakshana — will give protection.

TRANSLATION

Finally Kāśī Miśra told the Lord, "If You want to give protection to Gopīnātha, then Lord Jagannātha, who protected him today, will also protect him in the future."
