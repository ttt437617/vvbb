Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.123

gopīnātha ei-mata 'vishaya' kariyā

dui-cāri-laksha kāhana rahe ta' khāñā

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha — Gopīnātha; ei-mata — in this way; vishaya kariyā — doing business; dui-cāri-laksha kāhana — two to four hundred thousand kāhanas; rahe ta' khāñā — spends as he likes.

TRANSLATION

"'Having been appointed collector, Gopīnātha, in the same way, also generally spends 200,000 to 400,000 kāhanas as he likes.
