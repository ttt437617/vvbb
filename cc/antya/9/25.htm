Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.25

tāre nindā kari' kahe sagarva vacane

rājā kripā kare tāte bhaya nāhi māne

SYNONYMS

tāre — him; nindā kari' — criticizing; kahe — said; sa-garva vacane — words full of pride; rājā — the King; kripā kare — was very kind to him; tāte — therefore; bhaya nāhi māne — he was not afraid.

TRANSLATION

"Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka criticized the prince. He was unafraid of the prince because the King was very kind toward him.
