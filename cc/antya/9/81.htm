Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.81

pratāparudrera eka āchaye niyame

yata dina rahe teńha śrī-purushottame

SYNONYMS

pratāparudrera — of King Pratāparudra; eka — one; āchaye — is; niyame — a regular duty; yata dina — as long as; rahe — remained; teńha — he; śrī-purushottame — at Jagannātha Purī.

TRANSLATION

As long as King Pratāparudra stayed in Purushottama, he performed one regular duty.
