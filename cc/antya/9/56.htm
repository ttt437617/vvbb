Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.56

se kahe — "vānīnātha nirbhaye laya krishna-nāma

'hare krishna, hare krishna' kahe aviśrāma

SYNONYMS

se kahe — he replied; vānīnātha — Vānīnātha; nirbhaye — without fear; laya krishna-nāma — was chanting the Hare Krishna mahā-mantra; hare krishna, hare krishna — Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna; kahe aviśrāma — was chanting incessantly.

TRANSLATION

The messenger replied, "He was fearlessly, incessantly chanting the mahā-mantra — Hare Krishna, Hare Krishna, Krishna Krishna, Hare Hare/ Hare Rāma, Hare Rāma, Rāma Rāma, Hare Hare.
