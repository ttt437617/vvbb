Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.74

tāra duhkha dekhi' tāra sevakādi-gana

tomāre jānāila, — yāte 'ananya-śarana'

SYNONYMS

tāra — his; duhkha — distressed condition; dekhi' — seeing; tāra — his; sevaka-ādi-gana — servants and friends; tomāre jānāila — informed You; yāte — because; ananya — no other; śarana — shelter.

TRANSLATION

"It is not Gopīnātha who sent all those men so that You would release him from his plight. Rather, his friends and servants, seeing his distressed condition, informed You because they all knew that Gopīnātha is a soul surrendered unto You.
