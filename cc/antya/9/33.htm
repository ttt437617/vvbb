Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.33

yei catura, sei kuruka rāja-vishaya

rāja-dravya śodhi' pāya, tāra karuka vyaya"

SYNONYMS

yei — one who; catura — intelligent; sei — such a person; kuruka — let him do; rāja-vishaya — government service; rāja-dravya śodhi' — after paying the dues of the government; pāya — whatever he gets; tāra karuka vyaya — let him spend that.

TRANSLATION

"If one is intelligent, let him perform service to the government, and after paying the government, he can spend whatever money is left."
