Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.4

ei-mata mahāprabhu bhakta-gana-sańge

nīlācale vāsa karena krishna-prema-rańge

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhakta-gana-sańge — along with His devotees; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; vāsa karena — resides; krishna-prema-rańge — overwhelmed by the ecstatic love of Lord Krishna.

TRANSLATION

Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu lived at Nīlācala [Jagannātha Purī] with His personal devotees, always merged in ecstatic love for Krishna.
