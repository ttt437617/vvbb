Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.75

sei 'śuddha-bhakta', ye tomā bhaje tomā lāgi'

āpanāra sukha-duhkhe haya bhoga-bhogī'

SYNONYMS

sei — he; śuddha-bhakta — a pure devotee; ye — who; tomā bhaje — worships You; tomā lāgi' — for Your satisfaction; āpanāra sukha-duhkhe — for personal happiness and distress; haya — is; bhoga-bhogī — one who wants to enjoy this material world.

TRANSLATION

"Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka is a pure devotee who worships You only for Your satisfaction. He does not care about his personal happiness or distress, for that is the business of a materialist.
