Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.84

"deva, śuna āra eka aparūpa bāt!

mahāprabhu kshetra chādi' yābena ālālanātha!"

SYNONYMS

deva — my dear King; śuna — hear; āra — another; eka — one; aparūpa — uncommon; bāt — news; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kshetra chādi' — leaving Jagannātha Purī; yābena — will go; ālālanātha — to Ālālanātha.

TRANSLATION

"My dear King," he said, "please hear one uncommon item of news. Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wants to leave Jagannātha Purī and go to Ālālanātha."
