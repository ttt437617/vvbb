Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.151

caitanya-caritra ei parama gambhīra

sei bujhe, tāńra pade yāńra mana 'dhīra'

SYNONYMS

caitanya-caritra — behavior of Lord Caitanya; ei — this; parama gambhīra — very grave; sei bujhe — he understands; tāńra pade — upon His lotus feet; yāńra — whose; mana — mind; dhīra — sober.

TRANSLATION

The intentions of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu are so deep that one can understand them only if he has complete faith in service to the lotus feet of the Lord.
