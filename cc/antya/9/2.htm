Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.2

jaya jaya śrī-krishna-caitanya dayāmaya

jaya jaya nityānanda karuna-hridaya

SYNONYMS

jaya jaya — all glories; śrī-krishna-caitanya — to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dayā-maya — merciful; jaya jaya — all glories; nityānanda — to Lord Nityānanda; karuna-hridaya — whose heart is very compassionate.

TRANSLATION

All glories to Śrī Krishna Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the most merciful incarnation! All glories to Lord Nityānanda, whose heart is always compassionate!
