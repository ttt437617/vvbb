Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.118

miśra kahe, — "śuna, prabhu, rājāra vacane

akapate rājā ei kailā nivedane

SYNONYMS

miśra kahe — Kāśī Miśra said; śuna — kindly hear; prabhu — my dear Lord; rājāra vacane — to the statement of the King; akapate — without duplicity; rājā — the King; ei — this; kailā nivedane — has submitted.

TRANSLATION

Kāśī Miśra said, "My dear Lord, the King has done this without reservations. Kindly hear his statement.
