Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.37

"rāmānanda-rāyera goshthī, saba — tomāra 'dāsa'

tomāra ucita nahe aichana udāsa"

SYNONYMS

rāmānanda-rāyera — of Rāmānanda Rāya; goshthī — family; saba — all; tomāra dāsa — Your servants; tomāra — for You; ucita — befitting; nahe — is not; aichana — such; udāsa — indifference.

TRANSLATION

"All the members of Rāmānanda Rāya's family are Your eternal servants. Now they are in danger. It is not befitting for You to be indifferent to them in this way."
