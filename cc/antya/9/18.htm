Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.18

'mālajāthyā-dandapāte' tāra adhikāra

sādhi' pādi' āni' dravya dila rāja-dvāra

SYNONYMS

mālajāthyā-dandapāte — in the place known as Mālajāthyā Dandapāta; tāra — his; adhikāra — authority; sādhi' — soliciting; pādi' — collecting; āni' — bringing; dravya — the money; dila — gave; rāja-dvāra — to the door of the King.

TRANSLATION

"He served in the place known as Mālajāthyā Dandapāta, soliciting and collecting money there and depositing it in the government treasury.
