Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.58

śuni' mahāprabhu ha-ilā parama ānanda

ke bujhite pāre gaurera kripā-chanda-bandha?

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; ha-ilā — became; parama ānanda — very pleased; ke bujhite pāre — who can understand; gaurera — of Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kripā-chanda-bandha — mercy upon His devotee.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this news, the Lord was very pleased. Who can understand the mercy of the Lord upon His devotee?
