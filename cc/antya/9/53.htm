Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.53

'krame krame dimu, āra yata kichu pāri

avicāre prāna laha, — ki balite pāri?'

SYNONYMS

krame krame — gradually; dimu — I shall pay; āra — more; yata — as much; kichu — any; pāri — I can; avicāre — without consideration; prāna laha — you take my life; ki balite pāri — what can I say.

TRANSLATION

"I shall gradually pay the balance as I can. Without consideration, however, you were going to take my life. What can I say?"
