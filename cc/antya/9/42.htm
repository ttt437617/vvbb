Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.42

śuni' prabhura gana prabhure kare anunaya

prabhu kahe, — "āmi bhikshuka, āmā haite kichu naya

SYNONYMS

śuni' — hearing; prabhura gana — the devotees of the Lord; prabhure kare anunaya — entreated the Lord; prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; āmi bhikshuka — I am a beggar; āmā haite kichu naya — it is not possible for Me to do anything.

TRANSLATION

Hearing this news, all the devotees again appealed to the Lord, but the Lord replied, "I am a beggar. It is impossible for Me to do anything about this.
