Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.114

vishaya-sukha dite prabhura nāhi manobala

nivedana-prabhāveha tabu phale eta phala

SYNONYMS

vishaya — of material opulence; sukha — happiness; dite — award; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nāhi — is not; manobala — desire; nivedana-prabhāveha — simply because He was informed about it; tabu — still; phale eta phala — so much of a result was obtained.

TRANSLATION

The Lord had no intention of awarding His devotee the happiness of material opulence, yet simply because of His being informed, such a great result was obtained.
