Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.34

hena-kāle āra loka āila dhāñā

'vānīnāthādi savamśe lañā gela bāndhiyā'

SYNONYMS

hena-kāle — at this time; āra — another; loka — person; āila — came; dhāñā — in great haste; vānīnātha-ādi — beginning with Vānīnātha; sa-vamśe lañā — with his family; gela bāndhiyā — was arrested.

TRANSLATION

At that time another person came there in great haste, bringing the news that Vānīnātha Rāya and his entire family had been arrested.
