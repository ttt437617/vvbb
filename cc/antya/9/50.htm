Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.50

tumi yāi' kara tāhāń sarva samādhāna

dravya yaiche āise, āra rahe tāra prāna"

SYNONYMS

tumi — you; yāi' — going; kara — make; tāhāń — there; sarva samādhāna — all adjustments; dravya — goods; yaiche — so that; āise — come; āra — and; rahe — keeps; tāra — his; prāna — life.

TRANSLATION

"Go there and adjust everything. I want only the payment, not his life."
