Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.11

bāhire phukāre loka, darśana nā pāñā

'krishna kaha' balena prabhu bāhire āsiyā

SYNONYMS

bāhire — outside; phu-kāre — with a tumultuous sound; loka — people; darśana nā pāñā — not being able to see; krishna kaha — chant Krishna; balena — says; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bāhire āsiyā — coming outside.

TRANSLATION

Being unable to see Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, the populace outside His room would make a tumultuous sound. Thus Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu would go outside and tell them, "Chant Hare Krishna."
