Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.5

antare-bāhire krishna-viraha-tarańga

nānā-bhāve vyākula prabhura mana āra ańga

SYNONYMS

antare-bāhire — internally and externally; krishna-viraha-tarańga — the waves of separation from Krishna; nānā-bhāve — by various ecstasies; vyākula — agitated; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; mana āra ańga — mind and body.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu always felt waves of separation from Krishna, externally and internally. His mind and body were agitated by various spiritual transformations.
