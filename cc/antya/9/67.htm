Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.67

kāśī-miśra kahe prabhura dhariyā carane

"tumi kene ei bāte kshobha kara mane?

SYNONYMS

kāśī-miśra kahe — Kāśī Miśra said; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhariyā carane — embracing the lotus feet; tumi — You; kene — why; ei bāte — by these talks; kshobha kara — become agitated; mane — within the mind.

TRANSLATION

Kāśī Miśra caught hold of the Lord's lotus feet and said, "Why should You be agitated by these affairs?
