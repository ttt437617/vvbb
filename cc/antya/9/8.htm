Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.8

manushyera veśe deva-gandharva-kinnara

sapta-pātālera yata daitya vishadhara

SYNONYMS

manushyera veśe — in the dress of human beings; deva-gandharva-kinnara — the demigods, the Gandharvas and the Kinnaras; sapta-pātālera — of the seven lower planetary systems; yata — all kinds of; daitya — demons; visha-dhara — serpentine living entities.

TRANSLATION

The inhabitants of the seven higher planetary systems — including the demigods, the Gandharvas and the Kinnaras — and the inhabitants of the seven lower planetary systems [Pātālaloka], including the demons and serpentine living entities, all visited Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu in the dress of human beings.
