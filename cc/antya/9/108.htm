Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.108

paramārthe prabhura kripā, seha rahu dūre

ananta tāhāra phala, ke balite pāre?

SYNONYMS

paramārthe — for spiritual advancement; prabhura kripā — mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; seha — that; rahu dūre — let alone; ananta — unlimited; tāhāra — of that; phala — result; ke — who; balite pāre — can estimate.

TRANSLATION

By the mercy of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, one can certainly become spiritually advanced. Indeed, no one can estimate the results of His mercy.
