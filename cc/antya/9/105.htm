Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.105

rājā kahe, — "saba kaudi tomāre chādiluń

sei mālajāthyā danda pāta tomāre ta' diluń

SYNONYMS

rājā kahe — the King said; saba — all; kaudi — money; tomāre — unto you; chādiluń — I excuse; sei mālajāthyā danda pāta — the place called Mālajāthyā Dandapāta; tomāre — to you; ta' — certainly; diluń — I give.

TRANSLATION

The King told Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka, "You are excused for all the money you owe the treasury, and the place known as Mālajāthyā Dandapāta is again given to you for collections.
