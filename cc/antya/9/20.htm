Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.20

teńha kahe, — "sthūla-dravya nāhi ye gani' diba

krame-krame veci' kini' dravya bhariba

SYNONYMS

teńha kahe — he replied; sthūla-dravya — cash money; nāhi — there is not; ye — which; gani' — counting; diba — I can give; krame-krame — gradually; veci' kini' — purchasing and selling; dravya — goods; bhariba — I shall fill.

TRANSLATION

"Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka replied, 'There is no money I can immediately give you in cash. Please give me time. Gradually I shall purchase and sell my gross goods and in this way fill your treasury.
