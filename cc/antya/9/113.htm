Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.113

tathāpi tāra sevaka āsi' kaila nivedana

tāte kshubdha haila yabe mahāprabhura mana

SYNONYMS

tathāpi — still; tāra sevaka — his servant; āsi' — coming; kaila nivedana — submitted; tāte — by that; kshubdha haila — was agitated; yabe — when; mahāprabhura mana — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu's mind.

TRANSLATION

When Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka's servant went to Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu and informed the Lord of his plight, the Lord was somewhat agitated and dissatisfied.
