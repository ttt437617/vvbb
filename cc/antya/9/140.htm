Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.140

prabhu kahe, — sannyāsī yabe ha-ibā pañca-jana

kutumba-bāhulya tomāra ke kare bharana?

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Lord Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; sannyāsī — sannyāsīs; yabe — when; ha-ibā — will be; pañca-jana — five persons; kutumba-bāhulya — the many members of the family; tomāra — your; ke — who; kare bharana — will maintain.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "If you all adopt the renounced order and lose interest in dealing with pounds, shillings and pence, who will take charge of maintaining your large family?
