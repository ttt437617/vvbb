Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.46

"gopīnātha-pattanāyaka — sevaka tomāra

sevakera prāna-danda nahe vyavahāra

SYNONYMS

gopīnātha-pattanāyaka — Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka; sevaka tomāra — your servant; sevakera prāna-danda — to condemn a servant to death; nahe — is not; vyavahāra — good behavior.

TRANSLATION

"After all," he said, "Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka is your faithful servant. To condemn a servant to death is not good behavior.
