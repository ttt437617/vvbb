Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.110

kāhāń cāńge cadāñā laya dhana-prāna!

kāhāń saba chādi' sei rājyādi-pradāna!

SYNONYMS

kāhāń — on one hand; cāńge — on the cāńga; cadāñā — raising; laya — takes; dhana — wealth; prāna — life; kāhāń — on the other hand; saba — all; chādi' — excusing; sei — he; rājya-ādi-pradāna — awarding the same government post and so on.

TRANSLATION

Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka was lifted onto the cāńga to be killed, and all his money was taken away, but instead his debts were excused, and he was appointed collector in the same place.
