Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.28

"kaudi nāhi dibe ei, bedāya chadma kari'

ājñā deha yadi, — 'cāńge cadāñā la-i kaudi'

SYNONYMS

kaudi — the money; nāhi dibe — will not pay; ei — this man; bedāya — squanders; chadma kari' — on some pretext; ājñā deha yadi — if you order; cāńge cadāñā — raising on the cāńga; la-i kaudi — I shall get the money.

TRANSLATION

"'This Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka,' he said, 'is unwilling to pay the money due. Instead, he is squandering it under some pretext. If you issue an order, I can put him on the cāńga and thus realize the money.'
