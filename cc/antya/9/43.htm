Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.43

tāte rakshā karite yadi haya sabāra mane

sabe meli' jānāha jagannāthera carane

SYNONYMS

tāte — therefore; rakshā karite — to save; yadi — if; haya — is; sabāra — of all; mane — in the mind; sabe meli' — all together; jānāha — pray; jagannāthera carane — at the lotus feet of Lord Jagannātha.

TRANSLATION

"Therefore if you want to save him, you should all pray together at the lotus feet of Jagannātha.
