Antya-līlāChapter 9: The Deliverance of Gopīnātha Pattanāyaka

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 9.139

śuddha kripā kara, gosāñi, ghucāha 'vishaya'

nirvinna ha-inu, mote 'vishaya' nā haya"

SYNONYMS

śuddha kripā — pure mercy; kara — kindly bestow; gosāñi — my Lord; ghucāha vishaya — let me be free from all these material opulences; nirvinna — detached; ha-inu — I have become; mote vishaya nā haya — I am no longer interested in material opulences.

TRANSLATION

"Kindly bestow upon me Your pure mercy so that I may also become renounced. I am no longer interested in material enjoyment."
