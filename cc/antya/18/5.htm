Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.5

udyāne udyāne bhramena kautuka dekhite

rāsa-līlāra gīta-śloka padite śunite

SYNONYMS

udyāne udyāne — from garden to garden; bhramena — He walks; kautuka dekhite — seeing the fun; rāsa-līlāra — of the rāsa dance; gīta-śloka — songs and verses; padite śunite — reciting and hearing.

TRANSLATION

He walked from garden to garden, seeing the pastimes of Lord Krishna and hearing and reciting songs and verses concerning the rāsa-līlā.
