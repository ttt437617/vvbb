Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.15

bhaktera prema-vikāra dekhi' krishnera camatkāra!

krishna yāra nā pāya anta, kebā chāra āra?

SYNONYMS

bhaktera — of a devotee; prema-vikāra — transformations of ecstatic emotion; dekhi' — seeing; krishnera — of Lord Krishna; camatkāra — wonder; krishna — Lord Krishna; yāra — of which; nā pāya — cannot get; anta — the limit; kebā — who; chāra — insignificant; āra — others.

TRANSLATION

Even Lord Krishna is struck with wonder at seeing the transformations of ecstasy in His devotees. If Krishna Himself cannot estimate the limits of such emotions, how could others?
