Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.39

prabhura vicchede kāra dehe nāhi prāna

anishtā-śańkā vinā kāra mane nāhi āna

SYNONYMS

prabhura — from the Lord; vicchede — due to separation; kāra — of all of them; dehe — in the body; nāhi prāna — there was practically no life; anishtā-śańkā — doubts of some mishap; vinā — besides; kāra — of all of them; mane — in the mind; nāhe āna — there is nothing else.

TRANSLATION

In separation from the Lord, everyone felt as though he had lost his very life. The devotees concluded that there must have been some mishap. They could not think of anything else.
