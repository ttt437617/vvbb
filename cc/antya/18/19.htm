Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.19

premāra vikāra varnite cāhe yei jana

cānda dharite cāhe, yena hañā 'vāmana'

SYNONYMS

premāra — of ecstatic love of Krishna; vikāra — transformations; varnite — to describe; cāhe — wants; yei jana — which person; cānda dharite — to catch the moon; cāhe — he wants; yena — as if; hañā — being; vāmana — a dwarf.

TRANSLATION

One who wants to describe the transformations of ecstatic love of Krishna is like a dwarf trying to catch the moon in the sky.
