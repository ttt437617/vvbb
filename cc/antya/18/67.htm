Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.67

ebe bhaya gela, tomāra mana haila sthire

kāhāń tāńre uthāñācha, dekhāha āmāre"

SYNONYMS

ebe — now; bhaya — fear; gela — has gone; tomāra — your; mana — mind; haila — has become; sthire — pacified; kāhāń — where; tāńre — Him; uthāñācha — have you lifted; dekhāha — please show; āmāre — me.

TRANSLATION

"Now that your fear has gone and your mind is peaceful, please show me where He is."
