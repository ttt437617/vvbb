Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.3

ei-mate mahāprabhu nīlācale vaise

rātri-dine krishna-vicchedārnave bhāse

SYNONYMS

ei-mate — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nīlācale — at Jagannātha Purī; vaise — resides; rātri-dine — night and day; krishna-viccheda — of separation from Krishna; arnave — in the ocean; bhāse — floats.

TRANSLATION

While thus living at Jagannātha Purī, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu floated all day and night in an ocean of separation from Krishna.
