Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.74

sabe meli' ucca kari' karena sańkīrtane

ucca kari' krishna-nāma kahena prabhura kāne

SYNONYMS

sabe meli' — all together; ucca kari' — very loudly; karena — performed; sańkīrtane — chanting of the holy name; ucca kari' — loudly; krishna-nāma — the holy name of Krishna; kahena — said; prabhura kāne — in the ear of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu.

TRANSLATION

They all performed sańkīrtana, loudly chanting the holy name of Krishna into the Lord's ear.
