Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.110

eteka kahite prabhura kevala 'bāhya' haila

svarūpa-gosāñire dekhi' tāńhāre puchila

SYNONYMS

eteka — this; kahite — while speaking; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; kevala — only; bāhya — external consciousness; haila — there was; svarūpa-gosāñire — Svarūpa Gosāñi; dekhi' — seeing; tāńhāre puchila — He asked him.

TRANSLATION

Speaking in this way, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu fully returned to external consciousness. Seeing Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, the Lord questioned him.
