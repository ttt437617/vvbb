Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.73

ārdra kaupīna dūra kari' śushka parāñā

bahirvāse śoyāilā vālukā chādāñā

SYNONYMS

ārdra — wet; kaupīna — underwear; dūra kari' — removing; śushka — dry; parāñā — putting on; bahirvāse — on a covering cloth; śoyāilā — put down; vālukā — sand; chādāñā — removing.

TRANSLATION

The devotees removed His wet undergarment and replaced it with a dry one. Then, laying the Lord on an outer cloth, they cleaned the sand from His body.
