Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.80

"kālindī dekhiyā āmi gelāńa vrindāvana

dekhi, — jala-krīdā karena vrajendra-nandana

SYNONYMS

kālindī — the river Yamunā; dekhiyā — seeing; āmi — I; gelāńa — went; vrindāvana — to Vrindāvana; dekhi — I see; jala-krīdā — pastimes in the water; karena — performs; vrajendra-nandana — Krishna, the son of Nanda Mahārāja.

TRANSLATION

"Seeing the river Yamunā," He said, "I went to Vrindāvana. There I saw the son of Nanda Mahārāja performing His sporting pastimes in the water.
