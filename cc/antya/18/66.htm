Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.66

tāńra sparśe ha-ila tomāra krishna-premodaya

bhūta-preta-jñāne tomāra haila mahā-bhaya

SYNONYMS

tāńra sparśe — by His touch; ha-ila — there was; tomāra — your; krishna-prema-udaya — awakening of ecstatic love for Krishna; bhūta-preta-jñāne — by thinking to be a ghost; tomāra — your; haila — there was; mahā-bhaya — great fear.

TRANSLATION

"Simply touching Him has awakened your dormant love of Krishna, but because you thought Him a ghost, you were very much afraid of Him.
