Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.24

ei-mata rāsera śloka-sakala-i padilā

śeshe jala-kelira śloka padite lāgilā

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; rāsera — of the rāsa dance; śloka — verses; sakala-i — all; padilā — recited; śeshe — at the end; jala-kelira — of pastimes in the water; śloka — verse; padite lāgilā — began to recite.

TRANSLATION

Thus all the verses about the rāsa-līlā dance were recited. Then finally the verse concerning the pastimes in the water was recited.
