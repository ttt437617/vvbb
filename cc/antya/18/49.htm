Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.49

jāla khasāite tāra ańga-sparśa ha-ila

sparśa-mātre sei bhūta hridaye paśila

SYNONYMS

jāla — the net; khasāite — releasing; tāra — His; ańga-sparśa — touch of the body; ha-ila — there was; sparśa-mātre — as soon as I touched it; sei — that; bhūta — ghost; hridaye — in my heart; paśila — entered.

TRANSLATION

"As I tried to release the net, I touched the body, and as soon as I touched it, a ghost entered my heart.
