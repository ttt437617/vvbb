Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.50

bhaye kampa haila, mora netre vahe jala

gadgada vānī, roma uthila sakala

SYNONYMS

bhaye — out of fear; kampa — shivering; haila — there was; mora — my; netre — in the eyes; vahe — flow; jala — tears; gadgada — faltering; vānī — voice; roma — body hair; uthila — stood up; sakala — all.

TRANSLATION

"I shivered in fear and shed tears. My voice faltered, and all the hairs on my body stood up.
