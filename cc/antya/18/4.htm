Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.4

śarat-kālera rātri, saba candrikā-ujjvala

prabhu nija-gana lañā bedāna rātri-sakala

SYNONYMS

śarat-kālera — of autumn; rātri — night; saba — all; candrikā-ujjvala — brightened by the moonlight; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; nija-gana — His own associates; lañā — taking; bedāna — walks; rātri-sakala — the whole night.

TRANSLATION

During a night of the autumn season when a full moon brightened everything, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu wandered all night long with His devotees.
