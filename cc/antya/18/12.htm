Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.12

pūrve yei dekhāñāchi dig-daraśana

taiche jāniha 'vikāra' 'pralāpa' varnana

SYNONYMS

pūrve — previously; yei — as; dekhāñāchi — I have shown; dik-daraśana — only an indication; taiche — similarly; jāniha — you may know; vikāra — transformations; pralāpa — crazy talks; varnana — description.

TRANSLATION

As I have previously indicated, I am describing the mad speeches and bodily transformations of the Lord only in brief.
