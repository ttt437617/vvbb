Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.41

samudrera tīre āsi' yukati karilā

cirāyu-parvata-dike kata-jana gelā

SYNONYMS

samudrera tīre — on the seashore; āsi' — coming; yukati karilā — they consulted among themselves; cirāyu-parvata — of Cataka-parvata; dike — in the direction; kata-jana — some of them; gelā — went.

TRANSLATION

When they arrived at the seashore, they conferred among themselves. Then some of them sought out Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu at Cataka-parvata.
