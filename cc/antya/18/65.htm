Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.65

premāveśe padilā teńho samudrera jale

tāńre tumi uthāilā āpanāra jāle

SYNONYMS

prema-āveśe — out of ecstatic emotion; padilā — fell down; teńho — He; samudrera jale — in the water of the sea; tāńre — Him; tumi — you; uthāilā — brought out; āpanāra jāle — in your net.

TRANSLATION

"Because of ecstatic love, the Lord fell into the sea, and you have caught Him in your net and rescued Him.
