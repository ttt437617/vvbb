Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.117

prabhu kahe, — "svapne dekhi' gelāńa vrindāvane

dekhi, — krishna rāsa karena gopīgana-sane

SYNONYMS

prabhu kahe — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said; svapne dekhi' — dreaming; gelāńa vrindāvane — I went to Vrindāvana; dekhi — I see; krishna — Lord Krishna; rāsa karena — performs the rāsa dance; gopī-gana-sane — with the gopīs.

TRANSLATION

Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu said, "In My dream I went to Vrindāvana, where I saw Lord Krishna perform the rāsa dance with all the gopīs.
