Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.77

tina-daśāya mahāprabhu rahena sarva-kāla

'antar-daśā', 'bāhya-daśā', 'ardha-bāhya' āra

SYNONYMS

tina-daśāya — in three conditions; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; rahena — remains; sarva-kāla — at all times; antah-daśā — internal condition; bāhya-daśā — external condition; ardha-bāhya — half-external consciousness; āra — and.

TRANSLATION

The Lord remained in one of three different states of consciousness at all times: internal, external and half-external.
