Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.113

ei jāliyā jāle kari' tomā uthāila

tomāra paraśe ei preme matta ha-ila

SYNONYMS

ei jāliyā — this fisherman; jāle — in the net; kari' — catching; tomā — You; uthāila — rescued from the water; tomāra paraśe — by Your touch; ei — this man; preme — in ecstatic love; matta ha-ila — became maddened.

TRANSLATION

"This fisherman caught You in his net and rescued You from the water. Because of Your touch, he is now mad with ecstatic love for Krishna.
