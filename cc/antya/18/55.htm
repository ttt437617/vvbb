Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.55

sākshāt dekhechoń, — more pāila sei bhūta

mui maile mora kaiche jīve strī-put

SYNONYMS

sākshāt — directly; dekhechoń — I have seen; more — me; pāila — has entered; sei — that; bhūta — ghost; mui maile — if I die; mora — my; kaiche — how; jīve — will live; strī-put — wife and children.

TRANSLATION

"I have seen that ghost directly, and He is haunting me. But if I die, who will take care of my wife and children?
