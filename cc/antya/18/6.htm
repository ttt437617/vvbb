Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.6

prabhu premāveśe karena gāna, nartana

kabhu bhāvāveśe rāsa-līlānukarana

SYNONYMS

prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; prema-āveśe — in ecstatic love; karena — does; gāna — singing; nartana — dancing; kabhu — sometimes; bhāva-āveśe — in ecstatic emotion; rāsa-līlā — the rāsa-līlā dance; anukarana — imitating.

TRANSLATION

He sang and danced in ecstatic love and sometimes imitated the rāsa dance in emotional ecstasy.
