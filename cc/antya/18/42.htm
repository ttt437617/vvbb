Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.42

pūrva-diśāya cale svarūpa lañā kata jana

sindhu-tīre-nīre karena prabhura anveshana

SYNONYMS

pūrva-diśāya — in the eastern direction; cale — goes; svarūpa — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī; lañā — taking; kata jana — some persons; sindhu-tīre — on the seashore; nīre — in the water; karena — does; prabhura — of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; anveshana — searching.

TRANSLATION

Svarūpa Dāmodara proceeded east with others, looking for the Lord on the beach or in the water.
