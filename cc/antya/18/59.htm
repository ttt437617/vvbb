Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.59

othā nā yāiha, āmi nishedhi tomāre

tāhāń gele sei bhūta lāgibe sabāre"

SYNONYMS

othā — there; nā yāiha — do not go; āmi — I; nishedhi — forbid; tomāre — you; tāhāń — there; gele — if you go; sei bhūta — that ghost; lāgibe — will catch; sabāre — all of you.

TRANSLATION

"Do not go near there. I forbid you. If you go, that ghost will catch you all."
