Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.26

ei-mata mahāprabhu bhramite bhramite

āitotā haite samudra dekhena ācambite

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; mahāprabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; bhramite bhramite — while wandering; āitotā haite — from the temple of Āitotā; samudra — the sea; dekhena — sees; ācambite — suddenly.

TRANSLATION

While thus wandering near the temple of Āitotā, Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu suddenly saw the sea.
