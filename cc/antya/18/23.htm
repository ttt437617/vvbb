Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.23

jīva hañā kare yei tāhāra varnana

āpanā śodhite tāra chońye eka 'kana'

SYNONYMS

jīva hañā — being an ordinary living entity; kare — makes; yei — whoever; tāhāra — of that; varnana — description; āpanā — himself; śodhite — to purify; tāra — of that; chońye — touches; eka kana — one particle.

TRANSLATION

When an ordinary living entity describes the pastimes of Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu, he purifies himself by touching one drop of that great ocean.
