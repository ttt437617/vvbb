Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.28

yamunāra bhrame prabhu dhāñā calilā

alakshite yāi' sindhu-jale jhāńpa dilā

SYNONYMS

yamunāra bhrame — by mistaking for the Yamunā; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; dhāñā calilā — began to run very swiftly; alakshite — without being seen; yāi' — going; sindhu-jale — into the water of the sea; jhāńpa dilā — He jumped.

TRANSLATION

Mistaking the sea for the Yamunā, the Lord ran swiftly and jumped into the water, unseen by the others.
