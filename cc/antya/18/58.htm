Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.58

ei bhūta nrisimha-nāme cāpaye dvigune

tāhāra ākāra dekhite bhaya lāge mane

SYNONYMS

ei bhūta — this ghost; nrisimha-nāme — by the holy name of Lord Nrisimha; cāpaye — comes over me; dvi-gune — with doubled strength; tāhāra — his; ākāra — form; dekhite — seeing; bhaya — fear; lāge mane — arises in the mind.

TRANSLATION

"This ghost, however, overcomes me with redoubled strength when I chant the Nrisimha mantra. When I even see the form of this ghost, great fear arises in my mind.
