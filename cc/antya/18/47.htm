Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.47

jāliyā kahe, — "ihāń eka manushya nā dekhila

jāla vāhite eka mritaka mora jāle āila

SYNONYMS

jāliyā kahe — the fisherman said; ihāń — here; eka — one; manushya — man; nā dekhila — I did not see; jāla vāhite — while I was working with the net; eka — one; mritaka — dead body; mora jāle — in my net; āila — came.

TRANSLATION

The fisherman replied, "I have not seen a single person here, but while casting my net in the water, I captured a dead body.
