Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.9

ei-mata rāsa-līlāya haya yata śloka

sabāra artha kare, pāya kabhu harsha-śoka

SYNONYMS

ei-mata — in this way; rāsa-līlāya — in the pastimes of the rāsa-līlā; haya — there are; yata śloka — as many verses; sabāra — of all of them; artha kare — He explains the meaning; pāya — gets; kabhu — sometimes; harsha-śoka — happiness and lamentation.

TRANSLATION

In this way, He explained the meaning of all the verses concerning the rāsa-līlā. Sometimes He would be very sad and sometimes very happy.
