Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.40

"anishta-śańkīni bandhu-hridayāni bhavanti hi"

SYNONYMS

anishta — of some mishap; śańkīni — possessing doubts; bandhu — of friends or relatives; hridayāni — hearts; bhavanti — become; hi — certainly.

TRANSLATION

"A relative or intimate friend is always fearful of some injury to his beloved."

PURPORT

This is a quotation from the Abhijñāna-śakuntalā-nātaka.
