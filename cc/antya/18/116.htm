Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.116

krishna-nāma la-ite tomāra 'ardha-bāhya' ha-ila

tāte ye pralāpa kailā, tāhā ye śunila"

SYNONYMS

krishna-nāma la-ite — chanting the holy name of Krishna; tomāra — Your; ardha-bāhya — half-consciousness; ha-ila — there was; tāte — thereafter; ye — whatever; pralāpa — crazy talks; kailā — You did; tāhā — that; ye — which; śunila — have heard.

TRANSLATION

"When we chanted the holy name of Krishna, however, You came to semiconsciousness, and we have all been hearing You speak like a madman."
