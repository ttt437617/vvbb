Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.7

kabhu bhāvonmāde prabhu iti-uti dhāya

bhūme padi' kabhu mūrcchā, kabhu gadi' yāya

SYNONYMS

kabhu — sometimes; bhāva-unmāde — in the madness of ecstatic love; prabhu — Śrī Caitanya Mahāprabhu; iti-uti — here and there; dhāya — runs; bhūme padi' — falling on the ground; kabhu mūrcchā — sometimes unconscious; kabhu — sometimes; gadi' yāya — rolls on the ground.

TRANSLATION

He sometimes ran here and there in the madness of ecstasy and sometimes fell and rolled on the ground. Sometimes He became completely unconscious.
