Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.45

jāliyāra ceshtā dekhi' sabāra camatkāra

svarūpa-gosāñi tāre puchena samācāra

SYNONYMS

jāliyāra — of the fisherman; ceshtā — activity; dekhi' — seeing; sabāra — of everyone; camatkāra — astonishment; svarūpa-gosāñi — Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosāñi; tāre — unto him; puchena — inquires; samācāra — news.

TRANSLATION

Seeing the activities of the fisherman, everyone was astonished. Svarūpa Dāmodara Gosvāmī, therefore, asked him for information.
