Antya-līlāChapter 18: Rescuing the Lord from the Sea

Bhaktivedanta VedaBase: Śrī Caitanya Caritāmrita Antya 18.36

gundicā-mandire gelā, kibā narendrere?

cataka-parvate gelā, kibā konārkere?'

SYNONYMS

gundicā-mandire — to the Gundicā temple; gelā — has gone; kibā — or; narendrere — to the Narendra Lake; cataka-parvate — to Cataka-parvata; gelā — has gone; kibā — or; konārkere — to the Konārka temple.

TRANSLATION

"Perhaps He went to the Gundicā temple, or to Lake Narendra, or to the Cataka-parvata. Maybe He went to the temple at Konārka."
